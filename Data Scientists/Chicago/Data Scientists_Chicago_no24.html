Overview:
What does this mean for your career?


Every day, our teams embrace exciting opportunities with their unique skills and talents to pioneer advancing innovation and radically change how the rail industry navigates the future. At Wi-Tronix, we are inspiring and engineering the skills of tomorrow, today and we are looking for exceptional people who share our passion and are ready to break the rules and re-imagine rail. Big ideas happen here, ideas that keep people safe and an industry we love safely on track. Our culture is about agile collaboration and working here is not just a job-it's a calling to make a difference.

What can you expect?

We have a highly collaborative culture which involves continuous exchange and vetting of ideas, in person, with Executive Staff and subject matter experts throughout the organization.

Responsibilities:
Who are we looking for:

Analysis and Strategy
  • Communicate results and ideas to key decision makers in a way that can be easily understood by business counterparts.
  • Design and build new data set processes for modeling, data mining, and production purposes.
  • Develop and productionize working prototypes, proof of concepts, algorithms, predictive models, and custom analysis.
  • Models and analyzes complex problems, discovering insights and identifying opportunities through the use of statistical, algorithmic, mining and visualization techniques.
  • Collaborate with product management and engineering departments to understand company needs and devise possible solutions
  • Develop strategies around data cleaning, data structure, and data tagging
Research, Development, Learning
  • Keep up-to-date with latest technology trends
  • Determine new innovative ways to improve data and search quality, and predictive capabilities
Qualifications:
What will you need?

  • MS or PhD (preferred) in Computer Science, Mathematics, or Statistics with related experience
  • 1+ years of experience in developing, crafting or using any deep learning frameworks
  • Python programming; Other languages, such as R, preferred
  • SQL scripting and database knowledge
  • Proficiency in computer vision, image/video/signal processing, machine learning, feature extraction, statistical analysis, and classification.
  • Solid technical foundation in CPU and GPU architectures, networking, numeric libraries, modular software design
  • Initiative, proactive thinking, problem and situational analysis, results oriented
  • Familiarity with commercial production of research and modeling
