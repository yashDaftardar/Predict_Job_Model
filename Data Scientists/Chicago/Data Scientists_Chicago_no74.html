Position Summary:

The Data Science Manager is tasked with enabling talented team members with the necessary skills and opportunities to develop in their careers.

  • Manages and provides oversight for a team of data science professionals tasked with leveraging company and external data to build solutions to high profile business problems.
  • Helps leverage company and external data to develop predictive models and other insights to solve challenging, high profile business problems.
  • Innovate the way Gallagher approaches data, Data Science and team leadership.
  • Explore and learn the wide variety of tools available to Gallagher today, and those we need to pursue in the future.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Develops, analyzes, and models operational, economic, management, accounting and other organizational data to quantify the competitive performance of business segments, evaluates potential operational changes, and designs new approaches and methodologies.
  • Applies and integrates statistical, mathematical, predictive modeling and business analysis skills to manage and manipulate complex high volume data from a variety of sources.
  • Manages staff, providing guidance, setting goals, and ensuring that overall department goals are attained.
  • Apply industry knowledge to a data science context. Build real world use cases and explain how data science is best suited to solve them.

Qualifications:

  • Degree in quantitative field, such as data science, statistics, mathematics, computer science, finance, or economics.
  • 7 years of experience composed of some combination of the following:
  • Using statistical modeling and/or machine learning techniques to build models that have driven company decision-making.
    • AND/OR Experience in insurance, insurance brokerage, or consulting for clients in this industry.
  • Working in a non-Data Science related area of the insurance industry counts towards this experience requirement so long as sufficient technical skills are present.
