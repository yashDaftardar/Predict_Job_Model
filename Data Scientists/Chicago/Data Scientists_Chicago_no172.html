Manager, Data Science & Analytics – Digital Commerce

Job Description

**This role is with our team in Chicago, however, if you live in a hub location that include Denver, Seattle, Los Angeles or Atlanta you may work remotely. If you live outside of these areas, you would need to relocate to Chicago**

Who we are

Nordstrom is reinventing the retail industry through the power of data and artificial intelligence. The vision for Data Science & Analytics at Nordstrom is to be the best data-driven fashion retailer in the world, and we pursue that vision by being relevant to the customer through best in class analytics and machine learning solutions. We are part of Nordstrom’s centralized Data Science and Analytics team and we focus on Digital Commerce (i.e., homepage, checkout, product page, brand pages, etc.) across all Nordstrom channels. We build data science products and provide analytics consulting in support of strategic business decisions in the digital experience space. We work as the brain for the Digital Commerce team with passion for supporting data-driven decision making, a devotion to technical excellence, and creative team spirit!

Data Science and Analytics helps Nordstrom thrive. Every member plays a critical role in influencing the company strategy, driving significant impact and fostering a data-driven culture throughout the organization. The ideal candidate is a creative self-starter and strong technical contributor who is always looking for new opportunities to solve business problems with data-driven tools and that is passionate about building high performance teams. We welcome your curiosity about the business, your passion to unlock rich, nuanced insights from complicated data and the desire to communicate those insights in a way that drives positive business outcomes.

At Nordstrom, we live by five simple values that guide how we work together day-to-day and how we deliver analytics & data science products. We are customer-obsessed, owners at heart, curious and ever-changing, we extend ourselves to our peers and our customers, and we’re here to win! We're proud to be named to Fortune™ magazine's list of '100 Best Companies to Work For.' We believe this recognition comes from our desire to empower our employees to set their sights high and deliver exceptional service to customers.

If the idea of making a difference in this vibrant intersection of fashion, data science, and technology excites you, join our world-class data science & analytics team!

What you'll do

  • Lead a world-class team of data scientists and analysts, focusing on building a high performance team
  • Passionate about developing people, you will attract talent, coach and mentor team members balancing between projects timeline and team members' growth.
  • Rally and motivate the team. Communicate clear and inspiring purpose.
  • Lead the team in the creation of data products that help power Nordstrom’s digital business to drive data driven decision making and continuous improvement of customer experience.
  • Support the business with timely actionable insights, ML products, and testing and measurement that drive data-informed business decisions, strategy and roadmaps.
  • Lead design and planning, scoping and breaking down work into milestones, from problem conception to deployment to production, defining metrics to measure progress and business impact of projects while providing ongoing progress visibility to stakeholders.
  • Strongly partner with product managers to influence the roadmap, proactively identify opportunities, and execute the team strategy. Prioritize opportunities and plan work.
  • Collaborate with a cross functional agile team spanning product management, engineering, user research and design to build innovative product solutions.
  • Foster data-informed decision making, hypothesis generation, and experimentation throughout the product org and broader company leading best practices in experimental design, test & learn activities, dashboarding and data product development.

Requirements and background

  • You master analytics excellence, from best practices on how to do a thorough business analysis, to A/B testing and building high quality dashboards.
  • You master the entire end-to-end ML pipeline with solid proficiency of the families of techniques for each step of the ML product development and their trade-offs.
  • Familiarity of Python and ML frameworks and libraries (e.g., spark, scikit-learn, pandas, numpy, tensorflow, pytorch, keras, theano, seaborn, matplotlib, etc.)
  • Proficiency with plotting and visualization software (e.g. Shiny, ggplot, matplotlib, Tableau).
  • Experience with SQL, NoSQL and tools such as Hadoop, Spark, and DynamoDB.
  • Refined business acumen to proactively identify opportunities and risks.
  • Demonstrated successes in providing strategic vision and partnering with stakeholders to influence roadmaps through the power of data.
  • Demonstrated success in framing business opportunities into clear ML or analytics objectives and in effectively communicating complex technical concepts to a cross-functional audience.
  • 6+ years of relevant industry experience, 4+ of which with growing responsibilities as an analyst, data scientist or machine learning engineer. 2+ years managing data science/analytics teams, ideally in an agile environment.
  • MS in computer science, engineering or equivalent STEM degree. PhD is a plus.
  • Track record of peer reviewed publications and presentations at relevant ML conferences a plus.
