Wintrust Financial Corporation (Wintrust) is a financial services company based in Rosemont, Illinois, with approximately $45 billion in assets. We engage in the business of providing traditional community banking services, commercial banking, wealth management services, commercial insurance premium financing, life insurance premium financing, mortgage origination, short-term accounts receivable financing, and certain administrative services, such as data processing of payrolls, billing and treasury management services. We provide community-oriented, personal and commercial banking services to customers located in the greater Chicago, Illinois and southern Wisconsin areas through our 15 wholly-owned banking subsidiaries.


The Senior Data Analyst is a new and highly visible position within the CRA Compliance Team at Wintrust. This position serves as a key resource within the CRA Compliance Team to develop efficient analytics, testing, and compliance monitoring activities. Successful candidate will deliver automated reporting and analytical solutions that help measure, improve, and predict the CRA Compliance risk across the enterprise.
  • 40% Develop efficient and effective CRA reporting and dashboards that can be readily replicated on a timely basis, automating where applicable. Provide consultative analysis to CRA leaders regarding data visualization options in reporting.
  • 20% Deliver on project-based, and ad-hoc, data analysis and reporting requests.
  • 15% Determine best methods to model, manipulate and present data to identify CRA program strategy. Use advanced analytical techniques to generate regulatory/risk insights that inform the Bank strategy at an enterprise level.
  • 15% Manage/develop effective and regulatory reporting meeting regulatory and compliance expectations.
  • 10% Implement enterprise data management standards within CRA Compliance Team.

Qualifications
  • Bachelor degree in Math, Statistics, Economics, MIS or other quantitative field
  • 5+ years of experience working on reporting, data mining, statistics or analytical projects in a professional setting
  • Proficiency with SQL
  • Expertise with reporting tools such as PowerBI, BusinessObjects, and Tableau
  • Expertise with a scripting language such as Python or R
  • Demonstrates skill with communicating complex concepts simplistically through reporting and data visualization
  • Demonstrates strong analytical and problem solving skills
  • Desire to work in a fast growing environment with strong time management skills
  • Drive to positively impact communities and individuals through the use of analytics
