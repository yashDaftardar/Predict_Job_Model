POSTING TITLE: Business Strategy & Transformation Leader, Data Science

Kemper is one of the nation’s leading specialized insurers. Our success is a direct reflection of the talented and diverse people who make a positive difference in the lives of our customers every day. We believe a high-performing culture, valuable opportunities for personal development and professional challenge, and a healthy work-life balance can be highly motivating and productive. Kemper’s products and services are making a real difference to our customers, who have unique and evolving needs. By joining our team, you are helping to provide an experience to our stakeholders that delivers on our promises.

POSITION SUMMARY:

Reporting to the Chief Data Science, the Business Strategy & Trasformation Leader will liaise between Kemper’s Data Science and business product and operational leaders to create measurable business impact by using advanced analytical solutions and data science tools. This newly created position is aligned with Kemper’s continued commitment to growth by using data science as a core competitive advantage. In order to optimize Kemper’s delivery of data science capabilities, the BSC will need to identify opportunities in the business and functions to leverage data science in order to improve business performance.

PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

Key responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • Ensure complete understanding of the business’ pain points, challenges, processes, data and opportunities
  • Collaborate with the Data Science team to ensure data science tools reflect business knowledge and needs
  • Communicate how modeling affects the business, while maintaining an appreciation for regulatory and business constraints
  • Operate as a business problem solver that can translate data science capabilities into business value; and data science/analytical insights into actionable business solutions
  • Follow trends in technology and analytics and make recommendations for enhancing Data Science capabilities
  • Support Project Managers in communicating and reporting project status to key client stakeholders
  • Partner with business stakeholders and IT to implement, automate and operationalize data science tools into day-to-day business decision making
MINIMUM JOB REQUIREMENTS:

The ideal candidate will be a strong relationship and advisory talent with enough conversancy and experience in data science to be credible in liaising between analytical and non-analytical stakeholders. This leader will have been a driving force in leveraging data for unique competitive advantage for an organization a have a proven track record of creating measurable business impact. The successful candidate will have insurance experience having worked in a (re)insurer, broker, consulting firm and/or insurance servicer. Prior consulting experience is highly preferred. S/he will have a combination of technical knowledge and an ability to communicate at the executive level.

REQUIRED JOB SKILLS:

In terms of the performance and personal competencies required for the position, we would highlight the following:

Technical Acumen

  • Experienced with the development, ownership, and successful delivery of a data science strategy that has resulted in tangible business outcomes.
  • Experience working with large, complex data models to extract insights. Functional knowledge of holistic data science application, development and deployment processes.
  • Strong and pragmatic approach to converting data science tools into business knowledge and intelligence.
  • Effectively balances the need for broad change to create realistic goals and implementation plans that are achievable and successful.

Executing for Results

  • Passion for delivering high-quality results and adept at translating the vision into actionable, quantitative plans.
  • Sets clear and measurable performance objectives and monitors progress rigorously, planning and linking action to business priorities.
  • Comfortable with ambiguity and uncertainty, having the ability to adapt nimbly and lead others through complex situations.
  • Be a dynamic, self-aware and humble leader whose career experience buys immediate credibility across the organization.

Communication and Presentation

  • Leads by example and engages directly with the businesses in a collaborative manner.
  • Perseveres in the face of challenges and exhibit a steadfast resolve and relentless commitment to higher standards.
  • Self-reflective and aware of his/her own limitations and drives the organization's performance with an attitude of continuous improvement.
  • Presents with persistence and humility, having a penchant for continuous learning.

Relationship Management and Influence

  • Credibility, gravitas, a strong executive presence and influencing skills to communicate the opportunities for data science across the business, as well as the technical depth to ensure that those opportunities are realized.
  • Realize that the success of an organization is a team effort and maintains connections across multiple functional and business domains to drive outcomes.
