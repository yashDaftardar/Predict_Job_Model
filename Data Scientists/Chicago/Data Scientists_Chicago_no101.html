Full Job Description

Club Champion is the largest custom golf club fitting and building company in the industry. Our goal is to help customers enjoy the game through custom-fit golf clubs that are optimized to their swing. We deliver unrivaled results by providing the best products, hiring top talent, and using advanced technology to deliver Tour-quality custom clubs for every level of golfer.

We are searching for a skilled and highly motivated Senior Financial Data Analyst who would thrive in a fast-paced startup environment. This individual will play a crucial role in maintaining and developing our data warehouse while delivering analytics and KPIs crucial for Management to run the Company. The Senior Financial Data Analyst will report directly to our CFO.

Senior Financial Data Analyst Responsibilities:
  • Data Warehouse Design: Collaborate with department leads to identify which data to bring from Salesforce and other platforms to our external data warehouse and work with third party developers to ensure data is transferred efficiently and correctly.
  • Dashboard & Analytics Design: Learn metrics needed by each business group and design dashboards that will lay out all KPIs in an easy to use interface. Test data to ensure all metrics are being calculated correctly and communicate enhancements and changes feedback from decision makers to the developers.
  • Project Management: Work with key decision makers to develop all project plans, priorities and a tracker to monitor and provide up to date statuses.
  • Data Integrity: Constantly test and monitor data in the warehouse and all metrics on dashboards to ensure accuracy.
*Data Documentation: Create and maintain a definition guide for all metrics to keep all end users informed of how analytics are calculated. *Ad-hoc analysis and Other Duties as Assigned.

Senior Financial Data Analyst Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree in Finance, IT, or other Business related field.
  • Minimum 3 years experience in data systems, analysis or finance related setting.
  • Advanced skill in the most current BI tools.
  • Excellent computer skills and expert user with Microsoft Excel.
  • Background with Salesforce preferred.
  • Excellent written, verbal communication skills, and attention to detail.
  • Analytical ability to develop and implement improvements or recommendations.
  • Able to work well with senior executives and team, as well as mentor/lead other team members.
For more information and to apply, visit our website at www.clubchampiongolf.com. We are always looking for top talent regardless of the candidates’ location.
