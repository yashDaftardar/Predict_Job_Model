Full Job Description

As a Senior Data Scientist, you will use your expertise in Data Science and our software to help ModelOp customers drive value from their AI/ML initiatives. Additionally, you will help drive the data scientist-specific features within our product, offering key input into the user experience for the product.

Employment Type

Full-time

Location

Chicago, Salt Lake City, US (remote)

About ModelOp

ModelOp, the pioneer of ModelOps software, enables large enterprises to address the critical governance and scale challenges necessary to fully unlock the transformational value of enterprise AI and Machine Learning investments. Core to any AI orchestration platform, F1000 companies use ModelOp Center to manage, govern and monitor models across the enterprise and deliver reliable, compliant and scalable AI initiatives.

Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with the sales team during the pre-sales process, which may include developing sample AI/ML models and associated monitoring, leading demonstrations and/or presentations to a data science-focused audience, or helping to troubleshoot model-related issues during a Proof of Concept.
  • For existing Customers, support the training and enablement of their data science teams in using ModelOp’s software. This may include demonstrations, presentations, troubleshooting model implementation, assisting in developing specific monitoring metrics, as well as potentially advising Customers on model development and/or productionization.
  • Serve as the voice of the data science customer, helping to identify gaps in product capabilities, and prioritize accordingly with the Product Engineering team.
  • Guide Product Engineering in defining the optimal user experience and approach for data scientist specific features in the Product.
  • Develop a library of sample AI/ML models and associated monitoring metrics for the Product.
  • Assist Product and Marketing in creating relevant product documentation and other marketing content.

Qualifications

  • 3-5+ years of hands-on enterprise experience in a data science role
  • 3-5+ years of Python experience and the related Python ML ecosystem
  • Excellent communication and troubleshooting skills
  • Demonstrated ability to translate business needs into AI/ML/analytical solutions
  • Demonstrated ability to quickly learn new technologies
  • Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics, Computer Science, Engineering, or a related degree


Desired:

  • Advanced degree in Mathematics, Computer Science, Engineering, or a related degree
  • Experience with Spark and R
  • Experience with containerization, linux, and AWS/Azure

Benefits:

ModelOp offers a full benefits, including a retirement package and comprehensive health insurance.
