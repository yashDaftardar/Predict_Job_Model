Who We Are

The Chicago office of Milliman Inc. is looking for bright, enthusiastic and qualified candidates to work in their Financial Risk Management (FRM) practice.

Milliman’s rigorous, distinctly innovative approach to risk management is built on a foundation of actuarial expertise and shaped by some of the most advanced thinking in the industry. Whether you're looking to improve capital efficiency, comply with regulatory requirements, or guard against market volatility, Milliman offers a complete range of operational, strategic, and financial risk management solutions and tools.

We have a focused, multi-disciplinary team of actuaries, financial engineers/capital markets professionals and software developers working together to develop risk management solutions for the financial services industry. We currently advise some of the world's largest insurance companies in areas such as hedging strategy and operations, portfolio risk management, and capital requirements.

We hire the best in the business—and then trust them to do their work their way. It's about personal responsibility, creativity, flexibility. We believe great work happens in great work environments.

Our culture is highly collaborative with value placed on high quality work and innovation.

The Department/Team

The Life & Annuity Predictive Analytics (LAPA) business unit is a lean, agile, diverse, and geographically distributed data science startup within Milliman. We develop leading edge data products and deploy them on the cloud as web applications for the world’s premiere life insurers.

As a Data Science Engineer on the LAPA team, you will take a leading role in performing large-scale data science and machine learning analysis. You will contribute to developing, implementing, and applying advanced statistical analysis, machine learning, and data visualization techniques to model retail customer behavior and create the next generation of analytic solutions and data products for our corporate clients. You will participate in a variety of software engineering activities including design, development, testing, and documentation.

You will work closely with other data scientists, data engineers, software engineers/developers, and domain experts to continuously improve our data collection, data cleaning, data analysis, predictive modeling, data visualization, and application development. You will also investigate, evaluate, and present new technologies and processes for the team to use.

Your Role/What You’ll Do

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Perform large-scale data science / machine learning analysis using Databricks (Spark)
  • Support the design and development of a portfolio of web applications built using the Shiny web application framework for R
  • Architect and automate processes for data extraction, transformation, and loading
  • Develop cloud-data science and machine learning pipelines
  • Contribute to R&D based activities related to advanced statistical modeling, machine learning, and data visualization
  • Document and communicate analysis and modeling results

Your Qualifications

Skills and Requirements:

Required education:

  • BS degree (MS preferred) with a concentration in computing (e.g. Software/Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Data Science)

Required skills:

  • 2+ years of experience in a data science or data engineering role
  • Software engineering skills for data science (Python, R)
  • Experience developing and applying statistical models and machine learning techniques
  • Experience with distributed computing technologies (Databricks, Spark, H2O, etc.)
  • Experience working with databases and SQL
  • Ability to work in a highly collaborative, Agile environment with a strong desire to learn
  • Ability to take ownership of a technical challenge and see it through to a successful conclusion
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills

Preferred skills and knowledge:

  • Advanced knowledge of web app development including JavaScript, CSS, and HTML
  • Strong programming skills in Python and experience with PySpark
  • Advanced skills developing and deploying production grade Shiny applications
  • Experience working with cloud data warehouses
