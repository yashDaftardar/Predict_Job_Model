BDO’s data analytics practice helps clients to grow revenue and profitability through value-driven insights. The Data Analytics Manager is an entrepreneurial-minded individual who works with the practice leads to fulfill clients’ needs within specific Business Intelligence, Advanced Analytics and Automation deliverables. The Manager is also responsible for developing work plans for clients, processing, querying and analyzing data, working with the project manager, interacting with clients, researching trends and new tools as well as programs to advance the skills and knowledge.
