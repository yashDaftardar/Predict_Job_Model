Data Scientist - Remote

Department: Client Success

Location: This is a fully remote role

Term: Full time | Permanent


Mission

Founded in 2014, FNA’s mission is to make the financial system safer and more efficient.

About the role

As Data Scientist at FNA, you will accelerate the development of our award-winning analytics software while implementing and delivering important data science solutions for some of the world's largest and most influential institutions.

Apply your curiosity of machine learning and network science whilst working with a selective team of brilliant data scientists who challenge the status quo daily and are quietly pushing forward the bleeding edge of analytics with our advanced analytics platform.

  • Actively participate as a contributing member on a team of data scientists through the whole workflow from data access, processing and modeling, to data visualization
  • Partner with internal FNA stakeholders and client subject matter experts to refine analyses, gain insights and present new findings
  • Develop and present client specific solutions clearly and concisely, communicate analyses, recommendations, status and results to existing clients and prospects at business management and executive level
  • Operationalize models into production with the FNA Platform
  • Carry out research and development of new use-cases, algorithms and solutions - either as part of our product road-map or based on specific client requirements
  • Act as product owner for specific features or models implemented in the FNA Platform
  • Resolve client support requests in a professional and timely manner

Why join FNA?

  • Rapidly advance your thinking, skills and career in a high growth tech company
  • Work remotely with an international team of innovative and agile colleagues
  • £1k/yr for training and continuing education
  • Unlimited holiday lets you take a break when you need it
  • International team off-sites in sunny locations 2x/yr (when travel permits)

Who we’re looking for

  • The right candidates will have at minimum 2+ years experience as a Data Scientist in commercial or academic projects applying Network Science & Machine Learning models and presenting your results to stakeholders
  • You should be a team player who picks up implicit tasks, is self-organized and self-motivated, embraces autonomy and working remotely but enjoys the occasional travel to interface with clients for implementations, training and use case exploration
  • The right candidate is naturally inquisitive with the ability to communicate technical findings to non-technical personnel and isn’t afraid to present creative, data-driven ideas to stakeholders
  • Passion and curiosity for what is happening within RegTech/FinTech/SupTech, Cybersecurity and Cyber Threat Intelligence, Graph Databases and especially Graph Theory/Network Science
  • Efficiently balances accuracy and speed while getting things done
  • You're a team player with tenacity underpinned by self-awareness and empathy
  • Fluent written and spoken English. Additional languages are a benefit
  • Ability to travel approximately 25% of the time (Domestic and International)
  • Nice to Have's
  • BA, MS or PhD level qualification in mathematics, economics, finance, statistics, physics, engineering or related applied quantitative field
  • Spanish bi-lingual business-level language capability

About FNA

Used by the world’s largest central banks and financial institutions, our technology enables them to uncover hidden connections in large complex datasets, visualize them via interactive dashboards and use artificial intelligence to predict and reduce the impact of anomalies and stress events within their networks.

Demand for our technology and experience has grown considerably as organizations realize the interconnected nature of their systems. As a result, we’ve spent the last 12 months expanding our team significantly to serve our customer base and boost our brand.
