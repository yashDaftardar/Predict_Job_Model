Summary:
Being the market leader in Home Dialysis, at Baxter Renal Care, we are building Next generation Data Analytics and AI platforms and solutions to further enhance remote home Dialysis therapy efficiency and effectiveness and patient and clinicians experience by connecting patients at home and clinicians at hospitals/clinics, and integrating data across the Healthcare eco system.

This Senior Data Scientist will work in collaboration with the clinical and system domain experts in understanding and building the problem statements and hypotheses to address the patient and clinical unmet needs. Additionally, will define strategy for data collection, integration, mining and processing for building analytics, predictive algorithms and models, building and testing the AI & ML based algorithms and models addressing the problem statements. This position will work alongside Regulatory and Quality specialists for pre market approvals of the AI & ML Models. Collaborate with clinicians and key opinion leaders to validate and improvise the model performance. Lastly, the Senior Data Scientist will partner with commercial specialists in building productization strategy for the AI & ML models.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Understanding, defining and building strategy for Data Collection, Integration and Storage.
  • Data mining using state-of-the-art methods
  • Collaborate with Data Engineers to enhance data collection, ingestion and curation to build analytics systems
  • Processing, cleansing and verifying integrity of data for the analysis
  • Selecting features, building and optimizing predictive algorithms and classifiers using machine learning and deep learning techniques
  • Validate the models against hypotheses and problem statements both in-house lab and in clinical collaboration
  • Develop processes and tools to monitor and analyze model performance, data accuracy and life cycle management of the predictive models.
  • Work in collaboration with Commercial experts in building productization strategy
  • Prepare and present in senior leadership technical reviews

Qualifications:

  • Master’s degree or PhD in Computer Science with Specialization in Data Science (or) Statistics (or) related field with 12+ years of experience in Healthcare (or) relevant industry working on data and building predictive algorithms and models with focus on product development.
  • Experience in statistical modelling, machine learning, deep learning, data mining, structured and unstructured data analytics and natural language processing.
  • In depth understanding and strong hands on experience in building both supervised and unsupervised algorithms using Neural Networks and Deep Learning
  • Hands on working experience in - Bayesian Modelling, Classification Models, Cluster Analysis, Recommendation Systems, Neural Networks and Deep Learning etc
  • Demonstrated experience in ML techniques like regression algorithms, K-NN, Naïve Bayes, SVM and Ensemble Techniques like Random Forest, AdaBoost etc.
  • Strong knowledge on Data Wrangling and Exploration Techniques to identify the patterns, trends and outliners.
  • Deep Knowledge and practical experience with data science toolkits – Example: R, MATLAB, NumPy, Pandas etc.
  • Understanding of Bio Design Process
  • Experience in generating Intellectual Property – Patents, Publications, Trade Secrets etc.
  • ETL Scripting and programming skills such as Python
  • Experience with data visualization tools and working knowledge with databases like Hadoop, AWS Redshift, Snowflake etc.
  • Understanding of end to end product development life cycle, preferably medical devices.
