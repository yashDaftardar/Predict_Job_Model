Description:

The Statistician collects, prepare, analyze, and interpret material requiring a comprehensive knowledge of statistics. They work under general supervision of higher-level personnel.

Duties:

1


20%


Responsible for the compilation and interpretation of statistical crime data for the UIC PD and community.



2


10%


Prepares both descriptive and tabulated reports.



3


10%


Prepare data for processing by organizing information, check for inaccuracies, and adjusting data.



4


10%


Analyzes quantitative and/or qualitative statistical data.



5


10%


Processes large amounts of data for statistical modeling and graphic analysis using computers.



6


10%


Computes and provides requested data by using statistical, graphical, spreadsheet and database software such as Excel and Access.



7


10%


Report results of statistical; analyses, including information in the form of graphs, charts, and tables.



8

10

Performs other related duties as assigned.


Qualifications:

Minimum Acceptable Qualifications:

1. Bachelor’s degree with a minimum of 6 semester hours in statistics and a minimum of 6 semester hours in algebra, geometry, calculus, or statistics at the college level.

2. Two (2) years (24 months) of work experience that required the use of statistical, graphical, spreadsheet, and database software packages, such as SAS, SPSS, Excel, and Access.


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Accuracy
  • Thoroughness
  • Knowledge of arithmetic, algebra, geometry, calculus, statistics, and their applications
  • Knowledge of logic and reasoning to identify the strengths and weaknesses of alternative solutions, conclusions or approaches to problems
  • Ability to prepare, analyze, and interpret complex statistical data
  • Ability to choose the right mathematical methods or formulas to solve a problem
  • Ability to combine pieces of information to form general rules or conclusions
  • Ability to read and understand information and ideas presented in writing
