The Data Scientist, Insights and Analytics, is responsible for providing high quality and insightful data driven solutions that address key business questions with minimal oversight. This role supports the Insights and Analytics team by designing and implementing machine learning and statistical solutions. One will evaluate business requests, determine data requirements, and identify innovative methods to provide effective data solutions that fulfill the needs of the supporting value stream. One needs to develop pipelines for both training and inference, and to operationalize solutions. We are looking for someone who is not scared of braving the unknown, learning new concepts frequently, and looking to develop their skills into a full-stack data scientist.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

  • Design and implement machine learning and statistical processes to create solutions for business partners
  • Develop end to end data science solutions for both training and inference pipelines; including data prep, feature engineering, modeling, scoring, and required business post-processes
  • Document and design solutions that are reproducible
  • Participate in pair-programming sessions
  • Periodically do code and methodology reviews for Data Scientists across the Insights & Analytics Team
  • Contribute to the Data Science Center of Excellence to standardize and grow the Insights and Analytics Team’s data science initiatives and capabilities
  • Develop as a Subject Matter Expert on all supporting function’s related data sources
  • Prepare complex data sets from multiple internal and external data sources for use in advanced analytics
  • Grow skills through company educational resources

Qualifications

Education / Training

  • Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, Mathematics, Engineering or comparable technology related field

Related Experience

  • Minimum of 3 years demonstrated successful experience in data science, data analysis, data solutions, or related business data analysis roles
  • Minimum 3 years demonstrated successful experience in food distribution, retail, CPG, or related industries
  • Minimum 3 years demonstrated successful experience with database design, data warehouse/data mining, and reporting out of enterprise applications (e.g., Oracle)

Knowledge / Skills / Abilities

Business Strategy and Analytics

  • Strong business acumen and detail oriented
  • Excellent data manipulation and analysis skills
  • Ability to think logically and analytically
  • Able to take complex, ambiguous problems, break them down into smaller parts and problem solve to come up with a whole, integrated and strategic solution
  • Creative problem solver
  • Exposure to statistical methodologies; well-versed in basic statistical approaches
  • Self-direction and Teamwork
  • High degree of motivation
  • Ability to work independently and within a team
  • Ability to build consensus and lead work streams
  • Highly organized with the ability to multitask and prioritize workloads

Communication

  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Solid presentation skills
  • Ability to communicate complex concepts clearly
  • Ability to influence change within complex organizations
  • Ability to relate to others
  • Strong knowledge of information visualization best practices and strategies

Proficiency in the following required:

  • MS Office Suite, including: Outlook, Excel (e.g., pivot tables; slicer; vlookup, hlookup; formulas; if then statements) PowerPoint (e.g., update presentations; create graphs, tables and other data visualization techniques) and Word
  • Data querying methodologies and languages (e.g., SQL)
  • Coding using open source technology (e.g., Python, R)
  • Cloud-based data warehouse environments (e.g., Amazon Web Services, Snowflake/Redshift)
  • Other data mining and preparations tools (e.g., Alteryx, Talend)
