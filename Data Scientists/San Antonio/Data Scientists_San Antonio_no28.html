Purpose of Job

We are seeking a talented Decision Science Analyst Lead for either our San Antonio, TX office or 100% remote work available.

The candidate selected for this position is going to be a part of a major effort to transform and implement the Bank’s Customer Service platform Strategy for evolving the suite of technology our Member Service Representatives (MSRs) and leaders use today to serve our members in the contact center. This technology transformation will replace many core & ancillary MSR systems over the next three years. The main responsibilities of this role are to advise program stakeholders on the overall data and analytics strategy for future analytic consumption, guide and mentor the MSR Tools Data team through the technology transformation, facilitate communication between strategic stakeholders, and oversee insight/information delivery with new source data. The role of a Lead Decision Science Analyst will drive critical initiatives across the rollouts.

Provide decision support for business areas across the enterprise. Staff in this area will be responsible for applying mathematical and statistical techniques and/or innovative /quantitative analytical approaches to draw conclusions and make 'insight to action' recommendations to answer business objectives and drive change. The essence of work performed by the Decision Science Analyst involves gathering, manipulating and synthesizing data (e.g., attributes, transactions, behaviors, etc.), models and other relevant information to draw conclusions and make recommendations resulting in implementable strategies.

Minimum Requirements:
