Purpose of Job

USAA is looking for an experienced Decision Science Analyst Leader to help drive the future of our Actuarial Modeling, Research and Data Sciences team. In this role, you'll help drive the build, implementation and integration of impactful and scalable solutions to solve a variety of product and pricing problems across our P&C Personal Lines organization. You will be responsible for architecting, designing and building solutions using big data and highly scalable data science technologies to drive business value. You’ll be working alongside our Actuarial Modeling and Research and Data Science teams from ideation to deployment, rapidly researching and testing new, innovative approaches to loss cost modeling, member segmentation and other big data problems. The ideal candidate will be highly technical (experienced with predictive modeling, machine learning, fluent in Python, etc.), an excellent problem solver and able to jump in and work with the teams to drive value in the personal lines auto, property and customer domains.

Leads and develops a highly analytical staff that provides decision support for business areas across the enterprise. By serving as a strategic partner to the business this function will be responsible for applying mathematical and statistical techniques and/or innovative /quantitative analytical approaches to draw conclusions and make 'insight to action' recommendations to answer business objectives and drive change.
