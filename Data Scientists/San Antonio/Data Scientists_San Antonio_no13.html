Description

We are looking for a Senior Data Scientist to support the scale up of our Data and Analytics team. You have expertise in artificial intelligence through use of machine learning, data mining, and information retrieval to design, prototype and build next-generation advanced analytics engines and services. You will work with partners to establish the technical problem statement and hypothesis to test; develop efficient models that mimic our decisions and incorporates them into analytical data products or tools.
You will report to the Data Analytics Manager.
You Will:

  • Work with our teams to improve data models that feed business intelligence tools, increasing data accessibility and promoting data-driven decision-making across the organization.
  • Work with collaborators to develop novel ways to meet goals using cutting edge techniques and tools
  • Communicate the analytics approach and how it will meet and address goals to collaborators
  • Advocate and educate on the value of data-driven decision-making focusing on the "how and why" of solving problems
  • Lead analytic approaches, integrating work into applications and tools with data engineers, business leads, analysts and developers
  • Create repeatable, interpretable, dynamic and scalable models that are incorporated into analytic data products
  • Engineer features to find new ways to combine different data sources
  • Share your passion for Data Science with broader enterprise community; develop long-term processes, frameworks, tools, methods and standards
  • Collaborate, coach, and learn with a growing team of experienced Data Scientists
  • Stay connected with external sources of ideas through conferences and community engagements

You Have:

  • 5+ years experience with Bachelor's in Computer Science, Engineering, Statistics, Information Technology, Informatics and required OR Graduate degree in quantitative discipline and demonstrated Data Science skills, plus 2+ years work experience
  • Python proficiency working with DataFrames and writing complex SQL queries
  • Experience operating within an Agile team
  • Experience implementing hypothesis testing
  • Have proficiency with Machine Learning to solve clustering, classification, regression, anomaly detection, simulation and optimization problems on large-scale data sets
  • Can merge and transform different all data sets together to create new features
  • Experience deploying models in AWS; Sagemaker and Spark ecosystem
  • Experience supporting deployment, monitoring, maintenance and enhancement of models
  • Experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau, R Shiny, Plotly

Our Benefits:

  • Flexible PTO, Family Medical and Disability Leaves Available
  • 401k with a generous employer match
