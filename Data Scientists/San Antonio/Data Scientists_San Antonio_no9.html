Company Overview:
With approximately $26 billion in assets under management and 11 global offices, USAA Real Estate is an industry leader in acquiring, developing, financing and managing the highest quality real estate assets in North America and Europe. The mission of USAA Real Estate is to serve the financial interests of its investor clients by strengthening the profitability and diversity of the USAA investment portfolio, which includes multifamily, industrial, office and hotel properties, as well as e-commerce logistics and distribution centers, media production facilities and data centers. Aligned with a series of strategic partnerships cultivated over decades of co-investment, USAA Real Estate engages with preeminent sector leaders demonstrating proven domain expertise in a shared drive to achieve superior, risk-adjusted returns. Job Description:
Primary Responsibilities Include :


  • Strengthen the research/artificial intelligence platform with the objective of informing and enhancing company leadership, the real estate investment process, capital raising, and client services.
  • Create proprietary analytics and technical management of the platform, including the data/software infrastructure and architecture.
  • Implement cutting-edge machine learning/artificial intelligence techniques and models to identify real estate opportunities that meet management objectives.
  • Discover and acquire new data sources to improve model results.
  • Recognize opportunities to capture proprietary internal data and design plan to capture.
  • Take active, leadership role in industry organizations.
  • Manage junior-level data scientists to achieve team objectives.
  • Communicate complex technical ideas in a non-technical manner as appropriate to key management/stakeholders.
  • Present analysis and recommendations to USAA Real Estate management and investors.
  • Work with other researchers to create publications for investors and industry journals.
  • Automate and maintain presentation materials on key market trends.
  • Attend, and actively contribute to, research team meetings and discussions.
  • Manage the development of functional and technical specifications and reports.
  • Programming capability using Python, R, SAS, or other programming tools.
  • Use in-depth economic and real estate market analysis to underpin investment recommendations.
  • Work with research team members to support the management of real estate databases and subscriptions.
  • Ensure the databases of reports, market data, and forecasts are maintained and up-to-date.
  • Perform other essential duties as assigned.
