As a Principal Data Scientist in the Supply Chain team at Petco, you'll develop and lead the data science framework for solving impactful business problems and opportunities. You will apply cutting edge, best-in-class data science methodologies to a portfolio of projects impacting the entire supply chain. This includes design and execution of predictive and prescriptive analytics, network optimization, and data democratization. Data mining and generation: Join/aggregate disparate source data and develop strategies for agile data ingestion where needed. Analytical modeling: Data modeling, exploratory data analysis, and feature selection using advanced statistical methods and techniques. Guide and mentor: Manage a team of data scientists to ensure they are correctly applying best-in-class methodologies.
