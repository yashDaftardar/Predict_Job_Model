Statistician- Real Estate Research Analyst

- (21021570)


Description - External

Job Summary: Individual contributor to research and manage the data used in decision making for store real estate tactical and strategic plans.


Essential Functions / Process Responsibilities include the following; other duties may be assigned as necessary

  • Develops automated mapping and data processes in Esri and Alteryx
  • Maintains H-E-B, competitive, and demographic datasets
  • Maintains store location packages for executive management
  • Prepares and maintains data needed for sales modeling
  • Ability to follow technical lead on specific advanced analytics projects such as predictive modeling, customer segmentation, etc.
  • Independently develops and delivers analytical solutions to meet business needs
  • Completes routine tasks as needed

Education and Experience Preferred

  • Bachelor’s Degree in GIS, Geography, or Computer Science
  • Master’s Degree preferred
  • 3+ years of experience in an advanced analytics role
  • 3+ years of experience using Esri Suite and Alteryx
  • Working knowledge of relational databases and SQL
  • Proficiency in MS Office

Key Competencies preferred

  • Advanced spatial methods and modeling with an understanding of relational databases
  • Able to work with multiple departments and collaborate as needed
  • Effective written / verbal communication and presentation skills
  • Strong analytical and project management skills
  • Strong organization and time management skills with ability to multi-task and attention to detail
  • Ability to work complex issues with senior management
  • Ability to influence different audiences
  • Demonstrates initiative and willingness to take risks
  • Possesses personal leadership skills

Physical and other Requirements

  • Function in a fast-paced, retail, office environment
  • Travel by car or airplane with overnight stays
  • Sit for an extended period of time
  • Hand / Finger dexterity
