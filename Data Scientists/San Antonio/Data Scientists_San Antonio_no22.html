Benefits & Perks

Does Plus One offer benefits? Sure: Amazing people, robot playground, buckets of data (for you AI aficionados), “eclectic” office furniture… Oh, the actual benefits? Yes, those. They’re great too:

  • Medical, dental, and eye care benefits for you and your family.
  • Paid time off: 11 annual holidays plus your birthday.
  • Vacation days – yes!
  • Professional development opportunities.
  • Flexible work hours and location.
  • Paid maternity and paternity leave

Plus One Robotics is looking for a Senior Developer – AI to assist with the development of our artificial intelligence for robots in logistics and e-commerce applications. As part of our Product Development team, you will hit the ground running to plan, develop, test and support the deployment of our Pick One and Yonder software.

As a member of the R&D staff, you will research and evaluate the state of the art in AI/ML-based computer vision. You will also propose and execute projects to advance the state of the art and ensure Plus One Robotics maintains it’s lead in performance, quality, and reliability.

Degree focusing in a field such as computer science, machine learning, robotics or related field with a focus on machine vision. Must have the following experience in a product R&D capacity:

  • PhD – 0-2 years’ experience
  • Masters – 3-7 years’ experience
  • Bachelors – 5-9 years’ experience

Experience with the following:

  • AI and traditional computer vision approaches for robotic applications (object detection, segmentation and classification)
