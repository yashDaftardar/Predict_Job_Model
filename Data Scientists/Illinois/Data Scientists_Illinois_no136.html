A bout Magnolia Capital
Magnolia Capital is a real estate investment company focused on multifamily assets within the United States. The company employs a data-driven, relative value investment strategy to identify compelling, institutional-quality multifamily investment opportunities in primary and secondary markets in the United States. Based in Chicago, Magnolia Capital was founded in 2016 by three seasoned partners with more than $2.5 billion in transaction experience within the multifamily industry.

For more information, please visit magnoliacap.com
We are looking for looking for a Data Science Intern for the summer of 2021 to assist the Product Development and Data Science team with a pipeline of projects. This position is based in Chicago beginning May 31st through August 13th 2021.

Essential functions
  • Working with our team as we use data to find the answers to specific questions raised by stakeholders from across the business, through advanced analytics and data extraction techniques
  • Will be focused on helping us to improve and test the robustness of our predictive models which helps us identify markets that our investments teams should focus on.
  • Work closely with data scientists to design and develop data mining techniques to solve the real problems.
  • Participate in engagement and discovery sessions between technology team members to identify problems/gaps as well as innovation opportunities that can be solved or enabled by the data analytics and modeling.
  • Be encouraged and expected to innovate and be creative in your data analysis, problem solving and presentation of solutions.
  • Create and improve processes, tools and workflows to scrape web content for market research purposes.

Who we are looking for
  • Someone pursuing a BS/MS in Computer Science or Mathematics
  • Self-starter with a can-do attitude excited to work in a small but growing technical team
  • Someone willing to bring their own ideas to shape the future direction of technology development
  • Bonus points for interest or knowledge of real estate
