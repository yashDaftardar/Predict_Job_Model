Summary:

The Data Science Manager is responsible for leveraging structured and unstructured data sets in order to gain critical insights on how to drive WOWâ€™s networks, systems, and products forward. This individual should be an expert data scientist while also being able to lead small teams of resources as projects needs arise. Additionally, this role is responsible for creating machine learning models that help to better understand network capacity, system performance, or licensing consumption across out platforms. This role leverages data to identify opportunities for improvement and ultimately increase customer satisfaction.

Duties and Responsibilities:

Essential duties and responsibilities include but are not limited to those listed below:

  • Participate in several scrum teams focused on products, networks, and infrastructure to get a better understanding of the needs and opportunities of our engineering teams and ultimately our business
  • Collaborate with peers and stakeholders in and outside of the engineering team to understand all the sources of data that can be utilized and analyzed
  • Drives data collection and enablement for improvements in analytical capabilities and reporting in conjunction with engineers, developers and analysts across WOW
  • Create and curate machine learning and data modeling practices for the engineering team
  • Continuously monitor and curate data visualization tools and ensure they support presenting data to leaders and stakeholders in an easy to understand way
  • Drive and curate tools and systems that validate model performance and data accuracy
  • Utilize and monitor data sets that drive automated network and system repair to get to a state of self-healing networks and services.
  • Ensures that all system data and telemetry is appropriately consumed and utilized in analytical activities
  • Develops and maintains reports and visualizations for peers, leaders, and stakeholders and makes recommendations on potential actions from the data
  • Builds and creates self-service reporting and visualization capabilities to ensure that critical business functions can be continuously monitored.
  • Lead small teams of resources for short or long term projects as business needs justify
  • Other duties as assigned
