Consults with users to define detailed requirements, analyze business needs, consider current and future business implications, translate functional into technical requirements, validate solutions, and drive business process improvement efforts.

Manages complex projects independently. Uses machine learning techniques to develop predictive models. Designs, develops, and deploys algorithms, models, and workflows to discover valuable information in large volumes of data. Reviews and constructs models to create business applications while working closely with ARD clients and leaders to aid in strategic business planning.

Specific Responsibilities :

Strategic Planning

  • Consults with junior analysts, end users and peers to document and analyze functional requirements, identify gaps and alternative approaches to meet requirements, and ensure all deliverables completed (adhering to approved methodology and documentation standards).
  • Consults with functional users and reporting staff to determine best approaches, ensuring that approved reporting solutions are implemented in a timely and correct fashion.
  • Assists with the design of the platforms and tools to enhance the organization's analytic capabilities.

Administration

  • Participates in requirements gathering meetings in partnership with the Business Analyst to ensure the scope of the request is well documented.
  • Leads the efforts in querying and cleaning the data for predictive modeling.
  • Supports the development of the strategy and vision for large data analysis projects

Development

  • Uses machine learning techniques to develop predictive models.
  • Designs, develops, and deploys algorithms, models, and workflows to discover valuable information in large volumes of data.
  • Creates data visualization and dashboards in Tableau/Excel/R.

Performance

  • Collects feedback from users on application performance and escalates complex critical performance issues with technical resources and management.
  • Facilitates troubleshooting with technical, reporting and application resources to analyze and resolve production issues in a timely manner. Able to identify root causes based on prior system knowledge and advanced analytical skills.

Supervises

  • Coaches Associate Data Scientist.
  • Manages moderate to complex projects ensuring timelines, deliverables and expectations are met.

Miscellaneous

  • Performs other duties as assigned.

  • 4 years functional/business/system analysis or other relevant experience required.
