Position Description:

As Vice President of Data Science , you will be responsible for bringing the passion, work ethic and expertise needed to drive enterprise data strategy and operational efficiencies by leveraging data at a fast-growing tech-driven logistics business. You love leading a combination of data engineering, data science and machine learning teams that will be laser focused on driving customer and internal synergies. You are passionate about selecting and delivering new technology solutions in a cost effective and timely manner. You thrive on leading, influencing, and motivating teams to be focused on delivery, improving our technology solutions & adoption.


Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with key Business and Technology stakeholders to develop and execute on data science program roadmaps
  • Lead, manage, and further build out our current team of data engineers and data analysts, as well as our data scientists and ML engineers
  • Lead the data science team, which includes data, model and supporting analytics development
  • Lead Redwood's data strategy including data storage, data modeling, data engineering for use in analytics, data process automation, and business continuity
  • Work with leaders in our product, program and engineering teams to determine where data science can be used to enhance and grow our solutions
  • Evaluate talent; coach and mentor team to build a world class data science organization with a specific focus on digital dexterity
  • Evaluate and report on accomplishments to senior business leadership, including C-level executives and program steerco representation
  • Become a data expert for the logistics industry; focus on understanding pricing correlations to increase our yields

Qualifications:

  • 10+ years of experience or equivalent combination of education, qualifications, and experience
  • Master's Degree, Concentration in Math, Statistics, Physics, Engineering, Computer Science, Econometrics preferred
  • 3-5 years of experience managing a team at an Enterprise larger than $1B
  • Expert experience with cloud providers (AWS, Azure) and their machine learning tools
  • Must be obsessed with delivering results, insist on high standards, possess a knack for thinking big and "finding a way"
  • Past track record of innovation and implementation of emerging technology and capabilities
  • Experience with developing the design and operation of large, complex data structures and systems
  • Expert experience with programming languages such as Python, R and C(++, #); and query languages such as SQL
  • Prior experience in demonstrating ROI impact to business
