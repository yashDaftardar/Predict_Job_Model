With a recently completed 30,000 square-foot office and lab space in Chicago’s Lincoln Park neighborhood, we are embracing revolutionary science and artificial intelligence to find answers to critical global challenges like increasing crop yields, curing diseases, making tomorrow’s materials and cleaning our environment. Learn more about who we are, visit www.evozyne.com.

Job Summary:

The primary focus of this position is the development of deep learning tools to learn protein design rules from protein databases, create generative models of synthetic protein sequences, construct supervised models linking protein sequence to measured function, and packaging of these tools within user friendly software. The position will interface closely with the experimental gene synthesis and assay team in order to provide close coupling between the computational and experimental design loop. The successful candidate will lead the computational and software development efforts at Evozyne, train other team members, and contribute to the overall development of the company.

Responsibilities:

  • Construct deep learning tools to extract protein design rules from protein sequence databases.
  • Build supervised and unsupervised models for the computational design of synthetic protein sequences with desired structure and function.
  • Develop these tools into scalable and efficient software packages with user-friendly frontends.
  • Interface closely with the experimental gene synthesis and assay teams to integrate and execute the design-build-test cycle.
  • Lead small teams including training technicians and other staff.
  • Assist in analyzing prospective projects from a bioinformatics perspective and help to draft proposals for partners/clients.
  • Communicate results within the company and to external partners.
  • Be a productive collaborator in our company.

Competencies:

  • Strong skills and extensive experience in Python, C/C++, and bash programming.
  • Experience with Python machine learning libraries scikit-learn, PyTorch, Keras, TensorFlow.
  • Strong deep learning experience and familiarity with generative models and natural language processing.
  • Experience in software development and deployment (e.g., GitHub).
  • Familiarity with version control using git.
  • Familiarity with high-performance CPU and GPU computing in cluster and cloud environments, such as AWS or Azure.
  • Familiarity with Linux / Unix.
  • Strong written and oral communication skills.
  • Ability to work and solve problems in interdisciplinary teams.

Required Education, Experience and Certifications:

  • Education: A Masters or PhD degree in machine learning, computer science, electrical and computer engineering, applied mathematics, statistics, or allied field.
  • Experience: The ideal candidate will have prior experience in development of deep learning models for sequential data and/or NLP and software development.
  • Compensation will be determined based on experience and qualifications.
