Company Overview

Proudly Farmers First℠!

What do you get when you cross the best of Silicon Valley innovation and technology with the value driven mission of Family Farmers around the world?

Farmers Business Network, Inc. (FBN℠)!

FBN℠ was created by farmers for farmers and is on a mission to power the prosperity of Family Farmers around the world by leveling the playing field through insights, commerce and community. Our goal is to enable them to make smart business decisions that maximize their farms profit potential for generations to come, helping to ensure the fundamental economic viability and sustainability of family farms and rural communities.

Role and Responsibilities

Data is the foundation of FBN, core to who we are and what we do. Our farmer-facing digital tools are powered by massive geospatial datasets and sophisticated models, our R&D initiatives are powered by data science, and our internal company operations depend on effectively leveraging data to guide decisions. We are looking for an exceptional leader to lead our analytics and data science organization. In this role, you'll:

  • Build, develop, and mentor a team of:
    • Analytics engineers, who will build and operate our analytics infrastructure. This includes centralizing internal data in a data warehouse and building the right tools and systems for accessing that data and ensuring its quality.
    • Data analysts, who will be responsible for developing dashboards that enable colleagues across the business to easily track and report on key performance metrics.
    • Data scientists, who will leverage statistical modeling, machine learning, and agronomic/crop science expertise to develop actionable insights that power both internal and external-facing applications.
  • Collaborate closely with colleagues in product, R&D, marketing, sales, logistics, finance and other teams, serving as a thought partner to identify ways in which data and data science can power our products, commercial offerings, and company strategy.
  • Evangelize the benefits of using data to guide decisions, and foster a data-driven culture throughout the company by providing tools that enable self-service analysis.
  • Help your team distill the findings of data analysis projects into crisp communications that enable technical and non-technical audiences to use the results to inform key business decisions.
  • Work with your team and key stakeholders to plan, prioritize, and execute analytical projects.

What We're Looking for

  • 5+ years of experience leading teams comprising analytics engineers, data analysts, and data scientists, ideally at high-growth technology companies.
  • 7+ years of experience in data analytics, data science, or business intelligence roles.
  • A track record of building and growing exceptional teams
  • Experience working in roles requiring the communication of data-driven insights with non-technical audiences.
  • Demonstrated success in collaborating and building relationships with diverse business stakeholders across the organization.
  • Strong understanding of the fundamentals of analytics tools and systems, including data warehouses, dashboard and reporting tools, statistics, and machine learning.
  • Experience working in roles where productized data science analyses power key customer-facing products or services is a plus.
  • Experience working in agriculture is a big plus, but not required.

COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS

Compensation and title for this position may be adjusted to suit your experience and qualifications. We also offer an attractive benefits package that includes health, vision, and dental insurance.
