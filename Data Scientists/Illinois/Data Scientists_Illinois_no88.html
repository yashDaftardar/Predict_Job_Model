Who We Are:
MBuy is the leading everychannel media partner, the only managed service on the largest proprietary tech stack in the world – we plan, buy, and optimize your campaigns across all traditional and digital media types. We help our clients plan, buy, and optimize everychannel campaigns that engage consumers in a constantly evolving media landscape. We provide advertisers with the only managed service on Mediaocean software – MBuy operates on the largest media platform in the world that enables cross-channel buying, media budget savings, real-time optimization, and workflow solutions.

What You Will Do:
As a member of the Data Science & Product team, you will work on cutting edge industry problems and will be responsible for the success of our most influential clients. Your role will include various exciting responsibilities that involve Marketing Strategy, Campaign Optimization, Product Development, Business Intelligence, Data Management, and Decision Science. In simple terms, you’re going to apply data, technology, and strategy to transform how we service our clients and their marketing initiatives/investments.
Our mission is to develop solutions that will make a difference in the world of marketing while driving customer success, business growth, and just being flat out awesome at everything we do. If you’re a rock star, with Sherlock Homes-Like powers of observation, then you’re in good company. The ideal candidate for us is someone who is continuously looking to find better solutions and is not afraid to question the status quo. A true leader who shows empathy, thoughtfulness, loves solving problems and would devour data for breakfast!

Responsibilities will include:
  • Developing a thorough understanding of all digital advertising technology related to desktop, video, mobile, and social channels
  • Leading the transformation and translation of data into insights, recommendations, and results
  • Owning campaign performance and strategy for our clients, while setting goals for a measure of success
  • Collaborating with Account Managers to ensure campaigns are setup and positioned to best meet client needs
  • Consulting clients to understand their endpoint marketing objectives
  • Designing, developing and maintaining dashboards for tracking key metrics and benchmarks
  • Recommending technologies and products to improve company-wide processes
  • Helping MBuy become the only agency whose entire workforce is data-literate

Who You Are:

  • Practice empathy and have a passion for making others better
  • The idea of every day being different excites you
  • You believe in having standards, strong principles and values
  • Preference for delivering simple and effective solutions rather than complex and perfect
  • A desire to continuously learn and challenge yourself and a readiness to step out of your comfort zone
