This position will aid in the development, management, and manipulation of structured healthcare data with a focus on building advanced analytics models to support a variety of sales initiatives. Responsibilities include, creating and manipulating analytics models, developing data solutions, performing custom analysis, interpreting results, developing insights, and presenting recommendations. Additional responsibilities may include partnering with internal stakeholders to understand data requirements and develop tools and models such as dashboards, data visualizations, and decision aids that are easily consumed. To be successful in this role, candidates will need to possess an understanding of relational databases, strong communication skills, and problem solving to complete complex tasks.

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Create automated and self-service SAS programs
  • Serves as a key resource on complex and/or critical issues
  • Perform quality review of output provided
  • Partner with IT in implementing new processes into existing reporting
  • Solves complex problems and develops innovative solutions
  • Provides explanations and information to others on the most complex issues
  • Collaborate with internal stakeholders supporting new analytic models and tools
  • Generally, work is self-directed and not prescribed

You’ll be rewarded and recognized for your performance in an environment that will challenge you and give you clear direction on what it takes to succeed in your role as well as provide development for other roles you may be interested in.

Required Qualifications

  • Undergraduate degree or equivalent experience
  • 2+ years of experience in data analytics, which includes translating analytic concepts, approaches, and results
  • 2+ years of experience writing and executing code such as SQL or SAS
  • 1+ years of experience in conducting analysis using administrative pharmacy and medical claims
  • Advanced skills in Microsoft Excel
  • Strong commitment to customer service and ability to work collaboratively across multi-disciplinary teams

Preferred Qualifications

  • Advanced degree in data analytics, finance, mathematics, computing sciences, public health or related discipline
  • Experience working within a PBM or healthcare organization
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills are needed, including the ability to “translate” analytic concepts, approaches, and results
  • Critical thinking skills to apply variations of methods based on business needs
  • Ability to pivot quickly and change direction based on leadership feedback

Soft Skills:

  • Demonstrated ability to prioritize deliverables
  • Proven organizational skills with ability to be flexible and work with ambiguity
