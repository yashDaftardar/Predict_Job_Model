Full Job Description

Job Description:
Purpose:

  • Clinical Analytics is an ambitious team of Data Scientists with a unique blend of Business, Scientific, and Machine Learning expertise that seeks to unleash the full potential of Client's data assets by bringing technology and/or data and insights to the forefront of decision making via fit-for-purpose data analytics solutions.
  • The Senior Data Scientist – Visual Analytics Design role is a key technical leadership role to enable user-centered next-gen engaging and storytelling data visualization experiences.
  • Translates complex analytics and research for a wide range of audiences, including executive leadership, by fine-tuning the fundamental design details that help drive impact and influence evidence-based decision making.

Responsibilities:
List up to 10 main responsibilities for the job. Include information about the accountability and scope.

  • Influences and enables the development of creative data visualization to give users the best experience possible.
  • Prototypes and builds newly innovative capabilities and takes part in all stages of the product lifecycle by collaborating with cross-functional stakeholders.
  • Incorporates storytelling and compelling narrative into visual design.
  • Works with the clinical analytics team members to translate analytics output to user-friendly, integrated designs in multiple media formats, including but not limited to presentation decks, web-enabled data visualizations, dashboards, and infographics.
  • Anticipates and identifies issues that could affect timelines or quality and develops options and solutions.
  • Assists in developing best practices for data presentation and data insight dissemination across the organization.
  • Enables data-driven insights to support clinical development.
  • Ensures that data presented, and presentations, maintain study integrity. This role is required to support multiple use cases and should ensure that each product is fit for purpose.
  • Leads both cross-functional innovation and functional process improvement initiatives and 'lessons learned' events.
  • * Responsible for coaching and mentoring junior team members.

Qualifications:
List required and preferred qualifications (up to 10). Include education, skills and experience.

  • Bachelor's, Master's, or PhD in statistics, mathematics, analytics, bioinformatics, data science or equivalent field with 6+ years (BS), 4+ years (MS) or 2+ years (PhD) of related experience
  • Expert-level proficiency in R, Python, SAS or other statistical programming languages. Intermediate-level knowledge of statistical and data mining techniques
  • Expert proficiency with visualization tools like Spotfire, Tableau or equivalent
  • Proven expertise with Machine Learning and other advanced analytics techniques
  • Demonstrated effective communication skills. Demonstrated ability to communicate analytical and technical concepts in layman's terms
  • Demonstrated problem-solving and analytical skills
  • Demonstrated history of successful execution in a fast-paced environment and in managing multiple priorities effectively
  • Expert knowledge of front-end development languages (JS, HTML, CSS, jquery)
  • Experience developing animated data visualizations and/or data stories is preferred
  • Experience working in Cloud Computing environments (e.g., AWS, Azure, etc) is preferred
  • Experience with Hadoop platforms and related "big data” technologies is preferred
  • Experience with UI and/or UX is a plus
