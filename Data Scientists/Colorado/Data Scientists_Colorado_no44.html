Data Science Engineer II
at Sovrn
Boulder Office

About the Team

The Data Science Solutions team, a subgroup of the larger Data Science team, operates at the intersection of data engineering and data science. In its support of the Data Solutions business development team, the Data Science Solutions team builds and deploys internal and third party data pipelines while maintaining compliance with licensing agreements and other privacy regulations. The team also supports the company as a whole with machine learning and business intelligence tools.

About the Job

As the resident engineer on the Data Science team, the primary responsibility is to support members of the team by enabling the use of tools and practices that will promote success in their individual roles. Opportunities to solve problems are ever present, and you will use your experience and creativity to generate a combination of both short-term and long-term solutions. In addition to supporting the Data Science team, there will be opportunities to contribute to the development of tools or enhancements for other teams at Sovrn.

What You’ll Be Doing:
Assisting with deployment of Data Science machine learning models into production
Assisting with data availability for Looker, Spark, Hive, Snowflake and other future integrations
Researching, importing and promoting adoption of best-of-breed practices and tools for the Data Science team
Participating in data science and machine learning projects requiring data and software engineering expertise

About You

You’re driven, thoughtful, curious and organized. You enjoy solving problems and grow in tenacity the more elusive the solution seems to be. You’re comfortable working with and managing multidisciplinary projects. Above all, you’re not afraid to make mistakes, and you’re eager to learn from them.

The successful candidate will have:
Bachelor’s or higher degree in an analytical area such as computer science, physics, mathematics, statistics, or engineering or equivalent experience
System networking and automation experience
Strong knowledge in one or more of the following: Hive, SQL, bash, containers
Experience with Python, Spark, AWS and machine learning
Comfort with version-controlled codebase (Git)
Competence in working independently and efficiently with minimal supervision
Ability to efficiently multitask and work cooperatively and collaboratively with all levels of employees to maximize performance, creativity, problem-solving and results
Desire for professional and personal growth opportunities

Bonus Points:
A background in data products and/or the ad technology industry
Experience with Java, Golang or GCP

Position Reports to: Manager, Data Science Solutions

Location: Boulder, Colorado

We understand that no candidate is perfectly qualified for any job. Experience comes in different forms; many skills are transferable; and passion goes a long way. Even more important than your resume is a clear demonstration of accountability impact, and the ability to thrive in a fluid and collaborative environment. We expect you to learn new things in this role, and we encourage you to apply if your experience is close to what we're looking for.
