Overview:
A Business Intelligence (BI) Analyst shortcuts the organization’s path to decision making, through the use of data analytics, data visualization and data modeling techniques and technologies. The BI analyst will use these tools and concepts to identify trends that can help other departments, managers and executives make business decisions to modernize and improve processes in the organization.
This specific role will focus on modelling for Supply Chain.
Responsibilities:
  • Analyzes complex business problems and issues using data from internal and external sources to provide insight to decision-makers
  • Identifies and interprets trends and patterns in datasets to locate influences
  • Constructs forecasts, recommendations and strategic/tactical plans based on business data and market knowledge
  • Creates specifications for reports and analysis based on business needs and required or available data elements
  • Design and architect scalable machine learning-based solutions for handing unstructured content-based intelligence
  • Proactively identify opportunities to improve and evolve current intelligence systems
  • Translate complex business requirements into detailed technical program specifications and actionable stories within reasonable time estimates.
  • May provide consultation to users and lead cross-functional teams to address business issues
  • May directly produce datasets and reports for analysis using system reporting tools
  • Operate as a subject matter expert (SME) in data visualization and data storytelling
  • Work creatively and analytically in a problem-solving environment
  • Operate in a fast-paced agile development environment
Qualifications:
Minimum:
  • Bachelor's Degree
  • Minimum of 5 years' experience in data analysis, modeling or simulation
  • If candidate has a Master’s Degree, 3 years of experience in data analysis, modeling or simulation will be considered
Preferred:
  • Bachelor's or Master’s Degree in Data Science, Physics, Applied Mathematics, Statistics or similar quantitative field
  • Foundational understanding of SCRUM principles and the desire to expand in this area
  • Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to influence, challenge, communicate, motivate and persuade
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Proven analytical skills
  • Demonstrated success as a change agent
  • Goal oriented with the ability to balance tasks and team/individual needs to achieve desired outcomes
  • Interest in the Aerospace & Defense Industry

COMPUTER SKILLS
  • Strong data and reporting skills – SQL, R, Python, Microsoft BI
  • Advanced data cleansing and manipulation skills
  • Advanced exploratory data analysis skills
  • Demonstrated statistical modelling and machine learning skills
