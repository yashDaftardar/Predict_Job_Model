Full Job Description

Summary

The Director of Data Science will lead a team of Data Scientists in the application of statistics, machine learning, programming, data modeling, simulation, and/or advanced mathematics to explore data, recognize patterns & trends, and make value-add data discoveries to facilitate the achievement of business goals. This is a leadership role with a significant external and client-facing focus and will involve meeting with clients, designing presentations, and working with the sales team. This role will also be involved with the company’s product and services team and R&D in developing and overseeing implementation of new innovations.

What You'll Do

  • Executes the product-driven Data Science roadmap, working with leaders cross-functionally to identify where Data Science can drive impact.
  • Leads a team of data scientists who are a recognized authority in devising technical strategies for processing, analyzing, and interpreting operational data.
  • Champions and evangelizes a data-driven culture and long-term business value creation through the development of data science capabilities
  • Provides subject-matter expertise to design, develop, and evaluate predictive models and advanced algorithms that lead to optimal value extraction from the data.
  • Coaches the team to frame complex data analytics problems and structure clean and concise presentations to establish alignment with the leadership teams and encourage adoption of those insights.
  • Leads the development and implementation of solutions to extract, consolidate, and analyze key, complex information from unstructured, diverse, “big data” sources to generate insights.
  • Builds machine learning models through all phases of development, from design through training, evaluation, validation, and implementation.
  • Regularly shares data insights with management and provides recommendations on ‘best in class’ approaches to leverage data in support of business objectives.
  • Maintains up-to-date knowledge of data science innovations and trends through research, networking, and membership/participation in relevant organizations.

What You'll Need

Education
  • Undergraduate Degree or equivalent combination of education and experience in a related field.
  • Master’s degree in related field preferred
  • Related fields may include Computer Science, Engineering, Math, Finance, or Statistics
Skills
  • Excellent communication (verbal and written) and interpersonal skills including the ability to effectively communicate statistical concepts to business, technical, and executive teams
  • Proficiency in machine learning algorithms such as multi-class classifications, decision trees, support vector machines and deep learning
  • Familiar with natural language processing, text mining, machine learning techniques and cloud computations
  • Ability to collaborate effectively across multiple teams and stakeholders, including analytics teams, development teams, product management and operations
  • Strong ability to conceptualize complex data structures for dual stacks of data flow and business models in order to guide the team to be comprehensive on their analytics
  • Ability to lead team of data science/ML professionals
Experience
  • 7+ years of relevant experience in data science roles, including acquiring data, modeling, and consuming statistical models using relevant tools or comparable experience applying statistical solutions to business outcomes.
  • 2 years experience in a leadership/managerial role of ML teams that design and build highly scalable distributed models and strategic roadmaps
  • Expert knowledge and experience in data mining, SQL, ETL, etc. and using databases in a business environment with large-scale, complex datasets
  • Extensive knowledge of and direct experience with major ML libraries in Python and R. Proficient git
  • Extensive experience with statistical approaches and their limitations
  • Strong business acumen as well as data science fundamentals and a proven track record of building data and analytics products
Travel Requirements
  • Less than 20%
Working Conditions & Physical Requirements
  • Reliable internet access for any period of time working remotely, not in a Workiva office.
