Full Job Description

Kin + Carta West, formerly Spire Digital, is seeking a Data Product Owner to help design and grow our rapidly expanding data science practice. Kin + Carta has offices worldwide and offers an inclusive, fast-paced, exciting culture of passionate individuals working together to build awesome software and digital products for the world’s most important organizations, tackling some of its greatest challenges.

People who enjoy contributing to innovation, sharing expertise in a continuous learning environment, and are excited about data and AI technologies will enjoy this opportunity.

We’re seeking a Data Product Owner to join our team full-time. The Data Product Owner will work directly with our VP of Data Science in a customer-facing role to ensure the conceptual and technical goals of the project are met. In this role, you’ll be responsible for managing functional requirements, execution, and performance for new data products, as well as assisting our clients to deploy new AI solutions, while building data-driven culture. You’ll work closely with the product team, product designers, data scientists, data engineers and QA. Applicants should have functional experience as a data scientist, and be looking to expand their career to a strategic/leadership role.

Your experience level

  • 2-5 years experience in applied data science in industrial/commercial setting

  • Demonstrated creativity in problem solving, through data-driven solution

  • Project management experience in a scrum/agile environment

  • Demonstrated communication and persuasion skills, including contract negotiations and scoping

Impact and Core Responsibilities

  • Full ownership of the day-to-day development of a portfolio of projects, in various vertical industries

  • Work directly with VP of Data Science and Kin + Carta’s leadership team to develop long-term data product strategy

  • Develop relationships with Kin + Carta's enterprise stakeholders and customers

  • Hands-on tactical practitioner with explicit accountability for the quality of their work

  • Responsible for managing the tasks and output of an individual project

  • Manage time effectively and keeps the design and development team unblocked

  • Grow understanding of data science development and technical constraints

  • Work autonomously to define personal and team scope, priorities and delivery

  • Support the VP of Data Science to take on product for the data science capability

Craft

  • Must have has solid experience with Jira, R/RStudio, Python/Jupyter and SQL

  • Preferred skills: cloud platform certification(s) in GCP, Azure and AWS, Databricks, MLFlow, D3, LAMP development, MACH architecture, Tableau

  • PMP certification, scrum master experience

  • Has solid skills in research and competitive analysis

Communication and Culture

  • Lead client checkpoints and data product reviews

  • Can articulate the goals of their project as well as the rationale behind requirements during client demos

  • Prioritizes feedback and consistently responds and reacts productively and meaningfully

  • In the face of complexity, able to work with others to suggest productive first steps

  • Communicates proactively with clients. May require guidance on stakeholder management

  • Desire to learn and become a SME in a number of different industries

  • A demonstrated commitment to Diversity, Equity and Inclusion (DEI).

Salary Info:

  • Average salary expected for this role is $59,000-100,000, DOE. Benefits for full-time employees include unlimited PTO, three healthcare plans, vision, dental, 401K and Roth IRA plans, weekly yoga, annual education stipend and more.
  • Candidates must be able to work in the US.
