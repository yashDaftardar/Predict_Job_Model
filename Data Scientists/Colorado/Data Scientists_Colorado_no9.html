Full Job Description

The Aerospace Corporation is the trusted partner to the nation’s space programs, solving the hardest problems and providing unmatched technical expertise. As the operator of a federally funded research and development center (FFRDC), we are broadly engaged across all aspects of space— delivering innovative solutions that span satellite, launch, ground, and cyber systems for defense, civil and commercial customers. When you join our team, you’ll be part of a special collection of problem solvers, thought leaders, and innovators. Join us and take your place in space.

At Aerospace, we are committed to providing an inclusive and diverse workplace for all employees to share in our common passion and aspiration – to carry out a mission much bigger than ourselves.

Provide leadership, expertise, innovation, and direction to Aerospace teams working on the forefront of the digital transformation of the Space Enterprise, and in the discipline of Data Science.

What You’ll Be Doing

  • Supporting United States Space Force (HQ, SWAC, SPOC, STARCOM), United States Space Command (HQ, JTF-SD, CFSCC), Space Security and Defense Program, and others in the application of Data Science efforts
  • Working closely with Aerospace and Government leadership to help achieve Data Strategy goals while leading cohesive teams to provide Aerospace technical expertise
  • Provide technical counsel to help realize the Data-Centric goals of DoD, USSF, USSPACECOM, and other organizations
  • Leading teams to identify key opportunities for application of analytics, machine learning, natural language processing, artificial intelligence across a wide variety of use-cases and stakeholders
  • Providing guidance and advice on large scale data engineering efforts required to achieve digital missions
  • Leading independent technical assessments
  • Developing implementation plans to achieve data strategy and digital transformation goals

What You Need to be Successful

Minimum Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree from an accredited program in engineering, physics, mathematics, computer science or other applicable degree
  • 12+ years of experience in Data Science or related disciplines
  • A strong technical background and experience in large data enterprises, DoD data challenges, and DoD software development from a range of perspectives and domains
  • Demonstrated ability to lead diverse teams of engineers on multiple simultaneous efforts
  • Successful experience identifying technical support requirements and working effectively with matrix organizations (e.g. with ETG)
  • Demonstrated and exceptional interpersonal, teambuilding, strategic, leadership and communication skills
  • Strong understanding of all components of DoD data strategy, data engineering lifecycle, and how to implement incremental solutions towards achievement of these goals
  • Ability to lead simultaneous data science projects and teams across multiple stakeholders
  • Experience conceptualizing and implementing rapid prototypes to solve DoD problems by leveraging data science
  • Willingness to travel domestically up to 20-30% of the time
  • This position requires current TS/SCI, and the ability to maintain a security clearance, which is issued by the US government. U.S citizenship is required to obtain a security clearance.

How You Can Stand Out

It would be impressive if you have one or more of these:

  • Masters or PhD from an accredited program in engineering, physics, mathematics, computer science or other applicable degree
  • Relevant and specific data science experience in the Space Domain
  • Experience/expertise with conveying complicated data and scenarios to operations personnel and their senior leadership
  • Relevant experience with USSF, USSPACECOM, SSDP, and other space enterprise customers
  • Current applicable SAP/SAR accesses
