PADOVA | c/o EnginSoft - Via Giambellino, 7

EnginSoft is a leading solution provider in the field of Simulation Based Engineering Science (SBES): our mission is to be the partner of choice of industrial clients for solving innovation challenges. We provide state-of-the-art engineering simulation solutions, consulting, training and research support to our customers. Currently, we are in the process of expanding one of our business units (Industry 4.0 and Digital Manufacturing), with a particular focus in Automation and Production Systems. Our existing team of Industry 4.0 experts provides competencies in the following domains: Data Analytics and Data Modeling, Scheduling and Optimization, IIOT and cyber-physical systems, production systems modeling and simulation, robotics and virtual commissioning. For this unit, we are looking for result-oriented candidates, who will join of our team, and who are motivated to work in a dynamic environment responding to innovation challenges related to manufacturing. The ideal candidate should meet some of the following requirements:

Your tasks and duties:

  • Work in collaboration with industrial clients in the multiple phases of industry 4.0 projects: (requirements definition, development of quantitative models, implementation of algorithms and software programs, preparation of documentation, conduct training and support etc.)
  • Work with data preparation, processing and analysis using state of the art data analytics tools (e.g. Python, R) in order to process data sets and create models (e.g. for predictive quality, maintenance)
  • Create mathematical models and apply operational research and other numerical and heuristic optimization techniques
  • Collaborate with other EnginSoft’s units and external partners, to support research related activities

Background requirements:

  • Masters’ degree in mathematics, engineering, or equivalent, with minimum 2 years’ experience in data analysis, and software development
  • Knowledge of modeling tools, mathematical formulation of processes
  • Good knowledge of programming and development environments
  • Fluent in Italian and English

Are a plus:

  • Experience to conduct exploratory and in-depth data analysis in the areas of simulation, modeling and optimization, scientific computing
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to work in a team, good communication skills and working autonomy and willingness to learn
  • Experience in engaging with current and prospective customers to understand their needs, priorities in order to identify and document the right solution
