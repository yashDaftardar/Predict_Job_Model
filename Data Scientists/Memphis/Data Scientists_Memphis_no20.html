POSITION SUMMARY

View is seeking a skilled and innovative Engineer to join the Quality team in its high-volume manufacturing facility in Olive Branch, MS.

ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Automation of existing and new measurement and inspection systems.
  • Qualification, setup and release of various measurement and inspection systems for use in production.
  • Provide engineering support to factory operations related to quality concerns and system issues.
  • Monitoring system performance and respond quickly to resolve problems.
  • Learn, own and optimize existing processes and systems to improve performance and throughput, and ensure quality expectations are upheld.
  • Capability Analysis and Statistical Process Control of measurement systems to ensure reliable measurement for use in Production.
  • Data analysis and modelling to come up with creative solutions to production issues.
  • Updating and maintaining documentation of measurement processes and engineering changes.
  • Working with cross functional teams to implement innovative solutions.

DESIRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE

  • Hands-on experience in use of and Machine learning and Computer Vision Libraries like OpenCV, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Keras
  • Motivated team-player who is excited about learning new skills and taking on a wide range of projects and challenging assignments.
  • Strong Analytical and Problem-solving skills.
  • Strong engineering background and good understanding of statistics.
  • Ability to effectively analyze statistical data; familiarity with software such as Minitab, JMP or R is preferred
  • Hands-on experience in use of Machine Learning, Deep learning and Digital Image Processing techniques
  • Proficient in Programming Languages and platforms like Python, C/C++, Java, MATLAB.
  • Proficient in MS Office applications (Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint, Explorer) and basic computer skills
  • Excellent interpersonal skills; ability to communicate effectively both verbally and in writing

EDUCATION

Bachelor's in degree in a Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematical (STEM) fields of study and 2 or more years of related experience.
Master's degree preferred
