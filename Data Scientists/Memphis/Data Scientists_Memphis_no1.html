Organizational Information

The FedEx Global Sales Operations (GSO) team supports the global sales force with core sales operations systems and processes and directly supports the FedEx Services Sales team with planning and execution. GSO defines customers and matches them to Sales professionals, creates compensation strategies and executes compensation processes, and builds tools and resources to make the FedEx Sales team more effective.

Job Description :

  • Provide expertise and leadership at the enterprise level related to vast quantities of data (including disparate data), enterprise analytics, statistical modeling, and initiatives.

  • Leads staff in descriptive, diagnostic, predictive, prescriptive and ensemble modeling, advanced statistical techniques, use of database tools and/or other approaches in quantitative analysis of complex business situations.

  • Creates and executes solutions from initial concept to fully tested production, and communicates results to a broad range of audiences.

  • Advanced use of current and emerging technologies to evaluate trends and develop actionable insights and recommendations to management, via understanding of the business model and the information available for analysis.

  • Uses data, statistical and quantitative analysis, explanatory and high-complexity predictive modeling, and fact-based management to drive decision-making.

  • Mentors less senior staff. Lead cross functional projects and programs formally preparing and presenting to management.

  • Routinely work on multiple highly complex assignments concurrently.

  • Presents and interacts with Sr. Leadership on a normal basis.

High Level Expectations:

  • Understand critical business logic, processes and systems used by Global Sales teams to organize their customer base and assign representation

  • Effectively communicate, collaborate with Global Sales teams and provide timely solutions to problems in a fast-paced environment

  • Lead multiple work streams with competing priorities to completion in a timely manner with limited supervision

Knowledge and Skills Considered a Plus:

  • Develops experimental design approaches to validate finding(s) or test hypotheses.

  • Identifies/creates the appropriate algorithm to discover patterns.

  • Gathers relevant information, identifies relationships, deduces from cause to effect, and draws logical conclusions.

  • Finds patterns in the data and finding correlation in related or unrelated variables.

  • Works in iterative processes with the customer/business partner and validates findings.

  • Establishes scalable, efficient, processes for model development, model validation, model implementation and large-scale data analysis.

  • Develops innovative and effective approaches to solve analytical problems and communicates results and methodologies.

  • Develop metrics that can be used to drive business decisions.

Presentation/Strategic Recommendations

  • Story telling/presenting with data insights and actionable recommendations.

  • Helps the business understand the scientific principles behind their process in order to gain stakeholder buy-in.

  • Provides updates and analytical presentations to stakeholders across the enterprise.

  • Work with business users to assist and teach tools and techniques to the teams while partnering with the business users to solve their business problems.

  • Provides thought leadership and dependable execution on diverse projects.

  • Identifies emergent trends and opportunities for future business growth and development.

Desirable Skills (Not Required):

  • Experience solving Sales Operation problems, including but not limited to customer segmentation/classification, territory optimization, market potential prediction/forecasting

  • Helps the business understand the scientific principles behind their process in order to gain stakeholder buy-in.

  • Experience in solving propensity to buy, product mix, and other optimization problems


Minimum Qualifications:

  • Masters degree or equivalent in a quantitative discipline required.

  • Five (5) years work experience in measurement and analysis, quantitative business problem solving, operations analysis, marketing analysis, simulation and/or predictive analytics. superior analytical skills with diverse analytics and statistical software and applications.

  • Excellent interpersonal skills, written, and oral communication skills.

  • Proven leadership skills.
