Full Job Description

Position Overview

Responsible for SAS Programming (or R, Python, SQL, etc.) and data analytic support including data extraction, cleansing, match/merging and loading processes.

Job Responsibilities

  • SAS (or R, Python, SQL, etc) Programming and data analytic support including data extraction, cleansing, match/merging and loading processes.
  • Performs exploratory data analysis on a variety of datasets, derive adhoc reports and information - (e.g. Overlaps analysis, Allocation, Response reporting, Customer profiling, etc.)
  • Data Conversions - Converts data from any format into SAS or related datasets for use in Analytics.
  • Space and Data Management on the Server - (e.g. InSource File, Mailing data Files. Leads User Acceptance)
  • Testing (UAT) of data services provided both internally and externally.
  • Training, Learning, Growing, Developing skills and bringing new innovative ideas.
  • Participates in design and development of databases and data requirements.
  • Software Maintenance - Specialty Software including SAS (or R, Python, SQL, etc) and other function specific software
  • Subject Matter Expert on data issues and queries (e.g. Postal and USPS Zip Code).
  • Server monitoring and Maintenance - Daily disk drive maintenance.

Job Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in Business or a related field, and 4+ years of progressive analytics experience, or an equivalent combination of education and experience
  • Master’s degree preferred
  • Experience working with centralized and field based operations preferred
  • Experience in customer call center data utilized to support retail operations preferred
  • Expert knowledge of data infrastructure, analytics platforms and reporting functions, concepts, practices, and procedures (e.g. Pricing, Marketing, Operations data, etc).
  • Experience with complex structured problem solving and critical thinking
  • Knowledge of business process development and change management concepts and implementation practices
  • Ability to clearly define and communicate strategic vision and expectations
  • Ability to lead implementation and adoption of strategy and process improvement
  • Knowledge of solution development methodologies
  • Business process and systems analysis skills
