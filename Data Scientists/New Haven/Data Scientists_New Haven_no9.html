AS THE SR. ENGINEERING RECORDS AND DATA ANALYST YOU WILL:
  • Provide support to professional engineering staff in the release of engineering drawings, job packages, and/or other technical data for a program or a technical engineering group.

  • Understands data organization and management, familiarity using office software including PDF conversion and editing tools, file editing and document marking, record keeping and metrics.

  • Tracks, statuses, and retains control of the engineering release process for assigned area.

  • Controls procedural checking of engineering job packages and maintains central control of engineering release.

  • Proficiency working with technical engineering personnel and other professionals such as Legal and management and with a variety of departments such as Manufacturing Operations, Quality, and Supply Chain.

  • Must have excellent communication and demonstrated professional interaction skills, and the ability to work alone and in a team environment.

In addition to the responsibilities highlighted above we offer extensive benefits to include competitive 401k and insurance. We remain an equal opportunity company.
