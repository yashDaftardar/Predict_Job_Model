Senior Data Scientist

Quantum-Si
Guilford, CT

Company Description

Quantum-Si is pioneering a new generation of technology to create the market of Next Generation Protein Sequencing (NGPS). Similar to Next Generation DNA Sequencing (NGS), which gave us a blueprint of what could happen by studying our DNA, NGPS enables an unbiased view of the proteome through improved scale, resolution, and sensitivity leading to better understanding of disease and improved general health.

We operate at the intersection of biochemistry, chemistry, hardware, molecular biology, photonics, semiconductor chips, and software with a goal to drive a digital transformation of a $36 billion dollar proteomics market.

Join us on the journey to transform healthcare, motivated by the idea that the products you work on will change the lives of millions of people around the world.

Job Description

The System Integration team plays a critical role in forging together all parts of our system into products that will be used all around the world. Come work alongside a world-class team of scientists and engineers to define a new frontier for next generation sequencing.

As part of our team, your core responsibilities will be:

  • Drive ongoing improvements in chips, kits, and instruments by analyzing available data streams from manufacturing, internal testing, and sequencing assays
  • Coordinate closely with scientists and engineers to perform complete analyses, starting with data gathering and pre-processing, understanding expected performance and outliers, analyzing experimental data, and communicating findings
  • Develop dashboards, notebooks, and other interfaces to help scientists and engineers extract performance insights from a variety of data streams
  • Work with stakeholders to identify and capture new data streams of interest
  • Produce statistical and machine learning models that inform decisions regarding various aspects of product design and manufacturing

Qualifications

Baseline skills/experiences/attributes:

    • B.S. plus 5 years experience, or M.S. plus 3 years experience, or Ph.D. in data science, informatics, engineering, computer science, or other related field.
    • Proficient with Python data science libraries (numpy/scipy/pandas) or similar (MATLAB, R) with a track record of analyzing complex datasets
    • Ability to communicate effectively with scientists and engineers across multiple disciplines
    • Self-starter who takes ownership of tasks and problems and provides solutions
    • Demonstrated team skills: ability to maintain excellent rapport with colleagues in the face of tight development schedules
    • Love for working in a fast-paced environment

Ideally, you also have these skills/experiences/attributes (but it's ok if you don't!):

  • Industry experience in engineering or science R&D
  • Familiarity with one or more of the following: proteomics, next-generation sequencing, CMOS wafer processing, chemistry
  • Experience with machine/deep learning (sklearn/pytorch/tensorflow)

You deeply identify with core Quantum-Si values:

  • Efficient & Speedy - you get work done in a fraction of the time as industry peers
  • Intellectually Curious - you are thoughtful & inquisitive; people enjoy working with you because they learn from you
  • Mission-Driven & Committed - you are passionate about the company's purpose and are immensely productive
  • Team Oriented - you celebrate and take joy in the success of others on the team
