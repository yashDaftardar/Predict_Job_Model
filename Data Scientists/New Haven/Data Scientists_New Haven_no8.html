Full Job Description

As part of the Product Development team, SageSure is seeking an experienced Data Scientist that can drive the development and implementation of data science solutions. In this role, you will have the opportunity to work side by side with Data Scientists and work closely with our Underwriting, Actuarial and Information Technology departments in our Cheshire, CT office. You will be supporting the company’s goal of profitable growth in the residential property business by tackling challenging initiatives aimed at improving pricing sophistication and efficient growth.

What you will be doing:

    • Develop GLM Loss cost models using R
    • Build complex non-pricing models
    • Explore and utilize advanced modeling techniques (e.g., machine learning techniques and Elastic Nets) to build sophisticated models
    • Collaborate with Actuarial, Product Development and Underwriting
    • Act as a mentor and help develop junior team members
    • Develop model-driven solutions to complex business problems

Qualified candidates have:

    • Minimum of 5 years industry experience, or a PhD in a quantitative studies discipline with 2 + years of relevant experience.
    • Experience with predictive modeling and statistical software packages (e.g., SAS, Emblem, Python/R)
    • Advanced knowledge of data sources, tools, and the business (lines, systems, pricing plans)
    • Strong Excel, Microsoft office, SAS and database skills. Ability to build complex models
    • Effective interpersonal and written / verbal communication skills; strong team player and ability to work across organizational boundaries
    • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
    • Excellent organizational skills, including the ability to manage multiple assignment
