Description


The Data Scientist role will be tasked with building, developing, deploying and maintaining databases, data models, reporting systems, data automation systems, dashboards and performance metrics, supporting key business needs.


  • Build, develop, deploy data models, reporting systems, data automation systems, dashboards and performance metrics
  • Maintain reporting systems and dashboards
  • Support the creation of data visualization tools and Statistical Analysis Plans.
  • Collaborate with business development science, scientific services, project management, scales management, statistics, and data management teams internally, and external collaborators, to support data related processes and questions.
  • Support research, including scientific publications.
  • Maintain data management and scientific operations
  • Monitoring of study data in Cogstate’s data management
  • information in preparation for interactions with auditors
  • Identify and lead one or more of Cogstate’s continuous process improvement initiatives by seeking to understand client needs and engaging Cogstate staff across the company
  • If acting as SME, serve as the main point of contact for Operations and Project Management staff for issues related to the area of expertise including but not limited to managing all associated materials, training, process improvements and liaising with other departments as necessary
  • Review Rare Disease and Pediatric clinical trial protocols and advise on trial methodology and endpoint selection.
  • Interact with sponsors to resolve scientific queries and participate in study planning.
  • Support the development of rater training materials for Rare Disease and Pediatric trials.
  • Support the creation of Statistical Analysis Plans for Rare Disease and Pediatric trials.
  • Collaborate with scales management on creation of source documents for Rare Disease and Pediatric trials.
  • Interact with project management, scales management, statistics, and data management teams internally to support scientific processes and questions.
  • Write clinical study reports, analytic reports, and business requirement documents.
  • Drive research, including scientific publications, on Rare Disease trial outcome measures, rater training, and trial methodology.
  • Maintain scientific operations objectives and timelines.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Graduate Degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Data Science or closely related field
  • Minimum 3 years’ experience in data science.
  • Experience with data basing, data analysis, statistics, and data visualizations.
  • Strong programming skills (SAS, R), and query languages (SQL).
  • Experience with data science tools, data visualization tools, and cloud environments e.g., Azure, Power BI, SQL server, SAS visual analytics, Spotfire, JMP, R graphics, Firebase, Google analytics.
  • Other programming and database experience a plus.
  • Experience in a biomedical science setting preferred.
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills.
  • Proven ability to successfully manage a full workload across multiple-projects with limited direct oversight.
  • Excellent problem solving/decision making skills.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, team-oriented environment.
  • Exceptional time and resource management.

About Us:

Cogstate is a leading cognitive science company focused on optimizing the measurement of cognition (brain function) to guide decision-making in clinical trials, academic research and healthcare. Cogstate commercializes rapid, reliable and highly sensitive computerised cognitive tests and provides expert support for traditional neurological assessments. Science, innovation and excellence in delivery are at the core of everything we do. Cogstate offers health, dental, and vision benefits as well as 401K plan matching.
