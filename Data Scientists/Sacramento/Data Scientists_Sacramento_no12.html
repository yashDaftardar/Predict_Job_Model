Full Job Description

The Project Management Office (PMO) team consists of passionate project managers focusing on the delivery of both software and infrastructure projects that benefit the business and add overall value to Clear Capital. Small but mighty - even though the team is a small percentage of the tech department, they have a huge impact on the work managed and delivered for the organization. The PMO team is focused on process standardization (when applicable), managing and reporting metrics, department support, coordination of teams (sometimes cross-departmental), and delivery of projects. The contributions of the PMO team allow for clear insight into project and effort timelines and resource availability. We are considered the first line of defense when understanding and reporting on project statuses that affect Clear Capital as well as the industry. Join us and help make a difference.

What you will work on

  • Maintain inventory of all Data & Analytics and Machine Learning initiatives across Product, Finance, Compliance, Research and Innovation and maintain an integrated roadmap
  • Estimate work-effort required, create resource plans and adjust program plans based on dependencies and capacity constraints and timeframes
  • Identify, understand, and work to mitigate issues and risks proactively with the project team, business partners, and leadership
  • Work collaboratively with other business and technical teams to establish processes to efficiently collect and share project status information
  • Plan delivery sprints, create project plans, and work with senior leaders to identify resources necessary for accelerated delivery

Who we are looking for

  • 5 years (minimum) of hands on experience with data management processes, development and cloud data and analytics technologies
  • 5 years (minimum) of experience managing a portfolio of complex wide-scope data systems and analytics initiatives with successful delivery and conclusions
  • You have proven experience with typical software and data solution development lifecycle
  • You can manage all facets of project delivery including but not limited to, stakeholder relationships, work prioritization, planning, project risk assessment, implementation and reporting
  • You’re comfortable reading & writing code, and can describe what each part of the code is doing (Python, SQL, Bash, etc)
  • You’re great at taking constructive feedback on your work
  • You’re passionate about our mission to disrupt how banks, lenders and investors value real estate

About Us

Clear Capital is the premier provider of real estate valuation, analytics, and technology solutions. Powered by its more than 45 years worth of information on nearly every U.S. metro, neighborhood, and property, Clear Capital’s solutions are trusted by community credit unions and billion-dollar financial institutions alike. Clear Capital is headquartered in Reno-Tahoe with a team of more than 600 nationwide, dedicated to going wherever it leads, and doing whatever it takes.
