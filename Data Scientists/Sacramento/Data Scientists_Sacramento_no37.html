Who Should Apply?

You will work closely with other Department of Managed Health Care (DMHC) data analysts to analyze and document business requirements for Data projects that drive the department-wide decision-making processes. This includes developing requirements and design documentation, use cases and test cases that define complex and advanced data projects supporting implementation of enterprise data-driven solutions.

You are also responsible for developing Extract, Transform, Load (ETL) using Microsoft Structured Query Language (SQL) within SQL Server Integration Services and/or Tableau.

You will deliver solutions within DMHC’s Data Governance and Data Quality Management guidelines to ensure data is available to programs for analysis. You will participate in building a data-driven culture across the DMHC program areas as part of a team responsible for delivering enterprise data analytics solutions. This includes planning, organizing, controlling, and managing data initiatives and solutions for the DMHC.

You should apply if you are legally eligible to work in the United States, reside in the state of California (or are willing to relocate), and do not require visa sponsorship. Relocation assistance is not available.

What Is The Opportunity?

To join a newly energized and highly innovative data management team, eager to embrace new technologies to effect digital transformation at the California Department of Managed Health Care (DMHC). You will transform how the department uses data to make decisions!

What Is the Department's Mission?

Your work will support DMHC’s mission to protect consumers’ health care rights and ensure a stable health care delivery system!

What Will You Be Doing Every Day?

You will be a technical data expert for our Enterprise Data assets leveraging existing technologies to deliver high quality data and develop necessary analytics and reporting capabilities using technologies such as Tableau, Microsoft SQL Server Integration Services, Microsoft SQL Server Reporting Services, Microsoft SQL Analysis Services, Microsoft Power BI, MarkLogic Data Hub / Data Lake, ESRI ArcGIS geo-spatial data visualization platform and more!

How Will You Get It Done?

Under the general direction of the Chief of Business Intelligence Division (BID) within the Office of Technology and Innovation, you will lead our data analysis activities that lead to the development and delivery of enterprise data warehouse, analytic databases, analytics and enterprise reporting to the offices of the DMHC. You will leverage data analysis best practices to assess and document current reporting and analytic needs, and play various roles executing the development of solutions.

Who Will You Be Working With?

You will work closely with BI team members to achieve success! The team consists of a Business Intelligence Manager, Database and Analytics Developer, Reporting and Analytics Developer, Senior Database Architect and a Data Analyst (this position!).
