POSITION SUMMARY:
Under limited supervision, the Senior Business Data Analyst maintains a data collection system allowing for the organization of multi-contract operational data, multi-contract administrative contractual data and pertinent multi-contract financial data. This job class requires advanced knowledge of data consolidation and reporting software. Employees in this job class independently control to PRIDEâ€™s business needs in providing organized data for management leadership to make strategic and tactical operational, contractual, and financial decisions.
TYPICAL DUTIES:
1. * Provides data organizational expertise in the area of operations, contracts and finance.
2. * Provides technical system configurations for data to be used in Program Management Reviews, ad hoc multi-contract operational reviews, Contract compliance, and financial summaries.
3. * Utilizes various types of business data and compiles in a meaningful, business-related way.
4. * Audit data integrity, investigate concerns, and establish processes to streamline and improve data quality.
5. * Uses contract site management connections to assist in data collection.
6. * Develops professional working relationships with key Contract personnel as it relates to these job duties.
7. * Maintains data and reporting systems for all business lines.
8. Participates in management reviews.
9. Performs other duties and special projects as assigned.

MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:
  • Three or more years of years of data collection and reporting with business analytics experience;
  • Familiarity with FAR/DFAR/CAS, the DOD/federal government acquisition process, pricing and negotiation of government contracts a plus;
  • Advanced understanding of contract analysis and interpretation, and the ability to incorporate new contracts into existing databases;
  • Experience in reporting data by Contract for Program Management Review purposes;
  • Knowledge and understanding of the Government acquisition processes a plus;
  • Effective oral and written communication skills using appropriate business English;
  • Demonstrated organizational, common sense and problem-solving skills;
  • Human relation skills to build effective relationships with team and customers;
  • Effective customer service skills;
  • Advanced computer literacy including knowledge of word processing, spreadsheet, database and presentation software;
  • Ability to establish priorities and solve operational problems;
  • Flexibility to respond to changing work priorities and handle numerous projects at the same time.
