If this sounds like an environment you would thrive in, we have an exciting opportunity for an Engineer / Data Analyst

Your Day-to-Day:
  • Develop innovative, reliable, data solutions focused in data analytics, machine learning, and artificial intelligence for the civil nuclear sector of the energy market. Project areas of focus will include: Equipment Reliability and Supply Chain. Primary responsibilities for this position will be to help design, develop, test, and maintain DES (Digital Engineering Services) data processes and systems. Projects will include working with both internal and external customers to provide custom solutions.
  • Translate user requirements into a product design and give implementation estimates.
  • Work directly with customer on product integration with other third party applications.
  • Assist with customer support on live application issues and may have to diagnose/solve immediate customer needs in conjunction with management staff.
  • Analyze data from multiple sources to determine how it fits into software and other applications.
  • Understand different nuclear data sets and how they function together to develop customer solutions.
Who You Are:
As a successful candidate, you will bring the following to the team:
  • Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in Computer Science, Information Technology, or Bachelor's Degree in either Mechanical, Electrical, Nuclear Engineering with Data Analysis / RDBMS
  • Experience with Oracle and PL/SQL
  • Ability to think critically, problem solve, and develop new solutions
  • Ability to communicate effectively to customers, management, and peers
  • Experience with Extract, Transform, Load (ETL) processes and software
  • Experience with Power BI/Tableau or other business intelligence software
  • Experience with Microsoft Azure, Jupyter Notebook, Spark, and Databricks
  • Experience with machine learning and/or artificial intelligence technology
