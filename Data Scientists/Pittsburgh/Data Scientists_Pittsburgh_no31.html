Role Summary/Purpose

The Principal Data Scientist (PDS) will be the technical leader for data science and advanced analytic initiatives for the Digital Applied Innovation (DAI) organization at Wabtec. DAI is a cross-disciplined entrepreneurial group focused on developing and delivering new transformative digital solutions by leveraging the power of big data and advanced analytics. As the technical lead for Wabtec’s AI Center of Excellence, the PDS will lead a team of Sr. Data Scientists and help grow expertise throughout the organization and develop scalable cutting edge AI architecture solutions in recommendation engines, predictive maintenance modeling, perception systems and automation, while working closely with the application development team to deliver scalable solutions for Wabtec’s extensive industrial asset base.

Location: Pittsburg, PA. OR Remote, USA.

Essential Responsibilities

In addition, you will:

  • Help set and execute the vision for AI at Wabtec and drive a culture of applied innovation
  • Work closely with the DTAI solution engineers to build scalable AI solutions that drive business value for our business units and customers
  • Partner with the Director of DTAI and business development teams to engage directly with business unit and external customers to understand pain-points
  • Be a technical cross-functional leader with organizations such as, services, additive manufacturing, locomotive, marine and mining engineering groups to drive new digital solution opportunities
  • Lead AI education and awareness throughout Wabtec to build the pipeline for advanced data scientists within the organization
  • Be heavily involved in external collaboration efforts with startups, incubators and universities to grow Wabtec’s AI skill base and resource arm
  • Act as coach for to others in driving digital industrial solutions across all functional engineering areas

Qualifications/Requirements

  • Masters in Engineering, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, or Computer Science from an accredited university or college, or relevant and significant industry experience. Preferred specialization in the fields of statistics, condition-based-maintenance, predictive modeling, or AI/ML techniques.
  • A minimum of 10 years of technical experience in an engineering business
  • A minimum of 8 years of experience as a data scientist with established skills in advanced data analytics, predictive modeling, deep learning neural networks, simulations, optimization techniques and unstructured data analytics. Have had experience in deploying scalable AI solutions that solve industry problems

  • PhD in Engineering, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, or Computer Science from an accredited university or college, or relevant and significant industry experience. Preferred specialization in the fields of statistics, condition-based-maintenance, predictive modeling, or AI/ML techniques.

  • Demonstrated engineering thought leader with experience developing and executing engineering solutions for businesses to gain efficiency
  • Demonstrated commitment for process improvement
  • Experience with product management
  • Strong command & working knowledge of engineering workflow processes
