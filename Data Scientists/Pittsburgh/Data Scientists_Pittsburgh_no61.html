Job Title: Senior eBusiness Data Analyst

Requisition ID: 154759BR

When you’re a part of the team at Thermo Fisher Scientific, you’ll do important work, like helping customers in finding cures for cancer, protecting the environment or making sure our food is safe. Your work will have real-world impact, and you’ll be supported in achieving your career goals.


How will you make an impact?


Reporting the leader of the Analytics team in Digital Commerce, the Senior E-Commerce Business Analyst will provide analytics support for Thermofisher’s eCommerce platforms. This analytics team is part of a larger organization called eBusiness which is responsible for the overall digital experience Thermo Fisher provides for our customers. Our goal in eBusiness is to create an industry-leading digital customer experience that facilitates scientific planning, execution and analysis, with a seamless transactional experience and optimized lab operations management across the digital ecosystem. As part of the Analytics team in eBusiness, you will be responsible for analytics, reporting, consolidation, forecasting and planning - as well as deriving actionable insights that help drive overall efficiency, productivity, and revenue. The ideal candidate will have strong business acumen, communication skills and technically adept at providing analytical solutions.

What will you do?

  • Proven ability to lead large scale cross functional integration projects. Product management experience a plus
  • Make a business impact through analytical and strategic guidance and reporting.
  • Help develop KPIs and metrics to track initiative progress/performance to enable a focus on results, performance of our products/ diagnose problems.
  • Leverage technical skills to research and mine big data sources to understand, develop a point of view to guide the development of product experiences.
  • Help with data driven research for new product initiatives
  • Assess Data Quality / Validity checks.
  • Facilitate building of dashboards to monitor the performance of our products and diagnose problems to share insights and present to senior leadership regularly.
  • Illustrate key business trends and drivers through effective data interpretation and visualization.
  • Work with product managers to structure and execute disciplined experiments, measure and analyze results, and present findings, to drive product recommendations
  • Collaborate with cross functional teams across support, sales, success, marketing, finance.
  • Become an analytics/data subject matter expert.
  • Develop perspectives on key customer behaviors rooted in our product and enable stakeholders to actively measure impact.
  • Clearly and concisely present strategic and tactical recommendations.
  • Identify, trends, outliers, and patterns that uncover new insights to help guide business insights on web performance
  • Communicate regularly stakeholders to better understand revenue growth that provide additional data metrics that help show the full eBusiness "story”
  • Translate business needs to technical specifications

How will you get there?


Education

  • Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics, Computer Science or a related field.

Experience

  • 5+ years of experience analyzing large, complex data sets yielding opportunities for revenue and/or process improvement within an organization
  • Knowledge of R, SAS, SPSS, SQL, Tableau, PowerBI, and Adobe a plus
  • Knowledge of Agile/Scrum methodology a plus

Knowledge, Skills, Abilities

  • Interpersonal skills: exceptional verbal/written communication, listening and presentation skills with attention to detail
  • Understanding of product analytics, experimentation techniques preferred.
  • Experience working directly with product managers and software development teams.
  • Experience with automation of reporting to drive improvements in business processes.
  • Proven ability to tell a story through data and communicate effectively with senior leadership; mastery of advanced visualization techniques with an ability to translate findings for a general business audience.
