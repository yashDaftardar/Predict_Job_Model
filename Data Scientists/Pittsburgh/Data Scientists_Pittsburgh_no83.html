Company Description

The Bosch Research and Technology Center North America with offices in Palo Alto, California, Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania and Cambridge, Massachusetts is part of the global Bosch Group (www.bosch.com), a company with over 70 billion euro revenue, 400,000 people worldwide, a very diverse product portfolio, and a history of over 125 years. At the Bosch Center for Artificial Intelligence (BCAI), our research efforts shape the future of AI worldwide and enable the BOSCH Group as the global leader in industrial AI. As a part of BCAI Research, you will have the opportunity to work with large-scale data sets across various domains, and interact with multi-disciplinary teams around the world.

At our location in Pittsburgh, you will be part of a cutting-edge entrepreneurial research environment at the core of Bosch's AI effort and take advantage of academic partnerships via our multi-year sponsorship of leading international universities such as Carnegie Mellon University.


Job Description

We are looking for enthusiastic interns to join our research team for in developing new machine learning/deep learning algorithms for various real-world applications of AI, including but not limited to the areas of robust machine learning, Computer Vision, hybrid modeling / physics-based machine learning, and embedded AI. This internship role will focus on the following responsibilities:

  • Design and development of novel Deep Learning algorithms, implementation and evaluation using multi-modal Bosch data from application areas such as autonomous driving and robotics
  • Technical discussions and creation of new ideas within the existing Deep Learning research team, in order to improve upon the existing solutions
  • Clean, efficient, and well-documented implementation of new deep learning methods
  • Code contributions to our deep learning framework
  • Publish at leading AI conferences, and ultimately shape the future of Bosch products and services.

Qualifications

Required Qualification:

  • Pursuing a PhD degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Electrical Engineering, or Mathematics
  • 2+ years of programming experience, in particular Python
  • 2+ years of experience in Machine Learning, or Deep Learning
  • Broad knowledge of Machine Learning algorithms and principles
  • Established publication record in Machine Learning or Computer Vision or related fields
  • Excellent communication skills

Desired Qualification:

  • Experience with Deep Learning frameworks (PyTorch, Keras, or Tensorflow)
  • Proficiency in Machine Learning algorithms and principles
  • Experience defining personal research roadmap

Additional Information

Internship duration: 3 Months
