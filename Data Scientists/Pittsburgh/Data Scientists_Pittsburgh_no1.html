Help us shape the future of education.

Our mission is to build the best education in the world and make it universally available.

With 300 million total users worldwide, Duolingo is the most popular way to learn a language online. As a new grad at Duolingo, you'll make an impact by working on real projects that affect our millions of users, while developing your career through hands-on learning and mentorship.

As a Data Scientist, you'll get the chance to shape projects that are critical to the growth and future of Duolingo. Your role will focus on using analytics to drive product insights through analyzing learner behavior, purchase behavior, and identifying key opportunity areas for the monetization product teams. In the Product Analytics role, we emphasize skills relating to business insights, product intuition, and applying the right analytics tools for the job rather than someone focused primarily on machine learning and advanced statistical modeling.

Data Scientists at Duolingo work cross functionally, embedded within teams of Product Managers, Engineers, Designers, and User Experience Researchers. Data Scientists work on many different kinds of tasks such as defining new experiment metrics, creating forecasting models, conducting research studies of learner behaviors, and identifying ways to improve the Duolingo learning experience and monetization strategy.

More information on our University Programs can be found at university.duolingo.com

What you'll do...

  • Derive insights from data to influence product roadmaps and drive business results
  • Conduct exploratory analysis to address high impact, open-ended questions that are key to Duolingo's product strategy
  • Analyze and model the impact of product decisions that are easy to share and interpret, rather than relying on advanced analytics tools like Machine Learning
  • Partner with product and business leaders to explore, discover, and discuss new opportunities for our business

What we're looking for…

  • Currently pursuing an MBA degree (graduating in 2021) or a recent graduate
  • Bachelor's or graduate degree in a quantitative field (e.g.: statistics, economics, computer science)
  • 2-5 years of experience in analytics, management consulting, or related fields
  • Experience crafting compelling stories based on data and driving decision making with business stakeholders
  • Advanced SQL skills
  • Experience cleaning, analyzing, and visualizing data using tools such as Python or R
  • Strong ability to condense complex concepts and analysis into clear and concise takeaways that drive action
  • Able to relocate to Pittsburgh, PA

Exceptional Candidates...

  • Experience with digital subscriptions or in-app purchase mobile businesses
  • Passion for language learning and education

Why you'll love working here…

  • Company-wide Hackathon
  • PTO and company holidays
  • Frequent company-wide dinners, monthly celebrations, social clubs, and so much more!
