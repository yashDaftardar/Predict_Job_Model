Job Description

What You’ll Get to Do:

CACI has an immediate opening for a Senior Scientific Data Analyst.

More About the Role:

  • Works as an individual contributor as well as in a cross-functional team applying Agile Scrum development processes.
  • Offer creative technical solutions to data and analysis problems and clearly articulate designed solutions to fellow data scientists and stakeholders.
  • Create data visualizations using Microsoft Power BI, R Shiny and/or R Markdown.
  • Provides formal and informal technical assistance on data integrity and program quality
  • Analyzes data for reports, presentations and publications
  • Prepares/compiles monthly team reports and technical reports and briefings related to data quality and accountability


You’ll Bring These Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree or the equivalent is required plus 10+ years of related experience.
  • 4 years of experience in R development plus 1-2 years of experience in data visualization.
  • Must have strong Analytics Skills
  • Data wrangling experience with R, Python, and/or SAS
  • Experience with SQL Server Database / SQL Server Management Studio
  • Familiarity with the Agile Development methodologies and SCRUM
  • Excellent written and verbal communications
  • Highly organized with excellent interpersonal skills
  • Attention to details and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to plan and organize work to meet deadlines with minimal supervision
  • An active NACI clearance is required to begin employment. As a requirement of continued employment you will be required to obtain a NACI clearance.


These Qualifications Would be Nice to Have:

  • Familiarity with Public Health, Medical Data, HL 7, and/or Electronic Health Record.
  • Experience with source control software such as GitHub.
  • Experience in applying AI/ML in data management.
  • Working experience at CDC or other federal agencies.

What We Can Offer You:

  • We’ve been named a Best Place to Work by the Washington Post.

  • Our employees value the flexibility at CACI that allows them to balance quality work and their personal lives.

  • We offer competitive benefits and learning and development opportunities.

  • We are mission-oriented and ever vigilant in aligning our solutions with the nation’s highest priorities.

  • For over 55 years, the principles of CACI’s unique, character-based culture have been the driving force behind our success.
