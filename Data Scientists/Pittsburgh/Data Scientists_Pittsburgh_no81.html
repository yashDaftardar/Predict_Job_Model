OVERVIEW OF THE ROLE

The Data Scientist will help build machine learning models for the trucking industry to rank drivers and fleets according to their probability of experiencing adverse events such as serious accidents.

We have years of data from tens of thousands of drivers provided by customers and third party integrations. Because of the diversity of data sources we incorporate and the sparse irregular nature of some of them, we're unable to rely entirely on standard machine learning models. We build custom tensorflow/keras models incorporating RNNs and CNNs, as well as custom layers. We also develop explainable model architectures for certain tasks.

The candidate should have experience with the complete industry machine learning product development cycle including task definition, choosing datasets, feature extraction, model training, and deployment.

In addition to building models, the Data Scientist will be expected to derive actionable insights from data, and communicate them both internally and externally.

WHAT YOU’LL DO

  • Build and train custom machine learning models using tensorflow/keras or related tools
  • Feature engineering to improve model accuracy
  • Define new tasks, datasets, and evaluation metrics related to driver safety
  • Write reproducible pipelines to train, test, serialize, and deploy real time models
  • ETL tasks to incorporate new data sources
