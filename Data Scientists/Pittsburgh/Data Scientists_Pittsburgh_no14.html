As a Data Analyst , you would be responsible for participating in IT projects that consist of data modeling, application development, maintenance, and improvements. They provide specialized technical knowledge. They contribute to the achievement of business objectives by identifying opportunities for effective use of data, and technology. They analyze, develop, implement and support IT solutions on time and within budget.


Key Responsibilities :


Business Skills

  • Provide analytical and technical expertise within an assigned business unit/area, usually as a member of a project team, in the analysis, design, development, implementation and support of business applications and data models
  • Collaborate with customers, staff members, IT colleagues, and other partners to define detailed functional and technical requirements
  • Assess available technologies, then develop and present viable historical, real-time, and/or predictive solutions
  • Maintain general knowledge of relevant data ingestion, integration and visualization processes and possibly participate in planning processes.

Technical Skills

  • Translate business requirements into data model(s) and visualization designs, and/or communicate the need for predictive analytics when required to support PPG businesses decisions.
  • Perform feasibility studies, translate requirements into an appropriate design, formulate and define project scope and objectives, and implement complete solutions
  • Prepare detailed technical specifications, to include transformations, visualizations and calculations from which analytics solutions will be developed
  • Ensure data models and analytics are developed with standards and established methodologies and practices

Project Management Skills

  • Provide technical leadership on one or more projects and are expected to give direction to both PPG employees and contract resources. They may oversee multiple small projects simultaneously while managing a single large one.
  • Participate in prioritizing resources and tasks, developing risk contingencies, adjusting project scope, interacting with purchasing, and providing management feedback
  • Monitor and report to management on the status of project efforts; may be held accountable for controlling project plans and costs on smaller projects
  • Effectively assess objectives for the assigned project phases and recommend technical strategies and tactics to achieve business needs

Interpersonal Skills

  • Expected to influence application design and project priorities are vital to success
  • Fully demonstrate the ability coaching and mentoring skills and regularly coach other team members on multiple projects
  • Proactively foster and managing relationships with customers and IT colleagues to meet expected customer service levels
  • Display the requisite verbal, written, and presentation skills to articulate ideas / designs to colleagues in multiple functional areas and at varying levels of technical understanding.

Qualifications :


  • Bachelor’s degree in Information Management, Data Science, Computer Science, or related discipline, or equivalent work experience and technical training
  • Minimum of 7 years of experience in Data/Systems analysis, design, development, and implementation
  • Proficiency in Data Warehousing/Big data architectures, analytical and design techniques, database technologies, and programming languages, including Business Intelligence
  • Demonstrable ability to design systems using the appropriate architecture such as Hive, SQL DB, or AAS along with visualization tools like Power BI
  • Define, organize, plan, and execute projects from vision through implementation
