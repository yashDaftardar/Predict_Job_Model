As part of the Retail branch of Enterprise Analytics, the Professional Practice Analytics team is a dedicated function responsible for developing actionable business insights related to the professional practice department within CVS Health’s retail pharmacies. The primary areas of focus for the team include the monitoring of controlled substance dispensing and prescribing, patient safety, clinical decision tracking, and responding to regulatory inquiries. This role will be focused on building out advanced analytic capabilities to support the department across some or all of these broad categories. The successful candidate will lead the development and production of complex analytics and reporting suites that will improve visibility into the state of CVS’ retail pharmacies.

The day-to-day experience of the role will vary – aligning key stakeholders and team leadership to analytical projects, creating metrics and dashboards to drive decisions for field teams and senior leadership, and developing hypotheses and conducting analyses on complex issues related to stores, prescribers, and patients. This candidate will primarily partner with our Professional Practice team, as well as colleagues in Pharmacy Operations, Supply Chain, Legal, Compliance, and Information Services, to provide analytical support on key innovation projects.

Responsibilities include:
  • Acting as a thought-partner for key business stakeholders, making data-driven recommendations to business leaders
  • Collaboration with internal and external stakeholders to develop a deep understanding of the issues being solved for, gather analytical requirements, and tailor the analytic solution to the specific client need and constraints of delivery timelines
  • Conducting analyses of the data, ranging from descriptive ad-hoc analyses to in-depth investigations and outcomes with a variety of statistical modeling approaches
  • Create and maintain detailed Tableau dashboards that inform an assortment of business initiatives
  • Communicate findings using a variety of formats, incorporating compelling visualizations where appropriate, to influence multiple audiences including those without analytical backgrounds
  • Prioritize, structure timelines, develop project plans, and engage/align stakeholders across multiple groups

Required Qualifications
  • 3+ years’ experience with data modeling/analytics with SQL, Hadoop, SPARK, SAS, Tableau and/or other advanced analytical tools. The role will entail heavy usage and exploration of CVS Health’s Retail Teradata SQL databases
  • Extensive experience with Microsoft Office products – PowerPoint, Excel, Access, Word. Similar application suites (Open Office, Google Drive, etc.) are acceptable
  • Proficiency in processing large datasets (measured in hundreds of millions or billions of records) using SQL or SQL-like query syntax
  • Ability to quickly develop knowledge and understanding of new domains and underlying data sources
  • Demonstrated experience in working with business partners and/or clients to identify the business problem, identifying proper data, constructing analytic solutions to the business problem and then presenting the data insights along with creative recommendations
  • Ability to communicate effectively in a clear, logical and consistent manner
  • High level of attention to detail with the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously, adjusting priorities as needed and meeting deadlines

Preferred Qualifications
  • 5+ years’ experience with data modeling/analytics with SQL, SAS, Tableau and/or other advanced analytical tools
  • Healthcare industry knowledge and/or experience
  • Experience with Tableau and/or MicroStrategy
  • Deep knowledge of coding in statistical packages (like SAS, R, and Python) with strong quantitative skills, including relevant experience in logistic regression, cluster analysis, time series analysis, inferential statistics, and experimental design
  • Advanced skills and training in machine learning techniques and other quantitative research analytics such as: Non-Linear Regression Analysis, Multivariate Analysis, Bayesian Methods, Generalized Linear Models, Decision Trees and Random Forest, Non Parametric estimations, Neural Networks, Ensemble Models, etc.
  • High level of independent judgment and initiative
  • Excellent interpersonal skills, including the ability to interact and influence at all levels
  • Strong personal organizational skills and the ability to work across business units within a complex organization
