Description

This Associate Scientist II requires an analytical chemistry background and experience with Good Manufacturing practices (cGMP).

  • Full time analytical data review position
  • Comprehensive review of Quality Control Laboratory testing results including HPLC and GD-FID analytical results.
  • Verify documented procedures for compliance against internal standard operating procedures(SOPs) and USP monographs.
  • Verification of electronic data input in both a LIMS based software system as well as Microsoft Excel spreadsheets
  • Quality control testing is conducted on semi-solids (creams, ointments, lotions, gels, etc.), liquids, and suspensions products. Raw Material testing is conducted on actives, excipients, and preservatives.
  • Familiar with USP with the ability to translate and implement testing per monographs and general chapters.
  • An ability to multi-task is essential, as the candidate will be required to prioritize testing, data responsibilities, and additional duties based on manufacturing needs.
  • Associate Scientist should be able to work on the bench, manage Projects while working with other scientists and collaborating with other departments

Additional Responsibilities:

  • Author SOPs, investigations, analytical reports
  • Basic instrumentation troubleshooting
  • Reviews test results to ensure compliance with specifications

  • Pharm testing experience
  • Strong computer confidence with an ability to work within multiple electronic data collection systems (Ensur, LIMS, CDS OpenLab )
  • High proficiency in Microsoft Excel, Word and other Office Based software.
  • Ability to work through complex mathematical equations (comfort/confidence with understanding/verifying equations)
  • Ability to work as part of a cross function team. Strong communication and technical writing skills
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, deadline driven environment
