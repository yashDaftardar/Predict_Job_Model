Global Innovation Platform (GIP) is a privately funded full capability Internet of Things (IoT) solutions orchestrator. GIP enables the successful accelerated adoption of IoT to exploit a new paradigm of industrial and commercial data emerging from the built environment. From bespoke IoT application development to delivering integrated enterprise insight and continuous operational control, GIP delivers expertise and comprehensive solutions that transform asset data in business value.


OUR MISSION: To create productive and healthy built environments through interoperable IoT that produces actionable insights from useable data.


Global Innovation Platform is seeking a VP of Data Science & Analytics to join our team!


THE IDEAL CANDIDATE excels in a culture of collaboration, excellence, innovation and teamwork. With applied knowledge utilizing artificial intelligence methods and a highly analytical mindset, this candidate will round out our world class team.

JOB SUMMARY: The Vice President of Data Science & Analytics is responsible for owning formulation of strategy, team development, operational execution, and continuous efficacy of the data science and analytics function within Global Innovation Platform (GIP). From data ingestion and structuring to customer centered data visualization and interaction, this position will be expected to lead the conceptualization, development, and implementation of an evolving pipeline of data driven insights deployed on GIP’s proprietary digital platform.

LOCATION this position can be housed out of GIP's McKinney, Texas or Stillwater, Oklahoma office.

ESSENTIAL JOB DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES :

  • The position will be responsible for the recruitment, development, and management of a team of GIP data engineers and analytic developers, as well as oversight and management of external contractors in the performance GIP objectives.

  • The ability to establish and lead a culture of continuous learning and capability advancement in the domain of data science and analytics is expected. This will include the fostering and curation of relationships with customers, commercial partners, and research/academic institutions. Tacit experience utilizing various scientific methods, processes, algorithms and systems to extract knowledge and insights from structured and unstructured data utilizing standard and emerging techniques/ tools is required.

  • An applied knowledge of various forms of artificial intelligence such as machine learning (including deep learning frameworks) will be necessary in performance of the role.

  • The ability to oversee the generation of high fidelity discrete and/or continuous mechanical and electrical simulations will be central to the role.

  • Partner with multiple internal and external stakeholders and cross-functional teams to design, develop, and execute advanced statistical and non-statistical solutions.

  • Act as the primary liaison for multiple cross functional stakeholders managing competing project deliverables in a fast paced and ever-changing business environment.

  • Ability to manage multiple complex projects while meeting all deadlines and manage people to achieve optimal outcomes.

EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS :


Bachelor’s degree in computer science, data science, statistics, electrical or mechanical engineering or a related field required; Graduate degree preferred. 10 years of experience in IT, engineering, research/academia and/or MIS.

  • Highly analytical mindset.

  • Competent in one or more of the following data exploration and visualization tools.

    • Tableau

    • Power BI

    • SAS

  • Proficient in one or more of the following coding languages.

    • Python

    • Java

    • C/C#/C++

  • Applied experience working within one or more of prominent cloud-based data platforms.

    • Microsoft Azure

    • Amazon Web Services (AWS)

    • Google Platform (GCP)


ADDITIONAL PREFERRED EXPERIENCE:

  • Professional training and certification from an accredited organization in the field of data science and analytics.

  • 3+ years of previous direct people management experience.

  • Previous experience engaging and influence senior internal and external (customer) decision makers on profitable financial investments in data science initiatives.

  • A working understanding of evolving multijurisdictional data governance and usage requirements.

  • Previous experience building data driven Internet of Things (IoT) solutions within the commercial and/or industrial environment.


BENEFITS OFFERED INCLUDE : Medical, Voluntary Dental & Vision, 401(k), Paid Time Off, Life Insurance and more!
