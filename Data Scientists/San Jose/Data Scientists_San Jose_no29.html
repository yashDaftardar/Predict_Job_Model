Job Summary & Responsibilities

As the Data Research Scientist you will be part of an R&D team exploring and developing novel optical metrology concepts. Duties will include

  • The data scientist role includes creating various Machine Learning-based tools.
  • Should also be able to perform statistical analysis .
  • Undertake complete ownership of R&D projects related to ML.
  • Work collaboratively within a cross functional team to incorporate new algorithms into our products.

On occasion, you will also work with teams from Engineering and Applications, particularly during a technology transition from R&D to product development. You may interface with marketing & applications to define the system performance goals and software engineering to define sub-system requirements.

Qualifications

  • Expertise on programming and training machine learning algorithms.
  • The ideal candidate will be a creative problem solver with strong technical capabilities as well as a pragmatic, results-oriented focus.
  • Applicant will have a post-graduate degree in optical engineering, physics, EE or other relevant discipline. Ph.D. is preferred
  • Must be comfortable working with team members covering a wide range of engineering and scientific disciplines.
  • Should have hands-on experience in the design and construction of optical instrumentation.

Personal Attributes:

  • Strong sense of initiative, ownership and desire to be part of a winning team.
  • Ability to exercise sound judgment and make decisions based on accurate, quantitative, and timely analyses.
  • Strong oral, written and interpersonal communication skills. #LI -SH1
