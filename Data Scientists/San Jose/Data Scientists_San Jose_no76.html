Overview
The successful candidate for the data engineer role will work with internal partners to create data processing pipelines and analytics frameworks for vehicle security data. The data engineer also works with the analytics team to understand the data better.


Responsibilities

    • Work closely with cross-functional teams to identify data-related technical issues and analytics needs
    • Create data pipeline architecture
    • Build analytics tools that utilize data pipeline to provide actionable insights into vehicle data

Qualifications

    • Degree in a STEM field, such as Computer Science, Applied Math, or Data Science/Engineering.
    • Strong analytical skills with unstructured and structured datasets
    • Good communication skills
    • Proficient programming skills, with one or more languages such as Java, Python, or go
    • Familiar with AI/ML theories and models
    • Familiar with RDMBS/SQL and NoSQL

Preferred Qualifications

    • Familiar with cloud providers such as AWS, GCP, and Azure and their big data ecosystems
    • Hands-on experience with DNN
    • Experience with stream-processing frameworks
    • Previous experience building data pipelines and archictures
    • Knowledge of vehicle system architecture
    • Experience in information security, especially vehicle security
About NIO
NIO Inc. is a pioneer in China’s premium smart electric vehicle market. Founded in November 2014, NIO’s mission is to shape a joyful lifestyle. NIO aims to build a community starting with smart electric vehicles to share joy and grow together with users. NIO designs, jointly manufactures, and sells smart premium electric vehicles, driving innovations in next-generation technologies in connectivity, autonomous driving, and artificial intelligence. Redefining the user experience, NIO provides users with comprehensive and convenient power solutions, innovative Battery as a Service (BaaS) program, and other user-centric services. NIO began deliveries of the ES8, a 7-seater flagship premium electric SUV, in China in June 2018, and its variant, the 6-seater ES8, in March 2019. NIO officially launched the ES6, a 5-seater high-performance premium electric SUV, in December 2018 and began deliveries of the ES6 in June 2019. NIO officially launched the EC6, a 5-seater premium electric coupe SUV, in December 2019 and began deliveries of the EC6 in September 2020.
