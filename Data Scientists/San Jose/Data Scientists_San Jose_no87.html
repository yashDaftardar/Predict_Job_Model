Data Scientist, Machine Learning

The Moveworks Data Science and Engineering team is looking for an experienced and accomplished senior ML data scientist to solve complex product and content problems using machine learning and other quantitative approaches to increase user engagement and drive innovation in the search product.

As we scale globally, there’s plenty of space for you to grow with us. You’ll be part of a team that thinks in terms of We, not I. Together, we're focused on simplifying the lives of 1 billion knowledge workers by giving them an effortless, magical way to resolve their issues, powered by AI.

Who we are:

Moveworks is redefining how companies support their employees with an AI platform that resolves their problems at work — instantly and autonomously. Included on the prestigious Forbes AI 50 list and named a leader in The Forrester New Wave, Moveworks uses advanced natural language understanding (NLU) to resolve support issues, straight on collaboration hubs like Slack and Microsoft Teams.

We're doing this for IT today, and for the entire enterprise in the near future. This is a unique chance to join the team at an early stage as a key player in our growth strategy. If that sounds like fun, we can’t wait to start this journey together.

What you will do:

  • Be a strategic partner for engineering, product, and data science teams
  • Develop metrics and models to rank content and identify investment opportunities to improve content relevance to increase user engagement
  • Influence the roadmap and decisions made by the ML engineering team through presentation of data-based recommendations and effectively communicate model results to customer and internal stakeholders
  • Collaborate with data and analytics engineers to build scalable data pipelines and deploy models
  • Develop high impact ML artifacts including dashboards, reports, and analytical tools to scale ML analysis that leads to higher product impact and user engagement
  • Deliver effective presentations of findings and recommendations to multiple levels of stakeholders

Who you are:

  • You have an advanced degree in Math/Statistics, Engineering, Physics, Computer Science, Economics discipline
  • You are an experienced and accomplished data scientist with 5+ years of work experience solving analytical problems using quantitative and machine learning approaches
  • You have expertise in programming skills (e.g. Python, R), ML packages (e.g. PyTorch), Jupyter Notebooks, data visualization, data modeling, ETL, and the data warehousing concepts
  • You are enthusiastic about innovating in a fast-paced data science team
  • You are comfortable with ambiguity and able to thrive with minimal oversight and process
  • You are an effective communicator, you pay attention to the finer details, and you empathize with customers and stakeholder and build meaningful relationships
  • You are curious and bring genuine excitement to learn new subject areas and are comfortable to be scrappy as and when needed
