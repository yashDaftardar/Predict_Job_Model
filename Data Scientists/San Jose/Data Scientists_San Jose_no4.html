We are looking for an enthusiastic data collection scientist with a track record of innovation and success. 23andMe has the world's largest research database of participants with both genetic and self-reported data. Data collection for scientific research is critical to our mission and we are looking to add to our strong team. Individuals with education or experience in survey methods, study design or alternative self-administered instruments (e.g. cognitive assessment) are strongly encouraged to apply. The successful candidate will have substantial influence on data collection strategies and methodologies.

With the world's largest database of more than ten million genotyped customers, 23andMe is at the forefront of using human genetics to advance biomedical research and transform healthcare. Join our growing research team in translating genetic discoveries into insights into molecular biology of human traits and diseases.


Who we are

Since 2006, 23andMe's mission has been to help people access, understand, and benefit from the human genome. We are a group of passionate individuals pushing the boundaries of what's possible to help turn genetic insight into better health and personal understanding.


What you'll do

We are looking for an experienced data collection scientist to work on our dynamic and growing data collection team. This person will have a vision of the future of data collection and will help lead, develop, implement, and test new measures of health conditions and behaviors. In addition, you will also be involved in working with stakeholders across the company on data collection, measurement and other related research efforts.


What you'll bring

  • Advanced degree in relevant scientific disciplines (e.g. public health, psychology, sociology, demography)
  • Experience in design, deployment and analysis of surveys, self-administered tests, or similar measures
  • Experience working with human subjects in health research (e.g. data privacy, data protection, IRB protocols, community health etc.)
  • Familiarity with or interest in research participant engagement and recruitment metrics/techniques, including email messaging and testing
  • Strong communication skills and an ability to work on teams, in a fluid and highly collaborative environment
  • Flexible and capable of managing multiple projects with overlapping deadlines in a landscape of evolving priorities

Strongly Preferred

  • Interest in web content development
  • Familiarity with or interest in learning Python, R or another programming language
  • Experience with medical health record data or digital data collection
  • Experience designing and evaluating A/B tests for study engagement and methods improvement

About Us

23andMe, Inc. is the leading consumer genetics and research company. Founded in 2006, the mission of the company is to help people access, understand, and benefit from the human genome. The company was named by Glassdoor as one of the Best Places to Work in 2019, MIT Technology Review to its "50 Smartest Companies, 2017" list, and named one of Fast Company's "25 Brands That Matter Now, 2017". 23andMe has millions of customers worldwide, with more than 80 percent of customers consented to participate in research.
