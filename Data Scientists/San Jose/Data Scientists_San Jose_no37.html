Job Description Summary: Manager, Product Data Science At PayPal, we continue to push the boundary of what technology can do to make a difference in the world we live in. We believe that now is the time to reimagine money, to democratize financial services so that managing and moving money is a right for all citizens, not just the affluent. For that, we are seeking exceptional world-class problem solvers with a passion for data insights and relentless focus on execution.

Job Description:

About You:

  • You are curious and inquisitive: love digging into data and uncovering insights
  • You are hyper-analytical and intellectually honest, truly believing in data-driven decisions and communicating your ideas through storytelling
  • You are familiar with data science and machine learning techniques to improve product experiences
  • You are a highly motivated, result-oriented self-starter, enjoy working in a fast-pace environment, and can deliver successful results with minimal guidance
  • You are a fun-loving, adventurous team player

Role – As a member of the product analytics team, you will:

  • Become a domain expert in your product areas and conduct rigorous data analysis to help improve customer experiences and identify profitable growth opportunities
  • Work cross-functionally with product, engineering, design, and marketing teams to build world-class products and design hypothesis-driven experiments; make clear, coherent and holistic recommendations based on test results
  • Improve product experiences for 200+ million customers using data science techniques
  • Leverage the Objective & Key Results (OKR) framework to identify product metrics and monitor product performance through dashboards and ad hoc analysis
  • Present findings and recommendations to senior-level/non-technical stakeholders

Requirements:

  • Data driven mindset with degree in a quantitative discipline such as Computer Science, Statistics, Engineering, Mathematics, or Economics
  • 6+ years of relevant work experience in analyzing large, multi-dimensional data sets and synthesizing data insights into actionable recommendations
  • Fluent in SQL and Excel; experience with a statistical programming language (e.g., R, Python)
  • Experience with visualization tools such as Tableau or Qlikview, and web analytics tools (e.g., Adobe Omniture, Google Analytics) preferred
  • Exceptional written and verbal communication skills to influence cross-functional teams
  • Prior work experience in digital (website/marketing/product) analytics, management consulting, investment banking, and/or consumer financial products is highly valued
  • Experience/knowledge of employing statistical, data mining and machine learning algorithms (e.g. regression and clustering) on real world problems is a plus
