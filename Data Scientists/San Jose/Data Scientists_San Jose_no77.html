Job Description

US Bank is looking for an enthusiastic Data Analyst to join our Analytics team. Candidates should have strong business acumen with the desire to go deep and understand the core drivers of customer behavior and find answers to business questions. The ideal candidate is passionate about leveraging data for significant business impact. This role supports stakeholders in different business departments and external partners with high quality analysis and actionable recommendations.

Basic Qualifications
  • Bachelor's degree in a quantitative field such as econometrics, computer science, engineering or applied mathematics, or equivalent work experience
  • Five to seven years of statistics or analytics experience

Responsibilities:

  • Work closely with business process owners to integrate data analytics into business processes to improve business performance. Provide guidance to leaders and stakeholders on how best to capture data, extract meaning, reconcile assumptions, and identify logical path for action.
  • Develop data ETLs to migrate and prepare data stored across databases and platforms.
  • Perform in depth analysis, interpret results and develop actionable insights and recommendations for use across the company.
  • Hands on experience working with large data sets, including statistical analysis, data cleansing/transformation, data visualization and data mining.
  • Communicate effectively to both technical and non-technical stakeholders, collaborate with cross-functional teams to define data and design requirements for dashboard enhancements, develop reports and dashboards to empower operational and exploratory analysis.

Preferred Skills/Experience
  • Excellent SQL and data warehouse skills. Experience writing complex queries on large data sets.
  • Expertise with data visualization and dashboard tools such as Tableau
  • Hands-on ability in using Python and PySpark
  • Experience working with product usage and customer lifecycle analysis
  • Experience with machine learning and modeling
  • Experience with data ETL
  • Experience with AWS
