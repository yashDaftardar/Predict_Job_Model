Clinical Informaticist, Healthcare Data

Curai
Palo Alto, CA
Remote

Curai’s mission is to provide the world's best healthcare for every human being. This is an audacious mission, and we’re fulfilling it by building a virtual healthcare product, Curai Health, that people can use to become and stay healthy. Curai Health provides high quality virtual primary care with minimal barriers related to access, cost, & convenience.

AI is at the core of our strategy: Curai Health blends the best in high-touch clinical care, the smartest artificial intelligence and the most robust clinical data sources to create a scalable primary care model that gets smarter and more cost-efficient after every patient encounter. Curai Health’s AI sits alongside our care team, not only self-learning but also listening to providers, driving greater understanding of medical language, symptomatology and condition diagnosis. Curai Health’s learning healthcare system layers AI throughout the entire care experience and builds on the collective medical knowledge of the world's most renowned clinical data sources and state-of-the-art research.

As a member of the Clinical Innovation team, you will help develop innovative solutions to challenges in delivering high quality, scalable, and virtual healthcare. You will also help build the foundations for data driven applications focused on automation, clinical decision support, and interoperability.

Responsibilities:

  • Ensure that our healthcare data pipeline is of high quality and enables internal and external data interoperability
  • Manage our medical knowledge base of medical concepts and their clinical relationships
  • Use your clinical informatics acumen to perform knowledge engineering of clinical logic
  • Collaborate with clinical, product management, engineering, and automation teams in developing clinical technology solutions

An ideal candidate has:

  • Medical degree (MD or DO)
  • Masters, PhD, or fellowship training in medical informatics or a related-field
  • Demonstrated knowledge and experience in developing ontologies and medical terminologies
  • Experience with standards for health information exchange, integration, sharing, and retrieval such as HL7/FHIR and CDA
  • Informatics experience working with standard medical terminologies such as ICD10, SNOMED CT, RxNorm, LOINC, and UMLS
  • Experience in EHR development, clinical decision support, or other components of a data-driven health information systems
  • Demonstrated passion for innovation in clinical decision making and health care delivery
  • Strong communication skills and ability to work with cross-functional teams
  • Experience in projects related to natural language processing and machine learning
  • Experience in software development and implementation
