Full Job Description

Hewlett Packard Enterprise advances the way people live and work. We bring together the brightest minds to create breakthrough technology solutions, helping our customers make their mark on the world.

HPE Labs are committed to the fast and seamless transfer of advanced technologies into next generation products and solutions; enabling the delivery of advanced Hybrid IT and IoT solutions for our customers

We are currently seeking highly qualified, self-motivated interns for new Data Science applications, core methodologies and technologies in computing systems architecture at Hewlett Packard Labs.

Hewlett Packard Enterprise focuses on developing technologies, architectures and software to develop solutions in key areas of Data Science and Machine Learning. We are looking for an entrepreneurial mind-set, and a deep passion to take a Data Science problem and conduct investigation and come up with a solution with a fast turnaround time.

We are seeking highly qualified candidates to come join one of our team as an intern or a post-doc, with the possibility of longer term co-innovation and collaboration. We are particularly interested in individuals with a background in computer systems, computer architecture, memory technology, and interconnects, with a good understanding of the current state of the art, major trends and opportunities, and a demonstrated track record in making things real in innovative ways. The ideal candidate combines this interest with a broad, entrepreneurial interest in creating the next generation of HPE’s products and technologies.

We expect all our interns to provide thought leadership and technical influence both internally and externally to HPE, as well as take innovative ideas and make them real – contributing along the full range from initial novel ideas to design, development, implementation, evaluation, and technology transfer. The ideal candidate can thrive in an applied research environment, balancing significant technical and scientific contributions with the ability to bring such contributions to practice through innovative solutions that address the needs of our customers and partners. Excellent software systems building skills are a significant plus.

Education and Experience

  • Pursuing PhD degree in a relevant discipline (e.g. data science, computer science, electrical engineering, math, statistics, etc.)
  • Experience with innovative solution development, such as developing proofs-of-concept, first-of-a-kind solutions, and/or technology transfer.

Technical Skills:

  • Experience in data science
  • Extensive experience in developing data science software with Pythin, Scikit-Learn and Data Science frameworks
  • Strong programming skills and experience with Python required, and Java preferred
  • Experience in Python Web Frameworks – Django, Flask - a plus
  • Demonstrated effective communication and collaboration skills

Hewlett Packard Enterprise Values:

Partnership first: We believe in the power of collaboration - building long term relationships with our customers, our partners and each other

Bias for action: We never sit still - we take advantage of every opportunity

Innovators at heart: We are driven to innovate - creating both practical and breakthrough advancements
