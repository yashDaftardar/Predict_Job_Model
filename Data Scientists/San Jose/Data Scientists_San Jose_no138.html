Full Job Description

At WePay, our goal is to create a data-driven culture and to enable fact-based decision making. Data Analytics is tasked with helping the company achieve this vision by providing data and driving the adoption of analytics insights in everything we do. We are looking for a Senior Data Analytics to join us on this journey and help take our analytics to the next level.

In this role, this individual will be responsible for identifying key metrics and conducting in-depth analyses to guide business and inform strategy decisions. They will work with cross-functional teams such as Product, Sales, Marketing, Account Management and Risk to upsell and cross-sell WePay products and to help improve our partners and merchants’ experience.

The right individual will know how to frame a business issue and design the right analysis to find the answers, fluent in using SQL, R and other big data techniques, and adept at developing insights and building narratives to support key initiatives.

Please use a personal email such as gmail, yahoo, etc when applying to the role.

What You Will Do

    • Develop dashboards and automated reporting related to key performance metrics
    • Collaborate with functions across the company to develop insights and analytic solutions for internal and external clients
    • Conduct in-depth analyses and build statistical models to identify trends and key drivers that inform important decisions in product, sales, risk and operations
    • Build data visualization by leveraging existing tools or developing new ones, as well as providing ongoing enhancements to business dashboards
    • Implement processes and build tools to make data access, extraction and analysis more efficient
    • Build and grow relationships with internal teams to proactively identify business needs and address them using analytics

What We Are Looking For

    • 4+ years experience doing quantitative data analysis, ideally in online payments industry
    • Degree in a quantitative field -Statistics, Math, Engineering
    • Fluent in SQL, and experienced with a statistical software package and/or a programming language such as R or python
    • Solid experience in metrics and dashboards visualizations
    • Experienced with Tableau, Chartio, or other data visualization and BI tools
    • Strong attention to details and organizational skills
    • Comfortable handling sometimes ambiguously-defined problems, developing creative solutions and delivering against aggressive timelines
    • Excellent communication and presentation skills, and proven ability to translate data into actionable insights
    • A team player, and work well with teams across the company driving towards a common goal
About WePay

WePay’s mission is to make commerce seamless. Our products help software companies integrate payments into their applications – thereby empowering small businesses and individuals to get paid easily and quickly using their go-to apps and software. Our customers include BigCommerce, TouchBistro, Meetup and Freshbooks, just to name a few. By joining forces with JPMorgan Chase, a global financial services firm with over $2.5 trillion in assets that serves millions of customers worldwide, WePay is now able to connect our customers seamlessly into a range of banking services beyond payments. WePay is a unique place to work and offers the best of both worlds. WePay has a FinTech startup culture that emphasizes transparency, collaboration and career growth, with the ability to work on small, nimble teams. However, now combined with the power of JPMorgan Chase, employees are also able to create change at scale and have an opportunity to truly disrupt and shape FinTech.
