Full Job Description

As a senior member of the Data & Analytics team of Danaher Digital, you will own the design, development and delivery of machine learning (ML) solutions for Danaher’s operating companies! You will accomplish this by being hands-on and collaborating with other data scientists, software engineers and business groups. Your projects could come from any of our internal customers and use data from life sciences, medical diagnostics, industrial and other sectors.

Additionally, you will have the opportunity to mentor junior members of the team and help guide the strategic direction of the Danaher Digital data science organization!

Qualifications

Advanced degree in engineering, science, mathematics or a related field

10+ years of expert knowledge in the latest ML tools and methods, including time series analysis, survival analysis, and supervised and unsupervised learning, as applied in business contexts

Experience of having taken multiple ML projects from development to successful deployment

5+ years working with business executives as a trusted adviser in data science and data monetization

5+ years working with agile software development methods, such as scrum and Kanban and in-depth understanding of the ML life cycle

Ability to effectively summarize results from analysis to a diverse set of audiences with varying background and technical skills

Ability to travel up to 20-30% required (post-COVID)

Preferred Qualifications

Ph.D. in engineering, science, mathematics or a related field

5+ years working with cloud computing, including AWS and/or Azure

Experience in the fields of IoT, including fault diagnosis and prediction in life sciences, medical diagnostics, biostatistics and more

Experience with Deep Learning (DL) and associated tools, such as TensorFlow and GPUs

Consultative experience providing technology and solution consultation to customers and clients
