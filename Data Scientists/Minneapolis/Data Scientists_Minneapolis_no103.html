Sitecore is experiencing spectacular growth all over the world. We are currently looking for a highly skilled and experienced Machine Learning Engineer to join the Product Development team and contribute to Sitecore’s vision of enabling our customers to own the experience.

You will join a dynamic, international and cutting-edge organization with high growth rates and highly motivated employees.

The role
We are looking for a passionate machine learning engineer who thrives on building systems leveraging AI and machine learning. As a senior engineer and expert in machine learning applications, you will be working as part of a team to define and execute data science solutions.

You will work with the Director of Engineering on developing and executing our Sitecore AI machine learning roadmap, by collaborating closely with data scientists, engineers and product managers.


Responsibilities:

  • Work within the AI team to design, develop and deploy new Sitecore AI backend products and features.
  • Design and develop new features for Sitecore AI client-side plugin module.
  • Ensure superior quality code by developing and maintaining unit test coverage at high levels such as 90%
  • Build and maintain CI/CD pipeline and keep up to date with companywide best practices.
  • Maintain and keep Sitecore AI backend services running.
  • Support new and existing customers by performing DevOps and troubleshooting.
  • Perform code reviews with fellow team members.
  • Actively develop, demonstrate and encourage best engineering practice.
  • Build and maintain robust systems with a focus on quality and customer outcome.
  • Stay ahead of ML innovation and participate in decisions about technology choices.


Qualifications

  • Broad experience in engineering, with strong knowledge of Cloud and machine learning.
  • Independent, self-learner, who can find ways of solving hard problems in a fast-paced team.
  • 10+ year experience in the software industry
  • 5+ years of experience developing cloud based, highly scalable, external customer facing API's
  • Strong knowledge of several of the following: C#, .NET, Azure, Java, Python, Scala, JavaScript, PowerShell, AWS, GCP, Docker, Kubernetes, REST, gRPC, OAuth 2.0, Auth0, distributed and parallel systems, developing large software systems
  • 2+ years industry experience effectively applying ML and AI to solve business critical problems.
  • Excellent analytical, OOP and coding skills.
  • Strong knowledge of machine learning methods and industry applications.
  • Experience developing machine learning applications in Python, Scala or Java.
  • Experience leveraging big data in a cloud environment
  • Experience with very large datasets, high availability systems, SQL and NoSQL.
  • Experience with DevOps and CI/CD best practices.
  • Experience with implementing unit tests and integration tests
  • Strong knowledge in containerized application development desired.
  • Great communication skills (English) and the ability to thrive in a multinational environment.
  • BS in CS, ML or equivalent. MS preferred.
