Full Job Description

Digital Marketing is a critical part of every consumer journey, but many companies only measure its impact on their digital outcomes. Our focus on enterprise-level optimization at Ovative Group can't be found anywhere else, and our clients are household names that span multiple industries, including retail, healthcare, education, CPG, and hospitality.

This data science position is an opportunity for an experienced candidate to take the next step in a leadership role at a fast-growing company that has the highest standards for talent, fun, and client success. The ideal candidate demonstrates great critical thinking, sophisticated understanding of the data science landscape, familiarity with the marketing ecosystem, phenomenal communication skills and inspiring leadership abilities. They are excited to play a pivotal role in helping Ovative continue our journey towards measuring and optimizing against the true enterprise impact of marketing dollars, and ultimately unlocking powerful business results for our clients.

Responsibilities:

  • Help create the future of the DS – Platform Engineering team (30%)
    • Manage & mentor a small but growing group of talented data scientists
    • Spearhead team vision & identity across the Data Science group, and Ovative as a whole
    • Partner with stakeholders to prioritize workstreams and identify growth opportunities
  • Design, develop and deploy ML engineering tools & infrastructure to support the Ovative mission (70%)
    • Partner with your team and other technical leaders to establish ML engineering/ML Ops as a core tenet of the Ovative tech stack
    • Build machine learning components of our best-in-class measurement product, and partner with our broader tech organization to move those components into production.

Requirements

  • 4-8 years of data science/engineering experience with a clear history of technical project leadership
  • 4+ years leveraging design patterns and core software engineering principles to enable a clean, efficient & scalable codebase
  • 1-2 years managing other data scientists or software engineers
  • Demonstrated ability to understand client needs, identify solutions (and their potential impacts) and present recommendations to internal or external stakeholders.
  • Expertise in both supervised & unsupervised machine learning techniques, as well as the overall model development cycle
  • Capability to drive both technical and business aspects of a project, including requirements gathering and stakeholder communication
  • Ability to serve as a technical expert to the rest of the company
  • Fluent in R (tidyverse) or Python (Pandas/Numpy)
  • In-depth understanding of the software development lifecycle and deployment of production-grade machine learning models
  • Basic understanding of statistics, including hypothesis testing, parametric & non-parametric distributions, etc.
  • Preferred qualifications: Experience working within an Agile environment, Web app/API development experience, Prior focus on digital marketing analytics
