Full Job Description

At Foodsby, we bring better lunch experiences to office workers everywhere. Our team is guided by the shared belief that it is not just about the food—it is about how food makes us feel and what it enables us to achieve. Job Summary -

As an online food delivery marketplace like Foodsby, data science is an essential function of our business. The marketplace has grown to a level where machine learning models for user acquisition, demand prediction, delivery time prediction, dynamic routing, etc. make a big impact. We're looking for an experienced Data Scientist to join our data science team.

Data Scientists at Foodsby develop machine learning systems for the most important processes and modules of Foodsby’s online delivery platform. The work consists of owning machine learning components starting from prototyping all the way to maintaining and improving a production ready solution. We are using a variety of technologies and tools e.g., Python, SQL, Spark, AWS (Glue, Redshift, SageMaker), Looker, and ActiveMQ to get the job done.

What You ’re Responsible For -

You will focus on helping our Data Science team by researching and developing methods and machine learning models to select and schedule each restaurant partners delivery routes across each market. Your work directly impacts our customers and restaurant partners by building efficient and value packed deliveries. On a daily basis, you will closely collaborate with product leaders, engineers, and data scientists.

Requirements –

  • Hands-on experience with production level data science projects
  • Deep understanding and experience with machine learning and solid understanding of statistics
  • Experience with domains such as probabilistic modeling, constrained optimization and causal inference
  • Fluent programming skills in Python, our main language for data science projects, and experience with databases (SQL)
  • Experience writing effective tests against code and models to ensure quality
  • Analytical and curious thinking
  • A desire to learn

Preferred Skills/ Experience (a plus) –

  • Knowledge of experimental design and analysis, and A/B testing
  • Experience with:
    • Distributed processing technologies such as Spark (Scala)
    • Deep learning frameworks (PyTorch and/or TensorFlow)
    • Geospatial processing technologies
