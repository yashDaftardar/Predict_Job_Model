Description

We are seeking a Senior Data Analyst who will be responsible for developing marketing reporting and analysis data infrastructure for Government Programs using your skills with MS Excel, Tableau or other visualization tools as well as, your experience with data warehouses. You’ll be responsible for driving implementation of Government Programs view of consumer into reporting and forecasting and solving unique and complex problems with broad impact on the business.

Team members will typically use business intelligence, data visualization, query, analytic and statistical software to build solutions, perform analysis and interpret data. Positions in this function work on predominately descriptive and regression-based analytics and tend to leverage subject matter expert views in the design of their analytics and algorithms.

You’ll be rewarded and recognized for your performance in an environment that will challenge you and give you clear direction on what it takes to succeed in your role as well as provide development for other roles you may be interested in.


Primary Responsibilities:

  • Play lead role in developing marketing reporting and analysis data infrastructure for Government Programs
  • Deliver daily, weekly and monthly performance forecasting and reporting
  • Identify new data points to advance analytic capabilities and help turn into actionable information
  • Drive implementation of Government Programs view of consumer into reporting and forecasting
  • Predict emerging customer needs and develops innovative solutions to meet them
  • Build strong relationship with Finance, Product and Sales
  • Solve unique and complex problems with broad impact on the business
  • Influence senior leadership to adopt new ideas, products, and / or approaches
  • Direct cross-functional and / or cross-segment teams

Required Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Finance, Economics, Statistics, Data Science, Computer Science or related field or equivalent work experience
  • 3+ years of experience in building marketing data and analysis capabilities
  • 3+ years of experience working with SAS, SQL or R
  • Advanced knowledge of MS Excel, Tableau or other visualization tools, experience with data warehouses
  • Able to demonstrate an understanding of how core business drivers influence financial results
  • Ability to work in a rapidly changing environment and possess the ability to work collaboratively and influence across a matrix environment
  • Must be located within commutable distance to Minnetonka, MN


Preferred Qualifications:

  • MBA or other advanced degree
  • 7+ years of Database Marketing and project management experience within a matrix organization
  • Experience forecasting marketing or telemarketing campaigns
  • Familiar with Health Insurance products
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Demonstrated ability to manage people and prioritize deliverables
  • Proven organizational skills with ability to be flexible and work with ambiguity
