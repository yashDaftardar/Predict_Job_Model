POSITION SUMMARY

The SAP BI Data Architect is responsible to lead the design and development of system and data architecture for analytics and reporting capabilities. The position will help drive the design and deployment of all business analytics and reporting solutions. The day to day job involves the agile delivery of data structures and solutions, while ensuring adequate controls; reliability and scalable enterprise wide solutions. While the initial focus will be ERP data and specifically SAP, the role will encompasses all application data sources (internal and external) for the company into a homogenous Enterprise data model. This role is critical in ensuring we build and deliver scalable, flexible, and efficient database structures to effectively support key reporting, monitoring, and analytics solutions.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITES

  • Hands on database and solution architect for all business analytics projects
  • Work with end business users, business analysts, technical resources, and developers to define the data requirements and data structure for Business Intelligence and Analytics reporting solutions
  • Assure data architecture and supporting ETL processes are sound and scalable
  • Design and implement data models for the data warehouse or data mart (data structure, storage, and integration). Lead the database analysis, design, and build efforts.
  • Active participant in weekly business analytics status meetings with key functional team members to assure projects are on track and issues are being addressed
  • Coordinate with functional team members to drive data and reporting validation efforts
  • Analyze highly complex system and technical issues to implement technologies that align with strategic technical direction.
  • Deliver architectural data design that meets business requirements for reporting and analytics
  • Assist in system development and code structure (SQL queries, joins, etc)
  • Foster and maintain collaborative relationships with business partners and technology teams ensuring goals and customer satisfaction are achieved
  • Collaborate with the business to understand/influence business requirements
  • Maintain knowledge of current and emerging technologies/products/trends
  • Communicate solution options to business owners/stakeholders (i.e. architecture reviews)
  • Incorporate framework selection and application architecture into designs, proof of technology projects, and contributing working code, while ensuring alignment with portfolio technology
  • Develop and maintain documented system designs and strategy documents and other technical artifacts

Required Qualifications:

  • Bachelor Degree with 7+ years’ experience in Information Technology, Analytics, or Business Intelligence related fields
  • 7+ years of specific SAP experience required with one of the following: SAC (SAP Analytics Cloud), HANA Cloud, or Data Warehouse Cloud (DWC); Business Warehouse (BW); Business Intelligence / Business Objects (BI/BOBJ).
  • Demonstrated experience in BW on HANA or HANA/DWC implementations in a lead role
  • Outstanding SQL skills that include complex operations
  • Demonstrated ability to apply process, architecture and software engineering principles, methods and tools to business challenges
  • Experience in designing and building out a complex data management and analytics environment for use across business functions
  • Deep Data Analysis experience with strong understanding of data sources, relational and dimensional modeling, and effective uses of business data
  • Ability to provide critical architectural input including evaluating, proposing, and selecting appropriate design alternatives which meet functional requirements
  • Excellent business-facing skills and experience in delivering data and reporting solutions for these functional areas: Finance, supply chain, Sales and Distribution, Production, Quality and HR.
  • Possesses a strong aptitude/passion for creative problem solving when dealing with ambiguity

Preferred

  • Masters degree
  • Interest and experience in Machine Learning (ML), Artificial Intelligence (AI), Predictive Analytics and the tools that support such work
  • Manufacturing and/or food manufacturing experience
  • Experience in other presentation and data visualization tools such as Tableau or Power BI
