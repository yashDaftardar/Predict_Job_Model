The Role:
Global Quality Statistician will use data analytics and statistical techniques to assess the performance of Entegris and supply chain production processes. The assessments will be used to demonstrate statistical capability of the Entegris product supply chain and identify gaps in performance for continuous improvement planning. The position will focus on ensuring statistically capable processes for key attributes of incoming materials from sub-suppliers and suppliers as well as Entegris production processes used to transform raw materials into finished product. The Global Quality Statistician will be involved with developing reports for use in collaborating with global key Entegris customers and internal data reviews.
In this role you will:
  • Serve as a statistical resource to a manufacturing/research facility
  • Interpret quality and manufacturing process data, analyzing results using statistical techniques
  • Create and provide reports and summaries with conclusions
  • Acquire data from primary or secondary sources and maintaining databases, data systems and performance dashboards
  • Identify, analyze and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets
  • Identify and define new process improvement opportunities
  • Develop and deploy techniques to automate the gathering of key data
  • Provide coaching for Six Sigma continuous improvement projects
  • Work within Entegris PACE values driven culture
Traits we believe make a strong candidate:
  • Knowledge of Six Sigma tools, Data Mining, Design of Experiments (DOE), Process Control Systems (PCS), Statistical Process Control (SPC), Quality Systems, and mastery of basic to advanced statistical and Quality techniques
  • Technical expertise regarding data models, data mining and data analytic techniques
  • Strong knowledge of database and software reporting packages
  • Demonstrated ability for query and report writing and presentation of findings
  • Ability to effectively communicate (both oral and written) with global internal and external customers complex statistical models
  • Minimum BS in Mathematics, Engineering, Computer Science, Information Management or Statistics
  • Graduate Degree Preferred
  • Black Belts, Master Black Belts or Engineers with sufficient Statistical/Quality experience
  • Experience in running a sustained statistical training program
  • Solid knowledge of and facility with Minitab, JMP or another major statistical package along with MS Office skills
  • 3-5 years of relevant experience
Your success will be measured by:
  • Contribution to the creation, execution and maintenance of Statistically Capable Supply Chain business processes
  • Ability to effectively articulate Entegris’ goals and expectations for a statistically capable supply chain both internally and to suppliers.
  • Ability to find relevant gaps in supplier performance within the statistically capable supply chain framework and to close those gaps.
  • Demonstration of the PACE values of teamwork, accountability, creativity and innovation, and dedication to excellence
