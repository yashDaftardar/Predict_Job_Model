PartnerRe Ltd. is a leading global reinsurer, providing multi-line reinsurance to insurance companies. It is a dynamic, challenging, and rewarding place to work. Actuarial Internship

Internship Schedule:

The internship would require 3 candidates an opportunity for one full time and two part time interns based in our Minneapolis office.

  • Full time intern schedule:
    • 12 weeks (June 7 to August 30, 2021)
    • 5 days a week 35 hours per week from 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. one-hour lunch break
    • August 30 to January 28, 2022 schedule change to part time 20 hours per week
  • Part time intern schedule:
    • 22 weeks (August 30 to January 28, 2022)
    • 20 hours per week during the school year depending on the applicant's schedule

Your role:

  • Manage underwriting pipeline activity for new and renewal business.
  • Identify missing or incomplete information in Request for Proposals (RFPs)
  • Develop manual and experience rates for excess medical business.
  • Assist in Key Performance Indicator tracking, including close ratio, persistency, and RFP volume
  • Ensure work product is complaint with internal guidelines.
  • Complete actuarial projects as required.
