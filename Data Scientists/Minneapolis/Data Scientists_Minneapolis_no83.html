Senior Machine Learning Engineer

12traits
Minneapolis, MN

12traits is ushering in a new era of experience management by enabling personalization optimized for human potential and health. We enable global leaders and companies of all sizes across the gaming and healthcare industries to create and manage personalized experiences while obtaining a deeper level of audience understanding than ever before through our psychologically-based AI.

12traits is the empathy engine of digital experiences. We provide our clients with the right insights and tools which empower them - for the first time in the digital age - to truly resonate with their customers and end users. Our data science team is at the very core of creating this next generation of digital resonance. We do this by understanding the relationship between neuroassessment-based psychological data and behavioural data using innovative supervised, unsupervised and semi-supervised models that allow us to understand human beings more deeply and sustainably than ever before. As a part of this team, you will work closely with highly experienced data scientists and machine learning engineers as well as psychometricians, researchers, engineers, and UX designers.

If you want to join a purpose-driven company, thrive on solving challenging problems, are intellectually curious and constantly want to learn as well as grow, enjoy high performance environments, are highly productive compared to your peers and a true team player, then read on and apply - we are looking for you.


WHAT YOU WILL DO

  • Perform complex statistical analyses and architect / train ML models on large data sets of unique psychometric data
  • Refine our core ML algorithm that translates psychometric data into actionable insights for our customers
  • Leverage large quantities and diverse sets of behavioural data to drive our prediction algorithm
  • Work closely with engineers, researchers and UX team to incorporate the latest findings from machine learning and psychological research into a working product

YOUR BACKGROUND

  • 4+ years of highly relevant practical experience in data science or data engineering
  • Demonstrated experience with a wide variety of machine learning models and techniques (e.g. supervised and unsupervised learning, dimensionality reduction)
  • Exceptional command of Python and relevant libraries (Numpy, Scipy, Sklearn, etc.)
  • A strong understanding of the fundamental mechanics of machine learning algorithms
  • Solid background in statistics and its practical applications (e.g. determination of significance, uncertainty etc.)
  • Practical knowledge of deep learning frameworks and libraries (Tensorflow or PyTorch)
  • Familiarity with modern infrastructure and tools (e.g. Kubernetes, Docker, AWS) and large-scale data processing technologies (e.g. SQL, BigQuery, Hadoop, Spark)
  • Hands-on experience with leading commercial Cloud platforms (preferably GCP)

HOW YOU STAND OUT

  • A PhD in Computer Science, Computational Neuroscience, or related quantitative field
  • Experience with in-game behavioural data (time series, high dimensional)
  • Experience building personalization or recommendation engines
  • Experience in computer vision
  • Experience with semi-supervised learning
  • Familiarity with and / or interest in psychometric data
  • Experience in working in a small data science/engineering team in a fast-paced, startup environment
