The Wells Fargo Electronic Trading (Etrading) team is looking for a Software Engineer to support the following responsibilities below:

  • Responsible for handling highly complex quality assurance test execution and automation. This includes troubleshooting test environment issues in the Electronic Trading Domain (CREDIT, MUNI, Corporate Bond, Brokered CDs, MBS, Municipals, FX D2C and D2D trading flows).
  • Works with Electronic Trading business users and technology partners to establish and maintain a consistent test methodology and to resolve escalated issues during the testing process.
  • Analyzes the book of work for Liquid Products Electronic Trading Strategic Transformation and performs ambiguity reviews of business requirements and technical functional specification documents.
  • Uses advanced knowledge of Electronic Trading (CDS, TSY, IRS, MBS, CREDIT, FX), Venue Interaction and Corporate Bond management in identifying, evaluating and resolving highly complex test problems.
  • Provides direction and guidance to less experienced staff to resolve critical and non-routine scenarios.
  • Ability to work on well documented API's in any languages but not restricted to Java, Python, Perl etc. and leveraging a range of in-house developed tools

Required Qualifications


  • 7+ years of software engineering experience
  • 4+ years of experience in one or a combination of the following: securities, quants, artificial intelligence, or machine learning
  • 7 + years of Core Java experience
  • 6+ years of securities industry experience
  • 5 + years of experience supporting electronic trading (etrading) platforms
  • 5+ years of algorithmic development experience
  • 5+ years of Low Latency systems development or implementation experience


Desired Qualifications


  • A Masters degree or higher in computer science or finance
  • A professional certification in technology
  • Basic knowledge of industry regulations related to building technological solutions
  • 5+ years of equity markets experience
  • Knowledge and understanding of equity and option trading


Other Desired Qualifications
  • 8+ years of hands-on experience working on QA automation or development teams.
  • 5+ years of experience with load/performance testing of Electronic Trading applications.
  • 5+ years of hands on development experience in one of Java/Python/JavaScript.
  • 5+ years of developing and maintaining automation frameworks.
