Full Job Description

We are seeking a full time Senior Data Analyst to work under the supervision of the Assistant Director of Data and Evaluation. The Senior Data Analyst provides data management, data analysis, and development of data dashboards for all assigned evaluation and quality improvement activities. They are responsible for general project coordination, including managing data collection, data manipulation, advanced statistical analysis, and collaboration with partners in development and tracking of perinatal risk dashboard. Additionally, the senior analyst will provide technical evaluation support to project stakeholders and adhere to IRB protocols and HIPAA compliance when required. They will work independently in managing project scope, deliverables, and coordination of key stakeholders and will collaborate with partners on preparation of grant applications, development of proposals, and logic models. They will also be responsible for basic administrative tasks and other duties as assigned.
