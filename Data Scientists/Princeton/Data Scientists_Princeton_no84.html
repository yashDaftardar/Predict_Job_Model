OFS Laboratories is the central research division of OFS Fitel, LLC, a global supplier or optical fiber and cable for telecommunications, sensing, medicine, aeronautics and other applications. As a world class Center of Excellence for optical innovations, we generate commercially-viable technological breakthroughs that OFS business divisions take to market quickly. Scientists with OFS Laboratories have invented and developed many innovative fiber-optic technologies, now ubiquitous in multiple industries. Based in Somerset, NJ, OFS Laboratories continues to push the boundaries of optical sciences.

We are currently seeking a Development Engineer to join OFS Laboratories in Somerset NJ. The successful candidate will assist our Scientific Team Leaders in the development, implementation and testing of numerical tools for analysis and design of optical fibers and sensors, data science activities and other ongoing simulation projects at OFS Labs and supported business units.

Key qualifications:

  • Position requires at least an MS Degree in one or more of the following: Applied and computational mathematics, physics, optics, electrical engineering, and computer science.
  • Proficiency in Matlab and Python programming, numerical methods and fiber optics are expected, as well as very good documentation and presentation skills and a drive to work independently.
  • Knowledge of data science techniques, statistics, machine learning and artificial intelligence algorithms, as well as experience in C/C++ programming are considered a plus.

Employees are expected to serve as role models for safe work practices and behaviors. This includes following established health and safety policies and procedures, maintaining a clean, organized work area, wearing, and properly storing all personal protective equipment (PPE), and actively engaging as a safety conscious worker with personal safety and wellness as a priority.

Working at OFS means having the chance to realize ideas, innovate and discover new solutions for the future. In addition to our dynamic work environment, we offer competitive salaries and generous benefits programs, including medical, dental, and a matching 401(k) plan.

Interested applicants please apply on-line at OFS Careers https://www.ofsoptics.com/careers
