Vice President & Actuary (Job Number: 335887)

Description


This Vice President & Actuary position is responsible for leading and managing the actuarial staff in the Casualty pricing unit of Commercial/Small Commercial (CI/SCI) Actuarial. The role is based in our Whitehouse, NJ office and reports to the Lead Actuary for Commercial/Small Commercial.

Duties and Responsibilities related to these product lines:


  • Work closely with CI/SCI management to set priorities for Monoline GL, Package GL, Airports, Public Entity, Multinational and multi-line program business
  • Manage actuarial pricing staff including setting the staffâ€™s priorities/workloads and training/developing
  • Complete annual pricing studies, as well as ad-hoc pricing study requests
  • Oversee annual casualty loss trend analysis
  • Oversee large account pricing and collateral reviews
  • Work with CI/SCI management to develop/revise rating algorithms, including providing actuarial rate filing support for state regulators
  • Complete monthly, quarterly and annual reporting requirements for price monitoring and actual versus expected
  • Assist in developing the annual financial plan and quarterly evaluation of current accident year results
  • Support the purchase and placement of reinsurance
  • Work with the predictive analytics team to support CI/SCI underwriting initiatives

Qualifications:

  • BS Degree or higher in Mathematics, Actuarial Science, Statistics, Finance or a related field
  • ACAS; FCAS preferred
  • 10+ years of relevant actuarial pricing or reserving experience (not necessarily in these product lines)
  • Proficiency in Excel, familiarity with SQL, Access, other MS Office applications a plus
  • Prior managerial or supervisory experience strongly preferred
  • Excellent analytical and communication skills
  • Strong organizational and collaboration abilities
