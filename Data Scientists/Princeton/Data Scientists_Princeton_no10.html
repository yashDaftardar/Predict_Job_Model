Sr. Data Analyst – CPD Supply Chain

About L'Oréal: Founded by a chemist in 1907, L’Oréal leads the world of beauty with 34 global renowned brands such as Maybelline, Lancôme, Ralph Lauren, Urban Decay, Redken, Skinceuticals, Essie, and Kiehl’s. With $27 billion (Euros) in annual sales and present in 140 countries L’Oréal is a technologically advanced company which manufactures and supplies over 4.5 billion units annually to the world. L’Oréal Operations is an innovative, challenging, dynamic and diverse environment with the opportunity for cross-functional careers – from conception to consumer – in Manufacturing, Supply Chain, and Operational Package Development. We have 45+ manufacturing plants and more than 200 + distribution centers worldwide; L’Oréal Operations is committed to continuous improvement, people development, safety, quality and sustainability.

POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES/PROJECTS:

  • Generation and distribution of reporting to various groups and management levels (i.e. cuts, service levels, stock equation, etc.)
  • Creation of new planning tools and reports
  • Maintenance, continuous improvement, and troubleshooting of reporting, tools, and related data sources (Excel, PowerBI, SAP ECC & APO, data warehouses, manufacturing planning system, etc.)
  • Cross-functional collaboration with other divisions and project teams to align on reporting and tools
  • Assistance in inventory budgets and trends
  • Projects and additional ad hoc activities

QUALIFICATIONS:

  • BA/BS degree in Supply Chain Management or related field required
  • Experience:
    • At least 2 years in reporting and analytics required
    • 2+ years in Supply Planning, or supporting Planning functions preferred
    • 1+ years using SAP (APO & ECC) and data warehouses preferred
  • Technical prowess:
    • Strong Excel knowledge – custom macros and functions
    • Ability to build automated reports in Excel, Power BI, and/or other data visualization tools
    • Familiarity with programming languages (VBA, SQL, R, etc.)
    • Ability to learn new programs and concepts quickly and independently
  • Knowledge of supply chain KPI’s
  • Able to work and connect cross-functionally with other departments, including Demand & Supply Planning, Manufacturing, Finance, IT, and project teams
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills, comfortable communicating with all levels within organization
  • Takes initiative and is independent, can take a request and run with it.
