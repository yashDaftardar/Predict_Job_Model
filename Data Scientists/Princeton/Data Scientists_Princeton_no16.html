The Position

As a member of the Advanced Analytics & Innovation team, collaborates with team members and other CI&A teams to deliver advanced data and analytics solutions to Novo Nordisk Inc (NNI) Commercial Stakeholders to benefit patients, prescribers and other stakeholders. Works across the CI&A organization, as well as with stakeholders across the NNI organization and with consultants and partners, to implement advanced data and analytics solutions to critical business questions. Applies statistical and machine learning methods to deliver solutions leveraging internal and syndicated commercial datasets such as Xponent Plantrak, LAAD, Experian, Veeva, ICM and other. Collaborates with Associate Director, Advanced Analytics to deliver Marketing Mix and related promotional analytics.


Relationships

Reports to Senior Director, Advanced Analytics and Innovation within the CI&A team. Internal relationships include directors and members of other CI&A teams, Commercial Operations, IT, Marketing brand leads, extended brand teams and other Marketing stakeholders, as well as other Finance & Operations stakeholders (Finance, Pricing, Contracting, Operations and Reimbursement (PCOR), etc.). External relationships include secondary data vendors and consultants. Senior-level (Vice President (VP) and higher) exposure through presentations on Marketing Mix and related topics.


Essential Functions

  • Collaborates with AA&I team members, other CI&A and/or F&O colleagues, and/or line of business (LoB) stakeholders to define advanced analytics/ML opportunities, determine appropriate datasets and algorithms, identify patterns and formulate solutions, experiment and deliver analytics and models
  • Collaborate with internal and/or external resources (i.e., consultants and partner) to define and deliver innovative data and analytics solutions and services response to business requirements and priorities. Areas of focus will include, but not be limited to promotion analytics, patient analytics, managed market analytics, and related
  • Provide analytical and presentation development support of the Marketing Mix process and related analytics (buy-ups, ROI of specific programs, etc.)
  • Explores and evaluates new data driven approaches to generating insights and solutions to address business issues
  • Works with other CI&A team members, IT and data engineers to acquire and transform datasets (e.g., NPP, LAAD) that deliver critical business insights. Defines and creates metrics that can be leveraged by CI&A analysts to deliver insights to their stakeholders
  • Applies skill in data science and statistical analysis in support of organization-wide project and programs
  • Provides guidance and QC oversight to more junior team members, and colleagues in other CI&A areas in use and delivery of analytics and analytical tools
  • Delivers presentations on results of Marketing Mix and related analysis to senior leadership across the organization


Physical Requirements

0-10% overnight travel required.


Qualifications

  • Bachelor 's degree in Math, Computer Science, Statistics, or related field required; MS or MBA preferred
  • A minimum of five (5) years relevant experience in return on investment (ROI) analytics, business analysis, sales analysis, forecasting, quantitative finance, data science or other related areas are required. Master's and/or advanced degree may be substituted for up to 2 years of experience when appropriate
  • Pharmaceutical experience is preferred
  • Statistical and programming experience required
  • Proficient in two or more programming tools such as Python, R, SAS, Stata and demonstrated ability to learn new analytical and programming techniques required
  • Experience with IQVIA datasets and other industry sources preferred, but demonstrated experience analyzing and managing large, structure datasets to generate insighs is essential
  • Experience with leading visualization tools and capabilities highly desirable – e.g., Tableau, Qlilksense, Plotly Dash, ggplot, etc.
  • Strong written and verbal communications skills. Demonstrated experience presenting to senior-level audiences on complex topics
  • Ability to collect and synthesize complex information, making it relevant, understandable and actionable for key stakeholders. Able to tell stories around data and analytics that resonate with key stakeholders
  • Ability to solve complex, unstructured problems with minimal supervision
  • Advanced Excel and Powerpoint skills required
