The successful candidate will join a team of scientists, engineers, user research specialists, product owners, architects and developers in the agile development of market-responsive adaptive and personalized capabilities, prototypes, and product concepts. Position Summary:


The Senior AI Engineer leads on the development of capabilities required to construct personalized adaptive learning technologies that enable effective, efficient, and engaging learning experiences. The Senior AI Engineer will be part of a team that is actively pursuing technology innovation in the personalized adaptive learning technologies space. The Senior AI Engineer will join a team of Research, Learning, and Data Scientists, User Experience experts, Software Developers, and Product Owners that utilize the agile framework to execute rigorous backlogs and deliver high-powered solutions.

Responsibilities:

  • Work with senior data scientists to lead on the development of data ingest and data transformation infrastructure
  • Develop effective methods of AI/ML model testing during development, deployment, and recalibration
  • Lead on the training and retraining of machine learning models and provide metrics to document and track their performance
  • Lead on the exploration of data and identify differences in data distribution that could impact performance when deployed in prototypes
  • Contribute to the definition of best practices around design, coding, automated unit, regression testing, and deployment of AI/ML models to production
  • Keep current of latest AI research in the personalized learning and assessment field and contribute to the research
  • Lead on best practices in the responsible use of AI
  • Work closely with learning scientists and data scientists to define collection events and data transformations needed to drive personalized learning
  • Work closely with Director, Product Owners to understand potentials and limitations of AI/ML in the product
  • Work effectively in the agile-at-scale framework
  • Clearly communicate findings to senior leadership
  • Adhere to ethical standards and comply with the laws and regulations applicable to the job function
