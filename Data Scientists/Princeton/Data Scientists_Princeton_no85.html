Full Job Description

Zelis is a healthcare and financial technology growth company and market-leading provider of claims cost management and payments optimization solutions to price, pay and explain healthcare claims. Zelis delivers integrated network analytics, network solutions, payment integrity, electronic payments and claims communications for payers, healthcare providers and consumers in the medical, dental and workers' compensation markets nationwide. Zelis is backed by Parthenon Capital Partners and Bain Capital.

POSITION OVERVIEW:
Assist Business Intelligence Office team in the organization and management of all open data analysis projects and tasks. The Zelis Data Analyst will communicate and work collaboratively with internal customers for project work and internal data needs, including requirement gathering for business intelligence needs. The Data Analyst will recognize, document and improve standard internal processes related to business intelligence. The Data Analyst will be responsible for identifying unaddressed business needs and works with internal customers and development teams to deliver high-quality analytics. The Data Analyst will also coordinate execution of ad hoc reports and research requests.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:
  • Work collaboratively with internal business partners to gather and create business requirements to ensure that all aspects of the work have been considered while also confirming that each business requirement has clear acceptance criteria and is ready for development.
  • Organize, maintain, communicate and own the team’s business intelligence initiatives and projects.
  • Interpret business and functional requirements for business intelligence needs.
  • Develop development and QA timeline estimates in support of technology development initiatives.
  • Monitor and communicate business intelligence project and request progress, against plan, to technology and business operations stakeholder groups.
  • Provide data analysis and data mining on healthcare data.
  • Supporting development initiatives for reporting and visualization tools.
  • Help create and review testing scripts in support of QA work
  • Review and test data to ensure accuracy and validity.
  • Support and maintain application integrity by creating and maintaining a Data Dictionary and other documentation needs.
  • Contribute to creating, maintaining, and evolving best practices and standards that are leveraged across Zelis.
  • Follow established HIPAA, Compliance & Security policies.

EDUCATION AND PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE:
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Sciences or equivalent.
  • 3+ years of Data Analyst experience.
  • 2+ years of Business Intelligence experience.
  • In depth understanding of and experience with Agile and SDLC methodologies used to guide development projects.
  • Experience in navigating databases and the ability to run SQL queries in support of analysis work.
  • Strong technical and troubleshooting skills.
  • Ability to work independently and within a team setting with minimal direction/supervision.
  • Ability to think strategically, demonstrating critical thought in a complex business environment.
  • Demonstrated effective professional communication (written and verbal) skills.

Proficient in:
  • Microsoft Excel- experience with data aggregation, ad hoc reporting and pivot tables
  • SQL
  • Power BI
  • Tabular Models (preferred)
  • TFS (preferred)
  • Jira (Preferred)
  • SpiraTest (Preferred)

INTERPERSONAL AND PROFESSIONAL SKILLS
  • Advanced Analytical Skills – Uses sound and logical judgment to identify and analyze complex, multi-dimensional problems and drive test planning and test execution decisions.
  • Teamwork/Collaboration – Develops and leverages collaborative relationships to facilitate the accomplishment of work goals. Collaborates at all levels of the organization. Willingness to help others, as needed, to make the team and each deployment successful.
  • Communication – Communicates accurately and effectively in both oral and written form. Practices active listening. Expresses thoughts and ideas with confidence and respects opposing views
  • Managing Work – Ability to self-direct and multitask in a fast-paced, ever-changing environment. Identifies task importance; adjusts priorities as appropriate. Prepares and manages schedule to ensure goals are met. Takes prompt action to accomplish objectives.
  • Technical/Professional Learning – Keeps up with current developments and trends in the field. Demonstrates strong desire to advance in field and apply newly acquired knowledge in role.
  • Living the Vision and Values – Keeps the company’s vision and values at the forefront of decision-making and action.

WORK ENVIRONMENT:

  • A standard business environment exists with moderate noise levels.
  • Ability to lift and move approximately thirty (30) pounds on a non-routine basis.
  • Ability to sit for extended periods of time.
