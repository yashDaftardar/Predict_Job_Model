Job Summary

Otsuka is looking for an accomplished Data and Quantitative Scientist to lead the Data Science group in the Medical and Real World Data Analytics (MRWDA) organization. The role will provide data and analytics sciences expertise and oversight for advanced analytics to make inference from clinical data, data from sensors, and other real world data sets. The overall purpose is to lead and provide oversight to a group of data scientists who use analytics to convert data to evidence that characterize value and differentiation, QoL (Quality of Life) outcomes, understanding patient needs, behaviors, diseases and their burden, and value of therapies in the real world, market access discussion through analytics. The role will also actively engage stakeholders to prioritize and manage delivery of data products to stakeholders.

Job Description

  • Work closely with internal data science and data programming, and external stakeholders including Medical Affairs, Value and Real World Evidence to provide end-to-end advanced analytics.
  • Closely work with Medical Affairs to support statistical design, conduct secondary or exploratory analyses of clinical and other external data. Provides statistical consultation for ad hoc analysis requests including design of appropriate analyses to answer relevant questions.
  • Work closely with clinicians, programmers, other statisticians, data scientists, and publication managers to generate QC outputs for publications, review publications to ensure accuracy, quality and soundness of statistical methodologies; coordinate, oversee and ensure quality delivery from vendors and their resources.
  • Lead, design, conduct statistical analysis, interpretation and presentation of analyses results to stakeholders and leadership. Ensure quality and timelines are met. Provide technical direction and guidance on research, development, implementation, and evaluation of statistical methodologies
  • Responsible for team that will deliver analysis plans, including table, figure, listing shells, and review reports.
  • Foster a culture that embraces strategic use of data into decision making enabling greater business outcomes
  • Build a learning culture within the Data Science team, MRWDA, and external to MRWDA where individuals are provided growth and development opportunities. Attract, retain, develop, promote talent to build a strong team with a balance of skills, experience, and perspectives
  • Identify research areas that merit investigation, develop new hypotheses and methodological approaches
  • Identify and lead initiatives to explore new opportunities for deriving evidence towards characterizing or reinforcing value for therapeutic solutions by leveraging non-traditional analytical approaches
  • Act as advanced analytics SME in leading and conducting analyses to answer most pressing questions across business functions in the remit of responsibility

Qualifications

Required

  • A PhD or Masters in data sciences, engineering, physics, mathematics, or a related quantitative field.
  • 8+ year of experience in designing, delivering advanced analytics, including 3+ years in leading and managing a team of advanced analytics scientists
  • Understanding of a broad array of data science methodologies to design or oversee the development of quantitative (data science, machine learning) models for various types of datasets
  • Deep expertise and hands on experience working with large complex datasets from the real world
  • Proficiency in programming with excellent development and data analyses skills using state-of-the-art technologies and languages (python, R, Matlab, or other scripting languages)
  • Ability to design and/or oversee approaches for analyzing diverse data, including text/documents, sensor data, time-series or longitudinal data, surveys, and other varieties of health data
  • Strong leadership that balances line and performance management with hands on technical and project leadership
  • Experience driving large, complex change efforts, and drive results through influence, persuasion, and outcomes-oriented relationships
  • Participate in designing the strategy, roadmap, building the team, mentoring, coaching, and development of talent and resources
  • Work with extended teams, external service providers/vendors, Industry or Academic collaborators, manage budget and ensure quality deliverable for the group
  • Demonstrated thought leadership in ambiguous business challenges with ability to explain complex ideas to diverse audience ranging from executives to technical programmers
  • Excellent organizational skills with an ability to embrace change and effectively manage portfolio
  • Ability to present and defend advanced analytics at stakeholder meetings, Industry forums and other external meetings
  • Expertise in statistical and quantitative methods, (e.g. machine learning, data mining) and their applications to various types of data
  • Demonstrated experience and accomplishments in applying quantitative models to real life problems across disciplines
  • Hands on technical and project leadership with full accountability and team spirit
  • Strong Acumen to translate business problems into data science problems, solve them, and present as an end-to-end solution
  • Strategic and outcomes-oriented mindset that balances depth, breadth to demonstrate value
  • Strong technical writing, editing, and communication skills along with collaborative mindset that enables and empowers others
  • Finally, a desire backed by will and commitment to make a positive difference to human health

Preferred

  • Demonstrated ability to synthesize information to develop recommendations, and influence organization on a recommended path
  • Experience with sensor data, digital data, and other type of unstructured and structured data
  • Experience with latest statistical methods and Data Science
  • Experience with mathematical modeling and simulation
