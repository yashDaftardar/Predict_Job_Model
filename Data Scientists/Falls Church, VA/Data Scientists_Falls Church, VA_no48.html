Overview:

We are seeking a senior-level Data Scientist with a strong statistical modeling skillset to help us build advanced, readiness reporting software from the ground-up.

As a member of the InnovaSystems team, you will be empowered to do the best work of your career with challenging assignments that solve real-world problems and a steady focus on comprehensive leadership training and career growth.

In this role you will join a diverse and dedicated team in our Alexandria, VA office located in the Mark Center.

PLEASE NOTE : qualified applicants MUST possess an active, DoD-issued secret-level security clearance. Those who do not meet this requirement are not eligible for this opening & will be removed from consideration.

The Data Scientist's key responsibilities will include:

  • Turning innovative analytical concepts into working readiness software solutions
  • Contributing to the overall architecture of an existing large enterprise software system and the implementation of new, knowledge & graph-based software, data pipelines, and APIs.
  • Collaborating with government leaders and other technical teams to translate operational requirements into innovative solutions that support senior DOD decision makers.

Required education, skills & experience:

  • An MA/MS in a technical field such as Mathematics, Engineering, Statistics, or Computer Science combined with at least eight years of hand-on, professional experience (will consider a minimum of ten yearsâ€™ equivalent technical experience in lieu of advanced degree)
  • Knowledge of computer programming, data modeling, statistics, and advanced analytical methods.
  • Demonstrated proficiency with the following tools and programming languages (or similar/equivalent tools):
  • RDF, OWL, SPARQL, Hadoop, HBase, Spark, NiFi, C#, Python, SQL, and IBM COGNOS.
  • Proven experience developing large scale, web-based, enterprise software including API design and user interface implementation.
  • Familiar with semantic technology or other graph-based algorithms and databases, machine learning algorithms, or the application of other statistical, modeling, or simulation methodologies.
  • Prior experience with military operations and/or in military planning strongly desired.
