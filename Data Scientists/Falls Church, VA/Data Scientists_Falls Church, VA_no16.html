Job Description:
NetImpact Strategies is looking for a Senior Health Data Analyst. They will be responsible for data extraction from source databases, data analysis, ensuring dashboard accuracy. In addition, web and database technician, data research, mapping and scoring, day to day maintenance and updates of reports, dashboards, conducting analysis, validation of data, web and database technician. Data research and mapping and scoring.
Typical Responsibilities Include:
  • Analyze existing KSA metrics against available data points and data sets (i.e. readiness documentation, surveys, Lessons Learned, clinical practice guidelines, case registries, etc.).
  • Evaluate KSA metrics accuracy against key components of the program, including operating models, maturity models, scores, thresholds, readiness criteria, readiness checklists, and blueprints and related skills assessments.
  • Apply critical realism methods to ensure accuracy of specialty KSA metrics.
  • Generate KSA Metric Accuracy Report that contains recommended KSA metric development and provide a KSA Metric Cleansing and Quality Plan.
  • Analyze, Link, Map and Define strategic measures of effectiveness against Expeditionary Readiness and Platform Readiness and cross-check Readiness metrics to measures of effectiveness.
  • Develop Metric Update Calendar and align to the Revision Control Process and synchronize with release of critical MHS plans, CONOPs, and reports.
  • Conduct research that can be used for mapping and scoring of the KSAs.
  • Develop competency and proficiency readiness scorecards and a method to link and consolidate them to outcome measures.
  • Develop dashboards based on confirmed requirements and consider dashboard tool(s) to blend multiple data sets and sources, real-time analytics, animated dashboarding.
    • Enhance / update dashboards and their interactive capabilities, including revalidation, visualization modifications, and new functionality.
    • Provide support to develop, modify, and update Dashboards and/or a registry of KSA measures and metrics for all established specialties.
  • Apply quality management methods (e.g., ISO, PMBOK) for quality control, quality assurance, and continuous process improvement to deliverables and work products.
    • Develop quality checklists for reoccurring program deliverables and products, which detail quality requirements while identifying errors, omissions, and oversights.
    • Develops and implements quality control procedures to ensure compliance with quality assurance standards and guidelines.
    • Assists the quality assurance lead in maintaining test environments.
    • Creates test plans and test cases based on requirements and technical design concept.
    • Applies technical concepts to conduct back end testing and demonstrates proficiency in using SQL query tools.
  • Conducts and/or participates in formal and informal reviews at predetermined points throughout the development life cycle.
  • Maintains processes for evaluating products, services, and associated documentation.
  • Formulates recommendations, as needed, for process and product improvements.
  • Assists in preparation of milestone status reports.
Qualifications:
Required Skills:
  • Bachelor’s or Associate degree in Computer Science, Systems Analysis, Information Systems, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field
  • 5+ Years’ Experience working within relational databases and quality control
  • Knowledge of Data Management Tools, Data Marts, Web-Based Cube Technology and Ad Hoc Query Tools
  • Strong background in writing and executing comprehensive test plans for acceptance, regression, functional, and stress testing
  • Knowledge of COTS products for automating the execution of tests or developing test tools
Desired Skills:
  • Preferred Knowledge of Military Health System Management Analysis and Reporting Tool (MHS MART, or M2)
  • Experience in testing UI/UX best practices and 508 compliance
