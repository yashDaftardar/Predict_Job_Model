Full Job Description

The College Board, the national educational organization, is conducting a search for a Data Analyst for our Technology department . This position is based in our Reston, Virginia office or via remote work arrangement

About the role

The Data Analyst will be responsible for the successful maintenance of a portfolio of data provisioning requests and services within the BI Reporting team. The successful candidate will act as a key point of contact for both the customers, Data Governance, and Data Provisioning team members and will be responsible for provisioning data from initial request through successful delivery. The Data Analyst will work closely with the team to deliver requests, and will need to be flexible enough to provision key initiatives personally.

The Data Analyst will focus on efficiently provisioning information and analyses to internal and external College Board stakeholders. High priority will be placed on interacting with business stakeholders to understand and prioritize data provisioning requests.

The Data Analyst is expected to collaborate with fellow data analysts to determine the most opportune means of provisioning data timely and accurately. Together, the Data Analyst and data analysts will identify opportunities for automating processes and procedures for existing provisioning activities and self-service solutions. The Data Analyst will leverage available information assets and understand key indicators to identify appropriate metrics, discover trends, and provide summaries of analyses in concise executive views.

The Data Analyst will lead the transformation of the data request process towards a single data request process, working closely with research, data science, and program data requestors to drive efficiencies in the process, identify opportunities to improve processes and deliverables, and improve the communications and availability of the process, while managing and rationalizing demand across teams.

Responsibilities of the role

  • Operate the Single Data Request Process in Jira, and act as a concierge for data requests within the team.

  • Streamline data requests processes and find efficiencies to build capacity.

  • Communicate with business stakeholders and technical staff to provide good customer service throughout the data request process.

  • Collaborate with a team of data analysts, sometimes along with other Data Services technical staff, such as Database Administrators or ETL software engineers to successfully provision data and continue to improve on the existing processes, including:-

  • Requirements gathering / analysis / solutions / options.

  • Query creation and review, and improvements.

  • Communication practices.

  • Scope and resource management.

  • Documentation / project artifact management.

  • Risk management.

  • Quality assurance.

  • Leadership, providing updates, and project decision-making.

  • Leading planning, retrospective, and daily meetings.

  • Own and drive all assigned data requests/project details, issues, etc.

  • Provide regular updates to technology and business management.

  • Raise key decision points and risks to management for resolution.

  • Provision files and transfer data - upload files for transfer and access through secure file transfer protocol (sftp).

  • Demonstrate database literacy through data mapping and design of data flow diagrams.

  • Understand ETL processes in the legacy and Cloud – know how data moves and is transformed in order to map data and document use cases, as part of provisioning data.

  • Cultivate and maintain current knowledge of College Board products, data systems, and information assets.

  • Monitor and discover trends with particular emphasis on those impacted by new product releases, operational changes, and new data collection techniques.

  • Generate detailed, complex analyses and provide concise, insightful executive summaries from business questions.

  • Develop and drive incremental quality and agility essential to the deployment of data and business information solutions.

Qualifications needed for the role

  • Master’s or bachelor’s degrees in mathematics, statistics, or computer science preferred or deep experience in data management.

  • Five to seven years of demonstrated proficiency with data analysis and reporting required.

Preferred skills for the role

  • Must be able to manage multiple requests/projects simultaneously while providing appropriate direction and oversight.

  • Must be a results-oriented, get things done leader – with a history of success and the desire to help a team evolve and grow.

  • Advanced problem solving and negotiation skills.

  • Strong demonstrated customer service orientation.

  • Advanced SQL coding skills.

  • Comfortable with AWS technology components like S3, Athena, Glue.

  • Knowledge of Business Intelligence tools like Tableau Desktop, Tableau Server, Qlikview, PowerBI.

  • Strong knowledge of relational database systems, data structures, and data modeling concepts.

  • Experience in data management, data quality, data automation, and the development of information standards.

  • Experience in project documentation and the design of process and data flow diagrams.

  • Strong analytical skills with demonstrated experience providing information, reports, and analyses from unstructured and vague business questions and problems.

  • Strong organizational skills and the ability to prioritize projects and information.

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.

  • Able to function both with minimal supervision and in a team-oriented unit.
