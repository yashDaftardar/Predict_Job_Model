Description


Grant Thornton is seeking an Associate to join its Technology and Strategy service line and take an active role in engagement execution, project management and key business development activities.

Grant Thornton Public Sector helps executives and managers at all levels of government maximize their performance and efficiency in the face of ever tightening budgets and increased demand for services. We give clients creative, cost-effective solutions that enhance their acquisition, financial, human capital, information technology, data analytics, and performance management. For more information, visit grantthornton.com/publicsector.

At Grant Thornton, our professional staff applies traditional, cutting-edge approaches and methods to a variety of analyses. As part of our team, the Associate will utilize various methodologies and models to execute client projects.


ESSENTIAL DUTIES:

The Senior Analyst will provide day-to-day support for all daily initiatives, activities, and deliverables for specific task orders under the direction of the Task Order Manager. General responsibilities will include managing assigned tasks, maintaining, and delivering against work schedules, facilitate use of risk assessment tools, data collection and analysis, and day-to-day interaction with federal clients. The position will report directly to the Task Order Manager and will support day to day activities and responding to emerging requirements.

Primary responsibilities include: expertise with data collection, research, and analysis, design and lead mechanisms to analyze external and client requirements to identify new solutions, processes, and process improvements to meet business needs, apply expertise to develop new business strategies and processes and future initiatives to determine future process and needs, formulate solutions, prepare reports, studies and documentation, and support presentations and client meetings, facilitate use of risk assessment tools or methodologies to review, design, and implement solutions, coordinate with clients and field personnel across operational environment, communicating effectively, both orally and in writing, with Task Order Manager on status of deliverables and identifying risks and issues as necessary, developing project plans, ensuring there is accurate record keeping and no disruption in the quality of services to the Government at any time, contribute to new business proposals and proposal development.
Meet or exceed targeted billing hours (utilization).
Assist with business development activities, such as proposals, capture, account teams, whitepapers, conferences, and/or other thought leadership materials.
