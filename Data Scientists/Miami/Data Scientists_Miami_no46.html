Senior Data Analyst

American Bancshares Mortgage Llc
Miami Lakes, FL 33016

Job purpose

Reporting to the COO the Data Analyst will play a key role in orchestrating and automating the data analytics pipeline to make it more flexible while maintaining a high level of quality.

Duties and responsibilities

  • Working on transactional, CRM and external data to develop data models that can be used to generate revenue management insights and recommendations.
  • The Sr. Data Analyst will use data mining and statistical techniques to drive automated insights and operational directions based on structured and unstructured data.
  • Create or supervise the development of Value chains and Decision trees to leverage the business processes, operational and strategic needs to improve the efficiencies of the organization.
  • Perform statistical analyses on data sets to determine trends and develop business understanding of commercial activity.
  • Work with operations and sales teams to help shape business objectives and prioritization using a methodical, data-driven approach to decision making.
  • Assist in resolving ambiguous issues and find the insights that drive operational, support and product excellence.
  • Develop metrics and KPI’s used to measure WFS revenue management performance and business operations.
  • Understanding of the concepts and values of Advanced Analytics, Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, turning data into insight and direction.
  • May be involved in the Areas of Pricing Strategy, Credit Risk and Exposure Management, Inventory management, customer satisfaction, Purchasing strategy, Unit Margin Management, asset optimization and other corporate and operational areas.
  • Present findings in written and verbal format to business leadership.
  • Participate in projects that drive operational execution of insights developed thru data, statistical analysis and quantitative methods.
  • Build and maintain positive on-going cooperative relationships with internal stakeholders
  • Undertake professional development and research to acquire and maintain currency in AI/ML and specifically the AWS ML services
  • Undertake professional development and research to acquire and maintain currency in core database and machine learning technologies and trends
  • Be aware of, and able to use additional API tools and extend from the basic task simulation to additional automation features.
  • Provides support to the rest of the team and to Champions in the Business
  • Participate in regular team meetings to ensure alignment
  • Gather, communicate and manage issues and risks
  • Architect, build and Manage secure and efficient solutions in with appropriate data management discipline, aligning to any corporate or external standards that are communicated as guidance
  • Work closely with Infrastructure Team on any migration activities
  • Proactively monitor for and address alerts that risk the quality of solution provided to clients
  • Maintain awareness of tool and infrastructure security threats, ensuring timely application of patches and upgrades as required
  • Drive continuous improvement through measurement and monitoring
  • Remain abreast of technological change as it relates to infrastructure, deployments, performance, monitoring, log aggregation and alerting
  • Attend relevant technical meetups and events with a view to learning and sharing

Qualifications

  • At least 2 years of experience in the role of Sr. Data Analyst, with hands on experience of creating data interchange and manipulation into models.
  • At least 2 years minimum data management and reporting.
  • Experience using, modifying, and designing convolutional neural networks.
  • Experience with Deep learning libraries (preferably Tensorflow but also Torch and Caffe).
  • Proficiency in one or more of R, Java, C++ and Python.
  • Bachelor’s degree with an emphasis on quantitative or technical work (Operations Research, Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Engineering) or equivalent practical experience.
  • Proficiency in a language for statistical computing (R, SAS, Stata, SageMaker).
  • Ability to prioritize multiple conflicting priorities while driving towards pragmatic decisions/actions.
  • Computer vision libraries, e.g. OpenCV.
  • Deploying algorithms in distributed / cloud computing
  • Designing high performance, resilient implementations
  • Strong oral and written communication skills, including the ability to communicate complex findings in a structured and clear manner to a non-technical audience.
  • Knowledge of one or more visualization tools such as Tableau. Experience gathering reporting and analytics requirements from stakeholders, deriving business insights, and evaluating the impact of business decisions.
  • Master’s Degree in Business Analytics, Finance, Operations Research, Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics.
