Data Scientist



Talento is partnered with a Lead Generation organization seeking to build out their Data Science practice in Miami, FL. As the first Data Scientist in the organization, you will apply the art of data science and power of the modern AI/ML technology to unlock the value of this data and help increase the organization's revenue.

Responsibilities

  • Participate in defining and implementing technical direction for the development of the company's technology
  • Be the key data science advisor for the company, collaborating with leadership and our marketing and engineering teams to identify opportunities to apply algorithms and machine-learning in our platform to gain the greatest benefit possible
  • Drive business success by translating business requirements into ML algorithms and models to meet product functional objectives and key results
  • Help architect and plan for changes and enhancements to the product in a proactive fashion
  • Work with a data-centric platform in capturing, transforming, and ingesting large volumes of data from multiple sources to feed into your data algorithms
  • Harvest multiple yearsâ€™ worth of historical data to train your models across thousands of possible user-behavior scenarios
  • Work with our cloud-based platform in AWS, manipulating data through multiple AWS-provided services, such as Kinesis, Redshift, RDS, SQS, SNS, etc
  • Work with our data analytics team to implement the needed ETL/ELT transformations required to implement our predictive models

Requirements

  • 6+ years of data science / machine learning experience
  • 3+ years of data analytics experience
  • Strong knowledge of a wide variety of data algorithms and approaches (supervised, unsupervised, reinforcement, linear regression, logistic regression, Naive Bayes, K-Means, Random Forest, greedy algorithms, etc)
  • Strong knowledge of multiple database engines (MySQL/MariaDB, Redshift, DynamoDB, Redis, etc)
  • Experience with PHI, PII, and SOC compliance guidelines and controls
  • Experience with back-testing, A/B testing, regression testing, model testing, etc
