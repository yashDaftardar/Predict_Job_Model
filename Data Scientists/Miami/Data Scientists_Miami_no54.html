Restaurant Brands International Inc. ("RBI") is one of the world's largest quick service restaurant companies with more than $30 billion in system-wide sales and over 25,000 restaurants in more than 100 countries and U.S. territories. RBI owns three of the world's most prominent and iconic quick service restaurant brands – TIM HORTONS®, BURGER KING®, and POPEYES®. These independently operated brands have been serving their respective guests, franchisees and communities for over 40 years. We are looking for team players that show a true yearning to improve not only our business, but themselves. With meritocracy is at the forefront of our company's culture, we live and breathe a performance-driven environment where hard work and results speak for themselves. Top performers are rewarded for what they achieve, and with success, you can accelerate your career as quickly as your talent will allow.

Job Overview :

At RBI we are a growth company with ambitious goals. We know that we can only make our dream a reality by having the right talent on our side. Our Machine Learning team is growing! We are looking for ambitious Machine Learning Engineers looking to launch their careers by helping us scale big projects across our brands, including Recommendation Engines, Voice Recognition, Customer Segmentation, Site Selection, and Price Scraping. We're looking for recent graduates, as well as Machine Learning Engineers with 1-2 years of prior experience!


Responsibilities :

  • Build machine learning models in open source languages like Python/Java/Scala and deploy using cloud infrastructure available in Amazon Web Services
  • Understand the business requirement and suggest/evaluate the right features and technique best suited to train and test the model
  • Work on feature engineering to ensure most information going into the model for prediction accuracy
  • Collaborate with internal stakeholders to understand the business strategy and work towards supporting that by gathering the right data and building machine learning models
  • Follow machine learning model management and life-cycle best practice for all Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning models created

Required Qualifications & Skills :

  • A Bachelor or Master's degree – ideally in Computer Science, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Data Modeling, Data Mining, Computer Programming, Software Engineering, Data Science, Business Analytics, or a similar technical field of study
  • A performance-focused, results-oriented approach – you'll have a strong work ethic and a determination to achieve success
  • Strong ownership mentality and exceptional levels of self-motivation, ambition, and entrepreneurial spirit
  • Excellent interpersonal, communication, leadership, motivational, and critical thinking skills
  • Knowledge in on or more general-purpose programming languages

Preferred Qualifications & Skills :

  • SQL, Python, Excel, Database Concepts
  • Scala, Java, Hadoop, Apache Spark, Apache MXnet, Docker, Kubernetes, MangoDB, Neo4j, Splunk / DataDog, Linux, AWS
  • Open source project contribution experience
