Our Opportunity:

Chewy is seeking a Senior Merchandising Systems Data Analyst on the Merchandising Systems and Services team (MSS) in Dania Beach, Florida. The Merchandising Systems and Services Team (MSS) team is responsible for creating the systems, services, and process infrastructure for all Chewy businesses. Our team's mission is to be the backbone for Chewy's Business/Category teams, helping win long-term customer trust, business growth, and profitability. We own the Pricing, Catalog/Content, and Vendor Systems & Services that have automation, product, program, and operational components.

The ideal candidate will provide a strong mix of information systems and merchandising business process knowledge including data analysis, scenario modelling, SQL, functional knowledge of catalog and/or ERP systems, and technical knowledge of underlying system data structures and processes. This skill set will be applied to item-level data across the Chewy catalog ensuring accuracy and completeness ultimately influencing the customer experience.

What You'll Do:

  • Learn business processes and translate them to business requirements, user stories and system design to understand all facets related to development requests
  • Autonomously complete tasks within the track owned for a given system
  • Identify risks, issues or potential defects in any phase of the project life cycle and managing them through closure
  • Provides project stakeholders information relevant to make/influence decisions that may improve quality of applications
  • Synthesize large quantities of complex data into usable/actionable information
  • Participate in system implementation projects (requirements documentation, systems configuration, test documentation/execution, issue identification and resolution)
  • Provide support to end users as priority when it impacts business or provide a Standard Operating Procedure manual for systems

What You'll Need:

  • BA in Computer Science or other engineering fields, Economics/Finance or Master's degree (preferably in General Management/Finance/Computer Science/Tech Management/Engineering)
  • 3+ years of experience in a business systems or merchandising analytics capacity as a subject-matter expert
  • Background in creating, managing to and improving metrics (KPI) using quantitative analysis
  • Advanced experience with Microsoft suite of office products
  • Highly proficient in SQL and Tableau
  • Demonstrated ability to work in a team environment
  • Ability to think and react in a high-energy, fast-paced environment
  • Strong communication (verbal/written) and interpersonal skills to convey key insights from complex analysis in summarized business terms and an ability to effectively communicate with technical teams
  • An entrepreneurial mindset and a driving passion to change, challenge, and improve current processes
  • Position may require occasional travel

Bonus:

  • Relevant work experience using Python or R for data analytics
  • Prior experience in Software Quality Processes and Atlassian Tools such as (Confluence, JIRA, etc.)
  • Prior experience with ERP systems
  • E-commerce background is a plus
