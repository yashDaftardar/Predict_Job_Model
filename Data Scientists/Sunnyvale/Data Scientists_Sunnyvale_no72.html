Summary

We are seeking a Senior Materials Data Scientist to design, develop, manage, and execute various materials characterization, utilize programming expertise to identify patterns from the data generated, and to correlate sequencing performance with both orthogonal testing and in-line monitoring data. This team member will be critical in developing a deep intuitive knowledge of the sequencing workflow, and the impact of component properties on sequencing performance. The data and correlation generated will be used to develop new test specifications that reflect consumable device behaviors, and to develop, validate, and implement tests for process monitoring and improvement.

Responsibilities

  • Design, prepare, and execute a wide range of materials, mechanical, surface, analytical, and electrochemical characterizations

  • Develop an d select appropriate characterization methods to understand and minimize processes and components variability

  • Contribute in failure analysis and root cause analysis efforts

  • Perform correlation studies between orthogonal testing, in-line measurement data, and sequencing performance to develop predictive performance capability

  • Develop Computer Vision and Image Processing algorithms and methods for automated inspection machine

  • Use technical knowledge to influence and design new test methods, then expand the system to improve process and components reliability

Qualifications

  • BS in materials science and engineering or related discipline is required

  • 5+ years of industrial experience with BS, 3+ years with MS and 0+ years of experience with PhD

  • Strong understanding of analytical, surface, and materials characterization methods such as XPS, Auger Spectroscopy, AFM, TOF-SIMS, Ellipsometry, Surface Energy/Contact Angle Goniometry, tensile test, Raman Spectroscopy, ICP-MS, GC-MS, BET, and other relevant methods

  • Computer Vision expertise in areas related to camera image processing, such as: Intrinsic and extrinsic camera calibration, Multiple-view geometry, Bundle adjustment, Sensor fusion, Image enhancement, Knowledge of depth sensing technologies such as Lidar and structured light, Knowledge of Machine Learning methods and principles relevant for image analysis, object detection and recognition

  • Fundamental knowledge of material, mechanical, surface, electrochemical characterizations on polymeric, metallic, and ceramic materials.

  • Hands on experience on experimental design and execution are required to build correlation between processing, properties, and performance.

  • Strong proficiency in Python, SQL, R, JMP or similar data analysis and programming languages

  • Strong foundation in statistics

  • Experience building data visualizations

  • Evidence of being a self-starter and bringing projects to completion

  • Expertise in materials selection and compatibility, biocompatibility

  • Hands on experience working in laboratory environment

  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
