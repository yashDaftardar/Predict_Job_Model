Voya Financial has an opportunity for a Data Scientist to join our Enterprise Data Science team in delivering innovative data science capabilities to support Voya’s retirement, employee benefits, and investment management businesses. The ideal candidate is skilled at selecting and applying appropriate data science techniques to large data sets to deliver insights, discover patterns, and build machine learning models for a variety of business use cases. Strong interpersonal skills and business acumen are required to work with a wide range of stakeholders and functional teams from different business domains.

Profile Description:

  • Under the guidance of senior members of the Data Science team, work with business stakeholders and other team members to deliver high-impact data science solutions into the business.
  • Utilize a variety of data science technologies and tools, both on premises and in the cloud.
  • Apply business acumen and “number sense” to interpret findings, evaluate model effectiveness, and measure use case financial impact.
  • Access, prepare, and analyze data from structured and unstructured sources, including internal databases as well as non-traditional external data sources.
  • Present findings and results in a clear and understandable way that is tailored to technical or business audiences, including effective data visualizations as needed.

Knowledge and Experience:

  • Strong problem solving skills with an emphasis on solution design and development.
  • Experience with core data science languages (Python, SQL, etc.) to access and manipulate large data sets.
  • Knowledge of a variety of machine learning techniques and their real-world advantages/drawbacks.
  • Knowledge of statistical techniques and concepts (regression, properties of distributions, statistical tests and proper usage, etc.) and experience with applications.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills for coordinating across teams.
  • A drive to learn and master new technologies and techniques as well as new business domains.
  • 2-4 years of data science work experience and/or an advanced degree in a related field (statistics, mathematics, computer science or another quantitative field).
  • Working knowledge of core data science tools and technologies, such as:
    • Modeling algorithms and techniques: decision trees, neural networks, clustering, GLM / regression, random forest, NLP, etc.
    • Data access and manipulation: SQL, Python, R, etc.
    • Cloud-based data science solutions: Azure (preferred), GCP or AWS.
    • Distributed data/computing tools: Hadoop, Hive, Spark etc.
    • Data visualization: Tableau (preferred).

Critical Skills
At Voya, we have identified the following critical skills which are key to success in our culture:

  • Customer Focused: Passionate drive to delight our customers and offer unique solutions that deliver on their expectations.
  • Critical Thinking: Thoughtful process of analyzing data and problem solving data to reach a well-reasoned solution.
  • Team Mentality: Partnering effectively to drive our culture and execute on our common goals.
  • Business Acumen: Appreciation and understanding of the financial services industry in order to make sound business decisions.
  • Learning Agility: Openness to new ways of thinking and acquiring new skills to retain a competitive advantage.
