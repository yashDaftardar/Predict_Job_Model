Full Job Description

You are a driven and motivated problem solver ready to pursue meaningful work. You strive to make an impact every day & not only at work, but in your personal life and community too. If that sounds like you, then you've landed in the right place.

Are you a motivated business person or an existing business analyst that has always been interested in data? This role can expand your knowledge and your network, quench your interests, and help you learn about Global Specialty data. If you think this opportunity is a fit for your career you should apply. If you are not sure you can have a conversation with your manager.

The Enterprise Data Organization is seeking a qualified candidate to fill the role of Sr. Business Data Analyst to support Global Specialty data initiatives. The ideal candidate will be a dynamic person that has an awareness of agile methodology and can play a multi-faceted role within the team. This person will be adaptable to change and can work fluidly with a team of resources performing in different roles on the team.

The opportunity to work with our business partners in a collaborative environment is a unique chance to understand the internal data collection process as well as delivery of critical business data supporting all aspects of reporting and analytics. Projects include data integration across multiple systems and products within the Global Specialty organization and uses AWS cloud technologies / services.

This position offers opportunities to grow your skills in data analysis, data testing, consulting, and customer interfacing competencies.

Responsibilities:

  • Must be authorized to work in the U.S. without company sponsorship

  • Collaboration with a high-performing, forward-focused team, Product Owner(s) and Business stakeholders engagement
  • Be part of a Scrum Team – driving work independently and/or collaboratively towards achieving business outcomes
  • Applies techniques to draw out data and analytics business requirements
  • Structure and drive working and informational sessions that promote collaboration, synergy, and achieve desired outcomes
  • Understands the scope of the project and helps resolve scope questions and issues related to the work
  • Acts as a change agent to help and drive implementation of industry best practices.
  • Act as liaison, consultant between various business functions impacting organization and leadership
  • Supports testing needs
  • Clearly give and receive information, requirements and specifications (facts, ideas, perspectives) through verbal, written and presentation communication methods
  • Learn and independently perform data analysis, data discovery and data profiling using industry standard querying tools to support business requirements development that are complete, clear, unambiguous, well written and appropriate to audience
  • Learn and expand your analysis capabilities such as business process mapping, data analysis, data testing, data wrangling, and data transformation mappings to build data assets that enable business value generation. Stay up-to-date with technology’s constantly changing landscape
  • Provides support and consulting by answering questions, researching possible solutions to complex problems, and fostering open communication between the customer and development team
  • As an experienced teammate in the field, serves as a leader to others and challenges the status quo when necessary

Qualifications

  • 4+ years of deep practical experience in the role, with repeated experience performing business analysis in a variety of complex situations
  • Bachelor’s degree in relevant field
  • SQL and working with disparate datasets
  • Strong written and oral communication skill with the ability to communicate technical information to non-technical audience
  • Experienced facilitator with strong presentation skills
  • Strategic thinking skills: Ability to provide options and assess solutions, looking at the business holistically
  • Experience understanding integrations and data standards that develop quality documentation for data mapping documents and process flows
  • Ability to effectively interact with all levels off the organization (ie: Entry level end-user through to senior management) and demonstrate strong leadership skills
  • Experience working in a multi-sources offshore deliver centers and shared services environment is a plus
  • Experienced in developing estimates and prioritizing deliverables
  • Experience in Agile development including prioritizing, creating/updating features and stories, Test Driven Development, etc
  • Experience working as a member of a scrum team is required, experience with Scaled Agile framework is preferred
  • Motivated towards self-improvement in building their own technical and soft skills
  • AWS Cloud Practitioner Certification a plus
  • Ability to use data tools such as: Toad query or Toad End Point, Business Objects or Tableau
