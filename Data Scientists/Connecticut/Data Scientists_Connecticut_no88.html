Job Description Summary

BI Analytics & Research Data Engineering team is seeking a highly motivated and skilled Data Engineer to provide the design, development, and delivery of data and analytical solutions in support of a large data scientist team.

This role will partner with our data scientists on research projects in performing data acquisition, exploration, building pipelines and solutions for statistical models, machine learning, and artificial intelligence from development, through implementation and monitoring. Multiple technologies are used, with new approaches emerging quickly in this fast moving and exciting role.

For this role, the candidate should have proficiency with SQL, skilled with Python and cloud experience. Experience with AWS cloud platform is a plus. They should be able to work with different data environments (Snowflake, Teradata, Hive, etc.) and collaborate with multiple teams. The candidate will have a passion for working with data and problem solving.

Primary Job Duties & Responsibilities

You will:

  • Build and operationalize complex data solutions, correct problems, apply transformations, and recommend data cleansing/quality solutions.
  • Design moderate complex data solutions and lead medium scale projects.
  • Perform analysis of moderately complex data sources to determine value and use and recommend data to include in analytical processes.
  • Lead small to medium scale data projects and mentors junior team members
  • Provide design and develops data pipelines in python, spark by performing moderate to complex data transformations and derivations
  • Leverage standard data integration patterns, data feature engineering to build data pipelines for both on premise and cloud.
  • Implement Orchestration, Automation tasks and data movement for on premise and AWS cloud applications
  • Incorporate core data management competencies including data governance, data security and data quality
  • Perform data and system analysis, assessment and resolution for defects and incidents of moderate complexity and correct as appropriate.
  • Perform testing (functional and non-functional) on data movement and transformation code and data components.
  • Ensure work complies with Travelers standards, processes, and protocols.
  • Create moderate (technology and features) data visualization techniques to help support data exploration.
  • Utilizes business knowledge to explain technical activities in business terms.
  • Actively seeks opportunities to expand technical knowledge and capabilities.
  • Applies knowledge of current industry trends and techniques to formulate solutions within the context of assigned projects and/or enhancements.
  • Collaborate within and across teams to support delivery and educate end users on data products/analytic environment.
  • Builds effective relationships with stakeholders.
  • Provides guidance and mentorship to lower level technical employees.
