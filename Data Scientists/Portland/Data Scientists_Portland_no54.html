Job Description

As a LIMS Administrator at AbSci, you will play a critical role in how experimental data is managed, stored, and analyzed in our Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) Benchling. You will work directly on system configuration, troubleshooting issues, user access, reporting, authoring SOPs, and ensuring compliance to our data management rules. You will be a strong advocate across the company for data management best practices and you will help science teams adapt their workflows to make the most out of our LIMS system. You will work directly with every science team including Research and Development, Molecular Science, High Throughput Screening, Fermentation, Protein Purification, and Analytical Development and help them adapt their workflows to work with Benchling. You will learn a broad range of industry relevant skills, help shape AbSci's strategic development of core technology, and work closely with a very talented team with some of the deepest domain expertise in the industry. If you're excited about working on novel technology that empowers scientists to make the world a better place, we would love to hear from you.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Serve as a technical expert to assist and provide support to end users
  • Create Benchling Registry Schemas, Notebook Templates, and Result Tables for science teams to support their workflows
  • Manage user groups and project organization folders across all science teams
  • Promote data management best practices
  • Assist in authoring SOPs and technical specifications for LIMS related tasks
  • Manage user account information, system configuration, change request administration, report generation, data export, database management/validation, and system troubleshooting
  • Write SQL queries for generating reports

Qualifications/Skills:

  • 2-5 years of experience working with biological data
  • 2-5 years working with a LIMS system
  • Strong understanding of SQL
  • Experience working with relational databases (MySQL, PostgreSQL, etc.)
  • Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously
  • Strong written and oral communication skills
  • Basic skills and fluency in one LIMS system
  • Ability to work independently and in teams
  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Enjoy empowering scientists with novel software solutions

Nice to Have Experience and Knowledge:

  • Experience working with Benchling
  • Python scripting skills
  • Experience working with REST APIs
  • Experience working with medium-to-large size datasets
  • Project management experience
