Proven people data analyst who will be responsible for leading people analytics projects and analysis for all of Tektronix

As the lead people data analyst for Tektronix, you will work within our People Analytics team and closely partner with our global HR team, Finance, and Technology services. You will also partner closely with the Fortive reporting and analytics team to drive roadmap improvements and build new capabilities for use across all Fortive Operating Companies.

The Senior People Data Analyst strives to deliver useful and accurate reporting data and analytical insights to create People-related strategies across the business and embed data and analytics into Tektronix HR processes. This role will work closely with various cross-functional HR Partners and stakeholders to effectively fulfill ad hoc requests, ask appropriate questions to understand business problems and respond to requests with logical, organized, data/insights in a timely manner.

Responsibilities:
Creates elegant data visualizations that help our HR team and business leaders to better understand their people data, make better decisions, and drive better business outcomes

Collaborates with Director on People Analytics strategy

Assists in creating people strategies for business units by utilizing Tektronix people data

Serves as the primary point of contact for the global HR team in answering people data questions

Works with Technology Services to design and implement back-end data architecture solutions that enable Tektronix to unleash the power of its people data

Provides ongoing support and leadership for HR in raising its collective data acumen and data proficiency

Provides leadership and support for reporting and analytics projects at the Fortive level and across Fortive operating companies

Qualifications:
Proven ability to manage concurrent projects while working within a global team to drive outcomes

Strong communication skills and an ability to distill complex information into actionable insight

Excel experience including creating pivot reports, conditional formulas and formatting, VLOOKUPs, and charts/graphs partnered with analytical and problem solving experience

Knowledge of data management practices, specifically with data validation and auditing from multiple data systems

Proven ability to manipulate and clean large data sets

Experience initiating and driving projects to completion with minimal guidance
Experience having effective conversations with clients about their support needs and requirements, manage the intake process, and ask the right questions to scope and solve requests

Additional experience:
BA/BS in a field that emphasizes data analysis and visualization (e.g., computer science, social sciences, physical sciences, math, engineering, or statistics), or equivalent work experience

3+ years of experience working with data visualization tools, preferably Microsoft PowerBI or Oracle Analytics Cloud

5+ years of experience building data systems and solutions using data from multiple internal and external sources
3+ years of experience in delivering enterprise-wide HR data products and solutions
