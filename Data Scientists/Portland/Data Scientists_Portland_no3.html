Full Job Description

The Viewpoint Internship is a full time summer internship that will be approximately 12 weeks and will be based in our Portland, Oregon headquarters. Viewpoint is a provider of innovative construction-specific software solutions to the global construction and capital project industries. For over 40 years Viewpoint has been committed to bringing information technology-based efficiencies to construction projects and their stakeholders, engaging in a highly collaborative process with customers to ensure every product and service addresses key industry issues.

The internship program provides college students the opportunity to learn and practice job-specific as well as communication and teamwork skills in a creative work environment. Interns will gain first-hand experience with daily operations. Interns will receive hands-on experience working with teams of Engineers and Analysts with industry-leading, vertical software solutions in the construction market.

Join the Data Science team at Viewpoint, in the data analytics division to work with us to improve efficiency and profitability for our customers. You will work with experienced data scientists to analyze data, build models, and find insights in the data. You will build industry benchmarks from our extensive data repository. If you enjoy challenging problems and solving them by using a variety of technical and business skills, join us and bring your skills and point of view to the team!

Responsibilities

  • Participate in engagement and discovery sessions between data scientists to identify problems/gaps as well as innovation opportunities that can be solved or enabled by the data analytic and modeling

  • Analyze data and build statistical and machine learning models to improve business performance

  • Build indices and industry benchmarks

  • Implement and test models in a POC (proof of concept) scale to operationalize the outcomes of the predictive modeling and validate the results in the real-world.

  • Be encouraged and expected to innovate and be creative in your data analysis, problem solving and presentation of solutions.

Required Skills

  • Coursework toward a degree in mathematics, statistics, computer science, or similar

  • Ability to interpret data and apply results to business problems

  • Ability to multi-task and act independently as needed

  • Comfortable communicating with technical and non-technical business partners

Preferred Skills

  • Experience with SAS, R, Python or other programming languages or statistical tools

  • Experience with SQL

  • Experience using source control

Viewpoint is an Equal Opportunity Employer

DISCLOSURE for US-BASED POSITIONS ONLY: Viewpoint requires a criminal background investigation, and employment and education verification as a condition of employment.
