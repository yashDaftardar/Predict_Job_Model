Full Job Description

Grid Dynamics is looking for a Principal Machine Learning Engineer with background in product discovery and recommendation systems. Responsibilities
  • Design and supervise implementation solutions in search and recommendation space using state-of-the art techniques. Identify and prototype solutions.
  • Contribute to the creation of the product vision, roadmap and architecture in the space of product discovery and recommendation systems.
  • Reverse engineer and analyse existing systems, identify gaps and possible improvements.
  • Provide guidance on modern cloud-native ML deployment and management platforms.
  • Consult a team of up to 15 ML-, Data-, and Software engineers across different time zones
  • Consult Grid Dynamics customers on applicability of different solutions and techniques
  • Participate in pre-sales and project initiation
  • Produce technical articles and case studies in collaboration with the CTO office.
Qualifications
  • 7+ years of technical experience
  • 5+ years of experience developing ML-based solutions with classical and deep learning approaches
  • Working knowledge of server-side programming, Python, jupyter, and modern public cloud platforms
  • Strong systems analysis and data analysis skills
  • Excellent communication and technical writing skills
Preferred qualifications
  • Experience building information retrieval and recommendation systems
  • Experience with Lucene-based open source search engines (Elasticsearch and/or Solr)
  • Experience with natural language processing, using classic and deep learning based approaches
  • Experience building data-intensive applications and real-time data processing using hosted cloud-native data platforms
  • Background in statistics
  • Working knowledge of Java and JVM platform
  • Familiarity with AWS

We offer:

  • Opportunity to work on bleeding-edge projects
  • Work with a highly motivated and dedicated team
  • Competitive salary
  • Flexible schedule
  • Medical insurance
  • Benefits program
  • Corporate social events
