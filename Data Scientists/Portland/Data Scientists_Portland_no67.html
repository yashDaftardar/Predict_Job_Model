AbSci is a growth stage synthetic biology company located in a new, state-of-the art laboratory in downtown Vancouver, WA, just across the river from Portland, OR. AbSci is a global leader and innovator of revolutionary biomanufacturing technologies, designing and programming cells for next-generation biopharmaceuticals. Our Protein Printing™ technology results in dramatic reductions in the cost of producing biologics, acceleration of drug discovery timelines, and enables the production of previously difficult or impossible-to-produce molecules. Our technology is a new paradigm in protein production, as it increases discovery throughput, accelerates development, reduces manufacturing costs, and increases plant capacity for the biopharmaceutical industry.

Job Description

AbSci is making AI a core technology with the goal of accelerating and improving drug development by enabling in silico design of novel biologics and their associated producing cell lines. The Denovium Engine™ is the platform managing this technology, including data processing, deep learning model training, and model deployment. The Denovium Engine™ is continually being developed and requires an architect to coordinate developers, streamline code, and support testing.

Responsibilities

The Senior Software Engineer will be tasked with understanding the research needs of the Denovium Engine™, keep up with active projects and understand their goals and any bottlenecks, organize code for quality and extensibility, and coordinate QC and additional features. This role will be key in establishing standards for versioning, CLI interfaces and data structures. You'll also have the opportunity to guide scientist feature development and support ongoing R&D efforts. In this role you will have the opportunity to do development on real problems and be at the forefront of the blend of AI and biotechnology.

Qualifications

  • B.S. and 10+ years of hands-on software development experience
  • 4+ years of software project management skills - demonstrable experience with self-planning and execution
  • An understanding of software development best-practices, including a willingness to break the rulebook when it hinders active development
  • Excellent communication skills and a forward-thinking mindset
  • Experience working with multiple stakeholders

Huge plus (but not required):

  • Experience in Python, Tensorflow, and/or HPC
  • Background in cloud computing
  • Experience or education in synthetic biology or similar field
  • Advanced degree in computer science

We seek candidates with a passion for building a creative company culture that is collaborative, multidisciplinary, and committed to make a transformative impact on our industry. To be successful in this role, we look for passion in technology innovation, adept technical skills, and excitement about working in a dynamic startup environment. A demonstrable track record of success in functioning as part of a project team is essential.
