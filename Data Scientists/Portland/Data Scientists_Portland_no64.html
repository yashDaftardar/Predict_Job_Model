Job Description

AbSci is making AI a core technology with the goal of accelerating and improving drug development by enabling in silico design of novel biologics and their associated producing cell lines. The Denovium Engine™ is the platform managing this technology, including data processing, deep learning model training, and model deployment. We plan to further develop the Denovium Engine™ to support AI-driven cell line development for next-generation biologics. The Deep Learning Engineer will be the technical expert for the AI-guided cell line development capability of the Denovium Engine™.

Responsibilities

  • Designing and training DL models addressing the opportunities for AI-Guided optimization of AbSci's Protein Printing platform
  • Work closely with Data Engineers to automate the data processing of the relevant training data
  • Work closely with Computational Biologists to design and optimize model architectures, hyper parameter tuning, and performance metrics
  • Manage large datasets and deep learning models with increased complexity

Qualifications

  • Masters or PhD in computer science, bioinformatics or related field
  • 3+ years of industry experience developing and training deep learning models, preferably in the life-sciences domain
  • Strong Python programming skills, including with Numpy, Scipy, and Pandas
  • Demonstrated ability to work collaboratively on projects with multiple contributors
  • Experience with PyTorch, Tensorflow or similar platforms
  • Ability to understand cell line development and NGS, or learn quickly

Huge plus (but not required):

  • Experience in a Life Sciences company
  • NGS Analysis
  • Understanding of molecular science concepts
