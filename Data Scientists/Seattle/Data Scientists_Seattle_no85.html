Machine Learning-focused Software Engineer Internship

Remote

This full-time twelve-week internship position is scheduled to begin in May/June 2021

Cambia Health Solutions is working to create a seamless and frictionless health care experience for consumers nationwide. This presents a unique challenge and opportunity for innovative solutions.

Join the Cambia AI Engineering team to build AI-powered healthcare experience used by millions of people. We're looking for engineers who bring in fresh ideas from different areas, like distributed computing, scalability and storage, security, data analytics and visualization. We need our engineers to be versatile, demonstrate leadership qualities and be eager to tackle new problems.

As a Software Engineer Intern, you will be involved in requirements gathering, design and develop new features for the platform, and handle scalability and performance challenges. You will participate in a high-paced agile environment with deep focus on best software practices, cutting edge technologies, innovation and code quality.

Essential Function of the Data Science Engineer Internship:

  • Work and interact across the organization with a variety of business units.
  • Code high-volume and scalable software
  • Create backend services using Java, SQL, protobuf, grpc, and REST API
  • Partner closely with cross functional teams in design, product and other business units

Key Qualifications and Experience:

  • Must be pursuing an undergraduate or graduate degree in Computer Science or related field.
  • Must have a minimum 3.0 cumulative grade point average.
  • Solid data structures & algorithms background.
  • Strong applied experience. You’ve built, broken, and rebuilt software applications. We’re looking for creative thinkers who also know how to create real-world products.
  • Working knowledge of Java, and/or Python.
  • Excellent problem solving, critical thinking, and communication skills.
  • Strong ability to evaluate and adopt new technologies in a short time. A constant desire to grow, learn, and explore new things.

The following skills/experiences/knowledge are a plus:

  • Demonstrated experience with providing advanced data insights in healthcare, insurance, e-commerce, internet advertising or related field.
  • Coursework or practical experience with machine learning, data mining, and constructing analytical models and algorithms.
  • Strong proficiency writing production-quality code, preferably industry engineering experience with machine learning projects (e.g. recommendation, ranking, optimization).
  • Demonstrated proficiency in Relational databases and NoSQL databases.
  • Experience in one of AWS, Azure, Google Cloud or other cloud ecosystems.
  • Experience in Agile software development process, test-driven development and continuous integration.
  • Experience developing and maintaining production services and libraries (preferably in cloud environments), including strong knowledge of algorithms and data structures.
  • Demonstrated understanding of the principles of rigorous code review.
