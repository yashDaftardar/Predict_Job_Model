Data Scientist/Analyst, Security Engineering Fundamentals
Digital Security & Resilience, Microsoft Digital

Do you want to lead Microsoft into the future as the shining enterprise example for security done right? Are you passionate about making sure Microsoft’s security posture stays well out in front of its reputation and market value? The mission of Microsoft Digital is to power, protect, and transform Microsoft as the voice of our digital transition in the market. As part of Microsoft’s Cloud + AI Group, we are responsible for building, managing, and securing the platform, products, processes, and services that powers Microsoft. We build, maintain, and implement a cloud-first approach to our technology and experiences, from custom-built business solutions developing our campus of the future and our productivity and collaboration experiences like Teams and SharePoint, to horizontal 3rd party solutions like SAP and Adobe. As a steward of Microsoft and our customer’s data, a core function of Microsoft Digital is ensuring the security of every aspect of the business. Microsoft Digital is responsible for company-wide information security and compliance, with a strategic focus on information protection, assessment, awareness, governance, and enterprise business continuity. Microsoft Digital’s charter is also to influence and work alongside engineers across the company and with strategic partners to build and grow their cloud products and services. As customer zero, we deploy these services inside Microsoft and then share best practices with enterprise customers at scale across the globe.

Do you have a passion for information security? Do you get excited about protecting Microsoft by setting the security direction and guidance for the company? This is your opportunity to be a leader in protecting Microsoft corporate services that enable our business and supports our customers against current & future threats.

Microsoft Digital has a Data Scientist/Analyst role available within the Digital Security and Resilience organization in the Office of the CTO! Our team consists of engineering and business professionals, working on critical security initiatives, committed to delivering quality services and programs to the rest of Microsoft (End Users). As a Data Analyst, you will use your technical knowledge & experience to improve & expand the security of Microsoft’s Security Engineering Fundamentals program. As part of a high impact security team, you will be empowered to lead and work across the Microsoft Digital organization and multiple product teams to raise the bar for protecting the enterprise.

In this role, you will bring your advanced technical expertise to build a new tool that will adapt and collect telemetry from across the company in support of our compliance efforts on our key security metrics through company-wide Microsoft Security Fundamentals.

Responsibilities

Key responsibilities:
  • Work with security teams across the company to ensure that the data collected/aggregated in the tooling system is precise & accurate.
  • Work with program managers and software engineers to ensure awareness and plan for any adaptations or normalization of telemetry needed to drive toward planned results
  • Work with program managers to build models and methods that surface insights to engineering teams and Microsoft senior leadership clearly and concisely.
  • System design through well-defined interfaces across multiple components, code reviews, leveraging data / telemetry to make decisions.
  • Gain a working understanding of Microsoft businesses and collaborate with mentors and leaders to contribute to cohesive, end-to-end experiences for our users.
  • Stay educated on existing, emerging technologies and do Proof of Concepts to evaluate technology fits for customer needs.

Qualifications

Basic Qualifications:
  • 7+ years of experience in analytics with proven quantitative orientation including strong problem solving, technical and data analysis skills
  • 4+ years Programming experience with SQL or Databricks with an understanding of data structure and data storage solutions
  • 2+ years’ experience with one or more general purpose programming/script languages including but not limited to: Java, C/C++, C#, Python, JavaScript, PowerShell
  • BA or BS degree in Statistics/Math/Computer Science/Economics/Business or Engineering (or equivalent work experience)
Preferred Qualifications:
  • Prior cyber security or compliance related experience
  • Experience with technical design and development on highly availability systems in high scale cloud environments
  • Advanced working knowledge of large data manipulation and data mining.
  • Experience with big data technologies such as Azure Data Lake, HD Insight, Spark, Hive, etc.
  • Self-driven, and a proven ability to deliver on ambiguous projects with incomplete or dirty data
  • Ability to work in a team environment that promotes collaboration and succeed in a matrix structure by influencing new thinking
  • Excellent judgment, decision making skills, and ability to work with deadlines
