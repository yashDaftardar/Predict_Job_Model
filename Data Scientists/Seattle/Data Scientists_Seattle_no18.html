Characteristics of our team

T-Mobile Marketing Solutions (TMS), a T-Mobile division, is solely focused on creating products for the AdTech space. The Data Science team is responsible for developing the underlying AI that powers these products. We partner with other groups within TMS, including Data Engineering, Product Management, Sales, and Marketing, to develop our existing product roadmap's features and functionality. However, we also create entirely new ideas that represent the next generation of offerings for our marketing platform. TMS has more of a start-up culture, backed by a mature parent company (T-Mobile). The combination creates a unique atmosphere of entrepreneurialism mixed with solid business acumen and ongoing access to resources.
