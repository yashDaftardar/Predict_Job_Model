Senior Product Manager, Data, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning

As Expedia Group is building the world most intelligent travel platform, we are evolving our operations and technical architecture towards that of a true platform company that creates network effects by facilitating exchanges between customers and suppliers, a smart business powered by AI.

To help deliver these goals, we are looking for a commercially minded and capable Sr Product Manager, to drive the effort of building an extensible, robust, and fully integrated Data/AI/ML platform supporting multiple Expedia brands, divisions, and travel suppliers.

Are you passionate about the technology needed to drive a multi-billion-dollar business? Do you love building creative, highly scalable and expansible intelligent virtual travel agents from concept to platforms? Is bringing the world within reach through effective use of data, ML, and AI challenging and attractive to you?

What you’ll do:

  • You will help build and support a data/metadata repository (Datahub) and refine/advance its integration into the broader data platform and its constituent AI/ML components and clients

  • You are a recognized leader with a bias to action

  • You can translate AI platform strategy into a roadmap that drives the development with the latest technology, innovation, and realizing market value, against short term and long-term goals

  • You are responsible for the planning and execution throughout the platform lifecycle, including gathering and prioritizing customer requirements, defining the vision

  • Working closely with engineering, sales, marketing and support to ensure commercial realization and customer satisfaction goals are met

  • You can build strong and constructive working relationships to canvas and align alternative views into a single Data/AI platform and roadmap

  • You are adaptive and able to work through the conflicting requests, quick in making decisions based on the best interests of all EG, balance among time to market, serving each division properly, and deliver the work with engineering excellence

  • You pose good judgment in balancing between innovation and robust operation, able to optimize productivity and efficiency when working with the broader Technology, Data, Sales and Marketing teams

Who you are:

  • You have demonstrated growth mindset

  • You have the curiosity, humility and learning agility to continuously learn & grow from all experiences and feedback, constantly and enthusiastically looking for opportunities to grow and improve the business, and yourself

  • You welcome robust challenge and seek insights from others to improve your own learning and understanding

  • You are talented in strong relationship building, able to navigate through situations to ensure the most robust and pragmatic solutions

  • You have strong products and engineering background, efficient in developing a product plan, executing on it, and delivering commercial value to meet the market demand

  • You possess financial acumen and fully understand daily business performance, quick in getting to root cause, as well as build out financial models for new platform initiatives

  • You display an end-to-end solution thought leadership and partnership, known with the ability to resolve conflict and protect productivity

  • You are successful at influencing and working with diverse groups in divisional teams

  • You are skilled at canvassing opinions and alternative views

  • You are at ease with evaluating and making recommendations across competing and equally critical business needs

  • You demonstrated commercial results which balancing innovation and commercial realization, in time delivery to meet both short term and long-term goals

  • You are big picture focused whilst skilled at zeroing in on the product and engineering details and delivering continuous improvement

  • You have BS or BA degrees in Computer Science, engineering, and business field

  • You have 7+ years relevant experience

  • Product management experience in Expedia Group is desirable

  • Previous knowledge and experience in AI/ML/Data solutions

  • Critical Competencies for Success

  • Passion for Results

  • Drive high standards for individual, team, and organizational accomplishment; tenaciously work to meet or exceed challenging goals

  • Derive satisfaction from goal achievement and continuous improvement

  • Compelling Communication and Influencing Skills

  • Clearly and succinctly convey information and ideas to individuals and groups

  • Communicate in a focused and compelling way that captures and holds others’ attention

  • Create and execute influencing strategies that persuade key team leaders and members to act towards advancing shared interests and business goals

  • Analytical Abilities: Secure and compare information from multiple sources to identify core issues

  • Commit to an action plan after weighing alternative solutions against decision criteria; enthusiastically and quickly make sense of complex issues

  • Respond effectively to complex and ambiguous situations

  • Intuition for Business: Demonstrate a keen understanding of basic business operations and the organizational levers (systems, processes, departments, functions) that drive profitable growth; draws from personal experience to quickly evaluate business plans and processes to identify data or recommendations that need further investigation

  • Build and Maintain High-Performing Organizations

  • Establish systems and processes to attract, develop, engage, and retain talented individuals

  • creating a work environment where people can realize their full potential; thus, allowing the organization to meet current and future business challenges

  • Benefits & Perks: Competitive health and insurance benefits, Competitive salary, Annual target bonus or commission, Parental leave for up to 20 weeks (dependent on eligibility), Paid vacation and sick time, Employee Stock Purchase Program, Free snacks and beverages, Frequent company update talks with our leadership team, Free listing on HomeAway.com, Electronic, adjustable stand-up desk, Discounted Metro & Rail pass, Casual dress
