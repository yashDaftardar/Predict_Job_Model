Full Job Description

Conversica is the pioneer and leading provider of Intelligent Virtual Assistants helping organizations attract, acquire and grow customers at scale. Conversica Intelligent Virtual Assistants are AI-powered, SaaS-based software applications that serve as virtual team members and autonomously engage contacts, prospects, customers, or partners in personalized human-like, two-way interactions to drive towards the next best action across the customer revenue lifecycle; whether that’s scheduling a sales meeting, gauging interest to buy additional products or services, or politely but persistently collecting overdue payments.

Reaching out to over 100 million people on behalf of thousands of companies, Conversica Intelligent Virtual Assistants are built on a proven and patented intelligent automation platform integrating natural language understanding (NLU), natural language generation (NLG), autonomous action chains and deep learning capabilities that engage prospects over multiple communication channels and in multiple languages.

Winning multiple awards including Inc. 5000 fastest growing companies and recognized by Gartner as a Cool Vendor, Conversica is a portfolio company of Providence Equity, Kennet Partners and Toba Capital and is headquartered in Foster City, California in Silicon Valley with additional US offices in Seattle and Bellingham, Washington.

We are looking for an experienced data science professional who can lead our team of data scientists as Director, Data Science . If you are a strong multi-tasker and a collaborative data science thought-leader that is committed to continual improvement, teaching, mentoring and coaching others and if you thrive on driving innovation & best practice within a fast-paced, highly-dynamic culture, this role is for you.

Responsibilities

    • Lead and manage the activities of the data science team and provide advanced expertise on statistical and mathematical concepts for the department and the company.
    • Apply and inspire the adoption of advanced data science and analytics across Conversica
    • Formulate new projects and initiatives based on your knowledge of our business and the state of the art in AI/ML technology
    • Recruit, develop, and retain staff while building a culture of collaboration, data-driven decisions, and continuous learning
    • Create personalized development plans for members of your team to enable opportunities for growth and new capabilities.
    • Build relationships with your peer teams to ensure alignment on priorities and effective collaboration on projects.
    • Design, develop and program methods, processes, and systems to consolidate and analyze unstructured, diverse “big data” sources to generate actionable insights and solutions for client services and product enhancement.
    • Develop, improve, and experiment with methodologies and algorithms to support these efforts, using machine learning, deep learning, artificial intelligence and natural language processing techniques; watch for anti-patterns and take action
    • Act as the steward of strengthening our data, understanding its limits, and how to increase applicability for business initiatives.

Qualifications

    • Advanced degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or similar (Masters/PhD)
    • Demonstrated knowledge of state of the art and industry innovation in data science and the opportunities provided to Conversica.
    • 5+ years experience as a Data Scientist in a production grade ML environment; Competent in machine learning principles and techniques.
    • Proficient in production software development in Python and familiarity integrating with agile software development teams and processes.
    • Familiarity with deep learning and neural networks.
    • In-depth understanding of SQL.
    • Familiarity with the field of linguistics and experience in natural language processing.
    • Outstanding leadership and mentorship abilities.
    • Capacity to foster a healthy, stimulating work environment that frequently harnesses teamwork.
    • Ability to convey rigorous mathematical concepts and considerations to non-experts.
    • Experience working with annotation teams is a plus.
    • 5+ Relevant experience developing applications and mentoring development teams to meet privacy by design and security by design principles.
    • 5+ years’ Direct experience enforcing privacy and security perimeter control, access, service, and API design requirements based on roles against a least privilege, zero trust model. The candidate will be expected to manage a team of SDE that implement changes to Conversica’s application successfully according to Conversica’s SDLC, Secure Development Policy, Architectural standards, and Security Requirements.
    • General understanding of U.S. and international privacy laws and regulations, especially as it relates to database access controls and security design requirements (Applicable Conversica Regulations - GDPR, CCPA, CAN-SPAM, and COPPA). General understanding of applicable security frameworks (AWS CIS Benchmark, SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPPA, PCI-DSS)
