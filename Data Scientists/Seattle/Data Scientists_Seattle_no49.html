Description

At Weyerhaeuser, we are the world’s premier timber, land, and forest products company. Sustainability is the founding concept of our business and our values drive every decision to ensure we continue to lead the forestry industry in sustainability practices. And we know about sustainability – we led it in the forestry industry when we planted our first seedling by hand in 1938. We recognize that our success is dependent on the success of our people. For over 100 years, our Weyerhaeuser team has been making a difference in the world – from the seedlings we plant, to the forests and trees we nurture, we ensure every acre is managed with diligence, patience and pride. That’s the Weyerhaeuser way.

Our IT team is on a mission to continue transforming the timber industry. We’re not just in the cloud, we’re implementing technology that will keep us at the forefront of innovation in the forest products industry. We are looking for a talented senior manager who will be responsible for building and leading our Enterprise Data Analytics and Automation center of excellence team. This dynamic and critical team plays a key strategic role in developing and implementing an enterprise data and information architecture, management strategy and ecosystem that enables breakthrough machine learning, data analytics and business intelligence across the Weyerhaeuser integrated business landscape. The team is also responsible for the development of an intelligent automation roadmap and governance model for Weyerhaeuser, including robotics process automation (RPA), test automation, and other future technologies. Intelligent Automation in our language combines artificial intelligence, machine learning, process integration and automation to create smart business processes, workflows and business capabilities that learns and adapts to deliver business outcome. Well managed quality data is the foundation of our digital backbone, from seedling to customer. We are a passionate, service-oriented group of dedicated IT professionals, highly focused on delivering valuable solutions to our business partners and are always looking for cutting edge ideas on how to better provide our services. We push each other to think outside the box and bring new and innovative ideas to the organization, including our current journey to the cloud.

In this role, you will lead a critical multi-year data and information management, analytics & automation transformation enabling new capabilities and critical insights to improve our overall operational effectiveness working hand in hand with business partner leaders. We are positioning ourselves to digitally enable business intelligence, machine learning and analytics to provide new insights that improve business results. If you want to be part of a world-class technology team changing the world we live in – come grow with us!

Responsibilities

  • Primary responsibility is to build and lead multiple teams of IT professionals that are responsible for enterprise data and information management strategy and best practices, business analytics, data architecture implementation, machine learning, and intelligent automation.
  • Oversees the planning, approach, implementation and support of enterprise data and information management and architecture ecosystem and intelligent automation partnering with key business stakeholders.
  • Responsible for operational results, people development, demand planning, technical road-map development, and budget management.
  • Overall accountability and oversight for IT controls and governance of business intelligence and automation.
  • Develops a strong leadership and management team to maximize business and employee performance.
  • Promotes cross-department collaboration and relationship building with business leaders including at the executive level.
  • In partnership with business leadership, works effectively to identify, prioritize, and deliver value-added investments in information technology solutions.
  • Educates the company about how to use data effectively, creates appropriate governance, and drives towards better training and self-service.
  • Participates and/or leads data and automation workstreams as part of large, enterprise programs.
  • Researches industry leading solutions and is responsible for developing business aligned strategies for how emerging technologies can be implemented to benefit the business and enhance value.
  • Builds relationships with leaders at other companies to share best practices and utilize key learnings.
  • Anticipates, formally plans, and educates others on information technology trends, benefits, and risks, while effectively communicating the value and opportunity of emerging technologies.
Qualifications
  • Minimum of ten (10) years of progressive, relevant information technology experience
  • Minimum of 5 years of IT or similar management experience required
  • Minimum of 8 years experience in data or analytics domain
  • Skilled in developing and leading the execution of the Enterprise data strategy, vision and expectations, and how the data vision/strategy is aligned with and linked to the company vision and strategy partnering with key business stakeholders
  • Experience leading large scale multi-year projects implementing a data architecture ecosystem in a cloud-based environment
  • Experience with ERP and CRM implementations is highly desired
  • Strong understanding of data warehouse, data lake, data hub, master data management and metadata management platforms and tools as well as enterprise data and information management practices.
  • Experience with data pipeline, data integration, process integration / API architecture, and process automation
  • Strong understanding of AI/ML and building an intelligent automation center of excellence (COE)
  • Has deep and broad business acumen with the ability to quickly understand the Weyerhaeuser business model and the underlying business processes to ensure effective enablement of technical solutions
  • High-level technical knowledge across a broad set of information technology areas; solid understanding of how technology components fit together and can be used to deliver business value
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills
  • Ability to work collaboratively and effectively influencing others across a broad set of stakeholders and business leaders
  • Skilled in creating and delivering presentations to executive, business leaders and staff
  • Mentors others in the development of communication skills
  • Demonstrated experience in attracting and growing key talent
  • Skilled in communicating technical topics to non-technical audiences
  • Models and mentors others to adopt the company's key behaviors and core values
  • Skilled at conflict resolution, time management, process analysis, cost/benefit analysis, and risk management

Education

  • Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology or related field is required.
