Keen to become part of a truly global, collaborative team of professionals? Your journey begins here.

SUMMARY

The Data Science and Analytics Specialist will focus on delivering value and growth under the firm’s projects related to Data Strategy and Data Science. The role will apply advanced analytical methods and tools to drive value from the data that Hogan Lovells have through techniques such as predictive modelling, forecasting and other algorithms. As well as delivering on advanced analytical methods, the role will work closely with internal and external clients to advance these data related insights and incorporate them into how the firm advances its business.

JOB DESCRIPTION

Data Strategy

  • Contribute towards the firm’s overall data strategy

  • Design, build and support reusable analytical structures within a technical environment for the use of testing hypothesis posed by business and legal management as well as formulating and testing own hypothesis

  • Apply machine learning and statistical techniques, including regression modelling, random forests and support vector machines.

  • Provide support to external consultants appointed by the firm to deliver predictive models to be used by the firm

  • Support the firm's R&D initiatives to help develop new predictive analytical models

  • Provide written and verbal commentaries to senior leadership and management teams (inclusive of PowerPoint presentations)

  • Analyse and interpret large volumes of data to show trends and create recommendations.

  • Ad hoc analysis as required covering a range of topics and support on various projects.

  • All members of the firm are expected to participate in our Global Citizenship program.

  • Other duties as assigned.

QUALIFICATIONS

  • Candidate must demonstrate experience of analytical review, interpretation of trends and unlock themes that may not be readily apparent on the face of the data.

  • The candidate must have experience of analyzing information and having an enquiring mind so that they are prepared to extract and understand the details behind a number when necessary.

  • The individual must be diligent, have an eye for details and the ability to review critically and carefully check his or her own work as well as others.

  • The deliverables from this role will be highly visible and will have a very high profile and errors will be noticed.

  • The successful candidate will be able to prioritize and plan his or her own work with minimum supervision - a proven ability to plan ahead is essential.

  • A very strong working knowledge of Microsoft Excel, including pivot tables and standard Excel formulae

  • Working knowledge of PowerPoint.

HOURS

Core Hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00am to 6:00pm. Must be flexible to work additional hours.
