Full Job Description

The Data Mining Analyst’s focus will be on the reporting and analysis of the many systems that make up the Payment Integrity and Pharmacy divisions. This individual under the direction of the Data Mining Group Manager will respond quickly to ad-hoc reporting requests as well as assist in the analysis of key project results. This person will be responsible for or assisting in: uncovering complex data problems or issues, researching solutions, and implementing steps to achieve higher levels of efficiency.

About Us


The Rawlings Group provides payment integrity services and claims data analysis for health insurance carriers and their members. We work behind the scenes to make sure that members are receiving the full value of their coverage by ensuring that claims are appropriately paid. Providing payment integrity and recovery services to the industry creates a more equitable distribution of healthcare dollars so members can receive the care they need when they need it most.

Responsibilities

  • Meet regularly with Data Mining Group leadership for new project and report assignments.
  • Work independently with low to moderate levels of supervision and deliver results within defined time requirements.
  • Responsible for the proper communication and reporting regarding all aspects of each assignment.
  • Understanding business needs and objectives
  • Developing strategies for effective data analysis and reporting
  • Defining metrics and identifying relevant data sources
  • Perform other duties as assigned


  • Bachelor’s degree in mathematics, statistics, business, computer science or equivalent professional experience required.
  • One year of SQL experience with intermediate level SQL development skills including writing SQL code, extracting data, generating queries and reports, and integrating data between different data sources.
  • Experience with data analysis and reporting, and knowledge of data visualization software packages
  • Aptitude to learn a business that is unique to The Rawlings Group
  • Strong analytical mindset and communication skills
  • Proactive, inquisitive and creative in approach to work
  • Experience with building reports, analyzing results, and communicating findings
  • Understanding of data structure/integration and relational database structures
  • Ability to collaborate with partners and drive analytic projects end-to-end
  • Strong attention to detail and data accuracy
