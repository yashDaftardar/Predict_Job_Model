The AI/ML Developer will develop advanced AI, neural network, machine learning, data analytics, and optimization algorithms and applies them to create new intelligent devices, solutions and services, with prominent emphasis on vision-based problems. Fluent in convolutional neural network architectures and toolkits. Capable of building novel neural networks or using existing networks and optimizing to meet customer demands. Proficient in python and C++ development, and experienced building Vision Based AI applications (object detection, classification, tracking, segmentation, etc). Creates and maintains complex datasets and tools for annotating and training new AI models. Conversant in training of neural networks for temporal based problems (detecting human behaviors, etc). Builds AI applications for deployment on embedded systems or in the cloud, depending on customer need.
