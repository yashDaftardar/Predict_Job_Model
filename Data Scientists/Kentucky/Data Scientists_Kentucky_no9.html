Full Job Description

Direct Hire/Permanent role!

As part of growing Data Science department, we are seeking a senior data scientist who is passionate about the data and thrives in a fast-paced environment to have a positive impact on the business and the society. Data Science department develops tools, technologies, models to solve business problems and provide actionable insights by leveraging the large, complex datasets and domain expertise. Data Science department works in close coordination with other cross-functional business departments such as Data Engineering, Data Governance, and Product and Engineering.

Key Responsibilities

  • Proposes, designs, and develops predictive and prescriptive solutions in collaboration with cross-functional stakeholders; facilitates implementation leveraging Product & Engineering and other functions as necessary
  • Able to translate the high-level business requirements into detailed system-level and functional requirements for technical implementation
  • Investigates, analyzes, preprocesses large structured and unstructured datasets; performs necessary checks for reasonableness, completeness and accuracy.
  • Develops, trains, validates, updates, and deploys the machine learning models to satisfy business and technical requirements
  • Automating an end-to-end process from procuring the raw data to converting into actionable insights (decisions, monitoring, etc.)
  • Contributes to the architecture and design for scalable and efficient model deployments, including automated data model pipelines
  • Communicates models, model results, and insights to internal stakeholders
  • Understands, follows, and support internal and external compliance requirements/standards
  • Creates and maintains productive relationships within the technical community; keeps current with technology, methods, and best practices; participates in peer reviews and educating internal stakeholders
