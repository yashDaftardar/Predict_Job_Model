Job Description:

Do you have a strong data technology background, experience in developing Java or Python data APIs, hands on experience with AWS data and AI capabilities, peer technical leadership chops and an interest in helping design and build high visibility, innovative AI based capabilities to improve operations capabilities? The Workplace Investing AI Delivery and Platform Data Engineering Chapter team is seeking a Principal Data Engineer/Tech Lead to design, implement and enhance technical solutions for AI model compute, data pipelines and hosting and API access tiers in support of our Intelligent Automation AI Tribe. We are looking for an outspoken technologist with a deep passion for data and application design to help build AWS based AI solutions that extract data from both databases and unstructured data sources and harness AI algorithms to help automate difficult business problems facing our Workplace operations team. This role will be a great mix of peer mentorship/leadership, application, API and data pipeline design, and lots of hands on work, partnering with our data scientists and business partners to design, develop and deploy full stack AI solutions on AWS. It’s a great chance to work on a small agile team to help build innovative and high business value AI powered solutions to improve tough manual operations activities.

The Expertise You Have

  • Deep experience (7+ years) of designing, building and automating complex and customized data pipelines with multiple ingress sources and multiple consumers including API driven user-facing web applications

  • Proven (2+ years) data API development experience using Java/Springboot and/or Python. Front end development using Angular would be a huge plus.

  • Demonstrated work experience using AWS for data manipulation: establishing CI/CD pipelines to deploy code and services to AWS, familiarity with IAM roles and policies, using cloud- based data platforms like Snowflake or RDS for analytics data hosting, EC2 and or EKS compute set-up and work orchestration tools like Airflow. AWS Sagemaker experience a big plus.

  • Proven track record as an outspoken leader with peer coaching and strong communications and presentation abilities working on collaborative teams to deliver high quality data solutions.

  • Superior SQL skills and experience performing deep data analysis on multiple database platforms

  • Experience with 401k/DC operations and data and/or exposure to AI/ Machine Learning and Model Operations a huge plus.

  • Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent experience

The Skills You Bring

  • Driven, highly motivated and a quick learner

  • Be a hands-on technologist who works hard to rapidly prototype, test and deploy scripts, ETL & API code, and compute orchestration who proactively identifies technical gaps and areas needing automation.

  • Have an innovation mentality and demonstrated technical curiosity to learn and apply new technologies in the rapidly evolving data technology landscape and a desire to help coach and guide others what you leaned

  • Have an analytical and consulting mindset- being fearless in asking engaging questions to help tease out requirements details and to offer up technical implementation options among your technical team.

  • Excellent verbal and written communications and negotiation skills.

  • Passionate about data and technology and have experience with or an interest in the operations optimization space and an interest or experience working on Artificial Intelligence/ Machine Learning projects.

The Value You Deliver

  • Work with data scientists, business sponsors and technical peers to build AI powered applications and help improve tricky operational problems

  • Roll up your sleeves and develop and automate data pipelines, execute AI model code, establish on prem and AWS compute infrastructure and CI/CD pipelines and develop RESTful data microservice APIs and consuming applications.

  • Act as a peer technical leader, coaching and mentoring peers, performing code reviews and helping establish technical standards

  • Be the technical voice, representing technical solutions at cross squad and leadership sessions.
