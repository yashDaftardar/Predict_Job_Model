Full Job Description

Under the supervision of the Manager of Data Mining, you will be responsible for taking the needs of the business and delivering products that meet those needs. In this position you have the opportunity to own your projects from end to end, seeing them through from idea to finished product! As part of the Data Mining Group, you will work with a tight knit, highly collaborative team on projects critical to our success.


About Us

The Rawlings Group provides payment integrity services and claims data analysis for health insurance carriers and their members. We work behind the scenes to make sure that members are receiving the full value of their coverage by ensuring that claims are appropriately paid. Providing payment integrity and recovery services to the industry creates a more equitable distribution of healthcare dollars so members can receive the care they need when they need it most.

We offer opportunities for growth and development, continuing education, internal mobility and promotions, and unrivaled income earning potential. We are looking for individuals that have the drive and commitment to make a difference in the industry. We invite you to apply to become a part of The Rawlings Group team.
