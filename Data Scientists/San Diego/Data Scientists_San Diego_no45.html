R-3560 Sr. Data Scientist (Open)

Primary Location:

SAN DIEGO

Job Description:

*Open to any Neustar office location and/or remote*

Who are we?

As a company built on the foundation of Privacy by Design, for more than 20 years Neustar’s unique capabilities have made us the leader in the field of responsible identity resolution. Neustar enables trusted connections between companies and people at the moments that matter the most, with world-class and industry leading solutions in Marketing, Risk, Communications, Security and Registry services.

Job Duties

  • Algorithm Development: Develop a deep understanding of all the data relevant to the problem to be addressed. Establish deterministic and probabilistic linkages between data sources and develop ways to extract and summarize the sought information in the data using a wide variety of statistical, data mining and machine learning techniques.
  • Prototyping: Prototype data pipelines, machine-learning models, and perform the analyses at scale, provide documentation and help educate your colleagues in different function about the solution.
  • Support Implementation: Closely work with product, engineering and client teams to incorporate Data Science capabilities into Neustar’s products and services.

Qualifications/Education

  • Master’s degree in an analytical field such as computer science, machine learning, data science and/or statistics with 3 to 5 years of experience is a minimum requirement, or a PhD in a related field with background research experience in device fingerprinting.
  • We will consider candidates with Bachelor’s degrees with at least 5 years of relevant work experience.
  • Domain expertise in device fingerprinting, fraud modeling using machine learning, and risk analyses.
  • Minimum 2 years of hands-on experience with AWS tools such as EC2, EMR, and databases such a Redshift and S3.
  • Ability to learn APIs quickly and implement machine learning modeling solutions into an end-to-end scoring pipeline. For example, Sklearn, Boto3, Numpy.
  • Minimum 2 years of experience with wrangling large (terabytes) event level datasets.
  • Experience working with engineering teams to QA, debug, and deploy machine learning models in production.
  • Minimum 5 years of experience with Python and SQL, and one or more enterprise level programming languages such as Java or Scala.
  • Some experience writing Hive-SQL and code on a Spark platform.
  • Track record of building production models based on highly imbalanced datasets.
  • Some experience with TensorFlow or PyTorch.
  • Excellent oral and communication skills.
  • Ability and experience working in a cross-functional environment.

Basic Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree with at least 5 years of relevant experience.
  • Minimum 2 years of experience on a cloud platform such as AWS and/or GCP.
