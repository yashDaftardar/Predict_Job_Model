SeeScan is currently seeking the right person to join our team as a Senior Data Analyst. If our values, working environment, and needs align with your skills, mindset, and career goals, this could be a fantastic win-win opportunity.

Our company culture

Working at SeeScan is unlike working for other companies of our size. While we have the stability of a larger company—we’ve been in business for nearly 40 years and sell our products worldwide—the SeeScan environment feels like a smaller, tight-knit community. Absent of typical corporate culture, we care more about what you do than what you are called, and we want you to share our mission of constantly improving the way we do things. We’ll give you the tools and resources you need to succeed, and we want you to show us how it’s done!

What we’re looking for

Across our organization, we’re looking for people who want to solve big challenges to make the world a better place. Whether you’re designing the latest tech, building our products on the production floor, or keeping our business running and profitable, we want people who approach problems with a can-do attitude and who are eager to be part of a team.

Our goal is to create the best products in our industries and continue our trend of sustainable growth – not to line shareholders’ pockets. That means we’re interested in hiring people who are eager to work somewhere long-term and who share our values of continuous learning, community, and fairness.

We need you to bring your talent, experience, and enthusiasm for capturing and analyzing data to produce presentable information across our enterprise. You will help us focus efforts, optimize processes, and measure success for engineering, manufacturing, finance, and product management. We are looking for an innovative thinker who is solution-oriented, loves learning new things, and is passionate about what they do.

In this position, you will

  • Use data from ERP and other sources to derive actionable insights
  • Help setup and monitor KPI’s for departmental goals and process improvement efforts
  • Use BIS tools to extract and manipulate data
  • Analyze databases and make reports related to the information analyzed
  • Communicate complex concepts and analysis results clearly and effectively
  • Create or identify potential new data procurement and processing programs

About you

  • Analytical, detail-oriented, and capable of achieving consistently accurate results
  • Problem-solving mindset – you find satisfaction in designing elegant solutions to highly complex challenges
  • Highly proactive and communicative – ability to work with people across the organization and upper management
  • Flexible, capable of quickly re-prioritizing when requirements change
  • Experienced in a multi-level BOM manufacturing environment

Desired skills and experience

This is a sampling of the type of skills and experience we’d like to see in our ideal candidate. If you don’t check off all the boxes, that’s okay – we’re more interested in hiring someone who will be the right fit for our community and who demonstrates the capability to learn.

  • Understanding of networks, databases and other technology
  • Tools: Strong Excel skills, SQL, Power Pivot, Power Query, Power BI
  • Knowledge of ERP/MRP systems is required
  • Familiarity with AX is highly desired

We offer

  • A position within an established company, growing from humble beginnings in our founder’s garage to a team of over 300
  • A culture of transparency, growth, and innovation
  • Traditional and Roth 401(k) investment options, with a current company matching contribution of up to $50 per paycheck (biweekly)
  • Flexible Paid Time Off plan with no cap or “use it or lose it” requirement

In your application, please include your resume and a thoughtful cover letter explaining why you are the ideal candidate for this position. We are looking for a long-term, in-house team member to join us, not a contractor or consultant.

About SeeScan

Modern life depends on the infrastructure that brings water, electricity, gas, and communications to our homes and businesses. SeeScan’s mission is to deliver the highest quality products that protect these vital services. We are an original equipment manufacturer in San Diego, CA that builds diagnostic and utility locating equipment from the ground up, from initial product conception to prototyping, testing, and final assembly.

SeeScan is more than a collection of facilities, technologies, and products. SeeScan is a community of people. Working here, you’ll be part of a group of people who take pride in their work and their community. We’re passionate, but there’s more to it than that. We have a sense of ownership of SeeScan’s success and a shared commitment to continuous improvement. You won’t just be a cog in a corporate machine here – you’ll be our most critical investment, and we’ll do our best to empower you with the tools you need to find success at SeeScan.
