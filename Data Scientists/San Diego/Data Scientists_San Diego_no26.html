Summary
Under the direction of the Director of Strategy and Transformation, this position provides Sharp Health Plan with reporting and analysis that enables data-driven decision making. Responsibilities include collecting and analyzing large amounts of data to discover patterns or relationships related to strategic clinical programs and improvements. The Analyst is responsible for organizing and maintaining dashboards used to track program performance, including utilization, cost, and quality, and producing supplemental reporting to analyze root case. The Analyst compiles, analyzes, and prepares information for presentation to a cross-functional audience, including executive leadership.
