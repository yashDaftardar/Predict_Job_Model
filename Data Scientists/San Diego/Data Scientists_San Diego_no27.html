Why Lytx?:

If you are passionate about how data and analytics converge to drive strategic decision-making within a B2B SaaS company, then this is the role for you! Lytx is looking for an inquisitive Senior Marketing Data Analyst whose curiosity fuels your insatiable appetite for learning. You have a fascination for results-focused analytics and data-driven decision-making. The analyst we’re looking for knows that data is important, but it's the deep analysis and curation of the full story that are most critical. If you are an analyst that thrives in an environment of continuous testing and learning, you’ll thrive here!

You’ll Get To:

  • Develop a thorough understanding of all data elements, interpret them, and develop unique stories from data as they relate to customer acquisition, growth, and engagement.

  • Explore data to find actionable insights and make marketing recommendations through cohort analyses, long-term trends, funnels, user segmentation, regression models, and more.

  • Design dashboards and data visualizations to communicate meaningful metrics for our Marketing teams.

  • Unlock new growth by building and/or auditing existing predictive, attribution, and forecasting models to inform marketing decision making.

  • Build ETL data pipelines for dashboard automation for internal business usage.

  • Find opportunities for improvement in reporting; modify existing tools, techniques, or processes to improve our data-driven partnerships.

  • Analyze and report on marketing channel and campaign effectiveness across a range of channels including email, paid advertising, CRO, and ABM.

  • Perform segmentation analyses to identify differences in performance and opportunities to optimize throughout the funnel

  • Set up, analyze, and promote best practices for marketing experimentation and analytics.

  • Empower and guide marketers cross-functionally to work effectively and efficiently with data

  • Perform Other duties as assigned

What You’ll Need:

  • 5 years of experience in quantitative and analytical roles focused on web and marketing analytics, turning data into insights and recommendations

  • Strong SQL skills are required (Snowflake)

  • Experience with a Business Intelligence tool is required (Tableau)

  • Ability to build compelling presentations from data that summarizes complicated data and insights.

  • Experience in tracking and reporting on marketing funnel and digital performance metrics

  • Expertise in developing complex data models in Sheets or Excel

  • Deep understanding of statistical analysis and experiment design

  • Excellent communication skills. Ability to succinctly communicate results and tell compelling stories with data
