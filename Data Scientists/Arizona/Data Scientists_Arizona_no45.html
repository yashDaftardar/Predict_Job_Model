The position will support analytical and non-clinical studies to support diagnostic product development. The position will develop sound experiment designs, build sampling plans, conduct statistical analysis, and author statistical report. This position will be point of contact for statistical support and input from Research & Development as well as Quality and Manufacturing teams. The position will support design and analysis of analytical studies for interfering substances, limit of detection/quantitation (LOD/LOQ), exclusivity & inclusivity, and linearity. The position will also support quality and manufacturing teams in developing acceptance sampling plans, design of experiments (DOE), Gage study analysis, capability analysis, and tolerance/control limit specifications.

Are you a Statistician interested in solving quality control problems for a leading molecular diagnostics company at the Cepheid Sunnyvale site in California?

Responsibilities:
Develop study design including DOE

Compute sample sizes for study or sampling plans and provide rationale

Write and present statistical analysis report

Maintain up to date knowledge on statistical software such as SAS, R, JMP, and Minitab

Stay current on product development stages and issues by attending review and development team meetings.

Support clinical biostatisticians with statistical support as needed.

Qualifications:
Bachelor's degree with 2+ years of relevant work experience or Master’s degree in Statistics with 0-2 years of relevant work experience

A strong emphasis in quality and non-clinical statistics such as Quality Engineering and/or Manufacturing specifications

Expertise in use of software such as Minitab or JMP and programming skills in SAS/R

Knowledge of statistics including DOE, Regression, Contingency Tables, Probability Distributions, ANOVA, Control Limit Specifications, Quality Control Statistics

Must have good written and verbal communication skills
Preferred Qualifications
Master's degree highly preferred
Experience in Manufacturing or Quality Control in Biotech industry highly preferred
