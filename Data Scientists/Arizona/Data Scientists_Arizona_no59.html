Full Job Description

Pure Flix is the worldwide leader in selecting and streaming faith and family-friendly media that changes lives, inspires hearts, and lifts the spirits of members like you and those you love. We are seeking a Senior Analyst & Machine Learning Scientist to join our growing team remotely or in our Scottsdale, Arizona location.

Responsibilities include:

  • Translate business strategy into machine learning vision —break down the vision into short and long-term plans.
  • Work directly with a third party that provides recommendation technology, maintain responsibility for measurement and performance of recommendation engine. Create, maintain and iterate on machine learning algorithms and machine learning systems.
  • Collaborate with engineering (platform and data), UX research & design, and data insights teams to ensure we're driving the right metrics for our larger business. This may include work on user personas, predictive models around lifetime value and churn, A/B testing, sample balancing, and designing an achievement system.
  • Participate in the larger data science initiatives throughout the company.
  • Analyze traffic and performance, define and prioritize A/B testing opportunities based on effort and impact. Key member of conversion rate optimization team.
  • Critical thinking and development of solutions for machine learning (ML) and/or natural language (NL) applications.
  • Developing and/or applying statistical modeling techniques; in particular, deep learning, optimization, and other ML techniques to different applications in business and engineering.
  • Researching and implementing novel ML and statistical approaches to add value to the business.
  • Identifying problems in entertainment that can benefit from the latest advancements in ML.
  • Breaking down complex problems into approachable stages and bringing concepts to production.

Qualifications

  • 3+ years working in a Data Science or Machine Learning type position
  • 2+ years building churn models, LTV models, user segmentation, and/or other product and marketing focused predictive analytics
  • Any understanding or interest in learning the digital marketing landscape and measurement solutions - Google Analytics, Facebook Ads, HubSpot, Google Ads
  • General understanding of user segmentation, AB testing and performance marketing
  • History of leading projects alongside other functions that produced pivotal work
  • Extensive experience architecting complex algorithms/data products, extensive knowledge on recommendation systems preferred
  • Background in designing and instituting “production quality” machine learning algorithms
  • Deep understanding of Python
  • Hands-on experience with deep learning toolkits and frameworks like PyTorch or TensorFlow
  • Ability to design and execute on research agenda
  • Ability to write complex SQL queries and a strong understanding of relational databases
