Full Job Description

Make Next Happen Now. For more than 30 years, Silicon Valley Bank (SVB) has helped innovative companies and their investors move bold ideas forward, fast. SVB provides targeted financial services and expertise through its offices in innovation centers across the world. With commercial, international and private banking services, SVB helps address the unique needs of innovators.

Silicon Valley Bank is looking for a Senior Privacy Data Analyst to be part of SVB’s Privacy Engineering team which is responsible for building innovative solutions which support SVB’s privacy infrastructure and automate controls established by our privacy policies across the bank. This role will both help design and implement solutions which help to mature the bank’s privacy practices through the implementation of solutions to automate the bank’s data discovery and classification SVB information, establish and maintain an accurate inventory of personal data utilized across the bank’s data catalog, establish increased automation for data analytics and management reporting.

The Senior Privacy Data Analyst will work to optimize how data is discovered and classified across SVB’s structured and unstructured data repositories, develop quantitative models to identify & classify sensitive data and gain insights related to risks and control effectiveness. This role will also work with stakeholders to explain data models and data discovery results to business and technical owners of data. In addition, this role will be responsible for maintaining reporting and metrics of key privacy controls across the bank such as discovery of personal data, classification of data, data mapping, data obfuscation and automation of data subject requests.

The Senior Privacy Data Analyst will be responsible for working closely with SVB’s IT and Data Governance organizations to automate and expand the completeness, accuracy of SVB’s capability to complete data discover, classify data elements and verify a catalog of data with business owners.

Responsibilities also include implementing and automating technical and process-related privacy controls, conducting privacy impact assessments, perform testing, debugging of privacy platform solutions and documenting procedures for installation, operations and maintenance.

The successful candidate for this position is passionate about utilizing innovative solutions, automation and data-driven analysis to enable solutions to identify and protect personal data across SVB. Successful candidates will also have experience with data architecture, profiling, data quality as well as meta-data management. This role requires a blend of technical acumen, interest in data analytics and attention to detail required to effectively design privacy technologies across the bank and work with both business and system owners to ensure the effective implementation of privacy controls within SVB’s business units.

Qualifications
  • B.A. or B.S degree in privacy engineering, data analytics, computer science, computer engineering, information systems or related field of study.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills with the ability to influence both technical engineers and business leaders
  • Ideally experience with privacy data platform tools such as BigID, OneTrust, Collibra)
  • Ability to work with minimal supervision as well as ability to collaborate closely with a team that is not co-located
  • One or more professional certifications related to privacy such as Certified Information Privacy Technologist (CIPT), Certified Information Privacy Professional (CIPP) or equivalent (or willingness to obtain within 6 months
  • Demonstrated teamwork and collaboration skills including leading conversations with global and multi-functional teams
  • Experience presenting data visualizations for complex data sets for both management and detail-oriented information consumers
  • Interest in working in Data Privacy domain with experience working in a regulated industry or consulting on privacy matters
  • Demonstrated understanding of data structures utilized across both structured and unstructured data repositories
  • Data literacy: finding and managing data, cleansing data, manipulating data
  • Understanding of applicable privacy requirements and eagerness to help implementing Privacy controls within both IT projects and business practices
  • Demonstrated working knowledge of software engineering fundamentals
  • Awareness of data protection principles and data privacy practices
  • Integrity and high professional ethics
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Experience in compliance, risk, legal, data or project fields preferable
