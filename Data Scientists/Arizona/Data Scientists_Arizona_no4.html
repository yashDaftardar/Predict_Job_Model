Job Description


** This is a 2021 Summer Graduate Internship Job Assignment **

The selected candidate will be responsible for, but not limited to:

  • Using predictive modeling statistics.
  • Machine Learning Data Mining and other data analysis techniques to collect explore and extract insights from structured and unstructured data.
  • Developing software algorithms and applications to apply mathematics to data.
  • Performing large-scale experimentation and build data-driven apps to translate data into intelligence solve a variety of business problems and enable business strategy.
  • Assisting business with casual inferences amp observations with finding patterns relationships in data.


The ideal candidate should exhibit the following behavioral traits:

  • Communication Skills.
  • Analytical Skills.
  • Problem Solving.
  • Action Orientation.
  • Project Management Skills.


Qualifications


You must possess the below minimum qualifications to be initially considered for this position. Preferred qualifications are in addition to the minimum requirements and are considered a plus factor in identifying top candidates. Experience would be obtained through a combination of prior education level classes, and current level school classes, projects, research, and relevant previous job and/or internship experience. Position not eligible for Intel immigration sponsorship.


Minimum Qualifications:

Candidate must possess at least one of the following:

1. Currently pursuing a Master's degree in Industrial Engineering Mathematics, Computer Science or similar field. With at least 3+ months of experience in the below-mentioned areas of expertise.

2. Currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Industrial Engineering Mathematics, Computer Science, or a similar field. With at least 6 months of experience in the below-mentioned areas of expertise.

Expertise areas:

  • Proficiency in JMP OPL and Simulation tools MATLAB SQL etc.
  • Background in rapid application development and integration.
  • Software development debugging performance characterization optimization skills.


Preferred qualifications:

  • Expertise in relational database structures research methods machines learning Cloud-based technologies Big Data technologies like Hadoop, HBase, LuceneSolr analytics packages, R, Mahout, Octave, Weka.
  • Scripting languages like Python, Perl, and R.
  • Programing languages like Java, CC, and SQL.
  • Data Scientist Toolkit including time series models machine learning simulation optimization and the skills to develop models with structured-unstructured big data.
