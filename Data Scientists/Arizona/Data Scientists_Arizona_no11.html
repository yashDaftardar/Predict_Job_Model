The mission of the Analytics Center of Excellence (ACE) is to develop cutting edge analytic solutions that empowers Salt River Project to deliver innovative and cost-effective solutions related to the delivery of power and water to its customers across the Phoenix Metro area.

As a Data Scientist in the ACE, you will solve business challenges and answer business questions by applying analytical, statistical, and programming techniques to data. You will build analytical models leveraging large volumes of data, evaluate the models for bias and unintended consequences, deploy them for business consumption, and monitor their accuracy. If you: like to think of clever ways to solve complex data problems, find insights and value in a variety datasets, and work in a collaborative and high-paced environment alongside other dedicated data professionals, this is a great opportunity for you.

Job Responsibilities

  • Building descriptive, predictive, and/or prescriptive data models which leverage advanced algorithms, mathematical techniques, and statistics
  • Analyzing data sources, gathering and transforming data into formats that are useful for analytics
  • Consulting with business users to discuss potential data science projects and gather requirements
  • Communicating results of Data Science efforts via presentations, documentation, roadshows, and visualizations
  • Participating in analytics community of practice contributing solutions and thought leadership in order to grow and develop analytic skills throughout SRP

Education

Completion of a Bachelor's Degree from an accredited institution that prepares the employee for the assignment.

Experience

Promotion to Level 2 requires a minimum of two years of experience at Level 1; demonstrated capability to perform advanced and more difficult work as determined by the supervisor. Promotion to Senior Level requires a minimum of three-years of experience at Level 2; is fully competent in all aspects of functional area of assignment and as such would be recognized as a specialist in area of assignment and may have periodic or occasional lead responsibilities.

Required Skills:

  • Knowledge of a variety of data science techniques (clustering, decision trees, neural networks, etc...) and when they should be applied.
  • Experience using computer languages such as R, Python, or SAS to manipulate data and build models
  • Experience using mathematical and statistical methods to validate and analyze large data sets
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills and the ability to communicate complicated or technical information, clearly, to a variety of audiences from entry-level employees to executive staff
  • Able to thrive in an active collaborative team environment
  • Eager and able continually learn in a fast-pasted environment
  • Curiosity and passion about using analytics and data to solve problems
  • Results driven and self-reliant

Additional Information

Preferred Skills:

  • Experience accessing data from a variety of database technologies such as SQL Server, Oracle, Hadoop
  • Familiarity with reporting and visualization tools such as Power BI or Tableau
