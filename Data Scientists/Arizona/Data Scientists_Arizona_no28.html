Job Description


GM Analytics and Big Data will coordinate and lead data analytics and factory automation functions across the Technology Development Analytics and Automation (TDAA) organization. This leader is responsible for End to End Architecture, Tools for Analytics, Big Data, Big Data Governance, Meta Data Design, and unified GUI strategy for TDAA. The Analytics and Big Data GM oversees the TD centers of excellence focused on data engineering, data science required to enable machine learning, and advanced analytics. This leader drives strategic decision making at the top level of the organization, influencing technology roadmaps and initiatives, by leveraging experience in data analysis, business acumen, networking, and expertise in factory automation. This leader will manage a team of direct reports and will be responsible for hiring, developing, and mentoring talent within the group.

Responsibilities include:

  • Establish data objectives and strategies necessary to inform and execute TD and M/O Analytics and Data strategy to maximize the value of data to the enterprise. Ensure that the capability of the Analytics and Data function meets the needs of TD and M/O.
  • Establish, develop, and execute data, analytics strategies, including life-cycle capability road mapping, development, and responses to new and emerging technologies, external partnerships, and customer relationships.
  • Establish appropriate data governance, policies, procedures, quality standards and controls are developed and implemented. Oversee the process, policies, standards and methods for data protection.
  • Establish an environment that promotes innovation and idea generation; align functional teams with TD and M/O's strategic priorities and ensure coordination and resourcing (including budgets).
  • Lead through ambiguity and bring clarity through defined strategies and executable plans.
  • Lead the maturation of the data talent strategy, and the acquisition, training, development, and retention of the talent base.
  • Lead centers of excellence for TD analytics to build organizational data capability and capacity and deliver relevant and timely support for business decision making.
  • Take requirements from technology development groups and HVM to ensure the appropriate implementation, adoption and modernization of infrastructure, reference models and enterprise-level solutions.
  • Focus on strong customer engagement by using data to shape customer interactions. Support the business areas in developing new business opportunities for analytics and artificial intelligence
  • Grow distributed data talent and focus on creating a pipeline of AI/ML specialists with relevant experience and centralized team hiring on specialists and generalists. through university partnerships, industry conferences, consortia engagement, etc.
  • Experience in factory automation systems and ability to understand how data interacts with the factory infrastructure digital foundation.
  • Experience with data governance standards, policies, and regulatory requirements in the use and application of data.
  • Experience building training programs at all levels (from awareness training through technical training).
  • Experience translating business strategy into value creation through data, analytics, and artificial intelligence.
  • Experience in factory Automation data, equipment, and material handling systems.
  • Experience in using (and in managing teams who use) data and analytics to influence business and customer decision making.
  • Experience translating technology roadmaps and strategy to operational improvement.
  • Demonstrated ability to lead a technical organization to execute and operationalize strategies.
  • Demonstrable track record of delivering major business critical data and/or automation change programs with broad scope, high levels of complexity, cross functional remits, and challenging deliverables.
  • Ability to influence and shape outcomes with senior executive level stakeholders.
  • Ability to build cross-enterprise teams and leading large teams of technology professionals.
  • Demonstrated understanding of the relevant technology and industry trends.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • Experience setting data governance standards and policies.
  • Excellent business and financial judgment; strong analytical skills; excellent verbal and written communication skills.


Qualifications


  • PhD in Computer Science or Engineering
  • 20 years of practical experience in manufacturing automation with leadership of technical teams working with analytics/machine learning techniques and Data architecture.
