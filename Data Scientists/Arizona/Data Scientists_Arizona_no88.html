About Us:
At Raytheon Missiles & Defense, by combining our vast resources and investments we are able to dedicate ourselves to solving mission-level vs. product-level customer challenges – together we can anticipate more, move faster and make a bigger impact on the big picture.

Whole Life Engineering (WLE) is responsible for ensuring our products are Safe, Reliable, Maintainable and delivered on time. WLE consists of multiple disciplines that support engineering, our program offices and our customers. Our WLE disciplines participate in the total life cycle of our products from conception to deactivation. Our focus area is product support, including the following disciplines: Reliability System Safety and Supportability.

Job Summary:
The Engineering Intelligence Analysis (EIA) Team supports Raytheon Missiles & Defense (RMD) programs by pursuing synergies between the technical disciplines. Our responsibilities include providing the foundation for timely delivery of actionable data to our program customers, in addition to supporting the Engineering function by developing and/or maintaining software tools used for data analysis and tracking.

We are looking for a Junior Engineering Statistical Intelligence Analyst to join our Reliability team located in Tucson, AZ

Your Overall Responsibilities Will Include:
  • Interpreting data and analyzing results using advanced statistical techniques
  • Deploying automated solutions for process improvement
  • Regularly interacting with Engineering, Quality & Mission Assurance, Supply Chain, and Program Management for 'best-in-class" solutions

Responsibilities to Anticipate:
  • Implementing data collection systems and other strategies that optimize statistical efficiency and data quality
  • Acquiring data from primary or secondary data sources and maintaining databases/data systems
  • Identifying and analyzing trends or patterns in complex data sets
  • Participating in cross functional projects and initiatives
  • Coordinating, administrating and participating in Failure Review Boards (FRB) and/or Failure Reporting, Analysis, and Corrective Action (FRACA) activities
This position can be a Grade G06 or Grade G07 based on the candidate’s qualifications as they relate to the skills, experience and responsibility requirements for the position.

An advanced degree can be substituted for additional years of experience as follows: Master’s is equal to 2 years of experience or a Ph.D. is equal to 4 years of experience.

Qualifications You Must Have:
Education: Bachelor’s Degree. An additional 2 years of relevant professional experience may be substituted for each 1 year of college.

Employment Experience:
  • Less than 2 years’ experience with statistics or using statistical packages for data analysis (e.g. Excel, SPSS, SAS, Python, PowerBI, R, Tableau, JMP, Kinaxis, etc.)
  • Experience with Process Improvement activities such as Failure Reporting Analysis and Corrective Action (FRACA) or Root Cause and Corrective Action (RCCA)
  • The ability to obtain and maintain a US security clearance. US citizenship is required as only US citizens are eligible for a security clearance

  • Experience with Process Improvement activities such as Failure Reporting, Analysis and Corrective Action (FRACA) or Root Cause and Corrective Action (RCCA).
  • Experience with reliability or information systems

  • Understanding of Information Technologies (IT) and Intellectual Property (IP) issues
  • Strong understanding of the principles and concepts of information management

  • Strong analytical skills with the ability to collect, organize, analyze, and disseminate significant amounts of information with attention to detail and accuracy
  • Ability to develop solutions to routine technical problems
  • Self-motivated and detail-oriented; ability to perform in fast paced, deadline driven environments with constantly changing priorities
  • Strong and effective organizational skills with ability prioritize tasks; ability to respond efficiently and effectively to time-critical issues
