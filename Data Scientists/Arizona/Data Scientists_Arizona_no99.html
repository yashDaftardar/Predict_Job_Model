Full Job Description

Global Nephrology Solution (GNS) is the largest Physician-driven Nephrology organization in USA. It’s 350+ physicians provide care to over 400,000 Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) and Dialysis patients.

RCO Analytics, a wholly owned subsidiary of GNS, is a Healthcare IT company and uses advanced scientific techniques, forecasting, and machine learning to predict clinical and financial outcomes in order to guide investments and deliver results that improve health outcomes and reduce costs. In this exciting start-up environment, Senior Data Scientist will have the opportunity to develop data science processes from the ground up and work directly with decision-making executives to shape the future of GNS’s most important strategic initiatives.

· Apply data science to make a real and meaningful impact by working directly with Clinical Leadership, Operations Management and Analytics team at leading Healthcare analytics company in Nephrology specialty.

· Leverage the RCO Analytics data warehouse to develop advanced analytics; this includes clinical, operations, claims, revenue cycle, and finance data.

· Employ data analytics and statistical models to enrich and create new value on one of the largest Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) databases in the world and a host of related structured and unstructured data to improve clinical outcomes, patient engagement, payor analysis and population health studies.

· Deploy predictive analytics tools on the big-data and data warehouse platform Drive end-to-end process from creating and tuning algorithms to operationalizing the model into the workflows to measuring the impact. Partner with clinical research experts to develop the predictive models, analytics team to build the tools and deliver them to clinical and operations staff who in turn will use to improve patient care, healthcare operations, and financial performance.

· Leverage machine learning (ML) and artificial intelligence (AI) to develop sophisticated models based on large structured and unstructured data set, exercises considerable latitude in determining objectives and approaches to assignments.

Responsibilities and Expectations

· Deliver Analytics Solutions. Proven experience in delivering complex analytics initiatives, with a lead role in technical design, development and implementation.

· Consistent demonstrated success in data-driven change management including analyzing information, determining the “story” that the data tells, developing insights, and implementing change.

· Advanced knowledge of Business Intelligence and Analytics domains - Predictive analytics, Prescriptive models, text analytics, statistics, machine learning, data management, data architecture, data governance, etc.

· Experience using supervised and unsupervised machine learning techniques such as regression, random forest, xgboost, clustering and/or causal inference techniques such as hierarchical modeling, fixed effect/random effect models, propensity score matching, etc

· Experience and subject matter expertise in Healthcare vertical in United States - these include Value-based care, Improving Patient Outcomes, Nephrology, Kidney disease management, Population Health management, performance measure development (CMS / MIPS MACRA reporting), Qualified Clinical Data Registry, Healthcare administration, clinical care, health outcomes, social determinants of health, financial modeling/analysis, claims and revenue cycle management.

· Demonstrates professional standards related to data access, data security, HIPAA, data sensitivity, and data confidentiality.

· Knowledge of project management and agile delivery

· Knowledge of Python, Jypter, SQL, ETL, Data Visualization and other related technical competencies.

· Demonstrated strategic and analytical thinking

· Clear and concise oral and written communication skills, with a proven ability to translate complex methodologies and analytical results to higher-level business insights and key takeaways

· Ability to make decisions on moderately complex to complex issues regarding technical approach for project components

· Performs other job-related duties as assigned or apparent
