Full Job Description

UST is a leading provider of platforms; digital innovation; artificial Intelligence and end-to-end IT services and solutions for Global 1000 companies. We are transforming corporations through deep domain expertise; knowledge-based ML platforms; as well as profound anthropological efforts to understand the end customer and design products and interactions that create delight. We are deeply committed to developing a comprehensive understanding of our clients’ problems and to develop platforms to address them.

As Data Governance Analyst -Collibra/Data Management/Data Quality/Data Governance your roles and responsibilities as follows .

Core responsibilities include:

  • Be part of Data governance integrations program from data engineering side.
  • Participate in design, development of governance tools and technology solutions integration to Collibra, data catalog, Enterprise data lake, EDW, Data quality & Governance business needs.
  • Help in creating data requirements, Data mapping, data quality rules & meta data definitions.
  • Close interaction and collaboration with Project Managers, Lead Business Analysts, application and enterprise architects, application technical leads, vendor partners (both onsite and offshore), offshore developments teams and other data and application development teams.

Required skills:

  • 8+ years of experience as Data analyst and at least 4+ years of work experience with financial services domain.
  • 5+ years’ hands-on experience in managing data projects within Financial Services / Asset Management particularly in one or more of the following topics: Data Governance, Data Profiling, Data Quality, Data Glossaries, Data Catalogs, Taxonomy, Data Modeling, Metadata, Data Discovery, Business Semantics .
  • Must have 4+ years of hands-on experience implementing and using Collibra suite of tools (Governance, Catalog, etc.) to search metadata, establish data lineage, capture profiling statistics, build data quality scorecards, find relationships and similarities between data assets, and maintain business and technical glossaries.
  • Hands on experience in scripting & ability to write complex SQL queries to perform data analysis and user testing prior to actual UAT.
  • Solid understanding of Collibra API’s and their usage.
  • Solid understanding of data taxonomy ad their integration with data lake.
  • Solid understanding of metadata requirements and components.
  • Excellent understanding of semantics and their relationship to business data dictionaries.
  • Experience with and understanding of Data Quality principles and measurement.
  • Strong organizational skills and detail-oriented mindset.
  • BS in Computer Science, Information Systems, or a related field.
  • Coordinating and working along with Offshore resources.
