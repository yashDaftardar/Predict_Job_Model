Opportunity Overview

The Data Reporting Analyst will be responsible for maintaining and optimizing data visualization pulled from autonomous testing and scheduling for the Test Operations Team. This position will also be expected to deliver unique business insights by packaging data in ways that will assist the team in understanding complex analyses and data trends.

Role Responsibilities

  • Create and utilize databases, spreadsheets, or outputs to translate complex data and analysis into easily understood visualizations.
  • Generating standard or custom reports for review by executives, managers, clients, and other stakeholders.
  • Provide technical support for existing reports, dashboards, or other tools.
  • Develop and maintain data models, reports and dashboards using knowledge of internal data structures and metrics.
  • Identify and analyze data trends with business strategy implications.
  • Create or review technical documentation to ensure the accurate development of reporting solutions.
  • Conduct or coordinate tests to ensure that intelligence is consistent with defined needs

Education, Experience & Skills Required

  • Bachelors degree in Information systems, Business or Business Intelligence
  • Extensive data management experience in analytic platforms and business intelligence tools, dashboards, and reports with specific expertise in SQL
  • Adept usage of data visualization tools to build visualizations in Tableau, PowerBI, Looker or Google Data Studio
  • Advanced skills in Microsoft Excel (using formulas, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, etc for data manipulation/visualization)
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Problem-solving skills and data analytical skills
  • Strong organizational skills

Preferred Skills and Experience

  • Basic automotive electrical or automotive computer system understanding
  • Basic understanding autonomous vehicles
  • Basic understanding of commercial or heavy off highway vehicles
