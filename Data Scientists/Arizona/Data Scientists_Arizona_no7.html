Data Scientist will be responsible for a range of task including: data mining, ETL, analyzing of structured data from a wide variety of sources and identifying and scoping opportunities to automate repeatable processes

  • Create Complex queries to retrieve data and analyze discrepancies.
  • Interpret data, analyze results using statistical techniques and provide ongoing reports. Implement custom processes for data cleansing and transformation.
  • Identify, analyze, and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets.
  • Utilize multiple tools and programs to analyze and convert source data to a standardized format.
  • Perform data quality control reviews and data assessments to identify trends and anomalies.
  • Build and maintain documentation for data conversion processes while seeking out process improvements.
  • Work with product and engineering to define objectives and develop analytic tools and products.
  • Research new techniques and best practices within the industry.
  • Work closely with OE Team to prioritize information needs.
  • Determine data quality and accuracy.
  • Makes recommendations to ensure adherence to consistent definitions, business naming standards, development of standard calculations, security requirements, etc.
  • Identify and raise of data Quality issues for remediation.
Qualifications

  • Adept at query writing
  • Strong ability to collect, organize, analyze, and disseminate significant amounts of information with attention to detail and accuracy
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  • Passion for all things data and data-driven solutions.
  • Strong work ethic and high standards for achievement
  • Analytical, result driven with high attention to detail.
  • Self-starter, quick learner, problem-solver and someone who relishes challenges.
  • Accomplish targeted performance objectives with a positive and can-do attitude.
  • Strong communication and collaborative problem-solving skills
  • Bachelor’s degree from an accredited university or college in Data Analytics or Computer Science, or equivalent
  • 3+ years using SQL or other query and scripting languages to aggregate, gather, and manipulate data.
  • 2+ years reading and writing analytical programming languages (SQL, Python, R, or similar).
  • Advanced knowledge of Business Objects, SQL, and Microsoft Applications, including Excel and Access.
  • Proficient in analytic and visualization software (Tableau, Power BI, Alteryx, Databricks or similar)
  • Deep understanding of relational databases, ETL tools, data conversion and data cleansing methodologies
  • Experience with (or at least exposure to) cloud-based databases like Redshift, Snowflake, or BigQuery.
  • Strong Knowledge of statistics and experience using statistical packages for analyzing large datasets especially Excel and SQL
  • Experience with AutoSys and VB Macros to automate Data Analysis and daily reports
  • Process Improvement fundamentals
  • Masters’ degree from an accredited university or college in Data Analytics or Computer Science preferred but not required
  • Banking experience within Operations, technology and other support functions preferred but not required
  • Lean or Six Sigma Green Belt Certification – Preferred
  • Experience with Big Data technologies, such as Spark and Hadoop, is a plus.
