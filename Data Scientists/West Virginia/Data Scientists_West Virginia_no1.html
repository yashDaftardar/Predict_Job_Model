Full Job Description

The West Virginia University Research Corporation is seeking applications for a Data Scientist for the Rockefeller Neuroscience Institute. This position will assist investigators with data analysis, modeling, forecasting, and deployment of models focused on population health, clinical neuroscience, and human performance. This position will assist the director of data science to carry out research and development projects for the Rockefeller Neuroscience Institute.

In order to be successful in this position, the ideal candidate will:

  • Work independently and/or with data science and analytics team to carry out data analysis on large data sets;
  • Create visualizations, develop and validate models, and generate reports for research protocols associated with clinical, population health, and human performance.
  • Data mining to select features in order to build and optimize classifiers using machine-learning techniques to be used for models and analytics reports.


Qualifications
  • Master’s degree computer science, engineering, physics, mathematics, data, neuroscience, or related field. Ph.D. preferred.
  • Two (2) years of experience working with healthcare data.
  • Development of custom data models and algorithm on large data sets
  • Data model validation and optimizing
  • Development of machine-learning techniques
  • Creating data visualizations
  • Creating and working with data architectures
  • Solid technical understanding (data science, A.I., software engineering) and an ability to gut check technical feasibility and timelines; removing technical development barriers
  • Superior knowledge of statistical and analytical methodology and tools
  • A background in big data ecosystems
  • Knowledge of AWS Data Lake services, neural networks, and experimentation methodologies
  • Demonstrated experience conceiving and building successful analytics programs utilizing key technologies (e.g., ML/AI) within healthcare organizations
  • Ability to translate clinical experiences and realities of medical practice in to opportunity and vision for the future
  • AB testing and experimental design processes
  • Strong communication skills needed to collaborate, negotiate and influence effectively with all stakeholders at RNI
  • Advanced knowledge and experience with best in class data strategy design
  • Proven experience monetizing data and demonstrated ability to leverage data assets into various sectors
  • Analytically minded
  • Familiar with physiological data from wearables devices, data from cognitive and behavioral assessments, and medical data from Electronic Medical Record data (i.e., chronic pain, aging and Alzheimer’s disease, heart failure, addiction).
