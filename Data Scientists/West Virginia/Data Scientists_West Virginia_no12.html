Full Job Description

Experience your AI at Liquid Analytics.

Liquid Analytics is a collaborative environment where you have the freedom to create great business software as part of a team of caring software engineers.

Do you enjoy putting your computer science degree to work, creating modern cloud-native high-performance code in Julia? You love to code, dream in code, and envision your career at the forefront of computer science and AI, and working with customers to achieve their goals.

  • We are building a High-Performance Computing (HPC) Julia Language development team for our product Liquid DecisionsJulia programming language, VS-Code, Github, Flux

Experience & Requirements

You have a minimum of 5 years of Machine Learning and High-Performance Computing experience. You have working knowledge in either Natural Language Processing (NLP) or Computer Vision.

You have solid experience in development environments, data structures, algorithms, networking, user interfaces, APIs, software testing, and problem solving. You love to code!

Problem Statements and Algorithms: You must have an undergraduate degree in computer science and a PhD in Computer Vision or NLP. You have the mind of a scientist which means, you define problem statements, followed by hypotheses, and verified with experiments. As a Machine Learning researcher and modeller you can design Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Computer Vision systems with tools like GPT3 and YoloV4. This means you have working mathematics knowledge of linear algebra, calculus, geometry, statistics and logic. Solving difficult and unique problems with code is the art of programming.

Languages: You are joining our ‘Polyglot development environment’ where we write working code in a new language after three weeks of immersion. You work on both front-end and back-end applications. Visualization of algorithms is required for customers to experience their AI. Our primary Machine Learning language is Julia.

Development Environment and Testing: You have solid experience with GIT and Jenkins . Software unit testing is part of your development process.

Databases and Data Structures: You have strong SQL and NoSQL database skills with specific distributed database experience.

OS and Networking: You have strong networking experience that allows you to build high-performance computing applications in the cloud. You have working knowledge of APIs including using websockets, HTTP/REST, and gRPC libraries. You have very strong Linux OS skills.

You are eligible to travel anywhere in North America and like to work with customers to understand their needs and experiences. Building, maintaining, and evolving cloud applications requires proactive and assertive customer engagement.

We love to work with assertive creative problem solvers who love to create high-performance cloud solutions with state of the art software languages and libraries.
