Full Job Description

The Rockefeller Neuroscience Institute at West Virginia University Research Corporation is seeking applications for a Statistician to work with the Research, Development, & Data Analytics program on data analysis and modeling focused on population health, clinical neuroscience, and human performance. This position will assist the director of Research, Development, & Data Analytics to carry out research and development projects.

In order to be successful in this position, the ideal candidate will:

  • Research data management
  • Maintains databases
  • Assures validity of data and identifies outliers; data errors, resolves same.
  • Manages data files; maintains records of analyses and management decisions
  • Research Data/Statistical Analysis
  • Conducts data analyses and generates reports for PI and research team.
  • Interprets and presents analyses and results to PI.
  • Drafts statistical analysis sections of manuscripts for PI’s review and subsequent submission to peer reviewed meetings and journals.
  • Drafts statistical analysis sections of research proposals for PI’s review, and assists in sampling and power calculation.
  • Conducts literature reviews.
  • Assists the PI in the development of research questionnaires and surveys.
  • Attends center meetings and functions.
  • Development of custom data models and algorithm on large data sets
  • Data model validation and optimizing
  • Creating data visualizations
  • Creating and working with data architectures
  • Experience working with healthcare and performance data
Qualifications
  • Master’s degree in statistics or related field;
  • Two (2) year's of experience of data management and analysis, and exposure to large databases;
  • Or an equivalent combination of education and/or experience
  • Knowledge of statistics, algebra, geometry, calculus, and their applications.
  • Exceptional organizational and time management skills.
  • Skill in organizing resources and establishing priorities.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite required.
  • High attention to detail.
  • Ability to read, analyze and interpret general business periodicals, professional journals, technical procedures.
  • Ability to write reports, business correspondence, and procedure manuals.
  • Ability to effectively present information and respond to questions from groups of managers, clients, customers, and the general public.
  • Demonstrated ability to work in a team environment and provide effective communication with the team.
  • Demonstrated ability to communicate effectively, both orally and in writing.
  • Skilled at using computers and computer systems (including hardware and software) to set up functions, enter data, or process information.
  • Skilled at compiling, coding, categorizing, calculating, tabulating, auditing, or verifying information or data.
Requirements
  • CITI certifications preferred
