WHAT IS CARBON ALL ABOUT?

Carbon Health provides primary and urgent care for the family and has been working tirelessly to make COVID-19 testing, care and vaccinations available across the country. In its mission to make world-class healthcare accessible to all, Carbon Health leverages its unique technology platform to meet patients where they are - literally - with seamless, personal omnichannel care. With a growing team of expert providers, engineers, designers, and more, Carbon Health pushes the boundaries to deliver personal, accessible and high-quality care.

The Job at a Glance

As Vice President, Data Science, you will work closely with our technology and business teams to shape the evolution of Carbon Health’s technology platform with data analytics, insights, and data science/ML models. You will leverage your product-driven data science skills to develop and drive a strategic roadmap that will ultimately result in a higher quality of services for our patients while increasing the efficiency of our clinic operations. You will build a team of highly effective data scientists and data analysts and provide opportunities for career growth. You will be a thoughtful and well-balanced decision-maker.

What You'll Do

    • Build and lead a highly effective data team to deliver best-in-class data capabilities that will support Carbon Health’s mission and goals
    • Define key metrics and trends at the company level. Provide well-designed dashboards with drill-down capabilities to show more granular operational metrics by each business unit while providing key insights from analytics. Continue to evolve key metrics as our business grows and expands to additional categories.
    • Define the data strategy that will support the data roadmap that is tightly coupled with the product roadmap
    • Co-define and own the robust and scalable data infrastructure that will support the work of data analysts and data scientists
    • Evaluate, license, or adopt the latest tools for data analysts and data scientists productivity
    • Own the full data science life cycle: scoping to prototyping, testing, deploying, measuring value, and iterating
    • Partner with engineering teams (ML Engineering, platform) to integrate ML products into technical platforms and deploy real-time models at a large scale
    • Partner with operational teams to help guide business decisions through model outputs and findings
    • Collaborate with stakeholders to understand their needs to ensure alignment on the development and delivery of scalable data solutions
    • Identify and innovate new opportunities to drive better patient and business outcomes through data science
    • Foster continued growth of the data analysts and data scientists. Define career ladders and provide opportunities for career growth

About You

    • 7+ years experience in managing data science function in a high-growth tech company
    • Thorough command of general data science and machine learning techniques, good understanding of data engineering practices
    • Proficiency in SQL and in the computational language such as R/Python/Matlab
    • Good understanding of experimental and statistical techniques for the design of A/B tests to measure the impact of initiatives
    • Experience with distributed systems such as Apache Spark, and cloud-native infrastructure experience (AWS, GCP, Azure)
    • Familiarity with modern data stacks including ETL tools, data warehouse, and ML pipelines.
    • Ability to work on cross-functional projects and communicate with stakeholders at multiple levels of technical detail
    • Communication skills that can influence across organizations and at all levels
    • Strong technical leadership, project delivery, and people management
    • BS or MS in a quantitative field (computer science, statistics, mathematics, economics, operations research, physics, neuroscience, etc)
