Job Summary

We are looking for a Analyst that will help us discover the information hidden in vast amounts of data and help us make smarter decisions to deliver even better service for our customers and contain the rising costs of healthcare. Your focus will be in data visualization development and analysis of business and provider performance and create tools to store associated data within the organization to allow end-user reporting and analysis. You will work closely with operational leaders to help them understand and provide data leading to greater insight and efficiency. You will develop a strong relationship with the operational units and become a trusted component of their business process. Collaboration with operational and IT leadership, and your peers will be key to success. Bring your curiosity, creativity, and energy and help us turn our data into actionable insights.

Responsibilities

  • Develop, maintain, and manage Tableau driven dashboards & analytics and working knowledge of Tableau administration/architecture to create user-friendly, integrated reporting solutions.
  • Develop and leverage business context to give meaning to the data and information.
  • Identify and understand drivers of business challenges and communicate clearly with business partners.
  • Translate findings from data into consumable, actionable insights via visualization and user experience design.
  • Fulfilling analytical requests timely and accurately by collecting, organizing, and presenting information.
  • Proactively networks and researches to seek data to story tell, understand how it can and cannot be used, help align when groups are using data differently and propose standards.
  • Execute SDLC process in agile fashion for analytic development.
  • Work with business partners to identify and frame business challenge.
  • Identify and wrangle data needed and understand business context.
  • Conduct data analysis to identify drivers of the challenge.
  • Design the output in conjunction with business partners in iterative fashion.
  • Code/develop the solution.
  • Test the outputs in conjunction with the business.
  • Communicate and execute implementation.
  • Maintain the analytical assets, caring for any required changes based on process change, business requirements, or additional enhancements.
  • Prioritize incoming requests and determine highest value add activities in conjunction with management.
