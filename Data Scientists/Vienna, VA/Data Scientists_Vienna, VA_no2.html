This position is located in the Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency (CISA), Infrastructure Security Division.

Responsibilities

The Infrastructure Security Division (ISD) focuses on protecting critical infrastructure from all hazards by managing risk and enhancing resilience through collaboration with the critical infrastructure community, which includes private sector owners, operators, and employees; State, local, tribal, and territorial officials; and other Federal agencies.

The primary purpose of the position is to serve as the Data Scientist. Typical work assignments include:

  • Perform analyses of critical infrastructure, including their interdependencies, vulnerabilities, consequences, and other complexities.
  • Provide technical guidance to other analysts on the development of analytic capability applying operations research methods.
  • Determine applicability of various analytical methods and methodologies appropriate to the particular problem under study.
  • Provide structured guidance for and technical reviews of analytical work of other analysts
  • Evaluate study projects, proposals, reports and analytical techniques.
  • Recommend new products and techniques based on detailed knowledge of customer requirements.
  • Lead division data governance efforts.
  • Mature data integration and interoperability of critical infrastructure data.
  • Apply data analytic techniques in support of ISD's Performance Management Program.
