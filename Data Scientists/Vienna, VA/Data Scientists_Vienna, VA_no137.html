The Sr. People Data Analyst is responsible for ensuring we have actionable data at our fingertips. You will work closely with various People team members and other business functions to understand business objectives, gather data, and develop and execute accurate and actionable reports. This role requires a strong understanding of HR business processes and technical knowledge in Workday, Visier and data warehousing platforms (AWS). This individual must be passionate about data, technology and working in an agile environment.

Responsibilities:

  • Manage all employee life-cycle data

  • Design people dashboards, reports, KPIs and metrics

  • Audit data integrity, investigate concerns, and establish processes to streamline and improve data quality

  • Maintain HRIS/ATS hygiene to support accurate reporting and making recommendations on improvements if needed

  • Partner with internal / external stakeholders to understand requirements for report requests, understand data structures, availability and reporting limitations

  • Proactively develops reporting solutions to provide better transparency for decision making

  • Train and educate stakeholders on people data metrics and dashboards

  • Onboard new datasets into our people technology platforms (Workday, AWS, Visier)

  • Manage mass data changes related to re-orgs, foundational data updates and data migrations

  • Develop and maintain integration data flows

  • Ensure compliance with data privacy regulations and best practices

  • Collaborate on new system implementations

Basic Qualifications:

  • 4+ years of data analytics experience

  • Experience using HR tools, specifically Workday, to develop complex HR reports

  • Advanced excel skills

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Intermediate level of proficiency in SQL

  • Ability to audit data and recognize data issues and inconsistencies

  • Strong attention to detail

  • Ability to maintain confidentiality and exercise a high level of discretion

  • Excellent critical thinking and problem solving skills

  • Experience in analyzing data structures and recommending enhancements to support reporting improvements

  • Strong written and verbal communication skills

  • Ability to work individually or within a team in a fast paced environment

  • Ability to prioritize daily issues, tasks, and projects, and flexibility to change tasks and priorities when necessary
