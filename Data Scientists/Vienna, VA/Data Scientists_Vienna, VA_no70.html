The Center for Securing the Homeland (CSH) Data Analytics Department is seeking an experienced data scientist who will join a team of data scientists, analysts and engineers supporting the Department of Homeland Security (DHS). The explosion of data and complexity of problems facing DHS requires an innovative approach which MITRE is leading through the application of new technology and techniques. As a data scientist supporting DHS you will work on real world problems related to cybersecurity, infrastructure protection, immigration, disaster response, maritime awareness, and many others.

Job function for this role will include:

  • Leading small tasks, contributing to a team or working independently

  • Incorporate feedback and strive for a continuous growth mindset

  • Respect a diversity of opinion but also be willing to stand your ground when needed

  • Assess the audience and tailor feedback with the receiver’s point of view in mind

Basic Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree and 5 years of relevant experience

  • Applicants selected for this position will be subject to a government security investigation and must meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information or applicants who are eligible for security clearances.

Required Qualifications:

  • Ability to obtain DHS Fitness clearance

  • Demonstrate excellent written and verbal communication skills

  • Experience working with and analyzing large or diverse datasets and expertise with at least one modern programming language such as Python, Java or Scala

  • Proven skills related to machine learning and artificial intelligence to include assessment of model effectiveness, explainability and bias

  • Experience with Scikit-Learn, AWS SageMaker, Tensorflow or related machine learning technologies

  • Experience in the preparation of comprehensive written reports, presentations, and charts based on research, collection, and analysis of data

  • Candidate must be able to obtain and maintain a DHS Fitness clearance which will require US citizenship

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Prior experience working in a customer facing environment

  • Prior experience working on government projects

  • Familiarity with cloud-based environments such as Amazon Web Services, Google Cloud Platform, or Microsoft Azure

  • Experience with Natural Language Processing (NLP)

  • Experience visualizing multi-dimensional data using tools such as Tableau, Kibana, ggplot2, Plotly, matplotlib, or D3.js.

  • Experience incorporating spatial data and multispectral imagery in the creation of analytic products

  • Experience with data engineering tasks such as Extract, Transform, Load (ETL) and assessment of data quality

  • Experience with databases technologies (e.g., PostgreSQL, Oracle, MySQL, SQL Server, MongoDB, Neo4j)

  • Experience with SAS, R, or Matlab

  • Experience with business intelligence platforms such as Tableau, AWS QuickSight, Looker, or Qlik

  • Experience with distributed processing frameworks such as Hadoop or Spark

  • Possess DHS Fitness

This requisition requires the following clearance(s):

Fitness - DHS
