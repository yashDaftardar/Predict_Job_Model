Job Description

What You’ll Get to Do:

As a CACI-WGI (The Wexford Group International) Data Scientist, you will design data and statistical modeling processes to create algorithms and predictive models and perform custom analysis to support client missions.

More About the Role:

You will provide collect, leverage, analyze and document data wrangling and automated visualizations/analytics, while interfacing with end users. Also you will manipulate large data sets to identify trends and reach meaningful conclusions to inform strategic operationally-based decision and design, create, and manage a modern application that is new development and cloud native.

You’ll Bring These Qualifications:

  • Possess two years, minimum, work experience demonstrating technical expertise in the following:

  • Open-source programming languages to include Python and R language

  • Media analysis tools to include Python and R language, and their open-source libraries, Tableau or visualization software, Git, or social media platform application program interfaces (APIs)

  • Media analysis methodologies to include machine learning, natural language processing, or common statistical techniques

  • Applying advanced quantitative analysis to media analysis problems

  • Publishing finished media analysis projects including written assessments, web interfaces, or interactive graphics

  • Data visualization software

  • Experience supporting complicated problems and providing solutions that are highly creative and ingenious, exhibiting creativity and resourcefulness

  • Strong analytic, critical thinking, organizational, and interpersonal skills, and ability to work independently or in a team

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, Software Engineering or a related discipline

  • Active TS/SCI with polygraph security clearance

These Qualifications Would be Nice to Have:

  • Possess the above skills but with five-plus years of experience

  • Experience in the architecture and optimization of data schemas, petabyte-scale ETL, etc.

  • Experience in statistical, geospatial, link, temporal, and predictive analysis, especially for IC agencies

What We Can Offer You:

  • CACI-WGI (The Wexford Group International) is a wholly-owned subsidiary of CACI with a reputation for uncompromising standards of quality in its people and its performance. Joining the CACI-WGI team is a mark of excellence for those employees who complete our rigorous Recruiting, Assessment, and Selection (RAS) process.

  • CACI-WGI’s mission places its personnel against the government’s most critical emerging challenges. Work with us and you’ll be working with a team making a difference across the globe.

  • CACI-WGI offers competitive benefits as well as numerous learning and development opportunities.

**This position is contingent upon award**
