Role Purpose

As part of the Analytics team, Analysts help leaders throughout the organization understand and identify opportunities to improve and grow our business. This person will own specific projects where deeper analysis is needed to understand the overall trends we are seeing in the organization and provide insights to improve. This position will work closely with our Data Engineers along with Finance leadership and leaders around the company to understand analytics needs and provide accurate and actionable analysis.

What You Will Do

  • Provide deep analysis of business performance
  • Work with leadership to understand current business performance and identify areas that need further investigation
  • Design executive level reports that provide regular and meaningful data to the executive team and company leadership
  • Manage analytics projects by defining deliverables with stakeholders and identifying the needed resources and data to make it happen
  • Clearly present actionable insights resulting from analytics projects

Desired Competencies and Experience

  • At least 4-6 Years providing analytics to corporate management
  • History of providing actionable insights
  • Experience working with stakeholders to understand objectives and turning that into meaningful analysis
  • Ability to own an analytics project end to end
  • Extensive experience with BI software like Tableau, Power BI, Qlik etc.
  • Deep understanding of dimensional database models and architecture
  • Strong SQL programming skills
  • Degree in Economics, Statistics, Business etc. preferred
