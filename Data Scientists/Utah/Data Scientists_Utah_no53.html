Job Description:

As a Lead Analytics Data Engineer (Remote) - Data Science & Risk, you will support our data scientists and risk team by building stable data pipelines. You will form part of the analytics engineering organization responsible for enabling innovation through data exploration.

This role can be performed remotely anywhere in the US or from one of our office locations in Draper, UT or Glendale, AZ.


THE DETAILS

You will be at the forefront of this rapidly evolving field and working closely with other specialists in the data & analytics space (Data Analysts, Data Scientists, and DataOps Engineers). Most importantly, you’ll be part of a team that forms the backbone of a fast-growing company at the forefront of an exciting, growing industry.


ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS
  • Proactively collaborate with Data Science & Risk Analysis teams to drive business value
  • Enable our data analysts and scientists to perform data exploration activities with autonomy
  • Champion improvements to the data storage platform with the wider analytics engineering team (actively migrating from SQL Server to a combination of Databricks’ DeltaLake & Snowflake on Azure)
  • Contribute ways to improve our data pipeline frameworks (legacy SQL Server stored procedures migrating to a combination of Python, Airflow, DBT, Spark, and Snowflake code)
  • Help mentor peers and provide technical leadership in the analytic data engineering discipline
REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS
  • Bachelor’s degree in a technical field (Engineering, Computer Science, Information Systems, etc.)
  • 5+ years of professional experience in software engineering and/or DevOps
  • 1+ years of hands-on experience in cloud environments such as Azure
  • Expertise in build and deployment automation tools such as Jenkins, Docker, and Terraform
  • Generalized understanding of systems and technical architectures (including networking, compute, and storage)
PREFERRED SKILLS & EXPERTISE
  • Work in a self-directed but not isolated manner
  • Passionate about reliability, experimentation, and continuous delivery
