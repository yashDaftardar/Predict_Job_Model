Full Job Description

The Senior Machine Learning (ML) Engineer role is focused on building out and scaling up our Kubernetes micro-services AI platform based on reactive engineering principles. You will be wrapping our state of the art deep learning models in robust, reactive services on our event driven lambda architecture. You will leverage DevOps and MLOps to avoid repetitive or error prone tasks, and you will contribute in a significant way to system architecture decisions.

This role is an expert in machine learning and has experience building & scaling ML / deep learning systems. Past experience includes quantizing, pruning, dynamic sequence length, multi instance inference, & batch sizing of models. This role also understands model tuning and optimization, and handling of overfitting & under-fitting as well as dropout, momentum, and learning rate annealing. This person has a deep understanding of how all these parameters impact model accuracy, efficiency, speed and scalability.

Responsibilities:

  • Work alongside brilliant data scientists, AI product managers, and engineers solving some of the world's most difficult AI challenges.
  • Create fault tolerant, reliable, reactive, and independent micro-services connected by REST APIs, Kafka, gRPC, on Kubernetes infrastructure.
  • Leverage state of the art R&D deep learning adapters to keep our AI system architecture maintainable, cost effective, efficient, scalable and responsive.
  • Contribute your vision and ideas to improve our conventions, processes, software and customer experience.
  • Automate CI/CD and other routine processes such as unit and E2E testing.
  • Contribute to clearly defined requirements and research solutions.
  • Be a self starter who can take initiative and blaze your own trail when required.

Our ideal candidate will have most of these:

  • Bachelors or Masters in Computer Science or commensurate work experience.
  • Experience scaling software systems with millions of users.
  • Experience with Kubernetes micro-services & Docker.
  • Experienced in the design and implementation of REST APIs and Kafka.
  • Experienced in SQL schema design and writing complex SQL and queries.
  • Knowledge of data structures and algorithms.
  • Experienced with Agile methodology.
  • 5+ years of software engineering experience
  • 2-3+ years of ML engineering experience
  • Expert in both machine learning and software engineering
  • Proven experience building, deploying and delivering AI systems at scale
  • Experience with Google Cloud or another major provider (AWS, Azure).
  • Experience with Python and FastAPI, Flask, SQLAlchemy, and Graphene.
  • Experience with CI/CD pipeline automation.
  • Experience with Redis or similar high performance caches.
  • Experience with PostgreSQL, Redis and KVS (e.g. Cassandra, Cloud Datastore).
  • Experience with MLOps, DevOps, Ansible, Terraform, and shell scripting.
  • Experience with FaaS.
