Overview:
The Customer Experience (CX) Analytics team is looking for an outstanding Data Scientist to join its growing Data Science organization. This person will play a central role in one of our companyâ€™s most highly visible departments, pulling insights from the data warehouse to guide strategic decisions of C-Level executives and shape the way that we find and serve our customers.

As the Sr. Data Scientist , you will work closely with the Director of Data Science and a variety of cross-functional teams to identify opportunities to enhance customer experience, improve client retention and drive revenue.

You will work closely with the Data Science team on a range of compelling projects. In the past these have included churn prediction, segmentation models, recommendation engines, lead scoring, outbound call optimization, billing date optimization and predictive ML/AI models that improve core business functions. The successful candidate will use creativity and curiosity to make meaningful contributions across these efforts.

As an experienced data scientist, you have developed the ability to think carefully and critically about data, validation, measurement and experimental design. You are comfortable taking a complex or ambiguous problem and using data to extract meaningful insights and identify opportunities. You have a solid foundation in statistics, probability and machine learning, with the ability to dive deeper when required. You are fluent in SQL, have a good working knowledge of Python or a similar high level programming language and are familiar with development best practices and version control. You have a history of managing your time well in order to deliver projects end to end in a time-constrained situation. You area a capable data storyteller who can distill complex analyses into key business insights. You are a team player with the ability and willingness to mentor more junior data scientists and analysts. You are humble, open-minded, and always seeking to grow and develop your skills.
Responsibilities
  • Help design and deliver long-term analytics and ML projects: finding answers to business problems, implementing a solution, and tracking downstream effectiveness.
  • Communicate effectively with peers on the decision science team, internal clients, and company leadership regarding ongoing projects and future possibilities.
  • Carry out ad-hoc data extracts and analyses as needed.
  • Mentor more junior data scientist and analysts
Qualifications
  • 3-4 years of work experience in data science or 1-2 years of work experience plus an advanced degree (MS, MBA) or 0-1 years of work experience plus a PhD in a related field.
  • Fluent in English, SQL and at least one general scripting language (e.g. Python, R, Julia).
  • Attention to detail and overarching curiosity are both essential.
  • Willingness to learn new quantitative techniques and programming languages.
  • Responsible and reliable; able to set and meet deadlines.
