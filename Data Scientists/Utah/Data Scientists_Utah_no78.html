Asset Modeling Analyst II staff level position supporting the overall direction of financial asset modeling of Merrick Bank Corporation (the “Bank”) by performing, or assisting in the performance of, the primary duties listed below.
Essential Functions:
  • Actively participate in the asset modeling for new account acquisition for the installment loan business.
  • Develop, maintain, and update various financial models and structured datasets (MS Excel, SQL Server) in support of asset modeling responsibilities.
  • Actively participate in ad hoc financial analysis activities; including existing/new product pricing, “what-if” scenarios, and cost/benefit analysis. This includes being able to apply unconventional perspectives and modes of operating.
  • Periodically provide presentations and discoveries to senior/executive management.
  • Maintain necessary documentation and procedures consistent with an audited environment.
  • Prepare additional ad hoc reporting and data visualizations as needed to assist Management strategic business planning and decision-making. Ability to proactively conceptualize and develop additional supporting material for these types of exercises expected.
  • Actively maintain compliance with internal policies, procedures, & laws in accordance with a regulated banking environment
Compliance with Laws & Regulations:
  • Responsible for complying with all of the Bank’s internal control policies and procedures.
  • Responsible for understanding and complying with all laws and regulations to which the Bank is subject.
  • Responsible for communicating problems in operations, noncompliance with the code of conduct, noncompliance with laws and regulations, policy violations, or illegal acts.

Education and Experience:
  • BA/BS Degree required in Finance, Mathematics, Economics, Business Management or equivalent. 3+ years of Finance, credit risk or quantitative analytics experience.
  • Banking / financial services experience, with exposure in installment lending, preferred
  • Working experience with mathematical concepts such as probability and statistical inference. Ability to apply concepts such as fractions, percentages, ratios, and proportions to practical situations.
  • Advanced MS Office experience with emphasis on Excel. Possesses the ability to decipher, understand, and implement complex formulas. Ability to read and write basic Python and VBA scripts preferred. Experience developing complex financial models is preferred.
Summary of Qualifications:
  • Responsible professional with the ability to interpret instructions furnished in written, oral, diagram, or schedule form. This individual would also need to be able to work through unconventional problems to identify issues and provide possible resolutions. Ability to work under little, to no, supervision.
  • Communicates effectively both orally and in writing; present ideas in a clear, concise, understandable, and organized manner.
  • Practical understanding of financial reporting in US GAAP based accounting environment
  • Conceptual understanding and experience with relational databases preferred.
