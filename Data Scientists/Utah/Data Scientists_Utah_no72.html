GENERAL SUMMARY

The Senior Data Analyst obtains pipeline in-line inspection (ILI) data for the municipal water and oil & gas industries, performs qualitative and quantitative checks with proprietary Cypress software in accordance with regulatory and company standards, and accurately analyzes ILI data to provide comprehensive and detailed reports profiling the conditions of our customer’s pipeline systems. The role is also responsible for data confirmation, customer specification requirement review, advanced data analysis, industry code and standards interpretation, and establishing techniques and procedures.

PRINCIPAL DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES (other duties may be assigned)

  • Prepare ILI data assessments; generate reports.
  • Identify geometry, corrosion, and geospatial features captured in the ILI data.
  • Generate ILI customer reports utilizing proprietary software.
  • ILI data preparation and processing; final report analysis review.
  • Pipeline feature sizing, categorization, and evaluation as per client requirements.
  • Application of anomaly interaction rules in accordance with published industry standards.
  • Utilize standard and non-standard data analysis methods.
  • Perform all other duties as prescribed in ANSI/ASNT ILI-PQ2017 at a senior level.
  • Maintain skills, certifications, and complete additional training (internal/external) as required.
  • Actively participate in training sessions and pass required exams IAW API 1163 and ANSI/ASNT ILI-PQ-2017 standards.
  • Promote Environmental, Health, & Safety and follow safe working practices at all times.
  • Represent Cypress at all times with high moral standards while adhering to its “Code of Business Conduct and Ethics”.
  • Actively support the Cypress’ commitment to its Core Values including safety, leadership, equal employment opportunity, employee development, and operational excellence.
  • Know and understand quality policy and comply with all requirements of the Quality Systems Manual, Operating and Technical Procedures, and Workplace Instructions.

KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS & ABILITIES

  • Prior Pipeline In-line Inspection data analysis experience (Level II or Level III) required
  • Post-secondary education or technical certification required; high school graduate/equivalent and minimum of 2 years Engineering/Science degree required
  • Very strong analytical skills – determine the quality of ILI data to make sound decisions about next actions if issues are found, ability to solve problems, and recommend improvements and action accordingly
  • Strong communication skills (written and verbal) are required – ability to receive and communicate technical information professionally, accurately, and in a timely manner
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to work in a computer environment for extended periods of time
  • Proficient computer and software skills
  • Ability to work flexible hours based on business needs
  • Ability to pass a color blindness test
  • Ability to work independently or within a team environment to fulfill project scope
  • Ability to apply and practice knowledge from informal/formal training, classroom, and OJT to the assigned work area

Although the position is ideal in Salt Lake City , both Houston and Tulsa are viable for a seasoned, customer-centric and skilled Senior Data Analyst.
