Summary/Objective

As a Machine Learning Engineer, you will be part of a team to build new applications to provide productivity software for legal industry. The team will design/implement Natural Language Processing algorithm and deploy them to production at scale for providing various features for Legal documents. This team is responsible for provides a single platform for the entire lifecycle of developing, training, deploying, and testing machine learning models.

Multiple positions available with variable years of experience.


Essential Functions

  • Research and prototype state of the art models, A/B test, and launch legal text extraction-based features in production
  • Collaborate with user experience team and Software Engineers and Product managers from various team

Competencies

  • Analytical thinking skills
  • Detail oriented
  • Research Skills
  • Problem Solving Skills
  • Team Oriented & can work independently with some oversight
  • Eager to learn new technologies

Required Education and Experience

  • PhD/MS in Computer Science, Information Retrieval, Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing or a related discipline with 3-8+ years of experience
  • A solid foundation in computer science, with strong competencies in data structures, algorithms, and software design.
  • Industry experience of building and producing innovative end-to-end Machine Learning systems
  • Excellent programming skills in Python, experience using API Flask (REST) in production environment
  • Excellent written and spoken communication skills

Preferred Experience

  • Experience in development of large-scale NLP systems in a large industrial or government environment
  • Experience with developing microservices with Docker and Kubernetes to serve machine learning models
  • Experience with Azure/Google Cloud Platform / Amazon Web Services
  • Experience with Flask, Apache Spark, Apache Kafka, Redis is a plus
