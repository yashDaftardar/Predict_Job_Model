Northrop Grumman Mission Systems located in Salt Lake City, UT is seeking a Principal or Senior Principal Data Scientist to be responsible for creation and support of computer-based tools used in Manufacturing as well as database engineering, visualizations and analytics. The Engineer in this position will involve internal customer and external interfacing, requirements analysis, coding, design and validation testing in multiple software languages. Develop, upgrade and maintain database driven applications in a client/server environment and support various graphical user interfaces. Apply advanced techniques in Artificial Intelligence to analyze business, engineering, and manufacturing data. Extensive contacts with internal personnel at peer and various management levels concerning the ongoing implementation of the digital thread at MS/NGC. Conducts briefings and performs other related tasks as assigned

We are interested in highly motivated candidates with great interpersonal communication skills and a willingness to learn. Primary responsibilities will include but are not limited to:
  • Leading the SLC Site data analytics CoP
  • Leading the SLC Site efforts for implementing the digital thread as a holistic program
  • Creating, upgrading and maintaining software that may interface with equipment and with graphical user interfaces in multiple languages
  • Creating data-driven tools that simplify efforts related to a wide variety of functional areas
  • Connecting to, analyzing, and visualizing information from several disparate data sets in support of providing greater visibility on various areas of program performance
  • Automating reports and processes through software development
  • Leading the SLC Site in exploring a utilizing advanced Artificial Intelligence tools.
  • Must possess strong organizational and interpersonal skills to communicate effectively verbally and in writing
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills. This position may be filled at a higher grade based on the qualifications below. MANUMS
