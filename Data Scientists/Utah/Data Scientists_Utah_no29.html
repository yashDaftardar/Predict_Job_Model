Full Job Description

Summary

Data analysis, modeling, and forecasting is at the heart of our business operations. The Manager of our data science and business intelligence team is an expert in defining the business metrics that drive our success and in management, leadership, and utilizing data science to improve the performance of our company. This senior position requires an exceptional background in statistics, mathematics, modeling, and reporting along with experience managing teams. The manager works as in integral member of senior management team in setting strategy, executing on quarterly and annual objectives, and driving business growth.


Essential Duties and Responsibilities

  • Lead data science, business intelligence, and reporting staff in strategy, discovery, innovation, and development of long-term company modeling, forecasting, and operational improvements.
  • Partner with department executives to formulate, plan and implement changes that result in strategic business improvements.
  • Hold reviews with business owners to validate quality, completeness, and accuracy of analysis and reports.
  • Design, develop and maintain business intelligence applications and solutions.
  • Proactively collaborate and partner with departments and stakeholders across the company to manage reporting, analytics, and data science requests to meet business needs.
  • Develop strategy and plans to modernize and improve Sorenson's data infrastructure, including contributing to the enterprise data warehouse.
  • Test solutions for functionality and integrity; troubleshoot and resolve errors.
  • Utilize data mining techniques and develop data models to assist in the visualization and interpretation of data.
  • Lead team in building critical report logic for daily, weekly, monthly, and quarterly reports.
  • Interpret data to identify trends, conduct root cause analysis or problems, or suggest areas for process improvement.
  • Collaborate with developmental teams to design business intelligence solutions to facilitate data gathering, storage and retrieval.
  • Collaborate with software engineering to integrate forecasting services into production applications.
  • Use programming languages such as Python, R, SQL, and algorithms to produce predictive analytic packages for operational deployment.
  • Stay current and informed on the latest forecasting, data science, business intelligence trends and best practices.
  • Develop and apply algorithms or models to key business metrics with the goal of improving operations or answering business questions. Provide findings and analysis for use in decision making.
  • Evaluate personal and team progress by analyzing strengths and weaknesses in performance. Set quarterly and annual objectives that result in company, team, and individual growth.
  • Create and maintain documentation and knowledge bases.
  • Assist in hiring and onboarding new team members as required.
  • Provide business strategy recommendations and measures toward improving business results.

Knowledge/Skills/Abilities Required

  • Demonstrated management skill providing supervision, direction, and individual team member development.
  • Possess strong leadership skills with experience managing teams and projects.
  • Advanced experience with enterprise business intelligence solutions.
  • Expert knowledge in data science, math, statistics, and database structures of all types.
  • Advanced understanding of data tools such as Python, R, SQL, and other modeling and programming languages.
  • Ability to read, interpret, apply and computationally implement topics in advanced mathematics (such as probability and statistics, machine learning, time series analysis, etc.).
  • Proven track record of applying advanced analytics and data science to improve business results and resolve business problems.
  • Proven history of delivering actionable data products to business stakeholders.
  • History of use of the processes required to analyze very large data sets to forecast future trends and business requirements. Including a strong understanding of queuing models.
  • Knowledge of parallel (CPU & GPU), cluster, and cloud computing processing a strong plus.
  • Detailed-oriented and able to manage own schedule and time.
  • Ability to meet strict deadlines, work with little supervision.
  • Working knowledge of business financial and operational metrics and how they apply to company value.

Communication/Language Skills

  • Excellent communications skills including the ability to communicate complex findings to executives in various departments.
  • Must have strong interpersonal skills, non-verbal skills, and listening skills.
  • Comfortable giving and receiving feedback.
  • Able to effectively communicate in various team settings using clear, concise, and proper tone.
  • Is flexible and open to the ideas and opinions of others.

Experience/Education/Certifications

  • Advanced Degree in math, statistics, computer science or related fields, or equivalent job experience and training.
  • 6 or more years of educational or commercial experience in an analytics or data science role.
  • History of providing leadership on new methods for using data science to deliver insights and innovation.
  • 2 or more years of team management experience

Other General Requirements/Additional Notes

  • Ability to travel up to 15% of time.
  • Dexterity of hands and fingers to operate a computer keyboard, mouse, tools, and to handle other computer components.
  • Ability to sit at workstation for long periods of time completing analysis, testing, documentation, or other assignments.
  • Lifting and transporting of moderately heavy objects, such as computers and peripherals.
  • Positive attitude, team player, good interpersonal communication skills and able to work across company departments.
