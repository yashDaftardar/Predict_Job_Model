Full Job Description

BioFire Diagnostics, LLC. is seeking a bright and motivated individual to join our Data Science team. The candidate's primary responsibility will be to provide analytics for data collected during reagent manufacturing operations and Post Market Surveillance at BioFire Diagnostics to help improve products and product operations. The candidate is expected to partner with multiple product operations teams, so they should have strong interdisciplinary communication skills (both written and oral). The candidate will be embedded in the BioMathematics department, but will also have close collaboration with the Data Analytics, Manufacturing, Quality Assurance, and Post Market Surveillance groups among others.

The ideal candidate will have:


Level I:

Requires a Bachelor of Science in Math, Statistics, Engineering, or related field.

0+ years of work experience data analysis, software development, or a related field.

Level II:

Requires a Bachelor of Science in Math, Statistics, Engineering, or related field.

2+ years of work experience data analysis, software development, or a related field. Applicable graduate work can substitute as work experience.

Level III:

Requires a Bachelor of Science in Math, Statistics, Engineering, or related field (applicable graduate degree is preferred).

4+ years of work experience data analysis, software development, or a related field. Applicable graduate work can substitute as work experience.

  • Excellent programming skills
  • Experience with one or more of the following programming languages: R, Python, or C#
  • Knowledge of basic statistics, data visualization techniques, databases (SQL), and machine learning
  • Strong technical writing and presentation skills
  • Interest in learning and understanding BioFire's core technologies and operational processes
  • Interest in biochemistry, engineering, or manufacturing technologies
