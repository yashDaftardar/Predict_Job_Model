Job Purpose

We are looking for an experienced Data Analyst who is willing to wrangle with data, working alongside data scientists to pave the way for the creation of machine learning models. This person will be able to use analytics skills to produce insights from an expanding data platform.

What you’ll get to do…

  • Write scripts related to Data Mining & Data Wrangling

  • Analyze large data sets for patterns to create new pattern recognition algorithms and rules

  • Monitor system performance and success metrics across our data platform to identify customer pain points at the data level

  • Define, scope, and create new features to improve the data engine

  • Quantify data models, data strategy, and analytics roadmaps

  • Perform data analytics and statistical & mathematical modeling.

  • Data exploration, design of experiments (A/B and multivariate tests), and hypothesis validation to deliver meaningful insights and recommendations to the product team

  • Work with Sales and Client Services to help current and prospective clients understand our data enhancement engine

  • Analyze and model data to provide insights on product success, client success, and user financial health

  • Deliver findings and interpretation of results to the Product Management team through data visualization, presentation, and training sessions

  • Be able to present the results of your research to non-technical audiences in a meaningful and understandable way

  • Guide and educate other members of the team as a mentor and subject matter owner on best analytical practices

  • Lead strategic thinking in the areas of Top Offenders, System Transaction Rule Creation/Update, Confidence Test Creation/Update, & Validation of Model Predictions

  • Oversee the development of methods, processes, and procedures to test output or input based on data compliance, quality, and use requirements as established by product management, client specifications, and governance programs

  • Produce and update guidelines and documentation of data quality development processes, procedures, and standards

  • Perform data discovery and generate data stories with associated datasets for internal customers using system tools, queries, and scripts

  • Integrate refined data elements to produce requested or required data assets & products

  • Perform statistical modeling and analysis of structured and unstructured datasets to develop metrics, reports and visualizations of trends and patterns

  • Work with technology organization’s decision-makers and business end-users to understand data and analysis needs and develop technical requirements

  • Partner with development and data management roles to identify and capture data required from internal and external sources

  • Create and maintain statistical models for ongoing review and analysis of data

You’ll do it using your…

  • BS (Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Engineering, Computer Science, or similar) or BA (Psychology, Philosophy, Linguistics, or similar)

  • 3-5 years of experience utilizing quantitative research and analytics to drive significant business impact with some experience in increasing customer satisfaction, engagement, and product quality

  • Successful experience working with large datasets

  • Familiarity with Ruby and/or Rails

  • Familiarity with Jupyter Notebooks or RStudio

  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills

  • Solid understanding of experimentation, predictive analytics, and machine learning

  • Good algorithmic thinking, strong interpersonal skills

  • Strong ability to translate strategic mission objectives to an analytical framework and action items

  • Proficiency working with statistical tools (such as R, SAS), database query languages (SQL), and at least one scripting language (such as Python)

  • Prior experience presenting to and influencing audience of decision makers
