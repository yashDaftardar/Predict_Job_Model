Become a Superhero-In-Training
The Machine Learning Products and Services team is uniquely positioned to take a new product or service idea that utilizes the latest machine learning techniques from concept to deployment. We work directly with our data science team to build these products and services. These products are designed to protect our customers from the latest security threats and to save them money by streamlining their day to day operations.
Our projects change over time and so there is no guarantee that what we are working on today will still be our focus next summer. However, the current project would be to assist our senior engineers create a highly-scaled system that monitors potentially millions of email messages on a daily basis.

Your Superhero Profile
You have the ability and interest in working full time (40 hours/week) onsite in our Draper office .
