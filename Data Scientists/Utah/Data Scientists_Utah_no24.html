Job Purpose

We are looking for a VP of Data Science who thrives in a fast-paced environment and is ready for a challenge. You will be responsible for obtaining and defining business requirements, developing product roadmaps, achieving optimal design, ensuring effective prioritization, establishing OKRs, facilitating definition of business processes and attaining assurance of usability for end users as well as effective delivery of stakeholder requirements. As a VP of Data Science, you will be a data product leader, scientist with organizational leadership experience in the development of technology for data-driven management. You will be an integral part of the product team in managing the teams, coaching, training, and collaboration to ensure effective utilization of solutions.

What you’ll get to do…

  • Inspire and lead your team to deliver data and strategic and novel data science, and engineering approaches that help optimize and guide MX's decisions related to product.

  • Make a strategic impact at a senior level including interacting with product and company leadership on intricate issues and applying strong judgment and analysis toward important business decisions.

  • Participate heavily in MX’s product team: help set business strategy, represent content data science and engineering, initiate and debate key strategic topics and opportunities from your team’s work, and drive bold high-impact decisions.

  • Grow and expand your expert team with an eye toward diverse and forward-pushing skills that span machine learning methods, data engineering, tooling and revealing unknowns in financial data products, and analytics.

You’ll do it using your…

  • Successful experience in building, leading and guiding expert data science, analytics, and engineering teams comprising both leaders and individual contributors, and are adept at figuring out ways to help them flourish

  • 15 years of management experience in leading and developing data science teams

  • 10 years of data science, advanced analytics and machine learning experience

  • Strong verbal and written communication and leadership skills to collaborate cross-functionally and lead through influence across functional and organizational lines

  • MS or Ph.D. in Analytics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Statistics, or Machine Learning, etc.

  • High proficiency in Python, Spark, Scala, R, SQL, or MatLab

  • Broad technical background in data products and visualization, data and behavioral analytics, advanced metrics, statistics, machine learning, causal methods, and data engineering and architecture

  • Strong ability to solve complex problems in a fast-paced environment

  • Ability to improve the diversity of thought of the broader DS team through your background and/or experience

  • Proven expertise synthesizing and presenting research/technical findings to diverse audiences

  • Solid experience in the financial domain and familiarity with financial models

  • Acting with empathy and integrity
