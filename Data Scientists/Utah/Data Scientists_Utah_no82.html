Senior Machine Learning Scientist; Chemistry

(Senior or Principal Level Only)

Salt Lake City, Utah (remote options)

Salary: $150,000-$175,000

The Company:

This company operates in the biotech field and is an opportunity to use advanced machine learning to reveal advanced mechanisms of action, show potential toxicity, and the ultimate goal of decoding biology. Operating at the clinical stage, your contribution will lead to real-world results that have the ability to affect people globally.

Essential Requirements:

  • Domain knowledge in chemistry
  • Machine learning and deep learning experience
  • Ability to code and develop architectural solutions
  • Tools: Python
  • PhD in computer science, computational chemistry, or related field

Desirable Requirements:

  • Specific deep learning experience in GGNs, Transformers, CNNs
  • Pytorch, Pytorch geometric
  • Production level experience
  • Both small molecule/protein experience though priority on small molecule experience
  • Pharma Background

Responsibilities:

  • Produce an embedded space for chemical structures
  • Support collecting public and internal data
  • Predicting toxicities and properties of drugs and action of said drugs
  • Collaborating with computer vision scientists to modify the representations of biology and the biology of cells in images
  • Supporting computer vision and data scientists interpret what biology is happening in these cells
  • Ex. Turn the molecular structure to a vector to predict drug targets
  • Ex. Predict small molecule action to inhibit to catalyze a reaction

Soft Skills:

  • Bias to action
  • Growth mindset
  • Resilience
  • Ability to interface with colleagues outside of their own speciality
  • Goal-oriented
  • Understanding that output may not be published papers, but rather accomplishing company objectives
