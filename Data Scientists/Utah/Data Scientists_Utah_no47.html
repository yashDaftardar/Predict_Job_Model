Secure our Nation, Ignite your Future

ManTech is currently seeking experienced professionals for the Air Force Nuclear Weapons Center at Hill Air Force Base-Ogden, Utah. This will be supporting the Integration Support Contract (ISC) 2.0. Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) weapon systems.

As the Employer of Choice in the government services and solutions industry, ManTech seeks like minds who exhibit a sense of service, creativity and dedication. The ManTech family comprises a diverse yet united group of nearly 8,000 talented professionals around the globe, each bringing distinct backgrounds and skill sets to the team. Nearly half of us are veterans.

Your Opportunity:

Principal Mathematical Statistician

Required Experience:

Must be registered/certified or equivalent as appropriate for profession.

• Must possess working knowledge of commonly-used concepts, practices, and procedures within field of statistics
  • Expert experience in mathematical and statistical modeling.
  • Sound understanding of mathematical principles.
  • Analytical thinker with strong conceptual and problem-solving skills.
  • Meticulous attention to detail with the superb organizational skills.
  • Ability to work under pressure and meet tight deadlines.
  • Ability to work independently and/or as part of a team.
  • Excellent communication and IT skills.
  • Comfortable exercising a wide degree of creativity and latitude.
  • Principal skill set includes ability to perform all stated/implied duties within this tier and those reasonably derived from preceding tier

Responsibilities:

• Uses classical and modern statistical methods.
  • Analyzes and interprets data from various sources.
  • Compiles reports, charts, and tables based on established statistical methods.
  • Familiar with a variety of the field's concepts, practices, and procedures.
  • Relies on extensive experience and judgment to plan and accomplish goals.
  • Performs a variety of complex tasks similar to the following: post flight analyses of missile trajectories, using least squares and filtering techniques, error analyses of guidance hardware and software, reentry aerodynamics statistics, modeling and simulation, etc.
  • Performs statistical analysis for accuracy assessment on missile systems for Air Force ICBMs.
  • Collaborates with multi-disciplinary teams.
  • Relies on extensive experience and judgment to plan and accomplish goals.
  • Performs a variety of complicated tasks.
  • Leads and directs the work of others.
  • Reports to manager or head of unit/department.

Qualifications:

• BS degree in Statistics, Mathematics or a related quantitative field + 15 years of experience
OR
  • MS degree + 12 years of experience
OR
  • PHD + 9 years of experience

Must be able to remain in a stationary position 50%

The person in this position needs to occasionally move about inside the office to access file cabinets, office machinery, etc.

Constantly operates a computer and other office productivity machinery, such as a calculator, copy machine and computer printer.

Security Clearance Requirements:

• Ability to receive and maintain a Top-Secret Level Security Clearance
