Full Job Description

PREMIER Bankcard is looking for an individual to expand our analytical team and support Digital/ Self-Servicing applications (Desktop/Android/iOS etc.) for monitoring and improving the Customer Experience. This position will be responsible for creating performance reporting/dashboards, applying advanced statistical analytic techniques and tools, identifying data requirements, completing high level analytics, and make recommendations for improvement of PREMIER’s digital servicing platform(s) along with other self-service strategies.

Potential career pathing opportunities available. We support a promotion-from-within philosophy for strong performers!


Compensation and Schedule

Starting wage is determined based on prior work experiences and education.

Full-time Hours:
Monday - Friday 8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

  • Performs complex analyses for reporting that maximizes profits and/or asset growth and minimizes other risk exposures.
  • Expertise in working with data: cleaning data, building datasets, integrating with machine learning models, performing statistical analysis, data visualization and development of statistical models.
  • Engage in independent self-direction; must be able to fulfill the responsibilities of the position with little supervision; assesses priorities and changing needs and directs oneself accordingly
  • Must interpret links between data being pulled and the impact to the overall business.
  • Ensure analytic assumptions are accurate and based upon sound financial and/or statistical principles.
  • Seek out business opportunities and communicate strategies/proposals/solutions to key stakeholders
  • Research new and innovative analytical techniques in support of business initiatives.
  • Conduct tasks in a manner that complies with all consumer protection regulations and treats consumers fairly.

Skills and Qualifications

  • Experience with Power BI, SAS and/or other statistical modeling tools.
  • High level of inquisitiveness, initiative and critical thinking
  • Experience in a Financial Service industry with a focus on the customer experience, website/mobile applications, and other customer self-service systems.
  • Combination of education/experience (5+Years) that would enable incumbent to meet essential functions and required competencies of the position.
  • Excellent problem solver, disciplined attention to detail, great communicator.
  • Ability to retrieve, stage, and analyze massive and complex data housed in relational databases and formulate actionable conclusions.
  • Required Competencies include: PC skills, including competency with SQL, Excel, Word, and SAS.
  • Ability to handle stress, exercise self-control, adhere to company policies and procedures and uphold The PREMIER Way.
