DataRobot believes that automation of end-to-end data science workflows will transform not only the data analysis and business intelligence industries, but every industry. The increased accuracy and radically decreased costs that automated machine learning brings will allow every enterprise to be an AI-driven enterprise.

We are seeking an extremely talented Product Manager who is passionate about building solutions for Data Scientists in the world's leading enterprise AI platform. For this role in particular, we are looking for someone who also has proven experience in Data Science and who will take up the challenge to research, imagine, validate, and design world-class experiences for their Data Science peers.

We are looking for a candidate who is highly collaborative, always looking for ways to improve the solution, and capable of making quick and practical decisions under pressure, while remaining empathetic and passionate about your product area and the needs of customers. Above all, you are an effective communicator who is organized, articulate, confident, and polished in front of customers and decision makers. You thrive in a culture that values urgency and teamwork.

Product managers at DataRobot capture, synthesize, and communicate customer needs to engineering, while concurrently balancing strategic vision with a no-excuses emphasis on execution. Day to day, you will work with engineers and designers to define requirements and stories, with product marketers to clarify product positioning and messaging, and with users to make sure DataRobot continues to serve and delight them with each feature delivery. You will serve as the internal and external evangelist for the area of the product you are building, often working with the sales channel and key stakeholders. Ideal candidates enjoy data science, complex problem-solving, and high levels of stakeholder interaction.

Responsibilities

  • Maintain deep awareness of Data Scientist focused products available in the industry.
  • Create product positioning and journey maps for the Data Scientist role.
  • Validate urgent Data Scientist usability problems with customer interviews and user testing.
  • Deliver product requirements with prioritized features and corresponding justification.
  • Act as a thought leader within the company.
  • Apply creative ways to build features that are differentiated and compelling. You will write user stories, create wireframes, and work with UX Designers and engineers to iterate.
  • Ensure rapid, high-quality delivery of innovative capabilities to users.
  • Manage and prioritize product feedback to groom the backlog; manage the priorities of one or more distributed engineering teams to implement your roadmap.
  • Collaborate with other parts of the business (customer-facing data scientists, designers, engineers, technical writers, product marketers, sales, and other product managers) to build a strong business ecosystem around your product.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Statistics, Engineering, Physics, or a related technical field.
  • 2-3+ years of software product management experience, especially with a product-led growth organization.
  • 2+ years experience as a Data Scientist or role with specialized knowledge of Data Scientists.
  • Ability and eagerness to learn quickly.
  • Excellent teamwork and written/verbal communication skills.
  • Strong presentation skills and the ability to articulate complex concepts to cross-functional teams.
  • Track record of delivering valuable products and features with an extreme focus on customer experience.
  • Strong understanding of the ML tooling landscape/market and familiarity with a variety of tools and platforms.

Plus

  • Advanced degrees in Computer Science, Information Technology, Statistics, Engineering, Physics, or a related technical field, or MBA.
  • 5+ years of software product management experience.
  • Leadership experience in the Data Science community.
  • Hands-on experience building and implementing predictive models using machine learning algorithms, especially with R or Python.
  • Familiarity with a variety of technical tools for the manipulation of datasets.
  • Strong interpersonal communication skills and public speaking experience.
  • Experience in a fast-paced startup environment.
  • User experience design skills.
  • Experience in managing remote development teams.
  • Familiarity with Hadoop and related Big Data technologies.
