Abbott is a global healthcare leader that helps people live more fully at all stages of life. Senior Statistical Analyst I

Our nutrition business develops science-based nutrition products for people of all ages, from helping babies and children grow, to keeping adult bodies strong and active. Millions of people around the world count on our leading brands – including Similac®, PediaSure®, Pedialyte®, Ensure®, Glucerna® and ZonePerfect® – to help get the nutrients they need to live their healthiest life.

Our location in Columbus, OH, currently has an opportunity for a Senior Statistical Analyst I . In Research & Development (R&D) our ambition is providing innovative high-quality products, creating sustainable value, delivering superior user experience and enhancing health outcomes at all stages of life.

WHAT YOU’LL DO

  • Provides input to clinical study protocols, CRFs and edit check specifications
  • Collaborates with Statistician and Clinical Scientist to write analysis data set and table specifications
  • Programs statistical , analysis datasets, tables, figures, listings in line with clinical study protocol and statistical analysis plan using an appropriate statistical analysis system (i.e. SAS®)
  • Ensures the accuracy and consistency of data flow from case report forms (CRFs) and database definitions, through to specifications for analysis datasets
  • Performs complex data checking for Data Management group
  • Programs required data listings, tables and Key Risk Indicator output used by Clinical Study team members for Risk Based Monitoring
  • Programs adverse event data listings and tables used by Medical Safety group for routine safety reviews
  • Performs validation of analysis datasets and tables developed by other programmers
  • Modifies or creates new project specific SAS macros to aid in programming efficiency
  • Develops new processes and ensures all process improvements are implemented
  • Collaborates with other team members to develop quality reports for publications and regulatory submissions
  • May lead a small programming team with effective communication skills.

EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE, YOU’LL BRING

Required

  • BS in a Mathematical (i.e. Applied Math, Engineering, etc.), Statistical, Computer Science or Life Science subject
  • Minimum of 5 years of statistical programming experience desired

Preferred

  • Proficiency in SAS programming required
  • Excellent problem-solving skills
  • Diligence – attention to detail and ability to manage concurrent projects and activities
  • Must be able to work independently as well as part of a team
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills and ability to influence stakeholders
  • Nice to have knowledge of CDSIC standards and industry best practices
  • Knowledge of R programming a plus.
