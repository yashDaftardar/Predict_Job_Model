Our Columbus, Ohio client has a contract need for a Cloud Data Analytics and ML Engineer with in depth understanding of principles around data integration, database design, operational and master data management. Candidate should have solid experience in implementing and supporting large Data Warehousing systems.

Qualifications:

  • Experience with Google Big Data Services like Big Query, and Looker is a must
  • Experience with building Data Analytics and Data Warehouse Solutions is a must
  • 8+ years of overall experience in building Data Analytics and warehousing solutions.
  • 1 Year of experience with GCP Services, certification desired
  • Education: BS in computer or related field.
  • Strong Data Analysis background and visualization skills are important
  • Scripting technologies (PowerShell, & Python)
  • Experience building Automations in a cloud environment
  • SDLC and Continuous Integration and Continuous Deployment concepts
  • Detail oriented with excellent documentation skills/methodologies, who is able to successfully manage multiple priorities
  • Ability to learn new Machine learning technologies and apply to support project deliverables
  • Ability to work with minimal supervision, making decisions based upon priorities, schedules and an understanding of business initiatives
  • Ability to apply critical thinking to all aspects of the position

Preferred:

  • Experience with Cloud Cost Optimization is preferred
  • Experience with Google Services like Composer and DataPrep is a plus
  • Experience with Google ML Services like AI Platform, Big Query ML is a plus
  • Enterprise with Data Ingestion technologies (CDC, File and Batch ingestions) is a plus
  • Databases & Google BigQuery experience is preferred

Client does not provide sponsorship for this opening. Candidates must now and in the future be legally eligible to work in the US for any employer.
