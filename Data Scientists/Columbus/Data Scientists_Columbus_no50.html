Full Job Description

Be part of the team that's poised to transform the fight against cancer. Backed by the strength of a Fortune 8 company, our entrepreneurial organization develops technologies used by the oncology community to deliver evidence-based, personalized care, as well as insights used by biopharma companies to accelerate drug development and support the entire treatment journey. Our work powers informed decision-making at every pivotal moment in oncology - from the treatment options presented to patients, to the operational considerations for oncology practices, to the design of clinical trials, to the commercial launch plans for new therapies.

United by ourICARE values, our 78,000 employees work together every day to make better care possible for patients around the globe.

Job Description:
  • As a Senior Health Information Scientist at Ontada, you will design information systems that power analytics and data science to guide the delivery of precision cancer care
  • Organize data across contexts to identify opportunities for improvement
  • Own the conceptual and logical model for disparate forms of data
  • Lead the marriage of operational, clinical, laboratory and other data structures
  • Advocate for data quality and completeness across data formats
  • Produce data model and implementation policy documentation
  • Design

Minimum Qualifications:
  • 4+ years experience in clinical informatics, bioinformatics, clinical data science, or clinical data engineering
  • Proficiency in at least one programming language
  • Proficiency with data management systems, relational or otherwise (SQL / NoSQL)
  • Solid experience working with at least one interoperability standard FHIR, HL7v2, OHDSI OMOP
  • Strong understanding of the role of controlled vocabularies RxNorm, SNOMED, LOINC, etc. in clinical information systems

Critical Skills:
  • Familiarity with FHIR
  • Experience employing controlled vocabularies and ontologies to standardize health information
  • Significant Experience with procedural, functional, or object oriented programming
  • Some experience with command line tools and source control

Additional Skills:
  • MS or PhD in a directly relevant field
  • Experience implementing a FHIR-based data model or FHIR server
  • 1+ years of experience with software development process

Education:
  • Bachelor's degree in a directly relevant field or bachelor's degree in an unrelated field with substantial experience in informatics or data science
