Full Job Description

The Marketing Analytics Data Scientist is instrumental in the development of models and advanced analyses to drive measurable, profitable decisions across Safelite's marketing activities and digital properties. This position combines disparate data from multiple internal and external sources, determines the best model to address the business question at hand, creates actionable insights, and contributes to accurate holistic understanding of the customers' interaction with Safelite.

Essential Activities:

  • Apply sophisticated computational, statistical, and modeling techniques in support of decision-making and to drive KPI improvement; techniques include A/B and multivariate testing, predictive analysis, data mining, marketing mix modeling, attribution, incrementality, acquisition and churn analysis.
  • Evaluate and recommend innovative new opportunities and methodologies that segment and target audience in new ways and/or uncover new insights about the effectiveness of advertising to our audience by partnering with internal and external teams
  • Design and execute appropriate data models by compiling data from multiple sources
  • Serve as consultant to team mebers on statistical priciples
  • Other duties as assigned by management

Requirements:

  • 3 - 5 Years Related Experience
  • Bachelors Degree in Statistics, analytics or related quantitative field
  • Understanding of statistical principals
  • Experience with modeling for practical business execution
  • Familiarity with and understanding of digital marketing, digital media and technologies a plus
  • Strong problem-solving skills, detail oriented and ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously required
  • Computer Skills: Word, Excel, PowerPoint
  • Ability to write SQL and experience using analytics software such as R, Python, SAS.
