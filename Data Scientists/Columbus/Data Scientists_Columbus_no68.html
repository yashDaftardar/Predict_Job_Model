Job Summary

The Credit Analytics team supports Credit Management through advanced analytics and Research & Development (R&D) efforts that deliver value-adding predictive models, insights and data to underwriting, fraud, account management, and collections strategies.

The Sr. Data Scientist, Credit Analytics applies data science in advanced analytics such as predictive modeling and data mining in a big data environment in order to deliver predictive models and insights. The Sr. Data Scientist works with Credit Management to understand the philosophy and strategies utilized in underwriting, fraud, account management, and collections, and applies this knowledge to the analytical projects. The Sr. Data Scientist delivers actionable solutions that increase profitability, encompass the different product types and channels, and maintain regulatory standards. The Sr. Data Scientist ensures accurate and timely implementation of solutions and communicates results.

Job Description

Essential Job Functions

Analytics – independently completes the following: 1) extracts and samples data, conducts data integrity checks and applicable data pre-processing such as treatment of missing values and outliers, 2) conducts exploratory data analysis for preliminary data insights to drive the selection of modeling approach that best addresses the business problem, 3) reveals hidden data patterns by data mining using unsupervised learning techniques such as clustering analysis and factor analysis, 4) conducts feature engineering to create/derive model predictors with strong predictive power, 5) trains/tunes classification/regression models by applying supervised learning techniques such as generalized linear models assuming applicable underlying distributions such as logit and gamma, tree-based models such as decision trees, random forest, boosted trees, etc., and neural net models, and 6) conducts proper model test/validation, diagnoses and fixes model issues (e.g., over-fitting) when applicable. Sizes the impact of using the models in production as part of the current strategy. Presents results and business case to manager. Provides support for implementation and monitoring of solutions that are implemented.

Data Science innovation – independently researches industry trends in data science of new tools, emerging algorithms, advanced platforms, and alternative data to enhance modeling effectiveness and efficiency. Conducts use case testing for new tools/techniques/platforms/data and provides user input/feedback.

Model risk management - possesses knowledge on common model risks and related regulatory requirements; applies proper 1 st line of defense controls during model development process to minimize model risk; creates comprehensive model governance documentation and archives model data, scripts, and results; works with Compliance and bank officers for approvals of new models; collaborates with model risk management partners to complete model validation/auditing; completes remediation as required by model governance process.

Collaboration – collaborates with Credit Management to understand current strategies, philosophy and goals; utilizes this knowledge to propose, design and plan analyses. Translates analytical results into useful recommendations for review with Manager. Demonstrates strong communication skills when presenting results to various audiences. Collaborates with other data scientists in the company to share best practices and data science innovations. Demonstrates foundational knowledge of credit card operations, banking, financial, loyalty rewards, retail, and credit card regulations while working with the business. Meets critical deadlines established by manager by prioritizing multiple activities and complex projects simultaneously.
