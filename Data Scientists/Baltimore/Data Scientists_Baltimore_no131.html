Full Job Description

On one of ClearEdge's prime contracts, works on a highly visible system performing post-dissemination document parsing. Government leadership considers the project the crown jewel of the organization. This small team performs agile development using Java and JavaScript (Bootstrap, jQuery, Angular) development. The team incorporates new data sets, performs analytic development, and integrates machine learning technologies. Although each software engineer on the team takes ownership of certain capabilities, there is a lot of cross-pollination that occurs on the project. This position requires a close working relationship with Government analysts, data scientists, and leadership, so we are looking with someone who has great customer relationship skills.

Required Skills

  • Shall have at least seven (7) years of software engineering experience, including three (3) years development using Agile development model.
  • Shall have at least two (2) years of domain experience within the SIGINT mission environment, with an understanding of customer data formats.
  • Shall have at least three (3) years of multi-threaded Java design, development, test, and documentation.
  • Shall have at least one (1) year of experience using IDEs (Eclipse, Netbeans, IntelliJ).
  • Shall have at least one (1) year of experience using configuration management/versioning tools (CVS, SVN, Git)
  • Shall have at least one (1) year of experience using modern UI development tools (AngularJS, JQuery, Javascript)
  • Strong analysis experience highly desired
  • Basic understanding of cloud development fundamentals
  • Experience using AngularJS in the client environment
  • Experience with Docker
