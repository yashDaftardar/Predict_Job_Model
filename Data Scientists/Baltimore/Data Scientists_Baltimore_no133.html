Position Description

Are you passionate about acoustic anti-submarine warfare?

Do you want to work on impactful projects which set new standards of performance?

If so, Signal Systems Corporation wants you on our team.

We are searching for inspired signal processing engineers to develop the next generation of sonar systems, bringing together ideas from the signal processing, physics-based modeling, Bayesian statistics and machine learning domains. When you are on our team, you will be a vital part of maintaining and building our country's technological edge, and that's a role that brings pride to our organization. The team at SSC cultivates, demonstrates and renews its domain expertise in underwater acoustics. We are driven to produce state-of-the-art signal processing, modeling, and tracking research which transition into products that improves our country's war-fighting capability. Successful members of the SSC team come from a variety of backgrounds, bringing their unique talents to bear on challenging problems. At SSC, we believe in developing our talent, collaborating throughout the company, and constantly seeking the next major breakthrough.

As a senior member of SSC's technical staff:

* Your primary responsibility will be developing, analyzing and testing signal processing and tracking algorithms.
* You will make use of models to design and test new systems and algorithms.
* Your will present your results and discuss them with the whole SSC team, as well as with government partners and the wider technical community.
* You will collaborate with engineers from other organizations to support testing and integration of both SSC products and the products of other organizations.
* You will be responsible for the success of technical projects and tasks from conception through the engineering process including design, testing, reporting and alignment with customer objectives.

Qualifications
The minimum qualifications SSC is looking for are:

- A graduate degree in a relevant field (e.g. Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Engineering Physics, Statistics, Applied Mathematics)
- At least five years of experience with underwater acoustics, signal processing, and sonar system development
- Highly skilled in MATLAB
- Takes pride in their written and oral communications
- Comfortable working in closed areas and being mindful of customer and company data security.
- This position requires the ability to obtain and hold a US Government security clearance. Eligibility requirements include US citizenship.

You will really impress us if you:
- Are knowledgeable of underwater acoustic propagation models
- Have an expert understanding of Bayesian statistics and tracking
- Are a formidable C++ programmer
- Can design and build state-of-the-art machine learning and deep learning algorithms

Why SSC?
Signal Systems Corporation is a top research and development small business which brings together highly talented individuals to develop solutions to the most pressing challenges in underwater acoustics. In addition to our focus on technical excellence, our company culture is why SSC employees stay. Our generous benefits, schedule flexibility, collegial relationships among staff, strong retirement program and dedication to supporting employees to find and grow into their niche within the company are important components of making SSC an excellent place to work. Our location in Central Maryland lies at the intersection of the Baltimore and Washington metropolitan regions, close to historic Annapolis and within commuting distance of Maryland's Eastern Shore.
