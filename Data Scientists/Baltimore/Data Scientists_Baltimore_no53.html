General Position Summary:
The Senior Data Scientist reports into the Director of Supply Chain Analytics at Johns Hopkins Health System (JHHS). This position is part of an Analytics team that is flexibly deployed to support strategic supply chain initiatives. The analysts are cross-trained and can contribute to these strategic initiatives through a range of activities, including, but not limited to, supply chain data analytics, financial analysis, capital analysis, contract review and analysis, and project management and implementation. The Senior Data Scientist will play a critical role in enabling data-driven analytics to support Corporate Supply Chain, acute and non-acute hospital, warehousing, and logistics decisions and operational improvements. Individuals in this role will expected to facilitate collaboration across Supply Chain and other hospital network stakeholders to support transformational and ongoing sourcing, value analysis, procurement, and inventory management initiatives.

The Senior Data Scientist will be tasked with complex collection, cleansing, visualization and interpretation of supply chain data to inform business intelligence, drive strategic supply chain decisions, and ensure effective use of technology. He or she will help establish analytics as a core competency in the Supply Chain organization by designing and implementing advanced master data management, AI, machine learning, visualization, and other mature analytics capabilities. The Senior Data Scientist will lead cross-training initiatives in the Analytics team, standardize analytics processes with the Analytics and Supply Chain teams, and execute other tasks as assigned.

BUSINESS REQUIREMENTS

1. Leads discovery processes with JHHS stakeholders to identify the business requirements and the expected outcome.

2. Works with and alongside business analysts by suggesting other products of interest to stakeholders.

3. Models and frames business scenarios that are meaningful and impact critical business processes and/or decisions.

ANALYSIS

1. Develops innovative and effective approaches to solve analytics problems and communicates results and methodologies.

2. Works in iterative processes with stakeholders and validates findings.

3. Develops experimental design approaches to validate finding or test hypotheses.

4. Validates analysis using scenario modeling.

5. Identifies/creates the appropriate algorithm to discover patterns.

POLICIES, STANDARDS AND PROCEDURES

1. Develops usage and access control policies and systems in collaboration with the data steward.

2. Partners with the data stewards in continuous improvement processes impacting data quality in the context of the specific use case.

3. Recommends ongoing improvements to methods and algorithms that lead to findings, including new information.

METRICS

1. Provides business metrics for the overall project to show improvements (contribution to the improvement should be monitored initially and over multiple iterations).

2. Demonstrates the following scientist qualities: clarity, accuracy, precision, relevance, depth, breadth, logic, significance, and fairness.

Required Education:
Masters in mathematics, statistics or business analytics or related field; PHD degree preferred.

Required Experience:
Five or more years of relevant quantitative and qualitative research and analytics experience; experience in a supply chain and healthcare environment preferred.
