Job Description


The FireEye Data Science team develops innovative, data-driven solutions. We work across the company, partnering with the best security experts in the industry, to understand hard problems and deliver impactful solutions. FireEye’s comprehensive view of the advanced threat landscape provides a unique opportunity for the team to have major impact within our industry, company, and across our customer base.

As a Machine Learning Engineer on the Data Science team, you will design and implement software to support data science projects. That includes backend processing, data ingestion systems, model training and evaluation pipelines, and deploying models to different environments. Part of your responsibilities are to automate the model retraining process so that production models can be easily maintained. You’ll be in a fast-paced environment working as part of a team that strives to produce quality deliverables in a timely manner. If you are someone who wants to be on the cutting edge of a high-profile industry, who wants to make an impact by pushing both fields of data science and cyber security forward, you may be the perfect candidate to help us on our mission.


What you will do:

  • Develop, and manage data systems to ingest and process data at massive scale
  • Contribute towards the architecture and design of software solutions for long-term storage and retrieval, including managing large-scale datasets
  • Work with data scientists to productize ML models and assist product teams with ML model releases into end products
  • Collect requirements, design, and build backend components and tools to serve ML models, receive feedback, and generate model statistics
  • Create automation to support ML model retraining and maintenance
  • Work with data scientists to develop tools that identify problems during model training and evaluation
  • Contribute to the evolution of coding and design practices within the organization, implementing ETL processes and building data pipelines
  • Review code base commits and contribute to the growth of team members

Qualifications


Requirements:

  • Ability to work in an iterative, agile development environment
  • Strong experience developing in Python 3
  • Experience developing reliable distributed systems
  • Deep knowledge of AWS services and associated tools
  • Strong knowledge of a variety of operating systems, networking fundamentals, software design and programming best practices
  • Experience using a wide array of algorithms and data structures
  • Deep understanding and experience of going through the entire life cycle of building software platforms and products
  • Experience in schema design
  • Experience developing in at least one of the following: Java (preferred), Go or C
  • Experience with RDBMS, such as PostgreSQL or MySQL, as well as NoSQL
  • Experience with Kubernetes and Docker
  • Experience deploying products in AWS


Additional Qualification:

  • Ability to pick up, work with and explore new analytical tools
  • Strong experience with DevOps practices and common tooling
  • Understanding of machine learning concepts, especially with regard to metrics and model evaluation
  • Ability to work with loosely defined requirements
