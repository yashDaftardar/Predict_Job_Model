If so, we are looking for someone like you to join our team at APL.
Our team is looking to provide our country with ground-breaking capabilities in machine perception and reasoning. To succeed, we need talented engineers, scientists, and developers. We are looking for individuals with curiosity and a strong drive for learning! The Tactical Intelligence Systems Group (QAT) of the Asymmetric Operations Sector is looking for a research scientist to lead the Office of the Secretary of Defense, Intelligence (OUSD(I)) to lead test and evaluation for the organization, applying critical solutions for challenges in data science and artificial intelligence. This may involve: - Surveying academic research to engineer practical solutions to challenges in computer vision, sensor fusion, cognitive systems, and predictive analysis
  • Understanding large data sets and using statistical analyses, reliability analyses, modeling and simulation to develop state of the art metrics for Artificial Intelligence systems.
  • Collaborating with Laboratory, for-profit contractor and sponsor teams to address critical sponsor needs
  • Effectively communicating results with non-expert audiences and generating innovative ideas to benefit the country
As an AI Test and Evaluation Scientist...
  • Your primary responsibility will be to apply knowledge in quantitative analysis, machine learning, statistical modeling, and software development to data analysis problems for our sponsors.
  • You will work independently and on teams to engineer automated testing solutions, and lead large scale DoD Test and Evaluation events.
  • You will explore promising research and maintain/gain the technical edge required for projects, as well as share and develop new approaches and methods.
  • You will collaborate to document and support software analytics, and clearly present status and results to internal and external stakeholders.
You meet our minimum qualifications for the job if you...
  • Have a PhD in Engineering, Statistics, Mathematics, or related technical field.
  • Can demonstrate expertise in at least one of the following areas: computer vision, trust in autonomous systems, natural language processing and translation, knowledge representation and reasoning with evidence.
  • Have demonstrated programming experience, as well as basic knowledge of statistics and probability.
  • Are able to obtain a TS/SCI level security clearance. If selected, you will be subject to a government security clearance investigation and must meet the requirements for access to classified information. Eligibility requirements include U.S. citizenship.
You'll go above and beyond our minimum requirements if you...
  • Have two or more years of relevant Test & Evaluation experience.
  • Understand common machine learning algorithms and methods used to parse and analyze data.
  • Have the ability to rapidly prototype and possess expertise in programming languages such as C#, C++, and Python.
  • Are experienced with team-based development of software products and have the ability to lead development/research projects.
  • Possess experience with machine learning libraries such as Caffe, Tensorflow, MXNet, CNTK, and scikit-learn.
  • Are familiar with modern databases and parallel computation.
  • Hold an active Department of Defense Top Secret/SCI security clearance.
Why work at APL?
