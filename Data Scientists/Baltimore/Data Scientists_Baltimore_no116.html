Full Job Description

Booth Management Consulting, LLC (BMC) is a diverse CPA firm providing accounting, auditing, and management consulting services to public and private clients nationwide. With an employee-friendly company environment that emphasizes quality of life, initiative, creativity, strong work ethics, and loyalty, we are seeking qualified, motivated, and progressive individual to join our team as a STATISTICIAN (AUDITS) to work with our government client.


Overview

The objective of this task order is to evaluate the Coronavirus Relief Fund (CRF) prime recipient’s documentation supporting the uses of CRF proceeds as reported in GrantSolutions, and assess risk of unallowable use of funds. As part of the desk reviews, other publicly available information, such as Single Audit or other State Auditor reports, will be used to identify internal control matters that may pose risk or impact the prime recipient’s appropriate use of CRF proceeds. The desk review may result in a site visit to the prime recipient for a more in-depth review or a recommendation for audit. This engagement will require the use of a statistician to perform sampling as it relates to the audits and program evaluation.


Qualifications & Education


  • At least 6 years of current significant sampling experience in a government audit or program evaluation environment
  • BS degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Data Analytics, Data Science, Computer Science, or related field of study; advanced degree is a plus
  • Demonstrated knowledge and experience in the ability to design samples that meet GAGAS and applicable AICPA sampling auditing standards
  • Experience working on audits, with accounting firms on statistical samples preferred
  • Experience in conducting general audits in accordance with applicable audit requirements/guidance, preferably Single Audit Act audits
  • Experience in conducting other Federal financial statement audits
  • CPA, CISA, CISSP, CIA, CGAP, and other relevant certifications and credentials preferred
  • Be a current member in a professional statistical association


  • This position is remote until further notice
