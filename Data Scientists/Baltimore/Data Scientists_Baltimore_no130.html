Data Scientist 3
Orbis Operations is seeking a Data Scientist 3 to support its government customer in Annapolis Junction, MD. The successful candidate willl work as the lead Data Scientist working with the mission customer to perform Machine Learning exploratory data analysis on raw data to clean, enrich, transform and convert the raw data into the required format to solve a wide-breadth of CASA problems. Additional tasking includes executing Machine Learning analytical experiments and implementing analytical models into production by collaborating with software developers and machine learning engineers. Must have excellent communication skills and the ability to communicate analytic solutions to stakeholders and implement improvements as needed to operational systems.

Required Clearance: Active Security Clearance required at the Top Secret (TS)/Special Compartmented Information (SCI) level and appropriate level Polygraph
Task Required Skills:
  • Experience using the Unix command line
  • Experience writing scripts using Bash
  • Experience developing with multiple programming languages such as C++ and Python in a Unix computing environment
  • Experience with various Machine Learning tools, packages, and libraries such as TensorFlow
  • Experience with Jupyter Notebooks
  • Experience designing data modeling processes to create algorithms and predictive models to perform custom analysis
  • Experience processing and manipulating Big Data to identify trends and determine meaningful solutions
  • Experience with SQL technologies such as PostgreSQL and MySQL
  • Experience with noSQL technologies such as MongoDB and Elasticsearch
Task Desired Skills:
  • Familiar with containerization technologies such as Docker
  • Familiar with container orchestration technologies such as Kubernetes
  • Familiar with KubeFlow
  • Experience with the Atlassian Suite of Tools (JIRA, Confluence)
  • Experience with Git
Position Requirements:
  • Master's degrees in Computer Science or related discipline from and accredited college or university plus 5 years of experience
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related discipline from an accredited college plus 7 years of experience
  • 9 years of related experience
