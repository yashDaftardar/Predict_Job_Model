Full Job Description

Geon Technologies is seeking an experienced and motivated Machine Learning Engineer to lead efforts applying ML and signal processing to a variety of problem sets spanning RF, communications and image processing (EO and IR). Geon Technologies is actively exploring and creating solutions for multiple domains: RF signal detection, modulation characterization, spectrogram analysis with image processing techniques, and image processing for EO/IR payloads. End solutions may be cloud based, edge based (with little or no connectivity), or some combination (particularly with respect to training). The ideal candidate will have experience with several ML models including data driven, unsupervised models that can extract features from extensive datasets (such as a library of unlabeled RF signals).

Responsibilities:

  • Analyze and choose ML strategies and algorithms to solve signal processing and RF problems for software defined radio and signal analysis solutions, both at the edge and in the cloud
  • Define validation strategies for chosen ML algorithms and solutions
  • Define preprocessing or feature engineering for datasets and define data augmentation pipelines
  • Train models and tune their hyperparameters
  • Analyze models and their performance; then design strategies to overcome limitations and errors
  • Compress and deploy trained models onto edge hardware for use in operational environments with little or no connectivity to external networks
  • Research and establish innovative ideas to support proposals and internal research and development
  • Assist with proposal writing and drafting research and development plans
  • Mentor staff with junior ML skillsets

Required Skills:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Mathematics or a related field
  • Proficiency with a deep learning frameworks such as TensorFlow or Keras
  • Proficiency with Python and basic libraries for machine learning such as scikit-learn and pandas
  • Familiarity with image processing and related models
  • Knowledge of statistics, statistical analysis and algorithms
  • Ability to code in C++
  • Familiarity with Linux
  • Ability to obtain a clearance

Desired Skills

  • Strong digital signal processing background is a plus
  • Matlab/Octave experience is a plus
  • Experience with RF, software defined radios and/or spectrum analysis is a very strong plus
  • Experience with GNU Radio and/or REDHAWK is a plus
  • Experience with OpenCV is a plus
  • Experience with edge hardware such as the NVIDIA Jetson is a plus
  • Master’s degree in Computer Science, Mathematics or a related field
  • TS/SCI clearance with Polygraph
