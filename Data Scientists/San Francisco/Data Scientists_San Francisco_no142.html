WHAT IS CARBON ALL ABOUT?

Carbon Health provides primary and urgent care for the family and has been working tirelessly to make COVID-19 testing, care and vaccinations available across the country. In its mission to make world-class healthcare accessible to all, Carbon Health leverages its unique technology platform to meet patients where they are - literally - with seamless, personal omnichannel care. With a growing team of expert providers, engineers, designers, and more, Carbon Health pushes the boundaries to deliver personal, accessible and high-quality care.

The Job at a Glance

At Carbon Health ou Data Science organization builds state-of-the-art data products to improve health outcomes, increase patient and clinician satisfaction, and reduce operational costs. Further, the team defines high-quality metrics and insights readily available to all decision-makers at the company.

To achieve our goals, we collaborate closely with our colleagues in Engineering, Product, Design, Support, Clinic Ops, Partnerships, Real Estate, Billing, Finance, and Marketing. As a Senior Data Analyst at Carbon Health, you will define and deliver high-impact data products, drive actions by providing visibility into the state of care and operations, and implement reliable data processing and interpretation practices.

What You'll Do

    • Take ownership of data for a product area or a business category. Review and refine the data pipeline. Define key metrics and trends that matter.
    • Provide well-designed dashboards with drill-down capabilities to show more granular operational metrics while providing key insights. Continue to evolve the dashboard as our business changes
    • Identify drivers of key metrics through data analytics and partner with product and operational teams to improve those metrics.
    • Identify opportunities to drive better patient and business outcomes and partner with a data scientist to build and deploy data science models to support operational efficiency or new initiatives
    • Collaborate with stakeholders to understand their needs to ensure alignment on the development and delivery of data analytics solutions
    • Communicate results of analyses in a clear and actionable way to stakeholders
    • Support the hiring, mentorship, and retention of Data Analysts at the company

About You

    • 3 to 5+ years experience a high-growth tech company as Data Analyst
    • A proven problem solver who can quickly assess quantitative data sets and distill key insights using analytics and statistical methods
    • Familiar with data analytics platforms such as Periscope, Tableau, or Looker
    • Proficient in SQL
    • Ability to work on cross-functional projects and communicate with stakeholders at multiple levels of technical detail
    • Communication skills that can influence across organizations and at all levels
    • BS or MS in a quantitative field (statistics, mathematics, economics, finance, operations research, etc.)

Perks

    • Forward-thinking, transparent, and inclusive company culture
    • Collaborative environment within and across our clinic sites and at HQ in San Francisco
    • Pre-tax benefits including Commuter (transit & parking), FSA Medical and FSA Dependent Care
    • 401K with matching contribution
    • Competitive compensation with medical, dental, vision, 401K (with match) and other great benefits for full time & part time employees who work 20+ hours a week
