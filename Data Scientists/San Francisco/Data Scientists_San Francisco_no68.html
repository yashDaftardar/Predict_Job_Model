About The Team:

Our IT team’s mission is to push the boundaries of technology with the intention of going above and beyond to aid stores and customers and deliver timely solutions to benefit all members of Grocery Outlet. Our team consists of problem solvers and go-getters who are dedicated to being service-oriented and solving important problems.


About The Role:

As a part of the Data Science and Analytics team, The Senior Data Analyst will work closely with the business to derive actionable insights from raw data. In addition, the Senior Data Analyst will be using cutting edge technologies to clean and perfect the data to be used in Machine Learning and Data Migration projects. The Senior Data Analyst will also work closely with the Data Science team to derive features to be used in Forecasting, Predictive and Prescriptive analytics. The Senior Data Analyst will understand the lifecycle of data in the retail environment, from the creation to ingestion of it by users through reporting, dashboards, and visualizations.


Responsibilities Include:

  • Advanced data analysis using multiple sources of raw data to gain insights in a timely manner to the business
  • Works directly with business users to fulfill Ad-Hoc data reporting requests
  • Analyzes raw data and creates metrics and KPI’s based on business requests
  • Identify patterns and trends in data sets
  • Help to define new data collection and analysis processes
  • Clean and transform data into actionable insights
  • Assist in Ad-Hoc reporting for business requests
  • Data validation for new reporting and dashboards
  • Analyze requests for new metrics and KPI’s
  • Assist the Data Science team in curating and selecting features for building Machine Learning models

About You:

  • Ability to analyze, model and interpret data
  • A high level of mathematical and statistical ability
  • Advanced problem-solving critical thinking skills
  • Outstanding communicator
  • Advanced Excel skills including Visualizations, Pivot Tables, Data Validation
  • Python including libraries scikit-learn, keras, matplotlib, scipy, Seaborn, Tensorflow, NLTK
  • Understanding of ETL Processes and data pipelines around big data
  • Strong understanding of SQL (SQL Server, DB2, AWS Redshift)
