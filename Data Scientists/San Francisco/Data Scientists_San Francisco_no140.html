We are currently seeking a Statistical Data Scientist for our Data Sciences Practice in Menlo Park, CA. In this role, you will work as part of a team to rapidly develop data driven analysis and visualization solutions to support clients in a variety of industries including consumer electronics, utilities, and life sciences.

You will be responsible for:

  • Serving as an expert in data analytics able to use techniques for exploratory data analysis, statistical inference, and machine learning as appropriate
  • Providing support to internal and external clients when planning data collection, including framing the problem, experimental design, sample size calculations, and identifying appropriate populations and sampling strategies for data collection
  • Developing reports and visualizations to address clientsâ€™ scientific and engineering problems and to support litigation

You will have the following skills and qualifications:

  • Ph.D. in Statistics, Computer Science, or a related engineering/scientific field (such as Applied Mathematics, Electrical Engineering, Bioinformatics or Computational Biology)
  • Demonstrated experience and expertise in all of the following areas:
    • Statistical Analysis
    • Supervised Machine Learning
    • Experimental Design
    • Data Visualization
  • At least two years of experience in performing data analysis with either R or Python
  • At least two years of experience in applying statistics or machine learning methods to real data
  • Proficiency with any of the following is preferred: C/C++, SQL, Linux
  • Experience with any of the following is preferred: software development, R Shiny or equivalent dashboard development toolkits, open source ML packages such as scikit-learn and TensorFlow, public cloud computing platforms such as AWS and Azure, Apache Spark
  • The desire to work with a diverse set of clients and engage in data science work supporting a variety of subject areas
  • Strong practical data analysis ability combined with leadership and project management skills
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Ability to work independently and in multidisciplinary teams
