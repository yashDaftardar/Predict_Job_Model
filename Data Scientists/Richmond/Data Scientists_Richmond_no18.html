Description

  • Perform sophisticated data analytics encompassing data mining, inferential statistical analysis, and predictive analytics.
  • Identify actionable insights from various or multiple sources of data that measurably improve business outcomes or reduce business risk.
  • Collect and prepare data for analysis, performs exploratory to advanced predictive and/or modeling analytics, and identify data relationships, patterns and trends.
  • Provide consultation to business leaders and other stakeholders on how to leverage analytic insights to build actionable strategies.

Requirements

  • Must have a Bachelorâ€™s degree in a quantitative field such as Statistics, Economics, Mathematics, Analytics, or related quantitative field.
  • Must have 3 years of experience in Analyst positions performing/utilizing the following:
  • Solid understanding of principles, practices, theories, and/or methodologies associated with analytics to include encompassing data mining, inferential statistical analysis, and predictive analytics.
  • Experience with large databases and related querying tools, including SAS, SQL, Toad, and Tableau, to perform analysis functions up to and including machine learning.
  • Demonstrated knowledge of fundamental concepts, practices, and procedures related to data analysis and data integrity.
  • Developing and maintaining documented processes and procedures to ensure data integrity and accuracy.
  • Using exceptional detailed data analysis skills, problem solving, and communication skills.
  • Utilizing SAS Enterprise Guide, SAS Enterprise Miner, Machine Learning Algorithms including Python and R, SQL, and MS Excel.
