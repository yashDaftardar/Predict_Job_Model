About the Opportunity

The IT Strategy & Planning Department has an immediate opening for a Data scientist, reporting to a Sr. Manager.

As a data scientist on this team, this individual will build data analysis platforms and models to support the operational teams and process owners in tracking and improving IT operations and business outcomes. The data scientist will work with the teams to understand their requirements for data analysis to drive process and service improvement across all IT products in support of IT strategy and planning. They will work to understand what information is available in the current data sources, discover new data sources and make recommendations for new data gathering, if appropriate. They will then implement and automate the required data extraction and manipulation (cleaning, merging and structuring). This individual will create visualizations in dashboards or presentations. The visualizations will range from day-to-day team management to executive level overviews but all visualizations will be focused on driving business objectives. In addition to visualizations, this individual will build statistical and machine learning models to generate insights in support of IT operational metrics, IT workforce planning and analytics, and other critical areas.

What You Will Do:

  • Use AI, Machine Learning, statistical machine learning models, and other advanced analytic techniques to help IT operational teams provide better business outcomes.
  • Investigate available data sources for relevant information. If necessary, make recommendations for updates for processes for improved data gathering.
  • Create data visualizations for dashboards and executive presentations.
  • Create automated systems to connect to data sources, extract, transform and structure data sources.
  • Execute ad hoc data investigation as part of requirements gathering or for one off requests.
  • Utilizes available tools for automation of manual processes.
  • Work with a broader team operating under SAFe principles.
  • Performs other related duties as required.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree
  • 5+ years of experience
  • Data Visualization: Able to present complex data sets for a variety of audiences and uses including executive presentations, operational dashboards, and structured data for interactive investigation.
  • Statistical and Machine Learning Modeling: Understanding of Machine Learning modeling techniques and how they are applied to different use cases. Experience building models in either R or Python.
  • Data Manipulation: Able to perform cleaning, transforming and structuring of multiple disparate data sources using tools such as Python or R. Able to execute this transformation both in an ad hoc and automated manner.
  • Technical Skills: Highly experienced data manipulation tools like Python or R and SQL, highly experienced in data visualization tools such as Tableau, experience with data platforms such as Hadoop and SQL Server, experience with cloud platforms such as AWS or Azure preferred.
  • Decision Making: Works under direction of team sprint plans; follows established methods and defined policy. Makes decisions on tactical implementation of tasks or when established methods indicate action to be taken.
  • Team Interaction: works with broader analytics team under SAFe principles, mentors team on application of technical skills and solution design.
  • Communications: Strong interpersonal, negotiation, creativity, attention to detail, and oral and written communications skills tailored for the intended audience.
  • IT Knowledge (desirable): Understanding of IT Infrastructure components, systems and operational processes.
