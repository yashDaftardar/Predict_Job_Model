About the Opportunity

The ERP Support Office has an immediate opening for a Sr. Business Data Analyst.

The Federal Reserve System is transitioning human resources, finance and procurement technology systems into one integrated cloud-based platform using the Workday and SAP Ariba product solutions. To support this new platform, operational support capabilities are being reorganized to be singularly focused on one goal – to deliver a world-class platform in support of the Federal Reserve’s mission. As part of this effort, a new ERP Support Office (ESO) has been created to spearhead technical support of the new solution by establishing an agile team that will embrace change, drive innovation, and rapidly respond to the changing needs of stakeholders. Change of this magnitude does not happen quickly, so this transformation will occur over 18 months between 2021-2022. Positions are open within the ESO for subject matter experts to act as change agents as the transformation becomes a permanent reality.

The Sr Business Data Analyst is responsible for developing and implementing data management operation processes & procedures that support and meet the needs of business functions and district groups, and that are guided by best practices and standardized toolsets. The desired result is sustainable and scalable operations for our Finance, HR, and Procurement teams, driving discoverable and fit for purpose data and information.

This individual will develop and operate a new data management operations function to fulfill the needs of the data operational needs of the platform Data Strategy. Under this teams’ purview, as the program evolves this role will be responsible for maturing the operations. This role will drive a common, standard approach to metadata management, DQ management, data issues management, and end to end data clearing house process and offer consulting to ensure that standards are communicated well and easily adopted and drive maturity through the development of a data literacy program. This role will also be responsible for supporting the platform Data Steward and Data Custodian community and enable the priorities and requirements as set forth by the Data Governance Council (DGC).

What You Will Do:

  • Implement data management and governance frameworks in alignment with FRS direction
  • Gather requirements and ensure processes and procedures meet platform data management needs
  • Develops requirements to mature operational processes through automation, including DQ tools
  • Builds business data capability requirements in partnership with FRS businesses
  • Designs and delivers data management processes, leveraging automation where possible
  • Maintains a centralized inventory and view into all data related activities
  • Engages with Data stewards or their proxies to support and complete a wide range of collection, monitoring, and analysis activities
  • Partners with the Data Operations Team and a variety of IT business function and technology contacts across the System
  • Helps develop, document, operate and maintains the Data Management & Analytics Office (DMAO) processes and Procedures including Metadata Collection Process, DQ Metric Collection Process, Data Issues Process, Data Governance Escalation Process, The End to End Data demand Process & Data sharing Agreement Process
  • Helps Develop, document and maintains program metrics requirements to communicate value, impact and outcomes
  • Accountable and helps develop priority templates to support processes, i.e. Data Sharing Agreement Template
  • Executes User Acceptance Testing of all capabilities implemented
  • Helps develop, document and maintain, and performs acceptance testing, executes compliance scripts and reports results (future)
  • Ensures coordination of activities across all DMAO functions
  • Supports the Data governance workgroup by developing necessary communications, escalated data issues and data metrics on a periodic basis
  • Develop processes and procedures to support the platform Data management Policy’s and Standards approved by the DGC
  • Develop data literacy program working with key stakeholders across the Ariba & Workday platform support community, function business operations team, and the System
  • Provide consulting services for data management activities while maintaining the balance between consulting/advising and providing operational support
  • Champion the importance of good data management principles to all stakeholders
  • Develop DMAO operational runbook

Qualifications:

  • Five or more years of experience in data management operations and analytics roles with increasing responsibilities, and 10 years of overall experience
  • Experience developing operational processes
  • Experience implementing Data Governance and Management frameworks
  • Can explain data and technology concepts to business leaders
  • Ability to provide communications that convey technical topics in simple, concise terms, with all levels of bank management and staff.
  • Bachelor’s Degree in related field, advanced degree preferred
