Full Job Description

We are seeking a Senior Statistician Data Specialist for a position located in Richmond, VA. They are one of the largest global suppliers of lubricant additives and offers a broad line of fuel additives worldwide.

The Statistics Group Leader will lead a team of applied statisticians supporting product development and optimization initiatives across all of the company's strategic business unit. The successful candidate will provide expert statistical guidance to scientists, formulators, and engineers ranging from visualizations of data, experimental design, advanced statistical models, and machine learning techniques.

  • Primarily provide statistical support for R&D activities, including designing and analyzing experiments, building predictive & prescriptive models, summarizing and visualizing operational (streaming) data
  • Consult with manufacturing and process development groups as needed
  • Lead / mentor a group of Applied Statisticians

Provide technical expertise in:

  • Linear and non-linear regression (standard least squares, stepwise, logistic, generalized regression including penalized regression)
  • Multivariate analysis (principal components, partial least squares, clustering, discriminant)
  • Quality and reliability (statistical process control, gage R&R, reliability and survival)
  • Machine learning (classification/regression trees, boosted trees, neural networks)
  • Interface with teams working on improvements and enhancements to the company's data management systems
  • Interact with external customers (oil companies and OEMs) as required

Required Qualifications:

  • MS degree in Statistics
  • Thorough knowledge and understanding of experimental design, linear and non-linear regression, multivariate statistics, and machine learning algorithms
  • Minimum of 5 years of experience in either R&D or manufacturing and leading a team
  • SAS (base and Enterprise Guide), JMP, Minitab, SQL, Microsoft Excel

Preferred Qualifications:

  • PhD in Statistics
  • 10-15 years of experience in engineering, chemical manufacturing or R&D
  • ProMV, Design-Expert, Tableau, R, Python, Matlab

Skills / Abilities:

  • EHS Risk Assessment: Ability to identify EHS hazards and assess risks associated with the hazards. (ie. JSA, risk assessment, PPE assessment, ergonomics, IH monitoring and sampling, Process Hazard Analysis experience). Demonstrates understanding of level of risk, identifies where there is the need for immediate action and to flag risks for management review
  • EHS Risk Control: Ability to prevent, eliminate, reduce or mitigate EHS risks through management systems and a hierarchy of controls. (ie. elimination, work resign, engineering controls, administrative and PPE controls).
  • EHS Compliance & Conformance: Ability to identify, analyze, communicate and manage compliance (external regulatory requirements) and conformance (internal requirements).
  • EHS Management Systems: Ability to design, operate and maintain processes that support execution of the company's EHS policy and drive continuous improvement. (ISO-14001 and RC-14001 management systems standards)
  • Stakeholder Engagement: The ability to maximize the effectiveness of EHS by building the relationship and partnering with internal and external stakeholders (such as non-EHS colleagues, emergency responders, regulators, contractors, suppliers)
  • Analysis and Problem Solving - Solves difficult problems with effective solutions; asks good questions and questions all likely sources for answers; can solve complex problems and recognize complex patterns; utilizing all appropriate tools to reach a robust answer.
  • Communication - Understands the concept of "audience" and is able to tailor communications accordingly to maximize impact and value. Skilled at developing and delivering presentations.
  • Coaching - The ability to provide the structure, environment and support for teaching, training and development of individuals and teams to enable them to achieve specific personal or professional results or goals.
