About this job

At CarMax, we are industry disruptors. At the heart of our innovation is new digital products. Working on many different aspects of the customer experience, our Engineers II research and discover new opportunities and shape products to fulfill them. From inception to completion, you will develop tools and technology, learning quickly from our spirit of experimentation. You will have a direct impact on improving the performance of our business and ensuring customers can buy the vehicles they want in a way that is right for them!

Essential Responsibilities:


As a Software Engineer II - Machine Learning within CarMax Technology you will be developing solutions to productionize and operationalize algorithms as services for enterprise-level consumption. You will collaborate with Product Managers, Data Scientists, Technology teams, and Delivery Managers to deliver highly impactful capabilities that drive CarMax’s iconic omni-channel customer experience.


Minimum Qualifications:

  • 2-3 years’ software-engineering experience in an enterprise-level environment

  • 1+ years’ experience with microservices software architecture

  • 2+ years’ supporting design and development of scalable services to be consumed by the enterprise, including monitoring and production support

  • Intermediate level of Python programming

  • 1+ years’ experience building enterprise-level solutions with Microsoft Azure or equivalent cloud technologies

  • Experience in DevOps practices, testing frameworks, and CI/CD

  • Excellent communication skills

  • Experience on a fast-paced, highly collaborative agile team within a Product-oriented organization

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s in Computer Science, Engineering, Decision Sciences or equivalent area

  • Experience in an object-oriented programming language (i.e. C#, Java)

  • Familiarity with machine-learning and/or AIaaS

  • Familiarity with MLOps and industry-standard machine-learning Python libraries
