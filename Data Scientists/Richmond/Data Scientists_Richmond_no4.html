About the Opportunity

The ERP Support Office has an immediate opening for a Sr. Data Analyst.

The Federal Reserve System is transitioning human resources, finance and procurement technology systems into one integrated cloud-based platform using the Workday and SAP Ariba product solutions. To support this new platform, operational support capabilities are being reorganized to be singularly focused on one goal – to deliver a world-class platform in support of the Federal Reserve’s mission. As part of this effort, a new ERP Support Office (ESO) has been created to spearhead technical support of the new solution by establishing an agile team that will embrace change, drive innovation, and rapidly respond to the changing needs of stakeholders. Change of this magnitude does not happen quickly, so this transformation will occur over 18 months between 2021-2022. Positions are open within the ESO for subject matter experts to act as change agents as the transformation becomes a permanent reality.

The Sr Data Analyst is responsible for providing consultation on analytics needs across the platform and assisting with development of these products for cross-function needs. Other platforms may also come under this teams’ purview, as the program evolves and matures over time. This role will drive a common, standard approach to reporting and analytics and offer consulting to ensure that standards are communicated well and easily adopted. This team will also be responsible for the development, implementation and maintenance of higher, enterprise level reporting and analytics for consumption by the System Data Governance Council, as well as System advisory groups, governance bodies and key decision makers.

What You Will Do:

  • Helps develop and implement capabilities to enable Data Management Operations (DMO) processes and procedures including Metadata Collection Process, DQ Metric Collection Process, Data Issues Process, Data Governance Escalation Process, The End to End Data demand Process & Data sharing Agreement Process
  • Accountable and helps develop priority templates to support processes, i.e. Data Sharing Agreement Template
  • Support Lead Architect in the development of the platform analytic Conceptual and Logical Data Architecture
  • Accountable for development of a data issues log and data initiatives inventory repository
  • Builds business data capability requirements in partnership with FRS businesses
  • Develops requirements to mature operational processes through automation, including DQ tools
  • Executes User Acceptance Testing of all data management capabilities implemented
  • Develops and documents program metrics requirements to communicate value, impact and outcomes
  • Helps build priority templates to support processes
  • Mines and performs analysis of data management data across the Ariba and Workday landscape including metadata, DQ results, data issues log, etc.
  • Develops dashboard requirements that provide insight and transparency into our managed data environment (Ex: Data Quality, Data Landscape, Metadata); partners with Reporting and Analytics team with development of Dashboards
  • Executes ad hoc queries and reporting needed to support the mission of the Data Management & Analytics Office and Data Governance Council
  • Collects, organizes and manipulates large amounts of data using databases and other technologies.
  • Drives reporting and analysis in partnership with Lead Architect
  • Monitors data accuracy and compliance
  • Helps develop, document, maintain, and perform acceptance testing; executes compliance scripts and reports results

Qualifications:

  • 5 years or greater of database and data analytic experience
  • Keen analytical ability and strong knowledge of analysis and modeling
  • Expertise in both quantitative and qualitative data collection and analysis techniques that include analyzing trends, benchmarking, and performance monitoring
  • Expert in data mining and structured & unstructured information analysis
  • Excellent organizational and communication skills, creativity, attention to detail (compliance), and negotiation skills to interpret results, develop communications and recommend improvements
  • Ability to generate insights from information and present information within all settings in a credible, confident and influential manner
  • Competent in information taxonomy creation, information visualization, information modeling, and data warehousing techniques
  • Ability to synthesize and clearly translate large volumes of complex information into executive level scorecards, dashboards and reports that easily consumable by senior management
  • Excellent organization, multitasking, prioritization, and deadline management skills
  • High proficiency and working knowledge of data management, analytic tools and information presentation technologies and tools, such as AWS data and analytic services, Hadoop/Cloudera, Flume, Hive, Adaptive, Prism, Alteryx, Tableau, WebFocus, SAS, BOXi, etc.
