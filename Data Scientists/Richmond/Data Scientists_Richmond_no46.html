Job Location: Richmond, Virginia or Remote


Mission Lane's Data Science Team is looking for a Senior Data Scientist to join us.


As the Senior Data Scientist , you will:

  • Work at the heart of the business: how we make decisions, rise above legacy institutions, and live up to our social mission
  • Create models on a broad range of practical applications for machine learning and statistics
  • Teach and mentor others about best practices to help further our learning culture

To set you up for success in this role from day one, Mission Lane is looking for candidates who have :

  • Degree in a quantitative field and 1+ years of experience in a related role
  • Experience building, deploying, and monitoring supervised learning models in production systems
  • Familiarity with continuous deployment, test-driven development, code review, and refactoring
  • Proficiency with the PyData stack (numpy, scikit-learn, pandas, etc.), databases, and cloud services

You get bonus points for:

  • Experience in financial services
  • Experience with Spark or other distributed computing frameworks
  • Passionate about fairness, accountability, and transparency in machine learning (FAT ML) or explainable AI (XAI)
  • Being excited to find practical ways to apply modern innovations from diverse research areas like Causal Inference, Cost-Sensitive Machine Learning, and Time Series Classification to improve financial services for working class Americans
