Full Job Description

We seek an experienced Data Scientist with sophisticated data management and analysis skills to support projects initiated by UVA researchers in Pediatric and Neonatal critical care. The candidate will be expected to contribute to or lead the data processing, analysis, and visualization for multiple projects. The candidate will be expected to learn the established methods of the group, participate in learning and applying new methods, and contribute their own expertise on basic and advanced analytical approaches to clinical research questions.

  • Understand all phases of the analytic process including data collection, preparation, modeling, evaluation, and deployment.
  • Develop statistical, mathematical and predictive models related to Intensive Care Unit patient events and outcomes.
  • Visualize and report data findings creatively in a variety of visual formats that appropriately provide insight to the principle investigators.
  • Under general supervision, formulate and define analytic scope and objectives through literature review and research.
  • Understands all phases of the analytic process including data collection, preparation, modeling, evaluation, and deployment.
  • In addition to the above job responsibilities, other duties may be assigned.

MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS

Education: Bachelor’s degree or higher in Data Science, Computer Science, Mathematics, Systems Administration, Systems Engineering, Electrical Engineering. Master's Degree preferred

Experience: Three years of relevant experience. Candidates must have expertise in at least one of Matlab, Python, or R, and preferably should be proficient in programming and application of machine learning techniques.
