The Alaka’ina Foundation Family of Companies (FOCs) has a need for talent to provide Advance Search and Discovery (ASD) support to the Defense Threat Reduction Information Analysis Center (DTRIAC) and its nuclear Scientific and Technical information (STI) collection program out Kirtland AFB/Albuquerque, NM with auxiliary support out of Ft. Belvoir, VA.

POSITIONS/TASKS/SUBJECT MATTER EXPERTISE MAY REQUIRE :

  • Data Lake Development – Maintain the DTRIAC Data Lake in a manner permitting navigation, search, insertion, or extraction of information or files by the Government or other performers without requiring proprietary software, tools, or data other than widely available commercial-off-the-shelf (COTS) tools, and that the software that can be authorized for use on the DTRIAC IT systems; ensure that all methods and tools developed for management and processing of the DTRIAC Data Lake without requiring proprietary software, tools, or data other than widely available COTS tools, and that the software that can be authorized for use on the DTRIAC IT systems; Develop and implement a parallel data lake, based on proprietary software or tools.
  • Methods Development – Recommend a design for a comprehensive environment and infrastructure for ASD, including bills-of-materials and cost estimates any proposed software or hardware purchases; hold one or more Gate for presentation of ASD environment; implement the ASD; provide training to the Government and other performers in interfacing with and use of the ASD environment, including presentations and documentation; maintain and operate the ASD environment; provide role-based access to the ASD environment to the Government and other performers, as approved by the COR.
  • Processing and Information Extraction – Gather human training data through ASD SME Projects; Design and propose ASD SME Projects, including specification of workflows, records and SMEs; Design and propose ASD Projects and Analyses, design, test, evaluate, implement and document methodologies and tools for information identification, extraction, analysis, characterization, and organization for application to the DTRIAC digital collection including, but not limited to: ETL (extract, transform, load) processing; Information identification and extraction (scraping); Optical character recognition (OCR) in documents, images, and films; Data cleaning, including spell check and correction, syntax evaluation, identification of duplicated or irrelevant markings or other data.
  • Analyses and Project Support – Leverage the DTRIAC collection, capabilities, and team, focusing on ASD capabilities and team, to perform targeted analyses and studies, provide dedicated support to missions and end users, or to develop products including software.
  • DevOps, Optical Character Recognition (OCR), Articial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning, Algorithm Development, and Software Development

SUPPORT TASK AREAS:

  • Advance Search and Discovery (ASD)
    • Develop methodologies for the extraction, cleaning, organization, and discoverability of information from all media types in the collection;
    • Develop an IT and data environment, on specified DoD IT systems, for ASD;
    • Design, implement, and improve the DTRIAC Data Lake as the primary repository for the DTRIAC digital collection;
    • Deploy ASD-developed methodologies for routine processing of the collection under OPS tasks;
    • Define and setup workflows for collection of human training data, to be executed by the ASD SMEs under IAC tasks, and incorporate the resulting data into ASD development and analyses;
    • Perform targeted projects and analyses leveraging the ASD expertise and capabilities to support management of the collection and STARS;
    • Perform targeted projects and analyses leveraging the ASD expertise and capabilities in support of nuclear deterrent missions.

DESIRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE:

  • Experience in a DoD environment.
  • Experience and expertise DevOps.
  • Experience in Optical Character Recognition (OCR), Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning, Algorithm Development, and Software Development.
  • Experience and expertise in hardware/software data structures, environment & infrastructure, human training data, and data analytics & extraction in nuclear environments.
  • Experience supporting compliance with federal laws and executive orders.
  • Agility in a changing environment.
  • Ability to make updates and modifications to software in a timely manner.
  • May be required to travel.

DESIRED EDUCATION/CERTIFICATIONS:

  • Bachelor’s degree in a related field is preferred.

CITIZENSHIP AND CLEARANCE REQUIREMENTS:

  • Must be a US citizen.
  • Some positions may require a security clearance.
