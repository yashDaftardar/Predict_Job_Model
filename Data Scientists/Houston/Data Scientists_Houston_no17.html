Position Overview

The Senior Data Scientist will be responsible for providing technical support including designing, implementing, and optimizing algorithms for unsupervised and supervised learning on unstructured and structured data. This position will be based out of Houston and will report to the Data & Analytics Director.

Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with other data scientists and cross-functional teams to identify innovation opportunities and possible solutions.
  • Work closely with key stakeholders to understand their needs to develop new or improve existing workflows.
  • Plan and execute the complete analytics life-cycle for problem solving, including requirements gathering, problem formulation, data grooming, data exploration, model prototyping, model validation, and algorithm productization.
  • Provide expertise on statistical and mathematical concepts.
  • Review and refine existing data models to continuously deliver state-of-the-art insights in existing products.
  • Instrumental in helping the business continue its evolution into an analytical and data-driven culture.
  • Maintain academic and industry relationships for the purposes of research; maintain expertise and awareness of cutting edge techniques, technologies and potential business solutions.
  • Must be able to work in a highly interactive, cross functional, team-oriented, agile/fast paced environment.
. Requirements:

Qualifications

  • Experience with and deep understanding of various machine learning and data processing techniques.
  • Advanced programming skills in Python and R using Machine Learning libraries like sckit-learn, MLLib, TensorFlow etc.
  • Expertise with leveraging visualization BI tools, such as Spotfire/Power BI, to build out dynamic dashboards.
  • Proficiency with relational databases (SQL scripting) and understanding of NoSQL databases (e.g., MongoDB).
  • Capable of quickly adapting to changing priorities and generating innovative solutions in an extremely fast-paced environment.
  • Ability to handle multiple simultaneous projects and act as a technical lead for some of them.
  • Taking actions in a self-directed manner to identify issues and make recommendations to department leadership.
  • Ability to translate high level requests, questions, concerns or ideas into successful data and analytics implementations.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills, comfortable in engaging conversation with all levels.
  • Attention to detail and passion for quality with result-oriented approach.
  • Self-motivated and proactive needing minimal supervision.

Education

  • Preferred PhD or Master’s Degree in Computer Science, Statistiscs, Computational Mathematics or related STEM field.
  • 5+ years relevant work experience as a Data Scientist or Engineer demonstrating advanced programming skills.
  • Working knowledge of the oil and gas industry and terminology is a must.
