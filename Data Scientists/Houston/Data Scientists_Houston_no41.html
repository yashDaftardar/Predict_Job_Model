Principal Data Scientist, Strategy & Innovation

Job Summary

We’re a leading independent investment management firm seeking a globally minded Data Scientist to spearhead development and innovation within our AI team.

You will join a fast-paced agile development team building the latest in innovative and disruptive technologies for asset management leveraging the power of big data. In this position you’ll get to envision and develop cutting edge Financial Technologies (aka FinTech) that will contribute to innovation growth for Invesco and the greater financial ecosystem. You’ll be expected to use your quantitative background to develop machine learning and predictive analytics use cases in the investments space and build advanced machine learning models to create competitive advantage for our investment teams.


Dream job? Maybe, but caveat emptor, before you submit your application, you should know the following:

Excellence is par at Invesco. The environment is demanding, and you will be challenged. We expect that you are fluent in all things data, and you also understand the nuances of financial services and our investment capabilities. You’ll be a part of a dynamic, collaborative team that wants to hear your input because you have a sound foundation in quantitative analysis in the investments space.

You will have incredible opportunities for experimentation, so before you submit your application, here are some other things you should know about the position: Everything we do, we do to deliver a superior investment experience. Our Technology team operates under a OneTech mindset, so we are a highly collaborative crew. In this position you’ll be expected to translate your ideas to your team and motivate them to execute, and to execute at the pace of technology —FAST. You will be expected to ideate and partner with asset managers directly to build solutions, products that move the needle for Invesco. This position will be well-suited for someone who is a dreamer and executer, who values speed and innovation.


Key Responsibilities / Duties

  • Act as subject matter expert for investments projects in the data science space
  • Bring ML ideas within asset management to the table. Guide, collaborate with investors and your team to transform ideas into tools.
  • Mentor junior members on the team both on machine learning as well as asset mgmt. related topics.
  • Perform machine learning/statistical analysis methods, such as deep learning, classification, collaborative filtering, association rules, sentiment analysis, topic modeling, time-series analysis, regression, statistical inference, & validation methods
  • Analyze and model structured data using advanced machine learning methods and implement algorithms and software needed to perform analyses
  • Build recommendation engines, sentiment analyzers and classifiers for unstructured and semi-structured data
  • Extend IVZ data with third party sources of information, as required
  • Process, cleanse, and verify the integrity of data used for analysis
  • Perform ad-hoc analysis and present results in a clear manner
  • Perform exploratory data analysis, generate and test working hypotheses, prepare and analyze historical data to identify patterns
  • Design rich data visualizations to communicate complex ideas to customers or company leaders


WORK EXPERIENCE / KNOWLEDGE:

  • 3+ years of Asset management industry experience required
  • 6+ years of experience with machine learning required
  • 6+ years of experience in a role entailing quantitative research and analysis
  • Experience with deep learning, natural language processing
  • Knowledge of market microstructures and macroeconomic theories, econometric forecasting; capital markets and investments; risk & return; asset pricing, pricing models, and financial engineering; efficient market hypothesis, inefficient markets, and behavioral finance; portfolio theory; mathematics of high frequency finance and algorithmic trading desired
  • Excellent understanding of machine learning techniques and algorithms, such as k-NN, Naive Bayes, SVM, Decision Forests, etc.
  • Excellent applied statistics skills, such as distributions, statistical testing, regression, etc.
  • Experience managing and manipulating large data sets
  • Extensive predictive analytics experience with Python or R
  • Experience with data and machine learning services using Azure, Amazon Web Services (AWS), and / or Google Cloud
  • Agile (Scrum) experience a plus

  • Strong analytical and critical thinking skills
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Must demonstrate a positive, team-focused attitude
