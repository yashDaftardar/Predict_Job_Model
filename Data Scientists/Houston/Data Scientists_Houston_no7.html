Senior Data Analyst - HR - ( 2100018J )
Description


Intertek is searching for a Senior Data Analyst - HR to join the Americas Region HR team in Houston, Texas. This is a fantastic opportunity for a Data Analyst with HR experience to be part of a global organization where you can utilize your skills and expertise to support a diverse team of HR and Business professionals.

The Senior Data Analyst - HR will collect, compile, and analyze HR data, metrics, and statistics, for required reporting, and to provide data summary and analysis to support leadership with making people decisions related to retention, salary budget, recruitment, legal compliance, etc.

If you are a highly skilled professional with experience in HR data analytics that is detail oriented, self-driven, and enjoys partnering with business leaders, this is the opportunity for you!

Essential Job Duties:

  • Collect and compile HR people data from a variety of sources including the human resource information system (HRIS), payroll system, bonus database, employment records, etc.
  • Perform HR people data reporting such as monthly headcount, turnover, payroll, employee data changes, and bonus data.
  • Maintains in-house Access database for annual bonus program for Americas region, including data changes, creating queries, auditing for data integrity, financial reporting, design and run reports, and create output letter templates as needed.
  • Analyzes data for trends and patterns with attention to turnover, bonus, salary budget, and compliance with employment laws and regulations.
  • Prepares reports of data results, presenting and explaining findings to leadership.
  • Creates tables, fields, and indexes in SQL Server 2005 and later versions.
  • Works with HR team to integrate data changes directly from HRIS systems.
  • Create, troubleshoot, and maintain new and existing database applications in Microsoft Access 2010.
  • Ensures compliance with data privacy regulations and best practices.
  • Assist HR leadership and Finance with departmental budgets.
  • May guide and/or assist with benefit budgets, and the annual bonus and compensation evaluation processes.
  • Special projects as requested.
Qualifications


Position Requirements:

  • Associates degree in Human Resources, Business Management, or related field, or equivalent in education and experience
  • 4+ years of experience in reporting and data analytics, preferably within an HR environment
  • 2+ years experience writing end-user Requirements and writing end-user Application Documentation
  • Expert MS Excel skills including: ability to perform advanced formulas, power query, tables, conditional formatting, complex pivot tables, VBA and macros
  • Strong understanding of applicable data sources, tables, and data relationships
  • Experience developing and maintaining data in a relational database environment, such as SQL Server DBA 2005 or later versions
  • Proficiency with coding in VBA and MS Access 2010
  • Prefer experience in Transact SQ
  • Strong verbal/written communication skills and ability to interface with all levels of the organization
  • Strong problem solving skills
  • Excellent time management and interpersonal skills
