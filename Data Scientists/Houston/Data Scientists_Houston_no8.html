Full Job Description

Basic Function

Aramco Americas’ Houston Research Center has a Data Scientist opening in the Artificial Intelligence Technology Team. The Data Scientist will conduct independent research and development in formulating and applying AI/ML models and algorithms to various geosciences, petroleum engineering and geophysics applications, collaborate with subject matter teams and provide periodic updates through presentations, reports and prototype demonstrations, and finally contribute to technology delivery and deployment with algorithms, codes and software prototypes. In addition to that, this position is expected to publish their work in conferences and journals as well as patents.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Research and develop machine learning algorithms/models/prototypes for upstream applications in exploration, development and production, such as classification, regression, anomaly detection, failure prediction, and optimization
  • Work with subject matter research and business teams to develop, implement, and deliver robust, scalable and accurate machine learning solutions from end-to-end
  • Collaboratively develop innovative ideas, initiate new research directions, and effectively lead and execute on several technical projects
  • Maintain and grow state-of-the-art technical knowledge, via actively publishing and patenting, and establish as an expert in related areas of responsibility
  • Communicate ideas, technical approaches, project plans and results effectively via oral and written reports; work effectively with peers, management, operations groups, and outside organizations

Education and Experience

  • Master’s degree preferred (Ph.D. highly preferred) in computer science, mathematics, physics, applied science, engineering or similar disciplines with demonstrated research capability with 3+ years of relevant experience
  • Demonstrated experience in developing core machine learning algorithms in professional research and development settings, in building and applying machine learning / predictive modelling in real-world use cases, extensive working knowledge of developing deep learning models using tools such as TensorFlow, Keras, Pytorch, etc
  • Strong background in some of these listed areas: machine learning, time series analysis / sensor processing, deep learning, optimization/operations research, text analytics/NLP, computer vision
  • Extensive hands-on coding ability, proficient with machine learning models, frameworks and stacks, python, react and flask development and GPU based platforms
  • Past experience or knowledge of Oil and Gas domain is a plus
  • Track records of past publications in relevant topics
  • Excellent communication, verbal and written skills, capable of communicating complex quantitative analysis in a clear, precise, and actionable manner to both technical and non-technical audiences
