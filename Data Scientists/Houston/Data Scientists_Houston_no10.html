Job Purpose

Baylor College of Medicine (Baylor) is a health sciences university that creates knowledge and applies science and discoveries to further education, healthcare and community service locally and globally. Baylor educates healthcare providers and scientists in programs considered among the most elite in the world.
The Office of the Provost seeks a Statistician/Data Scientist to support outcomes-based research, data management, evaluation and studies across the education mission at Baylor College of Medicine.
This individual will work collaboratively with team members, the Associate Provosts, Deans, educational and administrative leaders at Baylor College of Medicine to fulfill key responsibilities including support for research methods, analysis of data sets, academic program data review and integration of technology with educational science to support a program of institutional research across both academic affairs and health systems science.
This position will report to the Associate Provost for Academic Affairs and Health Systems Science.

Job Duties

  • Provide statistical and data management support to institutional/educational research.
  • Complete statistical analyses on new and available data sets.
  • Develop systems, processes and procedures for timely data collection from educational, research and accreditation initiatives.
  • Collate evaluation data and other quantitative and qualitative data sources.
  • Facilitate data collection through surveys, focus groups and other methods.
  • Prepare structured data analyses and reports for presentation.
  • Support key educational program compliance activities, through evaluation of program data and metrics as a foundation for timely and meaningful inquiry and scholarship.
  • Support development of innovative educational scholarship across academic affairs and health systems science.
  • Contribute to evidence-based processes and procedures for program quality review and assessment of outcomes data.
  • Support submissions of academic scholarship for publication and/or presentation through preparation of methods, data presentation and related components.
  • Assist faculty in executing research projects that promote continuous quality improvement.
  • Participate actively in the development of training resources to support faculty seeking to conduct educational research.
  • Develop instruments, tools and rubrics to support continuous quality improvement across education mission.
  • Summarize findings, write executive reports and develop presentations for executive and other groups, as directed.
  • Evaluate annual assessment reports and student learning outcomes for each department to identify and analysis trends and outcomes.
  • Remain engaged with key principles of SACSCOC accreditation, to inform institutional research priorities and methodology.
  • Perform other duties, as assigned.
