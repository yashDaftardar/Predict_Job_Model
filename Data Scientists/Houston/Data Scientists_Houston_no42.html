What if you could...

Access vast amounts of structured and unstructured data on an Azure platform with the latest Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence capabilities -- driving transformational change in a dynamic business that delivers real time online advice services. Provide guidance and mentorship to other data scientists, data analysts, data Engineers, and DBAs to design, develop and execute high-impact solutions that help the business make better decisions in real-time?

Combine your Data Science passion, leadership, and technical expertise to operationalize continuous improvement models in a live environment impacting the business and customer experience.

Essential Duties of Role :

  • Develop and implement clearly defined operational plans that deploy AI and Machine Learning solutions that drive real-time automated decisioning.
  • Design feedback loop pipelines that refine and strengthen algorithms as customers, product offerings and the external environment evolve.
  • Select and implement strategies and techniques to advance the level of Sentience across complete ML/AI lifecycle development.
  • Communicate and educate employees, deploy, integrate and evolve the operational frameworks described in “ The Sentient Enterprise ” book by Oliver Ratzesberger.

If you are our next Data Scientist Leader, you would look forward to engaging in activities such as:

  • Serving the critical role as the bridge between Data Science and the practical application of our Machine Learning models to make autonomous business decisions.
  • Developing and implementing operational plans to unleash ML/AI solutions into our production environment.
  • Provide leadership to Data Scientists to enhance the efficacy of our analytic models and operationalize solutions at scale.
  • Partnering with Data Analysts, Data Engineers, DBAs, and full stack developers to leverage our Azure data lake and commercialize/operationalize AI/ML solutions.
  • Practically apply Data Science in a way that brings value and competitive advantage to the business.
  • Networking and collaborating with outside businesses, educational institutions and user groups.
