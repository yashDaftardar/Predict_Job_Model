THE COMPANY

Continental Tire has been around for 150 years, we’ve been changing the way the world moves while making it more safe, smart, sustainable and accessible to all. Our Fort Mill, SC location is seeking a highly self-motivated and detail-oriented Data Scientist to join our Fleet Services group. The ideal candidate has excellent communication skills, is dependable and accountable.

Are you ready to shape the future with us?

THE POSITION

As part of the organization Connected Tires - Technologies and Analytics, we are delivering innovative digital products in order to take on big problems to help fleet customers with our solutions around the world. As a data scientist you will work closely with data and software engineers, field intelligence and tire/vehicle experts to build up tire related prediction products for internal and external customer usage.

  • Design, develop and operate powerful prediction models that process large scale fleet, vehicle and tire sensor related data
  • Analyze large data sets on high performance cloud environment
  • Build up high-quality digital products, based on AI technology together with partners in the Digital Solutions organization for external Customers.
  • Transfer the knowledge gained to other R&D departments to improve our core tire products
  • Be a valued member of an autonomous and cross-functional agile team

Qualifications


BASIC QUALIFICATIONS

  • Relevant BS Engineering Degree.
  • 7 years of professional experience this does not included internships or co-ops.
  • Project management/ leading experience
  • Data acquisition and evaluation within a SQL.
  • Knowledge of working within an Agile environment
  • Programming experience with Python or R.
  • Experience with streaming technologies such as Kafka, AWS Lambda, etc.
  • Basic understanding of Tire performance.
  • Legal authorization to work in the U.S. is required. We will not sponsor individuals for employment visas, now or in the future, for this job opening.
  • Continental can pay relocation expenses for this opportunity.

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

  • 10 years of professional experience and master’s degree
  • SCRUM Master in Agile
  • Experiences with developing Machine Learning Algorithms
