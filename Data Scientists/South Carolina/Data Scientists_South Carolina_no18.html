IT Analyst II - Data Analytics (Maintenance Support)

Location: Gray Court, SC, US, 29645

Req ID 15034

The Data Scientist, will be actively contributing to ZF’s digitalization strategy by challenging, experimenting and adopting Artificial Intelligence and Data Science methods and technologies, fostering and transferring knowledge and experience within ZF to explore potentials and create value for our business partners.


Responsibilities:
  • Design, support, consult, and implement Data Science, Data Analytics and Artificial Intelligence in use case projects
  • Lead and execute development projects of prototype and scalable Analytics and Artificial Intelligence solutions
  • Evaluate technology (ex: Industry 4.0) trends and methods in area of Data Science, Analytics and Artificial Intelligence
  • Ensure a sustainable incorporation of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence skills and knowledge within ZF
  • Establish Data Science and Artificial Intelligence related routines, develop data and Analytics standards and guidelines, create, live & share a “ZF data mindset”

Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree in technical field (Masters preferred) with fundamental knowledge of data science, (big data) analytics and AI technologies, processes and procedure models
  • 3+ years of related experience in data science, (big data) analytics, statistics, artificial intelligence, application development and other related disciplines, ideally from the manufacturing industry
  • Very good knowledge of advanced analytics, machine learning and other related statistical and/or artificial intelligence methods (e.g. Deep Learning, NLP, Reinforcement Learning, Neural Networks)
  • Profound knowledge of programming languages and the associated data & analytics frameworks/libraries and analytics tools and technology (mainly platforms like Microsoft Azure, AWS, Databricks, NoSQL, MS Power Apps/BI, Apache Spark etc.)
  • Very good knowledge of written and spoken English, German is a plus
  • Willingness to travel domestic and international
