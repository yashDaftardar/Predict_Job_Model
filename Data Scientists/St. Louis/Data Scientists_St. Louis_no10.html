Full Job Description

A career with MilliporeSigma is an ongoing journey of discovery: our 58,000 people are shaping how the world lives, works and plays through next generation advancements in Healthcare, Life Science and Electronics. For more than 350 years and across the world we have passionately pursued our curiosity to find novel and vibrant ways of enhancing the lives of others. MilliporeSigma is a business of Merck KGaA, Darmstadt, Germany.

This role does not offer sponsorship for work authorization. External applicants must be eligible to work in the US.

At MilliporeSigma, the Data Scientist will help us discover the information hidden in vast amounts of data, which will help us increase the of our enterprise search engines. The primary focus will be on applying data mining techniques, doing statistical analysis, and building high-quality prediction systems integrated with our enterprise search engines.


  • Select features, building and optimizing classifiers using machine learning techniques
  • Perform data mining using state-of-the-art methods
  • Extend company data with third-party sources of information when needed
  • Enhance data collection procedures to include information that is relevant for building analytic systems
  • Process, cleanse, and verify the integrity of data used for analysis
  • Perform ad-hoc analysis and present results in a clear manner
  • Create automated anomaly detection systems and constant tracking of its performance


Basic Qualifications:
  • Bachelors Degree in Data Science, Information Technology, or related discipline
  • 1+ year of data analysis and or data mining experience


Preferred Qualifications:
  • Masters Degree
  • Excellent understanding of machine learning techniques and algorithms, such as MRR, k-NN, Naive Bayes, SVM, Decision Forests, etc.
  • Experience with data analysis platforms like Spark, Hadoop, Flink, etc.
  • Experience with common data science toolkits, such as deeplearning4j, R, Weka, NumPy, etc. Excellence in at least one of these is highly desirable
  • Proficiency in using query languages such as SQL, CQL, etc.
  • Experience with NoSQL databases, such as MongoDB, Cassandra, HBase, etc.
  • Good applied statistics skills, such as distributions, statistical testing, regression, etc.
  • Good scripting and programming skills in Java, Python, Bash, etc.
  • Great communication skills
  • Data-oriented personality
  • Ability to provide solutions based on the business requirements
  • Ability to work with a global team
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and be customer-centric
  • Experience with Agile methodology
  • Experience applying machine learning to enterprise search engines such as Solr
  • Experience with data visualization tools, such as D3.js, GGplot, etc.
