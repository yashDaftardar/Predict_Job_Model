About Benson Hill
Benson Hill empowers innovators to develop more healthy, tasty and sustainable food by unlocking the natural genetic diversity of plants. Benson Hill's CropOS™ platform combines machine learning and big data with breeding techniques and plant biology to drastically accelerate and simplify the product development process. This platform allows for cost-effective and more efficient ways for companies to analyze and make improvements in plant genetics. Benson Hill brings a unique and holistic approach to our product and platform development, as we span a broader workflow involving trait and seed development, genome editing, and breeding all the way to creating better ingredients and varieties that tap a strong consumer demand for more flavorful and nutritious fruits and vegetables. More information can be found online at www.bensonhill.com. Follow us on Twitter at @BensonHillInc.

Position Summary

Benson Hill is looking for a passionate and proven Senior Data Analyst to be part of our Technology Acceleration team. This role will be responsible for designing analyses, pipelines, and workflows to solve complex pipeline decision automation problems. The Senior Data Analyst, will work collaboratively with the Technology teams for translating business needs into the development of data and analytical work products to support Benson Hill R&D activities.

You will be responsible for:

(Essential Duties and Responsibilities)

  • Analysis of processes, data, or systems to identifying opportunities enabling scale through prescriptive solution design
  • Implement and protype machine learning frameworks that support experiments that test new predictive models
  • Execute SQL queries or Python Scripts to extract data from data warehouse and business applications to support reporting and predictive analytics.
  • Communicate data insights and findings with other cross-functional teams.
  • Work closely with other departments to gather new data and leverage existing data to make our CropOS product better for us and our customers

You Have:

(Competencies and Qualifications)

Education

  • B.S. in life science, data science, computer science, or other technical field

Experience

  • 4+ years of ML frameworks, libraries, data structures, data modeling, and software architecture.
  • 4+ years of scripting experience in (Python, R)
  • 4+ years of SQL and experience as applied to ETL/ELT tools (Informatics, Kettle, etc.)
  • Proficiency in data analysis and research methods
  • Experience with relational databases or NoSql

Preferred Qualifications

  • Knowledge of Biotech or Breeding research data and domain is a preferred.
  • Practical knowledge of data in various forms (data warehouses, SQL, unstructured data environments, HIVE)
  • Experience working in a cloud deployment such as AWS is a plus
  • Excellent communication skills including the ability to identify and communicate data driven insights

Other

  • Attention to detail
  • Strong customer focus
  • Collaborative
  • Ability to focus and work independently with little direction
