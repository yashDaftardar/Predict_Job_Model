Full Job Description

As an Artificial Intelligence Data Scientist, you will be presented with opportunities to focus on enhancing basic concepts in image analysis, modeling and interpretation, natural language processing (NLP), neural networks, and ML/AI model building and deployment in cloud platforms such as Google Cloud Platform, Amazon Web Services, and Microsoft Azure. Key responsibilities include:
  • Translating analytics questions into data solutions
  • Building appropriate models that predict a defined business outcome
  • Optimizing complex business space for new value creation
  • Interpreting and disseminating results to diverse audiences through mediums such as presentations, dashboards, or well-documented code
  • Developing and testing models in the cloud
  • Controlling and assessing quality of data

Requirements

    • PhD or Masters plus 3 years of professional experience in Statistics, Mathematics, Data Science, Computer Science, Engineering, or a related discipline with a focus on use of predictive analytics and optimization to influence real life decisions
    • At least one year professional experience with applying quantitative research (e.g. artificial intelligence) in optimizing human decisions using technology like machine learning and/or deep learning
    • Ability to communicate complex ideas in a clear, precise, and actionable manner
    • Strong skills in logical reasoning and problem solving
    • At least one year of professional experience with: R, Python, GUROBI/CPLEX, or MILP/MIQCP
    • Experience with common data science toolkits

Bonus Points

    • Ability to implement new algorithms efficiently and rigorously
    • Demonstrated industrial experience of applying artificial intelligence, optimization and statistics to drive digital product development
    • Familiarity with data management tools
