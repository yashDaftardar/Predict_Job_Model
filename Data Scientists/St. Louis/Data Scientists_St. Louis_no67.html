Full Job Description

Responsibilities
  • Design efficient, flexible, and scalable data models and solutions to meet business needs for operational reporting, analytical reporting, integration, application development, and master data management
  • Validate data model designs through performance and load testing
  • Analyze data residing in source databases to establish relationships and integration points
  • Design data mapping and transformation from source to target
  • Create and maintain a detailed Enterprise Data Dictionary/Catalog
  • Establish, maintain, and communicate data standards and policies to ensure data integrity, consistency, security, and privacy
  • Ensure data quality and standards compliance by conducting technical data design reviews
  • Evaluate software tools for data integration, data governance, master data management, and reporting
  • Assist with troubleshooting and performance tuning of SQL queries and ETL jobs
  • Analyze data to research anomalies, identify root cause, and recommend solutions for complex problems
  • Make decisions and set priorities consistent with the long-term interest of the organization and stakeholders

Qualifications

Qualifications:
  • BS in computer science or equivalent combination of education and experience desired
  • 7+ years of relevant work experience (e.g. Data Architect, Data Modeler) with a strong data-centric background
  • 3+ years of experience in master Data Management and data governance
  • Advanced data modeling experience supporting application and reporting needs (i.e. transactional and dimensional database schemas)
  • Advanced database skills and experience including analysis of execution plans, indexing, and stored procedure development (SQL Server preferred)
  • Advanced knowledge of batch and real-time integration technologies
  • Strong knowledge of data governance, data quality, and master data management
  • Strong understanding of data visualization techniques using tools like Tableau
  • Experience with Embarcadero ER/Studio or a comparable data modeling tool
  • Experience with full software development life cycle
  • Data science, artificial intelligence, and machine learning experience preferred

Skills:
  • Ability to explain complex technical terminology, concepts, and issues in terms understandable to non-technical personnel
  • Excellent communication (oral and written) and interpersonal skills
  • Excellent attention to detail and commitment to product quality
  • Strong problem solving, critical thinking, and troubleshooting skills
  • Strong listening and questioning skills
  • Must work well independently and in a collaborative team environment
  • Ability to lead meetings with technical peers regarding solution designs
  • Efficiently manage time and set priorities for multiple deadlines as well as ad hoc requests
