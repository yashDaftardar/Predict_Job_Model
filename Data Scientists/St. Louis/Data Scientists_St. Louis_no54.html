Duties:


The incumbent assists in guiding the data entry processes necessary to ensure the proper processing of agency survey and estimate programs. The primary purpose of the work is to develop and program the statistical software used in agency forms processing activities. The position strongly supports and facilitates the nationwide data collection efforts, enhancing the adequacy and quality of the data through data accuracy, improved response rates, and timely compilation and submission, the work is fundamental to the accomplishment of that mission. * Note: This position may work remotely a few days a week after six months of working in the office. This may change based on the Agency's needs.

  • Incumbent assists the Technical Lead in developing and programming the Viking and/or OCR/ICR software to be used in the Agency?s forms processing program. Using approved survey and census specifications, employee develops data entry protocols and data archiving procedures to be used in all forms processing activities at the NOD and Agency. Logical data checks are developed in the data capture protocols to perform specific consistency checks. Responsible for receiving, testing, and installing of new products and product upgrades. The supervisor follows up to ensure any problems have been resolved in a timely manner. 50%
  • Responsible for using software to monitor and assure the processing flow of questionnaires is moving toward established completion targets. Also, perform Quality Control checks which include assuring that questionnaires loaded to Blaise or Prism have a scanned image. 50%
