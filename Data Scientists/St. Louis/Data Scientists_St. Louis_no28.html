Full Job Description

EWS Data & Analytics (D&A) is seeking a driven Senior Business Data Analyst focused on data valuation and market insights to join our growing team. You will have the opportunity to gain an in-depth understanding of Workforce Solutions’ data assets, products, and business model to inform our data acquisition and mergers and acquisitions diligence moving forward.

Who is Equifax?

At Equifax, we believe knowledge drives progress. As a global data, analytics and technology company, we play an essential role in the global economy by helping employers, employees, financial institutions and government agencies make critical decisions with greater confidence.

We work to help create seamless and positive experiences during life’s pivotal moments: applying for jobs or a mortgage, financing an education or buying a car. Our impact is real and to accomplish our goals we focus on nurturing our people for career advancement and their learning and development, supporting our next generation of leaders, maintaining an inclusive and diverse work environment, and regularly engaging and recognizing our employees. Regardless of location or role, the individual and collective work of our employees makes a difference and we are looking for talented team players to join us as we help people live their financial best.

The Perks of being an Equifax Employee?

  • We offer excellent compensation packages with market competitive pay, comprehensive healthcare packages, 401k matching, schedule flexibility, work from home opportunities, paid time off, and organizational growth potential.

  • Grow at your own pace through online courses at Learning @ Equifax.

What You’ll Do

  • Quantify the revenue opportunity associated with new data, considering incremental database coverage, data quality, and current data utilization

  • Explain your results to business leaders and decision makers, through conversation, slide content, and written collateral

  • Interpret financial reports to understand potential acquisition targets’ financial models and data monetization

  • Establish best practices around data valuation to improve consistency and speed of analysis

  • Research the industries we serve to understand their value chains, revenue generation, and how EWS data is or could be leveraged

Qualifications

  • 5+ years of work experience in analytics, economics, finance, or a comparable discipline

  • Advanced Tableau skills or comparable data visualization tool; advanced SQL skills

  • 5 + years of experience using Microsoft Excel/Google Sheets including pivot tables and advanced functions

  • Strong analytical skills including the ability to rapidly learn new data assets, make data-driven decisions, and understand the business context of data

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, Economics, Finance, Engineering, or a comparable discipline; MBA or MS degree preferred

Extra Points for any of the Following

  • Understanding of financial reporting (10-Ks, 10-Qs, 8-Ks, S-2s, Annual reports)

  • Experience with mergers and acquisition diligence a plus

  • Experience with Python and Data Engineering best practices

  • Understanding of Agile methodology

  • Expertise with GSuite, Google Cloud Platform, and BigQuery

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills, including documentation, memos, and slide decks, with the ability to communicate with team members at various levels

  • Ability to travel <10%
