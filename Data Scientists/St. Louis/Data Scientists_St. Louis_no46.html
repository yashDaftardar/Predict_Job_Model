Job Description:

Parsons is looking for a sharp Expert Data Architect to join our growing Space & Geospatial Solutions team. This Data Architect will be responsible for designing, creating, deploying, and managing data architecture and will define how the data will be stored, consumed, integrated, and managed by different data entities and IT systems, as well as any applications using or processing that data in some way. This Data Architect will enforce/assist in enforcing adherence to GEOINT data standards and assure data assets are in line with the defined GEOINT data architecture. Typically, a data architect maintains the metadata registry, oversees data management, and optimizes databases and/or all data sources.

Duties include:

  • Support the Warfighter Support Office efforts to bring the latest relevant datasets to the warfighters’ mission(s).
  • Implement/integrate data/content architecture with Odyssey data architecture.
  • Coordinate with Chief Engineer, Lead Data engineer, Mission Tradecraft developer(s), data owners and capabilities engineers to determine warfighters mission needs and the interoperability with warfighter mission systems and applications.
  • Coordinate with multiple systems entities, including (but not limited to) Open Data Store (ODS), OCONUS Data Center (ODC), NSG Consolidated Libraries (NCL), Gateway, GRiD, and IC GIS Portal.
  • Coordinate with Warfighter Support Office deployed Capabilities Leads, GEOINT Expeditionary (GEO-X) Embeds, NSG Data owners and Data Corps.
  • Design and deliver operations research applications (databases, websites, tools, and products) that are geospatially enabled to support a multitude of intelligence issues.
  • Incorporate the latest methodologies such Big Data, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Data Harvesting and Content Curation to modernize the Warfighter Support Office's data pipeline.
  • Develop automation to streamline GA and IA processes to improve consistency, quality and availability of GEOINT content across multiple security domains.
  • Support cross domain data transfer.
  • Develop PL/SQL, stored procedures, and extract, transform, load (ETL) scripts to support this analytics application.
  • Must be familiar with Continuous Integration & Continuous Delivery (CI/CD) best practices for code build and deployments, DevOPS, SAFe AGILE, and/or any sponsored agile methodologies.
  • Document database design and develop optimum data ingest techniques from multiple data sources.

Required Qualifications :

  • Bachelor’s degree or higher in Computer Science, Information Technology, GIS, Management Information Systems, Engineering, or related STEM degree.
  • 18+ years of relevant information
  • Active TS/SCI Security Clearance.
  • Minimum experience of 5 years with relational databases such as Oracle, SQL Server, Sybase, RedShift.
  • Minimum 3+ years of experience with ETL technologies.
  • Senior-level experience in cloud-based data architecting, with emphasis on implementing content delivery network (CDN) solutions in disconnected, disadvantaged, intermittent and/or low bandwidth (DDIL) mission environments.
  • Senior-level experience in securely and efficiently moving data and operating across hybrid multi-cloud and on premise and multi-fabric environments – within enclaves on the same security domain, and across security domains.
  • Demonstrated experience with Amazon Web Services (AWS) Commercial Cloud Services (C2S) ecosystem, including AWS Database solutions.
  • Senior-level experience with the unique networking aspects of cloud computing, including low latency within the data center, and higher latency and limited bandwidth for remote users.
  • Demonstrated familiarity with NSG data standards.
