Secure our Nation, Ignite your Future

Become an integral part of a diverse team while working at an Industry Leading Organization, where our employees come first. At ManTech International Corporation, you’ll help protect our national security while working on innovative projects that offer opportunities for advancement. ManTech is seeking a motivated, career and customer-oriented data science and machine learning professionals to join our dynamic modeling team in Portsmouth, VA.

The Navy and Marine Corps Public Health Center (NMCPHC) is the Navy and Marine Corps center for public health (PH) services to maximize readiness through force health protection (FHP) strategies and solutions for current and potential PH threats. NMCPHC provides leadership and expertise to ensure mission readiness in support of the National Military Strategy.

NMCPHC’s core scientific and technical capabilities and product lines are to conduct Health Surveillance, Epidemiology, and Analysis; Disease and Injury Prevention; and PH Consultation and Support for the benefit of active-duty service members, retirees, and their families. This is carried out at the NMCPHC and its supported Medical/Military Treatment Facilities (MTFs), shore-based installations, and its Operational Medical Assets and field activities.

The Responsibilities of the Lead Data Scientist include but are not limited to:

  • Drives all modeling efforts of the team, and act as a primary gatekeeper to advise on which questions are suitable for modeling (and which are not)

  • Responsible for putting scope boundaries on each modeling effort, including helping the team sketch out the initial approach, algorithms, and timeline

  • Gathers existing research and applied work on a given effort, and brings best practices to the table so the team does not reinvent a solution that already has a significant body of knowledge for the approach

  • Identifies (and gathers) useful public data-sets and defines requirements for the team to synthesize new data-sets to support modeling efforts

  • Establishes standards for the team in terms of programming, tool use, and workflow

  • Conducts hands-on analysis with the team. You are likely not the “best” or fastest programmer in the team, but have a breadth of experience that helps ensure a consistent and high-quality standard

Basic Qualifications:

  • 10+ years of progressive and relevant experience

  • 5+ years of experience specific to predictive analytics and techniques based on parametric algorithms

  • 3+ years of experience related to machine learning and/or agent-based modeling

  • Hands-on experience and understanding of the tradeoffs between various types of models, particularly ensemble, regularization, and instance-based algorithms

  • Working knowledge of a variety of machine learning tools, including a basic open-source Python-based stack including elements such as Scikit, Pandas, NLTK, Keras, etc.

  • Experience with Jupyter notebooks and standards to ensure portability and easy of deployment to scripted production models.

  • Significant experience inside of the clinical sector, preferably related to clinical delivery improvement or public health strategy

  • Experience with a wide variety of health data sources, including EMR/EHR, bio-surveillance, and environmental.

  • Experience in data de-identification and creating synthetic data for use in model prototyping/exploration.

  • Graduate degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Data Science, Math, Engineering, Public Health, or another applicable area – or additional applied experience that demonstrates equivalent depth.

  • Must be a U.S. Citizen

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with SAS, R, and Tableau

  • Strong connectivity to either machine-learning SMEs in either academic or applied/industry circles

  • PhD with published work demonstrating depth in one or more areas of modeling

  • Additional certifications or training that illustrate passion for keeping up with advances in modeling (whether ML, ABM, or otherwise)

Security Clearance Requirements:

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a current Secret security clearance. Applicants that posses an active Secret clearance are strongly preferred.

Physical Requirements:

  • Must be able to remain in stationary position for up to 50% of the time.

  • Must be able to move about inside the office or IT environment to access computer systems, modules; equipment and IT machinery.

  • Must be able to constantly operate computers and other IT environment systems such as IT machinery, modules, etc.

  • The person in this position frequently communicates with co-workers, management, and customers, which may involve delivering presentations.

  • Must be able to exchange accurate information in these situations.

  • The person in this position must be able to detect, identify, observe, and assess written communications; presentations; and manuals.
