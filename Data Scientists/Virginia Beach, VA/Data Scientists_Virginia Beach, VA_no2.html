We are looking for a Data Scientist in our Virginia Beach, VA office to join our growing municipal and industrial business. The Data Scientist will help drive Digital Transformation for our clients across the United States.

General responsibilities include:

  • Design, analysis, and visualization of high-quality data and development of insights through use of analytics tools like Power BI, Tableau, Azure Analytics.
  • Support multiple analysis projects that provide business process governance and oversight.
  • Work to align processes and metrics with client risk framework, data management initiatives programs and goals.
  • Consistent application of reporting and measurement standards.
  • Consistent solution development for process improvements and efficiencies.
  • Interface with internal and client stakeholders on the above and driving action from any recommendations.
  • Support assessments, planning of new strategies and initiatives, perform analyses, conduct research, and support implementation of new business practices, information management tools, etc.
  • Perform analyses on large utility datasets from systems such as: GIS, computerized maintenance management system (CMMS), SCADA, Customer information system, financial information system, and hydraulic modeling datasets.
  • Familiar with data and information quality control.
  • Self-starter, possess a growth mindset, good communication and time-management skills; provide attention to detail, and flexibility to work in geographically spread team-oriented environment and client projects.
  • Develop and lean into digital and consulting skillsets we are using for client programs – like diagnostic/predictive analytics to stay at the forefront of relevant and innovative approaches as a consultant.

Requirements:

  • BS in Business Analytics or computer science; MS preferable
  • 0-4 years’ experience
  • Ability to travel
  • Proficiency in Python, R for data analysis and statistical analysis, query languages such as SQL, understanding of databases (e.g. Relational databases), cloud platforms like Azure, AWS, GCP for deploying cloud-based solution
  • Ability to work with Data Lake, Data Bricks and Cloud based Machine Learning service
  • Familiarity with Microsoft Power Platform, Business intelligence tools such as Power BI and Tableau
  • Experience working in an Agile environment
  • Some understanding of Software Development Life cycle (SDLC)
  • Some experience in infrastructure asset management, utility management, and/or data management strategies, particularly on water sector projects, is a plus.
  • Valid driver’s license and acceptable driving record
  • Highly motivated, willingness to learn and innovate, and the desire to grow in your career
