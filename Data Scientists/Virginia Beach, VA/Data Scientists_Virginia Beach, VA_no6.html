Data Science Director

Will be open to consider candidates from any of the below mentioned locations.

Richmond VA / Norfolk VA / Atlanta GA / Mason OH / Indianapolis IN

Responsible for participating in all phases of the design and development life cycle of analytic models, applications, and supporting tools, as well as providing consulting support to program portfolio sponsors. Primary duties may include but are not limited to:

  • Lead the design and execution of data science or predictive modeling projects to address specific business problems determined by consultation with business partners.
  • Oversee development of proprietary algorithms to build customized solutions that go beyond standard industry tools and lead to innovative solutions.
  • Lead the design, implementation, and continuous delivery of an insights data pipeline supporting the development and operation of custom analytic products/services across the organization.
  • Collaborates and communicates with business partners to develop and define solution scope and objectives, including impact to interfaces.
  • Analyzes and evaluates detailed business and technical requirements.
  • Mentors others on coding standards and performs code reviews.

Qualifications

Requires a Bachelor’s degree in a Quantitative discipline such as Statistics, Computer Science, Mathematics, Machine Learning, Biostatistics OR healthcare related discipline such as Public Health Management, Healthcare Administration, Nursing or Health Sciences; advanced degree preferred. Must have 10 or more years’ experience in at least one of the following areas: Advanced Analytics, Predictive Modeling, Data Architecture, Real-Time Applications, or BI Tools, as well as experience in Hadoop technologies. Knowledge of healthcare operations required; Medicare risk management experience preferred. Strong relational database SQL skills; including optimization techniques, with knowledge of NoSQL concepts. Knowledge of the predictive modeling methodologies such as Classification, Regression, Clustering, Model Selection, Feature Extraction or equivalent, or any combination of education and experience which would provide an equivalent background is preferred. Ability to lead advanced analytics or solutions teams required.
