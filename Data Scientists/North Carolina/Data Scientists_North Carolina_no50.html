Statistician I

Metabolon, headquartered in Research Triangle Park, NC, is a diagnostic products and services company offering the industry’s leading biochemical profiling platform. Due to our rapidly growing services business, Metabolon is seeking a Statistician I to be an integral member of our research team. The team member in this position will report to the Director of Statistics and Data Management and will be responsible for the analyses of standard and commercial projects. The successful candidate will have experience with basic statistical concepts.


Responsibilities

  • Perform analyses of standard commercial projects with minimal supervision
  • Responsible for clear and concise documentation of analyses of each study
  • Will assist project management team with statistical graphics


Qualifications

  • BS Degree or MS degree in statistics (MS preferred)
  • Clear understanding of basic statistical concepts (hypothesis testing, confidence intervals, linear regression, multiple testing)
  • Basic knowledge of experimental design (one-way design, paired data, two-way factorial designs, repeated-measures designs)
  • Ability to clearly communicate statistical concepts to scientists
  • Capability to program in R is an asset
  • Self-starter with good work ethic


About Metabolon

Metabolon, Inc., is the global leader in revealing biological insights on disease state and physiological reactions in the present time through metabolomics. Leveraging one of the world’s most diverse and rich patient data sets, Metabolon is equipped to deliver biologically relevant evidence to address some of the most difficult and pressing questions in the life sciences. Every day, our work is helping to accelerate research and product development success in the biopharma, population health, consumer products, agriculture, wellness and academic research sectors.

Metabolon was founded in 2000 and is based in Research Triangle Park, North Carolina with an international headquarters in Germany. For more information, please visit www.metabolon.com.
