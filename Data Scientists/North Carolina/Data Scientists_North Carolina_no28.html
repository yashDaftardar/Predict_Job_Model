COMPANY OVERVIEW:

TIAA is the leading provider of financial services in the academic, research, medical, cultural and government fields. We offer a wide range of financial solutions, including investing, banking, advice and education, and retirement services.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES AND DUTIES:

Sr Data Analyst
Provides business and technical expertise for the analytics process, tools and useful applications across multiple functions or business units.

Key Responsibilities and Duties

  • Ensure activities and team organization aligns with the business and project strategies.
  • Build trusted advisor relationship by partnering with Business and IT to best achieve expected business outcomes
  • Plan and utilize resources proactively and efficiently to achieve project objectives
  • Apply strategic thinking against very complex business and technical problems directing personnel and consultants on the proper objectives to develop and implement business requirements.
  • Support capabilities Release testing and provide business sign off for new capabilities
  • Help with supervising contingent workers and their daily tasks including onshore and offshore staff

  • University (Degree), Preferred

Required Skills:

  • 3+ years of Data Analysis experience.
  • 3+ years of experience coding SQL
  • Experience in systems analysis

Desired Skills

  • 5+ years of Data Analysis experience.
  • Financial Services and/or Retirement industry experience
  • B.S., Management Information Systems
  • Business Tools Knowledge including Microsoft Office, Project and Visio
  • Acumen to understand business logic and product offerings.
  • Adept at complex data models and writing complex SQLs.
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to work with a large team
  • Ability to lead multiple project deliverables simultaneously
