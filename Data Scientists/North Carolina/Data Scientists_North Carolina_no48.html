Full Job Description

MPF Federal is seeking a Data Scientist for a full-time job opportunity. The Data Scientist will provide statistical and computational support to experimentalists that produce large data sets. The Data Scientist will be responsible for sorting through large data sets, extracting it, and mining the data for Principle Investigators. In addition, the Data Scientist will create databases for future use and support various command groups. The successful candidate will have experience in an analytics field, including data analytics, data science, advanced mathematics or statistics. Experience with data gathering and analysis of large data sets, programming languages such as Python or Java and the ability to exhibit flexibility, initiative, and innovation in dealing with ambiguous, fast-paced situations.


ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE BUT ARE NOT LIMITED TO:

  • Handles raw data (e.g. structured, unstructured, and mixed datasets), and analyzes data through the application of various statistical techniques or tools.
  • Familiar with programming languages such as, but not limited to: SAS, Python, SQL, and Java.
  • Collect, clean, and mung data in a timely manner as part of a cross-functional team
  • Extract meaning from and interpret data using a variety of tools and methods from statistics and machine learning.
  • Collaborate with Principle Investigators and SMEs to design databases for future use

Required Knowledge/Skills:

  • 0-6 years of experience analyzing high-dimensional data
  • Solid experience with biological concepts, data and information types
  • Demonstrated experience with writing codes required, medical jargon and military jargon a plus
  • Experience in some of the following core competency areas: experimental design, batch correction/normalization, missing-values imputation, data visualizations
  • Experience with one or more statistical computing languages such as Java, SQL and Python
  • Experience with sophisticated statistical methods, machine learning and AI.
  • Proficiency in Windows and LINUX/UNIX environments
  • Ability to demonstrate scientific productivity and have excellent oral and written communication skills.
  • Ability to work collaboratively cross-functionally.
