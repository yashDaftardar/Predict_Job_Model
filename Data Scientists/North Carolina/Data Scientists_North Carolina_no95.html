Join a team recognized for leadership, innovation and diversity

Work with advanced technologies, global customers, and the most innovative, talented minds in the industry. Your career path will encompass diverse, challenging assignments that span product lines, job types, businesses, and countries, within Honeywell's Corporate Audit group. You will lead technology risk assessments, pre-audit planning and conduct independent and objective audit reviews across Honeywell's diversified organizations globally.

This is an opportunity to leverage Honeywell’s wide range of technology solutions to create advanced analytics and predictive models to solve complex problems in integrated supply chain (ISC). You will work directly with site leaders, engineers, SBG directors and VP’s to improve quality, cycle time, and energy consumption in our manufacturing and distribution facilities. You will develop mechanisms for monitoring accuracy and delivery of analytical solutions to key stakeholders.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop analytical solutions to improve operational efficiency in ISC sites
  • Create baseline and optimized supply chain network models and develop business cases to improve cycle time, quality yield, production output, variable costs, emissions, and energy consumption
  • Build standardized reusable modules to accelerate model development
  • Continuous improvement on model accuracy and processing performance
  • Balance stakeholder and customer expectations

YOU MUST HAVE

  • Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Engineer, Applied Mathematics or other technical or mathematical field
  • 4+ years developing predictive models in R or Python
  • 4+ years of experience building ML/AI models using multiple algorithms like random forest, neural networks, natural language processing, regression models, naive bayes
  • 2+ years leveraging API’s for automatic data collection

WE VALUE

  • Experience using cluster computing to execute large models
  • Proficiency writing SQL and creating workflows in Paxata or Alteryx
  • Experience using data science solutions hosted on Honeywell FORGE (Domino, Paxata, Dremio, Informatica, Collibra)
  • Strong communication skills to influence leaders using predictive models
  • Global company working experience
