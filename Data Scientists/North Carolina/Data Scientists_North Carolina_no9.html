Full Job Description

The Grouper and Risk Modeling Data Science Team is responsible for applying data science modeling and rigorous statistical analysis to ensure optimal use of Episode Grouper models and their data outputs (Clinical episodes and associated Risk models). Models and outputs are used for a variety of analytical processes (Risk and Cost Prediction, Risk Adjustment, Provider Cost and Quality measurement, Cohort Matching for Evaluations, etc.). The team also provides guidance to other analytics teams for analytical consumption of these models and data, as well as advice to senior leadership on Grouper and Risk Adjustment strategy.

Role Summary

The Data Scientist will apply statistical analysis to test and fine tune Episode Grouper and Risk Adjustment data for Bundled Payment and High Cost Procedure-oriented use cases. They will also research and develop homegrown ML approaches for Risk Adjustment model alternatives. The team member will be a key advisor to other Data Science teams on methodologically appropriate model use and output consumption. Finally, they will collaborate with team leadership to monitor evolving industry Grouper and Risk Adjustment trends to influence Episode Grouper strategy.

Key Responsibilities

Serve as team lead on statistical and methodological aspects of Bundled Payment and High Cost Procedure-oriented Episode Groupers (i.e. Prometheus, Health Qx, Episode Grouper for Medicare-EGM).
Perform statistical analysis and fine tuning of current Episode Grouper and Risk prediction models, inputs and outputs.
Innovate by testing and developing (ML and DL) alternative Risk Adjustment models for superior performance in various Bundled Payment Episode Grouper and Cost Prediction use cases.
SME/advisory role for sister Data Science teams on appropriate Episode Grouper data consumption.
Present findings/recommendations to technical and non-technical leaders to drive Episode Grouper implementation and strategy.

Qualifications

This position requires demonstrated acumen and experience applying rigorous statistical methodology and Supervised Learning models to claims and clinical data prediction:

Post-Graduate degree in Statistics, Biostatistics, Actuarial or Data Science ( Masters or PhD preferred ), with significant modelling experience ( 4+ years preferred ) using Python, R or SAS to build Supervised Learning models for predictive purposes.
Significant experience with Bundled Payment and High Cost Procedure-oriented Episode Groupers (i.e. Prometheus, Health Qx, Episode Grouper for Medicare-EGM) and related Risk Adjustment models (preferred) .
Keen eye for data and modelling detail, appetite for methodological nuances (required) .
Proven experience querying (SQL, Hadoop) and statistically analyzing (Python, R, SAS) large claims and clinical data as key step prior to modeling. (required)
Prior experience applying Supervised Learning models (Lasso Regression, Classification, other ML and DL) to for cost, risk score or other outcome predictions (required) .
Solid verbal communication and consultative skills. Ability to collaborate with technical and non- technical teams/leadership, establish working relationships, and drive business decisions based on sound methodologic points.
Prior experience contributing to research/development/thought leadership on Data Science applications for strategy, innovation and practical solutions.
A sense of humor.
Experience working in an agile environment.
