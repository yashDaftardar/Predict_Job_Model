Job Description:

Do you want to uncover hidden insights from data and use those to drive decisions and influence behavior to create business value for our clients? If so, Conduent LABS has an exciting opportunity for you. The LABS analytics team works closely with Conduent business groups to address real world challenges in domains such as healthcare, mobility and payment card fraud in financial welfare programs, using the power of Data Analytics. We adopt a multidisciplinary approach that combines techniques from diverse domains such as data mining, machine learning, statistical modeling, simuation, vizulaizaiton, optimization and use big data technologies to consolidate and analyze volumes of data from multiple sources in a scalable manner.

We are looking for a Data Scientist to work on machine learning, data mining, predictive modelling and prescriptive analysis of data pertaining to real-world scenarios. Successful candidate will be expected to use state of the art techniques in machine learning and statistical modeling with, design, develop, and deploy state-of-art, scalable systems for innovative analytics applications in a set of industrial verticals (e.g. healthcare, transportation, customer care etc.). Candidate should be comfortable with bulding predictive models involving Big Data, structured and unstructured. Text analytics and advanced forms of NLP/NLU is a big plus.

The successful candidate will be expected to work with a diverse set of data sources, such as time series data, spatial, graph data, semi-structured and unstructured data, and build statistical/machine-learning models in support of on-demand, real-time analytic services. The applicant should also have strong skills in explaining and interpreting the analytic models and outcomes in both techniques and application contexts.

Responsibilities
  • Design and prototype robust and scalable models based on machine learning, data mining, and statistical modeling to answer key business problems.
  • Create robust predictive models from A-Z and be able to integrate and deploy these models as part of a system design.
  • Build tools and support structures needed to analyze data, perform elements of data cleaning, feature selection and feature engineering and organize experiments in conjunction with best practices.
  • Work with development teams & business groups to ensure models can be implemented as part of a delivered solution which is appliacable and replicable across many clients.
  • Keep abreast of the latest developments in the field by continuous learning and proactively champion promising new methods relevant to the problems at hand.


Qualifications
  • BS/MS in Data Science or related field.
  • Demonstrated history of driving and delivering analytics models and solutions.
  • Deep knowledge of fundamentals of machine learning, data mining and predictive modeling, and experience applying these methods to real world problems.
  • Strong skills in analytics software especially in Python (knowledge of R, C/C++ is a plus), and various open-source machine learning and analytics packages to generate deliverable modules and prototype demonstrations of their work.
  • Ability and inclination to work in multi-disciplinary environments, and desire to see ideas realized in practice.
  • Demonstrated ability to propose novel solutions to problems, performing experiments to show feasibility of their solutions and working to refine the solutions into a real-world context.
  • Strong analytical, written, and verbal communication skills.
  • Ability to explain complex ideas in simple terms.
