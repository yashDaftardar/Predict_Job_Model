Job Title: Intern – Data Scientist

What you could be doing on a daily basis;

This is an excellent opportunity to work with the core data science team at VF. Items that you will be involved with on a daily basis include: data extraction, data manipulation and data wrangling.

You will be greatly involved in data exploration to find business insights and share with the team. You will work closely with DS team in developing optimization model and other analytic projects.

The types of strengths we would like you to bring;

  • Good understanding of data wrangling and efficient in writing codes
  • Pursuing a Master’s/Ph.D. degree in Computer Science/ Operational Research /Data Science
  • Technical Skills - Python, AWS, SAS
