Job Summary:
The Data Scientist is a critical member of the Tactegra Data Management and Governance, SyncPoint and Management by Fact teams. Senior Data Scientist may also lead Data Management and Governance teams. The Tactegra Data Scientist utilizes our proprietary accelerators to go beyond their analytical skills to collect, format, interpret, and publish data. The Tactegra Data Scientist collaborate with our clients to collect and present data for analysis and/or by setting up and managing new studies for the creation of metrics, KPIs and their respective reporting and presentation to better enhance their business decisioning processes.

The Data Scientist also support our client environments via nuanced data collection frameworks and automated tools, while developing various data reporting governance and reporting processes. They are instrumental in identifying, validating, and tracking performance metrics for client operations, programs, and projects. In collaboration with other members of the Data Management and Governance team, they influence the design and requirement creation for Data Warehousing and Reporting, and Functional Data Flow for the client's processes, systems and handoffs across various internal departments and delivery teams. A Tactegra Data Scientist generally possess a degree or certificate in Data Science or an affiliated field and is fluent in object-oriented programming language, with a preference for Python, R, Java, or Scala.

Salary is market competitive, commensurate with experience.

Essential Functions:
  • Highly proficient in coding languages such as Python, R, and SQL.
  • Critical thinking and ability to structure and analyze large data sets to include “Big Data”.
  • Present findings in reports and utilizing various visual techniques and tools like Power BI and Tableau.
  • Experience with statistical software such as Stata and SPSS.
  • Strong knowledge in gathering, cleaning, structuring, and transforming data techniques.
  • Collaborate with MBF team members to effectively present client solutions.
  • Strong familiarity with data warehousing and extraction techniques.
  • Conduct industry research and recommend best practices.
Education:
  • Bachelor's degree or higher in business analysis or related field.
Experience:
  • Minimum 3-4 years as a Data Scientist or equivalent work experience in the Data Management and Governance field.
Technical Skills:
  • Fluent in at least one object-oriented programming language, with preference for Python, R, Java or Scala
  • Experience with Power BI or Tableau is preferred.
Physical Requirements:
Prolonged periods of sitting at a desk and working on a computer.

Travel Required
None
