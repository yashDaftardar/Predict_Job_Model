We are looking for an innovative Data Scientist that can help drive our efforts within the Supply Chain organization. We have an ambitious plan for 2021 and are looking for a self-starter with real-world experience to come in and lead projects and data initiatives/modeling. This individual will work with business partners, leadership members and executives, so communication skills are a must.


Job Summary：
Responsible for leading and developing large data science projects from concept through implementation. Includes responsibility for systems architecture, and development and management of standard analytical tools, for the Supply Chain and Operational Services organization. Translate high-level business requirements to a detailed technical solution. Leverage existing systems architecture to develop and implement world-class analytic capabilities. Collaborate with internal and external business and IT stakeholders to design and develop the best technical solution. Provide training and governance that maximizes organizational usage/compliance. This position is preferred to sit in Davidson, NC, but remote is an option.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop systems to collect, analyze, and interpret large amounts of data to achieve a business result

  • Project management using company standard methodologies (Agile, Waterfall, CRISP-DM)

  • System Architecture (develop strategy, design, and implement successfully)

  • Manage multiple priorities and project timelines

  • Problem solving

  • Understanding Lean tools/skills to help customer develop the best solution

  • Increasing breadth of functional knowledge (Procurement, Accounts Payable, Finance, Legal, Trade Compliance, Logistics, Materials, Quality, etc.)

  • Align collaborate with other team members to develop execute strategy supporting the Supply Chain and Operational Services organization

  • Engage in training, communication, and operationalization of standard work and processes

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree required

  • 5+ years’ experience in supply chain environment building analytical tools

  • Strong communication skills – proven ability to communicate at all levels of the organization, both verbal and written.

  • SQL and relational database design

  • Experience with data warehousing and OBIEE

  • Strong Python coding skills (Django, Pandas, BOTO3, OpenPyXL, Matplotlib, etc.)

  • Experience with Amazon Web Services (SQS, S3, Fargate, etc.)

  • Ability to create and maintain REST APIs

  • Knowledge of the Docker platform

  • Proficiency with Git / Github / Gitlab

  • Machine Learning experience

  • Experience with various ERP/Operations systems

  • Project leadership that achieves customer requirements on time.

  • Strong Collaborative Skills, Positive Attitude, High Energy

  • Providing training and public speaking
