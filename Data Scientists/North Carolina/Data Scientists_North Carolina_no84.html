Our Insight Cloud+Data Center Transformation team is searching for an experienced, passionate and professional Data Center Architect to join our team.


At the heart of every great technology solution is great management, always making sure that the project delivers what it set out to do and matches expectations.


Opportunity:

As a Data Center Architect for Insight, you will combine a diverse technical and industry background coupled with a deep understanding of how technology impacts a business environment. The DCA is able to translate a business need into a technical solution and articulate the value of that solution back to business decision-makers supporting complex solution sales opportunities. The DCA takes a primary technical role in opportunity identification and qualification as a partner to one or more Account Executives (AEs). The DCA is focused on opportunities that may span multiple solution areas and services offerings. A DCA may leverage one or more Solutions Architects and Services subject matter experts to manage a technical sales campaign depending on the specific technical and services requirements of that opportunity.


The DCA takes a consultative approach to selling, focusing on understanding customer business requirements and translating those to appropriate technical solutions and services engagements. The DCA is expected to interact heavily with IT and business decision-makers within their customers to expand the scope of existing opportunities and to identify new opportunities proactively.


Insight is looking for talented individuals who are passionate about emerging technologies and excited about the opportunity to work for industry-leading organizations!


What you’ll do at Insight:


  • Owns overall technical relationship with assigned accounts,
  • Owns development of proposals that address complex opportunities with input from Solutions Architects.
  • Owns the technical aspect and is a participant in increasing share of wallet in target and strategic accounts.
  • Owns the development of high-level designs for solutions presented with input from Solutions Architects(s).
  • Identifies customer business and technical requirements for all opportunities.
  • Participates in all internal sales meetings as technical representative.
  • Develops Statements of Work (SOW) for all professional services activity, with input from Solutions Architects and Solutions Engineers as well as the Regional Engineering Manager.
  • Participates in validating the strategy of responding to RFPs. For valid RFP/RFI opportunities, coordinate the response to these cycles.
  • Obtain and maintain required vendor certifications as required to support Insight certification requirements or goals.
  • Reports to management on a weekly basis an accounting of time engaged in pre-sales activities, billable activities, and professional development and company initiatives.
  • Actively tracks opportunities being worked on, providing regional leadership a weekly report on the revenue, gross profit and services associated with the efforts of the Architect.


What you’ll need to join Insight:

  • Minimum eight (6) years of experience in technical architecture roles or similar function
  • Minimum four (3) years in technical pre-sales roles or similar function
  • A wide technology background with a heavy emphasis on networking and ideally some experience with cloud
  • A strong understanding of room presence, spoken and written communication, ownership, customer proactivity and peer leadership
  • Bachelor’s degree in relevant program of study, or equivalent documented work experience
  • Industry or vendor certifications in four or more Insight practice areas
