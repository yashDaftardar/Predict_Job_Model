SUMMARY

The Sr. Inventory Planner (IP) is responsible for assessing, determining needed actions, executing the developed plan and follow-up facilitating the highest level of inventory availability to their assigned stores and regions. The IP will partner with Category Managers and Inventory Replenishment, Store Planning and Design, and Visual Merchandising, but most closely will partner with the field leadership of assigned regions, up to and including RVP level. The IP will serve as a field liaison on availability-related concerns from store assortment planning to Regional assortment planning, and will be responsible for continuous improvement and process development for all existing and new processes to support initiatives.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES include the following. Other duties may be assigned .

Assortment Management

  • Perform assortment review and analysis, effectively communicate findings and make recommendations to Category Managers and Market Managers to drive positive business results
    • Use store clustering to identify and address gaps in assortment
    • Use Customer Product Now requests as feedback to fine tune assortments
  • Identify trends within business to quickly take action on over-performing/under-performing products to best position the product within the AAP store hierarchy
    • Identify lost sales opportunities and trending
    • Identify store to store transfer opportunities and trending
    • Identify product coverage opportunities and trending
    • SKU assortment expansions based on market specific findings
  • Build collaborative partnerships with Category Managers and Market Managers to develop strategic plans that will drive business performance
    • Product Line Expansions/Conversions
    • Market Driven Availability Initiatives

      Financial Management

  • Understand, effectively communicate and advise business partners on the financial impact of decisions related from assortment upgrades to Market Driven Availability initiatives.

    • Inventory Reduction Strategies

      Communication / Collaborative Relationships

  • Maintain collaborative partnerships with Field Leadership and Market Managers
  • Develop relationships with Category Managers, Strategic Managers, and inventory team through active involvement in business decisions
  • Communicate directly with Store Managers, Hub Managers, Commercial Account Managers, District Managers responding to operational concerns as appropriate
