Full Job Description

Job summary:
The Red Hat Products and Technologies (PnT) Business Insights team is looking for an experienced and well-rounded Senior Manager focused on data science to join us in Raleigh, NC. In this role, you will lead a team of skilled data scientists and engineers building and deploying machine learning (ML) models. You’ll need to be able to understand how to use data to answer complex business questions and to inform decision making. As a Senior Manager, you’ll also need to have experience managing data science teams, direct experience working with large and complex data, and a record of guiding data-informed decisions through cross-functional collaboration.

#LI-NS1
Primary job responsibilities:
  • Set and guide team direction through understanding Red Hat’s objectives and our PnT organizational priorities
  • Create alignment within our broader team and contribute to healthy cross-functional collaboration across Red Hat
  • Develop and improve on processes to manage team progress and ensure that it is resourced correctly
  • Recruit and manage the team to ensure that individuals learn, grow, and advance in their respective careers
Required skills:
  • 5+ years of experience managing data science or engineering teams, preferably within high-tech or enterprise software organizations
  • Solid understanding of statistical methods, theory, and application
  • Passionate about fostering good data science and engineering practices that scale and encourage reproducibility
  • Experience translating business and product questions into applications solvable with data science
  • Experience managing a team that has successfully built and deployed ML models
  • Solid ability to communicate with senior management and distill complexity into easy-to-understand concepts that demonstrate impact
  • Enthusiasm for providing feedback, teaching others, and learning new techniques
  • Experience managing distributed teams and remote associates
  • Experience deploying and maintaining ETL processes and ML models
  • Proficiency in languages like Python, R, and SQL
  • Familiarity with open source software development and open source business models is a plus
  • Familiarity with Red Hat's portfolio of offerings and open source software development is a plus
About Red Hat:
Red Hat is the world’s leading provider of enterprise open source software solutions, using a community-powered approach to deliver reliable and high-performing Linux, hybrid cloud, container, and Kubernetes technologies. Red Hat helps customers integrate new and existing IT applications, develop cloud-native applications, standardize on our industry-leading operating system, and automate, secure, and manage complex environments. Award-winning support, training, and consulting services make Red Hat a trusted adviser to the Fortune 500. As a strategic partner to cloud providers, system integrators, application vendors, customers, and open source communities, Red Hat can help organizations prepare for the digital future.
