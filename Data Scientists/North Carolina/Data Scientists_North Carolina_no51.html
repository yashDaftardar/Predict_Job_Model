Responsibilities:

PRIMARY PURPOSE OF POSITION

The Data Scientist will be responsible for developing robust, predictive statistical models capable of estimating material degradation and corrosion. Furthermore, a major part of the job includes uncertainty quantification. This requires an individual that is exceptionally strong in statistics and possesses the technical aptitude to work with engineers from a variety of disciplines. This candidate will be responsible for developing procedures concerning the gathering of existing data and/or generating new data to achieve project objectives. They should have experience with the capture of data using different tools, and an extensive knowledge on different data analysis and management methods.


ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS

  • Provide detailed information on the results of the research and subsequent meta-analysis
  • Perform research and data analysis focused on identifying:
    • Likelihood of occurrence of degradation/corrosion modes
    • Correlated rates of degradation/corrosion
    • Uncertainties within associated research, empirical models, and inspection / testing methods
  • Train other individuals in the development of predictive models and uncertainty quantification
  • Communicate statistical results to team members who are nonexperts in statistical analyses techniques
OTHER FUNCTIONS
  • Demonstrate the necessary interpersonal skills to thrive in a team-oriented work environment
  • Contribute to a strong collaborative team environment
  • Demonstrate strong follow-through skills with ability to meet deadlines
  • Maintain a positive and professional demeanor while representing FDH
  • Adhere to all policies and procedures of FDH
  • Additional duties as assigned by supervisor
Qualifications:

QUALIFICATIONS

  • Master’s or PhD Degree in Statistics or Applied Mathematics required
  • Minimum of three years of relevant experience. Experience in engineering or technology related field is a plus
  • Ability to obtain Top Secret clearance required .
  • Proficiency in statistical software. (R, JMP/SAS preferred)
  • Proficiency in statistical methods including meta-analysis, density estimation, sample size calculation, inferential statistics, ANOVA, multivariate analysis, linear and non-linear modelling, mixed effect models, and regression
  • Proficiency in the development, assessment, and introduction of statistical methodologies, including experience in statistical approaches (i.e. Frequentist and Bayesian methodologies)
  • Experience in uncertainty quantification of testing methods and predictive models
  • Ability to organize and efficiently analyze large sets of research data
  • Ability to make meaningful inferences from statistical results
  • Ability to work with a team to manage several project tasks simultaneously and meet deadlines in a timely manner
  • Ability to obtain security clearance required
  • Possesses the ability to communicate statistical analyses effectively (verbally and in writing) with all levels of management, employees, and outside contacts
