Full Job Description

Our teams here at Lexis Nexis are combining unparalleled legal and business information with analytics and technology to advance what’s possible for the way our customers work and to advance what’s possible in the world by strengthening the rule of law.

We’re delivering powerful, new decision tools to customers by applying machine learning, natural language processing, visualization, and artificial intelligence to our global legal database. Our vision is to help customers increase productivity, improve decision-making and outcomes, and generate value for their organization.

Growth of our teams means that we now need to add Data Engineers to our AI development team.

You will know you are a good fit if:

  • You believe that high quality data is the most important part of any AI innovation and passionate about using data and technology to make products that delight our customers

  • You enjoy the challenge of tackling complex and difficult problems

  • You know that the best solutions are developed by passionate people collaborating

  • You see every interaction as an opportunity to learn something new

  • You are excited by ETL pipelines

The successful candidate will be focused on the:

  • Design and development of ETL pipelines and data cleanliness solutions within the LexisNexis Data Lake

  • Design and development of pipelines for curation and storage of expert-annotated datasets

  • Creation of data visualization tools to assist the technical and product decision making processes

  • Design of complex data models.

Desired skills and experience:

  • 3+ years’ experience and familiarity with :

    • Deploying AWS solutions using EC2, Redshift, RDS, and S3 AND / OR

    • Provisioning private clouds in Azure using DataBricks as well as non-relational databases e.g. SQL Server, Cosmos, or SQL Data Warehouse

  • Hands-on experience with Athena, DynamoDB, API Gateway, Lambda, and EMR

  • Experience parsing data from XML documents

  • Experience in data modeling, design and manipulation, optimization, and best practices

  • Strong interpersonal, communication, and presentation skills applicable to a wide audience including team members, various levels of management, and end users.

  • Advanced Linux shell expertise. Must be able to analyze loads and tune job scheduling.

  • Familiarity with Natural Language Processing (NLP)

  • Expertise in server backup and recovery

  • Bachelor of Science Degree in Engineering, Mathematics, or Computer Science
