Full Job Description

Job Description:

The functions of this role include applications of data science and advanced analytics for employee benefits such as health, insurance, retirement savings, and other products. The successful candidate will work primarily with technical specialists in data science and technology and with managers of market research, technology, product, marketing, and finance teams across the firm. The assignments may include gathering internal and external data from multiple sources including data warehouses and survey research, data processing, exploratory data analysis, feature engineering, cluster analysis, predictive analytics, design of experiments, unstructured data mining, interpretation of findings, and presentation.

The successful candidate will be capable of supporting projects across many areas with the highest degree of quality. They will have a proven record of assuring quality of data gathering and analysis, be a teammate who thrives in a small, robust work environment and acts with confidence to communicate to all levels of Workplace Investing as well as associates and management from other business units. The ideal Data Scientist for this role should be able to tackle projects from start to finish. I.e., they can work with market research on survey design, capture required data (structured or unstructured) in any shape and size using any programming languages vital, process the data, analyze them graphically and statistically, develop and execute machine learning approaches, draw exciting and unique insights into the business, package the information into relevant and effective presentations, and finally be able to present to senior management. As a data scientist in Fidelity Workplace Investments, you will contribute to advancing the analytics and data science capability for a variety of employee benefit products and will take the organization to the next level.

The Skills You Bring

  • Data extraction and feature engineering skills for extracting, processing, and redefining big and small data into significant features for machine learning models. Proficient in SQL and at least one major relational database management system.
  • Statistical and data science programming with proficiency in Python (Anaconda, 3.0+) and common machine learning libraries. Able to develop organized, intuitive, and documented code in Jupyter and/or an IDE. Experience with R also preferred but not required.
  • Extensive experience with Cluster Analysis and related statistical & data science techniques for customer segmentation, and Predictive Analytics / Machine Learning
  • Familiarity with Text Mining & Natural Language Processing (NLP) such as TF-IDF, embedding, and Transformers
  • Additional knowledge of other quantitative skills preferred, such as: Survey Research Design & Analysis, Econometric Time Series Analysis, Deep Learning, Experimental Design, Causal Inference, Mathematical Programming, Reinforcement Learning, or other related fields.
  • Exploratory data analysis, statistical graphics, and effective data visualization.
  • Highly meticulous with proven track record to ensure quality of sophisticated data and analysis
  • Ability to pick up new knowledge fast and passionate about continuous learning
  • Solid verbal and written communication skills, including the ability to interact with technical professionals and various levels of management from different functions.
  • Proactive and takes initiatives, thinks creatively, drives projects to completion and insists on the highest level of quality

The Value You Deliver

  • Intelligently applying statistics, data science, and sophisticated analytics to support customer needs, improve customer experience, develop new products, and support marketing, sales & technology for employee benefit products
  • Proactively identifying business opportunities and solutions
  • Capturing internal and external data (structured or unstructured) from a variety of sources using any programming languages necessary
  • Processing and analyzing big and complex data as well as unstructured data
  • Researching ground breaking methodologies, techniques, tools, and systems
  • Coauthoring manuscripts of innovative techniques or applications for publication
  • Deriving and implementing the best approaches for supervised and unsupervised learning on structured or unstructured data
  • Interpreting results and drawing key business insights from advanced quantitative analyses
  • Presenting to broader audience and senior management
  • Acquiring or developing innovative methodologies
  • Leading and guiding analytics projects across business groups
