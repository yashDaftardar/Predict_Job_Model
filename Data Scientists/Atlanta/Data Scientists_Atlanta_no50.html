Full Job Description

Cutting-edge, high-profile, game-changing work - just another day as a Business Technology Analyst Intern at Avanade!

Avanade’s Global Data & AI Center of Excellence (CoE) is looking for a Business Technology Analyst Intern who is interested in supporting the incubation and acceleration of new cross Solution Area projects, evolve our go-to-market services and drive our existing offering portfolio development. Our Data & AI CoE is at the forefront of Microsoft AI innovation, keeping pace with the latest, cutting edge technologies. We ensure we continuously bring value to our clients. Our CoE offers constant opportunities for growth and learning, while providing an environment for interns to build skills and experience that will help launch them into exciting careers in Business and Technology.

About You:
  • Adaptable and eager to learn about new technologies and their business value impact
  • A self-starter who shows initiative when faced with new challenges
  • A strong team player and comfortable working with teams of diverse skills and backgrounds
  • Passionate about Data & AI and how it can be used to solve real world challenges
About the Role:
As a Business Technology Analyst Intern, you will support the incubation and acceleration of new cross Solution Area projects, evolve our go-to-market services, and may include both quick-hit initiatives to enhance our existing offering portfolio as well as broader-reaching strategic programs that bring new and innovative capabilities to life for our clients. Through the combination of creative and technology expertise, you will spend your time working with multidisciplinary teams to create highly valuable and impactful solutions for our clients’ biggest business challenges.

Day-to-Day, You Will:
  • Work with clients to understand existing business processes and contribute to new processes and system designs
  • Support Data & AI Leadership in developing our latest strategy for cross Solution Area plays
  • Leverage technology to connect a client’s business needs to the right Avanade solutions
  • Design and build our Data & AI go-to-market and supporting materials
  • Facilitate the adoption of new technologies or offerings for Avanade professionals through the delivery of trainings, guides and/or supporting materials
  • Evaluate and learn about new cloud technologies to provide competitive market perspective
  • Work with Avanade’s business and technology professionals to design compelling user experiences for solutions, demos and accelerators that are delivered/showcased to our clients
Your Skillset and Experience Includes:
  • Capacity to work as an independent individual contributor and cooperative teammate
  • Strategic and critical problem-solving skills
  • Time management and organizational skills
  • Detail-oriented and deadline-driven attitude
  • Passionate about IT projects and learning new technologies
  • Knowledge of or experience planning user research, conducting interviews, shadowing, etc.
  • Strong communications skills (written and verbal) and interpersonal skills
  • Education Background: You are working towards a Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in Business, Information Systems, or another relevant field
