Analyst, Data Science & Analytics

Porsche Financial Services Inc.
Atlanta, GA

Tasks

The Analyst, Data Science & Analytics at Porsche Financial Services, Inc. (PFS) is part of the business unit Risk Management & Controlling and represents the interface between IT and business operations for Porsche Financial Services (PFS), Bentley Financial Services (BFS), Lamborghini Financial Services (LFS) and Bugatti Financial Services (BgFS) in the US and Canada. The primary responsibilities include: Work closely with IT and business operations to understand data structures and requirements, and play a role in defining the reporting and analytics structure for the organization to drive continuous impact. This role will promote analytical thought process and make contribution to reporting, database developments, and transformation of new and legacy algorithms. The ideal candidate will have hands on experience in data querying and reporting with Business Intelligence and in analytical/statistical programming. Experience in building data analysis, insights reporting, scalability and automation is essential. This position reports to the Manager, Credit Risk Management and Analytics.


CHARACTERISTIC DUTIES

  • Create standardized reports for Risk Management & Controlling to drive analytical insights.
  • Act as a subject matter expert for all PFS departments on reporting and query questions and assist with data gathering, extracts, and report development for internal/external clients.
  • Recommend and implement improvements for reports, reporting processes and reporting systems.
  • Provide assistance in statistical analytics projects in Risk.
  • Assist in maintaining and enhancing the availability, integrity, and usability of the Porsche Financial Services central business data warehouse while considering internal data governance policies.
  • Participation in regular IT and Data Governance meetings to ensure compliance of the central business warehouse with external and internal regulation.
  • Work with third party software providers on various data management projects including system conversions and data imports and extracts.

Qualifications

MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS (Education, formal training, experience, specialized knowledge, and special skills.)

  • BS degree in a relevant field (e.g. Analytics, Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Data Science, Engineering, Economics).
  • 2+ total years of experience in analytics and/or data modeling.
  • Experience in developing dashboards and reports in Business Intelligence Tools such as Microstrategy, Tableau or Power BI
  • Exposure in relational databases such as SQL.
  • Proficiency with one or more statistical programming languages such as R or Python
  • Proven project management and time management skills including the ability to prioritize and contribute to multiple assignments simultaneously, setting clear goals, and managing (internal) customer expectations.
  • Ability to communicate and present analytic solutions to a non-technical audience.
  • Proven analytical skills and commitment to detail and accuracy.
  • Experience within Captive Finance Company finance area is a plus
