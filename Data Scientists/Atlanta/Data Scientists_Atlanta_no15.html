Do you want to work in a team that’s at the heart of the Microsoft’s public Elastic Compute and Storage cloud, as well as Microsoft’s private Bing cloud? The three key services a modern cloud offers to its users are deployment, monitoring and repairs. The Azure One Deploy team builds the smarts at the cross roads of all these three. It is responsible for multi-level monitoring of both service and infrastructure health. It dynamically applies the health data to safely stage and orchestrate deployments and repairs for customer and infrastructural services on a global scale, across millions of virtual machines while maintaining high availability guarantees on services themselves. It is used by third party Microsoft customers, first party Microsoft services like HDInsight, SQL Azure, Cosmos and IndexServe, as well as for orchestrating upgrades and repairs to core infrastructural service like compute, networking and storage stacks.

Azure One Deploy System holds the key to unlocking rapid innovation while providing the most up-to-date infrastructure for customers maintaining service availability and quality. The work done in the team has a multiplier effect for the entire company as it delivers on a smart underlying infrastructure for everyone to use. The traditional methods of software delivery cannot keep up with the demand of the developer innovation and business need, and so we are innovating across all aspects of service delivery.

We are looking for talented and motivated data scientists that are interested in making their mark in Azure and join a team with immense growth opportunities! The role of Data Scientist will require you to be innovator and great executor You are expected to be part of agile team and collaborate with partners on ambiguous topics to drive data-science solutions end-to-end.

Responsibilities

  • You’ll experiment, rapidly prototype, and evaluate new data-science solutions that could be used in our current and future products
  • You'll work with partner teams and make them more efficient, more effective, and more fun
  • You’ll deal with business problems in Azure One Deploy and deliver data driven solutions till achieving the impact
  • You’ll provide guidance and recommendations on data science investments across Azure One Deploy.
And most of all, you'll demonstrate honesty, responsibility, integrity, and fulfillment of commitments.

Qualifications

Required Qualifications:
  • 3+ years of experience to implement statistical models, machine learning, and analysis (Recommenders, Prediction, Classification, Clustering, etc.) in big data environment.

Preferred Qualifications:
  • Master’s/PhD degree in Computer Science, Statistic, Mathematic, Economics or related field, or equivalent experience.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and software development habits, including peer code reviews, unit testing, etc.
  • Enjoy dealing with ambiguity and complexity.
  • Ability to independently drive cross team collaborations and ship production features in a fast-paced startup environment.
  • Ability to provide technical leadership to other developers.
