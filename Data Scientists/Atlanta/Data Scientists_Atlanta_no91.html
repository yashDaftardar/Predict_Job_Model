This person can be based out of Atlanta or work 100% remotely if not local.

The Data Scientist, Principle Manager will provide both day-to-day insights and big picture analysis to help optimize performance across all elements of the business while utilizing knowledge and experience in data mining, machine learning, statistical modeling, analytics, and software engineering. Will guide team of Data Scientists in construction of algorithms that will result in massive improvements to the way Aaron’s does business. Responsible for developing and improving key decision systems and predictive models to drive maximum business impact. Will develop a profound understanding of Aaron’s customers, business processes, products, and financials by studying the data that describe each of these elements of the business.


Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Acts as a mentor to other Data Scientists and may perform supervisory duties
  • Builds, maintains, and improves highly complex key decision systems and predictive models
  • Leads the exploration of both internal and external new data sources and utilizes finding to further improve and optimize model performance
  • Designs, implements, and reads in-market experiments testing new algorithms, technologies, and processes
  • Recommends and supports complex strategic business changes through rigorous analytics, deep understanding of the business, and creative problem solving=
  • Spearheads the identification, evaluation, and implementation of emerging advanced analytics technologies and methods
  • Uses data visualization techniques to effectively communicate analytical results and support business decisions
Education and Experience:
  • Master’s degree in a quantitative or technical field (Statistics, Mathematics, Economics, Engineering, Business Analytics, Data Science or a related field) or equivalent work experience.
  • Five (5) years of relevant experience.
  • Experience in consumer finance and/or retail.
  • Experience implementing machine learning models using web services.

Required Skills and Competencies:


  • Possesses strong skills in data exploration and machine learning tools such as those found in R and/or Python.
  • Microsoft Azure platform skills preferred.
  • Demonstrated ability applying advanced statistical modeling techniques to solve problems.
  • Advanced skills with querying languages (SQL, etc.),
  • Demonstrates proficiency in several areas of data modeling, machine learning algorithms, statistical modeling & analysis, data engineering and data visualization.
  • Effectively resolves problems and roadblocks as they occur.
  • Ability to communicate and present highly complex concepts to all levels of management.
  • Strong leadership capability as well as the ability to work cross-functionally.
