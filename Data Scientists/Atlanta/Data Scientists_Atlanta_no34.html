Full Job Description

Do you enjoy collecting, analyzing and transforming data it into decisionable and impactful operational insights?

Are you a PowerBI, Tableau or Excel expert with and interested for developing creative solutions to help us answer some of the most important questions we want answers to benefit our customers? Do you know what ETL means?

As a Technology Data Conqueror, you will be critical to our ability to help locate, analyze and answer these and more questions, on a repeatable basis.

The goal of the Technology Data Conqueror is to answer any question that our hearts desire the answer to. You will achieve this by:

Creating the maps that connect our islands of data
Applying your technical knowledge and visualization skills to build simple / compelling visualizations (dashboards, charts, etc...) and reports that convey information, as well as context, that lead to data-driven decisions
Developing methods and techniques to capture, store, analyze and present a large amounts of data
Collaborate with our developers, product management, support teams and other leaders across Fiserv to produce meaningful insights into our solutions
Engage directly with our teams leaders to address their specific reporting needs and help them be more self-sufficient.
Build repeatable processes for collecting, processing and reporting
Recognize and respond timely to critical situations to ensure that key business processes continue with minimum disruption.
Keep up to date with the latest technology developments on PowerBI, PostgreSQL, Python, Azure SQL, and Azure AI in the cloud.
Contribute to effective development, deployment and adoption of an operational insights platform .
Engaging with our staff and valued clients to advance our analytics and business intelligence initiatives.
Preferred Experience

The list below isn’t exhaustive but rather intended to provide a general idea of what it takes to conquer our data .

They must have at least 5 to 7 years of overall experience in the tech space.
Technology operations and / or finance background
Experience in the Retail / Commercial Banking sector
Experience with Fiserv technology solutions
They often hold an MBA, CIS or business management degree
Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to lead and inspire mid-level managers to drive the outcomes we want.
Entrepreneurial spirit and high desire to work with an innovative team
Must be an analytical thinker with keen problem-solving; process development and improvement; as well as task planning and delegation skills.
Must be able to actively participate in meetings with C-Suite executives and contribute unique perspectives and value

Required Qualifications for Consideration:

5+ years of experience working with business technology and support teams

Strong attention to detail and ability to work independently in a fast-paced environment
