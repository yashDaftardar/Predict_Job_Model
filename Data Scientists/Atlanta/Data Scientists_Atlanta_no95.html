Senior Principal – Data & Analytics – Infosys Consulting

Infosys Consulting’s AI & A Practice is seeking an experienced Business Analytics Leader at a Senior Principal Level.

About the Role
Organizations are seeking to leverage Data, Analytics and Artificial Intelligence to create actionable insights that help increase revenue and reduce costs. As Business Analytics Leader, your role would be to plan and deliver relevant transformation programs for clients. The Business Analytics Leader leverages multiple capabilities: Business Analytics, Big Data and cloud solutions, Data Warehousing, Visual Stories, Predictive Analytics, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Master Data Management and Data Governance.

Responsibilities:
  • Senior Principal is part of the practice leadership team.
  • Work with client to drive transformation programs around Business Analytics, Big Data and cloud solutions, Data Warehousing, Visual Stories, Predictive Analytics, and Data Governance
  • Lead strategic transformation programs across all stages: Strategy, roadmap & business case definition, Design and implementation programs
  • Lead proposal development and pursuits for multimillion dollar programs. Manage pipelines from opportunity identification to contract
  • Lead project teams. Mentor, coach and develop consultants.
  • Contribute Practice Development: Identify data and analytics trends in the industry. Define relevant consulting offers and solutions. Author Point of Views and publish in industry journals

Required Skills:
  • Bachelor’s degree or foreign equivalent required. Will also consider three years of progressive experience in the specialty in lieu of every year of education.
  • At least 9 years of relevant work experience with at least three years of experience in comparable consulting services.
  • Ability to travel 4 days a week to multiple local, state and national client locations.
  • U.S. citizens and those authorized to work in the U.S. are encouraged to apply. We are unable to sponsor at this time.

Preferred Skills:
  • Experience in at least one industry sector: Consumer Goods, Retail, Manufacturing, Industrial or Energy
  • Experience in at least one functional area: Marketing, Sales, Customer Service, Customer Experience or Supply Chain
  • Experience in Strategy-to-Implementation programs related to Data and Analytics programs, including cloud-based programs
  • Experience in doing design thinking workshops for clients
  • Experience with Agile, Scrum methodologies

This job entails an extensive amount of travel. The job also entails sitting as well as working at a computer for extended periods of time. Candidate should be able to communicate by telephone, email or face-to-face.

Please note this description does not cover or contain a comprehensive listing of activities, duties or responsibilities that are required of the employee.
