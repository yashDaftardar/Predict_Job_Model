Cox Communications is looking for a Director of Data Science who will be a core member of the Analytics Center of Excellence (ACoE) reporting to the Executive Director of Analytics. This role will be instrumental in the long-term growth initiative of defining and building out advanced analytics capabilities. The Director is charged with supporting innovation within our Analytics CoE, developing opportunities in the whitespace, mobilizing a high performing analytics team, contributing to client teams as an analytics capabilities subject matter expert in order to optimize the Cox Communications capital allocation, operating expense and revenue.

Responsibilities:

  • Lead efforts to produce value from Cox Communications structured/unstructured data assets utilizing advanced analytics methodologies in AWS
  • Identify and oversee efforts to devise capabilities/frameworks/standards to accelerate and scale ACoEs analytics offerings
  • Manage enterprise grade analytics solutions throughout the analytics lifecycle to ensure value, quality, and timeliness
  • Supervise, grow, and mentor a high performing and engaged analytics team. Establish team practices to drive clear communication, visibility, collaboration, and accountability
  • Brainstorm with internal/external stakeholders to identify opportunities to advance data driven decision making, automation, and to streamline business processes
  • Convey advanced analytics solutions/technology to broader teams including non-technical audiences via presentations and reports. Publish, as applicable, white/conference papers
  • Establish and improve partnerships. Effectively communicate with partners to remove blockers and propel delivery
  • Liaise with key stakeholders across Cox Communications to champion analytics

Qualifications:

Minimum:

  • Graduate degree in Data Science, Computer Science, Analytics, Statistics, Engineering or closely related field
  • 7+ years of experience in analytics role in machine learning, artificial intelligence, research, predictive analytics, business analytics or similar area
  • 5+ years of experience designing and managing analytical projects/teams
  • Strong programming skills and ability to utilize a variety of data/analytic software/languages/tools; e.g., Python, SageMaker, Spark, Tensorflow, Docker, R, Gremlin, Scala, Java, SQL, Tableau, etc.
  • Experience developing and managing enterprise grade analytics solutions using Cloud platforms (i.e. AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, etc.) at every stage of the analytics lifecycle from ideation to production
  • Strong communication skills. The ability to comprehend and communicate advanced analytics artifacts, business insights, and resulting implications to non-technical business partners. Ability to work in a diverse team
  • Deep understanding of data domains, models, and implications for analytics use in at least one industry including telecommunications, cable, finance, media, advertising, or similar

Preferred:

  • Experience within telecommunications, cable industry, and high tech
  • Demonstrated experience in a consultant role
  • Practical experience applying advanced analytics approaches to mass transaction reduction and customer experience optimization
  • Experience in data visualization solutions and data visualization tools
