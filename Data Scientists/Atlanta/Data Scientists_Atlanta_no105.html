Senior Manager, Data Science

  • Responsible for development and management of predictive models.
  • Perform advanced data/multivariate analysis and data studies.
  • Design, simulate and implement new code.
  • Provide leadership in model development, data science and decision making processes to improve business performance.
  • Partner with IT, BI, and other business units to deliver improvements to current underwriting processes and systems.


Requirements: Master’s in Data Science, Economics, Engineering, Computer Science or closely related.

Min. 5 years’ exp. in advanced data analysis; multivariate analysis; interpreting results of data analysis; statistics; econometrics.

Min. 3 years’ exp. in logistic regression; statistical techniques; machine learning; artificial intelligence algorithms; and predictive model development (Python, R, or SAS).

Job Site: Atlanta, GA; 40 hours/ week, M-F. Telecommuting permitted from anywhere in the U.S. Apply online, or submit letter/resume to aarons.com/careers or to Tanya Zeman, Talent Acquisition Partner at Tanya.Zeman@aarons.com.
