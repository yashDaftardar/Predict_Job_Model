Job Summary:

FieldCore is seeking an energetic, committed intern to join our growing organization. As a FieldCore intern you will partner with the cross functional teams to build and execute world-class projects, programs and solutions. You will provide full service support in the areas of building business/market cases for novel solutions with consideration for the growth of our company and ever-changing market dynamics.

The Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence Intern will work to help FieldCore use the techniques of machine learning (ML) to solve various business problems. This can involve helping build the ML model, extracting and transforming historical and other input data to feed the ML model, and delivering the output of the ML model back to the user in a usable format.

Two use cases currently in progress are:

  • Project Nostradamus – Using Machine Learning for a Safer FieldCore. This uses historical data patterns to predict the risk of safety incidents at future jobs. Input data can consist of past safety incidents, timecard data, job site information, work scope, worker skills and job history, etc.
  • Tool Kit Management – Using ML to construct the optimal tool kit for upcoming jobs and predicting the turnaround time for such kits. Input data can consist of the location and scope of the job, past tools requisition data, warehouse inventory data, etc.

You may also be asked to explore the feasibility of using Machine Learning for other business use cases, including searching for new use cases not yet discovered.

Essential Duties & Responsibilities:

  • Understanding candidate use cases and the desired goals of using ML to solve them.
  • Researching the types of business data required for the use cases, and how to retrieve it.
  • Building, testing, and showcasing machine learning models to satisfy user requirements.
  • Documenting the logic and architecture of the ML model and data processing programs
  • Ensuring all data and I.P security and privacy requirements are met, including completing any necessary paperwork.
  • Providing regular updates, attending meetings, ensuring project schedules are being met.
  • Following up with business users once ML solutions are deployed.
  • Travel may be required
  • English proficiency required
