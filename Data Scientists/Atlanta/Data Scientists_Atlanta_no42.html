The Search, Data Science & Analytics (SDA) team is looking for a Sr Data Scientist for query understanding and knowledge graph. You will work with team members and cross functional partners and leverage machine learning, deep learning, NLP, graph algorithms and Big Data technologies (Spark, NoSQL databases, Distributed Real-time Systems), cloud platforms such as Azure ML, and general purpose & scripting languages such as Scala/Python/R to drive innovative and impactful Search, Recommender Systems and Personalization customer experiences for KP Digital.

Some of the problems that you will work on:

  • Build and manage a knowledge graph to store concepts in medical content and entities (including disease, symptoms, etc. ), taxonomy information (ICD-10-CM, RxNorm), and their relationships. Standardize data that’s stored in the knowledge base: ensure correctness, completeness and freshness of the data

  • Apply graph algorithms on the knowledge graph to discover latent insights to power analytics, search and recommendations.

  • Build and tune machine learning models to interpret and understand search queries to help retrieve relevant results. Search queries can vary from exact product search e.g. “KP office hours” to more natural language queries such as “pediatrician near me who speaks spanish”, “how do I treat an upset stomach”.

  • Models to determine query intent (is the customer looking for general healthcare information, or a specific symptom diagnosis, doctor information) , type ahead, autocomplete, spellchecking and query rewriting.

You will also: Design and interpret experiments intelligently to inform the product roadmap and own the end-to-end solution all the way to roll-out. Conduct deep dives into new research areas to solve tough customer problems. Provide technical leadership and mentorship to the data science and engineering community. Be a team player with strong empathy for our internal and external customers. Publish patents and research papers and represent KP Data Science at conferences.

Designs, develops and programs methods, processes, and systems to consolidate and analyze unstructured, diverse 'big data' sources to generate actionable insights and solutions for clinical and non-clinical operations enhancements. Interacts with mission- based teams to identify questions and issues for data analysis and experiments. Develops and codes software programs, algorithms and automated processes to cleanse, integrate and evaluate large datasets from multiple disparate sources. Identifies, interprets and communicates meaningful insights from analysis and experiments to managers.

Essential Responsibilities:

  • Design, develop, and evaluate state-of-the-art machine learning models for complex, large-scale data sets.

  • Optimize machine learning models to scale in real-time streaming applications.

  • Advanced experience with time series analysis, discrete data modeling and feature engineering.

  • Embrace a fast paced, collaborative environment dedicated to building cutting-edge technology solutions using machine learning.

  • Advanced application of statistical and machine learning techniques to provide clinical decision support as well as improve auditing and standards of care.

  • Works on problems of moderate scope where analysis of situations or data requires a review of a variety of factors.

  • Exercises judgment within defined procedures and practices to determine appropriate action.

  • Coordinates solution development with machine learning scientists, physicians, and other stakeholders.

  • Work closely with software engineers to facilitate model integration and deployment.

  • Works extensively with external stakeholders (physicians et al.).

  • Deliver insights and values from heterogeneous data to investigate complex problems in various domains for multiple use cases.

  • Seek out open-source software packages or existing software code that can be reused or applied to assigned tasks.

  • Drives informed decision-making and present findings to both technical and non-technical audiences.

  • Integrate and deploy machine learning components in a production environment.

  • Collaborate with stakeholders to identify domain relevant use cases, develop machine learning models, and validate impact.

  • Understands market and opportunities.

  • Serves as lead to lower level Specialist(s) on assigned projects.
