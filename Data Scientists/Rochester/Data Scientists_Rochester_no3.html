Job Description

Position Summary: As part of the Data & Analytics Team this role is both business and technologically focused to support the execution of Constellation Brands Ecommerce strategy. This role is responsible for the creation and maintenance of policies and best practices related to data sharing with Ecommerce vendors and retailers. The role involves collaboration with data management, platform solutions, and integration teams to align requirements for Ecommerce needs. Additional responsibilities include project management, data analytics, error resolution for data gaps between source and target systems and working directly with external vendors.

Responsibilities

  • Develop, document, and maintain datasets for sharing of master data to CBI Ecommerce platforms.
  • Research and create automated processes to obtain and publish data that consumers want visible on retailer’s digital shelf.
  • Continuously review and improve datasets to meet ever evolving consumer needs such as nutritional facts and accolades.
  • Develop Data Quality Audits for new datasets and perform regular data reviews to ensure integrity of the data.
  • Communicate data issues and resolution to technical and business partners. Escalate data-related issues to the Data & Analytics Leadership team that have significant business impacts.
  • Partner with Legal, Marketing, and Public Relations to develop processes to mask, hide or remove sensitive data elements that should not be shared with certain parties.
  • Create SQL scripts and source mapping of data for data sharing APIs and reference catalogs.
  • Manage and lead various projects related to Ecommerce, Data Sharing, and Master Data.
  • Partner with other Data & Analytics teams to align data sharing requirements with the appropriate master and transactional data needed for the specified use cases.
  • Continuously develop company-wide business relationships through alignment of the Data & Analytics capabilities and best practices.
  • Ensure data governance standards, policies and procedures are followed for all data practices.
  • Monitor and enforce internal service level agreements in support of business data requirements.

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer or Information Systems, Business Management, or equivalent experience.
  • 3+ years of experience with data domains, data sharing, or data governance principles.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office, SQL, Informatica, and SAP preferred.
  • Ability to lead and work on multiple projects concurrently with shifting priorities.
  • Strong aptitude for learning new business processes, technologies, and frameworks.
  • Team player with positive attitude and able to work with a variety of people at all organizational levels.
  • Wine or Beer industry, three-tier system, or consumer packaged goods industry experience are a plus.
  • Spanish, Italian, or other language a plus.

Core Competencies to be Successful:

  • Initiative and results oriented
  • Communicating effectively
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Analytic and research oriented
  • Process discipline and quality
  • Ability to build and maintain productive relationships

Physical Requirements/Work Environment

  • Must be at least 21 years of age. Must be able to sit and/or stand for long periods of time and work on a computer for extended periods. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
