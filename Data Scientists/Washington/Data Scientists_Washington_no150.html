Today, more than ever, artificial intelligence (AI) is changing the way we live, travel, and do business. It is one of the key technologies that Microsoft depends on to “empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more”.
The Azure Special Capabilities, Infrastructure, and Innovation (ASCII) team is building products and services that use machine learning, big data, and AI to help address customers’ needs.

We’re looking for an experienced, engineering-minded data scientist who is passionate about data (in all its forms) and is skilled at using AI to address a wide range of real-world challenges.

In this role, you will collaborate with a team of experts in deep learning, machine learning, and distributed systems. You will work on all aspects of the design, development, and delivery of deep learning-enabled solutions, including problem definition, data acquisition, testing and evaluation, and deploying end-to-end pipelines and solutions in production on Azure. You will apply these skills towards the development ofinnovativesolutions fortheDefense and Intelligencecommunity.

This role will require you to be an innovator. You would join a team that owns product direction, technical architecture, and business strategy around the deployment of AI and machine learning workloads for Mission customers. You would also play a crucial role in bringing new technologies to market and ensuring that amazing tech makes its way from the lab to the market over and over again.

You will work in an agile environment that values creativity, knowledge sharing, and employee growth. We seek out new and different perspectives, exercise curiosity, and embrace a culture of learning. You will have substantial opportunity to shape technical direction, make a tangible and positive impact, and obtain unique and valuable experience across an array of technologies.

This role is central to Microsoft's strategy, which is to build best-in-class platforms and productivity services for an intelligent cloud and an intelligent edge infused with Artificial Intelligence. At Microsoft, we believe in empowering every person and organization on the planet to achieve more. We believe in encouraging the development of AI applications that are fair, accountable, transparent and ethical.

Responsibilities


  • You’ll experiment, rapidly prototype, and evaluate AI new technologies that could be used in our current and future products
  • You’ll say abreast ofnewAzure servicesandbea technical scout for the organization
  • You’ll provide guidance and recommendations on technology investments and applications between Azure and the space sector.
  • You will work with dev leads, researchers, customers, and other Microsoft teams to plan and implement new AI platforms and capabilities.
  • You'll deliver AI software products that solve real-world problems, inspire customers, and generate buzz
  • You'll validate design and/or technology choices using empirical methodologies
  • You'll make partner teams more efficient, more effective, and more fun
  • And most of all, you'll demonstrate honesty, responsibility, integrity, and fulfillment of commitments.

Qualifications

Required Qualifications:

  • A Masters and/or PhD degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Physics, Math, Linguistics, or another technical field (or 2+ years’ experience working in machine learning in a professional setting)
  • 2+ years of experience building advanced analytics solutions in the industry using techniques from machine learning, deep learning, data science, statistics, optimization, large scale computing etc.
  • Experience creating, developing, delivering, deploying, maintaining online services

Ability to meet Microsoft, customer and/or government security screening requirements are required for this role. These requirements include, but are not limited to the following specialized security screenings:

  • Candidates must have an active TS and be willing to upgrade to TS/SCI (with polygraph) or have an active TS/SCI and be willing to upgrade to TS/SCI (with polygraph). This role will require candidates to maintain the TS/SCI (with polygraph) clearance.
  • Microsoft Cloud Background Check: This position will be required to pass the Microsoft Cloud background check upon hire/transfer and every two years thereafter.


Desired Qualifications:

  • Technical knowledge on Cloud (could be Azure, AWS, GCP) - Public/Private Cloud, IaaS, PaaS, services etc.
  • You're passionate about machine learning, data science, and/or artificial intelligence
  • You understand the current frontiers of AI algorithms at a deep level, and the principles of advanced statistics and machine learning.
  • You have demonstrated experience in any of the deep learning frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow, etc.)
  • You have system-level knowledge of deep learning (such as GPU or FPGA implementations) a plus
  • You are a capable software developer in one or more high level languages (C/C++/Java/C#) and one or more scripting languages (Python/R/Javascript)
  • You have a track record of publishing in related fields (deep learning, machine intelligence, data mining, etc.)
  • You have worked deeply for several years with fast moving, high-performing engineering teams.
  • You are passionate about helping developers connect people and communities through the cloud.
  • You're a self-starter that can manage and prioritize initiatives that will make our developers and platform the most successful.
  • You possess the ability to work well within a diverse, geographically distributed, multi-cultural environment
  • You can travel to Microsoft sites or customer sites when required
