Senior Occupancy Planning Data AnalystSeattle, WA

JLL is seeking a Senior Occupancy Planner with a strong analytical skillset to join a specific JLL client account in the Pacific Northwest. This position requires a hybrid skillset of create/design focused occupancy and space planning experience, along with a heavy emphasis on the analytical and data utilization/visualization components of occupancy management.

Our client has multiple locations across the PNW market. This role will require an individual on-site at the clients' corporate facility. Manages the supply, capacity and demand of a building or campus portfolio of space for a specific client at the business unit level.

The Job
Facilitates communication between Jones Lang LaSalle Occupancy Planning and Corporate Real Estate organization.
Ensures accurate reporting of information, and foster critical relationships between various individuals and organizations, communication channels will exist with: Move Coordination team to review and approve moves, develop migration plan and conduct facility audits.
Business Unit leadership to collect headcount forecasts, resolve headcount forecast discrepancies between leadership’s numbers and those collected at the group-level.
Corporate Finance group to gather top-down projections.
Corporate Real Estate to review and resolve planning forecasts, needs, concerns, and issues.
Collaborate with Project Management, Facility Management and Transactions on all Site Rationalization Efforts.
Project management to review stacking strategies and to provide workload forecast related to project moves that are planned to occur based on occupancy plans/strategy.
Other Occupancy Planners to ensure cross region strategy plans are developed.

Essential Functions
Within the portfolio, can answer: how much space is currently available, what are the workplace conditions, and what are headcount projections by business unit.
Provide a single point of contact for planning related questions within region.
Prepare/present summarized findings and recommendations for action or consideration by the client.
Report the status of major initiatives affecting planning decisions for the sites.
Participate in development of migration plans and sequencing of group-level moves / relocations.
Facilitate and/or resolve planning issues identified in the customer planning meetings.
Ensure planning is aligned with corporate guidelines, and that all scenarios maximize use of space.
Recommend, educate and enforce space policies/standards, procedures and protocols and notify client of customer requested exceptions.
Measure, monitor and report cost saving and value added contributions.
Assist in developing site-specific planning strategies with Senior Leadership.
Report and compare actual seat demand versus projected seat demand at the building level.
As necessary, participate in project and move coordination planning meetings.
Review building specific stacking plans.
Report the current supply and capacity, and forecasts inventory changes for a rolling three-year timeframe.

Sound like you? To apply you need to possess:
Bachelor’s Degree in Architecture, Construction Management, Design, or related field
5+ years experience in a corporate environment, specifically with experience in space/occupancy data analysis and management
Proficiency in a range of information technology tools and platforms.
Strong analytical, organizational, and presentation skills.
Excellent written and verbal skills.
Ability to understand complex ideas and communicate those ideas to others on the account.
Ability to solve problems individually and in a team setting.
Previous experience in space/occupancy planning at a strategic level, plus experience with interior office renovations.
