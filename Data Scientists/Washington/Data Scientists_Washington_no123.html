Job Description

This position is with the service now Capacity Planning and Analytics Team. The Capacity Planning and Analytics team is responsible for:

  • Global Capacity Demand Planning
  • Predictive modeling and forecasting
  • Data visualization design and delivery
  • Data Analytics
  • Data Engineering
  • Anomaly detection
  • Collecting, storing and real time access to large amount of data

The Capacity Planning and Analytics team is a foundation layer in the Service now Global Cloud Services organization. As part of the Cloud Capacity Engineering team our mission is to deliver data driven insights, actionable intelligence, predictive forecasts, and turnkey capacity plans for the timely fulfillment of server and data center capacity to our global infrastructure.

What you get to do in this role:


  • Actively contribute to design and implementation of data science and data analytics related projects for cloud capacity engineering.
  • Develop, optimization and improvement, supply and demand models using predictive algorithms to forecast capacity demand with data accuracy.
  • Design and develop engaging data visualizations and dashboards
  • Partner with Global Cloud Services teams to drive capacity planning initiatives and strategies
  • Aid with data collection, sanitization, normalization, querying and storage of capacity data.
  • Provide technical guidance for the development and optimization of capacity planning tools and automations


Qualifications

To be successful in this role you have:

  • Demonstrated expertise in a data science, predictive modeling, exploratory and causal data analysis
  • Experience using programming languages SQL/Python/R manipulate data and draw insights from large data sets.
  • Proven ability to develop interactive data visualizations and dashboards with Tableau or other BI tools
  • Experience in machine learning techniques (clustering, decision tree learning, artificial neural networks, etc.) and knowledge of their real-world advantages/drawbacks
  • Statistical analysis, forecasting and Mathematical Modeling.
  • Required MS Degree or PHD in Computer Science, Data Science or equivalent experience
  • 5+ years of overall experience with at least 2 years in Data Science, supply chain, or capacity planning positions

Desired Technical Skills

  • Solid understanding of data structures, algorithms, object-oriented design and patterns
  • Experience building highly scalable solutions in data storage, real-time analysis and reporting for multi-terabyte data sets using technologies like: Impala, Hive, Spark and Hadoop environment in general.
  • Experience with supply chain or financial accounting
  • Ability to learn quickly in a fast-paced, dynamic team environment.
  • Highly effective communication and collaboration skill
