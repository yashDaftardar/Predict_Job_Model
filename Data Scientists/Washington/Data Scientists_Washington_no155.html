Our goal is to grow a data-driven culture across all teams within the division. We are in the process of evolving a platform and the required tools to enable such a culture.

We are looking for a highly skilled and experienced data engineer/analyst/scientist to play the role of a data architect, who is passionate about big data, machine learning and insights that data can provide. Your job will entail diving through mountains of data and extracting insights to help us understand and drive product changes, to provide a great experience to our customers and to enable them to make the right decisions for their company. You would be expected to engage across the product, collaborate with partner data science teams outside of Identity, and drive collaboration and technical excellence within our data science community. You would also establish the “data vision” for the broader Identity team.

Do you love big data and the insights it provides? Do you like the idea of applying your technical and analytical skills to help millions of Microsoft customers? Do you want be part of team of smart, talented and mission driven people? Then this job is for you!

Responsibilities

  • Utilize Machine Learning to guide the development of data science models with large data stores across multiple platforms
  • Cultivate a data driven culture in driving product improvements across the organization and guiding business direction.
  • Contribute to data science infrastructure design and dev ops practices for in-product ML models
  • Build out automation, scale, and end to end "productization" of ML Operations.
  • Build a strong community between Data communities across our various teams in Microsoft.

Qualifications

Required Qualifications:
  • At least 8+ years of experience with Data Analysis/Machine Learning to help drive business decisions.
  • Experience with analytics and finding patterns and relationships in Big data.
  • Experience with cross-team collaboration
  • Experience with Data Analytics Languages such as R, Python, etc..
  • 2+ years of experience as a Data Architect in an enterprise level environment
Preferred Qualifications:
  • MS/PhD in computer science, machine learning, statistics, or quantitative domain
  • Demonstrated expertise applying machine learning to big datasets.
  • Proficiency in querying languages/big data systems (SQL, Hadoop, Spark, etc.)
