Summary

Posted: May 6, 2021
Weekly Hours: 40
Role Number: 200221362
Apple's Machine Learning Platform & Technology team is seeking an experienced program manager to help bring ML solutions to everyone. You will help us maximize the adoption and capabilities of Apple's ML solutions while minimizing the effort for ML development. To do this, you will partner with engineering teams that deliver cutting-edge performant machine learning frameworks, platforms, tools, and services while inherently respecting and actively protecting customer privacy. This role requires a proactive and motivated individual with strong communication, organization, and relationship-building skills who works well in a fast-paced, ambiguous and dynamic environment. As a member of the AI/ML EPM team, you will be responsible for guiding the team to better accomplish the mission of accelerating Machine Learning adoption.
Key Qualifications
  • 10+ years as a Product, Project or Program Manager on large-scale customer-facing feature development.
  • 3-5+ years of experience as a people manager leading teams, developing and growing their careers
  • A self-motivated and proactive problem solver who identifies problems, proposes and implements well socialized solutions, and delegates appropriately
  • Excellent collaborator with ability to influence without authority, positively motivate and rally teams behind solutions
  • Has shipped multiple large (multiple months of development) software feature to customers in a principal leadership role through the entire life cycle (planning, executing, and shipping).
  • Has been in a role that requires working cross-functionally with multiple teams; software and hardware engineering as well as other PMs, privacy, design, security, marketing, legal, and others.
  • Has been in a role that owns providing regular executive-level project and program status in both written and verbal form.
  • Knowledge of software engineering best practices and related dependencies.
  • Have a spotless record of reliability and integrity representing your products, program, and engineering organization; a demonstrable history of delivering what you promise when you promise it.
  • Preferred Qualifications:
  • Experience shipping multiple global products at a large company where software is one of their primary products.
  • Professional experience working on products or projects involving machine learning; especially on-device ML or hardware-optimized ML, open-source ML products like PyTorch & TensorFlow, and training ML models.
Description
You will be expected to identify, facilitate and drive the right cross-functional conversations to make Apple’s ML platform roadmaps both focused and compelling to internal and external ML teams of all levels of expertise. You will be driving collaboration and communication efforts both inside and outside of Apple. Your portfolio will cover multiple areas, including features with UI elements; client and server work; focus on internal & external adoption of Apple’s ML technologies; better collaboration with Apple Security, Privacy, and HW teams, and team-level representation of MLPT efforts. You will need to build and maintain new and existing relationships across Apple AI/ML organization, first-party App teams, the OS teams, relevant HW (Apple Neural Engine) teams, Privacy & Security, Marketing & Evangelism & Worldwide Developer Relations, Legal, and other Apple programs. Responsibilities include fostering trust and collaboration, negotiating alignment of priorities, and communicating and driving completion of dependencies. Making sure that good decisions are made by the right people is your priority. You will lead a team of EPMs, developing and growing their careers, skillfully delegating and providing directional guidance on their work items. You will provide comprehensive and effective leadership of large projects and features from inception through delivery; overseeing specifications, scope, requirements, timelines, and resource allocation. This leadership includes communicating planning, development status, and executive reviews. This role is expected to be very autonomous and to constantly turn ideas into action and creatively overcome obstacles.
