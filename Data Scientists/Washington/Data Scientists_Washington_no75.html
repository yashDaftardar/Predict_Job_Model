Machine Learning Engineering Intern - Bothell, WA


Internships are a great way for university students to learn about a technology or product they are passionate about while gaining practical work experience in a corporate environment. At Fujifilm Sonosite, we are committed to finding talented students who share our passion for medical device technology and this internship reflects that commitment! We have the opportunity, you have the talent! Read on to learn more about what we are looking for and if you think you’ve got what it takes to be successful in this role, apply today!


FUJIFILM, Sonosite, Inc . We are an innovative and high performance culture with tremendous opportunities. People who work here are given the unique opportunity to contribute to the advancement of healthcare throughout the world. Headquartered in Bothell, WA USA, Fujifilm Sonosite is represented by a global distribution network in over 100 countries. Since its inception, Fujifilm Sonosite’s lightweight, robust products have created and led the point-of-care ultrasound market. From sophisticated urban hospital emergency rooms to clinics in austere, remote villages, Fujifilm Sonosite systems are used by over 21 medical specialties and provide clinicians around the world with a cost-effective tool for improving patient safety and workflow efficiency. Fujifilm Sonosite holds a number of prestigious design awards and has in excess of 145 patents.

Job Summary:

The intern will assist the AI Technology team during the summer months of 2021. They will research state of the art approaches to machine learning, run experiments on AI models to improve performance, label and manage data and report on results achieved.


Essential Job Functions:

  • Become familiar with basic ultrasound imaging
  • Research state of the art approaches to machine learning solutions
  • Run various experiments on Deep Learning models
  • Learn about existing Deep Learning solutions
  • Research state of the art solutions to DL problems
  • Document experimental results

Knowledge and Experience:

  • Working towards BS in Electrical Engineering or Computer Science undergraduate program with an interest in artificial intelligence.
  • Prefer some knowledge of Deep Learning
  • Linear algebra
  • Strong object oriented Python programming skills
  • Knowledge of computer vision algorithms preferred
  • GIT familiarity is desired
  • Prefer some experience with TensorFlow, but not required.
  • Jupyter notebook experience
  • C++ experience desired
  • Android development a plus

Skills and Abilities:

  • Strong problem solving skills
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills.
  • Ability to communicate effectively both within other groups in
  • Engineering and throughout the company.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, small team environment with minimal supervision.
  • Excellent organizational and time management skills
  • Ability to lift 25 pounds, with or without accommodations.
