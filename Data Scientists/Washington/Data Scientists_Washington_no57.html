Role and Responsibilities

Position Summary and Project Details:

This summer you will have the exciting opportunity to work in our Data Science team as Data Science Intern. You will:

  • Develop machine-learning solutions for business needs.
  • Develop and enhance DS deployment framework.
  • Get hands on with Deep learning.

General Duties include but are not limited to:

Data Scientists are responsible for managing the successful design and production of major data initiatives across all customer-facing channels. They provide a critical link between the most technical areas of the organization and our business partners who use data science products to personalize customer experiences. Balancing a deep technical understanding with excellent business acumen is needed to 1) successfully build never-been-done-before solutions in a Big Data environment and 2) interface with Product Management and Engineering teams.

By the end of the summer you should be able to:

  • Handle vast amount of data and perform EDA, PCA, data massaging and cleaning.
  • Extract insights from data that helps in model building.
  • Feature engineer for machine learning problems. Train and test ML algorithms on real Samsung data.
  • Get hands on with Deep learning – RNN, CNN.
  • Build deep learning framework for model deployment.
  • Communicate to senior management about the awesome work you performed.

If you are interested in a career in:

In today’s fast evolving technology world, one aspect remains common – reliance on data to drive the next wave of innovation. The Data Science team’s core focus is developing best-in-class solutions that provide Samsung’s marketing and service organizations with a 360 degree view of the Samsung customers. We are looking for highly technical team members who are passionate about data, have the rigor needed to solve billion dollar problems, and possess an innate entrepreneurial spirit to explore the uncharted. Our organization combines the engineering backbone of a best-in-class Big Data Platform with the analytic expertise of predictive and prescriptive modeling. If you want to work among the very best talent in the industry, working on the most innovative products in the world, Samsung is the place to be.

Skills and Qualifications

Qualifications:

  • Programming experience: Python, Spark, R, SQL, Hive, JavaScript, Visual Basic, C++
  • Machine learning expertise: GLM Regression (Linear, Logit, Multinomial), Decision Tree (including Boosted Trees, Random Forest), kMeans/Hierarchical Clustering, Principle Component Analysis, Neural Network, Bayesian Regression, & Times Series
  • Experience using data with high-volume (1TB+) & high-dimensionality (500+ variables per schema), especially within a Hadoop framework
  • Strong interest in supporting consumer-facing functions for consumer products, retail, tech, or telecom industries
  • Self-starter. Self-motivated. Wanting to learn. Can work alone, as well as being a member of a team. Result-Driven person
  • Good verbal and written communication skills or reasonable communications skills for Bi-Lingual speakers
