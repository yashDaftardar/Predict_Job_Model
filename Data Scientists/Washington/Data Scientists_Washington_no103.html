Do you want to be part of a high visibility startup team that is determined to improve the lives of one billion users and become the next big consumer business for Microsoft?
We are a newly formed, fast-growing startup in Microsoft’s WebXT organization, home to Microsoft’s major AI-powered products and businesses such as Bing and Ads. Our mission is to connect students, parents, and educators around the world using a combination of our search, AI and collaboration tools to create high quality outcomes. We are looking for excellent engineers to develop, deliver, and iterate upon our first product offerings in this key space.
For this role, we seek an experienced applied scientist who loves building and driving the rapid execution of a v1 product that aims to solve our customer and educator’s needs. As a part of this role, you will work closely with a global team of data scientists, software engineers, machine learning engineers and product managers to build a best-in-class service that wins the hearts and minds of end users.
If we've caught your interest, keep reading!

Responsibilities

We’re looking for a person with product-centric experience who is ready to jump in and be a key contributor on the team, to:
  • Thinking through the product scenarios and goals, identify key challenges and design experimental process for iteration and optimization.
  • Offline model training and optimizing, collaborating with platform team on online serving and perf optimization for shipping.
  • Keeping on track of research trends in the fields of personalized recommendation, deep learning, and AI.
  • Work independently and collaboratively with other research teams and product teams.
  • Define, design, and implement complex services and experiences with an emphasis on quality, performance, simplicity, scalability, and maintainability.
  • Work closely with our PM and design teams to define feature specifications and build the next generation of products.
  • Build engaging end-to-end customer experiences across various Microsoft ecosystems.
  • Have fun and learn new things!
A qualified candidate should have a proven track record of shipping software, should have excellent technical knowledge, excellent communication, and cross-group/discipline collaboration skills. In addition, a successful candidate should have experience in Microsoft methodologies and tools development.

Qualifications

A successful candidate will have the following:
Required Qualification
  • BS in Computer Science, Engineering and/or equivalent industry experience on machine learning and AI.
  • 3+ years’ experiences in areas of machine learning, natural language processing or large-scale data mining.
  • Solid coding skill and good experience on deep learning framework like PyTorch, TensorFlow, CNTK, etc. along with software development proficiency in Python, C# or Java.
  • 3+ years of overall industry experience.
  • 2+ years of experience with SQL or NoSQL databases.
Preferred Qualification
  • Great at problem solving and analytical skills.
  • Excellent communicator, capable of building and maintaining relationships with various Microsoft teams.
  • Related experiences in search/recommendation algorithms, online education industry, consumer/marketplace data analytics are big pluses.
  • Knowledge on cloud development and micro-services serverless functions will greatly appreciated.
