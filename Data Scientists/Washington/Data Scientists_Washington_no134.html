As digital thieves are getting sophisticated and harder to detect, our customers are looking up to us to protect their data, accounts, subscriptions, and services from cyber-attacks – whether in their own data centers or in Microsoft’s cloud. In addition to recommending preventive measures like hardening infrastructure security, privileged identity management, multi-factor authentication, we have a unique opportunity to develop security solutions that automatically protect our customers using machine learning with security relevant data from various services, endpoints and applications.

As an engineer in our team in C+E Security division you will deliver end to end solutions that leverage machine learning techniques to detect unseen patterns of malicious and suspicious behavior that keep up with constantly changing threat landscape. You will create detection algorithms that will be applicable to multiple services and data sources, and create the infrastructure that runs these detections at cloud scale. Your detections will create a multiplier effect for security analyst’s investigations, resulting in reducing their burden and increasing their effectiveness. Feedback form first-party and third-party customers guides our algorithms to continuously improve their efficacy. Our work enables the combination of machine learning with security research and big data analytics to deliver the world’s best security offerings like Azure Sentinel, Azure Defender and Azure Identity Protection. Come and be part of this exciting new generation of security analytics!

Benefits/perks listed below may vary depending on the nature of your employment with Microsoft and the country where you work.

Responsibilities

Job Responsibilities:
  • Develop high-performance machine learning systems for detecting abnormality, intrusion, fraud, and cyber-attacks.
  • Develop world class data processing systems in Scala/C#/Python using cloud services like Azure Databricks, Azure Data Factory, Azure Service Fabric, etc,
  • Deliver end to end solutions for processing large scale data that originates from users, services, or other automated systems.
  • Actively engage with other teams across the company to identify security scenarios where machine learning/applied statistics can be used and lead the development of solutions to these problems.
  • Act as an expert in big data analytics with machine learning/statistics to serve the growing needs of C+E Security.
  • Work across teams in Azure Sentinel, Azure Defender and in other geographies like Israel to drive security solutions.

Qualifications

Required Qualifications:
  • 3+ years of educational or professional experience in developing data processing infrastructure or data mining for real problems with large amounts of data
  • Strong programming skills in Java/Scala/C# and/or Python, with understanding of cloud architectures and distributed computing.
  • 4+ years of experience developing production quality secure software OR developing security analytics solutions.
Preferred Qualifications:
  • A Ph.D. degree in Computer Science is highly desired. Candidates with a Ph.D. in computer science other quantifiable fields (e.g. Statistics, Bioinformatics) with applied practical experience and programming background preferred.
  • Experience with developing very large scale data processing solution in cloud using Apache Spark or similar technologies.
  • Experience developing high-performance solutions with service-oriented architecture.
  • Passion for working with customers and advocating for their requirements to be met by features.
