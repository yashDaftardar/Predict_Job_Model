Amazon is investing in building a customer centric, world class advertising business across its many unique audio, video, and display surfaces. In this role, you will be on the cutting edge of developing monetization solutions for Live TV, Connected TV and streaming Audio. These are nascent, high growth areas, where advertising monetization is an important, fully integrated part of the core strategy for each business.

You will be working with R&D organizations and will own data science product requirements and manage the complete life cycle of products and services you are responsible for. You will need to envision the end-to-end data science workflow to generate insights and recommendations, and deliver automated decisions. To accomplish this vision, you will develop requirements in the form of high-level epics and roadmaps, as well as detailed user stories for developing different components of the data processing ecosystem.

You will influence business leaders by presenting product vision documents to acquire funding for products. You will partner with science and engineering leaders to build the long-term and short-term roadmap and ensure timely release of products and solutions. You will work closely with scientists and engineers who are building the machine learning systems influencing product features and architectural choices that lead to better experiences for advertisers and consumers.

Responsibilities
  • Plan and drive products to optimize the ad spend for all Amazon advertising customers.
  • Create product vision documents to drive investments for one or more data science products
  • Work with a team of scientists and engineering leaders to define and deliver the product roadmap
  • Write technical requirements working backwards from advertising customer needs for data-driven decision making.
  • Presenting written recommendations for new product/feature development to deliver value to advertising customers.
  • Provide weekly reports on product development as well as key performance indicators and/or large ongoing projects
  • PhD with experience developing and delivering software products
  • Experience working on automated marketing technologies
  • Experience working on advertising solutions
  • Experience working on analytics with ability to use data and metrics to back up assumptions, develop business cases, and complete root cause analyses
  • Experience with Microsoft Office tools. Ability to use SQL and one or more of Minitab, SPSS, SAS, R, Python, MATLAB to do advanced data analysis
  • Experience working with AWS Services, consuming, managing large scale datasets
  • Experience working with multiple stakeholders, writing research proposals and project plans
  • Comfortable presenting research outcomes to large audiences with diverse background
  • Master's degree in Media Technologies/Business or a Scientific Discipline
