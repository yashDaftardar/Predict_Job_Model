Full Job Description

This role leads Data Science, Machine Learning & Analytics product engineering teams. It is accountable for managing product vision, delivery sprints and enterprise customer partnerships on analytics. These enterprise customers stretch across T-Mobile and include areas like Care, Supply Chain, Marketing and Finance. The role participates in Visioning, Design Planning to enable partners with value-driven product capabilities. They partner extensively with other leaders and teams to foster Machine Learning, Data Science features. Responsible for managing a high-performing group of data scientists, product managers, analysts with humility and care. Thrives in a complex, ambiguous environment to enable team members and partners find their north star with careful mentoring and support. Drives Data Science Academy, Monthly Machine Learning council and Enterprise Machine Learning operations to achieve greater success for business growth.
  • 5-8 years of experience building Data Science / Machine Learning teams with customer-facing products
  • 5 + years of experience in leadership/management roles leading diverse teams. Ideally looking for experience leading a team of data scientists as well as employees beginning the data science journey.
  • Strong knowledge of agile delivery, devops cycle, Release Management planning, product/project estimation, delivery cost management
  • Prefer knowledge of Telecom/Wireless data and Subscription services model (subscriber models) with an understanding of billing, retail, and digital interactions with customers.
  • Technical experience in cloud infrastructure design and operation to recognize the quality, reliability, and scalability of team’s work – knowledge of Azure, Databricks.
  • A strong bias for action and ownership; you take personal pride and joy from the output of your team and are continuously pushing the bar higher.
  • Empathy to a leadership role to function collaboratively, yet with focus to raise the bar for everyone
  • Understanding of Machine Learning, Data Science core knowledge: Regression, Clustering, K-Means, Decision Trees, Classification, etc.
  • Demonstrated work experience in Python, Azure, R Studio, Julia or other statistical platform/products.
  • MS in Computer Science or MS in Business Analytics (preferred PhD in Data Science)
Skills
  • Azure, AWS, PaaS, SaaS
  • Containerization, Kubernetes, Docker, Synapse, Neptune, Sagemaker,
  • knowledge of Analytics Solutions on Cloud
  • Leads data science, machine learning disciplines and maintains up-to-date technical skills in anticipation of future cognitive feature opportunities
  • Identifies, develops, and retains highly specialized Machine Learning & Data Science professionals with diverse Telecom industry focus
  • Define and design Advanced Analytics design that is secure, scalable and highly available on Azure; ensures data science services and products are simplified for business consumption
  • Work closely with multiple teams and helps define solution proposal, solution design, operational costs and identifies business value proposition for data science products
  • Collaborate with the Privacy and Governance team in refining best practices to identify, detect, protect usage of sensitive data in data science & machine learning initiatives
  • Develops Data Scientists to become enterprise coaches to enable greater success by DevOps teams
  • Mentors Product Managers to acquire Data Science acumen and drives their close collaboration with Data Scientists and Solution Architects
  • Responsible for compliance to ensure privacy, security of data/information in Data Science/Machine Learning applications
  • Nurtures relationships with Directors, Cloud Architects, Data Architects, Governance & Compliance Leaders
  • Provides visibility to product development/delivery progress and resolves roadblocks
  • Proactively assess the effectiveness of standards, methodologies and processes; driving change as appropriate for continuous improvement.
  • Coach, mentor, and develop talent
  • Orchestrate and lead the technical completion of client deliverables with quality and on schedule
  • Manages Customer partnerships and earns their trust for drive greater collaboration
  • Provides data scientists with business process guidance, data usage guidance and stakeholder mapping for clarity.
