WHAT YOU'LL DO

BCG GAMMA is an exciting venture into software, data and next generation consulting. As we undergo this transformational effort, there is a need to expand our services and offerings to drive and deliver impact with our clients. A core element behind this is access to data and leveraging that data to derive meaningful insights, new business opportunities and advanced digital services. BCG GAMMA will be undertaking a variety of projects, initiatives, and working teams in order to effectively roll out new business models and create new data offerings, while maintaining baseline service levels for existing offerings.

As a part of one of BCG rapidly growing digital businesses, the Analyst - Data Business Management will work on strategic and operational initiatives of the Data Business within BCG GAMMA, which provides data offerings to accelerate the impact of BCG teams. As an integral member of the team, you will interact with both local and international teams as well as clients to diagnose/solve client’s problems and ensure the delivery of quality projects. You will be working under supervision to develop new data offerings as well as build and enhancement efforts of the existing assets (e.g. different proprietary assets such as peer benchmarks, databases, diagnostic tools, etc..). Additional tasks involve development of internal methodology and processes. In this role, you will also work closely with BCG GAMMA Data business leadership, specific product teams, PAs and other BCG stakeholders. While the job includes a component of traditional project management responsibilities, the majority of work directly focuses on product development/delivery and making concrete and tangible impact on BCG’s performance.

YOU'RE GOOD AT

  • You have a high aptitude for strategic thinking, thought leadership and experience with data assets (e.g. benchmarks, databases, diagnostic tools, etc..):
  • Knowledge of BCG Practice Areas and/or software/data products helpful
  • Analyzing large data sets, finding patterns and providing a concise summary
  • Connecting the analysis of the data with prevailing industry trends and issues
  • Leveraging subject matter expertise to build, maintain, execute and deliver data assets
  • Driving new ideas, with limited guidance
  • Finding solutions to the client problems
  • Working and interacting in a global and virtual team
  • Developing solutions – even with imperfect information
  • Paying attention to detail
  • Listening to and looking for input from others
  • Time management and working across multiple work streams

YOU BRING (EXPERIENCE & QUALIFICATIONS)

  • University degree with demonstrated high academic achievement preferably in field of engineering, mathematics, economics, statistics or other data/analytical centric disciplines
  • 2-4+ years’ of relevant experience
  • Demonstrated experience data analytics with specific focus on development and delivery of data assets
  • Demonstrated project experience including scoping work, planning, conducting analysis and making recommendations, analytics support and/or developing presentations
  • Demonstrated experience in core tools/ methods/ technologies in own area of work, understanding of full methodology of data assets and business intelligence tools (e.g. Excel, SQL, Tableau, Python, etc…)
  • Solid general business understanding, actively keeping up with trends and latest developments, very good understanding of BCG business and environment
  • Ability to communicate effectively (written and verbal) with clients, case teams and also senior stakeholders. Speaking (and presenting as needed) confidently with clients and stakeholders
  • Teamwork and a collaborative approach to sharing ideas and finding solution, dealing with ambiguity
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, team and international environment, being effective at prioritizing and multi-tasking
  • Entrepreneurial and development/product enhancement opportunities-seeking mind-set

WHO WE ARE

Boston Consulting Group partners with leaders in business and society to tackle their most important challenges and capture their greatest opportunities. BCG was the pioneer in business strategy when it was founded in 1963. Today, we work closely with clients to embrace a transformational approach aimed at benefiting all stakeholders—empowering organizations to grow, build sustainable competitive advantage, and drive positive societal impact.

Our diverse, global teams bring deep industry and functional expertise and a range of perspectives that question the status quo and spark change. BCG delivers solutions through leading-edge management consulting, technology and design, and corporate and digital ventures. We work in a uniquely collaborative model across the firm and throughout all levels of the client organization, fueled by the goal of helping our clients thrive and enabling them to make the world a better place.

BCG GAMMA combines innovative skills in computer science, artificial intelligence, statistics, and machine learning with deep industry expertise. The BCG GAMMA team is comprised of world-class data engineers, data scientists and business consultants who specialize in the use of advanced analytics to get breakthrough business results. Our teams own the full analytics value-chain end to end: framing new business challenges, building fact-bases, designing innovative algorithms, creating scale through designing tools and apps, and training colleagues and clients in new solutions. Here at BCG GAMMA, you’ll have the chance to work with clients in every BCG region and every industry area. We are also a core member of a rapidly growing analytics enterprise at BCG – a constellation of teams focused on driving practical results for BCG clients by applying leading edge analytics approaches, data, and technology.
