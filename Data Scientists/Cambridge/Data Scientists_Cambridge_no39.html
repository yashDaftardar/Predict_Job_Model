Full Job Description

SUMMARY :
The Senior Analyst will be a skilled problem solver who has a passion for utilizing data to drive business growth. The position will involve working closely with the analytics and business intelligence teams for the planning, execution, and delivery of all KAGR analytics and data science products, which include a variety of predictive models, marketing analytics, forecasting, development of sales and business analytics, and more. Additionally, the Sr Analyst will participate in consulting projects focused on delivering insights to customers. This is a great opportunity for someone who wants to make an impact in advanced analytics and data science within the sports industry.
DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
  • Data mining and ability to translate raw data into meaningful insights and recommendations
  • Strong interest in learning and applying statistical methodologies, techniques and processes
  • Experience and understanding of advances analytical models and machine learning
  • Construction and maintenance of a variety of predictive models (lead scoring, look-a-like, retention, and other customized predictive models)
  • Develop revenue, ticket sales, attendance, and other forecasting models (familiarity with regression analysis and R)
  • Develop new approaches to present complex concepts using data visualization
  • Interest in learning and applying advanced machine learning algorithms to further enhance KAGR's analytical offerings
  • Interest in marketing analytics and advanced market research (factor analysis, stated-preference surveys, etc.)
  • Develop other custom solutions as required by KAGR clients
SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES
This position has no supervisory responsibilities.


SKILLS AND QUALIFICATIONS
  • Degree in Statistics, Economics, Mathematics, Computer Science, or quantitative discipline (1-2 years experience)
  • Understanding and willingness to learn statistical analysis and modeling
  • Ability to present complex data sets and analysis in easy to understand ways
  • Experience with R and/or Python required
  • Experience with SQL required for data mining
  • Experience processing and analyzing large data sets. Expertise with Excel is required
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to work within a diverse team and serve a variety of stakeholders with different priorities
  • Outstanding organizational skills and attention to detail
PHYSICAL DEMANDS
  • Sitting for extended periods of time
  • Dexterity of hands and fingers to operate a computer keyboard, mouse, and other computing equipment
  • The employee frequently is required to talk or hear
  • The employee is occasionally required to reach with hands and arms
  • Specific vision abilities required by this job include close vision, distance vision, color vision, peripheral vision, depth perception, and ability to adjust focus
  • Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
WORK ENVIRONMENT
  • Fast paced office environment
  • The noise level in the work environment is usually moderate.
