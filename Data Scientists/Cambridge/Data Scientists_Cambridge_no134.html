Mercer is seeking candidates for the following position based in our Boston office:

Senior Data Analyst – Investment Solutions

What can you expect?

  • Provide leadership as a senior member of a new global data team
  • Strategic stakeholder management with colleagues around the globe
  • Consolidating four global regions onto a single data platform
  • Contributing to Mercer’s global data governance framework
  • Oversee several projects and continually seek out ongoing improvement

What is in it for you?

  • Employee friendly policies
  • Health care and insurance for you and your dependents
  • Healthy Work life balance
  • A great working environment
  • Flexible benefits packages to suit your needs and lifestyle
  • Future career opportunities across a global organization
  • Single point of contact and leader for all matters related to the quality of data within a certain domain and geography.
  • Develop excellent working relationships with the Data Stewards who are responsible for the data in scope and with other key Data stakeholders.
  • Identify with the Data Stewards the root cause of data issues and investigate potential improvements to the processes or systems.
  • Coordinate these initiatives with other Controllers, Business Analysts and Technology in order to formulate detailed proposals to Data Stewards, management and stakeholders. Monitor and follow up the implementation of improvements that have been approved.
  • Drive engagement with stakeholders, propose and implement ways to continuously enhance Mercer’s data

We will count on you to:

  • Develop a thorough understanding of the business processes and systems and the data they generate.
  • Acquire a deep understanding of the data quality rules’ methodology and scorecards applicable to the datasets in scope.
  • Work closely with colleagues to improve quality rules, scorecards, or any other aspect of the data quality monitoring process.
  • Develop new tools where appropriate
  • Understand the direct link between data quality rules and downstream analytical solutions
  • Work to continuously improve the Data Quality team’s products, services and internal workings.
  • Participate in and own the delivery of business facing Data Quality projects
  • Participate in and own the delivery of internal process/service, analysis and technology transformation projects

What you need to have:

  • 10+ years relevant industry experience and a degree in Computer Science, Information Systems Management, Business or related discipline
  • Experience with agile methodologies
  • Strong technical skills and the ability to navigate through multiple systems without assistance, e.g. Experienced with Qlikview, Qliksense, Microstrategy & other BI products
  • Advanced knowledge of SQL (e.g. Oracle), database and data warehouse design concepts
  • Familiarity with ETL (Extract, Translate and Load) and quality tools for efficiently and accurately importing new data for various external/internal sources
  • Strong MS Excel, PowerPoint and Access experience.
  • Business process knowledge
  • Self-starter and continuous learner
  • Adaptable & agile
  • Ability to improvise
  • Problem solver
  • Stakeholder management skills
  • Strong team working skills
  • Attention to detail
  • Time management skills
  • Comfortable with responsibility for delivery

What makes you stand out?

  • Previous in-depth knowledge of Eagle Performance & Data Management.
  • Experience with Master Data Management and Data Acquisition
  • Proficiency with unit funds / NAVs (Net Asset Value) would be beneficial, ideally with a familiarity with aspects like swing pricing, distributing share classes etc.
  • Understanding of the financial services industry, specifically Investment Accounting.
  • Understanding of the software product management lifecycle and/or investment performance measurement and analysis.
  • Experience of business process improvement in asset management – for example, performing data investigations to determine root cause of data quality issues.
