SR Ecomm Data Analyst

  • Reporting directly to the Director, Omnichannel Planning and Analysis, this role will support growth of digital operations with data-driven analysis.
  • Provide analytics, data management, modeling expertise to connect information to business insights.
  • Build relevant reporting, dashboards while communicating insights and strategic business recommendations to leaders.
  • Operate testing programs to inform the business on best options
  • Support major cross-functional company projects in the area of consolidating and operationalizing Marketing data
  • Translate complex data, and effectively influence both peers and senior leadership
  • Understand how to make data visually appealing to both navigate and comprehend for end-users
  • Aggregate data from various sources to construct streamlined data pipelines and integrate data from multiple systems
  • Identify key metrics and trends to track progress of the business and ensure alignment with company strategic priorities.
  • Identify key business levers, establish cause & effect, perform analyses, and communicate key findings to various stakeholders to facilitate data driven decision-making.
  • Lead and participate in special projects/initiatives: innovate and implement large-scale quality improvements to processes and/or systems by conducting data analysis.

Requirements :

  • Bachelor’s degree, preferably in Finance, Computer Science, Math, Statistics or related field
  • 5-7 years relevant Digital experience
  • Preferred experience in retail or CPG setting
  • Prior experience with Marketing Analytics or Data Analytics
  • Ability to translate data into actional items and strategic business recommendations.
  • Strong financial, analytical and business acumen.
