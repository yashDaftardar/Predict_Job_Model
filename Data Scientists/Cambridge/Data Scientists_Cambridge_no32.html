Full Job Description

Invesco is one of the world’s leading global investment managers, entrusted with managing $1.2 trillion* in assets on behalf of clients worldwide. We are the 6th largest US retail asset manager and the 13th largest investment manager globally, and our more than 8,000 employees worldwide are dedicated to delivering an investment experience that helps people get more out of life. We are purely focused on managing a comprehensive range of active, passive and alternative investment capabilities, which we draw on to provide customized solutions aligned to client needs, our most important benchmark. (*As of May 31,2019)


Job Purpose (Job Summary) :
As a Data Scientist, you will shape and deliver on a data roadmap that expands our capabilities to use data to inform insights and improve our data science and analytics consumer experiences. You will collaborate with domain data offices and analytics groups , business functions, privacy, security, engineers, architects and other data managers to ensure delivery of data ingestion, quality, aggregation, access, visualization and analytics capabilities to deliver business results. This role requires excellent organizational and communication skills, ability to stitch together strategic goals to executional details, translate technical data science capabilities to business outcomes, and manage stakeholders in a global matrix organization. The Data Scientist will execute on the development of tools, methods and processes to utilize data and data science to enable effective and timely business decisions across the organization. The data science and engineering team is responsible for establishing and maintaining enterprise data science and analytic standards, including a uniform and repeatable science development lifecycle methodology and a common set of standards for advanced analytics and engineering.


Key Responsibilities / Duties :

  • The Data Scientist will be a collaborative leader who can effectively communicate and liaise with Domain Data Offices and other stakeholders to execute the Enterprise Data Office’s strategy.
  • They will need to demonstrate the ability to execute and support projects that produce results.
  • Develop and foster peer, cross-functional relationships, and community of practice to maximize data science and engineering value across the global firm.
  • Bring data science and engineering products from conception to launch, define requirements, create a roadmap and work with developers to build and launch.
  • Interpret and present data science concepts, principles and best practices in formats that are easy to understand.
  • Drive adoption of Innovations in Data across the enterprise, to support and enable business insights, analytics and compute pipelines, engineering and consumption methods.
  • Manage data science solutions through their lifecycle from business opportunity, build, deploy and business change management particularly clear communication to align stakeholders throughout data science lifecycle.
  • Solve complex business problems; communicate complex analytic solutions through storytelling.
  • Tell data stories to stakeholders by drawing insights from data analysis, bringing insights together to formulate the story and making recommendations for actions.
  • Partner with business to identify and understand new ways to use data to support insights and activate in consumer experience.
  • Work with cross functional teams to analyze and document requirements to deliver data projects on schedule and within agreed scope to deliver business results.
  • Write use cases and requirements, align data to business rules and requirements, lead functional and technical design discussions and work with delivery and engineering teams to deliver the product.
  • Create measurements to guide decision-making about the future of data science and engineering.
  • Understand consumer behaviors through data, provide performance analysis, and develop tools and services such as modeling, data science development, decision aids, and business case analysis to support programs and recommend advanced analytic services.
  • Assisting functional teams with documentation of current state and development of future state, using tools like process mapping, statistical analysis, etc.
  • Develop trusted partnerships with operations, business, and data systems leadership and their teams.
  • Effectively communicate complex data concepts and strategy to business and data systems partners, and quickly meld into organizational dynamics and geographic cultures.
  • Engage with critical business partners and initiatives to identify and prioritize information delivery needs that drive value.

Work Experience / Knowledge :

  • Proven experience explaining complex technical concepts in a clear, concise and credible manner to business people and technical experts so they can be debated for best solutions.
  • Demonstrated ability to synthesize information to develop recommendations, and ability to persuade / influence organizational pursuit of recommended path.
  • Strong prioritization and leadership skills, including timely and proactive communications of timelines, status, issue escalation.
  • You have the ability to simplify strategy into achievable actions with extreme attention to detail and ability to self-audit work.
  • Demonstrates intimate knowledge of, and/or proven record of success in, roles as a Data Scientist or Data Science Engineer working on Business data science, engineering and analytics development projects, preferably for a global team.
  • Having technical specialization regarding data models, database design development, data mining and segmentation techniques to include expertise with data science and engineering tools.
  • Working knowledge within the areas of diagnostic, predictive and prescriptive data analytics, possessing reporting and data science skills.
  • Working knowledge of statistics and experience using statistical packages for analyzing datasets.
  • Collecting data through means such as analyzing business results understanding and finding appropriate applications for data and analysis results.
  • Creating new, experimental frameworks to collect data, analyze and deploy data science engineering solutions.
  • Familiar with big data technologies, having advanced ability to perform exploratory data analysis, to include working with machine learning and AI.
  • Applying analytical skills to evaluate and document accurate business requirements and present these requirements in a manner that is concise, measurable and flexible enough to meet project and stakeholder needs.
  • Supporting the implementation of solutions from conception till initiation by providing information, managing stakeholders and engaging in all the key business analysis tasks that need to be completed to implement the solution.

Formal Education : (minimum requirement to perform job duties)
