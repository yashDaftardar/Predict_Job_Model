Full Job Description

Linguamatics, an IQVIA company, is a leading provider of NLP text mining solutions with an emphasis on high value biomedical and healthcare applications. Thanks to a great team and innovative use of natural language processing (NLP) based technology, our software is acknowledged as a leading solution in the pharmaceutical, biotech and healthcare industries and is used by 19 of the top 20 global pharmaceutical companies, major health systems, academic medical centers and governmental organizations such as the US FDA.


NLP Marketing & Event Specialist

Linguamatics, Marlborough, MA

We are seeking a proactive, well-organized and digitally savvy B2B marketing professional to drive sales enablement-related activities. Reporting to the Marketing Manager, you will take charge of our extensive physical and digital events programs and will work as part of the Business Development team in implementing our marketing communications strategy more broadly via email, website, social etc. to increase awareness, strengthen the brand, and support business growth for Linguamatics NLP.

Responsibilities

  • Plan and implement a robust webinar and virtual hosted events program in conjunction with the product and marketing teams
  • Manage key aspects of company-hosted events, conferences and trade shows, including booking and liaising with venues and suppliers, organizing logistics, managing speakers, adhering to budgets and working with sales and marketing teams to maximize registrations and attendance
  • Plan and execute tailored digital campaigns to generate leads for the sales team, monitor and report results
  • Work with other members of the business development team to ensure that leads are captured and managed effectively
  • Collaborate effectively with wider IQVIA marketing and events teams to maximize success
  • Contribute to maintaining and improving the CRM system, including importing/adding new data, updating records and performing necessary cross checks to ensure data integrity
  • Develop and manage collateral and sales tools requirements, for example, slide presentations, blogs, product datasheets, white papers, case studies
    Manage branding requirements and promotional item stock and organize additional orders
  • Work with the Marketing Manager and other key stakeholders to maximize relevant trade press coverage during events

Requirements

The successful candidate will be a flexible, modern marketer with get-up-and-go initiative, who can balance the management of events with other marketing-related tasks. They should be commercially oriented and have previous events experience in a B2B environment. He/she will ideally have the following skills, experience and qualifications:

  • Expertise and proven success running high quality virtual events, including best-practice utilization of cutting-edge virtual event technologies
  • Ability to effectively organize and promote conferences, trade shows, webinars and other marketing activities
  • Educated to degree level (ideally in business/marketing or science/technology) or equivalent commercial experience
  • A good understanding of marketing principles
  • Experience of marketing technical and/or scientific/medical software products, solutions and concepts in a B2B environment
  • Experience in managing multiple projects and events at one time
  • Budget management
  • Proven track record with ability to work under pressure to tight deadlines
  • Practical knowledge of CRM systems such as Salesforce.com
  • Experienced user of Microsoft Office
  • Excellent attention to detail
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills, including creating promotional materials, proof-reading, document management and reporting
  • Team player with excellent interpersonal, communication and organizational skills

Desired skills & Experience

  • Experience of website content management systems, marketing automation tools an advantage
