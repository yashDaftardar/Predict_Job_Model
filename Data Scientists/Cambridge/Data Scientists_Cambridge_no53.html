Data & Analytics Capability Lead


Job Location: Boston, Charlotte, Chicago, Cincinnati, Cleveland, Columbus, Detroit, Indianapolis, Louisville, Miami, Seattle, St. Louis

Join our team


Centric Consulting is not the typical consulting company. We live our core values and bring them with us in all we do for our clients and community. Our core purpose is simple: every day we strive to provide unmatched customer and employee experiences and foster a culture of respect, diversity, and transparency. It’s this culture, our people, and delivery that come together to make Centric a trusted advisor to local businesses.

Our Data & Analytics practice believes that data can drive business value and performance. But it takes the right people, skills and approach to deliver expected results. That’s where you come in! For us, Data and Analytics is more than just a set of technologies. It’s about skilled individuals who can align technical capabilities to bring value to a business. What we value the most: people who are dedicated to delivering high quality solutions. It's not enough for things to look good on the surface. They must be built on strong technical foundations. We’re looking for a Data & Analytics Capability Lead to join our growing team.

The primary objective of this role is to consistently grow Centric’s revenue in designated capabilities. Capability examples include machine learning, artificial intelligence, graph analytics, MDM, Databricks, Synapse, Snowflake, etc. Capability leads may be responsible for multiple related technologies.


In this role, you will:

  • Map out the capability growth strategy and plan.
  • Cultivate and mature delivery team.
  • Participate in staffing plans, recruiting, and hiring and other Data & Analytics leadership planning.
  • Build Centric community and center of excellence related to the capability.
  • Perform marketing activities, such as delivering webinars, speaking at conferences, writing blogs and whitepapers.
  • Define industry-aligned offerings and sales training.
  • Educate Centric colleagues who are engaged in business development on how to sell the capability.
  • Engage with Centric’s local leads to sell work.
  • Oversee delivery of projects, ensuring a high degree of customer satisfaction.
  • Function as a feedback manager for select employees on the National Data & Analytics team.
  • Identify and establish key vendor and consulting partnerships.

A successful candidate will have the following characteristics:


  • 10+ years of delivery experience in capability or related technologies.
  • 5+ years in consulting role.
  • Demonstrated experience building a consulting practice.
  • Strong executive communication and sales presence.
  • Demonstrated ability to sell $100k+ consulting projects.
  • Experienced in client delivery and communication.
  • Demonstrates awareness of the importance of feedback and coaching.
  • Holds others accountable for high standards of performance.
  • Comfortable with crucial conversations.
  • Excellent at listening for potential problem areas
