Full Job Description

Under general supervision the responsibilities of the incumbent Senior Pharmacy Data Analyst will:

  • Evaluate existing Pharmacy Benefits Manager (PBM) agreements
  • Benchmark against comparators and evaluate proposals, and support negotiations
  • Prepare annual review including but not limited to: claims data, plan information, and contracts to prepare annual market checks
  • Prepare detailed analysis of new rates, guarantees and proposed terms to evaluate planned versus actual performance
  • Identify ways to reduce costs and bring transparency to drug spend
  • Focus areas include but may not be limited to: Improve clinical outcomes and patient experience, clinical, network, drug pricing and administrative costs
  • Identify key discrepancies and cost outliers in existing formularies, benefit structure analyses/cost, and utilization impact of formulary change in evaluation of request for proposals from third party bidders; identify finalists, negotiate and analyze best and final offers and select winning bidder(s)

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s - Master’s preferred – Preferred Discipline: Healthcare Administration, Business, Finance, Economics, Analytics or Statistics
  • 4+ Years pharmacy or medical data analysis
  • 1+ Years' advanced experience in SQL and/or SAS
  • 1+ Years’ Experience with visualization tools (Tableau, Qlik, Power BI, etc.)
  • Experience with pharmacy (NDC, AWP, WAC, etc.), Medical Codes: (ICD-10, CP4, HCPC, etc.) and related revenue codes
  • Knowledge of pharmacy medical claims reimbursement, claim adjudication and downstream analyses
  • Experience with pharmacy formularies, drug lists, pharmacy benefits and configurations, drug pricing, specialty drugs, rebates, eligibility
  • Familiarity with pharmacy and provider networks: NCPDP Data, Medi-Span Data, FDB Data, and RJ Health Data
  • Strong technical and written communication skills
  • Strong attention to detail, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to incorporate large data sets from multiple channels
  • Ability to present technical content to non-technical audiences
  • Effective time management while working multiple projects simultaneously
  • Proficient with Microsoft suite and advanced knowledge of Microsoft Excel
  • Working knowledge of HIPAA, PHI and PPI, SOC1, SOC2, and HITRUST guidelines and descriptive statistical application and techniques
  • Data Warehouse experience
  • Possess intellectual curiosity, innovation and creativity
