Data Engineer/Scientist

Coverent is seeking a Data Engineering Consultant to lead program evaluation activities for a client in the US Intelligence Community. On this project, the Consultant will have the opportunity to demonstrate intellectual agility by leading data collection and data analysis activities to produce key performance insights. Furthermore, they will lead the implementation of recommendations when they are adopted by the customer. This project features the opportunity to interact with a wide range of key stakeholders, including those at senior levels. This is an exciting opportunity for an intellectually curious, energetic consultant to work on a project with real impact helping our client develop ways to understand and improve performance of activities that support delivery of the mission.

Position Responsibilities

  • Support the identification, prioritization, and scheduling of data modeling and processing requirements with users
  • Report the status of all data extraction, transformation, and load activities
  • Own development, design, and maintenance of big data architectures to support querying of diverse datasets
  • Apply semantic data modeling techniques to classify, aggregate, and generalize data stored in hierarchical, network, or relational database management systems to define the meaning of data within the context of its interrelationships with other data
  • Translate ambiguous business problem statements into data warehousing requirements
  • Produce scalable mechanisms to validate, monitor, and troubleshoot operational or data issues in data pipelines
  • Learn and navigate compliance related requirements for storing and using bulk data.

Required Qualifications

  • U.S. Citizenship
  • Must have a Bachelor’s degree and a minimum of 3 years' experience in a consulting role in the IC; Master’s degree in relevent technical fields preferred
  • Experience designing, optimizing, and maintaining relational and non-relational databases including MySQL, SQL Server, MongoDB, Cassandra, or other NoSQL database
  • Experience developing presentations and providing briefings to middle and senior management
  • Experience with ETL and building data pipelines
  • Ability to perform in an individual contributor role while also excelling in a team environment

Desired Qualifications

  • Experience with Azure or AWS Database Administration
  • Experience with Application development
  • Experience with a public cloud such as AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, etc.
  • Experience with Agile engineering practices
