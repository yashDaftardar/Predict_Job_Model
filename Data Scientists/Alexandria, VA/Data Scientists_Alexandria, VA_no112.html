Applied Research Associates, Inc. (ARA), Capital Area Division (CAD) has an outstanding opportunity for a Data Scientist to support prototype tool development and analyst tasking. Skills needed for this position include Jupyter Notebook/Python/Pandas/Numpy/R/Excel Wizardry. This position would also be ideal for a data focused developer with strong data modeling experience in SQL / NOSQL / API Building / Web Application Servers/Search Index. The ideal candidate is excited for new challenges, interested in working with users to characterize and solve their data science challenges, and undaunted by deep data management tasks from a wide variety of datasets/sources. The candidate should have experience in AI/ML techniques for deep-learning and pattern discovery. An example of data management tasks for a project could include modeling, acquisition (pulling, scraping, and query strategy), munging, model fitting, iterative characterization/querying to support analyst needs, storage, indexing, and delivery.

Applicants selected will be subject to a government security investigation and must meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information.

Required Experience:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Data Science or equivalent degree.
  • Minimum 5 years of experience
  • Experience with Jupyter Notebook / Python / Pandas / NumPy / R / Excel Wizardry
  • Experience in AI / ML techniques
  • Top Secret clearance with access to Sensitive Compartmented Information based on a current and valid Single Scope Background Investigation
  • Knowledge and use of Word, Excel and PowerPoint.

Desired Qualifications:

  • 7-10 years of experience
  • DevOps and continuous delivery experience
  • Containers/Docker
  • Knowledge of agile software development mindset and tools (JIRA, Git, Jenkins)
