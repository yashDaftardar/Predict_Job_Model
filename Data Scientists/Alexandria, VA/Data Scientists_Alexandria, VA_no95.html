Marriott International is the world’s largest hotel company, with more brands, more hotels and more opportunities for associates to grow and succeed. We believe a great career is a journey of discovery and exploration. So, we ask, where will your journey take you?



JOB SUMMARY

Establishes and leads the program management capability the Customer Experience (CX) Data & Analytics Function at Marriott. Develops, implements and manages a clear governance structure for project and program alignment and accountability across the portfolio. Owns and manages the work Intake and Prioritization processes for all initiatives within the group. Collaborates with peers on the CX Data & Analytics team as well as Senior Executives across Marriott’s Brand, Marketing, Sales & Consumer (BMSC) organization to ensure a clear prioritized agenda is set annually and that commitments are made throughout the year. Oversees resource staffing for all department initiatives. Establishes processes that measure overall department and process success and health. Own and drive key strategic initiatives for the CX Data Science organization such as Consumers Operations Goals Setting process, Legacy data platform retirement etc. Develop a Change Management and support strategy and model for Modern Data Platform, MRDW and Legacy Data Platform as well as Tableau and additional processes and technology components being used as part of the data platform.

CORE WORK ACTIVITIES

Own and Run Core Team Management Processes

  • Establish an annual planning cadence to capture internal CX Data & Analytics team needs and initiatives for the upcoming year
  • Establish an annual planning cadence/process to capture discipline stakeholder needs and initiatives for the upcoming year
  • Organize and facilitate key activities associated with the annual planning process and ensure proper connection points to the overall Marriott Budgeting and Planning processes
  • Collaborate with Peers to provide meaningful data to facilitate prioritization of initiatives across multiple major stakeholder groups
  • Establish and manage a more frequent intake process to enable commitments that can be appropriately staffed and delivered in the suggested time horizon
  • Manage the overall staffing model for both Project Management resources within the team
  • Oversee the staffing and slotting processes for new work, ensuring all of the right resources from the broader CX Data & Analytics teams are available to work on an assigned project within the allotted timeframe
  • Establish and manage a process by which ad hoc requests or escalations may be handled though presentation of tradeoffs and clearly communicated alternatives
  • Ensure clear metrics are in place for measuring process health and efficiency

Platform Management Processes

  • Manage a team of Reporting Automation resources
  • Define Automation strategy and demonstrate with examples. Set the standard approach and guidelines for BI development to ensure easy transition to Automated dashboards / reports
  • Define a team of Visualization developers
  • Develop standard visualization library. Evaluate different visualization technologies as needed to present data / analytics with appropriate visualizations
  • Manage the Data Platform support processes
  • Develop Change Plan and Operating Model for the Modern Data Platform in conjunction with Consumer Ops Change team
  • Develop Support strategy for the new capabilities being deployed with Modern Data Platform including new Data engineering processes, new analytics processes and tools as well as new operating model
  • Develop more robust processes for external teams such as Continent analytics team on bringing in new data sets

Set the Standard for Project/Initiative Delivery

  • Establish protocols and standards for how projects and initiatives get delivered
  • Provide guidance to other project management resources owning projects on the team
  • Ensure tight connection to other PMO’s to ensure consistency in experience and delivery within Marriott
  • Provide leadership or guidance for project delivery to those on the team without project management experience to ensure successful delivery of all projects across the team
  • Partner with team Leaders to evaluate the use of Agile Delivery methodology for project delivery

Leadership Accountabilities

  • Serves as a team leader for PMO associates
  • Functions as the thought leader for the project management discipline;
  • Advises direct reports in the execution of their priorities
  • Coaches PMO team on assigned capability areas
  • Actively drives maturity of project management model and methodologies (i.e. leads COE) and continuous improvement
  • Serves as escalation for project and program managers, if required.
  • Lead strategic engagements as such as Consumer Operations Goal setting efforts, retirement of legacy data platform and adoption of new platform globally
  • Training inititatives for CX data science as well as communication fo standard learning curriculum to the continent and other analytics teams
