Full Job Description

Overview:
  • We are interested in highly motivated candidates with a wide variety of expertise and interests in Wireless Systems Engineering and Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE). We are searching for technical experts in systems engineering to lead and support a range of projects involving wireless communications and networking technologies with an emphasis on commercial cellular.

    Candidates will join a high-performing team focused on the instrumentation, analysis, and augmentation of wireless signals (including 5G New Radio and 4G LTE) to address mission needs for performance, resiliency, and security. The team performs hands-on experimentation, signal collection and analysis, conducts security evaluations, and develops proof-of-concept prototypes.

    This position requires an individual who has the ability to obtain a Secret DoD security clearance upon selection for this role.
Responsibilities:
  • This position provides an exciting opportunity to participate in engineering activities to ensure successful product development and integration, timely execution of programs, and total customer satisfaction.
  • Perform requirements analysis, requirements definition, requirements management, functional analysis, performance analysis, system design, detail trade studies, systems integration and testing.
  • Perform detailed technical analyses for a subsystem or system elements.
  • Develop and evaluates systems, networks and information systems to ensure designs meet applicable governmental security specifications.
  • Utilize Modeling and Simulation to define and create wireless network models, test scenarios, and tools for collecting and analyzing results.
  • Defines processes for technical platforms, system specifications, input/output and working parameters for hardware and/or software compatibility.
  • Assess mobile wireless technology trends, commercial and government solutions for a variety of projects and government sponsors.
  • Help shape technical capability areas by leading research projects and recommending capital investments, training opportunities, and capability enhancements.
  • We are interested in highly motivated, enthusiastic candidates with a wide variety of skills and the ability and interest to dig deep on challenging technical problems.
Qualifications:
  • Minimum Qualifications:
    • BS (or higher) in Computer Science, Systems Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Modeling and Simulation (M&S) or a related field.
    • Five (5+) years' of related experience in Systems Engineering or Modeling and Simulation.
  • Experience:
    • Experience with CAMEO Systems Modeler
    • Experience with Systems Modeling Language (SySML) v1.5
    • Ability to generate digital representations of program and technical data, and produce artifacts detailing the information model of the system and major system components.
    • Ability to model interconnectivity of system components, interface standards, APIs and flow of data through the system (expected data rates)
    • Ability to develop a system architectural model to include behavior, structural, parametric and requirements data objects as defined by SySML
    • Ability to effectively communicate and present systems engineering concepts as a technical advisor or technical mentor.
    • Technical, task, or project leadership experience.
    • Passion for mentoring and developing junior engineers.
    • Contributing to business development and company leadership team for pricing models, feature definition and prioritization of go-to-market resources.
  • Preferred Qualifications:
    • Experience with:
    • Familiarity with commonly used wireless communications physical layer protocols.
    • 4G LTE or 5G NR radio access network architecture, configuration, function development, or evaluation.
    • Wireless network architecture, protocol, and security concepts
    • Experience with programming in languages such as MATLAB, Python, Java, C++, or C.
    • Strong software development practices including design and code reviews, testing and regression, revision control, and user documentation.
    • Azure DevOps
    • Proficient in configuration management and control
    • Containerized application software design and container orchestration systems (e.g. Docker or Kubernetes).
    • Network Functions Virtualization (NFV)
    • Machine Learning
    • Algorithm Development
  • Familiarity with:
    • MagicDraw
    • UML
    • RF Lab test equipment such as network analyzers, spectrum analyzers, and signal generators.
    • Spectrum Management
