Full Job Description

WILL Technology, Inc. (WTI) has an immediate need for a skilled, full-time Statistician to join our growing team in Quantico, VA. Candidates in the Chesapeake, VA, Norfolk, VA and Washington, DC areas are strongly encouraged to apply. Relocation may be available.

RESPONSIBILITIES:
In this role, the Statistician is responsible for performing tasks that manages statistical analysis and reporting to support forensic processes, examinations and Quality Assurance (QA). Some of the responsibilities for this position include:

  • Analyze and review statistical calculations for scientific validity, verification, reliability, and accuracy; recommend improvements to calculations and statistics, as appropriate.
  • Develop training materials through presentation development and / or Computer-Based Training content development.
  • Provide recommendations for optimal data presentation (i.e., Microsoft Power Point).
MINIMUM EDUCATION/TRAINING & EXPERIENCE QUALIFICATIONS:
  • Master's Degree or Bachelor's Degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Data Analysis disciplines, or related fields of study.
  • Minimum of twelve years of experience in the field of forensic DNA and population genetics.
  • Expert in human Mendelian genetics, population statistics, forensic DNA technologies, computer science, and bioinformatics.
  • Strong knowledge of forensic DNA and population genetics.
