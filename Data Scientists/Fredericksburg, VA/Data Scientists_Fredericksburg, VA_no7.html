Full Job Description

Professional Solutions Delivered, LLC (ProSoDel) is a total solutions provider for government and commercial customers in the areas of Program Management, Logistics and Information Technology Support Services. We are currently seeking a well-qualified Data Analyst to join our team of professionals in support of the Marine Corps Systems Command (MCSC) Science Systems Engineering Directorate (SED) contract. ProSoDel is responsible for assisting our Prime’s Government customer ensure that material solutions developed or acquired by MCSC are system engineered, integrated, interoperable, and certified to provide the warfighter with equipment required to accomplish their mission.

Essential Duties & Job Functions:

The role of a Data Analyst (DA) is to provide direct, specific support to the Chief Data Officer (CDO) in the definition, development, and implementation of a data management plan (DMP). A DMP provides a unified approach for modernizing management of acquisition data and the DA directly supports this intent. Acquisition data is not unified so the acquisition data landscape is not understood. A DA will help the CDO manage baselines at the enterprise, design resiliency and confidence in our equipment, and engage in efficient engineering and acquisition practices, especially related information technology (IT). The DA will elevate interest in and begin using artificial intelligence (AI) to unify data, which will enable data-enriched force projection, optimizes technology insertion and, ultimately, increase warfighting readiness in the combined physical and information domain. The DA will also focus analysis on the Defense Industrial supply chain. This job involves assisting the organization adhere to new policy, regulations, and processes to ensure the fidelity of acquisition.

  • Design and maintain data systems and databases, including fixing coding errors and other data-related problems
  • Use statistical tools to interpret data sets, paying particular attention to trends and patterns that could be valuable for diagnostic and predictive analytics efforts
