Full Job Description

Agilent inspires and supports discoveries that advance the quality of life. We provide life science, diagnostic and applied market laboratories worldwide with instruments, services, consumables, applications and expertise. Agilent enables customers to gain the answers and insights they seek - so they can do what they do best: improve the world around us. Information about Agilent is available at www.agilent.com .


As part of the One Agilent initiative and to sustain the company's growth, the organization is building and investing in a Complaint Management Center (CMC). You will be part of this first of its kind centralized approach for Agilent and as it continues to be a trusted leader in diagnostic life sciences and increases its footprint in the medical device space.

We seek an individual who is looking for an opportunity to work in a dynamic and progressive environment as a Data Analyst. In this role you will have the opportunity to develop and implement effective data analysis and visualization for all Post Market Surveillance product complaints within and support Agilent businesses. Activities would be in direct support of monitoring and detecting quality and safety signals associated with fielded product performance (e.g., adverse events, product malfunctions). The role ensures on-going regulatory, the quality of our products for our customers and can help serve to identify early signs of potential issues and contributes to continuous product/process improvements. You will have the ability to engage and work closely with Agilent's Quality and Regulatory teams as well as the Field Service Engineers, Product Support, Research & Development, and manufacturing organizations.

Come join our team of regulatory compliance professionals who are on the frontline of investigating global product performance, assuring Agilent's products are safe and effective for use for loved one's who are being diagnosed.

You are responsible for:

  • Managing the Post Market Surveillance trending processes and assuring proper statistical techniques are applied to all trending methods
  • Developing and deliver dashboards and data visualizations needed to optimally monitor Post Market Surveillance data to assure Patient Safety and Product Quality
  • Developing methods needed to effectively supervise PMS data to assure Patient Safety and Product Quality
  • Crafting reports for internal teams. Use graphs, infographics, and other methods to visualize data
  • Establishing tools to analyze data, make acceptance decisions, and improve process capability (Six Sigma, Statistical Process Control (SPC), DOE)
  • Automating data analysis methods
  • Working with the various KPI owners to maintain oversight of all KPIs and ensuring accuracy of data reported to the leadership team
  • Assuring the data integrity of all output via appropriate verification and validation activities
  • Detailing statistical-based trending procedures and trending analysis validation in compliance with regulatory requirements
  • Developing and delivering training materials to aid teams with their understanding of statistical-based trending methods
  • Explaining statistical rationale and trending procedures during regulatory audits, if vital
  • Prioritizing requests for ad hoc data & reports
  • Translating data and trends into actions to drive business decisions
  • Build presentations and reports based on recommendations and findings
  • Help identify product trends and drive measurable insights
