Full Job Description

For over 10 years, Zscaler has been disrupting and transforming the security industry. Our 100% purpose built cloud platform delivers the entire gateway security stack as a service through 150 global data centers to securely connect users to their applications, regardless of device, location, or network in over 185 countries protecting over 3,500 companies and 100 Million threats detected a day.

We work in a fast paced, dynamic and make it happen culture. Our people are some of the brightest and passionate in the industry that thrive on being the first to solve problems. We are always looking to hire highly passionate, collaborative and humble people that want to make a difference.

Zscaler is building a data platform to serve as the single source of truth for all data and reporting needs of its cross functional business teams. The ideal candidate for this role will be someone with passion for working with finance data, deploying and improving financial reporting and processes, and work cross-functionally with business and technical teams to understand and execute on data and insights’ needs for Finance (FP&A, Accounting, Sales Compensation, and Systems) teams.

What You will deliver:

  • Business Process Document(s) with key finance processes, systems, integrations, data flows & models, business rules & transformations
  • Documenting on-going functional and technical data requirements for finance teams and developing datasets to meet those requirements
  • Develop and maintain data dictionary and metric definitions for all finance datasets, metrics, and KPIs and review them regularly for changes and updates
  • Design Tableau dashboards for financial reporting when necessary
  • Conduct data enablement sessions for finance teams by communicating new datasets, metrics, KPIs, and processes

Who You Are:

  • A passion for building data platform and insights’ solutions for finance teams in SaaS organizations
  • Passionate about and focused on customer outcomes with the ability to translate finance business needs into functional and technical data requirements for data engineering team
  • Proficient in leveraging financial data (Revenue, Forecast, Commission, P&L etc.) to develop actionable insights and story-telling to drive business impact
  • Drives data literacy and enablement within the finance organization by building and utilizing data and analytics tools to enable improved understanding of data assets impacting business decisions

Core Requirements:

  • 8+ years of experience with data initiatives (data engineering, analysis, and story-telling) with focus on financial reporting and processes, translating the business needs into data requirements
  • Deep understanding of financial systems (NetSuite, Clari, Adaptive Planning etc), processes, and metrics
  • Proven track record of executing on data roadmap for Finance groups by translating business data needs into functional and technical requirements for data engineering team
  • Advanced SQL skills and proficiency in visualization tool such as Tableau
  • Strong experience collaborating with business stakeholders and users in the different finance groups to understand, document, and implement data & analytics needs
  • Experience implementing data governance framework and deliverables is desired
  • Experience with industry standard ETL tool (Matillion, Fivetran etc) preferred (not required)
