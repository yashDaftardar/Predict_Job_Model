About Athena Applied Intelligence

Athena is a cross-asset platform transforming technology at JP Morgan. It delivers innovative and efficient applications to a wide range of the firm's business, including sales, trading, operations, risk and research. Athena combines the best of open source technologies with innovative components developed internally, and serves a wide spectrum of users and stakeholders including the front office, quantitative researchers and technology teams.

In Athena Applied Intelligence team, we combine the power of Data Science (DS), AI, ML, optimization algorithms and automation to augment human intelligence and solve business problems at scale. We are on a mission to make better and smart use of data, identify and solve high-impact decision and optimization problems to unlock business value at scale.

You will be part of a highly creative, innovative and passionate team of engineers looking to generate actionable insights and advance the state-of-the-art in automated and scalable intelligent decision making. We embrace a culture of experimentation, collaboration, creativity and innovation, and constantly strive for improvement and continuous learning.

Responsibilities

Engineering a robust data platform to enable fast and effective discovery, retrieval, manipulation and presentation of clean, reliable and high-quality data is fundamental to the success of any data-driven, data science or AI project. Deploying solutions and integrating in existing or new products at scale are equally important. This is why we need you. Your role is highly critical and central to the mission of the team. You will be working hand-in-hand with Data Scientists and AI engineers to deliver reliable data-driven insights and robust intelligent solutions.

As a Senior Data/Software Engineer, you are expected to have strong hands-on experience in building scalable data engineering frameworks to enable effective use of data and productionization of DS/AI/ML solutions. You have proficient and practical knowledge of modern cloud-based data architectures and technologies enabling end-to-end data-driven services, including providing the appropriate data warehousing, data pipelines, catalogs, APIs, ETL, analytical and data quality tooling and solutions. You are a strong engineer with a passion for data intelligence and deep expertise in cutting-edge data technologies, robust design, development, coding and testing of high standard and scalable software.

You will lead efforts to architect and implement a solid data framework to address common data challenges including curating and exposing reliable complex datasets and integrating models to facilitate analytics, automation and DS/AI solutions at scale. Your deep expertise in modern practices and data engineering technologies for analytics, AI and ML will enable you and the team to intelligently transform data into value, innovate, create high-standard robust production capabilities and deliver increased business benefits. You will engage with data consumers and producers to design the best solutions that meet their needs. You will collaborate closely with data scientists and take a leading role in turning POCs to production engineering solutions. You will have ample opportunities to learn new skills including data science if interested.
