As a Data Scientist in the Source-to-Settle team, you will be developing statistical methodologies and models to monitor the effectiveness of the business, detect anomalies, and identify business improvement opportunities. This requires collaborating with various business leaders and senior management to understand business needs, provide accurate techniques to quantify effectiveness, and effectively present and communicate results.

Primary responsibilities will include:
Take defined business objectives and produce analytics to provide actionable insights

Effectively communicating with Procurement, Expense, Accounts Payable and senior business leaders to facilitate broader analytics understanding

Develop presentations and lead discussions on results of the analyses in a clear and effective manner through creative visualization

Researching, prototyping, and testing new methodologies for measuring effectiveness and building predictive algorithms

Developing post-hoc methods to improve the accuracy and efficiency of estimation

Be a leader in statistics and data analysis for the entire analytics team

Develop models for anomaly detection, fraud detection, recommendations, etc.

Identify new patterns through data mining

Miscellaneous tasks to support the reporting operations

Skills and qualifications:
Bachelor’s Degree in Data Science, Statistics or related fields

At least 3 years of experience in OBIEE/ Oracle Analytics Cloud, Data Warehouse, SQL, ML, NLP

Expertise with a statistical analysis software package (R, Python, or similar)

Exceptional problem-solving skills with focus on practical business implications.

Self-motivated, independent, organized and proactive

Ability to think critically and work autonomously with minimal supervision

Excellent communication and presentation skills

Procurement, Travel & Expense, or Accounts Payable analytics experience is a plus.

Capable of quickly becoming familiar with new approaches to Machine Learning.
