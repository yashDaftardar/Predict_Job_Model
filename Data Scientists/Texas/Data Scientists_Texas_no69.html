Full Job Description

ChampionX has an immediate need for a Director of Data Science & Analytics located in The Woodlands, TX . This is your opportunity to join a growing company offering a competitive base salary and benefits.”

What’s in it For You:

  • The ability to make an impact and shape your career with a company that is passionate about growth.
  • The support of an organization that believes it is vital to include and engage diverse people, perspectives, and ideas to achieve our best.
  • Thrive in a company that values sustainability, drives a safety-focused culture and has been recognized as one of the most ethical companies in the world.
  • Comprehensive benefits package starting day 1 of employment- medical, dental, vision, matching 401k, and more!

What You Will Do:

  • Oversee and directly contribute to the design, implementation, and maintenance of a complex suite of analytics and data science applications.
  • Ensure that all activities support the application-related needs of our company by aligning priorities with the business and customer needs.
  • Adept at working across organizational boundaries and partnering with leadership.
  • Report to the Leadership team on the progress and status of analytics projects
  • Work closely with business owners and subject matter experts to develop new analytics and AI requirements.
  • Work with vendors, peers, and stakeholders to develop and coordinate strategy, architecture, roadmap, budget, and execution of development services
  • Manage full cycle development process
  • Manage and mentor multiple software engineering teams both onshore and offshore
  • Gain a solid understanding of our development environment, identify technology gaps and lead projects to close those gaps
  • Creation of project architecture and detailed design documentation per required deliverables
  • Participate in defect triage and work with project team to resolve prioritized defect fixes
  • Create processes and supporting metrics to ensure efficiency and quality of application development implementation and maintenance
  • Partner with IT infrastructure, Software and Hardware development teams to design, secure, deploy, and support software products
  • Work on new technologies, proof-of-concepts, driving pilots, and product evaluations
  • Serve as Subject Matter Expert for existing applications and servers

Required Qualifications:

  • Bachelors or Masters in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics or equivalent with 10+ years of relevant experience
  • Experience querying databases and using statistical computer languages: R, Python, etc
  • Proficiency using query languages like SQL, Hive etc.
  • Experience with distributed data/computing tools: Hadoop, Hive, Spark, Oracle, SQL, Cosmos DB, Mongo DB,TimeScale DB etc.
  • Knowledge of extended technology products and components for Big Data and Cloud (Azure HDInsight, Data Lakes).
  • Strong problem-solving skills with an emphasis on product development.
  • Experience using statistical computer languages (R, Python, etc.) to manipulate data and draw insights from large data sets.
  • Experience working with and creating data architectures.
  • Must have experience managing teams of data scientists, data analytics
  • Strong knowledge of end-to-end data lifecycles across traditional data warehouses (Azure Synapse), relational databases (Oracle, SAP,SQL,etc), business intelligence reporting (Power BI), and big data analytics (Databricks).
