The Role


We are looking for Data Science Engineer / PyTorch / Keras / Tensorflow. If you are an engineer with an experience working on production level computer vision and deep learning models, then please read on!

What You'll Do

  • Working on a variety of computer vision and classification challenges, including vehicle collision estimation and part detection from images

  • Work with some of the largest and interesting integrated datasets around vehicle lifecycle, including estimation, repair procedures and salvage

  • Work on strategic projects powering the next wave of automation and intelligence around complete vehicle after sale ecosystem

  • Partner with a team of engineers across the USA, Spain and Switzerland

What You'll Bring

  • Working knowledge of data science ecosystem (scipy, numpy, pandas, seaborn, matplotlib, dask)

  • Must have a degree in a numerical field.

  • Proficient in PyTorch/Keras/Tensorflow.

  • Experience with image augmentation and preprocessing techniques using PIL, OpenCV, albumentation.

  • Expert in multi-label classification , object detection, image segmentation techniques.

  • Experience in building production software on Google Cloud Platform.

  • Disciplined developer well versed in Linux, Python stack.
