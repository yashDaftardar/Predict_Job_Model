We Are Hiring:
Summer 2021 Internship!
The Ascension Data Science Institute (ADSI) Internship Program is designed to provide an on-the-job learning opportunity aimed at gaining "real world" work experience in a large healthcare environment, supporting leadership with data driven insights that deliver value to those we serve. Ascension’s objective is to provide a working and learning environment where interns can develop the skills and competencies necessary to grow into successful professionals and potential Ascension associates.
What You Will Do:
Ascension’s Data Science and Outcomes Research Internship Program focuses on developing future healthcare leaders who will create innovative business insight and contribute to Ascension’s success. As part of the professional development process, participants will gain exposure to a variety of analytic projects. Work experience gained will enable participants to become well-rounded professionals and knowledgeable in several areas of statistics and applied data science. Such areas of practice include data engineering and automation, clinical algorithm development, predictive modeling and risk stratification, hypothesis testing and causal outcome evaluations, data storytelling and visualization.

Responsibilities:
  • Work with department leaders and team mentors to develop project plans and timelines.
  • Gain experience manipulating and validating data from multiple sources including EMR and claims databases.
  • Analyze data bringing your own curiosity and expertise to meet project objectives and deliver additional insights.
  • Present final project to team members, data science leadership and potentially clinical stakeholders.
  • Gain hands on experience working in Google Cloud Platform, Python or other preferred analytic tool.
What You Will Need:
Education:
  • High school diploma/GED required.
  • Enrolled in degreed program specific to discipline required.
Additional Preferences:
    • Enrolled in a Graduate program in Data Science, Mathematics, Biostatistics, Health Economics and Outcomes Research or related field.
Preferred Competencies:
  • Strong technical skills, specifically in writing queries, manipulating data and visualizing data.
  • Excellent SQL skills and major plus if versatile in R, Tableau and/or Python.
  • Demonstrated ability to find patterns and provide insights into the underlying data.
  • Ability to deliver results.
  • Deals well with ambiguity and “start-up” environments.
  • Excellent communication skills.
Why Join Our Team:
Ascension is a faith-based healthcare organization dedicated to transformation through innovation across the continuum of care. As one of the leading non-profit and Catholic health systems in the U.S., Ascension is committed to delivering compassionate, personalized care to all. In FY2020, Ascension provided $2.4 billion in care of persons living in poverty and other community benefit programs. Ascension includes more than 160,000 associates and 40,000 aligned providers across a national network of ministries. We offer rewarding careers across more than 2,600 sites of care – including 146 hospitals and more than 50 senior living facilities – in 19 states and the District of Columbia.
