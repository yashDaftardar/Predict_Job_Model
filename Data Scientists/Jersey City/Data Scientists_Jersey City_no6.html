Job Description:

As a Data Scientist within Fidelity’s Risk Data Science team, you will be developing real-time solutions that continuously improve how we protect our customers and reputation. The candidate will work closely with partners across Legal, Risk, and Compliance to cultivate machine learning opportunities and transform them into cutting edge risk management capabilities. Taking on some of Fidelity’s most exciting problems will require the successful candidate to exhibit creativity, independence, and a true send of ownership.

The Expertise You Have

  • Master’s Degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Data Science, Business Analytics, or similar technical field with at least 1 year of relevant professional experience
  • Detailed understanding of Machine Learning & Deep Learning algorithms
  • Knowledge of most of the following quantitative fields: Supervised Learning, Unsupervised Learning, Anomaly Detection, Time Series Forecasting, NLP/NLU, Deep Learning, and Experiment Design
  • The candidate should have extensive experience applying statistics, analytics, machine learning and deep learning techniques in a professional context
  • Intermediate statistical and data science programming skills in Python
  • Proficient in SQL

The Skills You Bring

  • Experience leading Machine Learning initiatives from inception to Production deployment
  • Experience with Big Data and Machine Learning in cloud ecosystems (AWS certification is preferred)
  • Experience with Deep Learning tools (e.g. TensorFlow, Keras, MXNET, H2O) is preferred
  • Experience with streaming technologies (e.g. Kafka, NiFi) is preferred
  • Proven ability to rapidly iterate on ideas, valuing pace over perfection
  • Financial Services experience is preferred but not required

The Value You Deliver

  • Carry out various aspects of data science projects, including exploratory data analysis, data cleansing, preparation and annotation, feature engineering, ML pipeline design/development, and model evaluation/selection
  • Lead multiple concurrent projects of varying scope and complexity
  • Deliver clean, commented, scalable code
  • Develop and present findings and recommendations to both technical and non-technical partners
  • Evaluate new technologies in collaboration with Product, IT, and Data Engineering teams
  • Use strong interpersonal skills to establish positive relationships with teammates and partners
  • Generates new insights and seizes opportunities by staying abreast of publications, tools, and techniques from the global AI/ML community
