Introduction
IBM Watson Advertising (IWA), formerly The Weather Company’s advertising sales division, uses the world’s leading artificial intelligence (AI) technology to unlock data and insights and help marketers make better decisions. IWA offers trusted, insight-driven media, data, and technology solutions that can be deployed across a Brand or Platform’s entire marketing strategy to help drive results.

Your Role and Responsibilities
We are hiring a Data and Technology Subject Matter Expert. You will provide subject matter expertise in AI-powered Watson Advertising solutions including: Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning, dynamic creative technology, predictive data solutions, conversational design, analytics, and social media strategy. You will also support sales and partnership’s team in technical expertise internally and client-facing to help drive revenue objectives.
  • Provide subject matter expertise across Watson Advertising’s suite of data and technology solutions in market
  • Work across a matrixed org to become well versed in product functionality
  • Support sales teams by way of identifying client needs, KPIs, infrastructure and helping to navigate their current ecosystem
  • Identify trends, gaps, and common themes coming from the market in response to our solutions
  • Collaborate with product, sales, and partnerships to help define roadmaps based on market feedback
  • Utilize current relationships to help evangelize tech and data products across the agency landscape to identify new revenue streams
  • Utilize various internal tools to track opportunities and contribute to accurate forecasting exercises
  • Prioritize tasks and accounts based on revenue
  • Provide weekly reports of activity, learnings, and feedback from the market.

Required Technical and Professional Expertise
  • 8+ years of Data & Technology Experience, either at client, publisher, or agency
  • Deep knowledge of total data and technology marketing landscape
  • Consultative sales and proposal writing experience


Preferred Technical and Professional Expertise
  • Experienced in identifying and establishing prospect lists
  • Collaborative abilities to work with internal production team
  • Proficiency & experience developing and executing strategic marketing plans
