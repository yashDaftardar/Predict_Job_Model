Full Job Description

Zeno Group is looking for an Intern to join the Research, Measurement + Strategic Analytics team in DC or New York for three to six months. This unique opportunity will enable the Intern to receive training as a Data Scientist supporting the team through analysis, performance reporting, market segmentation, audience insights, and general analytics duties related to the digital marketing, web analytics, and market research.

This role will be part of an integrated analytics and research team, working in a fast-paced environment, with a versatile set of quantitative and digital skills to help support the team in primary and secondary research, statistical analysis, predictive modeling, digital marketing, and web analytics.

ABOUT THE JOB
We are currently searching for a full-time Intern to join our burgeoning Research, Measurement and Strategic Analytics team. The ideal candidate will be passionate about detecting patterns in data, data storytelling & visualizations as well as the news, trends, and pop culture. Ideal candidate shows a desire to understand what makes distinct consumer audiences tick. In keeping with our philosophy of listening before we speak, Zeno is unique among PR agencies of our size due in large part to our dedicated data-driven planning function. As a result, we never do things based strictly on guesswork or mere intuition. We use data to find out what stories they tell us.
The team looks at relevant industry trends; we dig deep to comprehend the cultural and societal landscape, so that everything we do is anchored by a clear and resonant strategy. Equipped with a validated insight, we deliver a strategy that ladders-up to a creative platform and galvanizing idea that charts a path to optimal engagement.
This position will encompass a wide array of responsibilities, with a focus on analytics & research, data analysis, visualization, writing, presentation development and supporting initiatives for Zeno clients and account teams.

ABOUT YOU
Ideal candidate has a passion for building digital infrastructure, automating repetitive tasks, implementing system integrations, and constantly building efficiencies in team’s workflow. Ideally, you are a proactive self-starter. You don’t wait for assignments to be handed to you, but rather, you look for the next big idea or project and bring it to life. You pride yourself in being able to set and realize a clear and compelling vision. You are inquisitive and curious. You have the innate ability to connect the dots when others struggle, even when you don’t have all the facts.

More specifically:

    • Bachelor’s degree preferred
    • Familiarity with R or Python for segmentation, regression analyses, multi-variant testing, and building predictive models for marketing campaigns
    • Familiarity with data visualization and dashboard reporting techniques and platforms (if you have experience with Tableau, we strongly recommend to apply).
    • If you have hands-on experience with either HTML/CSS or web analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or Google Tag Manager), we strongly recommend to apply.
    • Ability to organize one’s thinking to establish a thoughtful point of view on work
    • Great organizational and interpersonal skills
    • Collaborative spirit, results driven with the ability to manage multiple priorities and work against deadlines
    • Resourceful and proactive with the ability to operate effectively both independently and within a team
