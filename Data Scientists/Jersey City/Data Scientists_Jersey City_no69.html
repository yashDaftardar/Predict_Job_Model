About the job

The Business Strategy & Operations organization provides business critical insights using analytics, ensures cross functional alignment of goals and execution, and helps teams drive strategic partnerships and new initiatives forward. We stay focused on aligning the highest-level company priorities with effective day-to-day operations, and help evolve early stage ideas into future-growth initiatives.

The Google Customer Solutions (GCS) Product and Solutions Activation (PSA) team is responsible for commercializing performance and brand solutions for small-and-medium businesses. As part of Google Customer Solutions’ Display and YouTube organization, the Strategy and Operations team provides business critical insights using analytics, ensures cross functional alignment of goals and execution, and helps teams drive strategic partnerships and initiatives forward. We focus on aligning highest-level company priorities with effective day-to-day operations, and help evolve early stage ideas into future-growth initiatives. As the Strategy and Operations Lead, you will work on strategic, cross-functional, data-led initiatives to drive product and go-to-market decisions that grow our GCS Display and/or YouTube businesses. You will help to drive business impact by leading strategic, cross-functional, analytical projects to drive product and go-to-market plans or initiatives. You'll also build, plan, operationalize, and maintain the organization’s end-to-end business rhythm to ensure strong execution of our objectives. Lastly, you will design and manage sales incentives that accelerate Display and Video growth.

When our millions of advertisers and publishers are happy, so are we! Our Google Customer Solutions (GCS) team of entrepreneurial, enthusiastic and client-focused members are the "human face" of Google, helping entrepreneurs both individually and broadly build their online presence and grow their businesses. We are dedicated to growing the unique needs of advertising companies. Our teams of strategists, analysts, advisers and support specialists collaborate closely to spot and analyze customer needs and trends. In collaboration, we create and implement business plans broadly for all types of businesses.

Responsibilities

  • Own the design, development, implementation, and management of business incentives to drive Display and YouTube performance.
  • Analyze business drivers and trends, partner with regional teams to derive deeper market insights; synthesize data and create analytical tools and/or presentations to communicate insights to senior management and the broader organization.
  • Lead cross-functional strategy and operations projects to grow the GCS Display and Video ads business; support implementation and oversight of those projects to achieve forecast objectives.
  • Coordinate with product leads and partner teams to define and manage the end-to-end business rhythm for business plans and reviews; identify operating efficiencies and create systems or processes to improve operations.
  • Develop strong cross-functional relationships and collaborate across Google to drive key initiatives: product leads, regional activation teams, Sales, Sales Finance, Marketing, Revenue Strategy and Operations, and others.
