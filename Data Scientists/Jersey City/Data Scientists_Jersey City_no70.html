Data Scientist
New York, NY

As a Barclays Data Scientist you are accountable for development of visualization models (including behavioral/predictive models) to be used in developing in-house trade surveillance solutions. In this role you will be utilizing a wide set of tools, including technical programming skills (Python, Bokeh, SQL), statistics, and a deep understanding of transaction monitoring and business objectives. This role sits with Markets Operational Rigour which partners with Trading, Distribution and other key stakeholders.

Barclays is one of the world's largest and most respected financial institutions, with 329 years of success, quality and innovation behind us. We've helped millions of individuals and businesses thrive, creating financial and digital solutions that the world now takes for granted. An important and growing presence in the USA, we offer careers providing endless opportunity.

What will you be doing?
• Co-ordination and development of the Supervisory Framework across the Investment Bank
  • Work closely with the technology leads, business analysts and business users to define requirements, oversee infrastructure builds, complete UAT and production deployments
  • Issuance of Markets risk and control related Standards and Procedures & Review of Group and IB Policies and Standards for a Markets Perspective
  • Front Office Self Assessments and the enhancement of risk & control processes
  • Responsible for running surveillance reports and escalation procedures as a BAU function
  • Surveillance (pan-IB) and Transaction MI in support of supervisory requirements (pan-markets)
  • Governance Framework and associated Markets risk and control MIS
What we’re looking for:
• 4-year Bachelor’s/Master’s Degree in Computer Science or Engineering degree
  • Experience in Front Office, Operations, Compliance, Regulatory, or Risk Management within a large global financial institution
  • Strong Python and related libraries, SQL, Conda framework
  • Exposure to Bokeh(Advanced), JavaScript, q/kdb, qpython(or pyq,Embedpy)
Skills that will help you in the role:
• Data Oriented and prior experience in understanding business requirements to deliver full scale working solution using Python (Conda framework).
  • Exposure to Unix environment
  • Data analysis on structured and unstructured data– predictive modelling, clustering
  • Solid understanding of cross asset classes Equity, Rates, FX

Where will you be working?
You will be working at our Americas Headquarters at 745 Seventh Avenue. This 37-story office tower is located in Times Square in the heart of Manhattan and features a cafeteria, fitness center and state-of-the-art LED signage on the facade of the building.
