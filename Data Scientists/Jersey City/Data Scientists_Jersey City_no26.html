Full Job Description

  • Responsible for developing data-driven innovative analytical solutions and identifying opportunities to support business and client needs in a quantitative manner and facilitate informed recommendations / decisions
  • Activities include developing and creating predictive models, behavioral segmentation frameworks, profitability analyses, ad hoc reporting and data visualizations

Responsibilities
  • Leads complex initiatives and projects to derive insights from extracted data to solve critical business questions
  • Translates client/ stakeholder needs into technical analyses and/or solutions in collaboration with internal and external partners and presents findings and outcomes to clients/ stakeholders
  • Identify rich data sources and monitors the melding and cleaning of datasets to ensure consistency
  • Accountable for delivering high quality project solutions and tools within agreed upon timelines and budget parameters and conducting post- implementation reviews
  • Guides other to develop sophisticated multivariate models and analytic solutions (e.g., dashboards, prototypes) utilizing complex statistical and data science techniques and methods
  • Delegates and reviews work for junior level colleagues to ensure downstream applications and tools are not compromised or delayed
  • Serves as a technical coach for junior-level colleagues and develops technical talent via ongoing technical training, peer review etc.

Experiences
  • Demonstrated success when contributing accurate, technically innovative components for analytics/ product development projects
  • Experience managing day-to-day cross-functional communications and collaboration to meet project deadlines
  • Demonstrated success collaborating with clients and effectively communicating project outcomes, methodologies and considerations to key stakeholders
  • Successful track record in independently conducting complex analyses and guiding junior team members through complex/ ambiguous issues
  • Served as an expert resource or go-to person for a specific consulting tool(s), system(s), and/or application(s)
  • Experience with network infrastructure reporting and related projects (End of Life, Asset Management, etc)
  • Proven track record of creating simplified reporting from multiple data sources for various audiences
  • Experience interpreting and analyzing data to drive key initiatives
