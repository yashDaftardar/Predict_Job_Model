ABOUT THE TEAM

J.Crew LLC is seeking a Data Scientist to join a growing Customer Analytics and Data Science team that is obsessed with data, customer insights and driving digital innovation. Come join our team and help us reinvent retail.


As Data Scientist you’ll report to the Head of Customer Analytics and Data Science. You will be a pioneering member of a performance-driven team. You will be the empirical, numbers-driven voice of reason that helps elevate all three brands’ customer strategies. You will be focused on building predictive data models to drive strategic business decisions and deliver insights across three unique brands (Madewell, J.Crew, and Factory). This is a high-visibility, high-impact, individual contributor role.


THINK YOU WOULD ENJOY DOING THIS?

  • Design and build models, ie. Clustering/Segmentation, Forecasting, Predictive CLV and Churn, Multi-Touch Attribution, Market basket, Product recommendation
  • Productionalize, maintain and recommend models to business stakeholders – identify opportunities to drive business with new analytic techniques or predictive models
  • Manipulating large datasets for analyses and/or developing automated processes for Insights democratization


YES? GREAT! NOW…


DOES THIS SOUND LIKE YOU?

  • 2-5 years of experience with strong analytical skills
  • Experience with customer behavioral analytics and/or retail industry experience strongly preferred
  • BS in Economics, Statistics, Information Management, Computer Science, Mathematics, or similar quantitative field
  • Proficiency with either Python or R; Experience with Snowflake, AWS, CDPs is a plus
  • Proficiency with SQL and Data Visualization and Insight presentation tools (Power BI – or Tableau, Excel)


BONUS POINTS IF YOU ARE…

  • Objective and scientifically hypothesis-driven: obsessed with finding the right answer; lets numbers define the story rather than finding numbers to support a hypothesis
  • Technical and business-savvy: not only able to write code and employ data/statistical methods, but also understands the business (the why)
  • A forward-thinking visionary, keeping up with Data Science/Analytics trends and always on the lookout to improve on the status quo
  • Comfortable working in an agile environment; creative, curious, and eager to learn
  • Familiar with the complexities and nuances of retail databases, CDPs (Customer Data Platforms), and customer behavioral analysis preferred
  • Able to communicate well with Analysts and Engineers as well as Business Stakeholders
  • Organized and able to work on multiple projects at a time
