Leadership Commitment

Cognizant leadership has strategically devoted a lot of attention to our Gen C program and fully recognizes these associates as the future of Cognizant. This stems from the investment dollars spent on developing our talent. Gen C AIA associates are introduced to the company culture immediately via a weeklong training program headed by Cognizant Academy. They are then provided around two months of role specific technical training to ensure a successful client placement. Career Launchpad

With so much innovation and creativity happening in one place, there is bound to be someone that has faced a similar challenge within the building. Responsibilities

A Data Scientist in the AI and Analytics Practice brings relevant context to complex data for business and IT for intelligent systems and human decisioning. They work within the domains of data management, business intelligence, descriptive analytics and advanced modeling. They will use a variety of machine learning and AI approaches to deliver better, faster, and smarter insights and intelligence to some of the world’s most successful businesses – our clients. Specific responsibilities include:

Building an in-depth understanding of the opportunity, challenge or issue in the domain and its associated data

Interact with and integrate large, diverse and impactful datasets across business functions

Research, design, implement, and evaluate advanced analytics, including machine learning models

Take initiative in evaluating and adapting new approaches and methods from data science research

Investigate data visualization and summarization techniques for conveying key findings

Responsible for effectively telling the “data and analytics story” to business users in a clear and concise manner

Communicate findings, obstacles and opportunities to stakeholders to help drive the delivery to market

Bring value from data to business users

Basic Qualifications

Graduate degree in IT-related field and 0-3 years of IT experience

Strong problem solving and analytical thinking skills

Strong business communication skills, both written and oral

Hands on experience in data cleaning and data quality assessment, hypotheses testing, data exploration and mining, visualization techniques, algorithm selection and building predictive models using traditional as well as machine learning algorithms

Hands on experience with traditional modeling algorithms (e.g. regression, log-regression, clustering, predictive modeling, etc.)

Hands on experience with Python and R/R Studio

Exposure to working with SQL, Relational Database Management Systems (e.g. SQL Server, Oracle)

Distinguishing Qualifications

Familiarity with advanced machine learning algorithms such as neural networks and other deep learning approaches

Familiarity with developing and deploying models on the Hadoop environment

Some experience with AWS Sagemaker, Google TensorFlow or MS Azure ML

Experience with collaborative development workflows (e.g., Microsoft VSTS)

Working knowledge of application development using Hadoop components (HDFS, Spark, Hive, etc.)

Working knowledge of Tableau or other BI visualization tools, Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics, SAS

Open Source Tools in any of the above with experience in D3/R/Shiny/Python/GitHub

Awareness of Narrative Science/ YSEOP/Automated Insights and concepts of NLG/NLP
