Data Analyst, Finance FP&A

Data Analyst, FP&A – Avis Budget Group

About Avis Budget Group

Avis, a Fortune 500, $5.4B global company is seeking a skilled finance Data Analyst to join our dynamic team. Our globally recognized brands - Avis, Budget, Zipcar, and others- are shaping the future of mobility with innovative, customer-focused solutions. Our global reach and strong data-based market intelligence position us well as a leading provider of mobility options, with more than 70 years of mobility experience and 11,000 locations. At Avis Budget Group, we believe mobility as a service should be completely connected, integrated, and on-demand for our customers and other businesses, connecting people to more.

The impact you’ll make:

ABG is seeking a dynamic, agile and creative Data Analyst with strong analytics capabilities, to help bring an innovative and empirically-sound, data driven point of view to our global Finance and Accounting team. You will help to architect new approaches that will leverage your data and analytics skills to provide better visibility into financial systems and strategy, in support of senior leadership, and with a strong voice at the table. You will help to develop insights that enable leadership to make effective, data-driven decisions, with broad impact across the organization. The ability to identify areas for process improvement, and to advocate a well-reasoned and empirically sound perspective to help further business objectives, is essential.

This individual needs to be articulate, decisive and confident and be able to represent business needs and bridge the communication with the IT and the BI and analytics areas. This person needs to articulate complex problems and promote solutions in front of a variety of audiences ranging from IT groups to Business stakeholders, VP level and above included.

This person is a strong listener, able to ensure collated business and technology strategies are reflected in the vision on how to build and operationalize analytics models using technology. This individual will provide hands-on consultancy, collaboration and support to internal clients and project teams in designing and delivering business and data analytics work.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

  • Support the accounting and finance stakeholders with automation and build out customized analytic reports for financial and operational reporting
  • Aggregate data points from existing ABG proprietary systems and commercial and external data sources, to create relevant reporting outcomes in support of FP&A.
  • Perform data analysis to generate reports and recommendations aligned to business needs and objectives.
  • Provides results from research to assist management in decision-making.
  • Analyze and determines data, system, and program requirements in addition to system specifications for departmental projects.
  • Monitor digital & software platforms/predictive analytics offerings available in the market. Make recommendations to further enhance Avis's capabilities to allow data to drive business strategy.
  • Work with business partners and IT integrations team to define and create reporting dashboards, and support ongoing data monitoring for financial data across systems
  • Works with ABG executive team, directors and managers to assess analytics needs
  • Monitors and provides analytics and data science consulting for projects, programs and initiatives in ABG
  • Consistently leads and influences broad, cross-functional teams, including IT and non-IT teams
  • Directs the analysis of ways to control costs, maintain efficiency, and improve effectiveness of our business processes

Required Experience

  • Minimum of 6-8 years of relevant work experience in data analytics
  • Bachelor’s degree required in Mathematics or related discipline or equivalent.
  • Demonstrated success in supporting Finance, FP&A or other business units in house in creating and enhancing data analytics reporting and research.
  • Advanced Degree in Operations Research, Statistics, or Business preferred

Required Knowledge, Skills and Abilities

  • Exceptional analytical, logical reasoning and problem solving skills
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Exemplary teamwork skills as well as excellent interpersonal skills
  • Past experience in designing or implementing enterprise-scale data analytics systems required
  • Self-starter: able to initiate, prioritize, and complete assignments with little to no supervision
  • Ability to work well with diverse teams, and comfort engaging senior leadership.
  • Excellent documentation skills required and the ability to work effectively with clients, IT management, and their staffs.
  • Tableau developer skills required
  • Eagerness and ability to learn and adapt technologies, industry practices, tools, and trends
