Job Title: US Securitized Trading - Data Scientist

Corporate Title: Associate

Location: New York, NY

Overview

The US Securitized Trading desk sits within the Global Financing & Credit Trading (GF & CT) business unit. The trading desk acts as a market maker in securitized bonds to provide liquidity to our clients. You will be at an Associate level and will be responsible for critically analyzing large data sets to generate profitable trading ideas for specific sectors/credits for the Deutsche Bank trading desk & our clients. You will partner closely with Trading, the Institutional Client Group (ICG), and Capital Markets to provide assess opportunities within the market & enable effective risk positioning.

What We Offer You:

  • We offer competitive health and wellness benefits, empowering you to value life in and out of the office

  • Retirement savings plans, parental leave, and other family-friendly programs

  • An environment that encourages networking and collaboration across functions and businesses

  • Active engagement with the local community through Deutsche Bank’s specialized employee groups

Hear from our people and look inside our office: DB@The Muse

Your Key Responsibilities:

  • Use statistical analysis to identify profitable trading opportunities for the desk & our clients

  • Build and analyze clean data sets from various database sources

  • Work closely with the Bank’s traders to ensure sensible risk positioning in trading books

  • Leverage the ICG Credit sales team to create an active feedback loop with our clients

  • Support Capital Markets by providing market color around the Bank’s led new issues

Your Skills and Experience:

  • Previous experience as a data scientist either on the buy/sell side

  • Existing relationships with market participants in this space

  • Previous experience with credit analysis and statistical modelling, ideally resulting in trading ideas

  • History of strong risk management and identifying profitable trading opportunities
