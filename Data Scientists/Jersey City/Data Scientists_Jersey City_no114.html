Job Description:
As part of MSK's new and exciting digital transformation, we are hiring enthusiastic data scientist team members to build and deliver a one-of-a-kind data facility. You'll have the chance to work with clinicians and researchers to achieve the goal of translating institutional information into meaningful insights in order to improve patient care, hospital operations, and clinical research.

About us – Unified Data Fabric

We are developing one of the most robust oncology data ecosystems in all of healthcare, the Unified Data Fabric. The integration of clinical, genomic, operational, and financial data will lead to countless insights, through the integration of high quality, authoritative, discrete, unstructured, streaming, and imaging data, by way of creating a unique and unprecedented healthcare data platform. This data platform will be a combination of cloud and on-prem technologies and will be built on state-of-the-art architecture that includes APIs, object stores, data streaming, image processing, DBMS pipelines and data warehousing technologies, with a foundation of enterprise data governance and stewardship practices including advanced semantics and machine learning to generate more data of value within the Unified Data Fabric.

What you'll be doing:

  • Participating in full SAFe development life cycle eg. performing analysis, designing, sourcing and standardization, sophisticated data quality, building, releasing, and maintaining data pipelines for ingestion and propagation of imaging, genomic, and clinical data
  • Working collaboratively on an agile product team with architects, engineers, scrum masters, product owners, and business owners
  • Partnering with the business to identify, define and standardize data for the clinical, research and administrative parts of the organization
  • Developing APIs and data pipelines to build UDF
  • Crafting efficient and reusable modularized components that drive complex applications
  • Mentoring and providing guidance to junior colleagues and new team members

Our ideal candidate is:

  • Solid understanding of data analysis tools, with data science preferred
  • Passionate about data and creating new solutions to empower cancer care and research
  • Strong knowledge of programming languages, ETL tools and their administration, and exposure to relational/non-relational databases OR
  • Bonus : familiar with Cloud providers and Knowledge agile methodologies i.e. SAFe a strong plus
