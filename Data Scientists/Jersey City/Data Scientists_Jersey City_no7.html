About the Role
We are a fast-paced and fast-growing company that is looking for enthusiastic interns to join our Fall 2021Internship Program! Our Data Scientist Internship will expose you to a 360-degree view of our business and our industry.

Here at Madison Logic, we like to pride ourselves on our unique company culture where we want to make you feel as comfortable as possible (here’s where that generic saying, “work hard, play harder” comes into effect). We’re here to teach you the basics and we encourage you to ask ANY questions you may have about your specific position, our company, or the industry! We want to help you as much as you’re helping us.

What You'll Do:

    • Act as a hands-on Data Scientist intern by tackling a rich set of tangible problems that have measurable business impacts
    • Participate in brainstorming sessions and being able to describe patterns in data using math/stats and visualisation softwares.
    • Apply your optimization, machine learning and AI skills to solve business challenges
    • Partner with internal stakeholders to assess and provide data analysis & strategy recommendations for business questions
    • Keep up to date on trends, tools and research

What You Have:

    • Rising Senior or recent Graduate from an accredited institution, pursuing a degree focused in Technology, Data, Statistics, or Mathematics
    • Experience with Python or R.
    • Familiarity with data visualization packages in R/Python or BI tools.
    • Working knowledge of Regression Analysis, model testing, logistic regression, GLM
    • Sound knowledge of statistics
    • Knowledge of one or more of: GBM, K-means, Neural Networks, SVMs, Bayesian Networks, NLP etc
    • Knowledge of SQL

Internship Details:

    • Fall Program Dates: August 23rd - December 3rd
    • $18 per hour
    • Hours: 9 AM – 5 PM
