Job Description:

The Nokia student co-op program runs parallel to a student’s current class work allowing them to remain in school while gaining work experience. The student will develop feature learning algorithms for time series data, collect IoT data for testing, evaluation of the algorithms on the collected data.
Co-ops must be actively pursuing an undergraduate or graduate degree to be eligible for the program, Remote work is possible due to covid situation.

Key Job Responsibilities:

  • Participate developing and evaluating data science solutions to automate machine learning
  • Design and implement efficient and accurate algorithms suitable for large scale data
  • Work will be conducted jointly with team members from various background

Skills and Experience:

Desired qualifications:

  • Ph.D. candidate in Statistics or Computer Science with training in Machine Learning, Data Mining (or a similar quantitative field)
  • Deep knowledge in statistical theory
  • Familiarity with classical and modern tools in statistics and machine learning
  • Ideally past experience in analyzing large scale data.
  • A potential for performing original research with strong, practical impact
  • A collaborative spirit, broad technical and application interests, and strong communication skills.
  • Experience in machine learning tools. Experience in text analytics is a plus.
Required Qualifications:
  • Familiar with R/Shiny
  • Must have at least a 3.0 GPA.
  • Able to work from the Murray Hill, NJ office.
