Job Title – Erwin Data Modeler

Responsible for the design, implementation, support and maintenance of relational database model systems

Creation/maintenance of conceptual, logical and physical data models,

Extensive experience in ERwin tool

Database solution design. In this role the candidate will recommend and monitor overall standards and procedures,

Provide solution design guidance to other technical staff, and assist in integrating various systems.

Consult with users to determine requirements needed to guide development of the data models.

Data warehouse expertise in OLAP and relational modeling (3NF/OLTP) with a wide repertoire of domain experiences

Working with day to day Repository Management activities like Model Merging, User Role Creation, User Access Grants, Model Freezing and Model Version Management

Responsible for reverse engineering and synchronizing existing legacy data models

Responsible for creating documentation, supporting ETL Architect with creation of mapping documents.
