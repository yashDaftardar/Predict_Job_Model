Your role

Do you enjoy consulting and leading data management and digital transformation projects? Are you looking for a unique opportunity to drive UBS's accelerating digital transformation? Are you passionate about new technologies like: Artificial Intelligence, Cognitive Automation, Advanced Analytics, Blockchain? Are you passionate about how businesses manage and process data to further digitize user experiences?

We're looking for someone like this to:
  • lead, consult and implement data management transformation following the UBS' Data Management Framework across all business functions
  • understand and consult on management of business process data and how to leverage the latest technological trends to achieve their data management strategies
  • build trust and strong working relationships with Business and Technology stakeholders
  • advise stakeholders on data management to address their specific needs, including discussing potential approaches to problems, associated costs and trade-offs, and recommendations
  • support Data management Sponsors in delivering their Data Strategy, in line with the firm's related divisional / functional strategic objectives
  • push forward the capabilities and performance of the team to strengthen and expand the service offerings of the practice

Your team

You will be working in the Digital Practice with a focus on Data Management Implementation. We are a dynamic and diverse team of transformation professionals with focus on emerging technology transformation. This unit is part of the broader created Group Internal Consulting department.

Our practice teams help UBS business functions implement their digital strategy and accomplish their ambitions in the areas of emerging technologies like Artificial Intelligence (AI), Advanced Analytics, Cognitive Automation and Blockchain. The Digital Practice is at the intersection of business, technology and innovative thinking. We accompany functions across all businesses to build a sustainable transformation; combining the power of emerging technologies with a profound understanding of UBS businesses. The team works in in close collaboration with the different functions, business divisions and external parties to achieve its goals.
