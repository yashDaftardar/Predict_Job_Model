Position Summary

The Senior Domain Business Data Analyst will work closely with business stakeholders to develop and implement strategies to leverage data for enhanced decision making and to generate actionable insights which drive incremental business value for both GreatAmerica and GreatAmericaâ€™s customers. Analytics projects will run the full spectrum of the business intelligence maturity model including descriptive, predictive, and prescriptive analysis. Mentorship of business partners to improve data literacy will be a core function of this role.

Essential Functions

  • Participate as integral component of the Operation Domain leadership teams. Help identify and drive material business capability improvements
  • Participate in the development of the Operation Domain operational reviews. Develop and populate actionable information for the operational review
  • Work with business stakeholders to identify opportunities for use of data to deliver incremental business value to the functional areas and/or business units
  • Gather, profile, aggregate and transform data to support analytic requirements
  • Develop actionable data visualization vehicles for use by business leaders on an intraday and next day basis to change business outcomes.
  • Perform data mining activities following the CRISP-DM process to solve business problems. This will include both predictive and prescriptive modeling
  • Build and manage models to forecast key company metrics and to better understand the underlying drivers of their performance. Conduct model validation activities post deployment
  • Communicate analytical results via both written and oral presentation to business stakeholders
  • Identify gaps in data collection and collaborate with engineering on data capture specifications
  • Educate and mentor business stakeholders on the application of analytical approaches (including problem statement definition, decision trees, trending analysis, forecasting, key performance indicators, etc.)
  • Promote a data driven culture throughout GreatAmerica via active participation in a data champion community utilized for knowledge and best practice sharing.
