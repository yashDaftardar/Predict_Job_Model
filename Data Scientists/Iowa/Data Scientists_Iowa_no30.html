Full Job Description

RFA Engineering (www.rfamec.com) is seeking a ML Ops Engineer (Machine Learning Operations Engineer) to join our engineering team in the Des Moines, Iowa area. You will work with our engineering staff to provide highly engineered advanced R&D solutions for our clients using state of the art engineering tools. This position will be temporarily remote until Covid restrictions are lifted.

ML Ops Engineer

This combines aspects of Data Science, Machine Learning and traditional Dev-Ops into a hybrid role to design, build and manage reproducible, testable and evolvable machine learning enabled applications in a CI/CD environment.

The successful candidate will build data pipelines used for training computer-vision based machine learning models. This involves capturing, organizing, and conditioning data from remote sources for use in development and training of new applications. Typical applications will relate to automation of processes for the next generation of agricultural and construction equipment and vehicles.

Job responsibilities

  • Build and maintain data pipelines and tools for ingestion, metadata ingestion, labelling, data segregation, data enrichment and training
  • Create and maintain organizational structure to meet various training needs using automated data processes, tools, and custom scripts
  • Work with other team members to optimize efforts for best efficiency and quality of outcome

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree or higher in Computer Science, Software, Computer, or Electrical Engineering or significant related experience
  • 2+ years of software engineering/programming experience in a professional environment
  • Significant experience with Python
  • Linux experience
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Ability to thrive in a team environment

Desired Attributes

  • Experience using Python with OpenCV and Pillow
  • Ability to create basic GUI with Python
  • Experience working with Docker and Kubernetes/Kubeflow
  • Proficient with AWS ECR or S3
  • Knowledge of SQL or NoSQL

About RFA Engineering

RFA Engineering has provided product development and engineering services to industry leading customers since 1943. Our primary focus is development of off highway equipment including agricultural, construction, mining, recreational, industrial, and special machines. Our work includes concept development, product design, documentation, problem-solving, simulation, optimization, and testing of components, systems and complete machines. Our engineering staff is located at our Engineering Center in Minneapolis, branch office in Dubuque, IA, and numerous customer sites throughout the U.S.
