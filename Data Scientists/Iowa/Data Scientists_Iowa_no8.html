Full Job Description

Overview:
Open to remote candidates or candidates within any of our UnityPoint Health locations/regions.

UnityPoint Health is building an industry leading analytics function by aligning resources with expertise in technology solutions, claims analysis, financial analysis, clinical operations, advanced data mining tools, and analytic techniques. This team will support the strategic objectives of UnityPoint and collaborate with customers across the care continuum to ensure the appropriate information is available to drive decision making and support business needs.
Responsibilities:
Analytics
  • Support the data science product life cycle: translate business problems into data science solutions, design and develop data pipelines, formulate mathematical methodologies, create user interfaces and visualizations, and support solution implementation and maintenance
  • Maintain a working knowledge of the data sources to accurately and efficiently report findings in a meaningful and understandable manner.
  • Execute ad hoc analyses involving descriptive, inferential, and predictive statistical or machine learning methods and make the results accessible and relevant to a wide variety of business users
  • Develop and refine analysis templates for problem diagnosis and opportunities assessment
  • Collaborate effectively with other analytical team members
  • Demonstrate initiative to improve quality and customer service by striving to exceed customer expectations.
  • Establish effective mechanisms of communication with staff, providers, and the organization’s management team to foster an environment of openness, trust and team work
  • Comply with UnityPoint Health data governance and development standards
  • Completes special projects and management reports as assigned
  • Balance team and individual responsibilities and put the success of the team above own interests

Qualifications:
Education:
  • Bachelor’s Degree in an applied technical field, including (but not limited to): mathematics, statistics, operations research, engineering, or physics.
  • Master’s Degree in an applied technical field, including (but not limited to): mathematics, statistics, operations research, engineering, or physics preferred.
Experience:
  • 1-3 years of experience in mathematical modeling is required. Preference given to applicants with healthcare experience and a deep understanding of the healthcare industry
  • Must be comfortable working almost exclusively in scripting/statistical programming languages such as SQL, R, and Python when creating solutions
Preference given to applicants with previous experience in:
  • Healthcare data science applications and knowledge of data common in an integrated health system
  • scripting/statistical programming languages such as SQL, R, and Python
  • querying databases
  • customer facing communication
Knowledge/Skills/Abilities:
  • Ability to synthesize complex data into user friendly visual presentations for use by Operations Management.
  • Requires highly developed communications skills to effectively collaborate with broad customer base.
  • Possesses excellent writing and verbal communications skills and the ability to understand and communicate concepts and recommendations.
  • Ability to understand and deal effectively with problems and opportunities, which arise, in a complex multiprocessing environment is crucial.
  • Ability to continuously learn and adopt new mathematical methods and technologies
