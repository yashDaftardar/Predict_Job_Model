Data Scientist
We're looking for Geniuses. Dreamers. Inventors. Innovators. People with the will to get stuff done. Doers who'll rip an idea out of thin air. Make it a reality. Own it. And amaze their peers. Sound like you? Welcome home.

As a Data Scientist, you will focus on applying statistical analysis and machine learning techniques to our data in order to drive smarter decisions and deliver better solutions to business units and customers. Our data scientists work on all parts of the data science workflow: from collecting, cleaning and analyzing the data to coding, optimizing, and deploying the super-smart models they build. You will also have the opportunity to present findings to executive leaders and work directly with teams in other business units in order to refine your product and show off the ROI you have created!

At Hy-Vee, variety is the spice of life! You will have the chance to work on a huge variety of projects coming from diverse business units across the company, such as marketing, supply chain and pharmacy. Our projects range from small in scope, such as forecasting a single item's sales to developing foundational data models that help Hy-Vee decide where to build its next store. On the data science team, you will find an environment that empowers you to impact all areas of Hy-Vee.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
  • Work with business stakeholders across many business units to identify opportunities to integrate data and data models into their existing workflows.
  • Collect, clean and analyze disparate datasets for analyses.
  • Design, build and deploy machine-learning models to predict customer behavior, ad revenue, etc.
  • Design, build and deploy forecasting models to project sales and item movement across Hy-Vee's business.
  • Model customer behavior to identify segments of customers for targeted marketing and personalized product recommendations.
  • Build end-to-end data pipelines that bring your analyses and modeling to life.
  • Collaborate with teammates to refine/improve analysis and share knowledge.
  • Research state-of-the-art data science and machine learning techniques, tools, and best practices
  • Present results of analyses to business stakeholders



KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE
  • Demonstrated ability to use data science/analytics tools to extract insights for business users.
  • Demonstrated ability to collect and clean disparate data sources for modelling.
  • Demonstrated ability to build and deploy predictive models.
  • Demonstrated ability to communicate results of analysis and modeling with business users.
  • Master's Degree in Mathematics, Computer Science, Statistics, or related field is preferred.

PROGRAM LANGUAGE REQUIREMENTS
  • Python or R, 3+ years experience
  • SQL, 3+ years experience
  • GitHub, preferred
  • Docker, preferred
  • PowerBI, preferred
  • Google Cloud Tools, preferred
  • Apache Airflow, preferred
