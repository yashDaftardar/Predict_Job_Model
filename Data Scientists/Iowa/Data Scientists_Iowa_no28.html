About Digital Diagnostics:


Our flagship product – IDx-DR – is an artificial intelligence algorithm where the computer, not the physician, performs the diagnosis. What We Are Looking For:

  • Publications in high-quality journals in applying deep learning to medical imaging problems for clinical decision-making
  • Demonstrable experience in OCT image processing, analysis, and classification
  • Experience programming in Python, and at least one common deep learning library (e.g. TensorFlow, MXnet caffé)
  • Experience with ITK or OpenCV
  • Two years of machine learning and algorithm development experience
