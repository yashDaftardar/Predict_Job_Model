Full Job Description

Overview:
Open to remote candidates or candidates within any of our UnityPoint Health locations/regions.

The UnityPoint Health (UPH) Analytics Center of Excellence is responsible for enabling clinical, operational, and financial outcomes improvement through consultative partnerships and the use of data and analytics. The team members are leaders in transforming UPH into a data-informed, continuous-improvement organization with highly reliable and sustainable results. As a part of the Data Science team, the Data Scientist inspires the adoption of data science in all corners of the healthcare enterprise by providing expertise in the application of the quantitative decision sciences to business and clinical problems. We accomplish this via a team-based, product management approach covering the design, development, and deployment of statistical models, simulations, and cloud-based machine learning systems into technology created through a user-centric, design-thinking process.

Responsibilities:
  • Make the customer relationship the highest priority in all interactions.
  • Interface directly with stakeholders to enhance existing data science solutions to meet functional and non-functional requirements.
  • Support the design and implementation of reliable and efficient data-pipelines from ingestion to consumption in both batch and real-time.
  • Support the development, validation, and deployment of quantitative models using statistical or machine learning methods into production pipelines while adhering to the principles of reproducible research.
  • Adhere to software engineering best practices and standards involving code development and versioning in a CI/CD framework.
  • Experiment with new technologies and practices to challenge the status quo and find creative solutions to complex problems.
  • Make data a joy to use in decision-making through the creation of visualizations and user interfaces grounded in design-thinking, using UX studies and observations to guide software development.
  • Inspire your peers to do their best work by establishing a culture of innovation, communication, and trust through design sessions, code reviews, and other open feedback.
  • Complete special projects and other requests as assigned.
  • Comply with UnityPoint Health data governance and development standards.
  • Behave in a manner consistent with all Corporate Compliance policies and procedures.
  • Maintain regular and consistent attendance at work.
  • Adhere to all UnityPoint Health Personnel policies and procedures, departmental policies and safety guidelines.
  • Work occasionally requires travel to other UPH facilities.
Qualifications:
Education:
  • Bachelors degree in a STEM discipline (e.g. Engineering, Statistics, Mathematics, Operations Research, Computer Science, or related) or equivalent experience in a technical role.
  • Masters degree in a STEM discipline (e.g. Engineering, Statistics, Mathematics, Operations Research, Computer Science, or related) or equivalent experience in a technical role preferred.
Experience:
  • Writing complex queries to extract and manipulate structured and unstructured data, specifically in the context of feature engineering
  • Executing the supervised machine learning training and evaluation process using parametric and non-parametric models
  • Self-starter with the ability to continually gain proficiency in related analytical methods
  • Data visualization and communication to a wide variety of audiences using both static and dynamic visualization applications such as Tableau
  • Requires communications and negotiation skills to effectively work with all levels of management throughout UPH, its subsidiaries and affiliates.
  • Demonstrates an ability to coordinate and manage large, complex projects and be able to negotiate and build consensus among groups or individuals with diverse interests and function effectively as a team leader in a team environment.
  • Demonstrated knowledge and practical use of health information standards.
  • Must demonstrate experience as an excellent oral and written communicator and exhibit excellent interpersonal skills.
Preference given to applicants with broader set of relevant expertise, which could include :
  • Successful enterprise application of experimental design, statistical/casual inference, estimation of uncertainty, simulation, or other operations research methods
  • Data pipeline/query optimization, tuning, and performance monitoring
  • Experience in Azure, or other cloud architectures (AWS, Google) for purposes of building machine learning applications
  • Application of software engineering principles (object-oriented programming, unit testing, readability and reproducibility, and versioning best practices)
Knowledge/Skills/Abilities:
  • 2+ years scripting in the R or Python ecosystems
  • 2+ years of experience of advanced SQL programming
  • 2+ years of experience with a business intelligence visualization tool (e.g. Tableau)
  • Strong communication to both technical and non-technical audiences
  • Experience with Healthcare preferred
  • Experience with Epic ecosystem preferred
