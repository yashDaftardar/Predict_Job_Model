We are GDIT, one of the largest IT and mission services providers to the government. We offer our customers the power of choice through a vast cloud ecosystem.

GDIT is your place. You make it your own by bringing your passion for accelerating the cloud. By owning your opportunity at GDIT, you are helping to ensure our mission is never interrupted.

In this role, responsibilities include:

  • Analyzes customer requirements and solves unusually complex technical problems and provides solutions which are highly innovative.
  • Provides expert knowledge for the design, development, build, analysis, evaluation, installation, and administration of database management systems to include database modeling and design, relational database architecture, non-relational database architecture and procedures, metadata and repository creation and configuration management.
  • Designs and implements databases with respect to access methods, access time, batch processes, device allocation, validation checks, organization, ETL tasks, protection and security, documentation, and statistical methods
  • Prepares system requirements, source analysis and process analyses and design throughout the database implementation.
  • Defines and oversees database organizations, standards, controls, procedures, and documentation.
  • Provides technical consulting in the definition, design, and creation of a database environment.
  • Advises applications development staff and users on data-based solutions to business problems, data architectures, database management system facilities and capabilities, and the operation and tuning of databases.
  • Maintains current and advanced knowledge of progressive and advancing technology as assigned.
  • Provides expert guidance and leadership to less-experienced database personnel.
  • May serve as a task or project leader. (Not a people manager)
  • Consults with executives and customers to deliver complex database solutions.
  • Recognized as an industry leader in database solutions

Required Experience

  • BA/BS (or equivalent experience), 10+ years of experience in source to target mappings from disparate data sources
  • Experience in writing and maintaining data dictionaries
  • Experience in contributing to System Design Documents (SDDs)
  • Experience in architecting solutions for both normalized and denormalized solutions, application database architecture
  • Relational database design and best practice approaches
  • UINIX and cloud command line interface (CLI) skills (AWS, Snowflake, Hive)
  • Experience with serverless architecture
  • Experience with code pipelines through lower environments to production; development, test, production (dev, tst, prd) Unit testing
  • Job scheduler tools design and development
  • Experience in designing around security principles such as row and column level security and secure views

Preferred Skills/Experience

  • Healthcare claims experience
  • Experience in data structures
  • Understanding strengths and weaknesses of Kimball and Inman data warehousing approaches
  • Machine Learning Artificial Intelligence concepts
  • Atlassian Tools (Jira & Confluence)
  • Experience working as a member of a Scrum development team
  • Experience in a cloud environment using native cloud services to process data (GLUE, Trifacta, Matillion, Apache etc.)
  • Experience in Git based repositories (Bitbucket, Code commit, Github etc.)
  • Experience in Hadoop, Elastic Map Reduce (EMR) Redshift, Snowflake, Databricks
  • Automation testing

Attributes for Success

  • Strong communication skills, a strong team player; able to accept and deliver ambiguous tasks
  • Self-starter with the ability to contribute unsolicited ideas with the team for continuous improvement
  • An individual who takes ownership and accountability of their work and processes
  • Innovator through implementing continuous processes improvements & tools
  • Ability to thrive in dynamic, fast-paced, fun environment
  • Must be able to work closely with all project stakeholders
