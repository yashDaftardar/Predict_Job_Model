Full Job Description

At EMC, you'll put your skills to good use as an important member of our team. You can count on gaining valuable experience while contributing to the company's success. EMC strives to hire and retain the best people by engaging, developing and rewarding employees.

Provides expertise on the development and the implementation of the Actuary Department’s data architecture strategy and/or rating engine development, maintenance and rating element capture strategies. Serves as leader for project execution strategies and estimation of timelines and data deliverables. Ensures the planning and performance of highly complex data and/or element assignments across inter-related data platforms to create desired outputs that can be leveraged by business users. Leads the development of solution specifications for the policy administration system's rating engine and rate change monitoring tools and validates implemented solutions are compliant with identified specifications. Provides expertise for cross-functional projects, including pricing enhancement research teams, line of business deep dives, in-depth industry vertical analysis, and branch business planning.

Essential Functions:

  • Provides expertise on the development and the implementation of the Actuary Department’s data architecture strategy and/or rating engine development, maintenance and rating element capture strategies
  • Performs research and analysis of data elements or rating algorithms, acquires necessary data and elements, and develops and executes associated testing plans
  • Ensures the planning and performance of highly complex data and/or element assignments, including data/element capture, aggregation, comparison, and extraction, for Actuary, including the parsing, joining, standardizing, cleansing, consolidating and filtering of data across inter-related data platforms to create desired outputs that can be leveraged by business users
  • Leads the development of solution specifications for the policy administration system's rating engine and rate change monitoring tools and validates implemented solutions are compliant with identified specifications
  • Leverages and communicates existing and available self-service tools and reporting solutions to satisfy customers' data needs
  • Serves as a subject matter expert in undertaking project segments and corporate initiatives involving assigned applications, business area datasets and source systems
  • Collaborates as appropriate with IT partners to develop solutions
  • Troubleshoots and corrects complex project problems involving data and programming logic errors
  • Proactively identifies data quality issues and immediately communicates issues to the appropriate technical steward for data fixes
  • Ensures all processes and projects are compliant with data governance policies and statutory requirements
  • Proactively engages in professional development related to the insurance industry, technology, data platform, reporting tool changes, statistical analysis, data management, and computer programming skills

Education & Experience:

  • Bachelor’s degree, preferably in computer science, actuarial science, data science, mathematics, or a related field, or equivalent relevant experience
  • Seven years of experience in data management, data analysis, or insurance analytics or related experience
  • Insurance experience preferred

Knowledge, Skills & Abilities:

  • Excellent planning skills in the areas of data management/extraction or insurance rating/monitoring intelligence
  • Excellent programing capabilities and/or insurance rating knowledge, including knowledge of at least two computer languages, such as R, Python, SQL, Visual Basic, JAVA or PostGreSQL
  • Excellent knowledge of Excel, PowerPoint and PowerBI and Word
  • Strong knowledge of system interactions and available data platform environments
  • Superior knowledge of analytical and data terminology, concepts and theory
  • Familiarity with actuarial terminology, concepts, and theory preferred
  • Strong analytical, investigation and problem-solving abilities
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills, including documentation
  • Excellent human relations customer service skills
  • Excellent attention to detail, organizational and multi-tasking skills

Ability to maintain a high degree of confidentiality
