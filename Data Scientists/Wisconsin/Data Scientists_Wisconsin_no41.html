What You Will Do:
  • Develop a strategy for how synthetic data will be generated and refreshed on-demand.
  • Responsible for overseeing batch cycles to load test data
  • Work with teams to migrate source code into various non prod environments
Skills and Experience You Will Need:
Required:
  • 10+ years overall experience
  • 5+ years of TDM experience
  • Hands-on work as well defining system architecture and design implementation strategy
  • Knowledgeable of Test Data Management (TDM) concepts and products.
  • Possess specific knowledge of TDM tools such as GenRocket , IBM Optim , Delphix TOM, Broadcom Test Data Manager, Informatica Test Data Management or other similar TDM tool(s).
  • Ability to help design and oversee processes such as:
  • Process for intake of test data requirements
  • Facility to catalog and reuse data
  • Facility to create synthetic data
  • Experience working with a mix of on/offshore teams
  • Excellent communication and facilitation skills
  • Very strong knowledge of RDBMS (SQL), NoSQL, and API orchestration
  • Experience with Unix shell scripting, Windows .BAT or PowerShell scripting
  • Experience in Data profiling, cleansing, sub setting and validation, and ETL
  • Experience in Test data preparation using automated data extraction and data masking
  • Experience implementing TDM solutions on Cloud Platforms, and on-prem solutions.
Desired
  • Proficiency in Java, Python, or R
  • Hands on experience with JCL, PROC, SORT procedures
  • Handling DB2 & IMS databases (query, load/unload)
  • Manipulation of datasets (PS / VSAM) to create mock-up data, use of copybooks
  • Knowledge of Pentaho Data Integration platform
  • Experience with the Insurance domain
