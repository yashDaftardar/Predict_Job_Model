People Analytics - Data Analyst
  • This is an on-site (not remote) position.

* Immigration sponsorship (e.g., H-1B) is not available for this position.

Join our team at the Gundersen Health System People Analytics Department and see the difference your analysis can make in enriching every life!

Your analytic talent will help innovate solutions to continuously improve the experience of patients and employees at Gundersen Health System. You will explore, find links, and create predictive models for diverse data and projects including patient experience, workforce planning, staffing projections, engagement, career planning, demographics, and more.

This is a salaried position reporting to the Manager of People Analytics.

Job Description:

Highly skilled in all aspects of data analysis, including descriptive, predictive, diagnostic, and prescriptive analytics, the Data Analyst combines theory and practice to identify, visualize, and communicate data-driven insights to support informed business decisions. Develops and implements processes to acquire data from primary and secondary data sources and maintain databases.

Major Responsibilities:

1. Analyzes data using statistical software and strong skills in research methodology. Uses appropriate analysis methods (descriptive and inferential statistical analysis) and communicates the results of the analysis in narratives, tables, and graphs, and provides recommendations based on findings to key stakeholders.

2. Create reports and maintain rich visualizations through data interpretation, data mining, analysis, and integrating various reporting components from multiple data sources.

3. Develop, implement, and maintain analytic systems and data collections systems, taking complicated problems and building simple frameworks.

4. Evaluate internal systems for efficiency, problems, and inaccuracies, developing and maintaining protocols for handling, processing, and cleaning data.

5. Performs independent analysis (descriptive and inferential) and converts data into useable information. Identifies the data necessary to establish the baseline and measure change.

6. Presents and communicates in a manner that builds constructive and effective relationships.

7. Acquire data from primary or secondary data sources and maintain databases/data systems.

8. Performs other job-related responsibilities as requested.

Education and Learning:

REQUIRED Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics, Computer Science, Statistics, Business Analytics or Related Field

DESIRED Master’s degree in Mathematics, Computer Science, Economics, Statistics, Business Analytics or Related Field

Work Experience:

REQUIRED 3-4 years of experience in data mining, analysis, visualization, presentation, and using business intelligence tools.

DESIRED 5-7 years of experience in data mining, analysis, visualization, presentation, and using business intelligence tools.
