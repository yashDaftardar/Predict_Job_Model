JOB DESCRIPTION

This position as Program Manager is in the Data Science and Analytics (DSA) PMO within the Core Data and Analytics (CDA) Organization. The role is responsible for leading one or more programs in collaboration with our Business Partners consisting of multiple projects!

  • Works with multiple teams and across Marketing functions to define scope, priorities and dependencies of projects.
  • Defines priorities and measures based on business partner objectives.
  • Identifies and mitigates program risks by removing impediments and advancing when applicable. Is proficient in risk management processes.
  • Lead, coordinate and participate in program ceremonies from concept to production.
  • Solves outstanding problems with broad impact on the business; requires conceptual and innovative thinking to develop solutions.
  • Collaborates with leadership to align resources across programs, projects or services; works within general department policies and industry guidelines.
  • Adheres to the organizational program structure and follows PMO standards to deliver on the program objectives.
  • Heavily passionate about strong communication to ensure key decisions and project status is clear to partners at all levels in an enterprise setting.
  • Responsible for reporting status, achievements and results in a timely manner and in accordance with PMO standards.
  • Maintains positive working relationships with stakeholders, business partners and team members.
  • Ensure accurate QA process is actively implemented across all initiatives.
  • Detail oriented and deadline driven.
  • Contributes to a positive team culture with a can do demeanor
  • Is an eager and constant learner.

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Computer Science, IT or related field, or an equivalent combination of education and work experience
  • 9-14 years of professional experience
  • Five or more years of project management experience at a program management level in a technology and/or data function.
  • Experience working within program management methodologies & governance
  • Proven understanding of project management / agile tooling, Jira a plus
  • Practical knowledge of database design and experience assembling technical data requirements preferred.
