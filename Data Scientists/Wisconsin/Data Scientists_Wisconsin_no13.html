Full Job Description

The Data Architect will be an integral member of the Data Science and Architecture teams providing the vision, strategy, and design of our evolving data platform. You will use your demonstrated skills in data modeling and data storage solutions to provide the next generation analytics environment. Your work will make data consistent, correct, and readily available across the enterprise. This hand-on role encompasses all aspects of data management, conceptual, logical, and physical.

Grounded in business, you are also fluent in technology, able to assess current technical environments and develop technology requirements and conceptual solutions to meet business objectives. You are a confident professional experienced in solving complex business problems, writing and presenting, facilitating and coaching. You thrive working with all levels of the organization, from the executive suite to the front line, yet you are equally comfortable working independently. The ideal candidate enjoys mentoring more junior data professionals as a means of growing the company's bench to satisfy future data architecture needs.

What You’ll Do and Impact:

  • Provide thought leadership in the areas of advanced data techniques, including data modeling, data governance, data quality, data access, data integration, data analysis, and data solution design and implementation.

  • Define and drive execution of data strategy and roadmaps including architecture, modeling, implementation, and data management for data storage, cleansing, and analytics.

  • Establish standards and guidelines for design, implementation, and sustainability of databases and data management systems.

  • Conceive, design and develop data models to reflect and accommodate the business model keeping in mind modeling conflicts due to differences in business significance/meaning of data.

  • Collaborate across business units (AI, BI, Product) to synthesize data requirements that satisfy business needs.

  • Serve as an expert in developing data models and architecture for AI and Machine Learning applications.

  • Create and revise enterprise logical and physical data models.

  • Operate as a Data Custodia, revising and enhancing data dictionary definitions, governance practices, and standards.

  • Function as the Data Steward thought leader, working with broader data teams on data consistency and cleansing.

  • Recommend and develop environment for data scientists to enable analytics, machine learning, and artificial intelligence research.

  • Provide leadership and guidance with enterprise data strategies.

  • Partner with software & security architects to ensure compliance with data security and privacy mandates while following software best practices for cloud or on-premise solutions.

  • Oversee end-to-end data lifecycle management activities.

  • Evaluate and recommend new and emerging data management and storage technologies and standards.

  • Partner with business users & data owners to capture data & analytics uses cases and outline actionable data requirements.

  • Mentor and teach other team members and business Partners.

  • Provide leadership and education on the value and use of information & operational data.

  • Participate in the definition of the global data strategy.

  • Identify and implement industry best practices.

What You’ll Need:

  • 5+ years of data architect experience for division of a mid to large-sized company

  • 5+ years of experience in creating data models (logical & physical) & streamlining data quality and use processes

  • 5+ years of experience with enterprise data management technologies such as database & ETL technologies, Big Data solution experience plus!

  • 5+ years of experience with SQL and ability to build and run complex queries

  • Proficiency in architecting, configuring, deploying, managing, monitoring cloud based data solutions, services and distributed applications (Experience with AWS and Azure a plus)

  • BS/BA in BS in Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics or related technical or equivalent experience, Advanced degree preferred!

Additional Items of Interest:

  • Proficiency in statistical and other tools/languages (R, Python…)

  • Experience with AI/ML solutions and Predictive Modeling

  • Experience working with data streaming technologies

  • Experience in data quality and data governance

  • Experience using Agile/Scrum practices

  • Experience with source control systems (git)

  • Ravenous appetite for continuous learning and field-testing technologies

  • Ability to innovate and drive rapid change in a positive manner

  • Influential in driving process change at a divisional or enterprise level

  • Excellent analytical thinking, problem solving, organizational and time management skills

  • Strong initiative with proven ability to meet deadlines

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills

  • Natural born mentor
