What You Will Do:

Responsible for facilitating key projects in the areas of data analysis and measurement to support improvement of clinical and operational outcomes.


Duties include:

  • Analyzes clinical and claims data to determine provider and organizational performance to developed goals, develops descriptive statistics and disseminates results via summary reports or dashboard format for key organizational initiatives.
  • Develops, analyzes and disseminates data to support organization in identifying and justifying improvement opportunities, developing a case for change, and monitoring the impact of implemented changes.
  • Coordinates with cross-functional teams in the development of reporting and analytic solutions by scoping projects, interpreting regulatory and payer requirements, developing detailed requirements and validating data output from internal and external sources.
  • Develops related communication and education in lay language for end users and delivers reports, presentations, insights and visualizations to communicate complex ideas

What you will need:

  • Bachelor’s degree required, Master's degree preferred.
  • At least 2 year related experience and/or training in a healthcare or hospital setting preferred.
  • Expert level proficiency in Microsoft Excel required.
  • Epic Reporting workbench experience preferred.
  • Proficiency in other Microsoft Office products and reporting packages required, SQL preferred.
  • Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts, and draw valid conclusions.
  • Ability to read, analyze, and interpret general business periodicals, professional journals, technical procedures, or governmental regulations.
  • Ability to write reports, business correspondence, and procedure manuals.
  • Ability to effectively present complex information and respond to questions from groups of managers, clients, customers, and the general public.
  • Ability to conduct descriptive analyses including such concepts as frequency distribution, determination of test reliability and validity, analysis of variance, correlation techniques, sampling theory, and factor analysis preferred.
  • Ability to interpret an extensive variety of technical instructions in mathematical or diagram form and deal with several abstract and concrete variables preferred.
