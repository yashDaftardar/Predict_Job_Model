How will you make an impact? (role and responsibilities)

As a Quality Assurance Analyst with MiLo Labs, you will:

  • Develop a formalized testing methodology and standardize techniques. Develop new testing techniques for various dynamic environments and products.

  • Construct and facilitate development of test plans and environments to identify software defects at all stages of development. Identifies reusable test plans for regression type testing.

  • Conduct a wide range of quality control tests and analyses to ensure that information systems, applications, and software meet specified standards, proper operations, end-user requirements, and are defect-free.

  • Coordinate with development team regarding testing and ensures testing is complete, successful, and documented.

  • Responsible for coordinating user acceptance testing, including documentation and communication of test plans and test results.

  • Measure and monitor testing completeness throughout the QA/UAT processes, keeping the manager aware of progress in a timely manner.

  • Document formalized procedures for program evaluation, testing and correction. Document all defects and work with business analysts and developers to track them through resolution.

  • Coordinate, configure, and/or participate in the implementation of automated testing tools. Research and review additional testing tools, alternative testing techniques.

  • Assist in development of and adhere to quality assurance standards.
