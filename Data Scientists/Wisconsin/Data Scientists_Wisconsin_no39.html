Who we are:

Modern Hire exists to help customers continually improve hiring through more personalized, data-driven experiences for candidates, recruiters, and hiring managers across the enterprise. Our virtual interviewing platform covers the entire hiring process, from AI-powered, scientifically validated pre-hire assessments and virtual job simulations, all the way to providing live video interviewing technology and on-demand video, text, or phone interview solutions.

We are looking for an I/O Data Science Intern to join our labs team! As an Industrial Organizational Psychology - Data Science intern on our Modern Hire labs team you will have the ability to support ongoing research initiatives that keep our products and our clients hiring processes on the cutting edge. Partnering with our I/Os and data scientists, you will have the opportunity to work with machine learning and deep learning models along with the opportunity to conduct research on natural language processing. This is a great opportunity to learn new skills and help our labs team change hiring for good.

What you'll do:

  • Support ongoing research initiatives that keep us on the cutting edge
  • Work with machine learning and deep learning models and conduct research on natural language processing
  • Drive the preparation of data used to train deep learning models and score unstructured data
  • Develop Virtual Job Tryout content (interactive, innovative web-based assessment), which may include development of new assessment content or strategic updates to existing content
  • Assure quality of our software in terms of content, scoring, and functionality
  • Performance modeling and data analysis
  • Extensive technical and business writing, including technical reports and item writing


If you can answer "yes" to the following, then we want you to apply!

  • Are you currently pursuing a PhD in Industrial Organizational Psychology?
  • Do you have a through understanding of statistics, personnel selection, and machine learning/deep learning?
  • Do you enjoying staying up to date on the latest research for tasks you are on and assessments as a whole?
  • Do you have an understanding of technology and the ability to be adaptable to new systems as they are introduced within the company?
  • Are you familiar with laws surrounding workplace assessments?
  • Do you have a good understanding of psychology, human behavior, and performance?
  • Do you have experience with Python and/or R skills?
  • Are you able to analyze big data and draw meaningful insights?
  • Do you have strong business report writing capabilities?
  • Do you have strong active listening and learning skills?
  • Are you able to solve complex problems and take in information from multiple sources (e.g. job incumbents, research, past findings) and apply them to solve problems?
  • Are you able to apply the scientific method and use it to come up with solutions?
  • Are you able to prioritize; arrange tasks in an order so that everything is done on time?
  • Are you a team player who is able to work in a highly collaborative environment to share your ideas and listen to those of others?
  • Are you self-motivated?
  • Are you able to process information including verifying, calculating, categorizing, and coding data?
  • Do you have strong deductive reasoning skills?
