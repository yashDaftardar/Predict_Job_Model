POSITION SUMMARY
The Senior Financial Data Analyst role supports the Finance department in making data driven decisions. This individual is responsible for delivering value-added business analysis and establishing meaningful operational metrics that lead to accurate business decision making. Working closely with business unit managers and team members, the analyst will implement and maintain technical tools and reporting solutions to solve problems and improve current systems and processes. The analyst will perform ad-hoc data analysis in multiple areas of the business.

JOB RESPONSIBILITIES
  • Serve as Autoforce Finance data champion on ERP system implementation project to ensure current data requirements (mainly Power BI reporting) is captured and solutioned in future state
  • Assist with integration of acquired companies for assigned areas of responsibility
  • Query relational databases using SQL to develop Power BI reports and tools
  • Maintain Power BI tools and CPM models and reports, including resolving issues and developing enhancements as business needs evolve
  • Partner with cross functional work teams to identify opportunities to utilize data analytics to improve business processes and business results
  • Model builder for the corporate performance management tool
  • Perform complex data analysis combining business and data tools knowledge
  • Serve as AutoForce Finance liaison to the IT department, Data and Insights department, and Corporate Finance department on data requirement enhancements and assist in setting direction for the companies data transformation initiative.
  • Data maintenance and project work as assigned
  • Facilitate trainings and be a resource for SQL, CPM, Power BI and other data intelligence and visualization tools.
  • Performs ad hoc projects and reports

QUALIFICATIONS
  • 4-year degree required (Finance or Accounting degree preferred but not required)
  • 7-10+ years of experience with a minimum of 2 years of SQL experience
  • Essential skills needed to develop new solutions and align existing tools with changes in the business: project management, facilitation, advanced analytical skills and problem-solving.
  • Proficient with business intelligence and dashboarding tools (Power BI preferred)
  • Advanced proficiency with Microsoft Excel and SQL
  • Ability to manage multiple projects and priorities
  • Resourceful self-starter who can work with minimal supervision
  • Ability to clearly communicate data insights in a manner which is easily understood by general business users
  • Ability to build rapport, cultivate working relationships and drive collaboration across teams
  • Demonstrate commitment to continuous improvement in a dynamic environment, striving to exceed expectations
  • Strong technical skills with the ability to leverage technology to improve processes
