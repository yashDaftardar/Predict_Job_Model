Business Intelligence Analyst – Power BI Data Analyst

Summary: Reporting directly to the Head of Information Technology the Business Intelligence Analyst will interface with internal business clients, technical leadership, and 3rd party vendors; and play a critical role in advising and managing the design, support and development of our Business Intelligence solutions as well as the technical direction and support of our SQL environments.
This position will be responsible for a variety of duties that include Dashboard development, Power BI reports, creating code, designing programs and developing interfaces to acquire data from multiple sources into a common data mart. This position will maintain database integrity and assist in management of our SQL servers.

As the Business Intelligence Analyst, the essential functions and responsibilities are:


Establish and maintain BI reporting environment (BI Product Owner)
  • Design, develop and manage data warehouse, data warehousing and reporting solutions.
  • Support BI technologies of SQL Server, SQL programming, SSIS, SSRS, and SSAS
  • Develop and maintain Power BI reports, and BI dashboards and Jet Reports to meet business requirements.
  • Develop and maintain and update documentation to support the BI environment to ensure consistent and reliable procedures and change management is completed.
  • Work with Microsoft NAV 2016 table structure and data utilizing the Manufacturing, Planning, Purchasing, Order Management and Finance modules.
  • Familiar with Jet Enterprise to pull information from Microsoft NAV into Jet Cubes.
  • Control and maintain access to reporting environment. Ensure that security requirements meet business security requirements. Able to implement row level security on data and understand application security layer models in Power BI.
  • Working with IT infrastructure team maintain database integrity.

Establish and support interfaces to data warehouse and other critical SQL applications
  • Able to write efficient queries for application integrations between ERP and third-party software
  • Setup and support interfaces so they meet business timing and requirements
  • Expert in utilizing views, Constraints, Triggers, Joins, Temp Tables, Table Variable, Functions, Batch Scripts and numerous stored procedures used in interface solutions.

BI /SQL Project Management/Training
  • Support (IT lead where required) a variety of analytical and business intelligence projects.
  • Ability to manage project from requirement definition, design, development, testing and implementation.
  • Able to successfully collaborate with users to fully develop solutions.
  • Train small subset of key users on BI tools and solutions.

As a Business Intelligence Analyst, the education and experiences are:
  • 10+ years strong experience as a SQL Developer, MS Power BI Developer, or similar role
  • Minimum of a bachelor’s degree required
  • Must have functional understanding of business processes and detailed knowledge of systems and applications of Microsoft NAV 2016, including Manufacturing, Planning, Purchasing, Order Management and Financial Modules.
  • Working knowledge of Jet Enterprise and Jet Reports in pulling information from Microsoft NAV tables into a Jet Enterprise Cube.
  • Must demonstrate keen problem-solving skills, ability to deal with complex situations and recommend a course of action in addition to analyzing and interpreting data to arrive at business solutions.
