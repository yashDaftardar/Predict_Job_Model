POSITION SUMMARY

We provide our utility clients with a wide range of energy-saving and renewable energy solutions. From individual utility customers and construction contractors, to utilities, municipalities, and government bodies, our clients have come to depend on our customized energy efficiency programs to help them to meet their energy saving goals.

The Senior Data Analyst - Corporate Support position provides technical and data support for the company’s ERP Reporting system. This position will help our executive team get visibility into the companies data in a meaningful way. The right individual will provide data analysis for corporate systems and use their skills to make insightful reports joining data across ERP and Operations systems. The Data Analyst works within a team environment. You will also be working with others who are passionate about energy efficiency and making a positive impact on the environment.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

This list of duties and responsibilities is not all-inclusive and may be expanded to include other duties and responsibilities, as management may deem necessary from time to time.

  • Ability to understand data models and determine data relationships for reporting
  • Represent data in a visual fashion using PowerBI (or other comparable tool)
  • Data security concepts and practical experience with protecting sensitive data
  • Self-motivated with the ability to dig into issues and find the right solution
  • For larger tasks, develop plans with sub-tasks and durations and report out on the progress
  • Provide day to day support for the company’s data analysis requirements – designing procedures and queries to meet stakeholder needs, optimizing performance
  • Write SQL queries and stored procedures using industry best practices for security and efficiency
  • Design and build SSIS packages, and deploy SSRS reports in our SharePoint environment
  • Database and code management, review and documentation
  • Work with all functional departments and Program Teams to provide technical solutions to solve Program and business needs
  • Manage and appropriately escalate issues in a help desk environment

POSITION REQUIREMENTS

Education and Experience

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Management Information Systems or Computer Science (or equivalent work experience required)
  • Experience and thorough understanding of SQL/TSQL
  • PowerBI as well as other reporting tools
  • Visual Studio 2010.NET 4.0 helpful
  • 3-5 years previous experience in MIS/Information Systems
  • Experienced in best practices for design, development, modeling and implementation of data warehouses
  • Experience writing SQL Server Stored Procedures, SSRS, SSIS, and SSAS development.
  • Salesforce.com - Preferred
  • Development/delivery of SSRS report through SharePoint (SharePoint 2013) - Preferred
  • Help Desk and/or system and user troubleshooting and service experience - Preferred
  • Experience with energy efficiency program implementation – Preferred
  • Experience writing complex queries using TSQL

Required Skills, Knowledge and Abilities

  • Ability to communicate effectively, both verbally and in writing with customers, clients and employees
  • Ability to analyze and interpret data and solve practical problems
  • Knowledge of mathematical concepts such as fractions, percentages and ratios

Licenses & Certifications

  • Valid driver’s license

Travel Requirements

  • Willingness to travel 15% of the time

PHYSICAL DEMANDS AND WORK ENVIRONMENT

  • Required to sit, stand, walk; talk and hear; and ability to touch and handle tools and/or controls
  • Ability to lift up to 10 pounds
  • Noise Level is typically moderate
  • Employee could be exposed to fumes and/or airborne particles and risk of potential shock

Note: Reasonable accommodations may be made for individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions of this position.
