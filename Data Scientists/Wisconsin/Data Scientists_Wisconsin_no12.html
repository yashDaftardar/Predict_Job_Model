What's the role?

Who We Are:

Data Science and Analytics plays a key role in the Core Data and Analytics (CDA) organization. We leverage data to provide key insights, business intelligence and data science products that advance NM's strategic priorities.

Role and Responsibilities :

As an Associate Data Scientist, you will be a part of the team that uses computer vision to convert unstructured data sources into structured data that can be used to train machine learning models. Our successful candidate will have a demonstrated ability to apply advanced statistical and mathematical analysis, data mining, and predictive modeling to find novel solutions to complex and varied problems. You will advance this work by building solutions from scratch and by leveraging existing vendor solutions. You will support the interactions with internal business partners by visualizing recommendations and metrics. You will share best practices with peers around core methodologies, patterns, standards, and processes.

Responsibilities:

We are looking for someone who will work diligently in a collaborative environment. This individual will:

  • Apply advanced statistical and mathematical analysis, data mining, and predictive modeling to find novel solutions to complex and varied problems.

  • Interpret available internal and external data resources to develop and deliver information solutions, including analytical information.

  • Develop and deploy testing hypotheses and analyze test results, providing the necessary analytical rigor to ensure data quality, consistency, repeatability, and accuracy of insights.
