Job Summary


Working with IT and Business leadership, the Business Intelligence & Data Analytics Leader will take a lead role in driving transformation of Enerpac Tool Group’s data analytics and related business intelligence (BI) initiatives across the enterprise. Reporting to the CIO, a successful leader will create a data & analytics strategy that incorporates both existing and new technologies to better enable digital capabilities of our employees as well as supports digital initiatives at the company. Under your guidance, the BI team will be expected to develop & implement an information architecture and execute a phased roadmap of initiatives. Not only will you lead these efforts, but you will be responsible for architecting, selecting, implementing, and deploying new technologies & capabilities to enable the strategy and deliver business outcomes. You will be expected to serve as the go-to expert on data and analytics across the business and will work closely with process owners and functional leaders to identify opportunities, implement solutions, and empower users as part of a self-service analytics operating model.

Job Responsibilities

Strategy & Architecture

    • Define a Data & Analytics strategy and work with CIO to create & implement a roadmap for the enterprise.
    • Build an analytics and reporting delivery model that meets the needs of the business, focusing on an analytics operating model that enables business self-service.
    • Establish an enterprise information architecture. Help drive data governance, data quality, and master data management practices.
    • Expand traditional data warehousing and operational reporting technologies and capabilities to capture and capitalize on big data, IoT and analytics.
    • Help introduce and identify use cases for data science (ML/AI).
    • Evaluate and recommend new processes and technologies.

Leadership

    • Lead and grow an IT Business Intelligence & Data Analytics team that can identify and develop solutions to business problems and serve as the data and analytics experts for the company.
    • Design and help execute BI and analytics training sessions for citizen developers and end users that will help fulfill a self-service strategy and drive user engagement in analytics.
    • Act as an ‘analytics mentor’ to citizen developers and business users
    • Demonstrate solid understanding of business processes and key functional areas; ability to mentor other BI team members to better understand the business & processes.
    • Stay abreast of market developments and serve as a subject matter expert on trends around data analytics, data science, and technology drivers relevant to the business.
    • Help drive innovation through analytics.
    • Help develop and attract best-in-class talent to support organization's growth and increasing investment in digital initiatives.

Delivery

    • Identify and scope projects to ensure the successful execution of BI and analytics initiatives.
    • Lead the design, development, implementation, and maintenance of complex data systems and solutions.
    • Partner and collaborate with all functions of the company to ensure data needs are addressed and the required data is modeled and available to power users, citizen developers and end users.
    • Help negotiate contracts and service agreements with vendors and suppliers for related technologies and services.

Job Requirements

  • 5+ years working on analytics / business intelligence / reporting in a global manufacturing environment. Familiarity reporting on Sales & Operations Planning, Inventory, Manufacturing, Finance and Sales analyses.
  • Minimum 8 years overall IT experience required and 3+ years of leadership/management experience.
  • Bachelor's degree with IT concentration or related field required. MBA, or other Master's degree in related field preferred.
  • Experience with both on premise and cloud-based BI tools and solutions, including Oracle Business Intelligence as well as 2+ years with Azure related technologies (as Databricks, Data Factory, Data Lake, Analysis Services, SQL Data Warehouse, Power BI).
  • Experience working as a change agent and data expert elevating the use of data & analytics within a business. Demonstrated ability to collaborate with business users and citizen developers.
  • Experience deploying a modern BI and data analytics self-service operating model.
  • Experience designing and conducting BI training programs that drive measurable user engagement.
  • Solid understanding of business processes in a Manufacturing environment. Prior collaboration and understanding of key functions such as Sales, Marketing, Product Line Management, Customer Service, Operations and Finance.
  • Hands-on experience with one or more ETL tools.
  • Experience with data modeling and data warehouse & data lake design.
  • Experience with various database technologies (e.g., SQL, NoSQL, Oracle, Hadoop, Teradata)
  • Experience with Oracle ERP data model strongly preferred.
  • Experience with "big data", analytics and visualizations required. Some hands-on experience with IoT, machine learning (ML), and artificial intelligence (AI) preferred.
  • Experience working with consultants and/or third-party service providers to deliver projects on-time and on-budget
  • Proven experience in formulating global IT strategies and developing roadmaps, especially for data and analytics.
  • Ability to regularly learn and adopt new technology
  • Strong understanding of business financials and good business acumen
  • Excellent English communication skills. Ability to deliver complex or technical content in simple business terms in writing, presentations and orally.

Other

  • Open to remote working arrangements; however, employee would be expected to commute to and attend in-person meetings at the company’s Menomonee Falls, WI HQ on a periodic basis.
