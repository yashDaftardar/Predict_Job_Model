Customer Success Senior Data Analyst

The Customer Success Senior Data Analyst will be an integral member of the Customer Success Data & Analytics team within Genesys Customer Success organization. The Customer Success Operations team is chartered with defining and executing an enterprise Customer Success strategy. This includes reporting process and implementation, data optimization, and content access/distribution in a partnership with business and IT stakeholders. The Customer Success Senior Data Analyst will help us on our journey to become a more data driven company.

We are currently seeking a Senior Data Analyst who has been successful in partnering with business customers to both translate their business needs into actionable deliverables for analytics, as well as educating the same internal customers on possible ways to fulfill their business needs leveraging data that exists today. The successful candidate will turn data into information, information into insight and insight into business decisions.

Attributes:

  • Interpreting Financial and Customer data, analyzing results using statistical techniques
  • Developing and implementing Financial data analysis, data collection systems and other strategies that optimize statistical efficiency and quality
  • Senior Data analyst responsibilities include conducting full lifecycle analysis to include requirements, activities and design.
  • Senior Data analysts will develop analysis and reporting capabilities. They will also monitor performance and quality control plans to identify improvements.

Responsibilities:

  • Interpret data, analyze results using statistical techniques and provide ongoing reports
  • Develop and implement databases, data collection systems, data analytics and other strategies that optimize statistical efficiency and quality
  • Acquire data from primary or secondary data sources and maintain databases/data systems
  • Identify, analyze, and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets
  • Work with management to prioritize business and information needs
  • Locate and define new process improvement opportunities
  • Ability to follow and understand data requirements with minimal supervision
  • Proven working experience as a data analyst or business data analyst
  • Strong knowledge of and experience with reporting packages (Business Objects etc), databases (SQL etc), programming (XML, Javascript, or ETL frameworks)
  • Strong analytical skills with the ability to collect, organize, analyze, and disseminate significant amounts of information with attention to detail and accuracy
  • Proven ability to complete deliverables on-time

Requirements:

  • Minimum 5 years experience in Computer Science, Engineering or related technical field required.
  • Financial Understanding and Reporting Experience required
  • SaaS or Software Industry experience required
  • Experience in data quality and governance
  • Experience with Snowflake or other RDBM systems a plus
  • Technical aggressiveness, demonstrated ability to learn new technologies
  • 4-5 years of experience in full lifecycle of analytics using Enterprise Tools: Tableau, QlikView, PowerBI
  • Ability for analytical thinking, problem solving, organizational and time management skills.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Experience in CRM and ERP systems
  • Some Customer Success experience a plus
  • Proven customer service and support focus
  • Ability to effectively deal with rapid change in a positive manner
