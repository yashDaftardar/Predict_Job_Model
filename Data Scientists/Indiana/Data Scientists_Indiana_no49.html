Full Job Description

Onebridge is seeking a Data Scientist to join a global client as they build and grow a Data Science as a Service practice within a global organization.

This does require local talent - seeking someone in the greater Indianapolis or tri-state area.

Meet the Team (https://vimeo.com/360284220)

Data Scientist | Quick Overview :

The Data Scientist will service in a key role within our Tech Services Team, analyzing large amounts of raw information and finding patterns to optimize performance and inform decision-making practices.

The right person will be highly analytical, deeply passionate about math and statistics, and possess a strong desire to innovate methods for conducting machine-learning and research.

Critical thinking and advanced problem-solving skills are core behaviors of the team.

Data Scientist | Job Responsibilities:

  • Research and detect valuable data sources and automate collection processes.
  • Perform preprocessing of structured and unstructured data.
  • Review large amounts of information to discover trends and patterns.
  • Create predictive models and machine-learning algorithms.
  • Modify and combine different models through ensemble modeling.
  • Organize and present information using data visualization techniques.
  • Develop and suggest solutions and strategies to business challenges.
  • Work together with engineering and product development teams.

Data Scientist | Preferred Skills:

  • 5+ years' experience working on Data Related Projects or Positions.
  • 4 years interfacing with business stakeholders and managing matrixed priorities and expectations.
  • Significant experience with data mining, machine learning, and operations research.
  • Expertise with Business Intelligence tools (such as Tableau or PowerBI) and data frameworks (such as Hadoop, Pig, Hive).
  • Requires a combination of R, SQL, and Python; familiarity with Scala, Java or C++ is a bonus.
