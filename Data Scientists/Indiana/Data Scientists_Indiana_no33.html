Job Description

Purpose:
Do you have a passion for collaborating to build predictive analytics algorithms that help drive decisions? Delta Faucet Company is looking for an analytics professional to join our team as part of the Analytics Center of Excellence (COE).
Reporting to lead of the analytics COE we will work together as a team to partner with the business channels to identify, develop, and deliver predictive analytics solutions in a fast-paced business environment. As a member of our team you will focus on the creation of varied forecasting models, building new predictive algorithms, and crafting the company's vision and use of machine learning.
We hope you'll join us and help Delta Faucet Company further harness the power of analytics!
Responsibilities:
We are seeking a data scientist or statistician to partner across our business to:
  • Build, review, and deploy a forecasting models in a variety of subject areas.
  • Design and execute business experiments in a sophisticated consumer market.
  • Brainstorm and implement new predictive models that drive difference making new insights to the company.
  • Utilize programming skills to explore and understand large amounts of consumer and product data.
  • Identify potential areas of opportunity for strategic data capture and communicate the potential improvements to the business.
  • Encourage data driven decision making and high-level statistical analysis across the business.
Education & Experience:
  • 3+ years of shown delivery of predictive analytics models to business users in a sales, marketing, or manufacturing environment.
  • 3+ years of collaborative design of business experiments with a focus on explainable hypothesis tests.
  • 3+ years of code development preferably in Python or R.
  • Experience with economic or market forecasting preferred.
  • Bachelor’s or master’s degree in Statistics, Data Science, Mathematics, Computer Science, Economics or other related degree.
