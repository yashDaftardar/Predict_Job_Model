Summary of the role:

The Senior Data Analyst is responsible for reporting and analysis on all aspects of Delivery Projects and Processes. This includes KPI, Financial and Process Efficiency reporting across the Hospitality Delivery organization. The successful candidate should be intellectually curious and quantitatively oriented able to identify and concisely communicate the "So what?" often buried within the data.


Are you the next Senior Data Analyst we are looking for?

  • You will be expected to derive insights from vast data sets that may vary by product domain.
  • You will be a key driver in informing future automation and process improvement opportunities.

In this role you’ll:

  • Generate and distribute pre-defined daily, weekly and monthly financial and operational reports to key stakeholders.
  • Report should include actionable insights generated by the Analyst.
  • Perform ad-hoc reporting and analysis as directed or as self-identified would be beneficial to potentially drive actionable insights.
  • Analyze operational processes and team workload to identify potential areas of automation or process improvement.
  • Assist in defining SLAs for key process steps by leveraging real-world data and time-in-motion studies.
  • Help to complete and manage a variety of special projects dealing with all functional areas of the business that may be assigned periodically as business needs demand.


About the ideal candidate:

  • Education: Degree in quantitative field preferred
  • Relevant Work Experience:
    • Minimum 5 years of Experience in Data Analysis, Project Management, Financial Analysis, Operations Process Improvement
  • Business Understanding:
    • Salesforce.com experience a plus
  • Skills:
    • Computing: Advanced Excel/VBA skills required
    • Languages: Excellent command of English, oral and written
