Job Description:
Pacific Life is investing in bright, agile and diverse talent to contribute to our mission of innovating our business and creating a superior customer experience. We’re actively seeking a talented Director of Data Science to join our Life Division Advanced Analytics team in Aliso Viejo, CA. This role can be on-site or 100% remote.

As a Director of Data Science, you will have an opportunity to impact Pacific Life’s growth and long-term success by developing and defining the Advanced Analytics area in the Life Insurance Division. This is a new role that will work with key stakeholders, mentor two senior data scientists in Advanced Analytics, help coordinate with offshore resources and collaborate with technical teams based in California and Virginia.

​While our team aims to hire at the Director level, candidates who meet most but not all requirements are encouraged to apply. Actual job title and offer will be based on experience and skillset.

How you will make an impact:
Provide peer review for advanced analytics projects throughout the life insurance division as well as mentorship for analytics practitioners.
Help set the AI/ML strategy for the life insurance division. Define and evangelize best practices.
Hands-on development of some of the most complex AI/ML models and pipelines
Participate in ongoing monitoring, governance, and risk management.

The experience you will bring:
8+ years’ experience in data science or AI/ML
Love of coaching, mentorship, and collaboration
Solid business acumen to put analytics in the appropriate context while identifying key metrics that measure and drive long term growth
Demonstrated familiarity with machine learning algorithms and techniques (deep learning, decision trees, etc.), experience and knowledge of statistical modeling techniques (GLM, logistic regression, etc.), and experience with common machine learning and cloud libraries (scikit-learn, XGBoost, TensorFlow, etc.).
Solid software engineering and pipeline building skills
Proven track record of solving real business problems through analytics, especially in insurance and finance
Solid communication skills. Explaining difficult AI/ML concepts to business stakeholders and incorporating their feedback.
MLOps experience, deep knowledge and understanding of AI/ML tools and techniques

What will make you stand out:
Experience in the Insurance or Financial industry
