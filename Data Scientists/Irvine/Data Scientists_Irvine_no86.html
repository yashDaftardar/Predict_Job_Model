We are seeking Senior Data Analysts to grow our Data Driven Solutions Practice and support our clients' growth in Southern California.

As a Senior Data Analyst, you will serve as the conduit for creating business value through data with the goal of deepening our clients capabilities. You will turn data into information, information into insight, and insight into outcomes. You will conduct full lifecycle analysis to include business requirements, understanding of data structures, and data quality on the ingestion and consumption end of the data value stream. You will support sprint planning and roadmap evolution based on data-driven insights. You will employ diagnostic and predictive analytics techniques to support our clients maturity toward prescriptive analytics. You will identify and define new improvement opportunities and solutions to achieve better outcomes.
