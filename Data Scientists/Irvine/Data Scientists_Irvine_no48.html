Full Job Description

Glidewell Dental is an industry-leading provider of dental technology products and services to dental professionals worldwide. Headquartered in Newport Beach/Irvine, CA, the Glidewell campus is home to 3,300 employees and complete with an employee gym, cafeteria, massage therapist, acupuncturist, and of course, a dentist. Glidewell Dental employs over 5,000 employees worldwide and is privately owned.

So what is a dental company doing with technology?

Glidewell is dedicated to building the future of dental through additive manufacturing, data science, and even machine learning. We research and develop new innovative products and services for the dental industry at affordable costs. Just ask your dentist!


Responsibilities:

  • Works with the team in designing, developing, testing, deploying, monitoring and optimizing website and mobile applications and APIs that assist in the machine learning process of predictive modeling and deliver ML results to product systems.
  • Works with the team in collecting, merging and organizing data from varies sources and developing user interfaces to help business to understand and manage customers and products better.
  • Works with the team in architecting and designing intelligent data science applications that serves the diverse requirement of edge computing solutions.
  • Shares the knowledge with code reviews and suggestions to ensure quality of product according to standards, processes and procedures.
  • Collaborates with Machine Learning Engineers to continue to mature the use of machine learning within the organization.
  • Assists with designing end-to-end system architecture, working with various technical teams.
  • Keeps abreast of upcoming and trending technologies in order to improve the Company’s applications, business workflows and processes.
  • Performs other related duties and projects as business needs require at direction of management.


Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Technology, Computer Science or related field required. Master’s degree preferred.
  • Minimum of one (1) year of relevant working experience required. Understands fundamental concepts, practices and procedures of software engineering.
  • Minimum of one (1) year of experience in development engineering required.
  • Minimum of one (1) year of experience with applications such as Python, Node.js, AngularJS, React required.
  • Minimum of one (1) year of experience in developing and integrating Restful web APIs to web & mobile applications and dealing with JSON format data required.
  • Minimum of one (1) year of experience in AWS Serverless architecture and AWS native services like Lambda, Step Functions, EC2, API Gateways, Cognito, CloudFront, IAM, AWS ML, SageMaker, VPC, SQS, SNS, SES, Route53 required.
  • Minimum of one (1) year of experience in relational, non-relational, and high-scale data processing and storage frameworks like SQL, AWS RedShift, Aurora, DynamoDB, RDS, MySQL, PostgreSQL, S3 and strong SQL query skills required.
  • Minimum of one (1) year of experience in continuous integration, delivery & deployment (CI/CD) process and AWS Developer tools like CodeStar, CodeBuild, CodeDeploy, CodePipeline, X-Ray and Cloud9 to create CI/CD workflow required.
  • Minimum of one (1) year of experience in developing voice interface software with Alexa skills, Alexa for Business, Flash Briefing and AWS Lex/Polly required.
  • Previous experience with machine learning technology and a minimum of one (1) year experience in integrating ML models and workflow into applications.
