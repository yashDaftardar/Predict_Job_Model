Full Job Description

Summary

  • We are looking for creative, tenacious problem solvers who are passionate about our product and excited to work with our rich, complex dataset. An effective Data Scientist at Spigen must have a passion and aptitude for turning sophisticated problems into concrete hypotheses that can be answered through thorough data analysis. In this role, your insights will influence both the performance of our business and the experience of our users.

Job Duties

  • Assess the effectiveness and accuracy of new data sources and data gathering techniques.
  • Mine and analyze data from company databases to drive optimization and improvement of product development, marketing techniques, and business strategies.
  • Develop processes and tools to monitor and analyze model performance and data accuracy.
  • Analyze, test, and interpret complex clinical data, algorithmic results, and Machine Learning model performance data.
  • Develop complex design control documents including algorithm requirement specs and risk documents.
  • Test and execute new opportunities to engage with external data sources.
  • Other duties as assigned.

Skills

  • Eagerness to identify and explore new product/marketing opportunities, potential improvements to our data infrastructure, and unanswered questions about customer behavior.
  • Ability to develop a nuanced business understanding, testable hypotheses, and meaningful findings from data.
  • Outstanding written and verbal communication skills, and a willingness to collaborate with business partners.
  • Experience with SQL and data visualization tools (e.g. Tableau or Mode) is highly recommended.
  • Familiarity with scripting languages, machine learning, advanced statistical techniques, and A/B testing is a plus.

Requirements

  • 1 or 2+ years of work experience in Management Consulting, Data Analytics, Data Science, or Product Management roles
  • Bilingual in Korean and English
  • BS degree in Computer Science, Math, Statistics, Economics, or other quantitative fields
  • Experience communicating and advocating with analytical insights to executive-level audiences to inform decision-making.
  • Familiarity with AB Testing/personalization process including test design, execution, analysis and develop an actionable recommendation.
  • Demonstrated experience with some or all of the following: data modeling, data visualization, statistics, experimental design, predictive modeling.
  • Experience guiding and managing others to solve broad, impactful analytical problems.
  • Proficient in SQL; Adept in at least one visualization tool such as Tableau; R or Python knowledge is a plus.
