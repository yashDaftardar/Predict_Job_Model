Job Description

The Analytics Team is seeking a motivated Co-Op pursuing a MS degree in Statistics, Math, IS, Analytics, or Data Science to develop reports and dashboards which drive enhanced business outcomes for Skyworks. Areas of focus will primarily Supply Chain Planning and Engineering Operations.

This Co-Op is designed to help MS candidates develop the skills and experience required to develop/maintain reports used for Supply Planning and Engineering Ops. The Analytics Teams provides a broad range of services creating value-add intelligence through data analysis. The primary purpose of the role is to assist the department in further developing its data analysis and business intelligence reporting.

The candidate will be embedded within Analytics Team focused on delivering value to the SCM and Engineering organization.
