Data Scientist - Junior

Minimum Qualifications Summary


Certification & Education
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Economics, Finance, Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Management Information Systems, Engineering, Business Analytics disciplines, or related area.


Experience
  • 3+ years of experience with data quality analysis, statistical analysis and/or modeling, financial or cost analysis, internal controls, and/or external/internal financial audit
  • Experience mining and analyzing electronic data with competency in one or more of the following: SAS, SQL, SPSS, R, Python, ACP, IDEA Tableau, MATLAB, Visual Basic, Business Intelligence, Hadoop or Alteryx
  • Experience with JavaScript Object Notation (JSON) and Application Programming Interface (API)
  • Experience in Data Cleansing and Data Engineering


Job Objective
The purpose of this requirement is to establish the organization’s analytical, statistical, and programming capabilities to collect, analyze, and interpret large data sets to develop data-driven solutions to difficult strategic challenges.
Responsibilities
  • Makes value out of data by asking the right questions to design the framework of the exploratory process.
  • Processes and cleans various data streams. Integrates and stores data from initial investigation through exploratory data analysis.
  • Engineers and builds AI tools that automate cross-functional team processes within the organization.
  • Generates data visualization to inform and support senior leader decision-making.
Minimum Job Requirements/Technical Requirements
  • Experience using data-oriented programming languages and visualization software.
  • Experience applying data mining, data modeling, natural language processing, and machine learning.
  • Experience working as a member of a cross-functional team.
  • Experience building tools to automate data collection.
  • Demonstrates working knowledge of statistics.
  • Possess good technical writing skills.
  • Experience with machine learning and AI
  • Excellent attention to detail
Education & Certification
Bachelor’s Degree in Economics, Finance, Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Management Information Systems, Engineering, Business Analytics disciplines, or related area.
Security Clearance
Active DoD SECRET Clearance is required
