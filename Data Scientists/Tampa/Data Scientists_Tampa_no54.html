Full Job Description

TriMark USA is one of the nation's largest providers of design and build services, equipment and supplies to the foodservice industry. TriMark offers operators an unrivaled level of customized service by combining the hands-on support and local market knowledge of a strong, regional supplier with the purchasing strength, industry expertise, and delivery capabilities of a national company. We partner with our customers, which include national chains, independent restaurants, healthcare facilities, K-12 and universities, entertainment venues, and many other segments, providing tailored solutions and essential products to operate with greater efficiency and realize better bottom line results. Our aim every day is to serve with excellence, meeting the needs of foodservice professionals so they can achieve their culinary vision.

Position Summary/Objective

The Senior Data Analyst is responsible for turning data in to actionable information via the use of tools such as Excel, Power BI, and any other relevant programs. The ideal candidate will be able to comprehend business requirements and transform those ideas in to well-designed and efficient visualizations. This position will have a regional scope with reports and dashboards that impact all levels of the organization, therefore the Senior Data Analyst is expected to be a strong communicator who can successfully speak with and translate data to employees possessing widely varied levels of seniority and data familiarity.

Essential Functions & Responsibilities

  • Organizes and builds data models and Power BI reports/dashboards.
  • Finds the most efficient ways to manage data to maximize report value while minimizing resources.
  • Gathers requirements, designs algorithms, and develops solutions to complex business problems.
  • Identifies, interprets, and analyzes meaningful trends in complex datasets.
  • Acquires data from sources including but not limited to SQL, Excel, Web Scraping, Smartsheet, and APIs.
  • Provides organizational training and troubleshooting for analytics solutions.
  • Proactively stays on leading edge of analytics, machine learning, and other relevant developing areas.
  • Comfortably communicates analysis and findings to any and all levels of the organization.

Competencies, Skills and Abilities

  • Possess integrity and ethical character with a clean financial background.
  • Understand and support the confidential nature of the position and all related functions.
  • Ability to train, mentor and coach others when requested.
  • Possess exceptional written, verbal, and interpersonal skills, including ability to articulate recommendations in a concise and timely manner.
  • Able to analyze, organize, summarize, and articulate financial information.
  • Ability to multi-task and pay attention to multiple details and directives at a time.

Education & Experience

  • Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Economics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Information Management, Data Science, or Statistics
  • 6+ years of experience in Data Analysis/Data Science
  • Must be highly proficient and experienced with Excel and Power BI, as well as in coding SQL, m, and DAX
  • Preferred experience with related languages and packages (SPSS, SAS, Python, R, SSAS, SSIS, SSRS, etc.)
  • Proficient computer skills with all programs used by the organization, including expert skills in MS Office and the ability to quickly learn and utilize company enterprise software
