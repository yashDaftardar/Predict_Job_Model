Longeviti LLC is searching for a Data Scientist Junior. Longeviti is a government contractor specializing in providing professional support services in fields of information technology, program management, agency administration, intelligence analysis, language translation, culture immersion, and serving as opposing forces/role players. We are a certified 8(a) and small disadvantaged business that excels in the market having earned our status as an ISO 9001 certified provider of services and are rated as CMMI Level 3 in both Development and Services. Quality and continuous improvement are basic tenets of the organization and giving our staff the tools to make it happen are a trademark. Customer satisfaction is paramount.

Longeviti seeks a Data Scientist Junior to provide mission support for the Secretary of the Air Force (SAF) Concepts, Development, and Management Office (CDM).

SECURITY CLEARANCE:
  • Must have an active Top Secret, SCI eligible, security clearance.
  • Due to federal security clearance requirements, applicant must be a United States Citizen.
DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
  • Develop algorithms, write scripts, build predictive analytics, apply machine learning, and use the right combination of tools and frameworks to turn that set of disparate data points into objective answers to help leaders make informed decisions.
  • Provide clients with a deep understanding of their data, what it all means, and how they can use it.
REQUIRED EXPERIENCE, KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, AND ABILITIES:
  • Up to 3 years of experience with one or more scripting or scientific languages, including Python, R, C, or Java
  • Up to 3 years of experience with applying machine learning techniques and the key parameters that affect their performance
  • Up to 3 years of experience in working with a wide range of predictive and decision models and tools for developing models
  • Experience working w/ open source visualizations such as Kibana
  • Experience working with Elasticsearch
  • Experience designing, developing, and implementing ETL pipelines
  • Experience in one or more natural language processing topics, including tagging, syntactic parsing, word sense disambiguation, topic modeling contextual text mining, and application of deep learning to NLP
  • Experience with developing experimental and analytic plans for data modeling processes, using strong baselines, and determining cause and effect relationships accurately
  • Knowledge of statistical measures, including confidence intervals and the significance of error measurements, development, or evaluation data sets
EDUCATION:
  • BA or BS degree in Data Science, Applied Mathematics, CS, Engineering, Statistics, or equivalent
