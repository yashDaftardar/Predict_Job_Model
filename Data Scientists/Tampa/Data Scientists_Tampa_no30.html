Marketing Data Scientist


We are looking for a Marketing Data Scientist who will research and identify ways to build from our existing customer base and acquire new clients.


This role will include analyzing existing customer sales, B2B behavior and exploring market trends and opportunities. To be successful in this role, you should have significant experience with writing SQL queries/code, advanced Excel proficiency, knowledge of ASP coding and be comfortable with processing and analyzing marketing data. This team member works closely with departmental managers to analyze how to maximize the company’s return on investment.


Responsibilities


Identify the appropriate data to investigate, then gather, analyze, and conduct interpretation


Produce regularly scheduled and ad hoc reports as well as analysis


Report findings and research to senior management


Make recommendations based on your findings


Analyze sales funnels and customer intent


Measure ROI of online and offline advertising campaigns


Report on marketing KPIs, trends, like leads, conversion rates


Consolidation of sales data from multiple platforms


Track email campaign metrics (delivery, open, click-through rates)


Conduct competitive research and analyze benchmarking data


Research and implement marketing tools to support our promotional projects (automation software, social media scheduling etc)


Work closely with sales and marketing teams to identify opportunities for new client acquisition


Requirements


  • 1+ year of experience working as a Marketing Data Analyst
  • Bachelor’s degree in Marketing or relevant field Work experience as a Marketing Analyst or similar role of 1+ year
  • Expertise in SQL, MS Excel, ASP
  • Experience with PPC / SEO campaigns
  • Strong analytical abilities
  • Ability to think critically and conduct research
  • Working knowledge of e-commerce platforms, preferably Shopify/Amazon.
