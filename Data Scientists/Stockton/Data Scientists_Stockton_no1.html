Full Job Description

Primary Purpose:
Grading assistant for second year MS Data Science Courses.

Essential Functions:
Grading second year MS Data Science quizzes and exams as directed by the course tutor. Provide grading input and flag any associated issues to the course tutor.

Minimum Qualifications:
Knowledge of the Master of Data Science course materials in Data Engineering, Healthcare Case Studies, and Customer Analytics.
Practical experience in applying data science computer programming methods in these specific areas, using R and Python.

Preferred Qualifications:
Graduate degree in Data Science.
