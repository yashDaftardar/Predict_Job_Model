Role Summary

Our engineering team is looking for a technical, service-oriented, talented Senior Clinical Data Analyst. In this role, you will design clinical data solutions as part of our Eureka SaaS platform. You will be primarily responsible for the generation of functional mapping documentation to be used by the software development team in the eurekaSaaS platform for Clinical Trials.


Responsibilities

  • Implementation of CDISC standards including SDTM mapping, analysis datasets specifications, creation of Define.xml files, etc.
  • Design, develop, process and analyze data collections and processing from diverse sources, large scale structured and unstructured data
  • Collaborate with the Quality Assurance team to test the application functionality
  • Provide technical guidance, training and support to other team members
  • Ensure compliance with industry quality standards, regulations, guidelines and procedures
  • Manage multiple timelines and deliverables (for single or multiple clients) and managing client communications as assigned

  • Minimum 5 years' experience in biostatistics and/or regulatory submission
  • Strong experience within data conversion or implementation of validation rules
  • Experience within CDISC, SDTM mapping and/or creation of ADAM datasets, and Define.xml files
