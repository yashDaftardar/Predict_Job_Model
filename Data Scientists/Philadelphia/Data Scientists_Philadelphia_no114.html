Create the products that are changing an industry

At Vanguard, we continue to invest in technologies that leverage machine learning and artificial intelligence to drive business outcomes by fostering an insights-driven culture. We are looking for a Senior Engineering Manager to lead a fast-growing team with the goal of building tools and platform capabilities to enable our CAI’s (Center for Analytics and Insights) data scientists and engineers to access our data store, deploy machine learning models to production, and test hypotheses to drive business outcomes.

The CAI Center of Excellence focuses on developing and delivering large scale, complex, and innovative AI/ML solutions that are aligned with key business priorities and OKRs, including driving advice and product growth through marketing and sales optimization, improving the client experience through a personalized understanding of client's needs, and utilizing AI to lower cost of operations. In addition, CAI works to stay abreast with the latest development in AI/ML outside of Vanguard to bring new concepts, ideas, tools to Vanguard through proof of concepts and development of scalable capabilities for the AI community across Vanguard to consume.

In this role, you will define objectives for key products in the CAI Machine Learning Platform, own the technical roadmap for the product, be accountable for delivering results, and enable our CAI function to accomplish its imperatives. You will do this by growing and developing a diverse engineering team, coaching engineers to grow and become technology leaders. You will define engineering and operational excellence standards for running the platform and holding your team accountable to higher standards. You will partner with other product leaders and cross-functional technology organizations to innovate and enhance the CAI-ML platform and accelerate business outcomes. You will continue to lead with deep technical expertise and drive the adoption of the platform at scale.

Requirements

IN THIS HIGH-VISIBILITY ROLE, YOU WILL:

  • Build and Lead and a cohesive, high-functioning team of talented, diverse engineers by fostering a culture of trust, respect, and inclusion
  • Work with internal stakeholders and the product managers to anticipate services and platform needs to enable Data Science life cycle capabilities (model development, model training, model management, data engineering, and model serving).
  • Own your team's deliverables and ensure we develop scalable, highly-available services and infrastructure that enables our internal users and stakeholders to experiment and rapidly iterate on AI/ML products.
  • Demonstrate collaborative working style with a strong bias to driving business outcomes.
  • Seek diverse perspectives to drive bottom-up innovation and create buy-in from all technical stakeholders inside and outside the team.
  • Build and maintain the platform in an AWS cloud based infrastructure architecture aligning security, compliance, performance, and resilience prinicples.
  • Champion New Ways of Working and drive continuous improvement to MarTech agile software delivery practices
  • Cultivate a culture of relentless innovation, diversity of thought, teamwork, and excellence within the organization

AN IDEAL CANDIDATE:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science/Engineering, Information Systems or a related discipline
  • 5+ years of software development, or an equivalent combination of technical experience
  • 3+ years of leading technical team with supervisory responsibilities
  • Experience with Domino Data Lab and/or AWS SageMaker ecosystem
  • Experience with AWS services (EC2, S3, DynamoBD, EMR)
  • Experience with Kubernetes, Docker and the broader container ecosystem
  • Experience using open-source machine learning and statistical frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, R etc.
  • Thorough understanding of the agile software development life cycle (SDLC) and experience working in Agile, Lean & DevSecOps environments
  • Experience with reporting tools like Tableau
  • Excellent communication, writing, and presentation skills
  • Client-focused and results driven

Qualifications

  • Minimum of ten years related work experience, with at least five years of project management experience.
  • Undergraduate degree or equivalent combination of training and experience. Graduate degree preferred.

Special Factors

  • Vanguard is not offering visa sponsorship for this position.
