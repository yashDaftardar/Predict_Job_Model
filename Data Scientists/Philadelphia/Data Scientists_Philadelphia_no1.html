Are you an experienced, passionate pioneer in technology? A system's professional who wants to work in a collaborative environment. As an experienced Data Scientist, you will have the ability to share new ideas and collaborate on projects as a consultant without the extensive demands of travel.

Work you'll do/Responsibilities

  • You will be responsible for managing day to day client business support of their IT landscape and applications
  • The Data Scientist will join a team responsible for developing advanced analytics products; applying data visualization and statistical programming tools to enterprise data to advance and enable key mission outcomes.
  • In this role, they will support all phases of analytic work product development, from the identification of key business questions, from performing analyses to delivery of insights to decision-makers, with particular attention to the interplay between data and the business processes that produce and consume it.

The Team

Our Strategy practice brings together several key capabilities that will allow us to architect integrated programs that transform our clients' businesses, including Corporate & Business Unit Strategy, Technology Strategy & Insights, Enterprise Model Design, Enterprise Cloud Strategy and Business Transformation.

Strategy professionals will serve as trusted advisors to our clients, working with them to make clear data-driven choices about where to play and how to win, in order to drive growth and enterprise value.

Strategy will help our clients:

  • Identify strategies for growth and value creation
  • Develop the appropriate business models, operating models, and capabilities to support their strategic vision
  • Maximize the ROI on technology investments and leverage technology and Cloud trends to architect future business strategies

Qualifications Required

  • Bachelor's Degree or equivocal BS/MS degree in an associated field with at least 7 years of experience.
  • 5+ relevant experience within data science
  • Programming skills to access, transform and prepare large scale data for statistical modeling.
  • Experience utilizing statistical and machine learning methods required.
  • Modeling concepts (logistic, Random Forest, NLP etc.)
  • Strong python, SQL
  • Experience modeling (especially NLP and marketing models)
  • Must live in or be willing to relocate to Philadelphia, PA
  • Limited Sponsorship may be available
