Summary:

The Manager of Data Scientist is responsible for identifying, developing, enabling and optimizing Radial’s fraud risk technology to enhance our Fraud Risk Decisioning. This includes working with internal and external partners to identify and implement the key fraud risk technologies that enable Radial to increase the speed and accuracy of all fraud review decisions. The Manager of Data Scientist will work closely with the Rules Management team and Overwatch team to optimize and manage the fraud risk decisioning tools for all incoming orders.

Responsibilities:
  • Develop, maintain, evaluate and enhance Machine Learning Models
  • Proactively identifies new methods and processes to increase revenue and reduce cost and friction
  • Work closely with Technology team to build Radial’s Data Science framework
  • Perform ROI analysis and business case justification / review for new vendor/partner
  • Provide a weekly report that shows the status and next steps for all Fraud Risk Technology Partners
Knowledge/ Skills:
  • Experience with Oracle/SQL, Python, and Microsoft 365 Tools (Excel, Word, Power Point, Etc)
  • Strong analytical and communication skills
  • Ability to interface with data architects, fraud and payment teams to help define and implement necessary Business Objects
Work Experience /Education:
  • 3-5 years of e-commerce, banking, fraud, or Finance experience
  • Master’s degree in Economics, Statistics, or experience in a business-related field
  • Strong programming skills in SQL, SAS, Python, and R
  • Familiarity with BI platforms like Tableau
