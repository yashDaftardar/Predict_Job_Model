Full Job Description

With operations in 35+ nations and ~ 27,000 employees worldwide, CSL is driven to develop and deliver a broad range of lifesaving therapies to treat disorders such as hemophilia and primary immune deficiencies, and vaccines to prevent influenza. Our therapies are also used in cardiac surgery, organ transplantation and burn treatment.

CSL is the parent company of CSL Behring and Seqirus. CSL Behring is a global leader in the protein biotherapeutics industry, focused on bringing to market biotherapies used to treat serious and often rare conditions. CSL Behring operates CSL Plasma , one of the world's largest collectors of human plasma, which is used to create CSL's therapies. Seqirus is the second largest influenza vaccine company in the world and is a transcontinental partner in pandemic preparedness and a major contributor to the prevention and control of influenza globally.

We invite you to take a look at the many career possibilities available around the globe and consider building your promising future at CSL by becoming a member of our team!

The ML/AI Lead role as a member of the I&T organization will evangelize break through AI/ML solutions for CSL Behring.

Role is passionate about process automation and analytics, driving personalization for stakeholders across a broad spectrum of experiences.

The Sr. Manager Machine Learning/ AI Lead will have design authority for ML/AI applications and solutions, leading the architecture and design of advanced ML/AI solutions that will power CSL Behring business strategy and have significant impact helping to achieve long-term competitive advantage for CSL Behring.

The Sr. Manager Machine Learning/ AI Lead will embrace and influence domain driven design and architecture principles of security, privacy, scale, uptime, and reliability.

The Sr. Manager Machine Learning/ AI Lead will collaborate with I&T stakeholders and vendors building the ML/AI standard framework and patterns for feature engineering, model building and foundational services.

The Sr. Manager Machine Learning/ AI Lead should guide deployment work streams implementing ML/AI monitoring tactical timelines, cost, and risks.

Contribute to ML/AI architecture guiding principles, development standards and training.

Work with multiple projects, work streams at one time. Deliver results based upon project deadlines.
