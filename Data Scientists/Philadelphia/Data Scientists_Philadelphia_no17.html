Full Job Description

Role purpose:

The services that Elsevier provides are becoming increasingly dependent on Smart Content to support our corporate strategy of greater volume, types and sophistication of con tent. Elsevier are now looking for a Junior Data S cientist with a focus on Machine Learning , NLP , & i mage processing techniques and also experience with deployment of ML and other data science solutions via web front ends. B ack end development experience to help build state of the art application s in the health sciences domain, and host them robustly on web platform or deploy as APIs for wide availability . Hands on coding of NLP/ML/statistical algorithms and experience with web development is an absolute must.

Th is person will partici pate in the d esign, prototyping and implementation of ML and automation applications for our businesses in health markets . These applications have two main goals: cost savings by building workflow efficiencies or revenue generation by working directly on products or product capabilities . You will work closely with the health sciences teams. Sample projects may include extracting m edical information from electronic health care records, integrating big data sets to perform predictive analytics, information retrieval, classifying and curating our image assets, content enrichment pipelines fo r clinical decision support and building customer facing solutions by rapid prototyping.

You will also work closely with the EMMeT (Elsevier Medical Merged Taxonomy) team providing data analytics on EMMeT and related terminologies to support decision-making, designing automated approaches to bulk updates, ontology validation and terminology mappings. You will work with a team of medical experts, as well as product leads to determine how to best build and leverage semantic capabilities and build prototypes to showcase ML and AI capabilities . Models developed will need to be hosted either as an API or through full stack web development, depending on the case. Knowledge of P ython , Flask, JavaScript/HTML are mandatory. Experience with Natural Language Processing and Image Processing application s is necessary, and experience with medical terminologies such as UMLS, SNOMED CT, ICD-9, ICD-10, CPT, LOINC etc is a plus.

Main Activities and Responsibilities :

Text and data mining

Bring active experience into the organization on extraction text and data information from structured and unstructured data. Applying and developi ng these techniques, you will drive the implementation of automated annotation processes. Also well-versed in machine learning, you will bring new processes into the organization in order to improve (in cost and time-efficiency) the data excerption processes in Elsevier. This work includes the application of Elsevier's taxonomy and ontology assets to a wide variety of content - as well as drive developments in the application of and expansion on these vocabularies.

Web deployment , prototyping and operationalization

The methods developed by the Junior Data Scientist and team must be shared with the larger group in a user friendly, accessible manner. ML models will routinely need to be integrated into APIs and web apps with custom UI. These will be deployed in production environments for companywide availability.

Data analytics to support businesses and products

Analyze extracted information to drive such processes as automated and manual data cleansing. Data analytics can also be used to identify research trends, drive decision for content acquisition, or merging big data sets to perform predictive analytics . Using visualizations tool s to present the extracted data to be ready for consumption will be another key ability.

Contribute to process and product innovation

T he Junior Data Sc ientist is also part of the wider Data Science, Health Content Operations team. Contributing to a culture of product and process innovation, this person will be a trusted resource in new development projects in Elsevier. The person will connect with IT developers and (content) subject matter experts, translating information needs into software development. As a specialist member of the team, the data scientist will serve as a specialist in th e i r own field.

Proving and showcasing methodology

This person will prove the utility of new methods in a scientifically sound way. To show the value of new types of extraction and techniques , visualization and presentation of the value of the extracted data will be another key ability.

Proven development experience in some relevant implementation platforms for ML/ NLP tasks – proficiency in Python (preferred) , Julia and /or R

Experience in web hosting and deployment – JavaScript, HTML, PHP, Tomcat, F lask, u WSGI , Swagger

Experience using *nix sy stems, open source software, libraries and cloud computing

Familiarity with supervised and unsupervised learning; model building, validation , and testing using state of art ML algorithms such as random forest, SVM , Logistic Regression, Bayesian modeling

Familiarity with taxonomy applications across scientific and healthcare disciplines is a plus

Knowledge and experience with web technologies such as React/Angular JS , LAMP is a plus.

Experience with internationalization, validation techniques, and using statistical techniques in decision making is a plus

Able to work with a variety of stakeholders at the mid and senior management level
