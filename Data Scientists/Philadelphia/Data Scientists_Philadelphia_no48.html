Senior Data Analyst

This role can be 100% remote

Job Description Summary

We are on a mission to evolve how we use data at NewRez to grow our business and fulfill the needs of our customers. We drive creation of delightful, frictionless, and compliant payment experiences while maximizing the value of our data. Join the Enterprise Data team at NewRez, reporting to the Director, Enterprise Data Process & Quality Governance. In t his individual contributor role, you will be responsible for rationalizing the current state of multiple manual and automated data orchestrations – in order to advance our goals of trusted data interoperability. You will partner with technical and business stakeholders to implement solutions balancing current and legacy technical standards, while keeping an eye towards scalability and resilience. You will have the opportunity to grow the capabilities of the org to leverage trusted data to deliver against revenue, efficiency, and assurance objectives. We are looking for someone who can be a self-starter and own the accountability of digging into complex legacy processes, bringing together multiple stakeholders and diverse viewpoints, documenting what “good” looks like and reporting on multiple activities in a consolidated, compelling view that tells the story of where we are and where we need to be.

This is a very exciting time in the firm’s evolution, and we need passionate, collaborative, and energetic team members to help us take things to the next level!

Job Description

Responsibilities:

  • Research and document the level of care given to identified Critical and Material Data Elements across the enterprise-focusing on the most critical data assets first. This requires coordination and collaboration with multiple business and technical stakeholders.
  • Document and maintain data flow process diagrams, mapping specifications, dictionaries, and lineage.
  • Manage inventory of critical reports (partnering with BI and Analytics teams) and their data dependencies.
  • Build and maintain the enterprise data quality dashboard.
  • Serve as crucial liaison to both special projects working groups and the standup of an enterprise working group/center of excellence.
  • Define and document business critical data quality rules, using SQL or Python and work with platform/process owners to get them operationalized.
  • Participate in monitoring compliance to policies & procedures.
  • Contribute to balancing the governance activities to enable innovation, while aligning to all facets of risk and regulatory compliance.
  • Consult with Project Teams to ensure good data quality practices are baked into the lifecycle.
  • Conduct periodic data quality audits/surveys.
  • Assist process, product, and platform owners in responding to high priority data issues as they are identified.
  • Document, Analyze and Solve data quality issues as prioritized by stakeholders.


Requirements

  • 5+ years of experience in data management, with at least 5 years of experience deploying enterprise-wide data governance programs.
  • 5+ years of experience in agile project, process or delivery teams
  • Experience with Collaboration, Data Quality and Metadata Management Tools and Platforms
  • Intermediate SQL or Python skills
  • SQL Server or AWS Cloud Technologies
  • Balancing technical and business focused stakeholder demands while prioritizing action and outcomes.
  • Strong networking skills and ability to work across multiple organizations to accomplish diverse goals
  • Good understanding of technical data forensics, able to independently research data structures (relational SQL, semi-structured, Excel, Csv, etc.).
  • Excellent verbal and written communication and collaboration skills to effectively communicate with both business and technical development teams
  • Comfortable working in a fast-paced, results-oriented environment
  • Experience working with data & analytic solutions and the science behind them, either as a builder/provider of these solutions or as a client that uses them.
  • Experience implementing lightweight data quality solutions that blend process and technology.

Preferences

  • Strong SQL background and or Python/R
  • Experience with MS PowerBI or Tableau
  • Experience with multiple Data Quality technologies
  • Explaining Data Quality and Process in layperson terms
  • Demonstrating processes and methods that drive towards value-based outcomes.
  • Proven track record empathizing and rationalizing competing points of view, creating structure in ambiguity, and driving change towards outcomes that measurably benefit the firm.
