Main Purpose of Role:

This role will be responsible for providing strong analytical and reporting support for the HR team to help build a series of HR metrics in order to identify opportunities to affect business results, change processes and support strategic initiatives. This role will also provide key support to the HR Systems team around data integrity, support requests and other HR initiatives. This is a great opportunity to leave more about business systems.

Major Responsibilities of the Role:

  • Lead data group dedicated to ensuring data integrity across all HR systems
  • Work with HRIS team to design and build HR reports and dashboards, utilizing Workdays capabilities along with other applicable data sources
  • Review report inventory and requirement to leverage core Workday functionality.
  • Serve as the point person for HR metrics, reporting, and predictive analytics utilizing Workday
  • Analyze and interpret metrics data to proactively identify and predict trends in the organization
  • Research and implement ways to further automate processes both within the HR Systems and Metrics team and the broader HR group
  • Analyzes data and statistics for trends and patterns with attention to recruitment, hiring practices, motivation, turnover, and compliance with employment laws and regulations.
  • Prepares reports of data results, presenting and explaining findings to senior leadership.
  • Identifies and recommends reasonable short- and long-term goals, milestones, and benchmarks for key performance metrics.
  • Based on metrics and analysis, makes recommendations for policies and activities to improve the organizational culture including suggestions to attract and hire qualified candidates, to motivate and retain employees, to address staffing issues, and to maintain legal compliance.
  • Conduct data mapping exercises related to M&A activity and integration of new organizations
  • Ensure accurate migration and maintenance of colleague data during integration activities
  • Liaise with HRIS team on data loads
  • Manage quality assurance and audit processes to ensure data integrity, accuracy and protection.
  • Act as point person for data requests from Internal Audit
  • Collaborate with subject matter experts across HR to gather and interpret reporting requirements to support the needs of the business
  • Ensure consistency of metrics and reporting across the HR organization through education and training of the HR team
  • Reviews process flows related to data input and recommends improvements to better streamline data collection and ensure accuracy of data
  • Understand day-to-day operations to maintain the accuracy and integrity of data when processing reports
  • Explores alternative ways of meeting user requests through established applications and tools.

Knowledge, Skills, and Capabilities Required:

  • Strategic thinker who is able to assess data sets, independently make updates and understand downstream impacts and data flow
  • Ability to work with and protect highly sensitive data in a confidential manner adhering to data privacy policies at all times
  • Good organizational and time management skills, juggling conflicting priorities
  • High degree of comfort with technology and reporting; advanced computer skills with high proficiency in Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.
  • Statistical or mathematical background combined with strong visualization skills desired
  • Proven track record of exemplary customer service and quality product delivery
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills to interact with all levels of management across multiple businesses and geographies
  • Demonstrated ability to translate business reporting requirements into meaningful, efficient and sustainable reporting solutions
  • Familiarity with HR processes and procedures a plus
  • Experience with Workday report development using the Workday Enterprise Interface Builder (EIB), BIRT, and Workday Studio a plus
  • Knowledge of Workday reporting capabilities and data architecture like core HCM, recruiting, absence management, time tracking, compensation, etc. preferred
  • Undergraduate degree or equivalent education preferably in HR, Finance or Technology
  • 3-5 years of data analysis including report design/development
  • Advanced experience in MS Excel (Pivot tables, graphs, VLOOKUP, macros)
