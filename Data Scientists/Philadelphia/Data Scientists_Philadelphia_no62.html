Position Description:

Execute end-to-end model development process, from business requirements identification, data collection to model development, validation, documentation and evaluation by working closely with internal/external stakeholders across multiple business units. Design and develop machine learning models and data mining analysis in multiple fields such as Customer Behavior Analysis/Prediction, Marketing, Operational Optimization, Risk Management, Customer Attrition Prediction, and Loan Fallout Prediction. Drive the collection of new data and the refinement of existing data sources. Conduct quantitative analytics and statistical modeling with structured and unstructured data to understand product performance and to identify improvement opportunities. Visualize complex data and findings in the format of business tables, graphs, and dashboards. Interpret and explain analytical results to other team members. Recommend realistic solutions using sound judgment and business knowledge. Evaluate external analytical models.

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Masters Degree in Engineering (any), statistics, mathematics, computer science, or any relevant science field and one year of experience in the field.
  • Must have experience in/with: predictive modeling, quantitative analysis, machine learning, classification, clustering, data mining, and optimization techniques in the field of finance.
  • Must have experience in/with: MapReduce, Hadoop, and NoSQL; NLP, text mining, and social media analysis; visualization tools like Tableau, PowerBI, QlikView, or Spotfire; and business analysis and ability to write technical documentation.
