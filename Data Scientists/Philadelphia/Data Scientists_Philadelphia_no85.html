MACHINE LEARNING ENGINEER

PNI Digital Media
Media, PA
Remote

Status : Remote work from any Canadian location. Relocation to Vancouver, BC may be considered. Work location may be re-evaluated based on pandemic status.

Our Machine Learning Engineer will be responsible for designing and developing high impact AI-based systems in the cloud. They will develop, evaluate and deploy computer vision and other machine learning models that will be the core of improved online experiences in the online personalization retail space. The role will work closely with our Data Science and Development teams to implement scalable platform algorithms and transition them into stable, scalable, near real-time productions systems.

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Design and build machine learning systems while balancing cost and model performance.

  • Apply AI/ML methods to massive data sets including real-time streaming data sets.

  • Design and implement proprietary machine learning algorithms and optimize them based on runtime system requirements and constraints.

  • Propose, implement, and monitor validation and performance metrics for the proprietary algorithms.

  • Conduct code reviews and work closely with the software teams to build MLOps pipelines to deploy the prototyped models to various environments.

  • Collaborate effectively with other members of the team and broader services group, including but not limited to Product Team, Data Science and Data Engineers, Development Teams and Release and Operations Teams

  • Work alongside our Product, UX, and Prototype Design teams, leverage your experience and expertise in the AI space to influence our product roadmap, developing innovative solutions that add additional capabilities to our product suite.

  • Optimize the machine learning experimentation cycle as well as training methods/approaches to enable quick implementation timelines for new customers.

  • Maintain the underlying AI infrastructure.

  • Navigate between traditional software development and machine learning implementations.

SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE:

  • 5+ years' experience focusing on data science, or machine learning

  • B.Sc. in Data Science, Machine Learning, Statistics, Computer Science, Applied Math, or an experience in equivalent highly technical field.

  • Hands-on experience in Computer Vision or Machine Learning projects.

  • Proficiency in Python development, and the usage of libraries such as scikit-learn, MLlib, NumPy, Pandas, and deep learning frame works, such as TensorFlow, Keras, PyTorch, NVIDIA Deep Stream.

  • Practical experience in building, evaluating, scaling, and deploying machine learning pipelines with Python.

  • Experience with big data pipelines for real-time data streaming platforms using existing on-prem frameworks, including Kafka, Spark Streaming, or etc.

  • Experience with SQL, accessing and organizing data drawn from relational and non-relational datastores using PySpark, Scala or similar.

  • Knowledge of cloud computing ecosystems (such as AWS or Azure) and experience of developing applications on the cloud platforms using various cloud services.

  • Experience building and maintaining production quality microservices with APIs.

  • Proven background answering open ended research questions using data.

  • Proven ability to ship quality software products and services.

  • Positive, proactive attitude with a strong interest in learning new technologies.

  • Able to work independently, but also confident working in a team with a diverse skillset and from diverse disciplines.

NICE TO HAVE SKILLS:

  • Experience in developing ML techniques for time-series prediction.

  • Experience with Natural Language Processing (entity recognition, multimodal content classification, etc.)

  • Experience with recommendation and ranking algorithms.

  • Experience in conducting AB testing, experimentation, and casual inference methodologies.
