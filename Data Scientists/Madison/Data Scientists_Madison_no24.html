Summary of Major Responsibilities:
The Senior Business Analyst on the Data and Analytics Platform at Exact Sciences will have an exemplary opportunity to influence building best in class data driven solutions for our Precision Oncology business unit. This position will perform a dual role as a Business Analyst and Product Manager as required. The role will be primarily responsible for leading business requirements elicitation, defining product features and influencing customers with a consultative approach to build right product to requirement fit while keeping long term vision in context.The role is focused on supporting the Clinical Affairs, Medical Affairs and Bio Statistics team within EXAS and hence needs to have a strong background in Oncology clinical & research data domain.
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
  • Produce business requirements document.
  • Develop as-is and to-be business process workflows.
  • Understand and map in depth business processes, people, and data systems detail.
  • Analyze gaps and propose to-be process flows.
  • Create dashboards, applications, reports, and other data and analytics product mock-ups using design thinking, purpose driven KPI design, and UI/UX focus.
  • Develop well-organized, informative presentations using appropriate tools.
  • Work with the product manager to understand the project scope and broader business vision.
  • Immerse in new business areas, as required, to gain domain expertise with proactive research and fast learning.
  • Elicit business requirements for the defined scope by engaging with appropriate stakeholders and leadership at all levels.
  • Handover business requirements to the technical team ensuring adequate business context, detailed feature description, and relative prioritization for technical requirements definition and development.
  • Develop manageable, implementable product features by dissecting complex product requirements.
  • Aggregate, assimilate, and analyze information and data from diverse sources towards decision recommendations.
  • Consult business teams on building right product features based on business requirement and understanding of where the product fits in the business workflow.
  • Evaluate existing analytical tools, processes, and systems to identify opportunities for improvement and bring operational efficiency.
  • Assist in data-product Lifecyle assessments to build a fit-to-requirement/size Data and Analytics product portfolio.
  • Ability to identify roadblocks to execution and escalate, as appropriate.
  • Contribute to establish best-in-class business analysis, requirements gathering, requirements handover, feature prioritization, and onboarding/training processes through the Business Analysis Center of Excellence focused on the Data and Analytics Platform.
  • Provide input and support to business teams during product release with necessary Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), training, and supporting material.

  • Identify business problems and design fit for purpose product features.
  • Manage backlog of projects and features.
  • Train and mentor Junior Business Analysts and Product Managers.
  • Provide project status updates in both verbal and written format.
