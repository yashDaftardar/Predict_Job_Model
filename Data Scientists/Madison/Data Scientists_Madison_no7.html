Full Job Description

Overview:
Quartz is seeking an individual who is interested in growing a career as a Senior Data Analyst – EDM/Quality Assurance within our Data Warehouse Team. This role partners with the business to evaluate data needs and translate into deliverables for internal users and analytics applications as well as external entities including third party data feeds, regulatory, and governmental data needs. This position is responsible for collecting requirements, turning them into technical specifications, monitoring the quality of deliverables, and monitoring and governing the overall quality of the Quartz Enterprise Data Warehouse and the processes that feed it.
Responsibilities:
  • Ensure that new and existing data feeds, analytics applications and data store content meet user’s needs and are delivered with high quality.
  • Create and independently execute test plans to validate that ETL development activities meets the expected outcomes while ensuring existing data remains intact.
  • Complete analysis to fully define defects and work with solutions analysts and ETL Developers to work to resolution.
  • Work with internal stakeholders to ensure all appropriate test cases are completed in the applications as necessary and that all test case scenarios are validated.
  • Work with testing groups at external organizations to ensure feeds into and out of the data warehouse work as expected to meet the business purpose.
  • Review analysis and other documentation created by junior analysts; provide feedback remediation to drive completion of a solid product.
  • Understand the business processes and rules to create the data including claims, enrollment, billing, etc. and use that knowledge to build appropriate structure to enable the business to be able to meet current and future needs.
Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent work experience will be considered in lieu of education requirement
  • 5 + years of relevant experience testing software or data products
  • Advanced knowledge of QA methodology and software development life cycle
  • Advanced knowledge of SQL, analytical software, and query tools
  • Advanced knowledge of data warehousing principles including provenance, lineage, modelling, etc. and applies best practices
  • Experience with issue documentation software and best practices
  • Ability to problem solve using analytical thinking skills.
  • Works well independently, under pressure, and be adaptable to change
  • Must be inquisitive and seek answers to complex questions without being prompted
  • Create strong relationships and provide positive experiences for internal partners
  • Well organized and ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously
Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, this full-time role will start out working remotely. As things improve with the pandemic, this position will have the option to transition to our office in Madison, WI.
