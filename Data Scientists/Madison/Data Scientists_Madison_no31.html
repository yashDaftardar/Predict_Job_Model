POSITION SUMMARY

Position Summary

The Senior Provider Data Analyst is responsible for subject matter and system expertise related to provider data. The incumbent leads inter-departmental initiatives related to coordination of system upgrades, implementation of new functionality, project planning, process improvement and analyzing interoperability with other applications and processes related to provider data. The incumbent collaborates extensively with other departments, end users whose business needs are impacted by the current systems applications and with other technical professionals to ensure accurate along with timely provider data is available.

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS

Required Qualifications

  • Minimum Bachelor’s Degree, business or insurance or related field or commensurate experience.

  • Minimum 6 years' experience with health plans/health systems.

  • Experience working with and understanding provider data, provider billing and/or provider networks.

  • Expertise in the concepts and terminology associated with group insurance contracts, including insurance benefit plan design requirements, claims processes, and provider contracts and networks.

  • Expert knowledge of the claims processing information system and extensive knowledge and experience applying systems analysis methodologies in practical applications including using structured analysis, flowcharts, and systems diagrams to convert project specifications into application solutions.

  • Extensive knowledge of operational and technical systems, as well as a comprehensive understanding of the operational needs and business processes the systems support.

  • Understanding of complex technical concepts pertaining to network resources, change management, coordination of multiple environments, SQL server, and client server applications.

  • Proficient with Microsoft Products (Excel, Outlook, Access Power Point, Word, Visio) and Reporting Software such as Power BI and SQL.
