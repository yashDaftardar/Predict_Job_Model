About the Role

We are looking for a Clinical Data Science and Analytics Product Manager who will drive material business outcomes with insights gained from our clinical data. The ideal candidate is adept at working with large clinical data sets to discover product opportunities. As that ideal candidate, you must have a proven ability to drive business results with data-based insights across a wide range of stakeholders and functional teams. We are looking for someone to identify problems we can solve and value we can generate. Do we have some ideas already? You bet, but the right person will very quickly go beyond what is already known and create new levels of value & insights from our data.

Responsibilities

  • Work with stakeholders throughout the organization to identify opportunities for leveraging health data to drive commercial solutions.
  • Create and execute on the analytics roadmap for Engineering, move data from Aspenti applications and other sources to Aspenti Healthcare’s Data Lake and to Analytics Data Warehouses.
  • Create and execute on roadmap for Data Architecture, resulting in clean, consistent and complete data, enabling Data Science.
  • Create and execute on the Data Science roadmap, resulting in internal and external products.
  • Identify and curate data sources that can enable the predictive analytics that increase the value of the Aspenti Healthcare offerings.
  • Think critically, and solve problems that are aligned with business objectives and strategic vision.
  • Mine and analyze data from company databases and external sources, including social determinants of health (SDoH) sources, to create insights.
  • Develop custom data models and algorithms to apply to data sets.
  • Assess the effectiveness and accuracy of new data sources.
  • Use modeling to increase and optimize customer experiences, revenue generation and other business outcomes.
  • Build a prediction service infrastructure, which will allow predictions from models to be delivered in production environments in a highly performant manner.
  • Exceed revenue and margin targets for the organization.
  • Preferred: Experience building HIPAA and/or GDPR adherent solutions

What It Takes

Delivering on our 3-part mission means providing quality results and exceptional patient care. We share a commitment to improving treatment outcomes through our work. We work closely with community organizations that advocate healthy lifestyles. Aspenti Health Colleagues are a team with compassion and collaboration to be an integral part of the recovery community.

Minimum Experience Required

  • Bachelor’s degree in a technical area
  • 5+ years of experience in a Healthcare Product Management role

Skills We’re Looking For

  • Familiarity with Healthcare IT and/or Healthcare Analytics
  • Experience delivering new insights or product capabilities using clinical data and SDOH
  • Experience using statistical computer languages (R, Python, SQL, etc.) to manipulate data and draw insights from large data sets. Experience working with and creating data architectures
  • Knowledge of advanced statistical techniques and concepts (regression, properties of distributions, statistical tests and proper usage, etc.) and experience with applications
  • Experience working with Engineering teams to overdeliver, on schedule, with high quality
  • Experience in Data Sciences, Data Engineering and/or Data Architecture
  • Experience working with market facing and sales teams
  • Ability to drive market discovery, to transform market pain points into needs, and then into solutions
  • Crisp written and verbal communications capabilities
  • Familiarity with cloud infrastructure and tools
  • Familiarity with data science tools
  • Ability to thrive in an entrepreneurial environment, comfort with ambiguity
  • Excited about Aspenti’s mission of improving the behavioral healthcare experience
