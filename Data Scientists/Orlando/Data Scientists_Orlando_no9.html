You Will Be Responsible For:

  • Coordinate the different tasks that must be completed by their team for a big data project. Tasks may include researching and creating effective methods to collect data, analyzing information, and recommending solutions to a business.

Qualifications


What You Will Need:

  • Bachelor’s Degree and 5+ years of experience in a quantitative or computing field such as Mathematics, Statistics, Econometrics or Industrial Engineering
  • Comprehensive knowledge of modern data science and product/web analytics tools and techniques, including Python and associated data science ecosystem, and Machine Learning
  • Broad knowledge and understanding of advanced statistical, machine learning, and/or data mining techniques

Knowledge and Skills Required:

  • Ability to mentor, lead, and coach a small team of data scientists while still completing projects of his/her own
  • Strong ability to plan and manage numerous processes, people and projects simultaneously
  • Working knowledge of data mining principles: predictive analytics, mapping, collecting data from multiple data systems
  • Experience working with and creating databases and data visualization tools using all relevant data to inform decisions.
  • Experience using analytics techniques to contribute to company growth efforts, increasing revenue and other key business outcomes
  • Strong problem solving, quantitative and analytical abilities
  • Excellent communication, collaboration and delegation skills
  • Ability to use advanced statistical modeling techniques to convert data into actionable market insights
  • Strong problem-solving skills with ability to think and act strategically
  • Superb communication skills explaining complex quantitative models to Network Development, Marketing, and other Senior Executives within AdventHealth
  • Achieve defined project goals within customer deadlines; proactively communicate status and escalate issues as needed
  • Comfortable using varied platforms and applications such as SQL, Python, and R
  • Quality-focused with attention to detail and accuracy

Job Summary:

The Data Science Manager is responsible for helping organization leverage on data, working with and through a team of data scientists to provide valuable direction and insight, for management to make informed decisions. The Data Science Manager’s primary responsibility is to lead a group of data scientists that communicate complex data or algorithms into simple conclusions that will empower others to drive business improvement actions across the healthcare continuum based on the derived insights. This individual will lead efforts to identify and quantify market opportunities, forecast market behavior, and analyze ways to maximize use of current physical assets. The Data Science Manager, Market Insights is an individual who is an effective communicator, self-motivated, and able to drive insights into actions that improve decision-making and business results. Participates in outstanding customer service and accepts responsibility in maintaining relationships that are equally respectful to all.
