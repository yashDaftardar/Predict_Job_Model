Lockheed Martin is seeking an experienced professional with 7+ years experience and/or Ph.D. with recent publications in Machine Learning, specifically using Deep Learning architectures such as deep neural networks. In addition, the successful candidate will have experience with using data analytics and applying analytics to better understand how to use deep learning to make decisions. The position will require the candidate to use deep learning and related decision-making analytics and apply it in various applications where processing resources and training data may be limited. Tasks include customer interactions and outreach for new DL business as well as support for MFC internal work, including classified programs.

Relocation: Relocation assistance is possible for this exciting position.

The Locale: Orlando is a city in the Central Florida area. This region is a great place to live and to raise a family. Central Florida offers lots of attractive residential housing options.

The Greater Orlando metropolitan area is the sixth largest metro area in the southeastern United States. The climate here is warm and sunny all year long for the most part. Orlando is home to Walt Disney World, Universal Studios Florida, and SeaWorld. Orlando is also home to the second largest University in the US, the University of Central Florida (UCF). Where can you get to from Orlando? Orlando is about an hour's drive from Cocoa Beach and the "Space Coast" to include NASA and the Kennedy Space Center.

Tampa is about 1.5 hours from Orlando. West Palm Beach is about 2.5 hours to the south and Jacksonville is about 2 hours to the northeast. Atlanta, GA is 6 hours to the northwest.
