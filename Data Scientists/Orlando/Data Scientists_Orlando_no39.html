Data Science Manager

Job Title: Data Science Manager

Location: Orlando, FL or Remote (TBD)

Reports To: Chief Risk Officer

About LSQ

LSQ provides businesses better solutions to manage their cash flow. Offering supply chain finance (LSQ FastTrack) and invoice purchase funding solutions, LSQ provides buyers & suppliers with a simple, secure, and transparent funding experience. With working capital from LSQ, a business can purchase more inventory, fill more orders, and take advantage of new growth opportunities. Our creative and timely approach to providing working capital allows our clients and partners to drive business success.

Position Summary

LSQ is seeking an exceptional Data Science Manager with the leadership and technical capacity to help substantially advance data science at LSQ. Reporting directly to the Chief Risk Officer (CRO), the Data Science Manager will be responsible for leading a talented team of data scientists and analysts to deliver transformational insights across the business. The position will be a part of our Operations/Risk/Data (ORD) team, and this manager will need to translate theory and research into practical application.


LSQ is looking to accelerate the pace of its data science journey. As the Data Science Manager, you will have the opportunity to work with 20+ years of invoice data across US corporates - rich B2B interactions between small, medium, and large companies - across market cycles, geography, and industries. We seek a leader who can balance strong technical foundation-building (i.e., statistical rigor, agile process, documentation), speed to execution, and practical application. Some examples of business problems we try to solve with data (today) include: (1) what is the credit quality of a company/corporation?; (2) what is the likelihood of an invoice being disputed?; (3) which supplier is likely to need working capital financing? We believe we are just scratching the surface.


This manager will recruit, lead and mentor data professionals. The manager will also form strong working partnerships with product, engineering, distribution, operations and risk colleagues - to formulate analytical roadmaps, implement agile processes, and develop longer term data strategy that aligns with LSQ’s goals. We believe that strong technical skills must be complemented with exceptional communication skills - to translate the technical into the practical.


Essential Duties And Responsibilities

  • Build and execute the data science strategy at LSQ

  • Solve business problems by unlocking 20+ years of LSQ data

  • Productionize data science algorithms for internal and external use

  • Lead and mentor the existing data team; recruit and onboard exceptional data scientists

  • Implement agile framework to data science discipline

  • Collaborate and stay aligned with product and engineering (including data infrastructure, infosec)

  • Obtain and acquire new data, that augments LSQ’s existing data

  • Demonstrate an advanced ability to communicate complex results to technical and non-technical audiences


The ideal candidate for this role will possess the following skills:

  • Strong oral and written communication skills; ability to tell a story with data

  • Strong knowledge of data science techniques, such as Machine Learning (e.g., regression, ensemble methods) and Statistical methods (Bayesian methods, experimental design, causal inference)

  • Advanced knowledge and understanding of programming (Python, .NET, Java), SDLC, version control systems (GitHub, CVS, etc.), APIs, and security protocol

  • Financial services (e.g., risk management) domain experience is preferred; more specifically, business lending/payments, supply chain finance, or middle market lending

  • Experience with the implementation, delivery & maintenance of production resources/systems, serving both internal/operational and external client users

  • Strong leadership skills, with the ability to develop fellow team members through training, communication and mentorship

  • Experience and knowledge of how to work in an Agile/Scrum-driven environment


Preferred Experience/Education/Qualifications:

  • Successful track record of building data science platforms (FinTech-specific is a plus)

  • An advanced degree (M.S., PhD.), preferably in Statistics, Computer Science, Physical Sciences, Economics, or a related technical field

  • A consistent track record of performing data analysis using SQL, Java, R, C and/or Python

  • Experience solving complex business problems using inferential statistics, machine learning, business intelligence and/or automation

  • Experience building and managing ETL processes

  • Strong foundation in Computer Science; can demonstrate competency with data structures, algorithms, and software design optimized for large-scale software systems

  • Experience performing code reviews through a version control system

  • Bonus: knowledge/experience in Invoice and/or Supply Chain Finance


Technologies we use:

  • Python (numpy, pandas, sklearn) & R, as well as some .NET and/or Java

  • SQL databases (MS SQL Server, MySQL, Postgres), Redshift

  • AWS (i.e. EC2, ECS, DMS, S3, RDS, RedShift, EMR/Spark, SageMaker, Athena)

  • Tableau & MS SQL Server Reporting Services (SSRS)

  • GitHub

  • Jira
