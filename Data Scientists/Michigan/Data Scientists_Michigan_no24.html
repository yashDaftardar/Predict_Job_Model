This position will work with large, sophisticated data sets and apply logic techniques to develop solutions and reporting. A successful candidate will be a robust data explorer and an inventive problem solver who is energized by difficult business problems.

Responsibilities include:

  • Creation and implementation of queries, compiling and manipulating data from separate sources to form unified sets for analysis
  • Become an expert in our data, software tools and business processes in order to provide guidance and resolve issues
  • Query development to address standard and adhoc reporting requests
  • Work closely with the Information Technologies (IT) departments to ensure data and tools are accurate and provide feedback on user acceptance testing
  • Build Dashboards and custom visualizations to highlight data insights
  • Communication with end users to address questions regarding report data and logic
  • Document business rules and processes for consumption by other data scientists and analysts

Additional Job Description

Basic Requirements:

  • Bachelors’ degree
  • 2 years experience in a technical or quantitative role
  • 5 years SQL experience (Oracle, SQL, or PL SQL)
  • High level of experience with database and data warehousing platforms (MS Access, SQL Developer, Teradata, HUE, Hive, Hadoop)
  • Ability to prepare dashboards and reports using Power BI
  • Front-end development experience utilizing customized SQL and VB coding to collaborate with Oracle and other databases

Preferred Requirements:

  • 2+ years’ experience with Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning algorithms and techniques
  • 2+ years’ experience with General Motors vehicle telemetry data
  • Masters’ degree a plus
  • Time and project management skills used to manage multiple incoming requests for unique reports
  • Ability to craft proof of concept systems to support business processes
  • Effective time management skills to balance workload across multiple simultaneous projects and meet deadlines proactively and independently
  • Flexible analytic approach that allows for producing results at varying levels of precision
  • Strong listening and communications skills, with ability to clearly and concisely explain sophisticated problems and technologies to non-expert and executive audiences
  • Evidence of being able to transfer solutions to business stakeholders for successful adoption and value realization
  • Dedication to constant learning to expand skills and keep pace with industry advances
