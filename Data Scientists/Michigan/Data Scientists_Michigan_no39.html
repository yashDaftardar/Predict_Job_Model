As a Data Scientist II at Red Ventures, you will create innovative solutions to solve business problems by using advanced machine learning, statistical modeling and optimization techniques and turning them into real world solutions for decision making online or offline. You will solve business critical problems inside a company that has a strong culture of measurement, testing, and optimization across all facets of its marketing and customer acquisition activities. You will see your contributions make a material impact to the business bottom line. This role is for the Financial Services Data Science team, which supports the Financial Services vertical within the Red Ventures organization. Financial Services is dedicated to making it easier for customers to navigate the difficult choices they face in their financial lives. You will work with other data scientists and data professionals to build new capabilities that will provide world class customer service and next-generation digital execution.

We welcome candidates from different backgrounds, as we believe that diverse, inclusive teams are better teams. Think of the bullets below as guidelines: if you only partially meet the qualifications on this posting, we encourage you to apply anyway!

What You'll Be Doing:

  • Understand business strategy and identify innovative data science opportunities that can lead to significant business impact in a rapidly growing business
  • Set strategy and the execution plan for large data science initiatives, projects and programs to drive the business into its next evolution
  • Bring relevant state-of-the-art data science technologies to the team and lead by example to establish best practices
  • Mentor junior data science team members and serve as a subject matter expert both within Financial Services and in the broader Red Ventures data organization

What We're Looking For:

  • 3 - 4+ year's experience as a Data Scientist or Master's degree in STEM with specialization in machine learning, statistical modeling and optimization
  • Strong knowledge of machine learning, predictive modeling, statistics and data science in general.
    • Knowledge and experience in marketing channel optimization, customer behavior/journey modeling and prediction, product recommendation systems, online testing and etc. is highly desired
  • Proficiency in one or multiple data science programming languages such as R, Python and Spark
  • Capability of communicating effectively with business and data science leaders on project status, timeline and technical results
  • Proficient at collecting and mining data from disparate data sources, and willing to dig deeper and understand the process that creates the data
  • Analytical and detail oriented with the ability to prioritize, execute and deliver projects on time
  • Capable of translating business opportunities into data science problems and defining the right project scope and performance metric to measure success
  • Must be comfortable with unstructured, fast moving and constantly evolving high-growth environment
  • Committed team player who is proactive, takes ownership over the success of their projects, and works hard to support those around them
  • Applied experience with machine learning on large datasets

Who We Are:

Founded in 2000, Red Ventures is a portfolio of growing digital businesses that bring consumers and brands together through integrated e-commerce, strategic partnerships and many proprietary brands including Bankrate, AllConnect.com and Reviews.com. Headquartered south of Charlotte, NC, Red Ventures has over 3000 employees in offices across the US, as well as London and Sao Paulo. For more information, visit www.redventures.com.
