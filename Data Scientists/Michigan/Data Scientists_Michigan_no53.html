Data Scientist Retail Optimization

Date: May 7, 2021
Brand:
Location: Milan (MI), Milano, IT

JOB SCOPE AND MAIN RESPONSIBILITIES:


The candidate will assist in the analytical efforts in Retail Optimization for the Global Assortment & Planning teams. This individual will help in identifying key business drivers to be used in modeling projects and assessing outcomes from previous implementations. They will be focused on innovation and the development of complex models to improve the business teams decision-making. They will act as a thought –provoker and create a culture to resolve business needs utilizing a fact-based approach.

AREAS OF RESPONSIBILITIES AND RELATED ACTIVITIES:


A - Evaluates existing retail activities to optimize and provide a recommended analytical approach that will improve overall business results

B - Interpret and present data in a meaningful and actionable format that allows the leadership team to derive relevant insights

C – Ability to scope and frame analytical questions and write necessary code/scripts to deliver analytics

D – looks for opportunities to improve our capital investment through various modeling and forecasting tools


NETWORK OF INTERACTION:


Retail Merchandise Directors and Managers, Retail Store & Assortment Planners, Category Managers, Countries


TECHNICAL SKILLS – PROFESSIONAL KNOWLEDGE:


  • Educational Background: Bachelors degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Information Technology or similar
  • Experience in Data Science, Data Mining, Machine Learning
  • At least 2 years relevant work experience
  • Must Have - Proficient in programming languages and software development tools: Python, R, SQL, GIT
  • Nice To Have - Experience with Data Visualization & Business Intelligence tools (PowerBI, Qlik) and Experience with Business Objects or similar reporting tools
  • Analytical and statistical capabilities: skilled in various forecasting, modeling and machine learning methodologies, able to interpret findings into relevant, actionable and understandable business insights
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills, able to synthesize analyses into simple and clear written communications


Job Segment: Retail
