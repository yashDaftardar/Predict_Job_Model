Job Summary/Basic Function

This position is responsible for a broad range of activities involving data collection, analysis and reporting in academic and administrative areas. The position is also responsible for reporting to external agencies such as state and federal units, accreditation agencies, and others. The position will report to the Vice President for Finance & Operations. The Director would oversee all Institutional Research activities and personnel.

Essential Job Duties:

 Gather, prepare and submit institutional data on behalf of the university to state and federal agencies, national and regional organizations.

 Respond to cyclical reporting needs, day-to-day operational data needs, and ad hoc requests for institutional data from internal units and external constituents

 Develop data querying, processing and reporting against the Ellucian Banner database

 Extract data to support institutional reports and analyses for university administrators, directors, and school chairs to make data-informed decisions

 Write complex PL/SQL statements to create database tables, views, etc. to provide extensible reporting functionality to the Banner database

 Responsible for web site management and technical support as it relates to data; oversees and/or develops web pages and uploads for institutional data.

 Participates in professional development through memberships in appropriate state, regional and national organizations.

 Benchmarking university data against similar metrics from other institutions

 Manage, maintain, and develop documentation for reporting

 Updates annual and semester reports on the university Institutional Research website

 Maintain Confidentiality of sensitive data

 Adhere to University policies and guidelines as well as state and/or federal laws and regulations regarding the dissemination of data

 Ensure accurate and reliable data is reported

 Create and maintain data-warehouses as needed

 Create and maintain dashboards as needed

 Represent LSSU at state meetings

 Other duties as assigned
