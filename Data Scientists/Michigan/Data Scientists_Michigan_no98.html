Job Overview: Statistical Modeler/Data Analyst 3

RCO Engineering, Inc. is seeking a technical integration lead between the sales team and our customer experience/ops teams to execute SXM programs. This is a cross-functional role, slightly along the lines of project management. Sales and marketing experience is highly preferred for this role. Also experience with Sequel technology would be greatly beneficial. The position is currently remote, but is expected to be on site when office re-opens.

Core Responsibilities

  • Conduct pre-campaign analysis to understand potential target populations and finalize target definition
  • Design in-market tests
  • Conduct bias testing among test versus control groups
  • Develop and execute analytic plans for evaluation the impact of marketing campaigns
  • Model campaign response
  • Develop meaning and actionable insights based on campaign results
  • Communicate campaign results to internal clients through standard reporting vehicles and ad hoc in-depth reports
  • Work on intra-departmental and cross-functional teams to support continuous improvement of overall campaign management process, including design of data environment, data integrity assurance, standardized reporting, and process automation.
  • Lead initiatives to identify and implement new methods in campaign analytics

Requirements:

  • 8-11 years of work experience in marketing, advertising, consumer research or related field
  • High level of analytical ability
  • High level of interpersonal skills to work effectively with other
  • Strong level of communication (written, verbal, and presentation) skills
  • Ability to effectively manage multiple assignments
  • Ability to travel
  • BA/BS in business, management, psychology, mathematics, economics, statistics or equivalent training
  • Prefer an advanced degree in marketing, market research, psychology, or business
