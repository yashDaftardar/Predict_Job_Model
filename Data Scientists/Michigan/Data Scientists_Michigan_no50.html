Amazon's mission is to be the Earth's most customer centric company. Do you have what it takes to take an active role in that mission? The Seller Customer Service team is uniquely positioned as the only team at Amazon that directly serves Buyers, Selling Partners and Third-Party Developers. Billions of times a year Amazon's customers rely on us to provide them with the tools necessary for the world's best buying experience.

Our primary customers are Buyers. We provide Selling Partners with the tools necessary to give Buyers the best experience possible. As a highly instrumented conduit for the customer experience, we make our data available to other teams and when our data raises deficiencies in another teamâ€™s tools, we will seek them out to resolve the issue. Our success is measured by monitoring the number and length of customer interactions. We prioritize product investments in three key themes 1) eliminating Selling Partner contacts, 2) increasing first contact resolution rate, and 3) reducing the number of messages required to resolve a Selling Partner contact.

To help make this a reality, we are looking for a Senior Technical Program Manager to drive cross-cutting initiatives across these products. The right candidate will have proven Program Management skills and the ability to translate technical needs and pain points, into projects or programs that scale. Additionally, the ideal candidate will have excellent communication skills (verbal and written), ability to work with technical teams across various groups to create strategy for and drive programs. The right candidate will have excellent organizational skills and proven program management experience with meticulous attention to detail and strong bias for action. To be successful in this position, you must be highly motivated, creative and a self-starter with strong time management skills who is able to see the big picture, execute flawlessly, and drive against timelines and project roadmaps. In addition, the Sr. Technical Program Manager must possess strong analytical capabilities and be comfortable drawing metric-based conclusions by managing an extensive amount of data. Our ideal candidate should possess strong business acumen with strategic and critical thinking. In this role, you will also drive strategic communications across organizations providing clarity in recommendations.
This role is located in Detroit, MI and will consist of 15-25% travel
