Domino’s, which began in 1960 as a single store location in Ypsilanti, MI, has had a lot to celebrate lately: we’re a reshaped, re-energized brand of honesty, transparency and accountability – not to mention, great food! In the rise to becoming a true technology leader, the brand is now consistently one of the top five companies in online transactions and over 65% of our sales in the U.S. are taken through digital channels. The brand continues to ‘deliver the dream’ to local business owners, 90% of which started as delivery drivers and pizza makers in our stores. That’s just the tip of the iceberg…or as we might say, one “slice” of the pie! If this sounds like a brand you’d like to be a part of, consider joining our team!

The Analytics & Insights team has been a key contributor to driving Domino’s industry-leading sales growth by pairing strategic thought leadership with the latest analytic techniques in the fields of data science, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and primary research. The A&I team leverages their skills to drive outstanding consumer and employee experiences to fuel future growth.

As an Analyst on the Marketing Data Science team, you will be primarily responsible for driving continuous improvement in Domino’s customer experiences with our brand by providing rigorous analytics and testing insights to key leaders on the Marketing team. In this role, you will be uniquely positioned to influence the evolution of Domino’s marketing strategies by leveraging analytics to identify areas of growth for customer-facing programs throughout the entire marketing suite. To be successful as an Analyst on the Marketing Data Science team, you should have a demonstrated background blending large data, statistics, and consulting into actionable outcomes.

RESPONSIBILITIES AND DUTIES

Marketing Variate Testing (40%)

  • Provide optimal statistical test designs for measuring and reporting the effectiveness of various marketing tests
  • Provide feedback on test elements such as creative, offers, and audience optimization
  • Analyze tests and provide recommendations including reporting on conversion, ticket and profitability
  • Shape and guide strategic business objectives with disciplined analytic approach

Ad-hoc Analytics (40%)

  • Consult with Marketing partners to prioritize initiatives and identify key issues that will help address business questions
  • Proactively monitor and report on key test-health metrics
  • Develop strong partnerships with internal departments such as the data warehouse team, providing strategic direction that supports long-term goals and needs for the organization
  • Assist Domino's teams with custom views into customer behavior data
  • Identify, develop, and present recommendations for further improvements and testing

Client Service (20%)

  • Regularly present test background and findings to Marketing Leadership Team
  • Collaborate with and provide data-driven insights to the Marketing team on follow-up and ad-hoc requests
  • Partner with and manage external vendors when needed to address key business issues

  • Bachelor’s degree in Data Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Marketing Science, or related field
  • 1+ years of professional related work experience
  • Proficient in SQL
  • Proficient in R or Python
  • Applied experience with statistical computer languages (R, Python, etc.)
  • Excellent project management skills
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office – Fluent in Power Point and Proficient in Excel
  • Advanced knowledge of a web-analytics tool such as Webtrends Explore and Adobe Analytics; Knowledge of web basics such as HTML, JavaScript, and CSS a plus
