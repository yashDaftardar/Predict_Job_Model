Job Title: Senior Data Analyst - Verticals


What this position is about:

The Senior Data Analyst organizes and analyzes data from various sources to transform data into meaningful insights that influence decision making. This role will be in support of the product vertical teams and will inform data driven strategies across supply, catalogue, users and more. Within this role, the team member will use a range of tools and methodologies to generate results which highlight insights and expose opportunities for improvement. The team member must have high-level communication skills and an understanding for how their analyses will impact the business. The Senior Data Analyst must be able to demonstrate leadership amongst their peers and data solutions beyond normal quality assurance standards.


What you'll do:

  • Drive analytical decision making on a wide range of product vertical initiatives
  • Analyze data with standard statistical methods, interpret results, and summarize results
  • Effectively communicate and present results to teammates and business partners
  • Work closely with engineering, marketing, and product to create holistic analytic solutions
  • Provide relevant analysis to help senior leaders make data driven decisions

What you'll need:

  • Experience providing solutions and expose possible areas of opportunity
  • Lead end-to-end data analysis projects resulting in business improvements
  • Demonstrates leadership skills, teaching or mentoring peers
  • 3-5 years of applied experience in performing data analysis
  • Demonstrated excellent communication standards in person, email, and presentations
  • Advance knowledge using SQL to create and manipulate datasets from various sources
  • Skills in cleaning data and other quality assurance methodologies
  • Intermediate statistical knowledge and usage to drive business decisions
  • Experience using industry standard analysis tools (R, Python, etc.)
  • Demonstrated experience visualizing (Tableau, PowerBI etc.)
  • Preferred Bachelor's Degree in relevant STEM field (Engineering, Economics, Mathematics, Applied Statistics, Computer Science, Finance) or equivalent experience
