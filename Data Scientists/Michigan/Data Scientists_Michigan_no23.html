Job Summary

The Senior Data Analyst specializes in organizing and analyzing data from various sources with the purpose of turning numbers into information to influence decision making. They use a range of tools and methodologies, including statistics and technical expertise, to generate results that highlight valuable insights and expose areas of opportunities.

Responsibilities

  • Drive analytical decision making on a wide range of initiatives
  • Analyze data with standard statistical methods, interpret results, and provide written and oral summaries of data analyses
  • Effectively summarize and simplify analysis results that are shared with teammates and business partners through a wide variety of mediums
  • Collaborate with our IT peers to create holistic analytic solutions
  • Consult with business partners and communicate the needs back to our technology team
  • Provide relevant analysis to help fellow senior leaders make data-driven decisions
