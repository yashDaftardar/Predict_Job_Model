JOB SUMMARY FOR DATA SCIENTIST:

  • Work as an integral member within the Strategic Innovations and Analytics team to provide key data science component to support the development of retail strategy. Support Strategic Innovations and Analytics Manager by developing analytical solutions to improve business results.

RESPONSIBILITIES FOR DATA SCIENTIST:

  • * Solve complex problems and present expert advice to leadership in a clear and thoughtful way – with a focus on actionable solutions
  • * Leverage state-of-the art data science and predictive statistical methodologies to enhance retail performance
  • * Utilize data science / developer skillset to develop products and analytical toolsets with the goal of improving business results

REQUIRED EXPERIENCE FOR DATA SCIENTIST:

  • * Substantial professional experience using data science techniques to improve business results
  • * Technical proficiency using R, Python, and other analytical programming languages
  • * Ability to query large data sets using SQL or Spark; experience with cloud-based environments preferred
  • * Demonstrated experience using data visualization software such as Tableau or Power BI
  • * Working knowledge of statistical and machine learning techniques such as regression, time series forecasting, clustering, optimization, etc.
  • * Strong interpersonal skills, including the ability to communicate the business benefits of analytics
  • * Ability to apply multiple solutions to business problems
  • * Ability to quickly comprehend the functions and capabilities of new technologies

SKILLS AND QUALIFICATIONS FOR DATA SCIENTIST:

  • * 4 +years of experience using data to solve problems, answer questions, and create visualizations
  • * 4 +years of experience in data quality analysis and data architecture
  • * 4 +years of professional development experience
  • * 4 +years of experience in Big Data technologies in Hadoop/Hive/Spark platform using Python/Scala
  • * Master’s degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Operations Research, Economics, Physics, Computer Science or other STEM fields

BENEFITS

  • Medical, Dental, PTO, Holiday tailored to meet your needs. 401K/Match, $15,000 Life Insurance, Award-Winning Employee Care Program. Established and highly regarded reputation with Green Card and H1 processing.
