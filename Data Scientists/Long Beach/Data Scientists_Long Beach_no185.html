Description:

The Data Strategist is a key contributor to TA Digital’s Center of Excellence – Data that collaborates across internal and external stakeholders in designing, auditing and reporting services for our clients. Overseeing integrated analytics, strategies and supporting best practices as you guide & assess customers data driven behavior to implement solutions for our client’s brands and business objectives.



Responsibilities:

  • Contribute thought leadership to build Center of Excellence practice standards in Customer Data Platforms, Measurement Strategy, Auditing Assessments, and Reporting
  • Collaborate with Data team leadership to create documents & artifacts to use and present to prospects in pre-sales efforts.
  • Manage a team of analysts to drive actionable insight to the client
  • 5+ years of Adobe Analytics or Google Analytics experience (and 2+ years of experience in the other)
  • Leverage data and analytics to provide support to campaign, content or product ideation, design and implementation and delivering actionable insight to the client.
  • Work efficiently with each team member and 3rd party partners to define, extract, and synthesize available data and share results and insight.
  • Glean actionable insights to support in-market campaign optimization
  • Analyze scenarios to assess the before/after impacts of target market selection, media choice and allocation on client’s business performance
  • Strong Social listening skills: Using tools, Queries, Data cleaning etc.
  • Assist with developing and measuring marketing stimuli (A/B and multi-variant) test results in collaboration with brand planning, creative, media, and technology teams to build robust marketing and advertising strategies and in-market programs for clients.
  • Design & Manage integrated analytics strategy/approach across client brands to create measurement frameworks and scorecards that synthesize large amounts of data (attitudinal and behavioral) to accurately deliver on client’s business objectives.
  • Enhanced the reporting process internally through automation for data extraction and processing.
  • Paid/Owned/Earned measurement leveraging native tools and 3rd party tracking (Facebook Insights, Twitter Analytics, Site Analytics, Paid Display, Social Listening, etc.)
  • Understanding of website design/management: SEO/SEM strategies, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, etc
  • Compiled weekly, monthly, and quarterly digital media analysis reports, including revenue tracking, to support clients' digital media efforts with actionable recommendations for optimizing in-market work

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Math, Statistics, Economics, Business, Marketing or equivalent
  • At least 8 years of work experience in a professional services environment: management consulting, market research, advertising, or interactive/technology consulting and/or contracting with Enterprise Brands.
  • Experience with web analytics tools and social listening tools likes Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, Adobe Analytics, Tealium IQ, Adobe Launch, CDPs (e.g. Segment, Tealium Audience Stream, AEP)
  • Fluent in using Microsoft Excel for data manipulation, analysis, and charting. Experience automating routine Excel tasks using macros/VBA is a plus.
  • Experience in Tableau and / or PowerBI for reporting.
  • Package and present analytical findings and communicate (both written and verbal) complex concepts to Client teams and internal teams in an easy and understandable way.
  • Python skills would be a plus (Scrapping, clustering etc.)
