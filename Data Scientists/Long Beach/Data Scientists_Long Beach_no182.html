Full Job Description

Enki is looking for talented, motivated and passionate individuals to join our team.
As one of our interns, you will gain a comprehensive introduction to the business, while supporting the sales & marketing team.

The intern hired for this position will help create, identify and track sales leads, generate sales reporting, evaluate success, trends and failures of sales campaigns, assist with sales forecasting, strategies and tactics. You’ll be both contributing and gaining a lot of valuable sales and marketing experience.

This position is perfect for those that have an experience as a data scientist or data analyst before; and now want to add a new dimension to their skill set and experience in terms of working with clients and partners.

What are we looking for in an Intern?

  • Enrolled in an undergraduate or graduate degree, ideally technical or related to marketing, analytics or any other related areas.
  • Creative and innovative skills with regards to knowledge and awareness of industry trends and digital advances as they relate to new opportunities and business needs.
  • Highly organized, persuasive and good with numbers.
  • Ability to learn quickly and apply learning to problems or activities.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  • An organized team player who can manage multiple tasks.

You'll be:

  • Learning about our products, services and operations.
  • Setting and achieving targeted goals and objectives for business development and sales.
  • Helping with business development and partnership work as appropriate.
  • Writing specs to design and improve our sales and marketing materials.
  • Managing and updating the company database and customer relationship management systems (CRM).
  • Helping with the planning and hosting of marketing events.
  • Researching and evaluating competitor marketing and digital content
  • Completing other projects and tasks as appropriate.

Additional Information:

  • Compensation – Interns receive a salary of $10 to $15 per hour (depending on skill level)
  • Opportunity – Interns may be considered for full-time employment after graduation, or as soon as appropriate

IMPORTANT:
In your application, please explicitly mention why you think you'd be very effective in helping our sales & marketing team. What makes you believe you're a cut above the rest in these?

The intern chosen for this position will finish their internship at Enki having gained a broad experience in many aspects of sales and marketing.
