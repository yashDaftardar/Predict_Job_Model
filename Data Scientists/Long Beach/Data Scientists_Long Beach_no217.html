Open Gym Premier, Southern California's #1 basketball event operator, is hiring NOW!

Conceptualized in 2009 and launched in 2011, Open Gym Premier (OGP) evolved after a group of passionate former hoopers recognized a lack of quality basketball facilities, programs, events and coaching in the Orange County area. The idea for Open Gym Premier was developed based on the experiences of Matt Kanne, Miguel Bennett and the entire OGP staff, who carefully evaluated their own careers to help create the ultimate basketball experience. Since then, OGP has expanded to include volleyball programming and a one-of-a-kind facility experience.

Open Gym Premier provides camps, club teams, tournaments and training to dedicated basketball and volleyball players. One of fifteen Jr. NBA Flagship Network Members, OGP has earned a reputation for providing quality coaching and training while also delivering innovative, well-organized events to the basketball community. OGP has expanded significantly since inception and now operates club programs in Anaheim and Thousand Oaks, as well as a private 12-court facility in Anaheim. The event model now includes an elevated camp experience for elite players through the Elite Basketball Circuit and a full-service tournament platform through Grassroots 365. OGP continues to expand with programs and events for Orange County Basketball Tournaments across the West serving over 50,000 basketball players and 5,000 teams per year.

We are currently hiring for basketball scorekeepers and statisticians, with multiple openings in the Anaheim and Thousand Oaks areas.

The ideal candidate will proficiently, accurately, and without extensive training or supervision, be able to score keep or record player statistics for youth-high school basketball games.

Candidates for this position should be courteous, professional, and detail-oriented. Prior knowledge of basketball rules and regulations is a must-have. Candidates who meet the listed qualifications below are encouraged to apply.

Job Description

  • Arrive 15 minutes prior to start of shift to retrieve scoresheets and mandatory uniform
  • Scorekeepers will be expected to proficiently record game time, running score, fouls, and timeouts, and assist statisticians.
  • Statisticians will be expected to proficiently record individual player statistics (minutes, points, assists, blocks, etc.) on a computerized, tournament-specific platform
  • Prioritize customer service towards all clients
  • Cooperate with game officials and coaches to accurately and professionally execute responsibilities
  • Maintain working relationships with facility personnel and contracted event staff
  • Communicate all issues or potential issues to the Tournament Supervisor

Basic Qualifications

  • Minimum 1 year experience scorekeeping basketball games
  • Must be punctual, courteous, and professional
  • Knowledge of basketball rules- experience playing/coaching/scouting preferred
  • Ability to work for long hours with minimal supervision
