Full Job Description

Provide expertise in the algorithm generation and data evaluation using Machine Learning and Data Science.

Key Responsibilities:
  • Use complex modeling to generate algorithms to understand clinical data and patient characteristics
  • Generated and update algorithm for optimization of the clinical trial processes and procedures
  • Conduct ad hoc analysis and exploratory analysis
  • Test and train algorithm based on machine learning models in real time
  • Provide input in processing of structured and unstructured data
  • Identify opportunities to leverage various sources of data to drive enhanced solutions
  • Other incidental duties

Education and Experience:
Bachelor's Degree in in Data Science, Computer Science, Statistics, Biostatistics, Bioinformatics or related scientific field, and 6 years of experience including either industry or industry/education or
Master's Degree or equivalent in in Data Science, Computer Science, Statistics, Biostatistics, Bioinformatics or related scientific field, and 5 years of experience including either industry or industry/education or
Ph.D. or equivalent in Data Science, Computer Science, Statistics, Biostatistics, Bioinformatics or related scientific field, and 2 years of experience including either industry or industry/education

Additional Skills:
  • Extensive knowledge and understanding of principles, theories, and concepts relevant to Artificial Intelligence (AI) and/or Machine Learning model development, and/or Control Systems
  • Experience with machine learning techniques (e.g., clustering, decision tree learning, artificial neural networks, etc.)
  • Experience using statistical computer languages (e.g, R, Python, SQL, etc.) to manipulate large data sets
  • Experience in data visualization tools (i.e. Qlikview, Tableau, etc.)
  • Proven expertise in statistical techniques (e.g. distributions, statistical testing, regression, etc.)
  • Experience with using web services (e.g., Redshift, S3, Spark, etc.)
  • Proven expertise on MS Office Suite (e.g., Microsoft Office Excel, PowerPoint, Word, and Access)
  • Working knowledge in related tools and applications (e.g., Oracle, JDE, Concur, Salesforce.com) preferred
  • Excellent documentation and communication skills
  • Excellent interpersonal relationship skills including negotiating and relationship management skills
  • Represents organization in providing solutions to difficult technical issues associated with specific projects
  • Adhere to all EHS rules and requirements and take adequate control measures in preventing injuries to themselves and others as well as to the protection of environment and prevention of pollution under their span of influence/control
