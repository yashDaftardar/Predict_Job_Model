The role:

The company aspires to be data driven with its business strategy as well as in how decisions are made within the company. To realize this aspiration, the company seeks to make analytics delightfully easy and timely. The Big Data Systems Architecture leader will lead the creation of a new function and the design of the new modern data and analytics platform for the company, as well as the monitoring and governing of its implementation. Additionally, this professional will continually evaluate new technologies to ensure the data and analytics platform always remains leading-edge with the capabilities it offers to the company.


The platform will gather every piece of relevant data across different datatypes, character, video, image, sound, etc. from within and outside of the company, streaming them to the data and analytics platform. Thereafter the platform will have capabilities to ensure data is curated to raise quality and to integrate it, data is cataloged for every business use Globally to understand data inventory, data is prepared by any person in the company regardless of technical skill for data science, artificial intelligence, or simply to answer a hunch, data is warehoused to ensure a sanctioned and trusted analytical repository is created for anyone in the company to gain quality insights. A machine, such as a chatbot, can directly identify, consume and deposit information from/into the Platform.


This role will work very closely with the Enterprise Architecture, Data Warehouse and Analytics Team, Artificial Intelligence Team, Data Preparation Team, Master Data Management Team, Data Quality Team, Data Architecture Team, Security and Privacy Teams, Business Teams, to ensure these partners’ needs of the platform are defined, designed and delivered.


This role report to the VP, Global Data, Analytics and Artificial Intelligence.


How you would contribute:

  • First Member of the Big Data Systems Architecture Team
  • Development of Big Data Systems Architecture Function vision, scope, roadmap to operationalize it by partnering with key business and technology owners
  • Development and adoption of the System Architecture policies, standards, and procedures
  • Collaborating with all partners to understand their requirements
  • Design System Architecture Blueprint across each of the Platform’s Zones: Ingest, Staging, Core, Catalog, Preparation, Warehousing, API, and other Zones as deemed vital, ensuring delightful end user experience, ease of operating the platform, granular User Data Access rights, data lineage, data security and privacy are accounted for.
  • Identify prospective technologies within each of the Zones within the Platform, benchmark them and select, analyze system bottlenecks, and propose solutions to eliminate them
  • Design and participate in developing the Operating Model for the Platform.
  • Document use cases, solutions, and recommendations.
  • Requirements analysis, the platform selection, technical architecture design, design of the application and development, testing, and deployment of the proposed solution.
  • Help program and project managers in the design, planning and governance of implementing Big Data Platform projects;
  • Perform detailed analysis of business problems and technical environments and use this in designing the solution;
  • Ensures effective staff training, interviews candidates for employment, evaluates staff performance and recommends hiring, promotions, salary actions
  • Establishes issue management and resolution processes
  • Maintain relationships with vendor and guiding partners to meet data architecture objectives/OKRs
  • Establish Systems Architecture objectives/OKRs, critical metrics and benchmarks
  • Work with business, data owners and system owners to create RACI model to align on “Big Data System Architecture Roles and Responsibilities” through data lifecycle
  • Perform additional duties as assigned

What’s special about the team:

You will be joining a newly formed global data, analytics and artificial intelligence team that is rapidly transforming to be able to deliver outsized business outcomes for the global corporation.
