Full Job Description

Data Scientist
If you are a Data Scientist with experience, please read on!

Our team is a revolutionary financial tech company using the power of block chain to help make identity and financial information more secure. Our product allows lenders to securely store, verify, and work with consumer information while avoiding fraud and breaches by transforming the data into cryptographically stored tokens within our network. Our team consists of experts in their field including our director of research who has over 35 years of experience in blockchains, payment systems, IoT, smart grid, and digital rights management to name a few.

What You Will Be Doing

In this role you will work with our data science workstreams, work on machine learning implementation, and provide technical guidance. Additionally you will be performing heavy credit risk modeling while also working to build a scalable and automated data pipeline. This will be done by working closely with product managers and engineers to help define our strategy and roadmap requirements.

What You Need for this Position

  • Risk Analysis
  • Python
  • SQL
  • Machine Learning
  • Random Forest
  • Financial Services
  • SPARK
