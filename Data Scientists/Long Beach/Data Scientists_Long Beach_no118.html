Role Summary

In this position you will use your analytical, statistical and programming skills to analyze and interpret large data sets for the demand planning organization to develop data-driven solutions to service parts forecasting. You will support our demand planning team with insights that will support data and process optimization in order to test different courses of action (what-if scenario planning). You must have strong experience using a variety of data mining and data analysis methods and tools. You must also have experience building and implementing models through the creation of algorithms and running simulations to support current and future business needs. Must be comfortable working and collaborating with stakeholders.


Responsibilities

  • Identify data sources required for service parts demand planning and automate collection processes

  • Support preprocessing of structured and unstructured data

  • Analyze large amounts of information to discover trends and patterns.

  • Build predictive models and machine-learning algorithms to improve part level forecasting accuracy

  • Present information using data visualization techniques; make recommendations on path forward

  • Assess effectiveness and accuracy of new data sources and data gathering techniques

  • Develop testing framework and model quality

  • Develop tools and processes to monitor and analyze model performance and data accuracy


Qualifications

  • 5+ years of experience in statistical modeling, programming, and manipulating large data sets

  • Masters in Statistics, Mathematics, or Computer Science – PHD a plus

  • Experience working with and creating data architectures

  • Knowledge of machine learning techniques (regression, simulation, scenario analysis, neural networks, decision tree learning, etc..) and pro/cons on each application and approach

  • Excellent problem-solving skills with emphasis on new part launch

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills for cross-functional collaboration

  • Strong collaborator, with willingness to drive change

  • Coding knowledge and experience with several languages: C, C++, Java

  • An ability to tell a story using data including visualization experience (Tableau, Microsoft PowerBI)

  • Experience using statistical computer languages and orchestration tools (R, Python, SQL, Alteryx, Apache Spark, Kubernetes, Docker, etc.) to manipulate data and support trend analysis from large data sets

  • Experience using agile methodologies - JIRA or Confluence experience a plus

  • Strong analytical and structured problem-solving capabilities with a focus on root cause investigations

  • Preferred expertise in spare parts forecasting, supply chain theories, and target service levels

  • Knowledge of Channel/Service network forecast desegregation (a plus)

  • Understanding and experience in implementing lean project initiatives
