About DRINKS
DRINKS is the online leader in adult beverage, reinventing wine buying for the 21st century. Through its pioneering Wine as a Service (WaaS) platform and Direct to Consumer (DTC) properties, DRINKS brings thousands of award-winning wines safely and conveniently to millions of customers across the United States in 1-2 days.

DRINKS created a new way to build a direct relationship with consumers by opening up the beverage alcohol category for online shopping. The company’s WaaS platform allows any company or brand to launch its own ship-to-home wine program quickly and easily, enabling our partners — including Fortune 20 and industry-leading digital marketers — to reach 96% of adults in the U.S. The company’s DTC division, featuring storefronts Wine Insiders and Martha Stewart Wine Co., is the nation’s largest online wine marketplace by volume.

DRINKS powers this unique ecosystem with its patented enterprise technology, including the ability to customize merchandising and deliver unique recommendations and messaging for each shopper.

We are proud to work with top influencers and tastemakers coupled with high-quality wine producers from around the world. DRINKS’ data-driven wine sourcing engine delivers exceptional unit economics for WaaS partners, with private label packaging designs informed by machine learning and optimized for online consumer appeal.

Forbes named DRINKS America’s 18th best startup employer in 2020.

The Role
The data science team is looking for an individual with a passion for discovery, innovation, and data science to help map out, model and leverage the complex relationships between wine, customer behavior, and wine labels, to enrich the products and services DRINKS provides. As Senior Data Scientist, you will develop and apply data science techniques to find solutions to problems ranging from machine learning, computer vision, recommendation systems to natural language understanding. You’ll work collaboratively with business partners across the organization to understand business problems, develop iterative solutions, and formulate actionable insights. This role will report to the Senior Director of Data Science and is a regular, full-time, exempt position based out of our Los Angeles, CA office.

What You’ll Do
  • Perform large-scale data science methodology to extract useful business insights and unlock new strategies and optimizations
  • Translate analyses to data stories, identify actionable solutions and recommendations to influence the direction of the business by communicating results to cross-functional groups
  • Gather and define business requirements for data science tools and models and work with internal partners to ensure pipeline for future business analyses
  • Establish best practices and provide guidance to global counterparts
  • Collaborate with our data engineers, business intelligence and engineering team to innovate potential data science products and solutions
  • Design, develop, deploy and iterate machine learning models and statistical analytics such as regression, classification, clustering, time series, optimization
  • Translate data and information into impactful insights
  • Develop unsupervised clustering and learned representations of wines and customer behavior
  • Identify, propose and ship models, analysis, recommendations and products/services
  • Communicate deep analysis and insights to all levels of the business

About You
  • Master’s degree or Ph.D. in a quantitative field such as Math, Statistics, Computer Science, Engineering or equivalent years of experience
  • 3+ years of experience doing data science projects and quantitative analysis within a fast-paced technology company
  • Expertise in either topic modeling and natural language processing (NLP/NLU) or computer vision and image processing
  • Proficiency developing and operationalizing machine learning solutions using Scikit-Learn, mlpy, MICE, Caret, PyTorch, Keras, or TensorFlow
  • Proficiency with programming languages such as R or Python
  • Experience of pulling, cleaning, and analyzing big data using tools such as SQL, NoSQL, PySpark, graph-based or Key/Value stores
  • Experience of building and maintaining operational models processing large quantity of data in real-world production environments such as SageMaker, AzureML or Kubernetes

Bonus points for
  • Experience building deep learning or transformer models
