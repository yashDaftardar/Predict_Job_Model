Job description

Job Summary:
Reporting to the Manager, Business Intelligence & Data, the Data Architect defines all aspects of data management, including storage, consumption access, governance and integrity and establishes, promotes and trains a network of key users that are data fluent with a culture of self-reliant, quick and effective data-driven decision support.
Summary of Duties:


    • Inventories all data content (External, sensors, factory, product, business and design)
    • Gathers and interprets data expectations from internal stakeholders
    • Defines and implements Data Architecture that responds to expectations
    • Works with Data specialists to implement data flows and data flows lifecycle
    • Defines how data is processed, stored, and utilized
    • Provides criteria for data processing operations
    • Establishes data flows across systems for data integration and archiving purposes
    • Owns, publishes and validates all reference data
    • Establishes and updates a Business Data Framework in coordination with key Business Stakeholders
    • Designs and implements security in a manner that enables effective and governed access to data

Job requirements

Education and Experience: Bachelor's degree, or equivalent, in computer science plus 8 years' experience in Data Science or related field; or 10 years' related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience.

Computer Skills:


    • Database Administration
    • Data Structures
    • Business systems design
    • Management and manipulation of unstructured data

Other Skills:


    • Data Quality
    • Data Governance
    • Cloud and Hybrid architecture
    • Master and Business-Referential data management
    • Data Exchange and processing (one or more of the following: EDI, API, ETL, MapReduce, Pig, Spark, Storm, NoSQL, MongoBD, etc)

Additional Preferred Skills:


    • Experience with presentation of insights to business or functional users at all/any level of the organization
    • Data science and advanced Analytics, Prescriptive Analytics, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning
    • Ability to effectively communicate in French

Description: Senior-level position within field
