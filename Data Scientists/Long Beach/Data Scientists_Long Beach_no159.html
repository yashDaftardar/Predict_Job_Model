The COAGEN Independent Research & Development (IRAD) team is looking for an Artificial Intelligence (AI) Systems Architect to perform algorithm development, systems engineering or SW development in support of AI and automation concepts exploration and analysis for future systems. The ideal candidate will have a technical breadth and depth in the field of Artificial Intelligence to aid in the successful development of discriminating technologies critical to Northrop's advanced capabilities group.

Basic Qualifications:
  • Bachelor's Degree with 2 years of related experience; or an additional 4 years of experience may be considered in lieu of a degree
  • Proficiency in one or more of the following: Python, C++, Java, C, or C#.
  • Current SCI access or a minimum of a DoD Top Secret clearance with the ability to obtain SCI access by start date
Preferred Qualifications:
  • Strong mathematics background and analytical skills.
  • Experience with Python.
  • Coding and design of algorithmic applications
  • Ability to collaborate with subject matter experts (SMEs)
  • Excellent communication, interpersonal skills, with the ability to interface with all levels of employees and management
