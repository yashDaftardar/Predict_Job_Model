Full Job Description

We’re looking for problem solvers, innovators, and dreamers who are searching for anything but business as usual. Like us, you’re a high performer who’s an expert at your craft, constantly challenging the status quo. You value inclusivity and want to join a culture that empowers you to show up as your authentic self. You know that success hinges on commitment, that our differences make us stronger, and that the finish line is always sweeter when the whole team crosses together.

We are excited to be adding to our expanding Data Science Team with the addition of a Staff Data Scientist position, accelerating our efforts to push the boundaries on data science process & model development for Alteryx. In this position, you will function as the technical lead for our internal Data Science efforts, leveraging your experience and expertise to build data science processes and models to revolutionize the way Alteryx interacts with our customers and goes to market. You will also lead our efforts around process and model management, ensuring the team leverages a consistent framework to develop, implement and maintain the solutions we develop with the business. As part of the role, you will work closely with our Data Engineering, Infrastructure and Governance teams to ensure our Data Science products are high-quality and robust end-to-end, from the data used in the model to the implementation & management strategy.

This is an exciting opportunity to help drive continued adoption of data science techniques throughout Alteryx while delivering exceptional value & insights for the company.

In addition, the team will be working to drive our Alteryx for Good mission, helping schools and citizen data scientists around the world become more data literate, more capable of answering questions, and more able to solve problems. It is a meaningful and exciting role to be in!

Responsibilities

  • Technical Lead : Leverage deep technical knowledge and expertise to quickly build MVP solutions and work across the broader Data Science team to drive the solution towards production. Function as technical lead on key Data Science initiatives, helping the team solution and deliver on timely, relevant and value-oriented business solutions

  • Data Science Best Practices: Lead team efforts on establishing an operating framework for data science product development, from data products & feature development to model implementation & management

  • Process & Model Management: Establish and lead team practices around process/model development & management, ensuring analytics/models are developed using common tools and are maintained/updated on consistent basis

  • Thought-Leader: Keep the team up to date on the latest Data Science, coding, and process best practices and techniques, helping the team to consistently push the boundaries of what is possible

  • Coach & Mentor : Upskill newer data scientists on team through 1:1 interactions and lead regular knowledge shares/lunch & learns on the application of novel techniques for Alteryx business problems

  • Creativity: Leverage & implement a wide array of statistical, econometric, ML and advanced analytic methods to drive business insights and support data-driven decisions, thinking ‘outside-the-box’ on the implementation and execution of these methods

  • Business Savy : Constant & persistent drive to assess and improve business processes and practices through the application of data science techniques

  • Customer-Centric: Always put customer needs first by delivering quick, agile and thoughtful analytics while continuing to find opportunities to provide incremental insights and capabilities to our partners

Qualifications

  • Graduate Degree in Statistics, Economics, Econometrics, Physics, Data Science or other Quantitative Discipline a plus

  • 5-10 years' relevant professional experience leveraging data & analytic techniques with direct business application (Prediction, Forecasting, Inference, Recommendation Engines, etc.).

  • Proven experience leading projects or small teams through end-to-end development of a Data Science Product (Problem Formulation -> Deployment, Maintenance & Management)

  • Experience in developing technical culture and capabilities within modern data science organization, mentoring & coaching team members on projects & best practices

  • Experience driving & executing data science process & model governance best practices while maintaining an agile, customer-centric focus

  • Experience with quantifying and measuring the impact and value of analytic process and models to the business

  • Expert in Alteryx, Python, R or other Data Science oriented languages

  • Ability to interface directly with customers to independently solve problems and facilitate solution implementations

  • Experience working in a fast-paced culture, managing multiple deliverables a plus

Bonus

  • Experience in Sales and/or Marketing Analytics within Software Industry

  • Solution Development/Delivery Experience

  • Experience with AWS or Other Cloud Platforms

Compensation:
Alteryx is committed to fair and equitable compensation practices. The salary range for this role in Irvine is $128,700 - $218,800. This position is also remote-friendly and, as such, compensation will ultimately be in line with the location in which the position is filled. Final compensation for this role will be determined by various factors such as a candidate’s relevant work experience, skills, certifications, and geographic location.

This role is eligible for variable compensation including bonus and stock grants.
