Responsibilities:

We are looking to Hire a Conversational AI / Chatbot Engineer to join our Digital Process Automation group.

Responsibilities:
This role will be responsible for integrating the following technologies and Services to enable natural language interaction between customers with Voice Virtual Agent, instead of IVR -
  • Google DialogFlow , Kore.ai any conversation assistant frameworks.
  • Integration with IVR products like Genesys Intelligent Automation engine
  • Google Integrated Speech Services - Speech-To-Text ("STT")/ Text-To-Speech ("TTS") Services

And will be responsible -

  • Development of Service Interfaces and Bot Environment
  • Deployment of GIA Service Orchestration Engine and Google AI APIs
  • Intent to Utterance Mapping
  • Design and Configure Dialog Component
  • Speech Services Tuning
  • Ground Truth Creation
  • Iterative Teach, Test and Calibration
  • Configure intents, entities and utterances for conversations
  • Establish the business logic within the use cases and connect it to Genesys Intelligent Automation
  • Design integration touchpoints in accordance to GCP speech and Natural language Understanding (NLU) Artificial Intelligence (AI) APIs;
  • Design and configure the Dialog Component in Google DialogFlow. Manage conversational flow by providing unique answer responses to each question intents related to the use cases.
  • Define the architecture to address functional, non-functional requirements and pain points; and to implement different components of architecture on Google Cloud Platform (GCP) and Genesys Intelligent Automation (GIA) on premise contact center platform.
  • It will also address a subset of questions that require disambiguation or context/entity information to determine the question intent to answer appropriately
  • Identification of Intents and Entities on the basis of priority use cases, Data collection, Identification, Cleaning and labeling of the Intents and Entities, and further training of Machine Learning Models in GDF to Identify the concerned Intent and Entity utterances
