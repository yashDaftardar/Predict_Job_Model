JOB
Under the general direction of the Health Commissioner, this employee directs, manages, supervises and coordinates the activities and operations of the Center of Health Information, Planning and Research and Health Department Information Technology. This employee functions in and administrative capacity carrying out special projects as assigned by the Health Commissioner. This employee will assist with key departmental functions, including coordinating assigned activities with other divisions, departments and outside agencies. Provides highly responsible and complex administrative support to the Health Commissioner.
