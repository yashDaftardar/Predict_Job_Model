Full Job Description

Thank you for considering a career at Bon Secours Mercy Health!

The Data Engineer will work closely with a multidisciplinary Agile team to build high quality data pipelines driving analytic solutions. These solutions will generate insights from the organization's connected data, enabling the organization to advance the data-driven decision-making capabilities of the organization's enterprise. This role requires deep understanding of data engineering, data analysis, reporting, and a basic understanding of data science techniques and workflows.

Essential Job Functions

Solve complex data problems to deliver insights that helps our ministry achieve our goals

Understands modeling process, major groups of algorithmic techniques, and model testing techniques

Learn about machine learning, data science, computer vision, artificial intelligence, statistics, and/or applied mathematics

Partner with business analysts and solutions architects to develop technical architectures for strategic enterprise projects and initiatives.

Understand platforms available and how they are used (e.g., Azure, Hadoop, Bluemix)

Perform tasks related to data ETL, data structuring and cleaning, and data pipeline creation

Understand rules on data, privacy and security, and can ensure quality of data

Integrate data from multiple sources
