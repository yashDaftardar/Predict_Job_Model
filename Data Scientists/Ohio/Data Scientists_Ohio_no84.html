Job Summary

Express has an exciting opportunity for a leading-edge role in Decision Science & Analytics. This advanced technical role is for a Senior Data Scientist who will contribute high visibility and high impact machine learning models in a collaborative, data-centric environment. This senior level self-starter will contribute to and create ML models spanning an entire retail environment including Planning & Allocation, Product Pricing, Customer Data, Marketing, eCommerce, and Store Operations. The Senior Data Scientist will coach and mentor junior team members in best practices, standards, and model development. The successful candidate will manage ambiguity and have the unique ability to identify business opportunities and conceptualize them independently as an analytics puzzle. This results-driven senior contributor will enjoy solving puzzles, understanding and explaining anomalies, and identifying and leveraging complex patterns in data.

Responsibilities

Key duties include:

  • The Decision Science Analytics team is responsible for providing high-quality analytical, predictive and machine learning work in a fast-paced and fluid environment
  • The team’s purpose is to create and deliver value by understanding the behaviors and needs of our customers, maximizing the return on marketing spend, and optimizing operational efficiency in the areas including but not limited to marketing, customer contact optimization, merchandising planning and allocation and inventory optimization
  • The team’s primary focus areas are predictive, prescriptive, and cognitive analytics, and we overlap with and enable descriptive and diagnostic analytics
  • Most work is in open-source data environments (Hadoop, MapReduce, Structured and Unstructured data contents) using mostly open-source data engineering and data science tools
  • This team works cross-functionally across the business, as well as with outside vendors
  • Analytics work encompasses building predictive models, model testing and optimization, forecasting using advanced analytics and machine learning as well as ad-hoc analytics projects
  • This individual will primarily be involved in projects that require decision science methodologies such as predictive modeling, statistical analysis, customer segmentations, store clustering, marketing channel attribution and mix optimization, demand forecasting, recommendation engines, predictive and prescriptive modeling, etc.

Essential Functions

· Advanced analytics on data: data preparation/cleaning/insights including and not limited to Google BigQuery, Teradata, Tableau, MS Excel

· Identifying pertinent data, determining appropriate data source(s), partnering with Enterprise Data Engineer partners to locate and access data

· Contributing to the vision and tactical approach to identifying, establishing, and leveraging ML models across business units

· Building and testing ML models

· Coaching and training junior associates on best practices and ML model development

· Analysis and debugging of model and data anomalies

· Creation of supporting documentation for models including execution playbooks and model design documents

· Demonstrate innovation, initiative, and curiosity through machine learning R&D

· Research and identify latest ML techniques and tools

· Constantly push the envelope to maintain leading edge technical acumen across all members of the team

· Communication across all levels of the organization: individual contributor to C-level executives

· Technical partnership and influence to prioritize access to critical data for ML modeling

· Business partnership and influence to identify and prioritize opportunities to drive value through ML models

· Executive presentation to explain, hindsight, and forecast ML models, tests, and new opportunities

Other essential functions may occur as directed by your supervisor

Essential Qualifications

  • Education: Advanced/Master's Degree in Data Science, Computer Science, Software Engineering, Economics, Statistics, Applied Math, or other quantitative disciplines; Bachelor’s degree with more than 5 years of experience is considered
