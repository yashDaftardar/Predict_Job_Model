Description
Position Summary
At Bath & Body Works we have a robust customer data warehouse and need an expert to help launch the data science function for the digital space in marketing. We need someone experienced in the application of data and predictive analytics to understand the potency of different points in the customer journey to identify conversion drivers and optimize spend.
Responsibilities

  • Be the trailblazer to help us evolve our tools to construct a rigorous data science practice
  • Lead the organization in developing dynamic multi-touch attribution models that inform marketers of the most opportune time for engagement and best type of engagement (content, product, promotion)
  • Construct analyses from multiple data sources including the internal data warehouse, digital analytics, transactional data and external marketing partners
  • Sustain rapid iteration of analysis and predictive models based on shifts in customer behavior and industry trends
Qualifications
  • Able to disseminate complex analyses for a broad audience
  • Thrives in a balance of collaboration and heads down work
  • Self-directed, innovative thinker who tethers the results of their work to driving conversion
  • Graduate degree in a quantitative field
  • Role can be done remotely or relocation assistance provided

Experience

  • 3+ years in marketing data science
  • Knowledge of digital landscape
  • Experience working with relational databases preferred (Teradata, Oracle)
  • Expertise in SQL, Python and/or R
