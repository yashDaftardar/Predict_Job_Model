Legal Department Description:

The Legal Department of JPMorgan Chase & Co is responsible for the provision of legal services to the Corporation. The department also includes the Office of the Secretary.

Legal is organized by Practice Groups that conform to the organization of the Firm's lines of business (LOBs) and corporate staff areas, allowing the legal staff to work closely with specific groups on a daily basis. Each line of business has an LOB General Counsel, who is the Practice Group Head and who reports to the Firm's General Counsel. This structure provides the means for the legal teams to communicate legal and regulatory changes to the businesses and staff areas, and for the LOBs and staff areas to communicate business developments that have legal or regulatory implications to the legal teams in a timely manner.

Office of the General Counsel Description:

The Office of the General Counsel helps attorneys make the best use of their time by providing centralized services and tools to i) develop professional skills, ii) manage projects and achieve business objectives, iii) optimize processes and technology, and iv) engage and connect employees. The OGC helps the Legal Management Team establish and execute the department's objectives by driving and supporting initiatives to i) implement firm-wide standards and governance, ii) provide financial discipline and risk controls, iii) guide the people agenda and iv) drive best practices.

Legal Chief Data Office Description:

The Legal Chief Data Officeenables better business intelligence and decisions through data that is accessible, trusted and understood. This team is responsible for the data governance program for Legal and partners with Technology and other groups within the OCG to deliver business-friendly solutions to make data-driven decisions.

Position Description:

As AVP for the Artificial Intelligence Program in the Legal Chief Data Office team, you will help lead the creation and management of AI driven solutions across applications and functions in Legal. You will be accountable for defining and delivering the product roadmap and cross-roadmap alignment with other applications' Product Owners .

You will have the opportunity to help build our AI strategy by working with our stakeholders to identify and define the problems and become an expert in AI and in our business domain .

Responsibilities:

  • Work with Attorneys, Business and Operations stakeholders to define the problems that can be solved using with AI. Help prioritize and rank the solutions, develop business cases.
  • Develop AI product roadmaps and timelines.
  • Understand the Data Landscape in Legal. Perform gap analysis on existing data. Identify new data sources.
  • Help develop training, validation and test data sets.
  • Help stakeholders productionize AI products
  • Analyze business, operational, end-user and non-functional requirements
  • Conducts end-to-end process analysis, documents data flows, identifies pain points and documents data mapping as needed.
  • Partners closely with Data Quality Team and Lead Development engineers in sharing requirements and goals, reviewing stories/story pointing, and throughout the development process
