Marketing Data Analyst (Job Family, Level I, II, Senior, Lead)

- (2100202)


Reviews project life cycle from segmentation, modeling, audience selection, testing strategies, execution and results reporting. Provides analysis to drive marketing decisions and analyze results. Provides marketing analysis and reporting processes associated with individual health insurance media and marketing channels (offline and online).

Responsibilities

  • Conducts marketing analysis and develops reporting processes
  • Focuses on developing and executing repeatable processes to gauge campaign success and affect marketing decisions
  • Owns responsibility for operation and configuration of web analytics tools in support of campaign tracking and execution; interfaces with IT as needed to maintain and improve tool capabilities.
  • Assists in the development of segmentation, modeling, targeting and testing capabilities.
  • Handles ad hoc campaign execution and reporting
  • Prepares monthly reporting and coordinates forecasting activity across marketing channels
  • Presents project and campaign results, including discussions of key results and metrics, to appropriate audiences
  • Performs other duties as assigned.

Qualifications

Marketing Data Analyst I

Education and Experience:

  • Bachelor’s degree in quantitative or statistical discipline, or related field.
  • 1 year marketing analytics experience/project work, which preferably includes exposure to SAS, SQL, Database.

Technical Skills and Knowledge:

  • Knowledge of linear and non-linear regression, factor analysis, decision trees, time series analysis, and marketing mix modeling.
  • Intermediate to advanced Microsoft Office (including Access) skills.

Marketing Data Analyst II

Education and Experience:

  • Bachelor’s degree in quantitative or statistical discipline or related field.
  • 3 years experience as a Marketing Data Analyst or equivalent relevant experience in marketing statistical analysis

Technical Skills and Knowledge:

  • Knowledge of decision trees, data mining, time series analysis, and marketing mix modeling
  • Intermediate to advanced Microsoft Office skills
  • Advanced database reporting skills
  • High level knowledge and experience with SAS
  • Demonstrated SQL knowledge
  • Basic model development skills

Senior Marketing Data Analyst:

Education and Experience:

  • Bachelor’s degree in quantitative or statistical discipline or related field. Advanced degree preferred.
  • 5 years of experience as a Marketing Data Analyst or equivalent experience with marketing statistical analysis, data mining, segmentation and modeling.

Professional Certification(s):

  • SAS, SPSS, R, or equivalent statistical analysis tool. SAS preferred.

Technical Skills and Knowledge:

  • Extensive knowledge of linear and non-linear regression, factor analysis, decision trees, data mining, time series analysis, and marketing mix modeling and the ability to apply advanced concepts
  • Advanced skills with SAS or equivalent statistical analysis tool
  • Advanced skills with SQL; familiarity with large, complex relational databases
  • Intermediate to advanced data visualization software (e.g. Tableau) skills
  • Advanced model development skills.

Lead Marketing Data Analyst

Education and Experience:

  • Bachelor’s degree in quantitative or statistical discipline or related field. Advanced degree preferred.
  • 7 years of progressive experience as a Marketing Data Analyst or equivalent experience with marketing statistical analysis, data mining, segmentation and modeling
  • Experience with data visualization software (e.g. Tableau)

Professional Certification(s):

  • SAS, SPSS, R, or equivalent statistical analysis tool required. SAS preferred.

Technical Skills and Knowledge:

  • Extensive knowledge of linear and non-linear regression, factor analysis, decision trees, data mining, time series analysis, and marketing mix modeling and the ability to apply advanced concepts
  • Advanced skills with SAS or equivalent statistical analysis tool
  • Advanced skills with SQL; familiarity with large, complex relational databases
  • Advanced data visualization software (e.g. Tableau) skills
  • Advanced model development skills
