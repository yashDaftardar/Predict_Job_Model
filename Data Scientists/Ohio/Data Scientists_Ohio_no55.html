Start your journey with us
The Predictive Analytics department has an internship opportunity in our corporate headquarters located in Fairfield, Ohio. We are looking for a highly talented graduate student who is interested in learning new things and is capable of contributing in a dynamic and broadly skilled modeling/actuarial department. This is an opportunity to work with experienced professionals.

Be ready to:

This role will be tailored to the abilities and experience of the intern that we hire and to the time constraints imposed by the internship schedule. The 6-8 week internship period can be flexible regarding time of year and days of work (with a minimum of three days, or 24 hours, of work per week). It will involve exposure to several different models and modeling applications that will likely include some or all of the following:

  • assisting with the development and maintenance of multivariate pricing and underwriting models for personal and business insurance

  • assisting with the development and maintenance of claim analytic models

  • conducting research and development leading to potentially useful predictive models in the future

  • interacting with actuaries and company management

  • interacting with several departments within the company, including Commercial Lines, Personal Lines, Claims and IT


QUALIFICATIONS
Be equipped with:

  • some experience with Excel or other spreadsheet software
  • experience with SAS, R and/or Python
  • strong work ethic and commitment to learning new things
  • strong communication, interpersonal and organizational skills
You've earned:

  • bachelor’s degree in mathematics, statistics, economics, physics, engineering, actuarial science or some other quantitative field of study
  • current enrollment in an advanced degree (PhD strongly preferred) program in a quantitative field of study
