Data Scientist

Cincinnati, Ohio 45242 (remote options)

Direct Hire


Description:

Ascendum Solutions is looking for a curious, motivated, and forward-thinking Data Scientist, to work on some of the most challenging and relevant issues across businesses. If you are someone with a strong quantitative and statistical background, with experience in machine learning, artificial intelligence, statistical modeling, data visualization, and data analysis, this role might fit you perfectly!


Job Responsibilities

Responsibilities include but not limited to

  • Effectively communicate and translate business strategy and goals into manageable problems with well-defined measurable objectives and outcomes.
  • Transform formulated problems into implementation plans for experiments by developing/identifying data sources, applying/creating the appropriate methods, algorithms, and tools, as well as delivering statistically valid and reliable results.
  • Conduct advanced analysis employing predictive modeling, machine learning, optimization, and other techniques to deliver insights or develop analytical solutions to achieve business objectives.
  • Work independently and collaborate with other groups to solve complex problems
  • Develop and manage algorithms that generate actionable insights and programs to improve long-term revenue of the business.
  • Understand the needs of the business and formulate, communicate, and create buy-in for analytics approaches and solutions.
  • Ad hoc reporting and analytics to support requests from the Bank's various lines of business.

Required Skills:

Candidate must have

  • +3 years of experience in predictive modeling, data science, and analysis
  • Experience building and deploying ML models or hands on experience developing deep learning models
  • +2 years of experience with Python (and libraries like NLTK, Spacy, Tensorflow, Keras, etc.) with documentation for reproducibility
  • Database and analytical experience required.
  • Critical thinker and problem-solving skills
  • Team player, good time-management skills
  • Great interpersonal and communication skills.

Additional Preferred Skills:

  • Linguistics or Statistics is preferred
  • SQL
  • Data Analytics
  • Data Engineering
  • Machine learning
  • Data Visualization
  • Data Modeling
