Data Scientist Lead
Job Number : 173690


As a data scientist on our Media group, you’ll develop models ranging from customer lifetime value targeting to identifying optimal media mix, all with the goal of attracting quoters and customers as efficiently as possible with our $2B media budget. Your focus will be to identify tools and techniques which can be leveraged by analysts in the media team for analyzing large data. As the granularity of our data has expanded and we have begun storing terabytes of structured data in the AWS cloud, our analytical tools must evolve to keep up. This is the first data science position in media, so you’ll have a significant potential for growth.

Must-have qualifications

  • Master's degree or higher with quantitative focus in Econometrics, Statistics, Operations Research, Computer Science or related field (e.g. Mathematics) and a minimum of 1 year of relevant experience in Statistical/Quantitative Modeling and/or Machine Learning tools (R, Python, etc) and in using various database tools (e.g. Hadoop, SQL) processing large volumes of structured and unstructured data.
  • In lieu of the above, Bachelor's degree with quantitative focus in Econometrics, Statistics, Operations Research, Computer Science or related field (e.g. Mathematics) and 2 years of relevant experience in Statistical/Quantitative Modeling and/or Machine Learning tools (R, Python, etc) and in using various database tools (e.g. Hadoop, SQL) processing large volumes of structured and unstructured data.
  • In lieu of a degree, a minimum of 7 years of relevant experience in Statistical/Quantitative Modeling and/or Machine Learning tools (R, Python, etc) and in using various database tools (e.g. Hadoop, SQL) processing large volumes of structured and unstructured data.

Preferred skills

  • Demonstrated skills in data mining, machine learning, programming language (Python, R and SQL), AWS and dynamic optimization
  • Experience with querying databases and apply advanced data manipulation techniques across multiple sources
  • Ability to perform in an ambiguous environment and to work with multiple stakeholders from various business units
  • Proficient in presenting data and analysis results in a simple and clear manner. Also, ability to simplify analyses for complicated business problems
  • Ability to identify and learn applicable new techniques independently as needed

  • Master Level: $101,200 - $144,600/year
  • Gainshare bonus up to 40% of your eligible earnings based on company performance

  • Paid & unpaid sick leave where applicable, as well as short & long-term disability
  • Parental & family leave; military leave & pay
  • Diverse, inclusive & welcoming culture with Employee Resource Groups
  • Career development & tuition assistance
  • Onsite gym & healthcare at large locations
