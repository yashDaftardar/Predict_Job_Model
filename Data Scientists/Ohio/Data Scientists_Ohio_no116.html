The Supply Chain Senior Data Analyst is responsible for tracking, reporting and analyzing the performance of supply chain activities (Field Service, Inventory Management, Scheduling & Capacity, Manufacturing) as it relates to standard operational activations as well as ad hoc requests. This role will lead supply chain data analysis using statistical programs, predictive analytics and other data-driven tools. This role will bring supply chain analytics best practices, staying up to date on advances in supply chain analytics in order to provide standard reporting/score-carding for all supply chain KPIs. They will use analytical skills to monitor and forecast supply chain trends; highlight opportunities for new initiatives. To be fully effective in this role, the Supply Chain Senior Data Analyst must be able to convert complex data findings into text, tables, graphs and data visualizations to influence decision making as it relates to supply chain budgets and activation plans.

The Supply Chain Senior Data Analyst will bring strong initiative, curiosity and proven leadership qualities. They will be able to problem-solve processing large amount of complex data translated to measurable results. This individual will partner within the Insights & Analytics Organization as well as across the Supply Chain groups. Effective written and verbal communication will be key in translating findings to all levels of the organization.

Essential Duties & Responsibilities
  • Producing meaningful supply chain KPI dashboards and delivering performance reports with actionable insights
  • Benchmarking performance across all supply chain activities and advising on KPIs based on performance analysis
  • Reporting on key metrics, analyzing and interpreting trends and providing actionable insights based on available analytics data
  • Analyzes A/B and Multi-variate tests, communicate results and provide recommendations.
  • Convert complex data findings into text, tables, graphs and data visualizations
