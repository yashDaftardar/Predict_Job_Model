Full Job Description

BMW Financial Services NA, LLC is one of the 26 worldwide subsidiaries of the Financial Services Division of BMW AG. Our momentum is moving at such a rapid pace that we continually exceed average international growth rates and we constantly create new opportunities for our employees worldwide. Be a part of our exciting growth by expressing an interest in our Sr. Data Analyst -Used Vehicle Business in our Hilliard, Ohio facility.

As the Sr. Data Analyst -Used Vehicle Business, you will work closely with Senior Managers of used car department pillars (Retail Sales Development, Customer Loyalty & Center Sales, Sales Channel Development and Pricing, Analytics & Reporting), Risk Management, various Operations departments, Sales and Marketing and Finance, Headquarters Finance, Risk, Strategy and Sales & Marketing.

In this role, you will maximize used car (wholesale) resale values and minimize residual losses through optimized wholesale and retail pricing, analysis, reporting and data management for all Brands. You will support optimization of used car pricing strategy for all wholesale channels. In addition, you will develop business cases for proposed retail and wholesale programs, including cost/benefit analyses, expected results, and alternative and recommended options. You will also serve as the analysis and reporting center for all used car department functions.

As the Sr. Analyst, you will work closely with internal partners and external vendors to evaluate the effectiveness of distribution channel and mix targets. You will develop, enhancements and maintain pricing strategies, methodologies and systems for all wholesale channels.
