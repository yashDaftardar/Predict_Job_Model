The Senior Data Science Engineer is part of the Data Governance Center of Excellence within the Corporate IT Enterprise Data Organization (EDO). The Senior Engineer will play a critical role in data matching algorithm creation and assessment/enhancement within the master data management and meta data management practices. Responsibilities will include the design, creation and curation of matching algorithms utilizing deterministic and probabilistic modeling and AI/ML capabilities. The incumbent will work towards guaranteeing compliance with data governance and data security requirements while creating, improving, and operationalizing these integrated and reusable data matching algorithms across the EDO environment. This role will also be tasked with working with key business stakeholders, IT experts and subject-matter experts (data stewards/data custodians) to plan and deliver optimal matching and data quality processes. The Senior Data Engineer will have a combination of IT skills, data governance skills, analytics skills, and knowledge with a technical or computer science degree. Success in the role includes engaging and having regular discussions with data consumers (data stewards / data custodians) on optimally refining the data linkage processes in effort to optimize and automate the match/merge process and data discovery.

Essential Functions

Strategy & Planning

  • Work with data governance teams (information stewards/custodians within these teams) to make sure that governance standards are applied to the data curation and merging algorithms.
  • Must be able to acquire and interpret business requirements, create functional specifications, provide accurate estimates, and meet deadlines on deliverables.
  • Work with the Data Governance team to review standards in determining the best design to meet project requirements. Responsible for implementing approved architecture design.
  • Submit and review technical and process improvement recommendations as a subject matter expert of the technical team.
  • Accountable for the overall application feasibility, system architecture, and system integrity for technical project solution designs.

Acquisition & Deployment

  • Architect, create and maintain data matching/merging models/algorithms.
  • Work with varied stakeholders within the organization.
  • Develop a close relationship with data science teams and with business (data) analysts in refining their data requirements for various data and analytics initiatives.
  • Demonstrate leadership in the design, application, and database components for xDM projects.
  • Perform analysis of business models, logical specifications, and/or customer requirements to design and build xDM solutions.

Organizational Management

  • Use innovative and modern tools, techniques, and architectures to partially or completely automate the most-common, repeatable, and tedious data analysis and matching tasks to minimize manual and error-prone processes and improve productivity.
  • Become subject matter expert regarding xDM SEMQL.
  • Become subject matter expert regarding xDM’ s probabilistic matching models.
  • Assist with renovating the data management infrastructure to drive automation in data integration and management.
  • Track data quality patterns.
  • Train counterparts such as developers/configurators, administrators and data custodians in data modeling, probabilistic matching models and SEMSQL.
  • Act as a subject matter expert in the discovery and investigation of critical production problems.

Incidental Functions

  • Research and make recommendations on new technologies, including tools, components, and frameworks.
  • Provides tier 2, on-call support for critical web application problems and issues.
  • Act as a technical expert in the discovery and investigation of critical production issues.
  • Analyze performance of matching models and take action to correct deficiencies based on consultation with clients and approval of supervisor.
  • Assist with projects as may be required to contribute to efficiency and effectiveness of the work.
  • Collaborate with other development teams across the organization to share technical approaches, recommended tools, and subject matter expertise.
  • Up to 25% travel as required (domestic and international).
  • Work outside the standard office 7.5-hour workday may be required.

Position Requirements

Formal Education & Certification

  • Bachelor’s degree or foreign equivalent in related field or equivalent experience including but not limited to computer science, statistics, applied mathematics, information systems, information science.

Knowledge & Experience

  • 5+ years of IT experience in data-related roles with increasing responsibility.
  • 4+ years of work experience in data management disciplines including [data integration, modeling, optimization, and data quality], and/or other areas directly relevant to data engineering responsibilities and tasks.
  • Strong experience working in cross-functional teams and collaborating with business stakeholders in support of a departmental and/or multi-departmental data management and analytics initiative.
  • 4+ years of experience with popular database programming languages including [SQL, PL/SQL, others] for relational databases and awareness on upcoming technologies for nonrelational databases.
  • 3+ year of DBA experience.
  • Knowledge of SEMSQL and xDM modeling is a strong plus but not required/compulsory.
  • Basic understanding of popular open-source and commercial data science platforms such as [Python, R others] is a strong plus but not required/compulsory.
  • Basic experience in working with data governance organizations.
  • Demonstrated ability to work across multiple deployment environments including [cloud, on-premises, and hybrid]
  • Adept in agile methodologies and capable of applying DevOps principles.
  • Deep knowledge or previous experience working in the business would be a plus.

Interpersonal Skills and Characteristics

  • Excellent verbal and written communications.
  • Highly responsive and alert to new learning opportunities, growth, and development of technical, interpersonal, and business skills.
  • Motivated and competent to develop objectives and timelines to accomplish goals.
  • Strong experience supporting and working with cross-functional teams in a dynamic business environment.
  • Strong collaboration experience with both the business and IT teams to define the business problem, refine the requirements, and design and develop data deliverables accordingly.
  • Experience with interfacing with, and gaining the respect of, stakeholders at all levels and roles within the company.
  • Is a confident, energetic self-starter, with strong interpersonal skills.
  • Has good judgment, a sense of urgency and has demonstrated commitment to high standards of ethics, regulatory compliance, customer service and business integrity.
  • Flexibility, able to adapt to change and embrace it.
  • Ability to understand automation and leverage it to do most work.
