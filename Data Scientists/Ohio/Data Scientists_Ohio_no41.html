Full Job Description

Majestic Steel is seeking a Data Science Intern to join our Innovation area! Data is a central hypothesis for how we think about increasing efficiency across the industry. Leveraging insights from our data is core to how we think about adding differentiated value to our customers and our associates. We believe that data should inform decision making across the company, data should be openly accessible and that all team members should be empowered with reporting tools and training, and that our data and reports must be accurate and performant. In this role, you will drive these values into practices.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

You will be heavily involved with the following data science projects:

  • DataRobot deployment
  • HRC predict
  • Sales forecasting
  • Demand planning
  • Plant intelligence/machine failure prediction
  • Machine learning development for proprietary systems and digital products

You will also have exposure to special projects and other job related duties as assigned

Education, Training and Experience Requirements

  • Pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Data Science/Data Engineering

Knowledge, Skills and Abilities Requirements

  • Statistical expertise and experience with statistical software (R, SciPy, TensorFlow, …)
  • Experience with varied programming and scripting languages (Python, C++, …)
  • Majoring in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Science, Mathematics, Statistics, or related fields.
  • Strong understanding of Machine Learning
  • Self-starter and entrepreneurial spirit with hands-on approach towards business.
