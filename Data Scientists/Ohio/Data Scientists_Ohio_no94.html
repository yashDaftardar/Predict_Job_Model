Digital IT Compliance Manager

**This position can be located within 50 miles of any Anthem office**

Works with system analysts, software designers and programmers to evaluate, design and implement compliance controls as applicable. Works with moderate guidance and is responsible for collaborating with stakeholders to analyze, evaluate and enhance compliance to internal standards, contracts, laws and regulations. Evaluates the internal control and risk environment within the Digital organization. Continuously assesses opportunities to improve the internal control environment within the Digital organization.

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Develop and document a technical framework that supports the primary Data Security policies
  • Performs comprehensive testing and monitoring of technical controls to ensure adherence to internal standards, contracts, and regulations. This includes conducting data integrity reviews to ensure data is accurate, complete, reliable and timely. Provides feedback to impacted parties for the purpose of improving quality going forward.
  • Identify current and emerging risks associated with digital compliance.
  • Document effective procedures to identify and address applicable Privacy and IT Compliance requirements, and to ensure that data is being collected and used in accordance with those requirements.
  • Develop and implement tests for validating only required data is being collected, that it is handled in accordance with applicable laws and regulations, and the appropriate methods for ensuring accuracy are in place.
  • Promote Privacy and Data Security awareness and assist with the development of applicable training.
  • Identify current and emerging risks associated with IT Compliance.
  • Monitor the development and execution of IT Compliance Corrective Action Plans and initiatives to resolve identified control deficiencies and achieve process improvement opportunities.
  • Work collaboratively with internal stakeholders across the enterprise to ensure that controls are in place and working effectively.
  • Analyze data to identify anomalies and risk mitigation actions
  • Work closely with other IT team members and end users to provide solutions based on business requirements during all phases of the development and life cycle management process
  • Participate in IT controls and compliance external audits and lead support of IT audits
  • Provides performance feedback to team members and resource managers

Qualifications

BA/BS in information systems or related major; minimum of 3 years technical compliance/auditing experience and 3-5 year’s experience in broad-based information systems, full life-cycle application development and/or building enterprise applications and/or data warehouse planning and development or an equivalent combination of education and experience required. Minimum of 5 year’s experience in a technical compliance/audit position.

  • Experience in digital transformation knowledge and concepts
  • CISA Certification Preferred
  • Experience working for a large technical company in IT Compliance
  • Knowledge and Experience with AI- Required
