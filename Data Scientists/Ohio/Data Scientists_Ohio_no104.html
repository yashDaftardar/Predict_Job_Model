Data Analyst Mid or Senior - Marketing Process
Job Number : 173744


As a Marketing Process Analyst you’ll play a critical role in ensuring our marketing dollars are spent efficiently to acquire new customers. In this position, you’ll develop statistical models to calculate the Lifetime Value of potential customers in our marketing funnel so media can accurately target customers at the right cost per sale. Building and measuring model accuracy will be important, as well as explaining the models to partners and estimating their impact to the business. This role provides the opportunity to own the entire model lifecycle, including development, deployment, measurement of outcomes and maintenance/improvement as business dynamics evolve.

Must-have qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree or higher in a quantitative field of study and a minimum of one year of analytical work experience
  • Instead of a quantitative degree, a bachelor's degree or higher and a minimum of three years of analytical work experience
  • Instead of a degree, a minimum of four years of analytical work experience

Preferred skills

  • Proven ability to develop predictive data, aptitude for learning and high proficiency in using data analysis reporting tools such as Alteryx
  • Strong proficiency in SQL, as well as programming abilities in SAS or R or Python.
  • Ability to gain a solid knowledge of Progressive's products, services, systems, databases and applications

Compensation

  • Senior level: $65,000 - $86,700/year
  • Gainshare bonus up to 24% of your eligible earnings based on company performance

  • Parental & family leave; military leave & pay
  • Diverse, inclusive & welcoming culture with Employee Resource Groups
  • Career development & tuition assistance
  • Onsite gym & healthcare at large locations
