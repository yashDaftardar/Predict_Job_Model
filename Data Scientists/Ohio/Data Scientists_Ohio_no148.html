Position Summary

This role resides in the Federal Reserve Bank of Cleveland’s Information Technology Services area and is part of the Data Solutions Team. In this role, you will partner with data science practitioners in the business lines we support to help scale and automate their work—from data ingestion to output. You will also utilize native cloud services and commercial products to provide advanced analytics capabilities, such as predictive modeling, natural language processing, and chatbots, to business areas without dedicated data science resources.

An AI Engineer implements and scales data science models leveraging big data tools and programming frameworks. The AI Engineer is responsible for scaling models to production for optimal resiliency, reliability, and repeatability The AI Engineer applies different tools and techniques to allow for processing data and develops and maintains AI/ML systems.

Essential Accountabilities

  • Supports data scientists in their development of predictive models that use machine learning, econometric modeling, and data mining techniques (text mining and natural language processing) to measure, monitor and predict various measures of financial risk.
  • Contributes to analytic projects through all stages of development, but is primarily responsible for end-user product development, deployment, monitoring, and maintenance of production models and tools.
  • Works closely with data scientists, data analytics developers, and business-line partners to improve existing machine learning models, tools, and processes.
  • Has intermediate level programming skills to transform large and moderately complex datasets in a manner that is optimized for models in production.
  • Visualizes and reports out on data sets, integrity and completeness using a variety of methods to promote understanding of key insights and actionable takeaways for both technical and non-technical audiences.
  • Delivers presentations, creates analytical reports and executive summaries of data findings.
  • Intermediate knowledge of the latest trends in technology and translates those into opportunities to enhance our customer's technology needs.

Education and Experience

  • AI Engineer: Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related data-driven field, 3+ years of related work experience, with 1-2 years’ experience working on cloud solutions
  • Senior: Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related data-driven field, 5+ years of related work experience, with 1-2 years’ experience working on cloud solutions
  • AWS Certification strongly preferred, specifically AWS Solutions Architect, Developer, DevOps, and/or a specialty data certification; other certifications or relevant experience will be considered
  • Understanding of and ability to build in the technology landscape including open source, commercial on-premises, and cloud-based tools. Experience working in hybrid environments with a blend of on-premises and cloud-based tools is preferred.
  • Conversant in application of advanced quantitative techniques in Data Science, Statistics or other quantitative field to support business decisions or risk monitoring
  • Knowledge of banking and financial markets a plus
  • Intermediate level experience with cloud-based architecture and AI tools
  • Intermediate level experience configuring and working with Amazon Web Services (AWS) native services that support advanced analytics and AI/ML (e.g., Lambda, SQS, EC2, Textract, Comprehend, Sagemaker, etc.)
  • Intermediate level experience deploying models and integrating models into business applications
  • Intermediate experience working with large data sets. Familiarity with big data tools and frameworks (e.g., Hadoop, Spark, Storm, etc.) is a plus.
  • Programming skill in Python or R. Familiarity with common statistical or machine learning packages within those languages a plus.
  • Intermediate experience with DevOps and automation tools like Git, GitLab, Kubernetes, Docker, DVC, Metaflow
  • Intermediate experience with Terraform
  • Intermediate experience with Linux-based systems
  • Familiarity with machine learning technology, especially in relation to Deep Learning / Deep Neural Networks, Natural Language Processing, or Optical Character Recognition (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn, spaCy)
  • Communication skills - both written and verbal. Ability to deliver presentations and draft publication-quality reports

Physical Demands and General Working Conditions

Employees typically sit most of the day, work with a computer and may answer/respond to phone calls. Physical movement consists of walking for meetings, breaks, etc. Ability to lift items weighing approximately 20 pounds on a limited basis is required. Employees may be required to travel by car/air.
