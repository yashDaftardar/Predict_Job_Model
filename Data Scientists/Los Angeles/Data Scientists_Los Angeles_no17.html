The Opportunity...

We are looking for a talented Data Scientist to join our Data Architecture team. Our Data Scientist will play a critical role in building and enhancing our modeling and analysis methodologies to provide key solutions to the business.




How You'll Contribute...

    • Query complex data stores to retrieve data
    • Research opportunities for data acquisition, feature engineering, and new uses for existing data
    • Describe data using statistics and data visualization tools/techniques
    • Build new models to reliably provide accurate predictions
    • Tune existing machine learning models to improve their accuracy or reduce run-time
    • Write reliable, reusable, code that follows team best practices and style guides

What We're Looking For...

    • Bachelor’s degree or higher in Statistics, Mathematics or related field
    • 2+ years of relevant experience working with data
    • 2+ years of real-world experience programming in R and/or Python
    • Proficiency in SQL
    • A strong ability in statistical modeling and analysis to provide rigorous solutions that address the business needs
    • Classification methods (e.g., Neural Net, Logistic Regression, Decision Trees, KNN, SVM, Random Forest)
    • Regression methods (e.g., Linear, Nonlinear, Boosted Regression Trees )
    • Clustering methods (e.g., K-means, Fuzzy C-means, Hierarchical Clustering, Mixture Modelling)
    • Time-series Modelling/Forecasting (e.g., AR, ARMA, GARCH, Exponential Smoothing)
    • Statistical Analysis (e.g., Hypothesis Testing, Experiment Design, Hierarchical Modeling, Bayesian Inference)
    • Strong problem-solving skills, and an ability to apply them in the context of a fast-paced business environment
    • Outstanding written and verbal communication skills including explaining advanced techniques to a wide variety of audiences

Bonus Points...

    • First author publications
    • Substantial experience or coursework in machine learning or algorithm design
    • Experience with AWS
    • Familiarity with SEM/SEO and digital marketing
