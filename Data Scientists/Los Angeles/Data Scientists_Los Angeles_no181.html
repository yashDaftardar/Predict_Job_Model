INFORMATION SECURITY DATA SCIENTIST

WHAT IS THE OPPORTUNITY?
The Data Scientist is driven by an interest in solving complex problems through data exploration and analysis. They are highly analytically minded and have a robust technical tool kit. They have substantial experience in analytics with a portfolio of successfully executed models. The Data Scientist will be responsible for executing all steps in the modeling process to produce high quality, reproducible results. They will work with a team of other analyst to support the development of processes and technologies to improve patient outcomes. This role is responsible for extracting, managing, and analyzing complex administrative healthcare data and applying Client/AI concepts to uncover opportunities for process improvement. The Info Sec Data Scientist has strong experience using a variety of data mining/data analysis methods, using a variety of data tools, building and implementing models, using/creating algorithms and creating/running simulations. The Data Scientist must have a proven ability to drive business results with their data-based insights.

Technology & Innovation Division
As a member of City National's Technology & Innovation group, you will drive, develop, and maintain solutions for clients and colleagues. This is an exciting time of technology advancement and innovation across the bank, particularly within our technology teams.

WHAT WILL YOU DO?
  • Develop algorithms and applications to apply mathematics to data, perform large scale experimentation and develop functionality
  • Build and advocate data-driven solutions that help customers improve clinical outcomes and processes
  • Design and build scalable production-ready analytics solutions using a wide array of techniques and methodologies in the field of statistical modeling, machine learning, and other AI technologies to meet the needs of given client engagements.
  • Proposes and evaluates innovative solutions for analyzing, clustering, associating, and classifying data.
  • Investigates various methods of data analysis and collection to manipulate data into a desired format or to ensure accuracy of test results.
  • Carries out development and testing of programs on systems, components, and materials concurrent with design, fabrication, or testing to better evaluate and minimize future problems. Develops alternate solutions to existing problems.
  • Determine data model design, data pipeline, data security design and Execute on the same.
  • Partner with other data science teams to share best practices and insights to support ongoing learning.
  • Demonstrate a high degree of originality and creativity when developing solutions to improve existing business processes within supply chain utilizing methods such as statistical analysis, regression modeling and optimization.
  • Document technical work as part of the product development process. Support patent application and scientific paper publishing process
  • Extends analytics platform for connected devices and sensors.
  • Research new projects for potential development. May include literature research for project utility and scope.
  • Present the tools to internal teams and perform technical discussions.
  • Resolves a variety of technical problems leading to advanced engineering studies designed to increase efficiency or reduce costs.
  • Effective communication skills at multiple levels of the organization, including senior management ability to communicate complex concepts in easytounderstand terminology.
  • Develop new tests with existing or novel methods, including targeted NGS, microarray, qPCR or ddPCR, etc.
  • Experience with network modeling tools and network optimization techniques long range planning is a plus.
  • Works with QA team to identify and cover every edge case imaginable.
  • Performs experiments designed to improve upon or to duplicate various materials and systems, and engages in research to develop new process methods.
  • Enhancing data collection procedures to include information that is relevant for building analytic systems.
  • Processing, cleansing, and verifying the integrity of data used for analysis.
  • Use statistical and econometric techniques on large corpus of datasets to measure results and show key trends, identify impact and attribution, analyze experiments, model & predict future performance of user and products;
  • Write and interpret complex SQL queries for standard as well as ad hoc data analysis purposes;
  • Aggregate and consolidate InfoSec data for metrics and reporting
  • Combine and examine reporting data and policy exceptions



WHAT DO YOU NEED TO SUCCEED

Must-Have*

    • Bachelor's Degree Data Science or equivalent
    • 5-7 years in the Data Science or similar field
    • 5-7 years of experience manipulating datasets and building statistical models


Skills and Knowledge

  • Master's or PHD in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science or another quantitative field preferred but not required.

  • Excellent communication skills both written and verbal.

  • Ability to work with large data sets from multiple data sources.
  • Basic knowledge and understanding of software used to analyze large sequencing data sets.
  • Solid knowledge of computing tools and big data technologies (e.g., Hadoop and SQL).
  • Eager to learn and implement new technologies
  • Knowledge on implementing machine learning algorithms and models.
  • Knowledge of current technological developments/trends in area of expertise.
  • Knowledge of scientific approach and methodologies.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Ability to design, organize, and coordinate scientific research projects.
  • Ability to present to a variety of audiences both technical and non-technical.
  • Skill in talking to others to convey information effectively.
  • Ability to work under pressure and meet close deadlines.
  • In-depth understanding and working experience of Data Warehousing/Data modeling /Data Hub methodologies required
  • Skill in collecting and analyzing complex data
