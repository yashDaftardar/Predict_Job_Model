Full Job Description

Major Responsibilities

  • Business Performance Insights: Be the primary partner for cross-functional stakeholders to understand performance of KPI’s. When a metric is ahead or behind expectations, dig in to drive meaningful, actionable insights that help improve business performance or double-down on success.
  • Deep-Dive Analysis: Use advanced techniques (statistical modeling, experimentation, causal inference) to analyze user behavioral data to identify patterns and uncover opportunities. Create a rich understanding of how people are interacting with the business and content and use these insights to then make recommendations.
  • Opportunity Identification and Sizing: Both to set goals and help evaluate potential opportunities, improve forecasting and opportunity sizing. This will help business stakeholders evaluate trade-offs in different approaches, and create targets for driving business performance.
  • Partnership and communication: Partner closely with business stakeholders to identify and unlock opportunities, and with other teams to improve platform capabilities around data modeling, testing platforms, data visualization, and data architecture.
  • Performs other duties as assigned.


Skills/Knowledge Required

  • Fluency in SQL, R, Python or other big data querying and programming languages
  • Hands on experience building models with deep learning frameworks like MXNet, Tensorflow, Keras, Caffe, PyTorch, or similar
  • Hands-on experience with statistical modeling and machine learning packages (scikit-learn, LightGBM, XGBoost, Keras, Pytorch, Tensorflow, statsmodels, Stan, etc.)
  • Working knowledge of the following Regression, Classification, Clustering, Dimensionality Reduction, Clustering, NLP, Computer Vision, Reinforcement Learning, and Multi-armed Bandits
  • Ability to gather data from multiple sources (SQL, Spark, etc.) and use data visualization packages (Seaborn, Matplotlib, ggplot, Plotly, etc.)
  • Ability to communicate the results of analyses in a clear and effective manner to a senior audience
  • Intense curiosity, high intellectual and analytical horsepower, passionate pursuit of analytics driven insights


Education and Experience

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Engineering or related quantitative field or equivalent years of experience
  • 5+ years of experience in quantitative analysis & data science or a related field


Preferred Qualifications

  • Familiarity with data exploration and data visualization tools like Tableau, Power BI
  • Experience with causal inference techniques, experimental design and/or A/B testing
