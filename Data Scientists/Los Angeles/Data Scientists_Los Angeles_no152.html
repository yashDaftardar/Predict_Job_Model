THE ROLE:

The Data Architecture leader will be responsible for the comprehensive understanding of the inventory of all data available to the company, Globally, ensuring acceptable data placement to enable enterprise confidence in data and reduce risk in its use while achieving company business outcomes.

This role will work very closely with the Data Quality Team to understand data flows within and external to the company, determine semantic meaning of data elements, develop an enterprise data model and data models for future state analytical systems such a big data staging, big data preparation, big data warehouse as examples. This role will work closely with the future Data Catalog Team to ensure business user visibility of data inventory. This role will work closely with the Big Data Systems Architect to define a data placement strategy that ensures the availability of timely and trusted data for the business to achieve their business goals. This role will work closely with the Security Team to define data protection standards. This role will work closely with Application development Teams to ensure new data structures are created only when necessary.

This role report to the VP, Global Data, Analytics and Artificial Intelligence.

HOW YOU WOULD CONTRIBUTE:

  • First Member of the Data Architecture Team
  • Leadership
  • Relationship Management
  • Project Oversight and Decisioning
  • People Management and Development
  • Development of Data Architecture vision, scope, roadmap to operationalize it by partnering with key business and technology stakeholders
  • Development and adoption of the Data Architecture policies, standards, and procedures
  • Collaborating with Data Quality Team, understand and document data flows across the corporation in order of business priority, and ensure alignment of data quality processes, including data labeling into Data Architecture.
  • Disambiguate Semantic meaning of data to Build, optimize and maintain conceptual and logical, and physical database/big data models across all on-premise and cloud systems
  • Analyze system requirements and implement migration methods for existing data to improve data redundancy, data security and data compliance with regulations.
  • Create data placement strategy, design, and implementation to optimize data flows and data repositories for availability of Trusted, Timely, Secure and Compliant data to our businesses, Globally. Ensure Application, Database, Security, Privacy and Big Data Infrastructure, Analytics Teams are consulted and gain buy-in from them.
  • Familiar with predictive analysis, self-service data preparation, metadata-based data and self-service visualization techniques, artificial intelligence using relevant tools and frameworks.
  • Create processes and buy-in for governing the structural requirements for new applications (transactional and analytical) to avoid redundancy and performance optimization. Implement these processes.
  • Provide insight into the changing data storage and utilization requirements for the company and offer suggestions for solutions
  • Participate in the migration of data from legacy systems to new solutions to ensure data trust is maintained.
  • Develop database design and architecture documentation for the management and executive teams
  • Co-operatively with Security, help maintain the integrity and security of company data
  • Submit reports to management that outline the changing data needs of the company and come up with related solutions.
  • Ensures effective staff training, interviews candidates for employment, evaluates staff performance and recommends hiring, promotions, salary actions
  • Educating and training stakeholders, and the data users on use of data architecture tools and process/procedures
  • Promote organizational change by overseeing the implementation of effective change management for data architecture in support of Data Governance
  • Establishes issue management and resolution processes
  • Manage relationships with vendor and consulting partners to meet data architecture objectives/OKRs
  • Evaluate tools as needed to improve automation and monitoring of data redundancy, semantic meaning.
  • Establish Data Architecture objectives/OKRs, KPIs and benchmarks
  • Work with business, data owners and system owners to create RACI model to align on “Data Architecture Roles and Responsibilities” through data lifecycle
  • Perform additional duties as assigned
