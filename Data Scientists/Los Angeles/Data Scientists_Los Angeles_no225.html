As the Digital Analyst, you will evaluate overall business trends and provides strategic thought leadership for all areas of digital marketing related business including marketing acquisition, retention efforts and trend analysis. Analysis could include: ROI for marketing campaigns, media mix modeling, CAC targets, LTV, campaign measurement, and customer cohort analysis.

In this role you will:

  • Oversee development of analytical and measurement plans including proper selection of methodologies, techniques, KPIs and metrics.
  • Support the team’s campaign optimization across all media channels by conducting analysis and providing insights as well as providing post campaign analysis to assist in future campaigns
  • Develop and streamline necessary dashboards and one-off analyses, providing ability to surface business critical KPIs, monitor the health of digital marketing channels and effectively communicate performance.
  • Partner with other analysts as well as data engineering and technology teams to support building a best-in-class marketing platform and data infrastructure.
  • Dive into a wide scope of digital advertising, marketing, and financial data to help us make the right decisions and achieve our ambitious goals
  • Analyze customer behavior and test various hypotheses in order to identify areas of opportunity and provide data-driven recommendations to improve overall customer engagement and retention.
  • Partner with media agency analytics team to align on analytics roadmap, KPIs, and ensure consistent information exchange

Qualifications:

  • 4+ years of relevant experience in a business analyst, data analyst or statistical analysis role
  • 3+ years working with digital media channels; paid search, social display, media landscape
  • 2+ years of experience with web analytics platforms, Google Analytics preferred
  • Strong analytical skills
  • Hands-on experience with Looker, Tableau or similar BI tool strongly preferred
  • Deep understanding of customer lifetime value, recurring revenue, eCommerce and subscription-based business models
  • Ability to gather insights from data and communicate findings clearly to a cross-functional team
  • Ability to manipulate complex data sets and numerical fluency
  • Strong relationship building skills and ability to work collaboratively and enthusiastically within and across multiple departments
