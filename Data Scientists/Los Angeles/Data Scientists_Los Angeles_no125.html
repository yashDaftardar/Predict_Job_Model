Responsibilities

Under supervision of the Principal Statistician, you will provide statistical analysis, data management and
consultation for projects in the Population Health Research Core, which includes projects across the UCLA School of Medicine
campus and outside of UCLA. You will perform professional statistical work using a variety of standard statistical
methods and programming techniques in order to complete assignments; and perform other related duties as required.

Under the direction of the Principal Statistician, you will assist investigators, which include faculty, fellows, residents,
medical students and fellow team members, in planning research designs on observational and interventional studies which may result in contracts or grants; determine from discussions the purposes and objectives of various studies and implement data collection and analysis strategies accordingly; with the Principal Statistician, meet with investigators to discuss statistical analyses to meet their research aims and objectives; summarize and interpret results to investigators after completion of analysis sets. You will report directly to the Principal Statistician, who will assign and monitor work flow.
