Do you love all things Data? Do you strive to increase customer growth and retention through data? Then we want to talk to you. This collaborative role will query data from multiple databases, perform a wide range of formal and ad hoc analyses, and work alongside our Digital Marketing team to drive campaign strategy across a diverse portfolio of marketing platforms. Our ideal candidate will be incredibly curious, technically capable, with strong communication skills to identify strategic opportunities and holistic impact of all marketing efforts.


What you'll do:

  • Work alongside the Digital Marketing team to provide data backed insights into digital marketing, traditional media, and brand performance to drive customer growth, retention, and lifetime value.
  • Own digital marketing analytics and forecasting, compiling data from multiple sources to create dynamic reports, dashboards, and ad hoc analyses, etc.
  • Support digital agency relationships, working with external partners on reporting and analyzing tactical channel performance.
  • Proactively identify areas for optimization, strategy, and growth through data, presenting recommendations to senior team members.
  • Partner with data engineering and technical teams to ensure data accuracy for reporting and analysis.
  • Communicate complex data concepts and insights across all levels of the organization.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree in Mathematics, CS, or other technical science field (or equivalent work experience)
  • 3+ years experience in an analytics role
  • 2+ years of experience in digital media, performance marketing, or campaign management (paid acquisition, CRM, multitouch attribution, etc.)
  • 1+ years experience programming with SQL and relational databases (Snowflake, BigQuery, etc.)
  • 1+ years experience with data visualization tools (Tableau, Looker, Google Data Studio, etc.)
  • Strong Excel skills (Pivot Tables, VLOOKUP, etc.) with experience manipulating, analyzing, and visualizing data
  • Desire to continually keep up with advancements in data team practices
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills

Other must haves:

  • Positive attitude
  • Entrepreneurial mindset
  • Desire to excel and grow with FIGS
