Job Summary

As a Data Science Engineer at Hoverstate, you will be a key part of a cross-functional team that is responsible for developing the company’s ability to extract insight through our data streams. You will also be a part of implementing systems to extract data from the company’s business systems and transforming it into actionable format. Our goal is to bring efficiencies to the company, and to the products we create.

In this role, you will:


  • Design, build, and deploy Robotics, AI/ML Automation use cases
  • Utilize leading automation delivery platforms
  • Design and build innovative applications using Robotics and AI/ML from functional proofs-of-concepts to production ready, scalable solutions
  • Bring end to end solution thinking to factor in licensing, security, scalability, and performance every use-case deploy
  • Actively participate and propose technical guidance in requirement gathering and process assessments for Robotics/AI Automation opportunities, including documentation of solutions and presenting to senior leadership
  • Use problem solving skills in the running of Robotics/AI Automation processes and provide timely responses and solutions
  • Design, develop, troubleshoot, debug, evaluate, and modify Robotics/AI Automation solutions
  • Work closely with the operations team to optimize existing Robotics/AI Automation processes and build an effective alert and notification framework to proactively identify problems before business impact
  • Effectively communicate with non-IT/business users, functional counterparts, and stakeholders to understand underlying business problems and communicate technical solutions
  • Work on developing and training ML models and APIs and enhancing existing processes with cognitive capabilities

The Ideal Candidate:

The ideal candidate will have a passion for data science along with a desire to transform data into accessible, new insights. Candidates should have a degree in Data Science, or a related field, and have some professional experience in the field as well.

Qualifications will include:

  • 1+ year building IT use cases and solutions with tools like TensorFlow, Torch, Theano, or MXNet
  • 1+ year working with web services/restful APIs/integration
  • 1+ year working experience on development with bot services and serverless services on cloud (I.e. AWS, Azure, Google Cloud)
  • Previous experience working with cloud infrastructure, security and deployment options (I.e. AWS, Azure, Google Cloud)
  • Prior experience working with agile/scrum/scaled agile/DevOps team environment
  • Certified in Robotics or AI/ML
  • Bachelor’s or post-graduate degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or similar field

Company Overview

Hoverstate is a digital consultancy with offices in California (Los Angeles, Oakland), Texas (Lubbock), Utah (Lehi) and Italy (Milan, Turin) specializing in Health and Life Sciences. We focus on: delivering customer-centric transformation and innovative digital products, forward-thinking technology strategies, leveraging compliant technologies, developing rock-solid code, testing our products with a robust QA team/automation, then backing it all with marketing support. If there is a problem to be solved in the digital space, Hoverstate can solve it.
