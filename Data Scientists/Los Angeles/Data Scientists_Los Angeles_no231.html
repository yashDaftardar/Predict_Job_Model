Zefr is looking for a Senior Data Analyst to join the Product & Technology team. You will work with Product, Engineering, Ops and other teams to organize, analyze and visualize data that informs key decisions across a broad range of initiatives. You should be able to think critically about our business, operate independently under ambiguity, and pay keen attention to detail. The ideal candidate is commercially minded and technically savvy with advanced SQL skills and broad exposure to transactional and analytical data storage systems.

Here's what you'll get to do:

  • Expand Zefr's suite of reports and dashboards for monitoring key metrics.
  • Perform ad hoc analyses and produce deep dive reports.
  • Answer business questions through investigation, experimentation and analysis.
  • Establish test-and-learn strategies to optimize and improve Zefr's advertising algorithms.
  • Establish processes and procedures to ensure cross-functional teams have access to the data they need when they need it.
  • Tell compelling stories with data and support a company culture of data-driven decision making.
  • Work with Technical Operations, Data Science and Data Engineering to ensure data quality and integrity.

Here's what we're looking for:

  • Minimum 3 years of experience in an analyst function eg. business, ops, data, finance
  • Expert SQL skills and sophisticated understanding of data models
  • Experience with data visualization and business intelligence tools such as Kibana, Domo, Looker, Tableau
  • Excellent communication skills and extreme curiosity required
  • Knowledge of streaming data systems (Kafka) and search based storage systems (Elasticsearch) a plus
  • Python experience a plus
  • Bachelor's or advanced degree in an analytical discipline required
