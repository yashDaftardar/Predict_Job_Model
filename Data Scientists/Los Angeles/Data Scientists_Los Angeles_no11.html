Project: LA County Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19) Epidemiology Data Project (Activity #2)

SUMMARY

This position will work with the core epidemiology team on data management, transformation and analysis of varied large datasets. The skillset needed for this position is experience with R-software (one of the tools for outbreak epidemiologists) including tidyverse packages (dplyr, tidyr, purr, ggplot2), geospatial packages (rgdal, sp, sf, leaflet, tmap), and interactive dashboarding packages (shiny, shinydashboard) and other data visualization and reporting packages (plotly, datatables, kable, markdown). Knowledge and experience in public health is desired.

Employment is provided by Heluna Health.


ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS

The duties of the Data Scientist/Epidemiologist include, but are not limited to:

  • Establish and maintain SQL databases for COVID-19 surveillance data
  • Use various R packages to link, clean and analyze data
  • Use various R and other data viz packages to display and update data
  • Prepare reports for submission to peer-reviewed journals
  • Assist with other COVID-19 data activities as needed

JOB QUALIFICATIONS

Minimum Qualifications
  • A Master’s degree from an accredited college with specialization in epidemiology, infectious diseases or equivalent
Desirable Qualifications
  • Experience in designing, conducting, evaluating and interpreting epidemiological studies or investigations
  • Knowledge in communicable disease surveillance and control, including interpretation and evaluation of laboratory test results and statistical data
Certificates/Licenses/Clearances
  • A valid California driver’s license, proof of vehicle insurance, and reliable transportation or the ability to utilize an alternative method of transportation when needed to carry out job-related essential functions
  • Successful Clearance of LA County Live Scan Process
