THE ROLE:

Reporting directly to the VP Global Data, Analytics, and AI, this leadership position will help define and lead the Herbalife's Global Master Data Management (MDM) strategy to enable data management transformation at enterprise level. You will be responsible for all aspects of defining and implementing master data management policies and practices supporting multiple data domains (such as Customer, Product, and Supplier) at Herbalife. You will bring the deep domain and technical expertise to develop and deliver the MDM capabilities including data integration, data provisioning, and data quality processes, operational data stewardship and governance, tools, and technology implementation. This position brings the thought leadership to help build the data literacy across the enterprise.

HOW YOU WOULD CONTRIBUTE:

  • Manage Existing Team who have developed home grown Master Data Solution
  • Developing MDM vision, scope, and roadmap for the global customer, product, and supplier
  • Engaging stakeholders across various business units to identify opportunities and to drive alignment and prioritize execution of data management efforts to deliver business values
  • Development of master data management policies, standards, and procedures
  • Leading the adoption of MDM solutions by ensuring the timely availability of accurate and trustworthy data to meet operational, compliance, and analytical business needs
  • Continuously accessing the business needs to evolve the MDM program across the enterprise
  • Maintaining and expanding the MDM strategy to align with the changes to business requirement and industry/technology trends
  • Working closely with other senior business and IT executives to ensure the Data Management strategy aligns with the business values
  • Developing and implementing data management best practices, governance processes, communication processes, and metrices
  • Advocating the MDM program across enterprise through awareness activities
  • Promote organizational change by overseeing the implementation of effective change management for MDM solution
  • Managing relationships with vendor and consulting partners
  • Building and expanding the global team to deliver and maintain the MDM program across the enterprise
  • Supervise staff including but not limited to organize and prioritize work, write/conduct performance reviews, train/develop, and manage work performance

WHAT’S SPECIAL ABOUT THE TEAM: (Give insight to the team dynamic and environment)

You will be joining a newly formed global data, analytics and artificial intelligence team that is rapidly transforming to be able to deliver outsized business outcomes for the global corporation.
