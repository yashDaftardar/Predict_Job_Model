Job Description


We are looking for a Manager of Data Analytics to join our Data Science & Analytics practice. This client-facing role will be the day-to-day lead of multiple data and analytics workstreams for a retail-sector client, including data strategy, web analytics, testing, reporting, analysis, and insights. You are a strategic thinker and storyteller who is not afraid to roll up your sleeves and dive into the data yourself. You will be responsible for working with clients and internal teams to formulate a strategic approach to collecting, analyzing, and activating digital marketing data. You will work with multidisciplinary teams to ensure Data is a key input and output of the work Razorfish delivers.

Responsibilities (in addition to what’s listed above):

  • Develops learning agendas / measurement plans
  • Develop, prioritize, and execute test hypothesis
  • Develop data visualizations and client presentations
  • Guides junior team members to build reports and presentations highlighting relevant results
  • Provide thought leadership, coaching and mentorship in the field of data analytics in working with cross functional teams, both internal and client facing
  • Assess client needs and requirements to ensure your team is adopting the appropriate approach to resolve client challenges
  • Guide, mentor, and manage data analysts
  • Understands and articulates client strategy
  • Helps clients see beyond current projects to longer-term opportunities


Qualifications


Skill Guidelines:

  • Thorough problem identification and analytical skills
  • 5+ years in consulting/agency background with experience in developing analytical solutions
  • Excellent oral and written communication and experience in developing data centered communications with senior clients
  • An understanding of methods in strategic analysis and it’s applications in customer segmentations, media and advertising optimization solutions, personalization systems and forecasting
  • Experience in interpreting results of statistical analysis
  • A/B testing
  • Experience in digital user experience, customer journeys, audience segmentation.
  • Offline retail analytics and loyalty experience a plus.
  • Excellent quantitative skills and the ability to tell a story using data
  • Passionate about turning data into actionable insights
  • Experience in statistical programming in R, SAS, SPSS, MATLAB or Python a plus
  • CRM, Loyalty, and/or Retail experience a plus
  • Mastery in data tools and platforms such as Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics, DMPs, Testing platforms, data visualization and dashboarding
  • Understanding of technical implementation of Google and Adobe Analytics a plus
