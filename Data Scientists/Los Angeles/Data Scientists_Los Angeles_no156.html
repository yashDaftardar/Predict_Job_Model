Full Job Description

APU is seeking employees who desire to engage in our thriving culture by cultivating community through professional interactions, demonstrating a posture of lifelong learning, and modeling Christ-like character. Working together, employees at APU commit to establishing a university atmosphere that is edifying to God and to one another.


The Senior Business and Data Analyst is responsible for the configuration, regular use and management of all of the academic modules in PeopleSoft and coordinates projects requiring a high level of functional expertise in Student Records for the Office of the Undergraduate Registrar. This position is also responsible for the official reporting of student records data to all university stakeholders as appropriate. In addition, this position sets standards for implementation and use of software in the unit and serves as a liaison between functional and technical staff. This position is engaged in a dynamic integrative-enrollment strategic plan focused on initiatives that improve the quality, effectiveness, and services impacting registration, transfer evaluation, class scheduling, advising, and graduation requirements for APU students. Additionally, this position continually analyzes and audits data in order to improve business processes as needed by staff. This position must translate between business, data, and technical personnel and resources daily. This position plays a critical role in training and IT project development.
