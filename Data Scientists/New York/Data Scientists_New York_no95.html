Hello, World! Codecademy has helped over 45 million people from around the world upgrade their careers with engaging, accessible, and flexible education on programming and data skills. We provide over 200 hands-on interactive lessons ranging from Python to R to Javascript and everything in between. Our learners have gone on to start companies, new jobs, and new lives thanks to what they've learned with Codecademy, and we're thrilled to be working to take that impact to the next level.


Codecademy was started in 2011 by two college students in a dorm room at Columbia that were frustrated by the huge gap between education and employment. A few years later, we are a rapidly growing, diverse team of 100+ in SoHo, NYC. We've raised over $40m in venture capital funding from top investors including Union Square Ventures, Kleiner Perkins, Naspers, Y Combinator, and more.

If you want to help build a business that impacts tens of millions of people each year and helps them lead better lives, join us!

Codecademy is looking for an expert programmer or academic to provide guidance as we build our newest skill path, Advanced Math for Data Science which we'll teach using Interactive Applets and Python. The Subject Matter Expert provides the Codecademy Curriculum team with specialized, up-to-date, nuanced insight into how mathematics topics such as linear algebra and probability are used in the Data Science field and how they should be taught. This is a paid, remote, contract position, with a typical commitment of 1-5 hours per week.

What You'll Do

  • Work with a Curriculum Developer to create and refine a high-level syllabus for Advanced Math for Data Science
  • Review the lessons, quizzes, and projects that other members of the Curriculum team create as we turn that syllabus into reality
  • Provide complex technical and pedagogical insights in such a way that other team members—and, ultimately, Codecademy learners—can understand

What You'll Need

  • 5+ years of experience as a professional programmer or educator
  • Expertise in Calculus, Linear Algebra, Probability, and/or Statistics and how they relate to Data Science
  • Extensive knowledge of and real-world experience with Python programming and Data Science
  • The ability to provide clear, actionable, and empathetic feedback and explanation
  • A strong command of written and spoken English

What Will Make You Stand Out

  • 5+ years of experience teaching mathematics, especially Calculus, Linear Algebra, Probability, and/or Statistics
  • 5+ years of professional experience in the Data Science field
  • Experience in both programming and education
  • Experience in online education at scale
  • Experience using the Codecademy platform
