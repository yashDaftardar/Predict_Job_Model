Company description:

Dow Jones is a global provider of news and business information, delivering content to consumers and organizations around the world across multiple formats, including print, digital, mobile and live events. Dow Jones has produced unrivaled quality content for more than 125 years and today has one of the world’s largest news gathering operations globally. It produces leading publications and products including the flagship Wall Street Journal, America’s largest newspaper by paid circulation; Factiva, Barron’s, MarketWatch, Financial News, DJX, Dow Jones Risk & Compliance, Dow Jones Newswires, and Dow Jones VentureSource.

The Wall Street Journal is America’s largest newspaper by paid circulation with more than 2.2 million customers. In recent years, the Journal has expanded its core content offering to include coverage of the arts, culture, lifestyle, sports, and personal health, building on its heritage as the leading source of business and financial news. Published by Dow Jones, one of the world’s largest news gathering operations with nearly 2,000 journalists in more than 80 bureaus, The Wall Street Journal now spans 12 editions in nine languages, engaging readers across newspapers, websites, magazines, social media, and video. The Journal holds 35 Pulitzer Prizes for outstanding journalism.

Position Statement:

Working closely with Sales and Product teams to define, implement, and analyze an analytics suite of reports and provide insight into the numbers to help improve business outcomes.

Primary Responsibilities/Accountabilities:

  • Overseeing daily, weekly, monthly and quarterly dashboards and building new dashboards using Tableau off of the new Customer Data Lake. Including:

Acquisition reporting suite

  • Buyer profiles by Sales Channel, Geography, Product of Interest, Industry, Job Function, Seniority, and Company Size
  • Breakdown of sales by lead type, lead close rates, ROI by lead type and marketing channel
  • Full sales and marketing funnel analysis
  • Penetration of top target organisations & supporting gap analysis
  • Propensity and lead scoring models

Retention & Upgrade reporting suite

  • Member/Account renewal rate, user renewal rate, revenue renewal rate
  • Cancellation analysis
  • Client engagement – Usage/attendance stats linked to CRR to predict churn indicators
  • Lifetime Value calculations – in order to direct future activity and spend
  • Xsell & Upgrade – identification of prime upgrade targets based on comprehensive account spend reviews
  • Interpreting trends from the data and working closely with sales and marketing stakeholders to enable them to understand the dynamics behind the data
  • Proposing future actions based on the insights learnt
  • Ensuring QA of data capture from various marketing sites
  • Hosting training sessions for stakeholders, and presenting data highlights from a business opportunity/benefit perspective

Preferred Tools/Skills:

  • Understanding of membership/subscription process
  • Understanding of ROI
  • Understanding of sales close rates and churn
  • SQL querying and connecting dashboards with custom queries
  • Advanced use of Excel
  • Creating dashboards in Tableau, Google Data Studio, Adobe
  • Use of Salesforce
  • Detail oriented in data quality, data cleansing and deriving insights
  • Self-starter
  • Strong written and oral communications needed

Knowledge/Experience: Ideally have worked as a Sales or Marketing Analyst within an organization that deals with membership and advertising models.
