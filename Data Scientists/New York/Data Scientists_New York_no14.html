Data Scientist focused on data analysis for web based investment management application suite for Bank of New York Mellons Wealth Management division. This will be a critical role working on a next generation web based platform for BNY Mellons wealth clients. As a data scientist, you will be embedded with the engineering team as the primary subject matter expert realated to data analysis, natural language processing and machine learning for the platform. The role will give the chosen candidate the opportunity to take on a wide ranging opportunity at a fast growing organization within the firm where you will be able to take on many responsibilities in a highly visibile role.

Qualifications

Strong Statistical and Mathematical Background

Proficient in performing data analysis, using python based libraries such as Pandas and Numpy

Strong understanding of Machine Learning algorithms: Supervised Learning and Unsupervised Learning

Understanding of reinforcement learning

Understanding of TensorFlow

Good Understanding of Natural Language processing using Spacy and other python libraries

Understanding of web crawling using python libraries such as Scrapy

Good understanding of developing RestAPI using Flask and/or FastAPI

Desired – Experience in Tableau visualization
