About Chartbeat Chartbeat’s (www.chartbeat.com) mission is to help content creators around the world better connect with their audiences. Leading media brands — including The New York Times, ESPN, the Washington Post, the BBC, and CNN — trust our suite of analytics and optimization tools to understand digital audience behavior and strengthen their content strategies. You'll be joining a group of focused, hard-working, people who are passionate about doing work that's challenging and fun—and who strive to maintain a healthy work/life balance.

About Data Science at Chartbeat The Data Science team contributes to the Chartbeat mission by providing insights from our data that help to drive decision making for both our customers and ourselves. We work closely with the Product, Engineering and Design teams to drive innovative data capabilities into our product and with customer-facing teams to provide data to answer their business questions. We also partner closely with Marketing to leverage Chartbeat’s unique metrics to surface stories that reflect the state of reader engagement across the US and the world.

Responsibilities

    • Collaborate on development of new data-science based product capabilities, including insight generation, content recommendations, content-based audience segmentation, and peer benchmarking
    • Provide innovative thinking and technical expertise to expand Chartbeat’s capabilities around natural language processing
    • Understand, diagnose, and update Chartbeat’s data pipelines that support data-science capabilities

Strong candidates will have:

    • 2+ years working as a practicing data scientist in industry
    • Experience developing, testing, and deploying Python-based machine learning algorithms in AWS environments
    • Solid knowledge of statistical and machine learning methodology, as evidenced by a degree in a quantitative discipline
    • Knowledge of common database systems, particularly Redshift/Postgres
    • Strong business communication skills and ability to work cross-functionally
    • A passion for understanding reader behaviors

Exceptional candidates will have:

    • Experience in development of machine learning features as part of a product team
    • Experience extracting insights from large text corpora using modern techniques
    • Prior experience in media
    • Active presence in the data science community, as evidenced by workshop or conference presentations, contributions to data science open source projects, involvement in pro bono data science initiatives, etc.
Compensation We are proud to offer our team members a competitive compensation plan that includes stock options for full-time employees and a 401K program with a match, excellent options for health, dental, and vision plans, along with multiple other perks, including parental leave, $30/month book
stipend, a yearly budget for Learning and Development, and flexible hours with unlimited vacation.
