Away is a global lifestyle brand with a mission to transform travel through products and content that inspire people to get away more. In support of our mission, we are seeking a Senior Data Analyst to join our Analytics team within our larger Data & Strategy function.

Want to help us transform the travel experience? The ideal candidate will be responsible for embedding yourself into cross-functional teams across the business in order to turn data into information, information into insights, and insights into key business decisions that steer the strategic direction of the company. The right candidate is a self-starter who can become a proactive driver of infrastructure and analytical improvements in order to develop and scale our data infrastructure, and ultimately change the way the company interacts with data. You will report to our Senior Manager of Data Analytics based in our New York City office.

As a company that values diversity, equity, and inclusion, Away seeks individuals of all backgrounds and experiences to apply for this opportunity. We're creating an environment where everyone can thrive. Our customers are global and diverse, so we're building a team that is too. Through initiatives like our employee resource groups, Anti-racism training and bias prevention initiatives, we're building the cultural foundation that gives people the emotional and physical space to bring their authentic selves to work.

What you'll do:

  • Become the driver of conversations with cross-functional team leads to help inform strategy and support data-driven decision-making (including Marketing, Global Operations & Supply Chain, Product Merchandising, Retail, and Digital)
  • Work with key stakeholders to transform business needs into powerful data models, visualizations, and dynamic reports
  • Help develop and maintain our data pipeline and business intelligence tools to make data accessible to all teams across the company
  • Foster your own curiosity through self-driven projects that meaningfully inform how we work - making us more impactful, more efficient, and more thoughtful
  • Work on ad-hoc analytics projects that span cross-functionally and involve more intensive data manipulation and modeling

Who you are:

  • You have 2-3 years of professional experience in a data-intensive role, ideally with hands-on modeling experience at a high-growth company
  • You're a self starter and able to act autonomously, including asking questions and adapting processes within an ever-changing environment
  • You have ample experience with cross-functional partnerships and the ability to put the right people in the right room to facilitate meaningful conversation
  • You can easily communicate technical concepts to a wide variety of audiences - from engineers to non-technical business stakeholders
  • Experience with business intelligence and web analytics tools (we use Looker and Amplitude!) or third-party platforms is must
  • Basic SQL skills preferred, but not required
  • Bonus points for experience with dbt and a clear understanding of ETL, data warehousing, and data modeling concepts and practices
  • Passionate about transforming travel (but that's a given!)

About Away:

Away is a global lifestyle brand with a mission to transform travel through products and content that inspire people to get away more. The company launched in 2016 with one perfectly designed carry-on and has since expanded to offer an array of luggage and travel essentials built for the modern traveler.
