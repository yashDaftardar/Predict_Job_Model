What you will do

  • Closely work with product managers and business partners to come up with innovative ideas and solutions on improving the processes.
  • Define data models that tie together disparate data across the entire organization.
  • Design and implement large scale centralized data warehousing infrastructure for the Finance organization.
  • Design, implement and maintain ETL pipelines.
  • Create data transformation logics to convert raw data to fit the new data models that make sense for the users.
  • Continuously design, develop and test data driven solutions.
  • Gain technical expertise in building a data platform at scale to solve business, product and technical use cases
  • Obtain hands-on experience with Google Cloud Platform and technology/languages such as BigQuery, Scala, Scio and Docker
  • Work hand-in-hand with the data science community to understand various insights that affect our users
  • Gain cross departmental exposure and flexibility to engage with many teams in the company in different countries in the world.
  • Communicate insights and recommendations to key collaborators, engineering and product partners

  • Strong analytical and problem solving ability
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Experience with data warehouses such as Redshift, Snowflake, or BigQuery
