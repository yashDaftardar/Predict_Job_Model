MetroStar Systems is an award winning IT management consulting firm that works with over 20 public sector clients across law enforcement, foreign affairs, military, and regulatory. We're a group of self-starters who've deliver solutions for citizens, warfighters, and government executives striving for digital transformation across the Government.

We are looking for a Senior Data Scientist that is passionate about research, design, develop, and test various AI/ML frameworks and models.

You're a great fit if:

  • You enjoy working with team members, IT business partners, and AWS infrastructure/technology teams to meet customer requirements
  • You understand end-end delivery of automation/AI use cases from pilot/design to release
  • You have experience with various AI/ML implementations and map it against applicable use cases.
  • You have experience in defining, designing, and train models for use in the isomorphic and production models.

You'll love our position if:
(This is the stuff you MUST have to be a fit)

  • 2+ years of experience with relational databases and/or AWS RDS Databases
  • 2+ years of experience in open source programming languages for large scale data analysis such as Python, R, and Scala
  • Experience with relational databases (SQL, Amazon Aurora, etc.) and data services
  • Experience with cloud-based machine learning services
  • Proficiency in Python, Java, Scala, or C++
  • Experience in building and deploying ML models using libraries such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, MXNet, Michelangelo, etc.
  • Bachelor's Degree plus 2 years of experience in data analytics, or Master's Degree plus 2 years of experience in data analytics, or PhD

We'll REALLY love you if:
(This is the bonus stuff that makes you stand out)

  • Development Experience with Conda, Jupyter, or Dask
  • Experience deploying models based on Deep Learning on isomorphic or generalized use cases
  • Experience coordinating with other Analysts using notebooks, repositories, and pipelines
  • You have an established portfolio of publications, projects and/or speaking engagements.
  • 3+ years working with cloud software design using distributed caching
  • Advanced knowledge of big data and machine learning technologies, especially in AWS and/or Google
