MITRE’s newly established Artificial Intelligence and Autonomy Innovation Center consists of a multi-generational, diverse team united by a passion and a sense of urgency to harness AI and Autonomy to help solve problems in the public interest. We discover, create, lead through empowering an inclusive community of collaborative innovators, learners, and technical creatives.

Within the Artificial Intelligence and Autonomy Innovation Center the Natural Language Processing Department focuses on developing, evaluating and integrating technologies that process the vast amounts of “unstructured” language in all its forms. The breadth of MITRE’s sponsors and our corporate missions drive our work across the full range of Natural Language Processing (NLP), including speech processing and generation, information extraction, machine translation, summarization, multi-lingual information retrieval, identity technologies and more. Our work in NLP benefits from the Department’s broad expertise in advanced machine learning.

The Natural Language Processing Department is seeking a leader to strategically shape our growing and multi-disciplinary team, driving innovation in all areas of human language technologies. The Department Manager will be responsible for setting the technical direction of the Department's capabilities, growing a team of world-class talent, supporting development of new opportunities, and building collaborative relationships across MITRE, our sponsors, industry and academia.

Responsibilities include:

  • Hiring, developing and retaining world-class staff

  • Serving as a mentor and fostering a culture of technical creativity and mission impact

  • Developing the next generation of leadership – thought leaders, project leaders, and people leaders

  • Advancing the Department’s capabilities through innovation, external partnering, and work program application in alignment with Division strategy

  • Collaborating across Innovation Centers and Program Divisions to provide cross-cutting and impactful solutions

  • Educating MITRE project leaders and sponsors on Department capabilities, expertise, and impact

  • Partnering with work programs to shape impactful work that benefits our sponsors and users and to match Department staff with meaningful and technically relevant work

  • Ensuring that Department staff performance and compensation management activities are fair and transparent

  • Responding to requests for information about the Department and its technical capabilities

  • Identifying and pursuing opportunities to improve the inclusion and diversity of the Department's work and staff
