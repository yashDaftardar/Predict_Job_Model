We are one SAIC, innovative, passionate, and empowered. Always acting with integrity and inclusion. We are excited about what tomorrow brings and with our values to guide us, there is no limit to what we can accomplish.

SAIC is seeking a Senior Data Analyst to join a proactive and impactful team.

  • The analyst will provide analytical judgment and trend analysis based on research and comparisons with past products.
  • You must be able to analyze complex data sets using commercial and customer software. The ideal candidate will obtain data (structured and unstructured) from multiple sources, synthesize it, and present the results effectively and concisely in written and graphic form.
  • The analyst will interact with customers to coordinate requirements and resolve data questions.
  • The analyst should have experience with the broader customer organization. Knowledge of field and technical operations related to the specific customer group is a plus.
  • The candidate must have the ability to research, analyze information, and work on several tasks simultaneously with minimal supervision.

Qualifications

Basic Qualifications:

  • Must have current TS/SCI with polygraph clearance
  • Must have a Bachelor's Degree in a related field (or 4 years of relevant experience may substitute in lieu of degree) and 10+ years of related experience
  • Requires minimum of 8 years of relevant experience in the customer organization and familiarity with issues involving field operations, intelligence tasking, collection, processing, analysis, and dissemination
  • Must have expertise in one or more of the following disciplines: business analytics, performance metrics, statistics, data verification and validation, data visualization, or intelligence analysis
  • Must have ability to apply qualitative data analysis methods to situational analysis and business analytics problems
  • Must be an expert in using Microsoft Excel for qualitative analysis
  • Must be skilled in creating and delivering briefings.
