Job Summary:

The Machine Learning Engineer creates and implements machine learning models to address engineering and operational issues and drive business decisions. The Machine Learning Engineer takes theoretical data science models and helps scale them to the level of model production for final prediction. To do this job successfully, you need exceptional skills in statistics and programming and preferably knowledge of data science and software engineering. Your ultimate goal will be to shape and build efficient self-learning applications.

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Research, design and develop new-generation machine learning tools and applications based on the business needs
  • Understand and transform data science prototypes. Select the Right Training Data Sets for ML Model Development
  • Develop and train the models and tuning their hyperparameters. Perform model tests and experiments.
  • Perform statistical analysis and fine-tune the test results. Choose and implement the right machine learning algorithm
  • Extend existing ML libraries and frameworks to reduce re-work in future projects.
  • Work closely with engineering, operations and executive teams and translate their requirements or thoughts into solutions

Essential Qualifications:

  • Advanced math skills (linear algebra, Bayesian statistics, group theory)
  • Good understanding and strong knowledge in algorithms and statistics
  • Understanding of data structures, data modeling and software architecture
  • Hands-on experience with machine learning frameworks (like Keras or PyTorch) and libraries (like scikit-learn)
  • Hands-on experience with scripting and programming languages like Python, R, GoLang, Julia, Java, C++
  • Knowledge of relational NoSQL and SQL databases like Oracle, Postgres, MySQL, Cassandra
  • Knowledge of software and tools like Kafka, Spark, Hadoop, Tableau, JMP
  • Knowledge of workflow management and pipeline tools like Airflow, Luigi and Azkaban
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving & communication skills
  • Ability to identify and analyze data patterns and quality discrepancies
  • Experience leading projects and coordinating with cross-functional teams
  • Experience documenting metrics and interpreting them as indicators of data quality.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Good understanding of various models and phases of SDLC
  • Experience working in an Agile environment
  • Knowledge of Docker and Kubernetes is a plus
  • Knowledge of Windows and Linux in Desktop and Server environment is a plus
