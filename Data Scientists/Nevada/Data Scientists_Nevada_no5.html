Konami Gaming, Inc. entered the US gaming market in the 2000s and we’ve spent the last two decades moving the industry forward with breakthrough games, head turning cabinets, and a casino management system that brings it all together. Over that time, we’ve garnered our share of awards and established a corporate culture of success and innovation.

A world leader in systems and game development, Konami can offer you the best of both worlds - stability within a dynamic, creative environment. We are excited to announce that Konami Gaming Inc. is growing and expanding into new product lines, including premium and standard games, and new market segments. As we enter a new growth period for the Company, we are expanding our reach to attract top talent in game studio operations, technology and product development.

We are on the hunt for passionate, honest, and fun individuals to join the future of play. Your future is here at Konami Gaming, Inc!

We are currently seeking candidates to fill the position of Mathematician . As a mathematician, you will be responsible for creating mathematical models for multiple games and researching and analyzing games from a game/math balance perspective.

What you will be doing:

  • Creates the mathematical models for game production based on producer directives in a schedule driven environment.
  • Creates proper game balance using mathematical models, utilizing Windows based PCs, MS Office, and Visual Studio (C#) using a proprietary Konami tool set.
  • Generate mathematical models for multiple markets for the same game – Class III, Class II, Bingo, Lottery, etc.
  • Facilitates the distribution of approved mathematical material and files to support product release.
  • Prepares relevant mathematical information for presentation needs, executive review, testing and compliance requirements.
  • Provides answers and explanations to relevant mathematical questions for producers, engineers, sales, marketing, testing, compliance requirements and other departments as necessary.
  • Provides all mathematical resource materials as needed to produce a game.
  • Brainstorms innovative and original game play mechanics for producer team.
  • Integrates new and innovative game play mechanics into proprietary tool chain using Excel and C#.

What we want you to have:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics/Statistics or equivalent experience; clear understanding of statistics and probability.
  • 2 years related experience in the gaming industry or professional programming experience preferred.
  • Minimum intermediate level understanding and usage of Microsoft Office products with an emphasis on Excel.
  • Ability to read, analyze, and interpret technical documents and governmental regulations.
  • Ability to develop expertise in slot game mechanics.
  • C# is mandatory for operation in this position.
  • This position is based in Las Vegas, NV.
