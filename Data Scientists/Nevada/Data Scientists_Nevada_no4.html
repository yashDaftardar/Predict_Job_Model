Our Media Buying & Optimization team is looking for a Data Scientist who can help with out Performance Marketing Analytics. This role is critical for Vshred to continue driving incredible marketing performance across the globe. The individual will work closely with the Media Buying and Optimization team leaders to find insightful answers that can accelerate growth and profitability.

Job Description
This role will tackle multiple and exciting challenges including customer acquisition, retention, marketing attribution, experimentation, LTV, and much more. If you enjoy diving into an incredible amount of data to find answers that will improve marketing performance in a fast growing company, then we believe this role is for you!

You will…

  • Work collaboratively to build a platform for scientific discovery across all paid media channels.
  • Create quantitative analysis to provide insights that help the media buying & optimization department better understand marketing performance, customer quality & user behaviors.
  • Build full-cycle analytics experiments, reports, and dashboards using SQL, R, Python, or other scripting and statistical tools.
  • Work with our Media Buying & Optimization teams to automate our marketing efforts.

Your resume should look something like this…

  • 5+ years work experience in a data science role
  • You have a degree in Math, Physics, Statistics, Economics, Computer Science, or similar domain (Ph.D., Master’s or advanced degree preferred)
  • Built systems that forecast or recommend decisions under uncertainty.
  • Managed evolving data delivery processes, balancing short-term and long-term delivery expectations.
  • You have experience working with media mix modeling, marketing measurement & attribution, targeting & creative optimization, online purchase funnel optimization, user segmentation, cohort analyses, time series analyses, regression models, etc.
  • You are proficient in one or more analytics & visualization tools (e.g. Chartio, Looker, Tableau).
  • You are able to take ambiguous problems and solve them in a structured, hypothesis- driven, data-supported way.
