Full Job Description

Overview:
The Data Scientist will support data-related activities in institutional effectiveness (e.g., institutional research, assessment, planning and accreditation) and in research (e.g., biomedical, health-related and educational research projects).
Responsibilities:
The Data Scientist will:

1. Provide data analysis for a diversity of projects:

a. Institutional-level information (e.g., institutional research, assessment, planning and accreditation)

b. Biomedical, health-related and educational research projects


2. Advise research investigators on research design and analytical approaches to optimize study quality

3. Develop informal and formal reports that effectively detail and summarize results

4. Develop and present educational materials on research-related topics as needed

5. Perform other duties as needed/assigned
Qualifications:
Education :

Master's degree (doctorate preferred) from an accredited college or university in an appropriate discipline (e.g., statistics, mathematics, biological or physical sciences, social or behavioral sciences, or other fields with expertise to satisfy qualifications listed above), and at least 1 year of experience in study design, data collection and statistical analysis

OR

Bachelor's degree from an accredited college or university in an appropriate discipline (e.g., statistics, mathematics, biological or physical sciences, social or behavioral sciences, or other fields with expertise to satisfy qualifications listed above), and at least 4 years of experience in study design, data collection and statistical analysis

Experience :

  • Demonstrated experience with collection, analysis and presentation of qualitative and quantitative data in narrative, tabular, graphical, visualization and/or interactive formats
  • Demonstrated experience working with statistical software packages (e.g., R, SPSS, JASP, Stata, SAS)
  • Demonstrated experience working with database/query tools (e.g., Microsoft Excel, Access, SQL), and word processing and presentation tools (e.g., Microsoft Word, PowerPoint)
  • Demonstrated experience with communication of statistical analysis results
  • Demonstrated experience with study design and/or experimental design
  • Demonstrated experience working collaboratively to accomplish research or institutional effectiveness aims
  • Experience working in a higher education setting
  • Experience with applying tools and strategies for data visualization and data mining (Desired)
  • One year experience with Tableau (Desired)
  • Two years experience with R statistical software (Desired)
  • Experience with analyzing large datasets (Desired)
  • Experience working in a medical school and/or health-care setting (Desired)
  • Experience working with clinical research studies and/or clinical research data analysis (Desired)

Skills & Abilities :

  • Strong analytical skills
  • Strong written, verbal and interpersonal communication skills
  • Ability to work independently and complete multiple tasks with strong attention to detail under time-sensitive conditions
  • Service orientation and commitment to excellence and personal growth
  • Strong general computer/database skills, including experience working with database query tools, designing and manipulating spreadsheets, producing documents using word processing and desktop publishing tools, and producing slide-style presentations
