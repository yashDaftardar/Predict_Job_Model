Full Job Description

Function/Duties of Position:
OHSU is looking for an image and quantitative data analyst/engineer to join the laboratory of Dr. Tania Vu. This person will develop and apply imaging method/processes on different commercial and custom data platforms to extract image data from next-generation, high-content single cell imaging microscopy platform for precision oncology.

Individuals applying for this position must be:
  • strategic, action driven problem solvers
  • flexible in changing direction and methodologies
  • able to excel at managing multiple priorities
  • successful in communicating verbally and in written form
  • comfortable with fulfilling their position responsibilities in a teamwork/collaborative environment
Responsibilities include:
  • Validating and establishing image processing methods to extract relevant information from imaging data
  • Rapidly prototyping methods for internal experiments & early R&D
  • Creating high-quality, robust implementations of production algorithms.
This position will involve self-management of clinical-translation, cancer-focused data pipelines and teamwork interaction in team-collaborative projects composed of biologists and clinicians, physicists, computational scientists in the Knight Cancer Institute and Center for Spatial Systems Biology, as well as industrial partners.
Required Qualifications:
  • Master’s Degree with major courses in relevant field and 3 years of relevant experience OR Bachelor’s Degree with major courses in field of research and 5 years of relevant experience
  • Excellent understanding of mathematical underpinnings of algorithms
  • A mind-set for rapidly implementing practical solutions
  • Experience with:
    • image platforms
    • MATLAB programming
    • basic coding
    • basic statistics
Preferred Qualifications:
  • Expertise in microscopy/imaging/design of imaging software
  • Proven ability to organize research results for publication and presentations
