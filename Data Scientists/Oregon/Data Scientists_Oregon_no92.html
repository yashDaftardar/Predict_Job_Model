In this exciting and challenging role as Data Scientist, you will drive the development of innovative new products using advanced machine learning and artificial intelligence. Senior Data Scientist Responsibilities
Identify valuable data sources and automate collection processes.
Undertake preprocessing of structured and unstructured data.
Analyze large amounts of information to discover trends and patterns.
Use predictive modeling and machine learning to increase and optimize customer experiences, revenue generation, prospect targeting and other business outcomes.
Present information using data visualization techniques.
Work with stakeholders throughout the organization to identify opportunities for leveraging company data to drive business solutions.
Coordinate with different functional teams e.g. product development and/or engineering to implement models and monitor outcomes.
Mine and analyze data from company databases to drive optimization and improvement of product development, marketing techniques and business strategies.
Extending companies data with third party sources of information when needed.
Enhancing data collection procedures to include information that is relevant for building analytic systems.
Processing, cleansing, and verifying the integrity of data used for analysis.
Doing ad-hoc analysis and presenting results in a clear manner.
Creating automated anomaly detection systems and constant tracking of its performance.
Assess the effectiveness and accuracy of new data sources and data gathering techniques.
Develop processes and tools to monitor and analyze model performance and data accuracy.
Actively work with internal and external customers to analyze business data needs, gather and document business and data architecture requirements.
Develop, implement and maintain data pipelines with IoT data (Telematics, Factory Robotics).
Perform data profiling, validation and acceptance testing for cloud integration.
Ensure highest levels of data accessibility, security, and quality at all stages.
Develop data and model pipelines with AzureML SDK, Azure Data factory, Databricks, Spark or related technologies.
Develop rapid prototype proof of concept solutions for viability studies.
Work within an Agile team and a dynamic, fast-moving, collaborative environment.
Communicate status to all stakeholders.
Keep current on latest data science technologies and products, including hands on evaluations and in-depth research.
