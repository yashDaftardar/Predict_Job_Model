About the job

You will be assigned to one or more projects. Your tasks will comprise from discovering and building new data sets to designing and implementing computer vision, machine learning and data science algorithms in Python/C/C++. You will have the opportunity to gain skills and hands-on knowledge, if you do not have them yet, about earth observation, data science and artificial intelligence. By tackling images from various sources you will be solving real-world planet-scale problems. You will be expected to interact with clients to understand business needs and translate technical results into relevant information. You will work in a dynamic environment to deliver high quality results against aggressive schedules.


This position is based in Spain, Argentina, Israel, Uruguay, US or Remote.


About you

You must be an eager learner who is familiar with programming. You are passionate, dedicated and able to work against the clock. You have a sound mathematical/statistical background focused on Deep Learning/Computer Vision/Remote Sensing and are motivated to face new challenges. You enjoy working as a team and are ready to deliver value through your work.

Required experience/skills:

  • BSc/MSc in Mathematics/Physics/Computer Science or other quantitative fields.
  • Strong software engineering skills in Python and experience with the Python scientific ecosystem.
  • Hands on experience in developing Image Processing, Computer Vision and/or Deep Learning algorithms using tools such as Scikit-learn, Scipy, Numpy, OpenCV, Keras, Pytorch, TensorFlow, Caffe, etc.
  • Creative problem solving. Inquisitive mindset.
