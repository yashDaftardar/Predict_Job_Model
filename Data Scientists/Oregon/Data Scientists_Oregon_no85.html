Job Description


Intel Machine Learning Performance team is looking for software development engineer to work in new exciting area of deep learning. In this role you will be responsible for design, development, and maintenance of oneDNN, a cross-platform open source performance library providing highly vectorized and threaded building blocks for deep learning applications. The scope of work includes development of new features and algorithms optimized for Intel processors, Intel Processor Graphics, and Intel discrete GPUs, performance optimizations for current and future Intel hardware, including assembly level programming and development of hardware specific parallel algorithms.


Qualifications


You must possess the below minimum qualifications to be initially considered for this position. Experience listed below would be obtained through a combination of your school-work/ classes/ research and/or relevant previous job and/or internship experiences.

Minimum Qualifications:

  • Masters with 6+ years of experience or PhD with 4+ years of experience in Mathematics or Physics or Computer Science or Computer Engineering or Electrical Engineering or Mechanical Engineering or Aerospace engineering or computational science or a related technical discipline

4+ years of experience in the following areas:

  • Software libraries design and architecture
  • Performance engineering and software performance optimizations
  • Mathematics, preferably linear algebra solvers, matrix-vector operations, and Fast Fourier Transforms
  • Algorithms and data structures
  • C and C++
  • Software development on Linux
  • Low level optimizations for Intel Architecture processors, OpenCL, SYCL/DPC++, C for Metal or CUDA
  • Parallel programming (OpenMP, TBB, or MPI)

Preferred qualifications:

  • 5 years+ High performance computing (HPC) applications development
  • 1 year+ Machine learning and deep learning algorithms
  • 1 year+ Agile software development environment
  • 1 year+ Intel development tools.
