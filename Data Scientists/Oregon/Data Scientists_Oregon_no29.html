Who are we looking for

We are looking for a hands-on data science director for our Personalized Experiences Data Science team. This role is responsible for helping develop our algorithm roadmap for Nike’s personalization engines and serving as a technical mentor for our data scientists, engineers and product managers. Our team owns and develops state-of-the-art models to personalize the consumer journey for Nike members worldwide, including Nike App, Nike.com and SNKRS.

What will you work on

In this role, you will…

  • Help create and execute on the algorithm roadmap for personalized experiences on Nike.com and Nike apps, which includes both product and editorial content recommendation as well as UX optimization.
  • Partner with the engineering, machine learning platform, and product teams to deploy models and evaluate the A/B test results.
  • Keep up with the latest research and disseminate the most relevant new ideas in e-commerce and recommendation.
  • Mentor skilled applied scientists and engineers.
  • Partner with other data science teams including those that work on search and marketing algorithms in order to have highly aligned technical roadmaps and reuse where possible.

Who will you work with

You will be a technical leader on the Personalized Experiences Data Science team, reporting to the Director of Personalized Experiences Data Science. This team works in partnership with the Data Engineering, Digital Product, Machine Learning Platform teams and is responsible for advancing Nike’s personalized experiences across Nike’s entire e-commerce business.

Personalized Experiences Data Science is part of Nike’s Consumer Data Science team and Insights function. In addition to working with the personalization team, you will be a member of the broader data science team at Nike, and participate in our data science labs, study groups, and team activities, and attend conferences.

What you bring

  • Practiced in e-commerce and recommender systems, including familiarity with recent research published in at conferences such as SIGIR, SIGKDD and ACM RecSys.
  • Adept in machine learning, particularly in ranking and deep learning models.
  • Mastery in the architectures of large-scale recommendation engines.
  • Experience with Python, and deep learning libraries such as TensorFlow or PyTorch.
  • Experience forging partnerships with engineering, product, and analytics teams.
  • A product and entrepreneurial mentality.
  • Experience with Amazon AWS and SageMaker preferred.
