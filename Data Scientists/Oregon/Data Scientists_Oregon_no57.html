Who we are looking for:

You are intellectually curious and always up for a good challenge. You are a natural at developing internal talent and nurturing lasting client relationships. You get a rush from solving problems and working with numbers and ideas comes easily to you. Always keenly observant and can't help but try to understand what makes the world click. You are a leader, a hacker and maker, driven to create. You are someone who challenges what's possible.

What you'll do:

  • Lead | Guide your team in building data driven narratives from scratch, setting the editorial voice and philosophy, overseeing story assignments, approving narrative direction, and final client deliverables.
  • Analyze | Apply open source tools to capture, process, analyze, and visualize proprietary and public data in new ways to tell compelling and meaningful stories.
  • Collaborate | Partner closely with team members across all disciplines and at all levels to ensure team needs are being met to turn deep consumer insights into amazing consumer experiences . Form direct, trusted client relationships to understand their needs and collaboratively shape opportunities.
  • Innovate | Work with the leadership of our Data Science team to evaluate new tools and processes, developing new intellectual property for the agency, outline procedures for applying or implementation of best practices and case studies

Who you are:

  • Passionate about creativity and social media | You view the creative output as your shared responsibility. You are especially excited about the potential for creativity and innovation within the social media landscape.
  • Open and Collaborative | Navigates strong collaboration between people, processes and systems. You bring team spirit and engaged collaboration.
  • Optimistic and Resilient | You are not afraid to dig in and figure out how to work around problems. You take care of yourself and your team.
  • A builder | You're invested in the growth of the department and the agency as a whole. You want to help shape the culture, keep the tools in our toolbox sharp, and push the team to stay on top of our game.
  • Low on ego | You're flexible in your role and will jump in where needed to make the team successful. You are able to gracefully handle multiple projects in a fast-paced environment.

What you'll need:

  • 10+ years of relevant experience.
  • Advanced knowledge and working experience in advertising, research, social platforms or marketing data is critical.
  • Advanced experience in more than than one of the following:
    • Data analysis and storytelling
    • Social listening (BrandWatch, Netbase or similar experience highly preferred)
    • Advertising or marketing theory
    • Quantitative research and Statistics
  • Experience managing high-performing teams with aggressive timelines.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills with the ability to be flexible and thrive in a rapidly-evolving environment.
  • A Bachelor's degree in Sociology, Anthropology, Psychology, Philosophy, or Statistics emphasis or commensurate work experience is highly preferred.
  • We are currently open to candidates that are looking to work remotely in the United States or flexibly near our office in Portland, Oregon.
