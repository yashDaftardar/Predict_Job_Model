As one of the worldÂ´s leading manufacturers of cardio- and endovascular medical devices, BIOTRONIK is headquartered in Berlin, Germany, and represented in over 100 countries by its global workforce. The work of our committed, highly specialized, exceptionally skilled employees results in crucial advancements in the world of cardiology and changes the lives of patients every day.

BIOTRONIK is looking to add a Manager, Finance Analytics & Governance to our Finance team. The Manager, Finance Analytics & Governance will be proficient in modern business intelligence platforms and have experience including direct financial data and analytics governance. The Manager will work with executive and management teams to directly influence business development, improve operational outcomes and raise risk awareness. In addition, this leader will directly influence the tools, techniques and internal processes that are utilized to transform both internal business intelligence and data governance capabilities.

The position will lead a team of business analysts and master data professionals. The leader will be accountable for efficient and accurate administration of customer rebates, master data governance, and corporate business intelligence.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Closely interact with executive leaders in Biotronik Inc., Biotronik HQ in Germany, and Biotronik Corporate Services to analyze and drive financial performance.
  • Partner with IT to improve and/or automate steps in financial processes and systems to improve accuracy and efficiency.
  • Oversee the development of key finance and operational insights within the Qlik Sense BI platform to guide users to relevant and actionable decisions
  • Understand the decision-making process, workflows, and business and information needs of business unit heads and service manager/owners
  • Translate business needs into analytics/reporting requirements to support executive decisions and workflows with required information
  • Utilize business outcomes to recommend, prioritize, oversee and implement data governance
  • Develop and ensure data & analytics governance policies, rules, standards and best practices
  • Support data engineers with best solutions to meet data & analytics governance needs
  • Lead data steward to identify, train, educate and advocate for business data stewards on data understanding and usage
  • Escalate and manage data quality concerns as appropriate
  • Oversee the administration of customer rebates, pricing master data, commission statement generation, and account territory master data
  • Manage and develop a team of business and master data analysts

POSITION REQUIREMENTS:

  • BA/BS required, preferably in a business related field or the sciences with quantitative focus (statistics, econometrics, mathematics, etc.)
  • Master degree preferred (MS in Statistics or Economics or other quantitative discipline is preferred, or MBA with Marketing Analytics/Decision Science Concentration) degree in Accounting
  • 5-8 years of progressively expanding experience in analytics, business intelligence and data governance
  • Demonstrated ability to communicate effectively with all levels of an organization, both technical and non-technical team members
  • Proven leadership of business intelligence, analytic and data governance initiatives in fast paced, multiple project environment
  • Hands on knowledge of statistical methods and techniques (predictive modeling, segmentation, regression, etc.)
  • Working knowledge of financial systems and business intelligence tools (preferably SAP, Qlik Sense, SalesForce)
  • Proficient with MS Office
  • Proficient in SQL or data query and system interaction for use in business analysis and testing efforts
  • Excellent interpersonal skills including verbal and written communication, teamwork, and issue resolution
  • Experience presenting findings and actionable recommendations
  • Project management experience and ability to lead cross-functional teams
  • Ability to lead, develop, and motivate a team of business intelligence and master data professionals
  • Health care/medical device industry experience preferred
