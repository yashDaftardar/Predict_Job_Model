Data Science Analyst - DART Program

The Data Science Analyst is responsible for assisting our core business units, including FP&A, Performance Management, Corporate Finance, Compensation, Marketing and our Digital Innovation and Information Technology Group with analysis, report generation and special projects that may arise. This position is part of the DART (Data Analytics Rotational Training) program and is a key role in providing analysis and insight for management to make informed decisions. The DART program is looking to develop business users who understand the value behind the insights to lead analytical initiatives. Rotations enable analysts to build competencies across a spectrum of subjects to accelerate growth. The specific role may be fine-tuned based on the candidate.

The Data Science Analyst reports to multiple managers as they work through the DART program.

Responsibilities

  • Integrating data from various sources, provide high-level insight to senior management
  • Provide key information to management by assembling and summarizing data; preparing reports; making presentation of findings, analysis, and recommendations
  • Collaborate cross-departmentally to understand business objectives and accomplish projects
  • Create trend analysis and forecasting models; identify and present organized findings and opportunities
  • Assist in the development and enhancement of reporting
  • Develop broad understanding of all organizational data and intercompany processes; offer improvements to workflow
  • Participate in refining and developing process and tools that support the team objectives
  • Complete any Ad-hoc reporting or special projects as necessary
