Together, with our passionate people, we are accelerating the pace of change in the energy industry, towards a more efficient and sustainable world. The Opportunity::

Enel X, a leading provider of sustainable energy solutions, has a need for a Computational Scientist Intern to join our world-class Engineering organization. The Machine Learning Engineer Intern will assist the team in developing and implementing unique time-series forecasting algorithms for a wide variety of energy applications.


This is a six (6) month internship program from July 2021 through December 2021. Applicants must be willing to commit to a 40 hour work week schedule while employed.

Responsibilities: The Machine Learning Engineer Intern should expect to complete one or more of the following projects: - Investigate performance improvements for existing time-series forecasting solutions - Benchmark forecasting algorithms used for controlling energy storage systems - Incorporate any improvements to forecasting solution(s) to existing production software What You'll Do at Enel::
The Machine Learning Engineer Intern should expect to complete one or more of the following projects:
  • Investigate performance improvements for existing time-series forecasting solutions
  • Benchmark forecasting algorithms used for controlling energy storage systems
  • Incorporate any improvements to forecasting solution(s) to existing production software
Qualifications: - Ability to clearly communicate results of work to peers - Detail-oriented - English-fluency - Ability to schedule communications with US Pacific time zone - Knowledge of power, energy markets, and tariffs What You've Accomplished::


Required Qualifications

  • Working towards advanced Degree in Engineering, Computer Science, Mathematics or Operations Research
  • Experience with machine learning, deep learning, and optimization
  • Experience using python
  • Familiarity with Git version control
  • Experience building, packaging, and/or deploying containerized Python applications
  • Familiarity with Jira or other agile software development
