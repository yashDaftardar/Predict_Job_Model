Job Description


Intel's AI Scale Team applies Intel's technologies to solve our customer's most complex AI use cases in various industries like Healthcare, life sciences, Energy, Industrial and Retail.

As a deep learning software engineer, your responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • You will apply machine learning techniques to solve customer challenges and optimize deep learning pipelines.
  • As part of this role, you will be expected and encouraged to keep your ML and Data Science skills current, and will have the opportunity to work with internal and external partners, putting AI solutions into production.
  • You will spend time working directly with internal and external partners to understand their needs, apply your data science expertise to ideate and solve complex AI problems.
  • Deep learning model selection, optimizing performance of AI models, deployment optimizations, and end-to-end AI solutions architecture.
  • Working with customers, you will also anticipate emerging trends, and work with the product groups to influence product roadmaps.
  • Presenting complex ideas to internal and external technical leaders and CxOs.
  • Selection and development of models using a variety of techniques and toolsets.


Qualifications


Minimum Required Qualifications:
Bachelor’s degree in Electrical/Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Applied Mathematics, or a relevant science/engineering discipline.

4+ years of experience in the following areas:

  • Applying AI techniques and/or developing applications to real-world complex AI problems.
  • Experience with one or more AI frameworks (Tensorflow, Pytorch).
  • Python and C++
  • Systems architecture.
  • Development of commercial AI solutions and/or the design and implementation of analytic algorithms.

Additional Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s degree preferred.
  • Development of AI solutions based on client input and domain research.
