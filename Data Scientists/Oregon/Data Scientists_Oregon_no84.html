Full Job Description

Aspen Capital is in search of a Lead Data Scientist to join the Portfolio Analytics team. This position will build and implement key projects for key financial products in the commercial and residential real estate space. You will have direct impact on the business and will work closely with senior leadership. Our ideal candidate is passionate about driving key strategic projects that will move the business forward.

Day to day you will

  • Drives continuous efforts to develop models from our own and external data to improve to meet strategic business objectives
  • Leverage trending technology and modern data science techniques to draw insights from data and analytics
  • Deliver crisp communication of recommended solutions to senior leaders, operators and other stakeholders
  • Provide guidance and mentorship to junior team members
  • Assist with other ad-hoc projects as needed

  • Well versed with applying Statistical and ML techniques
  • Experience working with big data technologies such as Hadoop and Spark preferred
