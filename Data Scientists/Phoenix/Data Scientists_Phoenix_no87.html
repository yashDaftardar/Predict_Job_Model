Full Job Description

Position Overview:

US Nephrology (USN) / Global Nephrology Solution (GNS) is the largest Physician-driven Nephrology organization in USA. It’s 350+ physicians provide care to over 400,000 Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) and Dialysis patients.

RCO Analytics, a wholly owned subsidiary of USN, is a Healthcare IT company and uses advanced scientific techniques, forecasting, and machine learning to predict clinical and financial outcomes in order to guide investments and deliver results that improve health outcomes and reduce costs.

Responsibilities and Expectations

In this exciting and high growth environment, the Data Architect will have a unique opportunity to advance technology architecture and data management framework to support the strategic growth initiatives.

  • Lead the design of big data solutions for the future, utilizing progressive ideas and strategies for managing the flow and storage of information, that will enable the growth and success of the organization.
  • Provide conceptual, architectural and technical leadership for all aspects of the company’s enterprise data warehouse initiatives.
  • Define the data architecture framework, standards, and principles, including modeling, metadata, security, reference data.
  • Demonstrate hands-on expertise in the design, development, integration, tuning and production support of the data warehouse stack: data management and modeling, ETL, operational data store, data warehouse and datamarts
  • Design the technology framework, including data streams, integrations, transformations, databases, and data warehouses.
  • Creating star, snowflake data models utilizing best practices and implementing them according to current and future data volumes/requirements as well as for the reporting application used to provide the information to the business executives.
  • Participate in data strategy and road map exercises, data architecture definition, business intelligence / data warehouse product selection, design and implementation.
  • Support the build of machine learning (ML) and artificial intelligence (AI) powered predictive and sophisticated models based on large structured and unstructured data set.
  • Bring new ideas, concepts and cutting-edge technology to the organization.
  • Incorporate data security and HIPAA compliance, along with data protection (transit and at-rest) to build a solid security model
  • Investigate, troubleshoot and correct data and user related system errors, and ensures data integrity.
  • Performs other job-related duties as assigned by supervisor.


JOB REQUIREMENTS

Qualifications

  • At least 10 years of hands-on experience in architecture, design or development of enterprise data solutions, applications, and integrations
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Systems or related is required
  • Demonstrated industry leadership in the fields of database, data warehousing or data sciences.
  • Deep understanding of database and analytical technologies in the healthcare industry SSIS, Data Warehouse design, BI reporting and Dashboard development
  • Advanced knowledge of Business Intelligence and Analytics domains - Predictive analytics, Prescriptive models, text analytics, statistics, machine learning, data management, data architecture, NLP, data governance, etc
  • Knowledge of data architecture and design patterns and the ability to apply them
  • Ability to learn and adapt quickly to new business domains and technologies
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills
  • Ability to conceptualize and articulate ideas clearly and concisely
  • Excellent communication, presentation and interpersonal skills
  • Hands-on experience with modern enterprise data architectures and data toolsets (ex: data warehouse, data marts, data lake, 3NF normalization and dimensional models, modeling tools, profiling tools)
  • Ability to manage multiple projects and prioritize workload
  • Self-starter, take ownership of projects
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, result-driven, and complex healthcare setting.
  • Healthcare experience preferred
