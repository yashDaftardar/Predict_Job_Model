Job Description


Who we are:
AI and Deep Learning are driving transformation in Internet of Things (IoT) vertical markets globally. Intel's Edge.AI team is expanding our OpenVINO product line and is looking for a Competitive Market Intelligence Engineer to develop and execute solution strategies for the fastest growing segments of the AI market.


We are part of the Internet of Things Group, which is a fast growing multi-billion-dollar business unit today. The industry is accelerating rapidly, and we are looking for the right team members to help drive our next wave of growth as an organization


The Competitive Market Intelligence Engineer responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • Partner with Product and Go-to-Market teams to provide industry expertise on the ecosystem of tools and platforms for Machine Learning.
  • Proactively track AI landscape and ecosystem platforms/technologies.
  • Drive technical competitive analysis, assessing the position of Intel's Edge.AI portfolio of products.
  • Drive competitive analysis, SWOT analysis and authoring competitive response approaches.
  • Establishing a feature level gap analysis & drive product strategy recommendations into the Engineering teams.
  • Drive recommendations for future product enhancements and creating competitive market intelligence briefs for internal/external audiences.


Willingness to periodically travel to industry and/or customer events.


Soft Skills:

  • Willingness to work with multi-cultural teams.
  • Willing to be inclusive and collaborate.


Qualifications


Education:

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or related field with 6+ years of industry experience or Master's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or related field with 4+ years of industry experience or PhD in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or related field with 2+ years of industry experience.

Minimum Required Qualifications:

5+ years of experience in the following:

  • Building, training and optimizing ML/DL/AI models.
  • Existing ML/DL/AI frameworks, industry tools and development platforms.
  • Experience to synthesize feature level analysis across multiple external tools and provide clear product feedback and recommendations to product owners and engineering teams.
  • Experience to communicate highly technical concepts to a variety of audiences, including executive level and technical decision-makers.

Inside this Business Group


Employees of the Internet of Things Solutions Group (IOTG) have an exciting opportunity before them: To grow Intel's leadership position in the rapidly evolving IoT market by delivering the best silicon, software and services that meet a wide range of customer requirements - from Intel® Xeon® to Intel® Quark®. The group, a fresh, dynamic collaboration between Intel's Intelligent Solutions Group and Wind River Systems, utilizes assets from across all of Intel in such areas as industrial automation, retail, automobiles and aerospace. The IOTG team is dedicated to helping Intel drive the next major growth inflection through productivity and new business models that are emerging as a result of IoT.
