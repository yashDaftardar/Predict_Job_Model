Full Job Description

Primary Purpose and Essential Functions: Take raw data from systems, databases and/or other sources and translate into impactful solutions for designated area. Compile and gather data across functional areas and provide in-depth analysis of what that data means in current business environment/culture. Integrate findings into existing strategic business goals/plans.
  • Develop, maintain, and enhance complex mathematical models, algorithms, and formulas to analyze data, prove theories, and solve problems.
  • Transform data complexities and results into a concise story with clear takeaways.
  • Identify the business data needed to produce the most useful insights.
  • Collaborate with executive leadership to identify and prioritize problems.
  • Analyze all possible solutions and present findings to leadership.
  • Utilize SQL query development to gather data for models.
  • Utilize Tableau to communicate results.
  • Collaborate with Business Intelligence Team for production level reporting and visualization tools.
  • Seek opportunities to broaden and deepen knowledge base and proficiency.
  • Challenge established policies and procedures when appropriate.
  • Proactively work to assist others in achieving the organization's objectives.
  • Skills: Must have data mining and analytical mindset. Must possess ability to effectively communicate orally and in writing. Must be able to multitask, prioritize and manage time effectively. Must be inquisitive and enjoy learning.
  • Education: Masters in Statistics or Mathematics or related field, or equivalent combination of education and/or experience required. PhD preferred.
  • Experience Required: Previous experience in transportation industry preferred. Previous experience working with SQL in Impala, DB2 or SQL Server required. Previous experience working with analytical tools such as Tableau, Power BI or similar tools preferred. Previous experience presenting data driven solutions to leadership preferred. Previous experience with Python, R or SAS required. Previous experience with Gurobi Optimization preferred.
