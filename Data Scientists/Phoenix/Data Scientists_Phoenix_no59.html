Job Description


The mission of the Emerging Growth and Incubation (EGI) group is to create an environment to identify, incubate and scale new billion-dollar businesses leveraging Intel's competitive advantages. EGI is a fast paced, dynamic and startup-like organization that is responsible for incubating new ideas within Intel. We have over 1,500 employees globally. Our portfolio includes HW, SW, Solutions and SaaS businesses.

Borne out of EGI, OmniBridge is a team of innovators using cutting edge AI to develop an innovative communication tool that goes beyond a simple American Sign Language translation app by creating immediate and seamless conversation between the hearing and the deaf. Achieve your career best with a newly formed internal venture where you will be working in a dynamic start-up environment. We are seeking a Machine Learning Software Engineer to develop tools and techniques to scale our AI operations while reporting to our Data/Machine and Deep Learning Lead.

I n this role, your daily agenda will be anything but ordinary. On a regular basis, it will include but not be limited to:

  • Building machine learning based products/solutions, which provide descriptive, diagnostic, predictive, or prescriptive models based on data.
  • Using or developing machine learning algorithms, such as supervised and unsupervised learning, deep learning, reinforcement learning, Bayesian analysis and others, to solve applied problems in various disciplines such as Data Analytics, Computer Vision, Robotics, etc.
  • Interacting with users to define requirements for breakthrough product/solutions.
  • In either research environments or specific product environments, utilizing current programming methodologies to translate machine learning models and data processing methods into software.
  • Completing programming, testing, debugging, documentation and/or deployment of the solution/products.
  • Engineering Big Data computing frameworks, data modeling and other relevant software tools.
  • Building machine learning model APIs and deploying models into the cloud and in-premise device
  • Designing version controlled, testable, and reproducible production code for model deployment
  • Designing and implementing the optimal machine learning architecture
  • Identifying and mitigating the challenges of putting models in production such as sending and receiving requests from deployed machine learning models


Qualifications


Candidates must possess the below minimum qualifications to be initially considered for this position. Preferred qualifications are in addition to the minimum requirements and are considered a plus factor in identifying top candidates. Requirements listed below would be obtained through a combination of Industry job relevant experience, internship experiences and or schoolwork/classes/research

Education

  • Bachelor's in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or related fields (Statistics, Applied Math, Computational Neuroscience).


Minimum Qualifications:

  • 3+ years of experience developing in Python and C++ for complex multi-language systems.
  • 3+ years building end-to-end data or software systems as a software engineer, back-end engineer, machine learning (ML) engineer, data engineer or similar.
  • 2+ years of model implementation or integration into an end-user application
  • 3+ years of experience developing machine learning based solutions (including performing data engineering and data analysis activities) using leading frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, or XGBoost.


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master's or PhD
  • Experience working with cloud computing and ML based systems.
  • Experience in scalable machine learning methodologies and best practices.
  • Experience building systems with deployment of ML (Triton Inference Server, Flask, Docker container, Kubernetes, and AWS).
  • Experience building machine-learning infrastructure that have been successfully delivered to customers.
  • Experience converting the models and optimizing on GPU.
  • Agile development, including knowledge of CI/CD toolsets.
