The Senior Provider Data Analyst of Provider Depiction, Analytics & Recovery (PDAR) is responsible for the project oversight and data analytics in order to meet a multimillion dollar targeted metric for the Medicare & Retirement line of business. The assigned projects include, but are not limited to: contract defect identification, working extensively with data and IT/Reporting areas to generate reports/analyze reports, data analytics, working with other areas to perform system corrections. Manage multiple recovery projects concurrently, while maintaining a high level of quality and commitment to project timeframes (gathering of requirements to execution)

You’ll enjoy the flexibility to telecommute* from anywhere within United States as you take on some tough challenges.

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Analyze provider and claims data to identify trends and issues related to provider loading.
  • Coordinate work activities with other departments including Network Management, Claims, Recoveries etc.
  • Assist to develop plans to meet project objectives.
  • Identify and resolve operational issues
  • Maintain status reports and communicate project status weekly/as needed
  • Analyze and investigate provider and claims data to result in recovery opportunities
  • Provide leadership with suggestions for possible recovery opportunities
  • Provide feedback on findings
  • Oversee assigned projects to completion
  • Creative thinking that allows for the formulation of defect scenarios and/or trends based on past professional experience, trends in contract and/or claims data, audit data, etc.


You’ll be rewarded and recognized for your performance in an environment that will challenge you and give you clear direction on what it takes to succeed in your role as well as provide development for other roles you may be interested in.

Required Qualifications:

  • 2+ years of data analysis and/or audit experience within the healthcare industry
  • 2+ years of experience in managing cross functional projects and creating presentations
  • 2+ years of experience in Contract Loading, Claims Processing/adjusting, Claims Recoveries, and or Reimbursement Analytics
  • Facility provider contract loading/claims/auditing knowledge
  • Professional provider contract loading/claims/auditing knowledge
  • Experience performing comprehensive research and identifying provider contract load issues, erroneous billing or reimbursement issues, and/or irregularities or trends that depict claim monetary overpayments to the providers
  • Intermediate level of proficiency in tracking, trending and reporting on metrics
  • Intermediate level of proficiency in MS Excel and MS Word
  • Experience in contract interpretation and industry standard reimbursement guidelines
  • If you need to enter a work site for any reason, you will be required to screen for symptoms using the ProtectWell mobile app, Interactive Voice Response (i.e., entering your symptoms via phone system) or similar UnitedHealth Group-approved symptom screener.
