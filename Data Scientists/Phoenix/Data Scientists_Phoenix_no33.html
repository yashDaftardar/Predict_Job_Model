About the team and position

Do you get excited by talking to all the smartest math and stats nerds? Are you passionate about the best in talent acquisition and about understanding the difference between network optimization and predictive modeling? If you love working with scientists on the cutting edge of all things data science and analytics, this is the role for you.

We're looking for an experienced Analytics Technical Recruiter to join a rapidly growing organization. The Analytics Recruiter will have the unique opportunity to build, develop, and execute full life-cycle talent acquisition for Carvana's Analytics and Data Science functions. No day will be exactly the same. You will be a job board and social media sourcing pro. You will interview, track, and follow up with all candidates, and develop strong business relationships with your (really awesome) coworkers. Most importantly, you will see your insights have an immediate, tangible impact every day in a well-funded and rapidly growing company.

What you'll be doing

  • You will be responsible for full life-cycle recruiting for all corporate functions including but not limited to Operations Analytics, Product Analytics, and Data Science, taking candidates from job application to job offer.
  • You will keep up-to-date with our business initiatives in all things analytics and learn from some of the best analytics professionals about the latest tools and analytics methods.
  • You will think outside the box and leverage your experience and creativity to match the right candidates to the right careers.
  • You are a strong business partner with the ability to connect with multiple levels of hiring teams, keeping them engaged from in-take conversation to new hire start date.
  • You're constantly thinking of new ways to improve all things recruiting including the candidate experience, interview practices, funnel-building, reporting, quality of hire, hiring manager experience, and more.
  • You will represent Carvana at various hiring and field recruitment events.
  • You will provide input on forming Carvana recruiting best practices (we LOVE some good collaboration!).
  • Other duties as assigned.

What you should know

  • A Bachelor's degree from an accredited undergraduate institution.
  • At least 3 years of work experience as an Analytics and Data Science Recruiter in a full-cycle recruiting role, preferably in-house at an e-commerce or fintech company. We expect a deep understanding of all things data science in this role.
  • Experience with virtual recruitment (social media, LinkedIn, resume sourcing) is required.
  • Experience working with an ATS, preferably Greenhouse.
  • Exceptional interpersonal and written and verbal communication skills.
  • You have a sense of humor. For realz.
  • You understand the value of teamwork and how teams work…that was deep. If you disagree, read it again. Out loud if necessary.
  • You have the ability to multitask, prioritize, re-prioritize and re-prioritize again like a ninja.
  • You will not leave your personality at home. We thrive from each unique perspective. Our strength is in our diversity of opinions. We mean it. We want to hear who you actually are.
