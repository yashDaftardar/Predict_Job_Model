We are currently seeking a Senior Post Market Surveillance Data Analyst to join our Sustainability Solutions division based in Tempe, Arizona or remotely within United States.

Who we want

  • Detail-oriented process improvers. Critical thinkers who naturally see opportunities to develop and optimize work processes – finding ways to simplify, standardize and automate.
  • Data translators. Highly effective communicators who can transform data findings into recommendations to compose reports and executive level presentations.
  • Strategic thinkers. People who enjoy analyzing data or trends for the purposes of planning, forecasting, advising, budgeting, reporting, or sales opportunities.
  • User-focused creators. People who imagine with the user in mind, developing technology that helps change patients’ lives.

What you will do

As a Senior Post-Market Surveillance Data Analyst, you are responsible for leading post market data acquisition, interpretation and analysis of data using statistical techniques to provide relevant, reports to the business.

Essential duties and responsibilities include:

  • Extract and format data from various systems per the respective Post Market Surveillance processes
  • Support cross-functional teams and stakeholder groups on ad hoc data analysis, data mining, data queries in support of product performance, regulatory, compliance and other product focused deliverables or requirements
  • Own generation and dissemination of Success Rate Reports in support of the Sales Force
  • Determine and implement data driven solutions to optimize support monitoring of customer and product quality issues
  • Provide support during external and internal audits relating to Post-Market data monitoring
  • Ensure Post Market data monitoring is verified and validated if applicable per the respective Post Market Surveillance processes and procedures
  • Provide guidance on Post Market Surveillance data monitoring processes and projects to key stakeholders and Post Market team members
  • Extract and prepare data as requested by Regulatory and Notified bodies
  • Provide analytical support for tracking recall activities and assist in completing product field action documentation.
  • Execute post-market surveillance activities including complaint trending and data extraction for annual risk review
  • Develop and optimize methods to visualize data (Tableau dashboards, charts, presentations, etc.)

What you need

  • Bachelor's degree
  • Minimum 2 years professional experience working with and visualizing large data sets
  • Proven statistical, mathematical and analytical acumen
  • Ability to be decisive utilizing critical thinking.
  • Excellent organizational, problem-solving, and analytical and time management skills
  • High level of proficiency in Excel and data visualization platforms
  • Ability to work effectively with cross-functional teams
  • Ability to write and communicate in a professional manner, including but not limited to, generating and presenting reports and presentations.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Engineering or Science
  • Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau or Power BI) and Quality Management Systems such as Trackwise
