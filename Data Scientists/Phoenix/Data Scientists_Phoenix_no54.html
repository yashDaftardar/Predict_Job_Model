Full Job Description

UST is a leading provider of platforms; digital innovation; artificial Intelligence and end-to-end IT services and solutions for Global 1000 companies. We are transforming corporations through deep domain expertise; knowledge-based ML platforms; as well as profound anthropological efforts to understand the end customer and design products and interactions that create delight. We are deeply committed to developing a comprehensive understanding of our clients’ problems and to develop platforms to address them.

Job Description

  • Design and implement modern data integration technologies and solutions to support microservices-based application architectures
  • Provide direction, assistance and education to teams on the appropriate use of databases (relational, NoSQL, graph, etc)
  • Provide technical and development leadership (Architecture, Design, oversight) for business solutions. This includes technical co-ordination and decision making for project work.
  • Define data integration flows (ETL, ELT, stream processing, events/event processors, etc.)
  • Define database and integration tool selection criteria, standards and best practices for application architectures
  • Architect and lead system availability, usability, performance and security database methodologies
  • Partner with other Application, Data and Infrastructure Architects to deliver best in class solutions
  • Assist with future release planning and data integration needs analysis
  • Be a team player with ability to drive for win-win solutions across multiple competing interests, teams and priorities

Qualifications required

  • Overall 10+ years of experience in software engineering with bachelor’s or master’s degree in technical discipline.
  • 5+ years of architecture experience in applications development in a complex, multi-platform distributed environment.
  • Thorough knowledge of RDMS Systems (MySQL, MS SQL Server, Postgres, Oracle)
  • Data Modeling Expertise (Conceptual, Logical & Physical in both Relational & Dimensional)
  • Experience using OLTP / OLAP / Graph / Multi-Model / NoSQL databases
  • Deep understanding of data privacy and data governance best practices
  • Demonstrated expertise, including industry recognized certification in cloud development
  • Expertise in the following: AWS Cloud infrastructure, Data Modelling, Building Data Pipelines
  • Hands-on Experience with Databases, AWS Data related services, ETL tools, Reporting tools, AI/ML skills
  • Strong Understanding of Data principles and Business Intelligence Report design best practices
  • Hands on experience with python, spark and SQL (Stored procedures)
  • Excellent SQL skills
  • Strong knowledge of CI/CD, experience with typical tools and best practices in the pipeline
  • Experience of architecting/designing enterprise applications for wealth management/commercial banking
  • Experience with regulated financial organizations is good to have
