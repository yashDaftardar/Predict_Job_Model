Imprint is building a payments and loyalty platform from the ground up to serve modern brands and their customers.

Imprint is backed by Thrive Capital, Affirm, Box Group, and leading angel investors, including the CEOs of Skims, AllBirds, WarbyParker, Plaid, and NuBank. We are focused on building a brilliant team who want to change payments and who embody Imprint's Values.

The Data Scientist will be part of the Data Science team with the responsibility to build AI/Machine learning models that help to drive meaningful improvement in customer engagement with our process/product capabilities and fraud/risk outcomes. This will create a significant competitive advantage for Imprint in our understanding of behavior of customers of digital merchants and create a deep moat around our business.

Your day-to-day

  • Work closely with Director Science and engineering team to conceptualize the AI/machine learning models that can help enhance Imprint product and customer/applicant processes
  • Lead the thinking in terms of creating experimental design to run test vs control that would help to optimize decisions across the customer life cycle in terms of how customers/applicants interact with Imprint product/processes. Two examples of decision areas would be: call to action we display on the start page of the application and how this changes the likelihood of the applicant starting the application, customer limit increase over time: what additional revenue and spend does this drive vs increase in default, etc.
  • Develop and deploy underwriting and customer management scorecards to predict likelihood of default based on bureau, bank cash flow, merchant and other information
  • Develop behavioral models to predict spend, revolver vs transactor behavior, balance etc. that are a key input into customer lifetime valuation models that determine approval/decline and limit assignment.
  • Develop application stage scorecard to predict likelihood of incoming application to be fraudulent by aggregating data from diverse sources 3rd party sources
  • Deep dive analysis into customer spend and reward redemption patterns and create customer propensity models that can help to drive increase in customer lifetime value for Imprint and empower consumers to maximize the rewards they earn and use
  • Track customer's interactions with Imprint app to understand features that drive value or create frustration for the customer and work with product/engineering to on how to optimize those features and their display to customers
  • Work under supervision of the Director of Data Science to drive data science research into new data sources and modeling techniques to improve discrimination of our decision models leading to improvement in business outcomes and create a sustainable business advantage for Imprint
  • Partner with program management and engineering teams to conceptualize and deploy infrastructure capabilities to house data that is required to build and execute AI/machine learning models
  • Create robust tracking processes to closely monitor performance of the AI/machine learning models to ensure that score cards are working as designed

We are looking for folks with

  • Strong foundation in data science, machine learning, and analytics, including statistical data analysis and A/B testing
  • 3-5 years of proven experience building data science driven solutions to solve business problems & drive customer experience in a consumer focused technology company
  • Ability to dive into the details of customer journey and product features in order to help define success criteria and the ways we can measure that via metrics and other approaches. This will involve close collaboration with product & engineering teams
  • Strong programming skills, including data-querying skills (SQL and/or Spark, etc.) and experience with a scripting language for data processing and development (e.g., Python, R, or Scala)
  • Solid exposure to machine learning and open source tools in terms of both model development & implementation
  • Strong communication skills and the ability to naturally explain difficult technical topics to everyone from data scientists to engineers to business partners and leaders
  • Interest in working at a fast growing startup and be part of the initial team
  • Cute or embarrassing picture of them as a child to put on our intranet
