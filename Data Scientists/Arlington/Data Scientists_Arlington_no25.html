Your Opportunity


Do you have a desire to lead change and passion for improving client experience ? If so, this exciting opportunity is for you! Charles Schwab Digital Services team is seeking a Head of Data Science to lead a talented data science team to deliver scalable machine learning solutions for dynamic financial products, as well as deliver business insights from unstructured data with clear narratives and creative visualizations. The Head of Data Science will play a key role in influencing and shaping data science delivery against a spectrum of business problems including unstructured data solutions, NLP/NLU opportunities, operations and servicing, predictive client solutions as well as capability deployments for speed and scale of impact in multi-dimensional partner operating environment.
The Data Science team is part of Charles Schwab's Digital Services group, which is responsible for leading digital disruption on behalf of investors and advisors. We collaborate with user experience and design teams, as well as business and technology partners across the enterprise to build software experiences our users are passionate about. We want every interaction for Schwab Clients to be simple and easy. We are on a journey to build the future of financial services and transform the way we work to get there – integrating how AI and Machine Learning drive the innovations in our client experience. Our mission is to drive significant advances in the speed, scale, and impact of data science-infused enterprise programs.

What you are good at


We are seeking an individual with exceptional analytical, technical, business and communication skills to be able to maintain strong relationships with Senior Leaders across Digital Services as well as define, prioritize, and solve key business questions. The successful candidate will be a proven analytics leader who is comfortable with ambiguity, demonstrates strong attention to detail, and thrives in a fast-paced environment.
  • Executive consultation experience, helping to identify and shape business opportunities
  • Solution design expertise, helping to scale new processes and technologies by applying data science capabilities
  • Collaborating with the Digital Services Organization and provide thought-leadership on how to maximize data and influence decisions across the Digital experience ecosystem
  • Managing relationships and support resources to gain alignment across partners and fully utilize resources to support a fact-driven and “through clients’ eyes” decision-making culture
  • Measuring and communicating the impact of the team’s work to ensure it is accessible, impactful, and drives business results
  • Leading and mentoring a highly engaged team of over 45 individuals

What you have


  • 10+ years’ experience in analytics, data science, AI/Machine learning; demonstrated proficiency as practitioner in the space, and as a leader/manager of others in the space
  • Advanced degree in quantitative discipline-Statistics, Computer Science, Economics, Mathematics, Operations Research, Engineering or similar quantitative field
  • Leadership experience in advanced machine learning and AI methods—including algorithm choices, explainable AI approach solutions, lifecycle management from development to deployment, etc.
  • Experience designing and selling data science opportunities to senior business stakeholders; as well as owning and delivering on enterprise data science roadmaps
  • Experience influencing and shaping data science delivery against a spectrum of business problems including unstructured data solutions (NLP/NLU), predictive solution development (modeling lifecycle, model performance optimization) as well as solution deployments for speed and scale of impact in hybrid operating environments (on prem/public/cloud)
  • Track record of successful recruiting data science talent, mentoring and developing teams, and managing and optimizing team execution performance
  • Knowledge of existing and emerging statistical and modeling packages including Python, R, and other prominent public/cloud computing utilities
  • Demonstrated ability to exchange ideas and convey complex information clearly and concisely
  • Financial Services Industry Experience preferred
