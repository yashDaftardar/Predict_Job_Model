Model Development Officer I
The Model Development Officer role will be responsible for developing forecasting/predictive models that are sensitive to the macroeconomic environment.
Position Competencies
Successful incumbents are expected to complete development analysis, observations, conclusions and communicate technical information to both technical and non-technical audiences; and convey business implications to the model development manager.
Position Responsibilities:
Model Development
  • Perform rigorous assessment of data quality and relevance of assumptions.
  • Develop models to forecast loans, deposits, income and expenses.
  • Recalibrate all the models annually and redevelop the models as model performance triggers are received.
  • Deliver comprehensive model development documentation.
  • Participate in development and presentation of quantitative insight to multiple product teams consisting of technical and non-technical audiences.
  • Provide analytic support for management overlays to compensate for data and model limitations and maintain appropriate documentation especially where the judgment is used.
  • Coordinate with model validation team in the assessment and mitigation of model risk.
  • Work closely with model implementation team to assist on model usage.
  • Conduct data management and reporting.
Reporting
  • Responsible for reporting model development, documentation, implementation, and monitoring to the manager.
  • Responsible for assisting model validation team in the assessment and mitigation of model risk.
  • Support in developing models and documentation.
    Generate analysis and presents data results to model stakeholders.
  • Support in validation assessment and model implementation usage.
  • Support decision making process in operational risk.


Position Qualifications
  • Master's degree from an accredited university in Statistics, Quantitative Finance, Economics, Econometrics, Applied Mathematics, or any other highly quantitative discipline
  • 2 years of experience performing high level financial analysis, particularly as it applies to the financial services industry using statistical modeling software (SAS, STATA, R, etc.)
  • 2 years of experience in quantitative analysis, statistical modeling including econometric model development
  • 2 years of experience in quantitative or econometric analysis with focus on time series or panel data
  • 2 years of experience using SAS (Statistical Analysis Software) programming, data mining and high proficiency with SAS STAT/MACRO/SQL
  • 1 year of experience with PC computer and statistical modeling software skill
