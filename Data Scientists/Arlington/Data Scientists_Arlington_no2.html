Full Job Description

The salary listed on this job posting is the starting salary range; amount offered will depend upon qualifications.


Responsible for analyzing of various data streams, utilization of large data sets to determine unique insights to further business needs. This includes finding innovative ways to combine fields of data that don’t naturally mesh together as well as exploratory data analysis to navigate a dataset and come out with conclusions based on certain assumptions. The ideal candidate will assist in building out the analytics eco system for the City and transforming data as a key input in solving challenging real-world problems. By using available data and information, the Data Scientist will help the City become smarter, more efficient and effective in delivering services and programs to constituents.

Essential Functions

  • Partner with key city agencies and work within the team to identify, scope, and execute analytic efforts that answer business questions, solve business needs and add business values.
  • Maintain a broad understanding of the key problem statement and be fully engaged with the project team, bringing an objective voice to the table and facilitating decisions grounded in data.
  • Work with departments to apply statistical analysis, pattern recognition with domain and program specific knowledge to solve business issues.
  • Use predictive modeling to increase and optimize customer experiences, revenue generation, and other business outcomes.
  • Develop tools to monitor and analyze model performance and data accuracy.
  • Work with Data Engineers to facilitate technical design of complex data sourcing.
  • Identify opportunities for leveraging data to drive program, service and department solutions.
  • Develop mechanisms to ingest, analyze, validate, normalize, and clean data.
  • Retrieve and combine data from databases, websites, department systems, etc.
  • Contribute to all stages of data science and decision modeling projects, including problem formulation to solution developments
  • Design dynamic, real time dashboards for program managers, department leaders, and executive leadership, as appropriate to project deliverables.
  • Build Community of Practice for staff in data-oriented roles and manage data analytics trainings.

Minimum Qualifications
