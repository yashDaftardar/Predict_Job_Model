The Gist

We’re looking for support at CBP’s Office of Human Resource Management - Strategic Transformation and People Analytics (STPA) division with workforce analytics and reporting, process improvement, and other program management-related functions. The Office requires robust analytics, dashboard development, and project management to achieve this task. We need a Senior Data Analyst to support our project teams and workstreams in developing data visualizations and analytical work products, such as dashboards, and integrating layered data sets, leveraged to advance workforce planning for CBP’s personnel.

Your Day Might Include...

  • Working effectively in collaborative team environments with little supervision; proactively reporting progress, risks, and/or challenges to program managers
  • Facilitate meetings with functional leads and subject matter experts to identify, develop, document, and validate requirements
  • Develop a core understanding of CBP mission and apply it to data analysis projects
  • Support requirements gathering efforts for dashboards, reports, or other analytical products
  • Develop project plans, project analyses, and reports, recommendations, implementation plans, among other project deliverables
  • Support analytical products such as dashboards to serve CBP requirements
  • Prepare and update briefings used by HRM leadership

About Amivero
Amivero is a team of driven IT professionals motivated by challenges that allow us to be positively influential in the advancement of our nation. Success is having fun in a friendly, collaborative environment while continually learning and growing, resulting in rewarding performance.

As a member of the Amivero team, your professional satisfaction will be cared for through workforce development initiatives, a stipend for ongoing tech training, a flexible work schedule, retirement support, and incentives for meeting performance targets. Gather with us at Amivero’s family events, give back with our volunteer opportunities, and be rewarded for referring great professionals.
