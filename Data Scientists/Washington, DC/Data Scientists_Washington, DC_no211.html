About this role

We are currently seeking a Quantitative Research Leader to join our Supply Chain practice. This individual will set strategy, lead a team of quantitative researchers, conductive quantitative research, and develop innovative quantitative applications and tools. He or she will report to the Managing Vice President in the Supply Chain Peer & Practitioner Research group.

What you’ll do

  • Set the strategy for Supply Chain quantitative analytics and data science (QUADs), in partnership with team and practice leadership. Own the Supply Chain QUADs product roadmap and manage execution against it.

  • Lead and oversee new-to-world quantitative research workstreams, including survey modeling, design, and writing.

  • Dissect problems using qualitative and quantitative research and analysis skills, inductive and deductive reasoning, abstract thinking, and root-cause analysis.

  • Identify and develop innovative quantitative applications and tools that leverage data assets.

  • Lead, motivate, develop, and coach team members on all aspects of quantitative research (including survey design, data analysis, and advanced quantitative methods) and tool and application development.

What you’ll need

  • Advanced degree (Master’s level or above) in an analytics-heavy field (e.g., Economics, Statistics, Policy Analysis, etc.)

  • 7-10 years of related work experience; experience in management consulting or business research roles preferred

  • Advanced survey design and analysis skills; ability to solve complex problems using qualitative and quantitative information

  • Mastery of a statistical package (e.g., SPSS, STATA, R, etc.) for advanced analytics (e.g., multi-variate regressions, logistic regressions, cluster analysis, factor analysis, etc.)

  • Experience designing and delivering analytics and/or automation-based products

Who you are

  • Driven and resourceful – you’re able to solve complex problems and think creatively about how to deliver insight to clients through data and tools.

  • An innovator and builder at heart – you’re excited about the opportunity to build out quantitative capabilities in Gartner’s Supply Chain practice.

  • A proven team and project leader – you excel in a fast-paced environment, can balance multiple priorities, and can inspire teams and stakeholders to achieve great outcomes.

  • Collaborative, team-oriented culture that embraces diversity

  • Professional development and unlimited growth opportunities
