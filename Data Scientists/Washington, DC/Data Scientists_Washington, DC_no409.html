Role Summary:

The Principal Decision Scientist role resides with the Enterprise Data Science and Analytics team and is a key player in

planning, executing, and delivering machine learning-based projects. This role is responsible for the use of statistical, algorithmic, data mining, and visualization techniques applied to complex data structures. Critical success factors include providing analytical insights, models, data visualizations, and analytical direction that shape the future strategy of VCI’s Mobility transformation.

The Enterprise Data Science and Analytics team has a clear vision – use data to make better business decisions that increase brand loyalty and customer centricity. Researching and developing applications for advanced analytics and artificial intelligence, the Decision Scientist II will support our mission to transform the business. Working cross-

functionally across the organization, the Decision Scientist II will deliver on various use cases that positively influence business decisions and direction. The primary responsibility for this position will be to elevate the level of our advanced analytics, statistical and ML models and the insights delivered to our business partners.

The successful candidate will be a collaborator that is skilled in bringing insights from data to life. This is a new position, in a new team, with the unique opportunity to lay the foundation of how data and data driven decisions become a core element to VCI’s strategy.

Role Responsibilities:

- Extract, curate, explore, and analyze data from large data sources (20%)

- Develop and deploy statistical and ML models that influenced business decisions (20%)

- Develop data/analytics algorithms and data structures (20%)

- Collaborate with business owners, stakeholders and leadership to methodically transform business problems into detailed and practical analytical requirements (20%)

- Support the definition and deployment of a machine learning environment (10%)

- Innovate new ML methods in the data sciences space and drive change throughout the business (5%)

- Research new data sources and analytical tools; contribute to new application development and data quality improvement efforts (5%)
