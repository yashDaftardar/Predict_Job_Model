Job Description: Thompson Gray, Inc. (TGI) is seeking a Data Scientist (with an emphasis on natural language processing) to lead an effort to identify and develop a new class of Emerging Technology Leaders (ETL) in the US Army. TGI is assisting the Army with research solutions that leverage data science and machine learning techniques to revolutionize the way Army recruits, develops, selects, and distributes talent across the workforce.

The Data Scientist will be responsible for creating analytical tools, data models, and predictive analytics that provide data-driven recommendations for personnel selection and placement, in the areas of skill assessment, talent selection, talent development and talent distribution. Skills assessment includes comparing, identifying and estimating an individual's skill and positional fit based on natural language processing of resumes and job descriptions. Talent selection entails assessing applicability of certain skills to the workforce need. Talent development identifies skill gaps and provides recommended actions to improve particular skills. Talent distribution leverages predictive analytics to optimize placement of particular ETLs to current and future areas of need.

The Data Scientist will work closely with TGI data scientists and client product owners to analyze, develop and deploy solutions to satisfy this need. The Data Scientist will have broad latitude to determine the form of the solution, which may include white papers, statistical models, analytical dashboards, or standalone software. The Data Scientist will lead the end-to-end analysis, design and development of the analytical solution. Responsibilities include:

  • Lead effort to analyze, define and design a talent analytics solution encompassing skills assessment, talent selection, talent development and talent distribution from early conceptual stages to delivery
  • Document research in the form of whitepapers, statistical models, functions, or algorithms
  • Use software and analytical tools to develop a prototype which demonstrates the solution across enterprise-scale datasets
  • Refine models and algorithms to align solutions to Army data and systems
  • Develop plan to scale analytics across the enterprise

Required Skills:

  • BS degree in Information Systems, Computer Science or related field/equivalent experience
  • MS degree in Data Science, Data Analytics or related field/equivalent experience
  • Development in advanced analytical concepts including, but not limited to, natural language processing, machine learning, neural networks, artificial intelligence, statistical analysis, data mining, data modeling and data visualization.
  • Coding and development via analytical tools including, but not limited to, Azure Synapse Analytics (Cognitive Services, Databricks, Azure ML, etc. or equivalent data science platforms in AWS, IBM Cloud, etc.), Jupyter notebooks, RStudio, Power BI, etc.
  • Demonstrated experience delivering advanced analysis projects from early conceptual stages, preferably those with emphasis on natural language processing.

Desired Skills:

  • Experience with Human Resource data (people analytics, talent acquisition, skill assessment, etc.)
  • Research and development (R&D) experience including data ingestion, cleanup, shaping, modeling, and exploration; documenting prototype models; and assessing scientific and technical feasibility for commercialization of said models
  • Army and knowledge of Army operations and processes
  • Ability to obtain a Department of Defense SECRET clearance or higher
  • Demonstrated experience translating business and technical requirements into comprehensive data strategies and analytic solutions
