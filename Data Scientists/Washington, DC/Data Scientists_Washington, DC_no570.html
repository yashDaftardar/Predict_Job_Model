Description:

A day in the life:

As a Principal Data Scientist, you will be part of a fast-paced environment to support immediate mission needs. This exciting opportunity presents the challenging responsibility of developing critical applications and systems for end-to-end data engineering capabilities to include aggregation, analytics, summarization and visualization on both large structured and unstructured datasets.


In this role, you will work closely with a variety of individuals while effectively advising, communicating and presenting your findings to multiple stakeholders in multiple disciplinary areas:

  • Data Architecture: Become an expert in characterizing, identifying and cataloging disparate datasets across multiple networks, systems and repositories while leveraging analytic/statistics for discovery and insight.
  • Data Engineering: Apply streaming and storage strategies for data-in-transit and data-at-rest respectively. Design and implement high-performance and efficient data catalogs and structures for maximal throughput and minimal overhead for queries and other types of interactions with large datasets.
  • Data Science: Apply natural language processing and machine learning/AI advanced techniques to train and optimize models for automating analysis, processes and insight. Deploy these trained models and processes to work on production at scale while leveraging the amenities of a commercial cloud environment - e.g. automatic scaling of the deployment and runtime of prototypes and workflows to distributed clusters.
  • Data Visualization: Create visualizations using custom-developed dashboards and/or industry standard commercial platforms to present actionable insights and interactive interfaces.
