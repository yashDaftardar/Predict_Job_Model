About the Position:
DPS is proud to support the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) in developing mission critical analytical tools. Many of these tools will leverage Machine Learning (ML) and Natural Language Processing (NLP) methods to identify safety signals in medical devices. This work is an opportunity to help improve the lives of millions of people using medical devices.

As a Machine Learning Engineer of a Product Team , you will build ML infrastructure to support machine learning models development and deployment, research and apply industry-leading MLops tools and techniques, participate in development and implementation of state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms for signal detection. You will be a part of a Scrum team of developers that includes data scientists and engineers, and full-stack developers. If you like learning new technologies, solving tough problems, and building systems that serve predictions of millions of data points, this could be the right job for you.
Responsibilities:
  • Designs and develops methods, algorithms, and systems that apply machine learning technologies to support advanced signal processing concepts
  • Suggest, collect and synthesize requirements and create effective feature roadmap
  • Build advanced feature extraction pipelines
  • Adapt standard machine learning methods to best exploit modern parallel environments (e.g. distributed clusters, multicore SMP, and GPU)
  • Translating insights into business recommendations
  • Stay current on published data mining, machine learning, and modeling techniques and competing technologies and share these findings with Scientists and Engineers in the organization

Qualifications:

Minimum Requirements:

  • BA/BS in Computer Science, Applied Math or related technical field
  • 6+ years of experience in software engineering/data science
  • Experience in being part of a multi-disciplinary project team in research or development
  • Strong experience with mathematical model tools and Python
  • Familiarity with Scrum framework; Scrum certification is preferred
  • Experience with software development best practices including Version Control; we use Git
  • Any of the following AI/machine learning frameworks, statistical packages, and libraries: Tensorflow, Amazon Machine Learning, Apache Spark, PyTorch, Scikit-learn, NLTK, SQL, d3.js, as well as open source and other emerging technologies/frameworks
  • Experience supporting Natural Language Processing (NLP) modeling processes
  • Programming experience (SQL, Python/Javascript)
