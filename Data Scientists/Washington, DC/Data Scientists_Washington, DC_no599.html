Senior Machine Learning Engineer – NLP / Cyber

Two Six Technologies
Arlington, VA

The Data Science Team applies expertise in machine learning, data visualization, statistics, and computational architectures to solve hard research challenges in AI security & privacy, synthetic biology, cyber security, computational social science, code analysis, biometrics, and other areas. We have built systems that summarize source code, predict protein stability, visualize graphs with millions of nodes, and detect stealthy denial-of-service attacks. Our team values creativity, initiative, collaboration, and diversity. We strive for a fun and collegial atmosphere that encourages intellectual cross pollination and professional growth. We are passionate about empowering our customer’s missions and enjoy working together at the leading edge of technology!


Responsibilities Include:

As a Senior Machine Learning Engineer , you will research novel machine learning methods, apply them to challenging problem domains, and present empirical research results. You will develop, apply, and evaluate state-of-the-art natural language processing techniques to solve complex cyber problems. You may contribute to technical proposals in new research areas. You will work collaboratively on a multidisciplinary team alongside experts in deep learning, cybersecurity, signal processing, and software development with clients that include DARPA and other government agencies.

Minimum Qualifications:

  • 3+ years of experience developing and applying deep learning models to challenging research problems in natural language processing (NLP) or other sequential tasks
  • 1+ year programming experience with Python and Python machine learning frameworks (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch, Sklearn, NLTK)
  • Bachelor’s degree in machine learning, CS, EE, Applied Math, Stats, or a related field, or 2+ years additional professional experience

Nice If You Have:

  • Graduate degree or publication record in a relevant field
  • Strong knowledge of CS fundamentals (compilation, assembly / low-level programming, operating systems, etc.)
  • Familiarity with common approaches in NLP (word embeddings, language models, sequence-to-sequence models, syntax parsing, etc.)
  • Skilled at communicating empirical research results in written, visual, and spoken forms
  • Applied research experience, especially with DARPA or a similar agency
  • Experience contributing to research proposals

Clearance / US Citizen:

  • Active DoD Secret security clearance required
