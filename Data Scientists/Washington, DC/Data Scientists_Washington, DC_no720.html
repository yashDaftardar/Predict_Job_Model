Job Title: Senior Software Developer & Big Data Engineer

Location: Silver Spring, MD

Job Description

IBSS is seeking individuals with interest in applying their experience in software development to challenging problems in areas such as environmental/natural resource monitoring where innovations in analysis of complex, large imagery datasets are needed to meet timely decision-making requirements. This is an exciting opportunity to work on and discover unknowns of the ocean depths! Our customer has a treasure trove of data, particularly image data , and challenging problems to solve.

As a senior software developer with our company, you will work closely with a small team of software developers and Machine Learning specialists to build, customize, and integrate new software applications for our customers. Our focus is the integration of computer vision models with new or existing software that scientists use to monitor marine species. Your work will also include evaluation of open-source and commercial-off-the shelf software products against customer requirements. You will be at the forefront of innovation in making our customers’ environmental/natural resource monitoring tasks easier, more accurate and more efficient. The position will also include updating existing systems and integrating improvements to the user interfaces already in operation. If you are a creative problem-solver with the technological skills to make your vision a reality, apply today.

Key Responsibilities

  • Work with team members to design innovative software programs that increase the efficiency and quality of computer vision-based environmental/natural resource monitoring for scientists
  • Conduct evidence-based evaluation of open-source and commercial-off-the shelf software products against customer requirements
  • Integrate new capabilities with existing software, and/or enhance capabilities of existing software tools
  • Perform intensive testing of all new and existing programs to ensure they are free of errors and function as intended
  • Support Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning colleagues in the development of AI algorithms and software tools they need for computer vision applications
  • Operationalize AI/ML image recognition capability for customers through integration of AI/ML- enabled image recognition capabilities with other software tools
  • Conduct data extract, transform and load (ETL)
  • Conduct data management (quality, access, taxonomy, etc.) monitoring and corrections, as well as data integration
  • Establish and manage big data warehouses
  • Establish and manage cloud-based environments for software development as well as rapid access to data that will be hosted on the cloud
  • Support the team in conducting quantitative data analysis using a variety of datasets, including developing retrieval, processing, fusion, analysis, and visualization of various datasets
  • Keep up to date on the newest technological innovations in the field and be ready to apply relevant innovations to our software systems

Required Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, or related fields
  • 7+ years of expert software development skills throughout the entire software development lifecycle including developing and maintain good production quality code
  • highly successful and relevant experience in Software Development
  • Experience using in Python, R, Java, Java Script, C++
  • Experience in development, operation, and maintenance of enterprise-level software initiatives — that is, initiatives that require high availability, disaster recovery, security, SLAs, and technical support
  • Experience in software product evaluations
  • Big Data Warehousing experience to include experience with rational and non-rational database systems (Mysql, Oracle, DB2, MongoDB, etc.)
  • Experience working with Big Data Computational Frameworks (e.g., Hadoop, Spark, etc.)
  • Experience with Data Management software (such as Amazon Relational Database Service (RDS), Oracle RDBMS, SQL Developer, IBM Informix, EMS SQL Manager) to optimize, secure, and manage large sets of data
  • Experience with data Extract, Load, and Transformation (ETL)
  • Experience with Enterprise iPaaS (EiPaaS) or suite of integration platform as a service (iPaaS) technology (such as SAP, Oracle, Informatica) to implement integration projects involving any combination of cloud-resident and on-premises endpoints, and APIs for the development, execution and management of integration processes and flows that connect multiple endpoints.
  • Cloud Computing experience, specifically, experience with Amazon Web Services (EC2, S3, SQS, RDS)
  • Ability to work closely with Domain experts to develop tools/algorithms needed to answer research questions in their studies
  • A self-starter, who can work through ambiguity and has a successful track record of delivering results
  • Excellent Communication Skills (with the ability to explain developed tools non-technical audience)
  • Ability to formulate operational problems in the Environment domain as technical problems that allows for reuse of leading research in the area.
  • Proven ability to work independently to learn new technologies, techniques, processes, languages, platforms, systems
  • Strong analytic, inferencing, critical thinking, and creative problem-solving skills
  • Self-starter with ability to work both independently and with a team

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with Deep Learning Frameworks such as PyTorch, Keras, Tensorflow, etc. Ability to apply these frameworks in the computer vision field
  • Experience with interpretability of deep learning models
  • Data Visualization (e.g., Tableau, Qlikview)
  • Web Development Skills
  • Practical experience with statistical analysis
