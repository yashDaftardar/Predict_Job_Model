Purpose: The Statistical Analyst (SA) will work out of the Diplomatic Security Service, Office of Overseas Protective Operations (OPO) and will compile data, analyze information, and to turn it into products that OPO Program Managers can use to make decisions. The SA will collect, analyze and report data pertaining to the Worldwide Protection Services (WPS) Contract.

Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Compile, manage and analyze large datasets from multiple structured and unstructured sources to create integrated views that can be used to drive decisions.
  • Analyze and interpret quantitative data.
  • Evaluate statistical methods and procedures used to obtain data and implement improvements.
  • Extract and process data for statistical modeling and graphic analysis and identify relations and trends in data.
  • Prioritize and provide data analysis for product/business requests.
  • Design and build reports, dashboards and present analysis results using a variety of data visualization tools.
  • Participate in process management reviews, collaborating with program teams to define data solutions to support program functionality.
