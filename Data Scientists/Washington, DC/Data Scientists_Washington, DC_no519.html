The future of autonomous surface transportation and next generation aviation depend on the viability and security of safety critical communications. Your efforts will advance thought leadership in areas of great national importance for many years to come. Use your knowledge of data mining and analysis, to perform safety analysis and predictive modeling techniques and apply it to various transportation modes.

  • Autonomous Surface Transportation: Safety techniques tend to depend on communications. Your role will include ensuring effective and optimal use of crowd-sourcing models, localized spectrum use, cybersecurity of communications infrastructure and data-at-rest as well as data-in-motion (e.g. V2X communications, over-the-air software updates).
  • Aviation: Aviation safety research requires the confluence of understanding current safety policy, understanding the current Target Levels of Safety (TLS), then creating a model framework to understand the areas where one can improve current safety. Using big-data analytics, you will help design and improve algorithms to model the risk and mitigations of current operations and predictive models that pinpoint areas for improvement, which could result in upgrades to current policy.
