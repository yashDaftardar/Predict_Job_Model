Job Description

Core Responsibilities:

As a Research Intern, you will join a team developing world class Media Analysis

technologies that extract, store, and deliver metadata associated with scenes and moments in video.

This includes identifying what is happening in the video; understanding scenes, people, topics, & audio; and determining content chaptering.

You will work with Researchers and Engineers to create and implement new detection capabilities for use within our Media Analysis Framework (MAF) that extract insightful metadata from the video.

You will work closely with other researchers and engineers to create algorithms for multi-modal analysis of video and exciting new projects.

Regular, Consistent and punctual attendance. Must be able to work nights and weekends, variable schedule(s) and overtime as necessary.

Other duties and responsibilities as assigned.
