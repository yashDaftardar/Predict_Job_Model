Full Job Description

CCI Health and Wellness Services is a group practice, empowering patients to partner with staff for an unparalleled healthcare experience. Through integrated teams in a learning environment, we deliver high quality, affordable, care to every patient during all stages of life.

POSITION SUMMARY:

The Senior Analyst, under the direction of the Chief Information Officer, supports data management and analysis from the e Clinical Works electronic medical record and the data analytics platform, Relevant, in support of decision making and grant performance monitoring. This individual will work with internal customers to identify, analyze and understand patient and clinical services data. In addition, as the Relevant analytics expert, this individual will lead management, data validation and support of this software application.

KEY FUNCTIONS & RESPONSIBILITIES:

Data Analysis

  • Leading the data support function for Clinical and administrative departments
  • Manage business process analysis in support of report development.
  • Produce data sets, analytics and complex, audience appropriate reports, including outcomes measurement.
  • Perform data standardization, transformation, reconciliation, analysis and visualization
  • Develop methods to ensure consistent application and use of analytics.
  • Identify data utilization solutions as decision-support approaches.

Relevant analytics platform Management, Maintenance, Training & Technical Assistance

  • Lead management and support of this software application.
  • Manage data mapping, cleanup and validation processes for all interfaced data.
  • Coordinate creation and maintenance of reports and dashboards.
  • Coordinate vendor trouble shooting and coordinating completion of system corrections.
  • Plan and Implement Relevant as a decision-support tool (i.e. workflow integration).
  • Lead training and other TA support to end-users with various levels of computer knowledge.

Other Duties

  • Participate in Quality Improvement/Assurance and other meetings as requested.
  • Supervise the Data Analytics Specialist.

MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS :

  • 3 to 5 years’ experience with healthcare analytics.
  • BA degree required.
  • 2+ years’ experience supervising staff and demonstrated leadership skills, preferred.
  • Intermediate skills in data management principles, data analysis and data visualization.
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint
  • Highly proficient with Relevant or other population health analytics software.
  • Proficiency with SQL language (Microsoft SQL Server preferred).
  • Strong critical thinking, analytical skills and decision-making.
  • Ability to balance concurrent complex projects.
  • Familiarity with various types of healthcare information, including but not limited to coding and health improvement indicators and interventions.
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills, and interpersonal skills.

CCI Health & Wellness Services is a 49-year-old Federally Qualified Health Center is a diverse and inclusive workplace that serves 60,000 medical patients and Women Infant’s & Children (WIC) participants in Montgomery and Prince George’s Counties. As a 501(c)3 non-profit, CCI is committed to providing high-quality, affordable primary healthcare to every patient across all life stages. Visit www.cciweb.org for more information.
