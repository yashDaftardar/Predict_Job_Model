Full Job Description

We are seeking a Data Scientist with experience in Digital Fraud Analytics to join our growing team support a federal agency located in the Washington, DC metropolitan area. This position plays a key role in supporting the agency's objectives to prevent and detect fraud, waste, abuse, and mismanagement (FWA&M); and mitigate major risks that span program and agency boundaries. Our customer works with Federal Inspectors General (IGs) to develop recommendations on measures to prevent or FWA provide support in conducting investigations, audits, and reviews relating to Coronavirus response and funding; and refers matters for criminal investigations. The position is contingent upon contract award.

Responsibilities:

A range of analytic techniques should be used to identify fraud, waste, abuse and mismanagement, with a focus on cross-agency and cross-program risks. The analytic techniques may include anomaly detection, risk modeling, social network analysis, review process automation and data matching to identify outliers, trends and patterns. As part of this activity, you will collaborate with the team and the customer in the conduct, delivery and documentation of its work. Related sub-activities include:

  • Providing thought-leadership in the area of analytics/data science.
  • Ideating, reviewing, and selecting use cases with Government officials.
  • Identifying, reviewing and profiling available data and determining gaps as necessary
  • Reviewing and selecting analytic technique and methods that will be used, including best way to visualize and present results for consumption.
  • Developing initial model(s).
  • Identifying potential actions that could be taken.
  • Documenting methodology used, data issues encountered, and findings.
  • Highlighting future activities necessary to deploy and improve the models using machine learning.
  • Building and manage data analytics pipelines using platforms including Python, Apache Spark, and Tableau.
  • Managing the Data Science Environment including installation, configuration, and maintenance of tools like R Studio Pro & Jupyter Hub.
  • Designing and implement new predictive machine learning and artificial intelligence technologies for real-world production use at scale.
  • Helping to manage and execute the ingest (extract, transform and load (ETL) operations), parsing, data normalizations, as well as performing data analysis at scale and implementing algorithms/models to find insights in large data sets and deploy applications in a cloud-based data processing environment.
  • Improving the accuracy, runtime, scalability and reliability of machine learning (ML) predictive and analytical models.
  • Using the Agile methodology to work to understand the customer's mission needs and transform them into user stories.
  • Communicating across a diverse audience of business analysts, architects, business users, and technical project staff.
  • Acquiring and preparing data for analysis and specify visualizations to promote understanding and ease of use of analytical insights.
  • Training customer staff on Analytical & Data Science tools, and actively supporting the transition into production.


Minimum/General Experience:

Position Requirements:

  • Excellent organizational, communication, and interpersonal skills, and a demonstrated an ability to meet deadlines, handle multiple tasks, and work successfully in a team environment.
  • The ability to obtain and/or sustain a Public Trust Clearance.

Education Requirements:

  • A Master's degree in a related field can substitute for 2 years of experience.

Desired Skills and Experience:

  • Excellent coding skills using standard Data Science tools, including Python, R, Linux/Unix Command Line and Shell Scripting.
  • Possess a deep understanding and have experience with machine learning predictive techniques including decision trees, neural networks and, deep learning, and statistical analysis methods to include classification, collaborative filtering, sentiment analysis, topic modeling, and time-series analysis.
  • Proven ability to interact, build a working relationship with stakeholders to develop an architecture strategy, processes and, deliver IT assets to meet the business objectives.
  • The ability to utilize advanced tools and computational skills to interpret, connect, predict, and make discoveries in complex data and deliver recommendations.
  • Exposure to Amazon Web Services (AWS) and cloud-based systems like EMR and AWS Sagemaker.
  • Experience with SQL and NoSQL database technologies, and ETL/ELT Tools.
  • Experience with RPA will be a plus.
  • Data Science and AWS or Azure cloud certifications.
