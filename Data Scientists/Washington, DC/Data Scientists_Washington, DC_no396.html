Full Job Description

Axle Informatics is a scientific research and information technology company that offers innovative computer services, informatics, and bioresearch solutions to research centers and healthcare organizations around the globe. With experts in software engineering, bioinformatics, molecular research, and program management, we focus on developing and applying technological tools and scientific techniques to empower the world of research. We work with some of the top research organizations and facilities in the country including multiple institutes at the National Institutes of health (NIH), which means candidates will be receiving the best on-the-job training possible in this field.

We are looking for a Data Scientist - Infrastructure with high experience in system identification, ML/DL, and other data science technologies (AI, risk analysis, and, statistical methods) to support a next-generation scientific computing environment for today's and tomorrow's needs in high-end data analysis and extreme-scale computing at NIH/NCATS. This candidate will play a lead role in our strategic efforts related to data ingestion, transformation, security, and, analytics. The candidate will help the Scientific Computing team to design and implement features and solutions relevant to multiple aspects of data analytics, to identify business problems, and, to provide solutions to the scientific community and partners at NCATS.

Responsibilities:

  • Apply analytical and technical abilities to extract insight from massive data sets
  • Research, design, and implement various data science algorithms to identify hidden patterns, outliers, and correlated events.
  • Demonstrate abilities in leveraging data mining and analysis, and overall different Big Data techniques to expand a suite of data analysis products aimed at uncovering trends and predictive models in large datasets
  • Apply advanced data mining, ML, and, DL techniques to specify domain problems for next generation solutions and implementations
  • Write code in Python, R, Scala, Java, C++ with documentation for reproducibility
  • Provide expertise in handling terabyte size datasets, diving into data to discover hidden patterns, using data visualization tools, writing SQL, and working with GPUs to develop models
  • Strong analytical view in solving problems in Big Data and overall scientific computing environments
  • Champion the data science bridge between subject matter engineering experts, scientists, and, business partners
  • Stitch together diverse data sources
  • Create dynamic data visualizations
  • Track new technologies and trends in data analytics, statistics, and their applications to gas transaction management systems
  • Collaborate with cross-functional team to champion and implement data analysis workflows throughout applications
  • Assess the effectiveness and accuracy of new data sources and data gathering techniques
  • Experience working in an agile development environment
  • Develop processes and tools to monitor and analyze model performance and data accuracy
  • Write and speak about technical concepts to business, technical, and lay audiences and giving data-driven presentations

Qualifications:

  • Master or PhD degree in a highly quantitative field (Computer Science, Machine Learning, Operational Research, Statistics, Mathematics, etc.) or equivalent professional experience
  • Experience with ML fields, e.g., natural language processing, computer vision, statistical learning theory
  • Experience in building and deploying ML models or hands-on experience developing deep learning models
  • 5-10 years of industry experience in predictive modeling, data science, and analysis
  • High knowledge in statistics, machine learning, and modeling algorithms
  • High knowledge with deep learning models via PyTorch or Tensorflow
  • High knowledge with using Pandas, Numpy, and scikit-learn libraries
  • Strong data visualizations skills
  • Strong problem-solving skills with an emphasis on product development; easy working on the big picture
  • Experience with Python, shell scripting, and/or, other programming languages
  • Experience with SQL, ETL, and databases
  • Experience collaborating on projects with multiple teams, managing relationships, and communicating findings to audiences
  • Proven problem solving and conflict management skills
  • Strong understanding of scientific computing environments
  • Excellent team player role
  • Strong work ethics, career goals, and accomplishments
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills for coordinating across teams

Advanced Qualifications:

  • 10+ years of experience manipulating data sets and building statistical models for enterprise-level scientific workflows and applications
  • Experience using statistical computer languages (R, Python, SQL, etc.) to manipulate data and draw insights from large data sets
  • Solid understanding of data science algorithms, machine learning algorithms, and statistical analysis
  • Knowledge of a variety of machine learning techniques (clustering, decision tree learning, artificial neural networks, etc.) and their real-world advantages/drawbacks
  • Knowledge of advanced statistical techniques and concepts (regression, simulation, scenario analysis, modeling, clustering, properties of distributions, statistical tests and proper usage, etc.)
  • Strength in creating models, visualizations, reports, or blogs to translate raw data/statistics and predict future data
  • Experience working with and creating data architectures
  • A drive to learn and master new technologies and techniques
  • Experience working with applications scientific software stack including Python, Matlab, R, etc.
  • Experience using database architectures, such as SQL and NoSQL
  • Strong experience with distributed storage and processing for Big Data
  • Strong understanding in High-Performance Computing systems and parallel filesystems
  • Strong experience in orchestration tools and containerization techniques
  • Highly experienced using cloud computing environments (AWS, GCP, Azure)
  • Strong understanding of scheduling tools and computing resources allocation
  • Experience using Kubernetes, Jenkins, Gitlab/Github, and, different CI/CD tools and environments
  • Experience with distributed data/computing tools: Map/Reduce, Hadoop, Hive, Spark, Gurobi, MySQL, etc.
  • A broad experience in data analytics
  • Experience working in scientific environments for Life Sciences and/or previous work within the government sector a huge plus
