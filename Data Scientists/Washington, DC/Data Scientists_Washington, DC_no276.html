Position Overview:
This is an exciting opportunity to join our growing Avantus Federal/USMS team supporting the United States Marshals Service, Office of Protective Intelligence. The position is located in Crystal City on client site.
Responsibilities:
  • Work with ambiguous information, deconstructs key questions, suggests methodologies, develops data schema to structured observations, and supports visualization of applicable data.
  • Respond to the analytic needs of the organization and pro-actively develop tools and techniques to constantly improved the effectiveness of the unit.
  • Work closely with non-technical analysts to develop tools and methods.
  • Identify and evaluate a wide range of existing methods, models, and algorithms to develop more accurate, complete, objective, and anticipatory analysis for intelligence production.
  • Identify, retrieve, manipulate, relate, and/or exploits structured data sets from all available sources.
  • Use scripts, visual analytic modeling tools, and Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for repeatable, widely applicable procedures.
  • Combine empirical or quantitative methods with data analysis for intelligence production of complex and data driven assessments.
  • Evaluate the capabilities and limitations of analytic methodologies.
  • Identify, coordinate, and deconflict process with Information Technology Division.
  • Conducts data analytics, data engineering, data mining, exploratory analysis, statistical analysis, and uses scientific techniques to correlate data into graphical, written, visual and verbal narrative products, enabling more informed analytic decisions.
  • Proactively retrieves information from various sources, analyzes it for better understanding about the data set, and develop tools and process to streamline analytical work.
Required Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s Degree (Master’s Degree preferred) in a quantitative field with a concentration in applied analytics, such as statistics, mathematics, computer science, and the social sciences
  • Minimum 5 years of experience in a similar position focusing on law enforcement or intelligence.
  • Experience with advanced statistical methods and modeling techniques such as logistic regression, HLM, Survival Analysis, SEM and ARIMA.
  • Ability to analyze unstructured data using natural language processing, sentiment analysis and new methods in machine learning for text analytics.
  • Skilled in data visualization and use of graphical applications, including Microsoft Office (Power BI) and Tableau.Experience with major data science languages, such as R and Python.
  • Skilled in aggregating, visualizing, analyzing data in R and Python.
  • Familiar with natural language processing libraries in R and Python.
  • Experience with managing and merging of disparate data sources, preferably through R, Python, or SQL.
  • Familiar with the ETL (Explore, Transition, Load) process in order to assist with the databasing and subsequent analysis of unstructured data.

Preferred Qualifications:
  • Master's Degree in a qualitative field
  • Active TS/SCI Clearance #Avantusclearedjob
