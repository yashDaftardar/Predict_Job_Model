Full Job Description

This is a full-time position, and requires a TS/SCI/Full Scope Polygraph Clearance.

2HB Incorporated is seeking a Data Scientist in order to support its government customer in McLean, VA. The program has a requirement for systems support. Lines of effort shall include direct operational support by facilitating data gathering in highly technical situations, providing engineering design and implementation of data triage capabilities, and providing technical expertise to support the development of the program’s overarching data acquisition and exploitation strategy. Priorities will be determined by the program in close consultation with the candidate project manager.

Work Requirements:
  • The candidate Team shall perform end-to-end quality assurance of data feeds and data sets.
  • The candidate Team shall provide engineering support for data triage and assessment.
  • The candidate Team shall design and deploy tactical applications (e.g. web apps) to aid end users in triaging and exploiting data from multiple sources.
  • The candidate Team shall identify and document areas for improvement in workflows or systems.
  • The candidate Team shall attend regular stand-up meetings.
  • The candidate Team shall provide input to code reviews.
  • The candidate Team shall cross-train on existing collection tools.
  • The candidate Team shall support related infrastructure and application development initiatives.
  • The candidate Team shall support monitoring, alerting, and reporting out (e.g. dashboards).
  • The candidate Team shall support new use cases.
  • The candidate Team shall research and document options for collecting or aggregating data from a variety of web based and internal program platforms.
  • The candidate Team shall evaluate web based platforms’ ability to detect or deny access.
  • The candidate Team shall make recommendations on approaches to acquire information.
  • The candidate Team shall use appropriate tools and computer programming languages, such as Python scripts, to collect and process data from a variety of sources.
  • The candidate Team shall use program-network APIs to programmatically access data and build user-facing applications to work with that data.
  • The candidate Team shall create, maintain, and enhance systems in support of data exploitation.
  • The candidate Team shall enhance existing systems to support new data pipelines.
  • The candidate Team shall create or improve custom collection scripts written in Python.
  • The candidate Team shall create or improve scripts leveraging APIs for collection needs.
  • The candidate Team shall automate data clean-up and conditioning of collected data.
  • The candidate Team shall automate data management and dissemination steps.
Position Requirements:
  • Demonstrated experience programming in at least one common compiled or interpreted language, e.g. Python, Ruby, Java, Perl, C, and C++.
  • Demonstrated experience working across team and office lines to gather and exchange data on specialized matters.
  • Demonstrated experience analyzing questions, formulating requirements, determining suitable analytic approaches, evaluating results, and communicating findings to partners and stakeholders.
  • Demonstrated ability to translate complex, technical findings into an easily understood narrative (i.e. tell a story with the data) in graphical, verbal, or written form.
  • Demonstrated experience working with data in a variety of structured and unstructured formats.
  • Demonstrated experience with a variety of database types, e.g. SQL, NoSQL, and SQLite.
  • Demonstrated experience collaborating with colleagues to developer customer-tailored products.
  • Demonstrated experience defining requirements and infrastructure for running large-scale graph analytics and natural language processing in a production environment.
This is a full-time position, and requires a TS/SCI/Full Scope Polygraph Clearance.
