About the Position:
DPS is proud to support the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) in developing mission critical analytical tools. Many of these tools will leverage Machine Learning (ML) and Natural Language Processing (NLP) methods to identify safety signals in medical devices. This work is an opportunity to help improve the lives of millions of people using medical devices.

As a Data Scientist of a Product Team , you analyze data and build models to predict signals. You will use NLP techniques to extract value from free text. You will be a part of a Scrum team of developers that includes other data scientists, machine learning engineers, and full stack and data visualization developers. If you like learning new technologies, solving tough problems, and building systems that present millions of data points in a web browser, DPS could be the right place for you.
Responsibilities:
  • Apply expertise in working with advanced analytics and data sciences
  • Conduct Exploratory Analysis
  • Leverage statistical, econometric, stochastic, operations research, predictive modeling, simulation, optimization (linear, mixed integer, constraint programming), and/or machine learning analytics techniques
  • Utilize tools/programming languages including SQL, Python, and Spark
  • Find ways to solve business problems with data
  • Formulate mathematical approaches and gather data to solve those problems, develop, analyze/draw conclusions, test solutions and present to the client
  • Clearly and effectively communicate complex analytic concepts and solutions both internally (to teammates) and externally (to the client)

Qualifications:

Minimum Requirements:

  • BS in Computer Science, Math, Physics, Engineering, Statistics or related technical field or equivalent practical experience, Masters Preferred
  • 3+ years of industry experience in Data Science and/or Machine Learning field
  • Big data and predictive modelling
  • Development experience in any scripting language (Python, Perl, etc.)
  • Experience communicating the results of analyses with product and leadership teams to influence the strategy of the product
  • Knowledge of statistics (e.g. hypothesis testing, regressions)
  • Experience manipulating data sets through statistical software (e.g. R, SAS) or other methods
  • Experience working with healthcare data is preferred
  • Comfort working as part of a dynamic team in a fast-paced environment, and able to effectively multitask with sometimes concurrent priorities
  • Ability to communicate complex information simply and clearly, both orally and in writing
