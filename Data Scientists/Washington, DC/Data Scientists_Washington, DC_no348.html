Position ID: MACSS_DataScientist_2021

Position Name: Data Scientist

As a data scientist, you can turn these complex data sets into useful information to solve global challenges.

Responsibilities:
Responsibilities
  • Use leadership and analytical skills to improve GEOINT data understanding.
  • Work closely with your customer to understand their questions and needs, and then evaluate their data-rich environment to find the pieces of their information puzzle.
  • Mentor teammates, develop algorithms, write scripts, build predictive analytics, use automation, and use the right combination of tools and frameworks to turn that set of disparate data points into objective answers to help senior leadership make informed decisions.
  • Provide your customer with a deep understanding of their data, what it all means, and how they can use it.
Qualifications:
Required Skills:
  • Possess an active TS/SCI security clearance
  • 3+ years of experience with data science and BA or BS degree
  • Experience with using scripting languages to conduct common data analysis tasks for exploration, conditioning, and advanced analytics
  • Knowledge of programming languages that include Java, Python, R, or SQL
  • Knowledge of modeling workflows to automate data acquisition
  • Ability to manipulate data from multiple structured and unstructured data sources
  • Ability to evaluate data methods, models, and algorithms
  • Ability to work independently
Desired Skills:
  • MA or MS degree
  • Experience with GEOINT domain
  • Experience with using JEMA
  • Experience with using GEOINT databases such as GIMS research, Web BVI, or CRATE
  • Experience with creating visualizations allowing consumers at varying levels the ability to gain decision insight
  • Knowledge of GEOINT databases such as GIMS Research, Web BVI, CRATE, or JEMA
  • Ability to create and apply mathematical models to reduce analytic uncertainty
  • Ability to explain complex techniques to a non-technical audience
  • Ability to apply machine learning techniques
