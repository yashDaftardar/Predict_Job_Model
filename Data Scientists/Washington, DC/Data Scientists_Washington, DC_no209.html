Cloud Data Architect (Technical & Presales)

*Remote*


We are Insight .

As a Fortune 500-ranked global provider of digital innovation, cloud/data center transformation, connected workforce, and supply chain optimization solutions and services , we help clients successfully manage their IT today while transforming for tomorrow .

From IT strategy and design to implementation and management , our employees help clients innovate and optimize their operations to run smarter .

  • Microsoft Worldwide AI Partner of the Year, 2018
  • Microsoft Worldwide Modern Desktop Partner of the Year, 2018
  • Microsoft US Partner Award for Intelligent Cloud - Application Innovation, 2019
  • Microsoft US Azure Team Partner Choice Award - Data and Artificial Intelligence, 2019

Our Insight Digital Innovation team is searching for an experienced, passionate and professional Client Facing Cloud Data Architect to join our team.


We’re an innovation and transformation consultancy that believes in the power of ingenuity to build a positive-human future in a technology-driven world. Our diverse teams of experts combine innovative thinking with breakthrough-technologies to progress further, faster.


Our engineers are working on projects that range from building systems that use drones, IoT Edge, and AI for real-time aerial inspections, to projects that monitor lice infestations in salmon populations to keep food supplies safe. Think about all the cool stuff you read about that others are doing and I’ll bet you that we have a team that is working on it right now; augmented reality with MSFT Hololens, conversation agents (chatbots) with AI and cognitive learning, real time IoT, the list goes on.


We are looking to hire a qualified Microsoft Cloud Data Architect.


Data and Big Data is a pretty hot topic right now, and our clients are investing heavily to ensure they are best placed in the market to offer exceptional value through creative, deployable Data solutions.

  • Experience with Databricks, Data Lakes and ETL technologies
  • Ability to work with clients in designing Data cloud solutions from scratch
  • Technical enough in coding the design so you can work with clients and dig into these areas on a solutions call with clients
  • Experience specializing in architecting the entire analytics technology stack.
    • Spark / Databricks
    • Data Lake
    • SQL
    • Data Warehouse
    • Semantic Models
    • ETL techniques
    • Visualization (Power BI, Tableau)
  • Good knowledge of Azure PaaS and IaaS
  • Good experience with merging traditional data warehouse modeling with newer cloud data technologies
  • General knowledge of data science techniques
  • Experience building and deploying solutions to Cloud – Azure.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills with the ability to use non-functional language when engaging with clients.



The team is made of proven Engineers/Architects who have demonstrated their understanding of software development and crafted their skill set in advanced analytics technologies. Joining them would offer the opportunity to work on major, high impact projects, solving organizations problems with the best tech in the industry.



The team is curious and encouraging, ensuring you continue developing your skills, helping others achieve their goals, and working consistently to support their customers.
