Full Job Description

SphereOI is a culture of Artificial Intelligence mixed with some actual intelligence.
Our studios engineer highly advanced AI/ML solutions for clients in several exciting industries. We help these industries realize the full promise of AI through superior engineering and novel thinking. At SphereOI, we cover all phases of innovation, from the fuzzy front-end of AI strategy all the way to building AI for operations. We are geeks, creators, and inventors who build intelligent stuff.

This position is for an AI Engineer who will work with our science and software development studios to deliver cutting-edge solutions. As an AI Engineer you will, over time, draw on your skills in statistics, traditional machine learning, deep neural networks, NLP, and other algorithms to solve our client’s most critical challenges. Data, big and small, is a ubiquitous element, and familiarity with modern data architecture and query languages is important.
What You Will Be Doing:
  • Learning new domains and problems
  • Experimenting with models and data
  • Collaborating with other experts
  • Developing reference and production implementations of models and pipelines
  • Presenting at weekly demos and various sessions
  • Supporting production engineering teams and developing automated tests
  • Building things
What You Need For This Position:
  • Talent for artificial intelligence/machine learning; CNN's are a plus
  • Hands-on experience selecting, tuning, validating models with TensorFlow
  • Experience with Python, TensorFlow and PyTorch; Other languages (e.g. R, C#, Java) are a plus
  • Bachelor’s degree in artificial intelligence, math, statistics, physics, economics or computer science


About SphereOI Studios:

SphereOI Studios is an award winning digital studio that develops data responsive solutions with deep analytics and artificial intelligence. We believe that data science that aligns with business value can increase market share and pricing power, enhance customer engagement or completely transform a product category. We fuse data strategy with data science and design driven innovation to create customer technologies for commercial and federal customers.
