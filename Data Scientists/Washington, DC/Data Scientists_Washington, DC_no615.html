Purpose of Position:

Palladium’s Data, Informatics and Analytical Solutions Practice (DIAS) is seeking a Senior Data Science Advisor to support the Data for Implementation (Data.FI) Project.

Data.FI is a five-year global project funded by the U.S. Agency for International Development (USAID) to support the HIV and COVID-19 response. Data.FI partners with programs to accelerate and sustain access to high-quality data to expedite HIV and COVID-19 epidemic control and maintenance. By improving global, regional, national, and community/local in-depth analyses of HIV and COVID-19 epidemiologic and programmatic data, Data.FI expedites program achievements to improve client services and attain and sustain control of the epidemics. Data.FI supports host country governments to enhance existing health information systems (HIS), informing management responses to gaps in programming and sustaining impact by supporting local partner transition.

The Senior Data Science Adviser oversees the design and execution of data science projects related to population and patient health using supervised and unsupervised machine learning and other data science tools. Applications leverage deidentified patient data, aggregate health reporting data, survey data, geospatial data, among others. Experience with health applications, particularly using sparse and/or poor quality data and data related to developing countries, is preferred. Expertise in NLP and geospatial analytics are of additional interest. Projects will result in outputs ranging from reports and analyses to deployed models generating information for decision makers in real time.

The home office for this position is Palladium’s Washington, D.C. office. Remote work options may be considered for exceptional candidates. The position will entail approximately 15-20% overseas travel from mid-2021.
