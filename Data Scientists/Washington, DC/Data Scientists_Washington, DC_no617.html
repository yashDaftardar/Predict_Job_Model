About Econometrica, Inc.
We are researchers and experts helping our clients make informed decisions using advanced analytics. We tackle challenges with innovative methods, research tools, and strategy to improve the quality of life. Whether examining policies to provide the best patient care, advising on housing finance strategies, or analyzing the benefits of space exploration, we apply hard work and dedication to each of our projects.


The Opportunity
We are looking for a Supervisory Statistician with strong project management skills to join our team. The primary responsibility of this position is to manage a team of statisticians evaluating different statistical methodologies used for sampling, extrapolation, and estimation of Federal overpayments. The applicant should demonstrate thorough knowledge of and extensive experience with supervision and management of multidisciplined workgroups.
Duties include but are not limited to:

  • Provide leadership and management to teams of statisticians to ensure efficient and effective services in study administration, data management, and statistical support.
  • Maintain and implement a project management plan that addresses communications, scheduling, risk, quality control, staffing, subcontractors, and cost reporting.
  • Provide strategic direction for the evaluation and drive the execution of the plan for the evaluation.
  • Identify and set timelines for assignments, projects, and methods/techniques to solve problems.
  • Coordinate the proper workflow of activities to accomplish the Statement of Work performance objectives.
  • Lead staff in distributing and balancing workload and tasks.
  • Supervise the implementation of the analyses used to evaluate the statistical methodologies.
  • Lead the preparation of manuscripts and drafts reports.
  • Conduct independent research and resolve statistical methodological issues.
  • Arrange and participate in project meetings.
  • Coordinate with other Federal contractors.
  • Lead quality assurance and quality control activities.
  • Ensure that agendas and meeting minutes are compiled and provided to the client.
  • Oversee the provision of formal briefings.
  • Oversee the maintenance and archival of all project libraries, documents, and files.
  • Address staffing changes, such as needs for staff replacements or additional support.
  • Develop and submit regular progress and expenditure reports.
  • Review and approve invoices.

Education & Qualifications
Minimum Skills and Experience

  • Certification in project management (e.g., PMP, PMI-ACP, CAPM, CSM, MPM, IAPM, etc.).
  • Project management experience with Government and/or private-sector entities.
  • Experience supervising statistical and analytical projects with ongoing review activity.
  • Ability to write and explain scientific and technical ideas in simple language.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Must be able to work well with others in a team environment.
  • Ability to work on multiple priorities at once.


Education

  • Master’s degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Information Technology, Computer Engineering, or a related field, and 6 years of relevant work experience.

Or

  • Bachelor’s degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Information Technology, Computer Engineering, or a related field, plus 8 years of relevant work experience.

Preferred Skills and Experience
