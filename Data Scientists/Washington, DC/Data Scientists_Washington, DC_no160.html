Responsibilities:


Job Description:

The Data Scientist will support United States Army Intelligence (G2) with a new cloud based security system. This individual will be involved with initial selection of the visualization and reporting tools for a cloud based intelligence support system. They will also assist with determination and development of the initial reports using the application and providing the first analytical tools and reports available to this organization to understand and interpret their data to improve operations and overall security within the US Army.


Job Duties Include (but not limited to):

  • Solve complex data problems, discovering fresh insights and delivering data to the user that is timely and establishes trust and confidence in the data
  • Develop visualizations of data to enable quick intuitive understanding of strengths and weaknesses in the program status and activities
  • Develop standard and custom reports of the data to assist with both summary and detailed understanding of the information
  • Assist a team with their first ever aggregation of data to develop their initial analytical tools
  • Selection of data visualization and reporting tool(s) for a big data stack
  • Identify and analyze data shortfalls, hidden insights or anomalies and make recommendations on ways to apply the data and help to codify new data collection or system requirements
  • Work with analysts to develop and document improvements or changes in the tactics, techniques or procedures if and when new approaches to the data require changes to TTPs or workflows
