Full Job Description

Equitus is seeking a Senior Data Analyst to support the Defense Intelligence Agency (DIA) plan for creating a modern Modernized Integrated Database (MIDB), in order to enable a more comprehensive, scalable, flexible data environment, and rigorous Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML)-ready analytic data environment. The new MIDB will be integrated and interoperable with DIA's Machine-Assisted Analytic Rapid-Repository System (MARS) environment. DIA is designing the MARS environment to fundamentally change the way DIA analysts conduct foundational intelligence analysis by implementing highly automated means of discovering, ingesting, comparing, conflating, and assigning confidence levels to vast amounts of traditional and non-traditional data.

This position will require occasional travel to Clearwater, FL to train at our company.

Responsibilities:
* Identifying data gaps or issues with data formats.
* Provides analysis of daily business processes, working with business process reengineers and human factors engineers to ensure realistic solutions are recommended to improve daily operations while ensuring confidence of the real-world users.
* Applies past FMI database experience and knowledge to interpret existing workflows and policies. Runs use cases/use stories of existing information flows and workflows.
* Collaborates with stakeholders and external FMI database managers/users to gather known issues and recommended improvements for a new environment and maximize the information available from external FMI databases.
* Filters and cleans data by reviewing reports, and performance indicators to locate and correct code problems.
* Implements controls and compliance metrics to reduce data issues and improve data quality and integrity. Identifies and leverages opportunities to continually improve the quality of data management systems, processes, and standards.
* Performs database queries, constructs data sets, and prepares analysis reports.
* Provides data definitions, establishes data quality expectations, ensuring compliance with those expectations, and analyzes and applies data.

Requirements:
* Bachelor's Degree in related field
* Minimum of 8 years of experience
* Hands-on experience in DIA and external FMI data repositories such as Cornerstone, FISHnet, FounDRI, GALE, MIDB, etc.
* TS SCI security clearance with the ability to obtain CI Poly

About Equitus:
Equitus is a VOSB (Veteran-Owned Small Business) that develops cyber-integration strategies for leading DoD organizations. Equitus develops vision and transformation plans for special operations and intelligence organizations, bridging the gap between current & future organizations. Equitus designs technology frameworks and architectures for joint intelligence programs, creating tool-agnostic solutions for fusing and delivering global data. Equitus builds, deploys and operates in-house big-data analytics capability to support commercial and government applications. In addition, Equitus designs technology frameworks, architecture & processes for joint intelligence initiatives, and completely tool-agnostic solutions for fusing and delivering global data.
