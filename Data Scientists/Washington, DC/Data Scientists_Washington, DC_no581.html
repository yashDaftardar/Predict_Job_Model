Full Job Description

Are you looking to join a highly ambitious media analytics & software startup? Do you want to bring a groundbreaking product new to the market? Cross Screen Media is founded by industry veterans and gives customers, by using a common currency for TV and digital, a new way to plan and execute video advertising campaigns. To achieve this, we need to build an amazing team. Here's where you come in.

We are looking to hire a Director of Data Science. This hire will start hands-on and build a team to evolve the methodology & algorithms at the core of our product, ScreenSolve.

We're currently 100% remote due to COVID, but we expect to spend at least 50% of our time in the office once it's safe to do so.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Lead definition and implementation of descriptive and predictive models across a variety of marketing tech functional areas
  • Collaborate with the product team, executives, & other stakeholders to define what's possible
  • Deliver output of the highest quality, including code, models, & documentation
  • Contribute to development of data pipelines
  • Deliver work on a predictable cadence

Experience:

  • MS in a math-related field
  • 5+ years experience in applied math/data science
  • Experience working in a startup/small company environment
  • Experience working with SQL
  • Experience working with Python & associated ecosystem
  • Nice to have: PhD in a math-related field
  • Nice to have: experience with Spark or other distributed data frameworks
  • Nice to have: Exposure to ad tech concepts & terminology

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary with high bonus potential
  • Collaborative and creative atmosphere, with inspired leadership
  • Career advancement opportunities
  • Recognition and reward for outstanding performance
  • Great medical, dental & vision Insurance packages
  • Competitive 401K with company match to plan for the long term
  • Unlimited paid-time-off
  • Transportation benefits & cell phone reimbursement
  • Casual dress all day, every day
  • Unfortunately we are not able to sponsor visas for this position.
