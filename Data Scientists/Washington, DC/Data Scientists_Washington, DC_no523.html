Position Description: Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning Developer $150,000 - $180,000.
ZTI Solutions LLC is seeking an experienced AI/ML Developer to join our team in Crystal City, VA. The ideal candidate will be very comfortable with the software development lifecycle. Candidates must describe different types of neural networks and readily identify which type is the most effective for operating against specific datasets. The candidate troubleshoots neural network malfunctions and identifies likely process steps where an error occurred. The candidate will be responsible for converting given requirements to specific programming tasks and submitting them before given deadlines. AI/ML Engineer responsibilities include creating machine learning models and retraining systems prototypes. To be successful, you will need exceptional skills in statistics and programming with the end goal of shaping and building efficient self-learning applications.
Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:
  • Ability to develop neural networks using foundation APIs and high-level computer programming languages.
  • Ability to inject a variety of different types into neural networks for training and evaluation purposes.
  • Model large neural networks using frameworks such as MATLAB
  • Troubleshoot neural network malfunctions and identify likely process steps where an error occurred
  • Describe different types of neural networks and readily identify which type is the most effective for operating against specific datasets.
  • Develops computer programs using both interpreted and compiled programming languages
  • Produce quality professional documentation based on programs developed.
  • Independently work towards a defined goal with limited supervision.

Requirements:
  • U.S. Citizen.
  • Active TS/SCI Clearance.
  • Detail-orientated.
  • Exercises Extreme attention to detail both in development and documentation.
  • Knowledge of TCP/IP programming for data acquisition is a plus.
  • Strong knowledge of the Nvidia CUDA API.
  • Proven experience as an AI/ML Developer.

Preferred:
  • Bachelor’s Degree or higher in an Information Technology Field.
  • Preferred 10+ years of experience.
  • Experience developing on Red Hat Linux.
  • Knowledge of ingesting, examining, and manipulating photographs.
  • Experience developing Generative Adversarial Networks.

Benefits:
  • 3-4 Weeks Paid Time Off (PTO).
  • All Federal Holiday’s Paid Vacation.
  • Four Percent Matching 401K.
  • Health/vision/dental benefits for the employee and family paid 100% by ZTI Solutions, LLC.
