Full Job Description

United States Citizenship is required for this and all positions due to security clearance requirements.

Expedition Technology in Herndon, VA is seeking mid-career to senior level software engineers to apply and integrate cutting edge machine learning models into operational software systems. Our ideal candidates will work collaboratively with a team of engineers to develop deep learning algorithms that will be deployed within software ecosystems designed to meet our clients’ specific needs.

Do you want to make a difference by tackling our Nation’s toughest defense and intelligence challenges? Do you want to perform meaningful work as part of a diverse team while maintaining flexibility and a work/life balance? If you are intellectually curious, excited about pushing the boundaries of what is possible with the integration of artificial intelligence and software engineering, we have opportunities for you!

We are looking for people who:

  • Are independent thinkers that possess intellectual curiosity and who don’t give up when faced with tough challenges
  • Are able to work autonomously within a cohesive team in an Agile/Scrum development environment
  • Have a growth mindset and are excited about applying cutting edge solutions to full-stack software applications

Required skills include:

  • BS/MS/PhD in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Data Science or comparable field (advanced degrees preferred)
  • US Citizenship required for US Government Security Clearance eligibility (All applicants must be clearable and willing to go through the process to obtain a Top Secret security clearance)
  • Minimum 3 years’ work experience in a related field using required skills listed
  • 3+ years’ work experience with at least one object-oriented language such as Python, C++, Java or similar
  • Development experience in a Linux environment (Ubuntu preferred)
  • Strong familiarity with DevOps patterns and practices
  • Experience with developing and deploying deep learning/AI applications in a Cloud environment (AWS preferred)
  • Experience with Docker, Kubernetes and Microservices
  • Experience with Pytorch, TensorFlow or similar frameworks
  • Strong communication skills and the ability to work as a member of diverse teams in roles that may change based on program type and objective

If you have these skills, it will really excite us:

  • Theoretical background with supervised and unsupervised Artificial Intelligence learning techniques (clustering, deep learning, neural networks, reinforcement learning, etc.)
  • Proven machine learning or similar algorithm development experience
  • Experience with integrating Computer Vision algorithms into production ready software
  • An active TS/SCI security clearance
