Director, Data Science


Location: Northern Virginia / Washington D.C.


Enabled Intelligence

Enabled Intelligence, Inc. provides extremely accurate, precise and secure data labeling to help our government and commercial customers effectively deploy reliable and unbiased artificial intelligence technologies. We leverage the unique talents of veterans, people with different abilities, and subject matter experts to unlock the value of data to improve the delivery of public missions and government services, including in agriculture, economic development, education, national security, public health, and transportation. Enabled Intelligence also develops training and test data sets for clients, and overtime will develop AI models for clients.

At EI we respect and celebrate individuals from all walks of life. Our diverse backgrounds, cultures, experiences, and way of thinking make us stronger together and result in the best work products. We are extremely committed to an inclusive, integrated, and welcoming environment where everyone can thrive and grow.

Director, Data Science

The Director, Data Science will lead the company's efforts to leverage our growing body of annotated data into reliable and powerful AI tools for the US government and its partners. The director will develop strategies for AI/ML data curation and tool development. As a member of the Enabled Intelligence senior team, you will develop and lead the strategy that uses our ultra-accurate and effective data labeling practices to create highly reliable and competitively dominant AI/ML models and products.

We are looking for expert data scientists committed to supporting U.S. government missions who want to leverage their creativity and intelligence to develop reliable AI/ML technologies for government and commercial use. Enabled Intelligence team members are individuals who are energized by the opportunity to help a new company grow and care about a people first culture. Joining our team means playing an integral role in the future of government AI/ML capabilities and the opportunity to build a strong leader in reliable, bias-free, and mission oriented AI/ML technologies.

All selected candidates will have detailed onboarding and training.

Responsibilities

  • Provide data science direction and expertise to ensure data annotation projects meet customer demands and enable meaningful AI technology development
  • Create and lead technical strategy to create valuable training and test data for our customers;
  • Lead company strategy and efforts to develop highly-reliable AI models and products from company provided, open-source, and customer provided data sets
  • Collaborate with the Director of Annotation & Analysis to ensure accurate and effective data labeling techniques
  • Work with project teams to develop meaningful customer focused data labeling taxonomies;
  • Interact with customers and partners and demonstrate the company's expertise in data science, AI/ML; and data labeling
  • Assist in evaluating annotation team metrics to help improve productivity
  • Keep VP of Operations and Director of Annotation & Analysis informed of platform enhancement ideas, risks, and issues

Required Qualifications and Skills

  • 7+ years of experience with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or a similar field OR 3+ years of experience with a Master's degree OR 2+ years of experience with PhD degree
  • Expertise in data science, AI/ML, data labeling, and innovations in AI/ML technology development
  • Ability to understand and apply guidelines for data labeling
  • Hands-on experience using deep learning and computer vision libraries such as PyTorch, TensorFlow, Caffe, MXNet, OpenCV, Tensorflow and scikit
  • Experience with containerization of machine learning applications (AWS DL Containers, Docker, Singularity, Apache Mesos Openshift, Kubernetes)
  • Prior experience in developing AI/ML tools and dealing with diverse data sets for model development.
  • Ability to create initial code and scripts in Python to enable development of prototype AI models and tools
  • Stay up to date on newest AI/ML technologies, algorithms, etc
  • Solution oriented with the ability to be flexible and thrive in an ever changing and growing environment
  • Ability to work independently and communicate with individuals of diverse backgrounds
  • Strategic thinker able to ensure operational program excellence while developing long-term company technical strategy
  • Ability to keep information confidential
  • Ability to obtain a security clearance

Desired Qualifications and Skills

  • Prior experience in analysis of specific types government data required for projects is preferred;

Physical Requirements

  • Prolonged periods of sitting at a desk and working on a computer
