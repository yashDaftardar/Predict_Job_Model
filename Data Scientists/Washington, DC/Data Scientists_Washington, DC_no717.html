Senior Research Scientist

Avalere

Avalere Health is seeking an experienced data scientist to join our growing Health Economics & Advanced Analytics (HEAA) Practice. The HEAA practice consists of staff with specialized experience in solving analytic and business challenges for healthcare stakeholders (e.g., biopharmaceutical companies, health plans, health systems, medical societies, etc.) using internal and external large scale, real-world datasets. In addition to data scientists, our team is comprised of health economists, health service researchers, evidence strategists, and project managers. We work closely with other functional areas within Avalere and Inovalon, applying the full scope of the firm's knowledge base to the development of multi-disciplinary, creative solutions on behalf of our clients. We are seeking a Senior Research Scientist to contribute subject matter expertise to the conceptualization, execution, and delivery of analyses in health economics, applied mathematics, comparative effectiveness, performance measure development and testing, outcomes research, predictive analytics, and/or epidemiology.

Responsibilities:

  • Interface with clients and internal colleagues to design and execute quantitative research studies using data derived from large-scale administrative and clinical databases
  • Lead, or lend subject matter expertise to, the direction and conceptualization of proposals, statements of work, and project budgets
  • Lead, or lend subject matter expertise to, the development of study protocols, statistical analysis plans, and client deliverables
  • Develop and author study reports, scientific publications, and conference abstracts and posters
  • Independently manage workflow and make direct contributions to the research process
  • Design and/or ensure rigorous internal quality control protocols
  • Collaborate with the HEAA leadership team to identify areas for delivering innovation to clients
  • Supervise a team of programmers, data scientists, or research associates as needed
  • Maintain compliance with Inovalon's policies, procedures and mission statement;
  • Adhere to all confidentiality and HIPAA requirements as outlined within Inovalon's Operating Policies and Procedures in all ways and at all times with respect to any aspect of the data handled or services rendered in the undertaking of the position; and
  • Fulfill those responsibilities and/or duties that may be reasonably provided by Inovalon for the purpose of achieving operational and financial success of the Employer.

Minimum Requirements:

  • Master's degree or PhD in epidemiology, biostatistics, data science, health services research, public health, or related discipline
  • 10+ years of experience working for a health payer/plan, health system, integrated provider network, or healthcare consultancy
  • Broad technical knowledge of epidemiology, health economics, outcomes research, and program evaluation theory and design
  • Proficiency conducting observational research studies using large administrative medical and pharmacy claims, EMR, and public and private databases
  • Intermediate to expert R, SAS, and/or Python programming experience
  • Intermediate to expert SQL programming experience
  • Advanced knowledge of statistical and econometric modeling techniques
  • Experience presenting research at scientific conferences and/or a record of peer-reviewed publications
  • Experience leading teams of programmers or data scientists

Additional Skills and Competencies:

  • Client/customer facing experience strongly preferred
  • Excellent writing, project management, communication, and presentation skills
  • Ability to independently manage multiple work streams with limited supervision
  • Demonstrated problem solving, analytical reasoning and decision-making skills and strong commitment to meeting goals and deadlines
  • Positive, flexible and self-motivated attitude, with excellent teamwork skills and ability to work in a multi-disciplinary environment
