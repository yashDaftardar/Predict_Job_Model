JOB TITLE: Senior Data Analyst (O*NET 15-2041, Statistician)


RESPONSIBILITIES AND BASIC FUNCTIONS:


  • Performs descriptive and in-depth analysis for problem solving and supports decision making, by extracting, cleaning, summarizing, Tabulating and formatting data using tools such as SQL, SAS, Oracle SQL developer, Python, Tableau.
  • Participates in and/or lead projects of moderate to complex scope as assigned and manages internal and external customers.
  • Performs data analysis and provides consultative and advisory recommendations to service area leaders supplying data for strategic operational and financial decisions.
  • Develops complex clinical reports, including but not limited to utilization, performance and efficiency relevant to patient care, for stakeholders use in delivery system management.
  • Develops and automates reports using SQL, SAS, Excel and visual analytics using Tableau.
  • Conducts ongoing stakeholder education and provides management consultation on the use and interpretation of analyses, reports, methodologies, source data and systems.
  • Partners with medical office controller in maintaining the budget system, managing department budget submissions, and monitoring service area financial and operations performance.
  • Develops and validates budget and target assumptions against historical results and industry expectations using internal systems and external data as available.
  • Participates in the planning and development of service area, departmental and/or functional budgets.
  • Demonstrates leadership and willingness to teach/inform stakeholders.
  • Works autonomously or collaboratively on stand-alone assignments or as part of a project team.
  • Monitors compliance with policies and procedures related to financial management and budget systems, data integrity and privacy of non-financial data, and other data sets and systems
