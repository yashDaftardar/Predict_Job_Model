The Business Analyst – Operations Analytics leads data analytics projects in support of the Order to Cash team, including playing a major role in scaling and automating manual reporting. Key areas of responsibility include partnering with stakeholders to identify requirements, creating and enhancing reporting solutions, and automating manual data analysis processes. This role will serve as a dedicated advisor to the Director of Finance Operations, providing ad-hoc support and analysis and contributing to strategic projects.

Primary Responsibilities :

  • Manages Order to Cash reporting lifecycle from end-to-end by leading design and development of new reporting solutions
  • Builds strong relationships with users in Finance Operations and serves as a trusted advisor for all data analytics issues
  • Creates new reporting or enhances existing reports based on requirements from key users
  • Develops Tableau dashboards to summarize information for business users
  • Writes SQL queries to compile and analyze data
  • Creates Alteryx workflows to automate manual excel-based reporting processes
  • Trains, guides, and coaches end users on the Operations team in using reporting effectively
  • Supports the Director of Finance Operations and other senior stakeholders with ad-hoc analysis and contributes to special projects

Job Requirements :

  • Proven experience working as a business analyst or business intelligence developer
  • Proven experience extracting, analyzing, and reporting on data from Salesforce or a similar CRM
  • Strong knowledge of Tableau or a similar tool (Power BI, Spotfire, Qlikview etc)
  • Intermediate knowledge of SQL. Comfortable joining multiple tables, using functions, and creating views.
  • Intermediate knowledge of Alteryx or similar tool (i.e. Pentaho, Talend, Informatica, Python, etc). Comfortable integrating multiple data sources and applying complex business logic. Experience with macros and creating analytical apps is a major plus
  • Intermediate Excel skills. Comfortable using advanced functions such as INDEX/MATCH
  • Experience gathering requirements, conducting interviews and facilitating brainstorming or collaboration sessions with internal stakeholders
  • Strong communication skills and experience presenting to senior stakeholders

Education and Experience :

  • Bachelor’s degree with courses in business, management information systems, computer science, or appropriate combination of education and experience required
  • A minimum of 4+ years of experience creating, reporting from, and analyzing large datasets from multiple disparate sources, producing analytic reports for high-level audiences, and presentation of information. Experience building complex reports and managing multiple projects
  • Experience in working in a Finance, Sales Operations, or Consulting environment is preferred

Special Requirements :

  • May be required to take a test
