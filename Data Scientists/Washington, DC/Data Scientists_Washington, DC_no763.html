Job Description


At Freddie Mac, you will do important work to build a better housing finance system and you’ll be part of a team helping to make homeownership and rental housing more accessible and affordable across the nation.

Position Overview:

Do you have passion for understanding customer needs and using groundbreaking technology to quickly deliver value? Do you thrive in a fast-paced and constantly evolving environment? It is said that a model is as good as the quality of data that feeds into it. Based on this, Freddie Mac Capital Markets is seeking a quantitative analytics senior to assist in the development and execution of data quality processes for model input data in support of model ongoing monitoring. Apply now and learn why there’s #MoreAtFreddieMac!

**This position can be performed currently from a remote location in the U.S. but will require presence in a Freddie Mac office in the future.***


Our Impact:

  • Our team is responsible for providing modeling and analytical support for the capital Markets group.

Your Impact:

  • Work with model owners and their teams to develop quality checks on model input data. Subsequently, assist in the implementation of these quality rules and in establishing a process to monitor the execution of those rules on an ongoing basis.

  • Monitor execution of data quality rules and work with different groups to identify, raise, and resolve any identified potential input data issues. Work with the model owner to document and maintain all resolution documentation.

  • Work with the model owner to build data quality dashboards that are presented during scheduled meetings to help communicate the quality of model data input.

  • Preparing and maintaining data governance documentation to promote compliance with corporate and enterprise risk requirements.

Qualifications:

  • Doctorate degree (or Master’s degree with equivalent work experience) in data science, statistics, data science or a related quantitative field.

  • Coursework or work experience applying predictive modeling techniques from data science, statistics, machine learning, and econometrics to large data sets. Qualifying coursework may include—but is not limited to—data science, statistics, machine learning, optimization, numerical analysis, scientific programming, computational methods, supervised learning, unsupervised learning, text mining, and image analysis.

  • Coursework or work experience writing computer programs to implement data science pipelines and predictive algorithms. Programming languages may include—but are not limited to—Python, R, SQL, Java, SAS, and MATLAB.

  • Coursework or work experience using technologies for manipulating structured and unstructured big data. Big data technologies may include—but are not limited to—Hadoop, Hive, Pig, Spark, relational databases, and NoSQL.

  • Data governance experience is preferred but not required.

  • Coursework or work Experience in data quality management including a clear understanding of data quality dimensions and how to apply data quality checks over them.

  • Coursework or work experience with AWS, Snowflake, Apache Spark, and other big data tool to analyze big data sets. Proficiency with PySpark or Scala is a plus.

  • Coursework or experience in creating dashboards using tools such as Tableau and MicroStrategy.

Keys to Success in this Role:

  • Deep curiosity to learn about new trends and latest and greatest in the model data analytics and governance space.

  • Stay current on policies and regulations, internal or external to the company, that might impact our existing data quality framework and data governance processes.

  • Strong verbal and written communication skills, including the ability to present content of varying levels of complexity to a range of technical and business audience.

  • Ability to work across multiple teams and be able to prioritize deliverables and to meet tight deadlines.

  • Working under limited direction, independently resolving and developing approach to solutions.

  • Attention to detail.

  • Familiarity with working within an Agile environment.
