Job Description:

Prescient Edge is seeking a Senior Principal Data Scientist – DRI to support a Federal government client. As a Senior Principal Data Scientist, you will:

  • Perform AI-related software engineering and development activities using an Agile DevSecOps model
  • Design and implement AI and machine learning approaches and algorithms that can be used to enhance analyst effectiveness using tools and techniques, such as Java, Python, R, PySPark, and TensorFlow, or similar frameworks
  • Create hypotheses for and identify relationships within data that can be leveraged using advanced analytic methods including AI and machine learning
  • Collaborate with other members of the scrum team to create and implement new data labeling and analytic algorithms and visualizations that codify new techniques
  • Develop approaches to cross-correlate data sources and ensure consistency among data-sets and build mathematical models using the data
  • Work with human factors engineers and Web software developers to create visualization techniques that aid analysts in understanding data
  • Monitor algorithms to measure efficiency and effectiveness and provide recommendations for improvements as needed

Please note that the availability of this position is contingent upon contract award.

Job Requirements:
  • Active TS/SCI with the ability to obtain a CI Polygraph
  • A Bachelor’s degree with a minimum of 9 years of relevant experience; or a master’s degree with a minimum of 7 years of relevant experience; or a PhD with a minimum of 4 years of relevant experience
    • An additional 4 years of experience maybe considered in lieu of a degree requirement
  • Minimum of 9 years of experience performing AI-related software engineering and development activities supporting, maintaining, and enhancing applications using Agile methodologies
  • Experience with AWS, Java, Python, JSON, Spring, Spring Boot, Docker, JIRA, Git, Junit, RDBMS technology, Elasticsearch and/or SOLR, NoSQL technology (Hadoop and/or EMR, Web UI JavaScript frameworks (e.g., JQuery, Angular, React)
