We are currently seeking a Data Analyst/Scientist in Food/Nutrition for a Scientist level position in our Chemical Regulation and Food Safety Practice in Washington, DC. In this role you will work as part of a team to provide scientific and statistical support to senior consultants on diverse projects related to Food Science, Nutrtion, Public Health and related areas. You will be mentored by other consultants in the Center and will have opportunities to collaborate with consultants in other science and engineering practices at Exponent.

You will be responsible for:

  • Building, reviewing, managing, processing and communicating results form various types of datasets
  • Conducting quantitative data analysis
  • Working with national survey data sets, specifically the National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey (NHANES)
  • Conducting literature reviews
  • Providing editorial and technical peer reviews
  • Supporting interdisciplinary teams on a wide-range of nutritoin and food safety related topics

You will have the following skills and qualifications:

  • M.S. or Ph.D. with a concentration in Biostatistics/Statistics, Data Sciences, Food Science, Nutrition, Public Health, or related sciences
  • 1-2 years of relevant experience preferred
  • Knowledge of statistical and research methodology
  • Experience in statistical and regression tools in analyzing data
  • Experience in development and management of scientific data
  • Strong computer skills, specifically with Microsoft Word, Excel, Power Point, and statistical software programs (e.g., SPSS, STATA, SAS)
  • Interest in working with, modifying, and/or developing computer programming codes
  • Intellectual ability and comfort to work with and communicate effectively with non-scientists
  • Ability to work independently and manage multiple assignments under rigid time constraints
  • Ability to collaborate with diverse colleagues and participate effectively in team projects is essential
