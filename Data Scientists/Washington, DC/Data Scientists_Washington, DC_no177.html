Your Impact:


Responsibilities:

  • Working independently and collaboratively in a team, on strategic projects to develop complex data models and solutions.
  • Analyzing, interpreting, and extracting data to support decision-making, engaging with customers to understand changing requirements, and recommending solutions based on quantitative analysis.
  • Independently compiles and reviews often disparate and inconsistent data to address complex intelligence issues or problems for internal/external customers.
  • May require constructing and performing complex statistical, mathematical, and data analysis independently, or in partnership with other data scientists or analysts.
  • Provides recommendations for the development of tools and applications that improve process effectiveness and efficiency.

Here’s What You’ll Need:


Here’s What You’ll Need:
Qualifications:

  • Experience supporting complex data science and analytic projects
  • Proficiency with Python or R
  • Experience using SQL to query large relational databases
  • Experience/knowledge of computer science concepts including programming/algorithm development, statistics, data architecture, data analytics, data science including visualization techniques
  • Experience with quantitative and qualitative analytics
  • Experience constructing and performing complex database search queries across multiple databases
  • Ability to effectively communicate complex technical findings to non-technical audiences in both written and verbal formats
  • Experience identifying, recommending, and implementing continuous improvement approaches
  • Overall 4 to 8 years of experience
  • Degree in BA/BS or MA/MS degree in a technical field such as mathematics, engineering, economics, or computer science
  • TS/SCI with Polygraph security clearance

Desired Skills:

  • Experience in modern machine learning, biometrics, social network analysis, or geospatial analysis
  • Experience/knowledge of link analysis tools ( e.g. Palantir, i2 Analyst’s Notebook)
  • Experience with cloud architectures including AWS
  • Experience with distributed computing and storage technologies
