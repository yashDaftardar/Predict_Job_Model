Full Job Description

Overview:
Acuity, Inc. seeks to hire a dynamic, self-motivated Technical SME for our Technology Solutions Group (TSG) to help drive business strategy by delivering high-quality insights and technical leadership in Business Intelligence, Data Warehousing, and Big Data. Your expertise will provide in-depth knowledge and thought leadership across multiple software and data engineering technologies to support Acuity’s Data capability services and solutions.
The Technical SME serves as an integral part of our fast-paced, growing environment by leading multi-disciplined engineering teams in the proposal, design, development, and delivery of superior technical solutions. S/he serves as the key interface between our TSG, proposal team, and project delivery teams. The Technical SME is a decision-maker, mentor, and motivator responsible for ensuring all technical performance, cost, and schedule goals on the program are met.
Responsibilities:
Data Engineering & Solutioning
  • Lead and participate in technical and staffing recommendations for proposal development.
  • Act as an advisor to top management and customers on advanced technical studies and applications.
  • Provide solutions and recommendations on data architecture frameworks, governance, guiding principles, best practices, reference architecture, and standards.
  • Establish standards for modeling, structuring, naming, describing, securing, and formatting data.
  • Evaluate and make recommendations regarding database design for data integrity.
  • Establish data normalization standards, processes, and procedures to scale, manage and deploy data marts that support business intelligence dashboards.
  • Diagnose, isolate, and resolve advanced problems on new and existing data infrastructures.
  • Architect and design all types of data stores such as application databases, ODS, EDW, Data Mart, Data Lake, etc.
Technical Management
  • Diagnose, isolate, and resolve advanced problems of new and existing data infrastructures.
  • Provide leadership for operating performance.
  • Evaluate operational processes and procedures, and develop strategies and implementation plans to improve and standardize all aspects of operations.
  • Collaborate, coordinate, and communicate effectively with corporate support functions.

Business Development
  • Actively participate in capture and proposal efforts, working closely with Capture and Operations.
  • Develop company presentations and present capabilities to prospective customers.
  • Attend market-facing events, identify and participate in speaking opportunities at industry events.
  • Able to prioritize and manage several projects and priorities simultaneously with the ability to be hands-on and strategic.
  • Apply technology to meet customer business needs and to shape solutions specific to customer requirements.
  • Prioritize professional services that will benefit the company, and impact key business metrics.
  • Ensure excellent customer relations, as well as engage in high-priority interactions with clients.
  • Manage customer relationships and communicate opportunities and issues to leadership.
  • Able to connect business development activities with other teammates to maximize the ability to deliver solutions.
  • Assist in developing and briefing bid/no-bid recommendations and assist in developing pursuit and capture strategies for new business opportunities; build business cases for advancing opportunities via comprehensive gate reviews.
  • Perform market research evaluating trends and new solutions.

Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science or Information Systems, Master’s strongly preferred.
  • At least seven (7) years of related experience in data architecture, including five (5+) years of data modeling and database design and administration.
  • At least two (2) years of experience writing technical documents.
  • Experience with Big data ecosystem and its relevant components.
  • Experience implementing a Data lake solution for a federal or commercial customer.
  • Hands-on skills working with PostgreSQL, Oracle, SQL Server. Desired skills: experience on MariaDB and neo4j.
  • Strong understanding of Cloud-based relational, non-relation, and graph-based database services, Cloud Native development, Cloud Infrastructure, Open Source Frameworks, one or more Cloud Environments (AWS, Azure, or other).
  • Proven track record of establishing Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Advanced Analytics, Data Governance, Data Management, Data Storage and Archival Strategies and executable roadmaps.
  • Experience with ETL technologies and BI tools.
  • Knowledge of advanced technology concepts like Big Data, Hadoop, Hive, Cloud, etc.
  • Familiarity with delivery frameworks (i.e., Agile, DevOps, Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC).
  • Federal Government contracting experience.

Leadership Attributes

  • Exceptional communication and organizational skills.
  • Strong interpersonal skills, outstanding oral and written communications, proactive, innovative, disciplined, team-builder, motivator, closer.
  • Responsive, fast-paced decision-maker who is highly driven to achieve business expectations.
  • Results-oriented driven by personal and team accountability.
  • A fact-based management style aggressively addressing business and customer’s tactical issues and strategic needs.
  • Maintains transparent, performance-based communications with leadership.
  • Strong influencing skills to work with partners and operations staff to offer the right resources and solutions to the customer.
Clearance:
  • US Citizenship is required, and the ability to obtain and maintain Public Trust suitability.
