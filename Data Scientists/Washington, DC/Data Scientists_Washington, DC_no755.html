Full Job Description

You are highly encouraged to apply if you are excited about latest technologies in wireless communications, networking, and machine learning; interested in performing research studies and applying them into applications throughout the software stack and hardware platforms. The candidate should have strong analytical and coding skills, be passionate about understanding how things work, and want to solve complex problems.


JOB DUTIES

  • Perform R&D in wireless communications, networks, and machine learning.
  • Develop protocols and prototypes in current and emerging R&D areas such as 5G, 6G, cognitive radio, software-defined radio, MIMO, Radio Access Network, CORE, network optimization, mmWave and THz communications, wireless security, RF fingerprinting, edge computing, Internet of Things, machine learning, deep learning, reinforcement learning, federated learning, and adversarial machine learning.
  • Setup a hardware-in-the-loop testing environment, program network/radio devices, test and evaluate the performance of the developed algorithms and protocols.
  • Summarize research results.
  • Work with a team to develop new algorithms and modules suitable for integration into production framework.
  • Develop, test and document software.


REQUIRED SKILLS

  • Proficient in programming languages (e.g., Python and C/C++) and software tools (e.g., TensorFlow and PyTorch)
  • Research publication record is a plus.


EDUCATION

  • A BS, MS or PhD degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering, Computer Science, or related field is required

About IAI


IAI is a technology innovation company specializing in providing advanced technology solutions and R&D services to federal agencies and corporations. IAI leverages agile R&D processes, a multi-disciplinary collaborative environment, and its substantial intellectual property portfolio to excel in developing concepts into market-focused products and customer-driven solutions. IAI’s core R&D areas include: AI & Advanced Computing; Autonomy & Robotics; Healthcare Research Technologies; Modeling, Simulation & Visualization; and Networks & Cyber Security; and Radar, Communications & Sensors. For more information about IAI please visit www.i-a-i.com .
