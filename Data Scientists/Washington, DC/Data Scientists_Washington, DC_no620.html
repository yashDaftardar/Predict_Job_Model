Job Description

Altron Inc. is seeking a Machine Learning (ML): Artificial Intelligence (AI) Engineer to build and manage a platform for machine learning products. This team member will design, discover, develop and implement analytical methods to support innovative approaches to data and information processing.

This position offers a competitive salary and benefits package, performance-based merit increases as well as the ability for stock options and financial bonuses.

Now offering a minimum $1,000 sign-on bonus!

Key Responsibilities

  • Study and convert data science prototypes

  • Perform statistical analysis and fine-tune models using test results

  • Train and re-train ML systems and models as and when necessary to create a complete and effective product

  • Research, experiment with and implement suitable ML algorithms and tools

  • Analyze the problem-solving capabilities and use-cases of ML algorithms and rank them by their success probability

  • Explore and visualize data for better understanding and identify differences in data distribution that could impact model performance when deploying it in live Navy Systems scenarios

Required Skills:

  • Extensive data modeling and data architecture skills

  • Experience with Apache Spark for use during implementation of programs

  • Programming experience in Python, R and/or Java

  • Background in Machine Learning frameworks such as TensorFlow or Keras

  • Knowledge of Hadoop or another distributed computing system

  • Experience working in a fast-paced, Agile environment

  • Possess Advanced math skills (linear algebra, Bayesian statistics, group theory)

  • Strong written and verbal communication skills

  • Advanced degree in Computer Science, Math, Statistics or a related field.


About Altron

Altron Inc. works on mission-critical Navy systems to support the US Navy by maintaining its technological dominance so the US can continue to be the leader in providing National Security Worldwide. Altron engineers work on state-of-the-art systems using the freshest technology and cutting-edge software development techniques to support this mission.

At Altron, we bring in the brightest and most highly motivated staff we can and give them an environment in which they can gain new skills and grow their careers. If you have an interest in working closely with some of the brightest engineers in the industry, this could be the position for you.
