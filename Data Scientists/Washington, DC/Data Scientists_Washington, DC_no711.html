Overview:
JHNA is seeking an Artificial Intelligence (AI) Subject Matter Expert with experience and expertise in cutting edge technologies and integration of these technologies into new and innovative warfighting capabilities.

The mission of OUSD (R&E) as the Chief Technology Officer is to foster technological dominance across the Department of Defense and ensure the advantage of the American warfighter. The OUSD (R&E) enterprise is composed of the Directorate of Defense Research and Engineering for Advanced Capabilities; the Directorate of Defense Research and Engineering for Research and Technology; the Directorate of Defense Research and Engineering for Modernization which provides strategic direction for the 11 modernization priority areas (Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning, Autonomy, Biotechnology, Cyber, Directed Energy, Fully Networked Command, Control & Communications, Hypersonics, Microelectronics, Space, Quantum Science, and 5G Network Technology).

Come join a certified Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) that is focused on providing a broad range of services that help make us "The Warfighter to Engineer Interface". Our services include Mission Engineering, System Engineering and DT&E in such development phases as: Planning, execution, and assessment of Developmental or Operational Test & Evaluation; Technology evaluation and prioritization; and Operational and System DoDAF View (OVs, SVs). JHNA offers highly competitive benefit packages and a culture that fosters teamwork and collaboration all while supporting our Warfighter!
Responsibilities:
  • Support PD oversight of and technical assistance to the Joint Artificial Intelligence Center (JAIC).
  • Develop and coordinate concepts and requirements for the application of current and emerging technologies as they apply to DoD's R&E program.
  • Provide programmatic expertise to the Government from system concept definition through technology transfer activities.
  • Develop technology roadmaps, risk management plans, and strategies to acquisition and programmatic decisions relative to USD R&E mission objectives.
  • Make recommendations to the development of Concepts of Operations (CONOPS) pertinent to mission needs and technical capabilities.
  • Assess and advise on Joint Capability Technology Demonstrations (JCTD) requirements, capabilities, limitations, and constraints as they pertain to defense mission resilience and national security.
  • Represent USD R&E on Integrated Product Teams (IPTs) and participate in Government-approved conferences and forums in addition to specific USD R&E projects.
Qualifications:
REQUIRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE
  • 7 years of recent Artificial Intelligence Program experience
  • Master's Degree in a STEM field +14 years of experience
  • Active DoD issued TS with SCI eligibility within the last 12 months
  • Previous experience with Department of Defense RDT&E mission and processes.
  • Previous experience in engineering, analysis of alternatives, integration, requirements, and testing.
  • Previous experience with command, control, and communications (C3) programs.
  • Experience with Cross-Domain Solutions.
  • Excellent writing, communication and briefing skills.

  • War College Experience
  • Acquisition Experience
  • Experience working in Joint Staff, with the DoD Labs, and DARPA
  • PhD
