Senior Data Scientist (voice/speech)

Location: United States (NOTE: Daon office is located in Northern VA, but recruitment location for the role is flexible)


Role Description:

Work with the latest voice biometric and speech technologies to:

  • Design & implement voice-based algorithms for use in identity authentication solutions
  • Develop voice algorithms for the telephony call center and IVR using state-of-the-art knowledge in machine learning, deep learning, speech, and voice biometrics
  • Create solutions to detect voice presentation attacks, identify replayed recordings, & prevent fraud
  • Write product code to run on servers and smartphones
  • Apply your expertise in speech research, telephony audio, & voice biometrics.

Person Specification:

  • 5+ years of relevant industry experience.
  • Passionate about AI and voice telephony algorithms.
  • Experience in developing & training complex state-of-the-art algorithms.
  • Strong machine learning and signal processing background applied to speech technology.
  • Proven experience with machine learning frameworks such as Tensorflow and Pytorch
  • Working knowledge of speech libraries & toolkits, including Kaldi and other open-source
  • Ideally, have commercial experience with voice fraud detection, voice biometrics, speech recognition, and speech processing.
  • Proficiency in C++ & Python
  • A quick learner with strong mathematical aptitude

At Daon, we know that ability comes in different shapes. We value passion, dedication, and a willingness to learn. Please consider applying if your experience is close to what we are looking for, as we believe that diversity is a fundamental strength.


You have:

  • Ph.D. or Masters in Computer Science, Engineering, a Math's related field, or equivalent
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Ability to work independently and as a member of a team
  • Excellent attention to detail
  • Excellent verbal and written English
  • Professional attitude and appearance

We offer:

  • An opportunity to work in the growing world of biometric and identity management technologies
  • Competitive compensation & benefits
  • Flexible working environment to create a very balanced work/life schedule for you and your family and much more.

Principals Only. Daon is an Equal Opportunity Employer.
