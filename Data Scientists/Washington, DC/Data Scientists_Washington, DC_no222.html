Responsibilities:
Kearney is currently seeking a Data Analyst Senior Consultant to join our Alexandria, VA team. The Data Analyst position will be responsible for supporting our Air Force engagement in performing various levels of data analytics, to including retrieving, cleansing, and making data easily digestible into other data platform. The individual will be required to advise, guide, train, and execute the process of the transitioning raw data into data visualization tools the client can present to the senior leaders. Individual will also be responsible for the preparation of deliverables as assigned under the supervision of their Manager and client. Other responsibilities include:
  • Demonstrate professionalism and competence in the client’s environment
  • Foster an efficient, innovative, and team-oriented work environment
  • Gather related data
  • Assists in drafting and preparing project deliverables
  • Utilize technology to share knowledge with team members, develop skills, and continually learn
  • Must be a self-starter, self-motivated, and results-oriented
  • Ability to learn new programming languages quickly
  • Proficient in building dash boards and data visualizations using Tableau
  • Ability to take and apply training in data analytics and robotics and machine learning
  • Demonstrate excellent verbal and written communication skills
Qualifications:
Required:
  • Masters degree from an accredited college
  • Minimum of 10 years of workforce experience.
  • Active secret clearance with the ability to obtain and maintain a secret security clearance (U.S citizenship required)
  • Knowledge of Microsoft Office – Word / Excel / Access
  • Highly proficient in Tableau
  • Competence in Python or R
Preferred:
  • experience in supporting the United States Air Force
  • Experience wtih AWS
