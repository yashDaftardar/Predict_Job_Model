Senior Data Scientist - TS/SCI Required

Position Description
CGI Federal is seeking a Data Scientist for an exciting opportunity within our Intel Community.

Functional Role: Data Scientist - Senior
  • Duties: Works in cross-functional engineering and product development teams with data at all stages of the analysis lifecycle to derive actionable insight. Utilizes data to provide innovative technologies that enable the integration of FMI tools and data, improve data ingestion, increase data scaling, and enhance data exploitation to ensure more interoperable and secure data. Assists in identifying and developing new and innovative data models (conceptual, logical, physical) and data relationships; support development of automated data cleaning algorithms and automated rescaling algorithms. Has the ability to apply supervised and unsupervised machine learning algorithms for clustering, classification, and dimensionality reduction.
  • Degree: Bachelors
  • Experience: 5-8 years experience obtaining data from multiple, disparate data sources including structured, semi-structured and unstructured data.
  • Desired: Experience with using the Joint Enterprise Modeling and Analytics (JEMA) framework or Apache NiFi to develop data processing workflows. Knowledge of commercial Cloud services used by the IC.
