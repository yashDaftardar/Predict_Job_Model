Job Description Summary: PayPal’s Global Credit Risk, Seller Risk and Collection (GCSC) team is looking to fill a critical role on our Risk Intelligence team. This role will support and guide analytic efforts that help inform and drive strategic decisions as our suite of Consumer Credit and Merchant lending products expand globally. This highly visible and versatile role will regularly engage with Risk and Credit Leadership, Consumer and Merchant teams, Finance, Collections and Seller Risk Operations teams to provide solutions for challenging business problems through a combination of routine reporting, monitoring, ad-hoc analytics and data story telling. As an analytics professional, you will manage the delivery of high quality monitoring and reporting solutions by managing the efforts of multiple business intelligence development teams. These efforts range from small, single stream outputs to large-scale multi-stream efforts in a complex, fast-paced, dynamic environment. In this role, you will identify, translate, prioritize and drive the execution of high-quality data solutions delivering actionable insights for our different stakeholders across the GCSC team. Together with the Business Intelligence development team and our Global Data Service, you will partner to create simple trustworthy data pipelines and build automated reporting to surface key metrics. Our team leverages a combination of tools and technologies used across the enterprise (e.g., Qlik Sense, SQL, Tableau, etc.) to develop and maintain a suite of monitoring and reporting solutions to support our most senior risk executives. As a thought leader, you will recommend new performance monitoring metrics and/or changes to existing metric frameworks ensuring what we measure stays relevant for our evolving Credit business. You will work with leadership to maintain an active roadmap of analytic projects and help guide the evolution and maturation of our Risk Intelligence solutions framework. You will manage the business intelligence demand intake (request) and prioritization (governance) process for existing solutions across a variety of internal stakeholders overseeing the delivery efforts for active projects. Additionally, you will aid in the development and implementation of processes for building new solutions from discovery through delivery. While your primary role is to manage and lead the efforts of others, you must be comfortable with being both player (at times) and coach. When required, you must possess the ability and willingness to “roll up your sleeves” to help perform analytical deep dives to identify problems, opportunities and outline specific action plans. During active projects, you will guide our internal clients through the requirements gathering and solution planning phases to formulate project scope, schedule and resource requirements. You will help articulate both business and technical needs to our developer team and data partners to facilitate building the best solutions possible. You will provide day to day project management, coordination of activities and communicate key project updates.

Job Description:

Responsibilities:

  • Ability to lead multiple workstreams and teams using agile management practices to plan, prioritize and execute cross-functional work
  • Ability to organize, prioritize and meet deadlines independently
  • Ability to synchronize weekly development efforts and corporate monthly/quarterly/yearly priorities
  • Ability to lead daily/weekly scrum meetings and manage project boards to ensure delivery of high-quality products across a globally distributed team
  • Strong project management skills with high attention to detail and a strong focus on meeting deliverables with time limitations – ability to multitask and prioritize is a must
  • Self-starter: you drive projects with minimal guidance and focus on high impact work
  • Understanding of data management, reporting tools and applications, along with quantitative/analytics experience that includes methodology development, data visualization (Qlik Sense, Qlik View, Tableau, Java, etc.), statistical modeling and programming (SQL, SAS, R, MATLAB)
  • Ability to establish and maintain strong relationships with key business partners and stakeholders and facilitate communications between business users and developers
  • Conduct exploratory analysis on new or previously unused data sets to uncover new insights for the business
  • Work with non-technical business users to understand their analytical needs, document and prioritize requirements and help them effectively use the analytical tools our team develops
  • Provides consultation and technical advice on business analytics projects to ensure results meet defined client expectations and adhere to internal standards and strategies
  • Possesses effective facilitation skills to elicit requirements from a diverse range of stakeholders and groups from all levels of organizational leadership
  • Ability to conceptualize, design and monitor portfolio performance and other predictive analytics in support of underwriting strategy changes and monitoring of delinquency and losses to PayPal’s credit products
  • Ability to communicate concise and actionable business results and proposed strategies from vast amounts of data to senior management
  • Ability to facilitate prototyping sessions with a variety of end users and stakeholders
  • Possess excellent written and oral communication skills, and can interact with and influence decision-making by non-analytical business audiences
  • Be a creative problem solver, flexible and proactive problem solver in a fast paced, ever changing environment

Qualifications:

  • 3+ years of work experience in similar analytical roles, including experience with high data volume environments
  • Prior management consulting or BI/Analytics consulting experience is highly valued
  • Prior project and/or program management experience, specifically on analytics projects, is highly valued
  • Fluent in SQL and Excel; experience with a statistical programming language with predictive modeling (e.g. Python or R)
  • Experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau or Qlik View

Education:

  • Bachelor’s Degree required with emphasis on coursework in a quantitative discipline (e.g., Statistics, Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics, Data Sciences, Operations Research, Financial Engineering, System Analysis)
