Material Data Scientist - 211063

About Us: Gore is a materials science company focused on improving lives through discovery, product innovation and rewarding careers for our Associates.
About the Industry: Externally focused, disciplined innovation drives Gore to develop new technology and create new products. Combining our materials science expertise with market insights, we deliver solutions that improve lives — from medical devices that heal patients, to fabrics that protect first responders in the line of duty. Learn more at gore.com/products/industries
About the Role: Core Technology is looking for a Material Data Scientist to join our Materials Capability Team in Elkton, Maryland. This individual will work with other subject matter experts to drive insight generation that enables business leaders and key stakeholders to make well informed decisions regarding Gore’s material capabilities, and competitive technologies.
Responsibilities:
  • Utilizing current and future data science methods, uncovering new insights from Gore’s comprehensive materials database; this could include activities of enriching the database with external and competitive materials, application of artificial intelligence/ machine learning techniques for pattern discovery, to accelerate materials innovation in Core Technology
  • Collaborating with other Subject Mater Experts to investigate, plan, and build tools to utilize a broad diversity of datasets to improve our ability deliver insights on our materials, processes, and competitors
  • Collaborating with team members to convey meaning, value, and limitations of generated insights based on investigation problem statements and available data
  • Ensuring the protection of Intellectual Property through appropriate access and data security measures
  • Ensuring the Materials data and databases are of high quality and available to the appropriate associates to facilitate material selection and innovation in our Core Technology organization
  • Actively engaging in continuous improvement efforts based on user feedback and usage data from Gore’s materials database and other tools to be developed
  • Communicating with users and key stakeholders regarding system upgrades, new tools/ features available in our team’s toolbox

Required Qualifications:
  • Minimum BS Degree in Polymer Science, Material Science or Chemical Engineering with a minimum of 3 years of academic or industry experience in Data Science field
  • Experienced in creating data visualization tools to communicate complex multivariate numeric and non-numeric data, images etc. to key stakeholders in a simple manner
  • Experience creating practical applications, linking data science tools to definable business value
  • Experience practicing both Intellectual courage to challenge others and defend decisions based on facts, and Intellectual humility to change one’s own mind & course of action when others make a well-reasoned, thoughtful, and logical argument
  • Programing experience with multiple scripting/programing languages (e.g., Python, HTML, JMP scripting language, MATLAB, SAS, Wolfram-Mathematica)
  • Skills in data analytics with experience of reducing tools to practice on structured/unstructured datasets to gain critical insights (e.g., Machine Learning algorithms, cluster analysis, etc.)
  • Demonstrated strengths in verbal and written communication skills with experience tailoring the message content to communicate effectively across business functions and geographic regions
  • Ability to travel up to 5%
Desired Qualifications:
  • Advanced degree in Material Science and/or concentration in Material Data Science
  • Experience (academic/industrial) in Polymer Processing and Drawing of Polymers using melt spinning, melt blowing, spun-bonding, electrospinning, etc.
  • Experience in using and interpreting data from characterization tools such as DSC, TGA, DMA, etc .
