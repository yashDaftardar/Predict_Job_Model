About DrFirst:
DrFirst is an innovative Health IT company with a mission to create user-friendly solutions so that doctors can treat their patients quickly, efficiently, and safely.

Over 300,000 healthcare professionals, 120,000 prescribers, 67,000 pharmacies, and 260 of the top healthcare IT vendors in the market depend on us every day to manage medication safety and efficiency, streamline care team communication and collaboration, reduce medical errors, and improve patient outcomes.

You will be part of a team, building and operating highly scalable software and statistical models using cutting-edge technologies for a variety of devices. You will get to solve the most complex problems that arise in the areas of distributed computing and high-volume transaction processing. Your solutions and analyses will reach thousands of doctors and touch the lives of millions of patients across the country.

Our culture is entrepreneurial, team-oriented, collaborative, and fast-paced. We look for talented people who are passionate about building world-class technology and data solutions. If you want to build solutions that help save patients’ lives, we want to talk to you!
Position Overview:
The Statistical Analyst is a key role that will help drive data insights to inform key business decisions. You will own analyses for business insights, solve problems to inform business decisions and support our growth solutions with data, analysis, and reporting. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in statistical analysis and exposure to a diverse set of digital platforms, data analytics, and data tools with a good sense of how to connect these to business metrics. The candidate is expected to be curious and has a strong point of view. This position offers an exciting opportunity to advise and work with some of the top brands in medicine, by making brands matter.
Who will love this job
  • A Strategist who is astute in monitoring and adapting to changes in the marketplace, being ever vigilant to identify areas of a new opportunity or trends that drive product awareness, product efficacy, and product adoption
  • A Problem solver: passionate about harnessing the power of data to adopt new tactics that will drive business decision making
  • A Collaborator : who can navigate internal departments and teams, brings people together to deliver world-class products that accelerate DrFirst’s market share and profitability
  • A Creative thinker who is familiar with the latest data trends and sources, and has a proven ability to leverage them to solve problems
  • An Influencer who relies on their data analyses, good ideas, and collaborative style, to influence and direct internal and external teams to achieve desired outcomes
  • A Self-starter who exhibits a can-do, team-oriented demeanor. Someone who is well organized and able to work effectively on multiple projects and project types, promptly, with limited management oversight
What you will work on:
  • Measure causal inference of interventions on patient outcomes on observational real-world data in a reproducible framework
  • Design experimental studies for A/B testing, and evaluate the impact on medication adherence
  • Work with clients to deliver critical follow-up analyses with statistical rigor
  • Collaborate with product, business development, and client teams to communicate outcomes and influence the direction of rapidly developing a set of offerings
  • Get hands-on with large data sets by analyzing trends and developing strategic recommendations to improve campaign effectiveness
  • Monitor and maintain key data sets and the algorithms used to integrate them, including finding solutions for data gaps and anomalies within our data sets
  • Act as a trusted advisor to colleagues and provide sound strategic guidance to clients, at all levels, to help expand the role of data in our client relationships
Qualifications:
  • At least three (3) years of experience in statistical analytics, marketing analytics, or related roles in data science
  • A strong foundational understanding of statistical modeling, reproducible analysis, and causal inference
  • Master’s Degree is a must, Preferred in statistics, data analytics, business, economics, or mathematics
  • Demonstrated problem-solving, critical thinking, and analytical skills
  • Strong professional communication skills
  • Expertise in R or Python for segmentation, cohort analyses, multi-variant testing, and building regression models
  • Strong working knowledge of SQL
  • Experience in pharma, clinical, or Health IT is a huge plus
  • Ability to work without sponsorship
