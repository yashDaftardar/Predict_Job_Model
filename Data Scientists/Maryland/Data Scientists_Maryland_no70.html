Senior International Survey Sampling Statistician

Job Description:

ICF seeks an experienced sampling statistician to support international population-based survey and evaluation activities funded by USAID’s Bureau for Resilience and Food Security and Bureau for Humanitarian Assistance (BHA). The ICF’s portfolio of food security and resilience activities include population-based household surveys and evaluations in Global Food Security Strategy target countries, resilience focus countries and BHA Development Food Security Activity countries. The sampling statistician will also participate in capacity strengthening activities for national statistical organizations and other data collection partners through training and mentoring. This is a full-time position based in Rockville, Maryland.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Developing sample designs for household surveys and other types of research studies in developing countries
  • Designing sampling strategies for surveys and apply sampling methodologies to data analysis
  • Calculating weights for survey data and estimating sampling errors
  • Preparing descriptions of sampling procedures for project reports
  • Providing statistical advice to the staff
  • Providing technical assistance and training in sampling frame construction, sample design, sample size calculations, stratification, sample selection and weighting
  • Contributing to curriculum development and teaching for in-country capacity strengthening

Basic Qualifications:

  • PhD in statistics or related field in demography, epidemiology, or economics with an emphasis on statistics
  • At least 10 years’ experience designing samples for surveys in international settings, including designing samples for household and/or facility surveys, calculating weights for survey data, and calculating sampling errors
  • Familiarity with a wide range of advanced statistical techniques
  • Experience in developing sampling capacity of counterparts in host counties
  • English written and oral fluency
  • Ability to travel internationally up to 20%

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Fluency in French, Spanish or Portuguese
