JOB SUMMARY


Are you passionate about the power of big data to drive customer value? Do you enjoy maneuvering complex data sets to build elegant machine learning models? Do you go beyond building models to ensure they are actionable? Are you continuously learning about all the developments in the world of AI? Do you list Python or R as one of the languages you speak?!


Marriott, the world’s largest hotel company, is seeking someone like you to help enhance machine learning capabilities to drive customer engagement and value. This role entails outstanding business to technical translation, structured yet creative thinking, cross-functional collaboration, hands-on machine learning model development and operationalization. This role is part of a highly visible team that is tasked with growth acceleration and reinventing customer experience for Marriott


What you will do

  • Enhance capabilities needed to serve our global customers at scale in a data-driven, personal and relevant way
  • Understand the biggest areas of opportunity for the application of data science and machine learning at Marriott, developing services, positioning, and messaging in a clear, differentiated, and value-driven way across channels
  • Lead internal, external, and cross functional teams to develop new data-driven models and evolve existing models for customer intelligence, digital personalization, and other key areas of customer-focused growth.
  • Collaborate with IT and other data platform support teams to lead enterprise efforts to bring in new data science technologies, frameworks and methodologies and scale data science models through distributed computing and platform implementation
  • Evolve applications of advanced analytics, machine learning, tools, data sources, talent and partners by incessantly bringing external best practices and trends into an existing team of data-savvy analysts
  • Evangelize adoption of advanced analytics and pervasive data-driven thinking in Marriott culture
  • Work effectively with stakeholders to understand ambiguous business problems, assess data needs, develop/maintain best suited models and help/strategize deploying them in production

CANDIDATE PROFILE


Education and Experience


  • 4-year degree from an accredited university in Computer Science, Applied Mathematics, Statistics, Operations Research, Engineering or other quantitative discipline required
  • Master from an accredited university in Computer Science, Applied Mathematics, Statistics, Operations Research, Engineering or other quantitative discipline required AND
  • 7+ years’ hands-on experience in machine learning (supervised, unsupervised, and ensemble methods), natural language processing; computer vision, demonstrating progressive career growth and a pattern of exceptional performance required; deep learning experience is a plus
  • PhD from an accredited university in Computer Science, Applied Mathematics, Statistics, Operations Research, Engineering or other quantitative discipline preferred.
  • Proven track record of developing, scaling and implementing statistical predictive models in customer facing environments
  • Proven track record of managing predictive modeling/ML/AI projects
  • Experience with big data and cluster computing environments

  • Additional experience using the following tools is desired
