Job purpose:

As a Principal Statistician , you provide critical statistical expertise and technical support to the different vaccine studies. You are responsible for a whole study from design to execution for different study designs.

Your responsibilities :

In this role you :

  • Identify the statistical methodology to establish study strategy (e.g. power modelling of different scenarios, set up power monitoring, set up mitigation plan to minimize the risk of statistical failure)
  • Provide input for the protocol (design, objectives, endpoints, randomization strategy, sample size, stat analysis) and for the study set-up
  • Develop Statistical Analysis Plans (involving sensitivity analysis to support the robustness of results) and coordinate review
  • Review the TFL by coordinating the review with the Statistical Analyst and ultimately to approve the TFL
  • Complete the QC of the statistical analysis methodology
  • Validate study-specific dataset & analysis program specification
  • Propose the SAR conclusion/s
  • Prepare the answers for the study-specific statistical questions (e.g. inspections, questions from regulatory, etc.)
  • Provide statistical support for possible independent analysis center/s
  • Provide consultancy support/advice and trainings on statistical matters to other Statisticians

Why you?

Basic Qualifications :

We are looking for professionals with the following required skills to achieve our goals (must-have):

  • Being based in Belgium, or willing to relocate in Belgium
  • University degree: Master’s or PhD or a Diploma in Sciences, Statistics, Biostatistics, Mathematics, Epidemiology or equivalent
  • At least 5-year experience in the pharmaceutical industry
  • Has been previously responsible for studies with different designs (dose range, superiority/non-inferiority, interim analysis rules,..)
  • Experience in SAS software and sample size computation for standard designs including simulations
  • Ability to work in a multidisciplinary team (e.g. give guidance to non-stat, provide feedback, engage), some being remotely located
  • Be able to lead the development of new biometrics/epidemiological methods to gain efficiency and meet product development needs.
  • Solutions driven develop solutions for non-standardization situation (process and statistical methodologies)
  • Act at all times as the subject matter expert: provide statistical expertise in specific topics and training of peers.
  • Truly acts as a mentor. Takes personal responsibility for supporting and developing others in the team.
  • Demonstrates experience in leading working groups; motivates other team members by involving them in issues that affect them.
  • Builds an environment that supports co-operation and cohesiveness among the work team; makes statements regarding common interests.
  • Listens to team members; trusts them and responds to their requests for assistance.
  • Be capable to work in a complex matrix organization and be flexible to work with different cultures and employees at different levels. Interaction will be expected with all teams based in Belgium, Netherlands, Germany, Italy, the US, UK and India.
  • Good oral and written communication in English including the ability to explain difficult issues and situations clearly and ensures meaning is understood. Other languages a plus (French, Italian, etc.)
