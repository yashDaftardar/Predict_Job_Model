Full Job Description

Radian is using our data to disrupt the real estate and mortgage markets. We treat our team like a start-up, but with the backing of a well-capitalized, publicly traded company. In the last year, our Data and Analytics programs have been recognized as a Top 100 Tech in Real Estate and a Top 25 Fintech Innovator. Data is at the very center of what we do as it helps us shape our portfolio of analytical products. We’re looking for a VP of Data Science to help us expand our data-centric strategy and deliver products. This work will involve a combination of data engineering, data science, and machine learning expertise and as such you will help lead our teams that do such.

You will report to the SVP, Data and Analytics and manage our data and modeling teams. You will work with leaders in our product, program and engineering teams and you will own the data strategy to support data science and be responsible for analytics strategy and analytical capability to derive new insights and develop models across all our business areas. In addition, you will lead, manage, and further build out our current team of data engineers and data analysts, as well as our data scientists and ML engineers.

The VP, Data Science/advanced Analytics will lead the data science team, which includes data, model and analytics development. You will be responsible for all aspects of design, build and delivery of data and data science models and services. You will work with leaders in our product, program and engineering teams and you will own the data strategy to support data science and be responsible for analytics strategy and analytical capabilities. You will work across all of the Radian business areas. In addition, you will lead, manage, and further build out our current team of data engineers and data analysts, as well as our data scientists and ML engineers.
