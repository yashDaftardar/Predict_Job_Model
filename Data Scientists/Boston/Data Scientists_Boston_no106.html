Position Overview and Department Description:

Sanofi is looking for a Head of Process Operation and Modeling to support the Process Development and Engineering department being formed in the Cambridge area. The team is responsible for the development of the small molecule NCE portfolio and the process improvement of the legacy portfolio. Reporting to the Head of Process Development and Engineering, this role will be responsible for building and leading the team in the U.S. while collaborating with colleagues in France. We are looking for a highly motivated individual with a mindset to implement changes who must be a recognized technical expert in the field of data and modelling with expertise in application on pharmaceutical processes.

This is an exciting opportunity to demonstrate, in a cutting-edge scientific department, a new way of working where modeling and data science will support the next generation of Sanofi medicines improving the quality of life for patients across the world.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Lead the modelling and data science initiative in Synthetics and the implementation into the portfolio.

  • Foster innovation in the field of data and modelling to process development and will manage an internal and external scientific network.

  • Contribute, strategically and operationally to the development of NCEs by providing team support and data strategic inputs.

  • Participate in the innovation program and the deployment of continuous processing and biocatalysis into the portfolio.
