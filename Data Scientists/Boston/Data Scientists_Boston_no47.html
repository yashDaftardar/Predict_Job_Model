Job Title: Data Scientist/Engineer

Job Location: EMD Serono, Inc., 45A Middlesex Turnpike, Billerica, MA 01821

Job Responsibilities:


  • Design, and develop state-of-the-art, scalable data engineering pipelines on AWS for biopharma and healthcare applications;
  • Engineer features or predictors for data science applications across biopharma and healthcare;
  • In collaboration with data scientists and data analysts, develop and implement scalable machine learning and artificial engineering applications;
  • Perform statistical analysis to uncover actionable signals hidden within large datasets;
  • Develop and implement robust testing framework for data pipeline and data science applications;
  • Communicate data driven insights and recommendations to a broad range of business partners;
  • Train data analysts and data scientists on newly developed business applications on AWS;
  • Develop a "lean and mean" data architecture for data science applications;
  • Drive data driven transformation of biopharma and healthcare businesses by developing novel business applications aimed at helping patients; and
  • Develop and sharpen healthcare business acumen in order to act as a thought leader in the area of data science and engineering for a wide variety of healthcare business applications.


Job Requirements:

Employer requires Master's degree in computer science or a closely related field and two (2) years of work experience working in a dynamic, highly quantitative and cross-functional environment focused on delivering sustainable business value by transforming data into actionable information.

In addition, the successful candidate must have:

1. Demonstrated ability with analysis of algorithms, cloud engineering, cloud platform administration & monitoring, continuous automated deployment of sophisticated data application on the cloud, developing, maintaining and deploying infrastructure as code templates, and developing and building applications using containers such as docker and data analysis gained through at least two (2) years of work experience.

2. Demonstrated ability in manipulating, transforming and cleaning data using AWS Athena, AWS S3, AWS Redshift, AWS Lambda, Python, Pandas, Scikit-learn, SQL and no SQL databases, and Tableau gained through at least two (2) years of work experience

3. Demonstrated ability in productionalizing/automating data science, machine learning and artificial intelligence applications using sound data and cloud engineering principles gained through at least two (2) years of work experience.

4. Demonstrated knowledge of healthcare business areas such as research & development, sales & marketing and patient support programs gained through at least two (2) years of work experience.

5. Demonstrated ability in visualizing, validating and testing the output of high-dimensional data science models such as machine learning and AI gained through at least two (2) years of work experience.

6. Demonstrated ability closely working with top data scientists in developing valuable predictors/features for a machine learning model gained through at least two (2) years of work experience.

7. Demonstrated ability to provide thought leadership on building data lakes, developing data driven applications and data pipelines on the Cloud with best practices and sound engineering principles gained through at least two (2) years of work experience.

8. Demonstrated ability onboarding and supervising external IT application developers gained through at least two (2) years of work experience.

All years of experience may be gained concurrently.
