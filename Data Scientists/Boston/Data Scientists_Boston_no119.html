In this role, you have the opportunity to

As our Global EMaaS (Enterprise Monitoring as a Service) Data Science & Analytics Manager to work as a specialist, in a new business venture for enterprise monitoring as a service. A key part of the value generated to hospitals in this model is our performance customer dashboard. You will collaborate closely with clinical and technical experts to develop advanced reports on patient monitoring system usage, performance and clinical impact. These reports will contribute to the performance dashboard helping customers and Philips understand usage, adoption and achievement of desired outcomes in areas never measured previously.

You are responsible for (but not limited to)

  • Measuring how customers are using Philips technology through advanced data analytics on usage, adoption of workflows and achievement of outcomes
  • Creating a performance dashboard on usage, adoption and outcomes
  • Reporting on internal performance measures, including asset utilization, service and equipment costs, as well as volume and revenue to plan that are critical to profitability
  • Developing new concepts, building proof-of-concept demos, validating results with customers and field teams
  • Interacting with business partners and customers to understand their problems, share insights and validated practices
  • Identifying gaps and data needs that limit successful measurements of the designed experience
  • Collaborating with other solution and functional teams (e.g., commercial operations, professional services, clinical education, financial administration) to find practical and ambitious solutions to these gaps and aspirations
  • Identifying critical success metrics with which to gauge the relative performance and progress of our managed service customers over time

You are part of

Philips Hospital Patient Monitoring (HPM), Strategy & New Business Venture, Enterprise Monitoring as a Service (EMaaS). This as a service concept shifts the way customers consume monitoring systems across their hospital enterprises. You will participate in various activities around clinical decision support, real-time diagnostic, clinical decision support, operational workflow improvement, and other healthcare-related applications, focusing on research, development, and evaluation of first-of-kind and prototypes for clinicians and other relevant partners.

To succeed in this role, you should have the following skills and experience

  • Masters in Computer Sciences, Biomedical Engineering, Bioinformatics, or a related field preferred
  • 10 + years related of work experience desired
  • Experience with programming, data visualization, and healthcare informatics as well as knowledge of physiologic monitoring systems
  • Strong background into database design, modeling and dynamic visualization
  • Proficiency with R and/or Python libraries commonly used in data science and SQL query experience as well as Power VI
  • Solid knowledge and understanding of hospital data flows (e.g., CPOE, EMR, RIS, LIS and PACS) and related data format standards (e.g., HL7, DICOM, FHIR and IHE), and healthcare terms and classifications (SNOMED CT, ICD10)
  • High affinity with applying new IT platforms and dash boarding software tools for reporting and experience
  • Python programming experience preferred
  • Ability to work with cross-functional teams expected
  • Self-motivated, committing to results, and team-player expected
  • Flexible and quick-learner expected
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills expected
  • Ability to manage complex projects
  • Demonstrated operational analytics and financial analysis capabilities

Your team

Enterprise Monitoring as a Service is shifting how we think and operate this business end to end to support a capex type of business and shift our customers to consumption. You will be helping HCPs make better and informed decisions, improving patient care, optimizing clinical and operational outcomes in clinical workflows.

In return, we offer you

The ability to collaborate with, learn and grow from colleagues in a highly complex, global organization where you can use your strengths to help drive strategic business initiatives for Philips. Additionally, we provide you a dynamic working environment in an innovative business, paired with a competitive salary, excellent benefits, and a supportive atmosphere where you can sharpen your talents with new challenges and career opportunities.
