Full Job Description

The Liberty Mutual, Global Risk Solutions, Advanced Analytics Hub is seeking an Analyst II/Assistant Director of Data Science within the Analytics Transformation team. The Analytics Transformation team is responsible for championing the future of Data Science, helping to incubate new ideas and encourage innovation with purpose across Global Risk Solutions, with a strong focus on leveraging Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) to drive business value. The team is looking for a passionate individual who is full of curiosity, open to new ideas, and willing to fail fast and embrace our culture of experimentation. This individual will join our Agile team and play on an essential role in building out in-house AI/ML capabilities and involve in the end-to-end product cycle, from ideation to model development/deployment to field implementation.

Responsibilities:

  • Buildout in-house AI/ML capabilities with other team members, including data scientists, IT developers, and business stakeholders
  • Research and develop computer vision, NLP, and other AI/ML models to improve operational efficiency, risk selection, claims response and pricing
  • Collaborates with business partners to develop data science solutions that enable data-driven strategic decision-making
  • Utilizes data science techniques to manipulate structured and unstructured data sets, identify patterns in raw data, and develop models to optimize business solutions.
  • Collaborate with other data science teams across the company to promote best practices and reproducible work
  • Act as a mentor and help develop less experienced analysts or interns on the team
  • Bachelors w/ 5+ years, Masters w/ 4+ years, or Ph. D with 2+ years of relevant experience.
  • Advanced degree in quantitative field or actuarial credentials preferred.
  • Experience with predictive modeling and statistical software packages (e.g., Python/R) highly desired.
  • Proven ability to quickly grasp new concepts and technologies and adapt to changes and demands.
  • Expert skills in Excel, PowerPoint highly desired.
  • Ability to exchange ideas and convey complex information clearly and concisely, both verbally and in writing.

Please note: This is a range posting for multiple grade levels.
