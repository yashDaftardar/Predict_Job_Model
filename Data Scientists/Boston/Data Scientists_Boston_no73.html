Aetna Resources, LLC, a CVS Health company, is hiring for the following role in Wellesley, MA: Sr. Data Scientist to work with Aetnas cross-functional team, delivering complex analytical projects, partnering with internal and external clients to improve engagement and impact of clinical programs. Extract and manipulate structured and unstructured data from multiple, large data sources, using advanced statistical techniques and advanced software to develop predictive models and actionable analytic insights that will be used to inform business decisions. Apply analytical rigor and statistical methods to analyze large amounts of data using advanced statistical techniques and big data technologies including Hadoop and Spark. Work on complex analytical projects including data exploration, model building, performance evaluation and testing. Collaborate with business partners to understand their business problems/goals and develop analytic solutions. Develop, validate, and execute algorithms and machine learning techniques to investigate problems, detect patterns and recommend solutions. This role will also involve in designing experiments and evaluating the performance of various outreach programs. Develop and participate in presentations and consultations with existing and prospective constituents on analytics results and solution.

Required Qualifications
TWO YEARS AS DATA SCIENTIST OR A RELATED OCCUPATION. MINIMUM 2 YEARS OF EXPERIENCE BUILDING STATISTICAL MODELS AND MACHINE LEARNING METHODOLOGIES. MUST HAVE EXPERIENCE WORKING WITH SQL AND BIG DATA STACK (HIVE/PIG/SPARK); DEVELOPING DATA PIPELINES AND PREDICTIVE MODELS IN HADOOP AND SPARK ENVIRONMENT; AND DETERMINING ANALYTICAL APPROACHES USING SUPERVISED, SEMI-SUPERVISED AND UNSUPERVISED TECHNIQUES. MUST ALSO HAVE EXPERIENCE DESIGNING AND EVALUATING EXPERIMENTS/STUDIES USING RANDOMIZATION, STRATIFICATION AND CAUSAL INFERENCE METHODS; DEVELOPING INTERNAL ANALYTICAL PACKAGES IN R, PYTHON; AND IMPLEMENTING STATISTICAL METHODS INCLUDING GLM, MIXED-EFFECT MODELS, REGULARIZATION, DECISION TREES, RANDOM FORESTS, BOOSTING, NEAREST NEIGHBORS, NEURAL NETWORKS, K-MEANS, HIERARCHICAL CLUSTERING, TIME SERIES ANALYSIS, POWER ANALYSIS, SAMPLING TECHNIQUES AND OPTIMIZATION ALGORITHMS.
