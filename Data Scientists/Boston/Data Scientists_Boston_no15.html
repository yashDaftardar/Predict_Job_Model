Description

Staples US Retail Pricing team is looking for an enthusiastic intern to join our team and help activate our Brand strategy. Staples US Retail is on a journey to transform to be the leader in working and learning, and this role is part of the team to bring it to life for our customers. You’ll be a part of one of the most exciting seasons of the year at Staples US Retail – Back to School. And will have the opportunity to roll your sleeves up in a fast-paced, dynamic environment with a fantastic group of team- and results-oriented professionals.


The DS Pricing intern opportunity is part of the US Retail Stores Data Science Team and is responsible for building and developing analytics to drive innovation within the pricing and promotions space at Staples. This successful candidate will assist in building capabilities for analyzing product/customer behavior and standardize the Pricing team’s reporting/dashboarding. The DS Pricing intern will also work on ad-hoc projects such as web scraping, and modeling elasticities to support business decisions. The person will need to effectively blend data wrangling capabilities with business/process knowledge as well as communicate insights/conclusions to the team and leadership.


The DS Pricing intern will interact with merchants and other pricing team members on a regular basis. The DS Pricing intern will work very closely with experienced Data Scientists, and Statistician within the team as well as spending time with the Sr. Director of Pricing to gain additional perspective on the role of Pricing within the organization.

Primary Responsibilities

  • Work with a wide array of high dimensional structured and unstructured data sources to build adaptive learning systems, data science products
  • Assist in the process of extracting, cleaning, aggregating, and maintaining data for model building and predicting customer behavior
  • Enhance web scraping capabilities, gathering data from internal and external sources.
  • Research new ways and develop analytical insights to drive more traffic
  • Develop price elasticity models to estimate the optimal price point for maximum demand
  • Perform A/B tests and validate data flows and prepare all ETL processes according to business requirements and incorporate business requirements into design specifications
  • Reliably produce accurate, high quality reports, building dashboards using Tableau/Power BI

Qualifications

Basic Skills

  • Bachelor candidates in Computer Science, Engineering, Operations Research, Statistics, Physics, or a related technical discipline
  • Proficiency in a mainstream programming language, such as Python, R, other languages
  • Experience working with databases, preferably with knowledge of SQL
  • Demonstrated strong proficiency at an advanced level in Microsoft Excel
  • Experience with data analysis and machine learning libraries/frameworks and methods.
  • Keen attention to detail, and ability to dive deep. The ability to think big, manage ambiguity to move quickly and deliver results in an entrepreneurial environment
