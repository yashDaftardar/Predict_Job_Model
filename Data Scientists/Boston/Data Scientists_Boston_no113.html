Job Description

Are you looking for a patient-focused, innovation-driven company that will inspire you and empower you to shine? Join us as a Director, Data Science Services in our Cambridge, MA office.

At Takeda, we are transforming the pharmaceutical industry through our R&D-driven market leadership and being a values-led company. To do this, we empower our people to realize their potential through life-changing work. Certified as a Global Top Employer, we offer stimulating careers, encourage innovation, and strive for excellence in everything we do. We foster an inclusive, collaborative workplace, in which our global teams are united by an unwavering commitment to deliver Better Health and a Brighter Future to people around the world.

Here, you will be a vital contributor to our inspiring, bold mission.

POSITION OBJECTIVES:

The Director of Data Science Services will be bringing high performance architectures, fine grained security implementations, and data structures that adapt to high dimensional, diverse data to R&D. This role will ensure that data science best practices are scaled for re-use, reproducibility, measures, and security. He/She will be:

  • Shape, apply, and assess data architectures and services through data analytics for multiple therapeutic areas across Takeda R&D and across pre-clinical and discovery data, operational and clinical trial data, clinical production data, real world data, scientific data, and related
  • Be a leader in the implementation of advanced architectures that adapt to data, reflect the rules of use in clear and consistent ways, and ensure that broad classes of data science is interactive and re-usable in ways that reveal opportunities, insights, and approaches for data usage
  • Drive the definition, design, implementation and validation of cutting-edge data structures that enable algorithms to analyze diverse sources of data to achieve actionable design, planning, and improvement of data services, machine-learning, visualizations, and decision making.
  • Measure, monitor, and collaborate with users, partners, and stakeholders on the use and socialization of techniques and new technologies throughout organization.

POSITION ACCOUNTABILITIES:

  • Lead the technical design and delivery of multiple data services that address unmet needs and gaps in the scientific exploration and statistical analysis of life sciences, real world, clinical, and operational data
  • Seamlessly integrate diverse data sources, such as performance data, usage, adoption, health and status, and scientific data more broadly for multiple therapeutic areas, business processes, and operational efficiencies
  • Deliver on data-driven projects through cross-organization partnerships, hands-on development, and close collaboration with end users
  • Actively listen, integrate input from diverse sources, and collaboratively translate engineering, scientific, or business questions into data and analysis requirements
  • Partner with experts to verify and drive data services that advance data science
  • Partner with IT to enable and measure data flow, performance, reliability, development using appropriate tools/technology and rapid prototyping that informs the design of analytical products
  • Coach, develop and mentor others on data science architectures, machine learning pipelines, and data governance models
  • Build external alliances to gain and share new insights from architecture analysis, technical information, and trends both internal and external.

EDUCATION, BEHAVIORAL COMPETENCIES AND SKILLS:

  • Advanced degree or equivalent experience in computer science or a data-related field with a clear evidence of implementation, testing, and tuning of very large-scale systems and services for secure, interactive access to complex data
  • Experience working in industrial-scale projects with experience in complex datasets
  • Demonstrated experience in exploring alternate approaches and novel solutions
  • Strong management and organizational skills.
  • Ability to effectively interact with and influence all levels of management.
  • Expert computer scientist or data scientist with experience in large-scale data analysis and industry models/standards
  • Expertise in advanced data architectures and deep knowledge of a wide range of analytic approaches, computing models, and data structures
  • Advanced hands-on knowledge of advanced database technologies, graph structures, modern software languages, precision security models, full stack development, analytic environments, and devops tools
  • Experience analyzing data from traditional healthcare databases or similar structured and unstructured datasets
  • Experience with big data methodologies, nosql, and cloud environments

TRAVEL REQUIREMENTS:

  • Willingness to travel to various meetings, corporate partners, additional Takeda sites.
  • Requires approximately 10% travel.
