Full Job Description

As a global leader in mass spectrometry, SCIEX delivers solutions for precision detection and quantification of molecules to help protect and advance the wellness and safety of all. Because of our 50 years of groundbreaking innovation, our customers can quickly respond to environmental hazards, better understand biomarkers relevant to disease, improve patient care in the clinic, bring relevant drugs to market faster, and keep food healthier and safer. At SCIEX, you’ll find a rewarding role that amplifies your impact on the world and helps you realize life’s potential.

SCIEX is proud to work alongside a community of nine fellow Danaher Life Sciences companies. Together, we’re pioneering the future of science and medicine, developing products that enable researchers in the fight to save lives.

Support the Global Customer Experience team in data driven decision making through the utilization of many diverse data sources. Use in-depth understanding of relational and non-relational database models as well as implementations of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to enable SCIEX’s customer experience initiatives. Creating one off datasets or analysis to answer specific questions as well as deployed solutions to gain real time insights, from internal sources and from customers’ feedback (solicited and unsolicited).
Develops or modifies program logic for new applications or software which may include coding, testing, debugging, documenting, implementing and maintaining software applications. Analyzes requirements, and maintains, tests and integrates application components.
Develops and maintains an ELT data pipeline from/to full spectrum of SCIEX’s enterprise platforms. Including ERP, CRM, IoT, Survey Platforms, Business Applications etc
Gather business and technical requirements to deliver analytics solutions to enable SCIEX’s customer experience program and strategy.
Development and enhancement of digital solutions to include visualization and advanced AI/ML implementations.
Delivery of BI/Analytics solutions with interactive reports, dashboards and visualizations according to specifications delivered by functional contacts.
Enablement of data driven decision making on an ad-hoc and constant ongoing basis.
Deep understanding of common database architecture to ensure data accuracy, quality and integrity
Developing Key Performance indicators for the function and developing automated mechanisms for attaining and reporting of these KPI’s
Documenting and creating unit tests according to the requirements of the technology being used
Responsible for solutions throughout lifecycle including requirements, PoC, Development, QA, Roll Out and Maintenance.
Experience with cloud-based data architecture and tool stacks
Gathers improvement opportunities for process automation and enhancements.

Critical Competencies / Leadership Anchors:
Customer Focus: listens and responds to customer needs
Builds People, Teams & Organizations: builds and maintains good working relationships with peers and other functions; works collaboratively; maintains composure under pressure; treats others with respect and consideration;
Leads through DBS: champions continuous improvement, has a problem-solving mentality and drives for results
Charts the Course: supports vision and purpose for SCIEX and the Customer Experience team; positively copes with change, prioritizes effectively, acts with speed and agility when new information emerges

Experience with ELT and building Data Pipelines.
