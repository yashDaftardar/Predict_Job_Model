Data Scientist Proteomics


Location

Boston, MA or Basel, Ch

Monte Rosa Therapeutics is seeking a talented and highly motivated Data Scientist / Bioinformatician to join the Data science/Proteomics groups. This is an exciting opportunity to join a team responsible for the development of state-of-the-art bioinformatics and biostatistics analysis to support proteomics profiling as well as the development, maintenance and optimization of analysis pipelines and procedures. The successful candidate will work in a highly dynamic and collaborative environment within our multi-disciplinary teams of scientist and will be exposed to, and contribute to, all aspects of drug discovery.

Principal Duties and Responsibilities

  • Responsible for the development of bioinformatics and biostatistical analysis, performing scientific data visualization to support the proteomics research group

  • Maintain, optimized, and develop the analysis pipelines and procedures for the proteomics data generated by the different workflows (TMT quantitative proteomics and targeted proteomics)

  • Interrogation of large proteomics profiling datasets to enable identification of new target

  • Integrated analysis of transcriptomics and proteomics data.

  • Interact with multi-disciplinary teams involving scientists, data scientists and software engineers

  • Experience with Git/BitBucket, Docker, AWS and Spotfire software

  • Excellent communication, data presentation and visualization skills both verbally and in writing, to bioinformatics audiences and non-bioinformatics audiences.
