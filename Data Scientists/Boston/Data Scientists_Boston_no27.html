Who we are:

Solaria Labs is a global team that explores how trends will shape the future and develops new products to protect our customers in a rapidly changing world. We combine the mindset of a startup with Liberty Mutual’s core capabilities and expertise. In partnership with teams across the company, we develop new products that solve unmet customer needs and expand the company’s product offerings. We’ve already launched a few products and one company since we started — see https://www.dwellbeingapp.com/ and https://www.getcertainly.com/. Please check out https://www.solarialabs.com/ for more details!

Why you may enjoy working at Solaria:

You will join the leadership team of a collaborative innovation lab that is helping to shape the future of a Fortune 100 company. You will lead research and development efforts for a diverse set of challenges, enabling us to build disruptive new products for the company. You will interact with product and engineering teams to implement your solutions within internal and customer facing software products. Responsibilities include:

  • Manage and mentor a multi-disciplinary team of 4 data scientists, along with interns, co-ops, and other staff on temporary assignments
  • Translate product ideas into well-posed data science challenges, and solve them
  • Lead the development of computer vision and natural language processing models, and deploy them into our products in collaboration with engineering and design teams
  • Translate and champion ML capabilities for non-technical audiences
  • Ensure the highest standard of technical excellence for all data science efforts in the lab, adopting best practices from other organizations and vetting new technologies or frameworks as appropriate
  • Maintain the data science infrastructure of Solaria Labs, including open source frameworks, to maximize reuse and sharing across projects

You’re good at and offer:

  • Bachelor's degree in engineering, computer science, mathematics, or related field plus a minimum of 10 years of proven progressive experience. Masters or Ph.D. level degree is preferred
  • Leadership experience within a corporate, academic, or startup innovation environment is beneficial
  • Extensive experience analyzing data and a broad understanding of core statistical and ML techniques
  • Expertise with Python and a proven track record of rapid prototyping using open source frameworks
  • Computer science background, comfortable working with both technical and non-technical staff to translate concepts and algorithms into working prototypes
  • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment with significant uncertainty
  • Possess strong analytical, strategic, project management, decision-making and problem-solving skills.
