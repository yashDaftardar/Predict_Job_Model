Job Summary

As a member of the Data & Analytics Team you will work with a team of dedicated data specialists to help make data driven decisions across business lines in the Federal Reserve Bank of Boston. The role will involve working with stakeholders to collect business requirements and understanding data needs, developing data pipelines to automate flow of information, building statistical models to perform descriptive, diagnostic, and predictive analyses, and creating visualizations. An important responsibility in this role will be to effectively communicate compelling stories to help business lines better understand their data and enable data driven decisions. This role will work cross-functionally and requires the ability to operate with substantial latitude for action and will be a good fit for someone who is a self-motivated team player.

Principal Accountabilities

  • Collect and analyze stakeholder business requirements and work with appropriate stakeholders to ensure quality and timely delivery.
  • Gather data and translate business requirements to provide appropriate analytical solution to solve business problems, ranging from basic reporting to predictive analytics.
  • Serve as a point of contact with business and technology teams to deploy best practices to extract, transform, and load data from a variety of data sources.
  • Deliver dashboards, graphs, visualizations, and supporting reports that enable teams to measure success and guide decision-making.
  • Present analyses to groups with varying degree of technical knowledge and subject matter expertise to help explain what the data is showing.
  • Analyze and document new and existing processes related to data management and recommend actions.
  • Work closely with other members of the team to collaborate on issues and address data quality improvement opportunities.
  • Serve as function representative on working groups for data science and analytics, reporting back to team on new tools, technology, or methods.

Other Accountabilities

Perform other duties as assigned.

Knowledge and Experience

Requires 2+ years experience plus Bachelor’s degrees in any of these fields: mathematics, economics, statistics, data science/analytics or similar quantitative field (master’s degree preferred)

Excellent problem-solving skills and experience working independently with a consultive mindset

Passionate about the telling data stories through visualization and dashboards. Committed to making analysis concise and digestible

Experience with gathering requirements from various stakeholders and presenting the results to technical and non-technical audiences

Applied experience with predictive analytics and other statistical methods to answer business questions preferred
