Central to the Marketplace team’s vision is the development of promotion tools for artists and labels powered by Spotify’s deep knowledge of listener preferences and behaviors. We’re looking for a Senior Data Scientist to join the Creator Promotion team dedicated to growing our products that help artists get their music into the ears of existing and potential fans.

In this role, you will partner with product teams to build the technology that makes this a reality by accounting for a complex set of artist needs, preferences, and goals. You will tackle complex problems to develop a behavioral understanding of customer needs, as well as to forecast and predict the value that our tools deliver to them & Spotify. In doing so, you will have a proven impact on bringing Spotify’s two-sided marketplace vision to life.

As part of the Product Insights team, you will partner with data engineers, product teams, and designers to help us define the next generation of promotion products for the music industry. You’ll also be connected to the broader Spotify insights community to expand the influence of your team's work.

What you'll do

  • Gain a deep understanding of how products and platforms influence user behavior by extracting insights from rich datasets to drive the Creator Studio roadmap.
  • Apply understanding of advanced machine learning practices to enhance product performance.
  • Identify the most impactful opportunities for product development based on user needs, and execute on them diligently and resourcefully.
  • Partner closely with other data scientists and data engineers to help inform, build and test hypotheses delivering actionable insights to fuel our products.
  • Define the metrics we use to measure the health of initiatives and track them through dashboards.
  • Work closely with cross-functional teams of data scientists, researchers, product owners, engineers, designers, marketers, and others across the company who are passionate about our marketplace.
  • Mentor and coach data scientists and tackle leadership opportunities within the larger data science community.
  • Be an advocate for data-informed decision-making, making recommendations about the product performance and product experience available to the Marketplace organization.

Who you are

  • 4+ years of demonstrated ability in a similar data scientist role.
  • Degree in data science, computer science, statistics, economics, mathematics, or a similar quantitative field.
  • Significant experience with advanced statistical methods and machine learning.
  • Significant experience with product analytics, including crafting success metrics, running power analyses, determining statistical significance, and presenting findings with clear product recommendations or implications.
  • Previous experience working with a team of data scientists and data engineers building machine learning tools in production and at scale.
  • You have hands-on experience synthesizing insights from data using tools such as Python, R, BigQuery, SQL, Tableau.
  • Strong communication skills and value building strong relationships with colleagues and partners as well as the ability to explain sophisticated topics in straightforward terms.
  • You’re a compelling storyteller who can communicate in succinct and inspiring ways to audiences with varied data science experience to influence real world product or feature decisions.
  • Comfortable working with distributed teams across multiple offices and timezones.
