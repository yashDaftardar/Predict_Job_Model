Full Job Description

Who we are looking for

The State Street Data Transport team supports multiple file transfer products across the business in conjunction with the daily operations team. The successful candidate will provide L3 engineering technical expertise to the State Street service delivery team and operations teams that support these products. The position requires effective communication and technical skills with a willingness to learn home grown file transfer technologies. The candidate must prioritize key initiatives and coordinate with the Service Delivery and daily operation teams. The candidate must be willing to engineer solutions, work on new technology projects and improve the current file transfer stack.

What you will be responsible for

As an L3 engineer on the Data Transport team you will;

    • Provide Level 3 engineering and technical support for Data Transport Infrastructure on various file transfer platforms with a focus on operations first.
    • Work with peers to design, development, implementation and documentation of automation based solutions for the Data Transport Infrastructure.
    • Engineer solutions to migrate to Best of Breed tools/solutions in the Data Transport space.
    • Work with peers under lead engineering team lead on compliance efforts and strengthen controls on Data Transport products.
    • Promote compliance with CIS controls and implement security awareness activities to reinforce controls compliance and educate employees on new and modified security controls for Data Transport.

What we value

Service Delivery’s Data Transport team supports a multitude of file transfer products across the business units with the service operations team. The candidate will provide technical engineering support to the State Street Service Delivery team and the service operations team that support these products.

  • Effective communication and technical skills – they are a key requirement of the position.
  • Willingness to learn home grown file transfer technologies.
  • Ability to manage high priority initiatives and coordinate the activities with SSC Service Delivery and service operations team to completion.
  • Initiate and manage vulnerability remediation, patching, upgrades and hardware life cycle to ensure we remain current with technology solutions and meet client needs.
  • Ability to work in a team setting with a diverse group: technical support resources, SSC client services representatives, application and service delivery teams and clients.

Education & Preferred Qualifications

  • 3+ years experience in file transfer server technologies (Axway Secure Transport, Primeur Spazio/Data One Next (a plus), IBM Sterling Connect Direct (NDM), Broadcom PGP, and all file transfer protocols (FTP, FTPS, SFTP, HTTPS).

  • 3+ years experience with file sharing including Samba.
  • 3+ years of experience with SSL and SSH security, protocols and key management.
  • Proficient knowledge and hands on experience with Shell Scripting, Python Programming, Ansible Core/Tower and Terraform, Cloud (AWS/Azure/GCP).

Additional requirements

The successful candidate will provide technical support of core data transport products including software life cycles, audit compliance/remediation efforts, vulnerability control, patch management. Must participate in service improvement/resiliency initiatives and key projects.

    • Ensure the Service Delivery Data Transport support teams satisfy applicable legal, regulatory and internal requirements surrounding Risk and Compliance.
    • Provide Information Technology risk management and compliance support to ensure effective identification, measurement, control and management of the relevant risks in the data transport areas.
    • Monitor applicable regulatory changes and provide effective change management to address such changes and keep abreast of new products, technologies and applications as well as their implications for assigned area.
    • Support a collaborative cross-business environment that fosters change utilizing the Change Management Framework.
    • Identify and manage IT risk by maintaining effective internal controls and escalating as appropriate any deficiencies to management and/or applicable technology governance boards.
