Senior Analyst (Statistician)

eMAX Health
Delray Beach, FL
Job Description
eMAX Health, located in White Plains, New York, a fast-paced, rapidly growing healthcare
research consultancy is seeking a Senior Analyst to work across its Outcomes Research and
Market Access business lines for its major pharmaceutical clients.
About eMAX
Founded in 2004, eMAX Health has a record of delivering high-quality independent research in
support of pharmaceutical product commercialization objectives. eMAX Healthcare Research
and Analytics™ focuses on global health outcomes, health information technology, pricing and
market access, marketing research & business development support. eMAX Health Network™
provides specialty pharmaceutical access programs such as copayment assistance coupons,
physician-office debit cards as well as other non-personal promotional products and services.
Responsibilities
Implementation and management of various aspects of research projects including:
  • development of analysis plans and study design,
  • extensive draft, review and reporting on various research assignments for the
pharmaceutical industry
  • extensive programming in statistical packages, specifically with software used for discrete
event simulation, modeling software and data analysis software
  • oversight and management of outcomes research using administrative claims, including
management of SAS programmers, collaboration with biostatisticians and study advisors
  • development of economic models including simulation models, decision trees, costeffectiveness analyses
  • preparation of narrative study reports, manuscripts as well as summary presentations
  • participation in client study meetings and presentations
  • participation in business development activities including writing proposals
  • participation in client discussions and meetings
Education
Masters of Science in Pharmacy Administration or related field required.
