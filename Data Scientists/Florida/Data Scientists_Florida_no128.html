Full Job Description

Overview:

The Sr. Data Analyst will support sales, vendor management, marketing, business stakeholders and COE colleagues. The focus of this role includes developing and delivering the information necessary for making business decisions and providing actionable intelligence that allows senior management to better manage the business. Additional focus for this role may include interpreting data, analyzing results using various techniques and extracting information which can offer ways to improve products and business approaches thus influencing business decisions. This includes understanding and reviewing current processes and coordinating and collaborating across business and technology functions to ensure comprehensive solutions are delivered. The Sr. Data Analyst acts in a lead role on projects, including communications with IT as well as key business stakeholders.

Essential Duties & Responsibilities Required:

  • Develop and lead presentations and training for Business and Analytics COE colleagues (15%)

  • Coordinate with Analytics COE and business colleagues to develop and create a meaningful reporting and analytics framework to identify business trends, risks, and opportunities. (15%)

  • Act as project lead for data and analytics projects (15%)

  • Work with the Business Intelligence and Analytics team within IT as the primary business partner to deliver solutions in support of business needs. (20%)

  • Work with business users to develop queries, portal views or complex analytical models to provide the Sr Leaders with the information and proactive analysis they need to make informed business decisions. (20%)

  • Evangelize data knowledge and insights: manage communications with stakeholders and other teams, collaborate with both technical and non-technical colleagues to complete data and analytics projects and ensure all parties can use data and analysis to further improve the business (15%)

Other Duties & Responsibilities:

  • Additional duties as assigned

  • Meets attendance and punctuality standards
