Head of Instruction - Data Analytics & Science

Are you passionate about data and the methods of recording, storing and analyzing data to effectively extract useful information? Beyond that, are you an avid technology supporter and driven to not only learn but teach about your skills and knowledge, as well as lead and support a network of instructors by equipping them with a robust curriculum to teach them about your passion? If you are looking for a way to utilize your expertise in data analytics and science and empower the lives of individuals seeking to break into the data science world, support and coach a growing network of instructors, as well as interact with nationally recognized Universities, then let us introduce you to HackerU!

We are looking for Head of Instruction - Data Analytics and Science to join us at our ever growing company.

This role is responsible for the development, execution, and maintenance of curated data analytics & science curricula, across all curated content mediums taught by HackerUSA instructors at nationally renowned universities. The Head of Instruction will have an opportunity to curate their respective team of curriculum engineers who are as passionate about data science as they are. This role reports into the CEO and will collaborate with counterparts across the organization to facilitate related operational activities for all stages of the content pipeline, from expert recruitment to content publication and life cycle management.

Who You Are

Our ideal candidate must be a combination of leader, trainer, education professional and curriculum developer, who is highly experienced in Data Analytics and Science theory, as well as collaborative within a network of peers. This person must be passionate about continuing education, coaching and advocating for the team of instructors they are equipping as well as the students they are teaching. Simply put, the incumbent needs to be dedicated to the world of Data Science and even more dedicated to equipping our instructors with the tools, resources and support they need, resulting in our students receiving the highest quality education and training required to land entry-level data science jobs upon graduation from the program. This person must have a management background, expertise in curriculum development, methodology, content, and delivery of interactive, facilitation-based training utilizing multiple teaching platforms including classroom, web-based, hands-on, and self-directed instruction. An established record of leadership in a collaborative setting is highly preferred.


Our Requirements and Your Experience

Research

  • Stay abreast of industry technology trends & adoption
  • Perform informal interviews with internal and external authors and other resources to evaluate emerging technologies and assess relevance to curriculum planning
  • Evaluate emerging frameworks and significant modifications to existing major frameworks and assess relevance to curriculum planning.

Development & Content Lifecycle Management

  • Help to shape and implement curricula plans under the direction of leadership across all platform-supported content types
  • Publish and maintain curriculum roadmaps
  • Forecast and assess library content needs
  • Collaborate with counterparts across the organization to maintain a comprehensive feedback loop and proactive lifecycle management for produced content

Expert and Content Acquisition

  • Assist with expert recruitment in collaboration with the technical recruitment team
  • Assist and guide the evaluation and approval of prospective authors.
  • Assist and guide the evaluation, development, and approval of author submitted proposals.
  • Attend trade shows, conferences, and other events as determined in consultation with the CEO to stay abreast of industry trends

Author Relationships

  • Develop and maintain positive working relationships with experts
  • Coach and engage in technical discussions with experts as needed
  • Regularly share curriculum needs and plans to guide author efforts and solicit author input

Organization Support

  • Support Director of Education with general curriculum communication to company
  • Collaborate with the business development team on partner content initiatives
  • Collaborate with professional services on content's process to investigate both new and old technologies to satisfy University demand
  • Collaborate with product/content marketing team to develop sales- and marketing-enablement collateral related to curriculum plans
  • Regularly maintain and share curriculum plans with the company

Required Education, Certificates/Licenses

  • S. in Information Systems, Computer Science, engineering or other technical areas which provide analysis and programming or equivalent experience, Master's Degree Preferred
  • Expertise in core technologies of:
    • Tableau Desktop
    • IBM Cognos
    • SQL
    • PL/SQL queries
    • Coding Knowledge - Java, C, C++
  • Must have 6 - 8 years of combined curriculum development experience or training program creation
  • Must have excellent writing capabilities to clearly and concisely communicate engaging instructions
  • At least 5 years of experience writing complex SQL, PL/SQL queries
  • At least 3 years of experience working with users in requirements gathering, developing and implementing analytics solutions and developing documentation
  • At least 3 years of development experience with at least one of the following technologies: Tableau Desktop, IBM Cognos
  • Solid understanding of database, data warehousing and ETL/ELT concepts
  • Experience developing exercises or lessons on Github
  • Experience in the following areas a plus:
    • Instruction at a technology bootcamp
    • Mentoring junior developers
    • Prior experience as a teacher
  • Exceptional and engaging presentation skills - particularly when it comes to complex technical information
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills
  • Strong analytical, conceptual and problem solving skills
  • A desire to work in a fast-moving environment with a high degree of ownership and independence
  • A love of data science and analytics and a passion for growing the careers of a diverse new generation of software engineering professionals
