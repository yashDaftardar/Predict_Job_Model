SCOPE:
This position reports to and is under the general supervision of the Director of Logistics. The Data Analyst provides support to supply chain management operations for the Company’s global government and commercial contracts via the utilization of Oracle WMS/Supply Chain Management to include; but, not limited to data authentication, formatting, extracting/loading data into various databases, review/approve patches, updates and upgrades, and diagnose/troubleshoot database errors. This position will work closely with the following departments: Procurement, Logistics and database end users.
BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:
  • Bachelor’s degree required or relevant experience in lieu of. Minimum of 5 years or experience in supply chain and database management.
  • Proficient with Microsoft Office applications, in particular Excel
  • Understands data management and formatting
  • Progressive experience as a database administrator
PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:
  • Bachelor’s degree in: Business, Supply Chain Management, Information Technology, Computer Science, or related field
  • Experience with Oracle WMS / Supply Chain Management
  • Progressive experience in a lead position as a database administrator

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
  • Data authentication and formatting
  • Extract / Load data into various databases
  • Review / approve patches, updates and upgrades and test in test system before applying to production
  • Diagnose and troubleshoot database errors and work with technical support as necessary to resolve
  • Assist with cyber security measures
  • Develop / manage user profiles
  • Provide training to users
  • Develop user manuals and processes. May be asked to write policies and procedures
  • Assist with policies and procedures
  • Configure database and hardware
  • Develop, run, and distribute reports
  • Assist end users with placing orders and determining their status throughout the supply chain
  • Develop dashboards, KPIs and establish metrics
  • Manage catalogues and create new parts in parts database

OTHER DUTIES:
  • May be asked to travel to provide training, and assist with supply chain activities. Travel less than 10%.
  • Assists Logistics department as required with shipping, receiving, inventory functions, etc.
  • Performs duties in a safe manner. Follows the corporate safety policy. Participates and supports safety meetings, training and goals. Ensures safe operating conditions within area of responsibility. Encourages co-workers to work safely. Identifies “close calls” and/or safety concerns to supervisory personnel.
  • Maintains a clean and orderly work area.
  • May be assigned special projects or responsibilities. Other duties as assigned.

PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:
  • Work is normally performed in a typical interior/office work environment as well as warehouse environment.
  • Work can involve sitting or standing for prolonged periods of time
  • May require bending, stooping and lifting up to 50 lbs; coordination of movement of heavier packages with equipment and machinery
