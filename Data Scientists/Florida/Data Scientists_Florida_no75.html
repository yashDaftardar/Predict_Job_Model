What does this role look like?
You will be responsible for designing, developing and identifying data solutions that have direct and measurable impact to the Company’s support operations.

Some of your main responsibilities will be:
  • Provide data science solutions for business operations by monitoring trends, identifying opportunities, developing and presenting recommendations, implementing the strategies, and measuring the impact.
  • Develop queries and performs extensive programming to access, transform, and prepare data for statistical modeling.
  • Identify and diagnose data inconsistencies and errors, documents data assumptions, and forages to fill data gaps.
  • Develops and implements analytics enabled solutions to improve business process, to generate insights, support business goals and strategy development.
  • Generates relevant, actionable insights based on iterative data analysis, translate data-driven output into business language, and to make appropriate recommendations to respective business stakeholders.
  • Summarize analysis using a wide range of data visualization techniques to determine the types of insights derived from the product and data limitations.
  • Build and maintain effective relationships throughout the organization, fostering communication between internal and external project stakeholders. which influence key decisions across stakeholder groups.
Qualifications
Must-haves:
  • Master’s degree or professional qualification in computer science, data science, mathematics, industrial engineering or a related field.
  • 3+ years relevant work experience in business analysis including market competitiveness, financial analysis, transportation and logistics network optimization. In addition, to experience with modeling linear regression, logistics regression, non-parametric statistics, probabilistic modeling and spatial modeling.
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office products, including Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint, Word, Access, Visio, SharePoint, Microsoft Projects or similar relational databases. Technical expertise regarding data models, database design development, data mining, SQL query language, report building and segmentation techniques.
  • Preferred certifications in Machine Learning & Artificial Intelligence, database management, cloud computing and/or ETL processes.
