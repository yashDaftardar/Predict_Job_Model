Principal Statistician

The Role

  • Applies advanced theoretical knowledge and contributes to the development of new principles and concepts
  • Provide statistical expertise, study design, and analysis and communicate data driven decisions for biologics process improvements, DS and/or fill finish process understanding, method development, transfer and validation, and stability studies in collaboration with personnel
  • Establish control strategies for manufacturing processes and analytical assays, evaluate process control and capability, and determine appropriate regulatory and release specifications
  • Maintain statistical expertise by learning new methodologies, choosing which methods to use in analysis, and justifying methods selected
  • Communicate study results and statistical concepts in one-on-one meetings, oral presentations, internal reports, and presentations at professional associate meetings
  • Develop and administer training courses for various statistical methods and software, including planning and leading workshops to help facilitate statistical thinking across the various functional areas

The Candidate

  • Master of Science degree in Statistics or related scientific field with 8+ years of practical industry experience applying statistical methods or
  • PhD in Statistics, or related scientific field with 5+ years of practical industry experience applying statistical methods
  • 5-8+ years of experience
  • GMP experience preferred
