Job Description
Sr. Data Operations Manager

Type: Full Time
Location: Palm Beach County, FL

Anticipated Role and Duties:

We are a venture funded technology startup, bringing to life a new video technology, invented at MIT. Our company develops AI-based video production software for major broadcasters, sports leagues and corporations. The company’s applications deal with using various forms of AI and computer vision for 2D-to-3D video conversion. We’re looking for the next, key, exceptional individuals who will fit our growing team and passionate culture.

The Sr. Data Operations Manager is an entry-to-mid level role reporting directly to the company CEO and CTO. The role is ideal for professions in IT, Data management, Project management, QA, Media management and related fields of study. We seek passionate professionals with team-attitude that feel their basic technical proficiencies would add value to our team while gaining new skills and experience.

Core responsibilities will include:

1) Managing 2-3 Jr. Data Managers and 2-3 offshore suppliers as direct reports

2) Managing the general data pipeline from acquisition to readiness for AI training

3) Close interaction with company engineers and business executives

4) Recommending and implementing incremental improvements to office systems including IT infrastructure and task workflows

Required Background:

Candidates for this position should possess the following background and capabilities –

· Team attitude - You exemplify this in everything you do, are the one that goes that extra mile, and have demonstrated leadership skills throughout your life

· Self-starter - you can keep up with the team, convert challenge to opportunity, deliver results without micro-management

· Preferred education – IT management, Data management, Project management, Media production

· You can manage large data or media libraries (e.g., video, images, metadata)

· Comfortable making mid-level business decisions that directly effect productivity

_ Bonus Qualifications _ (in addition to above requirements):

· IT networking and skills, local and/or cloud

· Video and image production and/or editing skills

· Programming skills: HTML, Java

· Interested in artificial intelligence technologies, emerging technologies, Virtual production, VR/AR
