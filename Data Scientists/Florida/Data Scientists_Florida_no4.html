In this role, you will join the Operations Information Systems (OIS) team providing innovative solutions to complex business problems identified as high value. You will be tasked to apply statistical models and analyses to answer the business questions in an actionable and reproducible manner.

In this role, you will have the opportunity to:
Create statistical models than can predict whether users will churn, their likelihood to take a given action, orders that will likely be late etc.
Leverage SQL and other programming languages to acquire the data to analyze in ad hoc analyses.
Perform data profiling to identify and understand anomalies in data.
Be a key player in the design and implementation of new actionable intelligence.
Automate data analysis and streamline processes to increase business efficiency.
Provide recommendations based on data trends uncovered when possible.
Ability to describe the logic and implications of a complex model to all types of business partners.

The essential requirements of the job include:
Working towards a Bachelor Degree OR Master Degree OR Doctoral Degree in Data Science, Statistics, Economics, Math, Computer Science or other relevant quantitative fields.
Looking for applicant who will still be enrolled in school after the summer internship.
Actual data science tools in your repertoire: R, Python, SPSS, data mining, etc or similar software.
Experience with relational databases and SQL.
Experience with a variety of supervised and unsupervised machine learning methods
