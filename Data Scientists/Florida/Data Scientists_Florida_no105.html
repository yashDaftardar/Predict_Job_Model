Associate, Rotational Development– Data Science Track BI &A - (USA00018N)

Description


OUR PEOPLE : Crowley is a privately-owned marine, transportation and logistics company providing worldwide solutions since 1892. We have over 6,000 high-performing team members in 35 countries and Caribbean territories, who are diverse, encouraged and deliver on their commitments. We are Crowley, the people who know .

THE CROWLEY WAY : We believe in and celebrate the power of collaboration between people with diverse backgrounds and experiences who share their differences and are driven to achieve business and personal goals. We support the development of our high-performers and are committed to guiding them every step of the way.

SUMMARY: Crowley’s Rotational Development Program is designed for recent college graduates to gain Data Science experience in various functions within our organization. The program lasts 18 months and consists of three rotations each lasting 6 months. Through a combination of training, job shadowing and hands-on experience, you will be you will be exposed to many different situations within the Digital Transformation, Project Management Organization and Design & Analytics departments. We are looking for individuals who have demonstrated strong analytical and leadership skills and can apply those skills to our business. By the end of the rotation, you will be expected to use these learning experiences to successfully perform the responsibilities listed below.

GENERAL RESPONSIBILITIES:

DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION:

Works with multiple levels of the business and leadership to better enable analytics and data across the supply chain organization in multiple regions. Leads the design, development, and deployment of data-driven predictive models to solve business problems using the most appropriate techniques in data mining, artificial intelligence/machine learning, and statistical modeling. Applies machine learning concepts to support Crowley’s analytics transformation through execution of use cases such as fleet optimization dynamic pricing, optimized financial analytics, product recommendations, optimization of warehouse operations, churn prevention, etc. Utilizes data science and statistical expertise to drive deep visibility of the business, supporting Crowley’s vision of delivering value to customer through end-to-end analytics platform. Works closely with data warehouse architects and software developers to generate seamless business intelligence solutions for business partners in an agile development project management. Pilots emerging “big data” technologies to help Crowley understand how to exploit new methodologies, technologies, and tools. Establishes relationships with various data stakeholders and working with leadership to support on strategies for advanced analytics at Crowley. Researches and advises business partners on how data and predictive models (processes, practices, and technologies) playing a critical role in improving business management and optimization.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT OFFICE (PMO):

Works with multiple levels of the business and leadership to better manage and support the end-to-end PMO framework, which includes leveraging best practices and techniques in identifying, quantifying, and tracking the realization of expected benefits defined in business cases. Applies management skills including Scrum, Kanban etc. to design and develop business solutions and improve processes for better efficiencies and effectiveness. Utilizes project management tools and techniques to drive deep visibility of the projects, supporting Crowley’s vision of delivering value to customer-centric solutions. Establishes relationships with various data stakeholders and working with leadership to support strategies for driving process efficiencies and effectiveness. In this role, you will be researching and advising business partners on strategy leading to cost efficiencies and competitive advantage.

DESIGN & ANALYTICS:

Analyzes, develops and delivers various business intelligent solutions in support of core organizational functions and business processes. Compiles and analyzes data to provide business trends to management and assist sales in business development. Assists management in the development of reporting, analysis and performance metrics which allow the business to align and track business goals, drive innovation, insight and decision making. References and builds business intelligence dashboards, analytic reports and data visualizations using various reporting tools. Maintains database reports and dashboards to ensure accuracy. Enhances and proactively design reports based on the business needs and changing environments. Presents training on report content, processes and the usage of dashboards.

Qualifications


EDUCATION: Bachelor’s or Master's degree in computer science, data science, mathematics, industrial engineering or a related field. Must be a recent college/university graduate.

EXPERIENCE: 0 - 2 years of experience in data analysis, business related research, and/or marketing analysis. Prior internship experience preferred.

TECHNICAL PROFICIENCY: Proficient in Microsoft Office products including Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint and Word. Experience with reporting tools including Tableau or Power BI, SQL, data models, database design development, data mining, report building, and segmentation techniques is a plus.

COMPETENCIES: Ability to communicate effectively verbally and in writing, including the ability to convey technical information to technical and non-technical users. Ability to establish and maintain effective working relationships. Ability to function effectively with, or as part of a team. Ability to prioritize, organize and perform multiple work assignments simultaneously. Ability to effectively manage projects that may vary in nature and scope. Ability to identify and understand complex problems and review related information to evaluate options and implement solutions. Ability to collect, organize, analyze and disseminate significant amounts of information accurately and with attention to detail. Ability to develop, improve and streamline processes to meet established goals and objectives. Ability to prepare clear and concise reports, policies, procedure, correspondences, and other written materials. Knowledge of methods to collect data, research and present. Knowledge of data analysis techniques and methodology.

JOB SCOPE: Position impacts multiple business unit/segment.

WORKING CONDITIONS: Normal office environment with little exposure to excessive noise, dust, temperature and the like. May require remote work as necessary. Extended use of a computer keyboard, viewing of a computer monitor and the ability to sit for long periods of time.

FLSA Status : The position is Non-Exempt. May require the ability to work overtime to meet required deadlines. Must comply with all work rules, including those that pertain to safety and health.
