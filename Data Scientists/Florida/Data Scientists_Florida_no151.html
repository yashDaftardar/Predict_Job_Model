Full Job Description

General Responsibilities:
This role is responsible for midterm and short-term planning, distribution/logistics of fleet, as well as fleet operations functions including getting cars in service, registered, deleting, etc. for North America Rent-A-Car business.

BUSINESS IMPACT:
This role has an impact on our fleet planning, fleet distribution and fleet operations.

Job Contributions- what will you do

FLEET FORECASTING

  • Build predictive modelling to enhance and support Hertz’s modeling and assist on technical and process solutions to Fleet Operations business problems, to incorporate impact of options, volume, incentives and other factors.

OPTIMIZATION

  • Enhance optimization of fleet planning by creating modeling and data analytics around logistics and short-term planning.

OPERATION SUPPORT

  • Utilize analytical tools and database language to aggregate data and build KPI report to increase efficiency of daily fleet operations.

PROBLEM SOLVING

  • Resolve a wide variety of issues that the fleet team might experience with their operational process, by leveraging fleet database and model forecasting.

TEAM WORK

  • Work in a fast-paced environment, efficiently and effectively to meet the quality, service, and performance goals for North America Rent-A-Car business. Supporting colleagues across all roles within operations to create better support and provide help for field colleagues.

CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT

  • Constantly seek solutions to improve fleet forecasting and related processes that overlap other functions including RM and Operations.
Essential Requirements:
  • Master’s degree with 3+ years of experience as data scientist.
  • Working experience with a statistical programming language (like SAS, R, Octave, Python etc.).
  • Experience with machine learning (regression, classification, neural networks). Preferably also have experience with constrained mathematical optimization models (Linear Programming, CPLEX).
  • Experience with SQL/Oracle databases.
  • Ability to work as part of a team.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Meet the pre-employment requirements to include background check, motor vehicle record check and a 5-panel drug test.
