Data Scientist
$2,500 bonus for new employees who start by June 30, 2021*
Certified Collectibles Group (CCG) is seeking an experienced Data Scientist supporting our fast-paced domestic and international multi-company organization.
Specific responsibilities include:
  • Analyzing internal processes such as receiving, grading, and shipping
  • Membership analysis - signups, churn, upgrades
  • Competitor analysis
  • User cohort analysis
  • Predictive modelling
  • Data structuring
Requirements:
  • Bachelor’s Degree in a quantitative field (Math, CompSci, Econ, Data Science etc)
  • 4 years experience in Data Science, Analytics or Data Engineering
  • Excellent SQL competency
  • Intermediate+ Python competency
  • Experience in a BI tool, ideally Looker
  • Comfortable dealing with disparate, unstructured, multilingual data sources
  • Comfortable suggesting and implementing data pipeline changes
  • Excellent written and verbal communications skills
  • Proven time management skills focusing on urgent and overlapping deadlines
Preferable:
  • An interest in collectibles (Coins, Comics, Stamps, Paper Money, Baseball Cards, Pokemon, Magic The Gathering etc)
  • Familiarity with of any of the following tools: Snowflake, DBT, FiveTran
Interview Process:
  • Phone screen to discuss role requirements
  • Coding screen - candidates will be tested on their Python + SQL ability in a live CoderPad
CCG offers the most competitive compensation in the industry along with career advancement opportunities and a generous full-time benefits package including health, dental and vision insurance options; 401(k) with company match; paid time off benefits, annual discretionary bonuses and more. Salary to commensurate with experience and ability.
About Us
Certified Collectibles Group (CCG) includes eight of the world’s leading expert services companies for coins, paper money, comic books, stamps, and other collectibles: Numismatic Guaranty Corporation (NGC), Numismatic Conservation Services (NCS), Paper Money Guaranty (PMG), Certified Guaranty Company (CGC), Classic Collectible Services (CCS), Authenticated Stamp Guaranty (ASG), Collectibles Authentication Guaranty (CAG) and Certified Sports Guaranty (CSG). Since 1987 the CCG companies have certified more than 62 million collectibles and maintain offices in Sarasota, Florida; London, England; Munich, Germany; and Shanghai and Hong Kong, China.
