Johnson & Johnson Vision Care, Inc., a member of the Johnson & Johnson Family of Companies, is recruiting for a Sr Analyst, Data Scientist Business Intelligence located in Jacksonville, FL with consideration for candidates throughout the east coast.

At Johnson & Johnson Vision, we have a bold ambition: to change the trajectory of eye health. That’s why, through our operating companies, we’ve developed solutions for every stage of life—to help people see better, connect better and live better. We partner with eye care professionals to provide some of the world’s leading products and technologies to address refractive error, cataracts, dry eye, and beyond. Follow @JNJVision on Twitter and Johnson & Johnson Vision on LinkedIn.

The Senior Analyst, Data Scientist Business Intelligence will help define and shape innovative trending and modelling techniques to analyze data, build insights and drive business impact across the US for the Americas Region. The key focus is to help accelerate sales growth across ACUVUE® Brand Contact Lenses for Independent Eye Care Professionals and Retail Outlets.

This is an exciting and meaningful position as an integral part of the overall Business Intelligence Team. This individual should bring passionate thought leadership and ideas from the data science space to help the Business Intelligence team shape and drive business impact using modern tools and data science techniques.

Proactive, curious, and empathetic individuals tend to be the most successful in this type of role. They can lead themselves well and seek out solutions without having to be told. They get more engaged with difficult problems. They seek to understand the people-side of the problem.

Responsibilities

  • Help define and shape innovative trending and modelling techniques to analyze data, build insights and drive business impact across the US
  • Apply analytical/statistical methods to complex and disparate data sources
  • Lead the creation of analytical models, such as clustering and targeting for customer groups, with focus on business growth
  • Help determine and solve business critical problem statements through modeling knowledge and discussion and collaboration with broader business intelligence team and marketing and sales partners
  • Lead targeting exercises to support key initiatives for marketing and sales
  • Communicate and collaborate model results with Business Intelligence leadership and other leaders in the organization
  • Work with business partners and business intelligence team to proactively identify data trends through data and modeling efforts

Qualifications
  • Minimum of a Bachelors degree required. Masters degree preferred. Degree concentration in a STEM field (Computer Science, Information/Data Science, Engineering or Mathematics/Statistics) highly preferred.
  • Three years total experience in data science or related field required, with experience analyzing real-world (noisy/imperfect/incomplete) datasets and developing algorithms to address problems required
  • Knowledge or experience with Machine Learning, AI, or other related applied data science methodologies is preferred
  • Prior experience with programming / software development is required, preferably in Python and/or R
  • Proactive engagement with partners to solve business problems
  • Superior attention to detail, organization skills, and the ability to balance multiple tasks
  • Ability to talk with business partner and help determine problem statement
  • Strong oral and written communication skills
  • The ability to work solo and as part of a team
  • Desire to grow career and influence how data science can provide business value
  • Experience working with data analytics, visualization software, and database tools such as Tableau, SQL, Alteryx, SAS, SAS JMP or similar systems is highly preferred
  • There is a strong location preference for Jacksonville, Florida, with consideration for remote applicants
  • Up to 10% domestic travel.
