Full Job Description

Your Opportunity

As the Data Science Lead in Advanced Analytics, you will actively lead and expand our analytics capability and drive key programmes to ensure continuous delivery of robust insights and analytics across the organization. You will create value by establishing a set of advanced analytics principles to enhance analytical and data to decisions agility and coordination across functions and regions. In addition, it will be a pivotal responsibility to lead business transformation by establishing and developing Data Science capability and competency programme at all levels of the organization. Finally, the Data Science Lead will be the most senior data scientist in the global organisation, will lead major data science programmes and initiatives, and will be a key participant in strategic data analytics and governance forums.

About You

Ideal candidates for this role have a strong Data Science background combined with deep functional expertise across marketing, commercial, and pricing domains. Being extremely comfortable in identifying complex patterns and extracting predictive and explanatory insights from a range of data sources is a must. The ability to influence, partner, and lead across all level of global organisation, while being agile to drive results and scalable benefit is critical.

Responsibilities

With our Consumer at the heart, your key focus will be to:
  • Lead the organization in adopting a balanced data and analytics driven business decision-making process
  • Drive organizational maturity into the predictive and prescriptive advanced analytics space
  • Provide guidance and oversight of marketing, commercial and pricing analytics efforts
  • Provide ad hoc analytical expertise for key strategic business programs at ALT/GLT and functional level
  • Establish a center of expertise for the advanced analytics initiatives across the global organization
  • Product ownership – lead and manage significant workstreams and efforts
  • Develop unique and novel solutions and procedures to complement and enhance existing capabilities
  • Partner with learning and development function to develop analytics competency at all levels of organization

Skills and Experience

The Skills & Experience needed to create your legacy
  • Extensive experience (10+ years) in applied Data Science, Decision Science, or Advanced Analytics (Python, R, DataBricks, other machine learning and AI tools)
  • Experienced in establishing, maturing, developing, and leading organisational capabilities at global scale
  • Advanced Degree in a Statistics / Computer Science / Engineering / Mathematics / Physics / Economics / Data Science
  • Apply data science techniques to present, communicate and disseminate data science products effectively, appropriately and with high impact, using appropriate medium to visualise data to tell compelling and actionable story relevant for business goals
  • Know how to apply scientific method, experimental design, exploratory data analysis, hypothesis testing to reach robust conclusions
  • Understand how algorithms are designed, optimised and applied at scale
  • Have a demonstrable understanding of how to expose data from systems (for example, through APIs), link data from multiple systems and deliver streaming services
  • Know how to build scalable data analytics products that can be used by strategic or operational users and can be further integrated into business systems
Personal Qualities
  • Taking ownership of building organisation-wide capability by aligning strategy with people development
  • Ability to lead, engage, influence, and motivate our internal and external partners with appropriate and effective presence at all levels
  • Demonstrate strong intellectual curiosity with an interdisciplinary approach, drawing on innovation in academia and industry
  • Solution oriented, with a positive & proactive attitude
Other
