SUMMARY:
Rakuten is looking for a Software Engineer with relevant experience of 2-3 years with strong knowledge of a modern programming language on a Linux/Unix Platform. This position is in Seattle, WA.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:
Translate requirements and functional specifications into software design and implementation
Analyze, Design, implement and troubleshoot systems
Develop prototypes and proof of concepts
Participate in code and design reviews
Create or update necessary artifacts (technical documentation) as per stakeholder expectation
Collaborate with Product Management, QA, DevOps and remote teams to ensure success of deliverables
Evaluate and recommend tools, technologies, and frameworks

MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS (Knowledge, Skills, Abilities)
To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.

Exposure to cloud technologies (GCP, AWS, Azure)
Experience with Docker and Jenkins
Experience developing highly scalable applications that handle large amounts of traffic and gigabytes of data would be a bonus
Experience with query languages
Experience in developing web services
Experience with debugging and profiling
Experience working in a fast-paced environment with short timelines
Experience working with remote and distributed teams
Experience with web services and related frameworks would be highly recommended
Excellent interpersonal, verbal, and written communication skills
Strong analytical, implementation and debugging skills

QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS:
At least 2-3 years of experience in a modern programming language
