Full Job Description

Zulily is a retailer obsessed with bringing our customers something special every day. Unique products from up-and-coming brands are featured alongside favorites from top brands, including clothing, home decor, accessories, toys and gifts, giving customers something new to discover each morning at an incredible price. We are fast-paced, innovative and metric-driven, with a team passionate for delighting our customers. Whether you want to work with incredible product selection, develop smart marketing plans, help us in our pursuit of faster fulfillment, or do something else awesome in between, we have a place for you. With locations in Seattle, Reno, Columbus, and Bethlehem, our team is excited to be changing the way people shop every day – and we are just getting started.

The Member Engagement Platform (MEP) team is Zulily’s primary communication channel with the customers. Last year, we sent over 10 billion emails, mobile push notifications, Facebook messages and launched our SMS channel. For 2021, our goal is to expand our use of Machine Learning to personalize the content, schedule and channel execution of every single communication so we can give each individual customer the best and most timely information pertinent to her shopping needs. Our vision is to build a cross channel marketing orchestration platform that’s optimized for each individual customer.


Our team builds cool and fun ways to reach out to our customers on many different channels. We are looking for a strong backend Software Engineer to help build the next communication platform that will leverage ML to optimize channel-based customer notification.

Our values are mutual respect, empathy, and support for each other. We win and fail as a team. We strive for technical excellence and strive to improve our quality of lives as engineers. We don’t say “minimally viable product”; we say, “minimally lovable product”. We use modern technology to solve cool problems. In our debates, data wins over opinion.


If this reads like the culture you want to live by, give us a shout – we've been looking for you.
