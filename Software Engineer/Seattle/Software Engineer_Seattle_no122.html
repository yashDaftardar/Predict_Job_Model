This Opportunity

We are seeking full-stack software engineers who:

  • Have knowledge and experience in using and designing distributed systems, data pipelines, APIs and consumption experiences
  • Can guide workstreams, mentor and inspire junior developers and cultivate a positive work environment
  • Strict adherence to Truveta principles in support of ethical innovation
  • Willingness to commit to ongoing training on key topics like privacy, ethics, and security

  • 2+ years of experience building production quality software in a team setting
  • 1+ years of experience working on modern cloud-native platforms and languages (AWS, GCP, Azure)
  • Some familiarity with crafting modular APIs and building engaging and intuitive web experiences
  • B.S. or M.S. in Computer Science or related field
