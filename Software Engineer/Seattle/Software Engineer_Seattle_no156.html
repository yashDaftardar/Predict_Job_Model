Full Job Description

Submittable is seeking a Software Test Engineer to join our rapidly growing team in Missoula, MT or Seattle, WA. Remote hires will be considered for candidates with requisite experience.

Your role will be primarily manual testing, using the software like a customer does. You will work closely with our Product and Engineering teams to create and execute test plans for new features. You'll also create and execute regression test plans, to ensure that we didn't break something unexpected.

The ideal candidate will have the skills and interest in contributing to our automated tests. While you don't need to be a hard-core developer, you need to have some coding or scripting experience and an interest in using it! This is a great opportunity for someone interested in growing their technical skills.

This is a full time salary position

The Software Test Engineer responsibilities include:

  • Write and execute test cases. Initially, this will be primarily manual, but expect it to be more and more automated over time
  • Find, isolate and report bugs
  • Become a product expert. Understand how our customers use the product. Use this knowledge to design test cases, prioritize bugs and anticipate issues with new or changed functionality
  • Work with your scrum team to ensure that new code isn't released to customers until we're sure it works
  • Contribute to product-wide testing efforts, such as deployment testing, automated smoke tests and load testing

An ideal candidate will have these qualities and experience:

  • Software testing experience. This could be writing unit tests, manual testing of your own or a co-workers code or a more formal software testing role
  • Competent in at least one scripting or programming language with an interest in learning others. We use javascript for browser and front end tests; python for load tests; and C# for backend unit and integration tests
  • Professional experience in an agile software development environment is a plus
  • Some formal software development course work or training and a Bachelor's Degree in a STEM field is preferred
  • We are interested in every qualified candidate who is eligible to work in the United States. However, we are unable to accommodate scholastic or employment visas

About Submittable

Submittable was started in 2010 by three creatives—a writer, a filmmaker, and a musician—as a simple way to submit creative work. Today it's a growing social impact platform used by thousands of organizations to manage their programs and maximize their impact.

Submittable has its headquarters in downtown Missoula, Montana, with a satellite office in Seattle, Washington and remote employees located around the U.S.
