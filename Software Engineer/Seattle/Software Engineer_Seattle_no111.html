SAP Concur is a pioneer in the travel and expense industry. The Receipts team sits at the center of the receipt processing within SAP Concur. The core mission on the Receipts team is to build a highly scalable platform to ingest, store and consume receipts flowing into SAP Concur.


Do you love the feeling of taking on a challenging initiative with high customer impact? Does the thought of building highly scalable distributed system and your work being used by millions of users excite you? Do you see failure as an opportunity to learn and grow? If so, come join the Receipts team and help us reimagine how SAP Concur Software works as we build out our next generation of services.


In the first 12 months you will:

  • Learn: Development of a deep understanding of the existing architecture at Concur, and of the architectural challenges facing the organization. We move fast and are unafraid to venture into the unknown if it can improve our software or the way in which we deliver that software. As a result, we are constantly learning new technologies and new approaches.

  • Ship: (several iterations, in fact). The most import thing that our team can do for the company is to ship production software. Aside from adding direct value to our customers, it is also the ultimate validation of our efforts.


So, who are we?


Concur, a part of SAP, imagines the way the world should work, offering cloud-based services that make it simple to manage travel and expenses. Founded in 1993, Concur services are used by 30,000 clients representing 27 million users in more than 150 countries.


Your career at SAP Concur is what you make of it. You can forge your own path, work in different offices, switch business units or solve a problem that no one has before. We’re inspired by change and by each other which is a major contributing factor to how we have retained the innovative culture that has created a market leading team of 4,800 colleagues.


What do you need to bring?

  • BSCS, BSEE, or equivalent years of work experience. Deep understanding of data structures and algorithms.

  • 5+ years of programming experience with at least one modern language such as Java, C++, or C# including object-oriented design

  • 2+ years of experience contributing to the architecture and design (architecture, design patterns, reliability and scaling) of new and current systems

  • Experience deploying applications (across multiple versions) to a public cloud platform such as AWS. Proficiency with AWS, container technology such as Docker, Kubernetes is highly desirable.
