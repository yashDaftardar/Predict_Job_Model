From the beginning, Starbucks set out to be a different kind of company. One that not only celebrated coffee and the rich tradition, but that also brought a feeling of connection. We are known for developing extraordinary leaders who share this passion and are guided by their service to others.

Payments and Anti-Fraud Technology is an exciting and fast-evolving engineering group within Starbucks Technology. Our mission is to optimize, protect, and deliver industry-leading payment experiences at Starbucks. For this role, the focus will be on developing solutions that support our in-app and web-based digital payment services.

As a Senior Software Development Engineer, you will…

  • Contribute directly to moments of connection for millions of daily customers
  • Build innovative, highly performant, resilient, scalable, extensible, and secure software solutions
  • Establish key, complex business capabilities and platform features
  • Deliver high quality software using Agile/Scrum development methodologies
  • Drive the realization of strategic initiatives and operational improvements

We’d love to hear from people with:

  • 4+ years of professional industry experience with software development preferably in C# or Java
  • Strong software engineering fundamentals including knowledge of design patterns and OO design principles.
  • Experience with architecture, design, implementation, and delivery of secure services that perform at scale
  • Experience working with distributed systems and architectures, including SOA, SaaS, and microservices approaches
  • Experience with cloud implementations in Azure or AWS
  • Experience with relational and non-relational, NoSQL databases
  • Experience in designing and developing a significant component or feature of a scalable service
  • Experience with financial, payments or fraud detection systems preferred
  • Strong verbal and written communications skills are a must, as well as the ability to work effectively across internal and external organizations
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field
  • A passion to demonstrate that the above is simply a punch-list, and a drive to constantly improve yourself & the partners around you
