The Webex team is looking for senior mobile developers to craft and architect our next generation collaboration technologies. We are looking for developers passionate in user experience, infrastructure, and/or modern tools supporting applications on the iPhone or iPad using iOS platform. In this role, you will contribute in design and development of new product features, product releases and quality for our industry leading Webex applications that are used by millions every day to keep the world connected. Who You Are

Required skills:
Software Developer with 4+ years experience in the development of mobile client applications
Thrive in a fast paced agile development environment and possess a passion for application development and delighting our users.
Bachelor degree in Computer Science, related technical field, or equivalent practical experience.
4+ years of application software development using C++, Objective-C, Swift or others.
Experience using Xcode or Git. Who You’ll Work With
