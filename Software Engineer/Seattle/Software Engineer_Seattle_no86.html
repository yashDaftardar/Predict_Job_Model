Full Job Description

Allogy is looking for a Software Test Engineer to test and validate our SaaS product which is deployed to web, iOS, and Android. The candidate should have experience with software testing and at least some programming background. This role will involve creating test plans and procedures, performing manual testing of our web applications and mobile apps, and developing automated test cases. In this position, you will take the lead of our testing and verification efforts and will be responsible for improving our testing process.

Allogy is a SaaS Company with growing presence in healthcare and government sectors. As a part of the Allogy core team you will be developing enterprise software products in collaboration with innovation teams at some of the largest hospitals and federal agencies in the country. Your job will include thinking critically and making decisions focusing on longevity and maintainability. As the team grows you will have a continued say in product development decisions and the roadmap for our products.

Responsibilities

  • Develop function test cases and procedures and execute these against the product
  • Create and execute manual tests
  • Collaborate with development team to ensure defects are resolved
  • Develop automated tests for web and mobile applications
  • Evaluate the system for compliance with contract requirements and relevant standards

Requirements

  • 2 or more years of software testing experience; including writing test procedures from requirements
  • Experience with testing mobile and web UIs
  • Experience with automated testing tools such as Selenium
  • Understanding of internet and web technologies

Preferred Qualifications

  • Software testing in agile teams
  • Jenkins CI
  • Java programming experience
  • Selenium programming
