Senior Front-End Software Engineer

The Company

Located in downtown Kirkland, Net-Inspect is an established software company that provides web-based quality improvement software for the aerospace and medical industries. Our customers range from small manufacturers to large Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) like Boeing, Pratt & Whitney, Honeywell, and Bombardier. We are currently looking for an experienced Senior Front-End Software Engineer .

Responsibilities

As a Senior Software Engineer primarily focused on UI development, you will

· Design and build new user interfaces using cutting-edge front-end technologies

· Work alongside a team of developers to design, develop, deploy, and maintain web applications

· Ship new features to a production environment on a regular basis

· Learn and evolve quickly to improve solutions

· Own development, planning, and rollout of the entire front-end side of solutions offered

· Mentor junior developers

· Take an active role in sprint planning and all technical discussions

· Work closely with business to make sure all intended objectives are met during design and development

· Perform regular code reviews

· Responsible for security of the solutions offered from a UI perspective

· Gain an understanding of the infrastructure of the entire product regardless of the primary area of focus

The Requirements

Net-Inspect is seeking a candidate with a minimum of 5 years of experience in the following areas:

· HTML

· SASS/CSS

· JavaScript

· AngularJS and Angular

· Modern build tools (Webpack, etc.)

· Contributing to a modern web application (not including websites). Please include details in your application.

Desired Experience

· Additional experience with any of the above

· Experience with TypeScript or ES6

· Experience with NodeJS

· Experience in mobile development

· Strong sense for visual and user experience design

· Knowledge of modern front-end tooling (languages, frameworks, build tools, module bundlers, CI/CD, etc.)

· Experience in C#, .NET and MSSQL

NOTE: Given we manage very sensitive export-controlled Aerospace and Defense data, our customers contractually require us to only staff UNITED STATES CITIZENS on our development team. Please only apply for this position if you meet this requirement.

If you are passionate about technology, love building software that can disrupt an entire industry and can enjoy working with a small, but young development team, this opportunity is for you!
