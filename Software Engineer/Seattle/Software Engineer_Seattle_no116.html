Job Description


At Microsoft Technology Center, AI and Developer Tools and Runtimes team, we believe in changing the world for the better by using innovative technologies to deliver software performance for Intel Architectures. Our work translates into faster and more efficient code execution across PC, Server, and Cloud applications. As a member of the team, you will help enable Intel Silicon features in managed runtimes. You will have an opportunity to work and collaborate with hardware engineering teams, external partners and Microsoft open-source communities developing software optimizations and features specific for Intel Architectures.

The responsibilities include just-in-time compiler and library optimizations for Intel platforms, including code generation and dynamic profile guided optimizations (PGO) technologies.


Qualifications


You must possess the below minimum qualifications to be initially considered for this position Experience listed below would be obtained through a combination of your schoolwork classes research and or relevant previous job and or internship experiences.

Minimum Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree and 3+ years of experience or Master’s degree with 1 year of experience or a PhD in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or Electrical Engineering or Mechanical Engineering or Physics or Mathematics or similar technical discipline
  • 1+ years’ experience developing high quality code in C/C++, C#, Java or similar programming language
  • 1+ year’s of experience with compiler design and theory (GCC/LLVM/V8 development)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • 1+ years of professional work experience developing JIT and compiler technology or performing low-level CPU or GPU performance optimizations
  • Experience with modern JIT/compilers, such as GCC/LLVM
  • Background in modern CPU, GPU architectures, runtimes, compiler code generation, software performance tuning on x86
