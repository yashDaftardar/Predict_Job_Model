Full Job Description

Working with ITTIA development team is exciting, innovative and fun, as you will be assigned important responsibilities and continuously grow. We offer an environment that is welcoming, honest, and based on partnership.

The ITTIA Junior Software Developer position starts part-time, with the potential to convert tasks and responsibilities into to a full-time position.

ITTIA Junior Software Developer is for YOU, if you have:

  • Ability to develop and apply testing processes for a database software product
  • Readiness to identify, record, document, and track issues
  • Attention to track quality assurance metrics, such as defect densities and open defect counts
  • Innovative approach to build software demonstrations
  • About 12-18 months until graduation

Performance Requirements:

  • Knowledge of C, C++, and SQL
  • Software development and quality assurance background
  • Experience in writing clear, concise and comprehensive test plans and test cases
  • Hands-on experience with both white box and black box testing
  • Major in Computer Science, Engineering or a related subject

Please forward your resume and cover letter to resumes [at] ittia.com.
