Full Job Description

The Trade Desk is a global technology company with a mission to create a better, more open Internet for everyone through principled, intelligent advertising.

Handling over 600 billion queries per day (more than 100X the query volume of search globally), our platform operates at unprecedented scale. We have also built something even stronger and more valuable: an award-winning culture based on trust, empathy, collaboration, and ownership. By working together across typical dividing lines, we are better as a team than any of us could be apart.

Do you have a passion for solving hard problems at scale? Are you eager to join a trust-based, globally-connected team, where your contributions will make a meaningful difference?

Come and see why Fortune Magazine consistently ranks The Trade Desk among best small-medium sized workplaces globally.

What you'll do:

Developer experience means speeding up all of engineering by improving efficiency and quality. We design and implement processes and tools that impact everybody and help the organization continuously scale. If you're the kind of person who encounters a roadblock and says "I want to fix this for the next person who comes across this" come join us! We focus where we can have the most impact, whether it's improving build performance, building new platform-wide technologies, rethinking code organization, or building completely new deployment frameworks, just to name a few. The Trade Desk's platform pushes the boundaries of modern software engineering and keeping it balanced requires tools and processes that do the same.


Who we are looking for:

  • Care deeply about improving developer experience, tools, engineering practices, and technical debt.
  • Communicate clearly and empathetically; your customers are your peers and you will work with them every day.
  • Drive innovative solutions while being sensitive to the current needs and realities of your fellow engineers.
  • Navigate differing opinions, ambiguity, and compliance requirements to build and advocate designs with clear objectives and trade-offs.
  • Proven JS development experience (any framework)
  • A passion for both user and developer experience.
  • Affinity for working closely with the UX team.
  • Proven shell scripting (Windows and/or Linux)

Bonus, but not required:

  • Experience working with configuration/management of continuous integration/delivery systems
  • Experience with configuration and management of cloud/virtualization platforms (e.g. AWS, Docker, Kubernetes)
  • Experience with frontend validation tooling like eslint, storybook, happo, etc.
  • Experience with frontend build systems like webpack, snowpack, etc.
