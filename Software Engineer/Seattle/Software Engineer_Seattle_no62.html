Calling all highly motivated, upcoming or recent college graduates with Mendix experience!

Did you get a taste of the Mendix, low-code platform through one of the Mendix University programs? If you did and you want more of it, we have the position for you!


Pike Street Labs is looking for a Jr. Front End Mendix Developer to join our software engineering team. We are a highly seasoned development team that builds platforms and user experiences for our parent company, WaFd Bank. By working with us, you'll get an opportunity to learn how to design and build software at scale, utilizing the most modern development technologies and techniques.

This position's primary responsibility is to be on the Online Banking Product Team at Pike Street Labs. Duties include designing, coding, and unit testing for new features for the WaFd Bank Online Banking portal. You'll be part of a scrum-based team that will follow agile methodology to deliver new features and user experiences for the WaFd Bank retail clients on an approximate 8-week cadence.


This is a fast-paced position where you'll work with business teams and technical members from Pike Street Labs to deliver innovative and compelling user experiences. Pike Street Labs is a culture of self-motivators and technical enthusiasts. To succeed you'll need to have a passion for technology, be able to work without a large amount of oversight, be an excellent team player and outstanding communicator.


Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience in relevant field.
Mendix certification and/or classroom-based experience with the platform
0-2 years of professional programming experience
Understanding of working with RESTful APIs
Web Programming Skills in HTML, CSS, Javascript
Familiarity with Agile/Scrum based development methodologies
