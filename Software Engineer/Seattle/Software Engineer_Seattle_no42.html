Xbox is one of the world’s most valuable brands and enjoys millions of players and passionate fans. An important part of our plan is to grow our global player base and our multi-billion dollar a year Xbox console and accessories business. At Xbox, we believe Gamer Happiness is at the core to our mission of empowering every person and organization on the planet to achieve more.

Gamer Happiness is at the core of our mission. Xbox Community (XBC) supports the growth and happiness of our broader gaming community, including ensuring the safety and security all our members. We provide thought leadership and execute on growing a truly inclusive community wherever people play games.

We are looking for an extraordinary engineer to help drive the key safety and family experiences and services that will make Xbox Live the best, safest community for all. In this role, you will join a vibrant group of engineers building experiences and services for gamers on mobile and web.

Responsibilities

We are looking for a Software Development Engineer for the Xbox Community team who is creative, highly motivated, independent and innovative. You will need to be passionate about building experiences in the mobile, PC and web space while also building highly reliable, secure and scalable services that enable tens of millions of gamers to play online every day. Our bold charter is to build new, pioneering experiences on mobile, PC and web, and we need you to help us take that next step. You will be defining, prototyping and building new features, products and services. We will bring these innovations everywhere gamers are – PC, mobile, web, Xbox console and beyond.

You are uniquely positioned to deeply impact Xbox's future and growth.
  • Help us drive architecture, design, development of Xbox services, mobile and PC apps, and web experiences
  • Collaborate with other client and service teams in gaming and studios to enable groundbreaking new experiences
  • Self-motivated and comfortable taking care of the ambiguity of V1 and incubation work

Qualifications

What you'll need:
  • Strong technical capability, cross group teamwork and verbal interpersonal skills
  • Ability to work independently and efficiently in a highly motivated team environment
  • To be adaptable and love dealing with ambiguity
  • A Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent experience in Computer Science or a related discipline
Bonus pointss:
  • Proven ability to deliver high-quality end-to-end experiences for developers and consumers
  • Previous experience shipping mobile or pc apps. C++, iOS, Android, Java, Swift, Objective-C, Typescript, React, React Native
  • Previous experience in cloud services and developer platforms. C#, .NET/.NET Core, Visual Studio, REST.
