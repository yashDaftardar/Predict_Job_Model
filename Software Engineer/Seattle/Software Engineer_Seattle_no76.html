This Position:
The Senior Software Engineer on the Commerce Integration team will focus on Platform Engineering, for billing transaction success, from scale to security, all in a dynamic environment with a 24x7x365 critical service and an awesome team. We're looking for a Senior Software Engineer to help us build out the future of the DocuSign platform. You will be focusing on various systems problems we have in the company. The Commerce Integration team works in an accountable-like atmosphere that is self-driven, passionate, and creates quality features. A successful person in this role will have a flexible attitude combined with an aptitude to interact with multi-teams, learn and share. This person enjoys working in a fast-paced agile environment, cares about their decisions, and is focused on quality driven development. This role is key to our fast-paced revenue growth as the Commerce Integration team is focused on the process to upsell and onboard new customers.

This position reports to the Senior Software Engineering Manager.

Responsibilities:
  • Revolutionize DocuSign core services and API's
  • Write high quality code that is easy to maintain and test
  • Think through how systems will scale and fail as they grow
  • Take complicated problems and break them down in to bite sized pieces, then ship every few weeks to get there
  • Demonstrates creative and useful ways of solving problems
and finding solutions using programming languages, best practices and/or tools
  • Raise issues proactively that might impact the business success
  • Sees upcoming unexpected behavior and drives to solutions before they become problems
  • Debug complex issues found in production between multiple systems or services
Basic Qualifications:
  • BA/BS degree or equivalent work experience
  • 5+ years professional experience developing software solutions using object-oriented methodologies and design patterns in a .NET/C# environment (C#, SQL Server, etc.)
  • Demonstrated ability to document technical specifications
  • B.S., M.S., or PhD in Computer Science
Preferred Qualifications:
  • Excellent problem-solving skills
  • Responsible to develop, promote and build consensus around platform architecture for the product and platform teams.
  • Experience building complex, scalable applications
  • Experience with consuming and building RESTful web-services
  • Experience with other OO languages
  • Experience working with remote teams
