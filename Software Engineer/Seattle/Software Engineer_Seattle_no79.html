Full Job Description

TikTok is the leading destination for short-form mobile video. Our mission is to inspire creativity and bring joy. TikTok has global offices including Los Angeles, New York, London, Paris, Berlin, Dubai, Mumbai, Singapore, Jakarta, Seoul and Tokyo.

Content Ecosystem is critical to TikTok's user experience, where people watch short-form videos from the vast content pool. This role reports to our TikTok Frontend team, who build end-to-end solutions to improve quantity, quality, and diversity of TikTok's content pool.
As a backend engineer of Content Ecosystem working on our Frontend team, you will be responsible for building data pipelines and APIs to interact with business critical data. As a backend engineer at TikTok, you will:
1. Work closely with product managers and frontend engineers to deliver key features;
2. Implement service to handle very high QPS, with security in mind;
3. Improve system design and architecture to ensure high stability and performance of the services;
4. Learn and understand how TikTok product works and help build end-to-end solutions;
5. Learn and grow from working with great colleagues and taking on challenges;
6. Help our team grow from your expertise, experience, and perspectives.
Qualifications:
1. BA/BS Degree in Computer Science or related major, 2+ years of industry experience in a software development environment;
2. Strong knowledge of data structures and algorithms, proficient in at least one coding language, including but not limited to Go, Java, Python, C++, Javascript, TypeScript;
3. Curiosity towards new technology and outstanding ability to analyze and solve problems;
4. Good grasp of software engineering and how to build a system in scalable and extensive manner;
5. Understanding of concurrency, database, and message queue is a plus;
6. Experience in the Big Data technologies is a plus.
