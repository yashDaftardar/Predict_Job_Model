Full Job Description

Looking for predominantly local software engineers to join an up and coming blockchain startup based in Santa Monica, CA and Seattle, WA; led by industry leaders in blockchain, entertainment and marketing!

We believe in a few core values. That we are compelled to make a positive impact. That everyone should have equal access to opportunities. That things should be simple. And that things should be done for mutual benefit. Blockchain and AI have created the opportunity to leverage real time data to finally revolutionize word of mouth marketing strategies.

Through the Raiinmaker Application, Content Creators and Brands are able to better reach new fans through influencer network targeting based on decentralized identity, better engage with current fans through the incentive placement on micro social actions and better reward their fans through tokenized digital rewards based on blockchain proof of influence.

Fans build brands and should be rewarded for it.

As a software engineer at Raiinmaker, you will architect, design, and build the Raiinmaker ecosystem. You will span technologies from cloud to blockchain and everything in between. You will look outward as well as inward, aligning our executive team and product strategy with changes in technology, and help us create an innovative culture.
*
What you are going to be doing:

  • Articulate a long term technical direction and vision for maintaining and scaling our web and mobile platform.
  • Create trustworthy user experiences by building interfaces that are simple, easy to comprehend, performant and reliable using modern tools like React, React Native, Typescript, Flutter, Node, and GraphQL.
  • Review all feature designs within your product area and across the company for cross-cutting projects. Be an owner of the security, safety, scale, operational integrity, and architectural clarity of these designs.
  • Debugging extremely difficult technical problems, and making systems and products both work better and are easier to deploy, own, operate and diagnose.

*
*
What we are looking for:

  • You have at least 2 years of experience in software engineering and at least 3 years of experience in developing web apps and shipping user-facing features with JavaScript and modern, component-based JS frameworks like React.
  • You've developed and shipped user-facing features using component-based UI frameworks.
  • You’re familiar with current trends and best practices in front-end architecture, including performance, security and usability.
  • You’re familiar with product and design life cycles, and collaborating closely with designers, engineers, and product managers.
  • You write high quality, well tested code to meet the needs of your customers.
  • Have experience with AWS, Docker, and Kubernetes
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills, and a bias toward open, transparent cultural practices.
  • Excellent skills of mentoring, maturity, dealing with conflict and communication - not just within the team but also across the product group and/or all of engineering.
