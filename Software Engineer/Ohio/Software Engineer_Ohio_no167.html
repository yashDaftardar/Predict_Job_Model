Job Description

This position can be remote, working anywhere in Ohio, as you will need the ability to travel to the Cleveland, OH office for training/meetings.

The Sr. Tech Support Engineer will have systems expertise with IoT products, automation and visualization, as you will join our Innovation Suite team where you will work with leading FactoryTalk & ThingWorx technology across a range of industries.

The Support Engineer may be asked to cover a shift after hours, and will occasionally carry a weekend support phone to provide support for 24x7 customers.

Responsibilities:

  • Provides technical support to field engineers, technicians, and product support personnel who are diagnosing, troubleshooting, repairing and debugging systems

  • Provide technical support for the FactoryTalk Suite of products with a concentration on the IoT products

  • Provide IoT Support Services to Developers, System Administrators, Field Consultants, and Engineers. (In the majority of cases, this support will be provided via telephone)

  • Responds to situations where first-line product support has failed to isolate or fix problems in malfunctioning equipment or software

  • Reports design, reliability and maintenance problems or bugs to design engineering/software engineering

  • Create and publish knowledgebase articles, blogs, or videos for reference by customers and Rockwell Automation employees

  • Support via email, or technical notes

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree

  • Legal authorization to work in the US is required. Rockwell Automation will not sponsor individuals for employment visas, now or in the future, for this job opening

  • Ability to work cover after hour shifts, and occasionally carry a weekend support phone to provide 24x7 support to our customers

Preferred Qualifications:

  • 5+ years of related experience

  • Systems experience with IoT products, automation and visualization

  • Working experience with ThingWorx and FactoryTalk Suite of products with a concentration on the IoT products

  • Experience providing technical support to field engineers, technicians, and product support personnel who are diagnosing, troubleshooting, repairing and debugging systems.

  • Experience responding to situations where first-line product support has failed to isolate or fix problems in malfunctioning equipment or software

  • Experience reporting design, reliability and maintenance problems or bugs to design engineering/software engineering

  • Experience with customer installation and training
