Essential Activities:

  • Understanding of application technical design, data models and data structures.
  • Understands the role of developers within SDLC and project management methodologies.
  • Participate in the task estimation and design sessions.
  • Deliver high quality software that adheres to best practices enabling maintainable and extensible applications.
  • Perform production support or on call support tasks.
  • Participate in root cause analysis of issues related to the application and technology stack.
  • Participate in self-learning in broader technologies and practices relevant to the application domain.
  • Familiar with the change control process and tool sets seeking automation of deployment and testing processes.

  • Possess the communications skills that can be utilized to effectively collaborate with a team.

  • Responsive Design
  • Experience integrating front end with back-end APIs

  • Min 3 years of IT experience
