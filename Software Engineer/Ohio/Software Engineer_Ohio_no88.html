Full Job Description

Job Responsibilities:

  • Works in a team of database developers, software developers, and business analysts. Delivers database analytics and web based applications.
  • Administers existing databases and the analysis, design, and creation of new databases.
  • Performs data modeling, database optimization, understanding and implementation of schemas, and the ability to interpret and write complex code.
  • Proactively monitors systems for optimum performance and capacity constraints.
  • Identifies, diagnoses and resolves database application bottlenecks on production level systems.
  • Performs quality assurance; configures the application in all environments; corrects all defects; completes system integration testing to ensure reasonable performance requirements.
  • Designs, implements and supports ETL processes.
  • Establishes database development standards, documentation and best practices.
  • Guides and coaches developers in tasks and projects.
  • Interacts with business units, project managers and leads to determine application and reporting needs.
  • Performs support, development and administrative activities as required. Provides periodic on-call duty to support department 7x24x365 applications.
  • Other duties as assigned.

Education:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering or related field required.
  • Master’s degree preferred.
  • Additional database development experience may offset the degree requirement.

Certifications:

  • Microsoft Certified Solutions Associate (MCSA) from Microsoft preferred.
  • Teradata Database Certified Associate (TDCA) from Teradata Certified Professional Program (TCPP) preferred.

Complexity of Work:

  • In depth understanding of database and .NET computing environment concepts.
  • Understands principles of client/server configuration.
  • Excellent knowledge of current technologies and project management skills.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills; strong organizational and analytical skills required.

Work Experience:

  • Five years database development (e.g., MS SQL and/or Teradata) or C# and/or ASP.net experience required.
  • Programming experience with an emphasis on coding that scales well and is optimized for use in a high volume environment preferred.
  • Experience in the physical and logical design of database architecture preferred.
  • Experience in Informatica Power Center a plus.
  • Diverse technology background required.

Physical Requirements:

  • Requires manual dexterity to operate computer.
  • Requires frequent computer entry, sitting, standing, and walking.
  • Normal or corrected vision, hearing and speech.

Personal Protective Equipment:

  • Follows standard precautions using personal protective equipment as required.
