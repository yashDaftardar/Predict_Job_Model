Full Job Description

Manages team members through specific team tasks, including system or process maintenance, new project delivery, and production issue resolution. Responsible for the day-to-day activities to ensure the operational availability of applications, including custom build, packaged and SaaS/Cloud bases applications. Works with IT Senior Management to develop department direction and vision. Sets goals to ensure the success of the Team and demonstrates a passion for learning the business, emerging technologies and staff development. Creates and manages the department expense budget. Performs all human resource management responsibilities for direct reports. Provides both team and cross functional leadership while ensuring the quality and timeliness of all team deliverables.

The Software Engineer-Leader may be a peer partner to the QA Leader, Scrum Master and Product Owner.

Essential Activities:

  • Manages team members through department tasks including design, development, and/or configuration of IT systems that meet technical specifications and end-user requirements.
  • Works with IT Senior Management to develop department direction and vision that ensures alignment with business strategy
  • Sets team goals to ensure the success of the department in accordance with milestones developed by IT Senior Management
  • Manages the team expense budget and staffing model
  • Leads and mentors direct reports through career development that matches associate skills/desires with company needs
  • Establishes and maintains key performance indicators for the practice of software engineering. Endorse and motivate team to support and ensure the Safelite Software Development methodology is followed.
  • Leads vendor review and selection process as appropriate
  • Leads sprint planning, story creation and determines assignments
  • Determines resource needs based on team work load and project requirement
  • Other duties assigned by management.

Requirements:

  • 7+ years' experience with management and supervisory practices; human resource practices; safety requirements and procedures; standard office practices
  • Experience with IT architecture and design concepts; software development lifecycle; project management; advanced and systems administration practices and principles; planning, design, and problem resolution
  • Experience with managing others and applying management principles to practical work situations; manage projects and programs
  • Experience with supporting and maintaining package and/or SaaS Cloud applications
  • Experience with application integration and large-scale application implementations
  • Preferred experience with Oracle Cloud ERP and/or Salesforce
  • Experience in managing external IT service providers
  • A technical foundation with the agility to be both a strong technology and business advisor.
