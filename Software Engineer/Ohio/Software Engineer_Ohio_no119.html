Delivery Lead, Manager or Director – Nexient, Ann Arbor, Michigan OR Columbus, Ohio

Nexient is a U.S. based domestic technology services company that designs, develops, and manages software applications and infrastructure for large US enterprises. Our business model is unique in that our services are delivered through remote IT service delivery centers (Ann Arbor, Michigan and Columbus, Ohio), with a contracting model that is largely driven by outcome metrics. Nexient works with some of the biggest and most technologically advanced companies to help them design, develop, implement, test and support technology products, applications and IT Infrastructure.
Nexient has pioneered Onshoring, a uniquely efficient, U.S. based service delivery model. We work with F-500 and next-gen technology companies across their application and data lifecycle in an innovative, agile and collaborative manner. We service customers from centralized delivery centers enabling our employees to build long-term careers with good work-life balance and have the opportunity to work across multiple technologies, projects, and customers.
We have an exciting [full-time, direct hire, onsite] opportunity {with company offered benefits} available as a Delivery Manager or Delivery Director for .Net, JavaScript and/or Java technologies located at our facility in Ann Arbor, Michigan.

A few words about the Requirements of all of our Delivery Lead OR Delivery Manager OR Delivery Director roles:

    • Manage end-to-end successful delivery of software application development projects
    • Manage all aspects of the projects including customer expectations, team expectations and delivery-related escalations
    • Must be able to multitask and manage multiple project teams
    • Responsible for maintaining cost of delivery targets
    • Provide guidance and mentorship to various teams and stakeholders to deliver assigned projects / services as per the agreed scope, time, cost, quality to realize expected business value and benefits
    • Build key talent and intellectual leadership, motivated and high performing team
    • Manage software professionals, coach and develop team members across various levels in the delivery organization
    • Responsible for team's capability development (technical training and certification)
    • Establish processes and procedures and manage performance, identify / progress on development areas of individuals by using appropriate mechanisms
    • Share best practices to enhance the quality and efficiency of Nexient’s service delivery
    • Software development, testing and support
