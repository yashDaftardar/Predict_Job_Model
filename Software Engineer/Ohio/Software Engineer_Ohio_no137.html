Software Engineer- Warehouse Execution Systems
Trew is looking for Software Developers to design and implement warehouse management solutions using our SmartMoves-WX product. This application-based work provides new and varied technical challenges from one project to the next. Application development and testing will take place in the office. Completed work is delivered and tested at the clients' facility.
This job is for you if...
  • Customer satisfaction is your #1 priority
  • Finding unique solutions gets you excited
  • Working on a team keeps you happy
You'll be...
  • Configuring standard software components and creating application software that is used to direct and track product through a distribution or manufacturing facility, manage inventory and order fulfillment
  • Customizing standard products to meet the requirements of each project
  • Meeting with both client and internal project teams to define applications and produce functional specification documents
  • Creating applications for User Interface screen design and reports
  • Writing interfaces to devices such as, workstations, mobile devices, wireless scanners, voice directed and light directed task devices
  • Database development for order fulfillment and inventory management production planning and tracking information
  • Writing application software for interfaces to business systems, ERP systems or other peer computer systems
  • Conducting in-house testing, in-house/internet WMS/WES interface preliminary testing and onsite testing of the system at the customer's facility
  • Providing customer training and support
  • Generating project specific documentation using standard company formats
  • Participating in design reviews with project team
What you'll need...
As a Software Developer, you must be self-motivated and dedicated with a strong sense of initiative and the ability to easily adapt to changing situations. You must also have strong technical, programming and team leadership skills as well as solid organizational, time-management and prioritization skills. It is also important for you to have excellent verbal and written communication, interpersonal and customer service skills with the ability to collaborate effectively with a wide range of people at all levels of expertise.
Specific qualifications for this Software Development position include:
  • BSCS, BSCE degree or equivalent
Experience with the following:
  • C# (.NET) Core
  • MS SQL Server Database
  • Strong troubleshooting skills
  • Excellent client follow-through skills
  • Flexibility to travel around the country, via air, 20% to 40% of the time
