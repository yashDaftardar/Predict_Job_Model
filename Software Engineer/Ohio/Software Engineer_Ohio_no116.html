Full Job Description

Applied Research Solutions is seeking a full-time Junior Computer Engineer or Computer Scientist to support data optimization for a customer at the Air Force Research Laboratory, on Wright Patterson AFB. This is an exciting new position, integrating various research projects.

Responsibilities include :

  • Using Python, develop tools, or extend existing tool code, for data optimization:
  • Using AI/ML techniques, develop tools to automatically tag targets within data sets;
  • Write and present technical reports on development activities;
  • Supervise Junior Data Truthing Analysts; and
  • Other duties as assigned.

Qualifications/ Technical Experience Requirements:

  • BS with a degree in CS, CSE and 3+ years of related experience;
  • Must be able to obtain and maintain a SECRET security clearance;
  • Strong programming/coding skills in LINUX, Python a MUST
  • Able to learn High Performance Computing servers
  • Experience in software development and data structures;
  • Strong written and oral communication skills; and
  • Ability to work unsupervised.

All positions at Applied Research Solutions are subject to background investigations. Employment is contingent upon successful completion of a background investigation including criminal history and identity check.
