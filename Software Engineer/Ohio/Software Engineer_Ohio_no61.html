CAS uses intuitive technology, unparalleled scientific content and unmatched human expertise to help companies create groundbreaking innovations that benefit the world. As the scientific information solutions division of the American Chemical Society, CAS manages the largest curated reservoir of scientific knowledge, and for 114 years, has helped innovators mine, assess and apply that information to keep businesses thriving. The CAS team is global, diverse, endlessly curious and strives to make scientific insights accessible to innovators worldwide.

CAS is currently seeking a Software Engineer. This position will be located in our headquarters in Columbus, Ohio.

Application Technology Solution Delivery creates, builds, enhances, operates, and sustains the CAS suite of online products and services including the Scientific Discovery Platform and Intellectual Property Suite. We have an opening for a Full Stack Software Engineer focusing on enhancing and maintaining Formulus, a key component of the Scientific Discovery Platform. As a member of a dynamic, trend setting, self-organizing team working with a plethora of technologies; individuals have a tremendous opportunity to impact design, development and delivery of features advancing our Scientific Discovery Platform.

Requirements:

  • 3-5 years of application development experience
  • Experience working with Linux
  • Java and Angular language knowledge and experience
  • Source Code Management tools preferably experience with GIT
  • Familiarity with the Software Development Lifecycle
  • Knowledge of Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment
  • Agile Methodologies primarily involving grooming and estimation of work
  • Solid communication skills both verbal and written to convey Technical topics and design
  • Public cloud knowledge preferably experience with AWS

Highly desirable:

  • Full Stack Development experience
  • Pair Development experience
  • Test Driven Development focus
  • DevOps focus with emphasis on sustainability
  • Spark
  • Atlassian tools including JIRA and Confluence
  • Big Data/Hadoop knowledge

CAS offers a competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package, including a generous vacation plan, medical, dental, vision insurance plans, and employee savings and retirement plans. Candidates for this position must be authorized to work in the United States and not require work authorization sponsorship by our company for this position now or in the future.
