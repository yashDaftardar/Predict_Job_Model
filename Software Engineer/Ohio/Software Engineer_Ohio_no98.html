Full Job Description

Responsibilities:

  • Leads IT projects, including planning, obtaining customer input and projecting resource requirements.
  • Reviews system requirements with customers and translates requirements into specific functions or deliverables.
  • Analyzes flow chart processes and recommends ways to reduce steps and increase efficiency through use of technology.
  • Plans, schedules and manages multiple projects requiring computer resources including project scheduling.
  • Utilizes process enhancement opportunities to improve the efficiency of data pulling, reporting, programming, operation and etc.
  • Works with DBA/Developer on standardizing and improving data pulling, cleansing and loading processes.
  • Utilizes SSIS, BIML and Informatica to perform daily ETL tasks on MSSQL, Oracle, Teradata and MySQL databases.
  • Performs data modeling and database optimization.
  • Interprets and writes complex SQL queries, functions and stored procedures.
  • Proactively monitors systems for optimum performance and capacity constraints.
  • Applies development skills to complete tasks.
  • Responsible for extensive T-SQL development, integration testing and other projects that may be necessary to help the team achieve their goals.
  • Performs basic data analysis to include data validation, investigation, and trending analysis.
  • Identifies technical issues and manages resolution for analytics programs.
  • Designs, develops and maintains systems for integrating data between different database systems.
  • Assists with scope and task plan development; provides input to resource requirement development.
  • Fosters innovation and learning through sharing design ideas and mentoring team members.
  • Must be able to effectively and efficiently multitask.
  • Other duties as assigned.

Education:

  • Bachelor's degree in Information Technology/Computer Science, Health Sciences, Healthcare Admin or related field.

Complexity of Work:

  • Understanding of database/distributed computing environment concepts.
  • Understands principles of client/server configuration.
  • Excellent knowledge of messaging technology and project management skills.

Work Experience:

  • 6 years of data analytics, data management, math or statistical modeling or machine learning.
  • Must have well developed object-oriented programming skills in languages such as C# preferred.
  • Highly proficient with advanced SQL preferred.
  • Basic understanding of systems like Hadoop, Hive and/or MapReduce is desired.
  • Must have excellent communication and presentation skills.
  • Strong analytical, administrative, presentation, and project management skills are required.
  • Must have strong communication skills (both written and verbal) and the ability to work independently.
  • Offset: High School Diploma / GED or equivalent and 9 years of experience.
  • Offset: Associates Degree and 7 years of experience.

Physical Requirements:

  • Requires the ability to sit and be stationary for prolonged periods of time, normal or corrected vision and manual dexterity sufficient to perform work on a personal computer.
  • Requires the ability to walk to various locations throughout the Foundation and to function in a stressful environment.

Personal Protective Equipment:

  • Follows standard precautions using personal protective equipment as required.

Keywords: C#, SQL, R, Python, software, programming
