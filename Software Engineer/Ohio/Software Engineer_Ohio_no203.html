Full Job Description

Welcome to the Park Place Technologies Internship Program! We are so excited to have you as part of the team.

Your Success is our Mission during your experience at Park Place Technologies. We are so ready to immerse you in a challenging, collaborative and engaging internship. We have identified your key learning objectives along with internship responsibilities that will help you achieve vital skills for your career in IT. You can expect to gain real life experience, a glimpse into a global mindset, a mentor in IT and training from some of the best of the best in the IT department at Park Place Technologies.

Did I forgot to mention that all your hard work will culminate in an end of year presentation to Management and C-Suite Executives? Don’t worry, we got your back. We’ll prepare you with everything you need during your 12 weeks with us to ensure it goes over smoothly.

LEARNING OBJECTIVES OF INTERNSHIP PROGRAM:

  • Learn the software development lifecycle
  • Execute development tasks in a team-oriented environment
  • Real world application of software design principles in the cloud
  • Understand the foundations of a successful career in IT!

WHAT YOUR DAY TO DAY LOOKS LIKE:

  • Develop software according to requirements and quality standards as well as demonstrate successful completion of tasks within defined timeframes
  • Demonstrate core competency in programming fundamentals and best practices such as SOLID, Object-Oriented design principles, DRY, and Domain Driven Design
  • Learn and adapt to programming patterns and standards
  • Participate in all phases of SDLC including design, development, testing, documentation, and support
  • Collaborate with business analysts to develop solutions that meet specific business objectives
  • Be a strong problem solver and possess an excellent work ethic and attitude
  • Collaborate, share, and learn from team members, and actively follow all team processes
  • Function as an excellent team player that can make significant contributions individually as well as a part of the team
  • Demonstrate willingness to work on your craft and take initiative in learning new skills
  • Effectively communicate both orally and in writing
  • Other duties as assigned

WHAT YOU NEED TO PASS GO:

  • Studying toward a degree in Computer Science or other related field
  • 1+ years of application development experience, education or equivalent skills across some the following technologies:

o .Net Framework/Core

o Angular

o C#

o JSON

o SCSS/SASS


o SQL

o Test Driven Development/Unit Testing

o Typescript, ES6

o Web API / REST
