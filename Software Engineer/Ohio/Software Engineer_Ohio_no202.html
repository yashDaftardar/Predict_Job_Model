Roles & Responsibilities:

    • Responsible for production and architecting of Objective-C/Swift for projects ranging from simple prototypes to complex applications
    • Contribute to a large variety of different types of projects, team sizes and client scales
    • Clearly communicate risk and implementation details to clients and project stakeholders
    • Give and receive engineering mentorship
    • Continuously improve our engineering process and capabilities
    • Experience architecting multiple applications with various architecture patterns (MVVM, MVC, VIPER, etc.)

Skills & Experience:

    • 6+ years of native mobile engineering experience working with iOS and related native mobile frameworks including Swift and UIKit
    • 4+ years of experience with of a variety of iOS frameworks and common build tools
    • Experience with iOS performance optimization best practices
    • Demonstrated knowledge of hardware compatibility and versioning strategy
    • Ability to apply your skillset broadly and expand it to meet client needs
    • Sizable knowledge of available frameworks and ability to quickly pick them up
    • Experience architecting projects that leverage SwiftUI and Combine
    • Participation in the open source community
    • Machine learning, AR/VR, OCR, wearable and/or conversational UI experience
    • A consistent passion for learning and honing your craft
    • Ability to work as a group contributor or independently if needed
    • A keen eye for aesthetics and desire to make things "pixel-perfect"
    • Ability to receive feedback and use it to improve your work
    • Effective verbal and written communication skills
    • Eagerness to learn from and work with others
