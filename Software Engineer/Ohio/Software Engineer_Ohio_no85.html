Full Job Description

Job Summary
Are you looking to join a team where innovation isn’t just a buzz word, but encouraged and rewarded? Do you want to make a difference and have a direct impact on the design and development of next generation technology and products? If your answer is yes, then VTI Instruments needs you on our team!

VTI Instruments is a leading supplier of instrumentation used for the world’s most demanding test applications, where a sustained focus on innovation and technology enables us to deliver solutions with unparalleled measurement integrity and data reliability. Our products are used to analyze the performance of critical components and systems in the aerospace and defense, national security, energy and power generation, transportation and infrastructure, and commercial electronics industries.

The Senior Engineer, Software is responsible for designing, developing, documenting and testing Object Oriented Software for VTI product line. As a part of team, you will be an integral part of a multidisciplinary engineering group focusing on next generation new product development and design.

Roles & Responsibilities
This position has responsibility for:
  • Design, document, develop, and test Object Oriented Software
  • Work closely with hardware engineers for end-to-end integration of products
  • Analyze, design and develop tests and test-automation suites
  • Continuous integration using tools such as Jenkins
  • Participate in design reviews, software process development activities

Competency Requirements
In order to perform the job successfully, an individual should demonstrate the following competencies:
  • Working with ambiguity - Achieves forward progress in the face of poorly defined situations and/or unclear goals; able to work effectively with limited or partial information
  • Displaying technical expertise - Keeps his/her technical skills current; effectively applies specialized knowledge and skills to perform work tasks; understands and masters the technical skills, knowledge, and tasks associated with his/her job; shares technical expertise with others
  • Critical thinking - Skilled at finding logical flaws in arguments and plans; identifies problems and solutions that others might miss; provides detailed insight and constructive criticism into problems and complex situations
  • Drive for results - Aggressively pursues challenging goals and objectives; will to put in considerable time and effort to accomplish objectives; takes a highly focused, goal driven approach toward work
  • Ability to work on cross-functional teams
Position Requirements:
Minimum Qualifications (Experience and Skills)
  • 3+ years software programming experience in structured Object Oriented languages
  • Trained in structured software design, documentation, coding standards and source code / configuration management.
  • Strong knowledge of OO languages such as C++, .NET, Python
  • GUI development using tools such as JavaScript & HTML5
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills

Preferred Qualifications
  • Experience with writing host drivers for instrumentation products
  • Working knowledge of network programming

Education Requirements
  • Minimum BS in Computer Science/Engineering or Electrical Engineering
