Full Job Description

Overview:
Credence Management Solutions, LLC, is hiring a Software Developer who will be primarily responsible for supporting AFLCMC/XA at WPAFB, OH. This position is currently not on contract.
Responsibilities:
  • Assist the Government with software sustainment.
  • Advise and assist in developing software data analysis and management tools.
  • Advise and assist in delivering zero defect software products for final assessment and acceptance in systems integration.
  • Advise and assist the Government in providing support for modifications to simulation programs, system integration interface programs, and continuous monitoring of overall software suitability.
  • Advise and assist the Government in building software for integration of specific weapon parameters.
  • Implement code and fix known software errors and bugs to meet approved objectives.
  • Suggest and implement new features, refinements, and tests.
  • Create, run, and refine tests and shall analyze test results, investigate problem areas, and suggest solutions.
  • Write descriptions of known software issues and proposed solutions.
  • Advise and assist in the use of data analysis tools to merge and or break apart segments of collected real and simulated data.
  • Advise and assist in utilizing data to compare and verify functionality, validity, sequencing, and structure of data for application in total systems software integration.
  • Assist with change review board meetings and archiving results.
  • Research topics of interest and gather related problems of interest and proposed solutions.
  • Create and refine required documentation.
Qualifications:
  • Clearance: Secret
  • BA/BS degree and three (3) years of relevant experience or
  • Five (5) years of directly related experience
