Full Job Description

Updox, an EverCommerce company, is seeking a Software Engineer to join our team. We believe that high quality, innovative software is built by craftsmen in a collaborative workshop environment, rather than by workers on an assembly line. Our team values passion and dedication over drudgery and busywork. To us, crafting software is a way of life, not just a job.

You:

You have a passion for breaking new ground every day, while delivering cutting edge software solutions, and are driven to continuously improve your craft while striving for perfection each and every day. You are excited by continuously learning new languages and technologies. You are looking for a chance to learn and leverage Agile practices, work on various layers of the architecture stack, build awesome web and mobile apps, and learn to love the safety of a well written automated test.

A Software Engineer is responsible for participating in many aspects of the Updox software development lifecycle for both new application development and existing system maintenance, including:

  • Requirements Estimation

  • Software Development

  • Software Testing

  • Software Deployment

  • Software Demonstrations

  • Systems Documentation

  • Daily Stand Ups

  • Retrospectives

Us:

Updox, an EverCommerce company, provides next-generation virtual care, patient engagement and office productivity solutions that enable practices to reduce costs and drive revenue. Based on increasing demand for solutions that seamlessly work together to improve practice efficiency and provide an engaging patient experience, Updox has continuously brought new functionality to market. We are committed to cultivating an environment that is diverse and inclusive—with equal opportunities for everyone. From employee recruitment to personal development, we are building a team that encourages all who step into our office, to be their authentic selves.

Where:

Updox is located in the heart of Dublin, Ohio at Bridge Park just above Pins and 16-bit Bar and Arcade. Our collaborative, open-plan office space is filled with lots of sunshine and a talented team who are passionate about what we do and enjoy taking care of our customers. This role can be based anywhere in the United States. You must be eligible to work in the United States without sponsorship in order to qualify for this role.

Experience and skills we are seeking:

  • 2+ years of experience with Java, C# or Ruby

  • 2+ years of experience with Javascript, HTML5 and CSS

  • 1+ years of experience with Angularjs (preferable), Ember.js or similar framework

  • Proficiency with Git

  • Working knowledge of Object Oriented development and Object modeling

  • Working knowledge of SQL and Object to Relational Mapping frameworks

  • Strong communication skills with the ability to adjust communication to fit the audience

  • Test driven development experience is highly desirable

  • Demonstrates understanding of algorithms and how/when to apply them

  • Propensity to overcome challenges and solve “hard” problems

  • Internal drive for continuous improvement (knowledge, standards, best practices)

  • Relentless desire to deliver, say what you’ll do and do what you say

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s Degree in Computer Science is preferred or equivalent experience

  • Experience with asynchronous development and RabbitMQ is preferred

  • Experience using MySQL server is preferred

  • React or Vue.js experience is preferred

Benefits and Perks:

  • Competitive salary with the opportunity to work remotely

  • Continued investment in your professional development

  • Robust medical, dental, and vision benefits

  • 401k with match

  • Gym stipend

  • Start with 17 days of PTO, 8 standard holidays and 2 floating holidays, and a day off to volunteer each year
