Full Job Description

Radiance Technologies is an employee-owned company with benefits that are unmatched by most companies in the Dayton OH area. Employee ownership, generous 401K, full health/dental/life/vision insurance benefits, paid time off, educational reimbursement, competitive salaries, interesting assignments and a pleasant work environment combine to make Radiance Technologies a great place to work and succeed. In fact, Radiance has been named by the Dayton Business Journal as one of the best places to work in Dayton for the past 4 years!


The Software Engineer will support the National Air and Space Intelligence Center (NASIC) by performing software activities focused on developing analytical tools for intelligence analysts. Successful candidates will perform software development on a collaborative Agile Scrum development team and will have strong problem solving and critical thinking skills. Work location may be at NASIC facilities, the Radiance offices, or a combination of both. Flexibility and the ability to work on a wide range of development platforms and software is a must.


Job Responsibilities:

  • Design and implement new capabilities
  • Resolve issues found in existing code
  • Integrate capabilities with external services and tools


Required Skills
  • Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science or relevant technical field
  • Exceptional problem-solving skills
  • Proven skill in conceptualizing, designing and implementing features based upon user acceptance criteria
  • Fluid polyglot programming skills
  • Ability to communicate technical details of work to individuals with varied levels of technical knowledge
  • Work with a diverse team in a dynamic environment taking ownership for segments of a project
  • Freely collaborate and give away knowledge


Required Experience
  • Minimum 2 years of full-stack application development experience
  • US Citizenship with the ability to obtain and maintain a clearance
  • Experience with using technologies such as Qt Modeling Language (QML) for building user interfaces
  • Competent knowledge of C++ through version 17
  • Experience utilizing RESTful web services
  • Experience with version control software. Git experience is preferred.
  • Experience with bug/issue tracking software such as JIRA

Desired Qualifications:

  • An exceptional candidate would have experience with Graduated Cloud Native Computing Foundation (CNCF) technologies
  • Experience with server-side languages, such as JavaScript and Python as well as creating, versioning and maintaining RESTful web services
  • A Docker enthusiast is highly preferred
  • An understanding of Elasticsearch is desirable
  • An attractive candidate will have a knowledge of distributed application development
  • Familiarity with container DevSecOps using tools such as Jenkins, Nexus and Terraform
  • Understanding of effective User Experience (UX) design
  • Familiarity with the application of Design Patterns
  • Competency in vector coordinate transformations and related mathematics
  • Interest in space-related topics
  • Unswerving desire to support the American warfighter
