Full Job Description

Summary/Objective

We are a high performing, technology forward team, focused on solving engaging problems and delivering value and efficiencies to our customers’ supply chain. This role will be a key member of the development team and will serve as a core contributor to our customer portal and related business systems. This position will be responsible for developing and maintaining web applications as well as contributing to various team initiatives.

Strategic Roles

  • Implement solutions that are efficient, effective and coherent to the existing architecture and design
  • Work within a pre-existing code base to maintain and develop new features in existing applications
  • Develop solutions in Java following design patterns and paradigms of existing projects
  • Implement scalable object-oriented designs
  • Develop and maintain Web Services (REST, SOAP, etc...)
  • Develop responsive web UIs with a strong sense of design and consistency
  • Work and communicate effectively in a fast paced, demand driven environment
  • Promote and practice of company values (Trust, Team & Opportunities) within team and company

Requirements

  • Degree in Computer Science (Related Field) or Equivalent Professional Experience
  • 2+ years of experience writing applications using Java, Typescript, JavaScript
  • Experience developing web applications with Spring Boot, Angular
  • Experience implementing and integrating to web services (i.e. REST/SOAP)
  • Strong background in SQL Development on MSSQL, MySql or other relational database platforms
  • Experience using Git or other version control systems

Nice to Have

  • Logistics and transportation experience
  • Experience in writing technical documentation
  • Experience with Golang
  • Agile/Scrum experience

Other Information

  • Full time position
  • 40 hour work week with flexible schedule (Monday – Friday only)
  • Casual Dress Code
  • Health, dental and supplementary insurance available on first day of the month after completing 60 calendar days of service.
  • 401K enrollment with company match. Available on the first day of the month after completing 90 calendar days of service.

Physical Demands

  • While performing the duties of this job, the employee is required to talk or hear. The employee frequently is required to stand, sit, walk, use hands to handle or feel as well as reach with hands and arms. Specific vision abilities required include close vision, distance vision, color vision, peripheral vision, depth perception and ability to adjust focus.
  • This role routinely uses standard office equipment such as computers, phones, photocopiers and filing cabinets.

Travel

  • Position is primarily at the Hamilton, OH office location. If business demands any travel, Management will review with the employee in advance.
