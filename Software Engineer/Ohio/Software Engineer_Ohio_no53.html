A Day in The Life Typically Includes:

  • Design, develop, and document applications, perform bug corrections release testing, and beta support which may require research and analysis.
  • Perform design, implementation and maintenance of single product modules/sub-systems or on lower level issues of multiple products.
  • Exercise judgment within generally defined procedures, practices and policies in selecting methods and techniques for obtaining solutions.
  • Review functional business requirements with business consultants.
  • Prepare technical documentation for product modules/sub-systems.
  • Prepare and conduct the unit testing and integration testing for functionality and limits.
  • Perform complex bug verification, release testing and beta support for assigned products.
