Role Summary/Purpose:

The Salesforce.com Engineer will help execute projects in support of our Salesforce Effectiveness team using the Salesforce.com CRM system. This individual will also work to drive continuous improvement of the Synchrony Salesforce.com environment.

Essential Responsibilities:

  • Partners heavily with Sales organization and IT Solution Delivery for strategy definition and implementation

  • Interacts with business users to gather and validate requirements and develop detailed specifications and designs for new architecture

  • Interacts with business users to gather and validate requirements and develop detailed specifications and designs for new architecture.

  • Works with an off-shore team for new application development and life cycle maintenance.

  • Leads architecture design and code reviews.

  • Leads systems, integration and user acceptance testing, and roll-out of new applications.

  • Perform other duties and/or special projects as assigned

Qualifications/Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree and or equivalent work experience with a focus on computer science or software engineering or equivalent

Desired Characteristics:

  • SFDC admin and developer certifications

  • Experience with other cloud platforms such as Pardot, Seismic, Birst, Tableau, crmFusion, SAML authentication, ETL

  • Experience with Mobility AirWatch MDM, Apple iOS, Bluetooth

  • Drive continuous improvement and lessons learned from prior initiatives

  • Able to understand and influence customers' needs

  • Strong analytical and technical skillset used to drive requirements, testing and production validation

  • Strong ability to liaison between Synchrony technology teams, customer technology teams, and Synchrony sales teams.

  • Able to link business strategy with IT technology initiatives
