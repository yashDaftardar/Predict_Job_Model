Associate Software Engineer
Combining a suite of software-enabled services with industry best practices, PartsSource empowers customers to drive costs out of healthcare supply chain delivery. By automating the highly complex workflow associated with procurement, PartsSource delivers the industry’s only online, fully-integrated medical products parts and repair services marketplace. PartsSource facilitates millions of transactions annually by connecting 6,000 OEMs and suppliers to over 3,500 hospitals and 15,000 clinical sites worldwide.

Founded in 2001, rapidly growing, and profitable, PartsSource continues to expand its marketplace of procurement and service solutions to ensure Healthcare is always on. PartsSource is answering the call for healthcare’s strong demand for game-changing eCommerce technology.
Position Summary
Develops effective information technology solutions for PartsSource by creating new, or enhancing existing internal or customer applications. Applies fundamental concepts of software programming and application development methodology in a team environment. Determines programming solutions from analyzing business and functional requirements and technical specifications.
Job Duties
  • Translate application storyboards and use cases into functional applications.
  • Design, build, and maintain efficient, reusable, and reliable code while ensuring the best possible performance, quality, and responsiveness of applications
  • Executes test procedures according to test plan and identifies problems; performs unit and integration testing for functionality and limits; performs bug verification, release testing, and support for assigned products. Writes module-level implementation and test specifications; tracks key activities.
  • Performs software product and hardware evaluation, including planning, installation, testing, and technical support for the applications.
  • Performs other duties of a similar nature or level to support the tasks assigned to the department.
Position Requirements
  • High school diploma or equivalent.
  • BS or BA in computer science or related technical field is preferred, or a corresponding number of years experience in Information Systems or Application Development.
  • 1-3 years experience with software development.
  • Strong problem solving and analytical skills.
  • Ability to understand, and relate to other members of the organization.
  • Ability to understand technical manuals, software specifications, hardware principals of operations, and systems software operations.
  • Ability to communicate ideas and problem solutions.
Skills Needed
Technologies –
Experience and knowledge of React, MVC, ASP.Net, caching (Redis), full stack JavaScript development. Skill sets include C#, JavaScript, ASP.Net, HTML / HTML5 / CSS and LINQ. Must be able to write code that is reusable and extendible. Experience with SOA. Experience with SQL (MSSQL, DB2) and NoSQL database technologies (RavenDB).

Experience with SPAs, .net core, ReactJS.
Experience with security standards and practices: OAuth, certificates, digital signatures desired, not required

Software Development Lifecycle – Experience with Agile, Scrum, or similar development approaches. Experience writing unit tests and testable code. Experience with software source control systems (SVN, Git). Must enjoy working in a collaborative team environment.
