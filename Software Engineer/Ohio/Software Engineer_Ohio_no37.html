Overview:

Piada Italian Street Food is leading the nation’s fine fast casual movement behind an intense passion for exceptionally better food and genuine hospitality.

The Junior Software Engineer is responsible for developing and maintaining Piada’s company sites, internal analytics dashboards, and point-of-sale systems with the goal of enhancing our technical infrastructure to better serve our Guests and Teams. This role is also responsible for maintaining menu management systems, managing third-party applications, assisting stores with point-of-sale issues, and partnering with internal teams to use technology to solve business problems. Our Home Office is a lean organization that needs a candidate who can also step out of their assigned duties to assist as opportunities and challenges arise, which are not always development related. The ideal candidate for this role will embody and demonstrate Piada’s core values, have a passion for development, and demonstrate the ability to collaborate with various cross-functional teams within the organization.

In this position, you will expand your technology knowledge and apply it to practical business requirements. The tech stack we employ at Piada utilizes many different programming languages and platforms, which you will learn to develop against. The technology we employ at Piada is well beyond our peers, and we seek to find an individual who will drive our efforts to remain at the forefront of the industry.
Responsibilities:

  • Responsible for the development and maintenance of mypiada.com, including updates to online ordering, and of our internal analytics dashboard
  • Collaborates with Marketing to create new content on mypiada.com
  • Collaborates with other departments to develop solutions to business problems, including creating integrations between existing platforms
  • Manages user accounts in cloud-based software
  • Maintains, updates, and creates menu items as menu changes
  • Supports stores with point-of-sale issues
  • Provides timelines and roadmaps for development projects and technology initiatives
  • Other duties as assigned, as opportunities and challenges arise

Knowledge, Skills & Abilities:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, CS, Computer Science and Engineering, CSE, Software Engineering, or equivalent technical certification
  • 2 years of experience in full-stack development, with preferences for:
  • Ruby on Rails
  • JavaScript
  • HTML/CSS
  • C#
  • Linux server maintenance
  • Understanding of user-experience theories and concepts
  • Ability to take business requirements and translate them into technology solutions
  • Strong written, verbal, and interpersonal communication skills
  • Ability to prioritize tasks
  • High attention to detail
  • Night and weekend availability to assist stores with menu availability and troubleshooting, menu launches, and software deployments

The Piada Group is an Equal Opportunity Employer.
