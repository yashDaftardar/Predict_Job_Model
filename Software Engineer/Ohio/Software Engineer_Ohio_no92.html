What does a great Software Development Engineer (.Net) do?

Under minimal supervision, support multiple systems in a 24x7 Operational environment. Participate in projects and releases to improve the UI and support application upgrades. Responsible for bug verification, release testing, and support projects which may require research and analysis. Participate in an Agile Delivery Team environment as a senior developer.

As a Software Development Engineer (.Net), you can look forward to :

  • For the assigned applications, possess an in-depth understanding of how they are used within the call center environment. Provide application solutions given the user’s objective.
  • Work with Delivery Team including Product Management to implement changes and enhancements to existing applications.
  • Make recommendations and changes to improve, support and consolidate applications, products and strategies.
  • Review application issues identified and communicated by customers through established process and determine proper course of action to resolve. (fix, delegate, escalate). Includes rotating 24/7 on call responsibilities.

You are someone who:

  • Work in a rapidly growing and changing team environment
  • Complete assignments with minimal supervision
  • Have a good technical background with desire and ability to learn new skill sets as identified
  • Ability to analyze and identify root cause of a problem and determine accurate course of action for resolution.
  • Effectively communicate with internal and external clients
  • Effectively communicate to upward management
  • Efficiently drive projects and tasks to closure, on time, and with quality
  • Meet internal customer expectations by providing documentation, communication and/or presentations to customers as required
  • Deliver accurate and timely information within the established corporate and department performance metrics
  • Work multiple projects concurrently within established timeframes
  • Possess an in-depth knowledge in software development methodologies, design and implementation – specifically Agile.
  • Have solid skills in research and analysis, project planning, implementation and communication

The above statements are intended only to describe the general nature of the job and should not be construed as an all-inclusive list of position responsibilities.

Required Qualifications for Consideration:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology
  • 5+ years’ experience in analysis, business intelligence, technical/application support or related field of study
  • Experience in C#, ASP.Net, Bootstrap, jQuery, .Net Framework 4.6 and higher, IIS 8 and higher, Visual Studio 2017 and TFS.
  • Experience with .Net Core, Rest API, WCF, NuGet and any automated unit testing tools
  • Experience with Microsoft (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Visio)
  • Highly Analytical with strong organizational and decision-making skills
  • Flexibility to work after hours/on-call/weekends as needed basis
  • Travel – up to 10%

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Familiar with Customer Care environment and call center tools
  • Experience with MVC (5 and higher), Angular and Octopus
