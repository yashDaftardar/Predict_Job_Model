Full Job Description

We believe that getting work done is fulfilling to humans. There is a drive in every person to do excellent, impactful, meaningful work. Cloverleaf's mission is to unleash people to do their best work. We bring our full selves to work each day and engage relentlessly to deliver a best-in-class product in pursuit of that mission. Each one of our team members is hugely influential to our ability to accomplish our mission, as well as in the culture and growth of our company.

What are you like?

You're a driven, curious, and detail-oriented engineer comfortable solving complex problems. You view software engineering as a creative problem solving craft and consistently deliver quality solutions under tight constraints. Fully believing teams go farther together, you seek to share your expertise as well as to learn from your teammates.

What is the role like?

You'll join the small team owning our core APIs—both REST and GraphQL—and underlying logic engine and data systems that power our primary application and third-party integrations. You will have wide-ranging responsibilities building and launching new functionality across our backend platform. Maintainability, security, and observability are key as we support nearly 1mm users and Fortune 100 enterprise customers, and you will have the ability to shape team best practices as we scale.

Requirements

  • 2-5 years of professional experience working on a software development team
  • Highly proficient with at least one server-side language. We use Node.js
  • Knowledge of web application design and micro-service architecture
  • Highly self-motivated, takes initiative
  • Experience building future-proof APIs
  • Understanding of data modeling and SQL query performance
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Knack for writing high-impact unit tests

Bonus

  • Experience working in an early-stage startup environment
  • Experience with AWS
  • Experience in TypeScript, Python, Go, or Java
