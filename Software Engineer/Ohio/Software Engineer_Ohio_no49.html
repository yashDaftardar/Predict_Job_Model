Job Description

Radiance Technologies is an employee-owned company with benefits that are unmatched by most companies in the Dayton OH area. Employee ownership, generous 401K, full health/dental/life/vision insurance benefits, paid time off, educational reimbursement, competitive salaries, interesting assignments and a pleasant work environment combine to make Radiance Technologies a great place to work and succeed. In fact, Radiance has been named by the Dayton Business Journal as one of the Best Places to Work in Dayton for the past 3 years!


The C++ Software Engineer will support the National Air and Space Intelligence Center (NASIC) by performing software activities focused on developing analytical tools for intelligence analysts. Successful candidates will perform software development and work with our customers to define requirements. They will work on a collaborative Agile development team and will have strong problem solving and critical thinking skills. Work location may be at NASIC facilities, the Radiance offices, or a combination of both. Flexibility and the ability to work on a wide range of development platforms and software is a must.


Successful candidates will have the following:

  • Great attitude with high degree of customer focus
  • Ability to work with other high-performing team members
  • Flexible and adaptable to changing priorities
  • Team player, supportive and cooperative with coworkers
  • Reliable and committed to getting the job done
  • Self-managed; able to work independently or with a team as required

Job Responsibilities:

  • Design and implement new capabilities utilizing C++ and Qt
  • Integrate capabilities with external services and tools
  • Installation of software at the NASIC facility


Required Skills
  • Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science or relevant technical field
  • Minimum 3 years of development experience
  • Active DoD Secret Security Clearance


Required Experience
  • Experience with Git or other similar version control software
  • Fluent in the C++ programming language (C++ v17 is a plus, Qt/Qt Quick/QML is a plus)
  • Experience with Python
  • Development Experience with Microsoft Visual Studio
  • Experience with object-oriented programming
  • Ability to communicate technical details of work
  • Ability to work in a dynamic environment, self-direct, and take ownership for segments of a project
