Senior Medical Device Software Engineers

CMD MedTech
Columbus, OH 43204

Posted on March 8, 2021

THE FOLLOWING REQUIREMENTS MUST BE MET TO BE CONSIDERED FOR THIS POSITION:

  • BS in Electrical, Software, or Computer Science Engineering w/ minimum of 6 yrs. experience in an interdisciplinary product development environment or a Masters Degree in Electrical, Software, or Computer Science Engineering w/ minimum of 4 years of experience in an interdisciplinary product development environment.
  • A minimum of 3 years of software and/or firmware development for medical devices under a medical device quality system, including the application of IEC 62304. This experience should include:
    • Software development planning and estimation
    • Software architecture and safety segmentation
    • Software requirements definition
    • Unit, integration, and system-level testing
    • Automated tools for testing and integration
    • Development using Agile methodologies


THE FOLLOWING IS DESIRED, BUT NOT REQUIRED TO BE CONSIDERED FOR THIS POSITION:

  • Experience with safety risk management (e.g., ISO 14971)
  • Experience with complex electromechanical medical devices, including robotics
  • Experience with clinical chemistry analyzers
  • Experience with implementing the software-related requirements of IEC 60601-1
  • Experience with Amazon Web Services and cloud-based software systems
  • US Citizenship or Permanent Resident
