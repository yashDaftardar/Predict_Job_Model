Full Job Description

Exodus Integrity Services, Inc is a rapidly expanding technology company headquartered in Northeast Ohio. EIS provides quality services to our clients by instilling honesty, commitment, and hard work to find the most qualified candidates to fill each opportunity. Currently, we are seeking individuals to fill a DevOps Engineer opportunity for our client in Cleveland, OH. This is a very exciting opportunity working with one of the top employers in the area. If you are interested in joining a vibrant organization where you are valued and rewarded for your contributions, and you possess the qualifications listed below, please forward your resume and salary requirements.

This opportunity is open to 100% remote at this time and onsite in either Cleveland, Ohio or Colorado Springs, CO after Covid!

The ideal candidate will have at least 6+ years of related work experience.

Required Skills:
  • Working knowledge of Splunk Products (Splunk Cloud and other Splunk Premium Applications)
  • Solid understanding of Infrastructure as Code (IAC) (Be able to write Terraform & Git)
  • Experience using AWS Cloud Services (EC2, DynamoDB, API Gateway, RDS, Lambda, CloudFront, CloudFormation, CloudWatch, Route 53, etc.), Azure and Google Cloud Platforms is also preferred.
  • Knowledge of Agile/SCRUM and DevSecOps methodologies
  • In-depth knowledge of automated deployment pipeline methodologies and tools
  • Fluency in version control and branching methodologies using various tools
  • Ability to quickly learn, adapt, and integrate emerging technologies
  • Advanced technical troubleshooting, diagnosing and problem-solving skills
  • In-depth knowledge of IT operations, such as operating systems components, utilities, production schedulers
  • Experience deploying and managing operating systems like Windows and various Linux distributions
  • Experience with container and server-less compute
  • Knowledge of various corporate security rules, legal and regulatory obligations such as PCI, SOX or similar standards
  • Ability to coach and help others gain knowledge or skills in a specific area
  • Listening, verbal and written communications skills
  • Interpersonal skills with the ability to work with various levels of management and external vendors

Preferred Skills:
  • Working knowledge of Cloud Solutions and experience with Docker, OpenShift and Kubernetes
  • Experience with both Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) and development using Python
  • Exposure to CI/CD tool chains, such as Azure and GitHub
  • Scripting and general software development knowledge

No 3rd parties at this time.
