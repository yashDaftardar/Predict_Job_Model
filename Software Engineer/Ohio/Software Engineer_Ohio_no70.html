Who we are:

The Ohio Department of Natural Resources (ODNR) is committed to its mission “To ensure a balance between wise use and protection of our natural resources for the benefit of all.” Our agency embraces the insightful use of our natural resources to assure the continued safety, happiness and prosperity of Ohio’s people.

ODNR owns and manages state parks, state forests, state nature preserves and wildlife areas. We license all hunting, fishing and watercraft; oversee and permit all mineral extraction, monitor dam safety, manage water resources and serve as the second largest Law Enforcement presence in the State of Ohio.

We are accepting applications for a Software Development Specialist 2 within the Office of Information Technology , headquartered at Fountain Square, assigned to Franklin County. The address is 2045 Morse Road, Columbus, OH 43229. To learn more about the Office of Information Technology , please visit the Department of Natural Resources .

What you will do…

  • The Primary Technology for this position is: GIS, Python (ArcPy)
  • Develops software applications & performs analysis & design
  • Performs post production support for business applications which may include routine maintenance, enhancements & need to perform research to aid in troubleshooting software production errors
  • Supports, maintains and develops customized applications that utilize (i.e., Python, html, ArcGIS JavaScript API, Dojo, .NET API, Esri ArcObjects and JQuery, MS SQL Server 2016, ArcGIS Online Arcade and Esri’s ArcGIS App Studio Qt)
  • Writes scripts & schedules jobs (Python and .NET API) to execute programs
  • Supports Esri GIS desktop & server applications
