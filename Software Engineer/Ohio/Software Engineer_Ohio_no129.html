Software Developer


Join Insight’s Digital Innovation team as a Software Developer and build solutions utilizing the most cutting-edge technology in enterprise, mobile, AR/VR applications! On our team, you will work closely with client stakeholders and an award-winning team of engineers, architects, and thought leaders to design and implement next-generation solutions. We know that our success is due to our smart, talented team! As a full-time, permanent team member with a competitive benefits plan, you’ll be inspired to grow and prosper in a supportive environment where you can be creative, develop professionally, and stay on the forefront of technology.

What our Software Developers Do

  • Design and develop cutting-edge enterprise web applications and cloud-based solutions in a fast-paced environment
  • Lead discussions with client leadership and work with a team of intelligent technology professionals to recommend application solutions with cloud-hosted platforms and/or a database backends
  • Utilize a wide array of modern codebase and cloud services to build solutions that are slated for continuous improvements in functionality, infrastructure, and operations as the solutions evolve
  • Encourage innovation and software engineering best practices internally and externally
  • Aggressively grow your skillset and expertise to meet emerging needs


What We Look For

  • 5+ years of practical, hands-on development experience in modern technologies including
    • Object-oriented design, implementation, and best practices, with preferred experience in the .NET platform (.NET Core, ASP.NET, C#, VB.NET)
    • Database technologies (SQL Server, Oracle)
    • Automated unit testing and test-driven development
    • Team Foundation Server (TFS) / Visual Studio Team System (VSTS) exposure
  • Knowledge of cloud foundation principles and standards; hands-on experience working with Microsoft Azure products & solutions is ideal
  • Exposure to mobile development preferred
  • Eagerness to learn new tools and technologies, and passion to deliver quality solutions both individually and as part of a team
  • Strong communication skills with both technical and non-technical stakeholders
