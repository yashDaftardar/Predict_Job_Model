Description:

Smyth Automotive is a privately-held automotive aftermarket company, owned and operated by the Smyth family since it was founded by George Smyth over 50 years ago. Smyth Automotive has grown and now owns and operates 18 auto part retail locations, 8 paint centers and 5 warehouse distribution centers serving Greater Cincinnati, Dayton, Columbus, Northern and Central Kentucky, Nashville Tennessee, and Indianapolis Indiana.

We are looking for an entry level programmer to be trained to develop and maintain our various information systems. This is an ideal position for recent graduates.

. Requirements:
  • Develop, create and modify general computer application software and specialized programs
  • Analyze user needs and develop appropriate solutions
  • Design or customize software to optimize operational efficiency
  • Improve existing systems by analyzing areas of modification
  • Analyze and design databases
  • Maintain systems by monitoring, identifying, and correcting software defects
  • Create technical specifications and test plans
  • Assist with Tier 1 Support as needed
  • Other duties may be added and/or assigned as needed

Essential Experience:

  • Training on how to write computer programs using C++, C#, Java and current web-based programming languages
  • General understanding of how to design, develop and maintain databases using SQL/Server and MySQL
  • Knowledge of business/system analysis and design techniques
  • Exposure on using platforms such as Linux, .NET and Windows
  • How to develop and maintain FTP data feeds
  • Technically competent, possess pride in ownership and a good work ethic
  • Strong desire to learn, possess good interpersonal skills and decision making/critical thinking skills
  • Be dynamic and adaptable to change. Strong customer service and troubleshooting skills
  • Ability to communicate technical information, both verbal and written, to a wide range of end-users

Helpful Experience:

  • .NET Platform
  • MultiValue Databases
  • D3 PICK basis-D3 database management language
  • PROC Processor
  • BASIC/FlashBASIC Debugger
  • BASIC programming language
  • GUI design and development

Preferred Experience:

  • Preference will be given to candidates with training, experience or certification in Information Technology or Computer Programming
