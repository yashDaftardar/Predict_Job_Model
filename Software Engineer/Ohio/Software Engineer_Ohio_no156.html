Summary & Key Responsibilities

The Software engineer will be a part of a Digital Product team working on new, emerging software products that are being built for various business needs across State Auto. The candidate must have a deep understanding of software engineering concepts and be able to develop & solutionize various Business objectives in the form of Digital products across the full stack. Understands and works through the needs/improvise the usability of all digital products.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Work as a software engineer writing code and collaborate closely with other engineers on the product team.

  • Prior exposure to SpringBoot, APIs using REST Web Services is preferable

  • Engage in and improve the whole lifecycle of Digital product engineering â€”from inception and design through deployment, operation and refinement

  • Build the framework and libraries that supports authentication, agent & insured user experience using cutting-edge tech stack across digital products

  • Support services before they go live through activities such as system design, developing software frameworks, hardware capacity planning

  • Deep expertise with TDD principles and ability to pair program with other software engineers

  • Constantly refine and monitor production and improve overall usability of the digital product

  • Maintain services once they the product is live by measuring and monitoring availability, latency and overall digital asset health

  • Provide recommendations and adapt to change in needs by collaborating with Digital Capability Lead

  • Scale systems sustainably through mechanisms like automation, and evolve systems by pushing for changes that improve reliability and velocity

  • Adapt DevOps, CI/CD concepts and fail fast as needed; Improve overall Quality of the digital product by infusing various quality checks across software engineering life cycle

  • Develop and reuse frameworks for developing strong Unit test bed across the Digital products

  • Practice sustainable incident response and root cause analysis, should there be any issues in production

  • Adapt repeatable & evolving design principles so as to help guide the peers across product teams

  • Understanding of application, data and infrastructure architecture discipline
