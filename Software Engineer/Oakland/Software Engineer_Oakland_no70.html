Job Description


Your Impact


  • Design, build, and optimize Kubernetes-hosted web applications for maximum speed and scale, using a number of backend storage technologies, including Postgres, Elasticsearch, and S3

  • Ensure your team delivers high output and mentor other engineers.

  • Continuously seek ways to make a substantial impact on your team and the broader engineering organization.

  • Help drive projects from requirements to complete design and implementation.

  • Anticipate challenges of architecting at scale, align architecture vision with strategic initiatives.


Qualifications


Your Experience


  • Have 2+ years of experience as a professional software engineer building customer-facing software for a SaaS business

  • Have experience in providing technical leadership of teams building enterprise features on a multi-tenant SaaS platform at scale (examples: role-based access, SAML integrations, feature flag frameworks)

  • Are excited to dig in, prototype, build, test, and see projects through to production; results driven, with strong analytical and problem-solving skills

  • Have the ability to balance shipping new products and features with addressing technical debt and investments in architecture and infrastructure

  • Are comfortable supporting your work in production

  • Have a thorough understanding of design patterns

  • Have strong familiarity with Java. Knowledge of Python is a bonus.

  • Have a solid understanding of various methods and tradeoffs for handling concurrency

  • Have strong familiarity with databases, data modeling, profiling, and performance optimization

  • Are experienced and opinionated about API design and distributed backend systems

  • Have knowledge of AWS or GCP—this is not required but highly desired

  • Have experience with Docker and Kubernetes
