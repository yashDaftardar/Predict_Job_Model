2021 Software Engineer Intern - UC Team

About Five9

Five9 provides businesses reliable, scalable, and secure cloud contact center software designed to create exceptional customer experiences, increase agent productivity, and deliver tangible business results.

We are driven by a passion to transform contact centers into customer engagement centers of excellence. Since 2001, Five9 has led the cloud revolution in contact centers, helping organizations transition from legacy premise-based solutions to the cloud.

The Role

The Software Engineer Intern will work as an integrated member of the Unified Communications (UC) team, directly contributing to the success of our products. The UC Team at Five9 primarily develops integrations for leading UC solutions (like Microsoft Teams, Zoom, etc.) so that Five9 agents can easily view all UC users along with their real-time presence status and reach them for consolation, call transfer or conference call. The team consumes partner APIs to connect with the partner UC system and utilizes various Five9 platform cloud microservices.

Responsibilities:

  • Be part of an agile team to develop a scalable, highly available application platform.

  • Participate and contribute to requirements analysis and grooming.

  • Understand performance and scalability principles and apply when developing software.

  • Automate Tests using test frameworks.

  • Document and communicate implementation solutions.

Qualifications:

  • Rising senior or above, pursuing an undergrad degree in Computer Science or related field.

  • Solid understanding of scripting languages like JavaScript, Perl and Python.

  • Good understanding of Java fundamentals.

  • Excellent problem solver.

  • Positive, self-motivated and enthusiastic attitude.

  • Strong verbal and written communication skills.

Five9 embraces diversity and is committed to building a team that represents a variety of backgrounds, perspectives, and skills. The more inclusive we are, the better we are. Five9 is an equal opportunity employer.
