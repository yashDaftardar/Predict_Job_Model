Position Overview
Autodesk, the leading Design and Engineering Software Company, is transitioning to the cloud! As a global leader in 3D design, engineering, and entertainment software, Autodesk helps customers imagine, design, and create a better world. Our flagship product, AutoCAD, has become synonymous with CAD and is a fixture in design shops worldwide.

We have industry-leading 3D solutions for industrial design and manufacturing; architecture, engineering and construction as well as media and entertainment. Over 10 million professionals in 185 countries are using Autodesk products to save time and money, gain competitive advantage, and change the way ideas are brought to life.

The success and growth of Autodesk can be attributed not only to its exceptional products, but also to its unique culture, which brings together the best and the brightest in a combined mission of innovation and creativity. Responsibilities

  • Build and run Forge platform services
  • Write code that is that is tested, readable, and maintainable
  • Engage in technical discussion and decision making to shape the future of making things
  • Participate in on-call rotation to support production systems
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to understand requirements, understand use cases and build towards a cohesive technical strategy
  • Mentor and empower engineers around you


Minimum Qualifications

  • 4+ years of relevant professional experience
  • Strong experience with Java development
  • Strong experience with SQL and NoSQL databases
  • Experience consuming, designing, and implementing REST APIs
  • Experience building apps on AWS using services such as S3, DynamoDB, IAM, EC2, and Lambda
  • Experience implementing unit and integration tests


Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with scripting languages such as Groovy, Python, Node.js, or Bash
  • Experience with using MongoDB in a production environment
  • Experience with configuration management tools such as Chef
  • Experience with infrastructure-as-code tools such as Terraform or CloudFormation
  • Experience with Scala development
  • Experience with Agile software development practices
  • Experience with using Docker to build containerized applications
  • Experience with CI/CD concepts and working with a deployment pipeline such as Jenkins
  • Experience with load testing frameworks such as Locust
  • Experience with Git
