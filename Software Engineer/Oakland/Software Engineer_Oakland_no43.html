Company Description Square builds common business tools in unconventional ways so more people can start, run, and grow their businesses. When Square started, it was difficult and expensive (or just plain impossible) for some businesses to take credit cards. Square made credit card payments possible for all by turning a mobile phone into a credit card reader. Since then Square has been building an entire business toolkit of both hardware and software products including Square Capital, Square Terminal, Square Payroll, and more. We’re working to find new and better ways to help businesses succeed on their own terms—and we’re looking for people like you to help shape tomorrow at Square.
Job Description


As a front-end Software Engineer within the Business Systems Engineering team, your goal will be to develop innovative solutions to solve problems for our business teams.

We are looking for a front-end engineer to help build the best possible experience for the new solutions we develop for our business teams. You will work closely with designer and engineers to shape these experiences

You Will:

  • Contribute in delivering solutions to solve of complex business problems
  • Work with other teams to understand needs and implement reconciliation algorithms.
  • Work on all parts of the website stack - split your time between frontend and backend components.
  • Write concise, code and sensible tests

Qualifications


You Have:

  • 2+ years of software engineering experience developing scalable production software .
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent practical experience.
  • Proficient in Ruby and one of the Javascript UI frameworks like React, Ember, Angular
  • A strong interest in working full-stack, from developing frontend web applications.
  • The desire to work collaboratively, particularly over video or asynchronously through online tools.
  • Excellent written and verbal communications skills mandatory
  • Understanding of how to write semantically correct and accessible HTML.

Technologies We Use

  • Ruby, Rails
  • Javascript
  • Snowflake, MySQL, BigQuery
  • AWS, GCP
