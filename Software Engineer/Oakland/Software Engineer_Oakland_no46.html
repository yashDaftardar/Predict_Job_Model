Company Description


Etsy is the global marketplace for unique and creative goods. We build, power, and evolve the tools and technologies that connect entrepreneurs with buyers around the world. As an Etsy employee, you’ll tackle unique problems alongside talented coworkers committed to Keeping Commerce Human. We're large enough that you'll focus on meaningful, complex challenges, but small enough that you can make a rewarding impact.


Job Description


About the Team

Our mission on the International Seller Experience team is to make Etsy an attractive marketplace internationally. We are passionate about helping sellers join our platform and building the tools they need to make their products attractive to buyers across the globe. In the coming months, we will be focusing on an exciting project to reinvent the seller onboarding experience. Previously we have worked on pricing-related projects, such as regional sales functionality (allowing sellers to target discounted prices to specific countries), expanding supported buyer currencies through currency formatting, and helping sellers make listing prices more attractive and competitive across currencies and borders.

About the Role

  • Contribute at all levels of our web application stack, ranging from front-end web interfaces (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) to back-end technologies (PHP, MySQL, BigQuery). Balance between front-end and back-end work will vary by project.
  • Provide detailed and constructive design and code reviews.
  • Work cross-functionally with various engineering teams and partners in product and design.
  • Gain insights through research, data analysis, and our A/B tests.
  • Push code to production early and often using our continuous deployment systems.
  • Empathy, communication, and technical skill are valued equally.


Qualifications
  • You are an experienced engineer in both back-end and front-end coding in PHP & JavaScript or similar codebases
  • You are customer focused, build with a user in mind and think of infrastructure and services in product terms.
  • You have a knack for organization and breaking down large problems into smaller ones.
  • You are a strong communicator and are willing to take on a mentoring role with teammates and peers.
  • You write understandable, testable code with an eye towards maintainability.
  • You enjoy thinking about the implications of your work on end users, and have an understanding of the various aspects involved in running a large-scale website.
  • You believe in Etsy's vision of a people-led economy, and care about building the products that enable that economy.
