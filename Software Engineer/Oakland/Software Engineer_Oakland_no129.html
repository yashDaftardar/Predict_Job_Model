Full Job Description

Role

This position's responsibility is to help building and operating network as a code. Build and maintain tools to increase visibility, automate network operation tasks and ensure the reliability and availability of Tesla’s datacenter network.

Responsibilities

  • Conceptualize, build and maintain tools to support network deployment & operations.
  • Build and support tools to assist in diagnosis and remediation of advanced network problems, drive innovative solutions to reduce failures, obtain data collection for reporting or analysis.
  • Model network configuration and operational data in a platform neutral structure.
  • Develop and maintain CI/CD pipeline for network changes.
  • Develop testing platforms to detect network configuration inconsistencies before changes are made.
  • Build reports to gain intelligence about the network and detect errors early.
  • Automate network monitoring and alerts triage as part of proactive approach to maintaining high level of service.
  • Develop automated methods to detect, mitigate and remediate network events.
  • Proactively identify gaps, risks and issues and navigates organizational structure to resolve them.
  • Document scripting standards with written procedures, processes and diagrams.
  • Proactively find gaps that impact multiple teams/orgs, come up with the execution plan and drive the project and influence other teams to reach there.
  • Help make the people around you move faster by identifying issues slowing the team down and driving changes to address them.
  • Lead/mentor other folks within and outside of the team. Work with other team members from partner teams, understand their challenges, define and deliver automation solutions to address challenges.
  • Drive requirements with vendors and third parties to align our internal tooling efforts.
  • Contribute to the planning, design, and implementation of new data centers
  • Management of multiple high-visibility, high-impact projects simultaneously.
  • Participate in on call activities and follow escalation process to provide 24/7 network support.

Basic Qualifications

  • Degree in Computer Science or equivalent in relevant work experience
  • 7+ years of experience building software, preferred around network infrastructure.
  • Basic networking protocol and process knowledge
  • Strong coding Experience with Python
  • Experience using DevOps tools such as Ansible, Terraform, Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins, Travis CI.
  • Experience with API development and writing custom APIs
  • Experience with monitoring tools Splunk, Prometheus, InfluxDB Grafana or Kibana
  • Experience with one of the IP management tools such as NetBox, Infoblox or SolarWinds IPAM
  • Experience in integration with various applications using REST or gRPC
  • Moderate level of experience with web-oriented frameworks such as Flask, Django, React, Bootstrap, HTML/CSS or jQuery
  • Experience in Linux environment, FreeBSD JunOS, IOS, EOS or any network vendor CLI is a plus.
  • Experience with one of the databases MYSQL, SQL, Prometheus or MongoDB. Able to update & modify database using APIs.
  • Ability to work within a global team in a fast-paced and dynamic environment with limited supervision.
  • Experience debugging issues within custom code.

Desired Skills/Experience

  • Network certifications such as JNCIA, JNCIS, CCNA or CCNP is a plus
  • Experience with firewall policy orchestration tools
  • Knowledge about Distributed Systems, SDN/NFV, Public/Private cloud technologies
