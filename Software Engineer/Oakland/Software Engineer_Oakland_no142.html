Overview:

Under the leadership of the Manager, Data Exchange, a Software Engineer is responsible for analyzing, designing, developing, maintaining, and providing production support for software systems in accordance with business requirements and the technology roadmap.

Specialization

Software Engineers specialized in Interfacing develop, deploy, maintain and support PriMed's data exchange systems. The work primarily involves transporting data between internal and external systems in both transactional and batch methods. They must be experts in BizTalk using protocols such as SFTP, HL7, and x12. Strong SQL knowledge and knowledge of data utilities such as MoveIt are also required.
