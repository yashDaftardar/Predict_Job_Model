Peraton is currently seeking an experienced Principal Software Testing Technician to join the Defense Travel System (DTS) program located in Fairfax, VA. Roles and Responsibilities:
  • Execute test cases for the Defense System Travel application software
  • Develop software test plans, test cases, and test reports, and document and track defects
  • Develop test cases, mapping software requirements across the system functionality
  • Develop and execute test cases to test for DTS’ 508 compliance
  • Perform analysis, research, and troubleshooting of software defects and other issues
  • Participate in meetings with developers, requirements analysts, and other team members to help identify defects, troubleshoot system/software issues, and provide needed inputs to these discussions
  • Effectively communicate to other team members’ information regarding code defects, code releases, requirements, and environments status and needs
  • Update system documentation and test plans
  • Participate in the elicitation and specification of program requirements
  • Perform software and system verification and validation testing using manual test tools and test automation processes


Basic Qualifications: A candidate, regardless of age and hiring source, must meet ALL of the below criteria. The candidate must:
  • Have a Bachelor's degree with a minimum of 9 years of professional technical experience
  • Minimum of 5 years of software testing experience
  • Minimum of 5 years’ experience analyzing data, troubleshooting, and solving technical issues
  • Minimum of 4 years’ experience working with the DTS application
  • Minimum of 4 years’ experience with 508 compliance testing
  • Minimum of 4 years’ experience working with HP ALM (Quality Center) to maintain test cases

Preferred Qualifications:
  • Minimum of 10 years’ of software testing experience
  • Minimum of 4 years’ experience working with 508 Testing Tools including JAWS, ZoomText, or others.
  • Minimum of 2 years’ experience with UFT (Unified Functional Testing) for automated testing
  • Minimum of 2 years’ experience working with Global Distribution Systems including Sabre, Worldspan, Apollo and/or Galileo
  • Proven experience communicating across a large team with a variety of technical backgrounds
  • Experience with Jira
