The Role

We are seeking applicants with a strong knowledge in Systems Administration - troubleshooting skills with Linux, Unix, and/or Windows. The candidate should possess a solid understanding of networking technologies such as DNS, TCP/IP, SSL, DHCP and Load Balancing. This role requires troubleshooting - supporting solutions with AWS (CSP) experience/knowledge.
***Must have flexibility to work 24/7, rotating shifts.

***US Citizenship is required for government clients support.

Responsibilities

  • Act as Tier 1 Support Engineer to triage and troubleshoot issues with AWS services like EC2, S3, RDS etc.

  • Document solutions for known issues using the company knowledge base system

  • Advocate customer requests, troubleshoot and resolve complex, escalate critical issues to L2/L3 and beyond and advocate for resolution

  • Prioritize technical issues, define, and track bugs and offer creative solutions

  • Work in a team setting in a shared services environment

  • Positive attitude expected - should be able to adapt and work based on business demand

  • Interface with external customers as needed
***Must have flexibility to work 24/7, rotating shifts.

Primary Qualifications

  • In depth knowledge of either UNIX/Linux or Windows Environment.

  • Good written and verbal English communication.

  • Open to work in rotating shifts in 24x7 model.

  • Minimum 1 years of experience in Tier 1 support

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering or equivalent

  • Proficient experience on System Administration (Linux / Windows) - Certified

  • Experience on AWS at the AWS Associate/SysOps/Developer Certification course level. - Certified
  • Linux Administration (RHCE Certification)
  • Scripting (Shell or Python)
  • ITIL experience in handling Incidents, changes, service requests
  • Performance Monitoring (Nagios, Zabbix, Datadog, or any other such tools)
  • TCP/IP Networking Knowledge
  • SSL / TLS Management

Preferred Qualifications
  • Jenkins CI/CD Pipelines Management
  • AWS Cloud Management
  • Terraform / CloudFormation
  • ELK Experience
  • Security Fundamentals
