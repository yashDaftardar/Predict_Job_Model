We are seeking a Junior Web Developer with a formal programming background who is eager to learn and apply cutting-edge web development technologies. This role is an ideal fit for the self-starter/problem-solver personality types.

What we are looking for in an ideal candidate :

  • Excellent object-oriented programming skills
  • Excellent HTML skills
  • The ability to take ideas at a high level and fill in the pieces as needed, to complete the solution
  • Provide meaningful input and make suggestions for product/process improvements
  • Highly self-motivated, self-starter mentality
  • Extremely high level of detail orientation

  • Easily handle and prioritize multiple incoming tasks

  • Flexibility to share some level of on-call support on a rotating basis
  • Perform bug fixes as required

Qualifications:
