Organization: Accenture Federal Services
Location: Reston, VA
We are:

Accenture Federal Services, bringing together commercial innovation with the latest technology to unleash the potential for our federal clients. Every day we bring bold thinking and diverse disciplines to solve problems in new ways. Ready to learn as much as you can? We’ll give you numerous opportunities from informal training sessions to courses and certifications to keep your tech smarts sharp.

You are:

Passionate about the latest trends in tech. You want to support the groundwork of a technology business. And you enjoy working across different areas of a software system. Driven by curiosity, you’re always looking to dive deep into new technologies and meaningfully apply them. You want to make an impact and improve the way the world works.

The work:
  • Develop, design, and test software across various technical areas
  • Work with senior engineers to build development skills
  • Perform as a prime developer for distributed systems
  • Advise on technology advancements and develop enhancements
  • Integrate business processes with infrastructure and with stakeholders to deliver solutions


Here's what you need:
  • Bachelor’s Degree in a technical major such as Computer Science
  • 3 years of experience with developing code for a distributed programming system with one or more: C#, .NET, Python, Java, C++
  • Demonstrated experience working on programs using software engineering practices and coding
