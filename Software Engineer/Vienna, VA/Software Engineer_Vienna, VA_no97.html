Job Description

Are you a high performing system/software engineer looking for a position that highlights your innovation skills? Are you looking to apply your innovation to customized solutions in support of some of our nation’s most challenging missions? Accelerate your career and advance your customer’s mission by joining a high performing and award-winning CACI team. We’re searching for a go-getter with advanced Oracle knowledge to own and deliver solutions for high-visibility and high-priority customer objectives. Bring your expertise to our team of seasoned experts and make your custom software development dreams a reality!

More About the Role

The candidate will be responsible for light software development tasks and unit testing of high priority, high visibility customer functionality and enhancements. The candidate will also provide hands on data analysis support for data migration purposes, by constructing and running complex database queries per customer requirements.

The ideal candidate will:

  • provide feedback and demonstrate active listening during requirement gathering sessions
  • provide forward leaning software solutions that support current and future customer plans and initiatives
  • demonstrate a sense of urgency regarding customer needs and timeframes
  • think out of the box - introduction and use of new technology and techniques is encouraged when appropriate
  • work constructively with other software developers on the team to provide “whole solutions” rather than stovepipe solutions, when appropriate
  • participates actively in multiple ongoing data migration efforts and advises on technical approaches and methods
  • participates in data migration

You’ll Bring These Qualifications


Minimum 10 years’ experience including:

  • Oracle knowledge and ability to construct and break down highly complex database queries and procedures.

These Qualifications Would Be Nice to Have

Extensive experience developing software within the Intelligence Community.
