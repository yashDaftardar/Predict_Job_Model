Duties

Summary

The incumbent reports to the Information Systems Manager and is responsible for performing a variety of programming and integration activities.


Responsibilities

The key responsibilities of the Software Developer/Engineer are to:

  • Design and develop web applications using a combination of Oracle Application Express (APEX) and JavaScript.
  • Integrate various systems via PLSQL Web Services, and APIs and maintain existing ones.
  • Conduct data modeling and design.
  • Develop and design reports in Business Intelligence (BI) Publisher and integrate with APEX.
  • Develop and maintain code using Oracle PL/SQL.
  • Conduct Data Transformation using ETL tools.
  • Perform additional duties as assigned.

    Qualifications

    MANDATORY QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS:

    Applicants must have an undergraduate degree from an accredited university or college in Information Technology, Engineering or a related field of study. Applicants must have four (4) years of demonstrated experience utilizing the technical skills listed below.

    One (1) year of specialized work experience equivalent to the previous level in the federal service is required. For example, one (1) year of specialized experience at the GS-11 level is required to qualify for appointment at the GS-12 level. Specialized experience is defined as progressively responsible work experience that has equipped the applicant with the particular knowledge, skills, and abilities that will enable the individual to successfully perform the duties of this position, and that is typically in or related to the work of this position.

    Applicants must have a strong technical aptitude, interpersonal, oral and written communication skills. Applicants must also have the ability to multi-task and must be able to work alone and in a team setting.

    TECHNICAL SKILL REQUIREMENTS:
    • Strong background in web application development using a combination of APEX and JavaScript.
    • HTML5 / CSS knowledge
    • Experience integrating REST APIs into APEX applications.
    • Web application security / Data security.
    • Strong Oracle database knowledge.
    • Experience with advanced PLSQL skills.
    • Experience with Integrating Systems using PL/SQL web services and APIs.
    • Data modeling experience.
    • Experience with Data Transformation using ETL tools. Experience should include checking data quality and use of regular expressions in the ETL processes.
    • Experience with Oracle BI (Business Intelligence) Publisher and integration with APEX.

    Highly competitive applicants will have:

    • Experience with Oracle Application Express 18.0+.
    • Knowledge of Oracle ODI (Oracle Data Integrator).
    • An understanding of Data Warehouse.
    • Knowledge of Oracle Jet (JavaScript Extension Toolkit) charts integration with APEX.
    PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
    Preference will be given to applicants with one or more of the following areas of experience:
    • Advanced degree from an accredited university or college in a relevant field of study.
    • Strong analytical, troubleshooting, and problem resolution skills.
    • Experience in obtaining and understanding business requirements.
