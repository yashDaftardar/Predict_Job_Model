Sharebite is an online food ordering platform with a social mission. Our platform is built exclusively for companies, allowing their employees to place meal orders with incredible restaurants while helping to address social injustice in our communities.


Our Mission
Sharebite is a mission-driven company focused on addressing a fundamental social injustice in our city: childhood hunger. In NYC, 1 of 5 children face food-insecurity. This has ripple effects throughout communities that affect educational attainment, employment opportunity, incarceration rates, mental health and more.

At Sharebite, we understand that food is fundamental far beyond nutritionâ€¦ it is the cornerstone to healthy and vibrant communities. That's why we partner with City Harvest and other charities to address the issue of childhood hunger. For every meal ordered through our platform, Sharebite donates at least one meal to children facing food-insecurity - at no additional cost to the customer or our restaurant partners. Join our team and help feed your city!
