Job Description


The Support Platform Squad aims to provide human and effortless support where and when our members need it. As an engineer on this team, you will play a major role in improving the experience for millions of Etsy buyers and sellers. We’re a cross-functional group of engineers, data scientists, product managers, designers, and researchers.

We are looking for a Software Engineer to collaborate and lead in building trustworthy software and outstanding user experiences.

Here’s a taste of the problems we’re solving:

  • Create a human and effortless support experience for our members

  • Identify recurrent issues using adaptive algorithms that can be resolved with a better experience

  • Equip agents with a best-in-class platform to provide personalized support

Etsy is built on Google Cloud Platform, and our Web stack runs on PHP and MySQL. On the front end, we’ve developed and migrated to our own design system, and mostly build interfaces using React. We were one of the earliest and are one of the largest practitioners of continuous deployment in the industry.

This is a full-time position within our Support Platform team located in Brooklyn, NY.

We’re also open to remote hires within all U.S. states except Hawaii and Alaska.

About the Role

As a Software Engineer at Etsy, you will:

  • Able to pick up new concepts quickly and put them into practice

  • Collaborating with our cross-functional teams and business partners across Etsy

  • Using data to validate your hypotheses and inform your decision making

We are proud to have an engineering culture that encourages career growth and learning. You can learn more about our philosophies, tools, and some of the challenges we’ve been solving on our Code as Craft, our engineering blog.


Qualifications


You will do well in this role if you:

  • Have experience operating in a full-stack environment.

  • Write understandable, testable code with an eye towards maintainability and scalability.

  • Can optimally and effectively use data to drive decision-making and solve problems.

  • Can be autonomous and self-driven.

  • Have excellent communication skills, enthusiastic and forward-looking!

  • Are invested in Etsy’s vision, mission and values.

Experience with the technologies we currently use is beneficial, but not required.
