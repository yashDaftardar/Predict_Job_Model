Full Job Description

We are hiring! Candidus is a horticultural lighting control company bringing a new and innovative energy-efficient lighting control technology to the market. We help greenhouse growers to reduce energy consumption and increase profitability by designing and implementing energy-efficient lighting strategies. In 2021, Candidus was the winner of a Grow-NY award and the recipient of a Conservation Innovation Grant from the USDA Natural Resources Conservation Services to implement our technology in greenhouses across the US.

We are currently seeking a Software Development Engineer. The ideal candidate is a proactive and motivated individual looking to use their experience in coding and engineering to lead our product development effort. Candidus is a start up with a key product for the fast growing Greenhouse and Indoor Agriculture market. As our new software development engineer you will contribute vital work across several parts of our company. You will be working with a small team with multiple startup successes and included in our sales, marketing, and operations decisions.

In this role, a keen eye for detail, as well as excellent communication skills are needed along with a passion about quality.


Required experience and skills:

  • BA/BS degree in engineering, computer sciences, information management or related area of work
  • 3+ years of experience in software development
  • 1+ years of experience in Node-RED
  • Fluency in Node-RED and JavaScript
  • Familiar in working with the Raspberry Pi
  • Self-reliant, autonomous working style
  • Excellent knowledge in testing skills (design test plan and strategy.
  • Executing test protocols and analyzing test data).
  • Experience crafting reproducible software build systems
  • Provide operational support for production deployments
  • Ability to document and troubleshoot errors.
  • Ability to work well under pressure and to meet multiple and occasional competing deadlines while maintaining a cooperative working relationship with our team.


Not necessarily required, but nice to see:

  • Experience with Angular, Vue or other frontend framework
  • Experience or a desire to learn R analytics
  • Experience with AWS, EC2, System Manager, S3, IOT


For this role specifically:

  • Open to all backgrounds, though software and general engineering skills are key
  • Recent graduate to Intermediate level


As a distributed team, some key qualities are particularly important for all of us at Candidus :

  • A strong sense of ownership and initiative
  • Clear and consistent communication
  • A collaborative mindset
  • Clear thinking and attention to detail for complex projects
  • Comfort with operating independently as well as part of a small, targeted team


Location : This position can be based in one of the following New York regions:

  • Central NY (Cayuga, Cortland, Madison, Onondaga, and Oswego counties
  • Finger Lakes (Genesee, Livingston, Monroe, Orleans, Ontario, Seneca, Wayne, Wyoming, and Yates counties)
  • Southern Tier (and Broome, Chemung, Chenango, Delaware, Schuyler, Steuben, Tioga, and Tompkins counties)


Benefits:

  • Comprehensive health insurance, paid vacation and sick time
