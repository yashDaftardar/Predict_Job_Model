Job Description:

Build your resume experience and enhance your marketability with real-work, hands-on practice! Saab's Surveillance U.S. Division is in search of motivated, enthusiastic Engineering students who are interested in applying their studies to real-world product development through a paid Co-Op/ Internship opportunity.

Saab is currently looking for Summer/Fall 2021 Software Engineering Co-Ops. Under the guidance of Senior Engineers, Software Engineering Co-Ops will:

  • Actively participate in the software development for Radar systems.

  • Work with senior software engineers to design and develop modular, configurable software that can be used on multiple programs.

  • Participate in internal technical meetings and design reviews.

  • Collaborate with software lead for task planning and scheduling of own activities.

Skills and Experience:

  • Enrollment in an accredited degree program focused in Software Engineering, Computer Science, Computer Engineering or related discipline.

  • Completed Sophomore year or greater at time of hire.

  • Cumulative GPA of 3.0 or better.

  • Knowledge of Java or C++ software development, and object-oriented experience.

  • Strong teamwork and communication skills.

  • Willingness to learn and take on new challenges.

  • Prior Co-Op experience is preferred, but not required.

  • Desired experience: UML modeling tools for illustrating the software design, software development in a Linux environment, utilizing a unit test framework, and Subversion (SVN) for configuration management.

Must be a U.S. citizen. Applicants selected may be subject to a government security investigation and must meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information. As a condition of employment, candidates will be required to participate in a background check that will include, at a minimum, a criminal record check, education and employment verification.
