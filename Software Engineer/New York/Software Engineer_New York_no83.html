The PACE (Public Affairs and Customer Excellence) Team is seeking a full stack engineer in support of our growing technology portfolio supporting Gov't Relations & Public Policy, Social Impact & Sustainability and Public Affairs Analytics. The incumbent will be highly skilled in front end web application development and will be tasked with modernizing legacy portfolio to meet current application architecture specifications. Engineer will be responsible for all data transformation and processing using Informatica across the application portfolio. We are seeking a highly motivated team player that can provide critical thinking to meet and align with strategic deliverables. This engineer will be fluent in Agile software development methodologies, will partner closely with the scrum team and will ensure all policies are understood and adhered to. This engineer will be highly skilled in the use of modern frameworks like Angular 2.0 and .Net Core.

  • Provide analysis and support for Product Team
  • Provide Informatica data transmission and transformation processing for the team
  • Ensure application quality standards are met while following software development practices
  • Assist Technical Analyst in defining requirements and conduct gap analysis
  • Work with technical teams to deliver on commitments within time and scope
  • Collaborate with source system and Authorized provisioning point (APP) teams
  • Understand business requirements with respects to data and reporting needs
  • Develop automated regression test capabilities for applications supported

  • 5+ years of software engineering experience
  • 5+ years of .net experience
  • 4+ years of experience with MS Azure cloud computing technology
  • 3+ years of SQL Server Integration Services (SSIS), SQL Servicer Reporting Services (SSRS) experience, or a combination of both
  • 3+ years of experience implementing marketing technology solutions
  • 2+ years of .NET Core experience
  • 2+ years of Oracle experience
  • 2+ years of Informatica experience
  • 2+ years of web development experience using version 2.0 of Angular or greater

  • Experience applying new technologies to business needs in a technologically innovative , change-driven organization
