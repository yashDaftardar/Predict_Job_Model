Full Job Description

Aurora North Software is an innovative solution provider in the Legal Technology space. Our products and software-as-a-service (SAAS) solutions provide dynamic capabilities for organizations to streamline operational workflows.

Are you a results-focused engineer with a keen sense of business and interest in providing innovative and quality solutions?

Do you have database design expertise, C# & MVC development skills, web service REST API familiarity and experience integrating different data sources in cloud hosted environments?

Do you have innovative spirit, possess genuine interest in solving problems and embrace entrepreneurial enthusiasm?

We are actively looking for developers with functional experience solving problems and an enthusiastic interest in embracing new challenges.  This position will work on a small team including the organization's lead architects developing unique solutions to push our product stack forward.

Required Skills and Experience

  • Microsoft .Net C# & MVC
  • Microsoft Azure
  • SQL Query Development and Database Design
  • MVC Experience
  • Web Services and REST API experience
  • 3+ years of experience with solid knowledge of software development, SQL Server, data and system integration best practices, project life cycles, and collaborative development

What We Offer

We offer a competitive salary and benefits package including full-premium-paid medical, dental and vision insurance, HRA, FSA, excellent holiday/vacation/sick time off, and 401(k). This position is remote within the U.S.

About Us

Our company is a highly respected, top-tier provider of legal software and associated services throughout the United States, Canada and Europe. We have been in business since 2005 and have an unparalleled reputation in the legal market. We are seeking a highly qualified, talented person who enjoys working in a small company environment.
