We are looking for an entry-level Automation Engineer to work with our senior Engineers on a wide array of applications for municipal water/wastewater clients. This position can be based in one of the following office locations: Syracuse, Hyanis or Harrisburg .


Day-to-day responsibilities of this role will include:

  • Working as part of a multidisciplinary team to assist our clients in the evaluation, planning, design, construction, and programming of new, expanded, and modified PLC-based control systems, e.g. SCADA systems.
  • Perform onsite visits to assess the condition of existing process control systems (PCS)
  • Evaluate, research, and collaborate PCS improvements and document in formal reports, including explanation of existing conditions, recommendation of improvements, projected construction and engineering costs, and the development of tables and figures to convey complex concepts effectively
  • Creating design drawings, such as P&IDs, elementary wiring diagrams, network architecture diagrams and schematic wiring diagrams. Drafting will be supported by others.
  • Designing process control system networks, site-to-site VPNs, and high-speed fault tolerant fiberoptic networks
  • Developing new HMI applications using GE iFIX, Wonderware System Platform, Rockwell FactoryTalkViewSE, and/or Siemens WinCC. Support and improve upon legacy HMI applications
  • Developing new and modifying existing PLC and OIT programs, including uploading, downloading, editing, interpreting, and editing PLC and OIT applications. Independently capable with Allen-Bradley, Modicon, and Siemens PLC/PAC software programs.
  • Planning, building and maintaining virtualized computer architectures for HMI applications
  • Integrating ancillary software with HMI applications, including alarm notification software, reporting software, and asset management/CMMS programs
  • Proficient in troubleshooting PLC hardware, common PLC panel components, wiring, networks, interpreting technical documentation, and other skills necessary to remedy issues common in modern PCSs. Familiar with the use and availability of tools commonly used in troubleshooting PLC-based control systems.
  • Collaborating with our group of Designers to develop design documentation I&C systems in AutoCAD
  • Working alongside control system programmers on construction engineering of I&C systems
  • Assisting in the development of technical proposals, technical reports, and design memorandums
  • Effectively collaborating with a supporting team and supervisor whom are remotely located in New York. Support will be available to the candidate from other individuals on the team, however the candidate needs to be capable of performing common PLC, OIT, and SCADA software functions onsite based upon prior direction and coordination administered by the team remotely.

For success in this position, you will bring the following skills and qualifications to GHD:

  • Bachelor’s and/or Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering
  • 0 to 3 years of experience in public municipal markets in engineering and programming PLCs, OITs, and SCADA/HMI applications.
  • Prior consulting experience working with municipal clients on water projects a plus
  • Understanding of supervisory software programs, reporting programs and software based alarm dialer programs
  • Ability to communicate complex concepts to a diverse audience effectively
  • Focused and able to perform quality work in varied environments
  • Self-motivated, detail oriented, enthusiastic, and outgoing
  • Passionate about process Automation, IIoT, and Engineering disciplines and focused on professional and personal development
  • Able and willing to travel to regional project sites, up to 40% of the time with overnights using your personal vehicle.
