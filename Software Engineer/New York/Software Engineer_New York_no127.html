Full Job Description

Bitly is used by hundreds of thousands of businesses around the world to build stronger connections with their customers across every digital channel. Bitly provides powerful technology to customize, manage, publish, and track links through an easy-to-use, self-service platform backed by scaled, reliable, and secure infrastructure. Bitly's extensible Link Management Platform provides customers with the building blocks to power digital experiences, applications and workflows, and understand customer behavior in unique, valuable ways. Businesses of all sizes use Bitly in a broad variety of use cases.

The Role:

You will use Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) principles to modernize and scale Bitly’s infrastructure. You will be a key contributor as we evolve our infrastructure to become more cloud-native and globally distributed.

What You'll Do

    • Design, build and maintain the infrastructure we need to support 10x the volume of requests on our platform
    • Improve the the way containerized workloads are deployed and managed
    • Build internal tools and libraries that improve observability (i.e logging, monitoring and tracing)
    • Improve the way services are designed, deployed and operated across our engineering team
    • Support our 24x7 reliability and availability guarantees as a member of the operations team

Who You Are

    • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent experience
    • Experience programming in Golang, Python, C++ or Java
    • Understanding of Linux and TCP/IP network fundamentals
    • Production experience running services such as load balancers, relational databases, messaging systems and container-orchestration systems
    • Experience with Infrastructure as Code
    • Experience analyzing and troubleshooting systems

Bonus Points

    • Experience working with globally distributed systems or infrastructure
    • Experience with Google Cloud or Amazon AWS
    • Experience with HBase, Cassandra or Cloud Bigtable
    • Experience with Elasticsearch
    • Operational knowledge of Kubernetes
    • Fluency in Golang and/or Python
