About the team:

Come join a diverse and collaborative group of researchers in the Sustainability Research and Modeling team within the Financial Modeling Group (FMG). FMG is responsible for the research and development of the financial models underpinning BlackRock’s Aladdin platform. The models developed by FMG span a wide array of financial products and enable analysis at many different levels – from analyzing the cash flows of a single bond to understanding the risks across an entire portfolio or enterprise. These analytics empower users with flexible, quantitative risk and investment insights – both across BlackRock and for clients of Aladdin.

The Sustainability Research team is focused on building innovative models to assess the financial impacts of climate change, climate policy and energy policy on large number of asset classes – from corporate equity and fixed income, to mortgages, municipal bonds, sovereign debt and alternative investments. As climate risks and sustainable investing become an ever-increasing focus for investors, we are developing the models and analytics to help drive a deeper understanding of risks and outcomes.

About the role:

FMG is looking for a Software Engineer to work on BlackRock’s sustainability initiatives. The role will be based in New York or San Francisco. The focus will be on sustainability data infrastructure, API, climate risk model implementation, and DevOps processes. You will work closely alongside climate policy experts, quantitative researchers, other software and data engineers, internal and external business stakeholders to deliver our climate and sustainability models as a product to our clients.

This is a key interfacing role between the research team, the application development team, and our clients. You will have broad exposure to BlackRock’s financial modeling process involving diverse types of data and asset classes. Through this role, you will help deliver a product that has the potential to reshape industry’s response to sustainability issues.

Key responsibilities include:

  • Design and implementation of data and compute APIs as well as associated backend for sustainability models and data, especially related to climate risk
  • Design and onboard storage and compute infrastructure to address ongoing modeling and research needs in sustainability
  • Work with the research team and product owners to implement sustainability models and deploy to production
  • Work with product management on planning and execution. Communicate across teams on project delivery.

Skills & Qualifications:

  • Degree in Computer Science, engineering, other quantitative disciplines such as physics, or equivalent work experience. Graduate degree and/or research experience preferred
  • Minimum 2 years of professional software development/DevOps experience
  • Working experience in at least one compiled language (Java/Scala preferred) and one scripting language (Python preferred)
  • Working experience with Unix-like operating system and shell scripting
  • Experience with large datasets; experience with unstructured data a plus
  • Experience with SQL or equivalent
  • Able to communicate and collaborate effectively in a diverse team environment
  • Demonstrated ability to learn and adapt
  • Passionate about sustainability issues and sustainable investing
  • Prior experience in climate science, climate policy or energy policy is a plus
  • Experience with financial and economic research and quantitative modeling is a plus
  • Experience with machine learning and NLP is a plus
  • Cloud and cloud native experience is a plus
