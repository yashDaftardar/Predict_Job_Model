Full Job Description

At Olo we develop an online food ordering platform used by many of the country's largest restaurant chains, reaching millions of consumers. Chances are if you've ordered directly from a restaurant brand's app or website, we've made that happen.

We’re looking for a Product Intern, who will be managed by our VP of Product Management, to work closely with our Product Management team. You will be working with and learning from the team with aspirations to build a career in Product Management.

The Product Intern will be responsible for assisting with market research and trend analysis, documenting and maintaining the authoritative list of epics and user stories for all development efforts. You will be accountable for the functional design and adherence of software development methodologies, as well as perform coordination across all technical resources throughout the software development lifecycle. Additional responsibilities include the creation and maintenance of various types of documents, testing support, and assisting in delivery activities.

What You’ll Do

    • Assist product managers with market research, trend analysis and suggest the product position on next steps.
    • Lead development of requirements and related specifications, documentation, and business cases.
    • Decompose, refine, and document requirements in the form of user stories using appropriate graphical & textual techniques, including use cases, diagrams, process models, wireframes, and functional and technical specifications.
    • Lead collaboration with internal and external customers to define scope for new projects, document risks and assumptions, develop the delivery approach, and estimate cost and timings
    • Assist with roadmap prioritization by balancing multiple factors such as value, impact, effort, and long term strategy and vision.
    • Act as a translator between the business and technical community interpreting user needs and translating them into more technical language for engineers (and vice-versa)
    • Write user training manuals, help files, and facilitate training sessions for large applications, as needed

What We’ll Expect From You

    • Have the ability to see the big picture and envision possible solutions
    • Strong oral and written communications skills
    • Demonstrated effectiveness in a fast-paced rapidly changing environment
    • Strong facilitation, organization, and time management skills
    • Restaurant technology experience or restaurant industry experience is a plus

About Olo

Olo powers digital ordering and delivery programs that connect restaurant brands to the on-demand world, placing orders directly into the restaurant through all order origination points – from a brand’s own website or app, third party marketplaces, social media platforms, smart speakers, and home assistants. Olo serves as the on-demand ordering and delivery platform for over 300 brands, such as Applebee’s, Checkers & Rally’s, Cheesecake Factory, Chili’s, Dairy Queen, Denny’s, Five Guys Burgers & Fries, Jamba Juice, Noodles & Company, Portillo’s Hot Dogs, Shake Shack, sweetgreen, Wingstop, and more. Learn more at www.olo.com.
