Company Description


Square builds common business tools in unconventional ways so more people can start, run, and grow their businesses. When Square started, it was difficult and expensive (or just plain impossible) for some businesses to take credit cards. Square made credit card payments possible for all by turning a mobile phone into a credit card reader. Since then Square has been building an entire business toolkit of both hardware and software products including Square Capital, Square Terminal, Square Payroll, and more. We’re working to find new and better ways to help businesses succeed on their own terms—and we’re looking for people like you to help shape tomorrow at Square.


Job Description


For consideration, please submit a link to your portfolio/work samples***

As a Creative Software Engineer, you'll be a member of a creative team specialized in creating digital experiences and marketing campaigns. You will bring technology to the creative process, the front-end interactions you bring to life will create remarkable experiences for our sellers.

You are well-versed in an appropriate mix of development and prototyping platforms and tools, and know how to select the best platform for the task. You'll provide code that is flexible and can be iterated upon and pride yourself on being up-to-date with the latest standards. Thriving on exploration and experimentation, you are at the heart of co-creating ideas and bringing them to life. You have skills that complement your application of technology including but never limited to: a precise eye for intelligent UX, great design, and good foundations in strategy and process.

You will:

  • Participate in concept development iteration
  • Work on projects from ideation and design through implementation
  • Take creative direction and expand on concept ideas for tactics, with contribution in creative reviews
  • Take part in brainstorms, partnering with colleagues with diverse teams including Strategy, Design, Digital Production and Copy
  • Implement hands-on technical development of prototypes coming out of ideation
  • Contribute to discussions about the digital aspects of creative campaigns
  • Present ideas or prototypes to mixed client marketing / technical audiences
  • Work creatively through and around perceived limitations imposed by the delivery platform to create delightful experiences for customers
  • Evaluate and document any new creative and engineering standards or patterns that could arise from your work

Qualifications
  • BS in Computer Science or a related technical field, and 4 years of relevant work experience
  • Front-end development – HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern tooling
  • Backend development – Familiarity of Ruby, Java, or other backend languages
  • Portfolio of work that displays design, high-fidelity animation, motion, iconography, typography, color, and imagery
  • Understanding of web standards, browser performance and best practices
  • Passion for digital marketing, advertising, and understanding metrics
  • Advocate for technology and choosing the right tool for the job
  • Familiarity of modern design tools (Figma, Adobe After Effects, Cinema 4D)
