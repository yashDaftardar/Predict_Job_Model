KATZ Media Group


Job Summary:

The Software Engineer is responsible for full stack software development on both existing and new systems, as well as patching and troubleshooting technical issues as a part of third level support. This is for Katz Media Group's CIS division.


Essential Functions

The Software Engineer performs the following essential functions:

  • Review provided specifications to identify potential issues and design optimal technical solutions to complex business problems.
  • Participate in estimation and design review as part of the engineering team
  • Build software to specification, working with other engineers and the product team as needed
  • Perform as a full stack engineer, building both front and back end code.
