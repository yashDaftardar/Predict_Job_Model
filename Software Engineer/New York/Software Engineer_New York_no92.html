ABOUT THE COMPANY

Tembo Health is a virtual medical practice that helps patients in nursing homes receive care in hard to access specialties like psychiatry and cardiology. Our mission is to improve healthcare outcomes. The status quo is unacceptable, as our seniors have difficulty receiving specialty care leading to worse healthcare outcomes including re-hospitalizations. By partnering with nursing homes, Tembo Health drives quality improvement with our network of world-class clinicians. Our technology allows our clinicians to provide both complex and quality care with a seamless user experience integrating medical data from various sources.


Our leadership team has deep expertise in clinical medicine, clinical transformation, operations, and technology with experience at top institutions including BCG, GE, Harvard Hospitals, Mount Sinai, Northwell Health, and Oscar. We’re backed by prominent investors including Bloomberg Beta, B Capital Group, and Resolute Ventures. We've proven product market fit over the past two years, have customer traction in NY, TX, and MI, and are scaling upon our success.


ABOUT THE ROLE

We're building an engineering team to grow our telemedicine platform and succeed at scale. This is a tremendous opportunity to influence the growth of a new team and build novel and impactful solutions in our fast-growing company.


Responsibilities

  • Build products that enable doctors and nurses to meaningfully improve the quality of care of our patients.

    • Work across engineering, product, and design to identify user needs and ship high quality products.

    • Contribute both to existing applications and develop new ones from scratch.

    • Leverage existing APIs (e.g. medical record systems, video conferencing, digital connected devices) to build feature rich applications quickly and efficiently.

    • Design and ship data insight tools for better clinical decision making.

  • Enable data driven decision making

    • Your code will help make us make better decisions in our business, so we can scale our impact on the world and help more people.

    • Leverage internal and external data sources to produce actionable metrics, help us grow our business and maximize our patient impact as quickly as possible.

  • Handle patient private data securely and privately

    • Everything we do must treat patient and partner data according to the highest standards of privacy and security. Integrate those principles into your daily workflow and designs.


Success in 30-60 days is:

  • Working collaboratively across eng, product, and design.

  • Understanding outstanding customer needs and designing features to help them.

  • Shipping real code that impacts our users’ daily lives.

  • Contributing to the way we work and our underlying infrastructure. Help support a growing engineering organization!


Success after that is:

  • Develop wholesale new applications as needed.

  • Gain experience talking to and iterating with real customers, so that we can help them more effectively.

  • We don’t know what we don’t know, so, you’ll contribute in meaningful ways you identify to the future of the engineering organization and the entire company.
