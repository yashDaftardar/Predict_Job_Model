Full Job Description

Software Solutions Engineer needed for our world-leading provider of recruitment process automation solutions. DaXtra Technologies, Inc. is growing our technical team and has an immediate opening for a Solutions Engineer with support capabilities. This position also serves as an escalation point for US clients’ support queries. There is great opportunity for mobility, as strong engineers will have good career progression through the company.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a technical science (i.e. Computer Science, Information Systems, Mathematics)
  • Minimum of 2 years experience in IT industry
  • 1+ years experience in Linux environment (Centos/Redhat)
  • 1+ years experience with object oriented or functional programming and concepts
  • Competency with regular expression
  • Experience with scripting languages. Ex (Unix Shell)
  • Proficiency with relational databases and concepts (MySQL, SQL Server)
  • Basic familiarity with network communication technologies including HTTP, SSL, SMTP, TCP/IP
  • Excellent communication skills both with technically based co-workers and external customers

Desired Qualifications:

  • Data structure concepts within a programming paradigm
  • Firm grasp of object oriented concepts (Java, C)
  • Experience with advanced scripting languages: Perl, JavaScript
  • Proficient competence with relational databases (MySQL, SQL Server)

What you’ll be doing:

Working with project manager and account managers to configure, install and support DaXtra software on customer systems. This person should be a self-starter, organized, and able to work in fast-paced environment. Priorities and tasks can shift based on customer needs. It is also important that his person be ready to raise their hand and ask for help when needed, to ensure projects keep forward momentum.

The DaXtra Team is cross-functional in that we wear many hats and work hard together.

This position is equal parts configuration developer, software tester and customer support.

What’s in it for you:

  • Competitive pay and benefits
  • Job stability along with career growth opportunity
  • A great work environment with collaborative co-workers
  • Opportunities for international travel

Specifics :

  • Work Remote/Richmond, VA
  • If not local, must be able to travel to Richmond – approximately once a month (post-COVID)
  • Must be authorized to work in the United States on a full-time basis for any employer

Please submit :

  • Resume detailing your current and past professional experience
