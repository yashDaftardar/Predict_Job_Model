As a Managing Director of Engineering at UDig, you will be responsible for helping define the strategic vision and direction for the Engineering practice based on market trends, customer feedback and prior experience. You will collaborate with other leadership members to drive the successful outcome of projects while developing and mentoring the consulting team within your area of practice. You will continually evolve the practice to allow for career growth opportunities while managing client and organizational needs. You will bring proven experience managing a Software Engineering team within a large enterprise or consulting environment. You will support the business development lifecycle and be an ambassador for the UDig brand through networking opportunities, speaking engagements and thought leadership.

Core Responsibilities:

  • Manage a growing team of 15-20 Software Engineering Consultants.
  • Define and develop our Senior and Lead Consultants. Empower, mentor and train them to manage their teams and grow their employees in line with organizational goals.
  • Participate in and partner with HR and leadership in making Organizational decisions.
  • Lead collaborative session across the engineering team fostering knowledge transfer, project outcomes and creating continuous client/consultant feedback. This will include architecture/code review meetings to ensure the team is providing exceptional products and delivery.
  • Participate in VA Market leadership meetings and discuss team and project needs as it relates to the Engineering practice.

  • Promote and foster participation in both technical and business-related networking, groups and conferences within the Virginia Market.

Key Qualifications:

  • 10-15 Years of experience in Software Development with 3-5 years in a senior leadership role
  • Proven track record and passion for building, leading and managing strong engineering teams know for excellent delivery, high morale and low turnover
  • Experience creating career development and promotion paths.
  • Developed structure and accountability plans for mid-level management in a consulting or enterprise organization
  • Extensive experience with DevOps and CI/CD practices
  • Extensive experience with Cloud Services (Azure and AWS)
  • Previous experience in a large enterprise or consulting firm is required
