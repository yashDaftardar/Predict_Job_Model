Full Job Description

Virginia Alcoholic Beverage Control Authority (VA ABC) is seeking a Senior .NET Developer with a passion for software development. The successful candidate will provide technical consulting or leadership on complex projects while leveraging the latest technologies. The candidate is responsible for gathering business requirements, program design, coding, testing, debugging and documentation. The candidate will have full technical knowledge of all phases of applications systems analysis and programming.

The primary responsibilities of this position will revolve around Virginia ABC’s site abc.virginia.gov. The developer will be responsible for the development, implementation, maintenance, and support of software solutions.
