Take your next career step at ABB with a global team that is energizing the transformation of society and industry to achieve a more productive, sustainable future.

At ABB, we have the clear goal of driving diversity and inclusion across all dimensions: gender, LGBTQ+, abilities, ethnicity and generations. Together, we are embarking on a journey where each and every one of us, individually and collectively, welcomes and celebrates individual differences.

ABB Smart Power is a global technology leader, with solutions that make power supplies smart, connected and protected. Its intelligent solutions make power more competitive by improving the energy efficiency, productivity and reliability of almost any operation.

Our R&D engineers and scientists develop breakthrough technologies that change the way the world works and industries do business. We constantly push the limits of convention, while retaining our focus on delivering solid returns for our customers and shareholders.

The role reports to the R&D (Research & Development) Team Manager and is located in Richmond, Virginia. As a R&D Software Engineer, will work under the direction of the Embedded Software Engineer Team Leader. Assignments can be broad in variety and scope.

Your responsibilities

  • Assists in designs, development, coding, testing, and debugging complex new software products, and makes enhancements to existing software packages
  • Design and develop cross-platform applications for PCs and servers
  • Develop and maintain product interface applications
  • Participate in requirements gathering, the design process, product reviews, and deployment activities
  • Help maintain code quality, organization, and documentation
  • Deliver high quality system-level software for various hardware components
  • Developing control system models for code-generation
  • Assists with driver development, board bring-up and new hardware enablement
  • Packaging and build system integration of various software
  • Prepare test cases from product specification and execute Verification and Validation activities
  • Provides software maintenance support for Active products

Your background

  • B.S. in Electrical Engineering (BSEE), Computer Engineering (BSCE) or similar required with minimum of 2+ years of professional design experience.
  • M.S. in Electrical Engineering (MSEE) or equivalent.
  • Candidates must already have a work authorization that would permit them to work for ABB in the US.
  • Proficient in embedded firmware development with C and/or C++ & PC Software’s.
  • Experience with DSPs (Digital Signal Processor) / Microcontrollers / Programmable logic devices.
  • Good understanding of UART, SPI (SmartPlant® Instrumentation), I2C communication & Network protocols like Modbus, CAN.
  • Good analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Strong hands-on testing and troubleshooting skills with oscilloscopes debuggers and similar lab equipment.
  • Exposure on version control management tools, build integration process.
  • Experience with MATLAB, Simulink, and model-based system design.
  • Experience in writing technical specifications, Validation test case & report generation.
  • Knowledge on single phase and three phase metering system a plus.
