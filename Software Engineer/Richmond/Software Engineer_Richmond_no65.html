About the Opportunity

The Developer Platform Group has an immediate opening for a DevOps Engineer, reporting to a CI/CD Pipeline product team.

We are looking for an engineer with hands-on experience working in guidance with senior engineers in supporting DevOps and who has a passion to help us move towards deploying to AWS.

You will working with a team of engineers supporting CI/CD pipeline tooling. This will involve working with app deployment teams for on-prem and AWS applications.

What You Will Do:

  • Support Application deployment and CI/CD pipeline tools
  • Work with developers to build deployment workflows
  • Build integrations between development and deployment platforms using Python and other scripting

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree or equivalent experience with 2-5 years of relevant work experience
  • DevOps, CI/CD, AWS certifications and/or experience
  • Hands-on development experience with coding and/or scripting
  • Python, Bash expertise preferred.
  • Working knowledge of the Agile methodology preferred
  • Experience with any of GitLab, Ansible, Jenkins, XL Deploy/XL Release or other deployment technology would be an asset
  • WebSphere experience is a plus
