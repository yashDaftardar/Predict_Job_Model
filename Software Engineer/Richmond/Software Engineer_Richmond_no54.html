About EAB

At EAB, our mission is to make education smarter and our communities stronger. We harness the collective power of more than 1,900 schools, colleges, and universities to uncover and apply proven practices and transformative insights. And since complex problems require multifaceted solutions, we work with each school differently to apply these insights through a customized blend of research, technology, and services. From kindergarten to college and beyond, EAB partners with education leaders, practitioners, and staff to accelerate progress and drive results across three key areas: enrollment management, student success, and institutional operations and strategy.

The Role in Brief:

Front End Engineer

Seeking a Font-end Engineer to solve deep problems with a platform-wide User Interface platform for rapid application development. The role involves supporting the development of a new programming language and the components it uses to construct interfaces.

This position is remote.

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Aid in the development of solutions that support consistent information architecture, taxonomies, visual standards, interaction patterns, and use cases across multiple applications
  • Self-start and steward your implementations through the software development process ensuring you champion the standards of the UX team in every development challenge
  • Follow common sustainable page design, management, and development methodologies
  • Perform testing at the page & source for presentation layer defects
  • Writing React Components
  • Writing in Typescript to solve deep software language and classical computer science problems
  • Developing accessible solutions across the platform

Basic Qualifications:

  • 2 years of front-end engineer experience
  • Bachelors Degree in Technical Field
  • Experience with ReactJS and usage of class based and functional components
  • Passion for making dynamic, performant, enterprise web applications
  • Understanding of SDLC and the technical and business challenges that come with development of a product
  • Self-driven and a desire to work efficiently and produce high quality
  • Thorough, methodical, problem solver and creative with extreme attention to detail
  • Knowledge of and ability to discuss HTML5, CSS, JavaScript solutions
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills. This role must easily be able to have clear communication over email, IM, phone, and in person.
  • Excellent customer service and interpersonal skills
  • Expert at time management as well as working & finding answers to questions independently

Ideal Qualifications:

  • Experience with hooks and Redux or any other state management library.
  • Experience with unit testing in UI frameworks
  • Experience with traditional software patterns and application architecture
  • Experience with a broad range of HTML5 API’s
  • Experience developing with accessibility standards in mind
  • Experience with object-oriented, functional, logic, and imperative programming
