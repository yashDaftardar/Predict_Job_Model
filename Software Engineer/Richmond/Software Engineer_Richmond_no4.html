Full Job Description

Plan, design, develop and maintain web applications for the Department. Works closely with business analysts and other programmers to ensure successful election management in the Commonwealth.



Minimum Qualifications
  • Experience programming in C#;
  • Experience with .NET Core;
  • Experience with MVC web-based applications;
  • Experience with JavaScript including JQuery or other Javascript libraries;
  • Experience with HTML and CSS;
  • Experience with mobile and responsive design practices;
  • Experience working with Microsoft SQL Server databases;
  • Experience in the Transact SQL language;
  • Knowledge of application security;
  • Ability to use logic and reasoning to identify strengths and weaknesses of alternative solutions, conclusions and approaches to problems;
  • Ability to provide information analysis proactively and interactively with business staff;
  • Possess good organizational and communication skills;
  • Be a team player;
  • Have business awareness and be willing to learn about new developments;


Preferred Qualifications

A college level degree in Information Systems is desired; however, appropriate experience may substitute.
