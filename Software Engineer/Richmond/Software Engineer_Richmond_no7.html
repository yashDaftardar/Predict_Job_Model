Full Job Description

Roundtrip is devoted to creating a world in which transportation is no longer a barrier to wellbeing.

We are looking for a Software Engineer to help design and deliver software solutions in support of Roundtrip’s marketplace and related components. Specifically, this role will be responsible for:

  • Serving as a hands-on engineer.
  • Collaborating with architects and product managers to find solutions to complex problems.
  • Being a contributor to all aspects of our SDLC from elaboration through testing and operations.

The ideal candidate will have 3 years of software development experience with web application and/or api frameworks while working in an Agile development organization. Candidates should have past experience working in a distributed team environment, be an active learner and want to grow their career in a fast-paced work environment.

This position sits on our Engineering team, is based out of our office in Philadelphia or Richmond (post-covid) or fully remote and reports to our Head of Engineering.

What You'll Do:

    • Document, architect, develop, and test software for our core product offerings.
    • Work closely with product and business leaders to understand problems and develop solutions.
    • Work with cross-functional teams to ensure our applications and services are highly available and reliable.
    • Serve as an advocate for ensuring quality, security and maintainability is built into our solutions.
    • Work with other engineers to help develop the collective group’s knowledge and skills.

What You'll Have:

    • 3 years of experience with Ruby on Rails, NodeJS or other web development frameworks.
    • Practical experience applying software design patterns & principles to build software solutions.
    • Experience in designing and optimizing relational database implementations.
    • Strong verbal and written communication skills.
What We Offer:

  • Health, Dental and Vision Benefits for you and your family
  • 401K with matching program beginning day 1
  • Unlimited PTO with a 10 day minimum
  • Company-paid lifestyle benefits you hand select twice a year (i.e. Uber, Hulu, NETFLIX, DOORDASH)
  • An annual allowance for professional development and growth
  • VTO: Spend a day making a difference volunteering for the cause of your choice
  • Feel Good Friday: We close our ‘offices’ every other Friday afternoon so you can take time for you. No meetings, no calls, no Zoom. Want to check out a movie? Great! Make an elaborate dinner for your family? Yum! Catch up on all your work emails so you’re ready for Monday? Well…that doesn’t sound super fun, but sure, go for it!
  • Fun work environment and an unmatched team!

About Us:

Roundtrip is the leading digital transportation marketplace for better health outcomes. We provide a simple, comprehensive patient ride ordering software that makes available a community of ride providers who complete the transport. The company is leading the industry in reducing patient no-shows through improved ride management.
Being a part of the Roundtrip team doesn’t just mean showing up to work. You’ll need to embody these core values as a part of what you bring to Roundtrip every day.

  • Stay Resilient
  • Take Ownership
  • Be Proactive
  • Keep it Simple
  • Listen First
