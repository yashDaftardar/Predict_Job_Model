Full Job Description

These are incredibly exciting times at the Virginia Department of Social Services (VDSS)! We are driving unprecedented, progressive organizational change to create platforms for industry-leading innovations with the goal of delivering the best possible outcomes for Virginians. Join our team, drive the change, and make a difference! We are offering an exciting opportunity for a dynamic and innovative senior executive leader to join the VDSS and serve as our Application Development Manager.

The Application Development (App/Dev) Manager position reports directly to the Cloud Application Development Division Director and is responsible for managing a team of IT software developers who develop, integrate, and implement solutions that create, enhance and stabilize VDSS enterprise software systems. The App/Dev Manager provides innovative approaches required to deliver efficient and effective application frameworks that are fundamental to business success, technical and engineering best practices. The Manager is an expert in recommending application workflows and streamlining application presentations layers into dynamic and active application solutions.

The Manager provides leadership and expertise in systems development, integration and implementation. The Manager ensures system integrity and validity of data components, assuring that all appropriate SDLC processes are adhered to. Establish software development foundations and integration practices and procedures including technical standards, software-coding standards, tools, conceptualizing functional requirements, architectural approaches, integration methodology and application and infrastructure design guidance to deliver high-quality and timely solutions and services that continuously meet business needs. Oversee software development, integration, and testing activities. Responsible for review of software in support of increasing the efficiency, integrity and security of artifacts produced during the development lifecycle. Ensures proper and effective integration of security components in and during the SDLC lifecycle. Guides the development estimation process to provide accurate input in the project management processes.

The Manager will lead a team of developers, which includes setting performance objectives and evaluating performance against them. Supervise the development, testing, documentation and implementation of those applications or other technical solutions and the maintenance of existing applications. Responsible for setting employee goals, assessing employee performance, providing feedback, and making pay recommendations.



Minimum Qualifications

Executive Management Core Qualifications:

Drive for Results - The ability to consistently communicate business goals, outcomes and performance measures, and clarify priorities. Translate ideas into actions and look for ways to quickly overcome barriers.

Build Coalitions - Demonstrated ability to recognize the interests of others in different parts of the organization, establish and maintain effective working relationships with internal and external stakeholders, state, federal, and local agencies, public officials, community groups, other division directors, employees, and the general public.

Know the Business - The ability to understand how businesses and strategies work, and how current and possible future policies, practices, trends, technology, and information affect the organization.

*Lead Change *- The ability to demonstrate support for innovation and for organizational changes needed to improve the organization’s effectiveness, by initiating, sponsoring and implementing them.

Drive for Results – With little direction, the incumbent will be extremely proactive in identifying, assessing, detecting, alerting, and resolving issues as they occur. The incumbent has a “can-do” approach to all matters and takes the initiative to take control and resolve customer issues as they occur. This includes contacting and coordinating with any staff or manager as needed, and immediately escalating issues as appropriate.

Connect with Presence - Demonstrated ability to communicate with confidence, composure, credibility, and connection, both orally and in writing, with colleagues, decision-makers and stakeholders.

Invite Employees to Engage - The ability to create an environment where employees are committed to the organization and willing to extend discretionary efforts to help achieve its goals.

Customer Service – The Manager is dedicated to customer service and has a deep commitment to supporting all customers. The incumbent will be extremely proactive in communicating with all customers in various forums and media, and will follow through in resolving issues. When necessary, the incumbent will escalate concerns to the appropriate management level.

Technical Qualifications:

  • Experience in business application design, Service Oriented Architecture (SOA) and implementation projects for large IT organizations using systems integration methods and technologies
  • Experience using new technologies and software development tools (e.g JAZZ, TFS/Visual Studio, Veracode, Enterprise Architect (CA), Rational Application, Developer (RAD), SQL Developer, TFS, GIT IIS, IBM WAS Base Edition, etc.)
  • Bachelor’s or Master’s Degree in Computer Science, Computer Architecture, Database Management, Information Systems or other related field, and experience related to the duties of the position; or an equivalent combination of training and experience.
  • Comprehensive knowledge of the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC), industry standard software development security methods, and Agile and Waterfall development methodologies


Preferred Qualifications
  • Experience using new technologies, software development tools and development of applications in the cloud (e.g Microsoft Azure, Amazon Web Services, SalesForce Lightning, etc), with an emphasis on low code application platforms (LCAP)
  • Certification in the IT Infrastructure Library (ITIL) Foundation
  • Knowledge and familiarity with the Software Engineering Body of Knowledge from the IEEE Computer Society
  • Knowledge and experience with development-security-operations (DevSecOps) concepts
  • Demonstrated experience with JAVA, Websphere, and application frameworks (SPRING, Hibernate, HTML UI, CSS, JavaScript,Fast4j, SPRING, SSL)
  • Experience with .NET programming language, with preference to C#, and proficiency in HTML and XML, and object-oriented programming
