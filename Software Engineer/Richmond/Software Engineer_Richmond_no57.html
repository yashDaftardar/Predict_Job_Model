End User Engineer (EUE)
The End User Engineer (EUE) will be responsible for providing business and IT solutions for complex managed services within a global end user environment of approximately 1200 users. You will be responsible for ensuring a defined specification for software and/or hardware solutions to help improve the end user experience, with a special focus on increasing automation across the stack.
The EUE will need to ensure the delivery of vital tools, as you will focus on arming our End User Support teams with current tools and technologies to support our business.
As a member of the Engineering team you will ensure that the engineering and designing of new and innovative solutions meet the End User’s and the End User Support team’s requirements.
You will be responsible for ensuring that documentation is provided and stored in a single location. Each member of the EUE team will need to present to line manager handover documentation for Support Teams.
The EUE is responsible to support in design, install, administer, and optimize the solution and related components to achieve and maintain high performance. This includes ensuring the availability of client/server applications, configuring new implementations, and developing processes and procedures for ongoing management of the environment. This position shares responsibility for the security and integrity of our deployments. Scope of responsibilities include, core Microsoft back office technologies, 3rd party infrastructure software and applications, and compliance/audit reporting with automation.

Responsibilities :

  • Provide automated solutions for Support teams.
  • Compile automated reports for compliance and security SCCM / End Point Manager [Intune]
  • Prioritize incoming work and report status. Manage multiple tasks simultaneously. Respond to changing priorities.
  • Ensure documentation within the End User Engineering team are stored and accessible from a single point.
  • Adhere to ITIL processes.
  • Responsible for new product and service evaluation, design, implementation, testing, debugging, and documentation.
  • Will serve as a forward looking technical professional; evaluates new or emerging technologies for the use of our employees, agents, or customers – especially those that increase automation across the stack.
  • Responsible for the evaluation, selection, and implementation of approved technologies that align with technical architecture, business plans and long term strategy.
  • Responsible for ensuring the tracking lifecycle management and quality assurance review of key productivity tools, hardware, and services.
  • This position provides general management for technology-based endpoint support for End User Services ( EUS ) and in support of IT Infrastructure functions working to agreed operation level agreements (OLA’s).
  • This position manages the endpoint components/systems used by EUE and other functions for daily operations, compliance, and desktop security, has shared impact on final results and has extensive directed freedom to act as required to maintain expected service levels. Responsible for development and implementation of business relationships necessary to expand and ensure program success.
  • This position requires a significant focus/passion for customer service and the ability to work to defined timescales and to a high level of quality. It includes constantly improving end user services related to endpoint management by exceeding customer expectations and improving and increasing the portfolio of services provided.

Qualifications :
  • Bachelor degree in an IT related field or equivalent experience.
  • Ideally 5 – 10 years experience in Desktop Engineering.
  • Good understanding of SCCM / End Point Management [Intune].
  • Strong Ability to communicate effectivity within IT Engineering Team and wider IT groups.
  • Experience in end user support and technologies with progressive responsibilities, such as MDT, SCCM, Power Shell, Windows 10, in tune policy building and GPO Building.
  • Requires strong ability with troubleshooting and providing output results of solution/solving skills.
  • Windows 10 Certification MD-100 and MD-101 Windows 10 | 70-698 Installing and Configuring W10 | Additional qualification within Azure and Intune is a plus.
  • Requires technical knowledge of Enterprise software and technologies.
  • Strong organization/project planning, time management, and change management skills across multiple functional groups and departments, and strong delegation skills involving prioritizing/reprioritizing workloads.
  • Advanced problem-solving experience involving researching and coordinating the resources necessary to effectively troubleshoot/diagnose complex issues. Extracting/translating findings into alternatives/solutions, and identifying risks/impacts and schedule adjustments to facilitate management decision-making.
  • Experience supporting, configuring and engineering Microsoft Technologies including Windows Operating Systems, Group Policy, Microsoft Deployment Toolkit, O365, Active Directory.
  • MySQL Database management.
  • Strong AV Management [TrendMicro and Microsoft Defender]
  • Windows 10 Deployment background.

Preferred Skills
  • Strong AV Management [TrendMicro and Microsoft Defender]
  • SCCM
  • End Point Management [Intune]
  • With scripting technologies such as VBScript and/or PowerShell
