About the Opportunity

The Solutions Engineering group has an immediate opening for a senior DevOps Automation Engineer, reporting to the manager in Automation and Orchestration group.

The DevOps engineer will design and develop automation solutions to support infrastructure-as-code applications and will work collaboratively with product engineering, test automation, and operations to deliver these new capabilities and streamline current processes. Where design/coding activities will be the primary responsibilities, we own what we build so the DevOps engineer will also be responsible for maintaining automation tools used to provision, test, and deploy.

Senior engineers are leaders in the organization and are responsible for setting technical direction, defining paths to accomplish target state, and working across organizational boundaries to achieve system goals.



What You Will Do:
  • Engineer will provide thought leadership to the team in new technologies, identifying / implementing / leading improvements that will significantly improve time to market for our automation orchestration platform.
  • Performs resolution of complex hardware, environmental software operating systems and subsystems. Oversees problem avoidance actions.
  • Performs change and problem management using standard tools. Ensures conformance and compliance with existing system standards. Measures performance to ensure proper operation.
  • Leads complex projects in partnership with other System staff and resources.
Qualifications:
Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science or equivalent with 5+ years of relevant work experience
Required Skills
  • 5+ years developing complex integrations in Ansible, Puppet, Chef, or other IaC language
  • 5+ years of application development / scripting experience (Python, PowerShell, JavaScript, JSON, Unix Shell, Ruby)
  • Proven technical leader with increasing level of responsibility
  • 3+ years of software/system design in a DevOps lifecycle
  • 2+ experience developing CI/CD pipelines
  • Significant testing background with knowledge of automated testing processes
  • Experience working in large Agile organizations
Preferred Skills / Knowledge
  • Cloud architecture and engineering
  • Knowledge of other Automation/Orchestration toolsets
  • Knowledge of virtualized infrastructure design and administration
  • Knowledge and skills related to Site Reliability Engineering (SRE)
