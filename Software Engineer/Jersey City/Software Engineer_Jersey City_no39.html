Software Engineer

Wyng Microexperience Platform
New York, NY

Wyng is transforming the way brands market and sell online. Our Privacy-First Personalization platform, powered by a zero-party data API and privacy by design principles, makes it easy for marketers to deliver customer experiences that are always welcome, relevant, and compliant. More than 250 brands and enterprises use Wyng to build direct, trusted relationships with their customers. We're based in New York City.


As a Software Engineer, you will join a fast-paced, agile, and passionate product development team. As a startup, we are innovating everyday and you will too. We have been early adopters of many cutting-edge technologies, such as ReactJS, Elasticsearch, Docker, AWS Kinesis and Lambda. You will never run out of learning opportunities. We build new applications in micro-service architecture with scaling and reliability in mind. Some tools we are currently using include Python, JavaScript, MongoDB, Elasticsearch, Docker, AWS, and Datadog.

Responsibilities:

  • Build scalable web services and APIs using Flask (Python) and Nginx
  • Build fully responsive web applications using ReactJS (JavaScript)
  • Build database models (both in MySQL and MongoDB) that fit business needs
  • Deliver working products that meet business requirements on time

Are you a fit?

  • You have 0 - 2 years of experience building web applications
  • You have a bachelor’s degree in computer science or equivalent experience
  • You love coding and never get tired of doing it
  • You enjoy solving the hardest problems and never give up
  • You are always eager to learn what you don’t yet know
  • You are passionate about owning the product you build
  • You are self-motivated and know what drives you to deliver and learn
  • You are comfortable with working on a fast-paced team, and embrace new challenges

Bonus Points

  • Professional experience with Python and JavaScript
  • Professional experience with Django or Flask
  • Professional experience with ReactJS
  • Professional experience with MongoDB
  • Professional experience with AWS
  • Professional experience with Docker
  • Professional experience with Facebook, Instagram or Twitter APIs

Why join us?

  • We are fast-paced and agile
  • We expose people to challenges that make them thrilled
  • We build products that are desired by a huge emerging market
  • We build microservices that scale, using Python & Flask
  • We build reusable UI with ReactJS and Redux, in JavaScript ES6
  • We are early adopters of latest technologies, such as MongoDB, Elasticsearch, Docker, AWS Kinesis, Lambda, ReactJS, and Redux
