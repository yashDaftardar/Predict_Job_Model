Full Job Description

In this role, you will be expected to drive Epsilon’s mission to grow our clients' businesses through the transformative application of data. Your key priorities will include but are not limited to:

  • Build new features and feature enhancements; contribute to ongoing maintenance
  • Contribute to ongoing refinements of our coding style and development processes
  • Embrace solid, agile development practices, including test-driven development, infrastructure as code, and continuous integration and deployment.
  • Design, create, maintain and optimize a modern web application platform for user interaction, data visualization and workflow management.
  • Think quickly, communicate clearly and work collaboratively with product, engineering, QA and operations teams.

Minimum Qualifications

To successfully accelerate and impact the business objectives in this role, you must bring a fresh perspective with prior experience. Qualification requirements and experience include but are not limited to:

  • 1 year of professional development experience, utilizing JavaScript or Java
  • Experience with one or more mature Rails applications and building Single Page Applications (SPAs), preferably using Angular
  • Leverages design patterns to build durable, testable code
  • Conversant with object-oriented programming (OOP)
  • Embraces TDD and knows when to test pragmatically
  • Fully conversant with building HTML5 mark-up, CSS and the DOM
  • Experience building data visualizations with d3.js or equivalent
  • Experience with relational database systems, data modelling and SQL
  • Comfortable with agile development practices
  • Outstanding communication skills (oral, written and presentation) and strong interpersonal skills
  • High energy level, strong team player and good work ethic
  • B.S. in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or related field

Secondary Qualifications

  • DevOps experience with AWS
  • Digital Marketing and Advertising domain knowledge
  • Experience creating complex data visualizations
