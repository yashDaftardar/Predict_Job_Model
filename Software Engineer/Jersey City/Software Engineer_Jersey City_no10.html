Job Description:

Associate Software Engineer.


Role :

Fidelity Capital Markets Technology is looking for an associate software engineer to work on their Equity Algorithmic Trading System. This role will be hands-on development, testing and delivery of world class application. The Developer will work closely with other Developers, product managers and business analysts to move ideas from concepts to scale and bring these game-changing ideas to market.


Expertise and Skills you bring :

  • Passionate about the work and has ownership mentality.

  • Willing to learn new technologies and the trading business.

  • Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Information Technology.

  • Programming experience in C/C++ preferably on Linux.

  • Understanding of low latency programming concepts.

  • Knowledge on debugging and performance measuring tools.

  • Exposure to Shell programming.


The Team :

Work on a model-driven automated trading system that will compete in the current and evolving electronic marketplace by executing in markets and optimizing the collective marketplace, whether or not liquidity is shown, achieving optimal performance by re-architecting the algorithmic trading product to make volume and pricing decisions based on an event-based model.
