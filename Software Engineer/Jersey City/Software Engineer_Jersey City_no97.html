Do you have an eye for quality and detail? Do you want to be a part of the talented team of Software Testers? Your search ends here. Zyxware Technologies are looking for Software Test Engineers with minimum 2 year of experience to join our fast growing QA team. The person will be responsible for analyzing requirements, writing test cases, testing web applications and performing overall QA in the system. Selected candidates will be placed at our office in Technopark, Thiruvananthapuram or at Kochi. Outstation candidates can avail work from home option on a permanent basis.

Specifically, the person will be responsible for the following:

  • Designing and creating the architecture/framework for test automation

  • Test planning

  • Developing test strategies

  • Test estimations

  • Hands-on in both manual and automation

  • Creating Test Cases (Test Scripts) using Selenium Webdriver

  • Good experience on creating Test scenarios and creating/modifying/maintenance Test Automation Resources (Function Libraries etc.)

  • Ability to select test cases for Regression Testing and modify Tests and perform Regression Testing.

  • Experience on responsive testing, Cross Browser Testing (Executing test cases against various browsers like Mozilla Firefox, Google chrome, IE and Safari etc...) and Parallel Mentoring, monitoring and managing the performance of resources

  • Experience in UI testing

  • Experience in mobile app testing

  • Experience in SEO, Performance, Accessibility.

  • Reviewing and signing off the QA artifacts and reports prepared by Test Engineers

  • Tracking the new requirements for the project

  • Planning and arranging the hardware and software requirements for the test setup

  • Risk/issue management & alert the management of the same with suggestions to solve them

  • Handle internal and external stakeholder meetings

  • Ensuring timely delivery

  • Prioritize, manage and track project tasks

  • Defect tracking and closure

  • Contribute to the development, delivery and maintenance of technology-based business solutions

  • Test coordination, resource planning and management activities.

  • Implement Corrective Actions aimed at process/quality/cost improvements

  • Good to have knowledge/experience in automation using Behat BDD framework



2 - 4 years of relevant work experience.We are looking for someone with the following attributes:

  • Good Analytical Skills

  • Good Communication skills, written & verbal
