Job Description


ELLKAY is looking for a mid-level Software Developer to join our Research and Development team to help design and build core systems for our healthcare products and services.

As a nationwide leader in healthcare connectivity, ELLKAY has been making interoperability happen for more than 17 years, providing solutions that solve the challenges our partners face in today’s data-rich world. We work with the nation’s leading hospitals, health systems, practices, EHR/EMR solution providers, laboratories, payers, and other healthcare organizations as their trusted resource for data connectivity, data conversion, integration, data exchange, legacy data archiving, interfaces and connectivity, and much more.

In-office preferred but remote will be considered.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Work with a tightly knit team to deliver high quality and thoroughly tested software
  • Partner with business analysts to identify and understand business problems and develop product requirements
  • Break down product requirements into technical specifications, road maps, and tickets
  • Apply an evidence-based approach to fixing bugs and solving performance issues using debugging tools, memory dumps, and profilers
  • Provide great supporting material for your work like conceptual documents, code documentation, and release notes
  • Participate in supporting other team members and projects the team owns via code reviews, pair programming, and technical discussions

Qualifications
  • A degree in an associated field or equivalent experience
  • Experienced with C#, the .NET ecosystem, Git, CI/CD systems, and SQL
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Able to debug code and troubleshoot performance issues
  • Attention to detail in work and ability to identify ambiguities in specifications
  • Experience with testing methodologies

This is a full-time, onsite position at our HQ currently located in Elmwood Park, NJ. Remote work is available within the US.
