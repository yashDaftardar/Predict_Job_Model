COMPANY OVERVIEW:

TIAA is the leading provider of financial services in the academic, research, medical, cultural and government fields. We offer a wide range of financial solutions, including investing, banking, advice and education, and retirement services.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES AND DUTIES:

Risk Technology Sr Advisor
The Risk Technology Senior Advisor role utilizes advanced technology platforms to perform data quality checks and analysis for reporting to Risk Managers. Under limited supervision, this job maintains product knowledge across the organization's assets and investment portfolios to best utilize available technology tools to inform overall risk monitoring, managing and reporting.

Key Responsibilities and Duties
  • Works with vendors in the area of risk to understand and resolve issues related to the calculation of risk metrics and to identify how technology utilization and enhancements can improve issue resolution.
  • Utilizes technology tools to enhance data analysis and data presentation skills to identify issues or gaps.
  • Works closely with investment risk managers to gather requirements and design optimal reporting solutions.
  • Partners with the technology teams on enhancement requests and User Acceptance Testing to receive sign-offs.
  • Designs and develops automated, standardized reporting for investment risk groups, including visibility reporting inclusive of regulatory authorities and the enterprise's stakeholders such as its Board.
  • Ensures daily, monthly and quarterly operational processes and data quality checks are completed in a timely manner.
  • Supports the team's regulatory reporting responsibilities as needed.
