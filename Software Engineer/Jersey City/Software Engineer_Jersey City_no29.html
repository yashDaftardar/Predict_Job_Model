Market Risk Technology (MRT) provides support to the Global Markets Risk Management, Global Risk Analytics, Enterprise Capital Management, and Enterprise Credit Risk teams to enable independent management of the firm's market risk. The set of Market Risk Technology applications support the identification, measurement, monitoring and control of market risk within the defined corporate risk governance policies. The platform also provide input to calculate the firm's capital and regulatory stress reporting (CCAR). A substantial program of work is underway within MRT to comply with numerous regulatory requirements and to migrate to an improved target state platform.
