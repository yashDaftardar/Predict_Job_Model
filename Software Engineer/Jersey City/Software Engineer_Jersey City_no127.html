The Opportunity

The Live Streaming team at Behance is looking for a product-minded senior back-end developer to help us deliver more experiences to our users!

As a part of the Behance team, your work will affect the creative world every day. You'll play a role in improving the lives of millions of creatives and the industry through our product, the Behance Network. Our hardworking team of designers, developers, and community managers work hand-in-hand to create products that connect and empower the creative world.

Our back-end developers are tasked with delivering performant, reliable and highly scalable APIs. As part of this role, you'll work directly with designers, community managers and other developers to implement new features and improvements within our products. We focus on using best practices and state-of-the-art tools to create maintainable, modular and high-performing code. Being focused on building features that encourage creators to showcase their best work and easily discover the creations of others, you'll be involved in gaining insights about how our products are actually used and participate in investigating new ways to deliver more to our users.

Do you get excited about solving complex problems and enjoy the freedom of going as deep in the stack as the feature calls for? Are you a curious developer who loves to learn and experiment? From working closely with designers to solving interesting technical problems, this role is all about finding creative solutions to real world problems.

In this role, you will:

  • Build strong development skills. Utilize state-of-the-art technologies to build modular and maintainable features and products.
  • Work with a dynamic team. You'll work most closely with our designers, community managers, and other software engineers.
  • Build features that make our products easier to use. We're constantly experimenting with how to improve our products to benefit our users, and you'll help track metrics and formulate ideas to improve and increase adoption.

What you'll Do :

  • Build new features using NodeJS, Express.js and serverless cloud technologies.
  • Work with 3rd party services to ensure stability, quality of service and new feature roadmap.
  • Continuously enhance the application in order to reduce costs, and improve system performance.
  • Use our continuous deployment and feature-flagging platforms to push code to production early and often.
  • Influence design thinking among development team. You have an appreciation for design-thinking and you believe that creating reusable code and consistent user experiences that work well together can empower engineers and designers to create better products.
  • Participate in pair programming and peer reviews. You'll collaborate with other engineers on problems of all shapes and sizes, from code to new ideas and methodologies.
  • Provide input on how to make the products more usable. You will use our analytics tools, user research, and in-house community team to capture information and gain insights as to how people use Behance to drive new ideas.

What you need to succeed:

  • Experience in working in cloud based environments such as AWS/Azure/Google Cloud.
  • Programming experience with NodeJS, Express.js, and MySQL
  • Strong troubleshooting and problem solving skills.
  • Desire to build large scale systems and influence how they impact the product and engineering process.
  • Desire to work with everyone from engineering, to design, to product management to take a project from concept to life.
  • You want to build important, appealing products that empower the creative world.
