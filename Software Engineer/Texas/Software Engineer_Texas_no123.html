The Systems Safety Engineer is responsible for ensuring that the products developed at Lockheed Martin Missiles and Fire Control and sold to the customer are safe for use. The primary job responsibility will be that of performing Hazard Analysis (Preliminary Hazard Analysis (PHA), System Hazard Analysis (SHA), Subsystem Hazard Analysis (SSHA), Operating and Support Hazard Analysis (O&SHA), Functional Hazard Analysis, Software Hazard Analysis, Fault Tree Analysis)) per MIL-STD-882 and contract requirements.

Duties include:

Employ safety analysis techniques which will be performed on the engineering product

Develop System Safety for environmental compliance, prohibited materials, hazardous materials management, and pollution prevention in accordance with MIL-STD-882

Develop, deliver and update System Safety Analysis and Risk Assessment, and Hazard Tracking database

Flow down of the System Safety requirements to suppliers and audit/review of the supplier's analysis

Independently analyzes and recommends safety features and controls for system design, software, training and procedures to mitigate or eliminate hazards

Perform and support the development of safety documentation to support customer/contract requirements

Support the development of integrated system hazard analyses

Perform software safety tasks and activities associated with the requirements of AR 385-17

Work with systems and software engineering on architecture, Use Cases and software safety analyses

Ensure hardware and software system safety requirements are identified and documented in appropriate plans, processes and procedures

Assure the program defines and collects evidence in meeting established software safety requirements

Prepare and deliver presentations and participates in safety review meetings, system design meetings, program engineering review and change board meetings

Ensure hazard controls are properly implemented and verified

Participate in the development of testing activities to assure safety requirements are met

Review and provide input to Systems Engineering, Design and Software documentation to include requirements development, design, development, verification, test methods and performance verification

Review specifications, technical documentation, and changes to those documents for impacts to system safety.

Plan, host and support System Safety working groups to validate the system’s design to meet requirements

Work closely with customer to develop Safety Cases, collaborates hazard mitigations and residual risk and obtain Safety Releases/Certifications

Review Engineering Changes, support Concept Screening Board (CSB) and Configuration Control Board (CCB)

Candidate will spend majority of time working in a team environment, providing inputs to engineers, performing analyses, and writing reports

There may be some travel
