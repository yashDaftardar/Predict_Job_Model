Full Job Description

Reports to: Senior Director of Technology and Information

Our company is looking for an energetic Software Engineer to join our team. At Word on Fire, you will have the opportunity to work across the entire stack from frontend to backend. The Software Engineer will be responsible for the development of new applications and websites, cybersecurity and administration of ministry infrastructure, data management, and enterprise integration with third party services and APIs. We are committed to professionally developing our Software Engineers to be experts in their field.


Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Work with the Senior Director of Technology and Information on designing, programming, and testing code for applications.

  • Implement responsive designs adhering to the Web Accessibility Guidelines.

  • Write effective unit and integration tests.

  • Respond to cybersecurity threats.

  • Manage Continuous Integration and Continuous Development pipelines for new projects.

  • Manage ministry server infrastructure on Google Cloud.

  • Develop professionally by learning and employing emerging technologies.

  • Triage, diagnose, and solve issues with production software.

  • Write effective and thorough documentation for both internal and public use.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering with 4 years of experience as a Software Engineer/ developer, OR 6 years of experience as a professional Software Engineer/ developer.

  • Professional knowledge of data structures, algorithms, object-oriented design, design patterns, enterprise integration, database administration, and system architecture.

  • Experience with key web protocols such as the HTTP request and response cycle.

  • Experienced in unit, integration, and systems testing.

  • Experience with Android or iPhone app development.

  • Mastery in at least 1-2 of the following languages at least one major programming language such as Java, Swift, Kotlin, JavaScript/NodeJS, or PHP.

  • Experienced with key web technologies HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and the Web APIs.

  • Familiar with version control systems and how to manage code development with a team.

Desired Skills

  • Familiarity with the work of Bishop Barron and Word on Fire.

  • Commitment to the mission of Word on Fire, which is to draw people into or back to the Catholic Church.

  • Experience with the OWASP top 10 and web security fundamentals.

  • Experience with any of the following technologies: NodeJS, ExpressJS, Jest, PHP, WordPress, SCSS, Webpack, Google Cloud, AWS, GitHub, VueJS, Docker, MySQL.

  • Linux server administration.

  • Experience working in a team that employs CI/CD tools in a cloud environment.

  • Familiarity with the Manifesto for Agile Development.

  • HIGHLY desirable if the applicant has experience with mobile app or TV development.

Organization Overview:

Word on Fire Catholic Ministries is a nonprofit global media apostolate that supports the work of Bishop Robert Barron and reaches millions of people to draw them into—or back to—the Catholic faith.
