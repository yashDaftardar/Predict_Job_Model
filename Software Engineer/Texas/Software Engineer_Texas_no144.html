Caterpillar SPM Oil & Gas currently has an opening for a IT Developer II, located in FT Worth, Texas. As a IT Developer II at SPM Oil & Gas , you will work with technical analysts, corporate sponsors, internal/external customers, and other team members to implement the Infor Syteline ERP Solution that supports business processes, and are aligned with business strategies. This position facilitates the optimization of business unit performance by enhancing the alignment between business process and information technology, and is integral to any decision-making involving technology.
