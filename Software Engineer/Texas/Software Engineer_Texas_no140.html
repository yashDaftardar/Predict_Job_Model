Job Description
Position Summary
Our multi-disciplinary team based in Austin, Texas, is looking for a key team member in the development of analytical instrumentation and software for life sciences, forensics, toxicology, and environmental analyses. As a part of the Chromatography and Mass Spectrometry Division at Thermo Fisher Scientific, your work will have real-world impact.

A Senior Software Engineer is required to lead the design, development, and integration of instrument control software for our gas chromatography and mass spectrometry product portfolio. The successful candidate will be an experienced and motivated professional with a focus on the future. As a development lead, this position will require the ability to work independently, prioritize goals, oversee multiple code bases, and follow through on tasks to completion. This position reports directly to the Manager of Software Engineering.

Key Duties and Responsibilities

  • Work with a multi-disciplinary team of engineers and scientists to design innovative analytical instruments which are used in forensics, toxicology, environmental analyses, and life sciences
  • Define system requirements based on market needs, evaluate development efforts, and perform design work as needed.
  • Develop instrument control software for mass spectrometers, autosamplers, gas chromatographs, and other scientific instruments.
  • Provide leadership in new product development and introduction (NPDI) projects
  • Assist in coordinating and conducting unit testing, design patterns, code reviews, and software verification to ensure the quality of developed software

  • Other miscellaneous duties as assigned.

Minimum Requirements/Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in a computer science discipline with 5+ years of applicable experience, or an advanced degree with 3+ years of applicable experience.
  • Significant practical experience developing for Microsoft .NET using C#
  • Significant practical experience with SQL and XML
  • Excellent communications skills
  • Strong decision making skills
  • Creativity with a focus on the future
  • Ability to work independently and multi-task effectively
  • Ability to work in a diverse team of professionals
  • Demonstrated focus on customer experience and product quality.

Desired Qualifications

  • Practical experience developing with C++, MFC, COM, and/or Python highly desirable
  • Knowledge and experience working with RESTful API, HTML5, JSON, JSON-LD, and PHP highly desirable.
  • Knowledge and experience working with scientific instruments desirable
  • Chromatography and/or Mass Spectrometry experience desirable
  • Passion for continued learning and professional growth
  • Demonstrate Thermo Fisher Scientific values – Integrity, Intensity, Innovation, and Involvement
