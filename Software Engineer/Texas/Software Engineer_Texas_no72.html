Full Job Description

At BioWare, our mission is to create worlds of adventure, conflict, and companionship that inspire you to become the hero of your story. Our teams inspire creativity using tech and tools such as the Frostbite engine and work with thousands of developers across EA's network of studios. We are passionate about games and their potential as a creative medium to reach and inspire people through emotion, artistry, and engaging storytelling. If you share this passion, this is an amazing opportunity to help shape the next generation of gaming.

What the Gameplay Programmer will do at BioWare:
You will work with our animation, design, and programming teams to develop the technology necessary to delight our players with fantastic new gameplay experiences . You will report to our Lead Engine Programmer.

Who You Are:
You are a collaborative developer with several years of experience developing systems for AAA console and PC video games. You have the capability to guide the development of technology features using solutions. You want to help us build the best games in the industry.

Responsibilities:
Work with artists and designers on solutions that further our games' vision .
Develop a variety of gameplay systems that meet technical requirements and achieve the creative vision.
Leverage and extend existing engine systems to support new and improved gameplay features.

Requirements:
3+ years of professional C++/C# experience and an understanding of software design principles .
Experience debugging and optimizing systems for AAA PC and console games.
Experience with gameplay, graphics, and engine concepts is valuable.

What's in it for you? Glad you asked!

We love to brag about our great perks like comprehensive health and benefit packages, tuition reimbursement, 401k with company match and, of course, free video games. And since we realize it takes outstanding people to make outstanding games, we offer great compensation packages and a culture that succeeds in creativity and individuality. At EA, we live the "work hard/play hard" credo every day.
