Full Job Description

Trend Micro, a global leader in cybersecurity, is passionate about making the world safe for exchanging digital information today and in the future. Artfully applying our XGen™️ security strategy, our innovative solutions for consumers, businesses, and governments deliver connected security for data centers, cloud workloads, networks, and endpoints.

Optimized for leading environments, including Amazon Web Services, Microsoft®️, and VMware®️, our layered solutions enable organizations to automate the protection of valuable information from today’s threats. Our connected threat defense enables seamless sharing of threat intelligence and provides centralized visibility and investigation to make organizations their most resilient.

With over 6,500 employees in 50 countries and the world’s most advanced global threat research and intelligence, Trend Micro enables organizations to secure their connected world. www.trendmicro.com

Responsibilities:

  • Work with cross-functional teams (Developers, Test Engineers) and create effective Test plans for various functional areas of network security products

  • Execute manual and automated test cases of TrendMicro Network security products and SaaS offering from Trend

  • Contribute towards enhancing test automation framework for functional and system test cases

  • Active participation in agile ceremonies to plan and execute software test plans

  • Collaborate with development teams in defect discovery, resolution, reproduction and verification

  • Setup of test environments in the test lab

  • Understanding new tools/technologies and implementing right test solutions

Qualifications:

  • Understanding of Linux environment

  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, collaborative Agile development environment

  • Attention to detail, work well in a team environment and highly motivated and willing to learn new skills.

  • Previous/current experience testing in a Linux and or Unix environments.

  • Experience with one of the Object oriented programming language (Java, Python) is a preferred

  • Good understanding of Agile development methodology

  • Familiarity with TCP/IP, 7 layer applications is a plus

  • Knowledge of cloud technologies are added advantage (AWS/Azure/GCP)

  • BS CS/CE or related field and/or equivalent industry experience
