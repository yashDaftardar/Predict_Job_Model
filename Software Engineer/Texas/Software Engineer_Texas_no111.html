Full Job Description

Software Engineer


Extreme weather, natural disasters, and bio-diversity loss….. what comes next? That’s right, data fraud/ theft and cyber-attacks. The world economic forum lists these as the top global risks in the modern-day.

Did you know, 88% of organizations struggle with cloud security, and over 44% of companies have suffered a cyber-attack in the last 12 months, at an average cost of over $4m per breach! Ouch.

These are huge challenges. Think you’re up to it?


Your Opportunity

Join the SailPoint team and be part of the voyage at one of the most exciting companies you’ve probably ‘never’ heard of. Before you say, “I’m not that into boats,” have a peek behind the sail and discover for yourself how we lead an industry we helped pioneer and how, as a fellow crew member, your impact will help solve for real-life issues and challenges around identity management, and uniquely helping companies protect themselves against data fraud/ theft and cyber-attacks.


We are evolving our vision by delivering future solutions in the Cloud and arming businesses with the tools they need to keep sensitive data safe and secure – the way it should be.

Do you have a college degree in Computer Science, Software programming, Cloud, or something similar?

Are you open to learning and adapting, love to code and tech-obsessed, look to continuously work smarter, a team player, and able to work independently?

Are you the kind of person who rolls up their sleeves and is hungry for more?

If you have answered YES to these questions, you may be exactly who we are looking for to join our crew! We have a fantastic opportunity to join our team full-time, beginning in the summer of 2021. You’ll be starting off alongside several other teammates from across the US to officially kick off your career with SailPoint. The multi-week experience will take place in our Austin headquarters and will prepare you for a successful journey on the team, all while learning about our unbeatable culture.

A little bit about us:

We’re a fun group (if we do say so ourselves) and also critical to ensuring our customers have the best experience of SailPoint. We like to work smart, so we can make time for ourselves, and we come from all different backgrounds (education, geography, previous work experience , etc.) and use this to our advantage.

As part of the Software Engineering team, you will have the chance to:

  • Hone your skills and develop in a technical role
  • Learn about the business of identity
  • Think creatively about ways to continuously improve how to develop and enhance products, features, and apps
  • Work with cutting edge technologies and be part of the SaaS journey for SailPoint
  • Work as part of a passionate, high performing team. Mentored by senior Engineers to help launch your career.

Join our Crew!
