Company Information
Ethos Group is a leader in the automotive industry with an ambitious vision for our future growth. We provide technologically advanced solutions which assist our clients in meeting the changing demands of the marketplace as well as developing strong foundations for success. We are also undergoing a large technology initiative which means we need driven people in order to meet goals. This position involves developing web-based systems that are used by both our customers and staff.

Dynamic Work Environment
We are dedicated to providing a world class facility for all of our employees. We have invested in a brand new 100,000 square foot corporate office in Irving, Texas which is surrounded by numerous restaurants, entertainment, and a live music venue. It has state of the art technology throughout the space, dedicated meeting and huddle rooms to encourage collaboration, sit to stand work stations, and a huge working cafe. All of this is housed in a light filled space with wrap around windows on every floor. Twenty five thousand square feet is dedicated to our IT department as it is rapidly growing and will continue to do so in the future.

About our Team
The IT team consists of members who work cooperatively in a professional environment that encourages initiative, positive attitudes, and collaboration. We provide mentoring to support professional development along with an opportunity to work independently as well as contribute new ideas. Our team actively uses the Scrum/Agile work philosophy creating an environment where you are valued, inspired to be the best, and empowered to make it happen.

Having a great company culture is one of our top priorities. We are involved in many extracurricular activities such as quarterly department events, sports, gaming nights, and camping. Working at Ethos Group is like working with family. We bond like friends and help each other achieve goals. It truly is a one of a kind environment.
