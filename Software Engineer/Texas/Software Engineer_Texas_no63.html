Full Job Description

Expedia Group is the world’s travel platform, looking to bring the world within reach. We are looking for a software engineer to help fully realize those goals by helping to build the platforms and products that give every employee of Expedia Group meaningful access to data and AI.

Our team enables the above by democratizing access to data and metadata, making it easy to find, understand, use and manage data assets across the entire organization. We need your help to help bring order and meaning to petabytes+ of travel data through outstanding user interfaces and build that platforms that will allow that data to scale.

As a software engineer, you’ll help drive the execution of features to collect, visualize, and aggregate data. You’ll participate in architectural direction, implementation, and delivery of our systems.

What you’ll do:

  • Code in languages such as Java/Python/Scala
  • Build services that communicate with the vast quantities of API- and stream-accessible data at Expedia Group
  • Develop features relying on event-driven data, microservices, and data science programming models
  • Translate designs and wireframes into high-quality code
  • Contribute to thoughtful code reviews, recommending ways to improve our processes, continually enhancing/refactoring code, and promoting great testing practices
  • Contribute to the architectural direction of our AI-focused products and platforms

Who you are:

  • Bachelor's Degree or higher, preferably in Computer Science, or equivalent experience.
  • Comfortable codeing in languages such as Java/Python/Scala
  • Comfortable collaborating on a multi-functional team including product management, architects, and UI designers
  • Flexible working on and understanding front-end frameworks and back-end services
  • Experience working in an Agile/Scrum team environment
  • Experience with Hadoop, Spark and No-SQL databases such as Cassandra, Elasticsearch are plus
  • Knowledge/experience of CI/CD tools (Jenkins, Git, Docker, etc.) a plus

Why join us:

Expedia Group recognizes our success is dependent on the success of our people. We are a global travel platform, made up of the most knowledgeable, passionate, and creative people in our business. Our brands recognize the power of travel to break down barriers and bring the world within reach – that responsibility inspires us to be the place where exceptional people want to do their best work, and to provide them the tools to do so.

Whether you're applying to work in engineering or customer support, marketing or lodging supply, at Expedia Group we act as one team, working towards a common goal; to bring the world within reach. We relentlessly strive for better, but not at the cost of the customer. We act with humility and optimism, respecting ideas big and small. We value diversity and voices of all volumes. We are a global organization but keep our feet on the ground so we can act fast and stay simple. Our teams also have the chance to give back on a local level and make a difference through our corporate social responsibility program, Expedia Cares.

If you have a hunger to make a difference with one of the most loved brands in the world and to work in the dynamic travel industry, this is the job for you.
