VEHICLE SYSTEMS SOFTWARE ENGINEER (STARSHIP)

This role is for a software engineer in the starship engineering organization focused on developing integrated operations and automation for suborbital and orbital launch missions of Starship and Super Heavy Booster product lines. Responsibilities include the over-arching conceptual operation as well as its implementation in automation that satisfies requirements and constraints of integrated vehicle and pad systems. Example projects include developing operations and associated automation to support Super Heavy launch/catch tower, vehicle commodity load, vehicle-pad interface systems, rapid launch recycle capability.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Develop countdown sequence for vehicle launch operations with inputs from vehicle and pad systems responsible engineers.
  • Provide operational impacts feedback on vehicle and pad hardware designs as they are developed.
  • Present automation at design and code review, take feedback from the team at large and incorporate into the overall plan.
  • Provide storyboards and CONOPs overviews and design reviews to ensure clear communication on how an operation is intended to work as well as provide a forum for feedback from team members.
  • Continuous improvement on ground automation to provide maximum flexibility for ground operations in a safe way, always looking for ways to improve operationally efficiency and flexibility.
  • Provide code review for launch ground support equipment automation (on things like propellant farm, gasifier, HEX, etc.)
  • Provide code review for vehicle automation team.

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Bachelor's degree in an engineering discipline, physics, math, or science field.
  • Experience programming in Python or C++.

PREFERRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE:

  • Bachelor's or advanced degree in computer science.
  • 2+ years experience writing code to control fluid or mechanical systems.
  • Experience writing and maintaining vehicle control software.
  • High proficiency with basic computer applications (word processing, spreadsheets, presentations, email).
  • On console operational experience.
  • Familiarity with software ticketing and version control tools (Jira and GIT).

ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS:

  • Ability to work long hours and weekends as needed.
