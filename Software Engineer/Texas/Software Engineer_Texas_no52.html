Frontend Software Development Engineer II

Are you passionate about Travel, Technology, and solving tough problems for the Customer and Supply Partner? Are you excited by innovation and at the same time committed to building robust, well-designed, and well-engineered distributed systems? Do you want to see the result of your work directly impact the company’s bottom line? Every month? If you answered, “Yes,” then we are eager to hear from you. We (Expedia Global Payments) are looking for top engineering talent to help drive the development of our payments platforms, seamlessly roll out new payment methods, and continuously raise the bar on our operational excellence and engineering practices. Our services are the nexus of a globally distributed system that spans many organizations. Our roadmap is deep and contains several innovative programs designed to make our services fault-tolerant, reduce the cost of payment processing, and improve both our customers (travelers) and partners (hotels, airlines, etc.) experience. This includes compensating for downstream services that are not in Expedia's control, providing visibility on what payment options are available and where, securely storing payment information, providing financial information and reports. We are well advanced in Expedia’s migration to the Cloud (AWS) and are now looking into supporting multiple regions and further scaling and solidify our infrastructure. This opportunity presents you with a great variety of technical challenges and the ability to have a tremendous business impact. We do this accurately and at scale, processing over $100 billion payments in 45+ currencies in over 155 countries every year across diverse payment options!

What you'll do:

  • Team up with peers across the organization to build an understanding of cross dependencies and shared problem-solving

  • Seek, learn, and use new technical skills in an engineering area

  • Share new skills and knowledge with the team to increase efficiency

  • Understand the relationship between front end and back end technology platforms

  • Develop and test sophisticated or non-routine software applications and related programs and procedures to ensure they meet design requirements

  • Write code that is clean, maintainable, optimized, modular with good naming conventions. You will also use software frameworks to write simpler code

  • Lead/clarify code evolution in code reviews and pull requests

  • Spot areas of inefficiency in code or systems operation and offer suggestions for improvements

  • Compile and generate reports on major operational or technical initiatives (like RCAs) to larger groups, whether via written or oral means

Who you are:

  • Bachelor's or Masters degree in a related technical field; or 2+years Software Development work experience

  • Proficient in at least one modern language (Java preferred, knowledge of React.js, Node.js, Catalyst, GraphQL etc)

  • Understanding of data patterns and storage

  • Understanding of software development theory and design

  • Understanding of how functions created fit into team projects

  • Understanding of lower complexity systems

  • A basic understanding of testing and monitoring

  • Experience debugging code

  • A basic understanding of how team`s goals fit a business need
