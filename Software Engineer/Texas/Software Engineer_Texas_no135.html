Full Job Description

We are seeking an in-house part-time or full-time web developer with an emphasis on application development using PHP, Javascript, and MySQL. This individual reports directly to the CIO and is responsible for content maintenance and assisting the Web Team with the ongoing development of existing web sites and rich internet applications, including page design and programming, site information architecture, traffic analysis, routine site maintenance and adding new features. This position involves fast-turnaround changes to a site under regular traffic and close teamwork with both in-house and remote programmers.

Qualifications:

  • bachelor's degree or 2+ years experience in web-based application design, development, testing, and implementation
  • Availability of at least 4 consecutive inhouse hours in a day totalling no less than 20 hours per week
  • Thrives in a collaborative work environment
  • Capable execution of interactive design based on provided concepts
  • Proven drive for completing projects in a timely and efficient manner.
  • Able to independently research new technologies and techniques to improve Website performance and functionality.

    • Skills Required:

  • Advanced understanding of PHP, MySQL, and AJAX communication
  • Intermediate or better understanding of basic web scripting (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • Intermediate or better analytical and debug skills
  • Working knowledge of security vulnerabilities and prevention

    • Bonus Skills:

  • LAMP server maintenance.
  • HTML5 Canvas
  • X-Domain AJAX
  • jQuery experience
  • eCommerce experience
  • Working knowledge of ActionScript 2.0 or higher
  • Experience with Version Control Software (GIT / Subversion)
  • BASH scripting experience
  • ImageMagick experience

    • Well-rounded, creative type with a talent for solving problems that may involve multiple markup styles. Flexibility, willingness to take on routine tasks as needed, team player, and a positive attitude are a must. Strong attention to detail and the ability to manage multiple assignments simultaneously desired.
