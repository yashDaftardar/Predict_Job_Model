Entry Level Software Engineer

Toyota Connected North America
Plano, TX

Toyota Connected looks for Software Engineers that will solve complex problems and work alongside talented software engineers, data scientists, and designers who have a passion for driving innovation in mobility, safety, and convenience services. We love people who think big and like to get their hands dirty to help us build exciting initiatives.

What will you be doing?

You will have the opportunity to work in a fast-paced Agile Scrum environment to develop applications to create real world solutions. Here are a few details about some of the teams at Toyota Connected:

Mobility: We provide flexible and on-demand mobility services to enable a new generation of entrepreneurs to build the mobility experiences of tomorrow. You will likely be working with Data Scientists, Machine Learning Engineers, Big Data Engineers, Software Engineers in Test, Scrum Masters, and Technical Product Owners. Some of the technologies this team utilizes are Hadoop, Kafka, Spark, Scala, Java, Python, Microservices, AWS, Docker, Kubernetes, and Terraform.

Safety: We built Toyota's next generation Safety Connect platform from the ground up to deliver safety services that save lives. You will likely be working with Software Engineers, Software Engineers in Test, Scrum Masters, and Technical Product Owners. Some of the technologies this team utilizes are Java, Spring Boot, Microservices, Salesforce, Docker, Kubernetes, and Azure.

Convenience: Demands of the day force us to prioritize mundane tasks that often rob us of opportunities to connect at a human level. We are developing exciting and new convenience services that will provide Toyota customers with opportunities to pause and focus on what really matters. You will likely be working with Machine Learning Engineers, Data Engineers, Software Developers, Software Engineers in Test, Scrum Masters, and Technical Product Owners. Some of the technologies this team utilizes are Machine Learning, Deep Learning, NLP, Neural Networks, TensorFlow, Python, Go, Microservices, TensorFlow, AWS, Google Cloud, Docker, and Kubernetes.

Shared Services Teams: Our Shared Services teams work with the Mobility, Safety, and Convenience teams and are vital to Toyota Connected. Those teams include Security, Technical Operations, and UI/UX/Mobile. Some of the technologies these teams utilized in Security are Okta, Cloud Security, Network Security, Application/API Security, Data Privacy, Python, Go, Terraform, Docker, Kubernetes, AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud. Some of the technologies utilized in UI/UX/Mobile are Android, Kotlin, iOS, Swift, Ember.js, and Vue.js.

Qualifications:

  • You want to make things work. Coming up with creative solutions comes naturally to you and you're comfortable being set loose without a lot of direction
  • You show a sense of ownership and pride in what you do, and you can make us believe you'll excel
  • You can easily point to multiple activities and projects that demonstrate how you go above and beyond
  • You're able to show empathy and work in a team environment
  • You have programming experience in Python, Java, Scala, C#, C++, Go, Ruby, JavaScript, Swift, and/or Kotlin
  • You are currently working on or have a Bachelor or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics, or related discipline; or have equivalent experience

What's in it for you?

In addition to an excellent compensation and benefits package, you'll also receive:

  • A Career Path that allows you to take your career to the next level.
  • Annual $2,000 Training Budget to help you grow and develop.
  • Unlimited time off and flexibility for a great worklife balance. At TC you'll be treated like the professional we know you are and left to manage your own time and work load.
  • Four months of paid parental leave. We want new moms and dads to have an opportunity to bond with their new little bundles of joy.
  • 401(k) with generous company match that is fully vested Day One.
  • Toyota and Lexus vehicle discounts to allow you to drive in style.
  • Annual Patent-a-thons and Hackathons to embrace your superior creative thinking ability.
  • Annual gym membership reimbursement. We have a Toyota Connected fit club that loves plank competitions!
  • Free catered lunches every day. We try to keep it healthy, but Fridays are "cheat" days, so you can get your carbs on!
  • Two fully stocked Nespresso bars for your coffee and tea fixes.
  • Stocked kitchens with healthy snacks including fresh fruit, almonds, mixed nuts, pretzels, trail mix, and flavored waters. Nobody is hangry here!
  • Fun game room for the gamer that lives inside you! Xbox, virtual reality, and Nintendo Switch gamers live here and are always ready for their next opponent.
  • No dress code! Come as you are. We know the smart people we hire don't need to be told what to wear.
  • Free covered garage parking to shield your vehicle from those sneaky storms and to protect your buns from the Texas sun.

Who Are We?

Toyota Connected is a new company created to infuse the power of big data and cloud intelligence into all aspects of the mobility experience so that driving a Toyota or Lexus is more personal, intuitive and safe. We create and enable technologies that delight, simplify and connect the lives of those who use our products. We believe this mobility revolution will empower our customers to use their vehicles in an array of exciting new ways. We support, celebrate and thrive on the opportunity to provide Mobility for All.

Making a connected life a more human experience takes humans from all walks of life. Toyota Connected celebrates and is committed to a diverse and inclusive workplace that embraces you regardless of your race, gender, religion, sexual orientation, skin color, age, disability, military or veteran status, big shot relative, or Instagram fame. Different makes us awesome.
