Full Job Description

What you’ll be called: Software Engineer Where you’ll work: KWRI Headquarters — Austin, TX or Remote Named a Happiest Company to Work for in 2020; one of the Best Places to Work in Austin, TX; and featured on the Training Magazine Training 125 list seven times, Keller Williams Realty International (KWRI) thrives within a creative and collaborative culture where transforming the real estate industry through technology is our primary goal.

What you’ll do: Our ideal candidate will have 3+ years of experience in developing web applications. You will have experience with the development of web-based applications and enterprise integrations as you will share responsibility for the success of both the technical and functional aspects of a given project. You will have both strong communication and collaboration skills allowing them to identify, troubleshoot, and resolve issues in a clear and precise manner to the development team, and also share solutions with a non-technical audience. You must also have a willingness to work in a fast-paced environment. Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
  • Create code/framework solutions that can be reused effectively and efficiently
  • Accurately estimate engineering time through proper channels based on personal skill-set and schedule
  • Responsible for writing code that follows best practices around design, maintainability and quality
  • Work effectively with a geographically dispersed team
  • Ability to adapt to projects that may fall outside of existing technology skills, and show a willingness to learn new skills
  • Perform code reviews; adhering to coding standards
  • Mentor junior team members
  • Work comfortably within a dynamic challenging environment within the parameters of delivery deadlines
  • Able to handle changes in priority and requirements, without loss of enthusiasm
  • Must have a collaborative and positive attitude
Minimum Qualifications:
  • Proficient with JavaScript or Python
  • Experience with JavaScript libraries and/or frameworks such as React, Angular, or Vue
  • Experience with programming paradigms such as functional, object-oriented, and procedural
  • Familiar with microservice design patterns
  • Familiar with Agile practices and methodologies such as Scrum or Kanban
  • Experience with building/supporting APIs, GraphQL is a strong plus
Preferred Qualifications:
  • Experience with SOAP & XML, REST/GraphQL & JSON
  • Experience with Test-Driven Development and writing unit tests
  • Experience with source-control tools and practices
  • Experience with working inside a DevOps culture
  • Experience with open-source servers such as Apache Tomcat and Apache web server
  • Experience with Java or C#
  • Familiar with database concepts (RDMBS and NoSQL) and systems such as Oracle, Postgres, MongoDB, or Cassandra
  • Familiar with cloud services platforms such as AWS, GCP, Pivotal Cloud Foundry, or Azure
  • Familiar with CSS3, HTML5, and client-side best practices
  • Familiar with Docker or Kubernetes
Who are we? Keller Williams Realty Inc. is the largest real estate company by agent count across the globe and is number one in units and volume in the United States. Founded in 1983, we pride ourselves on an agent-centric, technology-driven and education-based culture that rewards agents as stakeholders. Keller Williams Realty International (KWRI), is the company’s corporate headquarters located in Austin, TX. Here, through a focus on cutting edge technology, education, and products and services, we support our agents and associates to create careers worth having, businesses worth owning, lives worth living, experiences worth giving and legacies worth leaving.
