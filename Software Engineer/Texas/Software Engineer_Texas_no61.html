Software Developer Opportunity!

Locations: Open to any and all locations where we have offices or remote work.

Launch your career with a dynamic and innovative company! Computer Services, Inc. (CSI) is a leader in the banking industry, offering end-to-end technology solutions to financial institutions nationwide. And we are growing! With excellent benefits and a superb work environment, CSI is looking for a Software Engineer that has experience in working with web-based coding languages.

CSI is a leading provider of core and item processing solutions for financial institutions across the United States. This position would work on one of CSI’s full-stack software development teams to create user-friendly and innovative solutions.

Preferred applicants will possess the following skills, education and experience:

  • B.S. in Computer Science/Software Engineering, MIS, or equivalent
  • 3-5 years of experience developing in Angular, .NET (C#), SQL, and/or native mobile app languages (Java, Swift, Objective-C)
  • Proficient in HTML5/CSS3
  • Strong understanding of UI/UX design principles
  • Application design, development, deployment, and support experience
  • Strong understanding of object-oriented software design, microservice-based architectures, and containerization
  • RESTful Web Service development experience
  • Familiarity with hybrid app development frameworks (Cordova)
  • Ability to multi-task
  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Self-motivated to learn and stay current on new and emerging relevant technologies
  • Comfortable working in an Agile/Scrum environment
  • Good verbal and written communication skills

Employee Benefits:

CSI offers a comprehensive benefits package that includes health, dental and vision insurance, life insurance, 401k and profit sharing, tuition reimbursement, paid vacation and much more!

About CSI:

Computer Services, Inc. (CSI) was incorporated in 1965 with six employees and three customer banks. Today, CSI is one of the nation’s largest providers of dynamic technology solutions that help financial institutions and corporate entities remain competitive, compliant, and profitable. Our staff of knowledgeable, experienced professionals is among the best in the industry.

Today, CSI offers end-to-end technology solutions that range from core banking and Internet banking solutions to compliance monitoring and secure document management. As a company, we strive to deliver the best solutions the industry has to offer and back them with personal customer service that distinguishes us from the competition. Our efforts provide customers with a trusted technology partner, shareholders with a good return on their investment, and CSI employees with a great place to work.

We invite you to visit our website (www.csiweb.com) to learn more about CSI.
