The Space Dynamics Laboratory (SDL), a University Affiliated Research Center, is searching for a Software Engineer to join our team. SDL has been developing innovative technologies and solutions for cutting-edge DoD and intelligence programs for almost six decades. Our expertise is in space and airborne IR, VIS, UV, and RF sensors, small satellite technologies, test and calibration, cybersecurity, data visualization and assurance, and ground stations.


Key Responsibilities:

  • Analyze complex existing and emerging solutions through analysis of documentation, source code, demos, and various other resources
  • Work with internal and external stakeholders on all phases of the software engineering process: requirements definition and management, design, implementation, integration, test, and operations
  • Analyze and assess technologies that are whole solutions or components within a complex system
  • Work as part of a cohesive, multi-organizational project team
  • Serve as a point of contact for customers and contractors in various non-SDL organizations
  • Generate technical reports for delivery to the customer
  • Some travel may be required
