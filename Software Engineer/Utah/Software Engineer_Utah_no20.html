Job Description:

Who you’re committed to being:

  • An effective collaborator, comfortable working directly with native app client teams and indirectly with other cross-functional teams at Pluralsight

  • A frequent and clear communicator

  • A phenomenal teammate who gives and receives respectful, constructive feedback

  • Passionate about building reliable, maintainable software

  • Someone who continually improves and learns

  • Someone with a focus on quality and an eye for detail

  • Someone who lives Pluralsight’s values

What you’ll own:

  • Work with a team to deliver learning experiences to a worldwide audience

  • Balance stability and innovation to continually improve existing products

  • Work closely with customers and product management to build features our users want

  • Work collaboratively with developers building for iOS, Android, Apple TV, and other platforms by following the backends for frontends pattern

Experience you’ll need:

  • 0-2 years of software engineering experience

  • Experience working in .NET and C#

You’ll impress us more if you’re familiar with:

  • Building and monitoring APIs and services

  • RabbitMQ for messaging

  • Cassandra

  • Postgres

  • Visual Studio and Resharper

  • TeamCity, GitLab, and Octopus

  • Git and GitHub

Ways we work:

  • Software Craftsmanship - we want to be proud of our work. Come to utahsc.org

  • Test-Driven Development - we take responsibility for our code without QA engineers

  • Pair Programming - we value collaborative development

  • Continuous Delivery - teams independently ship code to prod every day

  • Kanban & Lean - no more backlog grooming, no more T-shirt size estimating

  • Continual improvement - we take time to sharpen the saw and adjust how we work

  • Autonomous & responsible teams - making their own product & dev choices

  • Cross-functional teams - collaborating through all phases of the product dev process

  • Customer research - we build what our customers actually want

  • Trusting leaders - who trust us to create and don’t impose deadlines or features

  • Solid technology - of the team’s choice, for the right job

  • Check out our Engineering at Pluralsight document for more details
