Job Description

The Utah State Board of Education (USBE) is seeking strong Software Developers to create innovative applications that impact the lives of students and educators in Utah in a positive way. We are looking for highly motivated individuals experienced in secure coding practices, Test Driven Development, Domain Driven Design, microservices design, and with the ability to incorporate/integrate data visualization capabilities into web design.

Principal Duties
As an IT Analyst II, your responsibilities will include:

  • Microservices development including domain modeling.
  • Utilize software development best practices to create software solutions.
  • Implement secure coding standards and practices.
  • Develop web applications using technologies such as .NET, C#, HTML, CSS, JSON, JavaScript, React.
  • Collaborate and communicate effectively in an agile environment.
  • Apply an engineering mindset.
  • Contribute to the team in analysis, design, and testing.

The Ideal Candidate
The ideal candidate for this position has the following:
  • Highly skilled and motivated full-stack developer.
  • Familiar with React and/or Angular or related frameworks.
  • Experience with .NET, C#, HTML, CSS, JSON, JavaScript, React, Angular, MS SQL Server, Kubernetes, Azure and other technologies and tools.
  • Experience using secure coding practices, Test Driven Development, Domain Driven Design, microservices design and implementation.
  • Able to be thorough with a high attention to detail.
  • Knowledge of agile practices and ability to work well in an agile environment.
  • Capable of applying systems engineering concepts, such as structured design, supportability, reliability, scalability, and maintainability to ensure that applications are optimized and loosely coupled.
  • Desire and ability to communicate and collaborate at a high level.
  • Strong customer service and customer relations skills.
  • Excellent communication skills.
  • Able to identify and develop creative solutions to problems.
  • Strong understanding of data collections and data flow.
  • B.S. in Computer Science of related field preferred.

Why You Should Join Our Team
