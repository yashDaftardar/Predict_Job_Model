Job Description

Our mission is to redefine the home experience through intelligently designed products and services delivered to every home by people who care.

Who Are We:

Vivint Smart Home is a leading smart home company in North America. Vivint delivers an integrated smart home system with in-home consultation, professional installation and support delivered by its Smart Home Pros, as well as 24/7 customer care and monitoring. Dedicated to redefining the home experience with intelligent products and services, Vivint serves more than 1.7 million customers.

Vivint was named to the Forbes list of “America’s Best Employers for Diversity” in 2020 and 2019, and to the Forbes list of “America’s Best Employers” in 2018. The company has a strong commitment to philanthropy and received a 2020 Gold Halo Award from Engage for Good for group volunteerism. Vivint is the largest tech employer in Utah and has received multiple awards for innovation, including being named among Fast Company’s “World’s 50 Most Innovative Companies.”

The Role: The Vivint Test Automation Team is looking for a Performance Software Engineer to join our fast-growing team.

What you will be working on: You will be writing fast and efficient code, finding inefficiencies, and influencing others to make performance improvements. You will be measuring and demonstrate performance results by building and running simulations. This candidate should be comfortable in discussing complex growth rates and engage in a project’s design phase for its performance and scalability expectations. You are the performance expert and can continue to drive a flawless customer experience.

Who you will work with: Our test automation team has been hard at work starting a cloud based performance test framework and integrating performance testing into our CI/CD pipeline. You will work with this team to get started and then primarily you will be working with individual dev teams that have performance requestions.

What we’re looking for:

  • 2+ years of software development experience
  • 3+ years in performance engineering
  • Strong programming skills in either C#, Javascript, or Swift.
  • Hands-on experience with performance measurement, evaluation, and optimization
  • Hands on experience building out performance test frameworks
  • Hands on experience with JMeter, K6, Gatling, or similar tool.
  • Docker and Kubernetes
  • Experience performing simulation to evaluate application and system performance
  • Distributed systems debugging
  • Ability to work cross-functionally
  • Passion for continuous learning and improvement technically and professionally
  • AS, BS in computer science or equivalent work experience

Optional Nice to have Skills/Experience

  • Database fundamentals
  • Able to model a system under load, identifying the correct benchmarks to represent it, and measuring the results
  • JIRA
  • Grafana
  • Demonstrated ability to identify root causes of performance bottlenecks
  • Jenkins
  • Azure
  • Linux
