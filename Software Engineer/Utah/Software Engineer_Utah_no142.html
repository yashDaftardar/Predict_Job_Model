Position Summary:

We’re bringing consumer to the enterprise. Be part of the team that breaks the enterprise hard to use mold to create an app that delights. Your work will be used directly by C-level executives at companies around the world. We’re tackling challenges no one else has attempted. Work with us if creating an app that solves problems at an enterprise scale and is as friendly and fun to use as a consumer app sound like fun to you.


Team Culture:

  • Work – If you find yourself always wanting to make things better, add a little polish, finish that extra feature, this job is for you.

  • Fight – Pick things up fast? Like to learn about a wide variety of subjects? Your ideas will be challenged. We expect the same from you. It’s all about making the best customer experience.

  • Innovate – Do you find yourself coming up with multiple exciting projects to work on at the same time?

  • Create – Like to build things? You’ll be in good company with a mind that loves to create new things.

  • Serve – We’re all about the customer. We expect you to be relentless in your defense of the customer experience. We’ll do the same.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design and implement cutting edge, data intensive, Android mobile applications. We use the latest libraries and design standards.
  • Work with Product Management and UX to deliver quick turnaround of new ideas and innovative features.
  • Help develop the team. Mentor more junior engineers, participate in code and architecture reviews, and help others to improve their skillset.
  • Improve the existing product. Look for opportunities to refactor and optimize the app to provide better performance and an improved user experience.

Job Requirements:

  • 6+ years of relevant software engineering experience, including experience with Java, C, C++, RESTful web services and SQL.
  • 2+ years of Android mobile development with Java.
  • Demonstrated ability to design and build dynamic multi-threaded mobile apps.
  • Understanding of the nuances of Android programming.
  • Experience with Android data management technologies, including content providers, loaders, and adapters.
  • Understanding of Android UI implementation, including custom views and layout, RecyclerView, and animation.
  • Familiarity with popular/useful 3rd-party libraries.
