Job Description:
Connor Group is a specialized professional services firm of Big 4 alumni and industry executives. Connor Group is a team of big brains helping financial executives with their most complex and significant matters, including Financial Accounting and Operations, IPO Services, and M&A Services. Our clients are the world’s top growth companies. We support them as they change the world! We currently have over 400 clients on six continents and a 50% market share for Silicon Valley IPOs in recent years.

Software and technology are one of Connor Group’s growth platforms. This team of engineers provides a spectrum of technology services which leverage Connor Group’s deep accounting expertise in Technical Accounting, IPOs, M&As, and Financial Operations to deliver technical solutions of unmatched value. We create, operate, and support groundbreaking companies. We work on multiple ideas simultaneously. Some ideas improve business operations for our partners. Other ideas are sold and find new homes where they can evolve independently. The very best ideas become new companies!

We are looking for a talented Senior Software Engineer to join our team, who will deliver high-quality tools and impactful features to match our growth. We use the latest tools and technologies to create high performance applications/services that run at scale.

Responsibilities:
  • Design and implement new web apps to help various groups fulfill their tasks
  • Modify existing apps to make them better
  • Participate in code reviews and disseminate best practices
  • Write unit tests/integration tests (nice to have)
  • Support critical bugs and issues
  • Work in a highly collaborative Agile development process
  • Develop solutions to high performance and scalability standards
  • Interact and communicate well with multiple cross-functional teams
Skills / Experience:
  • Strong understanding of complex technology environments, tools, and processes.
  • Senior experience of software design patterns and architectural principles
  • Senior experience with OO languages: C#, .NET Core
  • Senior experience with web development: HTML, JavaScript
  • Technical understanding of web technologies, SQL & NoSQL, Cloud/Azure/AWS, virtualization, networking, DevOps, and modern deployment automation.
  • 5+ years in software engineering development
  • Strong problem-solving and critical-thinking skills
  • Ability to thrive in an environment that requires:
    • Self-management and accountability
    • Flexibility in a rapidly evolving company
    • Proactive leadership
    • Creativity and courage
    • Strong verbal and written communication
    • Quick self-learning

Travel:
As needed, up to 10%. International travel may be required.
