JOB SUMMARY:

Vivint’s Core Apps Team has an opening for a technical position as a software quality assurance engineer. Primary responsibilities will include converting manual backend test cases into automated test cases, owning and triaging automation results, working closely with the developers and QA engineers. This individual should be self-driven to execute tasks with limited supervision.

JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Conform to automation team standards and utilize existing frameworks to convert manual test cases into automated test cases
  • Passionate about technology and the customer experience
  • Self-manage projects and tasks
  • Able to demonstrate solid software development practices
  • Take ownership of test automation for specific components and features
  • Accelerate the innovation and delivery of products
  • Play an integral role in the agile software development lifecycle
  • Work closely with developers and QA Engineers to reduce regression time and provide valuable automation for products

REQUIRED SKILLS:

  • Familiar with software testing principles
  • Ability to make decisions and risk analysis to maximize coverage based on risk and time
  • Familiar with Linux, test automation, scripting and databases
  • Superior written and verbal communication skills
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Demonstrated experience writing test plans and test cases and have a quality mindset
  • Experience with Java, Python or other Object Oriented Programming language
  • Solid understanding of Object Oriented Programming concepts
  • Demonstrated and proven ability to provide high ROI for test automation

MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:

  • 2-3 years of experience as a Software Quality Assurance Engineer
  • Experienced with agile development methodologies
  • Passionate about technology
  • Self starter with good decision making capabilities
