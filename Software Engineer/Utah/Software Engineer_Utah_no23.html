Junior Software Engineer

Headquartered in Ogden, UT, Becklar is the parent company of AvantGuard Monitoring and Freeus. Our mission is to save lives through our state-of-the-art mobile personal emergency response systems (mPERS) and alarm monitoring centers. Freeus is an award winning wholesale provider of mobile personal emergency response systems (mPERS). We develop two-way voice mPERS that work both inside the home and away from home, offering peace of mind to seniors and their caregivers. AvantGuard is one of the largest and fastest growing monitoring companies in the nation. We monitor alarms (security, fire, medical, environmental, IoT) and we are are growing at over 100,000 accounts per year. What makes us stand out is that We Care FIRST about our employees and our customers and you get to join a team that helps save lives every day!

We are at the cutting edge of technology working in IOT and AI and with our rapid growth we are looking to add a Junior Software Engineer to our Solutions software development team. In this role you will be responsible for assigned programming tasks and using programming languages such as Node.js, C#, and javascript. You will support other members of the team in executing and completing projects.

In addition to a competitive salary, you will enjoy:

  • Medical, vision, and dental benefits
  • 401(k) retirement plan with company match
  • 3+ weeks of paid time off
  • Company-paid Life and AD&D insurance
  • Short-term and long-term disability benefits
  • Work-from-home ability
  • Tons of growth opportunities
  • Other perks such as free snacks, company parties and events, an awesome team, and more!

What You'll Do:

  • Build and provide support and maintenance for critical systems within an AI environment

  • Execute full software development life cycle (SDLC) using Agile methodology

  • Develop flowcharts, layouts and documentation to identify requirements and solutions

  • Write well-designed, testable code

  • Drive the planning and implementation of projects

  • Follow established SDLC processes and continuous improvement

  • Coordinate with internal and external stakeholders to support, monitor, test and troubleshoot software and hardware problems

  • Produce specifications and determine operational feasibility

  • Integrate software components into a fully functional software system

  • Develop software verification plans and quality assurance procedures

  • Document and maintain software functionality

  • Troubleshoot, debug and upgrade existing systems

  • Deploy programs and evaluate user feedback

  • Ensure software is updated with latest features

Skills Needed:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Technology, or related field or equivalent combination of education and experience

  • 0 – 3 years work experience as a Software Engineer or Software Developer

  • Ability to develop software in JavaScript frameworks (React, Angular, Vue), or other programming languages

  • Solid foundational knowledge of SQL databases

  • Experience working in Tableau preferred

  • Proficiency in software engineering tools

  • Ability to document requirements and specifications

  • Demonstrate understanding of how business requirements impact technical requirements

  • Results-oriented, creative-problem solver

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills

  • Work well under pressure for extended periods of time

  • Attention to detail, focus, follow-up and follow-through
