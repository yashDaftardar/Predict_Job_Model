DESCRIPTION


About us:
We have grown from a back-of-the-napkin business idea to a billion-dollar unicorn and are looking for leaders that can help us achieve the next level of success. Shareworks is the leading equity plan management solution that provides advice and services to employees and companies wherever they are in their unique financial journey. This billion-dollar-napkin has led to interesting, difficult, and unique challenges in scaling our technology, people, and processes.


Opportunities and challenges abound as we continue to grow!


We are:
  • Moving to cloud architecture and infrastructure from an existing monolith
  • Maintaining the agile principles that got us here but need to do so at scale
  • Continuing to embrace a culture of change as we transform our organization


We are looking for Software Development Managers that can lead our teams through these generational challenges and opportunities to help us get to that next level of awesomeness!


Culture & Values:
As market leader, the talent and passion of our people is critical to our success. Together, we share a common set of values rooted in:
  • integrity
  • collaboration
  • gratitude
  • transparency
  • empathy
  • excellence
  • strong team ethic

We provide you with a superior foundation for building a career where you can learn, achieve and grow. We believe a diverse workforce brings diversity of thought, and deepens our ability to serve our clients, investors and shareholders. We are committed to creating a culture of inclusion and belonging for all employees and celebrating the different cultures, perspectives and backgrounds reflected in our workforce.


Book clubs, social hours, games hours, hackathons, and learning hours are a part of who we are.


Who you are:
  • You are passionate about developing people and building strong, self-organizing teams
  • You are a fierce advocate for the people and values they believe in
  • You invest in leader-leader relationships
  • You build a servant leadership culture throughout the organization
  • You are a pragmatic problem solver who can identify process inefficiencies and find creative ways to eliminate them
  • You have a bias to action and lead by example
  • You don’t need to know how to code, but you have familiarity with best patterns and practices
You know that agile is a description, not a dogma, and that continuous improvement is achievable
  • You remove impediments, see risks, and communicate issues in a proactive way

What you do:
  • Provide hands-on coaching and mentoring to Software Developers and Quality Assurance Analysts (QA) for soft and technical skill growth
  • Manage multiple product teams composed of Software Developers and QAs
  • Foster a team culture that encourages ownership, innovation and continual learning
  • Remove barriers to keep product teams close to stakeholders and product to find value in what we build
  • Champion product ownership throughout the team
  • Guide teams with consistent application of an agile mindset into practices that matter and seek continuous improvement
  • Work closely with product managers and stakeholders to contribute to the strategic growth and scalability of the platform
  • Ruthlessly streamline value delivery and use metrics to drive improvement

ORGANIZATION


Preferred Qualifications:
  • Scrum Master or Coach Certification
  • Software Developer or Quality Analyst experience
  • Agile Leadership experience

While we thank all applicants for their interest, please note that only those individuals selected for an interview will be contacted.
