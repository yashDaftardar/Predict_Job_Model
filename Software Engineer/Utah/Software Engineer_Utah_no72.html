Full Job Description

The Office of IT is looking for an engineer whose primary responsibility is the integration of applications. It is a key position within the organization to ensure our systems are populated with the right data at the right time. This position is a mid- to senior-level that requires depth and breadth of business knowledge to design efficient and quality integrations. They will be called upon to solve difficult issues and step in to support legacy integrations with little documentation. The portfolio this position supports is deep and wide and touches nearly every administrative system at the University.

Education and Experience:

This position provides an opportunity for people of varying levels of skill. If you have a lot of education, experience, and skill, we’ll compensate you accordingly. We are looking for someone with at least a Bachelor's degree or four years of a combination of education and related experience. Different levels of pay are assigned depending on experience/education/skills. Minimum requirements include:

  • Early Career Engineer (Level 53): Bachelor's degree or four years of a combination of education and related experience required. Bachelor's degree and related experience preferred. Supervision experience preferred. (Approximate starting salary: $62,500 to $80,500)
  • Engineer (Level 54T): Bachelor’s degree and two years of related experience, or an equivalent combination of relevant education and experience required. Bachelor's degree and two years of related experience preferred. Supervision experience preferred. (Approximate starting salary: $74,500 to $97,000)
  • Senior Engineer (Level 55T) Bachelor 's degree and 5 years of engineering experience required, or master's degree and 3 years of engineering experience, or an equivalent combination of relevant education and experience. Minimum of 2 years of technical supervisory experience required. (Approximate starting salary: $87,000 to $112,500)

Skills, Abilities, and Knowledge Desired:

You are not required to have experience in all areas listed below. What you don’t know we can teach you. We are seeking the most-qualified candidates; the more you have, the more likely you are to be selected. Compensation will be commensurate with experience and skills.

  • Expert understanding of how to integrate both on-prem and cloud-based, SaaS systems.
  • Experience with complex ETL processes.
  • Expert understanding of Web services/APIs/micro-services and Event consumption.
  • The ability to quickly learn a large number of University business processes.
  • The ability to work in an environment where the data architecture and strategy is evolving at a rapid pace. Be able to manage legacy architectures while looking ahead to future data structures.
  • Experience with platform-as-a-service integration tools such as Dell Boomi and Informatica.
  • Experience developing in third-party applications.

The following skill sets and knowledge areas are of value to us for this position. We don’t expect that candidates will have experience in all areas.

  • Application Development Frameworks (e.g. JavaScript, PHP, Java, C, Business Objects & Informatica, PeopleTools…)
  • Systems engineering (including architecture, design, integration, deployment, and performance assessment)
  • Programming languages/BI tools (JavaScript, NodeJS, VueJS, PHP, C variants, PeopleCode, Bash, Python, Perl, Boomi, Informatica…)
  • User interface frameworks and technology
  • Operating environments & security, (AWS, Docker containers, Linux, CodePipeline, Windows, iOS, Android, file mgt, access/permissions, process mgt…)
  • Database security (object access, data validation, role-based access, SQL/NoSQL injection, audit log, password mgt…)
  • Database technology (SQL, NoSQL, eventual consistency, transactions, referential integrity, relational modeling, schema-free structures…)
  • Software testing, continuous deployment, test-driven development, continuous integration, continuous improvement, DevOps, Agile
  • Web security
