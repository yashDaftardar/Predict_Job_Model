Description

Purpose of Position:

The Oracle Developer provides expertise in writing and producing customized software in an Oracle environment to better support the organization’s requirements. They will follow standard methodologies to develop software to support the company in its business strategies and plans.

Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Provide expertise in one or Oracle technologies like PL/SQL, SQL, forms, reports, or Applications.
  • Develop custom computer programs in support of existing software systems.
  • Customize or enhance the functionality Oracle software.
  • Develop and follow testing procedures for new software.
  • Train users in proper use of corporate computer systems and software.
  • Works with third party software vendors to ensure proper use of software and that vendor standards for customization are followed.
  • Troubleshoot software bugs and formulate plans to correct issues.
  • Stay current with industry changes to software development practices and languages.
  • Analyze data for trends to support business requirements.
  • Integrate software from multiple third party vendors with Oracle software.
  • All other duties as assigned.


Requirements

Qualifications:

  • 3+ years of experience in Oracle EBS.
  • 3+ years of experience in Oracle technologies like PL/SQL, SQL, forms or reports.
  • Knowledge on reporting platforms – Oracle Discoverer or Splash BI or Noetix.
  • Must know how to work in an Oracle database environment
  • Must possess excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Must be able to multi-task and keep up to date with new technologies and software development languages and practices

Education/Experience:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related technical field required

Certificates, Licenses, Registrations:

Specific course instruction in Oracle development or Oracle Certified Associate, Oracle Database Certified Associate or Oracle PL/SQL Developer Certified Associate certification preferred.

Supervisory Responsibilities:

None.

Work Environment/Physical Demands:

The work environment characteristics and physical demands described are representative of those an employee encounters while performing the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.

  • This position is considered sedentary and involves sitting most of the time, but may involve walking or standing for brief periods of time.
  • While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to stand, sit, talk, hear, use hands and fingers to operate a computer and telephone, and stoop/kneel to handle computer equipment
  • The employee may be required to exert up to 25 lbs. of force occasionally and/or a negligible amount of force frequently or constantly to lift, carry, push, pull, or otherwise move objects.
  • Specific vision abilities required by this job include near acuity at 20 inches or less due to computer work
  • While performing the duties of this job, the employee will experience a moderate noise level (i.e. business office with computers, phone, and printers, light traffic)

Travel Requirements:

This position does not require travel.
