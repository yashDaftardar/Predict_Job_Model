The Challenge:

Are you looking for an opportunity to not just develop software, but to build a system that will be used by our warfighters in the field? As a full-stack developer, you can solve a problem with a complete end-to-end solution in a fast-paced, agile environment. We’re looking for a developer with the skills needed to build enterprise applications from vision to production-ready system.

This role is more than just coding. We need a full-stack developer who will use their passion to learn new tools and techniques and identify and implement system improvements. Using the Scaled Agile Framework, you’ll work with the development team to build warfighter applications for timeline analysis and command and control. You’ll analyze the needs and the environment to make sure the solution you’re developing considers the current architecture and operating environment as well as future functionality and enhancements. Join us as we build systems to change national security for the better. This position is open to remote delivery anywhere within the U.S., to include the District of Columbia.

Empower change with us.

You Have:

  • 3+ years of experience with programming languages, including Java, Python, and JavaScript
  • Experience with Web protocols
  • Experience with interfacing software with databases, including Oracle, MS SQL, PostgreSQL, MySQL, or other non-SQL databases, such as MongoDB
  • Experience with the Agile software development process and tools, including the Atlassian Tool Suite
  • Ability to obtain a security clearance
  • BA or BS degree or 5+ years of experience with languages, including Java, Python, and JavaScript in lieu of a degree

Nice If You Have:

  • Ability to work independently and in a team
  • Ability to analyze and solve problems
  • TS/SCI clearance
  • CompTIA Security CE+ Certification

Clearance:

Applicants selected will be subject to a security investigation and may need to meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information.
