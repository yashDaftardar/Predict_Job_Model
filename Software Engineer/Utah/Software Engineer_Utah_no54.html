Primary Skills: Programming, Microservices, JavaScript, React, HTML5, CSS3, Angular, Java/Spring and Database SQL, Multi-Threading
Duration: 6+ Months (Possible Extension)
Contract Type: W2 Only

Job Scope:
As a Software Engineer, you will work with your Engineering team and partner with Product Management to take ideas for products and turn them into realized features. With the mindset of a T-shaped software engineer, you will work in an Agile environment and estimate assigned work, create OO designs, provide task breakdowns and implement features and microservices web technologies (JavaScript, React, HTML5, CSS3) and the Java Spring framework. You will use your coding skills to develop, test and release features assigned to you and your software engineering team. You will be joining a small team of highly regarded experts that provide accessible user experience services.

Roles & Responsibilities:
  • day-to-day interaction with the development team, project managers, and UX designers.
  • You will need to report progress and document results.
  • What You Will Do Lead the effort of updating or replacing legacy components with new React components while ensuring optimal application performance.
  • Collaborate with PM and UX design team to drive for the best possible end-user experience.
  • Optimize front-end applications for speed, scalability & reuse.
  • Meet with your team and tell them the awesome things you did yesterday and what unspeakably incredible things you will complete by this time tomorrow.
  • Collaborate with other software engineers to find the best way to test your work to ensure that, not only does it work as advertised, but will continue to work and perform at a mass scale when thousands upon thousands of people are using it at the same time.
  • Participate in discussions with your team about what emerging tools, technologies, and processes are coming that may impact how we improve our products and our SaaS platform in the future.

Qualification Required:
  • 3+ years of programming experience with 2+ years of recent experience in React.
  • Expert knowledge of React, HTML5, JavaScript, and CSS.
  • Demonstrate knowledge of Java/Spring and Database SQL.
  • Show that you understand basic and advanced design patterns and have the ability to write code for handling problems involving inter-process communications, multi-threading, parallelization, etc.
  • Ability to describe concepts and whiteboard designs dealing with high-availability and redundancy.
  • Demonstrate knowledge of Angular 1 and 2. Experience with GitLab, Java, Maven, IntelliJ, Yarn, npm, and git.
Education: Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering (or equivalent education) or equivalent work experience.
