Job Title: Senior Software Engineer
Department: Product Development
Location: Sandy, UT

We are looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join our engineering team. We are passionate about creating products that help our customers accomplish their goals in the most delightful way possible. This position would be working with co-located agile teams using the latest technologies. While we are looking for a full-stack developer, we would like you to have a strong penchant for front-end development.

About You

You are smart and passionate about what you do. You are accountable, self-motivated and always driven to do better. You have a high degree of initiative and are always looking for the best solution for customers. You have intelligent, risk taking tendencies - you are not afraid to fail, but fail fast, and learn from the mistakes making everyone around you better for it.

What will you do?

  • Design and develop high-quality software services and applications.
  • Be part of an integrated agile team.
  • Participate in the development process such as code reviews.
  • Support staff on customer/client issues.
  • Formulate new approaches, process, and architecture.
  • Mentor best practices and able to lead.
  • Become familiar with company engineering systems and tools.
  • Create new configurations and deployment strategies.
  • Participate in local community events such as meet ups and conferences.

Qualifications & Experience

  • CS degree or equivalent experience.
  • Minimum 6 years of experience in front-end development and object-oriented software development.
  • Minimum 2 years of experience with any Angular 5 through 11 and/or similar frameworks.
  • Strong knowledge of databases including relational, NoSQL, and file-backed stores.
  • Strong understanding of SDLC
  • Experience with C#
  • Experience with Docker
  • Experience with Agile Methodology and TDD.
  • Experience with ETL infrastructures and processes.
  • Experience with MVC and Common Design Patterns.
  • Experience with Windows/Linux Shell scripting or similar scripting tools.
  • Experience with AWS/Cloud a plus.
  • Experience with Angular with Webpack and CLI a plus
  • Familiarity with MicroServices/Modularity deployment and architecture.

About Crownpeak

Founded in January 2001, Crownpeak provides the leading, enterprise-grade, cloud-first Digital Experience Management (DXM) platform. The Crownpeak DXM platform empowers Fortune 2000 companies to quickly and easily create, deploy, and optimize customer experiences across global digital touchpoints at scale. Besides featuring content management, personalization, search, and delivery, it is the only digital experience platform that includes built-in Digital Quality Management (DQM) to ensure brand integrity, best practices, and website accessibility compliance. In 2017, Crownpeak acquired Evidon, the leading provider to technical solutions to complex Digital Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC) challenges. These solutions, including the Universal Consent Platform and TagControl are designed to help companies comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and other new country and state Privacy Regulations.

  • We are champions of our customers' success
  • We are never satisfied with the status quo
  • We work with pride and purpose, and own the results together.
