About TCN:
Founded in 1999, and privately held, TCN increases the profitability of businesses globally on a daily basis, including contact centers, BPOs, collection agencies, Fortune 500 companies, and many various industries including education and medical.

TCN is a fast growing technology company and provides all its services over the internet in a cloud based software-as-a-service model. TCN's technology stack and culture is positive and forward thinking.

Some of the services TCN provides are: inbound calling, outbound calling, agent management, automatic call distributing, call recording, agent optimization, reporting, artificial intelligence (smart agents, chat bots) among other things.
