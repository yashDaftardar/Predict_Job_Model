The Challenge:

Are you looking for an opportunity to not just develop software, but to build a system that will support the Air Force's ability to solve problems? As a full-stack developer, you can solve a problem with a complete end-to-end solution in a fast-paced, agile environment. We're looking for a developer with the advanced skills needed to build various systems from vision to production-ready system.

This role is more than just coding. We need a full-stack developer who will use their passion to learn new tools and techniques, identify and implement system improvements, and mentor teammates. Using Agile, Java, C#, and other technologies, you'll build various technology solutions. You'll analyze the needs and the environment to make sure the solution you're developing considers the current architecture and operating environment, as well as future functionality and enhancements. Join us as we build systems to change national security for the better. This position is open to remote delivery anywhere within the U.S., to include the District of Columbia.

Empower change with us.

You Have:

  • 5+ years of experience with programming languages, including Java, C++, C#, Python, JavaScript, and Perl and standard libraries
  • Experience with SOAP or Web protocols or data transfer protocols
  • Experience in interfacing software with databases, including Oracle, MS SQL, PostgreSQL, MySQL, or non-SQL databases, such as MongoDB
  • Experience with the Agile software development process and tools, including the Atlassian Tool Suite
  • Ability to obtain a security clearance
  • BA or BS degree or 7+ years of experience with programming languages, including Java, C++, C#, Python, JavaScript, and Perl and standard libraries in lieu of a degree

Nice If You Have:

  • Ability to work independently and in a team
  • Ability to analyze and solve problems
  • TS/SCI clearance
  • MA or MS degree in CS or Engineering
  • CompTIA Security CE+ Certification

Clearance:

Applicants selected will be subject to a security investigation and may need to meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information.
