Full Job Description

Orbit is the world’s leading retail brand for outdoor watering products and is a global thought leader in water conservation. The Orbit, Hydro-Rain, and B-hyve brands deliver innovation, ease-of-use, unmatched value and Smart Technology to homeowners across 6 continents and 40 countries. Orbit was founded in Davis County in the 1970s and is an active supporter of the community.

We are looking to fill our Firmware Engineer position! The Firmware Engineer works in collaboration with a dynamic design team and is responsible for the conceptualization, development, testing, and maintenance of firmware for new and existing products. This position provides technical and team leadership and will oversee communication of processes as well as timelines for various Company and departmental initiatives.

Direct applicants only, please!

If you feel that you’re the right match for the position then apply immediately! If you don’t feel you’re the right match then refer a friend, and check back for future job postings. We are always searching for new talent!


Our Expectations from You

  • Oversees communication between all electronic devices ensuring compatibility with hub and app
  • Designs and develops embedded firmware for products using microcontroller-based, pc-based, and other platforms
  • Works with and supports the development team in conceptualizing and development of firmware for new products
  • Develops, communicates and documents project timelines, strategies, and plans
  • Supports project initiatives collaborating in conjunction with the rest of the design team ensuring that development milestones and deadlines are met
  • Analyzes and provides feedback on peer designs in an open and collaborative design review forum
  • Creates and/or reviews product documentation for accuracy including documentation intended for customer use.
  • Evaluates product non-conformances and recommends design modifications
  • Evaluates, tests, and reviews designs to determine if program will perform according to requirements and specifications
  • Performs maintenance on existing products and firmware
  • Assists with new product designs
  • Follows processes for developing and maintaining firmware including proper revision control
  • Participate in code reviews on various projects
  • Research appropriate technologies for a given project
  • Assists with hardware selection, chipsets etc.
  • Determines directions taken for new technology
  • Supports product certification testing

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or other relevant discipline or equivalent combination of education and experience
  • 3-5 years of design engineering experience
  • Proficient with C language development including the use of cross-compile and debug tools.
  • Expertise in working in an embedded environment. Ability to work in Linux, OSX and Windows environments
  • Experience working with embedded RTOS’s, specifically FreeRTOS, ThreadX and MicroC
  • Experience working with real-time operating systems
  • Experience with real-time signal processing and hardware
  • Experience working with various communication and networking protocols
  • Experience with RF technology

Preferred Qualifications

  • While not required, knowledge of C++, Java, Python, Java Script, and Node.js would be beneficial
