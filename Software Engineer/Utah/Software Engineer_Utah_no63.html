The Role:

Sr. Software Engineer

What you will be working on:

You will be working on TechGenie, Vivint’s proprietary mobile application for Field Service Technicians who install Vivint Smart Home products. In your role you will help the team develop and implement a migration from existing legacy monolithic backend services to a distributed Microservices architecture running in a Kubernetes cluster.

Who you will work with:

You will work with a dedicated team of 7 developers and 4 QA in a fast paced environment.

What we’re looking for:

  • Expert in C#
  • Familiarity with Design Patterns
  • Unit Testing and Test Frameworks
  • Exposure to both Microservice and Monolithic applications w/ any recent migration experience is a plus.
  • Knowledge and experience with Kubernetes
  • Knowledge of common tools like Git, Jenkins, Postman and Jira
  • iOS development knowledge and Javascript frameworks for mobile apps a plus but not required.
