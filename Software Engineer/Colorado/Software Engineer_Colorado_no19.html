Job Description

We believe if you do what you love, you’ll love what you do. There’s a lot to love at Workday Boulder. Part of a global, high-growth technology company, our Workday Boulder team has the unique opportunity of developing the next generation of Workday’s game-changing collaborative products supporting a customer base of more than 31 million strong. You will solve complex problems and influence teams across Workday as you build enterprise software. Thrive in our fun, people-first culture that creates an environment focused on your success and ability to do your best work. Join us as we change the way millions of people work. The Drive team is looking for exceptional client engineers to help build out the next generation of Workday integrations with a focus on data analysis, discovery and decision making capabilities for our customers. You will join our team in Boulder to help iterate and enhance the React/Redux Drive application for new integrations within Workday. You'd be involved in bringing the product from idea to delivery and beyond.

You have:

  • Experience in software development, object oriented and functional programming principles.

  • Prior experience in a technical role.

  • Understanding of full-stack software engineering, with a preference for experience with front-end technologies (JavaScript or TypeScript and CSS/HTML) and applications.

  • A strong focus on front-end application testing experience.

  • Candidates ideally will be proficient in understanding and authoring TypeScript code, and have a proven track record of delivering production quality applications.

  • Candidates will have built delightful user experiences in their products, with attention to detail and well constructed micro-interactions that are efficient and intuitive.

  • Experience working with designers and taking into account user feedback, while clearly communicating limitations and capabilities of underlying technology platforms.

About the Role: ·

  • Work closely with product management, creative and peers to prototype, implement and test an entire product.

  • Continually test, refactor and maintain code.

  • Perform code reviews.

  • Contribute ideas for continually improving the team's productivity, job enjoyment and code quality.

  • Propose and execute on mini-research/prototype projects that introduce an innovative solution to new or existing problems.

  • Have fun engineering software!

About You: ·

  • Strong collaboration skills accomplishing tasks will often require working across teams and coordinating work on your own team.

  • Proficiency in writing stable, robust and reliable unit, integration and system level testing.

  • Passionate for creating scalable and maintainable software.

  • Desire to learn and mentor.

  • Ability to take a technical leadership role.
