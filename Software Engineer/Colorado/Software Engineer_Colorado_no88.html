SOFTWARE ENGINEER I

Envirofit International
Fort Collins, CO

Envirofit International is a social enterprise that designs, develops, and distributes clean energy cookstoves that reduce pollution and enhance energy efficiency in developing countries. Envirofit’s enterprise-based model represents a new approach to development through the design and sale of high quality, affordable, consumer driven products that yield health, environmental and economic benefits. As a global leader in the development of clean cookstoves and a pioneer in the international social enterprise space, Envirofit is on the cutting edge of the design and delivery of clean energy products for emerging economies.

Join a dynamic team driven to deliver a triple bottom line impact. We are looking for leaders across the world, passionate about improving livelihoods for those without access to clean energy, to work in our headquarters office in Fort Collins, Colorado and our regional offices in Kenya, Nigeria, Honduras, Guatemala, Mexico, and India.

As we are rapidly scaling business across Asia, Africa, and Latin America, we are seeking a Program Manager to work with fast-paced teams to help deliver on products that meet customer needs to ensure Envirofit is consistently producing the highest quality lowest cost clean cookstoves in the world.
