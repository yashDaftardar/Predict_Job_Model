Summary

Posted: Jan 29, 2021
Role Number: 200050593
Apple's Technology Development Group is working to solve some of the most ambitious problems in AR and computer graphics. Do you have a desire to tackle some of the biggest open questions in CG as well as reach millions of people? Here you will have the chance to work with accomplished engineers, researchers and artists from across academia and entertainment to help craft ground breaking new products that open new avenues and build significant opportunities for the world.
Key Qualifications
  • At least 2 years of relevant experience
  • Strong ability for writing high performance code in C or C++
  • 3D math or other numerical experience
  • A strong drive to solve novel and challenging problems
  • Experience with iOS development or embedded hardware is a plus
  • Advanced algorithmic experience in either 3D geometry deformation, animation, ML, simulation or rendering is a plus
  • Proven experience developing systems for high fidelity content is a plus
  • Demonstrable & notable public work in the areas of visual effects, video games is a plus
Description
Responsibilities include: - Design and develop complex software systems and APIs - Collaborate with other Apple teams to integrate state of the art research - Drive technical direction of the product
Education & Experience
BSCS or equivalent industry work experience
