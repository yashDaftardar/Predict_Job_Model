Software Engineer - Quantitative Modeler

Ascend Analytics, LLC
Boulder, CO 80302

We are looking for a Software Engineer - Quantitative Modeler with a serious commitment to energy systems modeling to join our team in Boulder, CO. If you want to help shape a new future of a cleaner and more cost-effective energy supply and work with an industry-leading software company with dedicated and talented people who are passionate about our future, this could be a great fit.

Ascend Analytics is an innovative “green tech” software and consulting company focused on energy analytics that are transforming the electric grid to renewables. Ascend's solutions provide the analytics to support critical power supply decisions from operating strategies over the next hour, to hedging and budgeting decisions over the next five years, to long-term investment and resource planning decisions over the next thirty years.

For more information, visit www.ascendanalytics.com

Job Description

As a Software Engineer at Ascend Analytics, you will be responsible for advancing software solutions and analytics to support the clean-tech power revolution. Your strong analytical skills and ability to develop innovative problem-solving solutions will support mission critical decision analytics for renewable and battery storage power providers around the globe. Responsibilities include prototyping new solutions and extending existing models. Required skills include knowledge of machine learning and AI forecasting techniques. Familiarity with electrical energy systems and markets, as well as experience with mixed integer linear programming is preferred.

Job Responsibilities

Solve complex analytic problems to make mission critical decisions for energy supply by:

  • Translating requirements into model formulation
  • Implementing optimization, forecast and simulation models for operation and valuation of renewable resources and battery storage
  • Develop and implement prototype models and support existing production software

This position also involves computational stochastic optimization, decision making under uncertainty, statistical learning, algorithms in statistics and finance applications, stochastic search, simulating high penetrations of wind and solar energy, optimizing generation under uncertainty, and planning and control of energy storage.

Education & Experience

BS or MS in Engineering, Physics, Computer Science, Operations Research, Economics, or Power Systems

Preferred Skills & Knowledge

  • 3+ years academic or professional software coding experience in engineering and mathematical modeling with languages such as MATLAB, Mathematica, Python, C/C++, Java, R, SAS
  • Financial engineering, time series modeling and statistics
  • Optimization, including constrained linear and nonlinear methods
  • Numerical Simulation methods, Monte Carlo
  • Data Science, machine learning
  • Referential databases and SQL
  • Ability to communicate with impact and confidence, both verbally and in writing
  • Strong interpersonal skills, teamwork skills, and work ethic
  • Knowledge of wholesale electricity markets
