Full Job Description

Description

Provides computer systems programming and analysis services. Under direct supervision, modifies application programs from detailed specifications. Codes, tests, debug documents and maintain programs. Receives formal training and on-the-job instruction in conjunction with a progression of increasingly more difficult work assignments involving flow charting, coding, debugging and documentation of application programs. Carries out work assignments designed to prepare developer for promotion to the next level of application development.

Examples of Duties

Essential Job Functions

  • Provides support of existing applications for outside counties.
  • New development programming and testing for requested and designated enhancements.
  • Travels to on-site locations for system set-up, training or maintenance as required.
  • Performs systems analysis studies and develops system specifications.
  • Analyzes user operations; develops system specifications; codes and tests programs.
  • Writes and tests computer programs.
  • Prepares technical and user documentation.
  • Trains user staff in operation of systems and provides user support; troubleshoots and resolves problems.
  • Coordinates system development efforts with user staff and other County units.
  • Determines technical training needs and/or receives training requests from employees.
  • Prepares class materials, schedules classes and either conducts training or arranges for instructors.
  • Monitors and reports on training budget; evaluates and reports on effectiveness of computer training.
  • Provides software support to County departments.
  • Researches and evaluates new software products.
  • Performs other duties as appropriate or necessary for performance of the job.
General Requirements
  • Fills in for other staff as necessary and/or required.
  • Attends meetings, classes and trainings as required or requested by supervisor.
  • Encouraged to recommend changes to improve procedures that would result in efficiencies, cost savings and safety.
  • Communicates actively, clearly and regularly with supervisor and co-workers on all work-related topics.
  • Understands, observes and complies with all Governmental, Pueblo County and Departmental policies, procedures and protocols when performing job duties.
  • Possesses excellent oral and written skills to communicate effectively.
  • Establishes and maintains effective working relationships with Pueblo County employees from same or different departments/offices, entities and customers.
  • Effectively manages multiple projects at once.
  • Effectively deals with stressful situations.
  • Easily adapts to sudden and unexpected changes in work assignments.

Typical Qualifications

Knowledge, Skills and Abilities

Basic Proficiency:
  • Technical knowledge of computers and networks.
  • Knowledge of the concepts and techniques of computer systems analysis as it applies to the requirements of the department.
  • Effective methods and techniques of training.
  • Experience with computer programming databases; other languages: .Net C#, VB, HTML, Javascript.
Full Proficiency:
  • Knowledge of programming languages and concepts.
  • Ability to perform technical systems analysis studies and write effective computer programs.
  • Troubleshoots and resolves problems.
  • Develops and presents training courses.
  • Establishes and maintains effective working relationships with users.
  • Ability to balance and manage multiple tasks when support workload is high.
Requirements

Education:
Associate’s Degree from an accredited college or university in Computer Science and two years of progressively responsible experience in computer programming and analysis required.

or

Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited four-year college or university with an emphasis in applications development required within six months from date of hire and no experience required.

Minimum of one year of successfully completed coursework in C language, VisualBasic, Java, systems analysis, calculus and accounting preferred.

Experience:

Minimum of two years of two years of progressively responsible experience in computer programming and an Associate’s Degree from an accredited college or university in Computer Science required.

or

No experience and a Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited four-year college or university with an emphasis in applications development required within six months from date of hire required.

Licenses and/or Certificates:
Valid driver’s license at the time of application and a valid Colorado driver’s license at time of hire required.

Special Requirements:
Must successfully pass background check.
