Come be a part of our mission! Northrop Grumman Space Systems (NGSP) is looking for you to join our team as a Software Engineer based out of Colorado Springs, CO. What You’ll get to Do:
  • This position is for a software engineer to support one of the Software intensive product teams on C2BMC in Colorado Springs.
  • The candidate will be responsible for software development in the Java language.
  • Designs, codes, tests and implements tools for operations automation.
  • Works on most phases of software systems programming applications, and will require instruction and guidance.


Basic Qualifications: If applicable, please note your updated security clearance and IAT/relevant certifications on your resume.
  • An active Secret clearance is required.
  • 2 Years with Bachelor’s degree in a related field; 6 years’ experience in lieu of a degree.
  • Experience with Java or C++ software development.
  • Understanding of searching/sorting algorithms and data structures.
  • Candidate must have effective oral and written communication skills and an ability to work effectively in a rapid paced, team environment.
  • Familiarity with Java foundation classes and toolkits.
  • Experience working as part of an Agile team.
Preferred Qualifications:
  • Automated test and pipeline development experience desired.
  • Test driven development desired.
  • Experience developing software for Unix/Linux and Windows platforms.
  • Experience with Software Integration testing including developing automated test scrips would be desirable.
  • Strong problem solving skills, including the ability to review performance issues, identify root cause, recommend and develop a solution.
  • The successful candidate will have good verbal and written communication skills, have the ability to work independently and will possess the poise and flexibility required to work in a highly dynamic environment.
  • Applicant should have good working knowledge of MS Office products.
