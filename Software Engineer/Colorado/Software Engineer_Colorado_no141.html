Full Job Description

Overview:
As a Test Automation Engineer and an active participant on a scrum team, the candidate is responsible for all aspects of quality assurance. The candidate must be proficient with automation and testing tools. This role works closely with software engineers, fellow QA engineers, and business analysts. Flexibility to work collaboratively and knowledgeably with team members of each area is vital. The candidate documents and works to resolve problems, reports progress on problem resolution, devises improvements to current procedures, and develops models of possible future configurations.

Remote options available!
Duties & Responsibilities:
  • Design, develop, and execute automated tests
  • Work with common test automation frameworks and tools
  • Integrating with CI/CD tools and pipeline (i.e. Jenkins)
  • Triage, troubleshoot and document defects
  • Black box manual and/or exploratory testing
  • Document QA procedures for team
  • Follow Agile methodologies

Requirements:

Required qualifications:
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Computer Information Systems, Engineering or related field and 8-10 years of expereince or equivalent
  • Extensive experience in manual REST API testing
  • Extensive experience in testing web applications
  • Proven experience developing test plans and test cases from requirements/design
  • Ability to document QA procedures for the team
  • Experience with Agile methodologies
  • Extensive experience with functional, system, regression, and integration testing
  • Ability to multi-task under tight deadlines and report status
  • Experience with Java
  • Experience with Katalon
  • Experience with TestLink
  • Experience with JUnit, TestNG, or similar RestEasy or similar API automation

Preferred qualifications:
  • Knowledge of the following areas a plus
o Git
o Intellij
o Kubernetes
o Postgres
o Section 508
  • Hands-on experience in working on complex distributed applications, understanding large complex environments, and knowledge of requirements-based testing
  • Excellent verbal, written communication and documentation skills
