Job Description:

Role : Staff Software Engineer

Overview

Commerce Solutions and Services Engineering’s mission is to simplify commerce by providing a platform for all customer segments.

We are the customer-facing teams within PayPal’s Commerce and Payments Platform building the touch-points that interact with our customers across all stacks (PayPal, Braintree and Hyperwallet), across all channels (online and in-store, web and mobile) for our Merchants.

We are looking for a staff software engineer to join our Multi Account Management (MAM) team. This is a software engineering leadership role. You will help us build a scalable and extensible Unified Onboarding solution to support our various onboarding API products. Using enterprise software technologies and frameworks, you will help design and build extensible experiences to support onboarding use-cases empowering our customers to facilitate global commerce.

Culture Fit
  • Drive to get results
  • Be proactive and anticipate/handle issues before they escalate.
  • Exhibit courage and challenge the status quo when needed.
  • Demonstrate a high level of curiosity and be aware of the latest technologies.
  • Show pride of ownership and strive for excellence in everything you do.
  • Express passion for helping businesses thrive around the globe.

Responsibilities

As a member of our team, you will work with Domain Technical Leads, Product Owners and Architects building new payment capabilities and improving existing products, developing robust software solutions and scalable products for PayPal’s merchants and Partners. You’ll estimate engineering efforts, prioritize projects, plan implementations, production deployments and triage production issues. You need to be dynamic, collaborative and curious, as you’ll work in a fast-paced environment where continuous experimentation and innovation are a given.

Additional Responsibilities
  • Contribute to the design, development, and testing of features delivered as applications and/or services.
  • Ability to go in-depth on the technology stack, across different technologies to understand end-to-end system workflow.
  • Actively participate in code reviews to attain the highest standards.
  • Drive teams to follow clean code principles.
  • Collaborate with peers and leads both within the team and across the organization.
  • Work with scrum teams and product managers using agile methodologies to deliver high quality solutions on time.
  • Work with operations teams to ensure applications and services are highly available, scalable and reliable.
  • Support applications and/or services as and when required in all time zones.

Qualifications
  • 12+ years software development experience.
  • BS or higher Computer Science or related technical discipline (or equivalent experience).
  • Proficient in backend development using Java, JS, or C++ technologies and applying good design patterns to build clean solutions.
  • Solid understanding of core JS concepts
  • Experience with popular front-end solutions such as React is preferred
  • Experience with Spring is required
  • Experience with build tools such as webpack, grunt, gulp, babel, maven etc. is preferred
  • Experience with modern test frameworks
  • Experience with GraphQL is preferred
  • Strong OOP skills, with ability to analyze requirements and transform into scalable and extensible software designs.
  • Strong knowledge of HTTP, REST API's and competent designing and building web services/microservices in an enterprise system
  • Strong leadership skills and the ability to lead a team of software engineers.
  • Strong ability to present technical solutions and new architecture to cross functional team members
  • Have a passion for quality and writing clean, solid, readable code that scales and extends well.
  • Ability to evaluate new technologies, weigh tradeoffs, and make sound technical decisions.
  • Proficient in SQL and other/NoSQL/modern database and storage technologies. Desired understanding of Oracle, JDBC, and ORM frameworks (e.g. Hibernate).
  • Competent in design/implementation for reliability, availability, scalability.
  • Thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic environment. You have a bias towards action and results.
