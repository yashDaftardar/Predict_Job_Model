Company Description

Microchip Technology Inc. is a leading provider of embedded control applications. Our product portfolio comprises general purpose and specialized 8-bit, 16-bit, and 32-bit microcontrollers, 32-bit microprocessors, field-programmable gate array (FPGA) products, a broad spectrum of high-performance linear, mixed-signal, power management, thermal management, radio frequency (RF), timing, safety, security, wired connectivity and wireless connectivity devices, as well as serial Electrically Erasable Programmable Read Only Memory (EEPROM), Serial Flash memories, Parallel Flash memories, and serial Static Random Access Memory (SRAM). We also license Flash-IP solutions that are incorporated in a broad range of products.

Job Description

CIM/Automation Engineer

SUMMARY

Designs, modifies, develops, writes, implements, tests and maintains a variety of heterogeneous software programming applications for Computer Integrated Manufacturing (CIM).

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Develops, installs and supports software applications critical for 24x7 manufacturing and planning
  • Functions as a key resource for fab customers and other CIM colleagues
  • Participates in the testing process through test review and analysis, test witnessing and certification
  • Supports future positioning for yield improvement and cost reduction projects
  • Performs a variety of tasks and works under minimal supervision

Job Requirements

REQUIRED SKILLS

  • Motivated, self-starter with the ability to learn quickly while delivering significant value to the customer (will hit the ground running)
  • A high degree of creativity, latitude and teamwork
  • Expertise with the following: Java, .NET programming, MS SQL, Windows, Unix
  • Manufacturing applications and factory automation expertise
  • Excellent communication skills, written and verbal
  • Ability to rely on experience and judgment to plan and accomplish goals
  • Familiar with standard concepts, practices, and procedures within the software development lifecycle

    PREFERRED SKILLS
  • SECS/GEM experience and expertise
  • Station Controller development and support experience
  • Expertise in process control systems such as SPC, FDC, APC and R2R control
  • Experience integrating AMHS
  • VMS knowledge
  • Statistical analysis expertise

    EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE
    BS degree, MS preferred, in related field required and within two years of graduation.
