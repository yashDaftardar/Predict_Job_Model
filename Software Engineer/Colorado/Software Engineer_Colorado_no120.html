About the Opportunity:

Odyssey Space Research, L.L.C has an exciting opportunity for an experienced full-time scrum master to support our Boulder, CO office with occasional travel to our Houston, TX office. Responsibilities will include tracking and reporting development of flight software for a human-rated space vehicle in an Agile environment. This role will report to the Program Manager and work directly with the Technical Manager and other technical leaders to ensure technical progress across multiple development teams. The expectation is that this position will be responsible for maintaining the technical issues database; enabling and ensuring traceability between the issue database, higher level requirements, builds and other continuous integration system artifacts, and verification artifacts; and gathering software metrics that are used to report status to the customer on a monthly (or more frequent) basis.
