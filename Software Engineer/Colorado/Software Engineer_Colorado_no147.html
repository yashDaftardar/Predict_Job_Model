Full Job Description

Role:

Join our team of .Net Software Engineers delivering customized solutions on the ESRI GIS and ArcFM Solution to Utility companies around the world.

As a member of our Professional Services team you will be responsible for supporting the design, development, and deployment of enterprise-level web and desktop applications using C#, ArcObjects, and the .Net framework.

The ideal candidate for this role will be eager for a customer-facing software engineering role with responsibility for tasks in the full software development life cycle from design to end-user support.

Primary Responsibilities

  • Support the design & development of enterprise-level software solutions by triaging, executing, documenting, and managing initiatives and issues for the Azure DevOps, ESRI ArcGIS and Utility Network, Amazon Web Services, ArcFM Solution Suite, and Oracle & MS SQL Server database technology stacks.

  • Write and implement code with a variety of technologies (C#, ArcObjects, SQL, HTML, and JavaScript) relating to extending and customizing desktop, web, and mobile based solutions for electric, gas, water and wastewater, and telecom utility clients.

  • Serve as engineering support to a geographically dispersed team that are primary contacts to a client base that is knowledgeable, technical, and often in operation-critical functions at utility companies.

  • Solve internal technical issues ranging from configuration and software defects to infrastructure innovation for next-generation offerings.

  • The ideal candidate will be able to take direction and immediately apply that knowledge to the task at hand independently, or as part of a solutions team, and have excellent problem-solving skills.

Qualifications

Requirements

  • Bachelor of Science degree (or within 2 years of obtaining degree) in Computer Science or Computer Information Systems preferred, or related field with computer emphasis.

  • Experience developing end-user applications and interfaces in a .NET development environment. C# coding experience preferred.

  • Experience developing end-user applications and interfaces with an object-oriented programming language.

  • Experience with commercial RDBMS such as Oracle and SQL Server required.

  • Must have experience in Windows-based software development tools, including Visual Studio.

  • High level of professionalism in oral and written communication skills.

Preferred

  • Experience with Azure DevOps.

  • Experience with Continuous Integration and/or Jenkins.

  • Experience with Windows Installer XML (WiX).

  • Experience in customer service support.

  • Experience working with, and/or domain knowledge in, electric, gas, water and wastewater, or telecom utility companies.
