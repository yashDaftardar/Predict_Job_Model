Job Description:

Assembly and Test Engineer Job Responsibilities:

  • Responsible for qualifying, characterizing, and transferring BSN products into production per schedule.

  • Interface with Internal (NPI, Design, FAE, Ops Groups) and External (Customer, Vendors) Groups

  • Drive projects milestones to support timely release of Test Automation files to production, and work as project lead for new test solutions.

  • Drive Cost reduction, and Yield improvement projects

  • Customer Support (product issues, service issues, quality support)

  • Develop functional test solutions using Python script

  • Analyze test script for repeatability and stability.

Requirements:

  • BS degree in Engineering with concentration in Mechanical, Electrical Engineering, or Industrial Engineering and 8+ years work experience. Or MS degree in Engineering and 6+ years work experience.

  • Work experience in electronic test manufacturing focused on high volume, high mix environments.

  • Extensive experience with Python language.

    • Linux working experience is plus

    • Network experience is a plus

  • Strong communication, analytical and organizational skills; attention to details in engineering analysis.

  • Solid knowledge in complex system build and test strategies.
