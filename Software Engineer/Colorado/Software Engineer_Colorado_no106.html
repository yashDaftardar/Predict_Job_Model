Job Description

About the Team

The Workday Assistant team is building a highly scalable, extensible, collaboration platform and services for Workday. We are looking for highly motivated, passionate, and experienced engineers to join us. Working in a team of fun engineers, you will use our standards-based technologies and toolset to expand Workday's business offerings. This position offers the opportunity to drive the design, development, and delivery of shared components and collaboration frameworks used by Workday customers. Ideal candidates will be exceptionally strong individual contributors with a passion for innovative software development, eager to learn about cutting-edge technologies, and have proven ability to deliver quality software with optimism and positivity.

Your responsibilities will include all aspects of software development management, from design and analysis to development, implementation and maintenance. You must be a proactive and hardworking individual.

About the Role

  • Work in across teams to deliver high quality software products & services that meet our business goals

  • Collaborate with fellow engineers to continually improve methodologies, standards, processes, and tools to make teams more efficient

  • Lead and mentor other engineers on the team

  • Be flexible and team player as part of a fast-paced product development team

  • Use your troubleshooting and triaging skills to provide assistance to the support and operations staff in solving escalated production and customer issues

  • This challenging position will require a self-starter who can effectively manage multiple projects simultaneously, meet aggressive deadlines, and who thrives in a dynamic environment. You can expect a high-profile position on a great team with many challenges and rewards

About You

  • Excellent knowledge of software engineering best practices, including but not limited to coding standards, continuous build and deployment best practices, testing, performance, scalability and operations efficiency

  • Experience with distributed and service oriented architectures

  • Expert in Java development

  • Have Object Oriented or Functional design/development skills and experience driving projects from concept to prototype to implementation

  • Bachelor's Degree or higher. Computer Science/Engineering major is preferable

  • 8+ years of software engineering experience

  • Passion for operational excellence who cares about their code and its customer/user

  • Product Delivery - designs and leads substantial features with a strong focus on non-functional requirements. (scalability, performance, monitoring, maintenance, security, usability and quality etc.

  • Leadership - mentors and grows other engineers. High technical influence within the team, with ability to lead technical & functional discussions.

  • Innovation - continuously suggests enhancements for the product and build pipelines. Leads and completes an innovative idea that enhances our products & service

  • Strong service ownership - responsive and drives resolution of production issue

Desired Skills

  • Spring Boot development experience

  • Experience leveraging Jenkins for software pipelines

  • Experience developing cloud software services and an understanding of design for scalability, performance and reliability, and tradeoffs therein

  • Experience developing distributed and concurrent systems; secure, fault tolerant, highly available, recoverable, consistent, scalable, and predictable and the tradeoffs therein

  • A love for learning and a passion for helping others

  • Experience developing in an Agile/Scrum environment

  • Machine Learning experience
