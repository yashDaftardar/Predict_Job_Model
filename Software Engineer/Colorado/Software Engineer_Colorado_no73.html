Job Description

Danfoss Editron is leading the electrification revolution. Our mission is to end pollution with electrification.
Editron specializes in hybrid and electric powertrain systems for the marine, off-highway and on-highway markets. A business division of Danfoss, it develops and manufactures high-performance power systems for heavy-duty vehicles, machines and marine vessels based on synchronous reluctance assisted permanent magnet technology.
We are growing fast globally and would like to invite you to join the award-winning team and to work on the technologies that will enable the world of tomorrow to do more with less.

As a constantly growing company, Danfoss is looking for motivated employees who want to work with us on our success. We are seeking the ideal Embedded Software Engineer to join our diverse team.

In this role, you will be part of a team responsible for the electrification of on-highway vehicles through embedded software development and functional safety experience.

Job Responsibilities

  • Performs Complex and detailed Embedded code development and related activities, following the V-model, A-spice and Danfoss SW PDP
  • Conceives and develops innovative new methods and algorithms to improve motor control functions
  • Focus and passion on improving safety, efficiency, power density and hardware protection for electric drive systems in electric vehicles
  • Conceives and develops innovative new methods and algorithms to improve functional safety functions for the safest possible electric vehicle operation.
  • Exhibits Proficiency with C-Code, C++ and Matlab development tools.
  • Works with functional safety engineers to facilitate software for safety
  • Develop test case specifications for software requirements and fault injection
  • Perform bench/HIL tests and system validation of all software requirements
  • Coordinate engineers working at the software development level within Danfoss and suppliers
  • Support management of software requirements and traceability in a Requirements Management system
  • Ensure Verification and Validation Plans are derived from the Safety Concept, they are aligned with the Safety Plan and are in compliance with customer requirements and industry standards
  • Software development related to Safety and hardware level code
  • Assists with Producing work products related to software including: Risk Assessment, Safety Concepts, ASIL assignments/decomposition, Safety Requirements, DFMEA, and Safety Verification and Validation activities (planning and execution)

Background & Skills

  • BS in Electrical Engineering or Computer Science degree (or equivalent)
  • Experience with embedded coding and ability to write and understand C and C++
  • Strong knowledge of Hazard Analysis and Risk Assessment (HARA)
  • 3+ years in software development role
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office tools including Word, Excel and PowerPoint
