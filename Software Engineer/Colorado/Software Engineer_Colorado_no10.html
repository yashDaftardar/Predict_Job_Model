Software Engineer, Level 1, New College Grad – (Colorado Springs, CO)


NETSCOUT’s nGeniusPULSE team is responsible for helping customers gain insight into business service availability and performance in a fast changing and complex digital environment. Our customer’s business continuity depends on IT infrastructure and applications that are both on-prem and in the cloud, physical and virtual, located anywhere. Our solution enables visibility without borders, helping users identify and isolate availability and performance issues quickly. We care deeply about our engineering culture, looking after our customers, delivering a great user experience, and shipping a best in class product.

You will take a role in designing, developing, and delivering end-to-end features as part of a feature development team in a growing software business. We are looking for a bright, innovative, self-starter who sees things through to the finish line.

Requirements

  • B.S./B.A. degree in Computer Science or related field; or equivalent work experience
  • Computer Science fundamentals in systems design, data structures, algorithm design and problem solving
  • Experience or a strong understanding of agile product development principles
  • Working knowledge of at least one modern programming language: Java, JavaScript, or C++ preferred
  • Experience with Python, Cassandra, MongoDB, Elasticsearch, JavaScript, React, Node.js, AWS, Git, and Linux are all valuable pluses

Additional Skills & Experience

  • Knowledge and interest in computer networking and application monitoring concepts
  • Knowledge and interest in professional software development/engineering practices and system design best practices
  • Experience in communicating with business stakeholders, users, and other technical teams to envision and deliver innovative solutions
  • A track record of personally delivering working software

Location: This position will be in our Colorado Springs office (currently working from home)

Education Requirements: Bachelors (or equivalent work experience), New college grads encouraged to apply

Job Type: Full-Time

Preferred Language: English

About NETSCOUT

NETSCOUT is a market leader in real-time service assurance and cybersecurity solutions for today’s most demanding service provider, enterprise and government networks. NETSCOUT’s Adaptive Service Intelligence (ASI) technology continuously monitors the service delivery environment to identify performance issues and provides insight into network-based security threats, helping teams to quickly resolve issues that can cause business disruptions or impact user experience. NETSCOUT delivers unmatched service visibility and protects the digital infrastructure that supports our connected world.
