Full Job Description

This is an opportunity to join a high-profile software development team, in a technical managerial role. Working for a global tech industry leader.

Oracle Advertising (formerly Oracle Data Cloud) is one of the leading suppliers of advertising technology and data. Your team will be building the first of a new suite of products which bridge the worlds of contextual and audience based advertising. Primarily based out of our Colorado campus, you will also get to work closely with our teams in Cambridge, England. Working on solutions employing machine learning, data science, natural language processing, information retrieval, as well as cloud hosted and highly available services at scale.

As an engineering manager you will directly manage software engineers. You will be responsible for supporting them in their career development, and ensuring they receive the training, coaching and mentoring they require to succeed.

Additionally, you will have accountability for the roadmap, working with product management and technical partners to ensure you are delivering the most value.

Key responsibilities:
Own and communicate the roadmap for your team.

Professional development and support of your staff.

Enable top performers and challenge them to excel.

Assessment and selection of best candidates to recruit, and onboarding of new team members.

Represent your team to the wider organization.

Help your team successfully navigate corporate processes (security and architectural review, budgeting, quarterly planning etc.)

Facilitate the operation of the agile process and ensure successful delivery of commitments.

Monitor staff wellbeing and undertake activities to strengthen this.

Preferred background:
Previously managed a small software development team.

Career in hands on software engineering.

Experience of delivering highly available cloud services at scale.

Driven by helping others succeed.

Track record of successful delivery.

High degree of knowledge of agile software development.

Able to communicate in a clear and engaging manner.
