Full Job Description

At HPE we bring together the brightest minds to create breakthrough technology solutions that advance the way people live and work. We see a future for our customers that will be edge-centric, cloud-enabled and data-driven. To lead HPE into this future, we are looking for an experienced software engineer to join our team focused on as-a-Service products to provide a cloud-like experience for on-premise HPE hardware management and monitoring.


In this role, you will be responsible for the engineering, testing and deployment of the next generation of the OneView product. The first step of this journey is already underway to re-platform services to Kubernetes. The role is multi-disciplinary, requiring a broad technical background with hands-on experience in many areas including Linux, Java, Spring, Docker, Kubernetes as well as hardware infrastructure layers (compute, storage, and network).


In a typical day as a Software Engineer, you would :

  • Working to re-platform existing services using Docker and Kubernetes including Java based applications as well as PostgreSQL, RabbitMQ, Redis and Kafka

  • Development of new product capabilities for management and monitoring of compute, storage and networking hardware using Java, Spring and other technologies

  • Validation and testing of end-to-end product


Minimum Required Skills

  • Bachelor or Masters degree in Computer Science or similar or equivalent experience

  • 7 years+ industry experience

  • Excellent verbal and written English communication skills

  • Experience with Agile methods and practices

  • Strong hands-on experience with servers, storage and networking hardware

  • Hands-on experience creating cloud native services using Docker and Kubernetes

  • Hands-on expertise in operating systems, languages and tools, including Linux, Bash, Java, Python, PowerShell, etc.


Additional Skills

  • System management tools experience (OneView, SIM, CMU, etc.)

  • DevOps tools experience including Ansible, Chef, Puppet, Terraform, etc.

  • Virtualization technologies (VMware, KVM, etc.)

  • Hands-on experience constructing distributed, event-driven systems built for high scalability, including streaming data and non-blocking application development
