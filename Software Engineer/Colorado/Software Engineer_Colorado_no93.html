Maxar is looking for a Software Engineer to join an agile and fast-paced development team creating software to support operational needs around the world. Do you have the drive to contribute to our nation's security? In an increasingly data-rich environment, threats to our country are harder to detect and require the augmentation of intelligence by machines. As a part of our Maxar team, you can help turn this complex problem into a real solution for important US Government customers.

What you will do with your teammates:

  • Develop and enhance applications and services to exceed our customer expectations. This includes full lifecycle development and ownership of the entire requirement/solution.
  • Foster and support the ongoing growth and practices of agile development within the organization.
  • Identify improvements to processes and software applications
  • Develop your own expertise within the software architecture.
  • Collaborate with teammates. To us, this means being a good listener and bringing an attitude that creates an environment where innovative ideas can arise.

  • 2 Years Related experience in Software Development
  • Strong Python, C, C++, or Java programming skills

Preferred Skills:

  • Agile software development lifecycle experience

  • Javascript/React experience
