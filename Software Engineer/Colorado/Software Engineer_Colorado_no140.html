Full Job Description

KEMET Electronics Corporation, a YAGEO company is seeking an experienced software engineer to lead and grow a team in the development of high-impact software applications. This full-time position is a great opportunity to grow your skills, make an impact and work with colleagues who are as passionate about new technology as you are.

Work of Significance

KEMET’s products significantly promote sales and reduce costs. We accomplish this by building and maintaining electronic components data content and powering it with unique software which “thinks like a Subject Matter Expert.” The result is a suite of customer facing software applications and tools which bring efficiency to component selection, cross reference, customized specification sheets and quote/Bill-of-Material processing.

KEMET offers a collaborative, energetic and casual work environment. We embrace new technology by constantly integrating some of the latest software tools and frameworks into our workflows. Our comprehensive benefits package includes affordable health insurance options, paid time off, a generous 401k match and much more.

Job Responsibilities

As a member of our team, you will lead software engineers in the development of web applications, mobile applications, APIs, and supporting software. The Lead Software Engineer contributes to the development of these applications and provides architectural and design guidance to the rest of the team. Additional responsibilities include the day-to-day management of the software engineering team, interfacing with key stakeholders globally, and ensuring objectives are reached.

Skills and Experience:

We are looking for at least 5 years of experience developing software, leading, or managing a team, and experience with many of the following technologies and methodologies:

  • Java
  • JavaScript
  • AngularJS/Angular
  • MVC Frameworks (i.e. Spring)
  • ORM Frameworks (i.e. Hibernate)
  • PHP
  • HTML/CSS
  • SQL
  • WordPress
  • Linux
  • Agile Scrum

Other Qualifications:

  • Bachelor of Science degree (Engineering, Computer/Technology)
  • Creative problem-solving skills
  • Self-motivated and positive with a desire to tackle challenges
  • Enjoy leading, mentoring, and collaborating
  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills, both verbal and written
  • International experience or language skills is a plus, parent company is based in Taiwan
