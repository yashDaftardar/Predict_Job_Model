Full Job Description

Established in 1977, basys is a leading provider of software solutions and consulting services in the highly complex, regulated benefit administration software industry. As a paid Engineering/Technology Intern in our Enterprise Solutions team, you will work alongside our Enterprise Infrastructure Architect in developing the tools and utilities to continuously enhance the implementation and configuration of the basys system with our customers. This internship program is a proven career-launching experience with the potential for long-term professional growth with basys following your graduation.

A Look Inside the Job:

  • Write code for web-based/windows-based applications, create robust high-volume production applications, and develop prototypes
  • Participate in all aspects of the software development process including application design, construction, testing, and deployment
  • Maintain documentation and follows the SCRUM methodology
  • Proficient written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to translate technical terminology and concepts in terms understandable to both technical and non-technical resources
  • Strong work-ethic, ability to handle multiple assignments and meet deadlines

Required Technical Experience:

  • Familiar with C#, .Net 2.0/3.5, ASP.Net, Windows or Web applications, and Web Services
  • Understanding of: Software Development Lifecycle (SDL) and Agile Methodology/other software development methodologies.
  • Understanding of object-oriented design, data structures and algorithms
  • Experience using MS Office Suite or Google Workspace

Internship Specs:

  • Must be pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science, Information Systems or related field
  • Have and maintain a minimum GPA of 3.0; however, a 3.5 or higher is preferred
  • Requires a commitment to work up to 20 hours per week remotely or from our HQ in Halethorpe, just south of Baltimore MD when it is determined we can safely work onsite
  • Paid internship at an hourly rate, based upon experience.

Basys is an Equal Opportunity Employer
