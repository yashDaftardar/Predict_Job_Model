Vidoori, Inc., a rapidly growing small business that specializes in building strong Engineering teams that provide high quality, modern technical solutions to our clients, is seeking a Software Development Engineer who:

  • Applies extensive expertise in software development
  • Solves complex problems that require the regular use of ingenuity and creativity
  • Performs work without need for direct supervision
  • Makes sound decisions that achieve critical organizational objectives
  • Can function in project leadership roles and represent Vidoori as the prime customer contact on significant technical matters

Experience and Skills

Required Qualifications:

  • Demonstrate expertise in C#, Java, or C++
  • Translate requirements, user scenarios, and technical constraints into fully developed and tested software
  • Integrate software features, systems, and COTS products across an established enterprise domain
  • Troubleshoot and debug deployed applications in a live production environment
  • Demonstrate best-practice engineering, including Agile methodologies, unit testing, refactoring, code reviews, build and deployment processes
  • Improve quality of code base using proper technical practices, automated testing, and code quality reviews
  • Capable of clearly and effectively conveying issues, reports, and other deliverables to engineering and customer counterparts
  • Familiar with and effectively utilize CM processes/tools

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience writing multi-threaded applications and socket programming
  • Flexibility in programming languages (C#/Java/C++)
  • Experience with continuous development/integration methodologies and tools
  • Experience communicating with users, technical teams, and management to collect requirements, describe software product features, and discuss technical designs
  • Understanding of maintenance and patching processes for a production application
  • Capable of capturing system requirements; e.g., operational, usability, and customer experience requirements

Technologies and Tools: C# or Java or C++, .NET, HTML5, CSS, XML, JSON, Git, SVN, MySQL, Unix/Linux, Windows, Jenkins

Experience: Seeking three (3+) years of experience performing software development activities, with a Bachelor’s Degree, or one (1+) years of experience with a Master’s Degree

Location: Maryland/DC Region

Residency: US citizen, US green card

Employment Type: Full-Time
