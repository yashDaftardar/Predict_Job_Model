Software Engineer

What will I be doing?
The Software Engineer is responsible for implementation and testing phases of the software development life cycle, occasionally providing input into design.

In this position, you will also…
  • Implements software for specifications of medium complexity for limited scale systems.
  • Participates as a team member on various engineering projects.
  • Suggests alternative methodologies or techniques to achieving desired results.
  • Develops and maintains understanding of products, manufacturing processes, and product applications.
  • Manually tests and unit tests all assigned applications.
  • Writes application technical documentation.
  • Reviews and revises new procedures as needed for the continuing development of high quality systems.
  • Maintains knowledge of technical advances and evaluates new hardware / software for company use.
  • Follows departmental policies, procedures, and work instructions.
  • Works closely with higher-level engineers to increase functional knowledge.

Reasons you should apply...
1. You enjoy working with new technologies!
2. You enjoy collaborating with other teams!
3. You want to work in a purpose driven organization!

This job may not be a fit if...
1. You don’t have experience with Java.
2. You do your best work as a solo artist; the rest of the group just slows you down.

Qualified Candidates must have…
  • Bachelors’ degree or higher in related field, such as Computer Engineering or Computer Science, plus a minimum of 2 years of software development experience, or equivalent combination of education and experience.
  • Java development experience required (or Angular development)
  • Ability to learn and master standard programming languages.
  • Ability to write detailed design specifications based on user requirements.
  • Ability to work with standard office tools.
  • Ability to work with source control systems.
  • Ability to work with design tools (e.g. Visio).
  • Ability to work in an agile environment.
Superstar Candidates will ALSO have…
  • Experience with Angular development.
  • Ability to understand and manipulate XML.
  • Familiar with AWS.
  • Familiar with relational databases (preferably MySQL).
  • Familiar with REST API design.
  • Familiar with the Unix command line.
