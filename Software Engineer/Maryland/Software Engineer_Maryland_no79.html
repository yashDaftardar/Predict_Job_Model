If you are a talented and motivated early career software engineer looking to put your skills to use in a challenging environment that includes supporting military tactical operations and military intelligence, then this is the opportunity for you! MITRE’s Army Division has openings for recent graduates in computer science, software engineering or similar degree programs to work the full software engineer life cycle on a variety of cutting-edge projects.

These software engineering projects are in information warfare, command and control, fire support (artillery), smart munitions, intelligence and much more. The technical work areas include software development for the Android operating system, web development, software testing such as performance testing, requirements development, UML for software designs, and data modeling/management. Modern technologies such as React, Node.js, Angular, Protobuf, git, NOSQL, Atlassian, Slack, and more are utilized. Opportunities in research and development exist in such areas as Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, and Distributed Computing to support the Army command, control, communications, and intelligence capabilities of the future!

Responsibilities include:

  • Execute full software engineering life cycle processes (concept vision through test)
  • Develop software using Agile processes and tools (JIRA, git, Jenkins/gradle, and more)
  • Deliver software designs and prototypes to Army R&D and Acquisition, Logistics and Technology sponsors
  • Evaluate technologies and Commercial products for potential military applicability

Required Qualifications:

  • BS in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a similar degree
  • Programming Language experience with either
    • Java or similar object-oriented language
    • JavaScript and HTML for web development
    • Python
  • Understanding of software design patterns and best practices
  • Understanding of the Software Development Life Cycle
  • Enthusiasm and a desire to learn and grow
  • Candidate must be able to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret Clearance.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with Android programming including understanding of Android IPC mechanisms such as Intents
  • Experience with client and server-side JavaScript, MVC frameworks, React, and Node.js
  • Building software with languages/frameworks such as Maven and Gradle
  • Machine Learning experience or course work
  • Understanding of databases (relational or NOSQL)
  • Familiarity with serialization formats including JSON, XML, and Google Protobuf
  • Technical writing skills such as drafting software designs and technical guidance
  • Experience or understanding of all phases of the Software or Systems Development Life Cycle
  • Strong interpersonal, briefing, and communication skills
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Top Secret/SCI Clearance.

This requisition requires the following clearance(s):
