Description

Summary of the Position

This position is responsible for providing integration solutions to solve both business and technology needs. The incumbent will work with various development teams to define, develop, deliver, and support integration processes on the DELL Boomi platform integrating multiple Cloud and On-Premise Applications. This individual should be well versed in the native features, capabilities and customization of the DELL Boomi platform including Integration, Master Data Hub (MDH) and Electronic Data Interchange (EDI).

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Performs design, development, testing, deployment, and maintenance of integration processes between cloud and/or on-premise applications using the DELL Boomi AtomSphere platform
  • Adheres to sound standards and best practices during all phases of software development life cycle.
  • Collaborates with team to provide technical solutions to support long-term technology strategy.
  • Stays current on technology trends and identify ways to enhance and improve USP’s integration capabilities.
  • Provides production support, enhancements and ongoing maintenance for custom programs that interface with third party vendors and other internal systems

Basic Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree required in Computer Science, Information Systems, or related field, or equivalent combination of education and experience
  • Four (4) years experience using the DELL Boomi platform
  • Combination of education/experience will be considered

Preferred Qualifications

  • Skilled problem-solver, able to prioritize and is detail oriented.
  • Possesses ability to handle multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment.
  • Experience integrating with web based services such as Salesforce, Oracle EBS, Relational Databases and REST & SOAP
  • Experience in the use of the Integration, MDH, and EDI components of the Dell Boomi platform
  • DELL Boomi Certification

Supervisory Responsibilities

No

USP brings together more than 1,100 talented professionals across five global locations to deliver its mission to strengthen the supply of safe quality medicines and supplements worldwide.
