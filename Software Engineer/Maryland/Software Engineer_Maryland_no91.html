EchoStar Corporation (NASDAQ: SATS) is a premier global provider of satellite communication solutions. Headquartered in Englewood, Colo., and conducting business around the globe, EchoStar is a pioneer in communications technologies through its Hughes Network Systems and EchoStar Satellite Services business segments. For more information, visit echostar.com . Follow @EchoStar on Twitter.

Hughes has an exciting opportunity for an Entry Level Software Test Engineer. This position will be located at our headquarters in Germantown, MD.

Responsibilities:

  • Perform qualification Testing of OSS-BSS software for the Service Delivery Group
  • Working with the team to support the test environments specific to application setup, configuration, troubleshooting and verification.
  • Testing and verification of deployed software, issue reporting and tracking, and delivery of quality releases to production.

Basic Requirements:

  • Bachelors degree in computer science, computer engineering or related technical field

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with testing and test management tools, and knowledge of scripting and/or test automation
  • Good verbal and written communication skills, demonstrated problem solving skills and strong interpersonal skills, and be able to work as part of a dynamic team.
  • Knowledge of Oracle database, Linux, NodeJS, Apache and Salesforce is desired.

Will be eligible for discretionary bonus, with funding based on company performance.
