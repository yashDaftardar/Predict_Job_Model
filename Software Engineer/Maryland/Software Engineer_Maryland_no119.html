Robotic Research, LLC specializes in robotics, intelligent control, sensor processing, and computer programming; including design, development, and testing of state-of-the-art autonomous mobility software on a variety of ground, air, and maritime platforms.

We are looking for engineers to join our team, developing cutting-edge technologies in the field of Autonomous Systems.

Job Summary: Robotics Software Engineer to support development of autonomous, unmanned systems, with specific focus on software development, and especially use of C/C++. Multiple positions available, entry to mid-level. Software will support autonomous navigation, path planning, sensor processing, perception and intelligent control for ground vehicles ranging from 20 pounds to 25 tons.

Duties include: Design and implement software using C/C++ in a Linux environment. Develop/improve algorithms for data processing and intelligent control of unmanned vehicles and sensor systems. Document activities, prepare technical reports, present results, and demonstrate system capabilities. Travel for field tests and project meetings may be required. Testing in outdoor conditions may be required.

Qualifications:

A highly qualified candidate will have the following:

  • Strong programming ability in C/C++
  • Familiarity with development in a Linux environment
  • Knowledge and hands-on experience with algorithm and software development applied to robotics is strongly desired. Desired areas of experience include:
    • Sensor processing (LIDAR, cameras, RADAR, inertial, etc.)
    • Computer Vision and Deep Learning
    • Path planning
    • Localization/navigation algorithms
    • SLAM
  • Candidate should be a self-motivated, flexible team player with good written and oral skills, able to work effectively in a fast-paced environment

U.S. citizenship required. Ability to pass a government security clearance investigation and background check a must.
