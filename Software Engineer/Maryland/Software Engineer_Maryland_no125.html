Job Description:

Although we work with numbers, the accounting, tax and consulting process is still all about people. At Mazars, we pride ourselves on building long-lasting relationships with our clients, our colleagues and the communities where we serve.

Mazars in US is a high-performing accounting, tax and consulting firm with significant national presence in strategic US geographies. Since 1921, our dedicated professionals have leveraged technical industry expertise to develop customized solutions for clients in a broad array of industries, providing services to growth-oriented enterprises and individuals. As the independent US member firm of Mazars Group, we deliver seamless access to the expertise of 24,000 professionals in 90+ countries.

Our Consulting practice is looking for an experienced Microsoft Software Engineer to join our Management and Technology group (MTC) as a Software Consultant. In this Consultant level role, you will be responsible for designing and implementing software that will integrate and extend our clients’ line of business systems.

This position can take place in any of our U.S. office locations.

Our firm has offered our employees the option to work remotely at this time.

Professional Responsibilities:

  • Act as a liaison between IT and functional area business units; communicate and collaborate with the project manager to drive tasks to completion.
  • Responsible for working alongside functional consultants to identify system requirements.
  • Work with system architects to design and build software solutions based on Microsoft technologies.
  • Responsible for gathering business requirements, fit-gap analysis, configuration, testing, operational assessment readiness for the D365 migration
  • Translate business needs into technical requirements
  • Gathering and documenting business requirements using a variety of methods and techniques such as: interviews, document analysis, requirements workshops, surveys, business process descriptions, and workflow analysis
  • Work with management to prioritize business and information needs; locate and define new process improvement opportunities.
  • Coordinate and perform system unit testing and support user acceptance testing.
  • Help design a set of reports and dashboards.
  • Learn the current state of the company's systems and data repositories, understand the data, including its limitations and potential
  • Interpret data, analyze results using statistical techniques and create reports; develop and implement databases, data collection systems, data analytics and other strategies that optimize data quality
  • Identify, analyze, and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration, Industrial Engineering, MIS or a Computer related field of study
  • The candidate should have natural inter-personal skills that reflects with their clients, able to speak with confidence and demonstrates care and integrity
  • The candidate has been a consultant for 3+ years
  • Experience with pre-sales would be beneficial
  • The candidate must have 5+ years of experience implementing software solutions using Microsoft technologies.
  • The candidate should have good communication skills. Verbal communication should be clear
  • Must be familiar with the concepts around cloud topologies and cloud patterns
  • Must be highly competent on “Microsoft Azure”
  • Must have experience architecting solutions targeting the Azure platform.
  • Must be able to strategize and participate in building of intellectual property (IP), has built "software for the market"
  • Must be able to work with development teams varying in size from 3 to 25
  • The candidate is familiar with cloud models (Saas, PaaS, Iaas, iPaaS)
  • The candidate is familiar in the following base infrastructure concepts within Azure
    • Azure Active Directory
    • Azure Networking concepts
    • Azure Managing Network and Applications
    • Azure Firewall
    • Azure Containers
    • Azure Automation
  • The candidate is competent in the following Azure technologies
    • Azure Storage Solutions
    • Azure Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) and Virtual Machines
    • Azure Managing Network and Applications
    • Azure Integration Platform as a Service (iPaaS)
    • Logic Apps
    • Function Apps
    • Azure Service Bus
    • Azure Key Vault
  • Experience on ERP implementations would be highly beneficial
  • Knowledgeable on Azure DevOps with 2+ years of experience including projects, wiki, backlog, change management, and test planning
  • Must understand Microservices architectural style and design principles
  • Domain-driven design or problem-domain design
  • Experience in the integration of Dynamics 365 solutions is a plus
  • Experience with Microsoft D365 for Finance and Operations or Customer Engagement is highly desired.
  • Experience designing and implementing solutions on the Power App Platform
  • Excellent organizational analytical and project management skills
  • Customer-oriented attitude and successful client relationship development skills

What We Offer:

  • Competitive base salary and incentive compensation plan
  • A comprehensive, competitive benefits package that includes medical, dental and life insurance plans, a generous paid days’ off (PDO) plan, and a competitive 401k Plan
  • A dynamic, diverse work experience in a growing global organization
  • An inclusive, client-centered environment where you can develop new skills, enhance your overall business knowledge and work with world-class professionals
  • Intensive customized training, including in-house leadership development programs, continuing professional education courses, and both classroom and on-the-job training
  • Opportunity for International Mobility experiences are also made available
