Senior Software Engineer

LOCATION: REMOTE

NOTE: We are unable to provide visa sponsorship for this role at this time. No candidates requiring visa sponsorship will be considered.

Overview:
Oracle's Hospitality

business unit is a global provider of on premises and cloud-based hospitality solutions with tremendous market presence and serving a dynamic mix of globally recognized hotels, resorts, casinos and cruise lines. We are committed to empowering the hotel industry to offer exceptional, differentiated guest services that foster loyalty.

We are looking for senior software engineer for our products with a passion for developing cutting edge solutions that enable Oracle Hospitality Cloud applications to seamlessly connect with a variety of cloud applications, both internal within Oracle Cloud such as HCM, ERP and CX but also third-party applications.

Responsibilities:
Provides significant contribution and oversight in the design and development of software products/solutions and solution prototyping

Delivers high quality products/solutions and ensures that best practices and sound engineering principals are applied in all phases of the software development life cycle

Tasks include writing functional specifications, design documentation, coding, unit testing, debugging, integration, performance tuning, of software modules

Possesses sufficient knowledge to constructively challenge current practices or approaches; identifies trends that have impact on work unit, or products and articulates implications to upper management

Has the ability to gain working knowledge of other ORACLE departments and how these areas can benefit his/her area

Provides training and mentorship to less experienced software engineers including code reviews

Qualifications

Bachelor's degree in technical discipline (preferably in computer science or related field)

5+ years of experience in software design and development

Experience in all aspects of the Software Development Life Cycle

Expert level competency in core Java, SOAP/REST web services, microservice architecture, JPA/ORM, JUnit and Oracle database

Knowledge and understanding of iPaaS technologies

Experience implementing integration solutions using Oracle Integration Cloud (OIC) and related Oracle technologies

Exposure to Open Source integration tools such as Apache Camel, Apache Airflow to name a few

Understanding and experience implementing cloud native architectures

Passion and a genuine interest in integration

Good communication skills

Preferred Skills:
Actual experience with Open Source integration tools such as Apache Camel, Apache Airflow etc. a big plus

Experience with hospitality industry a plus

Experience with HGBU Opera software a plus
