Job Description Summary: Job Description PayPal is seeking a Software Engineer to join its Merchant Credit product development team. This team is responsible for engineering, designing, and maintaining the PayPal Credit integrations merchants use to offer their customers financing on their e-commerce web sites and mobile applications.

Job Description:


The team engineers SDKs and other client side libraries that merchant developers integrate with to access PayPal's ad delivery network. The team also develops REST and GraphQL APIs that deliver contextual messaging for PayPal Credit financing offers on merchant sites in the form of lightweight text ads, responsive banners and PayPal Smart Payment Buttons. These SDKs and APIs are used by thousands of merchants, millions of times a day across the world.

Besides writing scalable and maintainable code, you will be challenged with building products that have an exceptional user and developer experience. If you are passionate about software engineering and take pride in writing clean code while solving some of the most interesting challenges the web can offer, then take your first steps to join the PayPal team by applying today.

Required Qualifications

  • Strong critical thinking skills - you will need to understand our challenges & opportunities and apply them to innovative software solutions
  • Strong communication skills in a collaborative environment
  • Highly proficient with vanilla ES6+ Functional and Object Oriented JavaScript
  • Node.JS and express application design and development
  • React or other front-end frameworks (e.g. Angular, Vue)
  • Experience with consuming and designing REST and GraphQL APIs
  • Experience with Git source control and effective branching and release management practices
  • Ability to debug, diagnose and resolve complex JavaScript bugs throughout the stack

Bonus Skills

  • Previous experience within the adtech or fintech sector(s) a plus
  • Familiarity with Webpack and other build and packaging systems
  • Strong automated unit and functional test suite experience
  • Familiarity with analytic instrumentation, querying datasets using Hive/Hadoop, Teradata, BigQuery, Kibana and/or Splunk is a plus
  • Continuous integration and delivery tools such as Jenkins
  • Experience with Java
