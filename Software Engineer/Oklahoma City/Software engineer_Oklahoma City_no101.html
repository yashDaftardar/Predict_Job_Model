Full Job Description

Our goal is always to help people live better lives .

Carter Healthcare is an industry leader for technology tailored to our operational outlook. We are looking for a creative software engineer to add to our team of outstanding developers. As a software developer for our company, you will have creative freedom to develop and refine ways to make our company more efficient.

You will work with our executive team daily to:

  • Refine data and format final product and in many cases be involved with the clinical and marketing staff that will use this application or report daily
  • Maintain and modify programs, make approved changes by amending flow charts, develop detailed programming logic and coding changes, and test and develop programming modifications
  • Evaluate simple interrelationships between programs, such as whether a contemplated change in one part of a program would cause unwanted results in a related part
  • Code, test and troubleshoot programs utilizing the appropriate hardware, database and programming technology.
  • Resolve questions of program intent, data input, output requirements, and inclusion of internal checks and controls.
  • Analyze client/server and micro-computer based software solutions compatible with company requirements
  • Document programming problems and resolutions for future reference

We have always been proud of our development team and their ability to understand our industry and our business model. Come be part of a team that is a pioneer in innovation!
