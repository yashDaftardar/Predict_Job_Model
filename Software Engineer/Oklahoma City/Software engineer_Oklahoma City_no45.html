WHO WE ARE:

As an industry leader, GlobalHealth is an Oklahoma-Based health insurer covering individuals in all 77 counties. We aspire to be the employer of choice in our industry, attracting and retaining a highly talented workforce. Our passion is Genuine Care and Optimal Health for the members we serve. We are unique by providing high touch, high value and a partnership to our members. We go above and beyond to provide personalized, engaging, and responsive services to our members. We work hard to offer affordable health insurance coverage with the benefits people truly want and need. It is our hope to be more than just a health insurance company we want to be long-term partners with our members. We are looking for future employees who exude our core values of taking accountability through ownership, being driven, innovative and who have a passion for continuous learning.


HOW WILL THIS ROLE ADD VALUE TO THE ORGANIZATION?
The Software Developer is responsible for developing, testing, implementing, and maintaining software applications. This position troubleshoots system problems and issues and looks for ways to improve the application and mentors junior Software Engineers.

ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:

  • Writes well designed, testable, efficient code by using best software development practices.
  • Creates site layout/user interface by using standard HTML/CSS practices.
  • Mentors junior developers in software development best practices, tools, and methodologies.
  • Creates site layout/user interface by using standard HTML/CSS practices.
  • Integrates data from various back-end services and databases.
  • Gathers and refines specifications and requirements based on technical needs.
  • Creates and maintains software documentation.
  • Research / analyze emerging technologies/industry trends and recommend how and why we should apply them into operations and activities.
  • Cooperates with external designers to match visual design intent and provide technical input to help influence and improve the design.
  • Maintains internal and third-party portals, such as enrollment platforms, member portals, and provider portals.
  • Performs other duties as assigned.
  • Must maintain confidentiality of business information, including Protected Health Information (PHI), as required by HIPAA and company policy.

EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:

  • Requires a Bachelors Degree in Computer Science, MIS, or a related field of equivalent experience. (Less is acceptable with requisite experience).
  • Three to five years of experience in the target skills and technologies.

KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES:

  • .NET application development, or similar technology. (i.e. Spring Boot)
  • Java Script expertise
  • Knowledge of front-end technologies (HTML, CSS, Webpack or similar, npm, etc.)
  • T-SQL and/or ANSI SQL experience.
  • Experience using source control tools such as git.
  • Experience with web hosting and cloud services, e.g. Azure, AWS.
  • Ability to present your designs and sell your solutions to various stakeholders.
  • Strong communication skills.
  • Ability to solve problems creatively and effectively and present those solutions to technical and non-technical audiences.
  • Experience working in an Agile/Scrum environment.
  • Experience with mobile application development using web-based tool is preferred. (e.g. Apache Cordova, Xamarin Forms, or similar)

WORK ENVIRONMENT:

  • This position works in a climate-controlled office setting.
  • May be subjected to a fast-paced decision-making environment.
  • May require working on a computer for long periods of time, using a keyboard, viewing a digital screen.
  • Occasional lifting (up to 10 pounds of computer equipment or paper supplies).

TRAVEL:

Minimal travel is required for this position.


SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITY:

This position has no supervisory responsibilities.
