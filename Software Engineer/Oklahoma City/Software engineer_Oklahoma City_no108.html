GENERAL DESCRIPTION

A C2ISR Software Engineer (SE) is responsible for designing, developing, and modifying corporate software applications. The SE is expected to generate concise feature requirement specifications and design documents. Additionally, the SE is expected to track development timelines, lead the design phase, and negotiate feature sets with the product development leads as well as product managers. The SE is required to rely on experience and judgment to plan and accomplish assigned development goals. This position reports directly to a Program Manager.


DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Performs detailed software designs as well as implement and/or maintain code according to duties and responsibilities
  • Rely on experience and judgment to plan and accomplish assigned projects
  • Comply with all applicable product development processes
  • Perform a variety of complicated engineering tasks
  • Generate detailed software design as well as implement and/or maintain code according to duties and responsibilities
  • Manage and adhere to schedules, deadlines and development goals
  • Other duties as assigned


REQUIREMENTS

  • Bachelor's in computer science or related field, Master’s desirable
  • 2+ Years of Professional Object-Oriented Software Design and Implementation Experience or 1 Year of PLEXSYS Junior Software Engineer Experience
  • Demonstrated organizational, leadership, decision-making and communications skills
  • Demonstrated ability to rapidly learn new technologies
  • Demonstrated critical thinking skills
  • Ability to apply creativity and latitude in solutions is required
  • Ability to periodically travel as business needs dictate
  • Knowledge of modeling and simulation concepts and practices desirable
  • Ability to obtain a US Security Clearance – Secret

DESIRABLE

  • Experience writing software using C++ and SCALA
  • Experience working with modelling and simulation software products and concepts
  • Military experience in a Command and Control or Intelligence Reconnaissance and Surveillance career field
  • Ability to setup, configure and troubleshoot a local area network
  • Familiarity using JIRA, Confluence, Visual Studio, IntelliJ and Perforce
  • Familiarity with SISO and DMO Standards
  • Experience in Database Management
