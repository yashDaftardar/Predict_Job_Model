Full Job Description

This Senior Software Engineer must have a passion for software engineering and test automation. This role requires moderate supervision. As a Senior Software Engineer you will work as part of a team to design, build, and support brand system applications while supporting the full development life cycle. Primary responsibilities will include software development, system/software configuration tasks, document creation, and test-driven development while debugging both cloud and in house software systems/applications. As an experienced professional, you are expected to understand the “bigger picture” while keeping business needs in mind while developing technical solutions. This role will participate in software design meetings to gather and analyze user needs to develop technical requirements.


RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Lead and/or participate in small to medium-scale projects or assist on larger projects inclusive of participating in software design meetings and gathering/analyzing user needs to determine technical requirements.
  • Designs, develops, troubleshoots, and debugs moderately complex software programs for enhancements and new products including configuring, customizing, diagnosing, and enhancing vendor systems software.
  • Maintain and develop software and tools in support of design, infrastructure, and technology platforms – including: operating systems, compilers, routers, networks, utilities, databases, and internet-related tools.
  • Consult with end user(s) to prototype, refine, test, and debug programs to meet needs, Mentor, support, and guide junior developers, including code reviews, best practices, pattern reviews.
  • Document technical details of coding projects through a standardized process of creating code comments, software architecture and flow diagrams.

EDUCATION QUALIFICATIONS

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Information Technology or equivalent work experience required
  • Master's Degree in Computer Science or equivalent work experience preferred

EXPERIENCE QUALIFICATION

  • 5 years’ experience working as part of an Agile Scrum team
  • 7 years’ experience developing and supporting large scale, highly transactional and available technology platforms
  • Experience with restaurant or retail technologies, such as point-of-sale integration
  • Experience mentoring junior developer
  • Experience with continuous integration / continuous delivery automation

REQUIRED KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS or ABILITIES

  • Must have passion for software engineering and test automation
  • Must be a team player and able to work in a collaborative environment
  • Ability to think in terms of OO and application design pattern knowledge
  • Expertise in one or more of the following programming languages: C#, Go, Java, Python
  • Experience working with Agile, lean, CI/CD, and/or XP practices
  • Ability to thrive in a fun, but fast-paced environment
  • Strong communications skills – both written and verbal
  • Technical Solutions Architecture and design leadership.
  • Clear Communication, including the ability to create functional charts
  • Preferred Qualifications:
  • Knowledge to build highly scalable, robust, and fault-tolerant services
  • Ability to work with ambiguous problem definitions
  • Passion for learning new skills and working on high-impact products
  • Container technologies such as Docker on Kubernetes
  • Experience in RESTful Microservices
  • Experience in API design, development, and management
  • Experience of shipping high-quality product features on schedule
