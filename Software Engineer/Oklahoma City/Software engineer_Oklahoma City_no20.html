Job Description

The DevOps Engineer is responsible for maintaining our application infrastructure, creating, supporting and testing our CI/CD pipeline, and working to optimize our production environment. Additional responsibilities may include application support, internal customer interaction, audit reporting and more.


Key Responsibilities

  • Oversees and performs deployments made to production system
  • Manages internal communication for deployed application changes
  • Maintains production applications on servers
  • Implements deployment automation for CI/CD pipeline
  • Optimization and organization of production system environment
  • Performs maintenance of systems alongside Development and IT
  • Monitors for system issues for proactive correction
  • Performs application support for internally used services
  • Researches and develops resources that create timely and efficient workflow
  • Participates in projects/tasks as instructed by the CIO, or Director of Software Development
  • Other duties and responsibilities as assigned


Qualifications

  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Attention to detail
  • Technical problem-solving skills
  • Experience with Containerization
  • Strong understanding of object-oriented programming a plus
  • 1-3 years SQL or other database query language preferred
  • 1-3 years Linux/Bash experience preferred
  • Experience troubleshooting in a live production environment preferred
  • Experience with large datasets preferred
  • Experience with high volume, mission critical applications
  • Experience with version control (GIT, CVS)
  • Able to read Java/C#
  • Proficiency in MS Office Suite
