Job Description

Innovate, Grow and Do Extraordinary “Together”, is at the core of everything we do at Karsun!

At Karsun we defy assumptions and enable transformation as IT modernization experts. Our people and client-first focus, innovation and performance driven culture empowers and encourages our employees with the autonomy to innovate, and be accountable to take ownership and seek advancement opportunities for personal growth.

This role provides information technology services in development server system analysis, testing and administration; development database analysis, testing, and administration; website analysis, coding, and testing; web application analysis, coding, testing; and related IT systems functions.
What You Will Do:
  • Provide full life cycle development with JAVA technology
  • Understand coding standards and implement them within code being developed
  • Troubleshoot problems and provide solutions
  • Document changes and follow the Systems Development Life Cycle process independently.
What You Bring to Karsun:
  • Hands on Java development experience
  • Hands on web development experience, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Minimum one year SQL query writing experience
  • Minimum one year Maven development experience
  • Minimum one year RESTful Web service development/ REST API experience
  • Willing to learn and adopt new technologies in a short period of time as required by the Project.
  • Requires a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or other IT degree. Engineering, math, and/or science degrees are acceptable substitute degrees
  • Minimum of 1 year of relevant experience
  • Relevant Certification
  • The ability to successfully obtain and maintain a U.S. Suitability/Public Trust Background Clearance
