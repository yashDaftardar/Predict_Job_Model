Full Job Description

CONTROLS PROGRAMMER
Join our team of leaders in control and power system integration and engineering. Due to continued growth, we are seeking experienced PLC Programmers with field experience and strong knowledge of Power Systems to work out of either our Wichita Falls, TX, San Antonio, TX, Midland, TX, Oklahoma City, OK, Phoenix, AZ, or Albuquerque, NM offices. Shermco Systems Integration (SSI) is an industry-leading electrical engineering and systems integration firm.

The Opportunity
We offer the opportunity to develop and utilize a broad engineering skill set at an accelerated pace. SSI is an ideal fit for smart, motivated PLC Programmers that want to continuously learn and solve complex problems. Our Programmers work in a dynamic environment where there are no limits on personal or professional growth.

Job Responsibilities and Expectations:

  • This is a Programmer position. Some existing knowledge of programming is expected.
  • Understanding of Datatypes
  • Capable of Logical thinking / problem solving
  • Understanding of Ladder Logic / Boolean Algebra
  • Working knowledge of Excel and use of basic formulas
  • Basic understanding of Instrumentation
  • Basic understanding of IP Networks
  • Basic understanding of electrical circuits and troubleshooting
  • Good Customer relations
  • Domestic (USA) travel will be required

Desirable Qualifications :

  • Degree in Computer Science
  • Ability to understand and apply AOIs and UDTs
  • Basic understanding of RS-485 Networks
  • Working knowledge of Allen Bradley or Siemens or GE PLCs
  • Working knowledge of SQL / Database Management
  • Ability to read, interpret and apply C&E and Sequence of Operations
  • Process Automation experience
  • Experience with pipeline and/or oil and gas processing a plus.
  • Must have experience with low and medium voltage controls.
  • Must be able to clearly and effectively communicate in English via verbal and written methods and be comfortable and effective doing so directly to customers and co-workers.

Possible Controls Equipment to be used include :

  • Allen Bradley ControlLogix / CompactLogix / PLC5 / SLC500 / FactoryTalk / RSView (PLC/HMI)
  • GE RX3i / RX7i / 90-30 / 90-70 / QuickPanel / iFIX / Cimplicity / Proficy (PLC/HMI)
  • Siemens S7-300 / S7-400 / S5 / TI / WinCC / WinCCFlex / TIA (PLC/HMI)
  • Schneider (SquareD) Modicon Quantum / ClearSCADA / (PLC/HMI)
  • DeltaV (DCS)
  • Wonderware InTouch / ArchestrA / System Platform / Win911 (HMI)
  • OPC Systems SCADA (HMI)

Industry experience: Oil and Gas, Utilities, Agriculture, Metals, Mining, Paper, Data Centers, Health Care
