Training Systems is currently seeking a Software/Systems Engineer to focus on operating system configuration/customization, visualization and software integration. Training Systems is responsible for the design, manufacture, development, and maintenance of a wide variety of commercial and military training devices. The applicant would be primarily supporting the ASC2 AWACS Training System platform, in Oklahoma City, OK.

Position Responsibilities:

  • Participate as a member of a high performing, collaborative team and be self-motivated with a strong work ethic, excellent time-management, and interpersonal skills
  • Integrate commercial and Boeing developed software into Windows and RedHat Enterprise Linux operating systems
  • Use Agile methodologies to support all aspects of product development and project management
  • Scripting to support application of security settings on Windows and RedHat Enterprise Linux platforms
  • Designing systems to meet current and future software and application requirements
  • Have the ability to work with multiple support teams to identify and resolve issues
  • Implement and test software/systems using a variety of technologies to include scripting to support automated testing
  • Networking and support of Ethernet and Fiber Channel networks
  • Support VMWare and Linux virtualization applications
  • Attend design and review board meetings to help facilitate system maturity and lifecycle support
  • Storage Area Network and Network Attached Storage administration
  • Occasional domestic or international travel may be required.

Security Clearance:

This position requires the ability to obtain a US Security Clearance for which the US Government requires US Citizenship

Basic Qualifications (Required Skills and Experience):

  • Experience having System Administration experience of Linux/Unix or Windows platforms
  • 1+ years of experience in software systems integration
  • 1+ years of experience in scripting activities in a Linux or Windows environment

Preferred Qualifications (Desired Skills/Experience):

  • Bachelor, Master or Doctorate of Science degree from an accredited course of study, in Computer Engineering, Software Engineering, Computer Science, Mathematics, or other technical degree
  • Experience computing administration to include managing Microsoft active directory design, deploy and operational support
  • Experience using PowerShell
  • Experience with networking communications standards, IP Networking, and protocols (e.g. Transport Control Protocol/Internet Protocol
  • (TCP/IP), File Transfer Protocol (FTP), Secure Transport Protocol (SCP), Extensible Mark-up Language (XML)
  • Experience in Object Oriented Design and Programming
  • Experience working with Virtualized Systems (i.e. VMWare) design and implementation

Typical Education and Experience:

Education/experience typically acquired through advanced technical education from an accredited course of study in engineering, computer science, mathematics, physics or chemistry (e.g. Bachelor) and typically 2 or more years' related work experience or an equivalent combination of technical education and experience (e.g. Master). In the USA, ABET accreditation is the preferred, although not required, accreditation standard.
