Minimum Qualifications
Masters or Ph.D. level degree in the discipline (CS or IS) or in a related discipline (MBA, etc.) from an accredited school AND Received graduate credit with a grade of B or better in the course applied to teach, OR one year work experience outside the classroom using HTML, CSS & JavaScript.
The Computer Science department prefers you address their Core Learning Outcome’s with your submitted course justification.
Course Description
This course provides an introduction to the various languages, tools and programming techniques used for client-side programming on the World Wide Web. Concepts will be put into practice by using HTML, cascading style sheets, and JavaScript. Due to the particularly dynamic nature of the web environment, course content will change as appropriate.
