Our mission at Tailwind is to make world-class marketing easy for everyone.
It’s an ambitious goal. For 8 years, we have created software that has impacted over 500,000 brands across the globe. Our 70-person company has grown quickly in recent years. And we are excited to continue that growth! That's where you come in.
We are looking for a software developer to join a growing special project team to continue creating new and exciting capabilities that are changing the way people market their businesses. This role will be focused on video and graphics development, building a new foundation for several major product expansions in the coming year and beyond. .
Key attributes for our ideal software developer candidate include:
  • Full-stack know-how: You embrace the full-stack concept along with infrastructure, and you aren’t afraid to switch between languages and platforms.
  • Video and Image experience: You have experience working with web graphics libraries such as Canvas, WebGL, or other image and video processing/rendering technologies.
  • Agile: You’re comfortable working in an agile development environment, where requirements and goals might rapidly evolve as we learn from our users.
  • Embracing cross-team collaboration: You want to be on a team that’s radically collaborative and free of silos, where engineers work in partnership with team members from across the company (product, design, customer success, marketing).
  • Problem-solving: You have exceptional analytical skills, are an out-of-the-box thinker, and are comfortable with quickly learning, evaluating, and deploying new technologies - even in ambiguous situations.
  • Constant learning: You’re always learning patterns, practices, and tools to improve your and the team’s development experience - including being involved in the dev community. You may have side projects too.
What you’ll love about this role:
  • You’ll be building products that small business owners use daily, helping them to pursue their dreams and thrive financially using our platform.
  • You’ll be spearheading the establishment of a new area of expertise and capability for the company
  • You’ll be an early member of a new, talented team
  • Your work will have a big impact across the organization, with several major product expansions relying on the foundation you build .
  • You’ll play an active role in the entire development process — from planning, designing, architecting, and coding — to implementation and documentation.
  • You’ll have the freedom and support to challenge yourself with difficult problems without being afraid of failing, with opportunities to learn from mistakes.
  • Our engineering team is a collaborative group of programmers who want to learn from you and help you learn!
  • You’ll dive into our modern tech stack and toolkit, and start shipping code on day one; immediately influencing the direction of the product, the team, and the company in a big way.
  • We’re nimble and fast-moving, without corporate red tape, approval layers, or politics.
  • You’ll get ownership shares, company-paid insurance premiums and extremely competitive benefits package, salary refreshes twice a year with annual bonuses, and an unlimited vacation policy
Our technology stack:
You’ll need to have production experience with at least some of our technologies, frameworks, and languages. Or at a minimum - similar ones that will help you learn and start contributing quickly.
  • Front-end: Javascript, React + Redux + Typescript + Component libraries (antd, and others)
  • Back-end: AWS API Gateway + Lambda (Typescript runtime) + SQS, PHP 7 + Laravel + Beanstalk
  • Testing: PhpUnit, Jest, Tape, Enzyme, Puppeteer, Detox, K6
  • Database: MariaDB (with InnoDB and TokuDB engines), PostgreSQL (RDS), Aurora, DynamoDB, Redshift
  • Hosting: AWS (S3, Lambda, API gateway, SQS, SNS, SageMaker, EC2, EKS, Cloudfront, VPC), Rackspace (dedicated + virtual servers + CDN),
  • Agile Communication: Clubhouse, Slack, and Github
This position must be based in our Oklahoma City office or our New York City office.

NOTE: Please don’t talk yourself out of applying if you don’t “check all the boxes.” We support each other’s growth and celebrate our differences at Tailwind. If you’re passionate about growing successful teams and hiring, feel free to apply. We’d love to hear from you.

  • Our growing team blends deep early stage DNA (team + alumni have started 17 companies, and helped build 18 other startups) with industry expertise born out of leading names in tech and marketing such as Google, YouTube, AOL, AWeber, a la mode, BigWing Interactive, Paycom, Apple, Yodle and PCI
  • We believe our industry’s lack of diversity hurts us all and work hard to foster a company culture that’s inclusive and appreciative of everyone’s talent, authenticity, and ideas — regardless of our differences
