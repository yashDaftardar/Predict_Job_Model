We are looking for someone to develop insternal tools to support the ASSC and ASDE-X systems. These tools are used to aid in testing and troubleshooting of hardware and software problems. Typical tasks would include generating data to input into a particular piece of the system and then monitor the output to see if the results are as expected. Developing tools to test new software features before they are released to the field.

This position provides second level engineering support to the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) for the following Surface Surveillance Systems: ASDE-3, ASDE-X, RWSL and ASSC. These systems are located at the largest airports throughout the United States. Join our growing team by applying for this position today!

Responsibilities:
  • Provide engineering support services for reviewing software related problems and issues
  • Provide software development services such as code reviews, analysis and changes/updates
  • Develop software test plans, procedures and reports
  • Develop National Change Proposals (NCPs) and System Support Modifications (SSMs)
  • Support technology refresh of legacy system hardware and software
  • Assist with unit, regression and integration testing
  • Attend weekly ASDE-X site telecons to learn/discuss new issues and possible solutions
  • Occasional travel to some of the largest airports across the nation to provide hands
