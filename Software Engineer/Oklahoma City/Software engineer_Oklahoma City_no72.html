Tailwind is a 70+ person tech company growing quickly — roughly doubling year over year. Our visual marketing software is loved by 500,000+ brands, small businesses, and influencers who use it to take their marketing efforts to new heights.
We have a big mission: Make world-class marketing easy for everyone. And we’re looking for a QA engineer to help us achieve it even faster by improving the quality of our application and ensuring we release features to our users that are bug-free and operate smoothly.
What you’ll love about this role:
  • You’ll be testing our suite products small business owners use daily, helping them to pursue their dreams and thrive financially using our platform. This includes both web and mobile applications.
  • You’ll be identifying and creating automated end-to-end tests for our products so we can ensure our functionality works as expected during the development process and prevent issues down the line.
  • We are using new and exciting technologies like Jest, Puppeteer & Detox
  • You have a desire to empower other engineers on your team to improve quality as part of their daily workflows, helping us move faster by incorporating a quality-minded approach to development and introducing test automation as part of everyone’s workflow, thinking long-term about problems we could face and cut them off early.
  • You’ll be a big part to contribute to and help us execute our QA at Tailwind vision. We’re at the early stages of this journey, but we have lofty goals to build software off excellent quality.
  • You’ll have access to our entire codebase, just like all engineers. See a bug breaking and test and want to fix it? Go ahead! You can do that. Want to pair with engineers to figure out a complex issue? We love pair programming!
  • We’re nimble and fast-moving, without corporate red tape, approval layers, or politics.
  • You’ll get ownership shares, company-paid insurance premiums and extremely competitive benefits package, salary refreshes twice a year with annual bonuses, and an unlimited vacation policy

About you:
  • Pursuing Excellence: We are building a world-class system. You know that the end goal is excellent quality for our users and the automation is a means to that goal.
  • User-focused: You approach each situation with the user in mind. You don’t settle for “it’s clunky but it works”; you raise the bar to treat a bad user experience as a bug.
  • Ownership: You are excited about the responsibility of owning quality and you don’t let buggy software out the door. You see an issue and communicate that to the team to correct it.
  • Agile: You’re comfortable working in an agile development environment, where requirements and goals might rapidly evolve as we learn from our users.
  • Embracing multi-disciplinary collaboration: You want to be on a team that’s radically collaborative and free of silos. You will be working with software engineers, UX designers, product managers, customer success associates, and more.
  • Efficient: We want someone who is practical, organized, highly collaborative in their approach, and can write code for tests quickly and cleanly.
  • You're a doer, builder, a self-starter, and the thought of an entrepreneurial endeavor excites you.
Our Testing Technology Stack:
You’ll need to have production experience with at least some of our technologies, frameworks, and languages. Or at a minimum - similar ones that will help you learn and start contributing quickly.
  • End-to-End Testing: Jest, Puppeteer, Detox
  • Front-end: Javascript (React), HTML5, CSS, Typescript
  • Back-end: PHP 7, Laravel, Beanstalk, Memcached
  • Database: MariaDB, TokuDB
  • Version Control: GitHub
  • CICD: CircleCI
  • Agile Communication: Clubhouse, Slack, and Github


NOTE: Please don’t talk yourself out of applying if you don’t “check all the boxes.” We support each other’s growth and celebrate our differences at Tailwind.

  • We believe our industry’s lack of diversity hurts us all and work hard to foster a company culture that’s inclusive and appreciative of everyone’s talent, authenticity, and ideas — regardless of our differences
