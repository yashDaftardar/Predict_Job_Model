Full Job Description

JMA Solutions, a leading government contractor for the Federal Aviation Administration, has a full-time opportunity for a Software Engineer providing assistance to the National Airspace System (NAS) Engineering Air Route Radar Systems and National NAS Defense Programs (NDP) located in Oklahoma City, OK.

This position is responsible for the implementation of cybersecurity controls and management into the FAA National Airspace (NAS). The services will be targeted towards Long Range Radar (LRR) systems (e.g., CARSR and ARSR-4) but may extend into many other areas of the NAS due to the nature of cybersecurity management and compliance.

Duties include but are not limited to:

  • Support Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) Air Traffic Organization (ATO) initiatives and activities associated with assessing risk and providing recommendations to prevent National Airspace Systems (NAS) and Non-NAS systems from unauthorized access or sabotage, and enhancing the stability of those systems to survive security threats in an operational environment.
  • Support for development of security authorization/ISCM packages for information systems under the purview of the NAS Defense Programs Office.
  • Support coordination and facilitation of authorization documentation, NAS Cyber Activities, risk mitigation, and cyber defense initiatives for the information systems under the purview of the NDP NAS Defense Programs Office.
  • Support the assessment and monitoring of modifications to LRR systems; integration of new equipment, software, and firmware into the NAS; management of high security networks; and facilitating remediation activities.
  • Develop of status reports and other documents to support management and remediation processes.
  • Provide other information systems security-related support as delegated by NAS Engineering Support Services for the NDP NAS Defense Programs Office.
