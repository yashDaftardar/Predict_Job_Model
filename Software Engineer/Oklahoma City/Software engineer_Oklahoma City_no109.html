Full Job Description

Homeschooling is our mission. It could also be yours!

Teaching Textbooks is seeking coders with a passion for homeschooling to be a part of our dev team, producing a wide range of apps with an HTML/JS codebase, to run on multiple platforms and in Web browsers.

The homeschooling industry has more than doubled in size over the past year, and Teaching Textbooks, a longtime leader in educational software, is rapidly expanding our product line to meet this demand.

We need to fill several development positions , and are looking for candidates with skills in these technologies:

  • HTML/CSS/SVG
  • JavaScript/TypeScript
  • Node
  • Phaser
  • React
  • Cordova/Electron
  • PHP/MySQL

If you are an expert in some or all of these, and want to be part of making the best homeschooling software in the industry, please send a résumé, along with links to relevant samples of your work .
