Based out of either Littleton, CO; Sunnyvale, CA; or Valley Forge, PA this role will offer you the following challenges:

  • Engage with technical and business users to identify opportunities to drive business operation efficiencies and business growth
  • Create innovative solutions to complex analysis requirements
  • Engage in critical design reviews and provide insights into alternate design choices
  • Develop machine learning components and integrate into business processes
  • Plan, conduct, develop, and operationalize machine learning models on datasets that include time-series, text, etc.
  • Conduct statistical analysis, set up experiments, formulate hypothesis and evaluate statistical significance to identify meaningful insights, communicate findings and provide recommendations
  • Maintain awareness of emerging analytics technologies
  • Craft whitepapers, research papers, follow industry trends in Machine learning and AI to
integrate solutions
Basic Qualifications:
  • Bachelors degree from an accredited college in a related discipline, or equivalent experience/combined education, with 9 years of professional experience; or 7 years of professional experience with a related Masters degree. Considered an emerging authority.
  • Familiarity with one or more of the AI disciplines (e.g. Natural Language Processing, Anomaly
Detection, Markov Chain and Monte Carlo (MCMC) Methods, Machine Learning, Predictive Modeling, Statistical Analysis and Hypothesis Testing)
  • Demonstrated proficiency in designing efficient algorithms and performing analysis with a
numerical scripting language such as Python, R or MATLAB (preferably in Python)
  • Strong working knowledge of basic statistical concepts such as point estimation, probability
distributions, hypothesis tests, and how they can be applied
  • Ability to implement algorithms into software to train systems to recognize patterns and perform specific functions
Desired Skills:
  • Ability to evaluate, develop and present Analytics best practices
  • Demonstrated pursuit of technical knowledge and education required to acquire and maintain
an expertise of the latest technological trends and advancements
  • Track record of being a strong independent contributor
  • Demonstrated strong organizational skills and high work ethic behaviors and excellent
interpersonal skills and team building abilities
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills, including the communication of findings and
their relevance in business terms
  • Proven ability to communicate and present to program management and executive leadership
  • Ability to build strong relationships with stakeholders, customers, colleagues and suppliers
and to communicate complex quantitative analysis in a clear, precise and actionable manner
