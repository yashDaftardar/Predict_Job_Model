Position: Staff Software Engineer
Location: Denver, CO / Mountain View, CA / Cleveland, OH
About ViewRay
ViewRay®, Inc. (Nasdaq: VRAY) designs, manufactures, and markets the MRIdian® radiation therapy system. MRIdian is built upon a proprietary high-definition MR imaging system designed from the ground up to address the unique challenges and clinical workflow for advanced radiation oncology. Unlike MR systems used in diagnostic radiology, MRIdian's high-definition MR was purposely built to deliver high-precision radiation without unnecessary beam distortion, and consequently, help to mitigate skin toxicity and other safety concerns that may otherwise arise when high magnetic fields interact with radiation beams.

At ViewRay, our vision is clear. We’re dedicated to giving doctors new and better ways to treat cancer with radiation therapy.

We’re an entrepreneurial company with big ambitions, unlimited potential and a passion for improving the lives of patients with cancer. We’re always on the lookout for talented people who share our commitment and values. Contact us if you’re enthusiastic about working with a close-knit team and motivated by the prospect of making a difference.
The Vision for Success:
Implement and deliver quality software for MRI-guided Radiotherapy technology. Support direct manager to successfully execute committed development roadmap .
  • Work on development, enhancement and maintenance of MRIdian Treatment Planning and Delivery application software, with the focus on front-end visual elements
  • Work with product manager and UI designer to define how application looks as well as how it works and bridge the gap between graphical design and technical implementation
  • Deliver software system requirements, design, and test documentation, as well as execute unit testing for product enhancements introduced. Author use cases, software requirements specifications, software design documents, and test specifications. Participate in peer design and code reviews
  • Work with software engineers and cross discipline engineers to integrate software with the overall Treatment Planning and Delivery system. This includes testing, debugging and analyzing issues during integration and verification
The Value you Will Bring
  • BS in computer science or a related field, minimum 3 years of industry experience of web application development using HTML5, CSS3, and ReactJs
  • Extensive experience with React/Redux in a dynamic and content-rich application
  • Top-notch coding skills and in-depth knowledge of modern web technology
  • Strong debugging skills across all major browsers
  • Experience with REST-based Web Services development
  • Aggressive problem diagnosis and creative problem-solving skills
  • Ability to work and thrive in a fast-paced environment, learn rapidly and master diverse web technologies and techniques
Bonus Points
  • Extensive experience with WebGL shader is a strong plus
  • Extensive experience with rendering frameworks WebGL is a strong plus
  • Knowledge and development experience in medical imaging, image processing, 3D rendering, advanced mathematical algorithms, parallel computing is a strong plus
  • Experience with test-driven development practice is a plus
