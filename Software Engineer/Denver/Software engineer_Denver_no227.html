Software Developer
Littleton/Fort Collins/Denver/Boulder Colorado area
American Innovations protects people and the environment by providing proven compliance solutions to oil and gas professionals from the field to the office. More than 30 years of experience drives innovative solutions that address the need for efficient data collection, reporting, and analysis – an integrated family of hardware, software and professional services backed by relentless customer service.
We are seeking a talented, motivated individual to join our Integrity Management Team. You will be joining a dynamic team of various expertise as we continue to enhance our flagship product for pipeline integrity management, RIPL, and geospatial consequence analysis, RiskCAT.
You will encounter a management team that is very supportive, really understand and values your contributions, and provides you every opportunity to be a success. If you are looking for a career, and not just another job, this is the place for you!
Position Alignment
What you can offer us:
Software Development – 70%
  • Deliver on-time, on-budget, high quality code; communicate and help resolve any issues that arise. Be a positive and productive member of the team.
  • Work closely with QA to ensure no code is released without proper testing and review. Promptly address quality issues; participate in prioritizing bugs – apply your experience to help explore possible short comings. Quality over delivery date.
  • Demonstrate initiative and ownership – create great code with the whole solution in mind instead of just achieving the minimum required.
  • Seek out help from a Sr. Developer when you have come to a stalemate; explain what you have done and what help you need.
Planning and Design – 10%
  • Understand your part of the product and service road map; with the team lead(s) and Development manager, begin to understand our road map and plan how we will execute it; give input at to what can be done technically.
  • Help Design great products; ensure a common look and feel across product lines; conform to consistent coding practices and adhere to the common architecture.
  • Understand the requirements you are responsible for delivering, make every reasonable effort to clearly know what “done” means before you start planning or coding; use requirements to drive the complete solution. Look for gaps and propose alternatives to address gaps.
  • Look to use common code; ask how this has been done in the past; create reliable estimates; identify and communicate potential risk areas; follow coding standards.
  • Improve your understanding of user needs & experiences by connecting to the outside world (user meeting, customer visits, etc.) & apply that in your planning & design.
Support and Service – 10%
  • Help customer service provide exceptional technical support and software insight; propose options and recommendations for implementation and issue resolution.
  • When directed by your manager, provide Tier 3 customer support in a timely and constructive manner – help validate issues, develop workarounds, give input on issues and provide technical answers to customer questions.
Process and Efficiency – 5%
  • Understand the metrics for Software Development that drive performance; give input to managers on effective metrics; regularly and honestly report your results; challenge yourself and others to improve.
  • Raise issues and concerns about the resources and time necessary to do the job correctly.
  • Document how we do things so we can review and incrementally and systematically improve how we code and delivery outstanding products.
  • Actively participate in project post-mortems; discover great practices we want to adopt & areas for improvement; removing inefficiency & barriers; communicate & implement improvements as directed.
Professional Development – 5%
  • Develop skills to fill technical needs on the team; identify and develop personal growth opportunities.
  • Stay abreast of current technology and incorporate that into your deliverables appropriately; paying close attention not to fall behind the technology curve.
  • Help my team understand my strengths and where best I can be utilized.
  • Grow and craft the career I want. Identify what motivates me.
  • Support the 121 process, evolve my understanding and growth with the 121 process.
  • Understand my strengths and weaknesses, develop a plan to get better. Improve my development skills, learn software best practices, be a resource to other developers.
  • Support and strengthen the culture, clearly understand and communicate purpose and culture.

What do you need to be successful?
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science or closely related field, desired.
  • Excellent communication skills (verbal, written, and interpersonal)
  • Able to clearly articulate technical issues, collaborate on complex design problems, have impeccable integrity, and a positive can-do attitude.
  • 3+ years of experience with C# - windows forms/GUI, and SQL or NoSQL experience
  • 1+ years of experience with Angular Web Component development, Typescript preferred, and geospatial programming functions.
  • Proficient with ASP.NET Core or .NET Core.
  • Versed in use of software patterns and practices; reactive programming.
  • Experience with 3rd party software components, open-source software, and use of Application Lifecycle management such as CSTS and Git source control.
  • Experience building MSI installers and compilers.

What we can offer you:
  • Hiring Salary Range: $75,000-$90,000 (Salary to be determined by the education, experience, knowledge, skills, and abilities of the applicant, internal equity, and alignment with market data).
  • Competitive benefits focused on your physical well-being, including Medical, Dental, and Vision insurance and company provided Life and Disability.
  • Programs to improve your financial well-being, including a 401(k) plan with an employer match up to 4% with immediate vesting and financial education courses.
  • Opportunities to give back to the community, including paid volunteer hours.
  • Supportive and collaborative environments, happy hours, and fun events.
  • Assistance to further your learning and development.
  • A rewarding culture, with a focus on positive business practices and protecting the environment.
