Full Job Description

Diablo Media is looking for a full-time DevOps Associate Engineer to join our technology team. This person will be responsible for wearing multiple hats and is a proven self-starter with the ability to problem-solve and participate in diverse team projects. The DevOps Associate Engineer will help add infrastructure to streamline development and deployment of strategies to drive growth and efficiency across the business.

This is a full-time position and will report to the Developer. This role can be remote or based out of our office in Denver, CO.

Responsibilities Include:

  • Design, build and maintain a stable and efficient infrastructure to service delivery across production, QA, and development environments through the development cycle
  • Monitor, troubleshoot, maintain, and improve building, packaging, and deployment process
  • Implement automated infrastructure capabilities inclusive of backups, security tools, and monitoring
  • Consistently utilize a DevOps approach to improve all phases of the process to drive end-to-end quality across functions
  • Manage all aspects of onboarding/offboarding from a technology standpoint
  • Manage equipment inventory, support, and installs across the company
  • Technical research and administration of new cloud services
  • Administrative responsibility of AWS resources, and refactoring into Infrastructure-as-Code

The Ideal Candidate:

  • Has excellent organizational skills
  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills (written and verbal) to work collaboratively
  • Self-starter, knowledgeable, and works well within a team environment
  • Keeps up with web technology best practices and emerging technologies
  • Strong problem-solving skills

Experience & Knowledge:

  • Degree in Computer Science or related field
  • 1-2 years of experience in DevOps
  • Knowledge of version control repositories and tools (GIT, GitHub)
  • Knowledge of continuous integration and pipeline delivery (Jenkins, Github Actions)
  • Knowledge of AWS, containers, and serverless technologies
  • Experience working with network infrastructure, database, cloud and data center operations, and security protocols
  • Experience with programming/scripting languages (Python, Perl, Bash, PHP, Javascript, SQL)
