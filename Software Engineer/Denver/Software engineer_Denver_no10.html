Xactly Corporation was founded to solve an important challenge: how to help people everywhere connect and unleash their human potential through seamless, perfectly aligned Sales Performance Management. Xactly’s comprehensive Sales Performance Management (SPM) suite leverages data from a sales performance AI platform to provide timely insights across the entire SPM value chain, from sales planning to execution, including compensation to optimization. Xactly takes Sales Performance Management from a dark art to a science, providing decision-makers with the data insights they need to tap the motivational power of their incentive compensation. With access to predictive analytics and benchmark data, enterprises can now optimize territories, design the right compensation and incentive plans and accelerate attainment and optimization of sales performance.

THE OPPORTUNITY
As the Backend Engineer on our Platform team at Xactly Corporation, you’ll be an essential member of a fast moving agile development team building the latest Xactly products. You will contribute to all aspects from design and development to deployment and support working alongside product management and customer support. You’ll be responsible for taking personal ownership in both your code and others, this includes ensuring proper tests, documentation and communication around development.

THE TEAM
Xactly’s engineering team is a tight-knit rapidly growing team with diverse backgrounds. We have people that came from startups, Fortune 500’s, and everywhere in between. We pride ourselves in building teams with a diversity of opinions in order to build the best software possible. We understand that not everyone works the same way and strive to create a flexible open environment to allow you to create the work/life balance that works best for you.

THE SKILL SET

  • Experience in web application development and architecture.
  • Experience using open source software libraries
  • Experience in at least one MVC architecture or application of the pattern
  • Hands on experience with Java
  • Familiarity with end to end continuous integration and deployment infrastructure for micro services
  • Familiarity in an agile (Scrum) development process
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or other relevant technical degree or equivalent experience preferred
