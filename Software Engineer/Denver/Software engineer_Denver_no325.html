As a Lead Back End Developer, you will:

Together with your team, take lead on designing, building and maintaining scalable modern backend solutions with quality, longevity and security in mind in a DevOps setup. You will work in close collaboration with your DevOps team and product owners following Agile principles and aim to deliver outstanding customer experiences. You will be passionate about architecting straightforward systems to solve complex problems using modern technologies. You will obsessively simplify solutions and eliminate complexity wherever you find it, making our systems robust and long-lived.

The Lead Back End Developer will have:
  • Bachelor’s Degree in a related discipline; Equivalent combination of education and/or experience will be considered in lieu of a degree
  • Bi-lingual abilities are a strong bonus in this role
  • Proven track record from architecting and shipping scalable modern cloud systems in an Agile environment
  • Designed and developed microservices in an enterprise environment
  • Familiarity with cloud computing concepts and have hands-on experience working in cloud platforms like Azure or AWS
  • Very strong knowledge of .Net Core, GraphQL and proficient in using modern programming languages (e.g. Node.js, C#...) and NoSQL/SQL Storage
  • Proven ability to mentor other developers
  • Experience in working collaboratively in agile DevOps teams
  • Strong commitment in writing reliable, scalable, testable and maintainable code
  • Professional demeanor at all times with internal and external customers, employees and stakeholders
  • Residency in the Broomfield, CO area

Company presentation:

Epiroc is a leading productivity partner for the mining, infrastructure and natural resources industries. With cutting edge technology, Epiroc develops and produces innovative drill rigs, rock excavation and construction equipment, and provides world-class service and consumables. The company was founded in Stockholm, Sweden, and has passionate people supporting and collaborating with customers in more than 150 countries. Epiroc had revenue in 2020 of $ 4.3 billion USD and has more than 14,000 employees worldwide. Principal product development and manufacturing units of Epiroc are located in Sweden, the United States, Canada, China and India.
