Full Job Description

The Sr Software Engineer will help ensure that our software, systems and infrastructure are designed and implemented to the highest security standards. Regularly designs, develops, implements and supports internal business systems/applications, systems interfaces, databases, reporting, and/or business intelligence systems, as required to deliver proactive methods to enhance our overall security posture. This software must meet customer’s requirements, scale easily, remove the limitations of traditional networking solutions, and support deployment in highly available environments. The Sr Software Engineer will work with a team of Software Engineers on complex projects and normally receives little instruction on daily work. Technical assignments require sound judgment when resolving issues and making recommendations as well as considerable initiative. Works under the guidance of a Principal Engineer and often leads and mentors other Software Engineers.
  • Bachelors Degree Computer Science or Engineering
  • Masters Degree Computer Science or Engineering
  • 5-7 years Technical engineering experience.
  • 2-4 years Experience in security technology or related field
  • 2-4 years Experience Security and knowledge of current technological trends and developments in the area of info security.
  • Knowledge of information security policies and regulatory controls (per team function)
  • Some knowledge of critical security elements, threats, vulnerabilities, and safeguards
  • Subject matter expert in a few facets of network & information security, including Firewall policy design, SSL Certificate management, vulnerability analysis & mitigation, and other topics as assigned.
  • Knowledge of Scripting tools (Python/Perl/Shell/HTML/PHP).
  • Knowledge of federal & compliance regulations e.g. SOX, PCI & CPNI
  • 2-4 years Experience in a dedicated security engineering role
