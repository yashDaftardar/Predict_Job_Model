Full Job Description

We are looking for strong Software Developers to join our team and help us build out our new product suite. Responsibilities of this role include being an active participant in the design and architecture of our products, working with others to design and implement features as prioritized by product, and troubleshooting technical issues as needed.


Organizationally, you’ll be aligned within product work streams that will have one or more developers working together. You must be able to work well and get along with others, and have a strong focus on supporting your peers and our customers. You must also have a passion to help ensure high quality software to maintain the trust of our highly valued customers as well as have a strong desire for continuous improvement that pushes you to learn and become involved in how our software is developed.


We imagine a successful candidate to be one who thrives on learning new technologies and architectures. They exhibit traits such as thoroughness, attention to detail, curiosity, is easy to work with, strong written and verbal communication skills, being a team player, and the ability to learn new technologies quickly. We believe in team centric, agile development where each member is a vital contributor to, and is accountable for, our customer's, and our success. Our development team believes strongly in delivering high quality and a testing discipline that ensures our customers are confident in our company and our services. Our developers have a passion for learning and teaching. We integrate knowledge sharing and research into every iteration and give our developers time to discover the right way to solve our problems. Our developers believe it's equally important for that knowledge to be shared with the entire team so that any developer can work on any story at any time. We believe agility isn't just about sprints and planning days, it's the way we think, plan, and prioritize to make sure we deliver what is needed, when it's needed.


Our products are all cloud-based, with a mixture of technologies that include Salesforce, Ruby on Rails, Typescript and React, with some of the applications hosted on Amazon AWS. Some of the tools we use include Slack and JIRA.


Software Developer responsibilities include:

  • Review product requirements and collaborate with team to develop new features
  • Actively participate in Agile ceremonies such as planning and retrospectives
  • Ability to learn new technologies and methodologies and apply them to what we are building

Software Developer requirements are:

  • Interested in being a full-stack developer with an area of expertise (backend/frontend)
  • Understands what it means to build scalable, secure, SaaS solutions.
  • Is a self motivated life long learner with a passion for their field, and a desire to share their knowledge and experience with their team.
  • Wants to be part of a company that is constantly innovating and looking for new opportunities.
  • Thrives in a team centric culture.
  • Understands and believes in agile development.
  • Has a college degree, or relevant work experience to interact with both business and technical audiences every day.
  • Believes that they are the best person to ensure the code they write is solid, tested, and ready for customer use.
  • Believes in automated (unit / integration) testing and is capable of writing tests that ensure their work is ready for customers no matter how long ago it was created.
  • Bonus if they have worked on consumer as well as business web applications
  • Can adapt and adjust schedules with their team to ensure everyone is able to balance their work and personal lives while still nailing each delivery date.

Why You’ll Love Working Here:

  • Competitive compensation package including 100% paid coverage for the employee for Health, Dental, Life, Disability benefits and 50% coverage for Health and Dental for dependents
  • Flexible time off + 12 paid holidays!
  • Opportunity to work for an entrepreneurial company in an exciting phase of growth
  • Great company culture and office that includes foosball, ping pong, a full kitchen for enjoying great food and drinks together, regular team and company social events, and much, much more!
  • 401(k) with matching

While we support remote work arrangements at this time, this position is only open to local candidates who will be able to work from our Denver or Broomfield offices.
