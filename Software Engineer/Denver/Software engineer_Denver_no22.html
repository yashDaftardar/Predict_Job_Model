Software Support Engineer

Allegion is seeking a highly motivated, Software Support Engineer to join our team, ensuring the highest quality of customer service using the latest technologies and tool sets. Our Software Support engineers combine a passion for learning new and existing products with their skill of empathizing with the customer to deeply understand needs. The ideal candidate should have proven expertise in diagnosing issues, creating elegant solutions and tools to better support our customers. Excellent written communication is a must combined with a passion for clarity and curation of self-service support portals.

About You & What You Will Do:

  • Perform deep and detailed analysis on customer issues, find the root-cause, and address them fully.

  • Curate and build a high-quality, lean and relevant knowledge base to empower customers to self-service and enable agents to provide elegant and delightful solutions.

  • You have solid OOP understanding in C# or Java with a passion and ability to learn quickly and follow best practices during development.

  • You enjoy diagnosing customer issues to great depths and working with test engineers, development engineers and product owners.

  • You are curious beyond the obvious - looking for solutions that are elegant and delightful as well as opportunities to streamline existing tool sets and workflows.


What You Need to Succeed:

  • Provide thoughtful, optimal solutions or else address any gaps with permanent solutions that improve the overall customer experience.

  • Curate a complete knowledge based that is lean, relevant, easy to search and easy to understand.

  • Excellent written communication skills and ability to diagnose issues based on written reports.

  • Passion to deeply understand customer’s needs and desire to diagnose issues and find the root-cause.

  • Enjoy code – both reading and writing with experience in Java or C#.

  • Design solutions that are elegant and delightful with a full understanding of a system.

  • Enjoy working with engineers as well as empathizing with customers.

  • Are passionate about helping others as well as providing tools for others to help themselves.

Bonus points for:

  • Fluency in native mobile languages (Java, Kotlin, Swift or Objective-C).

  • Familiarity with Cloud software, microservice architecture and one or more cloud providers, including Azure, AWS, etc.

  • Creating, contributing or maintaining a blog, support forum or similar online community

Allegion is a Great Place to Grow your Career if:

  • You're seeking a rewarding opportunity that allows you to truly help others. With thousands of employees and customers around the world, there’s plenty of room to make an impact. As our values state, “this is your business, run with it” .
  • You value personal well-being and balance, because we do too!
  • You’re looking for a company that will invest in your professional development. As we grow, we want you to grow with us.
  • You want a culture that promotes work-life balance. Our employees enjoy generous paid time off, because at Allegion we recognize that you have a full life outside of work!
