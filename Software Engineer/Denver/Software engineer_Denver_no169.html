Viasat has an immediate need for a talented Software Applications Engineer to join our growing Global Broadband Operations Organization (GBO). At the Global Broadband Operations organization, our mission is to provide exceptional customer service using world class processes, tools, visibility, and communications so that customers around the world trust Viasat services to work reliably. As a Software Engineer on the GBO team, you will create and develop software solutions and platforms that scale with Viasat’s growth as a global ISP. You will be identifying issues and common patterns to improve the quality of service to Viasat’s customers. You will engage with state-of-the-art anomaly detection systems to reduce our time to detect network issues. Our Software Engineers interact with data-driven workflow engines to identify opportunities for improvement and design solutions to improve critical network communications across our DevOps community. In this position, you will:

  • Work with a team that embraces the lean startup methodology
  • Have a big impact and influence on the direction of the products and technology stack
  • Use a wide array of technologies to build innovative solutions for global scale
  • Partner with teams across the company to help develop robust and integrated end-to-end solutions
  • Design and build creative, customer-focused tools from the ground up
  • Engage in evolving business critical solutions that have a global impact on operations and help streamline communication
  • Utilize the Agile Scrum process to deliver iterative products
  • Employ machine learning techniques where applicable
Requirements

  • 4+ years of software development experience
  • Experience leading a small software team
  • Experience with designing scalable software architecture and utilizing design patterns
  • Embraces DevOps software development practice (design it, create it, own it)
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • A degree in computer science or software engineering, or equivalent experience
  • US Citizenship required
  • Up to 10% travel
Preferences

Familiarity with:
  • Cloud technologies
  • Data analytics platforms such as ELK, Kibana, Grafana, Splunk, Druid
  • High-level networking principles including subnetting and firewalls
