Summary/Objective:

Elevations is in the process of taking the foundation of its software development practice to the next level. We are looking for an individual with a passion for best practices across the QA and Agile and a strong passion for delivering quality products.

The QA Engineer will be part of a growing QA organization. This position will be responsible for the design, development, and execution of test cases to ensure software products meet business needs. You will be responsible for catching bugs through manual testing processes. You will work closely with our Business, Product Managers and Developers to ensure that all code developed is properly tested.

Essential Functions include:

  • Participate in review sessions of business requirements, technical requirements and design documents to ensure the software changes are understood
  • Coordinate with multiple business stakeholders to develop and ensure end to end test coverage
  • Develop and manually execute test cases based on requirements
  • Provide continuous updates on the state of testing and risk factors to schedules
  • Work closely with other team members in the development, execution and tracking of corrective action plans that drive improved compliance, continuous improvement, increased performance and efficiencies
  • Communicate effectively with both technical and non-technical colleagues at all levels in both verbal and written communications
  • Contributes technical knowledge and understanding to training programs, support processes, and operating procedures

Reports to:

IT QA Manager

Manages:

This role does not have supervisory responsibility

Required Competencies:

Decision Making, Quality Orientation, Planning and Organizing, Continuous Improvement, Influencing, Building Networks

Required Education and Experience:

  • B.S. in Computer Science, Information Systems, Engineering, or equivalent
  • 3-6 years of similar or related experience
  • Understanding of agile development processes
  • Ability to effectively use troubleshooting and technical skills
  • Intellectually curious & analytically minded
  • Ability to clearly communicate about software defects, including steps to reproduce
  • Ability to work independently as well as in teams

Preferred Education and Experience:

  • Preferred field of study: Computer Science or related field
  • Developing automated test scripts with Selenium or related automation tool
  • Experience with Python or Java
  • Prefer knowledge of and experience with client and server side JavaScript, Azure or AWS cloud vendors, Linux and relational databases
  • Prefer knowledge of and experience with IBM’s AIX operating system, Jack Henry & Associates (Symitar division) products including Episys and related (PowerOn) development
