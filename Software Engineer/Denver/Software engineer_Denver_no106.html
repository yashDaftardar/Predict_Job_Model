Lead Software Engineer (Front-End)

Software Engineer roles can be demanding but offer massive returns and celebrate talent, creativity, teamwork, and perseverance. What makes it even better? Working for a company with a cool product and a team that makes coming to work fun. Switchfly offers an exciting, collaborative and fast-paced environment where ownership, problem-solving and teamwork are rewarded. If you have a sense of humor and embrace the ‘Extreme Programming’ values and practices, we have an opportunity for you…


A Day in the Life…

As Switchfly’s Lead Software Engineer, you help shape our web technology innovation as we continuously improve our platform. You lead & collaborate on the design and implementation of new features and write elegant, maintainable, and extensible code in a team environment. With help from the QAs amongst the scrum team, you lead and implement unit & integration tests to ensure the delivery of high-quality features. You welcome the opportunity to lead a variety of senior & junior engineers (as direct reports) as you collaborate and impart your experience to enhance their skill sets and deliver top-of-class SaaS products. You help review code changes by team members and develop or provide tools to improve productivity.


The Fine Print

You are strong in front-end design and have deep experience in using JavaScript plus HTML and CSS with modern front end SPA paradigms. Experience with RESTful API and API to API integration as it relates to front-end code a must as well. Experience with Java on the back-end is helpful as well, bonus if you drink from both mugs. Extra credit for experience with VueJS and Ember You love working within various portions of our codebase and using technology to solve business problems. You are excited to be a servant leader to other experienced engineers, and you also enjoy working in teams and can manage your time effectively. You have a passion for technology outside the workplace with an interest in various libraries and tools. Most importantly, you consistently exhibit Switchfly’s core values within your own team and across the organization.


Salary Range: $120K-$130K/year as base
