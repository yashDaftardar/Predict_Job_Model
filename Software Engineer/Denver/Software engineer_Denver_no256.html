Who is Institutional Cash Distributors Technology (ICD Tech)?
Institutional Cash Distributors Technology, LLC (“ICD Tech”) is the technology development and technical operations subsidiary of a highly successful financial services company and industry leader, collectively known as “ICD”. Our impressive client list includes many of the world’s largest and most influential corporations, who use ICD’s web-based portal, ICDPortal.com, to manage their short-term cash investments. Our best-of-breed products and services and our longstanding commitment to excellence and IT security have earned us a stellar reputation with both our clients and partners.

At ICD Tech you will find a tight-knit group of motivated and talented individuals who are both serious about company success and passionate about our team and culture. Our work environment is fast-paced, collaborative and fun. We value work-life balance and treasure the relationships we’ve developed with our colleagues.

We are seeking thoughtful team players who are willing and excited to embrace ICD’s mission, become part of the ICD Tech family, and go above and beyond to achieve even greater success.

Software Engineer Responsibilities:
  • Design, develop, test, deploy, and maintain distributed systems written in Java, Groovy, Python, and JavaScript. Participate with the team in the collaborative design and development process.

What does it take to be successful in this role?
  • 3+ years of backend development experience
  • Experience with one or more general purpose programming languages including but not limited to: Java or Python
  • 1+ years experience coding in JavaScript related front end frameworks
  • Interest and ability to learn new languages and technologies as needed
  • Working proficiency and communication skills in verbal and written English
  • Commitment to unit test best practices
  • Ability to estimate programming tasks with reasonable accuracy
  • Ability to coordinate work effectively with other team members.
  • Work with the team and perform tasks involving TDD, ATDD, releases, CD/CI
  • Required Skills:
    • Java/Python
    • JavaScript / AJAX
    • SQL
    • Spring / Django, flask
    • Hibernate or other ORM framework
    • Git
    • Dependency management using Gradle / Maven / Ant
    • Linux environment & tools such as bash, ssh, sftp, rsync, cron

  • Nice to have Skills:
    • Cross-browser web development (IE, Firefox, Chrome, Safari)
    • Test driven development (TDD); domain-driven design (DDD)
    • Experience with UI/UX development is a plus
    • Docker and Kubernetes
    • PostgreSQL
    • JQuery

  • Technology we use but you aren't required to know before you start:
    • Groovy
    • Grails
    • Security & crypto technologies
    • Virtualization technologies such as VMware
    • Continuous integration tools such as Jenkins
    • Modern JavaScript technologies such as React and ag-Grid
    • Agile / Scrum / Kanban, software release process; familiarity with Rally is a plus

The Impact You'll Have at ICD Tech
At ICD Tech the Customer is a big deal! We help our Customers' Corporate Treasury groups streamline their day with a world-class financial services portal and a multitude of integrations with their mission-critical financial systems. ICD Tech has successfully integrated with all major treasury management systems, and with numerous other business partners including major financial institutions, transfer agencies and clearing/custody firms.

We need a few smart, versatile software engineers to help us solve the most pressing challenges in the financial services industry. You will be working alongside other dedicated and passionate engineers and product managers to build the systems to power the next generation of ICD Tech applications. Your efforts at ICD Tech will help our Customers invest more reliably, effectively, and securely.

While you don't need a fancy degree to work here, you do need to embrace learning and continuous improvement, have a strong technical skill set, and favor simple solutions over complex ones. You should have great communication skills and be able to create requirements from business objectives. We primarily use Java, Groovy and JavaScript for our systems although there are several other tools you'll encounter. The people we hire are pragmatic, versatile, data-driven and self-directed.

People First
At ICD Tech we are building an extraordinary company that helps people conduct their business activities more reliably, effectively, and securely. We put people first, do the right thing, and succeed together.

We have a strong belief that our people are the most important part of our business. We incorporate this philosophy into our practices and we live it every day. This means we have competitive salaries and generous traditional benefits like paid time off, paid sick leave, health insurance and take a lot of time to focus on quality management and leadership development. We also strive to ensure each person in the company is in the right job, knows how their job contributes to the overall company mission, and has a clear sense of career growth and direction.

The ICD Tech experience is a two-way exchange. We expect you to give us your best every day, and you can expect the same from us. In exchange for your talent, your energy, and your unique contributions, we will strive to give you what you need to live a balanced, healthy life.
