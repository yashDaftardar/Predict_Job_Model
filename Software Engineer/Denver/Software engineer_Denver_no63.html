Software Developer

Welcome to Campspot! Whether you are an avid camper, the occasional glamper, an expert RV-er, or new to the outdoors entirely, we're interested in working with you. Our team values diverse perspectives as we seek to create an easy-to-navigate and meaningful camping experience for everyone.

Particularly as a Software Developer, we are looking for someone who is always asking, learning and growing. Our dev team believes we are all both students and teachers. For us, that means all questions are welcomed and everyone can learn from each other.

We hope you will consider joining our team! ALL human beings are welcome, and we are committed to helping you thrive here. We believe our team, as well as the outdoors, is for everyone.

Your day to day:

  • Design, develop, test, and deploy solutions for our software on your own or in collaboration with other developers
  • Review the code of other developers on your team
  • Work with product owners and designers on your team to create workable solutions

Software developers at Campspot work on small agile teams to build our products. Campspot developers are truly full stack, working on both front and back end code, as well as managing the whole lifecycle of a feature from planning through delivery.

Campspot is built on a modern tech stack using the latest versions of Java and Kotlin on our backend with the Dropwizard framework, and TypeScript on our frontend using Angular. Our products are SAAS applications that run on AWS cloud infrastructure and we use modern tools like Terraform to manage that. We believe that skilled developers can be successful in any tech stack and set our expectations for ramp up time accordingly, but having experience in any of the technology above is a big plus.

Development at Campspot is heavy on collaboration. Developers are expected to collaborate with each other via technical design meetings, pair programming, and code reviews. Developers also collaborate directly with our product owners to scope, refine, and test features. Lastly, developers need to collaborate with our designers to create solid UX for new features in our app.

Automated testing is another key skill for developers at Campspot. All new code coming into our application is expected to have unit and integration tests written for it. We utilize automated CI builds to constantly make sure that our app builds correctly and all of our tests pass.

Experience that is typically helpful:

  • 1+ years of experience with web application development
  • Experience identifying and solving software bugs
  • Excitement for learning new technologies
  • Solid communication - receiving and giving feedback on code and architecture, ability to talk through ideas and approach
  • Collaboration, including sometimes doing pair programming with other developers

Bonus points:

  • Experience with Java, Kotlin, or TypeScript
  • Experience with AWS or other cloud infrastructure
  • Experience in an environment that does TDD or pair programming

Compensation Range: $85k - $120k annually.

About Campspot:

Founded in 2015, Campspot was created by campground owners for campground owners. This is because our founders saw a tremendous need to operate their multiple parks more efficiently, while delivering a modern online booking experience for guests. Campspot is truly setting the standard for what the best all-in-one campground management and reservation software can be.

We are a motivated team of outdoor enthusiasts and tech professionals with decades of combined experience in the RV park and campground management industry. Most importantly, we know how hard you work and we’re here to help. Our mission is to provide our customers with the online tools needed to easily grow their businesses and deliver their campers the highest quality of service.

Campspot is headquartered in Grand Rapids, Michigan, and our software development teams are based in Denver and Chicago. Together, we’ve built more than just a growing customer base: We’ve built a family of dedicated partners that we love serving.
