Company Description


We are seeking a Senior Software Developer (RPA) to join our team in the Denver Tech Center!

*Due to public health concerns, many of our Denver office associates are working remotely during this time. However, our ideal candidate will have daily Denver Tech Center office availability post-pandemic. AIR offers a flexible working environment, however this position is not considered a remote-work role.*

At AIR, we exist to serve our residents by creating quality apartment homes and exceptional environments. Leveraging fast paced technical solutions supporting our team members, and residents is key, and success in this role means high execution, customer satisfaction, achievement of company performance targets and value-added projects that support the strategy and goals.


Job Description


A Day in the Life:

  • Provide development support for Blue Prism automations and infrastructure
  • Provide technical support for RPA processes including making administrative changes, creating and enhancing workflows in the domains of accounting, marketing, and real-estate property management
  • Support for other tools used to support RPA processes. For example, SharePoint Lists and PowerApps
  • Limited C# development and SQL Query enhancement within automation scope
  • Stay current with the latest in RPA tool and development trends and make recommendations to the AIR IT Executive Management Team

Supervisory Responsibilities: Non-Supervisory


Qualifications


Education & Experience:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, engineering, or equivalent years of experience
  • Minimum 2-4 year of relevant work experience in robotic process automation
  • A solid foundation in software development life cycle and source control patterns & practices.
  • A standout candidate will have PowerApps and SharePoint experience
  • Experience writing and debugging C# and SQL code preferred
  • Passionate self-motivator that thrives on results.
  • Strong time management and organizational skills, appetite for continuous learning and documentation skills
