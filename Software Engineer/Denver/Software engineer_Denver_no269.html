Full Job Description

Grow your career with a growing organization

Whether they’re helping people reach their long-term financial goals or providing personal wealth management strategies, every associate contributes to changing the lives of those we serve for the better. When it comes to job satisfaction, that’s hard to beat. And from a personal satisfaction perspective, you’ll enjoy the freedom to support causes that matter to you and experience a truly inclusive work environment. Your future starts now.

This Associate Web/Drupal Developer will assist to elevate our digital and web strategy to offer a compelling, personalized web experience to plan sponsors, advisors, plan participants and individuals with retail choice. This will position requires strong collaboration and communication skills with the web master, other developers, project managers, UX/UI teams, and business strategy teams.

What you will do

  • Assist and maintain the Drupal CMS and Acquia environment for our marketing websites.

  • Be the lead developer and content administrator for our learning center website.

  • Support other developers with non-Drupal, custom developed software applications such as part-hub pre-login websites, sales demo and the campaign command center web application.

  • Build new features, components, and modules based on the need of the business.

  • Install and maintain new code bases, multi-site configurations and Lift personalization sections.

  • Gather requirements for new pages and determine which components will best serve the content and overall experience.

  • Participate in the requirement gathering process, daily standups and retrospective meetings.

What you will bring

  • Bachelor's degee

  • 1-3 years’ experience in Drupal 8 development.

  • 1-3 years’ experience with complex enterprise or multisite Drupal implementations.

  • 1-3 years’ experience using Composer and Drush.

  • 1-3 years’ experience using Twig.

What will set you apart

  • PHP and object oriented development experience.

  • Solid Frontend skills (HTML, CSS, JS, React.js, Node.js)

  • Knowledge of Database Architecture (MySQL, PostgreSQL)

  • Experience writing custom modules for Drupal 8 and integrating with external 3d party APIs

  • Familiarity with Drupal 8 configuration management, including config-split

  • Ability to work effectively in the command line interface, Git, GitLab.

  • Ability to develop, review, and test custom Drupal modules and themes

  • Good communication and relationship building skills.
