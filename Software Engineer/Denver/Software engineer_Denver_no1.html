The team behind Slack’s Desktop application is looking for a Software Engineer to join us in building one of the most heavily used pieces of software in the world.
As a team member, you’ll be designing and building solutions that lies at the heart of Slack’s most used client. Your team is responsible for a critical layer of infrastructure between Electron – the open source framework that powers our app – and the frontend of Slack. Ultimately your work will empower the tens of millions of people that rely on Slack every day. You’ll focus on bringing the latest native capabilities to our platform, ensuring consistent integration with the OS, and laying a high-performing foundation for frontend engineers throughout the organization.
Slack has an empathy driven, diverse, and supportive culture and in turn we look for curious, inventive people who strive to grow a little every day. If this sounds like a good fit for you, get in touch.
What You Will Be Doing
  • Writing code that impacts millions of users every day
  • Evaluating and implementing new technologies to improve performance and stability across the desktop ecosystem
  • Empowering product teams with the technical capabilities to build delightful, thoughtfully-integrated experiences
  • Collaborating with support teams to understand and resolve the most challenging issues our customers face
  • Building and maintaining the tools our frontend teams use in their day-to-day work
  • Setting boundaries and maintaining a healthy work-life balance
What You Should Have
  • Fluency in JavaScript and the web ecosystem
  • 2+ years of experience building desktop applications, perhaps but not necessarily with a web-based framework like Electron
  • Familiarity with frameworks like React, Redux, and RxJS
  • Comfort with the shell and a variety of operating systems
  • Strong problem-solving skills and a tenacious attitude towards open-ended challenges
  • Excellent communication skills and a collaborative mindset
Slack has a positive, diverse, and supportive culture—we look for people who are curious, inventive, and work to be a little better every single day. In our work together we seek to be smart, humble, hardworking and, above all, collaborative.
