Full Job Description

Position Overview :

This part-time, temporary role will assist our development teams in producing scalable software solutions. They will be part of a cross-functional team that’s responsible for the full software development life cycle, from conception to deployment.

The ideal candidate should have had some exposure to both front-end and back-end coding languages, development frameworks and third-party libraries.

Essential Job Functions :

  • Working within the defined Software Development Life Cycle process, coding standards and best practices
  • Improving existing programs by analyzing and identifying areas for improvement
  • Supporting Quality Assurance in testing, bug fixing and eliminating defects
  • Prepare test data and executes detailed test plans.
  • Maintain very good working knowledge of the supporting application(s), development environments and the supported client environment.
  • Understand and adhere to the Company's risk and regulatory standards, policies and controls in accordance with the Company's Risk Appetite.
  • Works under the guidance of senior development team members to develop, document, test, and debug new and existing software systems and/or applications for market sale or large-scale proprietary software for internal use.
  • Uses Microsoft .NET Framework and supporting programming languages (e.g., Visual C#, JavaScript, T-SQL).
  • Supports, maintains, and documents software functionality.
  • Analyzes code to find causes of errors and revise programs as needed.
  • Ensure software satisfies all non-functional requirements such as quality, security, modifiability, stability, etc.
  • Such other duties as assigned by your manager

Qualifications/ Requirements :

  • Actively pursuing Bachelor’s degree or equivalent from four-year College or technical school in a Computer Science, Software Engineering or related field
  • Working knowledge of Microsoft SQL Server, Visual Studio Development Software and Microsoft Office Suite
  • Working knowledge of programming and systems analysis procedures
  • Demonstrates attention to detail, accuracy, and thoroughness
  • Strong organization, administrative and documentation skills
  • Ability to work independently and creatively, learn quickly, and solve complex problems in high-pressure situations
  • Adapts to every changing environment and changes approach or method to best fit the situation
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills

Special Requirements :

  • The employee may be required to report to a different local office as a normal, contemplated, and mandated incident of their employment

Working Conditions :

  • Office environment with frequent computer, mouse, keyboard use
  • Alternating between sitting or standing as needed
  • Hearing, talking, reaching, grasping
