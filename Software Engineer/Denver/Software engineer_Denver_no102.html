Who we are:
Efficient Forms provides human capital management (HCM) software and tax credit processing. The Company's SaaS platform, Efficient Hire, provides employers feature rich solutions for sourcing, recruiting, and applicant tracking, onboarding, I9 /E-Verify, Work Opportunity Tax Credit (WOTC), and ACA compliance. With over 2,500 customers and 1 million employees onboarded annually, our customer base spans across industries including restaurant, QSR, convenience store, retail, hospitality, staffing, and home healthcare. Founded in 2004, Efficient Forms has a legacy of innovation and industry-leading customer service.

The Role:
As a Mid-level Software Engineer Integrations, you will be responsible for designing and developing modules and features within the Efficient Hire product focused around 3rd party system integrations such as background screening, payroll systems, and pay cards. This position will work with our existing engineering and product team, as well as clients to develop, maintain, and support 3rd party integrations.

We are looking for people who are ready for an agile environment where you will be asked to work as part of a team in large projects as well as smaller projects on your own from start to finish. This is an opportunity to take charge of your future and to grow and learn, while at the same time making a positive impact in the lives of others and disrupting the employment hiring process.

Our team is a close-knit fun group that is very supportive and eager to help, those that take advantage of the opportunities and are self-driven will be most successful. You will be given the ability expand your technology skill set, and be exposed to other business disciplines as well.

What you'll do:
Work with project stakeholders to clarify business requirements for integrations with new partners.
Generate technical requirements based upon project needs
Manage project timelines and deadlines with integration partners.
Develop the software integrations with 3rd party integration partners.
Collaborate with 3rd party integration staff for test and deployment.
Learn and support our existing 3rd party integrations.
Provide technical support to our application support team for all integrations


What you'll bring:
BS/MS in Computer Science or related discipline
4-6 years experience in a software engineering role, demonstrating a progression in responsibility and skill set.
Experience and desire to work across the full stack
Experience in SaaS web applications
Proficient with SQL, creating complex queries and query tuning. We use MSSQL in shop.
Development Languages & Tools: .NET (VB, C#, ASP), Classic ASP, Visual Studio, Github, VSCode, Jira
Experience with JSON, REST, SOAP, WCF
Desired experience with system integrations using web services (any flavor)
Excellent written and verbal communication skills with an attention to detail
Exceptional problem-solving skills
Ability to work effectively in a remote environment
