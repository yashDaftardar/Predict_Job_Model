Title: Software Support Engineer

Reports to: Director, Software Services

Location: Lakewood, CO

Travel: Less than 20%


MedKeeper creates cloud-based mobile applications to help health care professionals better manage pharmacies and track medications. We're always looking for smart, talented, hard-working people to join our team. We offer a full range of benefits, but the most important one is a fun and casual work environment. We work closely together and we're excited about what we do. We believe that exceptional people create a great culture and ultimately, an outstanding business.


We are an award-winning company based in Denver, Colorado. MedKeeper has been named the KLAS Category Leader for IV Workflow Management since 2017 as well as “Top Vendor-In-Use” in 2020 by Pharmacy Purchasing and Products (PP&P) for our PharmacyKeeper Verification application.


The Software Support Engineer (SSE) is responsible for understanding code, testing, and maintaining MedKeeper’s premier products. The SSE uses innovative technical problem-solving skills on a daily basis and must have the ability and desire to work autonomously and “own” their work. The SSE needs to have the ability to work effectively along with other individuals and departments as needed.


Job Responsibilities:

  • Effectively troubleshoots and replicates reported issues and works with support and development teams to provide workarounds and resolutions.

  • Partner with development and operations in any necessary troubleshooting or analysis of software related issues

  • Prioritizes critical issues, providing direction and ensuring appropriate client support is delivered in a timely and effective manner.

  • Provides technical solutions to customer needs that cannot be solved by support staff.

  • Creates and maintains tools used by support staff.

  • Performs analyses on software application functionality and suggests improvements.

  • Consults with the software development team, internal users, and clients to improve application performance.

  • Investigates the root causes of application errors and escalates serious concerns to a department manager.

  • Monitors application performance metrics and diagnoses performance degradation.

  • Participates in an on-call after-hours support rotation.

  • Other duties as assigned


Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in computer science or relevant field

  • 1-3 years of experience in Java programming. Experience based around mobile development, web development and/or database development a plus.

  • Must have a passion for problem solving.

  • Self-starter, self-motivated, self-directed, self-sufficient. Able to hit the ground and start running.

  • Possess a desire for continuous improvement.

  • Exceptional attention to detail and technical aptitude.

  • Ability and desire to work autonomously in a progressive, fast paced environment.

  • Ability to work cooperatively and as a resource with internal teams, as well as external customers.

  • Communicate effectively with technical and non-technical audiences


Compensation:

The position includes competitive compensation and benefits including medical, dental, and other insurance, as well as paid holidays and time off. (Will vary by candidate)
