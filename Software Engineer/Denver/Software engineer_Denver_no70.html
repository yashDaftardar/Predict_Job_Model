Position Description

We are looking for a talented Staff Software Engineer to help us advance the suite of Conga products. This is a great opportunity for someone who is looking to use his or her expertise in development as a member of a dynamic team that designs, develops, tests, and maintains the Conga products. We want to hire unbelievably bright developers, who are a joy to work with, love to work with others, solve problems, listen well, and lead as appropriate. We believe in using the best tools for the task at hand so the ability and desire to learn new programming languages and technologies is a must. All of this adds up to an exciting, challenging, and always interesting place to work, where hard problems are found and solved every day.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop a deep understanding of the relevant business areas and various components in the stack
  • Take ownership of, architect, build and scale business critical services
  • Be introspective, always trying to improve yourself and the team around you
  • Participate on a DevOps / agile team full lifecycle and full stack, including design, development, testing, planning, backlog grooming and support
  • Develop and maintain related application code â€“ full stack development
  • Develop automated tests to run in a continuous integration environment
  • Identify and implement best practices for coding as well as development processes
  • Perform as a trusted contributor requiring little to no supervision and demonstrating the ability to meet deadlines, exceed goals, and create benchmarks for success

Knowledge, Skills and Experience:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related discipline.
  • Solid communication skills and ability to convey technical solutions to the end-user of all levels of ability.
  • Demonstrated professionalism interacting with both internal and external audiences.
  • Proactive, self-driven and enjoys solving complex problems, not just in the primary area of responsibility but across boundaries.
  • 8-12 years of experience as a full stack engineer working on front-end technologies like AngularJs and ReactJs and building back-end services in C# .NET or other object oriented language.
  • Programming experience in .NET, C#, JavaScript, Java, or C++ with demonstrated drive to learn and progress in the technical skill and nuanced art of development. Direct Java experience is preferred.
  • Familiarity with Salesforce CRM or prior experience with CRM integrations.
  • Demonstrated success executing development work within an Agile environment.

Nice to Have Skills:

  • Has worked on different cloud technologies.
  • Has exposure to cloud patterns and drawbacks.
  • Prior experience with ElasticSearch, MongoDB
  • Exposure to Actor Model Programming would be an added plus
