About Autodesk

Autodesk is the world's leading design software and digital content company, offering customers progressive business solutions through powerful technology products and services. Autodesk helps customers in the building, manufacturing, infrastructure, digital media, and wireless data services fields increase the value of their digital design data and improve efficiencies across their entire project lifecycle management processes.

Position Overview

FormIt Pro in the AECGD (Generative Design) group and Building Infrastructure and Design division (BID) is responsible for the product marketing, product management, and development of FormIt Pro on clients: Windows, Mobile and WEB. We are seeking a full stack Software Engineer to help this effort by providing strong software design and development expertise in the creation of the tools, platform and services that make up the 3D design products and services. As a Software engineer, you will have a huge impact on how we design, build and deliver our tools and services to millions of people. We are looking for engineers who love finding efficient and thoughtful solutions to a variety of technical and product challenges. You should strive to write efficient, maintainable code and you should enjoy pushing code every day.

Responsibilities

  • Develop high-level software design, APIs, prototypes and production code to implement 3D conceptual design applications and solutions

  • Collaborate with software architects, product designers, product managers, test engineers and others to implement high-quality applications and solutions

  • Thoroughly unit test, debug and solve defects in implemented software solutions

Minimum Qualifications

  • BS or MS in Computer Science, Mathematics, Engineering or equivalent work experience

  • Strong programming skills, including C++ and Javascript

  • Strong experience with frontend web technologies including HTML5, CSS, jQuery and WebGL as well as backend web technologies including Node.js

  • Solid understanding of the full web technology stack involving HTTP, cookies, headers, asset loading and caching and the ability to find and resolve performance bottlenecks

  • Knowledge and experience in 3D design, geometric modeling and content delivery applications

  • Hands-on experience with application development on Windows, Apple, web platforms

  • Experience with development on mobile platforms, iOS is preferred

  • Ability to convert complex software solutions into well written technical design documents

  • Familiarity with the software development process, including specification, quality assurance and documentation

  • Comprehensive knowledge of software development methodologies such as Agile

Preferred Qualifications

  • Strong communication skills with the ability to converse fluently with multiple types of audiences ranging from managers, customers, partners and software development personnel

  • Passion for developing robust software and writing maintainable code

  • Keen interest in new technologies and their timely adoption

  • Ability to learn large software systems quickly

  • Ability to work well in a team to deliver on team goals Flexibility under changing conditions and ability to multi-task between projects

  • Self-directed and require minimal supervision

  • Ability to communicate with local as well as remote and international teams
