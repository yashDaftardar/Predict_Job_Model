Raytheon Intelligence & Space is seeking a highly motivated and qualified Section Manager for the Configuration, Data, and Software Configuration Management team in Aurora, CO.

The Section Manager serves as the direct line manager for ten or more Configuration Analysts, Data Analysts, and Software Configuration Managers. This is a hands-on leadership position that requires the candidate to be directly involved in the CM, DM, and SWCM activities performed for the programs within the assigned mission area. Responsibilities include management, training, mentoring, staffing and professional development of employees in the Configuration, Data, and Software Configuration Management roles supporting selected programs.

The candidate will provide sound Configuration Management/Data Management/Software Configuration Management (CM/DM/SWCM) related technical judgment and leadership, be highly proactive, and knowledgeable in CM/DM/SWCM processes across the business areas supported by the CM/DM Department.

Responsibilities include managing employees who perform all aspects of configuration, data, and software configuration management including: development of Configuration Management planning, establishment of program Engineering Review and Configuration Control Boards (ERBs/CCBs), deployment and maintenance of the Work Product Management System and other activities related to the identification, control, and status accounting of systems, software, and/or equipment in accordance with contractual requirements.

The Section Manager will interface with internal and external customers, coordinating process and product reviews, establishment and maintenance of product baselines, and the review and release of engineering work products. The Section Manager will review current and future staffing and training requirements to ensure that a plan is in place to meet all program needs. The Section Manager also develops and administers budgets, schedules, and performance standards and prepares and reviews bids for new business.




Required Skills:
  • Minimum of 8 years of Engineering or related experience to include Configuration, Data, and Software Configuration Management experience in a DoD environment
  • Comprehensive understanding of the Configuration, Data, and Software Configuration Management disciplines
  • Ability to lead and mentor junior team members
  • US Citizenship
  • Active Secret Clearance
Desired Skills:
  • PTC Windchill Experience
  • Minimum 3 years of experience with an infrastructure as code or software version control repository such as BitBucket, GIT, IBM Rational ClearCase or Rational Team Concert, or Subversion
  • Minimum 3 years of automation experience shell scripting in LINUX, Unix, or Windows
  • Minimum 6 years of experience in software development, software configuration management, or systems administration
  • Familiarization with RIS policies and processes
Required Education (including Major):
  • Bachelor's Degree in STEM - Computer Science or related discipline
Benefits offered: medical, dental, vision, life insurance, short-term disability, long-term disability, 401(k) match, flexible spending accounts, EAP, education assistance, parental leave, paid time off, and holidays.
