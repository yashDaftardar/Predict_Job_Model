Veeva is a mission-driven organization that aspires to help our customers in Life Sciences and Regulated industries bring their products to market, faster. We are shaped by our values: Do the Right Thing, Customer Success, Employee Success, and Speed. Our teams develop transformative cloud software, services, consulting, and data to make our customers more efficient and effective in everything they do. Veeva is a work anywhere company. There are options to work remotely from home or in an office on any given day. As a Public Benefit Corporation, you will also work for a company focused on making a positive impact on its customers, employees, and communities

Senior iOS Engineers at Veeva develop apps used by our pharmaceutical customers as they meet with health care providers around the globe. We are looking for highly technical, detailed, and creative engineers who are able to partner closely with fellow engineers and product managers. You will play a key part in creating apps that deliver an exceptional user experience while retaining quality at scale. As a Senior Software Engineer, you will work as part of our team building high-quality enterprise iOS applications for one of the fastest-growing public tech companies.

What You'll Do

    • Develop large scale features
    • Solve complex problems
    • Create and implement in-depth technical designs
    • Leverage unit tests to build resilient, high-quality enhancements
    • Own features within your areas of responsibility
    • Collaborate with Product Managers and Engineers
    • Participate throughout the software development life cycle
    • Deliver features within schedule
    • Support continuous improvement
    • Find and investigate new technologies to present them for architectural review

Requirements

    • 5+ years building applications for the iOS ecosystem
    • Strong Object-oriented background
    • High proficiency with Swift/Objective-C
    • Experience working with iOS frameworks (Core Data, SQLite, Core Animation)
    • Familiar with high-level software design principles
    • Understanding of Apple’s design principles and interface guidelines
    • Experience with resource management and performance tuning
    • Strong communication skills
