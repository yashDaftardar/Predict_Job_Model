JOB SUMMARY

We are looking for an experienced and enthusiastic DevOps Engineer to join our American Financing team. You will utilize your knowledge and experience in systems administration, scripting and development to support initiatives to automate tasks, standardize environments and deploy Continuous Integration and Continuous Deployment solutions. You should understand and work well in Agile environments and have hands-on experience with DevOps, including administration, configuration, development and deployment.

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Provide the tools and methodologies for the development team to achieve CI/CD
  • Automate systems administration of Linux and Windows-based environments
  • Maintain proper documentation of solutions and environments
  • Provide technical support to developers around tool usage and build environments
  • Create and maintain build automation and deployment code for production environments
  • Provide guidance to establish and implement build methodologies
  • Evangelize best practice DevOps and Agile within the broader organization

QUALIFICATIONS AND EXPERIENCE

  • BS in Computer Science, Information Technology or equivalent experience
  • 3+ years experience as a software or DevOps engineer
  • 3+ years experience in systems engineering
  • Experience with deployment tools (preferably Jenkins and TFS)
  • Experience with automation tools (preferably Ansible, Chef or Puppet)
  • 1+ yearsâ€™ experience with container technologies (preferably Docker and Kubernetes)
  • 3+ Scripting experience (preferably Bash, JS, or PowerShell)
  • Experience deploying to Cloud Platforms (Azure, AWS, preferably GCP (GKE)
  • Experience with ELK stack and/or other Monitoring tools
  • Self-starter, independent thinker
  • Ability to work in a collaborative team environment
  • Hands-on administration experience with Linux, Windows servers, Apache, IIS, NGINX, MS SQL, PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB
  • Experience with Cloud Environments (preferably GCP)
