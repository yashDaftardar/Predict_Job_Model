Overview:

Strive Health

Strive Health is built for purpose- to transform a broken kidney care system. We are fundamentally changing the lives of kidney disease patients through early identification and engagement, comprehensive coordinated care, and home-first dialysis. Strive’s model is driven by a high-touch care team that integrates with local providers and spans the entire care journey from CKD through ESRD, leveraging comparative and predictive data and analytics to identify patients at risk. Strive Health’s interventions significantly reduce the rate of emergent dialysis crash, cut inpatient utilization, and significantly improve patient outcomes and experience. Come join our journey as we create THE destination for top talent in the Healthcare community and set a new standard for how care kidney should be done.


Senior Software Engineer

The Senior Software Engineer will have considerable influence on architecture, processes and team building. You'll have a ground-floor opportunity to significantly influence the design and operation of every aspect of a multi-tenant SaaS for multiple product lines and a highly-available, high-performance API. We constantly seek to improve our team's impact and morale as we work together to improve health and happiness for hundreds of millions of people.
Essential Functions:
  • Use cloud-native infrastructure and modern software development methodologies
  • Share ideas freely to keep our software and processes accessible, scalable, and sustainable
  • Iterate frequently on our software and our processes to enhance outcomes for ourselves and our users
  • Experiment with emerging technologies to accommodate the expanding scope of this ambitious project
  • Celebrate each other’s success.
  • Live and demonstrate our Core Values (Care for Others First, Innovation, Resilience, Tenacity, and Fun) every day.
Qualifications:

Minimum Qualifications

  • Embrace and learn from failure.
  • Are self-motivated and detail-oriented.
  • Communicate politely, clearly, and precisely.
  • Celebrate diversity, equality, and inclusion.
  • Seek to simplify code, systems, and processes.
  • Enjoy learning from and mentoring your teammates.
  • Can adhere to data security and confidentiality protocols and procedures.
  • Enjoy participating in the code’s complete lifecycle, from feature design to production support.
  • Are a life-long learner who's enthusiastic about learning and evaluating emerging technologies.
  • Enjoy soliciting and incorporating design and feature feedback from users in small, short iterations.
  • Are excited about helping to vastly improve healthcare outcomes for hundreds of millions of people.
  • Enjoy discussing technical design, creating proofs-of-concept, and helping to plan feature development.
  • Have experience with AWS technologies such as Data Lake, Kinesis, Lambda, and Glue.
  • Are fluent in relational databases (MS SQL).
  • Ideally, have additional experience with distributed systems, observability, DevOps principles and agile software development, Elasticsearch, MongoDB, Azure, Entity Framework, Hibernate, RabbitMQ, Kafka, Python, Golang, Docker, and Kubernetes.
  • Experience with SalesForce/HealthCloud would be a bonus.
