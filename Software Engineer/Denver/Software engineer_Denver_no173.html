Full Job Description

Your Opportunity


The FDM team provides financial instrument and other data via applications and services across Charles Schwab. This is an exciting opportunity to lay the foundations for the tools and technologies powering our next generation of services as we build out our modernization platform. Our team works on fresh, dynamic challenges every day and prides itself on delivering robust, well-engineered solutions that tackle these opportunities.

What you are good at


As a Web Developer for the FDM team, you will be responsible for getting safe and resilient production code out the door as quickly as possible. You will be producing highly usable and scalable web software solutions in both our new and legacy application environments. You will work with business stakeholders and development leadership to understand requirements and help develop the solutions looking from technology and also from client’s perspective. You will work with other engineering groups to understand the domain applications to come up with a solution that can be used across different applications.

What you have


  • 2-3 years’ experience with Angular, Javascript, Typescript, HTML5, and CSS3
  • 2-3 years’ experience connecting web UIs to back end web services utilizing REST and SOAP
  • 2 years’ experience with older Javascript frameworks such as jQuery and Data Tables
  • Experience with Java and Spring Boot or other similar Java backend development frameworks including legacy technologies such as JSPs and Struts a plus
  • Experience with package managers such as NPM
  • Experience with unit testing patterns (TDD/BDD) and methodologies
  • Experience and comfort communicating with senior technical staff, leadership, partners, and peers
  • Strong interpersonal skills and must exemplify the vision and values of Schwab
  • BA/BS degree in Computer Science OR related practical experience
  • 4+ years of experience working in cross-functional projects and programs with tight deadlines
  • Knowledge in building highly scalable, highly performant, and time critical systems
