Position Summary:
The Software Engineer III develops and/or provides technical leadership in the development of software involving application of new technologies with significant technical risk. He/she prepares detailed plans, which may span over a year for programs or complex projects. The Engineer determines test philosophy, goals, and objectives. He/she participates in the formation of project goals, scope, and schedule.

Essential Responsibilities (includes, but is not limited to):

    • Analyzes, designs, and develops complex application features
    • Designs and develops a processing platform using various configuration management technologies
    • Mentors junior level Software Engineers
    • Provides ongoing maintenance, support, and enhancements in existing systems and platforms
    • Collaborates cross-functionally with data scientists, business users, and other engineers to achieve solutions

Ideal Skills, Experience, and Competencies:

    • At least seven (7) years of relevant software development experience
    • Effective leadership abilities
    • Advanced experience with relevant programming language (e.g., Java, Javascript, Groovy, etc.)
    • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
    • Experience with innovative application design and implementation
    • Effective problem solving and troubleshooting abilities

Required Education & Certifications:

    • B.S./B.A. degree in Computer Science or related field; or equivalent work experience

Our Stack

    • Frontend -> VueJS 2, Bulma
    • Backend -> Java/Groovy, Kotlin, Spring Framework, Relational Database System, NoSQL

Compensation:

    • Qualified candidates can expect a salary beginning at $110,000 or more depending on experience
