Job Title: Sr. Software Engineer

Location: Westminster, CO

Division: Trimble Geospatial

The Geospatial engineering team in Westminster is looking for a highly skilled, highly motivated Software Engineer with C# .NET experience interested in working in a high-paced, agile and international development environment. This individual will be a key part of the team developing Trimble’s flagship office software for surveyors.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Understand and analyze marketing requirements, lead and propose innovative designs

  • Develop, test and maintain our desktop application

  • Coordinate efforts with worldwide distributed hardware and software teams

  • Align work with our various centers of expertise, integrate specialized components into the software solution

  • Full commitment to an Agile development process

Skills & Experience Required:

  • BS or MS Computer Science

  • 3+ years of experience as a developer in a C# .NET environment

  • Experience in UI development, using tools like Infragistics, WPF and Windows forms

  • True team player able to work with and support remote teams, reactive & proactive

  • Experience with SQL or other database is desirable

  • Experience in writing and consuming web services (MS Web API, AWS, Azure, …) is desirable

  • A strong math background and experience with optical survey, GNSS, GIS, CAD, Civil Engineering, 3D scanning or photogrammetry are a strong plus
