Full Job Description

Job Description:


Under supervision, the Intern, Manufacturing Automation Engineer will work directly with engineers, technicians, manufacturing and quality functions, and will assist in the Design, develop, of automation and control systems, equipment, and machinery. This position will assist in efforts to transition from manual or semi-automated processes to automated processes and smart factory manufacturing

Essential Functions and Basic Duties

  • Assist in Optimizing existing automated and partially automated processes to improve quality, efficiency, throughput, and cost
  • Seek and qualify novel technologies, and if appropriate, validate and integrate to manufacturing site/production processes
  • Contribute to the development of process automation to new product and product enhancement programs, coordinating and integrating specialized processes into a lean manufacturing environment
  • Contribute to the Design, development, and implementation process automation (hardware & software) to improve productivity, quality, equipment performance, energy consumption, and downtime reduction
  • Design and programming of new custom equipment and equipment modifications
  • Work closely with Automation Engineering and other Engineers on process and programing issues
  • Program, troubleshoot and repair of automation equipment as necessary
  • Create and maintain documentation associated with custom equipment
  • Proficient in SolidWorks 3D CAD to create fixturing and production support systems
  • Perform other duties as required and directed by Mechanical Engineering Manager

  • Superb computer skills and demonstrated proficiency in a C# programming

  • Support multiple projects while handling multiple tasks in order to successfully meet deadlines.
