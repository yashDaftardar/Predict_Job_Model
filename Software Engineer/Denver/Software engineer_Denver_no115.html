Position Description

Ricoh is looking Software Quality Development Engineer to work on agile project team in support of development, qualification and delivery of Print Workflow Management software. Team member will be responsible for identifying, planning, and implementing test coverage to support continuous release of the product and customer focused testing. High emphasis on automation and CICD methodology to support on premise and cloud applications.

Job Duties and Responsibilities

  • Work on agile scrum team: Participate in Standups, Grooming, Demos, Retros
  • Analyze requirements and identify test coverage
  • Write automation to support new function delivery and regression
  • Identify, document, and verify defects
  • Document, track, and report test status
  • Provide development support as needed: coding, defect fixing, tool maintenance
  • Support product field activities
  • Work with and support global product team
