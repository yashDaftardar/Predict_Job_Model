KPMG is currently seeking a Manager in CIO Advisory for our Consulting practice.

Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with the Modern Delivery team on business development activities, including identification, proposal development, and other pursuit activities
  • Perform comprehensive assessment of the current state at the client and work with KPMG and Client cross-functional team to deliver transformative recommendations to adopt Agile and DevOps practices
  • Engage with KPMG leadership to continuously improve the Modern Delivery services and build capabilities for the engagements
  • Lead and manage the engagement plan and develop the deliverables for the Clients as outlined in the engagement contract
  • Build a short-term and long-term transformation plan based on the assessment results
  • Coach and mentor cross-functional teams on Agile and DevOps practices and principles

Qualifications:

  • A minimum of five years of experience in Software Development and information technology
  • A minimum of three years of experience driving the adoption of and achieving successful Agile and DevOps practices across organizations and a minimum of one year of hands-on experience in a Technical Agile Coach role
  • Bachelor's Degree from an accredited university in Computer Science or related discipline
  • Experience in professional services consulting delivering large transformation services and experience DevOps leadership and IT Operations transformation with custom solutions
  • Experience with agile planning, deployment, integration, test/validation, and configuration custom software across multiple DevOps tooling platforms; Experience with configuration management tools (Puppet, Ansible, Chef, or similar) and experience in automation by scripting in Bash, PowerShell, Perl, Ruby, JavaScript, and Python
  • Strong practical administration skills in Cloud and Virtualized environment (GCP, AWS, Azure, VMware, etc.) and experience in cloud management technologies, specifically with the GCP, AWS, Azure and OpenShift
  • Travel as needed
  • Applicants must be currently authorized to work in the United States without the need for visa sponsorship now or in the future
