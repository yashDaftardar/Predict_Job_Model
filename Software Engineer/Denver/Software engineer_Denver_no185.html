ABOUT US

AUTOPAY is an innovative FinTech company that is powering finance in the age of mobility. We function as a virtual Finance & Insurance office, finding our customers the perfect lender for their car loan or refinance. This means we're able to solidify our customer's auto loan prior to going to the dealership, and our marketplace of lenders ensures they get the lowest rate available.

WHY WORK WITH US

We come from all kinds of backgrounds and experiences to create something new: A better way to do car loans. We support a casual work environment, positive work culture, and develop our talent to become successful leaders. Our core values of Hard Work, Optimism, Growth, and Integrity help us create a collaborative team mindset and a better place to work.

SUMMARY

The Software Developer Engineer in Test will contribute to AUTOPAY by creating and designing test automation solutions for AUTOPAY systems. You will work directly with Developers, Product Managers, and Technical Product Managers to ensure quality development and quality of our applications and systems. You will be responsible for driving the software development process toward quality-centric methodologies.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES include achieving positive results in the following key areas:

  • Understand and demonstrate the principles of AUTOPAY’s Mission, Vision, and Values.
  • Create, Enhance, and Extend AUTOPAY’s test automation frameworks.
  • Identify and document quality assurance tasks and strategies.
  • Monitor, maintain, and investigate the health and performance of AUTOPAY systems.
  • Collaboratively work with team members in managing and testing the features/bugs in a sprint.
  • Manage individual project priorities, deadlines, and deliverables.
  • Work within the product and design teams on ensuring the quality of a variety of platforms and products
  • Adapt to business requests and requirements to meet company goals.
  • Continue to learn and grow your craft.
  • Additional duties as required.

Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities

  • Education: Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Business, Finance or related field or equivalent experience required.
  • Experience:
    • Minimum of four (4) years of experience in an agile software development environment as a SDET or QA Engineer with automation experience.
    • Experienced in web test automation.
    • Demonstrated experience in test framework design and development
    • Demonstrated ability to develop and deliver requirements for complex feature sets.
    • Proficient with a wide arsenal of software tools, including Git, Development IDEs, Jira, Insomnia/ Postman, and more
    • Usage of AWS and awareness of the plethora of capabilities provided.
  • Language/Communication/Interpersonal: Ability to speak read and write fluent English. Must possess exceptional written, oral, interpersonal, presentation and collaboration skills. Must be self-motivated, detailed-oriented, able to define ideas down to a specification level. Must be able to drive projects, debate the merits of approaches, and work effectively with others.
  • Analytical: Demonstrated ability to measure and track results of work. Including defining key success metrics for projects, incorporating measurable business objectives, revenue optimization, and client experience best practices.
  • Reasoning Ability: Excellent judgment and creative problem-solving skills, including negotiation and conflict resolution, consensus-building skills.
  • Other : High level of personal motivation, professionalism, and integrity required. Ability to exercise discretion and judgment.
