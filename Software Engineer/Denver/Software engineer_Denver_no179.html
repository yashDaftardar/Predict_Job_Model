At KPMG, we are reimagining tax with you in mind. Become an integral part of a dynamic team within a collaborative, future forward environment at one of the world's top tax firms. Be surrounded by deep tax knowledge and equipped with the latest in cutting-edge tax technologies. And join an extensive network of functional, industry, legislative and technical specialists with access to our Ignition Centers, where technology innovation accelerates. We welcome people with a passion for tax, a spirit for new thinking, and a commitment to providing outstanding client service today, and tomorrow.

KPMG is currently seeking a Sr Associate to join our Tax Ignition practice.

Responsibilities:

  • Research, analyze, design, and deliver of Tax solutions that are appropriate for the Tax business and clients
  • Define and develop new Tax solutions leveraging programming technologies for example ASP.NET, .NET, C#, Java, Python, HTML, JavaScript technologies and frameworks such as AngularJS, KnockoutJS, BackboneJS, ReactJS, database technologies such as SQL Server, ORM frameworks such as entity framework and cloud platforms such as Azure, Google and Amazon
  • Design and development of solutions that integrate with vendor tools seamlessly utilizing REST services and other integration design patterns; Prepare technical artifacts to help document technical architecture, design and deployment diagrams
  • Adapt and learn new technologies such as Cloud, Intelligent Automation, and Data Analytics in a fast paced dynamic environment; able to take ownership and accountability of development process through the entire software lifecycle which includes Architecture, Design, Development, and DevOps
  • Practice standard development process leveraging agile methodologies such as SCRUM and enforce development standards defined by the department on best practices
  • Review and analyze business requirements and provide technical feasibility and estimates, as well as work closely with Tax business team, clients and various technology teams to ensure development of a quality product

Qualifications:

  • Minimum three years of experience in developing, unit testing and troubleshooting applications software using ASP.NET MVC, .NET, C#, HTML, and REST APIs
  • Minimum two years of experience in leading development projects in a fast paced client facing dynamic environment
  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Engineering or Computer Science from an accredited college/university
  • Experience with JavaScript technologies and frameworks such as AngularJS, KnockoutJS, BackboneJS, ReactJS and SQL Server
  • Advanced level of knowledge on application development using these technologies is preferred and three years of experience in designing database schema, develop stored procedures and other database objects on SQL Server (similar experience in comparable technologies such as Oracle is accepted)
  • Prior working knowledge of web debugging tools including Fiddler, Browser developer tools, and debugger
