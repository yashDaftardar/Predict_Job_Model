Full Job Description

We are looking for a highly motivated, dynamic, self-directed UI|UX designer with strong interests and capabilities in the design and development of engaging user interfaces and user workflows. Our team is focused on driving a culture of experimentation and bold thinking through rapid build/measure/learn cycles. You will be working and coordinating with colleagues who bring engineering, industry domain, business development, design and analytical thinking skill sets to the table, and who, more importantly, are dedicated to taking a customer-centric approach to developing innovation that will deliver lasting, and differentiable value.

Job responsibilities will include, but not be limited to:

  • Work with stakeholders to determine requirements, articulate user needs, and translate into concepts and elegant interface designs by using storyboards, process flows and sitemaps
  • Collaborate with stakeholders on persona development and customer journey maps
  • To get regular and iterative feedback from users on designs, and responding to that feedback
  • Verify UI user stories for to ensure design objectives are met in implementation.
  • Develop and maintain lo-fi wireframes, hi-fi mockups and interactive prototypes
  • Be involved upfront and have your voice heard as it relates to user experience, interaction and visual design. You are the expert, bring your ideas to the table!
  • Research interaction design and technology trends.
  • Identify and troubleshoot UX problems (e.g. responsiveness)
  • Adhere to style standards on fonts, colors and images

Qualifications:

Required:

  • BS Computer Science or equivalent
  • Has five or more years of UI or UX design and/or development experience for software, web applications in Agile environment
  • Oil field or other hands-on Oil & Gas experience and expertise

OR:

  • Graduate/ Post Graduate/ UX/UI Specialized course, Exposure to Agile Methodologies
  • Has 2 years of UI or UX design and/or development experience AND oil and gas expertise and experience.
  • Possesses a solid understanding of web/mobile UI standards, responsive design frameworks and usability.
  • Experience with HTML5, CSS3 & JavaScript for rapid UI prototyping.
  • Possesses excellent written and verbal communication skills.

Desired:

  • Ability to lead and coach peers in UI design and development
  • Has working knowledge of modern UI/UX design toolchain, e.g. XD, Sketch, Axure, Zeppelin, Marvel, Realtime board, Photoshop, Illustrator, and/or associated design tools.
  • Exposure to Modern JS frameworks such as Angular, React and Vue
  • Has visual design chops and a desire to delight users with simplicity.
  • Has experience conducting user testing and/or analytical research.
  • Willingness and experience to work UI/UX design work into an agile Scrum process
  • Excels in front of a white board and can run point during a design sprint.
  • An avid learner, willing to expand skill sets and seek advice from others when needed.
  • Self-starter, self-directed with strong work ethic and ability to work independently with minimal direction
  • Full-stack development skills/experience as well as focus on front end development
