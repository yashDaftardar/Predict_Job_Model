Mobile Software Engineer, SketchUp Mobile | XR Team

Location - Westminster, CO Or USA Remote

This Opportunity

If you’ve ever used SketchUp, you know it’s special software: 3D modeling that’s easy to learn, intuitive to use, and accessible to everyone. Try SketchUp for free in your Android , iOS , HoloLens , VR devices or on your computer browser , or explore creations from hobbyists and professionals on 3DWarehouse . Come work with us and serve millions of users every day.

As a member of the engineering team focused on SketchUp for mobile, you’ll be working to both refine and re-imagine how we deliver cutting edge 3D software on iOS and Android, helping people everywhere express and communicate their ideas.

We’re looking for someone who is highly skilled, motivated, collaborative - and pretty low-key about it all. You should already have some experience writing scalable native mobile applications and have a ‘quality-first’ mindset, and you’re excited to roll up your sleeves for the next big challenge.

About SketchUp, a Colorado startup all grown up

Part of Trimble, SketchUp is one of the most popular 3D modeling and design tools in the world. Today, millions of architects, engineers, contractors, woodworkers, artists, and other creatives use our software to design transit centers, treehouses, and everything in between. We support a healthy work/life balance and high-quality standards for software and coffee.

About Trimble, our parent company

Trimble is transforming the way the world works by delivering products and services that connect the physical and digital worlds. Our core technologies in spatial positioning, modeling, connectivity and data analytics enable customers to improve productivity, quality, safety, and sustainability in design, architecture, and construction.

Primary Duties:

  • Develop client code using one or more of the following technologies: Swift, Objective-C, C++, Java, Kotlin, C#, Unity.

  • Add features collaboratively designed with the Product Manager.

  • Analyze, design, implement new features and improve performance of the applications.

Education Requirements:

  • BS/BA degree in Computer Science or Engineering, or equivalent experience.

Experience Requirements:

  • 3+ years experience developing rich, graphical iOS applications.

  • Experience developing mobile applications in ObjC or Swift using Xcode.

  • Experience and/or strong interest in design, graphics, 3D software.

  • Familiarity with common commercial software development best practices.

  • A test-driven development mentality.

  • Strong problem-solving skills and eager to learn new technologies.

Additional Skills/Experience desired:

  • Experience in one or more of the following technologies: Android Studio, Unity, Visual Studio.

  • Developed apps for the Hololens, Windows Mixed Reality, HTC Vive, Oculus Rift or similar AR/VR headsets.

  • Android, iOS, Java, Kotlin, Objective-C, and Swift.

  • SketchUp C SDK or similar.

  • Python, Batch, and Shell scripting.

  • Universal Windows Platform (UWP) client development.

  • JIRA, Git, macOS, Windows.

  • Graphics programming with OpenGL or similar.

If interested, please apply using a PDF doc resume with links to your published apps or equivalent.
