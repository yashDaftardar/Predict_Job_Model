Senior Front End Engineer

The Senior Front End Engineer is a master practitioner, communicator, and natural leader. Working in a Scrum team, this position will leverage strong technical knowledge of front-end development frameworks to provide customers with a high-quality user experience on both mobile and desktop applications. The Senior Software Engineer is a highly technical member of the team with a mastery of the tech stack, front-end development tools, and practices. In addition, this role will help coach less experienced engineers within the team.

This role is 100% remote eligible.


Job Responsibilities

  • Lead the design and development of scalable, sustainable, maintainable, performant, and secure software to provide customers the best possible user experience.
  • Develop dynamic client-side interfaces using complex JavaScript frameworks.
  • Act as technical expert of our tech stack, tools, practices, and front-end technologies.
  • Complete complex modifications, refactoring, and bug fixes to existing functionality without the need for oversight.
  • Lead technical discussions and code reviews.
  • Collaborate effectively with product and backend teams.
  • Audit existing codebase for performance enhancements.
  • Responsible for thorough testing of output in all supported browsers and devices.
  • Maintain design standards and integrity throughout product interfaces.
  • Drive positive change to improve product quality and development process.
  • Partner with the Scrum team to collectively deliver on sprint goals and commitments.
  • Develop a deep understanding of the business context around the product and see the application not only as a developer, but also from the perspective of a user.
  • Mentor and coach less experienced team members.
  • Stay current on user interface trends and technologies and suggest new tools and methods as necessary.

Requirements

  • Relevant years of experience with JavaScript ES6.
  • Experience and deep knowledge of React and Redux or other modern front-end frameworks such as Angular or Vue.
  • Experience working in a full stack environment preferred
  • If you have worked as a Front End Engineer, Javascript Developer, Front End Developer, Web Developer, you are encouraged to apply.

Skills

  • Expert knowledge of front-end frameworks such as React, Redux, JavaScript ES6, Angular, and Vue. Seen internally as a leader in this area.
  • Mastery of modern front-end build systems such as Webpack, React-scripts/CRA, front-end unit and in-browser automated testing, GraphQL, and REST APIs.
  • Mastery of professional practices such as continuous integration, continuous deployment, test driven development, pair programming, and agile practices such as such as Scrum, Kanban, and XP.
  • Expert knowledge of writing quality front end unit and integration tests.
  • Mastery of front-end development skills, CSS, Semantic HTML, and CSS processors.
  • Mastery of design patterns.
  • Able to perform tasks effectively and pragmatically. Able to debug and diagnose complicated issues. Able to build from scratch implementations following best practices.
  • Capable of owning projects through design, implementation, and deployment.
