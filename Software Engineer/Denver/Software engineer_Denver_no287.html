The Opportunity:

Imperva is a leader in cybersecurity, with a passion for protecting our valuable customer's applications & data. Imperva is known as the cybersecurity experts - trusted by industry leaders - and recognized as best-in-class by both our customers and independent analysts alike.

We are looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join our team to assist in developing Imperva’s fast growing Account Takeover Protection and Client-Side Protection SAAS offerings.

If you have a passion for new technologies and getting hands-on experience creating enterprise-level solutions, you will fit right in.

What you can expect in this role:

  • Engage in full-stack development of an enterprise-level distributed SAAS solutions
  • Utilize cloud technologies and modern programming practices
  • Participate in the team’s agile development process
  • Collaborate, demo and share your work with team members, product managers and stakeholders
  • Communicate with customers and frontline teams to better understand use cases and pain points
  • Utilize modern data analytics frameworks to propose new real-time anomaly detection and mitigation algorithms

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or equivalent experience
  • At least five years of programming experience
  • At least 3 years of experience working with Java - version 8+
  • Passion and ability to learn new software and technologies quickly
  • Responsible for your own work and the team’s success as a whole
  • Willingness to learn and participate in full-stack development tasks
  • Understanding of HTML, HTTP and networking
  • Experience working with distributed SQL/NoSQL DBs systems
  • Ability to lead a project from requirements to completion, through design, implementation & testing

Advantages:

  • Experience developing enterprise-level, scalable web applications
  • Experience with JavaScript - React, Node
  • Familiarity with any NoSQL database
  • Familiarity with microservice-based architecture
  • Experience using Kubernetes and Docker
  • Experience with Kafka and Spring Framework
