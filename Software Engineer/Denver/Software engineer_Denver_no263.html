Full Job Description

Faction is a leading multi-cloud data services provider that pioneered cloud-adjacent storage powered by patented technology that provides data access over low latency, high throughput connections to all the major clouds, including AWS, Azure and Google Cloud Platform. Faction
is also a leading managed service provider for VMware Cloud on AWS, including disaster recovery and production deployments, and was the first to offer cloud-attached storage solutions that integrate natively with VMware Cloud on AWS. Faction’s private and multi-cloud platforms give clients the ability to move, access, scale and protect data between clouds, without the fear of cloud lock-in. Faction, a VMware Cloud Verified provider, is also recognized as an Advanced AWS Consulting Partner and VMware Premier Cloud Provider. Follow Faction on Twitter (@FactionInc), LinkedIn, and BrightTALK. For more information, please visit www.factioninc.com.

Faction’s culture encourages and cultivates an entrepreneurial spirit every day, and embodies out-of-the-box thinking, which all translate to our company values: We are genuine; We are innovators; We are relentless problem solvers; We are passionate about client delight; We are radically transparent; We are a professional team, and we do our best thinking with a shared beverage. Can you manage challenges while maintaining a positive client-focused attitude?

The Position:

As a senior engineer you will contribute to Faction’s customer portal for multi-cloud storage services, as well as internal software projects. Daily tasks will include participating in scrum ceremonies, decomposing product requirements into tasks, collaborating with teammates and writing code for product improvements and new features

Responsibilities:

  • Turn user stories into reality as a member of an Agile team.
  • Responsible for the accuracy of development against requirements

Required Qualifications:

  • Minimum of 5 years of experience as a software engineer.
  • Minimum of 1 years of experience as a senior engineer.
  • Experience with Python and/or NodeJS
  • Experience with microservice and/or serverless architectures.
  • Experience using relational and NoSQL databases.
  • Experience with REST API consumption and production, including schema definition.
  • Experience with version control systems, preferably Git.

Desired Qualifications:

  • You enjoy rapidly mastering new technologies.
  • You like to participate in a high-communication environment.
  • You are the sort of person people enjoy working with
Bonus Points if you also have:

  • Expertise with .NET Core
  • Experience with React and/or Angular
  • Knowledge of PostgreSQL and/or Cassandra
  • Experience integrating with 3rd parties like Salesforce, ServiceNow, LogicMontior, etc
