Embedded Systems Engineer

Qualifications

  • Proficient in C or C++

  • Comfortable working in a Linux environment

  • Working experience with Serial, SPI, I2C, SDIO, or some other embedded serial bus

  • A passion and curiosity for creating and building things

  • Strong organizational skills

  • Proficient in Python preferred

  • Experience working with CAN bus, J1939, OBD-II, or other industrial machine bus preferred

  • Experience with networking systems and wireless technologies preferred

Software Engineer, Controls

As a Control Systems Engineer Intern, you will be working with designing, implementing, and testing a variety of control algorithms. This involves taking controllers from a basic proof of concept in simulation to full vehicle testing. The position may also entail troubleshooting, debugging, and improving upon existing controllers.

Qualifications

  • Currently pursuing a Bachelor’s or Master’s in Engineering or Computer Science

  • Proficient in C or C++

  • Comfortable working in a Linux environment

  • Working experience with Serial, SPI, I2C, SDIO or some other embedded serial bus

  • A passion and curiosity for creating and building things

  • Proficient in Python, Matlab, Simulink preferred

  • Experience with post-processing of large data sets preferred

  • Experience with sensors, actuator, programming microcontrollers, and robotics preferred

  • Experience with system modeling, system identification, discrete filtering preferred

  • Experience with networking systems and wireless technologies preferred

Research and Development Internship

  • Familiarity with GNSS, timing and synchronization, wireless access, and backhaul technologies and networks a plus
