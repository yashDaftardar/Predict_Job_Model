Position Summary:
SomaLogic is a privately-owned biomedical science company that developed a revolutionary proteomics technology capable of measuring thousands of different human proteins in a single small sample. We are now moving towards commercializing the health-related information derived from that technology to help people worldwide proactively manage their health for life. As a part of our commercialization effort, we are seeking additional expertise to join our dedicated team of scientists, bioinformaticians, statisticians, and software developers to help us realize that vision.
We are seeking an experienced DevOps Engineer to implement the company's strategy regarding microservices deployment to the AWS cloud. You will be working with the Software Development team, Bioinformatics team, and IT to build and maintain an automated web application infrastructure.
This is a full-time position.
Key Job Responsibilities:
Develop and deploy microservices architecture to AWS environment following HIPPA, PHI, GDPR compliance standards
Design and implement automated environments for CI/CD pipelines
Implement infrastructure as a code using Terraform, CloudFormation, Ansible, Chef
Work collaboratively with IT, BI, developers, project managers, and QA to develop automation and processes that enable teams to deploy, manage, configure, scale and monitor their applications internally and in the cloud (AWS)
Build and maintain tools to continuously improve our visibility into our systems and applications with advanced monitoring, metrics and log analytics
Troubleshoot systems and problem solve across platform and application domains
