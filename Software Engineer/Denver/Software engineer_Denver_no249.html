Job Summary

The integration engineer will work as part of the product development team and is responsible for assisting partners and clients with building integrations into the AR Boost product line. This individual should have excellent communication skills, as a large part of this position is interacting directly with customer technical teams. This position requires a software development background and a solid understanding of both SOAP and REST APIs.

Responsibilities

  • Act as the AR Boost technical lead on projects involving customers integrating with the AR Boost APIs
  • Troubleshoot customer integration issues when integrating with the AR Boost APIs
  • Provide updates to key internal stakeholders on project progress
  • Work with customer support to assist in technical research and problem solving
  • Contribute as a member of the development team by working on bugs and enhancement requests
  • Work with management to help prioritize development efforts
  • Participate in internal and external audit activities
  • Identify and recommend technologies that may be incorporated into the system
