THE ROLE:

As part of the Cloud Operations team, you will participate in the design, implementation, and ongoing operation of the tools that automate building, operating, and monitoring of Code42 systems. You will participate in software development, including writing maintainable code, comprehensive tests, and mentoring/collaborating with the team in all aspects of application service delivery. These services must be highly available and performant in a 24x7 environment. You will support the Amazon Web Services (AWS) technologies leveraged by the Code42 Cloud eco-system. You will be executing a diverse portfolio of projects within an Agile management framework and will author documentation including standard operating procedures, analysis, and project artifacts.

WHAT YOU’LL BE RESPONSIBLE FOR:

  • Automating the build, deployment, operation, and monitoring of the Code42 Cloud eco-system
  • Troubleshooting complex systems, remediating issues, and taking steps to prevent recurring issues
  • Contributing to other support and automation efforts as needed
  • Adhering to FIPS, PCI, and SOC 2 security compliance in accordance with standards, policies and procedures
  • Proposing and developing new technology standards and best practices as appropriate
  • Collaborating as part of a team of engineers dedicated to putting the customer first


WHAT YOU BRING:

  • Minimum 3 years’ work experience
  • Degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Software Engineering, or Physics (or equivalent experience)
  • Good written and verbal communication skills
  • Linux administration and security experience (Amazon Linux, Ubuntu, RHEL, Debian, or Centos)
  • Experience in filesystem and RAID array creation, maintenance, and recovery
  • Experience with computer networking (Internet routing, load balancing, DNS, etc.)
  • Experience with managing Cloud orchestration tooling (SaltStack, Chef, etc.)
  • Experience working in an Agile Operations/Development environment
  • Versed in programming software (Java, Python, GoLang, Git, etc.)
  • Experience with multi-cloud provider software deployment strategies and tooling
  • Experience with AWS Systems Operations
  • Experience with Ansible and Docker
  • Experience with Continuous Integration, Continuous Delivery (Jenkins, Artifactory, etc.)
  • Experience with Hashicorp tools (Terraform, Vault, Packer, Vagrant, etc.)
  • Available to participate in the on-call support rotation

The salary range for this role is approximately $60,000-$115,000.
