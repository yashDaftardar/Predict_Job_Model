Full Job Description

We are looking for a results-oriented, and enthusiastic Senior Engineering Manager to lead a strategically significant team tasked with implementing our next evolution 5G Internet Service . An “at-home” wi-fi experience at 30,000ft, traveling 400mph? That’s what we’re building!

The ideal candidate for this role will have a solid and practical understanding of the entire development lifecycle from analysis, design, development, and operational support with an emphasis on automation, improved efficiencies, and increased productivity. It is essential that you are forward-thinking, eager to learn and have a passion to explore and experiment with the latest technologies. This will be a key role in Gogo’s Engineering department and will revolutionize the aviation industry.

COME ON BOARD THE GOGO BUSINESS AVIAITON ENGINEERING TEAM!


How will you make a difference?

  • Lead a growing team of highly talented Software Engineers in all of the agile software development phases of various projects, drive roadmap execution, provide technical direction for the project(s) while planning for new ones
  • Manage/mentor employees and provide career growth and learning opportunities for all team members
  • Collaborate with system analysts, engineers, architects and subject matter experts to establish the functional/technical vision and maintain a roadmap for your team
  • Create and evangelize coding standards, engineering and testing best practices
  • Ensure that non-functional requirements such as application security, performance, and quality are baked in; not bolted on
  • Oversee designs and reviews development activities; ensure designs and the product align with the requirements and specifications
  • Work with Senior Management to establish common business goals and then deliver on those goals through technical excellence

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science , Engineering , related discipline, or equivalent work experience
  • 8+ years of experience in progressively more responsible engineering positions
  • 4+ years of managing technical teams
  • A track record of delivering multiple projects on time and experience working with enterprise focused engineering teams

Required Experience, Skills and Talents

  • Extensive experience with software development
  • Ability to think strategically and translate business needs into effective engineering execution by removing roadblocks and providing guardrails for the team to succeed
  • Experience with attracting and retaining top talent
  • Strong technical leadership background with ability to inspire and empower those around you
  • Demonstrated passion for people management, mentoring and growing software engineers to meet their career goals
  • Expertise with Agile Scrum development
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills and the ability to work in a fast-paced team environment
  • Self-motivated individual, able to work through ambiguity and under pressure to meet commitments within tight time constraints

Preferred Experience, Skills and Talents

  • Solid understanding of the AWS technology stack (e.g. EC2, S3, Lambda, DynamoDB)
  • Experience with microservices architecture and SaaS
  • Previous experience with React, Java, JavaScript, Node.js, Go and/or Python programming languages a plus
  • Strong appreciation of CI/CD
  • Test Driven Development (TDD) and an automated testing mindset

Gogo is the leading global provider of network and broadband connectivity products and services for the business aviation market. We offer a customizable suite of smart cabin systems for highly integrated connectivity, inflight entertainment and voice solutions. Gogo's products and services are installed on thousands of business aircraft of all sizes and mission types from turboprops to the largest global jets, and are utilized by the largest fractional ownership operators, charter operators, corporate flight departments and individuals.

There are nearly 1,700 business aircraft currently flying with Gogo’s AVANCE L5 or L3 installed. As of third quarter earnings reported on Sept. 30, 2020, Gogo had more than 10,000 connected aircraft, comprised of 5,577 aircraft flying with its air-to-ground (ATG) systems onboard, and 4,737 aircraft with satellite connectivity installed.
