Junior Developer
Junior Software Engineer - ATM Services
Getcoins is one of the largest multi-cryptocurrency ATM Networks in the world; serving hundreds of thousands of users at hundreds of branches across the USA. Our mission is to provide a familiar and approachable interface to cryptocurrency adoption to consumers throughout the country.

Getcoins is seeking full stack developers to join our team to build out the next generation of our ATM software and online trading platforms. You will solve unique, large scale, highly complex technical problems, bridging the constraints posed by web-scale applications and blockchain technology. You will help build the next generation of systems to make cryptocurrency accessible at local retailers throughout the country.

Due to the tight integration of hardware resources for the project, work will be done in our office on Broadway in Denver most days under non-Covid-19 conditions. Given the current circumstances the job will start as remote and transition to be in-office when possible.

What you'll be doing:

  • Maintain and expand existing ATM application
  • Maintain and expand existing online trading platform
  • Work closely with engineers, product design and senior leadership to develop a product that serves our customers
  • Design scalable systems to solve novel problems with modern cloud technology and industry best practices.
  • Write high quality, well tested code to meet the needs of our customers.

What we look for in you:

  • Strong experience with Go
  • Strong experience with JavaScript
  • Strong experience with GNU/Linux
  • Strong experience with VueJS/Vuetify
  • Strong experience with SQL and relational databases
