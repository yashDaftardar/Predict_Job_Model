Full Job Description

Grow your career with a growing organization

Whether they’re helping people reach their long-term financial goals or providing personal wealth management strategies, every associate contributes to changing the lives of those we serve for the better. When it comes to job satisfaction, that’s hard to beat. And from a personal satisfaction perspective, you’ll enjoy the freedom to support causes that matter to you and experience a truly inclusive work environment. Your future starts now.

Leads and develops a technical team of professionals to directly support the objectives of Empower’s Information Delivery through BI, Analytics and Data development. Through partnership with associated business leaders and the value stream, establish coordinated plans to realize a shared vision and then manage the execution and support of those plans.

What you will do

  • Lead team(s) that develop, implement, and maintain highly complex proprietary and/or purchased software systems in support of the Retirement, Life & Annuity, and Asset Management businesses.
  • Fulfill responsibilities of system architect within assigned areas.
  • Oversee the execution and continuously improving Agile software development method to design, code, modify, test, debug, integrate, document and deliver software systems that realize business/product specifications and satisfy customer expectations. Make recommendations and directly influence decisions regarding system enhancements.
  • Assign teams and individual staff to projects of varying scope. Assist business leaders and business systems analysts with an Agile process of prioritizing and structuring work so that teams deliver business value in frequent and regular increments.
  • Establish and maintain procedures and best practices by which subordinates operate. Bear responsibility for team performance and overall success of assigned projects.
  • Ensure subordinates perform work as prescribed in order to achieve shared business objectives.
  • Assist Director of Investment Systems in publishing, maintaining and fulfilling a strategic technical roadmap for Investment Systems.
  • Make recommendations on pay and decisions on performance appraisals, work schedules, discipline and hiring. Estimate staffing needs and schedules and cultivate the team's self- organizing capability for delivering business value.
  • Define team and subordinate performance goals and development objectives then actively mentor individual team members to support goal achievements and assess outcomes.
  • Work with associated line of business leaders to establish and maintain a clear product road map.
  • Forge and maintain strong business partnerships.
  • Provide performance management to assigned application development team members by defining team and individualized performance objectives and actively manage their realization.
  • Ensure the assigned team's work and responsibilities are fulfilled in accordance with procedures and quality standards.
  • Participate in budget planning.
  • Make recommendations affecting staff (compensation, performance appraisals, development plans, work schedules, discipline and hiring).
  • Define and execute tactical steps that realize a strategic technical vision. Facilitate the creation of work plans in accordance with defined business priorities. Perform functions (as needed) of an Agile SCRUM master or project manager.
  • Define and implement continuous improvement processes to elevate organizational capabilities and business value.

What you will bring

  • Bachelor s degree in Computer Science or Information Systems or equivalent experience.
  • Experience in Business Intelligence platforms and reporting/analytics data
  • Master's degree in related systems or business field is strongly desired.

What will set you apart

  • Minimum eight (6) years experience including at least two (2) years experience directly managing people, including hiring, developing, motivating, and directing people.
  • Possess a strong work value for making others productive (vs. being an individual producer).
  • Financial/Insurance Industry knowledge (Investment Accounting, Reporting and Trading).
  • Knowledge/experience implementing reporting solutions using SAP Data Services and Business Objects.
  • Software development background.
  • Exercise authority to make exceptions to policy or procedures when required.Investment Reporting and Control, Variable Asset Admin, Actuary Valuation, Investments, IT Infrastructure, Corporate Systems, Legal, Recordkeeping Systems, Digital Delivery, Vendors.Directly manages individual contributors (Business System Analysts and Developers of all levels).
