Title: Software QA Automation Engineer

Department: Trimble Buildings MEP

Our Company

For more information about Trimble (NASDAQ: TRMB), visit www.trimble.com.

Job Summary:
The Automation Engineer will support content products and databases. The successful candidate will be responsible for analyzing functionality of applications and designing tests to validate performance.. There will be close collaboration with product management and other senior developers in an Agile product delivery.


Primary Responsibilities:

  • Work with software design teams to discuss verification protocols.

  • Identify application weaknesses and areas for improvement.

  • Collaborating with other Automation Engineers to establish standards and guidelines for future development.

  • Collaborate with onshore & offshore development team members, including reviewing code and troubleshooting quality issues.

  • Troubleshooting automation software and finalizing system procedures.

  • Collaborate with remote teams


Minimum Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or a related field or equivalent years of professional experience

  • 3+ years of experience as a Software Automation Engineer in a professional environment

  • Familiarity with C#, .NET Core, .NET Framework

  • Experience collaborating in an Agile environment

  • Experience in web frameworks like Typescript, Angular, ServiceStack, SCSS / CSS

  • Demonstrated ability to learn and master complex technology

  • Experience collaborating and coordinating with remote coworkers

  • Strong attention to detail


Preferred Skills:

  • Experience with Nunit / Xunit frameworks

  • Able to provide guidance on integration, performance, and stress testing best practices

  • Experience supporting the quality of enterprise grade cloud scale websites

  • Experience working with leadership and key stakeholders throughout the business

  • Familiarity with cloud technologies
