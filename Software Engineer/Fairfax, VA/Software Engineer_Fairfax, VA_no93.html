About the opportunity

Ellucian is seeking an innovative hands-on Software Test Engineer to be an integral part of the design, build out and adoption of our Colleague solutions. You must be able to work with a wide range of resources: architects, business analysts, developers, automation engineers, managers and product managers to support rapid deployment and adoption activities in an Agile Scrum based methodology.

Where you will make an impact

  • By continuously collaborating with scrum team members on a daily basis to analyze product priorities and requirements, and develop testing needs to create and execute a comprehensive test strategy that includes automated and manual testing
  • Understanding the Ellucian Automation framework, the productâ€™s automation framework and contribute to writing automation test scripts, execute and maintain them across all browsers
  • Taking ownership of automation and managing it with minimal supervision, writing and executing effective test plans to ensure adequate testing coverage, including regression testing
  • Working with developers to ensure a solid understanding of changes being made and ensuring test plans coverage is complete
  • Contributing to Scrum ceremonies, aiding in estimating work effort, and managing work to meet sprint and delivery schedules
  • Documenting defects and track issue resolution and escalating issues to the agile development team and management. Adhering to process, tools, and metrics standards within the development team
  • Continually improve test processes and practices based on inspection/adaption of previous iterations by proactively seeking ways to incorporate new/creative ideas and methodologies into testing efforts


What you will bring

  • Computer Science Degree or related field
  • Minimum 2+ years of experience in a test engineer or QA role
  • Minimum 2+ years hands-on experience in writing and executing automated test scripts and manual test plans and test cases, including documenting defects and tracking issue resolution
  • Experience with Web Application test automation including accessibility and performance testing
  • Experience with functional testing
  • Experience working in a Lean/Agile SCRUM environment
  • Hands on experience with the Selenium framework
  • Familiarity with tools such as GitHub/BitBucket, Jira, QA Complete, and Confluence
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to analyze requirements and issues
