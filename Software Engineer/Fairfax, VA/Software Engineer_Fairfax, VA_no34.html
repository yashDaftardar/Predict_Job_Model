What You’ll Get to Do:

CACI is seeking fully cleared, dynamic technical professionals to join our TETRA program in the Northern, Virginia area. TETRA is an Enterprise IT Program that supports and protects the IC mission. The program is a portfolio of hundreds of projects funded by numerous customers providing Engineering and Sustainment services for a Global Information Technology Enterprise. As a team, we architect, develop, engineer, maintain, and provide continuous sustainment of enterprise-level communications in support of the customer’s mission worldwide. We also provide secure, reliable, and cost-effective enterprise-class data center hosting services, in which we develop, integrate, and maintain. Be a part of something greater than yourself and make a lasting impact at CACI.

More About the Role:

CACI’s Business and Information Technology Solutions (BITS) Sector is seeking a Software Engineer who will design, develop, and modify monitoring solutions from concept to delivery, including application instrumentation, monitoring configuration, creation of health rules, and custom dashboards. As a Software Engineer, you will also conduct software patching, version control, testing, upgrades, debugging, and product refinement to deploy and maintain quality monitoring solutions.

Your responsibilities will include the following:

  • Assisting clients to instrument their application to be monitored by AppDynamics
  • Creating and modifying AppDynamics monitoring solutions and defining enterprise events and alarms
  • Working directly with clients to create customized health rules and dashboards
  • Assisting with the implementation of change requests and tasks for the development team
  • Assisting clients to configure their AppDynamics monitoring
  • Systems restoration, especially after patching or upgrades
  • Integrating AppDynamics solutions into the event management ticketing system
  • Assisting with managing and maintaining the client’s core monitoring systems (e.g., IBM Tivoli OMNIbus and IBM Tivoli Monitoring)
  • Supporting the integration of the customer’s element manager systems
  • Utilizing the Xacta 360 product for Assessment and Authorization (A&A) processing
  • Creating and/or modifying systems documentation and standard operating procedures (SOPs)

  • Preparation of program and end-user documentation, as needed


You’ll Bring These Qualifications:

  • Experience with Agile development methodology
  • Experience with Amazon Web Services (AWS)
  • Experience with enterprise monitoring
  • Experience with AppDynamics


Candidate must have one of the following:

  • 10 Years of job-related experience and High School/GED diploma

  • 8 Years of job-related experience and Associates degree

  • 4 - 7 Years of job-related experience and Bachelor’s degree

  • 3 Years of job-related experience and Master’s degree

  • 2 Years of job-related experience and Doctorate

These Qualifications Would be Nice to Have:
