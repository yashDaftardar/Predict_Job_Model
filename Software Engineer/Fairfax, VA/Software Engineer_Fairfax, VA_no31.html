We are looking for a Software Engineer to develop and support software solutions for Micron's Factory Automation. You will participate in a strong team environment both locally as well as globally in all aspects of the Software Development Process.

In this role, you should have excellent technical and analytical skills. The successful candidate should be energized by difficult challenges and possess great tenacity. Your objective will be to develop reliable, high-quality software that is aligned with Micron’s business goals.


Areas of responsibility include, but are not limited to the following:


  • Design, develop, test, and release software related to the Host Automation software architecture
  • Monitor and react to line & IT system performance metrics/indicators
  • Design and build various support, maintenance, and metrics gathering utilities. Gathering and analyzing data to support automation and customer teams, present data to various groups and levels of management
  • Define and make suggestions that improve performance and process issues
  • Participate in meetings, management reviews, and IT group coordination where applicable/necessary
  • Build, maintain, and manage training and support knowledge material
  • Provide coordination and action with Major Incident events affecting operations including management of any required post-mortems and associated communication
  • Participate with Micron Worldwide Problem Management (local and/or global)
  • Solve software defects
  • Participate in a 24x7 support on-call rotation.

Required:

  • Practical experience with command and control of physical machines via software integration
  • Proven experience in scripting languages such as Python and TCL
  • Solid understanding of ITIL Incident, Release, Change, and Problem Management practices.
  • Have experience in Scrum development process.
  • Software testing experience.
  • The ability to work and thrive in a team environment.
  • Strong organizational and project management skills, technical direction, and vision.
  • The ability to work on several critical tasks at once as well as work with minimal supervision.
  • Ability to drive for results, eliminate distractions and place the proper focus on the tasks necessary for success.

Desired:

  • Practical experience in developing SQL.
  • Experience with DevOps tools such as Team Foundation Server and/or Jenkins
  • Experience with continuous monitoring tools like Splunk
  • Working knowledge of containerization and/or on-prem cloud solutions

Education

B.S. degree in Computer Science or equivalent education
