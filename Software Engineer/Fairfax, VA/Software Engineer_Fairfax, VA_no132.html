Job Summary: The LabVIEW Engineer is responsible for the creation and maintenance of LabVIEW programming that supports manufacturing and test of deliverables reporting to the Functional Manager of the Manufacturing Engineering Department at the Space Systems Integrated Thermal Systems in Beltsville, Maryland. Responsibilities: Perform as systems administrator for all LabVIEW applications Perform maintenance and updates to existing LabVIEW applications Design/Develop solutions to evolving testing requirements Design/Develop solutions for automation and complex manufacturing processing Assist and develop testing solutions to meet program requirements This position may be filled at a level 2 or a level 3.

Skills & Qualifications: Level 2 Qualifications Bachelor’s degree from a STEM program and a minimum of two years of experience in a manufacturing engineering environment, or a Master's degree from a STEM program. A self-starter possessing strong communication, interpersonal, organizational, time management and decision making skills Detail-oriented but able to understand the big picture and external customer needs Must be a US citizen with the ability to work on ITAR controlled products Level 3 Qualifications: Bachelor's degree from a STEM program and a minimum of 5 year's of engineering experience in a manufacturing environment, or a Master's degree from a STEM program and a minimum of 3 years of engineering experience in a manufacturing environment. A self-starter possessing strong communication, interpersonal, organizational, time management and decision making skills Detail-oriented but able to understand the big picture and external customer needs Must be a US citizen with the ability to work on ITAR controlled products Preferred Qualifications: Experience in systems design or test engineering preferred Proficiency creating and documenting software programming packages preferred Proficiency reading and creating electrical schematics and wire lists preferred Proficiency in the use of electrical test equipment preferred Demonstrated leadership characteristics required, direct leadership experience preferred Northrop Grumman is committed to hiring and retaining a diverse workforce.
