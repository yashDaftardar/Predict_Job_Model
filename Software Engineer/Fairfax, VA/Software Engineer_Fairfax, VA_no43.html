Position: Website Developer

Location: Fort Belvoir, VA

Clearance: Secret

Education: Bachelor’s Degree

Responsibilities:

The Website Developer will support the United States Army’s Program Executive Office (PEO) Enterprise Information Systems (EIS) Strategic Communication Support Services. PEO EIS’s vision is to be the recognized leader in delivering integrated and cost-effective information systems and information technology (IT) solutions across the Department of Defense (DoD). The Strategic Communications Directorate is responsible for planning and executing integrated communication plans and events to inform and educate stakeholders, to include our internal workforce and senior leaders across the Army and DoD, industry, our customers, Congress, and the media.

Specific responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • Plan, design, develop, test, implement, and manage intranet activities (including other computer-based visual media).
  • Develop systems and applications.
  • Install, configure, implement, and manage websites.

Qualifications:

The candidate must have the following qualifications:

  • A minimum of 8 years of experience in web development, serving as the internet expert and Webmaster.
  • Experience developing, coordinating, and implementing comprehensive internet programs.
  • Knowledge of computer and networking applications, principles, concepts, and methods.
  • Must have certifications in and expert knowledge of the Drupal CMS.

Travel: Some travel may be required

Other Requirements:

We seek:

  • Highly motivated self-starter
  • Resourceful individuals with extraordinary intellectual capability and the ability to rapidly learn and apply new concepts
  • Individuals who have a “ let me try ” attitude and are resilient, present an opinion/position, justify it, and then accept whatever decision is made and charge forward
  • Individuals who view criticism as an opportunity to improve (“ let me try again ”)
  • Individuals who think and create, enhancing the company with a steady flow of fresh ideas, perspective, and energy.
