We are looking for CS/CE students to join our Engineering organization in May and June.

Job Summary:

You will design, develop and deploy different services that scale, are highly available, and are easy to extend. In this role, you will get to explore and work with cutting-edge technologies and a tremendously large (and fantastically rich) data set. You will work to solve problems alongside a great team of deeply technical engineers. You collaborate closely with other experts and become a valued member of the team.


Engineering Requirements:

  • Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or similar.
  • Knowledge of Java or C# or any other Object Oriented programming language
  • Knowledge of Angular or React, JavaScript, CSS, HTML
  • Have a systematic problem-solving approach coupled with effective communication skills
  • Enjoy working on a team where you constantly learn, experiment, and iterate quickly
