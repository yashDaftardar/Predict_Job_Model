What You'll Do

We are looking for a purposeful and results-oriented leader to lead and develop a team of forward-thinking, fun, diverse engineers to develop and support Industry leading campus switching and routing solutions.

Technical knowledge in Computer networking and embedded high performance software development are required for this role.

You will own and manage forwarding features on industry leading Cisco Cat9000 series switches.

You a e expected to:
1. Deliver high-quality, high-value software by adhering to security and quality standards as part of the development process.
2. Define metrics and leading them for continuous improvement. Running and operating what you build with high availability and high resiliency.

3. Recruit, retain and adjust to keep your team at the top of their game. Mentor, coach, and build a high-performing team with a strong 'bias for action'.

Who You'll Work With
You will be partnering with Product Management as equal shareholders of product ownership. You will work with Architects and Engineers at all levels inside and outside of your immediate teams; running up, down, across, diagonally, inside and out - whatever it takes to deliver customer value. You will have the privilege to work with members of the PLM, Customer Success, Support, and Marketing areas of the business unit.

It will not be unusual for you to meet with and present to cherished Partners and Customers. You will engage with the broader community inside and outside Cisco. You will constantly look for ways to establish relationships and partnerships that help you recruit and optimize your ability to deliver value

Who You Are

You are a Software Engineer by training or profession. You have a bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field, or equivalent training and work experience.

You have at least three years of experience as an Engineering Manager.

You are excited to explain complex technical concepts and share your knowledge with diverse audiences both internally and externally. You have seen a lot of architecture and designs.

You can anticipate future technical needs for the product and craft plans to realize them. You are experienced in building roadmaps and can develop a long-term team vision in partnership with Product Management and other engineering teams on the Webex Contact Center Platform. You get things done and never give up.

You are driven and biased towards execution and delivery - taking on what needs to be done and owning it through to the end and beyond. You lead by example with your tenacity and utmost ownership ethic. You understand the importance of objective feedback for career growth and have a track record of having practiced it successfully
