The Role

The Application Support Engineer is responsible to provide successful implementation and management of Tesla services (applications) in the production environments and Provide Level II support to ensure the service meets the quality, security and compliance standards, providing the documentation for the ongoing support and maintenance of the systems. This position will be located at our Fremont, CA office.

Responsibilities

  • Assign all Help Desk and customer inquiries, including technical assistance, best use of product, additional products and services.
  • Provide outstanding customer service to Tesla Motors application users.
  • Provide second level technical solutions, according to established service levels.
  • Offer a single point of contact and ensure that all application support requests are recorded in a timely, courteous, and accurate manner.
  • Escalate issues within the IT department to ensure issues that cannot be addressed at first level are responded to timely, working to maximum uptime for customers.
  • Follow-up on tickets resolved by internal IT teams to ensure communication to the customer with high degree of satisfaction based on results and level of service.
  • Liaison for Level III Information Technology development support.
  • Resolve production issues by fixing defects in application source code wherever feasible.
  • Execute, monitor, and complete assigned tasks on multiple computer systems and platforms.
  • Document and maintain shift documentation in ticket management systems.
  • Work closely with offshore counterparts in ensuring timely resolution of issues.

Requirements

  • Minimum of 2 years' experience with Windows, Unix, PL-SQL or the equivalent in experience and evidence of exceptional ability (Minimum of 6 months of experience for Master's engineering degree)
  • Minimum of 2 year on handling EDI or Supply Chain or delivery systems (Minimum of 6 months of experience for Master's engineering degree)
  • Technical skills: .net, SQL Server, JavaScript, HTML, MS Windows
  • At least 1 year experience in Application support working in a globally distributed team is preferred (Minimum of 6 months of experience for Master's engineering degree)
  • Good experience with ITIL, BMC Remedy or Service Now and ticketing workflows is preferred
  • Good verbal and written communication skills is preferred
  • Master's / Bachelor's degree in Engineering or equivalent in Computer Science or the equivalent in experience and evidence of exceptional ability
