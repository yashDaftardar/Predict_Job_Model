The Business Entity
Cisco's Service Provider group has a very rich product portfolio covering multiple Routing platforms and ASICS/Optics. The diagnostics team is responsible for developing innovative software solutions to validate hardware designs and support product manufacturing.
The Team
You will be part of a Global Hardware Diagnostics Development team, a very dynamic group working in a fast-paced environment. As a Diagnostics Engineer you will be exposed to state-of-the-art technologies used in Core, optical and Edge routing Cisco products. Your primary role will be to ensure high quality of our products, by owning development of component and system level embedded diagnostic software.
We are looking for motivated individuals, who enjoy challenging tasks. Excellent Software background and strong understanding of Hardware is required. We offer a great opportunity to grow your technical skills set and gain visibility and recognition among cross-functional groups.
Role & Responsibilities

In this highly visible role as a Software Engineer, you will:
Define, design, and develop diagnostic application for FPGA, ASIC and system design verification
Collaborate with HW design, SW development and Manufacturing teams for system bringup and validation
Develop and execute detailed software test plans and automate software tools to validate diagnostics software.

Minimum Qualifications
Excellent C and Python programming skills
Hands on experience with embedded system software development
Good knowledge on modern operating system design such as multitasking, kernels, filesystems, etc.
Proficient in Microcontrollers and embedded peripherals (I2C, ADC, DAC, USB, etc.)
Ability to translate engineering and manufacturing customer requirements into design objectives
Ability to design, plan, and schedule a complex diagnostics project
Strong problem solving and debugging skills
Excellent verbal and written interpersonal skill
Self-motivated and able to work effectively in a team environment

Desired Skills
Linux device driver development experience
Understanding of Layer 2 and Layer 3 network protocols and technologies such as TCP/IP, routing and switching protocols
High speed serdes hardware or software experience.
Relevant industry experience with routers/switches.

Education
MSEE/CS combined or MSCE with 2+ years of related experience
BSEE/CS combined or BSCE with 4+ years of related experience
