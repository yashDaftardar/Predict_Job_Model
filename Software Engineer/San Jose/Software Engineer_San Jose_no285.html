What you’ll get to do:

The goal of the Payments Team is to provide buyers and sellers everywhere with a frictionless payments experience on our platform,

  • Have a critical role in building and maintaining our payments platform processing high transaction volumes
  • Manage all technical aspects of relationships with payment providers and integrate new payment methods into our payment platform
  • Implement and deliver new payment features leading to higher checkout conversion
  • Build reporting and monitoring tools to ensure the stability and security of the payments system

What You’ll Bring:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field or equivalent work experience
  • 4+ years of industry experience building data driven systems, but we do realize quality of experience is more important than actual years.
  • Experience developing APIs and integrating third-party services.
  • Proven track record of working successfully with cross-functional teams
  • Strong sense of web design and attuned to the fundamentals of user experience.
  • Experience with one or more of the following: large systems software design, development of distributed or parallel systems, database design, machine learning, application development.
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills
  • Excellent problem solving and analytical thinking skills

Bonus:

  • Experience integrating with payment service providers (e.g. Stripe, Adyen, Marqeta)
  • Familiarity with our tech stack (Kotlin/Java, CockroachDB, AWS, Micronaut, Spring Boot, microservice architecture)
  • Hands-on experience building custom components with one or more ecommerce platforms (Shopify, Salesforce, or SAP Hybris).
