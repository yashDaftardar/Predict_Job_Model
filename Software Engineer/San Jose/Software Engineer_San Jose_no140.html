Responsibilities

  • In this role you will:

  • Develop, maintain and extend existing calibration, characterization and diagnostic software programs.
  • Work directly with hardware engineers to develop new calibration, characterization and diagnostic tests, as well collect and help analyze data for design validation of new and existing products.
  • Identify hardware and or software issues and either recommend or implement solutions to the designs.
  • Work directly with Product Engineering and Manufacturing to transition new product releases between the Engineering group and the Manufacturing group.

Basic Qualifications & Skills

  • Good understanding of common analog and digital circuit elements:
  • DC-DC converters, power supplies, transistors, diodes, op-amps, etc.
  • Understanding of noise, its sources and filtering techniques
  • Digital to analog converters and analog to digital converters.
  • Able to read electronic circuit schematics and device specification sheets.
  • Able to translate hardware register/memory maps to software models/objects.
  • Develop software solutions for hardware test requirements.
  • Use of digital oscilloscopes, digital multi-meters, logic analyzers, protocol analyzers and frequency counters.
  • Present ideas to groups of people
  • Self-directed self-starter
  • English Proficiency


Desirable Skills

  • Experience working with Automatic Test Equipment (ATE).
  • Experience communicating over various busses; GPIB, I2C, SPI, PCIe, etc.
  • C++ Object Oriented programming skills.
  • Familiarity with Microsoft Visual Studio development tools, the Standard Template Library, and working with Dynamic Link Library projects.
  • Device Driver design and development.
  • Proficiency with a source code control system (Git experience preferred)

Education

  • MS with 0 yrs. or BS with 2 yrs.Electrical Engineering degree
