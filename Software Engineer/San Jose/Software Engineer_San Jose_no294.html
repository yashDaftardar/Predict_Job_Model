Full Job Description

Minimum qualifications:

  • Master's degree in Computer Science, Communications, or related technical field or equivalent practical experience
  • 15 years of professional wireless system experience
  • 15 years of wireless standards experience
  • Experience in communication, technical and thought leadership.


Preferred qualifications:

  • Experience with wireless communications systems, protocols, and radios in embedded devices
  • Experience with Android connectivity stack on mobile devices
  • Experience with Android operating system.
  • Solid understanding of domestic and global carrier networks.

About the job

Google is proud to boast a network that provides service to millions of Internet users around the world. The Network Engineering team is responsible for operating that network reliably and at scale. As a member of the team, you have a direct impact on design and feature enhancements to keep our systems running smoothly. You also ensure that network operations are safe and efficient by monitoring network performance, coordinating planned maintenance, adjusting hardware components and responding to network connectivity issues. Google's complex network generates a constant stream of challenges which require you to continually be innovative with an evolving set of technologies. Keeping the network reliable ensures that our users stay connected with our suite of applications, products and services.

We enable amazing wireless technologies to Android devices, such as 5G network slicing for service-oriented networks and UWB (ultra-wideband) for optimal and secure ranging capability. On this team, you will help build the software stack for each technology, adhering to the Android connectivity stack's core principles, and providing application developers a rich set of APIs to accelerate the ecosystem adoption and prevent fragmentation across Android. You will also participate in related standard bodies (e.g., 3GPP, GSMA, FiRa, CCC) to contribute wireless industry.

In this role, you will help shape the connectivity experience for smartphones worldwide with a team that defines, delivers, and takes pride in continually improving the connectivity experience on mobile devices. Android connectivity delivers an exceptional connectivity experience throughout the day that is robust, seamless, secure, private, power and performance optimized for every single radio technology (Cellular, Wi-Fi, BT, NFC, UWB).

Making the world's information universally accessible and useful doesn't stop at the desktop. The Mobile team builds tools to get you the information you need no matter where you are. Android has become the world's most popular mobile ecosystem, powering billions of devices, from smartphones to tablets, watches to TVs and everything in between. Whether adding to the core Android experience, forging new markets for digital content, creating immersive and portable versions of our products or managing relationships with a global community of developers, the Mobile team is giving you Google on the go.

Responsibilities

  • Represent Google in standard bodies and promote Google principles
  • Identify areas of technology investment to shape Android connectivity feature road map
  • Innovate in key technology areas to bring benefits to Android users
  • Work with external partners (carrier, Original Equipment Manufacturers, vendors, application developers) and internal partners (device and service product area, product managers) on end-to-end technology enablements through Android software
  • Use in-depth system knowledge to analyze and solve complex user pain-points in wireless communications areas
