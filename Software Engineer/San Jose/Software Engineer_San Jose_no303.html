Job Description

The Reporting application is central to empowering users of Workday Planning to visualize finance, sales and other planning data through powerful, self-service and ad-hoc reports. We leverage our powerful compute engine to provide best-in-class reporting that scales against very large data sets and complex planning models.

We are looking for a fast and eager learner to join our engineering team and continue revolutionizing our reporting application.

Required Skills:

  • 3+ years of software development experience.

  • Proficient developing software in Java.

  • Strong in algorithms and data structures.

  • Experience working with SQL and databases like Postgres and/or Oracle.

  • Strong grasp of design patterns, coding best practices and be able to write unit-testable code.

  • Strong communication and organizational skills and ability to balance multiple priorities.

  • BS and/or Masters in Computer Science, Engineering or related field preferred.


Preferred Skills:

  • Experience working on a SaaS product.

  • Working knowledge or exposure to UI development (Javascript, React).

  • Attention to detail and deep understanding of engineering best practices.

  • Experience with Agile development process.
