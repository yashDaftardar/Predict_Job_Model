Who We Are
Cisco’s Customer Experience (CX) organization is one of Cisco’s fastest growing teams, and the Customer and Partner Experience Engineering (CPXE) group is redefining how Cisco delivers value to our customers & partners via our product portfolio. In this time of unprecedented change, we are looking for top talent to lead business critical capabilities that will redefine success for Cisco and our customers
What You’ll Do
As a critical member of our Data and AI Services group , you will play a significant role in defining the shape and scope of our products. Being responsible for the roadmap of machine learning products, phasing development to sprints in collaboration with our team leaders, mitigating the adoption risks, and positioning the product to internal customer .
Who You’ll Work With
This role is part of a fun loving, diverse team, with a broad culture. Duties require collaborat ion across C X, and potentially outside to mine business opportunities and define technical requirements. Y ou will engage with the leaders and individual technical team members to usher the development of the technical aspects. Once the development is com plete you will position and translate the technical concepts in the right way for the customers .

Specifically, You will:
Perform quality assurance and quality control duties for the Cisco Customer Experience Data Insight & Growth team for data products development life cycle
Document bugs for developer review as well as provide supporting test results to assess the overall quality for the data product deliverable
Work with different XFN teams and data engineers to understand user requirements and scope for data integration and data fulfillment initiatives to develop comprehensive test plans
Assist project teams with the development, documentation, and mapping of requirements to test cases
Define, prepare, execute and implement data validation, unit and integration testing methods to ensure data quality
Participate in project team meetings by taking notes, providing feedback, and representing QA interests
Work with enterprise and other functional data teams to understand various data sets and participate in building a comprehensive data catalog.
Document data subject areas and relations models using ER-diagrams and share data knowledge with others
Understand how to successfully evaluate roadblocks and develop appropriate solutions
Strategize, understanding the goals of the company, and create effective test plans to achieve those goals
Qualifications: Work with programming languages, such as SQL, Python, and visualization tools such as Tableau to combine various data sets for adhoc and standardized reporting
Knowledge of BigData frameworks like Apache Spark, Hive and Hadoop . Good understanding of Data pipelines.
Familiarity with Cloud platforms like AWS, GCP Cloud and/or Snowflake
Proficient knowledge of MS Office tools & relational databases
Self-motivated, detail oriented and organized
Strong problem solving, analytical and interpersonal skills
Bachelor’s Degree in engineering, CS, MIS, or other related fields
2+ years of data and software QA experience
E xperience working with distributed teams desired
