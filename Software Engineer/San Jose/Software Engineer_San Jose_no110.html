What You'll Do
We are seeking an experienced DSP Engineer to implement production quality audio communications algorithms. You will work closely with our algorithm team and take reference algorithms developed in Matlab or neural network frameworks and convert them to optimized implementations, targeting PCs, mobile, x86, ARM and accelerators. You will develop methodologies for combining and testing subsystems connecting DSP components together.

Who You'll Work With
You will be part of the Babblelabs team, a startup with a startup mentality recently acquired by Cisco. We develop deep learning based speech software as part of the Webex collaboration software. Our speech enhancement software eliminates background noise from conference calls, enabling our customers to communicate clearly without distractions. Our command recognition software enables voice UI controls without sacrificing privacy.

Who You Are

The ideal candidate will have audio DSP algorithmic knowledge together with real experience porting to DSPs or deep learning accelerators.

Key skills and experience should include:
  • Strong programming skills in C/C++. Experience with deep learning accelerators a plus.
  • Experience in digital signal processing algorithm development, ideally in voice or audio.
  • Knowledge of linear algebra
  • Hands on experience with floating point and fixed point arithmetic.
  • Matlab experience
  • Experience with GPUs and SIMD architecture
  • Experience with neural network frameworks and accelerators
  • Experience with build processes
  • BS or MS in Computer Science or equivalent degree and five years experience.
