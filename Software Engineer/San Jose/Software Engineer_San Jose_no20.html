What You’ll Do

Innovium Inc is looking to hire recent college graduate that is interested in developing game changing, disruptive products for the cloud infrastructure, specifically in switch embedded system software development. You’ll work closely with your peers on a team of skilled engineers designing and developing networking software for Innovium Switching Products.

Who You Are

You have a bachelor’s or master’s degree in Computer Science or related field

You have a knowledge in C programming and data structure concepts

You have a strong problem solving and troubleshooting skill

It would be a big plus if

You have a good understanding of Layer 2 / Layer 3 forwarding, networking protocols, ACL etc.

You have past experience in embedded system software development
