Job Summary

Interested in our Remote Software Engineering Internship?

Work on the next-generation of technology that will change how businesses around the world manage and unlock value from their data. Rubrik is the leader in the cloud data management space. We're revolutionizing how businesses protect, search, secure, and analyze all of their data with simple and scalable solutions. As cloud infrastructure continues to grow at an astounding rate, we’ll be solving some of it’s most interesting challenges while building a product unlike anything seen before. This is an exciting challenge with opportunities in search, analytics, scalable systems and much more! We’re just getting started so there is a lot of opportunity for personal growth and contribution.

Rubrik partners with universities around the world to offer the next class of interns an opportunity to make an impact from day one! This is a full-time (40-hrs per week) remote internship during the fall of 2021 running approximately 12 weeks from early September through mid-December. You will work on real projects and be mentored by our senior team members to support your career development and experience.

We're looking for interns who are curious, who want to be entrusted with real responsibility and embrace the opportunity to write code that will ship . You'll be a full member of the engineering team and learn from our incredible engineers while making a measurable impact. Our Engineering teams are building and maintaining multiple products at Rubrik. These teams have clear ownership over features & products ensuring they have all the context to make impactful decisions. Some product teams are full stack, while others are specialized either in back-end, or front-end. Teams are utilizing code reviews, pair programming and tech talks for ensuring engineering growth, collaboration and mentoring. Check out our engineering blog to learn more about what our engineers are working on and to see where you could fit in.

Desired Requirements:

  • Currently pursuing a BS, MS, or PhD in Computer Science or a related field
  • Strong understanding of computer science fundamentals, algorithms, best practices, and logical skills
  • Strong coding capability
  • Good team player and exceptional communicator
  • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment
  • Ability to research, learn and become proficient in new technologies

What You Will Do

  • Experience first hand working at a market leading cloud data management company
  • Work closely with a mentor on a specific project important to Rubrik’s overall goals
  • Be part of a team of engineers that share and learn from each other
  • Show off your work with a presentation on your project at the end of your internship
  • Have a great time and make relationships with people that will last your entire career!

ABOUT RUBRIK

Rubrik is one of the fastest growing companies in Silicon Valley, revolutionizing data protection and management in the emerging multi-cloud world. We are the leader in cloud data management, delivering a single platform to manage and protect data in the cloud, at the edge, and on-premises. Enterprises choose Rubrik to simplify backup and recovery, accelerate cloud adoption, enable automation at scale, and secure against cyberthreats. We’ve been recognized as a Forbes Cloud 100 Company two years in a row and as a LinkedIn Top 10 startup.
