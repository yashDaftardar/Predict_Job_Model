Embedded Software Development - WD30111524071


What you will do

Looking for a dynamic and creative Software Engineer Intern who is passionate about building great products, enjoys working with a team of talented developers, and who is eager to take on big challenges.

How you will do it:

  • Do you like working with a diverse, dynamic team of highly motivated and talented people,
  • Do you enjoy mentoring, sharing, and learning from others
  • Do you enjoy working on your own software development projects


Responsibilities May Include:

  • Assist in developing Android and/or IOS applications
  • Evaluate data and predict bandwidth requirements
  • Assist in testing features of our Security Panel, triage issues, and investigate root cause.

Qualifications


What we look for
Required Qualification:

  • Current undergraduate or graduate student pursuing a degree in computer science or computer engineering or electrical engineering
  • Knowledge of object oriented concepts
  • Results oriented with high level of initiative and a proactive nature
  • Experience with Java development, especially developing applications using Android Studio will be very helpful
  • Excellent communication skills with the ability to work well in a team
  • Available to start on a PT basis now and full-time during the summer
