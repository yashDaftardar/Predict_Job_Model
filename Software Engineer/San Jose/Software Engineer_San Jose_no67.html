The Opportunity

It’s an exciting time to be at Adobe, especially if you’re an innovator and a leader. In Adobe’s Digital Video and Audio group, you will experience the energy of a start-up company backed by the resources of one of the greatest software companies in the world. It’s a perfect place to grow your career and unleash your potential. Come to enjoy the thrill of crafting a phenomenal product and the rush of developing it with people who share your passion!

The Premiere Pro team is looking for a hardworking, self-motivated software developer join a team of engineers with a passion for delivering a world-class, innovative, and compelling video creation application to a world of passionate and creative users. Premiere Pro is the industry leading video editing platform and our users depend on our application and tools to deliver amazing video experiences. You will have the opportunity to participate in and contribute to this success by delivering innovative new solutions. We invite you to join a dedicated team of high-caliber individuals who enjoy working together as we continue to deliver great experience and product to the markets.

What you'll do

  • Work collaboratively with a team of engineers to make Premiere Pro an outstanding application on upcoming desktop machines
  • Lead design, development, and delivery of software running on new hardware architecture
  • Demonstrate a commitment to quality and iterative improvement by writing high-quality testable code, steadfastly reviewing code submissions from others, and continually focusing on the overall customer experience your product delivers
  • Develop software solutions by collecting requirements, understanding development processes, and investigating problem areas which coordinate with other developers
  • Coordinate with internal and external teams to help ensure accurate workflows through the entire app experience

What you need to succeed

  • Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science or equivalent practical experience
  • 7+ years of relevant industry experience
  • Passion for building high-quality and high-performing software
  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Good time management skills with strong attention to detail
  • Expertise in building desktop applications
  • Strong coding skills in C/C++
  • Experience with commercial product/application development
  • Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written
  • Experience in audio and video processing a plus
