Job Description Summary: We're looking for a lead engineer to join the Shopping team and help bring the next generation of intelligence and customer personalization to our Shopping Experiences. This position will need experience defining domain architecture, technology strategy in alignment with enterprise architecture, business requirements and technology roadmaps. Provide technical leadership, mentoring and governance to the engineering teams.

Job Description:

Responsibilities:

  • Responsible for providing technical leadership, define engineering best practices and work with Engineering and Product counterparts across the organization.
  • Lead multiple complex business domains and lead engineering strategy and innovation for these domains.
  • Lead and Mentor engineers, work with managers and operations to ensure that systems meets reliability, accuracy and performance goals.
  • Drive both technology and product innovation through rapid prototyping and iterative development.
  • Innovate in Shopping, Personalization, Incentives and Rewards space to improve overall efficacy and efficiencies using state of the art technologies (machine learning, block chain and others)
  • Collaborates with leadership across the board to effectively to deliver solutions and projects.
  • Work with remote geographic and cross functional teams to build quality and timely solutions.
  • Responsible for developing multi-year engineering development strategy and products roadmap, including architecture blueprints.

Requirements:

  • 10+ years of experience in product platform design, engineering/architectural leadership for FinTech and eCommerce, consumer facing platforms engineering teams.
  • Deep hands on experience in complex system design, object oriented system design, scale, performance tuning in Java and related technologies.Deep hands on experience in API design, REST, RPC and micro-service architecture.
  • Deep hands on experience in variety of software stacks/technologies including but not limited to Java, JavaScript, Python, Kafka, and various SQL and noSQL databases and cloud services.
  • Demonstrated experience with BigData technologies (Hadoop ecosystem, Spark and others).
  • Demonstrated experience with innovation in FinTech and personalizatiospace using state of the art technologies (machine learning, block chain and others) – patents, published papers and others.
  • Demonstrated experience in building product platform as-a-service, SaaS and multi-tenant deployments.
  • Demonstrated experience in solving complex business problems using AI / ML technologies, specifically in NLP area
  • Experience presenting to and working with C-level leadership
  • Strong system and data flow knowledge. Ability to learn and adapt to new product flows and software technologies.
  • Strong analytical, research, problem solving and evaluative thinking skills.
  • Acute attention to detail and well-developed organization and time management skills.
  • Strong communication skills - including ability to listen to the needs of others and comprehend complex matters, articulate issues in a clear and concise manner, and present effectively in both oral and written presentations to all levels in the organization.
  • Team-oriented approach – can effectively lead a project or participate as an effective team member as well as work cross-functionally with other organizations.
  • High level of energy and a desire to thrive in a large, complex, fast-paced organization; ability to handle multiple projects under pressure.
  • Strong innovation acumen with expertise in enterprise software development stack, industry tech best practices and data driven decisioning / machine learning.
