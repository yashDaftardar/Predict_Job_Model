Company Overview

KLA is a global leader in diversified electronics for the semiconductor manufacturing ecosystem. Virtually every electronic device in the world is produced using our technologies. No laptop, smartphone, wearable device, voice-controlled gadget, flexible screen, VR device or smart car would have made it into your hands without us. KLA invents systems and solutions for the manufacturing of wafers and reticles, integrated circuits, packaging, printed circuit boards and flat panel displays. The innovative ideas and devices that are advancing humanity all begin with inspiration, research and development. KLA focuses more than average on innovation and in 2019 we invested 15% of sales back into R&D. Our expert teams of physicists, engineers, data scientists and problem-solvers work together with the world’s leading technology providers to accelerate the delivery of tomorrow’s electronic devices. Life here is exciting and our teams thrive on tackling really hard problems. There is never a dull moment with us.

Group/Division

With over 40 years of semiconductor process control experience, chipmakers around the globe rely on KLA to ensure that their fabs ramp next-generation devices to volume production quickly and cost-effectively. Enabling the movement towards advanced chip design, KLA's Global Products Group (GPG), which is responsible for creating all of KLA’s metrology and inspection products, is looking for the best and the brightest research scientist, software engineers, application development engineers, and senior product technology process engineers. The RAPID division is the world leading provider of reticle inspection solutions for the semiconductor industry. The company provides inspection solutions to both the mask shops and the semiconductor fabs to ensure that lithography yields are consistently high thus enabling cost-effective manufacturing.

  • Drive and contribute to feature development from requirements to delivery following software development practices
  • Identify ways to improve software development practices
  • Participate in formal and informal code and design reviews.

  • Develop complex applications in LabVIEW and other suitable technologies and deploy systems into the lab and the field.
  • Develop cutting-edge algorithms for hardware tuning to image patterns at the nanometer level
  • Perform root cause analysis for failures and implement solutions
  • Work as a team member with physicists, engineers, computer scientists, and external vendors.
  • Occasional global travel to customer sites to support installations
  • Utilize advanced knowledge to develop, design, implement complex software; develop and execute test plans, guide the completion of projects and contribute to the development of organizational goals.
  • Will spend time in clean-room environments in performing some tasks (10%-20%)


Qualifications

Required skills

  • 5+ years of programming in LabVIEW software. Experience at a senior level will be a plus
  • Certified LabVIEW Developer
  • Excellent design, analytical, troubleshooting, and problem-solving skills.
  • Experience developing medium/large scale software application
  • Experience working in multi developer software teams
  • Strong understanding of OOP concepts
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Self-motivated to excel in assigned tasks and responsibilities
  • Track record of completed projects in LabVIEW and other NI development suites
  • Possess high curiosity, be a fast learner, and have the drive to turn prototype ideas into quality production code used in mission critical applications.
  • Knowledge of and laboratory exposure to electrical, mechanical and optical measurement instruments

Preferred but not required

  • Project management experience
  • Hands on development in semi capex business segment
