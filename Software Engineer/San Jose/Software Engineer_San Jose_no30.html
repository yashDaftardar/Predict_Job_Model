Job Summary:

Baidu Autonomous Driving Technology Department (ADT), located in Silicon Valley CA, is offering R&D internships primarily focusing on researching advanced technologies in autonomous driving for the summer and fall 2021. As an intern, you will have the opportunity of working with a group of engineers and researchers to design, develop, and test the next generation of driverless technology that will completely transform and innovate the mobility in the future.

Here's what we'd like to see in you:

  • BS/MS/PHD student in Computer Science (or equivalent).
  • Proficient in one of the system and cloud technologies, such as Linux, Docker, Kubernetes and other cloud-native apps.
  • Proficient in one of the major programming languages, such as Bash, Python, and C/C++.
  • Experience with big data is a plus, such as MapReduce, Spark, and Flink.
  • Experience with web development is a plus, such as Flask and React.
  • Experience with either Tensorflow or PyTorch is a plus.

Culture Fit:

  • Mission alignment: If you want to be part of a team to accomplish this great mission, we will provide you the best possible platform to do that.
  • Self-directed: We work best with people that are driven, motivated, and aspire to greatness. Are you the sort of person that, if you had time on your hands, will independently find interesting and useful things to do?
  • Hungry to learn: We are eager to see you learn new skills and grow. But learning is hard work and this is something we hope you want to do.
  • Team orientation: We work in small, fast-moving teams. We don't believe in lone wolves. We watch out for each other and go after big goals together as a team.
