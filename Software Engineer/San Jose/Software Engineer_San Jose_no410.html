Cisco Webex is the World's #1 business collaboration and customer engagement platform. It is used across all industries in the world being used to support governments, business, education, and more including use by 85% of Fortune 500 business. Cisco is the clear market leader in in unified communication and contact centers with state of the art technology integration with People Insights, Webex Assistant, Webex Experience Management and now BabbleLabs noise cancelation. These technologies have already been rolled out in the core Webex application suite which includes calling, meetings, messaging, team collaboration and contact center applications.

Webex is built on a very modern SaaS platform with a strong culture of DevOps that operates worldwide with engineers in locations such as the US, EU, China, and India. The platform is used by 100s of millions of people around the world, and we’re looking to expand the team that delivers this amazing technology to the world .

What You’ll Do

As a Software Engineer , you will:
Help build and contribute heavily to designs and implementation in a thriving development team that loves practicing continuous integration and deployment
Make DevOps a primary concern to ensure services are reliable, scalable, manageable and supportable
Develop new micro services that will be built on Kubernetes PaaS and similar platforms
Enhance and Extend Webex Telemetry systems for analytics and continuous improvement
Be a gatekeeper of high-quality code, designs, practical approaches to scale
Bring alignment between product teams, architecture decisions, and implementations - Be a servant leader and an inspiration to engineers in the team

Requirements

Loves to code in Java.
Experienced backend engineer who has developed multiple versions of products
Understanding of SQL and NoSQL databases at scale
Demonstrated experience building large scale application using event streaming platforms like Kafka
Demonstrated experience with REST, API design patterns, and scalable containerized systems and microservices
Demonstrated experience integrating with external and internal microservices using REST/JSON that is scalable and high volume
Experience or demonstrated interest with real time communication/streaming protocols and video technologies

Nice to Have

Experience with Signaling protocols like SIP, Media protocols like RTP, RTCP, SRTP and Misc protocols like DTLS, TLS, HTTPS
