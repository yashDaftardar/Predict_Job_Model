Full Job Description

At SERES, we’re forging a new kind of mobility company by combining the DNA of advanced automotive engineering and design with that of state of the art smart technologies and connectivity to revolutionize the future of premium electric vehicles. From our corporate headquarters in Silicon Valley, we’re bringing the best and brightest together with a common goal of creating the next generation of smart, clean, connected vehicles for you.

Our vision is to deliver premium electric vehicles that enhance the daily lives of our users, allowing them to live more connected, productive lives through the integration of clean technology and advanced hardware and software. The future of mobility is more than getting from A to B, it’s about keeping connected to provide users with customizable features when they want it.

We are looking for an Embedded Software Engineer to join our connected car team.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop OTA embedded software in TBox to interact with vehicle ECUs and Cloud
  • Implement OTA software code/algorithms in ARM-based Linux and RTOS
  • Optimize OTA software performance
  • Bring up OTA software on new TBox model
  • Cross compile/build third-party libraries for aarch64 architecture
  • Test, debug, analyze and troubleshoot software and system issues
  • Provide production support when necessary

Requirements:

  • BS/MS in computer engineering, computer science or equivalent
  • 2+ years of experience in embedded software development
  • Knowledge of computer architecture, Vehicle ECU, Cloud communication
  • Strong programming experience in C/C++, Bash scripting, and software development experience with ARM-based GNU toolchain
  • Experience in developing software with UDS protocol and CAN Bus protocol
  • Experience in developing software with HTTP, CURL and MQTT networking protocols
  • Team player and good communication skills

Candidates must be legally authorized to work in the United States and verification of employment authorization will be required at the time of hire.
