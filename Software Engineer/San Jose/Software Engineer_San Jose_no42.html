About the Role

As a backend engineer on the Storage Access Gateways team, you'll develop a next generation storage access layer interfacing between Uber's business-critical applications and the underlying scalable storage systems. The team has a vision to provide easy to use, scalable and high-performance access to online storage systems offered at Uber.

This is a unique opportunity to combine a deep knowledge of distributed and database systems with a passion for making developers happier and more productive in working with such systems. You will have a profound impact on how Uber engineers build applications accessing various storage systems.

At Uber, we ignite opportunity by setting the world in motion. We take on big problems to help drivers, riders, delivery partners, and eaters get moving in more than 10,000 cities around the world.

We welcome people from all backgrounds who seek the opportunity to help build a future where everyone and everything can move independently. If you have the curiosity, passion, and collaborative spirit, work with us, and let's move the world forward, together.
