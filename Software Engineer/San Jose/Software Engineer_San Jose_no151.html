Front End Engineer

Curai
Palo Alto, CA
Remote

Curai is a health tech company working on expanding healthcare access and delivering the best care to everyone. We run a virtual primary care clinic, and leverage AI and machine learning to help our doctors work more quickly and effectively. Curai has a small but diverse team that includes senior engineers, researchers, and leaders previously from companies like Google and Netflix, colleges like Stanford and CMU, and coding schools like Hackbright and Rithm School. Software engineers at Curai come from a variety of backgrounds, and are passionate about making a difference in the lives of millions of people.

In this role you will:

  • Have a huge and direct impact on the early stages of a consumer product
  • Develop innovative solutions and user Interfaces using modern web frameworks and paradigms
  • Work with a diverse multi-disciplinary team that includes software engineers, machine learning researchers, physicians, product managers, and UX researchers
  • Help build a high-performance and inclusive work environment

An ideal candidate has:

  • 3+ years of experience developing mobile and desktop web applications
  • JavaScript and front-end development experience and familiarity with React / Redux
  • Experience with front end technologies and/or front end frameworks like React, Redux, Vue, Ember, HTML, CSS
  • Experience building responsive and accessible pages
  • Computer science or similarly technical degree, or equivalent practical experience
  • Experience with cloud environments such as AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure

We are headquartered in Palo Alto, CA, but are remote-first and have employees all over the country. Relocation is not expected. Employees receive a package of benefits that includes medical, dental and vision coverage, 401(k) matching, a remote work stipend, and parental leave.

Our interview process typically consists of an initial call to meet your future manager and a technical phone call with a future teammate, which includes role-related coding and system design. Those initial calls are followed by a series of conversations where you meet cross-functional partners and other teammates at the company. This gives us a chance to learn more about you, and you a chance to learn more about us, our mission, our product, company culture, and vision. We always try to tailor our interview process to your background and interests.

We are highly committed to building a diverse and inclusive environment at Curai. In keeping with our beliefs and goals, no employee or applicant will face discrimination or harassment based on race, color, ancestry, national origin, religion, age, gender, marital domestic partner status, sexual orientation, gender identity, disability status, or veteran status. To promote an equitable and bias-free workplace, we do not negotiate compensation outside of our existing salary bands, which we base on external data.For this role, the Bay Area base salary range is typically $130K to $180K. In addition to base salary, engineers receive equity as part of their total compensation package. We also adjust compensation based on location.
