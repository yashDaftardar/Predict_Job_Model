About our Company

Founded in 2013, NovaSignal Corp. is a medical technology company whose mission is to save lives by unlocking the hidden power of blood flow data. We get up every day with the goal of making the world a better place by improving the lives of people suffering from brain driven diseases such as stroke. Our FDA-cleared, AI-driven, robotic ultrasound system is revolutionizing healthcare and will help millions of people across the globe as we continue to expand our reach. We are looking for people to join our rapidly growing team who are not afraid to roll up their sleeves, get to work, and make a difference in the world!


If you are someone looking to make a big impact, partner with outstanding talent, and take your career to the next level, we encourage you to join us! You would be an excellent fit if you pride yourself in being a self-starter, adaptable, highly organized, big thinker, analytical, and have a strong eye for detail.


About the Role

We are seeking a motivated Software Engineer to join a fast-paced medical technology company whose mission is to save lives by unlocking the hidden power of blood flow data.


In this role, you will help develop software subsystems for a robotic ultrasound system. You will design and develop next generation cross-platform GUI framework for innovative clinical user interfaces, software controls, and data interfaces for cutting-edge robotic systems deploying an ultrasound sensor. You will help further our code services and system architecture, participate in brainstorming sessions, supply ideas to our technology, algorithms, and products, and work with the product and design teams to understand end-user requirements, formulate use cases, and deliver results.


We seek the highest levels of technical talent and programming skills, as well as a keen desire to deeply understand our products and services to push our technology forward. You will work alongside the best and the brightest engineering talent in the industry. As a core participant of your team, you will estimate engineering efforts, prioritize projects, plan implementations, and triage production issues. You need to be dynamic, collaborative, and curious as we build new experiences, improve existing products, and deliver innovative GUI solutions.


About You

  • Analytical : Able to convert abstract concepts into viable products using CS fundamentals
  • Resourceful : Have the ability to do a lot with a little. Be able to aggregate information from various places and build relationships with key partners to get the information you need
  • Data Driven : Able to use data to frame out and solve problems

As a Software Engineer, we need

  • Programming expertise. Be proficient in object-oriented programming concepts, design patterns, Web GUI frameworks, and SDLCA technical connoisseur. Be comfortable with algorithms and data structures such as dynamic array, linked list, stack, queue, binary search, binary search tree, hash map, depth first search, breadth first search, and merge sort.
  • A go-getter. Seek new opportunities, do not shy away from challenge, and bring energy and enthusiasm to the team.


Qualification and Skills

  • Must be currently enrolled in and in the process of obtaining a Bachelors or Masters degree in Computer Science or a related field or have recently completed an aforementioned degree
  • Knowledge or experience in developing Python or Java based Web GUI frameworks in Linux or Windows platforms
  • Proficiency of programming in a compiled language such as C, C++, C#, or Java
  • Proficiency of Python programming
  • Embedded programming, Linux and cloud-related knowledge is a benefit
  • Knowledge of software engineering tools (IDE, Debuggers, analyzers, VCS)
