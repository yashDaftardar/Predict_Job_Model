Full Job Description

Introduction
You bring the software engineering chops; we provide the challenge to gain new skills by expanding Watson Assistant’s capabilities to integrate with solutions that matter to our customers!

Our mission is to revolutionize customer service by integrating AI into products and solutions employed by our customers via the latest conversational artificial intelligence (AI), natural language processing (NLP) and speech technologies in IBM’s industry-leading chatbot platform Watson Assistant.

Integrating Watson Assistant’s chatbot capabilities with 3rd party and customer-developed solutions provides our customers an end-to-end solution by meeting their customers on their channel of choice (website, WhatsApp, Zendesk, Slack, etc.) and seamlessly integrating with required systems such as support ticketing and CRM. We are looking for a strong developer that desires a challenge to change the chatbot landscape!

Your Role and Responsibilities
This position is for a senior backend engineer in IBM Watson Assistant’s integrations team. Your responsibilities will include designing and architecting micro services, and developing extensibility capabilities and required infrastructure to connect Watson Assistant to external solutions.

We are looking for an experienced and strong back-backend developer with several years of experience with application development in NodeJS and Java, preferably using container-based (e.g. Docker runtime) micro-service architectures on the cloud (e.g. on a Kubernetes cluster).

Your responsibilities will span the entire lifecycle of a component, including gathering requirements, design and development, continuous integration/deployment and operations. You will work with researchers, user experience designers, front end and back end developers and product managers to understand requirements, create technical solutions, and deploy to cloud environments. As part of the role, you will provide technical leadership to junior members of the team.

You will use the latest software development tools, techniques and approaches and work with leading minds in the industry to build solutions you can be proud of. You will work in a collaborative, welcoming environment where new ideas drive technological innovation.

Above all, we are looking for team members with a keen sense of ownership, resilience and drive. If you like dynamic projects and enjoy being challenged, if you can make progress when faced with ambiguous requirements and uncertainty (and don’t mind making a mistake or two on the way), then we may be the perfect team for you!

Location preferences: New York, NY; Cambridge, MA; Austin, TX; Littleton, MA; San Jose, CA; Raleigh–Durham, NC

Required Technical and Professional Expertise
  • 5+ years of industry experience with NodeJS and Java developing backend applications
  • Experience with design and development of production-grade applications.

Preferred Technical and Professional Expertise
  • Experience with container-based micro services for cloud applications (e.g. Docker runtime, Kubernetes)
  • Experience with SQL databases (e.g. Postgres)
  • Experience with deployment and troubleshooting micro services on Kubernetes
  • Experience with CI/CD (e.g.: Jenkins)
