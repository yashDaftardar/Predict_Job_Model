Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE) advances the way people live and work. We bring together curious minds to create breakthrough technology solutions, helping our customers make their mark on the world.

HPE makes Hybrid IT simple. HPE helps customers to design the right mix of Hybrid IT solutions to serve their unique needs. We bring next generation infrastructure that uses intelligent software to simplify and accelerate the delivery of new apps, services and business insights. Our mission is to empower our customers with new ways to deliver and manage IT on-premises and in the cloud.

Are you looking for a unique, truly innovative role? What if it could be with one of the most impactful IT companies in the world? Then we have the right opportunity—we are looking for a Systems/Software Engineer to join us! In this amazing role, you will work with the latest in hardware and software technology and provide an excellent opportunity to learn and gain experience in an exciting, high profile, team-oriented, and fast-paced environment. If you have a track record in and a passion for system/software engineering, want to be part of a business that is growing—with great opportunities, and are ready for your next role, then please apply today.

How you’ll make your mark:

  • You will provide engineering support for the OS team. Tasks can range from setup and deployment of servers to installing/updating OS/firmware on servers and troubleshooting errors.
  • You will represent HPE as onsite engineer at VMware. Tasks can range from setup and deployment of servers to installing/updating OS/firmware on servers and troubleshooting errors.
  • You will help track defects filed with VMware and interface between HPE and VMware to help resolve open issues.
  • You will collaborate and communicate with management, internal, and partners regarding program status, progress, and issue resolution .

About You:

  • You have a Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, or equivalent.
  • You have 4-6 years experience.
  • You have experience and/or understanding of OS architecture (Linux, Windows, or VMware).
  • You have strong analytical and problem solving skills.
  • You must have ability to be a multi-tasker, and problem solver with high energy levels and strong organizational abilities
  • You must be organized and attentive to details
  • You have understanding of basic testing and debugging procedures
  • You have excellent written and verbal communication skills; mastery in English
