Cisco Webex is the World's #1 business collaboration and customer engagement platform. It is used across all industries in the world being used to support governments, business, education, and more including use by 85% of Fortune 500 business. Cisco is the clear market leader in cognitive collaboration for unified communication and contact centers with People Insights, Webex Assistant, Webex Experience Management and now BabbleLabs noise cancellation. These technologies are being integrated into Webex calling, meetings, messaging, team collaboration and contact center.

Webex is built on a very modern SaaS platform with a strong culture of DevOps that operates worldwide with engineers in locations such as the US, EU, China, and India. The platform is used by 100s of millions of people around the world, and we’re looking to build a team that can expand its reach to other powerful use cases that developers can integrate into their applications and services.
What You’ll Do

As a Backend Software Engineer, you will:
Work with a thriving development team that loves practicing continuous integration and deployment
Make DevOps a primary concern to ensure services are reliable, scalable, manageable and supportable
Develop new micro services that will be built on a Kubernetes PaaS
Enhance and Extend Webex Telemetry systems for analytics and continuous improvement
Help drive architectural consistency in the SDK portfolio Requirements
Loves to code in C++, C#, Java, or Go.
3+ Years of experience as a Full Stack engineer who has developed multiple versions of products
Understanding of SQL and NoSQL databases at scale
Demonstrated experience with REST, API design patterns, and scalable containerized systems and microservices
An expert in building web APIs or applications in frameworks such as ASP.NET, SpringMVC, Express, Rails, or Flask.
Well-versed in front-end development with modern JavaScript architectures such as React, AngularJS, Vue, or other frameworks.
Experience or demonstrated interest with real time communication/streaming protocols and video technologies Nice to Have
Experience with Signaling protocols like SIP, Media protocols like RTP, RTCP, SRTP and Misc protocols like DTLS, TLS, HTTPS
