About Bill.com

Bill.com is a leading provider of cloud-based software that simplifies, digitizes, and automates complex, back-office financial operations for small and midsize businesses. Customers use the Bill.com platform to manage end-to-end financial workflows and to process payments. The Bill.com AI-enabled, financial software platform creates connections between businesses and their suppliers and clients. It helps manage cash inflows and outflow. The company partners with several of the largest U.S. financial institutions, the majority of the top 100 U.S. accounting firms, and popular accounting software providers. Bill.com has offices in San Jose, California and Houston, Texas.

Mission:
Bill.com is looking for a Staff Software Engineer to work on the emerging payments team to enhance our platform and build new capabilities for the company's next generation innovation ideas. Your mission will be to enable customers to pay and get paid with different options and enable instant delivery of payments across channels. Additionally, you will generate new revenue streams for the business and work closely with PMs to define the best customer experience for such products.

Key Responsibilities

    • Responsible for the design and development of products using Java language
    • Working closely with PM and other cross functional teams to solidify product roadmap and help execute the roadmap with the agile team
    • Building a high-level roadmap to execute over 3-6 months (idea to reality) especially working with financial partners
    • Driving architectural changes required to deliver a new ideas, create and share HLD to larger organizations
    • Coaching and guiding younger engineers to deliver for the large and complex projects
    • Drive scrum teams as necessary

Qualifications

    • 8+ years of experience in JAVA and distributed systems
    • 2+ years of experience coaching and mentoring junior engineers
    • 3+ years of experience of detailing out large project with technology details
    • Ability to drive scrum teams as necessary
    • Experience driving large software projects
    • MS in CS or equivalent

Competencies

    • Functional/Technical Expertise
    • Values: Fun, Humble, Authentic, Passionate, Dedicated
    • Learning Abilities/Tech Savvy
    • Communication
    • Team Player

Expected Outcomes in 12 Months

    • 3 Months: Ability to learn current architecture and start producing features for current roadmap
    • 6 Months: Ability to help product team to develop project roadmaps based on overall vision
    • 12 Months: Ability to lead and deliver large scale project (esp. new banking/processor integration)
