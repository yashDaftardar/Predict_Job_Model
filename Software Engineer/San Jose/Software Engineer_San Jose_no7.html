Who We Are
At PX Application Engineering we are working on Cisco's world-class product to connect all of Cisco's offerings through one cloud-based partner portal. We will be enabling our partners and customers to see how their Cisco products are working for them, how they can increase the value of their purchases, learn about new opportunities, engage with specialists to tackle their specific problems, and more. What You'll Do
You will build the "front door" for the entire CX Cloud Platform, crafting amazing user experiences for Cisco's customers by building new features and implementing simple and elegant solutions that balance current needs while keeping an eye on the future. You would be expected to contribute in the following ways:
Write readable and reusable code, following SOLID principles and other guidelines
Focus on understanding long-term goals and minimizing technical debt
Use systems thinking and mindfulness to develop a deep understanding of how individual modules align with the entire product
Commit to building quality products, and take ownership of performance and security
Learn from mistakes and embrace the continuous improvement philosophy Who You'll Work With
You will work with best-in-class engineers and technical leads on an agile team that owns their outcomes. We care about stunning design, great UX, and good engineering practices -- we love to write software that is secure, tested, easy to maintain, responsive, and performant. This is an outstanding opportunity to be part of a group that is driving an innovative technical roadmap for next-generation applications and cloud services using today's most advanced technologies. Who You Are
Your passion is crafting delightful user experiences, specializing in the front-end. You love improving the efficiency and quality of code. You have deep knowledge of your domain and the ability to learn and adapt to changing needs and priorities. Staying on top of fast-moving trends in web development is likely a hobby for you. While you love coming up with great ideas, you are also humbled by those with different perspectives and the incredible value they add to your work. Required skills and experience:
8+ years of experience in enterprise UI development
Excellent written and oral communication skills
Strong knowledge of Angular, HTML5, CSS, consuming RESTful services
Proven experience on at least one large-scale project
Experience with Cloud Services in one or more platforms; AWS, Azure, Google from a developer perspective
Working knowledge of accessibility standards
A drive for efficiency and collaboration, with a history of establishing great relationships with UX and Product Management teams across time zones
A commitment to quality, technical excellence, and results
Creativity and flexibility in using various technologies to solve human problems You may be good at:
Serverless technologies like AWS Lambda, Firebase functions
CI/CD tools such as Jenkins, CircleCI, Octopus, Azure DevOps
