What You’ll Do
In this role, you'll work on the Webex Graph and People Insights features. We’ve built a proprietary data platform that scours billions of pages on the web and uses intelligence and machine learning to create the richest, most real-time profiles in the world for hundreds of millions of people and tens of millions of companies.

To do this we tackle problems like - How do you decide which parts of the web to crawl? How do you extract structured data from unstructured text? How do you efficiently keep this data up to date?

We’re now working to combine this rich person and company data with real-time information about people's interactions, to unify all the Collaboration data across our Collaboration portfolio into a networked graph of all your interactions and relationships. It’s an enormous and exciting massive-scale data problem with a huge amount of whitespace problems to tackle from scratch as part of that effort. How do you store and query all this data efficiently? What unique deductions and insights might you make from it? It’s also one which we feel has huge product implications for being able to help improve people’s lives at massive scale during this time of almost entirely remote work.

Webex Graph seeks to provide our customers with perfect context on the people they are meeting and collaborating with, right when they need it. We seek to provide users with all the context the need to succeed... Who are you meeting with? What are the most important facts about the company they work for? Do you have anything in common with them?

Our team is ripe with exciting technical challenges, most of which have yet to be solved here and some of which have yet to be solved anywhere!
