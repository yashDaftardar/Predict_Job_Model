Job responsibilities
As a member of Kuiper MAC-SW, you will be responsible for developing user and control plane SW to be running on ground networks, on customer terminals and on satellites. Come and join the innovative and unique wireless project Kuiper. You would be focused on designing and developing wireless stack software for Kuiper. You will be a key member of a cross functional team of innovators, working on unique wireless technology that will enable millions of people to stay connected.

The Role
  • Applying the whole SW process from requirement to testing
  • Conduct data-flow performance analysis and vet different designs/implementation
  • Design and develop wireless Kuiper-MAC-SW in both control and user plane
  • Mapping SW components to physical resources
