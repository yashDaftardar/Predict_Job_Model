Job Description


Primary responsibilities will include the following:

  • Develop tools/flows to automate layout generation and verification
  • Develop test cases to validate tools/flows
  • Collaborate with layout designers to improve overall productivity

In addition to the qualifications listed below, the ideal candidate will also have:

  • Strong communication skills verbal and written
  • Problem solving skills and works well in a team environment


Qualifications


You must possess the below minimum qualifications to be initially considered for this position. Preferred qualifications are in addition to the minimum requirements and are considered a plus factor in identifying top candidates.

Minimum Qualifications:

The candidate must have Bachelor's in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Computer Science or other related disciplines with 4+ years experience OR a Master's in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Computer Science or other related disciplines with 3+ years experience in the following:

  • Understanding of VLSI layout design
  • Software Programming languages experience
  • Understanding of CAD algorithms


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Knowledge of CAD tools

Inside this Business Group


The Design Engineering Group is a worldwide team responsible for the design, development, validation, and manufacturing of IPs and SOCs. Our mission is to deliver leadership products through groundbreaking innovations.
