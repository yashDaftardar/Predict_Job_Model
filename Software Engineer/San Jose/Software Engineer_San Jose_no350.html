Leading the future of luxury mobility

Lucid’s mission is to inspire the adoption of sustainable energy by creating the most captivating luxury electric vehicles, centered around the human experience. Working at Lucid Motors means having a shared vision to power the future in revolutionary ways. Be part of a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to transform the automotive industry.

The Enterprise team at Lucid is responsible for systems that help engineers build our first product, the Lucid Air. We're looking for early engineers to join our small team in Newark. As a Full Stack Engineer, you'll build mission-critical systems used to scale electric car production.

You Will:

    • Build internal tools for our business and engineering teams.
    • Design, build, and maintain user experiences, APIs, and systems.
    • Debug production issues across the entire technology stack.
    • Improve engineering standards, tools, and processes.

You Bring:

    • Enjoys moving quickly and is excited about working on various green-field projects.
    • Experience working with modern web technologies and frameworks (Javascript, Golang, React.js, Node.js)
    • Experience as a generalist working on both frontend and backend code.
    • Experience with Kubernetes or Docker.
    • Can set the technical and cultural standard for engineers in the team.
    • Experience building, maintaining, and integrating with complex enterprise systems.
Be part of something amazing
