Rivian is on a mission to keep the world adventurous forever. This goes for the emissions-free Electric Adventure Vehicles we build, and the curious, courageous souls we seek to attract.

As a company, we constantly challenge what’s possible, never simply accepting what has always been done. We reframe old problems, seek new solutions and operate comfortably in areas that are unknown. Our backgrounds are diverse, but our team shares a love of the outdoors and a desire to protect it for future generations.

We operate development centers in Plymouth, Michigan; Southern California (Irvine, Carson & LA); Silicon Valley (San Jose and Palo Alto); Vancouver, British Columbia; and Surrey, England; as well as a manufacturing facility in Normal, Illinois.

CONNECTED SYSTEMS MISSION:
Keep our customers exploring the world by proactively monitoring, diagnosing and learning from our product fleets through custom processes, services and tools.
Come join our mission, to develop custom software tools, providing API services and user interfaces to end users across the organization. You will have the opportunity to join a growing team driving software architecture, design and implementation for a ground up diagnostics tool enabling the next generation of vehicle designs through data driven decision making and lessons learned. Be part of our mission to drive overall excellence. We are looking for the best talent to come join a team of exceptional engineers, so if you are humble with a bias towards action; ready to take on the unknown and build something that has never been done before, we are excited to talk to you!
THIS IS WHAT YOU’LL DO:
  • Participate in next generation vehicle tool development from prototype to end of life
  • Develop reactive user interfaces for a number of different personas
  • Build and optimize highly reliable, scalable, and distributed micro-services
  • Use automation as an opportunity to reduce roadblocks and achieve deliverables
THIS IS WHAT YOU’LL NEED:
  • Experience in software development and automation (Python)
  • Experience with front end development and TypeScript / JavaScript frameworks (React.js/Redux)
  • Unit testing experience and test-driven development
  • Systematic troubleshooting and Root Cause Analysis experience
  • Knowledge of Agile Development of Accessible Software Tools
  • Strong understanding of OOPs
  • Bachelor’s degree with 2+ years of experience with Software Engineering, Computer Science or another related degree.
NICE TO HAVES:
  • Master’s Degree in related field
  • Git experience
  • Jenkins experience
  • Experience with bus standards such as CAN, DoIP, FlexRay, LIN
  • Knowledge of UDS ISO-14229 / ISO-15765 / SAE J1979 / J2534/ FuSA ISO-26262
THIS IS WHERE YOU’LL WORK:
Department: Connected Systems
Location: San Jose, CA (eventually moving to Palo Alto, CA)
