Full Job Description

Tilt Five, Inc. has created the world's first affordable mixed-reality glasses that deliver spectacular up-close holograms on any tabletop. It brings entertainment to life in ways that have never before been possible.

Following a hugely successful Kickstarter campaign in 2019, Tilt Five aims to revolutionize tabletop gaming with glasses so lightweight and comfortable you can play all day. Our lenses are large and wide open, so the glasses don't block your vision. You can see your friends, pizza, and drinks, while you play games with your friends and family around the table. And with "far-eye projection" everything you look at is in focus whether you're leaning back in your chair or pressing your chin to the board to get a better look at the details.

We're looking for talented and driven technologists to help deliver the future of home entertainment. Have a look at the description below. If this sounds like you, send us your details!

As a Software Development Engineer in Test at Tilt Five you will be creating test infrastructure and tools to enable fast iteration on our tabletop augmented reality glasses software while ensuring quality remains high. You’ll be joining a small team of passionate engineers, where you’ll be responsible for developing a testing strategy from the ground up, having an opportunity to impact all aspects of the product and development process.

Qualifications include:

  • 5+ years of experience in software development/hardware
  • Experience developing automated testing infrastructure
  • Experience with Continuous Integration systems (e.g. Jenkins, Travis, etc.)
  • Experience working with embedded systems and firmware
  • Excellent collaborative and communication skills
  • Knowledge of C, C++, higher level languages (e.g. Python, Java, C#, Go)
  • BS in Computer Science or equivalent experience

Responsibilities:

  • Build and maintain automated testing infrastructure to validate our tabletop AR glasses
  • Help create test strategies and test cases to assess product quality
  • Integrate with SCM infrastructure to establish a continuous build and test cycle
  • Establish processes and tools to run automation scripts and generate regular test reports
