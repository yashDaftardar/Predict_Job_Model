Job Summary

ABOUT THE TEAM

Metadata-Infra is a dynamic team whose mission is to provide a consistent, available, and easy-to-use metadata platform for the development of Rubrik’s internal and public APIs and UI. Among the many core abstractions owned by the Metadata-Infra team is the global hierarchy of all objects protected by Rubrik, including VMs, Oracle DBs, Microsoft Office, and much more.

As a member of the Metadata-Infra team, you’ll work cross-functionally, designing and building systems and frameworks that empower Rubrik’s product developers to implement new features in a scalable and efficient manner.

ABOUT THE JOB

  • Define and drive the ongoing vision of how developers will interact with and develop on top of Rubrik’s metadata platform.
  • Design, implement, and evolve critical large-scale distributed systems responsible for collecting, organizing, and serving metadata at scale.
  • Evangelize framework best practices and continuously look for opportunities to build new abstractions that will increase developer velocity and clarity.
  • Identify and resolve bottlenecks and improve resiliency, scalability, and availability.
  • Have full-stack ownership of features, from design to implementation to testing.

ABOUT YOU

  • A degree in Computer Science or equivalent
  • Strong experience in software design, implementation, testing, and performance tuning
  • Experience with SQL and distributed databases
  • Experience with multithreading and asynchronous processing
  • Experience building large-scale distributed systems or working with public clouds such as AWS, Azure, or GCP
  • Ability to work in a team environment and collaborate across teams

ABOUT RUBRIK

Rubrik is one of the fastest growing companies in Silicon Valley, revolutionizing data protection and management in the emerging multi-cloud world. We are the leader in cloud data management, delivering a single platform to manage and protect data in the cloud, at the edge, and on-premises. Enterprises choose Rubrik to simplify backup and recovery, accelerate cloud adoption, enable automation at scale, and secure against cyberthreats.
