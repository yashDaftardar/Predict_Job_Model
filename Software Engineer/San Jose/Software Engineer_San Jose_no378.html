Software Integration Engineer

Job Summary:

Our client in Mountain View, CA is looking for a software Integration Engineer. This is a direct hire position.

What You Will Do:

The software integration engineer will be closely working with the software or firmware development of the vehicle. They will be collaborating closely with us to develop the communication structure between multiple ECUs. The position will focus on the development of PIDs, controls, message structure and bus loading. A strong working knowledge of CAN communication is preferred. SPI and I2C is a plus. Must have worked on development and testing of master - slave or master - multiple slave or multiple master - multiple slave system. Strong understanding of OBDII, vehicle diagnostics. Experience in creating .dbc files and performing HIL, SIL testing.

Basics:

  • Design of vehicle systems software architecture
  • Proficiency in designing software architecture
  • Proficiency in interface control, change control, and requirements control
  • Experience in designing ARINC or CAN communication protocols among multiple party controllers
  • Experience in software simulations (SILS/HILS)

Preferred:

  • Experience in developments using CAN 2.0B / CAN open
  • Experience in MATLAB/Simulink
  • Familiar with power management and battery management system
  • Familiar with propulsion motor control
  • Familiar with internal combustion engine control
  • Experience in aerospace or automotive software developments
