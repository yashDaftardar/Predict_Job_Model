Precision medicine for pets with cancer

ImpriMed is integrating cutting-edge tools from oncology and data science to deliver personalized drug therapy for pets with cancer. We aim to support veterinarians treating pet cancer patients with precision medicine technology by informing them of the most effective anti-cancer drugs for each patient before the final treatment decisions are made. Our service is based on drug-cell interaction data generated by our high-throughput drug sensitivity assay, which uses a patientâ€™s live cancer cells to measure the efficacy of FDA-approved drugs. Constructed from pet cancer patient data, the ImpriMed database and AI- based prediction models will also be a valuable resource for translational applications in human oncology. Using sophisticated data management, integration, and analytics methods is fundamental for us to provide accurate predictions within a clinical decision-making framework. In addition, building communication channels among clinicians, patient owners, and the company is essential.

We are seeking a highly motivated and qualified mid-to-senior level Software Engineer to join our team for this key position. The ideal candidate has a strong desire to work with a new and rapidly growing company delivering complex health information and laboratory results to clinical and pet owner clients. This role is full-time and will be based in our headquarters, Palo Alto, California with options for remote work.
