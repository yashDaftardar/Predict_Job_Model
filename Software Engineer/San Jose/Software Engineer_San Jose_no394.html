The Associate Embedded Software Engineer will be a hands-on developer, interacting with cross-functional engineering teams to develop embedded software for Acacia’s rapidly growing and expanding product portfolio. The individual will be a member of Acacia’s Software team and contribute to the software life cycle of our products and will work within a multi-disciplinary Hardware, Software, Optics, and DSP engineering organization. The Associate Software Engineer will produce high quality code that is robust and maintainable.

Key Essential Functions: Collaborate with other team members to drive product quality thru enhancing the regression tests and test environment Development and maintenance of in-house tools to increase the team’s productivity Graphical User Interface software development Troubleshooting software bugs within Acacia’s real-time embedded firmware Hands on experience with state of the art fiber optic telecommunication systems in a laboratory environment Data acquisition and trend analysis

Other Duties Other duties as assigned

Minimum Qualifications, Experience, Skills, Education and Certifications: BS or MS (preferred) in Computer Engineering, Electronics or Electrical Engineering with 2+ years of experience in embedded software development and support in the telecommunications industry Proficiency in C# or C++, knowledge of scripting languages preferably Python™ Experience with RTOS and embedded CPUs and microcontrollers (ARM® preferred) Experience with a revision control and defect tracking systems (Perforce™, GIT, Bugzilla, JIRA) Self-motivated, good interpersonal, written, and verbal communication skills Organized and be able to manage their time well

Highly Desired Skills : Experience working within a team environment developing embedded software in a multi-processor/multithreaded environment Experience with collaborative code review tools (such as Jira Crucible®) Enjoys working in lab environments, likes to be challenged to understand system and figure out ways to break it Ability to handle minimally defined or ambiguous tasks Previous internship experience

All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, disability, or veteran status.
