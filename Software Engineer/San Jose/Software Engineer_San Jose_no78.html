Full Job Description

Coherent is a world leader of lasers and laser-based technology for materials processing, microelectronics, scientific, life sciences and medical customers. The light we deliver touches almost every aspect of human life—vision correction to cell analysis, precision welding to 3-D metal printing, gravity wave detection to brain research. Put your talents to work at Coherent where you can create and innovate enabling technologies that will push the frontiers of science and technology.

In our global IT team we provide IT services for our colleagues in Europe and worldwide, covering the areas of workplace management, networks, telephony, security, servers and data centers. In addition to the classic client service activities we manage the installation and operation of the network and server landscapes, the support of business applications and the monitoring of IT systems.

The management and interaction of internal IT systems and operational processes is becoming increasingly demanding and complex. This position will support our internal IT by designing and implementing automation solutions. You will reduce manual activities, accelerate processes and enable your colleagues to concentrate on complex tasks that cannot (yet) be automated.

The position is located at one of our major sites in Germany (Dieburg, Gilching near Munich, Göttingen, Lübeck, Mainz) or Glasgow (Scottland).

The Role:

  • Analysis and optimization of existing IT processes to realize automation potential
  • Participation in internal local and global projects
  • Recording and evaluation of automation requirements of the departments
  • Designing and creation of software or scripts for process automation
  • Functional tests of your solutions
  • Creating and maintaining the documentation of your solutions

Education and Work Experience:

  • Broad IT knowledge and a good technical understanding
  • Experience in software development and / or scripting (e.g. Powershell)
  • Experience in process automation
  • Independent, structured and result-oriented working methods
  • Strong communication and team skills
  • Skilled in consulting, problem solving and designing solutions
  • Very good English language skills - German language skills are an advantage
  • Willingness to take occasional business trips and participate in cross-location and cross-time zone virtual meetings outside regular working hours
  • You feel comfortable in a global IT environment, which is characterized by Windows, O365, Azure, Intune, Citrix, Oracle, ServiceNow
  • Knowledge in the following areas is an advantage but not absolutely necessary:
    Windows client and server operating systems, Javascript, web services (REST, JSON), Oracle, ServiceNow, Citrix, SCCM, Office365, MS Power Apps, MS Power Automate (Flow)
