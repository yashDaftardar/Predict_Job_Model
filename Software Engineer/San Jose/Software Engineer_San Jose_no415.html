Full Job Description

We are a stealth startup building perception systems for autonomy. We are based in the San Francisco Bay Area, funded by NEA (https://www.nea.com/), and our core team includes faculty entrepreneurs (Stanford and UC Santa Barbara) and industry veterans (Uber, Apple, Amazon Lab126, Rohde & Schwarz), who have successfully shepherded signal processing and machine learning innovations to large-scale software for location improvement and safety at Uber, led the development of state-of-the-art computer vision technologies that shipped over millions of Amazon devices, and delivered zero-to-one product experiences at Uber and Box. Our core product grew out of 5+ years of university R&D by our co-founders. You can find out more about us by visiting our website (https://www.plato.systems/).

Our mission and team expertise spans beyond software to advanced sensor systems, algorithms, embedded systems, signal processing, and machine learning. Our team is building and deploying edge software and cloud services for real-time customer facing products as well as internal big data tools. We look for people with a depth of expertise and experience in one of these areas, and with the intellectual curiosity for interacting with, learning from, and teaching world-class experts in areas outside their expertise.

We currently have an exciting opportunity in the area of signal processing in San Carlos, CA. You will join a multi-disciplinary group of scientists and engineers and will have the opportunity to collaborate with cross-functional product and research teams. An ideal candidate will have a proven track record of driving zero-to-one innovation for large-scale system projects.


Responsibilities

  • Integration and optimization of signal processing techniques and algorithms in Python/C++ in production software for embedded systems on the edge.
  • Handling optimization of advanced signal processing related algorithms such as estimation, detection, prediction, and tracking of people and objects using active depth sensors.
  • Be involved in all stages of algorithm development including prototyping, data collection, benchmarking, and testing of algorithmic solutions and pipelines.
  • Design and implementation of benchmarking scripts to evaluate individual algorithms as well as the performance of the full pipeline.
  • Participate in tuning algorithms to meet performance requirements, including precision and recall.
  • Optimize various algorithms such that they operate within resource constraints (e.g., CPU / GPU / memory limits).
  • Own, maintain, and improve software pipelines, including continuous evaluation, gap analysis, benchmarking and fine tuning.

Basic Qualifications

  • Graduate degree in Electrical or Computer engineering (MSc or PhD).
  • 3+ years of experience in designing and developing Python/C/C++ applications with a focus on signal processing algorithms.
  • Industry experience deploying at least one signal processing algorithm or pipeline onto production systems.
  • Prior working experience with at least one active depth sensor (e.g., Time-of-flight sensors, Sonar, Radar, LiDAR, etc.).
  • Strong fundamental knowledge and skill sets in signal processing, probability theory, fixed-point arithmetic, and numerical methods.
  • Prior experience with software development best practices, including debugging, profiling, and collaborating (e.g., code review).
  • Self-motivated with excellent problem solving and technical communication skills
  • Independent with the grit to investigate and debug signal processing artifacts
  • Writes clear, complete, and useful documentation.
  • Enjoys taking initiative and working with multidisciplinary teams.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Prior experience with implementing algorithms in embedded systems and GPU optimizations for real-time processing.
  • Knowledge of advanced signal processing algorithms for depth sensors and/or Radar systems.
  • Working experience in one or more the following areas of signal processing:
    • Theory and algorithms for signal detection and estimation
    • Algorithm development for sensing and perception for active depth sensors (Radar, Sonar, ultrasound, or Time of Flight, .. )
    • Localization and tracking algorithms, including Kalman and particle filters
    • ADAS architectures, signal pipelines, and signal processing schemes
    • FFT based processing, MUSIC algorithm, filtering, clustering and feature extraction
    • Point cloud processing and filtering
  • Prior experience developing sensor fusion algorithms using data from different sensing modalities.
  • 5+ years of experience with product level development and support.

Cultural Fit

  • Ability to work in a small and fast-paced environment.
  • A positive attitude, team-oriented spirit, and enthusiasm for our goals.
  • A curious outlook - willingness to learn new things and solutions to fit the problem.
  • A strong sense of ownership and passion for completing a job well - attention to detail.
  • Ability to work well with all levels of company regardless of seniority.
  • Sense of humor and ability to proactively problem solve.
