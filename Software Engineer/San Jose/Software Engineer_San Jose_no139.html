What's it like to work at Agilent in R&D? Watch the video"

Agilent encourages and supports discoveries that advance the quality of life. We provide life science, diagnostic and applied market laboratories worldwide with instruments, services, consumables, applications and expertise. Agilent enables customers to gain the answers and insights they seek - so they can do what they do best: improve the world around us. Information about Agilent is available at www.agilent.com.

Want to go further in Research & Development? Here's where you take the next step, working with the groundbreaking teams taking Agilent's world-leading products from the drawing board to the real world. You'll not only own the projects, you'll also resolve approach taken to each assignment, coordinating with colleagues in other functions throughout the process.

Designs Agilent products and specifies the components that make up the products. Designs and develops products, components, processes, and systems using broad engineering concepts and research techniques. Documents research and design specifications. Solves sophisticated problems including customer application issue of varying scope and complexity. Uses product and customer applications knowledge to settle, define, develop and validate design concepts and methods. May facilitate transition of product design and improvements to full production/delivery. May lead product definition and system design for sophisticated features or coordinate a small group in feature development and integration.

Key Responsibilities

Agilent Technologies is developing a range of innovative, enterprise and cloud-based software products for the design, analysis and interpretation of genetic data. As a future Senior Software Engineer in our team we expect you to:

  • Maintain, strengthen and improve our Enterprise and Core SaaS Platforms for genomics design and data analysis.

  • Lead and drive new technical Components, Services & Applications.

  • Take ownership in getting code to production and a hands-on approach in troubleshooting.

  • Research, recommend, develop, and implement improvements across the software stack in a pro-active manner.

  • Maintain and share expertise in multiple technologies that are used in our software products.

  • Reduce broad concepts, business strategies into structured projects.

  • Own the design and delivery of new product, process and service technology advancements.

  • Tackle sophisticated development issues.
