Full Job Description

Are you interested in building products and technology to change the future of video? Do you want to make an impact with a close-knit team within a 110-person company? Do you want to ship code as soon as you join and scale systems at a successful and growing startup?

Tubular goes beyond real-time insights about the performance of online videos across all countries, devices, and all major platforms, by also making available the performance of everything else: competitive analysis, marketing demand, industry and thematic trends.

We have empowered 5,000+ creators and 200+ Enterprise customers to create content with confidence, improve their organic and paid promotion, find and partner with influencers, and report it all out efficiently. Tubular analyzes the engagement of the world of digital video viewers and tracks 7+ billion videos and live streams across the major platforms, including YouTube, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and Twitch.

Come watch your favorite videos on comedy, gaming, fashion, electronics, and more as you build cool visualizations, scale systems tracking billions of engagements, and create an application layer of insights on top of the activities of 1+ billion online video viewers.

Reasons to be a full stack engineer at Tubular:

    • Learn how to create amazing user experiences that affect every single user
    • Collaborate broadly within the company to solve complex problems and bring an idea to life
    • Grow your experience and skill sets by working on a project end-to-end, from frontend to middle layer to backend services using cutting edge technologies
    • Accelerate your career with mentorship from top engineers and opportunities to own the component/services you build, while building on a product users rave about
    • Enjoy working with a fun group of people who love food, games, coffee chats, and more

What you bring to the role:

    • A desire to learn new technologies and build great software used by key people in the digital video entertainment industry.
    • An innate ability to solve problems and communicate your technical insights effectively.
    • Experience with Python or JavaScript or the ability to learn them quickly.
    • Enthusiasm to be an active and contributing member of a high performance engineering team.
    • Previous internship experience is a plus.
    • B.A./B.S., M.S., or Ph.D. in Computer Science or an equivalent field.

The perks:

    • Competitive salary, equity and benefits.
    • Flex PTO.
    • Free lunch.
    • Top of the line equipment.
    • 401(k) - retirement plan.
    • 100% employer-paid Medical, dental and vision plans.
    • Life/AD&D & Disability Benefits
    • Company happy hours and team off sites.
    • Hackathons
    • Amazing company culture.
    • And more!
