The Role

Are you looking for an exciting full-stack development role with a focus on customer experience? Adobe is seeking a Software Development Engineer to join us on Adobe Sign, the award-winning signature service in Adobe’s Document Cloud suite of products.

Adobe Sign is the fastest and easiest way for you to get contracts signed, tracked, and filed. Unlike virtual fax or e-signature software, Adobe Sign offers an end-to-end solution by automating the entire contract process from the request for signature to the distribution and filing of the executed agreement. Adobe Sign instantly shows you what’s out for signature, what’s been signed, when and by whom. All documents are converted to PDF and automatically routed to all parties.

You will be joining a dynamic, agile software development team focused on producing secure, scalable, and high-quality enterprise solutions. Your work will impact millions of users worldwide and help contribute to one of Adobe’s fastest-growing services.

What you’ll do

  • Join a team of feature developers within the engineering organization to influence a customer-centric product design.
  • Work closely with our product management organization to define requirements and create detailed implementation designs.
  • Lead architecture reviews to ensure features adhere to a high level of quality, scalability and robustness.
  • Collaborate across teams of Product Owners, UX Designers, Developers, Quality Engineers, and Customer Support teams to develop features that are critical to growing and solidifying our global customer base.
  • Actively identify service soft spots, design and implement mitigation strategies.

Keys to your success

  • BS degree in Computer Science or equivalent software development experience.
  • 5+ years of experience in full-stack Web Development
  • Experience designing and building enterprise applications. Demonstrated ability to develop architectures that incorporate high availability, operations supportability and scalability is critical.
  • Hands-on experience with Java/Spring/RESTful API/JMS development, preferably within large, complex implementations.
  • Expertise in OOP and OOAD methodologies and principles.
  • Experience in JavaScript frameworks, such as React, Backbone, AngularJS, and jQuery would be a plus.
