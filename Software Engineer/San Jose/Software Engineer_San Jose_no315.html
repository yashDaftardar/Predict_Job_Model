Description
The PSE SW Team is seeking a highly motivated individual with a background in Software Development. In this position, your primary responsibility will be collaborating closely with other Apple SW Development teams and various hardware and testing teams to design and develop innovative products and software solutions. The PSE Software Engineer will also be responsible for maintaining and improving existing software. Responsibilities will include: Working alongside developers to define requirements and architecture Develop scalable software and present in Design reviews Develop unit test plans and work closely with QA team to help develop integration plans and help debug production issues Should be well versed with SVN and bug tracking systems with the ability to use them in day to day development processes Provide constructive feedback for testability and suitable solutions relying on data to justify technical decisions.
