What you'll do

You will be working on enhancing as well as building new functionality for the next generation SaaS offering of a Highly Scalable, Intent Based Management and Orchestration platform to manage network fabrics that span multi-domains and multi-clouds. You will develop applications mainly using Golang and Python in a microservice based architecture deployed on a Kubernetes cluster. You will be part of a team that is responsible for the entire development process of the features you own right from design, coding, testing to releasing to production.

Who you'll work with

You will have the opportunity to work with some of the brightest problem solvers and thinkers in the Application Centric Infrastructure (ACI) Team that work on next generation of cloud native networking, management, and orchestration solutions for multi-domain, multi-cloud environments. You will be part of a deeply engineering focused team that fosters innovation, collaboration and diversity.
Read more at: https://blogs.cisco.com/datacenter/cisco-fulfills-vision-for-aci-anywhere

Who you A re

You are a passionate and highly skilled software professional who thrives in a dynamic start-up like environment. You are independent and driven to get things done in a collaborative environment and you take pride in developing well designed, high quality code. You are looking for an opportunity to shine in a team where you can thrive, one that recognizes your capabilities and rewards you accordingly.

Mandatory Skills

  • Programming languages: Expertise in one of Go, Java, C++, Python
  • Foundation in data structures, algorithms and software design with strong analytical and debugging skills
  • Familiar with agile practices, and believes in test driven development
  • Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science or related field (MS preferred)
Preferred Skills

  • Familiar with microservices software architecture
  • Experienced in crafting, building and debugging large scale distributed systems.
  • Full stack software engineer
  • Experience with building and delivering SaaS offerings
  • Experience with public cloud (AWS, Azure, GCP)
