Come join the PowerPoint team as we deliver modern, intelligent, and collaborative experiences that will delight millions of PowerPoint 365 customers. We are located in heart of Silicon Valley in Sunnyvale, CA. The PowerPoint team has the responsibility for the design, implementation, and testing of the PowerPoint ecosystem of products, which includes native client and web experiences. In addition, the team is on the forefront for developing scalable cloud services deeply integrated into PowerPoint and other Office applications.

Responsibilities

Team members work closely together on every detail of design and implementation of the product. From fast evolving new features to long-range infrastructure and architecture investments, you will get the chance to ship world-class application software and services. We also have a great management team with lots of experience to learn from who can help you move your career forward.
We have mature development and engineering systems. Our engineers enjoy the benefits of rich telemetry, data driven decisions, rapid experimentation and building on top of a world-class platform. Our products and portfolio are rapidly expanding, and we are excited to meet the challenges of scale, performance, efficiency, and reliability as we build the world's best storytelling software.

Qualifications

  • 4+ years of software development experience in application or services development.
  • Fluency in C++, C#, or Java.
  • Full understanding of all aspects of the software development process with experience shipping at least one release.
  • Effective communication skills and ability to work cross group and across disciplines.
  • Excellent skills in design, debugging and problem solving.
  • Passion for customers, product quality, service excellence, test engineering and having a growth mindset
  • 3+ years experience in shipping high-quality products or services.
