Position Description:

Softdocs is seeking qualified applicants for the role of a Dev/Ops Engineer. Working closely with Softdocs’ engineering and infrastructure operations teams, this position requires a deep understanding of technology at both the infrastructure level and the application development level, as well as the ability to recognize where gaps often exist between these two layers. It is the responsibility of a Softdocs Dev/Ops Engineer to fill any gaps that are detected and take proactive steps to prevent application emergencies from ever happening.

Applicants should have technical expertise in software build and deployment methodologies, as well as test pipeline management. If hired, you will oversee and manage continuous integration & deployment (CI/CD) across all technology solutions and work with dev leads, product owners and test engineers to implement governance and controls to enforce quality standards across the software delivery pipeline and continuously improve release management practices. You will also oversee adherence to deployment windows and release processes, and facilitate appropriate communication channels across teams and with stakeholders.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Build, update and automate CI/CD processes for new and existing applications
  • Advance the sophistication of Softdocs’ alerting and escalation mechanisms
  • Stay up-to-date with contemporary tech trends, advocating when relevant
  • Increase system performance with focus on high availability and scalability
  • Propose, scope, design and implement various infrastructure architectures
  • Collaborate with development and product team members to deliver end-to-end products and features, from conception to delivery

Skills and Experience:

  • Strong knowledge and demonstrated understanding of enterprise-class business applications with low-latency, high performance and 24x7 environment
  • Successful experience at taking an organization from early stages of automated code delivery to enterprise-grade code integration and code delivery
  • 3+ years of hands-on working experience to build CI/CD processes using Microsoft .NET technologies using C# along with build and setup tools specific to MS.NET
  • 3+ years of experience as a DevOps/Build/Release Engineer
  • 7+ years of IT experience
  • A BA/BS degree in computer science, management information systems, or relevant field of study is preferred

Why Softdocs:

Softdocs produces a cloud-based Enterprise Content Management (ECM) system for the education market. As a company, we are passionate about taking care of our customers, because our customers are working hard to produce the next generation of leaders for our world. The less time they spend on administrative headaches the more time they can spend bettering the experience of their students. Our solutions allow them to do this.

We are seeking to hire the right new employee, someone who has been through the CI/CD evolution and understands the success factors and mistakes made along the way, whose experience will provide a positive impact on our company from day one.

At Softdocs, our culture highly values the ideas, experience and thoughts of every employee. Each one of us can come to work and make a difference in the lives of our teammates and the customers we serve. If hired, you will be listened to and see your feedback valued at all levels of management.

We expect our staff to work hard, but we also value work life balance and feel that a healthy employee in all aspects does their best work.
