Full Job Description

Under the direction of the Director of the Office of Project Management and Application Management, the person in this position provides expertise and skills in web design and development for the agency. Creates and supports web-based applications using object oriented program languages and other technologies using approved technologies and agency development standards. Manages and supports the agency website content management system. Develops and maintains the agency policies and procedures including, but not limited to, accessibility and standards for user interface design. Is considered a subject matter expert and mentors junior developers in technical and professional skills.

A bachelor's degree in communications, computer science, or a related field and experience in information technology. Related experience may be substituted for the bachelor's degree on a year-for-year basis.

ONLY THOSE APPLICANTS CHOSEN FOR AN INTERVIEW WILL BE NOTIFIED BY LETTER THAT THE POSITION HAS BEEN FILLED.
