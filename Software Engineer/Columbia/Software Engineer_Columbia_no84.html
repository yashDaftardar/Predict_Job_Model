Full Job Description

Do you like writing mean and clean React code? Come join our team and help us build amazing things. We’re looking for someone with a passion for programming and for writing beautiful code to work on projects that connect brands to insurance carriers in order to unlock revenue streams and bring insurance products to consumers. You will join a team of exceptional developers working in a fast-paced, distributed environment to deliver world-class software. As a front-end engineer you will have the opportunity to greenfield new applications using best practices and refactor existing applications to bring them up to modern standards. This position can be done 100% remote for anyone authorized to work in the US or CANADA. Optional 4 day Work-week is available for this position.

REIN is a venture backed technology company building smarter insurance and risk platforms for an autonomous world. Our first platform product is www.droneinsurance.com with subsequent products being developed and launched in autonomous vehicles and other verticals. We have an intelligent and motivated staff. We have offices in Massachusetts, Chapel Hill, NC and Halifax, Nova Scotia.

Candidates should be prepared to share examples of code they’ve developed and be willing to take a coding test.
