Description/Job Summary

AgFirst Farm Credit Bank provides financing, as well as a range of technology and other services, to a network of agricultural lenders in the United States and Puerto Rico. Located on Main Street in Columbia, S.C., AgFirst is committed to providing a comfortable, inclusive work environment. We believe in investing in our employees so they can help lead our $35 billion company into the future. If you are looking to fuel your professional development and gain exposure in the field of Information Technology, apply today!

The Software Integration Engineer will design and develop enterprise business applications and data integration modules between our ERP and EPM systems, enterprise data warehouse, loan accounting and general ledger systems. Works closely with customers, colleagues, and other stakeholders to design, purchase, and implement business systems and upgrades.


Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Provide timely and accurate corrective actions to production support matters for assigned applications

  • Apply software patches, install upgrades, and correct errors to applications

  • Work across teams to solve production problems and issues

  • Available off hours for production problems when needed and notifies management and/or backup when not available

  • Study, analyzes, recommends and actively determines system needs and development

  • Proactive in systems monitoring, support issues, process analysis and re-engineering; formulates and makes recommendations to management

  • Learn and apply key technologies and practices required to support assigned applications

  • Understands the company's current architecture and the future vision

  • Designs or ensures that technical designs are consistent with the larger architecture vision by consulting with the Manager and if necessary with the Enterprise Architect

  • Coordinates the presentation, review and approval of technical designs
  • Collaborates with other technical areas to create integrated solutions that are consistent with the enterprise architecture vision


Requirements:

  • Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, IT systems management, related field or equivalent work experience

  • 4 - 6 years of experience in Applications Integration technologies

  • Proficient in Windows and web application development in an Object Oriented technology stack (.NET preferred)

  • Demonstrates excellent technical, teamwork, and communications skills

  • Proficient with Microsoft SSIS, SSRS, Crystal reports, Relational Database Management Systems

  • Working knowledge of application lifecycle management, application architecture frameworks, application analysis and coding, and software development methodologies
  • Expertise in support, design and development of highly available business-critical applications
