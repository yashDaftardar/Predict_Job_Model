Company Description

Harsco is recognized for technical leadership and worldwide experience in virtually all major aspects of railway track maintenance. We enable railroads to operate at peak efficiency over smooth, precisely aligned track that increases railway safety while reducing fuel consumption and other key operating costs. Our broad array of equipment and services support every type of railway operator, from major national and international railway systems, to short lines and high-speed urban transit networks.

Job Description

The ideal candidate will serve in a lead technical role in Design and development of embedded software used to control complex railway maintenance equipment and will participate in full-cycle development projects from requirements through delivery and subsequent field support for a variety of rail maintenance equipment and machinery.

Primary Responsibilities:
o Design and develop real time embedded machine control solutions for railway maintenance equipment including graphical user interfaces and machine I/O
o Apply communication and problem-solving skills to solve software issues related to the design, development, deployment, testing, and operation of systems as a team lead individual contributor both individually and within a dynamic team environment.
o Provide accurate estimates for assigned tasks, deliver the assigned task on time meeting specified functional expectations.
o Provide hands-on field support to test, commission, and troubleshoot machine functions.
o Perform other reasonably related tasks as assigned by management.
o Communicate project status through reports, presentations and design reviews.
o Meet deadlines to ensure projects are completed within acceptable time and cost targets.
o Willingness and ability to travel up to 15% of time.
o Other duties may apply as required.

Qualifications

Minimum requirements:
o Master’s/ Bachelor’s degree in Software, Electronic, Computer or other similar Engineering discipline
o Minimum 10 yrs. experience in design and development of embedded software used to control complex machinery
o Creation of embedded control code in C and C++
o Experience with Visual Studio and Windows CE
o CANBus network experience (SAE J1939 or CAN Open) and other fieldbus systems.
o Experience with configuration management tools such as SVN
o Must have ability to work autonomously with little direction and direct others.
o Must have excellent communication skills.

Preferred requirements:
o Experience with complex controls of heavy mobile equipment, including hydraulics and pneumatics
o Must be able to develop original algorithms, create code and write test cases
o Exposure to requirements management (Jama) and issue tracking (Jira)
o Robotics, device control and distributed I/O controls
o Support hydraulic and electrical controls engineering
o Experience with wired and wireless communication protocols
o Experience working as part of a multinational team

Essential Functions

o The ability to predictably and regularly attend work
o The ability to read and speak fluent English
o The ability to work cooperatively with others
o System installation may require the ability to lift at least 40 lbs, walk over uneven surfaces (gravel) for up to 1 mile and climb vertical ladders up to 10'.
o The ability to drive a typical automobile.
o The ability to deal politely with customers and coworkers
o The ability to perform several tasks at once
o The ability to stand/bend/stretch, etc.
