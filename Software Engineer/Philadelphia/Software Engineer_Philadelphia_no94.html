Full Job Description

Eastern Standard is looking for a seasoned Web Developer to join and lead our development team. The team lead is charged with being a strategically-minded, collaborative team member who can bring out the best in our development team by:
  • Applying best practice approaches to our development work
  • Providing mentorship, guidance, and professional growth to the development team
  • Coordinating with other departmental team leads to guide process improvements and deepen collaboration
  • Working on select projects and communicating directly with clients, as needed

The Role
You’ll provide direct supervision and leadership to the development team, and will be empowered to make decisions.

  • Provide mentorship to developers, both in terms of technical proficiency and career trajectory.
  • Conduct weekly one-on-one sessions with your direct reports.
  • Perform quarterly performance reviews.
  • Make recommendations around hiring, staffing, promotions, and resource allocation.

  • Coordinate directly with and report to the company partners to understand and guide company-wide initiatives.

You’ll set the standard for our development practice, and play an integral role in project architecture and implementation

  • Provide technical solutions to complex problems, and ensure that those solutions meet budgets, timelines, and functional requirements.
  • Enforce best practices around DevOps workflows, testing, and overall QA.
  • Work with both our in-house team and remote contractors to maximize efficiency and output
  • Consistently improve our internal workflows and processes, defining and leading internal initiatives in collaboration with other team leads

  • Write code and directly solve project problems when necessary (most of our work is built around open source CMS platforms; we also incorporate a lot of custom PHP, HTML, CSS/SCSS, and JavaScript.)

You’ll lead by example, with strong communication skills.
  • Communicate with internal project teams and clients clearly and effectively regarding technical requirements, limitations, risks, and proposed solutions.
  • Interface directly with clients, providing client education support to the project Producers.
  • Champion best practices for code structure and nomenclature.
  • Work in conjunction with other developers, providing development support, guidance, and professional growth.

  • Introduce new and emerging patterns and technologies into our workflow.

You’ll coordinate with other team leads to drive initiatives and improve processes.

  • Work closely with design and project management teams to define and improve the ways in which everyone works together.
  • Bring a constructive, ego-less attitude to the conversation, and build consensus around priorities.

  • Be involved in the sales process when needed, providing support for the creation of estimates and joining client pitches.

What You Bring to the Table:
  • Minimum of 7 years working professionally as a web developer
  • Minimum of 3 years leading a team of developers
  • Well-developed communication skills, with a strong sense of diplomacy and the ability to be perceived as firm but fair
  • The ability to balance technical solutions with practical business constraints
  • Extensive experience with object-oriented code in PHP and/or Ruby
  • Extensive experience with Javascript (including headless implementations)
  • Deep functional understanding of web requests (e.g., HTTP protocol/AJAX)
  • Strong knowledge of git/version control
  • High degree of competency with UNIX terminal/command line
  • Experience working on large-scale, custom website builds
  • Commitment to best practices and well-organized code
  • Experience building, testing, debugging, and troubleshooting MySQL queries, RESTFUL API integrations, and general PHP and JavaScript libraries/modules/plugins
  • Extensive experience with HTML and CSS (including SASS)
  • High degree of competency with compilation tools, such as grunt and gulp
  • Detail-oriented with a commitment to quality
  • Demonstrated ability to troubleshoot and creatively solve problems
