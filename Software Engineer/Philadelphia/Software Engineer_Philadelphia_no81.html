Full Job Description

The front-end developer focuses on creating the best possible user experience for Orion HiddenLevers product users. Front end developers will use a combination of HTML, CSS, javascript, and javascript frameworks to deliver rich user interfaces. Front end developers will also work with product management and designers to iteratively improve the user interface to help the products stand out from a design and user experience perspective.


Essential Functions of the Job

  • Builds new product features under the guidance of Orion’s product management.
  • Helps debug and fix HTML and CSS issues to improve the overall user experince.
  • Troubleshoots product issues and is prepared to help rapidly fix software defects.
  • Collaborates with Java and full stack developers to deploy new functionality based on HTML, CSS and Vue.js.
  • Works with product managements to perform rapid prototyping of different UX solutions, using both design tools and HTML/CSS.
  • Updates existing functionality based on CSS, HTML, and JQuery.


Skills Needed to Perform the Job

  • Knowledge of HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript including Vue.js or frameworks like Angular and React.
  • Understanding of user experience challenges and solutions of form factors ranging from big screen to desktop to mobile.
  • Understand and make edits to content and code embedded in web frameworks.
  • Ability to multi-task while maintaining careful attention to detail.
  • Ability to work effectively both individually and within a team environment.
  • Ability to work with a sense of urgency to meet deadlines and address competing priorities.
  • Proficient skills with Microsoft Office software including Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook.
  • Effective written, listening, and verbal communication skills.
  • Effective problem solving and organizational skills.


Education Needed to Perform the Job

  • Minimum of a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field preferred.
  • Orion Industry Certification must be obtained within 90 days of employment.


Experience Needed to Perform the Job

  • Minimum of 2 years of experience in javascript preferred.
  • Experience with user interaction design or user experience preferred.
