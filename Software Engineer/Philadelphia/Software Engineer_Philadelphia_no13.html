Associate Software Engineer

King of Prussia, Pennsylvania

Job ID: 550227BR Date posted: Feb. 15, 2021 Locations: King of Prussia, Pennsylvania Description: Does the thought of crafting state-of-the-art solutions for innovative products excite you? Come join the C4ISR group within Rotary Mission Systems (RMS) at Lockheed Martin as we revolutionize segment solutions for our Department of Defense customers. You will be building premiere solutions that support our customers.

We are seeking hardworking Software Engineers who love determining how to solve problems through the use of technology. This role will focus on performing software development and integration for the United States Air Force Operational Realtime Combat Analysis (ORCA) program. Working as an important part of an agile development team, on a day to day basis your role would be centered around designing and developing groundbreaking solutions utilizing the latest Microsoft stack in addition to the latest JavaScript frameworks. We seek a well-rounded software developer with a keen eye for detail and with proven skills in web application design, development, and test. The role will consist of coordination and task execution of a tiered web application development including GUI prototyping, external web service integration, development of new web services, design/development of logic layer, SQL DB integration, and IIS hosting. Having strong technical writing skills and an understanding of system and software engineering principles is important to know for this position. In addition, there will be ongoing opportunities to help design and develop various data management and visualization solutions, from requirements development and planning through full implementation, test & integration, deployment and O&M.
