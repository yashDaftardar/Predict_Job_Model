Job Summary

Responsible for leading a team of Software Development Engineers in the planning and designing of software and web applications. Manages projects from design to testing, including new programs, enhancements and modifications. Collaborates with Quality Assurance team to conduct testing. Has overall responsibility for developing/administering performance standards for organizational unit. Manages team which may include exempt and non-exempt employees. Provides subject matter guidance to employees as required. Develops processes and procedures to drive department efficiencies and assists in the development and meeting of departmental budget.

Job Description

We are seeking an Android Development Manager to join our Client Experiences team, which is responsible for creating mobile and TV-connected experiences that are used by millions of customers each and every day. On our team we value close collaboration with designers, testers, product managers, and other developers across the organization. While the primary responsibilities will include leading the people and technology on the Android development team, you will have opportunities to contribute to all aspects of product development. You are encouraged to pick up new tools and technologies quickly, be a great leader, and effectively adapt to changing business and customer demands. The ideal candidate will have experience leading teams that build native mobile applications which operate at scale.

The Role:

In this role you will be leading an outstanding group of junior and senior Android engineers, as well as managing the development efforts for Comcast’s entertainment applications on the Android platforms (including Amazon Kindle and Fire TV). This will include contributions at all stages of the development cycle from conceptualization, analysis, design and architecture, development, testing, deployment, and operational monitoring. It is critical that these applications are of the highest quality, fully accessible, and have the appropriate monitoring and logging in place to be able to accurately gauge the customer experience in production.

Attributes for Success:

  • Effective management of engineering career paths and goals

  • Strong understanding of Java and Kotlin

  • Experience creating mobile applications for a wide variety of devices and Android OS versions

  • Knowledge of Android libraries and frameworks such as RxJava and Dagger

  • Solid background in architectural styles and patterns (MVP, MVC, MVVM, or MVI) is preferred

  • Experience maintaining build processes and CI/CD pipelines

  • Involvement in all phases of the software development lifecycle: product specification, design, implementation, testing, release, and operations

  • A strong sense of ownership and responsibility for code quality that follows standards

  • A passion for learning and adapting to new technologies

Core Responsibilities:

  • Manages goal setting and career planning of existing team

  • Leads the staffing efforts when there are opportunities to expand the team

  • Designs new software, supports applications under development, and customizes current applications

  • Keeps current with technological developments within the industry. Monitors and evaluates competitive applications and products

  • Works with Quality Assurance team to determine if applications meet requirements

  • Consistently exercises independent judgment and discretion in matters of significance

  • Represents the Android development team during internal and external meetings

  • Effectively maintains code health, tech debt, and operational visibility on the Android platform

Employees at all levels are expected to:

  • Understand our Operating Principles; make them the guidelines for how you do your job.

  • Own the customer experience - think and act in ways that put our customers first, give them seamless digital options at every touchpoint, and make them promoters of our products and services.

  • Know your stuff - be enthusiastic learners, users and advocates of our game-changing technology, products and services, especially our digital tools and experiences.

  • Win as a team - make big things happen by working together and being open to new ideas.

  • Be an active part of the Net Promoter System - a way of working that brings more employee and customer feedback into the company - by joining huddles, making call backs and helping us elevate opportunities to do better for our customers.

  • Drive results and growth.

  • Respect and promote inclusion & diversity.

  • Do what's right for each other, our customers, investors and our communities.
