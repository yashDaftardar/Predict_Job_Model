POSITION SUMMARY:
The Engineering team, organized by product groups, is responsible for the architecture, design, development and operations of all systems and applications supporting FiveBelow’s Retail footprint. The systems/apps are a combination of enterprise solutions and custom software. Engineering Product groups at FiveBelow are aligned to the domain functions. Accordingly, we have Merchandising and Inventory Management, Stores and Digital, Logistics, BI and Data Platform, Ecommerce Engineering and Tools. The job description outlined below is for an Ecommerce Senior Engineer. We are in the process of building and modernizing our next generation Ecommerce platform, that powers all our digital experiences. The Senior Engineer will report to the Director of Ecommerce Engineering and will have responsibility for the design and development of our “Bottom of the Funnel” engineering group

We are looking for an energetic hands-on engineering leader who will grow and manage a global team of 5+ engineers, both onshore and offshore, and provide steady, experienced leadership as FiveBelow expands its footprint.

Job Responsibilities:
Develops code, scripts, and designs per business requirements.

Lead and Facilitate Agile rituals –daily standups, iteration planning, story huddles, retrospectives, creation of burn up chart’s inceptions, document technical work in technical stories using JIRA board, and estimate (using agile points/story sizing techniques) the work effort required to complete those stories.

Conducts code reviews

Good knowledge of AWS cloud services

Contributes to the development of the IT engineering strategy

Provides technical leadership, coaching and mentoring to on shore and offshore team members

Oversee/coordinate supporting technical work required with other Five Below IT teams, including infrastructure (e.g. for servers, network) and upstream and downstream system owners (e.g. warehouse management systems, order management systems, InfoSec, etc.)

Work with Five Below program managers to prioritize on business stories and to ensure technical execution risks & issues are captured and mitigated.

Collaborate and work with other Senior Engineers/Technical Managers/Application Engineering teams to ensure data platform capabilities meet the application requirements.

Oversee the work of developers and QAs to ensure that agile stories being played support the release plan defined by the product owner and are delivered with high quality.

Accountable for the team's impact; guide and mentor the onsite/offshore team to achieve a high level of technical productivity and reliability.

Qualifications:
5 years of engineering experience, and 2 years of technical leadership

Deep experience in developing applications with Ruby and Rails, preferably with React frontend

Strong command of the following AWS services: RDS, EC2 / ECS / Fargate, and S3

Solidus / Spree and /or Elixir experience is a plus

Retail / Ecommerce experience is a plus

Software delivery leveraging agile and lean practices at scale

Highest level of personal integrity, and the ability to professionally handle confidential matters and exude the appropriate level of judgment and maturity

Exceptionally self-motivated and directed

Dedication and commitment to top-quality service and to meeting customer expectations
