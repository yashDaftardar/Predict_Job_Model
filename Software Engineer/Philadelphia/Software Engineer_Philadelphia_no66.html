The Member of Engineering Staff (MES) /Tool Developer/Maintainer will perform software engineering development tasks including requirements analysis, design, implementation, and test/verification for assigned tools/applications. May be assigned to perform other software engineering development or support tasks as necessary to support the team workload.
Basic Qualifications:
  • BS degree in Computer Science, or Computer/Software Engineering or equivalent.
  • Linux experience
  • Professional level experience in a modern programming languages, databases, and scripting
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a Security Clearance at the SECRET level
Desired Skills:
  • Experience with development and support of Jira, Git, and GitLab
  • Professional level experience in scripting including Perl, Python, UNIX shell
  • ClearCase/ClearQuest experience
  • DOORS experience
  • Solaris and Windows experience is a plus
  • Knowledge of database structures and queries (e.g., MySQL)
  • Static code analysis experience including Fortify and Coverity
  • Experience with Web Application frameworks/languages/servers (Tomcat, HTML/JSON/JS/JSP/CSS, JERSEY/REST)
  • Capable of working in a fast paced environment, supporting multiple projects and tool products.
  • Experience in software engineering processes and tool/application development/maintenance with particular emphasis on baselining requirements, design implementation and verification.
  • Experience in Agile/team work environment leveraging Atlassian tools set (Jira, Confluence, etc.)
  • Experience in process and workflow automation.
  • Demonstrated interest and ability to learn new software development tools and technologies and successfully leverage those to successfully complete assigned project in a timely manner.
  • Demonstrated ability to communicate clearly and effectively both verbally and in writing.
  • Ability to effectively work in a team environment and to work independently, cooperatively and collaboratively with peers and seniors.
  • Strong desire to learn and keep up with technology.
  • Process discipline with strong focus on developing and producing high quality products.
  • Customer service oriented to ensure the products satisfy customer requirements and expectations.
  • Ability to effectively work in a virtual team environment.
  • Strong process focus with ability to recognize, communicate and implement opportunities for improvement or process automation.
