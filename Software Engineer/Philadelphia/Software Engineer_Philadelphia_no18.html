The Office of Innovation and Technology (OIT) oversees most major technology projects for the City of Philadelphia (CoP), ensures continuity of the City's technology operations, and finds new ways to help the public interact meaningfully with technology. OIT uses technology to help City employees do their work better and helps residents get what they need from municipal government. The Streets Department IT unit is responsible for supporting all IT systems within the Streets Department.

The Philadelphia Water Department (PWD) is an agency responsible for managing the water, wastewater and storm water infrastructure for the City of Philadelphia. Philadelphia Water Department employees take pride in providing safe water for residents to drink, supply water for industry, and protect the environment and the region’s water sources. The PWD IS&T Application Team is responsible for building software applications to streamline PWD operations and support its core business. The successful candidate will collaborate with other team members to deliver custom IT solutions to meet department needs.


Job Description


The primary responsibility of this position is to provide application development for the Philadelphia Water Department, City of Philadelphia using Microsoft .Net framework. The position would provide the candidate with a wide range of opportunities in software engineering processes.

This includes designing and implementing custom software solutions and system enhancements to optimize the operations in the existing/new systems to support PWD business processes; designing multi-tier business logic-based systems using design patterns to solve common software design problems; Investigating, analyzing and documenting reported defects; Performing maintenance programming and correction of identified defects; Creating custom queries and reports for managers, city, state and federal agencies.

Essential Functions

  • Responsible for the development of applications (web and otherwise) in .NET to support PWD Business processes.
  • Responsible for upgrading legacy applications to newer technologies and comply with enterprise security policies and standards.
  • Responsible for the design of relational databases including organizing the information in tables, setting up primary keys and foreign keys and table relationships, create views, functions and stored procedures.
  • Develop Mobile GIS enabled web applications using newer technologies such as MVC, Bootstrap, Angular 9+ and ESRI framework.
  • Develop and maintain interfaces to COTS solutions such as Maximo, Cityworks and CIPIT.
  • Assists in working with clients to develop technical requirements to meet their business needs as well as with system support staff to identify technical requirements, including, connectivity, resources and other platform requirements.
  • Help to coordinate the implementation of short- and long-term projects, objectives, policies and procedures for the production and maintenance of all application development related issues.
  • Follow SDLC methodology including requirements gathering, solution architecture, coding, and testing.
  • Follow best practices and industry standards to develop .NET applications.
  • Collaborate and assist other team members in all the software development cycles to ensure adherence with the design requirements and the business needs.
  • Proactively identify application inefficiencies and propose solutions.
  • Identify, create, and maintain development frameworks and code libraries. Create prototypes and functional specifications for application development projects.
  • Other duties as assigned.
