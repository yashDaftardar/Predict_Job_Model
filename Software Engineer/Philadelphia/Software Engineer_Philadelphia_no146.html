Full Job Description

This position is part of the team responsible for supporting building related technology at Brandywine. This role will act as corporate development and support to employees and vendors handling integrations and analytics. Candidates must be eager to pick up new, unfamiliar technology. The position reports to the Senior Director of Digital Engineering at the corporate headquarters.

Highlights of primary responsibilities include :

Data Integrations 50%

  • Work with team members to write scripts to automate operations
  • Enable and research APIs or Webhooks to pull data from systems for reporting and analytics
  • Work with existing data accessible via ODBC, Native SQL, JSON, XML, CSV exports
  • Extract and normalize data from devices and systems like EMS/BMS, Meters, Access Control, and sensors
  • Write scripts to push data to cloud platforms and services

IOT / Embedded systems 30%

  • Identify and program microcontrollers and sensors
  • Troubleshoot devices that may be offline or not correctly reporting data. Digital and analog inputs
  • Educate and enforce security and technology policies onto vendors and property management
  • Install micro sensors and devices to acquire additional data that will benefit operations or leasing
  • Work with low cost sensors and controllers to determine future use cases

Building Technology Review and Research 20%

  • Understand and study available building technology for Access Control, HVAC, Cameras, Sensors, etc.
  • Review vendor proposals for existing building technology upgrades
Qualifications

2+ years experience with at least some of the following technologies:

    • Digital and Analog sensors
    • Raspberry PI, ESP8266, ESP32 or similar IOT micro controllers
    • RS232/RS485 data extraction
    • Python, Powershell, etc.
    • SQL or Cloud Data Warehouses

Desired:

    • Bacnet and Modbus
    • Access Control, BMS, EMS. Siemens, JCI, Tridium, AutomatedLogic, Distech, Honeywell
    • MQTT or other similar data pushing
    • Energy and water metering knowledge
    • Dashboard and Analytics

  • Works effectively with co-workers, with employees in remote locations, and with vendors
  • Good ability to organize and produce quality work, within required specifications, to meet rigid timelines
  • Demonstrated ability to perform all required job duties under minimal supervision
  • Demonstrated initiative as a proactive self-starter, willing to go beyond specific job functions to ensure business goals are achieved and/or exceeded
  • Demonstrated ability to manage multiple assigned tasks
  • Ability and willingness to respond to emergencies during off-hours
  • Ability to travel to remote locations for troubleshooting and installs
  • Ability to lift 30lbs and use ladders
