Description/Job Summary

The Server Engineer is responsible for supporting a wide variety of RHD's server technologies and development of technologies that enable easy deployment, configuration, and management of desktop technologies throughout the enterprise. The server engineer is additionally expected to assist in the development of solutions for customer infrastructures including engineering and implementation. Provides problem investigation, diagnostic testing and resolution of systems, hardware and software.


Resources for Human Development is an equal opportunity employer. All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, age, religion, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, national origin, genetic information, veteran, or disability status.


Responsibilities/Duties

  • Complete configuration, installation, and support of servers in a Microsoft Windows environment - both physical hardware and virtual.
  • Manage Microsoft Windows Server operating systems and all components/services including AD, DNS, DFS, NTFS, DHCP, Print Services, IIS, FTP, TCP/IP, GPO deployment, and others.
  • Configuration, installation, maintenance and support of RHD's virtual (VMWare) environment.
  • Develops solutions for new site implementations in collaboration with the Network Engineer and Technical Support Services Team.
  • Provide 2nd level support for active directory, integrated systems, and assist the network engineer with the development of branch-office infrastructure
  • As needed, provide 2nd level support for issues related to desktop operating systems.
  • Provide support for server operating systems, Active Directory, and integrated services like OKTA.
  • Work with 3rd party vendors to quickly and accurately deploy base configurations to desktops deployed throughout the enterprise.
  • Develop and maintain the site server implementation process, deploying server technologies both to new and existing sites and programs throughout the enterprise.
  • Monitor servers using various tools such as PRTG and others.
  • Utilizing best practices, establish protocols for the support, implementation, and configuration of server and desktop technologies.
  • Maintain and implement SSL Security Certificates on all web-facing servers

Required Experience

  • Minimum 5 years of experience in systems administration, desktop systems configuration management, and systems configuration.
  • Minimum 3-5 years of experience in Enterprise Windows Server 2008/2012 in an engineering roll.
  • 3 plus years of VMWare ESXi and VCenter in an engineering roll.
  • Working familiarity with SCCM, WSUS, MDT, and Network Policy Service (RADIUS).

Required Education

  • Bachelor's Degree in MIS/IT, or Business. MCSE, or other certification plus relative experience may be considered a substitute for a Bachelor's degree

Required Qualifications

  • Ability to take rotational on-call responsibilities that include nights, weekends and holidays.
  • VMware Certified Professional, and MCSE icertifications are a plus.
  • Experience with Cloud Environments like AWS and Azure is a plus
  • Knowledge of Exchange/Office 365 is a plus
  • Experience with Avamar is a plus.
  • Must be able to work at Central Office/Data Center and CoLo
  • Ability to lift equipment up to 50lbs.

Program Summary

Resources for Human Development has a technological footprint that extends to 14 States in the US and over 100 site-to-site VPN connections. RHD's data center is located at the central office, but also includes a COLO site for replication and a DR site. Our team is small and although each team member brings their own specific skill set to the team, no role is siloed. The correct candidate for this position will have a desire to learn new technologies as well as be self-motivated and have impeccable troubleshooting skills.
