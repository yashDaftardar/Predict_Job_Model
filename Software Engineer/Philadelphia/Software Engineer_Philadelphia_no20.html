SOFTWARE ENGINEER

Position Overview

We are looking for a Software Engineer to join our dynamic team! Software Engineers are highly driven to own features and deliver on products, while learning the latest languages, frameworks, tooling, and processes. The person must be eager to engage technical teams, listen and learn from customers to establish understanding of product direction and subsequently deliver on target. Ideal candidates are passionate about their craft and have taken the initiative to develop and ship products/projects.

This is a full-time, salaried position with benefits. The successful candidate will be expected to work out of our Philadelphia office with occasional client visits in the Greater Philadelphia Area.

Areas of Responsibility

  • Developing functional and complete software products on web and mobile platforms
  • Understanding customer requirements and effectively communicating with customers
  • Communicating actively with development team
  • Thinking independently and utilizing available resources
  • Learning as much as possible

Job Qualifications

  • Several years of work experience in the field of software development
  • Demonstrated proficiency in full stack web/mobile technologies
    • e.g., Python, Angular, React Native, Ruby/Rails, C#/.NET
  • Understanding of development tools, compilers, source control, debuggers, etc.
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills
  • B.S. degree in Computer Science or a related field preferred

Benefits of Working at Arcweb

  • Team-oriented, collaborative environment encouraging continuous learning and ambition
  • Competitive salary, 401k, and health benefits (medical, vision, dental)
  • A Macbook will be provided to you for your business activities
  • Flexible work schedule
  • Basic membership to Indy Hall available, if desired
  • Catered Friday lunches
