Full Job Description

Hamilton Lane is looking to expand their team to satisfy the needs of our growing client base. Hamilton Lane is built on collaboration, teamwork and integrity. Our employees pursue excellence and always strive to do the right thing. We invest in our employees, clients and partner relationships, as well as, in the technology and resources necessary to remain competitive, working in a competitive environment that inspires innovation.

Hamilton Lane is seeking a Software Developer to support the next phase of growth for its Cobalt for Limited Partners technology solution ( www.cobaltlp.com ). This is an exciting opportunity to join an entrepreneurial team set within a successful, supportive, and innovative market leader. At Hamilton Lane, we are dedicated to delivering technology-driven solutions to our clients, and you will be working on a technology product that is growing rapidly.

We are looking for help across our entire technology stack. Skill, aptitude, drive to learn, and cultural fit are valued above experience in any individual technologies or languages. Your energy and drive to tackle new challenges will meet a high degree of autonomy in a collaborative team environment. If you have knowledge in any of the following disciplines, you will be ahead of the curve:

Relevant Technical Disciplines:

  • Python: Flask, SQLAlchemy, Alembic, Gunicorn
  • Databases: SQL, Postgres, ORMs
  • JavaScript: React, ES6, JQuery, Knockout, RequireJS, PagerJS, Webpack and tons of other libraries
  • AWS: EC2, RDS, VPC, Cloud Formation
  • Keyed storage: Mongo, Redis
  • CSS: SCSS
  • Dev tools: Git, Vagrant, Fabric
  • DevOps
  • Web services and web API development (REST, SOAP, JSON)

Key Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with other developers, product owners and managers, and business stakeholders to craft new product features and improvements for our platform
  • Operate with a large degree of independence, and with the support of our senior technology leaders
  • Bring projects from initial concept through all cycles of development: from project definition to development, debugging, initial release and subsequent iteration
  • Build enterprise level solutions, ensuring scalability, supportability and maintainability
  • Operate with integrity and work collaboratively with your peers in an open, supportive environment

Qualifications

  • Bachelor of Science degree or higher in Computer Science or similar technical discipline, or equivalent work experience
  • 3+ years experience as a Software Developer, Software Engineer or similar role utilizing open source technologies
  • Strong experience with Python and or other object-oriented languages
  • Comfort with full stack software development, including computationally intense back-end development and service design, data storage and access models, and modern web development frameworks
  • Understanding of AWS and its various services: EC2, RDS, VPC, Cloud Formation
  • Experience in a SaaS based product and comfort working effectively in a corporate setting is desired
  • Proclivity towards refactoring and continuously improving object-oriented code through Agile development methods
  • Strong software engineering fundamentals including object-oriented design, data structures, testable code, and algorithms
  • B2B financial services or alternative asset management experience is a plus
  • The ability to work in the US without immigration support

We offer a competitive salary, annual discretionary bonus and a comprehensive benefits package which includes: Medical, Prescription, Dental, Paid Time Off, 401k plan, Life and Disability Insurances, Tuition Reimbursement, Employee Stock Purchase Program, Health Club Reimbursement and Flexible Spending Accounts.
