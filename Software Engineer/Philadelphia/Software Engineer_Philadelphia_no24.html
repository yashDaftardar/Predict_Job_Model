Company Summary:
NMS Labs is the nation's leading independent provider of professional laboratory testing services, specializing in clinical toxicology, forensic science, and other vitally important areas of esoteric testing and expert services. We employ more than 360 highly trained professionals who work closely with leading healthcare providers, medical researchers, and various members of the criminal and civil justice system to provide integrated testing and consultative services.
NMS Labs is an ISO accredited independent provider of clinical and forensic toxicology, endocrinology and criminalistics laboratory services for the health care and forensic sciences communities. Since 1970, we have built a reputation as a provider of innovative, first-to-market and hard-to-find testing that has evolved to a broad menu of over 2,500 laboratory tests.
Summary
The Software Engineer reports to the Manager of Application Development or designee. The software engineer is responsible for developing and maintaining solutions for our business, client portal and all custom developed software in support of the business or our customers. The Software Engineer is hands on in many different areas of technology. The Software Engineer designs/implements/maintains our applications from the backend (DBA) to the frontend using modern web development (MVC/Bootstrap/JQuery). The Software Engineer also is involved from the hardware (VMs) to the OS (System Admin/Security). A team, comprising of Development/Infrastructure members, meets bi-weekly to discuss technology to continually provide value to the business.
Education and Experience:

  • Associates degree or higher in computer or science related field, and up to 3 years overall experience
  • Experience in programming using an object-oriented language (OOL)
  • Demonstrated knowledge of OOL concepts and development practices.
  • 0-2 years C#/Java or other object-oriented programming experience
  • 0-2 years SQL programming experience

Major Duties and Responsibilities for Software Engineer I

  • Works with senior staff to collect specifications and user requirements
  • Writes programs from technical specifications
  • Creates technical documentation (code comments) for newly rewritten and legacy code
  • Develops and execute unit test plans
  • Analyzes problems and requirements
  • Implements solutions for the lab and our customers
  • LIMS (Horizon/StarLIMS)
  • Instrument Interfaces
  • ECM (OnBase)
  • Customer Portal
  • Develops and execute unit and system integration test plans
  • Assist in support IT systems (IIS, Oracle, MS SQL and OSes)
  • Provide 24x7 support of the NMS Systems ensuring system availability in accordance with NMS SOP’s. (On Call rotation)
  • Maintains regular and reliable attendance
  • Other duties as assigned

Knowledge, Skills & Abilities

  • Demonstrated ability to complete complex programming projects
  • Knowledge of OOL concepts and development practices
  • Demonstrated experience with SDLC (software development life cycle) including project planning, systems analysis, systems design, implementation, integration and unit testing, software installation and deployment.
  • Database experience with Oracle PL/SQL or Transactional SQL
  • Knowledge of Windows and Linux Operating Systems, Oracle, SQL Server, Laboratory Information Management System, Document Management Systems, Enterprise Reporting, Source Control (SVN, Git)
  • Demonstrates a strong understanding of object-oriented analysis and design methods
  • Possess the ability to consistently deliver on defined dates
  • Effective verbal and written communication skills
  • Able to organize and lead technical discussions
  • Able to multitask and organize time to work on multiple projects
  • Exhibits professional and effective interpersonal skills
  • Comprehends regulations and SOPs and apply them to work operations

Physical Demands

  • Moving from one work location to another to sit and stand, sometimes for extended periods of time.
