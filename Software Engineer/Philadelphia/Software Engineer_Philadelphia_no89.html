Overview:

Wilmington Trust Wealth Management Technology supports the technology related to the client lifecycle of M+T’s Wealth Management business: From Prospect Management over Planning to Private Banking, Trust and Investment Management; then to Client Onboarding and Service Provisioning and finally Compliance and Risk Management. The technology stack addresses the data needs of the Wealth Management business, a significant investment in a SOA middleware and a Web and Mobile based UI infrastructure. The team maintains both – inhouse written applications and vendor provided functionality.

Scope of Responsibilities:

You will need to be effective in working autonomously and in teams on high level systems analysis and technical phases of development. The position interacts with senior management, other technology personnel, clients and vendors.

Tech Stack:

  • SQL (writing stored procedures, scheduling tasks, creating job dependencies & supporting batch work)
  • C#, .Net
  • CICD pipelines
  • 3rd Party Trading Applications such as Charles River, Longview, & Perform.
  • Web APIs: SOAP and RESTful

The successful candidate will have:

  • Minimum of an Associate degree and 6 years’ systems analysis/application development experience, or in lieu of a degree, a combined minimum of 8 years’ higher education and/or work experience
  • Worked on multiple projects involving complex systems analysis, design and complete application development
  • Strong SQL experience including writing stored procedures, scheduling tasks, creating job dependencies & supporting batch work.
  • Experience with Agile Methodology
  • An ability to build out products & products enhancements from idea through to launch
  • Skills to collaborate with the entire team and customers on feature requirements and prioritization
  • A team player mindset with an ability to thrive and effectively communicate in a fast-paced, constantly evolving environment
