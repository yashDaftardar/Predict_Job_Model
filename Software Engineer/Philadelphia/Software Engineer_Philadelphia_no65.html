Full Job Description

CTDI is a large scale Engineering, Repair, and Logistics company that services the country’s largest wireless telecommunications providers, as well as the largest High-speed Internet & Cable providers. CTDI has around 9,000 FT US Employees in over 58 world class US Operations. CTDI also has a global footprint with more than 16,500 employees worldwide.

CTDI is looking for multiple Entry-Level to Junior Electrical Engineers to join our corporate Engineering team and will work as part of a dynamic team of Mechanical, Software, and Robotics Engineers, in a fast-paced environment with an ever-changing set of businesses and facilities to support.

Responsibilities include:

  • Build, test, debug and develop C#, JTAG, CLI or LINUX applications.
  • Develop automated test and repair capabilities
  • Analyze technical requirements and effort associated with test systems and products.
  • Generate engineering documents, including schematics, diagnostics, flowcharts, and project status reports


Required Skills
  • Must have a combination of Software Engineering and Electrical Engineers skills.
  • Understanding of motherboard architecture
  • Understanding of analog, digital, and microprocessor circuits.
  • Understanding of audio, video, and RF circuits.
  • Ability to troubleshoot circuits.
  • Strong organizational and follow-up skills.
  • Excellent analytical, written, and verbal communication skills.
  • The ability to manage multiple tasks.
  • Ability to interact professionally with the customer in written and verbal communications


Required Experience
  • Bachelors Degree with Majors or Minors in Electrical Engineering OR Computer Engineering OR both, is a must.
  • This is an entry-level to junior-level role so 0 to 3 years’ of experience with Software and/or Electrical systems is good.
  • Academic projects involving Software and/or Electrical systems is acceptable if you have no work-experience.
  • Programming skills in C#, C, C++, or similar is a must. Academic or Professional programming knowledge is acceptable.

Nice-To-Have Skills:

  • Telecommunications technical experience and overall understanding
  • Proficiency in MSOffice (Word, Excel, Access, and Project), report preparation, and data analysis
  • Experience with Test/Manufacturing/Electrical equipment.
  • Knowledge of LEAN is helpful.
