Job Description Summary
Penn Engineering's world-acclaimed faculty, state-of-the-art research laboratories and highly interdisciplinary curricula offer an experience that is unparalleled. Innovation and technology drive every program and transform the fundamentals of what future engineers are learning. http://www.seas.upenn.edu/

Professor Dan Roth's Cognitive Computation Group ( http://cogcomp.org ) is looking for a Research Software Engineer to join the group and manage the development of its Machine Learning-based Natural Language Understanding and Information Extraction tools.

Job Description

The Research Software Engineer in the Cognitive Computation Lab will manage the development of its Machine Learning-based Natural Language Understanding and Information Extraction tools. Interact and collaborate with a large team of researchers at multiple levels, helping them to develop and deliver high quality software products; may have opportunities to publish. Manage a small team of part-time students, working as software engineers. Software development includes Python and Deep Learning development environments, using software engineering methodologies and tools.

Qualifications:
Master's degree in Computer Science or related field with experience in NLP or an equivalent combination of education and experience; broad expertise in software development (including Python and Deep Learning development environments) and software engineering methodologies and tools; knowledge of PHP and MySQL; and a passion for helping researchers to develop and deliver high quality software products.
