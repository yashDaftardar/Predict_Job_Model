Automation engineers

AIV Solutions
Township of Mount Laurel, NJ

AIV Solutions is looking for motivated automation engineers who seek to make a difference in the industries we service. Our team has extensive experience and is driven by the success of our customers.

Responsibilities

  • Apply engineering principles and practices in the design of automation software and process control systems as required.
  • Prepare Functional Requirements, Detail Design Specifications, and Software Test Plans.
  • Program various control system platforms, such as PLC (e.g., Rockwell, Modicon, Siemens), DCS (e.g., ABB 800xA, ABB MOD300, Emerson DeltaV), and/or SCADA (e.g., Wonderware, Intellution, Rockwell Automation).
  • Travel, as needed, in order to perform functions such as construction site inspection, information gathering, commissioning and/or client meetings.

Requirements

  • BS degree in chemical or electrical engineering or related field with appropriate experience.
  • Experience with one or more of the above control system platforms. Emerson DeltaV and/or ABB 800xA is preferred.
  • Ability to perform engineering assignments commensurate with number of year's experience.
  • Knowledge of practices applied to the preparation of hardware/software specifications and related contract documents.
  • Effective oral and written communication skills.
  • Knowledge of cGMPs as they apply to the Pharmaceutical Industry is a plus.
  • Knowledge of industry-standards such as IEEE, ISA, GAMP, and 21 CFR Part 11 is a plus.

Benefits

  • Comprehensive Medical and Dental Plan for Employee and Family
  • 401(k) Plan
  • Basic Life Insurance
  • Long Term / Short Term disability insurance
  • Attractive Bonus Program
  • Relocation Allowances
