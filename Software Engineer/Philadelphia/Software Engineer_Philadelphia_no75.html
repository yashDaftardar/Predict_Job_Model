Job Summary

MachineQ, a Comcast company, is scaling the platform and products that deliver advanced Internet of Things (IoT) services. We are looking for a result driven, self-directed Principal Engineer with a strong background in high volume low latency data processing, cloud solutions for big data problems, and product delivery from conception to production. Reporting to the Director of Product Development Engineering, you will thrive in a fast-paced, agile environment and support an engineering culture where innovation is balanced with predictable delivery and operational excellence.

Job Description

As a Principal Engineer, you will bring your technical and engineering best practices to ensure the continued success of our SaaS IoT platform. Serve in the role of guiding your fellow engineers through design, development, and support of our platform. This role will partner closely with multiple product engineering groups within the organization and will communicate regularly with an incredible team of infrastructure, site reliability (SRE), and DevOps engineers.

Core Responsibilities

  • Lead the development of new features for our SaaS platform.
  • Set the technical direction for the team and anticipate scaling-related challenges via thoughtful planning, modeling, and load testing.
  • Assist in the design and implementation of long term strategic technical initiatives to meet business, product growth targets, and SLA targets.
  • Contribute to the architecture of MachineQ products by partnering with product teams to ensure the systems can meet security (DevSecOps), availability, and performance targets.
  • Assist with the development, monitoring, tracking, and analysis application performance metrics.
  • Engagement in complex production issues to minimize impact to customers.
  • Decompose business requirements into small technical requirements
  • Participate in rotating on-call production support.
  • Train junior Software Development Engineers on internally developed software applications.
  • Oversees the researching, writing and editing of documentation and technical requirements, including software designs, evaluation plans, test results, technical manuals and formal recommendations and reports.
  • Keeps current with technological developments within the industry. Monitors and evaluates competitive applications and products. Reviews literature, patents and current practices relevant to the solution of assigned projects.
  • Assists with technical leadership throughout the design process and assists in guidance with regards to practices, procedures and techniques. Serves as a guide mentor for Software Development Engineers.
  • Presents and defends architectural, design and technical choices to internal and external audiences.
  • Regular, consistent and punctual attendance. Must be able to work nights and weekends, variable schedule(s) and overtime as necessary.
  • Other duties and responsibilities as assigned.

Requirements

  • BS (or higher) in Computer Science or related technical field involving coding (e.g., physics or mathematics), or equivalent technical experience.
  • Experience with algorithms, data structures, complexity analysis and software design.
  • Demonstrated experience scaling customer facing SaaS applications from 100s per second to 1000s per second to 10000s per second.
  • Minimum of 5 years of working with large datasets.
  • Experience building and deploying distributed and robust cloud native (Azure, AWS, GCP) applications and infrastructure.
  • Experience in one or more of the following: Go, C, Python, Erlang.
  • Demonstrated experience with containerization and microservices solutions (e.g Kubernetes, Azure Kubernetes Service, Amazon EKS, Amazon Farsgate).
  • Experience operating and scaling structured relational databases (e.g. PostgreSQL, Azure Database for PostgreSQL, Amazon RDS), loosely structured data stores (e.g. Redis, DynamoDB), and time series databases.
  • Strong Linux background - Hands on with working in Linux environment. Should be well versed with the Linux OS, PKI Certificate management, SSH Key management.
  • Experience developing with and scaling event streaming platforms (e.g. Apache Kafka, Azure Event Hub, AWS Kinesis/SQS).
  • Experience developing with queueing infrastructure (e.g. RabbitMQ, Azure Service Bus, Amazon SQS).
