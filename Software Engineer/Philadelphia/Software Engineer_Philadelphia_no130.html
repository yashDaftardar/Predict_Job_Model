Company Details:

Berkley Technology Services (BTS) is a dynamic company committed to providing world class IT services. We offer a unique culture, enabling our team members to be on the cutting edge of technology while delivering high quality solutions. Our functions include working with various third parties to develop, integrate, and support insurance systems of WRBC's operating units. BTS strives to provide these functions in a holistic manner including helpdesk support, system connectivity, and operational support. Additional responsibilities include coordinating communications regarding best practices in the use of our supported systems and researching new technology. BTS is constantly growing and expanding to meet the changing demands of one of the most successful insurance organizations in the world.

Responsibilities:
  • Work with various teams and stakeholders to create and maintain digital experiences for multiple insurance brands.
  • Evaluate new technology and form data-based opinions to ensure continued high level product offerings.
  • Use an understanding of digital customers’ needs to drive digital product decisions.
  • Build and maintain digital interfaces to core systems.
  • Estimate units of work, participates in design discussion and code review.
Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or equivalent combination of education and experience
  • 2-4 years’ experience using JS frameworks and libraries including React, Angular, or Vue
  • 2-4 years working in front end development using HTML5, CSS, and JS
  • 2-4 years’ experience with CSS frameworks including Bootstrap, Foundation, or Semantic UI
  • 1-3 years’ experience with CSS preprocessors and RESTful services and APIs
  • 1-3 years’ experience with front end tooling including Grunt, webpack, npm, or Bower.
  • 1-3 years’ experience implementing responsive design and cross-browser compatibility
  • Excellent communication skills with strong customer service commitment

Preferred qualifications:

  • Experience building content to be reused across multiple experiences
  • Experienced in leveraging CMS systems across multiple brands
  • Experience with Node, Express, Apollo, GraphQL
