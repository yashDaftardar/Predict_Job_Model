Overview:
JBT is a leader in the automated, self-guided vehicle systems to the automotive, food & beverage, hospital and consumer product manufacturing industries JBT has offices in more than 100 countries all over the world. We are growing both organically and by acquisitions and are continuously looking for new employees that want to grow with us! We offer you the growth potential of a company with a developing technology that is unique in its field, plus a collaborative working environment where your individuality, imagination and talent will be recognized and rewarded.
We are looking for a Sofware Engineer in our Chalfont, PA location to provide technical and software solutions for systems that control a fleet of automated guided vehicles.
Responsibilities:
  • Designs and develops software solutions that control a fleet of automated guided vehicles.
  • Develops and implements software within the Microsoft Windows development environment using JScript, C#, ASP.Net, XML, and a variety of ODBC/ADO compliant database packages and SQL Server.
  • Completes assignments as part of a multidisciplinary project team.
  • Travels, as needed, to customer sites.
Qualifications:
  • Bachelor's degree (B. S.) in Computer Science or Engineering.
  • 3-5 years related experience desired.
  • Proficiency in SQL programming required.
  • Proficiency in JScript and/or Java Script required.
  • Knowledge of C# preferred.
  • Prior programming experience required.
  • Experience with ASP.Net a plus.
  • Excellent communication skills.
  • Able to travel up to 25% (domestic and international).
  • Valid driver’s license required.

What we offer


  • The ability to work in an established international and fast growing company with great opportunities for personal development.
  • A work environment where we value individual initiatives.
  • Continuous development and improvement, and always together as a team.
  • Company culture of Relentless Continuous Improvement (RCI).
  • Competitive salary and benefits – medical, dental, vision, life insurance, and disability.
  • Benefits begin your first day of employment.
  • Education reimbursement benefits.
  • Robust retirement benefits with company match.
  • Vacation, sick, and holiday paid time-off.
