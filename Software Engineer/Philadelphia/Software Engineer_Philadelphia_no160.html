Full Job Description

ASI is seeking a highly motivated Front-End Engineer to join our growing team. Our Front-End Engineers work closely with members of the product development team to build the user experiences of our enterprise web applications. You will get to work in a challenging and cutting-edge environment where you will be encouraged to refine your skills, share your ideas, and test your creative boundaries.
Essential Functions:

  • Working in an agile project team to ensure that high quality delivery is at the heart of the development process.
  • Participating in code reviews and help maintaining high code-quality standards, including but not limited to adherence to architectural, security and scalability standards.
  • Developing and maintaining a component library.
  • Researching, engineering, and experimenting with new ways to improve the developer experience.
  • Being an advocate of automation, test driven development, and CI/CD.

Requirements:

  • Bachelors degree in Computer Science or related field required.
  • In-depth knowledge of the Angular framework and working on enterprise Angular web applications.
  • Robust knowledge of TypeScript, HTML, CSS and writing well-structured code.
  • Has an eye for design, and is opinionated about how a UI looks and feels.
  • Good understanding of web security (OWASP top 10) and web accessibility (W3C).
  • Experience with unit testing frameworks (Jest/Jasmine).
  • Highly motivated and with a desire to make an impact.
  • Productive when working either collaboratively or independently.
