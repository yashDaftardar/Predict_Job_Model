Full Job Description

At Exyn Technologies, we are excited about the future of aerial robotics and autonomy. Creating that future requires great minds, and we are looking for dedicated and passionate people to be a part of our growing, talented team.

We are looking for an experienced software engineer to work with a dynamic, multi-disciplinary team to implement state of the art algorithms derived from world-class research. You will be responsible for software design and implementation. You will work closely with roboticist to turn prototypes into maintainable and robust software. Your work will be critical to the efficiency, modularity and scalability of our current and future robots.

In this role, you will be responsible for

  • Working with our robotics team to develop well-written scalable code from our core autonomy algorithms
  • Optimizing existing code for deployment on resource-constrained devices and GPUs
  • Developing software architecture for ExynAI that will improve our engineers & customers ability to use our products
  • Participating in agile software development to implement customer-driven features

We are Looking For

  • 3 years of experience using C++ 11 or newer in an industrial setting
  • Fundamentals of data-oriented software development
  • Experience with multi-threaded and asynchronous programming
  • Experience with systems design & engineering, algorithm design & development
  • Experience with standard development practices, including version control, testing, code checking, and code review
  • Experience with template programming
  • B.S. in Computer Science or related field

We would also like to hear if you have:

  • Experience with robotics
  • Experience with aerial systems in general or drones
  • Experience with hardware and software testing + QA
  • Testing automation expertise
