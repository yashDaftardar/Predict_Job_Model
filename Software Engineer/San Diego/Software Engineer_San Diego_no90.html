5G RF EMBEDDED SOFTWARE ENGINEER

Sigma Connectivity
San Diego, CA

JOB DESCRIPTION

We are seeking a 5G RF Embedded Software Engineer in San Diego!

In this role you will implement C/C++ embedded drivers for cutting edge RF and modem hardware technology for 5G. The successful candidate will have a proven experience commercializing cellular modem products in the North American market. You will participate in the product development life-cycle from bring-up, optimization, verification and commercialization. This role is is highly geared towards Modem 4G/5G power and thermal optimization on Qualcomm’s cutting edge Modem technology. You will preform mmWave bring up and factory support tasks, such as provisioning, fusing, as well as Operator bug fixes.

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS

  • Minimum Bachelor’s in computer science or equal
  • Strong, demonstrable coding skills in C and C++ or Java
  • Experience with RF test equipment measurements for power/thermal or spectrum or waveform quality.
  • Multiple years’ experience in cellular software development, debugging, and optimization
  • Understanding of North American Cellular carrier requirements
  • Experience with debugging embedded software using an in-circuit emulator (Lauterbach Trace32 JTAG) for an ARM processor
  • Experience in wireless modem or smartphone devices certification in association with 3G/4G/5G
  • Experience with cellphone products development based on Qualcomm Snapdragon 4G/5G modem implementation is preferred
  • mmWave bring up experience
  • Good understanding of CA Band combination
  • Strong documentation and writing skills

PREFERRED ADDITIONAL SKILLS, PERSONAL TRAITS AND EXPERIENCE

  • Team player
  • Proactiveness in collaboration and execution
  • Flexibility to switch technical context
  • Willingness to learn new methods / tools / processes
  • Ability to prioritize and multitask
  • Strong problem solving and critical thinking skills
  • Strong passion for creativity and solving cutting edge industry problems
