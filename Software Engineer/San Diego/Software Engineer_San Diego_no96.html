Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Staff DevSecOps Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. The selected candidate will be working with the latest technology to modernize software production across multiple programs.

Essential Functions:
  • Standup of large scale software factory used by software teams
  • Integration and system automation to facilitate continuous build, continuous integration, continuous test, and continuous deployment pipeline
  • Strong communication skills are a must to work with chief engineers and development team
  • Maintainance of virtual infrastructure
The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, and collaborative/team settings across all levels. DIVSW

Basic Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline and 14 years of related experience, or 12 years of experience with a Master’s Degree in a STEM discipline, or 9 years of related experience with a PhD
  • Ability to obtain an active DOD TOP SECRET security clearance
  • Experience with software development (Java/Javascript, C++, C# or any modern object-oriented language) and its life cycle
  • Experience with build tools (i.e., Maven, Ant) and/or continuous integration environments (i.e., Jenkins, Bamboo)
  • Extensive Direct experience virtualization (i.e., VMware), Agile methodology, and Linux/Unix/Windows administration
Preferred Qualifications:
  • Experience with the full Atlassian tool suite (Confluence, JIRA, Bitbucket, Crucible, FishEye, Crowd)
  • Experience with containerization (i.e., Docker, Kubernetes)
  • Experience with automation/configuration management using either Ansible, Chef or an equivalent
  • Experience with developing formal builds within the DoD industry
  • Certification from VMWare and Cisco is desired
