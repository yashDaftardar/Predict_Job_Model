About Dexcom:

Dexcom, Inc. empowers people to take control of diabetes through innovative continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) systems. Headquartered in San Diego, California, Dexcom has emerged as a leader of diabetes care technology. By listening to the needs of users, caregivers, and providers, Dexcom simplifies and improves diabetes management around the world.


Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Under General supervision, engineers, and designs automated systems
  • Assists and provides input on integration platforms for complete automation end to end
  • Assists IT in problem solving manual systems in IT, Manufacturing, and other areas of the business
  • Analyze manual processes and assists with automated solutions
  • Uses knowledge & experience, problem solves existing automated systems
  • Assists in developing code using PowerShell, Python, and other programming languages
  • Work with Project Manager on various assignments requiring IT Resources
  • Works closely with other Engineers to create workflows for automation platforms
  • Documents integrations within systems
