Position Summary:

Are you ready to join the market leader in helping to drive genomic innovation? Do you enjoy working in a collaborative, fast-paced environment that provides technical challenge and career growth? In this role, you will contribute to the test automation effort on multiple instrument and web applications that power Illumina’s industry-leading DNA sequencing devices.

The successful candidate must be a fast learner with a strong sense of motivation, be detail orientated, and looking to have fun challenging themselves in a deeply collaborative environment.

Responsibilities:

  • Contribute to the test automation effort on multiple instrument and web applications that power Illumina’s industry-leading DNA sequencing devices.
  • Develop and continuously improve test automation frameworks. Research and introduce new tools and methods.
  • Develop and execute automated API/UI tests to enable continuous delivery
  • Work with Manual Test engineers to drive all aspects of the software testing process for the team, from sprint planning to daily scrum meetings and sprint demos.
  • Participate in the Instrument SW leadership team helping to deliver high quality software by shifting left
  • Promote and drive innovation in technologies, processes, and tools for cutting-edge automation development
  • Learn and have fun with some of the smartest cross functional engineers in Product Development

Requirements:

  • 2-5+ years of experience in the software test/development field, embedded SW experience a strong plus
  • Strong knowledge of agile scrum methodologies and experience in using those methods to deliver complex software components
  • High level of comfort with SW test architecture and object oriented design concepts
  • Exceptional problem solving and troubleshooting skills
  • Self-starter mentality with excellent communication skills
  • Experience participating in and driving cross-organizational or inter-company collaboration
  • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic environment with challenging schedules
  • Experience with .NET Framework, WPF, Node.js, UI Automation (CodedUI, Selenium), strongly desired
  • Experience in biotech is a plus, as is experience with developing SW to FDA clinical standards

Education:

Bachelors or Masters Degree in Computer Science, Bioinformatics, or related engineering field, or equivalent experience
