Full Job Description

Sony Electronics, Inc. look for the risk-takers, the collaborators, the inspired and the inspirational. We want the people who are brave enough to work at the cutting edge and create solutions that will enrich and improve the lives of people across the globe. So, if you want to make the world say wow, let's talk.

As a part of Sony Electronics' engineering team you will build fast, responsive, applications and features for Sony devices and related online services. We build high quality, user focused solutions for Sony devices including Sony TVs and Blu-ray Players where performance and user experience is critical.

Responsibilities

  • Work closely with project sponsor and cross-functional teams to plan and define develop scope, deliverables, and timing for deliverables
  • Work closely with customers for requirement capturing, deliverables definition, and technical support
  • Develop product software for Sony digital television products, including requirement capturing, product specification, architecture design, module design and development, test case design and product verification
  • Collaborate cross-functionally with other teams for product development
  • Quickly prototype ideas to assist in the design process for new functionality and new user experience

Required Qualifications

  • 6+ years of relevant working experience in related field
  • At least Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or its equivalent
  • Proven track record in Android application development
  • Versatile knowledge of digital media processing
  • Deep knowledge of web technologies, especially in front-end trouble shooting

Desired Qualifications

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Experience in delivering software on tight schedule with high quality
  • Experience in dealing with ambiguous situation
  • Drive for product quality and attention to detail
  • Self-disciplined, self-managed, self-motivated, able to work with little or no supervision
  • Willing to do international travels
