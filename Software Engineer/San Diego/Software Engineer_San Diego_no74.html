Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Software Engineer 2 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Rancho Bernardo, CA. Essential Functions:
  • Develop computer systems specifications that address business requirements and that fit with the company's system architecture standards
  • Establishes and documents system parameters and formats
  • Ensures hardware and software systems compatibility and coordinates and/or modifies system parameters in terms of existing and projected computer capacity and capabilities
  • Revises existing systems and procedures to correct deficiencies
  • Maintains more effective data handling, conversion, input/output requirements, and storage.
  • Experience working in development teams and multiple technical work-streams
  • Develops Business Applications and Project Visibility components such as user interfaces, automated workflows, data repositories, charts and dashboards
Candidate should be highly motivated with a strong passion for problem solving and innovation and the ability to provide complex solutions with limited direction.

Basic Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline from an accredited university and 2 years of software engineering experience OR a Master's degree in STEM
  • Minimum of 1 year of experience with frontend development using ASP.NET, HTML, and CSS
  • Minimum of 1 year of experience with backend development using C#
  • Minimum of 1 year of experience with SQL databases

Preferred Qualifications:
  • Degree in Computer Science
  • Knowledge and understand of industry best practices related to Web and JavaScript development
  • Experience with JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap 3 framework, Entity Framework, Telerik, and/or Linq,
  • Experience developing rich, web based user interfaces
  • Experience building and managing enterprise grade systems software using Agile methodology
  • Strong conceptual skills and ability to apply rapid prototyping techniques
