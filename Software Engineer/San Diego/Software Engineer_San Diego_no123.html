Full Job Description

The Senior Software Engineer role requires deep experience as a software developer with a focus on designing and developing web-based applications and services.

ELEMENTS:

Education/Experience: 5+ Years of software design and development experience with .NET and C#, B.S. in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or similar degree, or equivalent experience, strong experience in middle and back end web development; experience in .NET technologies; experience in cloud-based web service design; experience in back-end web service design; solid understanding of good programming practices, architecture, design patterns and unit testing; experience across the entire software development lifecycle. Further, preferred experience would include these areas: Linux, automation/configuration management, SQL and MySQL, working in a regulated environment, HIPAA and CLIA compliance, web security, and developing workflow related software in a laboratory environment.

Communication Skill : Good basic skills, effective listening/critical questioning skills to understand design requirements and clarify points of uncertainty

Interpersonal Skill: Works effectively both as an individual and part of a team; can create and manage relationships with external customers effectively.

Judgment: Able to complete work with little supervision, reasonable ability to craft a solution and persuade others it is the right approach; ability to foresee potential system interactions and some ability to identify long term solutions, ability to work with others to share specifications and the reasoning behind constraints

Execution: A reasonable mastery of design/coding; the ability to offer recommendations to resolve specific issues, ability to defend/critique choices in order to produce an optimal solution

ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:

  • Ability to integrate third party API’s into a single, seamless platform
  • Ability to design internally consumed API’s
  • Ability to design and implement middle and back end web applications

ADDITIONAL DUTIES:

May perform additional duties as assigned or directed by management from time to time.

REQUIRED COMPETENCIES:

  • Performance Orientation – Is driven by personal performance; achieves all objectives detailed in scorecards and comfortable with quantifiable assessments.
  • Impact Orientation – Performs to stated objectives and driven to make an impact on company goals and patients’ lives.
  • Competitive – Dedicated to a competitive spirit that supports the Epic Sciences’ goal to be the premier CTC technology in the industry.
  • Sense of Urgency – Has the speed and agility dedicated to the company’s goals and performance.
  • Ability to Handle Pressure and Ambiguity – Comfortable with the pressure to be the best and the ability maintain composure and focus in situations of ambiguity and uncertainty.
  • Confidence - Confident in one’s skills and abilities; pursues everything with energy; demonstrates the drive and a need to finish especially in the face of resistance or setbacks.
