Full Job Description

Foresight is currently seeking a senior software engineer with expertise in backend systems, scaling, and security as well as aptitude and interest working in a full stack role. As an early engineer at Foresight, you will get the opportunity to work all over the stack and have a strong influence on the direction that the codebase and development process take. Our team values autonomy and open communication, while also encouraging mentorship, collaboration, and professional growth.

In this role, you’ll be providing a high quality experience to our members, operations team, and health care providers by ensuring that our infrastructure maintains high availability, performance and security. The ideal candidate will have broad knowledge of cloud-based systems, an ability to work in a fast paced environment, and a passion for improving quality and access to mental healthcare.

What you’ll do

    • Design and implement services using Laravel/PHP and AWS infrastructure
    • Help build scalable and performant architecture
    • Create tooling and documentation to improve the efficiency of the team
    • Work closely with other members of the product development team to ship new features and enhancements
    • Ensure current and new work maintains high security standards
    • Collaborate and share context in order to level up the team

What you’ll need

    • 4-8+ years of experience with backend server development
    • Experience successfully maintaining and scaling an AWS/Linux based cloud system
    • Broad knowledge of service oriented system design
    • Excellent communication and collaboration skills
    • Desire to grow and learn

Nice to have

    • Knowledge of PHP and the Laravel web framework
    • Experience implementing/maintaining RESTful APIs
    • Experience with JS and frontend technologies
    • Knowledge of HIPAA/healthcare data security
    • Experience working at a technology start up company
    • A Github account that shows off your best work

About Foresight Mental Health
We’re a modern mental health company built to tear down the traditional stigmas against access to high quality care at affordable prices. Foresight’s mission is to empower people to become happier and healthier by creating a mental healthcare experience that exudes empathy, reduces uncertainty, and instills optimism in our members. We do this through an integrated approach that pairs a world-class clinical team with high-leverage technology and data tools for members and providers.

The mental health space is seeing explosive growth with demand for high quality care and a rewarding experience. While the space is becoming increasingly crowded, Foresight is differentiating itself by creating state-of-the-art, clinically-proven treatment pathway models to improve the lives of our members.

We’ve grown from 3 locations to over 40 in the past 12 months, and are continuing to scale rapidly to meet the accelerating demand for the world’s best mental healthcare By building our own clinics from the ground up and equipping them with cutting edge technology, Foresight redefines the mental healthcare experience and quality of care for its members.
