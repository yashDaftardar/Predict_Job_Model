Job Summary and Primary Responsibilities

As an Associate Software Engineer, Testing you will be part of an Agile Software Development Team. You will analyze requirements, update and execute manual and automated testing efforts for assigned projects, ensure projects meet the quality standards set by product owner, effectively and efficiently test across the entire system stack with a focus on the core applications, application frameworks and the related areas.


  • Track testing activities and assigned work for each sprint and help resolve issues that are escalated within the Project and/or Scrum team.
  • Execute and carry out of the Test Activities within Scrum Team
  • Develop and maintain existing automated Testing code base
  • Attend Daily Scrum Standups as directed by the Manager and actively involved in the testing process.
  • Assist in the preparation of test plans and test approaches.
  • Track and report testing activities, defects discovered and their status, etc. for each release.
  • Log all related issues in the defect tracking tool identified for the project.
  • Possess Technical background and an analytic mind
  • Operate with a customer-focused attitude
  • Performs other duties as assigned
Required Skills / Capabilities
  • 1+ years’ experience with Software Quality Assurance, testing methodologies, and application software development processes.
  • Solid understanding of the Software development lifecycle.
  • Experience working with C# or Java
  • Proficient and accurate with word processing (Word), spreadsheets (Excel), charting, flow charting, and graphing and internet research as well as internet proficiency.
Education and Experience
  • Typically requires a Bachelor’s degree and no previous professional experience
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Management Information Systems or related technical discipline preferred

About NuVasive

NuVasive, Inc. (NASDAQ: NUVA) is the leader in spine technology innovation, with a mission to transform surgery, advance care, and change lives. The Company's less-invasive, procedurally integrated surgical solutions are designed to deliver reproducible and clinically proven outcomes. The Company's comprehensive procedural portfolio includes surgical access instruments, spinal implants, fixation systems, biologics, software for surgical planning, navigation and imaging solutions, magnetically adjustable implant systems for spine and orthopedics, and intraoperative neuromonitoring technology and service offerings. With more than $1 billion in net sales, NuVasive has approximately 2,700 employees and operates in more than 50 countries serving surgeons, hospitals, and patients. For more information, please visit www.nuvasive.com.
