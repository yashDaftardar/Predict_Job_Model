Company

Zebit is a fast growing, eCommerce and financial services technology company located in San Diego, CA. The Company’s mission is to reinvent how credit is provided to over 100 million U.S. consumers who either have no traditional credit score or are below the mainstream threshold to access cost-effective credit or emerging buy-now-pay later payment options. These “underserved” consumers make up the largest credit segment in the U.S. and have historically been relegated to a very limited and costly set of product financing options such as Rent-to-Own and Lease-to-Own or have been forced to take out Payday loans to buy what they need. These options can cost two to four hundred percent of a product’s retail value, and end-up trapping consumers in a perpetual cycle of debt by charging them approximately $66 billion per year in interest, fees, and penalties when payments are missed. Zebit is the first company in the U.S. to offer everyday consumers a large selection goods across more than twenty-five product categories (such as electronics, appliances, home décor, furniture, and beauty) and store credit to pay for their purchases over six months.

We are seeking a hands-on and results-driven associate to join our Customer Service team. The optimal candidate has worked in an eCommerce company or has prior customer service experience.

Title: Software Engineer – Backend

Reports to : Director, Engineering

Job Description: Zebit is revolutionizing how financial services are provided to the under-banked. Zebit is building out its entire cloud platform using a code-first strategy and is obsessive about producing and shipping high quality software. The ideal candidate will have both a broad and deep background in web technologies, technology architectures and software development best practices.

This role will serve as a technology expert responsible for driving and contributing to the technical direction in the development of our product and platform. He/she will provide technical guidance and hands on experience to the Engineering team during the entire software development life cycle. Candidate must understand the business impacts of technology choices, and be proficient at communicating those impacts to the entire organization. The candidate will be responsible for all areas of the development process of assigned projects, including but not limited to: data modeling, system architecture and design, technology selection, unit and regression test design and implementation, feature implementation, deployment and operational concerns. The position will work very closely with leaders in IT to ensure quality and timeliness of work product.

Zebit Technology Stack: .NET Core/ASP.NET, PHP, Magento, JavaScript, Linux, Nginx, Redis, MySQL, AWS, Kubernetes

This position will focus primarily on the .NET Core (C#) service layer and underlying MySQL databases.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Design and deliver platform features that are distributed, latency tolerant, and highly available
  • Oversee application designs, automated and manual test plans, and rollout strategies
  • Evaluate proprietary, open source, and home grown solutions and drive our technology stack
  • Work throughout the entire hardware/software stack
  • Overall responsibility for scalable and reliable design of the systems for meeting business growth expectations.
  • Establishment and enforcement of coding standards and practices.
  • Work very closely with IT leadership to ensure schedule and quality compliance.

Qualifications:

  • Proven and demonstrable experience architecting, designing and implementing enterprise-scale, high volume, high availability systems
  • BS or higher degree in computer science or engineering preferred
  • 7+ total years experience in software design and implementation in distributed environment
  • Strong experience in different middleware technologies and servers, including both open source and proprietary technologies
  • Strong relational database knowledge, including data modeling and performance tuning
  • Fluent with design patterns, particularly MVC frameworks
  • Experience working on tiered distributed architectures (SOA)
  • Experience with web services (preferably REST+JSON)
  • AWS or other cloud provider familiarity is helpful but not required

Additional Information:

  • Exercises sound judgment and discipline, avoids over-engineering solutions
  • Understand that operations/support are a priority when designing and building software systems, and engineer solutions to improve support and lower operational costs
  • Self-directed professional, knows what needs to be done and operates with a sense of urgency, focus and discipline
  • Positive, ‘can do’ attitude with a focus on the success of the team over individual successes
  • Applies ingenuity and determination in finding the best possible options to support business needs
  • Must be an effective communicator, presenting facts not feelings and balancing business goals with engineering goals
  • Takes ownership of outstanding issues, is accountable, and establishes a high level of credibility with others in the organization
  • Respects other people’s opinions and suggestions and works to create an ‘environment of trust’

Supervisory Requirements : No direct reports.

Experience Requirements: Minimum of 7 total years’ experience in software design and implementation in distributed environment and at least 3 years of first-hand experience with C#
