ANDROID SOFTWARE ENGINEER

Sigma Connectivity
San Diego, CA

JOB DESCRIPTION

We are seeking an experienced, well-rounded Senior Android Engineer in San Diego!

This is an opportunity to work on a leading exercise fitness platform connecting users and devices. Work closely with the product teams to design and develop new features and functionality for mobile applications. You will rewrite an existing React native application to native Android in Kotlin. Help contribute to an overall mobile engineering roadmap, including research of the latest processes, frameworks, and user experience design. You will ship new features and functionality across locales and continuously improve the client-facing experience to bring that additional polish.

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS

  • 3+ years of experience in an Android engineering role for a high-trafficked customer-facing application, with fluency in Java and Kotlin.
  • A working understanding of JavaScript and React is helpful; bonus points if you have experience with React Native and Typescript.
  • Knowledge of Modern Android Development principles and the overall Android ecosystem. Extra bonus points for understanding the NDK.
  • Demonstrated ability to work well with agile, high-quality engineering, design, and product teams.

PREFERRED ADDITIONAL SKILLS, PERSONAL TRAITS AND EXPERIENCE

  • Team player
  • Proactiveness in collaboration and execution
  • Flexibility to switch technical context
  • Willingness to learn new methods / tools / processes
  • Ability to prioritize and multitask
  • Strong problem solving and critical thinking skills
  • Strong passion for creativity and solving cutting edge industry problems
