About Us:

LightStream is in the innovation arm of Truist Bank, specializing in providing digital products to borrowers through multiple channels. Using its proprietary technology, LightStream offers a completely digital and paperless experience to our customers, highly competitive rates and products, and an exceptional customer experience.

Our main offices are located in San Diego and Atlanta, surrounded by fantastic weather, positive people, and an open and creative environment.

About You :

We're looking for a passionate Software Engineer II to join our growing team. You are comfortable on a small team who is responsible for the entire life cycle of a product. You are passionate about both the art and the skill of creating a great user experience for our customers.

LightStream's software engineers develop next-generation banking and finance technologies. We're looking for engineers with experience working in financial institutions who are ready to raise the bar, bring new ideas, and change the way that banking technology is built and delivered.

You will be responsible for the design and development of an exciting new products. You will work together with product management, marketing, and the rest of the development team in the delivery and evolution of this product. This position demands a self-managed individual who thrives in a fast-paced team environment and enjoys collaborating with others.

PLATFORM Experience:

  • Experience using C# and .Net Backend and/ or Full Stack Developer

  • Experience working with Microsoft Web Service technologies (ASP.Net and WCF) to create SOAP or REST APIs for high transactional systems

  • Experience designing and developing SOA/microservice APIs very desirable

  • Experience working with ADO.Net and/or Entity Framework

  • Knowledgeable in OOA/D and N-tier application best practices

  • Be able to demonstrate a good understanding of database design and development principles (T-SQL)

  • Experience with any of the following UI technologies a plus: ASP.Net MVC; WinForms; WPF; UWP; JavaScript; AngularJS; CSS.

Requirements: The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.

  • High School diploma or GED

  • Two years of experience as a developer or an equivalent combination of education and work experience

  • Knowledge in information systems and ability to apply that knowledge in practice

  • Ability to manage competing priorities

  • Ability to solve problems in straightforward situations by analyzing possible solutions using experience, judgment and precedents

  • Experience participating in IT solutions

  • Ability to communicate complex information in straightforward situations

Please provide URLs to live sites you have built or a few relevant code samples. If you contribute to community forums or open source projects, please mention those as well.
