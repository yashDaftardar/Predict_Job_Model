About Dexcom:

Dexcom, Inc. empowers people to take control of diabetes through innovative continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) systems. Headquartered in San Diego, California, Dexcom has emerged as a leader of diabetes care technology. By listening to the needs of users, caregivers, and providers, Dexcom simplifies and improves diabetes management around the world.


Summary :

The Sr. Software Development Engineer in Test is responsible for advancing the test framework, test tools, and test cases. The candidate will develop and extend automated test cases, to verify full coverage of product requirements, and will be responsible for completion of software test activities for projects from the software requirements phase through release of the software. The candidate will also be required to generate and record documentation during the process and provide updates to the team as needed. Skills in object-oriented programming, test, integration, and verification activities and an understanding of the full software development lifecycle are required.


Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Troubleshoots code for firmware (IC embedded code) applications.
  • Support the test team in automating the feature validations of Dexcom CGM Transmitter
  • Support the test team in automating the feature validations of Dexcom Medical Receiver
  • Record task status and track issues in the bug tracking system.
  • Analyze failures and collaborate with Firmware Engineers to ensure root cause as investigated.
  • Strong knowledge on understanding of IOS, Android, IOT based BLE logs.
  • Analyze field performance both from customer and partner data sets that might include manufacturing data, supplier data and cloud data.
  • Be hands on in setting up user simulated tests to re-create reported issues.
  • Update documentation when needed for application notes, firmware and hardware change requests, defect tracking system (JIRA) and other needs.
  • Communicate status to cross functional teams .
  • Follow design and requirement procedures
  • Create requirements traceability matrices which demonstrate test coverage of all features.
  • Write test reports documenting detailed results of validation, and report test progress to management.
  • Follow internal software development, cybersecurity, and validation procedures that comply with medical and security regulations.
  • Work in regulated environment: Create/update software validation plans, and software risk analyses, for software used in commercial products.
  • Validate Dexcom tools and products using automated/manual testing procedures.
  • Performs other duties as assigned.


Required Qualifications:

  • Minimum of 5 years of related experience in embedded system programming or embedded system test.
  • Minimum of 4 years of Object-Oriented Programming experience (e.g. C#/C++)
  • Ability to work in a fast paced / dynamic team environment and possess time management skills to meet schedules.
  • Experience in test frameworks (e.g. Junit) and IDE (e.g. Eclipse, MS Visual Studio).
  • Moderate experience with Android and/or iOS devices, including debugging, installation, and configuration.
  • Familiarity with BLE is a plus
  • Firmware testing experience is a plus.
  • Familiarity using Lab Test Equipment
  • Strong written skills and ability to follow Dexcom software procedures and generate required software documentation.
  • Review product specifications, software requirements, and software design specifications.
  • Knowledge and exposure to hardware design.


Experience and Education Requirements:

  • Typically requires a Bachelor’s degree in a technical discipline, and a minimum of 5-8 years related experience or Master’s degree and 2-5 years equivalent industry experience or a PhD and 0-2 years experience.


Functional Description :

Debugs software products through the use of systematic tests to develop, apply, and maintain quality standards for company products. Develops, modifies, and executes software test plans, automated scripts and programs for testing. Analyzes and writes test standards and procedures. Maintains documentation of test results to assist in debugging and modification of software. Analyzes test results to ensure existing functionality and recommends corrective action. Consults with development engineers in resolution of problems. Provides feedback in preparation of technical appraisals of programming languages, systems, and computation software. Ensures quality computer integration into the overall functions of scientific computation, data acquisition, and processing.
