Senior Staff Software Engineer

Chimney Vigor Group

We are seeking a full stack Senior Staff Software Engineer to join our dynamic team in San Diego to help develop & launch Edisen , a new scalable SaaS solution built on the MERN stack. Edisen is a one-of-a-kind SaaS solution aimed at brands who want to globalize and expand their reach with creative content, and do it FAST. With Edisen, you can order all your content creation services, collaborate with our team of world renowned creatives, and optimize your assets for maximum reach.
Some of the tools and technologies we use are ReactJS, NodeJS, Express, MongoDB, MySQL and AWS. We are looking for someone with strong experience in one or more of these technologies combined with creativity, problem-solving skill, and the desire for technical and product-focused leadership.
WHAT YOU'LL DO

Build and develop a high functioning, agile, and collaborative engineering culture by example and action

Promote and enforce software engineering best practices through clean & reusable code, unit test coverage, and code reviews for all steps of development

Approximately 70% technical, hands-on software development work and 30% leadership and management

Oversee the design, architecture and implementation of all new systems, with a strong focus of delivery to our customers

Use creativity and skills in combination with your leadership to innovate elegant solutions for problems at every level of our tech stack

Lead a collaborative team to define the long term technology vision

Set best practices influencing not only the team, but the entire Engineering Organization

Research and champion adoption and use of new technologies

Maintain a high operational excellence with a dedication to reliable, maintainable, exceptional products for our customers
WHAT YOU NEED

10-15 years of professional software engineering experience, of which at least 4 were devoted to using MERN stack to build SaaS products

Superb coding abilities in JavaScript, ReactJS, NodeJS or any other modern programming language combined with deep knowledge in object-oriented design

Strong understanding of MongoDB and MySQL

Experience with Cloud Architecture (AWS preferred)

BS or MS in Computer Science, Computer/Software Engineering, Information Systems/Technology, Information Science, or equivalent experience
WHO YOU ARE

Strong ability to execute as an engineer, with a pragmatic focus on ROI when delivering the product

Strong ability to mentor the engineering team through code review as well as face to face

Strong interest in thinking about product, including user empathy and an understanding of how a product works as a complex system

Strong communication skills and the ability to work cross-functionally with designers, product managers, marketers, etc. to deliver a great product

Ability to discern technical constraints, resources and opportunities available; willingness to take a creative approach to problem solving

Ability to estimate and prioritize

Possessing high integrity

Lives our core values: honesty, humility, exceptionalism, hard work, commitment, and innovation
