Qualcomm Wireless Research, based in San Diego, California, is working on strategic projects for the development of new technologies related to 5G, C-V2X, WLAN, industrial-IoT, and XR. You can learn more at https://www.qualcomm.com/research/5g. Individuals in this role will work with multi-disciplinary teams to develop software for small and large-scale prototype systems for R&D, industry trials and commercial products. We seek highly innovative and motivated engineers who can get things done. You will be working with a team of multi-disciplined engineers in a fast-paced, intellectually challenging work environment to shape the future of wireless communications. Successful applicants will be creative, enthusiastic innovators who are equally comfortable with researching new technologies and implementing code that would go into commercial devices.

*The position is located in San Diego and will require relocation to San Diego.


Description:
You will be part of a team delivering a custom high-performance MAC and upper layer protocol stack software. Your responsibilities will include working on next generation radio network architecture and developing data and control plane modules like PDCP, RLC, MAC, RRC/RRM, NAS, and associated Machine Learning aspects. You will be working on prototyping UE devices and network platforms.

Minimum Qualifications:

· Bachelor’s degree in Engineering or Computer Science

· 2+ years of Software Engineering or related work experience

· 2+ years of experience with at least one programming language such as C, C++, Python, etc.

· Excellent ability in design, implementation and debugging complex software systems

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s degree in Engineering or Computer Science
  • Wireless protocol stack development experience
  • Object-oriented software design and development
  • Distributed systems and networking protocols
  • Knowledge of protocols such as 5G NR, LTE and 802.11
  • Real-time and embedded software development for general purpose and heterogeneous processors including GPUs and DSPs
  • System performance optimization, multi-core, multi-processor, multi-threaded development
  • Linux based real-time application development
  • Experience in complex software debugging
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced collaborative and geo-diverse software team environment
