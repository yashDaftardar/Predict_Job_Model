POSITION: Software Engineer (.NET) Lively Ordering and Provisioning
FLSA: Exempt

ABOUT THE TEAM:
The Lively Ordering and Provisioning team is the group responsible for enabling our internal teams and channel partners to sell, provision, and manage mobile service for our Lively series of products. Our software developers, QA, and product managers work collaboratively to deliver products and features that help hundreds of thousands of seniors live safer, more independent lives.

ABOUT THE JOB:
As a Software Engineer II on the Lively Ordering and Provisioning team, you will be joining at an exciting time and will be a member of a recently formed platform team utilizing event-driven architecture. You can expect to work with technologies such as: AWS, .NET Core, Kafka, GraphQL, MongoDB and more.

RESPONSIBILITIES:
  • Design and develop enterprise Web and N-Tier applications
  • Work closely with product owners and teams to lead feature implementation and source third-party solutions when appropriate
  • Contribute to standards and best practices around development processes, coding, and peer reviews
  • Optimize existing systems to improve performance and scalability through service/application tuning and code refactoring
  • Fully and properly test code to ensure the highest levels of reliability and maintainability
  • Research and recommend software solutions and tools that help fix inefficiencies in the development process, team collaboration, etc.
  • Provide aid to IT, CM, QA and support personnel as needed to troubleshoot lower environment as well as production issues.
  • Other duties as assigned

  • Minimum 3 years of developing object-oriented applications
  • Prior experience with Unit testing
  • Prior experience designing and developing well-defined API based services
  • Prior experience with source control solutions such as Git or Mercurial
  • Prior experience using SQL or NOSQL databases

  • Prior experience with Amazon Web Services (AWS)
  • Prior experience with .NET Core preferred
