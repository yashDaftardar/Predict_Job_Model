Full Job Description

Software Engineer– Web Applications/Backend
Location: San Diego
Your Impact
Your excellent implementation skills will be essential for building interactive web applications, enriching products with features, and making them robust. Your fellow team members will rely on your skills to quickly build stunning and robust interactive Augmented Reality applications.

Your Team
The Vuforia R&D organization offers and builds the world’s leading industrial Augmented Reality solutions. Working from our office in San Diego, CA, you will be part of a highly focused team responsible for Vuforia Chalk, our Remote Assistance Solution – hailed as “The Future Of Education, Service And Support” (Forbes) and “an incredible example of how [Augmented Reality] can help your everyday life” (Business Insider). The team is very collaborative, and practices iterative, continuous software development following the DevOps philosophy and agile processes.
Day-To-Day
You will prototype, design, implement, test, deploy, and maintain web applications and services that support Vuforia’s existing AR solutions. You will coordinate closely with teammates, assist product owners with product definition and story breakdown, and be an active, shaping participant in our agile workflow, in brainstorming and design sessions, and in code reviews and implementation workstreams.

PTC is a place where innovative thinking is encouraged, and new ideas are always welcome. We’re looking for smart, passionate people that want to work together to make a difference. If this sounds like you, let’s talk.

Most Critical Skills and Experience :
  • Strong knowledge of distributed systems and a cloud platform such as AWS or Azure
  • Excellent programming skills with Node.JS on a full stack system
  • Experience with REST APIs
  • Proactive communication and strong problem-solving skills

Preferred Skills and Experience:
  • Experience with working in teams, agile development workflows, git, JIRA, code review workflows, CI/CD
  • Experience with one or more of the following:
  • Authentication & authorization concepts & protocols
  • SQL based DBMS
  • NoSQL based DBMS
  • docker, container orchestration tools such as Kubernetes
  • network/systems engineering; websockets
Basic Qualifications:
  • M.S. (preferred) or B.S. in Computer Science or related area
