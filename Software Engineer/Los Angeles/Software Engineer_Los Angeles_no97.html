Veeva is the leader in cloud-based software for the global Life Sciences industry. Our platform and applications allow customers to operate more efficiently and safely in order to bring life-saving diagnostics, treatments, and medicines to market faster than any time in history. This industry along with our vertical-specific software is in a period of unprecedented growth over the next five years, and we’re looking for mission-driven software development problem-solvers, thinkers, and doers to keep up with growth.

Veeva is a work anywhere company. Our mobile team is located in our Los Angeles Hub, you can live anywhere in the Pacific or Mountain time zones while having the flexibility to work from home, at a customer site, or in an office on any given day. As a Public Benefit Corporation, you will also work for a company focused on making a positive impact on its customers, employees, and communities.

Veeva is a market-leading software company with a focus on product excellence.

Join a startup team to work on a mobile product that will profoundly impact the world and the speed at which critical treatments, vaccines, and medicine get into the hands of patients who need them. In this role, you will work with Vault platform and application teams, designers, engineers, architects, and customers to design and build flexible, intuitive, and elegant mobile capabilities to support multiple mobile apps for many of Veeva Vault applications with an initial focus on our core Veeva Vault platform.

We write natively in Swift and Kotlin to get the best performance and usability on each platform.

Veeva is a Work Anywhere company so you can work at home or in an office. The mobile team will have its hub in the LA area. So, you can live anywhere in the Pacific or Mountain time zones.

What You’ll Do

    • Help define a brand-new product's technical direction, including CI/CD strategy, testability, and measurability for other non-functional requirements. Own end to end development of an IOS or Android application as part of a team
    • Working very closely with product managers who are working every day with customers
    • Collaborate teams working on other applications, so we all get better

Requirements

    • 6+ years of software engineering experience and have to love doing development.
    • More senior people will of course play more senior roles
    • Mobile experience is preferred and appreciated.
    • You need to be a team first type of person because that is the environment we are building.

Perks & Benefits

    • 1% Giving. Ask us about it.
    • Company culture that is committed Customer Success and Employee Success for the long term
