At the heart of Defining Possible is our commitment to missions. In rapidly changing global security environments, Northrop Grumman brings informed insights and software-secure technology to enable strategic planning. By joining in our shared mission, we’ll support yours of expanding your personal network and developing skills, whether you are new to the field or an industry thought-leader. At Northrop Grumman, you’ll have the resources, support, and team to do some of the best work of your career. Your opportunities for achievement are limitless with Northrop Grumman The qualified candidate will become part of Northrop Grumman’s Engineering Manufacturing Systems and Infrastructure (EMS&I) organization working on the Transformational, Research, Integration and Demonstration (TRIAD) network program. The selected candidate will work in a distributed team environment where he/she will assist with development of a Python-based (Flask) web application, including developing input forms and back-end logic/processing of collected inputs. Help to develop automation capabilities in a network environment using primarily Ansible, for data collection and automation of network operations processes (e.g. involving network routers, switches, firewalls etc.). Research and test other automation tools, including determining fitness for a network environment and integration with existing toolset. The selected candidate will work in a dynamic people-focused environment where he/she will interact with users, customers and other developers. As an intern, you’ll participate in a summer internship program to develop competencies in a variety of software development specializations that may include: Roles and Responsibilities: •Basic software development experience used to develop TRIAD Request Portal applications using Python, JavaScript and other tools based on requirements •Use GitLab for code management including bug tracking, testing and push to production •Ability to research, solve problems and carry out tasks with minimal supervision •Create and/or update documentation / instructions as directed •Ability to work well with a virtual team The pay ranges for this specific opportunity are as follows:
