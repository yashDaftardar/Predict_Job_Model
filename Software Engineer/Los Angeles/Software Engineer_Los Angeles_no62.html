Full Job Description

Tinder brings people together. We operate in 190 countries, with tens of millions of users, our reach is expansive — and rapidly growing. Your work here can change the world. Tinder engineers are dedicated to delivering high-quality software through emerging technology and design into one seamless user experience.

What you'll do:

    • Collaborate on the design, functionality, and development of Tinder's newest features
    • Build robust, lasting, and scalable products that serve millions of users
    • Work on systems that handle billions of daily events
    • Learn how large scale multi-channel communications work together to keep users well-informed (push notifications, emails, and in-app messaging, and more)

What we're looking for:

    • Must be currently enrolled in a full-time degree program pursuing a BS/BA, MS, or PhD in Computer Science or a related technical field with a graduation date no earlier than December 2021
    • Aspiring mobile developers who are excited to work on large scale challenges with cutting-edge technology
    • Strong knowledge of basic Computer Science principles: data structures and algorithms.
    • Proficiency in coding with at least one language: Swift or Obective-C
    • Prior mobile internship experience is a plus

Bonus points if:

    • Have iOS/Swift experience.
    • Familiarity with AWS: EC2, Dynamo, RDS, etc
Tinder's Summer Internship Program aka Tindership will be held remotely summer 2021
