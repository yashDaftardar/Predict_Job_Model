The Job

We are the team working on all the cool video details on the server side. If you know about or want to learn about video technologies like HLS and DASH or just want to work on the details of video processing, we are looking for Engineers like you.

As a Software Engineer on WarnerMedia's Content Processing team, you will build and support backend systems for processing millions of videos for our OTT streaming video applications including HBO Max. We are ensuring that videos are prepared and processed as fast as possible and look incredible on all our supported devices. By working on our team you can say you built the very best streaming platform in the world!

The Daily
  • Join a growing team with many opportunities for you to expand your scope and influence
  • Use your experience to make smart engineering decisions and own critical areas of a product used by millions of customers
  • Get the opportunity to work in multiple areas of the product, both on the client-side and server-side
  • Design and deliver software for distributed systems, including working with other engineers to help deliver the end solution.
  • Collaborate with your team to design solutions to complex problems
  • Ensure consistency of your team's’ code and those teams you integrate with
  • Help your organization grow into greenfield technical domain areas

The Essentials
  • Have a solid understanding of software data structures and algorithms
  • Ideate and innovate on software solutions
  • Describe technical concepts to non-technical audiences in a way they can understand
  • Be passionate about software engineering with a strong sense of ownership for the code that you and your team write
  • Seek self-improvement and are adaptable to suggestions and feedback
  • Hold yourself and your team to high standards while maintaining friendly, respectful relationships

The Nice to Haves
  • BS degree in Computer Science, or a related field
  • 1+ year of hands-on familiarity with both client and server technology
  • Experience with a variety of back-end persistence, queuing and networking technologies, especially Kubernetes and AWS
  • Familiarity with Golang, C++
  • Knowledge of video streaming technologies and DRM systems (HLS, DASH, PlayReady, Fairplay, Widevine)
