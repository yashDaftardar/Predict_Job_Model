JOB SCHEDULE:

ABOUT US:

ViacomCBS Streaming (formerly CBS Interactive) is a division of ViacomCBS that encompasses both free, paid, and premium streaming services including Pluto TV, Paramount +, CBS Sports Digital, and CBS News Digital.

DESCRIPTION:

Role Details:

The Software Engineering Role is a Full Stack Development Engineer with a specialization in Data Products. This person possesses a deep sense of curiosity and a passion for building smarter products and insights around data. They will work backward from analytical and experimental requirements to devise data, BI and Product solutions. Participate in multi-functional teams to implement data-intensive plans designed to address business problems. Work closely with the Manager and Business Intelligence teams to provide insights and conclusions that drive business decisions.

Your Day-to-Day:

  • Meet with members of the Product, Engineering, Marketing and BI teams to discuss and advise on projects and implement data-induced solutions that drive the business.
  • Design and develop web applications for use by business partners.
  • Create automated anomaly detection systems and an unswerving tracking of their performance.
  • Design and build products that integrate with in-house as well as vendor solutions.
  • Participate in code reviews during the design and development phase
  • Develop and promote standard methodologies in engineering
  • Staying knowledge of developments in web applications and programming languages.
  • Break down and communicate data and design problems into simple, feasible solutions.

QUALIFICATIONS:

What you bring to the team:

You have -

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science.
  • 2 plus years of experience with full-stack application development.
  • 2 plus years of experience building and deploying applications on a cloud platform (Google Cloud Platform preferred)
  • Proficiency in Python.
  • Proficiency in fundamental front end languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Experience with a Python web framework such as Django or Flask.
  • Experience designing and developing APIs.
  • Proficiency in distributed computing and large-scale data.
  • Familiarity with SQL skills for MySQL, Postgres, and BigQuery to perform common types of analysis
  • Familiarity with data management systems, both relational and NoSQL (e.g., HBase, Cassandra, MongoDB)
  • Familiarity with exploratory data analysis using tools like iPython Notebook, Pandas & matplotlib, etc.
  • Familiarity with Workflow systems such as Airflow.
  • Strong problem solving and creative-thinking skills.
  • Familiarity with version control systems. (Git)

FUNCTION: Software Engineering
