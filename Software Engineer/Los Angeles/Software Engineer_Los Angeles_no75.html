About EVgo

EVgo is the nation’s largest public fast charging network for electric vehicles, with more than 800 fast charging locations in 66 markets in 34 states. EVgo is also a first mover and innovator in the area of fleet electrification.

As a provider of turnkey fleet charging solutions, EVgo facilitates all aspects of charging station installation and operations: from fleet transition planning to equipment provisioning and infrastructure deployment; to fleet charging management software solutions, and operations and maintenance for mission-critical fleet operations.

Founded in 2010, EVgo is driven by a singular mission: to increase the pace and scale of transportation electrification by delivering the most reliable charging networks and best-in-class services.

Internships at EVgo

Our Internships at EVgo are intended to introduce newcomers and enthusiasts to the EVSE Infrastructure sector. We are seeking currently enrolled students interested in professional and personal growth working in partnership with their Manager, Mentor, and team working on meaningful projects. Internships are expected to start around May or June 2021 and continue through the entire Summer (i.e., through August or September).

Quality Assurance Intern

About the team

  • EVgo’s Software Product Team works daily to define, design, test, and release quality software applications. From ideation and requirements gathering to conducting user research and enhancing user flows to finally, manually and automatically testing the latest software builds, the Software Product team is the guiding force behind EVgo’s next-gen products.

    Students with a Product, UI/UX, Software Development background is preferred. This role is Cross-functional with Software Dev.

Responsibilities:

  • Manually testing applications, writing test cases.
  • Skilled at identifying and documenting bugs in software products.
  • Familiarity with Jira and other product management tools.
  • Successful testing: the specific questions related to what you are testing.
  • Participate in SCRUM ceremonies and build out test plans for upcoming features to test.
  • Document the bugs you find and retest when fixes are released.
  • Learn to test software products and develop base skills for a job in QA.

Recommended Skills and Experience:

  • Comfortable with a technical environment.
  • Qualitative and quantitative abilities.
  • Communication and troubleshooting skills.
  • Able to collaborate and win over a team with ideas.
