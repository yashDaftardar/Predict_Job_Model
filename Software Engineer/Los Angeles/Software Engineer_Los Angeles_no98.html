The UCLA Center for Health Policy Research's IT Team is looking to hire a full-stack (front-end and back-end) Drupal Developer and website administrator to manage our external public-facing websites.

The developer will primarily support web modernization, maintenance, and redesign projects. The developer will be responsible for designing and implementing new features and functionality, establishing and guiding the website's architecture, ensuring high-performance and availability, and managing all technical aspects of our various content management systems.

The best candidate must have a strong understanding of industry trends and popular content management systems, experience with responsive and adaptive design, excellent knowledge and experience with SEO, and an understanding of the entire web development process, including design, development and deployment.
