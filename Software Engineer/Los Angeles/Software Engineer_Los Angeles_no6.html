About Property Matrix

Property Matrix is the future of property management software. Utilizing cloud services, our property management platform was designed to be both powerful and scalable to meet the needs of mid range to enterprise businesses around the globe. We deliver value to our clients by providing streamlined solutions that modernize their business operations. We are revolutionizing how people pay rent online, submit maintenance requests, and apply for apartments online. Our software also helps manage shopping centers, malls and office space for commercial management companies. We include powerful features such as document scanning, email and text communication, as well as posts to Zillow, Trulia and other popular apartment listing sites. Tenant portals, payment processing and eSignature simplify tenant communication and interaction while reducing the amount of overhead when people move in and out of their building.

If you’ve ever lived in an apartment building, you’ve definitely experienced some of the inefficiencies of property management. We’re changing that.

Your Role

Property Matrix is looking for recent graduates who are ready to launch their professional career in the software industry.

As an Entry Level Software Developer, you’ll take part in the design and development of software for the current and next generation of Property Matrix. In this role, you’ll be developing exciting new features with a team goal of streamlining business operations and improving overall user experience.

The ideal candidate is a self-starter with a strong desire to increase efficiencies, and make an impact while contributing to a cross-functional team.

We need our engineers to be versatile, display leadership qualities and be enthusiastic to take on new problems across the full-stack as we continue to push technology forward.

Your ability to creatively collaborate and execute team goals will affect scalability and directly contribute to the company mission of streamlining the operations of property management companies everywhere.

Key Responsibilities

  • Implement new features to existing software according to specifications
  • Troubleshoot, debug, and test new features
  • Suggest viable solutions to resolve issues with existing software
  • Specify implementation details for proposed new functionality and features
  • Maintain up-to-date records of issues with existing code, and propose potential improvements
  • Maintain documentation for newly-written code
  • Implement fixes and modifications to front end code
  • Support other developers by assisting in the implementation of new functionality
  • Write tests and fix existing tests in the automated testing suite
