SUMMARY

Hulu's Web team is seeking a Sr. Software Developer with a passion for developing performant, highly available, consumer-facing experiences using modern web technologies. Our team collaborates with both technical and non-technical teams to deliver engaging experiences for our subscribers. As a developer on Hulu's Web Subscriber Experience team, you'll build and deploy the core content discovery and playback UI experience used by millions of viewers. If you're passionate about the Web and interested in changing the landscape of television, we'd love to meet you!

WHAT YOU'LL DO

  • Develop software & infrastructure that powers the Hulu.com content discovery and playback UI experience
  • Partner with the UX design team to transform mock-ups into viewer-first web experiences
  • Collaborate with Product Managers to ensure we're building the right features for our viewers
  • Support and mentor developers through code reviews, knowledge-sharing, and technical leadership

WHAT TO BRING

  • Deep knowledge of web technologies and web systems architecture
  • Strong passion for building sophisticated web applications that are performant, secure, and highly-available
  • 5+ years developing large, well-tested JavaScript applications
  • Outstanding verbal and written communication skills
  • Bachelor's degree in computer science or equivalent work experience

NICE-TO-HAVES

  • Experience shipping a production React/Node.js application at scale
  • Experience with developing and deploying applications on a cloud platform (AWS)
  • Experience with GraphQL
  • Experience working with Agile methodologies
  • Solid understanding of web accessibility fundamentals
