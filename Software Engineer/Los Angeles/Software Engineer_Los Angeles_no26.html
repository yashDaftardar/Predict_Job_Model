The University of Southern California Institute for Creative Technologies (USC ICT) is looking for a software engineer/Gameplay Programmer to strengthen its central tech integration group, with 2 years industry experience and is able to design, code, test, and deploy complex, distributed, integrated platforms in support of military simulations and virtual humans research and development. This is a 6-month fixed term position with possibility of extension.

Responsibilities

  • Work with designers, artists, researchers and other programmers to stand up, expand and polish Unity-based military prototypes and applications
  • Enhance existing C# APIs to provide new functionality or quality of life improvements
  • Assist others in on-boarding and providing support for using common in-house development platforms
  • Create examples, tutorials, and documentation in support of researchers and developers
  • Resolve technical problems related to Unity, C#, and other tools and operating systems
  • Work closely with other programmers and artists to solve various design, pipeline and/or programming issues

Preferred skills and experience

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or equivalent with a strong background in C#
  • 2+ years’ experience in software/game development
  • Proficient with Unity
  • Strong knowledge of programming/scripting languages and debugging techniques
  • Experience with versioning software (e.g., Git, SVN)
  • Multi-platform programming experience a plus (e.g., Windows, iOS, Android, web, Mac OS, AR, VR)
  • Web services programming and/or DevOps experience a plus (e.g., AWS, Azure)
  • Digital character creation experience a plus
  • Tools design and development a plus
  • Demonstrated problem solving ability
  • Desire to collaborate in a research environment
  • Ability to quickly assimilate new information
  • Desire to expand knowledge base
  • Attention to detail
  • Good communication skills

About USC ICT

The University of Southern California's Institute for Creative Technologies (USC ICT) is where academia, entertainment, and the military meet. Located in the heart of Silicon Beach in Playa Vista, no other institute offers a more diverse collaborative environment where basic and applied research work hand-in-hand to transition cutting-edge technologies.

We are world leaders in virtual human research, computer graphics, computer training simulations, learning sciences, body computing, and immersive experiences for decision-making, cultural awareness, leadership and health. ICT employees are encouraged to develop themselves both professionally and personally, through workshops, invited guest talks, movie nights, social events, various sports teams, a private gym and a personal trainer. The atmosphere at ICT is relaxed, informal and flexible, while encouraging initiative, personal responsibility and a high work ethic.


Minimum Education: Bachelor's degree, Combined work experience and education as equivalent Minimum Experience: 1 year, Combined education/experience as substitute for minimum experience Minimum Field of Expertise: Sound knowledge of programming and documentation procedures, programming methods, program flow charts and operator instructions. Knowledge of one or more appropriate computer languages.
