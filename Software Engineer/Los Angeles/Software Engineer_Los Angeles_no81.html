Job Summary:

Software engineering – SENIOR Engineer I

Location: Hollywood, CA, USA

Division: Ticketmaster North America

Line Manager: Manager, Software

Contract Terms: Permanent, Full Time

THE TEAM

Imagine millions of fans discovering events and selecting the best ticket options from an interactive seat map. As a member of the Checkout team, you'll help convert intent to purchase in a secure, efficient and consistent manner.

THE JOB

Ticketmaster is searching for highly skilled, curious and motivated software engineers to reinvent the fan experience. Our goal is to inspire fans to attend their favorite events by removing friction at every corner of the discovery and purchase process.

Behind the scenes you'll use the AWS serverless stack (lambdas and step functions) to securely orchestrate purchase steps including ticket reservation, delivery, ticket insurance, parking, and method of payment. On the front end you'll build responsive UI/UX to abstract away the complexities and keep fan excitement levels high. Your work will directly affect products used by millions every day and will have a material impact on our business.

WHAT YOU WILL BE DOING

  • Develop and maintain web applications and APIs using a modern tech stack
  • Contribute to the entire development life cycle (requirements, design, development, testing, deployment, maintenance)
  • Collaborate with product owners, project managers, business stakeholders and engineers
  • Employ engineering best practices to code, security, performance and scalability
  • Lead teams of engineers to deliver high priority projects on a quarterly basis

WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW (or TECHNICAL SKILLS/COMPETENCIES)

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science
  • 7+ years of relevant software engineering experience
  • Experience creating and consuming REST or GraphQL APIs
  • Strong experience with 1 or more of the following: JavaScript (React), Node JS, Java, Python
  • Experience with CI/CD tools such as Gitlab CI and Jenkins
  • Experience building distributed scalable systems
  • Experience with AWS serverless technologies (lambda, step function, dynamo DB, kinesis, S3)

YOU (BEHAVIOURAL SKILLS/COMPETENCIES)

  • Results-driven, highly motivated, and rigorous about using data to inform decisions
  • Ability to learn quickly and independently
  • Motivated by seeing your work have a measurable impact
  • Ownership mindset: you build it, you ship it, you run it
