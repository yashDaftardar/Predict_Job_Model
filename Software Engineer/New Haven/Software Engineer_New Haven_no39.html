Robotics & Automation Engineer

Company Description

Hyperfine Research is on a mission to democratize healthcare by making MRI technology accessible to everyone around the world. We are reinventing medical imaging and championing a new era of healthcare by building the first ever bedside MRI system. Our breakthrough technology enables portable point-of-care MRI and significantly lowers the cost, power, and weight versus that of traditional fixed MRI systems.

Hyperfine is a well-funded, rapidly growing health-tech company founded by award-winning scientist and highly successful serial entrepreneur, Dr. Jonathan Rothberg. Hyperfine benefits from being a 4Catalyzer company, an incubator of health-tech companies (most notably Butterfly Network) who has raised over $700 million in funding. The Hyperfine device was 510k FDA cleared in February 2020.

Joining Hyperfine Research is the opportunity to redesign the future of healthcare through the power of technology. Embark on a journey with us to maximize global impact, motivated by the idea that our products will change the lives of millions along with the people you love.

Job Description

As part of our team, your core responsibilities will be:

  • Closely work with R&D to design, prototype and maintain automation and robotic systems including image guided surgery and/or automated manufacturing and assembly robotics.'
  • Design and maintain the automation system for production.
  • Closely work with outside vendors and contract manufacturers.
  • Development and maintain the software tools for customer-developed equipment.

Ideally, you also have these skills/experiences/attributes (but it's ok if you don't!):

  • BS in EE, ME or CS with robotics background.
  • Hands-on, Self-motivated and be a quick learner in a fast pace dynamic company.
  • Programming experience in Windows , Linux using python, c/c++.
  • CAD experience in Solid Works, can operate machine shop tools,
  • Experience in using source control tool, such as Git.
  • Prefer to have experience in control system design and test, industry automation control, such as PLC, EtherCAT, and servo system.
  • Prefer BS with minimum 2 years industry experience or Advanced Degree.
