Company Description:
Personalized immunotherapies are the future of the fight against cancer, and IsoPlexis (www.isoplexis.com) is Making the Difference in enabling the lofty goal of employing immunotherapies to combat our toughest diseases. Our integrated systems, named #1 Innovation by Scientist Magazine & World-Leading Design by Red Dot, are changing cancer research by connecting biological readouts to what is actually happening in patients. Our game changing hardware technologies, originally from Cal Tech and Yale, combined with our next generation software and data visualizations, are powered by our amazing R&D team and used throughout the world. We work with a growing list of leading researchers who are publishing findings that connect our readouts to what is truly happening in patients, and that excites & drives all of us to do more! If you like working at the intersection of biological sciences and healthcare, and you enjoy intellectually challenging yet fulfilling projects, give us a call. Our fast growing, 200+ person team has a sense of integrity, energy, and urgency to ‘make things happen’ in our collective careers and in the broader world, and we look forward to talking.

Job Description:

IsoPlexis’ software department is looking for a Director to join the team and oversee development of our data analysis software applications. They must demonstrate a proven ability to work with other leaders across multiple functional areas, such as Project Management, Consumable Development, Instrument Development, and Marketing, and understand the appropriate balance between technology strategies and organizational priorities. The Director will use their expertise to help design innovative solutions to create significant value while owning the end-to-end platform from concept to delivery and post-launch support. This position will report into the SVP of software development and be responsible for ensuring delivery of high-quality software solutions to our customers. Developing our team members is a top priority for this role, and the Director would be responsible for the career development and growth of the software development team. Likewise, an important focus of this role will be to determine suitable development process improvements and work with IT and DevOps team members on any associated infrastructure updates. This position requires an engaging, innovative, and collaborative leadership approach. The ideal candidate comes from a software development and agile background, with experience in the biotechnology field and experience building and implementing full lifecycle projects.


Responsibilities:

  • Process improvement, team structure, staffing and role development
  • Leadership of the software teams, with an emphasis on hiring and nurturing great talent
  • Work closely and help coordinate cross-functional application dependencies around technology or resources with other managers and team leads
  • Immerse the team in Agile Scrum methodologies and toolsets, adhere to the Agile process, including monitoring team velocity for issues and course correct where appropriate to help the teams succeed
  • Lead and manage groups within the software development team to deliver software, infrastructure and architecture that meets the needs of both internal and external users
  • Ensure delivery consistent with Agile processes across the team
  • Work effectively with Product Managers and Product Owners to ensure delivery against agreed priorities and meeting current and future user needs
  • Accountable for on time and quality delivery of software development projects
  • Create, implement and maintain a continuous improvement plan for software development infrastructure, in coordination with DevOps and IT personnel


Skills and Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s in Computer Science or related degree is required
  • Advanced degree is a plus
  • 5+ years of software delivery experience
  • 5+ years managing and building high performing engineering teams, including a track record of managing projects to success
  • Experience working in the biotechnology field is strongly preferred
  • Several years of experience with C++
  • Significant experience with consumer-facing UI development
  • Experience with Qt is a plus
  • Experience with Atlassian products (Jira, Bitbucket, Confluence)
  • Experience with Agile Scrum development
  • Understanding of the full development life cycle
  • Fantastic motivator and leader of teams with a demonstrated track record of mentoring and developing staff members
  • Experience in developing and shipping high-quality applications
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Highly organized and detail oriented regarding both technical work and communication
