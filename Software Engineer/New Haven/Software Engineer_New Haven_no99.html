The Sikorsky Aircraft Real-time Simulation team is seeking a software developer with experience in C++ and Linux OS systems. Participate in the entire development life cycle including requirements, design, implementation, integration, installation and test. Typical tasks include development to interface real aircraft components to our simulation environment; supporting pilot evaluations of aircraft systems; developing simulated versions of aircraft subsystems; re-hosting aircraft software into the simulation environment. The candidate will also have technical leadership responsibilities which include mentoring, providing technical feedback to proposal estimates, and supporting the development of standard technical practices for the group to follow.

In addition to the responsibilities highlighted above we offer extensive benefits that include competitive 401k and insurance. We remain an equal opportunity company.
