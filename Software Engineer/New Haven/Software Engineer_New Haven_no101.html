About FCP Euro

Since 1986, FCP Euro has become the leading online store for OE, OEM, and Genuine replacement parts for European cars. Check out our website at www.fcpeuro.com if you haven’t already done so.

Because of unprecedented growth and an incredibly enthusiastic community, we’re expanding our company, and we are growing fast! With the right people, we’re convinced the next few years will be an incredible period of growth and excitement. To meet this challenge, we’re looking for new team members.

We first look for chemistry, ambition, and compatibility, then we focus on the appropriate skills for each position. Here at FCP Euro our culture is key!

Right now, we’re in one of the most exciting phases in our history. You’re going to sense urgency and energy when you become part of the FCP Euro family. Everyone has an opportunity to grow with us, especially people who show entrepreneurial spirit, ambition, and brilliance.

Job Summary

We are looking for a sharp Full-stack Web Developer. We require a solid foundation in data structures and algorithms, not just someone who is familiar with the Rails API.

Do you enjoy data structure study? Excellent, we are beyond a trivial Rails app. You will have a chance to work on high IO PORO and API projects at this position. Do you understand algorithms? We are currently implementing pathing graphs for our in-house warehouse picking app. Are you as excited as we are about the Ruby 2.7 release? Fantastic, when we started, Ruby MRI 1.8.7 was the production standard.

Responsibilities and Duties

  • Work directly with stakeholders issuing improvements the entire organization’s internal app portfolio
  • Work alongside colleagues to translate business specifications into fast, maintainable and exception-resilient web applications
  • Estimating, prioritizing, planning and executing work within a Jira workflow
  • Identify, record, document bugs
  • Mentor junior members of the team
  • Proactively build features with test coverage, identify and fill lapses in existing feature coverage
  • Be aware of modern Rails patterns (and more importantly anti-patterns) when performing periodic audits

Qualifications and Skills

  • Strong communication skills, we mean it
  • Proven work experience in software development
  • A positive attitude, growth mindset
  • Strong knowledge of computer science foundations
  • Experience writing automated specs
  • Ruby on Rails experience

Preferred Qualifications

  • Competency in a spread of various languages
  • Open source activity and engagement
  • Experience speaking and presenting to peers as a subject matter expert
  • RSpec BDD experience
  • Experience with performance and/or security testing is a plus
  • BS/MS degree in Computer Science, Engineering or a related subject

Benefits and Perks

  • PTO (Paid Time Off)
