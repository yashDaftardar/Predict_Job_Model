Description



Position Summary:

The Programmer Analyst role is a key position and is a valued member of Keyseries team. The primary focus of this position is to get the client requirements from the Business Analysts and provide Workday deliverables in given timelines. This individual serves as a technical point-of-contact for assigned functional areas and assists subject matter experts with ensuring data integrity, testing of system changes, report writing and analyzing data flows for process improvement opportunities. This Program Analyst also supports Workday upgrades, patches, testing and other technical projects as assigned.

Reports/Integrations (% of time: 40)

Write, maintain and support a variety of reports and integrations utilizing appropriate tools. Assist in development of standard reports for ongoing customer needs. Help maintain data integrity in systems by running reports and analyzing HR data.

Issue Tracking (% of time: 20)

Maintain ticketing systems and issue tracker to analyze, resolve and update day to day issues raised by Business Analysts and Customers.

Production Support (% of time: 20)

Provide support for Workday including, but not limited to, researching and resolving problems, unexpected results or process flaws; perform scheduled activities; recommend solutions or alternate methods to meet requirements.

System Maintenance (% of time: 15)

Assist in the review, development and testing of Workday system upgrades and enhancements. Collaborate with Business Analysts to coordinate requirements for fixes and enhancements.

Training (% of time: 5)

Develop user procedures, guidelines and documentation. Train customers on new processes/functionality. Train new system users.
