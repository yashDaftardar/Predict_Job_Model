Full Job Description

Do you love to take designs and make them come to life? Do you code CSS using LESS or SASS and make awesome interactive experiences? Do you want to learn how? Do you take pride in owning a project and knocking it out of the park? Are you great at juggling multiple projects and troubleshooting issues for clients? If you’ve answered “Yes” to these questions, we’ve got the place for you!

WORX is a fast-paced, high-energy agency that takes digital to the next level. We’re looking for an entry level front end developer/web support specialist to join our team and get slicing.

We are looking for developers who have any of the following:

  • 1-3 years of professional experience slicing layouts and using CSS to make web site experiences.
  • CSS, including LESS and/or SASS.
  • Experience slicing sites using framework like Bootstrap in a CMS like Wordpress
  • JQuery.
  • Enjoys the challenges of working on many different types of web sites.
  • Experience troubleshooting web site issues from initial outreach to successful resolution.
  • BONUS if you have experience with, or a desire to learn: PHP, MySQL, Wordpress, Concrete5, Linux.

Some of the things you’ll be asked to do:

  • Take visual designs and work with designers to slice the front end sites in an MVC framework (for example: Concrete5, Wordpress).
  • Participate in all aspects of web site projects, from initial discussions to working with creative to coding and delivery.
  • Communicate effectively with all team members to keep projects moving on the technical side.
  • Be able to work well and finish tasks correctly and on time, without us having to look over your shoulder all the time.
  • Be on the lookout for new technologies and ways of doing things – we’re always open to new ideas that make sense for us.
  • Troubleshoot web site and other technical issues from initial outreach to successful completion using our ticketing system.

This position is a full-time, entry-level position that requires the candidate to be in our Prospect, CT office location. No relocation assistance available.
