Job Title : Staff Software Engineer
Requisition ID : 155602BRJ

When you join us at Thermo Fisher Scientific, you’ll be part of a smart, driven team that shares your passion for exploration and discovery. With revenues of more than $24 billion and the largest investment in R&D in the industry, we give our people the resources and opportunities to make significant contributions to the world.


Location/Division Specific Information
Guilford CT / Specialty Diagnostics Division Group, Clinical Next-Generation Sequencing Division


How will you make an impact?
The Staff Software Engineer to be a part a cross-functional engineering team designing instrumentation to aid in cutting-edge Next Generation Sequencing. You will work closely with both the hardware engineering team developing the instrument and the biologists defining the problems the instrument and software are intended to solve.


What will you do?
You will assist in the development of new applications for our Next Generation Sequencing Instrument suite; participate and collaborate in product development for existing and new products; and support manufacturing (product testing).
  • Perform all phases of software development including design, implementation, testing, integration, maintenance of software.
  • Development and support of GUI, analysis, communications, and instrument control software.
  • Interact with verification and validation teams to create test requirements based on system software requirements and design.


How will you get here?
Education
BS in Electrical Engineering/Computer Science with 8 years related experience, or an advanced degree with 6 years related experience.


Experience
  • Knowledge of C/C++, Java, Python
  • A basic understanding of digital hardware used in real-time embedded systems that include FPGA’s, DSP’s and microcontroller devices
  • Software architecture design
  • Knowledge of current software development methods, including requirements generation, design documentation, and unit testing
  • Desire and ability to work in an established R&D group, as well as interface effectively with customers, manufacturing and customer support
  • Experience using JIRA, source code management
  • Experience with Test Driven Development in an Agile environment
  • Experience with Linux
