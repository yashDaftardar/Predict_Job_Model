Software Developer Job Description

About Clariti

Clariti helps ASCs, hospitals, and surgeons excel in the new reimbursement environment by offering a patient cost estimator and quote generator that improves cash flow, provides pricing transparency, improves patient loyalty, saves business office staff time, and ensures compliance in states with fair billing laws.

Summary of Position:

As a member of Clariti’s software development team you will be responsible for all aspects of the software development lifecycle. This includes, but is not limited to, planning new products, interpreting requirements, creating specifications, programming, testing, and supporting applications and APIs.

Essential Duties & Responsibilities

1. Create new features and support/maintain existing functionality and programs.

2. Take business requirements and develop working software, on time

3. Produce supporting documentation and diagrams as appropriate.

4. Identify and triage bugs, based on priority

5. Participate in discovery, grooming and prioritization sessions

6. Consult with key stakeholders, including customers to understand and clarify requirements, and offer alternative solutions where appropriate

7. Ability to learn on the job, including non-programming activities such as HL7 interface development and implementations support.

8. Quality assurance activities such as creating test plans, executing test cases, and unit testing where appropriate

Education/Qualifications:

-Bachelor's degree or expected degree by June 2021 in Computer Science, Information Systems or other related field.
-0-3 years related work experience.

- Full stack developer with experience in object-oriented programming, SQL, and modern web technologies

-InterSystems Cache/Ensemble/IRIS experience preferred

-Agile software development and experience with related tools such as JIRA/TFS preferred

-HL7 and X12 experience preferred

-Strong understanding of information technology concepts (Networking, SDLC, etc)

-Experience in the healthcare technology industry, especially in the domains of scheduling and billing, are preferred

-Strong analytical and problem solving skills
-Strong communication skills and the ability to communicate effectively with a variety of staff levels
-Excellent organization and time management skills, and ability to manage multiple projects and competing tasks/priorities
-Driven self-starter with high initiative and confidence
