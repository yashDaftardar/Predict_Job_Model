Software Engineer, Front End

Hyperfine
Guilford, CT

Company Description


Hyperfine is on a mission to democratize healthcare by making MRI technology accessible to everyone around the world. We are reinventing medical imaging and championing a new era of healthcare by building the first ever bedside MRI system. Our breakthrough technology enables portable point-of-care MRI and significantly lowers the cost, power, and weight versus that of traditional fixed MRI systems.

Hyperfine is a well-funded, rapidly growing health-tech incubator founded by award-winning scientist and highly successful serial entrepreneur, Dr. Jonathan Rothberg. Hyperfine benefits from being a 4Catalyzer company, an incubator of health-tech companies (most notably Butterfly Network) who has raised over $600 million in funding. The Hyperfine device was 510k FDA cleared in February 2020.

Joining Hyperfine Research is the opportunity to redesign the future of healthcare through the power of technology. Embark on a journey with us to maximize global impact motivated by the idea that our products will change the lives of millions along with the people you love.

Job Description

At Hyperfine you will be responsible for designing and implementing software solutions for an exciting, new, and disruptive medical technology. Ideally, you will be able to thrive in a highly collaborative workplace and actively engage in the development process. This is an excellent career opportunity for a professional with an impressive scientific software background and strong interpersonal skills.

Responsibilities Include:

  • Designing and developing new features for an existing web-browser-based user interface on our portable MRI scanner.
  • Maintaining and improving the backend for a dedicated web server that resides on the scanner, and keeping it in sync with the web interface.
  • Supporting a UI that can be used by both MR sequence developers and clinicians.
  • Making our scanner interface fast and easy to use.
  • Evaluating tools and technologies to support development.
  • Fostering scalability, both in terms of application development and in the support of production.
  • Generating robust code and test fixtures to support reliable and rapid development.

Qualifications:

Baseline skills/experiences/attributes:

  • 3+ years of experience developing web-based, low-level HTML/CSS/JavaScript.
  • 3+ years of experience in a development role.
  • Proficiency with a variety of programming, scripting, and configuration languages including Python.
  • Proficiency with Linux platforms.
  • Experience in web development for a variety of browser platforms, particularly Safari, Firefox and Chrome, with an understanding of the differences and compatibility quirks between them.

Ideally, you also have these skills/experiences/attributes (but it's okay if you don't!):

  • Understanding of containerization and orchestration technologies such as Docker or Kubernetes.
  • Experience/understanding of monitoring and observability solutions and modern tools such as ElasticSearch, New Relic, Kibana.
  • Current understanding of best practices regarding system security measures, including encryption and cryptographic signing.
  • Experience working together across teams to facilitate the orderly execution of a proposed project plan.
  • Experience with WebGL, shaders, or other 3D pipeline development.
