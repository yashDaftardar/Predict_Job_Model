Associate Engineer, Software/Computer Engineering

Stratford, Connecticut

Job ID: 545981BR Date posted: Dec. 15, 2020 Locations: Stratford, Connecticut Description: Sikorsky Aircraft Corporation (a Lockheed Martin Company) is a world leader in the design and manufacture of advanced helicopters for commercial, industrial and military uses. Sikorsky helicopters are used by all five branches of the United States armed forces, along with military services and commercial operators in more than 40 nations. Sikorsky is based in Stratford, Connecticut, USA. We are currently seeking a Software Engineer with 0–5 years of experience designing implementing and maintaining software solutions for avionics systems. The successful candidate will be responsible for small projects or multiple tasks within the functional discipline and will make significant contribution to their team by applying their knowledge of the discipline. The Software Engineer works with moderate guidance on projects with short-term focus and has a solid understanding of the fundamentals of the discipline and role while continuing to develop specialized knowledge.
Basic Qualifications:
Bachelors degree from an accredited college in a related discipline, or equivalent experience/combined education.
Desired Skills:
- Knowledge of C/C++ with ability to adapt and learn other programming languages quickly- Knowledge of Operating Systems and Debug Environments and other software tools.- Software Modeling and Database Modeling skills.- Knowledge of Software Life Cycle process Other Important Information You Should Know Expression of Interest: By applying to this job, you are expressing interest in this position and could be considered for other career opportunities where similar skills and requirements have been identified as a match. Should this match be identified you may be contacted for this and future openings. Work Schedules: Lockheed Martin supports a variety of alternate work schedules that provide additional flexibility to our employees. Schedules range from standard 40 hours over a five day work week while others may be condensed. These condensed schedules provide employees with additional time away from the office and are in addition to our Paid Time off benefits.
