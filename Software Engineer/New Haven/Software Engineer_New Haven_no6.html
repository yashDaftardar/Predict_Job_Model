Full Job Description

Defibtech currently has Summer 2021 internship opportunities within several functional area across our team: Legal & Compliance, Research & Development Engineering, and Marketing.


ABOUT OUR INTERNSHIP PROGRAM

  • Defibtech’s internship programs provides qualified undergraduate, graduate, or recent graduate candidates an excellent opportunity to gain hands-on experience in their field of study while collaborating with all levels of the Defibtech team.
  • Interns will have guidance and support while working on valued business projects over the course of 8-10 weeks from the beginning of June through mid-August.
  • Assignments will be in a cross-functional team environment encompassing a full range of product / project management activities including problem solving, team communication, planning and presentation, and assisting with implementing projects.
  • At the conclusion of the internship, interns will present their work to various team members, including the Executive team.

Defibtech promotes a challenging, collaborative and friendly culture that is centered around our values: Passion, Innovation, Integrity and Ownership. If you’re looking for an internship with hands-on career experience and some fun too, you’re in the right place.

OVERALL INTERNSHIP QUALIFICIATIONS

  • Active enrollment in an undergraduate or graduate program with coursework that aligns with the internship focus areas below. Recent graduates may be considered.
  • Ability to successfully collaborate and challenge the status quo, while bringing solutions and recommendations.
  • Engaged with a can-do attitude and drive to achieve goals and move the work outcomes forward.
  • Excellent organizationa, interpersonal and communication skills with the ability to work in a team-base environment that promotes collaboration and resourcefullness.
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite including Word, PowerPoint.

2021 INTERNSHIP FOCUS AREAS

Legal & Compliance :
Fields of study: 1 year of law school

  • Reviewing and analyzing contracts and supporting compliance matters
  • Assisting with implementation of contract management system including contract entry
  • Evaluating compliance workflow to be included in contract management system
  • Conducting legal research and preparing supporting documents and memoranda

Research & Development Engineering:
Fields of study: Software Engineering, Computer Software Engineering or Electrical Engineering

  • Providing hands-on support to R&D projects in software development and testing
  • Writing software code, authoring test results and developing project summaries
  • Working cross-functionally through project management activities to move the project forward
  • Presenting findings of lessons learned, technologies used, and future recommendations

Research & Development Engineering:
Fields of study: Mechanical or Biomedical Engineering

  • Providing hands-on support to R&D projects for validation, verification, and reliability test automation
  • Developing test plans, test automation scripts, and project summaries
  • Working cross-functionally through project management activities to move projects forward
  • Presenting findings of lessons learned, technologies used, and future recommendations

Marketing :
Fields of study: Business, Marketing, or Advertising

  • Providing hands-on supporting in the development and execution of our essential sales and marketing strategies
  • Conducting essential market research and analyzing competitor marketing, social media, and sales materials
  • Providing innovation and support to social media efforts, creation of marketing materials, and coordination of marketing materials for tradeshows, events, etc.
  • Assisting with the growth and development of social-corporate responsibility programs
