Flexi Software, an enterprise software company, has an immediate opening for an UI / UX Developer Intern. Flexi develops commercial browser-based and client/server enterprise level accounting systems.

  • Pursuing Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering or related discipline
  • Good verbal and written communication skills
  • Working knowledge of:
    • JavaScript, Ajax, jQuery, HTML, CSS, XML and REST
    • Microsoft technologies such as ASP.NET, C#, WCF and SharePoint a plus
    • Crystal Reports or Microsoft SQL Server Reporting Services and understanding of multiple table joins, groupings and sorting is a plus
  • Basic understanding, college courses, in finance or accounting a plus
  • Understanding of development of browser-based hosted systems a plus

Candidates should be prepared to discuss specific experience, examples of work, and elaborate on the technologies listed as requirements for this job. Candidates that do not meet these requirements will not be considered.
