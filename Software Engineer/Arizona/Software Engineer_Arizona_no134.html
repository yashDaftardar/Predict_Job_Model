Full Job Description

Are you ready to get some hands-on experience with an amazing team? Sparklight is looking for a Software Engineering Intern for the summer session. As a Software Development Intern, you will spend 12 weeks learning and developing software using C#, SQL, and other programming languages. Our interns will have the opportunity to shadow Senior Software Developers and learn from their experience, in addition to working on projects individually. You will have our team's support every step of the way!

What you will do

  • Work with our technical teams to develop technical specifications
  • Write C# code to develop software and web services
  • Write SQL scripts and stored procedures
  • Edit and update technical documents
  • Have a positive impact on both small and large software development projects

For this position, you will need:

  • Enrolled in an undergraduate degree program (preferably in Computer Science)
  • Experience working with C#, ASP.net, and/or SQL
  • Able to troubleshoot and think outside of the box
  • Excitement to learn and prepare yourself for your upcoming career!

Skills that aren't required, but it would be great if you had:

  • Experience or knowledge of web service development preferred
  • Experience or knowledge of web front-end GUI development preferred
  • Familiarity with Microsoft Visual Studio, SQL Management Studio, and Azure Dev Ops

What you will gain:

  • On the job training
  • Knowledge of how enterprise software development is accomplished in a real-world setting
  • A collaborative space to work with and interact with a team of experienced and talented software developers
  • Experience in the day to day operations of a Software Developer
  • Opportunities to discuss goals, career, and education in a one to one setting

Internship duration and schedule: 20-30 hours per week; 12 weeks.
