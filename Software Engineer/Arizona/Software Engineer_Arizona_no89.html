Full Job Description

As a WebPT Software Engineer, you will work inside a collaborative team to deliver innovative software solutions to help solve the many challenges faced by rehab therapy businesses and therapists. To provide scalable and flexible solutions, our applications utilize the latest in modern software technologies such as Kubernetes, AWS, ReactJS, and microservice architecture. As a member of the team, you will work closely with fellow members to participate in all aspects of the design, development, and deployment of software features.

Responsibilities

  • Work with the product team to refine user stories and clarify requirements
  • Provide technical leadership in the design and debugging of software solutions
  • Code software features on a weekly (or less) delivery cycle
  • Reliable and punctual in reporting for work

Abilities and Knowledge

  • Be willing to learn new technologies
  • Be flexible and collaborate openly with others
  • Be a self starter who enjoys problem solving
  • Challenge assumptions and find ways to improve productivity

Education and Experience

Required:

  • Completion of a B.S. degree or 3 years of professional software development experience
  • Familiarity with a variety of Software technologies
  • 1 year of experience with Software repository tools such as Git or SVN
  • 2 years of SaaS development experience
  • 1 year of experience in Software testing (Unit / Integration / End to end tests)
  • 1 year of SQL and/or NoSQL data storage solutions experience
  • 2 years of Javascript experience

Preferred:

  • Experience with ReactJS
  • Experience with Github and build pipelines
  • Experience with provisioning script such as Terraform
  • Experience with Docker containers
  • Experience with Kubernetes
  • Experience with AWS, particularly EC2, Lambda, EKS, S3, SQS/Kinesis
  • Experience with Agile practices
