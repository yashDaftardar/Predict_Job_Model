The Embedded Software Engineer will be involved in the complete product lifecycle with focus on product releases of our embedded and data driven software solutions. Primary responsibilities include core SDK product development as well as providing direct and in-direct customer support of those products.
The duties listed below are intended only as examples of the various types of work that may be performed. The omission of specific statements of duties does not exclude them from the position if the work is similar, related or a logical assignment to this class.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

    • Develop new technologies for our products-INNOVATE!!
    • Work with cutting edge technologies including voice assistant, cloud integration, home automation and machine learning
    • Make products that impact millions of consumers daily
    • Adapt to new projects, you will work with various languages and tools here, never just the same tech stack
    • Support of new feature implementations and product releases
    • issue/bug resolution and sustaining support of existing releases
    • Work as a member of the Agile cross-functional development team; release and iteration planning; development of user stories, unit test and feature implementation.
    • Participate in dialog with other developers in discussing current and proposed architecture(s), defects, enhancements, and other issues

Education

    • B.S. degree in Computer Science or related technical field, M.S. preferred

Skills/Experience

    • 3-5 years’ experience developing software for consumer products and/or embedded environments.
    • Passion for clean and efficient code.
    • Deep knowledge of C and modern C++ (11, 14, 17)
    • Extensive experience with Linux based debugging tools, including memory debuggers and profilers.
    • Demonstrated knowledge and experience of multi-threaded coding techniques
    • Experience with cross-target development on Linux platform
    • Experience with Ruby, Android NDK, Python is a big plus.
    • Must be able to work with small teams in a fast-paced development environment
    • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
    • Energy and passion for learning and innovations

Work Conditions

    • 40-hour on-site work week;
    • Some travel may be required
    • Physically able to participate in training sessions, presentations, and meetings.
    • Sitting for extended periods of time; Dexterity of hands and fingers to operate a computer keyboard, mouse, power tools, and other computer components.
Universal Electronics Inc. (NASDAQ: UEIC) is the worldwide leader in universal control and sensing technologies for the smart home. UEI’s many first-to-market innovations have helped transform the home entertainment control, home security, and home energy management and sensing industries.
