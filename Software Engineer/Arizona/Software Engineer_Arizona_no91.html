Full Job Description

As an entry level Web Application Developer and Data Support Analyst, you will be part of a team of highly skilled IT professionals that supports a talented group of auditors and investigators, together working to improve Arizona government. We do this by helping identify areas of potential fraud, waste, and abuse; assisting in acquiring, preparing, and analyzing data; and developing applications that enhance analysis and business process efficiency and effectiveness.

All of our positions are in-person. However, due to the current COVID-19 pandemic, employees might be given the option to telecommute. During the COVID-19 pandemic, we have implemented safety protocols for our team members to follow to help ensure their and agency personnel's safety.

Job Duties:
You will work on a wide variety of challenges and technologies, sometimes all on the same day.

An entry level analyst would typically work on:
  • Acquiring, securing, transforming, and optimizing data from governments and other sources for use on audits and reviews.
  • Analyzing data to identify trends and potential areas of concerns for audit and investigative follow-up by designing strategic deliverables including reports and data visualizations.
  • Designing, developing, testing, and documenting applications that automate processes that help make data more accessible and improve day-to-day efficiency.
  • Consulting with auditors and investigators on business intelligence needs and areas of process improvements.
  • Analyzing and resolving production issues on established processes and recommending improvements to existing code as needed.
  • Developing and/or delivering office training sessions.

Knowledge, Skills & Abilities (KSAs):
Minimum qualifications include:
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or other similar IT-related major, with a minimum 3.0 GPA.
  • Experience mentoring others in technical matters.
  • Experience with basic productivity software, database development and management (SQL), and coding (C#, R, Python, PowerShell, and/or HTML) is preferred.
  • Experience with analysis software and development of software applications and/or websites are a plus.
  • Ability to learn new software programs/languages as they relate to data analysis, reporting, data visualization, and application development.
  • Strong oral and written communication skills.
  • Ability to work in a team environment.
