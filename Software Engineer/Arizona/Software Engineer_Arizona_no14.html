We are looking for a Plant Vehicle Software and Integration Engineer to be a part of our Vehicle Development team.

The role:

    • Support all aspects of vehicle software in manufacturing.
    • Collect, maintain and report out on a range of product assembly metrics.
    • Troubleshoot all software and network communication issues with the manufacturing team.
    • Work with Low Voltage Systems Engineers to troubleshoot electrical issues during the assembly process.
    • Ensure consistent vehicle software configurations.
    • Support vehicle bringup.
    • Work with Manufacturing Diagnostic Engineers on EOL tests.
    • Regular Travel is required between the factory in Casa Grande, AZ and Engineering HQ in Newark, CA.

Qualifications:

    • 5+ years of experience with automotive software and EE integration.
    • Fundamental understanding of electronic control units, sensors, actuators as well as ECU communication.
    • Experience with software upgrade and diagnosing vehicle subsystem through UDS based tooling.
    • Experience with standard EE lab instruments (oscilloscope, multimeters) and data bus analysis tools such as CANalyzer, VehSpy.
    • Ability to read wiring harness schematics.
    • Experience in automotive manufacturing plants OEM or Tier1.
    • Previously participated in prototype builds or launch events.
