JoB DESCRIPTION- Backend ENGINEER II

Location: North America (Remote)

Division: Ticketmaster Tech

Contract Terms: Permanent, Full-Time


THE TEAM: ISMDS


A career at Ticketmaster will challenge and engage you. We support the creators and producers of shows and live performances, while connecting more passionate fans to these events. The pace here is fast, the atmosphere is fun and a passion for live events is a common thread that ties us together. As a global and growing business, we can truly offer a world of opportunities to expand your skills and develop your career. Our Teams are a diverse mix of passionate employees, helping fans around the globe connect with the artists, teams and events they love. It truly is a unique and rewarding environment.


THE JOB


We are looking for a backend engineer with huge growth potential to help build and augment the backend services that power Ticketmaster’s marquee shopping experience. Our servers and lambdas are primarily written in Java, and employe a range of design patterns including event-driven programming, and traditional RPC/REST. We deploy to both on-prem datacenter and AWS, through containerization and Kubernetes.

Today we sell tickets not only on Ticketmaster.com but on partner e-commerce sites and the biggest social networks.

As we continue to grow we need outstanding engineers. That’s where you come in.


WHAT YOU WILL BE DOING


  • Write well-designed, efficient code with unit tests, logging and comments
  • Automate testing, deployments, performance and recovery
  • Assist with maintenance and modernization of legacy code and services
  • Collaborate with tech and product representatives within the purchase vertical
  • Build features for highly-available, high-traffic APIs
  • Instrument backend components using OpenTracing
  • Monitor application performance and system logs to asses application health
  • Participate in standups and other Agile ceremonies
  • Pair program, assist with code reviews, and contribute to system design


WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW (or TECHNICAL SKILLS/COMPETENCIES)

  • Demonstrable experience (at least 3 years working in technology)
  • Expertise in Java, and professional experience working with JVM technologies
  • A bachelor’s degree in CS/Information Systems or related field
  • Experience with Agile methodologies
  • Backend development skills
  • Experience with Design Patterns and SOLID principles
  • Experience in DevOps
  • Working knowledge of one or more database products (e.g. Oracle, MySQL, Mongo, DynamoDB)
  • Understanding of Web Services, Service Oriented Architecture, Micro Services, Messaging.
  • Hands-on experience with the Spring Framework and Spring Boot
  • The ability to build, test, and deploy code on cloud infrastructure
  • Work-life balance – our industry is fast-paced and we develop software at a steady rate
  • Experience with messaging platforms like Apache Kafka is a bonus


YOU (BEHAVIOURAL SKILLS/COMPETENCIES)

  • You embrace creativity and take risks as we encourage innovation
  • You promote cooperation and commitment within the team to achieve goals and deliverables
  • You are a team player who helps others in the team succeed
  • You enjoy hackathons and devjams
  • You can identify and apply opportunities for own learning & development
  • You are a results-driven, highly motivated, and have proven experience in distributed, scalable systems.
  • You keep abreast of new products, technologies and Dev releases as we face exciting new challenges every day and are constantly evolving with emerging technology
  • You can communicate suggestions and information clearly and concisely
  • You can assess reproduction and testing of reported issues; issue bugging and documenting
  • You anticipate, identify, and define problems; seek root causes including developing and implementing practical and timely solutions
