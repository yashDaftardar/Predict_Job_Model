We seek an Advanced Software Engineer who will work closely with a tenured experienced team of software, firmware, hardware engineers and architects to develop and maintain network drivers in Windows, Integrity, and Linux.


Key Responsibilities:

  • Innovative architecture, design, development, documentation and maintenance of network protocols and drivers
  • Developing, Testing, and Deploying Network Protocols, Linux, or Windows Drivers
  • On a regular basis, you will use tools such as Wireshark for analyzing network traffic protocols and application network behavior and Windbg or equivalents for debugging drivers

YOU MUST HAVE

  • 5+ years of C/C++ experience with deep understanding of pointers and data structures such as hash tables
  • Experience with Kernel-Mode Driver Development and debug in Windows or Linux
  • Knowledge of Linux internal and low-level firmware
  • Strong problem solving and troubleshooting skills

  • Excellent team orientation

  • Due to U.S. export control laws, must be a U.S. citizen, Green card holder or permanent resident

WE VALUE

  • Knowledge of Microsoft Network Driver Interface Specification (NDIS)
  • Deep understanding of networking stack and TCP/IP protocols
  • Experience with Git-based source repositories like BitBucket
