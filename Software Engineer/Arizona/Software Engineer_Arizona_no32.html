About Us:

EDGE3 Technologies is an advanced vision analytics company that offers the most versatile, robust and scalable vision technology using both single and dual camera configurations. EDGE3 is focused on solving problems that improve the safety of automobiles, the efficiency of shipping logistics, or the security of one’s home. EDGE3’s software platform combines world class depth mapping capability with state of the art artificial intelligence technology to deliver unprecedented vision analytics solutions across multiple vertical segments. EDGE3’s software applications are embeddable and can also be deployed on a “vision- as- a- service” basis, using existing cloud infrastructure. To date, EDGE3 has an IP portfolio of over 70 awarded and pending patents in the various fields of: Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, Computational Stereography, Structured Light, Time-of-Flight, Organic Sensors, and the Internet of Things.

Job Summary:

EDGE3 is looking for a talented software engineer with tireless passion for technology, loves coding, and desires to work on next generation machine vision and AI products that are being rolled out in the automotive, smart home, and industrial logistics industries. You will join our small, but extremely talented, smart and ambitious core engineering team. Our organization is hard working, nimble, and we are proud of the entrepreneurial and egalitarian culture we’ve created.

As a software engineer, you are going to be working on a series of projects involving the development and creation of next-generation technology. You will be expected to bring your own creative process to the table, become an expert in the technical aspects of our products, analyze high-level requirements, and then run a solid and precise execution of such requirements.

You are someone who is able to pick up an API or SDK in JAVA, C# or C++ and run with it with minimal guidance. Having the ability to be an independent learner, while still learning and sharing with everyone else.
