Position Details

Position Summary
The Web Developer performs requirement analysis, documents technical specifications, writes, reviews, tests and debugs moderately complex code to support new or existing business initiatives.

General Accountabilities & Deliverables for Software Developer


  • Improves student or business department system experiences by:
    o Developing or configuring solutions in single or smaller, simple, stand-alone systems or of a larger system with some integrating components

o Performing moderately complex development or configuration in more than one technology and refers more complex activities to higher-level staff.
o Making relevant suggestions to fit the business needs with some acquired business domain knowledge through team and limited interactions with business partners
  • Resolves urgent or important production system issues by troubleshooting technical issues of medium complexity and referring more complex activities to a higher-level staff
  • Contributes to dynamic and high performing team growth with periodic coaching to Associate Developer
  • Maintains and improves the quality of systems by testing, following coding and process standards
  • Maintains efficient systems by designing solutions with system performance, scalability and optimization in mind

General Accountabilities & Deliverables for all members of the Development Team
  • The ideal candidate easily partners with others and will be intrinsically motivated to find solutions within a given structure while having the ability to deal with abstract concepts, systems and innovative ideas in an organized self-disciplined way.
  • Discovers useful information, suggests conclusions, and supports decisions by analyzing data
  • Improves the efficiency and productivity of team by recommending solutions to prevent or automate production support tasks
  • Cultivates an innovative Marketing culture by recognizing and supporting the implementation of new ways to solve business problems using current or new technology
  • Other duties as assigned

Knowledge, Skills, & Abilities
Education / Experience
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, IT, or related field or equivalent experience and demonstrated ability required
  • Minimum two - three years of experience as a web developer or other relevant web discipline required
  • Familiar with the full software development life cycle and agile software development practices required
  • Previous experience as a support or help desk technician desired

Skills
Skills for the Software Developer
  • Possessing beginning to working knowledge of more than one technology
  • Intermediate data analysis skills
  • Ability to work under direct supervision

Skills applicable to Development Team
  • Attention to detail.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment where deadlines are essential.
  • Ability to function in flexible and adaptable work schedules.
  • Ability to maintain professional image and work environment.
  • Ability to organize, plan, prioritize and follow through on work using basic time management skills
  • Ability to gain, understand and apply information and data as it relates to Information Technology
  • Strong interpersonal communication skills.
  • Ability to use good judgment, problem-solving and decision-making skills
  • Ability to learn new applications, tools and technologies

Abilities
  • Must be able to lift, carry, push, or pull up to 5 pounds or less 5% of the workday
  • Must be able stoop, kneel, crouch, or crawl 5% or less of the workday
  • Must be able to talk, see, hear, concentrate, think, learn and reason for all of the workday
  • Must be able to sit and walk or otherwise move around for prolonged periods of time throughout the workday.
  • Must be able to use a keyboard and do manual tasks for prolonged periods of time throughout the workday.
  • Must be able and willing to work off hours when required to support 24/7 critical business applications
  • Will require about 0% of overnight travel.

Work Environment
  • Work is performed indoors in a climate controlled environment.
