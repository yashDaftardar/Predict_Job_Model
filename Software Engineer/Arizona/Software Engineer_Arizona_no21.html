Full Job Description

Our Smart Cities Applications team is looking to add a junior software developer to the team in our Phoenix office. Make a difference and have an impact at Kimley Horn working with a close knit team. We have consistently been ranked (13 times) as one of Fortune Magazine’s “100 Best Companies to Work For.” Kimley-Horn prides itself on hiring high-achieving, dedicated, and reliable professionals.

Position Requirements:

  • In the process of obtaining an ABET accredited engineering degree (Bachelor or Masters) by summer of 2021 in these majors:
    • Software Engineering, Computer Science, Systems Engineering, or related field of study
  • Familiarity with relational databases such as MS SQL Server or Oracle

Desired Qualifications:

  • Web development experience building complex applications with HTML5 & JavaScript framework, WPF Applications, or C# Services
  • Experience with two of the following: ASP.NET, HTML5/CSS, MEAN stack, REST web services, C#/.NET, WPF
  • Experience with AngularJS, TypeScript, and JavaScript/jQuery
  • Please provide a link to your portfolio or examples of your work and skills


Applicants must be legally authorized to work for Kimley-Horn in the U.S. without employer sponsorship. We do not typically sponsor H1-B or any other work visa petitions.
