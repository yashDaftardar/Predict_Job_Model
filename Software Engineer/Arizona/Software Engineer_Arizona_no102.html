Fender Musical Instruments Corporation ("FMIC") is located in Scottsdale, AZ . Our brand mission is to accompany each player at every stage with products and brand experiences that fuel the pursuit of musical expression. Within Fender Global Research & Development , we are focused on building products that resonate with our customers.

An American icon, Fender was born in Southern California and has built a worldwide influence extending beyond the studio and the stage. A Fender is more than an instrument, it's a cultural symbol that resonates globally.

We are looking for a Principal Software Engineer, Music Applications who is comfortable developing on both desktop and mobile. The ideal candidate has dabbled in enough audio and DSP development to name drop some C++ frameworks and/or knows who RBJ is.

About the Job:

  • Provide thought leadership, architecture, coding, and support for our Fender Tone apps and new apps on both mobile and desktop
  • Review pull requests from internal and external teams
  • Collaborate on and maintain team coding standards and architectural practices
  • Support and improve existing applications using C++, Objective-C, Swift, Java, Kotlin and core libraries
  • Partner with Embedded and Hardware team to design and integrate new APIs
  • Ship new applications and updates in collaboration with product, marketing and executive teams
  • Lead by example, foster an innovative, collaborative and fun working relationship with others and believe strongly in refining your craft on a daily basis

What we need

  • 10+ years of experience in C++ for commercial software development
  • Track record of developing apps with excellent UX
  • Experience working with platform teams in either writing or using SDKs and working with REST APIs
  • Experience writing performant, multithreaded asynchronous code, especially network bound
  • Worked on collaborative engineering projects (continually integrated, tested and deployed)
  • Writes testable and test covered code
  • Experience with USB, WIFI, and Bluetooth communications
  • Experience with audio plugin formats
  • Excellent problem-solving skills, and the ability to work in a cross functional team
  • Prior experience with audio and music technology
  • Experience working on an Agile team
  • Experience using CMake
  • Experience with JUCE and/or Qt frameworks
  • Experience working on IoT products
  • Professional Java, Objective-C or Swift experience
  • Experience working on embedded systems in C++
  • Bachelor's degree or higher. CE/CSE, CS, or EE preferred.
  • Preferred: Music or audio background

About Fender Musical Instruments:

Fender Musical Instruments Corporation (FMIC) is one of the world's leading musical instrument manufacturers, marketers and distributors, whose portfolio of brands includes Fender®, Squier®, Gretsch®, Jackson®, EVH®, Charvel® and Groove Tubes®, among others. For more information, visit www.fender.com.
