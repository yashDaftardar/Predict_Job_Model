Description

Under close supervision, maintain the performance of the plant control system (PCS) by identifying and reporting poor performing process areas and process automation components or platforms.
  • Identify and report poor performing processes by having process understanding of hydrometallurgy (leaching, solve extraction, and electro winning) and/or mineral processing (comminution, flotation, and solid/liquid separation)
  • Identify and report poor performing discrete, continuous or advanced control, controller equipment and software issues, and HMI problems
  • Identify and report faulty process instrumentation by having understanding of basic sensors and actuation
  • Identify and report issues with PCS communication and plant historians
  • Identify and report issues with alarm management by having understanding of alarm philosophy and alarm performance metrics
  • Perform other duties as required

Qualifications

Minimum Qualifications
  • Bachelor degree in an appropriate Engineering discipline with no experience required

Preferred

  • Industrial Mining Experience
  • Capable of identifying instrumentation and some electrical field devices
  • Understanding of networking fundamentals
  • Understanding of historical data collection systems
  • Capable of reading and interpreting electrical schematics, P&ID’s, drawings and other technical documentation
  • Able to provide red lined drawings
  • Ability to identify electrical components within an enclosure to work safely
  • Basic understanding of industrial networks and protocols
  • In Depth understanding of how the control system’s actions impact the end user
