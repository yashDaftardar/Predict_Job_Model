Job Responsibilities
The Broadband Terminals Software group is responsible for connecting all kinds of users to Viasat’s world class satellite networks including residential home users in the United States and abroad, airline passengers, government and military personnel, and rural communities. Our team has our hands on many of Viasat’s exciting projects.

As part of this team, you will be involved in integrating the latest software features and automating interactions with the modem software to support continuous deployment to production users. Our team members enjoy working closely with each other utilizing an Agile development methodology which gives you flexibility to work on and learn new aspects of our system. We build and maintain our own Python libraries and scripts, control terminal software deployment to our test bed using tools such as Git, Artifactory, and Jenkins, and monitor software performance in test and in production. Being a member of the Terminals team gives you exposure to a wide variety of Viasat’s projects such as the video streaming partnership with Disney+, hybrid satellite and terrestrial Internet solutions, and the Viasat-3 next generation satellite.

Requirements
  • Bachelor's degree or higher in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Systems Engineering, Networking, Telecommunications and/or a related field
  • Previous coding experience in Python, Java, and/or C/C++
  • Ability to pick up new software, frameworks, and APIs
  • Experience with or desire to learn Linux
  • Strong communication and collaboration skills
  • Ability to travel up to 10%
Preferences
  • Knowledge of signal processing and communication theory
  • Previous experience in automation, software test, or software development
  • Knowledge of Python
  • Knowledge of TCP/IP network fundamentals
  • Knowledge or experience with deployment tools such as Git, Artifactory, Jenkins, and Amazon Web Services
  • Knowledge of wireless communication networks
