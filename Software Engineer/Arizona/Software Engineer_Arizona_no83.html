Full Job Description

We are actively hiring for a Software Engineer, Production Support. We invite you to join us and support CSAA Insurance Group, a AAA Insurer, in achieving our goals.

You will be working with a hardworking group of team members in a setting that features next-generation tools, capabilities, and platforms in both on-prem and cloud-based environments. Most meaningfully, you will have the opportunity to influence our ability to deliver outstanding service to our internal customers that result in the achievement of driving our business forward for another 100 years!

Essential responsibilities:

  • Respond to and resolve production incidents received from internal customers within response/remediation SLA’s

  • Work directly with software developers to understand user issues and provide solutions in real-time

  • Understands the end to end work flow of our enterprise data applications and the systems they integrate with

  • Resolve issues that require immediate solutions if a permanent fix is not immediately available

  • Create/maintain knowledge articles/support documentation to empower IT, support staff, to appropriately manage the customer experience

  • Create/maintain automation and proactive monitoring systems to optimize utilization of our data capabilities and ensure SLA adherence

  • Own a specific production problem and provide active coordination between various groups including internal and external teams

  • Work as an incident commander of production critical calls that require coordination between cross-functional teams

  • Monitor and understand cloud compute cost patterns and make recommendations to reduce overall cloud cost

What would make us excited about you?

  • BS or MS (MS preferred) in Computer Science

  • 3+ years of software engineering experience

  • Experience developing software tools and applications and integrations

  • Experience with programming languages like Java, PHP, and JavaScript

  • Familiarity with IDE and developing plug-ins

  • Familiarity with Cloud Service Providers (AWS preferred)

  • Experience with Linux environments and scripting languages like Shell and Python

  • Experience with SQL and Postman

  • Experience with Software Version Control systems like Git/GitHub

  • Experience with Continuous Integration systems (for example, Jenkins)

  • The ability to handle and prioritize multiple tasks simultaneously

  • The ability to work independently and locate available information

Why join us

  • Make a difference. We don’t just go to work. We’re a team of employees committed to AAA members, our communities and each other.

  • Lifestyle. We drive strategy through innovation. We do honorable work, and we live our core beliefs.

  • Recognition. We offer a total compensation package, performance bonus, 401(k) with a company match, and so much more!
