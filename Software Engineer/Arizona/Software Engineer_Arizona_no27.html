Amazon’s Operations Technology teams are the technical engines that support Amazon’s mission of being Earth’s most customer-centric company. We build the software and services that touch every Amazon customer globally and innovate solutions for logistics, supply chain optimization, and transportation.

As a senior member of our engineering team, you will own:
  • The full stack technology and drive the technical direction
  • You will be solving complex problems, and working on difficult challenges in software engineering as the scale and suite of our services grow
  • You will work with key stake holders in product management and executive teams to define strategy and requirements
  • You will lead cross functional development of new technology from design to deployment
For additional information Operations Technology: https://www.amazon.jobs/en/business_categories/operations-technology

We are seeking Senior/Lead Software Development Engineers to join a variety of teams:

FULFILLMENT and ROBOTICS
We solve complex problems and invent for almost every aspect of fulfillment. We work with ambiguity and large problems, and work in a startup-like environment. Our robotics teams around the world are driving improvements in the safety and autonomy of our worldwide operations. We re-imagine what now looks like.

TRANSPORTATION and DELIVERY
We exceed the expectations of our customers by ensuring that their orders are delivered where and when they need them, as quickly, accurately, and cost effectively as possible. We strive to innovate and provide best-in-class service levels through the introduction of pioneering new designs, products and services. (i.e. Prime Air, Last Mile, Amazon Flex, Delivery Service Partners)

DELIVERY EXPERIENCE
We build and support the technology tools that Amazon businesses use to deliver packages into the hands of our customers. Our technology is used by multiple businesses and product lines within Amazon. We leverage company-wide technology solutions or design them from the ground up. (i.e. Amazon Scout)

INVENTORY MANAGEMENT
We streamline our processes utilizing the latest applications in science, machine learning, and scalable distributed software on the cloud. We optimize inventory and shipments under the ever-changing landscape of demand, pricing and supply.
