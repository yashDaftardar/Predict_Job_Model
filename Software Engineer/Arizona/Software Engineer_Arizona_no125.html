Our mission is to deliver stable, secure, scalable, and innovative services at speeds that delight and satisfy our customers and unleash the skills potential of our employees.

This position will join the e-Business Application Scrum team supporting Home Lending marketing systems by developing and implementing complex internet and intranet applications on one or more platforms. Software Engineers are responsible for the design and development of highly scalable and robust applications, systems and research technologies to solve complex business problems. The role demands ability to significantly contribute at all stages of project lifecycle and strong collaboration. The individual should have deep background in core computer sciences and engineering, with experience in Distributed systems and Services at scale. Work is done in a fast-paced iterative SDLC process using Agile methodologies.

Acts in a role providing technical guidance and consultation with application teams, clients, and Engineering Managers for their technology domain environments including but not limited to cloud, middleware, operating systems, applications, Software-as-a-Service, and Platform-as-a-Service products. Design, code, test, debug and document programs using Agile development practices. Provides the strategy and design for projects associated with their technology domain including, upgrades and deployments. Utilizes advanced technologies and methods to integrate their technology domain with other technology domains, enabling the creation of new or expanded heterogeneous environments. Develops new documentation and departmental technical procedures. Directs the gathering of performance statistics to assist in determining hardware and communication tools needed to support projected transaction volumes, response time and system availability targets. Assures quality, security and compliance requirements are met for supported technology domain.
