Full Job Description

Your Opportunity

Drive the future of our business through the creation of innovative software in New Relic’s Incubator. The fast-paced, get-it-done startup spirit meets the resources and support of an industry-leading, public company in this small, senior team. With the voice of our users to guide us, we create new lines of business and product advancements, taking novel SaaS products and solutions from concept to market. You’ll work in close collaboration with product, engineering, and design partners to build customer-centric solutions, including data processing, API development, complex UX interactions, and novel data visualizations.

We are excited to consider a remote engineer for this role. Remote team members will be encouraged to work out of their home office.

What You’ll Do

You will collaborate closely with the team to understand user needs and define implementation plans. Software development will be a mix of solo and small group work. Our primary stack is Spring-based Java services and React UI using the New Relic One SDK, but we use other languages and frameworks when they're the best fit for a particular need.

Your Qualifications

Must-have:

  • 5+ years full-stack expertise developing high-throughput services in Java and React, or equivalent experience. 10 years strongly preferred.
  • Ability to make technical design decisions based on the unique needs of each project
  • Experience working with large and complex datasets/data structures/APIs to enable highly interactive interfaces.

Nice-to-have:

  • Domain expertise in building and operating web-scale software platforms
  • REST and GraphQL API experience
  • High-throughput streaming data processing; Kafka
  • Familiarity with observability tools / open tracing / modern cloud architectures
  • Understanding of software security best practices for legacy and cloud environments
  • D3 and other data visualization library experience
  • UX and API design
Please note that visa sponsorship is not available for this position.

About Us

New Relic (NYSE: NEWR) is a cloud-based platform that gives developers, engineers, operations, and management a clear view of what’s happening in today’s complex software environments. So they can find and fix problems faster, and deliver delightful experiences for their customers. That's why the world’s best engineering teams rely on New Relic to visualize, analyze, and troubleshoot their software. It’s the simplest, most powerful cloud-based observability platform, built to create more perfect software. All from one place.

Founded in 2008, we’re a global company passionate about building a culture where all employees feel a deep sense of belonging, where every ‘Relic’ can bring their whole self to work and feel supported and empowered to thrive. We’re consistently recognized as a distinguished employer and are committed to building world-class products and an award-winning culture. For more information, visit newrelic.com.
