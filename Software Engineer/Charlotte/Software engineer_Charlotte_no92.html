Job Summary

Are you passionate about creating amazing experiences for developers?


Allstate’s Systems Engineering group is focused on creating products for our global developer community by designing and delivering frictionless creator experiences helping product teams move at the speed of ideas. Developer experience and productivity are absolutely critical, and our goal is to provide Allstate developers with the most powerful tools to create amazing digital products for our customers.


We’re looking for an experienced Senior Managing Software Engineer to expand and lead our North American engineering teams. The portfolio currently includes:

  • Marketplace - Provides a single, well-known destination for the Allstate developer community so that product teams can get started creating and maintaining application infrastructure quickly and efficiency.
  • AppFabric Frameworks and SDKs - “AppFabric makes it easier to rapidly build great products at Allstate” It weaves together Allstate's tools, processes and industry standard frameworks to create a secure, compliant and deployed application in minutes, ready for your ideas.
  • API Central - Provides API Management as a Service enabling Allstate API providers to easily and securely expose data and services to customers via an enterprise centralized API catalog to discover, explore and consume APIs.

As a Senior Managing Software Engineer you will report to the Director of Engineering to build, coach and grow leaders of engineering teams contributing to our developer experience and productivity products. You will lead engineering teams creating an optimized coding experience allowing Allstate’s development community to move at unprecedented speeds. The ideal candidate will combine excellent technical and management skills to create value for our developers and product teams building upon our industry leading technology stack. Key Responsibilities

  • Provide leadership in the design and development of developer experience (DX) product features to increase developer discoverability and productivity.
  • Determine resource needs, develop plans and track progress to those plans; resolve conflicts to ensure timely delivery of features.
  • Provide hands-on leadership to a growing team of software engineers and software engineering managers.
  • Develop your teams by hiring, mentoring, motivating and rewarding team members.
  • Leverage a strong understanding of product, design and business priorities to solve high impact, highly complex problems.
  • Continuous delivery of enhancements for new features and capabilities. This means excellent development practices, careful code architecture creating open and software defined systems, and an organization built around rapid releases.
  • Champion software engineering best practices, including XP, Agile methodology and Scrum framework.


Job Qualifications

  • 5+ years of experience in a software engineering leadership role, managing other leaders and individual contributors working on highly technical products in a fast-paced environment
  • Entrepreneurial with experience shipping modern software
  • Experience operating in an environment driven by KPIs where you had the accountability to determine the best course of action to meet your goals
  • Proven track record of building and managing high performing engineering teams. Clear passion for topics of technology, developer tools and an ability to identify possibilities to innovate in our product space
  • Ability to proactively anticipate problems and seek opportunities in both people management and code
  • Ability to analyze and interpret complex concepts
  • Understanding of and experience working in a development environment including experience with Agile or Agile XP
  • Ability to communicate complex and innovative concepts to a broad range of stakeholders
  • Bachelor's degree in computer science or equivalent
