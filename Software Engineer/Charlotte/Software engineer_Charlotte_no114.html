Full Job Description

You are a driven and motivated problem solver ready to pursue meaningful work. You strive to make an impact every day & not only at work, but in your personal life and community too. If that sounds like you, then you've landed in the right place.

The Hartford’s Claims IT team is seeking a highly motivated Developer in our Charlotte, NC office for a high profile, complex and critical invest program. Successful candidates will be expected to demonstrate strong leadership skills, proficiency in managing matrix technical teams, technical acumen, experience in delivering quality technical solutions as well as setting and executing on a strategic technical vision.

Candidates must have strong technical skills and competency in Java and related frameworks. Candidates should have technical skills on Guidewire technology.

Exposure to Python and exposing Data Science models as REST services is a big plus.

Candidate must have the ability to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment. Successful candidates will have a willingness to understand existing processes and systems, possess strong interpersonal and communication skills and the ability to translate business needs into technical solutions (communication with non-technical team members is as essential as communicating with technical team members). They should be able to make decisions quickly in consultation with team members and subject matter experts and be able to build strong relationships, actively participate in team work and understand the dynamics and critical nature of the business.

Responsibilities of the position include:

Development

  • Function as subject matter expert and advocate for the software applications supported, requiring depth and breadth in their knowledge of the business, technologies, applications, integration and interfaces.
  • Proactively address technical issues and risks that could impact maintenance release schedules and/or financials.
  • Work closely with stakeholders to design and document automation solutions that align with the business needs and are consistent with the architectural vision.
  • Develop code that conforms to The Hartford coding standards and industry best practices.
  • Work closely with development team, BA’s, App managers, QA test leads and architects using Agile methodology
  • Debug and troubleshoot defects.
  • Develop technical documentation for various project deliverables and phases.
  • Provide innovative ideas and process optimization solutions
  • Work in Guidewire product to consume Data Science Services.
  • Added advantage on Claim Center knowledge

Cross Functional Interaction

  • Lead change by influencing and educating Business, IT and Sourcing partners.
  • Facilitate continuity between vendor and HIG partners, other IT Groups and Enterprise Architecture.
  • Work across the organization to provide business process or design recommendations consistent with long term business and IT strategy
  • Identify opportunities to reduce total cost of ownership in the operational application support areas
  • Work closely with the architecture team to ensure that the technical solution designs and implementation are consistent with the architectural vision.
  • Support and consult on disaster recovery and reverse demand planning and testing.
  • Participate in reviews of architectural designs, requirements and test cases.
  • Assist in performance analysis along with application reporting and monitoring.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelors’ degree in Computer Science or related field
  • 3+ years of experience developing web based Java/J2EE application and Restful APIs
  • Hands on experience on developing application using Guidewire
  • Working Knowledge of DevOps pipeline: Github, Jenkins, Nexus, Docker Containers
  • Experience with deploying application to public / private cloud
  • Hands on experience writing code in Python
  • Strong communication, analytical and problem solving skills
  • Strong understanding of Object Oriented design and methodologies
  • Familiar with Agile.
  • Experience working on development teams for large internet based front-end applications
  • Ability to conduct effective code reviews.
  • Hands-on experience, working knowledge and strong technical skills in the following technologies:
    • Two or more years’ experience in Spring Framework.
    • Proficiency in designing, implementing and supporting packaged Vendor products.
    • Application Server, Weblogic, JBoss.
    • SOAP/Restful Web Services, XML and XSD.
    • Database technologies such as Oracle, ORM.
    • JavaScript, AngularJS, AJAX, JQuery, CSS and HTML5.
    • Source Code version tools such as Git Hub.
