Description

Randall-Reilly is a rapidly growing B2B data company that provides strategic services and marketing campaigns for 2,200+ client. Our data products are unique in our markets thanks to our rich, proprietary data with 30 years of historical transactional data. With a capture rate as high as 80%, more than 2,200 customers use us today for market share analytics, lead generation, sales performance assessment and heavy equipment marketplace. Our vision is to optimize their marketing and sales efforts by leveraging predictive lead scoring and digital marketing.

We are looking for a well-rounded Front-end Developer with an eye for design to join our Business Technology team. Our developers design, build, and support new features and upgrades to our enterprise products within the Equipment Division. A developer would be responsible for ensuring the alignment of web design and user experience requirements, optimizing web pages for maximum efficiency, and maintaining brand consistency across all web pages, among other duties.


Some skills that will be necessary to do the job:

  • Thorough grasp of HTML5, CSS (SCSS), JavaScript, jQuery and Responsive Design
  • Experience working with PHP (Laravel framework)
  • Familiarity with leading JavaScript Frameworks (We love Vue)
  • Understanding of SEO practices and the ability to spot mistakes
  • Ability to design and develop components with all screen sizes in mind
  • Comfortability reading and debugging legacy code

And skills that stand out:

  • Familiar with agile environments and SCRUM methodologies
  • Knowledge of Object Oriented Programming and best practices
  • Design skills or UI/UX development experience
  • Experience working with Ads and Google Ad Manager
  • Understanding of git and git workflows
  • Experience using RESTful APIs
  • Debugging, Testing, and Documentation zealotry
  • Experience developing for accessibility (ADA compliance)

Now we’d like to hear from you! We are an open and collaborative team that encourages learning new things and provides the tools to do so, helping you grow and expand your career. We look forward to reading your resume, browsing your Github, and, eventually, beating you at lunchtime board games!

Additional requirements:


Education

  • A bachelor’s degree in computer science, web development, or equivalent experience.

Communication

  • Excellent communication skills with high emotional-intelligence. Written and verbal communication in English.
