Role/Responsibilities

Looking for a new opportunity? Enjoy building technical systems and executing projects on a global basis?
Come join the Unified Communications and Collaboration team at Moody’s!

We have an open position on the UCC engineering team for a Associate Automation Engineer. Our team is
globally distributed, with team members and work colleagues located in AMER, EMEA and APAC regions.

This team is responsible for the following areas:
Video conferencing
Conference room technology
Digital signage
IPTV
Streaming
Collaboration software such as Zoom, Cisco Jabber, Miro Board, Slack, Trello, MS Teams etc. (windows
and Mac)
Mobile collaboration for above mentioned platforms on iOS and Android.

Moody’s UCC team is seeking a Junior Developer / Engineer to join our innovative and growing team. In this role you will work alongside our UCC Engineering & Operations team to design and implement solutions for our services.

Associate Automation Engineers at Moody’s have the opportunity to use cutting-edge technology while working on high performance/low latency systems. Moody’s loves Junior Developers who are self-starters and have the ability to problem solve and think outside of the box; Identify a problem, develop some code to solve that problem & help us “automate the boring stuff”. You’ll also be working on data aggregation projects, “single click” executables, and bots within our tools. We value innovation and hard work and want you to make an impact in the firm. Whether you are interested in Video conferencing, Collaboration tools, Instant messaging, performance engineering, hardware, data capture and analysis, or front-end user interfaces, there's work to be done. If you are excited to jump in and make a difference, Moody’s could be the place for you.

Key technical skills which applicants should have a strong demonstrable background in:
Ability to build a story board for software roadmaps & updates
Ability to build a roadmap for End User Experience in each of the afore mentioned tools & technical areas.
(How do we get from A -> B, and all the steps in-between, for example)
Experience using APIs and creating bots that generate value for technical and non-technical end users,
including block kit.
Experience with programming languages — e.g., PHP, Python, Ruby, Go, C, or Java (used with and without
frameworks)
Identify bugs, test, reproduce, report, and work with the engineers on other teams to assist with a fix and
test/verify fix versions
Ability to develop code / scripts from the ground up, not relying on outsourced materials, and package the
code / script into a simple executable.
Ability to write technical specifications for the code developed, what its doing, when, and why, etc.

Qualifications

Ability to prioritize and work under pressure is essential.
Be able to work on own initiative and accept accountability.
Strong organizational skills
Have the aptitude to ‘lead by example’.
Have the tenacity to challenge requirements.
Able to work in corporate environment managing project status reporting
Able to firefight and triage day to day challenges that occur.
Strong desire to learn and be trained.
Project management skills to manage multiple concurrent system deployments

Additional
WORKWEEK & HOURS:
40 hours - Mon – Fri. Late shift is a possible requirement at times, must be flexible to work
additional hours including weekends as required in order to meet project deliverables.
Perform all other duties and responsibilities as required by the immediate manager/supervisor.
