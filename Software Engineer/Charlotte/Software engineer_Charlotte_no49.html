Our mission is to deliver stable, secure, scalable, and innovative services at speeds that delight and satisfy our customers and unleash the skills potential of our employees.


It's an exciting time to be a part of Wells Fargo's Middle Marketing Banking Technology team. This newly created position will help shape and transform Wells Fargo's technology landscape. As an experienced Java engineer in Wells Fargo's Technology group, you will be responsible for designing, building and deploying complex solutions using best of breed technologies built on cloud based services running on Java and supported by Redhat JBPM for business processes, rules and complex event processing.

Your primary responsibilities will involve working with the broader technology teams for integration with multiple platforms along with the engineering teams to leverage enterprise CI/CD platforms and code scanning & security testing platforms.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE:

  • Design, build, and optimize highly scalable data business process driven solutions to support our Middle Market banking business needs across multiple lines of business and markets.
  • Actively participate in the banks journey to microservices architecture to deliver reliability, scalability, security and speed to market.
  • Contribute to the evaluation of new software techniques and technologies, providing user
testing and preparation of results.
