Help us build a better Wells Fargo. It all begins with outstanding talent. It all begins with you.

Technology sets IT strategy; enhances the design, development, and operations of our systems; optimizes the Wells Fargo infrastructure; provides information security; and enables Wells Fargo global customers to have 24 hours a day, 7 days a week banking access through in-branch, online, ATMs, and other channels.

Our mission is to deliver stable, secure, scalable, and innovative services at speeds that delight and satisfy our customers and unleash the skills potential of our employees.

The Foreign Exchange Technology within Corporate Commercial and Investment Banking Technology (CCIB) builds and maintains multiple applications and services that customers, Traders Internal Sales users for Foreign Exchange payments. This position is part of a team cross functional Agile team that design, develop, test and support these complex applications. As part of this team, you will act as a key Senior Engineer to design, develop and test utilizing a thorough understanding of applicable technology, tools and existing designs. Analyze highly complex business requirements, design and write technical specifications to build complex computer platforms and applications. Provide help to less experienced engineers and develop highly efficient and quality code according to Wells Fargo coding standard. Assure quality, security and compliance requirements are met and contribute to creation of or updates to and testing of the business continuation plan.


Required Qualifications


  • 5+ years of software engineering experience
  • 7+ years of application development experience
  • 3+ years of Agile experience
  • 5+ years of Java experience
  • 5+ years of JavaScript development experience
  • 5+ years of JavaScript framework experience (YUI, Angular, Node)
  • 3+ years of Multi-Threading Experience
  • 5+ years of database experience with 2 or more of the following platforms: Oracle, DB2, SQL Server, MongoDB
  • 3+ years of Spring framework experience such as Spring Boot
  • 1+ year of Pivotal Cloud Foundry (PCF) experience
  • 2 + years of experience in one or a combination of the following Enterprise Pipeline Tools: uDeploy (Urbancode Deploy), TFS (Team Foundation Server) GIT, Sonar, Fortify, Blackduck
  • 3+ years of UI (User Interface) experience using in one or a combination of the following: Angular, React JS


Desired Qualifications


  • An industry-standard technology certification
  • Strong verbal, written, and interpersonal communication skills
  • Knowledge and understanding of application and web security
  • Knowledge and understanding of API programming with Perl, XML, REST or JSON
  • Knowledge and understanding of Selenium software testing framework
  • Experience with financial services businesses in a regulated environment


Other Desired Qualifications
