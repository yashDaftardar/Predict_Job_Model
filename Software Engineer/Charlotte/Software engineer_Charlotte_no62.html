Full Job Description

You are a driven and motivated problem solver ready to pursue meaningful work. You strive to make an impact every day & not only at work, but in your personal life and community too. If that sounds like you, then you've landed in the right place.

As a Senior Software Engineer, you will be a hands-on lead engineer in our journey to automate data science life cycle. You will be a part of our Personal Lines Data Science organization which is looking to strengthen their software development skills. Your leadership will be essential to the success of our agile team as we automate the process. To be a senior engineer on our team, you need to love building great solutions. That means we expect our senior engineers to think big, plan and model solutions. Keep in mind your customers could be Data Engineers and Data Scientists. You will think object oriented, scalable design, write awesome software and help other engineers do the same.


Responsibilities:

  • Can be located anywhere in the United States 100% remote (permanently).
  • Have a keen eye for automation. Perform hands-on multi-tier application development using Python, PySpark and SQL.
  • Responsible for CI/CD, Design, test, development, deploy, and maintenance.
  • Consistent work with AWS.
  • Advocate for S.O.L.I.D principles and their application and have the ability to span the full-stack whenever necessary.
  • Proactively identify, analyze/research and address technical issues/defects along with risks that could impact projects, enhancements or an applications stability and provide solutions to quickly resolve them.
  • Responsible for technical deliverables through the software development lifecycle, including the validation of the completeness of estimates, quality and the accuracy of technical designs, build deployment and implementation.
  • Develop technical documentations for various project deliverables and phases.
  • Mentoring less experienced engineers.
  • Work with a highly skilled agile team to create/advance automation.
  • Willingness and proven ability to learn new technologies/methodologies and apply them.
  • Writing tests that are automatable and can provide broad coverage of implemented functionality.


Minimum requirements:

  • Must be authorized to work in the US now and in the future
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related discipline.
  • 6+ years of work experience in building large scale applications.
  • Strong experience with Python.
  • Experience building and deploying CICD pipelines.
  • Exposure to cloud platforms.
  • Experience working with Git or other version control.
  • Experience working with SQL.


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Pyspark
  • Docker
  • Kubernettes
