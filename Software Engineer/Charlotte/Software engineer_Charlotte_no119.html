Overview:

Celanese is a Fortune 500 global chemical innovation company that engineers and manufactures chemicals used in products essential to everyday living. Headquartered in Dallas, TX, we employ over 7,700 dedicated people working between 43 facilities across 18 countries. We are committed to employee growth and creating shareholder value to ensure continued opportunities make a difference. Our company focuses on the safety of its employees, provides competitive compensation (including benefits starting day 1), and emphasizes giving back to the community. For more information about Celanese and our products please visit www.celanese.com


The industry’s leading Celanese Automotive Team is looking for a Senior Application Development Engineer. Your primary responsibility of this role is to drive growth of Celanese plastic’s products in automotive applications for interior, exterior, power-train, electrical and thermal systems. This new opportunity to join our growing team is offered remotely (with travel) targeting to be based our of the Southeast region (NC, SC, AL, GA)

Responsibilities:
  • Drive growth at new and existing automotive customers
    • Position and secure new business with Celanese solutions in targeted applications
    • Develop deep customer relationships and understand their needs for plastic solutions
    • Prospect new customers to facilitate growth of Celanese solutions
  • Maximize value capture in securing new business
    • Understand application requirements, understand next best alternative solutions and position optimal Celanese solutions
    • Manage external and internal requirements throughout the development process – i.e., milestones, testing, qualifications
  • Internally champion automotive systems
    • Use market knowledge to foresee trends that will enable growth of Celanese solutions
    • Communicate & influence across the organizational
Qualifications:
  • 6+ years in plastics, automotive or related industry
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering, Science or Technical Field - MS or MBA a plus
  • Technical experience required and business/market/application development experience helpful
  • Established experience and network within the automotive plastics industry
  • Ability to work with OEM and Tier customers to develop plastic solutions
  • Customer Focus and Urgency
  • Ability to apply technical knowledge to solve new problems
  • Ability to draw connections to foresee future trends and to develop opportunities to grow and innovate
  • Ability to interact, influence and facilitate at several levels of the organization
  • Must be willing to prospect for new customers/projects and develop strategies for identifying, targeting new customers
  • Structured, organized and proactive with a strong focus on results
  • Effective verbal and written communication skills
  • Microsoft office skills, Sales Force experience helpful
  • Willingness to travel 50-75%
