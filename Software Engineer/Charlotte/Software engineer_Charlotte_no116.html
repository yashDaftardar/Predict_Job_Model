About SCOR

SCOR, the 4th largest reinsurer in the world, provides insurance companies with a diversified and innovative range of solutions and services to control and manage risk. Using its experience and expertise, “ The Art & Science of Risk ”, SCOR provides cutting-edge financial solutions, analytics tools and services in all areas related to risk – in Life & Health insurance as well as in P&C insurance. Our specialist teams operate in over 120 countries, developing value added and innovative products and services and making long-term commitments to their clients, namely insurers and large corporations.
SCOR's aim, as an independent global reinsurance company, is to develop its Life and P&C business lines, to provide its clients with a broad range of innovative reinsurance solutions and to pursue an underwriting policy founded on profitability, supported by effective risk management and a prudent investment policy, in order to offer its clients an optimum level of security, to create value for its shareholders, and to contribute to the welfare and resilience of Society by helping to protect insureds against the risks they face.

Job Summary

This role supports the growing Underwriting Innovations cloud environment primarily through coordination with third party service providers and internal stakeholders and through direct involvement in deploying and maintaining solutions used to meet business objectives. Skills and experience in cloud architecture and programming combined with an ability to help achieve business outcomes with technical solutions make this a role that prepare us well for our digital future.

-Key duties and responsibilities

  • In coordination with senior team members or within an appropriate range of discretion, d esign, develop and maintain standards, best practices, and accompanying tooling, for the services and infrastructure which support the Underwriting Innovations environment (including Velogica).
  • Identify, propose, and implement opportunities to improve the efficiency, effectiveness, and security of delivery solutions . Facilitate communication and knowledge sharing of those opportunities between the relevant teams.
  • Leverage automation and repeatable “Infrastructure as code” (for example, creating and versioning CloudFormation templates for building out Velogica environments based on the requirements of the Infrastructure team).
  • Support the needs of Underwriting Innovation department, including:
    • Design and implement managed infrastructure changes required by project work.
    • Review, approve and execute high level and detailed technical plans.
    • Provide back up support for architectural reviews and code reviews as necessary.
  • Responsible for participating in production support rotation coverage in coordination with 3rd party support provider (including availability to support early Sunday morning patching schedule).
  • Foster and participate in a culture of continuous improvement and psychological safety.
  • Adhere to all Information Security policies and best practices, including security awareness training and other information protection initiatives.
  • Create and maintain process documentation as required.
  • Participate in audit evidence gathering.
