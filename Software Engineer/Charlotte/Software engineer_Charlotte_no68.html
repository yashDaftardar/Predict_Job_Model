The Software Engineer will be responsible for the development and level 3 support of business process automation built in BluePrism 6.3 and UIPath 2019.x

The Software Engineer will be part of development team in a platform support group {internally} facing role, to drive delivery of new automated processes as well as managing the changes required for the automations for our Wells Fargo Technology team. The team member will ensure consistency and synchronization across various builds, delivered codes and updated information on all the changes in progress.

The ideal candidate will need at least 7+ years of experience in delivery functions, 3+ years of experience in robotic automation delivery. The ideal candidate will also work on performance optimization, robotic process automation and maintenance by following operational procedures for maintaining technology as required.
