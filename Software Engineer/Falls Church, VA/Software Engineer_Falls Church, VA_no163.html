Full Job Description

As an engineer on Strider’s Platform Team, your job is to define and drive simple, efficient software and systems design, taking hard problems, and finding efficient solutions to power our industry-leading platform. You will also provide leadership and guidance to establish best practices for engineering teams.

We’re looking for someone who is passionate about technology, stays on top of the latest trends within the field, and loves to engage with the developer community.

Responsibilities

  • Leverage Python, Java, JavaScript, and other modern programming frameworks, languages, and tools to build scalable, secure software.
  • Collaborate with subject matter experts to build tools which automate and enhance the collection and analysis of vast amounts of data.
  • Build out and iterate on the Strider data model, including optimizing on the “right” data store.
  • Work on a wide range of interesting technical and business projects.
  • Investigate, prototype, and implement new solutions to a wide array of complex system design challenges.
  • Develop and maintain software using established best practices for creating readable and maintainable code.

Qualifications

  • 2+ years of experience in software engineering
  • Ability to work alongside a team of engineers and subject matter experts to determine effective and time-efficient solutions
  • Ability to manage time efficiently and focus on clear goals
  • A pragmatic approach towards tackling unforeseen challenges
  • Knowledge of web spider technologies
  • Experience with Python
  • Experience with Java and/or C++
  • Experience with JavaScript
  • Experience with Unix-like operating systems
  • Experience with Elasticsearch
  • Experience building cloud-native solutions (AWS, Azure)
  • Experience building containerized solutions
  • Experience providing in-depth code reviews
  • Infinite appetite for learning
  • Experience with Scrum methodologies and remote teams is a plus!
