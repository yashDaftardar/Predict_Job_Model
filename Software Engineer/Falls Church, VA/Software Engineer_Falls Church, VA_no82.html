Full Job Description

If you're a Mid-Level Software Developer or a recent graduate with a degree in Computer Science or similar technical degree and have at least 2 years of experience, this position is for you. Learn from senior developers as you work on new software development projects. We're seeking the best and brightest up and coming software developers that can learn new skills quickly.

You will develop custom applications utilizing technologies such as C#, .Net, Java, REST API, SQL, AJAX, XML, JavaScript, jQuery, JSON, and Web Services. You will work with a team of developers and business analysts to design, develop, and implement solutions based on customer requirements. Design and develop in an Agile software development methodology. The work is challenging and fast paced and includes lots of new development. Training and mentoring will be provided to ensure you acquire and master the necessary skills required.

This is an excellent opportunity to build your career by learning from senior developers in an environment that will foster your growth. Don't let this opportunity pass you by. Please apply so you can find out more.
