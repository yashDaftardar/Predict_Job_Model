Full Job Description

Overview:
Ace Info Solutions, a Dovel company, is currently seeking a Mid-Level Automation Test Engineer to join our team working REMOTELY.

The mid-level candidate is responsible for the design, development and execution of automated tests for a distributed enterprise application with external and internal interfaces utilizing Microservices and business automation patterns to modernize an existing system from the ground up. As an active participant on a scrum team, the candidate is responsible for all aspects of quality assurance. The candidate must be proficient with automation and testing tools. This role works closely with software engineers, fellow QA engineers, DevOps engineers, and security professionals. Flexibility to work collaboratively and knowledgeably with team members of each area is vital. The candidate documents and works to resolve problems, reports progress on problem resolution, devises improvements to current procedures, and develops models of possible future configurations. The mid level candidate will focus on the automation for testing of the software built on a new technology stack using industry standard tools. It is essential that this candidate have exceptional communication skills and be flexible with technology, environment and priorities.
Duties & Responsibilities:
  • Design, develop, and execute automated tests.
  • Extension of existing test automation frameworks and tools .
  • Integrating with CI/CD tools and pipeline (i.e. Jenkins).
  • Triage, troubleshoot and document defects.
  • Document QA procedures for team.
  • Work closely with engineering and QA to manage and improve the development pipeline.
  • Perform and write automated functional, system, regression, and integration testing.
