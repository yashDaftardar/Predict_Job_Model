Position Summary:

Handles all technical duties associated with cloud computing, including design, planning, maintenance, management, and support.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
Duties include, but not limited to:
  • Provide systems engineering support for large-scale systems, and interfacing systems within a cloud or virtual environment
  • Operates within enterprise architecture objectives and stakeholder requirements to deliver well scoped solutions.
  • Manages and prioritizes multiple tasks simultaneously in a deadline driven environment and thrive in a fast-paced, customer service and collaborative team environment.
  • Applies a variety of business and compliance frameworks to existing and proposed technology solutions.
  • Communicates, engages, and collaborates with business stakeholders throughout the solutions development lifecycle.
  • Translates business needs into technology solutions and then develop, build, and test those solutions utilizing best practice methodologies.
  • Bring strong curiosity for new technology, willing to try to understand, utilize, and deploy new creative solutions and technologies.
  • Assists development teams with designing new applications with a “cloud-native” approach to application architecture

Requirements:
Minimum Requirements
  • Must have 5-7 years formal IT experience consisting of 3-5 years of experience as a Cloud Administrator of virtualization resources and services, Microsoft Azure, Microsoft 365 and must be familiar with of Scripting and Automation and 1-3 years developing cloud solutions.
  • Must have 1-3 years supporting corporate, private sector, back-office IT infrastructure and be able to demonstrate flexibility with technological adoption and working environments.
  • Solid understanding of PaaS, IaaS solutions and the evolution of solutions to more to the cloud versus simple lift & shift approaches. Ability to transform from traditional IaaS infrastructure to utilize PaaS offerings.

Desired Experience
  • Hands on experience with Microsoft Government Community Cloud offerings (GCC or GCC High).
  • Direct experience with Docker container implementation and configuration
  • Associates degree in computer science or other technology realms
  • Posses relevant Microsoft certifications (e.g. AZ-104, AZ-900, MS-900).
