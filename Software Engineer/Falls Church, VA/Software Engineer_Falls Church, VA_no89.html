AEGIS.net, Inc. is currently seeking Software Testers skilled in functional testing with experience testing financial management systems (e.g., CGI Momentum) . This is a great opportunity to take your testing skills to the next level. We are searching for candidates with the following qualifications:

General Skills, Knowledge and Abilities

  • 3 years job experience and Bachelor's degree

  • Experience analyzing requirements and identifying functional test cases
