What’s your missio n?

IronNet’s mission is simple: To deliver the power of collective cybersecurity to defend companies, sectors, and nations. For decades, companies have been defending against cyber attacks on their own while adversaries have been organizing themselves into sophisticated hacker networks … until now, with IronNet Collective Defense. In 2014, General (Ret) Keith Alexander, former Commander U.S. Cyber Command, launched IronNet to strengthen cybersecurity defense against highly sophisticated adversaries, across all borders and sectors.

In response to cyber adversaries who increasingly collaborate for collective offense, leading organizations in our critical infrastructure are using collective defense strategies and solutions to meet these powerful and ever-changing threats. We believe that collective defense is our collective responsibility and we are leading the charge.

The Opportunity

IronNet seeks a talented Software Engineer to join our Digital Detect team. We’ll provide the challenges, you provide proven technical expertise. You’ll work with a dynamic team of talented engineers to help lead the transformation of our IronNet products to a multi-tenant SaaS system.

You must be confident in your abilities yet agreeable to work with. You will have proven experience building large scale, high volume, data ingest and processing platforms running in the cloud.

To be successful in this role you must be able to . . .

  • Demonstrated experience developing large distributed systems deployed at scale in the cloud
  • Automate the processes you develop
  • Keep a broad vision in mind while making detailed technical decisions
  • Interact sociably with teammates yet be able to defend your design
  • Focus on quality

You may be the person we need if your background aligns with the following . . .

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Math, or a related field or comparable experience
  • Demonstrated and progressive software engineering and architecture experience throughout your career
  • Strong skills with more than one programming language including at least one of Go, Python, Java, or Scala
  • Knowledge of and experience with AWS services like EC2, API Gateway, Lambda, DynamoDB, RDS, SQS, SNS, Kinesis, Glue, Cognito, IAM, CloudFormation
  • Expertise with databases and data modeling for both RDBMS and NoSQL
  • Understanding of the value and importance of ongoing testing and testability in building quality solutions
  • Ability to make and justify decisions and choices in a world where there is no single perfect solution to any problem
  • Insatiable desire to learn and employ new software development tools, techniques, and programming languages as required
  • Ability to effectively convey ideas through discussion and documentation for audiences across the organization and at various levels

Any of the following would be a big plus . . .

  • Programmatic AdTech experience
  • AWS Certification
  • Master’s Degree in Computer Science or Math
  • Understand the difference between “Agile” and “agile”
  • Have read both “The Mythical Man-Month” AND “Peopleware”

We recognize that few candidates meet every requirement. We hire smart people and expect them to grow and learn and that many skills are transferable. If your resume doesn’t match all of the requirements, apply, but please address any skill gaps in a cover letter-our recruiting team and hiring managers are not as knowledgeable about you as you are.

More About IronNet:

IronNet delivers unmatched collective cyber threat detection for enterprise on-premise, cloud, and hybrid networks. We do this through the application of advanced behavioral analytics, AI, and machine learning techniques. Our team combines the tradecraft knowledge of the best offensive and defensive cyber operators in the world with world-class mathematicians and data scientists to engineer solutions that empower companies to defend against advanced threats.

Our founder and Co-CEO, General (Ret) Keith Alexander , is a recognized cybersecurity innovator and a frequent speaker about current cyberthreats and effective defenses. We have a leadership team with deep government and commercial cyber experience, and the company is advised by a board of esteemed security and venture investment professionals, including Jan Tighe Retired Vice Admiral, Former Deputy Chief of Naval Operations for Information Warfare and Director, Naval Intelligence, US Navy; and Jack Keane Chairman, Institute for the Study of War, Retired Four-Star General, Former Vice Chief of Staff, US Army.
