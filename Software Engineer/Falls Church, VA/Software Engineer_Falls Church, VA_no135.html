About QOMPLX:

Developer of enterprise operating system designed to support the development and management of decision platforms in virtually any business domain. The company's system delivers customers advanced detection and monitoring, adaptive response, and risk optimization capabilities against advanced attackers, enabling clients to quickly and easily integrate all of the disparate data sources across the enterprise into a unified analytics infrastructure to make better decisions.


Job Summary

As a Software Engineer, you will be responsible for designing and developing Services/Daemons/Drivers that enable information gathering and monitoring of system and network events. This work includes the design, development, and Tier 3 support across a variety of platforms and operating systems. This team member will also leverage their expertise to provide supporting inputs to overall architecture, data modeling, analytic use cases, operationalization, and product discussions around the potential and feasibility of future features.

The position is highly technical requiring comprehensive, kernel-level knowledge of operating systems and significant experience with protocol level network programming. Our ideal candidate will have a strong work ethic, fantastic attitude and be comfortable addressing new challenges on a daily basis. We provide significant flexibility and autonomy to team members, have high expectations and expect everyone to contribute meaningfully to our broader collective goals.

Responsibilities

  • You will be working with functionally diverse teams and individuals to collaboratively design, develop, support and maintain software applications.
  • Design applications as part of a multi-discipline team for Cyber, Insurance, and Finance offerings.
  • Collaborate with cross functional teams on efforts to maximize performance and minimize customer system impact
  • Write exceptional code in either C/C++ or C# to meet Product team’s requirements, including development of all automated unit tests.
  • Conduct root cause analyses of performance and instability of systems and designs with respect to accuracy and performance.

What you bring to the team

  • Experience with network programming, Windows Services & Drivers, and Linux Drivers & Daemons
  • Protocol-level knowledge of programming (OSI 2, 3, 4)
  • Expert-level understanding of authorization and authentication systems in computing environments
  • A positive attitude around learning new skills and capabilitiesAs a Senior Software Developer specializing in Windows OS, you will be tasked with building and maintaining best-of-breed software including Services, NDIS drivers & filters, and kernel drivers for industry-leading security tools as part of our development team in our established Cyber group. QOMPLX focuses on a strong customer engagement model and feedback process to ensure our products are designed to deliver value to our customers. When new product ideas are identified, our Product Engineering teams are responsible for designing, developing, testing, and deploying the products from the ground up, while continuously iterating with Product Management, customers, and SMEs for feedback and input.
  • This role requires an experienced MS Windows software engineer who can develop the necessary code/results with little guidance
  • Building and maintaining QOMPLX products as part of our agile development team
  • Working on industry leading and innovative solutions to real customer problems
  • Focusing on a strong customer engagement model and feedback process to ensure our products are designed the right way from the beginning while working with product management for feedback and input


Qualifications

  • Experience developing / interoperating kernel code for security tools
  • A focus on both quality and performance of the products you produce
  • Demonstrates sound judgment for balancing between rapid development, and long-term code
  • Expert knowledge of C/C++ with Windows Drivers / Filters / Services.
  • Communicates effectively, both technically and non-technically, both written and oral
  • Supports the need for writing automated tests as part of development
  • Skilled debugger who can put out fires under pressure when necessary
  • Resolve technical issues through diligent research, reproduction, and troubleshooting.
  • Work to resolve internal network service issues on AWS and/or Azure
  • Work with our Support and Operations teams as an escalation point for both cloud and customer on-prem network issues.
  • Mentor and deliver training to other technical staff in Network engineering and troubleshooting topics.
  • Work closely with software engineering teams to improve and recommend features, maintainability and reliability of network services
    Identify, gather, analyze and automate responses to key performance metrics, logs, and alerts
  • Ensure compliance with Federal, DoD and Commercial based security standards
  • Both support and conduct post-mortem meetings to analyze failures and prevent future occurrence
  • Designing, developing and testing new product ideas and/or optimizations of current solutions
  • Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience, CS / Engineering Degree preferred
