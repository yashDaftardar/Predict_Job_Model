ECS is seeking a Software Tester/Automation Engineer to work in our Fairfax, VA office.

Job Description:
We are a rapidly growing company that considers our employees to be our most important asset. People truly are number one at ECS. We are situated in Fairfax, VA - well positioned for most commutes in the Northern Virginia area. Our newly renovated office spaces are first class and provide an inspirational environment. We offer opportunities for telework and have some of the most interesting advanced IT business in the DC area. ECS has extraordinary customers who are thought and action leaders in the AI/ML field. We offer highly competitive compensation and benefits to the right person for this key position.

ECS is looking for candidates to aid in the development and testing of an enterprise data system. This position will work as a tester on an agile scrum team. This position will be responsible for ensuring quality of software releases through formulation of test strategies/plans and development and execution of automated tests using tools such as SonarQube, Selenium, Cucumber/Java, Jmeter, Elastic's Java Testing Framework, and JAWS/Dragon Naturally Speaking. Testing work will include a mix of automated and manual testing covering security, unit, functional, performance, and 508 compliance testing. The position will focus on increasing automated test coverage of the platform, requirements coverage, and automated traceability and reporting. The candidate will work closely with Technology Lead, Beta Users, and Developmental Security Operational Engineers to automate build/test/deploy processes from Dev->Test->Pro. The successful candidate will bring experience automating and running tests in an agile environment and working with customers to validate that the solution meets requirements.

  • Software Tester and Test Automation Engineer for an enterprise big data and analytics platform that utilizes emerging technology to ingest data in real-time at extreme volumes and high velocity; Full development life cycle and production support / application maintenance in Agile rapid development environment.
  • Perform manual and automated testing of web applications and back-end data processes for a big data and analytics platform
  • Work with architects and developers to define test approach, prepare test data, develop manual or automated test scripts using automated testing tools and frameworks (e.g. UFT), and validate that software meets requirements / acceptance criteria.
  • Work with Business Analyst to define and execute functional end-user test cases
  • Execute automated regression testing.
  • Support production deployment and validations.
  • Support production application - perform data quality checks; investigate production inquiries and issues.
  • Work on sprint team in agile, rapid development and deployment environment.
  • Communicate status to team lead / project oversight.
  • Continually strive to automate and streamline operations and maintenance processes by identifying areas of improvement, writing automation scripts, and documentation.

Required Skills:

  • Must be a US Citizen
  • Must have the ability to obtain a Secret clearance
  • Bachelor's Degree in a related field
  • 3-5 years of relevant experience
  • Familiarity with:
    • HP UFT
    • SQL or other structured query languages
    • Software testing
    • Agile development methodology
  • Strong communications skills (both verbal & written)
  • Ability to work and communicate with all levels in team structure
  • Team player with the ability to prioritize and multi-task, work in a fast-paced environment, and effectively manage time

Desired Skills:

  • Active Secret clearance
  • Transactional and BI reporting systems (e.g. OLAP and data warehouse)
  • Big Data systems and data analytics
