Want to help bridge the gap between graphical design and technical implementation to define how an application works and functions? Do you thrive in a creative, fast-paced and dynamic environment alongside other developers, designers, and ministries?

THIS IS US:
We're a growing, multisite, Bible-based community in the greater Louisville area with a passion for connecting people to Jesus & one another. We value:

  • Connection over production (John 15:5).
  • Risk & adventure over safety & comfort (John 3:8).
  • Innovation over tradition (Luke 5:19).
  • Personal engagement over attendance (Luke 9:62).
  • Expressive worship over religious rituals (Luke 7:38).
DOES THIS SOUND LIKE THIS YOU?

You'll be slaying it after 6 months if: You're passionate about turning business/ministry requirements into technical solutions that delight end users.
You should keep walking if: You don't have a good eye for design or an online portfolio.

WHAT YOU'LL ACTUALLY BE DOING:
  • Develop and maintain Southeast's web and mobile presence
  • Work with designers to plan new features of public facing assets
  • Coordinate with backend developers to complete complex projects
  • Stay current in front end technologies and solutions
  • Write and extend consistent design libraries
  • Support all quality initiatives that are implemented during each phase of the development life cycle
  • Develop and maintain strong working relationships with other developers, ministries, and product owners
OUR MUST-HAVES:
  • Excellent communication and writing skills
  • Experience developing dynamic front end solutions
  • Skilled with HTML(5), CSS(3), and JavaScript
  • Experience with CSS and JavaScript libraries (Bootstrap, JQuery, VueJs, etc.)
  • Familiarity with responsive design, SASS/LESS, Git, Agile
  • Ability to work independently as well as on a team and manage multiple task assignments
  • Experience with Adobe Suite is a plus (especially photoshop)
  • Engaged member of Southeast (or willing to become one) who supports our mission, submits to the leadership established by the church, and lives out our church mantra in tangible ways.
If this sounds like you, apply today and attach your online portfolio!
