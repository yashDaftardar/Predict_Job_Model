Full Job Description

As well as working for one of the best technology companies in the world, under the leadership of the best CEO (voted by Glassdoor), working on the VMC on AWS team will help build out the next stage career journey.

Insights into your Role:

The VMware Cloud on AWS Availability team is part of our Cloud Platform Business Unit. This R&D organization is responsible for vSphere, our Core Virtualization Infrastructure, and Solutions for Enterprise Virtualization, Cloud initiatives.

As a member of Availability team, you will architect, design and build software to provide Resiliency, Availability and MultiAZ features for virtual machines and containers in the cloud.

At VMWare innovation is fostered and encouraged. Come join the VMware Cloud on AWS Availability team and be a part of a team that will help build out the next stage career journey.

Responsibilities:
As a member of the VMC Availability team you’ll be part of a bright engineering team that is working on some of cutting-edge Cloud features. If you have a real passion for building cloud systems that are highly scalable, manageable, and fault tolerant, then you will fit right in.

You’ll create innovative solutions and solve complex problems and take ownership of meaningful, big-picture projects to springboard an impactful career. Become immersed in all aspects of our innovative and collaborative culture, and ensure you get the full VMware experience. You’ll interact with industry thought leaders at one of our world-class campuses and enjoy networking, community service, and career development events.


You’ll be responsible for:
  • Design and development of Availability solutions for VMware hybrid cloud
  • Using Agile methodologies to develop and test the new features
  • Collaborate with other team members to support the lifecycle of new features
  • Fostering a culture of innovation within the team
  • Leading and delivering cross team projects

Required Skills:
  • MS or PhD in Computer Science, or equivalent degree and at least 2-3 years of experience working on similar industry technologies
  • Strong coding skills in working with Java programming
  • Must be motivated, detail-oriented, and the ability to thrive in a team environment
  • Strong analytical skills, good judgment and passion for your work
  • Industry experience involving significant design and development
  • Experience with Cloud based services, Microservices, Cloud Computing or similar experience
  • Experience developing distributed, multithreaded applications
  • Good teamwork and communication skills

Preferred skills:
  • Exposure to container technologies, orchestrators, Kubernetes, Mesos, Docker Swarm Mode, Rabbit MQ is highly desirable
  • Ability to work independently as well as in small teams
  • Solid verbal and written communication skills
  • Cross-platform experience is desirable
  • Knowledge of distributed systems, availability zones/fault domains
