Who We Are:

Do you want to work for a company named to the Best Places to Work in Rubber and Plastics? Zeon Chemicals is the leading developer and supplier of innovative polymers, including synthetic elastomers and specialty chemicals and other selected products used in countless applications worldwide. We develop superior elastomers for the hottest parts of automotive engines, polymers for longer lasting laser printer rolls, synthetic rubber to survive oil wells' most aggressive fluids and cyclo-olefin plastics for today's advanced medical and electronic applications.

Our team is outstanding at what they do, and we enjoy a great company culture and work environment! This combination truly makes us a best place to work!


Purpose:

The Applications Development team at ZEON provides front-line technical support for ZEON’s customers. This team engages with ZEON’s customers from early stage polymer and compound recommendations thru formulation/recipe recommendations before transitioning to mixing, processing, and trouble-shooting support for our customers. Working together with ZEON’s sales team and Product/Business Managers, the Applications Development team works internally and with ZEON’s customers to advance technical projects at customers from discussion and sampling phases to mass production.


Essential Functions:

1. Technical support to ZEON’s customer base:

  • Obtains deep understanding of customer requirements via 1:1 discussion with technical staff of customers, interaction with ZEON’s sales team, and discussion with OEMs/Tier 1s
  • Makes recommendations of ZEON polymer(s) and start-point rubber formulations (compounds) suitable to meet customer specification, processing, and end application requirements
  • Evaluates performance of formulation recommendations via ZEON’s full-service Tech Service center to simulate customer processes + applications
  • Attends trials (virtually and in-person) at customers
  • First point of contact for technical troubleshooting

2. Technical promotion:

  • Prepares + leads technical webinars for current and prospective customers
  • Makes technical presentations at industry technical conferences
  • Builds technical relationship with OEMs and Tier 1s to influence specifications that favor ZEON polymers

3. Product Development + Product Improvements:

  • Designs + executes testing of prototype Polymers from ZEON’s R&D team with focus on considerations of end application and customer processing.
  • Works directly with ZEON’s R&D team and Operations team to evaluate product/process changes for technical impact on customers
  • Provides technical input for ZEON’s StageGate process

4. Customer Relations:

  • Displays comfort and effectiveness interfacing with technical staff of ZEON’s customers whether in a 1:1 setting or when joining with other ZEON staff
  • Builds a strong network of technical contacts at allied suppliers + equipment makers


Minimum Job Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s in Chemistry or Another Related Field;
  • 7 Years of Related Experience
