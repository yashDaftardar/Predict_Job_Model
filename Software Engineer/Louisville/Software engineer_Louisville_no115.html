Full Job Description

We are actively recruiting for Software Developers to join our growing application development team. You will have the opportunity to design, code and test software with cutting edge technology to take our business and industry forward.

About Us

The Rawlings Group provides payment integrity services and claims data analysis for health insurance carriers and their members. We work behind the scenes to make sure that members are receiving the full value of their coverage by ensuring that claims are appropriately paid. Providing payment integrity and recovery services to the industry creates a more equitable distribution of healthcare dollars so members can receive the care they need when they need it most.

We offer opportunities for growth and development, continuing education, internal mobility and promotions, and unrivaled income earning potential. We are looking for individuals that have the drive and commitment to make a difference in the industry. We invite you to apply to become a part of The Rawlings Group team.

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Working with customers, managers and other Software Developers to understand requirements for simple-to-complex business systems required by group
  • Working with other IT staff to develop technical specifications based upon functions specs as created
  • Developing or participating in development of systems using .Net architecture with Visual Studio in C#
  • Following typical systems development life cycle processes in executing software development projects
  • Analyzing and performing modifications to systems as requested by manager or customer through manager
  • Analyzing and assisting in correction of problems which may occur in system operations
  • Researching, designing, defining, developing, testing, documenting and implementing new computer applications or changes to existing applications
  • Installing software packages and/or personal computer software
  • Assuring that hardware and software operate efficiently
  • Utilizing programming tools effectively to ensure accuracy and enhance productivity
  • Consulting and/or advising business unit associates by written and/or oral communication on inquiries concerning existing or proposed systems
  • Participating in committee meetings on developmental projects, large-scale improvement projects and internal Information Services technical committees.
  • May chair committees and/or subcommittees
  • Performing other duties as assigned by management


EDUCATION REQUIREMENTS

  • Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent combination of education and experience in the health insurance, legal, property casualty or related fields, which would provide the necessary knowledge, skills, and abilities to successfully perform the work
  • Experience with Microsoft SQL Server including queries, triggers and stored procedures
  • AGILE principles/methodologies, SCRUM Framework
  • Experience with the following development technologies: Angular, Unit Testing, nuGets, ASP.Net MVC, WCF, Linq, Team Foundation Server and SharePoint
  • Experience in data mining techniques, mining software and understanding of statistical analysis
  • Experience in healthcare IT (provider, insurer, third-party, claims processing, other healthcare) desirable but not mandatory


PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS

  • Frequently remains in sitting position for long periods of time to perform duties
  • Frequently concentrates for long periods of time, paying close attention to detail
  • Frequently uses and views the computer using fingers, hands, and vision
  • Frequently works in a well-lit, tempered environment
  • Occasionally walks through office to communicate with other employees
