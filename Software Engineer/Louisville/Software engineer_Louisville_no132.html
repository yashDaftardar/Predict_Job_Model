Full Job Description

Writing code. Creating Applications and Services. Developing external facing software. Greenfield Development. Cutting Edge Technology.

If that grabbed your attention, come grow with us!

American Auto Shield (AAS) specializes in Vehicle and Home Service Contract development and superior claims administration.

We may not look like a tech company but we’re building a world class technology function. We’re taking a fresh look at our IT department and looking for a Senior Software Engineer to write code that supports our technology platform and create applications and services that will add value to the company and help to lead the future of a company that is experiencing unprecedented growth.

Introductions first!

You-

Are results-oriented, not afraid to jump into the deep end and be an agent of change in the organization.

Believe in creating software with a product minded and iterative approach to solving problems.

Thrive working in a collaborative environment and enjoy helping others.

Enjoy thinking outside the box to drive changes that will impact our industry.

Love what you do, and it shows every day.

Us-

We’re leading our industry into the future by creating and shaping the future of Service Contracts and Claims Administration. We’re developing systems from the ground up and will give you the opportunity to be a part of our quest for greatness.

We offer the ability to pursue your fullest potential and never settle for second place in your quest to grow your career. You’ll join an incredible team filled with smart, fun, passionate people who love making an impact.

We believe in creative environments, progressive ideas and excellence in operational effectiveness.

The safety of our employees is our top priority. AAS employees are almost 100% remote and many will remain WFH even after the pandemic is over.

We support our own. When things get tough, the company will always come together to meet the challenges of the business and of the world together.

We love what we do.

What the Software Engineer will do:

  • Develop scalable applications that solves real business challenges with intuitive design.
  • Write software that is easy for others to maintain and is highly testable.
  • Debugging applications to determine root cause and solutions for bugs or edge cases.
  • Aligns with coding standards and participates in code reviews.
  • Participates in an agile development environment and contributing to the success of agile ceremonies.

What you need to bring:

  • 5+ years of professional software engineering experience.
  • Experience in C#, .NET, JavaScript.
  • Experience building and consuming RESTful services.
  • Worked with data in SQL or NoSQL databases.
  • Desire to learn and embrace cutting edge technology and techniques.
  • Thrive working in a collaborative environment and embracing Team First spirit.
  • Ability to take initiative, understand the big picture, compromise, weigh pros and cons of decisions, and assess risk.
  • Love to help others.

Why work for us? What we offer:

  • Competitive compensation package, with total compensation including performance incentives.
  • Flexible working environment. We’ll provide the equipment to set your home office up for success.
  • Strong medical, dental, and vision insurance after the first full month of employment, including a monthly spending account for wellness.
  • 100% company match, up to 4% of your pay and eligibility to join our 401(k) plan in 90 days.
  • Vacation/PTO – All new employees will accrue 13 days of PTO the first year and it increases each year following.
  • An atmosphere where our employees are encouraged to be creative and think outside the box.
  • Stability – While other companies were laying people off as a result of COVID, we exceeded our growth forecast for 2020.

Job Location:

Software Engineer will base out of the AAS corporate office in Lakewood, CO. This position is currently 100% remote but will work in a hybrid capacity when it is safe to return to the office.

Salary Range:

$90,000- $120,000 per year base plus performance-based incentive comp. Can be negotiable based on qualifications and experience.

Who we are:

Our Mission is to be the right choice in service contracts.

Our headquarters are located in Lakewood, Colorado, and we specialize in third-party administration of vehicle and home service contracts and related automotive and home claims administration. Our expertise complemented by our strategic partnerships allows us to develop and deliver products that provide superior coverage and service.

We continue to experience tremendous growth, which creates great opportunities for our employees. Within the past 12 months we've doubled in size. Today we have grown to more than 400 full time employees across multiple locations that include our Denver metropolitan HQ and satellite offices in Missouri, Florida and Arizona.
