Full Job Description

Responsibilities:
  • Job Responsibilities: Participate in the analysis, build, testing and implementation of Epic's Clarity reports (Crystal Reports) to meet the Norton Healthcare System end user reporting needs. Provide expertise and perform report configurations and design to augment the Report Writing Team. Be able to create complex operational and project related reports including design, documentation, development, testing, implementation and ongoing support. Provide analysis, design, documentation, development, testing, implementation and maintenance of Reporting user interfaces including alerts, prompts, screens, dashboards and templates. Analyze, configure, document and test Epic Clarity and Chronicles reports. Use of OLAP and OLTP technologies along with knowledge of data warehouses/data stores, data marts and Universes to execute SQL query techniques to access relational and dimensional databases. Utilize skills and experience as Business Intelligence (BI) Report Developer to develop, test and implement Reporting Solutions leveraging SAP Business Objects Enterprise BI Tools. Ability to translate business requirements into reporting solutions, created from potentially disparate data sources (e.g. Epic and Non Epic). Will be able to translate user requirements into functional and design specifications. Provide documentation and training to transfer knowledge and operational support to other team members on the Report Writing Team.
Qualifications:
Required:
  • With Required Associates Degree: One year clinical or business report writing
  • Without Required Degree: Three years clinical or business report writing
  • Associate Degree
  • EPIC Certification - Must obtain Epic credential within six weeks of completing Epic training.
Desired:
  • Three years business systems report writing
  • Bachelor Degree
