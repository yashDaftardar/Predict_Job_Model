Full Job Description

The Lead Software Engineer codes software applications based on business requirements. Works on problems of diverse scope and complexity ranging from moderate to substantial. Standardizes the quality assurance procedure for software. Oversees testing and debugging and develops fixes. Researches complaints and makes necessary adjustments and/or recommendations to resolve complex software related issues. Advises executives to develop functional strategies (often segment specific) on matters of significance. Exercises independent judgment and decision making on complex issues regarding job duties and related tasks, and works under minimal supervision. Uses independent judgment requiring analysis of variable factors and determining the best course of action.

Responsibilities

KEY ACCOUNTABILITIES

Planning, Analysis and Design

  • Understand business purpose of software solution requests

  • Contribute to major feature planning

  • Contribute to process of estimating costs for proposed solutions

  • Contribute to analysis of how to best meet purpose and features resulting in logical requirements documentation (can include build, buy or hybrid solutions)

  • Design solutions to meet objectives (data structures, system processes, services, user interfaces, etc.)

  • Mentor and guide team members in the completion of their assignments

  • Work with architecture team to explore new tools, techniques, etc. to improve solution delivery

Development, Integration, Testing and Implementation

  • Write, update and maintain in-house engineered software solutions utilizing Humana Military approved tools

  • Write, update and maintain software to integrate purchase software solutions utilizing Humana Military approved tools

  • Configure purchased software to meet business and IT requirements

  • Mentor junior team members and guide their software engineering tasks

  • Prepare comprehensive test cases for functional and regression testing plans

  • Assist testing teams with automating test cases

  • Assist end users with UAT as needed

  • Prepare detailed deployment plans to include rollback

  • Contribute to system disaster recovery planning, documentation and execution

  • Prepare system documentation as directed
