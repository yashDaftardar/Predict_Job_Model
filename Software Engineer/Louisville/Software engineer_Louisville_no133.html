Full Job Description

Beyond is calling for innovative creators to join our Agile Team and help us build and evolve our sales, boarding, and integration platforms. As a Software Engineer, you will work in a Lean-Agile environment with a cloud first mindset.


With roots in integrity, a future in innovation, and a dedication to entrepreneurship, the possibilities are endless!


Cultural Expectations

Within this culture you will:

  • Believe in and display our core values of Teamwork, Happiness, Kindness, Integrity, Agility, Growth, and Energy on a daily basis
  • Support a culture of Lean thinking, and Agile Mindsets
  • Desire to constantly learn and share knowledge
  • Thrive in a team environment with lots of collaboration and healthy debate(s)
  • Wear multiple hats as situations demand, to help your team reach their goals

If you love what you do and enjoy sharing your talents in a motivating environment, join our team now.

Essential Duties

  • Develop with a quality first mindset
  • Active participation in Team ceremonies and meetings
  • Collaborate with technical architects and business users
  • Design, implement and test applications in cloud first/mobile first fashion.
  • Work with diversified technology stack(s).
  • Communicate effectively, both written and verbal
  • Ability to learn new technologies with self-direction


Required Skills

  • Excellent understanding of OOPS concepts and SOLID principles.
  • Comfortable working in Microsoft .Net framework (core/standard) and related Technologies such as WebApi, EntityFramework, NUnit/XUnit .
  • Experienced with RDBMS (MS SQL Server) database
  • Experienced with REST API’s, both implementing and consuming.
  • Comfortable in a Cloud environment, such as Azure or AWS , (serverless functions, DBs, storage, etc.)
  • Experienced in writing unit tests.
  • Experience working with popular JavaScript frameworks, jQuery, Angular, etc.
  • Experience with JavaScript testing tools such as jasmine, karma, chutzpah
  • Familiar with logging libraries such as Serilog .
  • Experience working in a Lean-Agile environment preferred


Education & Experience

  • Bachelor’s degree or comparable training and experience.
  • 2+ Years working as a Software Engineer (or equivalent role)


Desired

  • Prior experience or understanding of Node.js and its use.
  • Intermediate or better knowledge in native/hybrid mobile development
  • Experience working in an XP (Extreme Programming) environment a bonus
