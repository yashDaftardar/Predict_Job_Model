Job title: AUTOMATION / MACHINE DESIGN ENGINEER

Summary/Objective : The Automation/Machine Design Engineer will work both independently and collaboratively on projects to design and develop Automation, Manual Tooling, and/or Fixtures. This includes seeing those projects through concept, design, manufacturing, validation testing, and integration into production.

Essential Functions / Responsibilities :

  • Using CAD software (Solidworks), Design all mechanical elements of either manual, semi-automatic, or fully automatic tooling and equipment used in volume production in Samtec’s assembly processes.
  • Communicate in a collaborative environment to provide mechanical design support for the design needs of internal customers.
  • Ensure Product Quality and Process Reliability are designed into all projects and validated with qualification.
  • Develop clear, measurable requirements for success based on the established standards and in concert with project teams.
  • Understand the purposes and review of project costing and margin requirements with applications engineering
  • Lead the creation and maintenance of a detailed project schedule to update Management of the status on the progress of the project in regular basis.
  • Responsible for the project specifications, evaluation of suppliers, their quotations and cost control of the project budget.
  • Perform tolerance analysis on production components and apply relevant design practices to ensure machine functionality.
  • Ensures the project delivered is compliant with all relevant OSHA, ANSI, and local standards in respect to the design and engineering work practices.
  • Conduct regular project meetings with the project team throughout the project on:
    • Machine/Equipment/Tooling design and status.
    • Controls/Systems design and status.
  • Review project specifications, deliverables and requirement with applications engineering.
  • Track all changes with a written Project Change Notification and maintains a revision summary.
  • Record, address and resolves issues that impact project timing and/or scope.
  • Develop contingency plans or recovery plans as required.
  • Monitor the timing and cost of the fabrication to deliver the project on time and within budget.

“The responsibilities as defined are intended to serve as a general guideline for this position. Associates may be asked to perform additional tasks depending on strengths and capabilities”

Required Experience :

  • 8+ years relevant working experience with an Engineering Degree. Experiences in:
    • Automation Machine Design
    • Design for Manufacturing
    • High Speed Assembly Equipment (optional)
    • Machine Tools
    • Precision Tooling
    • Industrial Machine Control Systems (optional)
  • Solid interpersonal communication skills, a professional demeanour, cooperative personality and a team attitude are all required. Works well with others and interacts effectively and efficiently with internal and external people and groups.
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Ability to use SolidWorks and MS Office Suite, MS Project, SharePoint Document Management and general computer literacy
  • Extensive cross functional team expertise including technical and non-technical work
  • Analytical and creative thinker, problem solver, result-orientated and focused
  • Highly organized, ability to prioritize a varied and fast-changing workload
  • Motivated, high achiever, persistent and conscientious.

Preferred Education :
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Mechanical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering Technology, or Manufacturing Engineering from an Accredited College.
Training required :
  • On the job training as appropriate.
