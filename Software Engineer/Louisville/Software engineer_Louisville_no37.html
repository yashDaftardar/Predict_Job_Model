Full Job Description

Keen to become part of a truly global, collaborative team of professionals? Your journey begins here.

Hogan Lovells is a leading global law firm that is serious about innovation. With a presence in the world's major financial and commercial markets, we are well placed to provide excellent business-oriented advice to our clients locally and internationally. Our people are the key to our success, which is why we seek to recruit and retain the most talented individuals in all regions of our global practice.

Our Advanced Client Data Solutions (ACDS) team is a team tasked with solving any kind of data analysis problem that comes in the door, both internally and externally, and they are looking for a senior developer to fill a creative, multi-disciplinary role. ACDS provides expert analysis, machine learning tools, data visualization, custom solutions, consulting, project management, and case team support around all types of matters involving our clients’ data, including investigations, litigation, arbitrations, M&A due diligence, intellectual property, and contract analysis. Our matters typically involve very large volumes of both unstructured and structured data from systems ranging from enterprise IT (both COTS and custom), social media, mobile devices, security systems, public databases, and IoT data/log files. We also assist with internal projects of process automation and process efficiencies for our General Counsel’s office. We handle unsupervised and supervised machine learning, NLP, sentiment analysis, outlier/anomaly detection, machine translation, media file processing (e.g., voice-to-text), structured data analysis, and data visualization - but something completely new and different could show up tomorrow.

This position may be located in any U.S. office, with two days per week work from home. Post-COVID remote work arrangements are possible.

JOB DESCRIPTION

ACDS is building a new technology practice for dealing with our clients’ data in the course of complex, high-stakes legal representation. Our custom apps are built with Python, React, TypeScript, and PHP, with some inner loops written in Rust. We also use custom code, and RPA tools to stitch together COTS platforms - such as Relativity, Kira, and HighQ Collaborate - into custom systems that do exactly what our clients need. We rely on PostgreSQL for structured data and Power BI for data visualization. We’re increasingly exploring cloud ML services, and also platforms like EKS for ad hoc, unoptimized scripts developed in a hurry. The entire firm has recently standardized on Azure DevOps and we’re looking to make good use of it, including CI/CD pipelines to AWS.

Our people are the key to our success, which is why we seek to recruit and retain the most talented individuals in all regions of our global practice. For this role, we’re looking for someone who can:

  • Hit the ground running with at least one of the following: AWS, Python, React, PHP, Azure DevOps;
  • Support ACDS experts, consultants, and project managers on specific matters, with tasks that may include:
o Data-munging scripts.
o Mass processing/analysis of loose files, mobile data, structured data, etc.
o Assisting with automation of workflows, including usage of APIs.
o Analyze and interpret large volumes of data to show trends and create recommendations.
o Ad hoc analysis as required covering a range of topics and support on various projects.
  • Identify and create tools/utilities to help ACDS team deliver better service, including:
o CLI, web, Linux, and Windows utilities.
o Plug-ins for COTS platforms like Relativity.
o Connectware.
o RPA (UiPath) apps.
  • Keep abreast of emerging technologies and current, potentially useful COTS offerings that might be useful.
  • Collaborate with other technical teams and resources inside the firm to find efficiencies and creative solutions to problems.
  • Participate in meetings/conferences with industry leaders.
  • Show us an interesting or useful machine learning, NLP, or data visualization technique;
  • Understand technology well enough to explain it to non-technical colleagues
  • Appreciate the elegant, efficient approach over the showiest or most technical;
  • Can stay cool and prioritize when a bunch of new cases come in at the same time;
  • Enjoy learning new things, solving puzzles, fixing problems, and building stuff; and
  • All members of the firm are expected to participate in our Global Citizenship program.
  • Other duties as assigned.

QUALIFICATIONS

REQUIRED/TECHNICAL SKILLS

  • Can efficiently take ugly scripts with re-use potential and turn them into applications that can run at cloud scale, using something like AWS ECS/EKS, S3, PostgreSQL RDS, etc.
  • Ability to think like the CTO of a startup:
o Prudent but aggressive selection of tech stacks
o Identify MVPs and quick wins
o Ability to manage technical debt, taking it on strategically where necessary but having a plan to pay it down
  • Able to prioritize and plan their own work with minimum supervision – a proven ability to plan ahead is essential
  • Must be diligent, have an eye for details and the ability to review critically and carefully check his or her own work as well as others
  • Excellent organizational skills and follow-through.
  • Strong judgment, confident to make independent technical decisions and the ability to engender trust.
  • Sense of urgency; diligent and conscientious work ethic.
  • Calm demeanor, emotional intelligence, and tolerance for ambiguity.
  • Takes data security and privacy seriously.
  • Have an enquiring mind, so that they are prepared to experiment and explore.

EDUCATION/EXPERIENCE

  • Five (5)+ years of relevant experience preferred.
  • Bachelor’s degree in computer science preferred. Graduate degree or law firm experience very desirable.

HOURS

Core hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. Must be flexible to work additional hours.
