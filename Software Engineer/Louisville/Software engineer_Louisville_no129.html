Software Developer, Full-Stack

Phase Ortho is a rapidly growing startup focused on manufacturing orthodontic clear aligners. Phase Ortho strives to continuously innovate and deploy new technologies to improve our processes. Our culture is collaborative, progressive, and welcoming we are always looking for amazing talent.

We have an immediate opening for a full-stack software engineer to take on complex projects and help us grow our automated manufacturing development. The right candidate will have a passion for front-end implementation, API integration, database structure, automated processes, and data analytics. Sound judgment with respect to UI/UX and design elements.

Minimum qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, HCI, or equivalent practical experience.
  • Experience with JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.
  • Experience in designing and implementing user interfaces (UIs).
  • Confident with API integrations (REST/SOAP).
  • Comfortable with back-end development and support.
  • Team player, self-managed, confident, humble.

Preferred qualifications:

  • 5+ years experience working across the full-stack.
  • Experience with JavaScript frameworks (Angular, React, Vue) and CSS pre-processing frameworks.
  • Experience with scripting languages: Python, Ruby, etc.
  • Comfortable with designing appealing visuals and smart, extensible code.
  • Ability to juggle multiple projects at once and work through ambiguity.
  • Self-directed and demonstrated project leadership, time management and mentoring skills.
  • Strong communication skills and ability to work with others not in the same field.

Working at Phase Ortho:
Our team members all share a positive attitude and problem-solving abilities. We offer competitive benefits such as health, vision and dental insurance, paid holidays, paid time off, 401K, company branded apparel, and a great working environment. Phase Ortho also offers continuous opportunities for growth. Don't miss the chance to join our team!
