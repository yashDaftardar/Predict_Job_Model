Overview/Job Summary

Neustar is revitalizing its engineering culture and adopting modern software design and delivery techniques. We are looking for intellectually passionate people who learn quickly, thrive in a fast-paced environment and stay current with the latest technologies. We want people who fit our emerging Agile/DevOps culture, enjoy exploring and learning from differing opinions, and have a high level of engagement. Since strong programming and software design skills are essential, we'll ask you to lay out how you would solve complex management situations to understand your thought process and approach. In particular, we are looking for Software Data Engineers to join our UltraDNS Data Services team.

A good engineer will build, own and operate the Ultra data pipeline for our DNS services in the Security Solutions organization. This includes the development, enhancement and maintenance of data processing services used for customer reporting and billing. Engineer will be working in a Linux environment with multiple data stores and models (RDBMS, Hadoop, etc), multiple data processing technologies and multiple software languages across multiple Data Centers and in the Cloud. Neustar Ultra data ingests over 10TB/day. Engineer will ensure performance, high availability and redundancy of the services.

Is this you?

  • Takes ownership?
  • Demonstrates initiative?
  • Drives for continual improvement?
  • Curious - investigate new technologies?
  • Doesn't accept the status quo.

What you will do at Neustar as an Ultra Data Software Engineer:

  • Deliver value to our customers and do everything necessary to achieve this.
  • Develop Services and Products as part of an Agile (Scrum/Kanban) DevOps team responsible for end to end platform ownership from requirements, through the entire SDLC to production support. This includes building appropriate monitoring, alerting and runbooks to allow for detection, response and resolution.
  • Help develop/enhance/maintain large scale Data/Big Data platforms. This includes modeling, ingesting and processing billions of records daily across multiple Database platforms.
  • Help develop/enhance/maintain code (Go, Perl, Python, C/C++) for complex ETL data pipelines.
  • Develop proper instrumentation to measure the health and performance of the services you help build, easily pinpoint and address deficiencies and strive for operational excellence through continuous improvement.
  • Document everything
  • Automate everything – build, test and deploy software and infrastructure.
  • Continuously learn new concepts and technologies and share with your team and the greater Neustar community.
  • Leverage and contribute to Neustar's evolving shared infrastructure.

Key Qualifications

  • Experience in data-modeling and data-architecture optimized for big data patterns, data warehousing, focusing efficient storage and query performance.
  • Knowledge of data security and privacy techniques.
  • Experience building and running large-scale data pipelines, including distributed messaging such as Kafka, data ingest to/from multiple sources, bulk load and provide near real-time availability to downstream consumers.
  • Strong ETL development skills
  • Experience with C/C++, Python and Perl. Proficiency in GoLang is a strong plus.
  • PL/SQL
  • Shell scripting
  • Experience with Cloud Platforms and services such as AWS (EC2, S3, RDS, Redshift, Lambda, etc) and/or GCP, Azure or other cloud equivalents.
  • Experience with infrastructure and software automation and tools (Terraform, Ansible, Jenkins, etc)
  • Experience with NoSQL datastores and traditional relational databases required.
  • Knowledge of DNS protocol and Networking is preferred.
  • Strong Linux skills, including OS installation, configuration and administration, library management, troubleshooting, etc.

Education & Experience

  • Four year degree in IT, Computer Science or related field.
  • 8+ years experience developing enterprise data solutions.
