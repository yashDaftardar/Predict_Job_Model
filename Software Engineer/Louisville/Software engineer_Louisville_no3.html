Summary:

The Research and Development group within MHS focuses on novel approaches to automation within the material handling sector. R&D is seeking an experienced Software Developer to join the Computer Vision group.   • Unit tests, Profiling, Refactoring

  • Optimize code for speed and accuracy

Required Qualifications:

  • Strong problem solving and critical thinking skills
  • Strong attention to detail
  • 5 years of industry experience with C/C++ and Python
