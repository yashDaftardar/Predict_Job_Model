Full Job Description

Goodwill Industries of Kentucky is looking for an energetic, self-motivated, dynamic professional Applications Development Manager to join our team!

The Applications Development Manager will manage the DEVOPs group, a team of software developers and will be responsible for the organization's Salesforce applications development in collaboration with an outside developer partner. This role will maintain existing programs in Salesforce and assist career service teams with the launch of new programs in the Salesforce case management system, engage stakeholders to evaluate existing applications to determine project needs and requirements, technical changes, testing, and monitors project timelines. Manages team in the day-to-day performance of their jobs. Ensures that project/department milestones/goals are met and adhering to approved budgets. If you are looking for an opportunity that allows you to reach new goals while striving in a high-energy, fast paced environment, the Applications Development Manager opportunity is for you.

Goodwill’s Mission is to connect Kentucky job seekers with the resources and services they need to find and maintain long-term employment and a career path. We serve Kentuckians who have disabilities or experience other challenges finding success in the workforce, such as criminal backgrounds, language barriers, limited education, lack of transportation and chronic poverty.

Job Type: Full-time, Exempt

Pay: $70,000+

Essential Duties and Tasks:

  • Manages a portfolio of software projects and provides oversight and development of management reports and dashboard for in-house developed software, system integration, and Salesforce-based case management system.
  • Will serve as a subject matter expert and administrator of custom Salesforce Sales Cloud platform.
  • Responsible for the implementation, administration, enhancement, and report building of Salesforce Case Management platform.
  • Prepares and maintains all project documentation, such as initiation, delivery plans, and budgets.
  • Develops and maintains Program Plans and reporting documentation as necessary to ensure timely communication and successful delivery of assigned projects.
  • Ensures projects are planned and executed in-line with the project management approach and ensures deliverables meet required quality standards.
  • Develops and maintains a communications plan and keeps retail, career service, and administration leaders informed of project plans and related implications.
  • Responsible for the management and integration of third-party applications with Salesforce Platform
  • Provides project management support as necessary to business change projects.

Education and Experience:

  • Must have a bachelor’s degree in a related field.
  • Must have three (3) years relevant experience in Information Systems Management, Development, Integration, Implementation, or Support.
  • Three (3) years Salesforce experience, Salesforce Certified Administrator highly desired.
  • Must have a strong working knowledge of Microsoft office and spreadsheet manipulation
  • Must have excellent oral and written communication skills; the ability to communicate at all levels of organization and external clients.
  • Must have solid organizational skills including attention to detail and ability to prioritize multiple projects.
  • Must have an ethical work performance, strong sense of urgency with a positive “can-do” attitude, personal qualities of high integrity, credibility, dependability, and dedication.

Physical Demands:

  • This job may have physical requirements that range from sedentary to medium work. Sedentary work involves sitting for long periods of time; occasional bending, squatting, kneeling, stooping; good finger dexterity and feeling; frequent repetitive motions; talking, hearing, and visual acuity and occasional lifting up to 10 pounds. Medium work involves more walking and standing, and lifting to 50 pounds
