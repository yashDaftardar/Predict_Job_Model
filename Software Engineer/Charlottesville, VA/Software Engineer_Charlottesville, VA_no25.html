Position Overview:

Our Senior Software Support Engineer is responsible for the maintenance and minor enhancements of all of our applications and integrations that we have implemented for clients with a support and maintenance agreement.

This position can be done 100% remotely. We are open to both "Contracted to Full Time" and "Full Time" applicants.

Essential Functions:

  • Troubleshoot and triage customer-reported issues
  • Ability to multitask and operate independently, with a strong attention to detail
  • Quickly learn a variety of business processes, software applications and technologies
  • Maintain and document software functionality and issue resolution steps
  • Design and support release management procedures and technologies
  • Design, configure and execute smoke testing procedures
  • Perform software development and bug resolution
  • See projects / assignments through to completion while adhering to business timelines
  • Improve upon current skills while also developing and mastering new technical skills
  • Negotiate or escalate in a timely manner if issue is out of scope

Qualifications

  • At least 3 years of technical experience in product support, application development and design
  • Experience with the following technologies:
    • Angular, Bootstrap
    • WebAPI, MVC, .NET, C#, PHP
    • Windows, Linux
    • MSSQL, MySql
    • WordPress
    • AWS, Azure
    • Jenkins, Git
  • Solid development skills and passion for developing software; Strong desire to learn and work on multiple technologies/languages/platforms
  • Tenacity in troubleshooting issues and identifying root causes
  • Software debugging skills; ability to debug applications
  • Adaptable and embraces change
  • Excellent organizational and communication skills
  • Client relationship skills (e.g. navigate a high tension moment)
  • High ethical standards and integrity
  • Team player with high performance standards

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience being part of a technical support group
  • Some experience with some or all of the following technologies:
    • jQuery, VUEjs, React
    • Ruby on Rails, NodeJS
    • Swift, Kotlin, Android, iOS
    • Postgres
    • Drupal
    • Azure DevOps
    • Azure Repos
