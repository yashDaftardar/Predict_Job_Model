The role:

Ticketstoday is a division of Ticketmaster Music that specializes in delivering solutions for artists to reach their fans directly, through special ticketing offers, packages, programs, and fan communities. This role will work directly with our team of engineers, UX designers, and fan club producers primarily in support of our LAMP-based fan club platform and content-management system. The position will require excellent front-end skills, a good “eye”, and an interest and ability for staying current on web technology and UX trends.

What the job is:

  • Work with UX designers and other engineers to develop and enhance full stack of artist fan club sites running on proprietary platforms
  • Work with engineers on other products within the organization to assist and improve front-end experience and technology
  • Stay current on front-end best practices and trends
  • Advise and recommend tools and technologies to improve performance, stability, ease of development, and fan experience
  • Work with project manager to provide estimates of proposed work

What a qualified candidate should possess:

  • Bachelor’s degree required. Strong preference for CS or IT-related discipline
  • 3+ years of work experience
  • Strong front-end ability to skillfully and accurately translate designs into high-quality, responsive web experiences
  • Solid PHP background required; knowledge of other stacks like .NET, Java, Rails, or NodeJS a definite plus
  • Experience developing mobile-first and for desktop
  • Strong knowledge of current JS and CSS frameworks, libraries, and best practices
  • Knowledge of advanced JS Frameworks such as ReactJS, Angular, Backbone, etc.
  • Knowledge of Web build tools such as SASS/SCSS, npm, Webpack, Grunt, etc.
  • Knowledge of Git and good code versioning and management practices with a DevOps mindset
  • Familiarity with AWS or other cloud-based deployment strategies
  • Familiarity with Zend 2/3/Laminas Framework
  • Experience with supporting high-traffic sites is a plus; understanding proxies, CDNs, caching, etc.
  • Knowledge of MySQL or other databases
  • Experience with SEO strategies and best practices
