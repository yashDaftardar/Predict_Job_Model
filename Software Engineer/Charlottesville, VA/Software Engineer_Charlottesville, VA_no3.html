Software Engineer - Senior

Bluestone Analytics
Charlottesville, VA

IMPACT


We are looking for a skilled Senior Software Engineer to join the company’s Engineering department. This role will contribute significantly to the company’s development of applications and operational tools in support of specific contract obligations, as well as our proprietary SaaS products. The ideal candidate will be adaptable, curious, persistent, and will thrive in a start-up environment where not all processes are clearly defined yet and projects can change to meet new priorities.

ABOUT YOU

  • Strong project management and organizational skills, using methodologies like scrum and agile within development sprint cycles.

  • Ability to communicate clearly with both technical and non-technical audiences.
  • Experience supporting and working with cross-functional teams in a collaborative and dynamic environment.
  • Strong judgment and critical thinking skills to process and analyze information.

  • Self-motivated, proactive, and enthusiastic in dynamic environments.
  • High-output developer when working independently – taking initiative to solve problems and complete tasks.
  • Experience establishing and defining development practices like unit testing, linting, code reviews, and pair programming.
  • A willingness to independently pursue greenfield projects and share responsibility for maturing legacy systems.

IN A TYPICAL WEEK, YOU MAY

  • Develop incredible solutions to difficult national security and law enforcement problems.
  • Work with smart, talented, fun engineers who are driven to succeed and to help one another.
  • Learn new things by working hands-on with unique technical environments you won’t get to see anywhere else.
  • Attend sprint planning meetings and daily stand ups to coordinate work with team members.

SKILLS AND REQUIREMENTS

  • 5+ years of experience in software engineering roles using JavaScript or Python.
  • U.S. citizenship with eligibility to obtain a security clearance.
  • Experience using git with Github for version control and source code management.
  • Experience turning user stories and product requirements into design concepts and development tasks.
  • Able to take a software project from idea through requirements gathering, design, planning, development, testing, to deployment independently.
  • Strong architecture and system design skills both for applications and for cloud-based services.
  • Experience with REST APIs (building, consuming, or both) and working with JSON objects as data stores/
  • For front-end developers:
    • Strong experience with modern web technologies, like React, Vue, or Angular (React preferred).
    • Expertise in writing and using cutting edge React patterns such as Hooks, Context, and await/async in novel ways.

    • Familiarity with design systems (Material UI, Ant, Bootstrap).
  • For back-end developers:
    • Experience with web-related Python libraries like requests, scrapy, Django, and Flask.

NICE TO HAVE

  • Associates or Bachelor's degree
  • Active Public Trust security clearance or higher.
  • Understanding of basic networking principles like the OSI model, TCP/IP connections, load balancers, web hosting, and proxies.
  • Experience with cloud technologies and serverless architectures, especially within AWS.
  • Experience with Elasticsearch or other NoSQL databases that rely on JSON objects.

ABOUT US

Bluestone Analytics is a mission-focused, veteran-owned business that solves the world’s toughest problems through custom, targeted data collection augmented with artificial intelligence. We are committed to innovation, inclusion, and pushing the boundaries of Open-Source Intelligence, and we purposely foster an employee-centric culture of fast-paced achievement and innovation.

  • Healthy work/life balance - accruing 20 days PTO to start plus 10 paid holidays.
  • Formal bonus program tied to performance and company revenue goals.
  • Monthly cell service stipend.
  • Personalized retirement plan with company match
  • A rich company culture driven by private ownership and intentional leadership inspiring employees to engage in our healthy, high-performing, and values-based environment.
  • Quarterly culture awards
  • Coaching and development to support personal and professional growth.
  • Remote work friendly and a casual office environment
