Full Job Description

The Role :

CCRi is looking for a senior software engineer grounded in the full software lifecycle, from architecture and design through coding, testing, documentation, deployment, and support/maintenance. Our engineers take cutting-edge machine learning research and build reliable products. You'll help integrate machine-learning analytics, streaming data processing pipelines, and appealing user interfaces into end-to-end mission systems. These tools will be used to provide real-time situational awareness and expose advanced analytics to end users. As a senior engineer, you will be expected to balance technical responsibilities (design and development) with team responsibilities (technical planning, roadmaps, ticket creation and delegation, mentorship). You’ll likely conduct demos and presentations, and will be expected to contribute to the software engineering culture and ecosystem at CCRi, across projects, though opportunities like design reviews and shared learning opportunities.

Technology : Hadoop, Spark, HBase, Accumulo, Storm, Kafka, AWS, Docker

Languages : Scala, Python, Java, JavaScript (Angular, TypeScript)

What you’ll do :

50%: Design, write, and test code.

50%: Perform ancillary tasks in support of the code base, including documentation, deployment, performance evaluation, sprint retro/planning, devops, and peer review of your colleagues' work.

Requirements

  • US citizenship required. We do not offer sponsorships.
  • Bachelor’s Degree in a technical field, with at least 5 additional years of professional software development experience
  • Ability to work on a team

Bonus Points :

  • Database technology
  • Distributed analytics and processing
  • Geo-spatial data and analytics
  • AWS infrastructure and tooling
  • Active TS Security Clearance
