Full Job Description

We are seeking a creative and execution-oriented Marketing Web Developer to help develop CoConstruct’s web presence through creating exceptional web user experiences with our web properties and digital marketing assets. This person will be an integral part of the marketing team.

The Marketing Web Developer will have the opportunity to work on a diverse set of web development initiatives that span marketing, sales, support, and product teams, and that will drive overall revenue generation. The right candidate will love the challenge of translating designs into beautiful code, creating a delightful user experience on our public-facing web properties, and working alongside the core team responsible for growing revenue at CoConstruct.

What You’ll Do

  • Collaborate with marketing, sales, service, and product teams, as well as designers, to maintain and enhance CoConstruct’s public-facing web properties (marketing website, community website, support website, etc.)
  • Use your creativity and experience to communicate CoConstruct’s story and value to our target market.
  • Own all CoConstruct’s public-facing websites, which includes leading on architecting, developing, and maintaining each web property.
  • Translate designs from our designers and broader marketing team into exceptional, responsive user experiences on the web and on email, including website page templates, email templates, digital marketing assets, microsites, and landing pages.
  • Embrace, enable, and help build a hypothesis-driven culture of experimentation on the marketing team.
  • Partner with customer-facing teams to identify their web development needs and ensure those needs are met.
  • Execute marketing and sales operations’ plans for web user to lead flow that align with our demand generation funnel and work well with our marketing automation and CRM platforms.
  • Assist in the collection of marketing analytics data by ensuring analytics tags and tools are properly set up, deployed, and maintained.

Required Skills/Experience

  • 3-4 years of website development experience
  • Experience working in Webflow CMS system plus image and script optimization
  • Expertise with HTML, CSS, and some experience with JavaScript.
  • Superb communication, writing, and follow-up skills.
  • Excellent time management and multitasking skills.
  • A passion for writing scalable code for responsive and accessible web experiences across all our public facing web properties, including accelerated mobile pages.
  • Curiosity and drive to learn new web frameworks needed to create excellent web experiences for our users.
  • Meticulous attention to detail, style, and brand integrity.
  • Ability to work with various stakeholders in developing web and email assets.
  • A proven track record of execution and an ability to focus on driving key success metrics across our marketing and sales teams.
  • (Bonus) Experience with principles of technical search engine optimization.
