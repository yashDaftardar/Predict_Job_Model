The Challenge:

Are you looking for an opportunity to not just develop software, but to build a system that will enable DoD organizations to take advantage of Cloud technology, reduce data center infrastructure, and significantly enhance overall security postures? As a DevOps engineer, you can solve problems with complete end-to-end Cloud solutions in a fast-paced, agile environment. We’re looking for a senior DevOps engineer with the advanced skills needed to lead the development of Cloud infrastructure components from vision to production-ready services.

This role is more than just coding or being a team lead. We need an experienced senior DevOps engineer who will use their passion to guide a development team to learn new tools and techniques, identify and implement system improvements, and mentor teammates. Using Agile processes, multiple programming languages and frameworks, you’ll enhance a DevSecOps pipeline across multiple networks and lead the software development of complex software, Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), and Platform as a Service (PaaS) solutions for DoD clients. Your team will analyze the needs and the environment to make sure the solution you’re developing considers the current architecture and operating environment, as well as future functionality and enhancements. As a technical leader, you’ll identify new opportunities to build digital solutions to help your customers meet their toughest challenges. Join us as we build systems to change the DoD for the better.

Empower change with us.

You Have:

  • 10+ years of experience with working in designing and implementing information management systems
  • 3+ years of experience with product development using software programming, scripting, or markup languages, including Java, JavaScript, Bash, Ansible, Puppet, Salt, Python, Golang, C, C++, C#., XML, HTML, JSON, or YAML
  • 3+ years of experience as a software development lead, including directing control and responsibility for subordinate developers working the development of a Web-based and data-intensive system
  • 1+ years of experience with provisioning, installation, configuration, operation, and maintenance of software and infrastructure-as-code in a DevOps and continuous delivery environment using automated deployment and testing frameworks
  • 1+ years of experience with commercial cloud service providers, including Amazon Web Services or Microsoft Azure, containers, serverless architecture, micro-services, Web services, and RESTful API design
  • Experience with Linux operating systems and command-line interfaces
  • Experience with software version control systems, including Git
  • Ability to obtain a security clearance
  • BA or BS degree
  • Active DoD 8570 or 8140 IAT Level II Certification, including Security+ Certification or ability to obtain within 30 days of start date

Nice If You Have:

  • Experience with Amazon Web Services (AWS)
  • Experience with Kubernetes
  • Experience with Atlassian tool suite, including JIRA and Confluence
  • Experience with Agile development techniques and ceremonies
  • Experience with API development and integration
  • TS/SCI clearance

Clearance:

Applicants selected will be subject to a security investigation and may need to meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information.
