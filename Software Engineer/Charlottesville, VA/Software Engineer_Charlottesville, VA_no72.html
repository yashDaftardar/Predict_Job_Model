Job Description

CACI has a great opportunity if you are someone who is passionate about agile and delivering quality software. We are looking for a DevOPS Engineer to join an established agile team based primarily in Charlottesville, VA and inclusive of several remote development and testing resources. In this position you'll be involved with implementing a new software system using an open and flexible architecture that will integrate with several critical mission applications. As a CACI DevOPS Engineer, you will find ways to make automation do the heavy lifting, will enhance and maintain Continuous Integration (CI) infrastructure, automated deployments, and design scalable maintenance practices.

Position Description:

  • Work with cutting edge technologies (Kubernetes, Helm, Packer, ArgoCD)
  • Implement CI pipelines for projects and use DevOps principles and practices to get the most out of the tool chain.
  • Automate environment deployments into varied and complex environments – using virtual machines, container technology and cloud environments.
  • Collaborate with team members from all parts of the development lifecycle, from development to operations to security, solving technical challenges, and providing and receiving guidance and mentorship.
  • Provide support to build, automated test, integration, and deployment of software baselines using DevSecOps and CI/CD best practices, including definition of processes, metrics, tools selection and automation.
  • Provide rapid response to troubleshoot and triage production issues.
  • Apply information security best practices and respond to security events.
  • Provide support to the physical and virtualized infrastructure, to include the monitoring of infrastructure components, applying OS patches, and troubleshooting activities.
  • Support maintenance activities in the evening and weekends. Provides on-call support as needed.

Required Quailifications:

  • Secret clearance is required
  • AWS experience - EC2, S3, RDS, ElastiCache, Route 53, IAM, CloudFront, CloudWatch, etc
  • Experience with Devops tools, including Jenkins, Gitlab, Nexus, Sonar, Nessus.
  • PaaS - Cloud Foundry, OpenShift, Kubernetes, etc
  • Network configuration
  • Strong Linux skills
  • DevSecOps experience in AWS environments
  • Typically requires bachelor's degree or equivalent, and a minimum of 10 years of related work experience
  • Experience configuring and maintaining DevSecOps continuous pipeline based on Jenkins and Docker containers.
  • Knowledge of version control systems
  • Proficient with scripting languages (Powershell, Bash, Python, JavaScript/ECMA Script, Perl, Ruby, etc.)
  • Compiled languages (.NET, Java, PL/SQL)
  • Experience with VM Server management

Desired Qualifications:

  • Experience developing and delivering software on Amazon Web Services (AWS) or similar cloud services
  • Understanding of agile development practices, including Agile Scrum methodology.
  • TS/SCI clearance with CI polygraph
  • AWS Associate or Professional certification: Solutions Architect, Developer, or SysOps Administrator
  • Off-the-shelf test automation tools (Junit, Selenium)
