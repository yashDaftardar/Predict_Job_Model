Full Job Description

Musictoday is seeking a Director of Engineering and Software Architecture to join our growing and dynamic team.

Position Summary:

The Director of Engineering and Software Architecture is responsible for the final architectural and design decisions, including enhancements and new development that is either part of a current system or a new system that does not affect the major functionality of a system, at a corporate level. This position will also participate directly and in the management of assessment, analysis, design, development, testing and production support for Musictoday's proprietary software stack plus integrated commercial subsystems.

The Director of Engineering and Software Architecture is a people management position as well which will oversee and directly supervise the Engineering team. The ideal candidate will have management experience and a deep background in Application Development to focus, facilitate, and enable the team toward building the next level of software stack tools for the company.
This position will report to the CEO and collaborate with other Department Heads to execute against the core objectives to meet the company goals.

Ideally, at least 10 years development and design experience and Database Administration experience in SQL/SRV and MySQL is helpful. Qualifications may include:

  • .NET architecture/OO Design experience, ASP, ASP.net, VB, VB.net, C#, SQL/SRV.
  • Java, PHP, Groovy/Grails, NodeJS, MySQL, Data Architecture (ER and ETL) and API integration.
  • May include solid understanding of Networking, System, Dev ops, AWS.


Essential Duties & Responsibilities:

  • Design, develop and implement feature enhancements to Musictoday's e-commerce, business platforms, and their integration.
  • Ensure code quality by ensuring thorough unit and integration tests from the team. Participation in code reviews.
  • Facilitate and enable reliable solutions via the SDLC and/or Agile processes.
  • Promote and advocate best practice development methodologies and help facilitate an Agile software development lifecycle.
  • Help produce, or facilitate via the team, supporting documentation for the platform solutions in diagram or written documentation.
  • Explore and understand new tools and technologies, with a pragmatic and deliberate examination of how they complete the big picture.
  • Be able to participate in long term Architecture vision process for the company.


Skills & Qualities Desired :

  • Familiar with multiple programming languages and frameworks, primarily .NET, C#, Java, and Groovy/Grails.
  • Understanding of relational database skills, specifically with SQL Server and MySQL.
  • Collaborative, team based mind-set .


Education & Knowledge Desired

  • Bachelor's degree in CS or related field. Masters degree preferred
  • Knowledge and experience with .NET, Groovy/Grails, C#, Java, and open source development stacks.
  • Knowledge of open-source technologies such as Apache and Linux.
  • Knowledge or skills with Dev Ops, Networking, AWS is helpful.


About Musictoday:

We are the preferred end-to-end ecommerce provider for a roster of over 500 clients in the music, sports, and entertainment industries. We offer a robust benefits package to our full-time employees including competitive compensation, medical, dental and vision insurance coverages, life and disability insurance, a 401(k) plan, paid time off, paid company holidays, discounts on many of the products that we sell, and many other perks.

Musictoday is an equal opportunity employer and offers an exciting opportunity to qualified, career-minded professionals to work in a winning, successful and pleasant environment.
