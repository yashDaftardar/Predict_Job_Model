Job Description:

The Hodges University marketing team is looking for a Webmaster who is responsible for the planning, development, implementation and measurement of the School's website platforms in alignment with the university-wide marketing and branding strategy. The successful candidate has the knowledge, creativity and technical skills in specialized web programming languages, tools, and technology; the exercise of judgment to troubleshoot, analyze, and resolve problems; and the ability to work independently. The successful candidate stays informed of, evaluates and recommends new and emerging web technologies. The successful candidate will be responsible for maintaining, updating/uploading institutional web page documents on a regular basis according to University specifications, and reviews institutional pages for errors or necessary changes.

Build functional website pages that are visually attractive, drive calls to action and are easy to use
Regularly updates the look, feel and functionality of the website
Tracks and analyzes usage of website, web portal and works collaboratively with University departments to improve website Search Engine Optimization (SEO) strategies
Collaborate with marketing staff and vendors on website ad campaigns
Create a website style guide for cohesive design elements across all pages
Ensures website ADA compliance
Continually test website using different devices, browsers, and operating systems
Update content on the website to keep pages fresh and intriguing
Review the site content and update with current SEO trends
Monitor, analyze, and report on all online traffic
Analyze site performance
Ensure web site and web server functionality and efficiency
Make sure related hardware and software are functioning
Respond to user complaints quickly and appropriately
Manage access rights for users
Maintain and update firewalls
Optimize site loading speeds
Fix broken links and images
Meet with the AVP of Marketing to report regularly on current functionality and plans for future functionality
Minimize website downtime
Communicate ways to improve website functionality and usability
Respond to user comments and questions
Perform research and set benchmarks on current trends and audience preferences
Create graphic content and set specific objectives tied to ROI and generate monthly reports



Requirements:

The successful Webmaster candidate will have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Design, or a related field, with previous experience as a Webmaster or Web Developer. Required Technical Skills include experience with WordPress content management, fundamental JavaScript, HTML5/XHTML and CSS, Google Analytics, and Adobe Creative. The candidate must have excellent interpersonal skills and strong communication abilities.

The position is based on the Fort Myers campus, with travel and evening and weekend hours as needed.
