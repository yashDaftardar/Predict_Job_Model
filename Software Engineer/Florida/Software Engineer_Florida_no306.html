Carrier has a summer internship opportunity for candidates interested in a career in Firmware Engineering. The Carrier internship experience offers interns an environment that provides practical, challenging, and meaningful on-the-job work experience. Interns will also have the opportunity to apply knowledge and demonstrate strengths in an effort to assist Carrier in achieving its business strategy.
As a Carrier Firmware Engineering Intern you will gain experience that includes, but is not limited to, the activities and tasks listed below:
  • Participate in design and code reviews
  • Set up and maintain build server
  • Develop and update unit test and system integration/verification test cases
  • Configure different systems in preparation of test cases execution
  • Troubleshoot and document defects
Education / Certifications
  • Must be pursuing a degree in electrical engineering or related field.
  • Minimum of 3.0 Cumulative GPA and completion of 30 credit hours.
Experience / Qualifications
  • Highly energetic and success driven team player
  • Excellent verbal and written communications skills
  • Self-directed and able to tackle new projects and ideas
  • Effectively able to interact with technicians and other departments and suppliers
  • Ability to persist and succeed with cross-cultural communication on technical issues
  • Strong technical skills and analytical proficiency
  • Highly organized with strong attention to detail, and superior follow-up and follow-through skills
