What you will be able to do
As part of a Testing Team, you will assist in performing Functional and Software Integration Testing leading to the release of software in future Ford vehicles. You will have the opportunity to utilize your creativity to develop, maintain and execute various types of automated tests, such as functional, integration, regression and negative tests. Together, these tests help to ensure the product we release is of the highest quality. You will:
  • Contribute towards developing test coverage for new feature implementations and maintain existing ones
  • Develop automated test scripts and tools in Python to verify new feature functionality
  • Perform Manual and Automated test execution and results analysis, root cause issues and validate fixes
  • Collaborate with related teams including product development and test to understand testing requirements and implement ideal test coverage covering sunny and rainy scenarios
  • Ensure issues found are accurately tracked throughout the Software Life Cycle
  • Effectively communicate status and issues with Test Primes, Development, Project Management and other Partners
  • Take ownership for quality of the product/component that is under test (e.g. test plan creation, execution, communicating risk assessments, work around, etc.)
  • Participate in ongoing process improvement (e.g. test case update, attaching missing requirements, etc.)
  • Participate in an Agile scrum team responsible for all aspects of software delivery leading to feature release to Ford customers
The minimum requirements we seek
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Engineering or Natural Sciences
  • 2+ years of experience with Manual, Automated and/or Performance testing tools (e.g. Python, Squish)
  • 2+ years of experience with bug tracking and debugging
Our preferred requirements
  • Experience with C, C++, C# and/or other object oriented development
  • Knowledge of Java, Python, Perl, PHP, Tcl/Tk
  • Experience with UNIX, Linux, GitHub
  • Strong aptitude for troubleshooting and problem solving
  • Ability to work in a multi-site team environment where self-driven and self-motivate capabilities are key qualities
  • Able to produce creative, yet effective solutions to problems
  • Detail oriented and adept at detecting and diagnosing software defects
  • Analytical, problem solver and strong communication skills
