Job Title: Software Engineer II

Location: Orlando, Florida 32826

Clearance Level: Active DoD - Interim Secret

**Selected applicants may be subject to a government security investigation and must meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information. **

SUMMARY:

Agile Defense is seeking a Software Engineer II with experience in design and development to support a Navy Surface Warfare Center program for the development of a 3-D training environment for maintenance tasks on shipboard systems through the use of simulations and physical mockups. Majority of the work will be remote with occasional travel to contractor and government facilities.

DUTIES:

  • Analyze and study complex system requirements.
  • Design software tools and subsystems to support software reuse and domain analyses and manages their implementation.
  • Manage software development and support using formal specifications, data flow diagrams, other accepted design techniques and Computer Aided Software Engineering (CASE) tools.
  • Estimate software development costs and schedule.
  • Review existing programs and assist in making refinements, reducing operating time, and improving current techniques.
  • Supervise software configuration management.

Qualifications

Education and Certifications:
  • Bachelor's level degree in Computer, Electrical or Electronics Engineering or Mathematics with field of concentration in computer science or more than 6 years of professional experience in computer design, software development or computer networks

Background Needed and Years of Experience:

  • 3-6 years of computer engineering experience
  • Experience developing 2D / 3D modeling and simulation programs
  • Experience performing research, analysis, definition, design, development, improvement, enhancement, prototyping and review of capabilities and requirements at the systems/subsystems level for both software and hardware implementations.
  • Experience with Agile software development practices (SCRUM)
  • Experience compiling source code and creating and delivering software executables

Additional Skills & Qualifications

Preferred Technical Skills:

  • Experience with Navy shipboard systems desired but not required
  • Experience collecting and assessing performance test and evaluation data to determine and report areas of opportunity for improvement in system, subsystem, and component design.
  • Minimum of 3 years of professional experience in computer design, software development or computer networks.
  • Experience with the following: JAVA, C++, C#, JMS, MQTT, Atlassian tools (Bitbucket, Jira, Confluence, Bamboo), Unity Game Engine

Non-Technical Skills:

  • Strong communication skills
  • Strong time management skills
  • Must be a team player. Ability to collaborate well with others and lead.
  • A learn-while-doing attitude with good problem-solving skills.
  • The ability to remember and put to use both important details and big-picture thinking.

WORKING CONDITIONS:

Contractor site with 0%-10% travel possible. Possible off-hours work to support releases and outages. General office environment. Work is generally sedentary in nature, but may require standing and walking for up to 10% of the time. The working environment is generally favorable. Lighting and temperature are adequate, and there are not hazardous or unpleasant conditions caused by noise, dust, etc. Work is generally performed within an office environment, with standard office equipment available.

Strength Demands:

Sedentary – 10 lbs. Maximum lifting, occasional lift/carry of small articles. Some occasional walking or standing may be required. Jobs are sedentary if walking and standing are required only occasionally, and all other sedentary criteria are met.
