About Atos

Atos is a global leader in digital transformation with over 110,000 employees in 73 countries and annual revenue of over € 11 billion. European number one in Cloud, Cybersecurity and High-Performance Computing, the Group provides end-to-end Orchestrated Hybrid Cloud, Big Data, Business Applications and Digital Workplace solutions. Position Title -

Software Engineering Intern

Location -

Boca Raton, FL

Duration -

Must be available to work on-site for 12 months

Position Summary -

Are you looking for an engaging opportunity to gain real-world experience as a software engineer? Do you want to help us develop an exciting product? Atos is seeking hardworking, creative students to join our team to support the software development cycle of our industry leading web collaboration application, Circuit. Enterprises worldwide use Circuit for their employees and partners to collaborate using messaging, audio, video, and screen sharing.

This is your opportunity to increase your knowledge with an internship that offers well-rounded and relevant hands-on experience.

Core Responsibilities -

In this position, you will support software development (requirements, development, automated and manual test and validation) for Circuit - www.circuit.com.

You will have the opportunity to support a text, audio, video, screen share (using WebRTC) product that runs on Chrome, IE, Firefox, iOS, Android, Electron
Engineer interns will work on related plug-ins, clients and SDKs to interface and integrate with other products like Microsoft Exchange, Google Drive, VMWare Virtual Desktop, etc.
You will work in a dynamic agile team setup.
You may be assigned in any area of the development cycle.
You will work with a product development team and learn to use leading edge technologies to develop your skills.

Minimum Qualifications -

Junior or senior level student enrolled in an accredited university and pursuing a graduate or undergraduate degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, or equivalent.
Minimum 3.0 GPA on a 4.0 scale.
Demonstrated knowledge in several or more of the following technologies: Java, JavaScript, Swift, Linux command line, Node.js, AngularJS, HTML, CSS and are an eager learner.
Ability to transform manual tasks into code.
Good verbal and written communication skills.

Preferred -

Strong foundation in software, leadership, problem solving, analytical thinking, and multi-tasking abilities.
