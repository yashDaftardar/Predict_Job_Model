SOFTWARE/EDI DEVELOPER

Minimum qualifications:

  • BA/BS degree in Computer Science or related technical field involving coding (e.g., physics or mathematics), or equivalent practical experience.
  • 2 years of experience in implementing, troubleshooting, and RESOURCESing Unix/Linux operating system, Internet-based applications and/or web servers.
  • Experience with scripting and actively writing/modifying code to improve monitoring or deployment (C, C++, Java, Python, Go, Perl or Ruby).

Preferred qualifications:

  • 5 years of experience in implementing, troubleshooting, and supporting Unix/Linux operating system, Internet-based applications and web servers.
  • Fluency in multiple programming languages.
  • Excellent customer service, leadership, organizational, communication and interface skills.
  • Experience with algorithms, data structures, complexity analysis and software design.

About the Company

The UniLink Group is a company based in the United States that provides cloud-based supply chain management software to retailers, suppliers, third-party logistics providers and partners. The company's headquarters is in Venice, Florida and we have a growing remote and distributed team.

Responsibilities:

  • Work in a fast-paced environment while ensuring the uptime and availability of our products to customers.
  • Respond to tickets within team defined Service Level Objectives (SLO).
  • Work with customers in all phases of development including requirements, coding, testing and post-deployment production support.
  • Participate in comprehensive code reviews, estimation sessions, architectural designs and reviews and adhere to best practices.
