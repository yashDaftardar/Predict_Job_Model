Software Engineer

At SumTotal, we are all about making work matter. We believe every team member has the potential to be AMAZING. We are bold, sharp, driven and most of all, real. Join us in our quest to democratize learning and help individuals unleash their edge.

Sumtotal is looking for a Software Engineer to join our Reporting team in providing an inclusive and expanding reporting solution. We are looking for individuals who are eager to grow in their position while learning different aspects of our enterprise suite. This position will allow individuals to explore different technical areas whether it be working within SQL Server optimizing queries for reports and ETLs to diving into our newly implemented reactJS framework delivering front-end functionality for direct customers use. Individual will need to understand and be comfortable with working in an agile deliver model.

Opportunity Highlights:

  • Learn to review, design, implement, and modify aspects of approved applications.
  • Write code, complete documentation, and unit test systems and applications.
  • Assist in converting project specifications and procedures into detailed logical flow charts for coding.
  • Responsible for coding, unit testing, implementation and maintenance of new and existing modules and applications.
  • Work collaboratively with Quality Assurance (QA) engineers through participation in the testing process and review of QA documentation.
  • Ensure that the long-term architectural and performance strategies are considered when designing and coding solutions.
  • Consistently deliver work products that exceed department quality metrics.
  • Impact of a Software Engineer's work will be seen by making our customers more efficient and have better working environments.

Skills & Qualifications:

  • Bachelors or Masters in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Information Systems, or other technical related degree
  • 1-3 years of professional software development experience
  • Strong analytical skills and object-orientated design capabilities
  • Strong development experience using C#
  • Development experience using C++ is added advantage
  • Knowledge in JQuery and JavaScript frameworks like AngularJS Or Typescript based framework Angular
  • Database Management skills in MS SQL Server (2019)

SUCCESS QUALITIES:
