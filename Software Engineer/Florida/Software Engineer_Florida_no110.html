Job Description

Danfoss in Tallahassee, FL is searching for a Software Engineer to join their team.


As the pioneer and world leader of the oil-free centrifugal compressor product category with its award winning Variable Speed, Magnetic Bearing Compressors, Danfoss Turbocor is dedicated to the design, manufacture, marketing and support of the world’s most efficient commercial refrigerant compressors specifically designed for the HVAC industry.


The Software and Electronics team is beginning a new project which will design a single cross product platform to support all existing products while allowing for expandability in support of future products in our compass. The Software Engineering team will develop an entire new code baseline for the embedded controls (e.g., bearing control, motor control, PID, etc.) and customer experience. The customer experience side will include heavy focus on web interfaces. Additional areas for software development include automated testing tools, desktop UI for internal engineering support tools, as well as production line support tools.

Job Responsibilities

  • Participate in full Software Development Life Cycle tasks: requirements elicitation, design, implementation, verification activities, documentation, deployment and maintenance
  • Will develop product requirement specifications and verification test procedures
  • Will implement or maintain applications in both the Embedded
  • Read and interpret product specifications, drawings, schematics and designs
  • Work collaboratively within the team and with other teams at Danfoss Turbocor
  • Participate in new technology research and development projects as requested
  • Other duties may be assigned

Background & Skills

  • Bachelor of Computer Sciences or Computer Engineering is required
  • Minimum 3 years’ of professional experience in the Full SDLC is required
  • Experience developing PC or Embedded applications using C or C++ (Linux experience preferred)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Automated Software Testing experience
  • Embedded Linux
  • Peripheral (SPI, I2C, USB, WiFi, Ethernet) driver design and troubleshooting
  • TI C2000 MCU family or ARM7

Other Competencies:

  • Strong analytical and technical skills with a desire to learn
  • Capable of developing in Linux and Windows environments
  • Self-motivated individual
  • Proficient understanding of code versioning tools, such as Git
  • Basic project co-ordination skills and a personal drive for results
  • Must be comfortable guiding/delegating interns with development tasks
  • Up to 20% travel availability
