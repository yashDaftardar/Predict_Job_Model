THE ROLE: Associate Engineer, DevOps

Presidio is seeking an Associate Engineer, DevOps to join our team in a remote capacity.

The Associate Engineer, DevOps will be responsible to administer, operate, and maintain the core IT environment of the Presidio vSOC team. Candidates must have the capability to work effectively in a result driven environment, both independently and as a team member; ability to interact well with all levels of management and technical resources. Self-motivation and dedication to objectives is essential. The position will be participating on an on-call rotation and may be called upon to work off-hours to support customer escalations. The candidate will be expected to interact with peers and management via phone, IM, video, and email to ensure issues are resolved per internal and contracted SLA’s related to the Managed Security Solutions.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Work with team members to successfully monitor and report in real time the status of the Presidio vSOC core environment, to include decision-quality statistics on both internal and client data
  • Develop processes and documentation to magnify the benefits of existing Presidio vSOC tools
  • Use methods such as coding, scripting, data collection, data management, and custom-dashboards to drive the creation of decision-quality business intelligence for Presidio vSOC stakeholders and clients
  • Use methods such as scripting, data collection, data management, and custom-dashboards to drive business efficiencies and time-saving capabilities for Presidio vSOC stakeholders and clients
  • Review Presidio vSOC reporting and SLO goals, and verify Presidio vSOC intelligence platforms are accurately reporting on and meeting these goals
  • Administer, operate, and maintain the Presidio vSOC core environment, including

ake Oswego, OR. installation, configuration, backups, and general health maintenance of major components

  • Upgrade and patch the Presidio vSOC core environment as necessary
  • Execute the Engineering operations and maintenance standards of the Presidio vSOC core environment
  • Create collaborative environment that encourages growth and information sharing including mentoring and educating team members
  • Provide the highest level of support for the Presidio vSOC environment

Additional Desired Skills:

  • Previous experience or aptitude to be successful in a System Administration and Operations capacity
  • Experience in maintaining multiple operating systems, to include Windows, CentOS, and Ubuntu
  • Strong communications skills including the ability to communicate technical information in using non-technical language
  • Strong data management and collection skills in working with technologies such as APIs, Microsoft SQL, and others
  • Ability to stay current with latest trends in system administration and data quality for on-premise and cloud solutions
  • Collaborates with team members to identify risks and business requirements for intelligent alerting
  • Has strong ability to identify risks and translate that information to non-security internal/external teams
  • Recommends and coordinates the implementation of administrative and technical controls to support and enforce defined operational policies
  • Supports the development and implementation of operational policy, standards, guidelines and procedures to ensure successful ongoing maintenance
  • Researches, evaluates, designs, recommends, and plans the implementation of information security technology and processes, and analyzes its impact on the existing environment
  • Ability to understand business and technical requirements and develop associated documentation and system administration methods.

Education and Experience:

  • Bachelor's Degree or the equivalent work experience and/or military experience
  • 1+ years’ applicable experience in the IT industry.
  • Previous work experience utilizing scripting
  • Previous work experience working with API
  • Previous work experience administering Windows and Linux Operating systems
  • Excellent problem-solving and technical skills
