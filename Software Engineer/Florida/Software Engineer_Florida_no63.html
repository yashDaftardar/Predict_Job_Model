Full Job Description

Job Summary:
We specialize in designing, developing, implementing, and supporting integrated advanced technology systems to address complex business challenges. Our core proprietary technologies incorporate artificial intelligence applications, including video analytics-based security applications, intelligent process controls, and information technologies for a wide range of global customers. For more information, please visit our website at http://www.duostech.com/.
We are seeking a C# Software Engineer with proven experience in application and windows services design. The C# Software Engineer will interface with engineering, and technical personnel to clarify solution intent, identify problems, suggest changes, code and develop documented solutions. This is a full-time position located at our corporate headquarters in Jacksonville, FL and reports to the Vice President of Product Innovation.
Responsibilities and Duties:
  • Design, build, and maintain efficient, reusable and reliable code.
  • Work independently and with fellow senior engineers on the development of new complex firmware/software projects.
  • Participate in the full development life cycle of edge device and back-end systems, help design innovative new products, write code, and create Unit tests.
  • Support Engineering in solving design errors and/or modifications of existing, released products.
  • Ensure good coding practice: code review, document code and choices, share knowledge with other team members, and be able to reuse code without having to reinvent the wheel.
  • Respond to time critical issues in a timely manner, while delivering projects on time.
  • Identify bottlenecks and bugs, and devise solutions to these problems.
  • Actively participate in team meetings including daily stand-ups, effort meetings, etc.
  • Periodically perform code reviews for other team members.
  • Collaborate across all departments to ensure solution viability.
Qualifications and Skills:
  • Excellent/5+ years of C# coding abilities, with strong system fundamentals.
  • Understanding of data structures.
  • Understanding of networking and multithreading.
  • Solid understanding of industrial communication protocols and interfaces, such as RS-232, RS-422, RS-485, Ethernet, Wi-Fi, Modbus RTU/IP, and Ethernet/IP.
  • Experience with other programming languages or programs such as C, C#, Java, LabVIEW, and TestComplete preferred.
  • Ability to write and document clean, maintainable code.
  • Ability to understand and write complex algorithms in C++.
  • Background in CLI marshaling between native SDK and C# applications.
  • Experience in developing C/C++ projects for Linux/Unix platform a plus.
  • Experience with industrial automation and machine vision preferred.
  • Experience with Artificial Intelligence/Deep Learning a plus.
  • Strong analytical and creative problem-solving skills.
  • Able to work independently and within a team.
  • Excellent communication skills.
  • Excellent time management and organizational skills.
  • Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment.
Education and Experience:
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field. A combination of education, training and experience will be considered.
  • 4+ years’ experience in similar position.
Physical Requirements:
  • Work environment is indoors, in a clean, well-lighted office environment that is temperature controlled.
  • Required to work near others, sharing an open office space.
Benefits:
Compensation package with incentives based on performance. Benefit package includes paid group health, dental, life, and disability insurance; voluntary life insurance, vision, critical illness, and off the job accident insurances, a 401(k) plan with 4% company match, Employee Assistance Program, a Paid Time Off (PTO) Plan, and 10 paid company holidays.
