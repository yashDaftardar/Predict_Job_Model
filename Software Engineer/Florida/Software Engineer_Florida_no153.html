Sr. DevOps Engineer

Spathe Systems is seeking a Sr. DevOps Engineer for Seneca Innovation supporting SOCOM

Spathe Systems is a rapidly growing SOF led, 8(a) defense contractor headquartered in Tampa, FL with offices in Fayetteville, NC and strategic partner locations in Virginia Beach and Coronado. As a small business with a tight nit family feel, Spathe empowers its employees to solve problems and make decisions.

Clearance: TS (or ability to get one)

Job Description:

DevOps Engineer Sr. (Platform): The DevOps Engineer Sr. (Platform) develops and manages the CI/CD pipelines of a wide variety of applications as well as the software repositories. They oversee, mentor and support DevOps Engineers. They provide deeper levels of experience within CI/CD pipelines to include automation, connection the cloud instance and platform the pipeline resides on.


Requirements:

  • Security first Mindset
  • Infrastructure-as-Code (IaC) Mindset.
  • Strong communication between App Devs, Pipeline-CI/CD team, and Infrastructure.
  • Implement industry standards for EKS/ECS/OpenShift standard practices.
  • Write/Manage CloudFormation/TerraForm/Ansible Templates & Jenkins Groovy Scripts
  • Heavy involvement in Infrastructure architecture
  • Provision and manage AWS Infrastructure such as Ec2 Instances, S3 Storage, EBS Volumes, Load Balancers, VPCs, Subnets and AWS Security Groups using Terraform, CloudFormation, or AWS CLI
  • Creating and implementing Backup Policies with Snapshots and/or S3 Glacier
  • Create RBAC Models for underlying infrastructure for Pipeline Teams and AppDevs
  • Create encryption architecture based on project requirements (data-in-flight & data-at-rest)
  • Least Privilege Approach for IAM Roles & creating service accounts for various tasks
  • SSL Cert Initialization and Installation on various solutions
  • Familiar with Git and remote repos such as GitLab, GitHub, etc.
  • Familiar with Jira and other Agile tracking tools.
  • History of overseeing Jr/Mid DevOps Engineers.
