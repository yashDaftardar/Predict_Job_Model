This position is for a Software Engineer Sr supporting the F-35 Training Systems program. This candidate will work closely with the TMS Operations Support team and will assist with troubleshooting field issues as well as developing, analyzing and testing break fix solutions. The software engineer will be responsible for design, development and integration and unit test of system modifications of varying degrees of complexity as part of a DevOps (cross-functional) Training Management System (TMS) and Learning Management System (LMS) team.

The candidate for this position on the F-35 Program in support of the TMS team will be involved in the design, development, and sustainment of the Training Management System and the Learning Management System. The candidate must have knowledge and experience in software design, development, implementation, testing, documentation and maintenance of software systems. Development experience with Java Web Frameworks (JavaServer Faces is preferred) is required.
Basic Qualifications:
Bachelors Degree in Engineering or related discipline. Minimum of 5 years of engineering experience including two years of experience in a Software Engineering role.
– Experience with software development and design
– Experience with Java Web Frameworks (Java Server Faces preferred)
– Demonstrated ability to handle multiple time critical assignments
– Experience with Windows Servers and workstations
– Experience with the Software Development Lifecycle
– Experience with agile development methods
– Experience with model-based design methods
– Strong planning, organizational, and interpersonal skills
Ability to communicate effectively and work in a collaborative environment
Desired Skills:
The following skills are desired but not required:
– Front end development experience with JavaScript, CSS, HTM
– Excellent troubleshooting skills
– Experience with scripting using Python, PowerShell & VBA
– Experience with information technology (including systems and network administration)
– Experience administering Oracle and MS SQL databases
– Experience in Program problem reports (PPRs), Action Request (AR) process.
– Experience in Virtual environments platforms such as vSphere and Hyper-V
– Experience with development tools such as Jira and Confluence
– Strong analytical skills.
– Strong customer support/service skills.
Support of occasional travel to customer sites CONUS & OCONUS
