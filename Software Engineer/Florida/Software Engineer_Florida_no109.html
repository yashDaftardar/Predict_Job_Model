JOB SCHEDULE:

ABOUT US:

ViacomCBS Streaming (formerly CBS Interactive) is a division of ViacomCBS that encompasses both free, paid, and premium streaming services including Pluto TV, CBS All Access, CBS Sports Digital, and CBS News Digital.

DESCRIPTION:

Brand Overview:

CBS Sports is a division of ViacomCBS, and is a leading publisher of premium digital content with a focus on the invested sports fan. Properties include CBSSports.com, MaxPreps, 247Sports, Sportsline, and more. Our ecosystem spans mobile and desktop apps as well as online streaming services such as CBS Sports HQ and affiliations with the CBS All Access subscription service.

Role Details:

CBS Sports Digital is seeking an experienced DevOps engineer to help develop and improve our multi account and support role structures. This team supports multiple teams and accounts spanning various engineering disciplines including video, mobile, data technologies, content management systems, as well as frontend and backend site infrastructure.

This role requires awareness and hands on experience of Amazon Web Services, serverless technologies, infrastructure as code, security & compliance management, observability, configuration management, devops automation, development lifecycle management, frontend (cdn and load balancing) and backend (DB, caching, etc) delivery/management, and agile methodologies.

Your Day-to-Day:

  • Responsible for service requests and problem resolution.
  • Assist development teams with automations including continuous integration and delivery as well as ensuring observability of said integrations.
  • Assist development teams with infrastructure deployment and code delivery.
  • Will be expected to maintain and participate in pagerduty rotation and schedule.
  • Deliver and support shared services including but not limited to AMI, VPC networking, IAM, logging infrastructure, and account management.
  • Review and assist in security & compliance audits and remediation as it relates to respective team accounts.
  • Provide devops consultation and mentorship to help improve team autonomy.
  • Develop documentation and presentations for peer review.

Key Projects:

  • Will help with automating cloud security remediation.
  • Will work closely with assigned development teams to provide and extend parity in support coverage.
  • Will help provide coverage of CBS events, including the NCAA Tournament, Upcoming and future Super Bowls, Champions League and Europa League Soccer, Masters Golf Tournament and SEC College Football.
  • Will work to improve and extend our shared services catalog.

QUALIFICATIONS:

What you bring to the team:

You have -

  • a strong working knowledge of AWS (or equivalent)
  • worked closely with security teams to improve, automate and remediate security concerns issues in cloud environments.
  • experience scripting in a variety of languages (Bash, Python, Node, etc.)
  • experience in monitoring & logging technologies including agent, agentless, synthetic, etc and are able to identify bottlenecks and assist with debugging, analysis, and optimization
  • a strong understanding of automation, CI/CD and configuration management concepts.

You might also have -

  • cloud or related certifications.
  • managed multi account structures in cloud environments.
  • a strong background in Linux/Unix & Windows Administration
  • experience with serverless frameworks, concepts and architectures
  • experience with mySQL, postgresql and MongoDB (or DocumentDB)
  • knowledge of VCL (varnish)
  • understanding of gitflow
  • experience with terraform, cloud formation or the like

FUNCTION: Software Engineering
