Job Description

U.S. Bank is seeking a DevOps Engineer with process improvement and implementation experience to contribute toward the success of our technology initiatives. Responsible for implementing, maintaining, and providing support for application environments in multi-tiered, multi-platform environment. Responsible for implementing, maintaining and providing support for CI/CD pipelines which build and deploy code into non-production as well as production environments. Installs and monitors software that is between the applications and the operating system. Plans and provides back-end connectivity infrastructure components required for the application. Works with internal infrastructure teams and vendors to provide recommendations for hardware and system software upgrades.

Basic Qualifications
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field, or equivalent work experience
  • At least 2 year of experience with utilizing, configuring, and installing software for connecting distributed software and services across heterogeneous platforms
  • At least 4 years of experience within a total information technology (IT) environment

Preferred Skills/Experience
Working experience in:
  • IT environment
  • CI/CD Pipelines using Jenkins/Cloudbees

Extensive experience in:
  • Deploying and maintaining .NET applications
  • Deploying and maintaining Azure environments
  • Technical troubleshooting
  • Web trends and directions
  • Performance measures and tuning
  • Capacity planning and management
  • Contingency and disaster recovery

Subject matter expert in:
  • System and technical integration
  • IT standards, procedures, policy
