Job Summary:

As a Software Engineer on the Resort Technology team you will be creating innovative solutions by developing and implementing applications, and being part of a team of developers implementing technology products and applications for Walt Disney theme parks globally. As an Engineer, you will be responsible for helping plan and oversee development activities being executed by both internal and vendor development resources. The Agile delivery methodology will be the predominate working model for this team. The Tech role will be regularly expected to participate in meetings, interface laterally with technology and line of business stakeholders, and assumes the main responsibility of release qualitied initiatives steering the technical work of developers / engineers working on assigned projects.

The Tech is a hands-on software engineer who will:
  • Work closely with product managers and story owners to design features and prioritize tasks.
  • Assemble releases and drive improvements in the delivery and release processes.
  • Designs and develops highly scalable software systems and applications in a designated functional foc area(s).
  • Help with recruiting and selection of technical resources.
