Northrop Grumman Defense Systems sector is seeking a Test Engineer to join an agile systems development team designing and building advanced, state-of-the-art, mission planning software for the Mission Planning and Decision Support (MPDS) Operating Unit within the Mission Applications department. This position is located in Melbourne, FL. Roles & Responsibilities: The Test Engineer will have the following roles and responsibilities: - Perform smoke testing, integration testing, regression testing, and formal qualification testing in an agile systems development environment. - Document system problems and verify fixes. - Design, develop, and maintain system test plans, test cases, test procedures, and automated tests in compliance with program functional, technical, and performance requirements. - Work with the lead test engineer and a team of systems engineers and software developers to establish and develop test methods and techniques. - Troubleshoot and modify test tools and test scripts. - Generate test data, perform test data analysis, and develop test documentation. - Perform system integration of COTS, GOTS, and organic hardware and software. - Participate in formal inspection process, customer meetings, and user events. - Provide support to customer testers during acceptance testing, including test procedure walk throughs, documentation of testing issues and test results, and answering questions on system functionality.

Basic Qualifications:
To be considered for this position, you must minimally meet the knowledge, skills, and abilities listed below: - BS degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field with 2 years of experience or a relevant Master's Degree with 0 years of experience. Four years of additional experience will be considered in lieu of a college degree. - Experience with generating test plans, test cases, and test procedures. - Experience with test execution, test analysis, and troubleshooting. - Ability to function in multi-disciplinary, team-oriented environment with frequent customer contact. - Ability to rapidly adapt to changing work priorities. - Willingness to learn and participate in non-test activities, such as requirements development, customer interface, and risk management. - Familiarity with agile software development. - Experience with MS Windows applications and proficient in the use of MS Office software. - Strong interpersonal, listening, verbal, and written communication skills. - Active In-Scope DoD Secret Security Clearance that has been granted / renewed within the past 5 years. - Ability to obtain and maintain access to special programs. - Ability to obtain and maintain a Top Secret Clearance.
Preferred Qualifications:
Candidates with these desired skills will be given preferential consideration: - Experience with US DoD mission planning. - Experience with Joint Mission Planning System (JMPS) Framework (FW). - Experience working with agile software development teams. - Experience with one or more automated test tools, such as TestComplete and Squish. - Experience with collaboration and development tools, such as Microsoft Visio, TeamForge, JIRA, and Confluence. - Experience with one or more automated IA test tools, such as Fortify. - Experience with one or more scripting languages to facilitate automation and tool development. - Experience with security architectures, firewalls and network security controls. - Current DoD 8570 IAT II Certification (Security+, CEH, CISSP, etc.). - Active Top Secret Clearance.
