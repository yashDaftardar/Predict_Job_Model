Who are Provoke?
At Provoke, we combine the exceptional talent of our people with the latest technology to drive business change for our customers.
Our vision is simple; deliver a service that places as much emphasis on user experience and software adoption as it does on technical brilliance to drive value for our clients.

What you’ll do
You’ll lead projects and be part of making critical design, technical and deployment decisions, while actively encouraging teamwork. You’ll have a strong desire to innovate and apply current thinking to new technology and methods.

What are we looking for?
We’re after a creative, solution focused Senior Developers with an excellent understanding of Object Orriented Programming. We work with .NET, so you'll have a keen interested in learning this if it's not your core programming language.

We're looking for people that don't want to just cut code, but who wants to change the world for our clients through excellent work practices. Someone who can bring their experience and enjoys sharing best practices with their team mates. Specifically, you’ll need:
  • Experience having lead projects
  • Solution design/documentation experience
Technologies we use are as follows:
  • Microsoft platform: ASP.NET Core, C#, MVC, .NET, SQL, Cosmos, Azure Services
  • Non-SQL databases: Mongo, DocumentDB
  • Latest Angular/React including Node
  • REST API creation & consumption
  • Unit testing, TDD or similar
  • CI/CD implementation
  • HTML5 and CSS3 (Sass, Less)
What's in it for you?
Where do we start? How about an excellent package that includes your salary plus the following perks?
  • You’ll spend your days working with talented and energetic people
  • We care about each other and foster a culture of innovation, authenticity and trust.
  • We have some neat perks to look after your health and wellbeing, making life just that little bit easier.
  • Combine all that with strong ties to our Wellington, Auckland and US offices and you’ve landed yourself a pretty awesome new role!
  • Free health insurance
  • Subsidized mobile plan
  • MSDN subscription with free Azure credits
  • Free training offers and exams
This role can be based fully remote or in Auckland, Wellington
