We are looking for a C# .NET software engineer to join our ERP development team. We have office locations in Lititz PA and Tampa Fl, and are offering 100% remote work for candidates who reside in one of the following states: (DE, FL, GA, MD, NC, PA, SC, TN or VA)*.

The Clark ERP engineering team is responsible for building from scratch software which acts as the heart of the organization, controlling procurement, warehouse and inventory management, logistics, sales order processing, vendor and customer management, and company financials. We operate across multiple business units, integrate with numerous other systems, and support new business initiatives and data collection for one of the fastest growing companies in PA.

Technology-wise, the ERP software engineering team uses a Microsoft .NET development stack with C# as our language of choice. The main system we support is a WPF desktop application; however we also work on numerous web services, queued services, front-end web interfaces, and mobile apps.

  • Ability to keep track of complicated requirements and business rules.

  • Self-driven with a strong desire to contribute, learn, and improve.
