About The Internship

At e-Builder, we are committed to providing an exceptional experience for our interns. We tailor our program to provide participants with real, impactful work, contributing directly to the continued success of e-Builder.

The intern program is a 10 to 12 week program that runs approximately from June to August.

You will work on real projects/features used on the web, Android, and iOS devices that address real customer needs.

Depending on your background and interests, work on web UIs, back-end Java code, development tools, test automation, or iOS & Android development.

Interns will participate in the full development life cycle, taking part in all Kanban ceremonies from planning and estimation through demonstration of work completed. You will collaborate with Product Managers, Software Architects, UX experts, and fellow Software Engineers where you will be mentored and challenged.

Responsibilities

  • Build software that is a joy to use! - Easy, intuitive, and allows users to accomplish their work more efficiently than without the software.

  • Participate in all aspects of product development as needed, from requirements analysis through product release utilizing Agile methodology.

  • Assist with testing strategies, conditions, and evaluation of expected results.

  • Develop and communicate implementation approaches for assigned projects.

  • Regularly and proactively communicate status, risks, and other issues related to projects that are under development.

  • Collaborate with UI/UX design, product managers, and engineering to solve challenging usability problems.

Qualifications

To be considered for an internship opportunity, you must be a current student or recent college graduate.

  • Enrolled in a Computer Science (or related) program of study

  • Must have completed your sophomore year

  • Can commit to a full-time, M-F work schedule

  • GPA is 3.0 or higher

  • Genuinely passionate about engineering

  • Excited about working with new technologies

  • Driven by curiosity

  • A problem solver

  • Self-motivated

  • Results-driven

  • Collaborative

  • A strong communicator

  • Able to thrive in a dynamic, fast-paced environment
