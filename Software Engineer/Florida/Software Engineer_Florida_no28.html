General Description / Primary Purpose

The IT Software Engineer, under the direction of a Manager or above, assists in the design, development and implementation of departmental processes and/or programs.

Essential Duties

Job Function
  • Provide upper tier technical support for Banner
  • Monitor, maintain, develop, enhance, and patch Banner attached report, web, and integration solutions. Familiarity with all aspects of these solution implementations including job / job scheduling, required host scripts for jobs and data loads, and data level and web development.
  • Capability with the following technologies: PL/SQL, Bash shell scripting.
  • Spearhead technical Quality Assurance efforts for Banner and its attached web and integration solutions
  • Participate in requirements gathering, translating customer needs into actionable development specifications.
– During declared campus emergencies, this position may be required to perform specific job related duties at a designated off campus location or place of residence.


Percentage Of Time

Marginal Functions
  • Attends meetings of Information Technology Services staff; utilizes staff resources when situations require additional expertise.
– Serves on committees, task forces, and work groups that pertain to applications support, as assigned by higher management.
