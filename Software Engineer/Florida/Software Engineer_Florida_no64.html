Minimum Qualifications

At least 6 years of software development experience in a professional environment and/or comparable experience such as:

  • Familiar with Agile or other rapid application development methods
  • Experience with design and coding across one or more platforms and languages like Java, C#
  • Hands-on expertise with application design, software development and automated testing
  • Experience with distributed (multi-tiered) systems, algorithms, and relational databases
  • Experience with ProcessUnity or similar applications in ThirdParty LifeCycle Mamagement and Contract LifeCycle Management area
  • Confirmed experience with object-oriented design and coding with variety of languages
  • Knowledge of Test Automation Frameworks
  • Knowledge of DevOps Practices
  • Understanding of the evolving compliance and regulatory requirements and environment and how to incorporate technology, infrastructure and security requirements into the product design
  • Excellent communication skills with the ability to engage, influence, and inspire partners to drive collaboration and alignment
  • High degree of organization, individual initiative and personal accountability
  • Bachelor’s Degree in computer science, computer science engineering, or related experience required; advanced degree preferre
