Full Job Description

Halfaker and Associates, an award-winning high growth small business, creates innovative and customer-centric technology solutions in the areas of Digital Services, Data Analytics, Cyber Security and Cloud Services to improve the health, security and well-being of all Americans. Our commitment to excellence and our vision to “Continue to Serve” has resulted in steady growth and an expanding client base across government agencies in the health, defense, security and intelligence sectors. Our strong focus on internal culture has helped Halfaker achieve several workplace awards including Great Place to Work Certification™ and Tampa Bay Top Workplaces. Headquartered in Arlington, VA, we have employees nationwide. Please take a moment to browse through our website and learn more about what it means to serve with Halfaker.


Halfaker has an opening for a Software Engineering Manager to join our talented, dynamic team. The key responsibilities for this position include:
  • Partner with the Program Manager, Dev Managers, Product Owners, and key stakeholders to plan and support software development and application enhancements
  • Lead the teams in all Agile/SAFe/DevOps standards and processes
  • Manage the teams via the CA Agile Central “Rally” and GitHub tools Coordinate release and sprint planning with the Veterans Administration (VA)
  • Conduct SCRUM meetings and other Agile ceremonies
  • Manage and track resources to ensure program success Provide leadership and guidance to coach, motivate, and lead team members to their optimum performance levels
  • Create and review technical designs for large and complex features
  • Provide oversite of the Software Development and Testing environments
  • Lead project’s architectural approach, requirements, design, environment setups, configuration management, development, testing, user acceptance, release and operations/maintenance activities as well as all DevOps related automation
  • Manage and coordinate process requirements for various projects
  • Lead Agile requirements management activities; lead the analysis of user needs to determine functional, cross-functional, and technical requirements
  • Responsible for collecting, analyzing, managing and reporting key Program and Product Team execution status and metrics across all aspects of the Agile product development lifecycle using VIP as the minimum performance baseline
  • Responsible to support through leading communications, program baseline support, strategic planning and analysis, data collection, quality reviews, risk management, and overall management of supporting projects
  • Monitor, track and provide client reports on project schedule, work plan, risks and budget to ensure project meets targeted dates and goals
  • Capture and monitor action items status, produce and communicate meeting minutes and drive action to their proper resolution or conclusion
  • Manage the collection, tracking and monitoring of functional and technical requirements
  • Identify and remove Blockers and impediments impacting the team's progress
  • Develop and maintain product road maps Facilitate accurate configuration management for the product team related artifacts
  • Verify traceability amongst requirements, test cases and potential risks and impediments
  • Provide daily supervision and direction to support staff as needed


Required Skills
  • Experience managing development roadmaps in an Agile environment
  • Experience in preparing and leading Program Increment (PI) Planning Ability to work with development teams to remove impediments and escalate issues as needed.
  • Experience working in the Veterans Affairs (VA) VistA Environment
  • Experience working with a Program Management Office (PMO) in support of Software Development and Release Management Activities
  • Experience using CA Agile Central “Rally” and/or JIRA Experience with Microsoft Azure Gov Cloud and/ or Amazon Web Services (AWS)
  • Must possess excellent problem-solving skills Highly motivated and analytical; proven problem-solving abilities
  • Ability to take initiative
  • Ability and enthusiasm for working in a fast-paced environment
  • Willingness and enthusiasm to learn new skills, new client environments, and grow domain expertise
  • Ability to learn quickly
  • Ability to conduct independent research to lead or support the resolution of complex questions and issues
  • Excellent verbal communication and oral presentation skills
  • Excellent analytical, written, and overall communication skills
  • Excellent planning and project oversight skills
  • Excellent leadership, management, and delegation skills
  • Excellent prioritization and time management skills
  • Excellent attention to detail and ability to meet deadlines and stay organized


Required Experience
  • Bachelor’s degree in Engineering, Computer Science, Systems, Business or related scientific/technical discipline
  • 10+ years of relevant experience
  • 2-4 years of experience with the Release Management Process (or Business Analyst X.12 Medical Claims)
  • Must be able to receive a Security Clearance / Public Trust
