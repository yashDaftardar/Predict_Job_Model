What does this role look like?
Do you have a passion for working on emerging technologies? The Fleet Ballistic Missile (FBM) program is experiencing significant growth and we need your expertise to deliver amazing new technologies to our customers. We have opportunities in the FBM Information Systems (IS) Applications group to get in on the ground floor of a modernization effort that will shape the future of the organization. This is a rare opportunity to work closely alongside our Navy Strategic Systems Program (SSP) partners to field several future strategic systems applications that the nation urgently needs.

The FBM IS Software Engineering Associate Manager Responsibilities will include:
  • Management for a 8 to 15 person team.
  • Manage personnel resources to support financial plans.
  • Build and maintain a staffing plan, coordinate forecasts with the LOB, recruit and hire to develop the proper skill level and diversity mix.
  • Focus on providing guidance to staff members to meet critical schedules and resolve critical/complex
technical or operational problems.
  • Perform administrative management duties for software staff. This includes timecard approval (timekeeping/overtime authorization), compliance training, promotions, job actions, performance
reviews, awards, and terminations.
  • Own, execute, and status department level initiatives.
  • Change agent in responsible area with focus on continuous improvement, advancing technical capabilities and innovation.
Basic Qualifications:
To be successful, you need to possess these basic qualifications:
  • Software development experience including requirements analysis, design, coding, test planning, unit-testing, etc.
  • Experience as a technical leader working on complex technical systems
  • Agile methodologies, processes and practices in the full lifecycle of software development
  • Demonstrated capability to proactively work through difficult technical, project and/or resource challenges
  • Strong business acumen and whole systems thinking
  • Ability to collaborate with multi-functional teams
  • Strong communication skills, both verbal and written, with the ability to influence and communicate at all management levels and customer organizations
  • Must be a US Citizen, and be able to obtain and maintain a SECRET clearance, to support this government contract.
Desired Skills:
It is desired that the candidate has experience with one or more skills below:
  • Oracle SQL, PL/SQL, and/or Oracle Forms/Reports
  • Continuous Integration / Continuous Deployment Pipeline development
  • Basis of Estimate (BOE) Development
  • Java, .Net Core, and/or Angular Software Development
  • RESTful microservices
  • Containerization and Cloud-Based Software Architecture
