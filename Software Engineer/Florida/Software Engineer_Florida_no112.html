Job Overview :

Restaurant Brands International Inc. ("RBI") is one of the world's largest quick service restaurant companies with more than $30 billion in system-wide sales and over 25,000 restaurants in more than 100 countries and U.S. territories. RBI owns three of the world's most prominent and iconic quick service restaurant brands – TIM HORTONS®, BURGER KING®, and POPEYES®.

As a DevOps engineer at RBI you will be responsible for building a modern and scalable CI/CD pipeline for our consumer applications across all our brands in multiple regions, which serve millions of users and process hundreds of thousands of daily orders. Additionally, you will help manage our cloud infrastructure in AWS, add monitoring to ensure uptime of our serverless application stack and work with various engineering teams across our organization to architect solutions.

Responsibilities :

  • Build out a continuous delivery platform for a high throughput engineering team
  • Design and manage cloud infrastructure
  • Guarantee system reliability
  • Work with security team to ensure integrity of our application and data

Required Skills :

  • 3+ years of engineering experience in a cloud environment
  • Production experience building and maintaining a deployment pipeline
  • Experience managing infrastructure with code (Terraform/Serverless)
  • Experience with scripting languages

Preferred Skills

  • Well organized and motivated, able to prioritize work according to business needs
  • Aware of industry best practices for release management and SRE
  • Experience with CircleCI or other CI systems such as Travis or Jekins
  • Experience with NodeJS
