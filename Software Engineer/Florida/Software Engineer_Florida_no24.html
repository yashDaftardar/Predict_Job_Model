Full Job Description

Overview:

World Travel Holdings is seeking a Software Engineer for its Business to Business (B2B) Division – Dream Vacations, CruiseOne and Cruises Inc., based in Ft. Lauderdale, FL. The Software Engineer will design and implement solutions and components that meet business requirements and evolves our enterprise application. The ideal candidate has 2+ years of Java experience, and is a forward-thinking, problem solver with exceptional analytical skills. Influence the architecture and design of next generation technology while learning what works and doesn’t work at this scale.


About World Travel Holdings
World Travel Holdings is the nation’s largest cruise agency and award-winning leisure travel company with a portfolio of more than 40 diverse brands. In addition to owning some of the largest brands distributing cruises, villas, resort vacations, car rentals, resort day passes and luxury travel services, World Travel Holdings has a vast portfolio of private-label partnerships comprised of top leisure travel providers, including almost every U.S. airline, leading hotel brands and prominent corporations. The company also operates a top-rated travel agency franchise and the country's original host agency, and is consistently recognized as an industry leader in work-at-home employment. Its global presence includes operating multiple cruise brands in the United Kingdom. World Travel Holdings has offices in Wilmington, Mass.; Ft. Lauderdale, Fla.; and Chorley, England. Responsibilities:
  • Design and develop clear and comprehensive front-end solutions
  • Lead/participate in design discussions, prototype development and code reviews
  • Ability to work in an agile team environment, and collaborate cross-functionally to develop in sprints
  • Debug and troubleshoot existing program code, and determine optimum solutions when required
  • Ability to manage priorities and schedules, including status reports to project management team
  • Comply with coding and project standards and processes
Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree in computer science or related field
  • At least 2-3 years of web development experience utilizing Angular, Typescript, Javascript
  • Experience utilizing Spring, Hibernate DAO/JPA, and Java a significant plus
  • Practical, in-depth knowledge of the fundamentals of object-oriented design and programming
  • Exceptional analytical skills and problem solving ability
  • Very strong, delivery focused work ethic that takes ownership and produces results
  • Ability to multitask and switch tracks as needed
  • Solid verbal and written communication skills
  • Ability to cultivate and maintain collaborative relationships within the team and the enterprise at large
