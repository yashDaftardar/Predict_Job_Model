Halfaker and Associates, an award-winning high growth small business, creates innovative and customer-centric technology solutions in the areas of Digital Services, Data Analytics, Cyber Security and Cloud Services to improve the health, security and well-being of all Americans. Our commitment to excellence and our vision to “Continue to Serve” has resulted in steady growth and an expanding client base across government agencies in the health, defense, security and intelligence sectors. Halfaker has an opening for a Senior Software Engineer (Front-End) to join our talented, dynamic team. The key responsibilities for this position include:


  • Design, build, and implement custom applications using the declarative customization capabilities of the Force.com platform
  • Develop customized solutions within the Salesforce platform that will be used externally by both internal and external stakeholders in various SalesForce Communities
  • Create applications to integrate Salesforce, MuleSoft, and other business systems
  • Work as, and work with, architects on solutions design and delivery, including integration with existing architecture, while ensuring proper change management and auditable software delivery
  • Exhibiting extensive experience in utilizing and evangelizing agile software development practices and structured software development methodologies in the role of Software Engineer, Architect or Sr. Developer
  • Build, develop, and grow business relationships vital to the success of the project
  • Assess and recommend technical design choices to software developers, including but not limited to platforms, coding and technical levels
  • Play a role in the functional business requirements elicitation and elaboration ensuring that every business need is achievable and aligned with the technical roadmap
  • Develop automated test capabilities including scripts and execution and reporting framework for all layers of the application stack as part of the build process
  • Coordinate testing across multiple, escalating test environments, to facilitate unit, functional, performance, and user-acceptance testing
  • Contribute to an atmosphere of cross-functional teamwork within the organizations Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe) project lifecycle, including key interactions with Product Management and QA
  • Support the development and delivery of measurement reports to provide on-going tracking and reporting of delivery results. Prepare high-quality management status reports
  • Provide communications and follow-through on development issues that manifest as a result of changes/deployments
  • Maintain a keen view of the product from the customers perspective, and experience
  • Perform other administrative and technical duties as required


Required Skills
  • Strong knowledge of EAI/SOA best practices, development methodologies & standards
  • Proficient with Lightning Components, Apex, Visualforce, SOQL, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and APIs
  • Solid understanding of the non-code features of Salesforce
  • Proven background launching significant system enhancements and release cycles
  • Salesforce certification
  • Familiarity with Agile software development methodology
  • Experience and knowledge with: Object-Oriented development, web applications development, databases and other program languages
  • Experience with all phases of software development life cycle from Business Requirement Analysis to Production implementation. Experience with Agile methodologies
  • Experience troubleshooting technical issues related to data transformation and flow
  • Strong awareness of latest web standards (OAuth, SAML, OpenID Connect & others)
  • Proficiency in handling large dataset, writing optimized API’s
  • Proficiency in Data extraction, file load, file creation, insert/update data in XML table
  • Experience utilizing Test-Driven Development and developing automated unit test and guiding QA team to conduct end-to-end testing
  • Development experience and API integration with Oracle
  • Interacting ad collaborating with business users and IT personnel
  • Exhibiting a strong commitment to quality by actively measuring and reporting software quality metrics that improve rapidly
  • Acting on opportunities to improve software quality through the use of Static Code Quality Analysis to paint a story that depicts continuous improvement
  • Excellent written & verbal communication skills
  • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic environment
  • Be Self-motivated with the ability to work well both in groups and independently


Required Experience
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related technical field
    • 8 years of additional relevant experience may be substituted for education
  • 8 years of relevant experience
