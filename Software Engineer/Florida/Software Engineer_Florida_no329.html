Full Job Description

Gubagoo is a fast growing provider of messaging and commerce solutions for automotive dealers changing the future of how people find, buy and service their cars. We’re helping dealerships and consumers to modernize the vehicle purchasing and servicing experience by providing them with powerful messaging and commerce tools to transact online. We are also a two-time member of the Deloitte Fast 500 list – honoring the fastest growing companies in North America.

Role

We are looking for a passionate individual to join our team as a front-end developer. This is a full-time position. The development team works remotely (from home) using video and other solutions for daily communication. Very rewarding work environment with a team that is passionate about learning and delivering great software products. Team and culture fit is important.

Our technology involves real-time transactions for over two million visitors per day. As a front-end Javascript Developer, you'll work with primarily with Javascript, React, Redux, HTML, CSS, and Vanilla JS building the front-end experience of our dealership facing applications, enabling dealerships to engage and communicate with car shoppers. Our system is extremely high-load providing a wonderful learning experience in a professional high-load environment. We have multiple applications that comprise our system including both desktop and mobile applications.

What you will be doing:

  • Working within a cross-functional squad to complete tasks that contribute to meaningful user and team outcomes.
  • Working on our web-based consumer and dealership facing applications to make delightful user experiences.
  • Releasing code to production on a weekly basis to drive iterative improvements in our products.
  • Helping generate ideas to produce valuable new features and enhancements for our customers.
  • Working with your squad to hone processes to improve working efficiency.
  • Being a positive presence on your squad to help the team achieve its goals.


What we would like to see:

  • 5+ years software development experience
  • 3+ years experience with front-end Javascript
  • 2+ years experience with React and Redux
  • Up-to-date knowledge of modern HTML and CSS
  • Experience interfacing with third-party APIs and frameworks
  • Strong personal and communication skills
  • Experience with Git
