Company Description


Chromalloy has expanded from a gas turbine parts repair business into an OEM supplier of production parts and advanced repairs and services for gas turbines used in aviation and power applications. Chromalloy serves the airline, military, marine and energy segments with a broad range of services at locations around the globe.

Chromalloy is a subsidiary of Sequa Corporation. Sequa Corporation is a diversified industrial company with operations in the aerospace, metal coatings and automotive industries. Sequa is a Carlyle Group company. For additional information, visit www.sequa.com.


Job Description


Will be responsible for the deployment of Teamcenter, NX, and other mechanical design tools, supporting a variety of Teamcenter applications.

Primary Accountabilities:

  • Function as PLM architect with focus on Core Teamcenter.
  • Translate business requirements into specific PLM solutions for complex projects.
  • Develop/document AS IS and TO BE Solution Specification for team members to work from.
  • Work with support team to Investigate and troubleshoot Teamcenter data to continuously improve the application.
  • Provide Technical guidance and mentor support team members in advanced PLM functionalities.
  • Lead designing, developing, coding and testing the architected solutions.
  • Support project management activities to control scope, cost, schedule and quality.

Challenges:

  • Keeping the training materials up to date and managing timely delivery for global population.
  • Developing critical core capabilities globally.
  • Working in a highly matrixed organization

Competencies:

1. Interpersonal Savvy – Build constructive and effective relationships, have strong interpersonal and service-oriented skills with the ability to interface well with engineers, and other staff members at all levels.

2. Organizing – Can orchestrate multiple activities at once to accomplish a goal; arranges files and information in a useful manner

3. Systems Management – Can design best practices, training tools, and corporate procedures


Qualifications


Education: BS in Engineering or Computer Science; MS Preferred

Experience: 10+ years designing Teamcenter solutions; 5-7 year’s work experience demonstrating knowledge and experience in the administration of all phases of Teamcenter

Skills:

  • Ability to present complex information and communicate in an effective manner
  • Experience in computer science, information management, system engineering, or related technical field.
  • Experience with programming languages: C, C++, JAVA, Perl and XML.
  • Extensive experience with PDM information system infrastructure.
  • Experience with development tools and processes.
  • A self-starter with strong communication skills.
  • Demonstrated experience with Siemens, Teamcenter application usage and development and rollout.
  • Knowledge of Configuration Management Practices
  • Strong BMIDE, client side, server-side customization, SOA and Integration with ERP systems. Requires excellent problem solving, oral and written communication skills.
  • Knowledge of PC and UNIX Workstation operating systems and application implementation as related to CAD.
  • Highly motivated individual with the ability to complete tasks with minimal supervision.
