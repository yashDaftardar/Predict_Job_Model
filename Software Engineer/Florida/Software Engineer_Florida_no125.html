Magic Leap is looking to expand its Lumin Runtime team, and we are looking for exceptionally talented senior programmers who are ready for a challenge to help in the production of our proprietary Spatial computing application framework.

Responsibilities

  • Implement the systems, input mechanisms, interactions and responsive elements for mixed reality environments.
  • Work closely with UX designers to realize their specifications and designs.
  • Write elegant, maintainable, reusable code.
  • Work within a custom runtime engine and expand the capabilities as required.
  • Troubleshoot and resolve software defects and other technical issues.
  • Mentor junior engineers on technical and professional skills to enable career growth.

Qualifications

  • 8+ years of experience in a relevant industry.
  • Very strong programming skills with extensive experience in C, C++.
  • Understanding of computer graphics, 3d engines and 3d technology principles a plus.
  • Understanding of the art pipeline a plus.
  • Strong computer science and maths background.
  • Ability to write clean, fast, reliable and highly scalable software.
  • Ability to do task breakdown and write documentation.
  • Excellent problem solving, troubleshooting and debugging skills.
  • Must be a team player and know the importance of listening to others.
  • Must have a high attention to detail and extremely high bar for the quality of their work.
  • Able to work independently, prioritize and handle pressure.
  • Must have great time management skills to perform in a busy work environment.
  • Exceptional communications skills.

Experience

  • BS, MS or PhD in Computer Science, Math, Physics, or a related technical field
