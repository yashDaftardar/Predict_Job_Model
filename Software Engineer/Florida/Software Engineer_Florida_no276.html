Full Job Description

We are seeking a Software Engineer to join VMware’s Cloud Management Automation SaaS team. We build creative, comprehensive cloud management solutions, products and cloud services that manage hybrid (multi-cloud) environments running anything from traditional to container (cloud native) workloads delivering availability, performance, capacity and cost management.

VMware's Cloud Assembly SaaS service turns all your private and commercial cloud environments into one integrated cloud. You build and deploy your computing resources wherever you want, when you want. VMware Automation provides dev-ops capability to respond quickly as your needs change.

Role and Responsibility

Strong programming skills, are well versed in foundational aspects of CS and OOP

Passion for test driven development, quality and excitement for solving problems

Self-motivated and have the ability to work in an agile manner

Deliver responsive, high performance and scalable user interfaces

Collaborate with UX, product managers and other technical teams.

What you bring

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science

3+ years related full lifecycle development experience

Experience with modern Javascript and UI frameworks (e.g Angular 2, ExtJS)

Practical understanding of OOP/OOD & design patterns

Bonus

Master's degree in Computer Science

Familiarity in services oriented architecture, AWS, Kubernetes, Docker, or GIT

Basic understanding of Sass/Less

Knowledge of UI security, performance and testability
