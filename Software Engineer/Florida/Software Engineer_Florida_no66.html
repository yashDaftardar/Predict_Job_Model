SUMMARY:

The Software Engineer reports up through the Engineering Management team and performs software design, code, unit test, integration and testing to satisfy project requirements. Additionally, this position will be assigned other projects or tasks in alignment with the level and scope of the position.


ESSENTIAL DUTIES / RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Creates use cases and performs object-oriented analysis and design with close interaction with customer and domain experts.
  • Develops testing routines and/or procedures.
  • Creates flowcharts and other documentations to identify requirements for software and to propose possible solutions.
  • Coordinates with hardware engineers and other staff to forecast whether the design will be feasible under cost and time constraints.
  • Adheres to strict CMMI quality procedures to ensure first time quality in delivered products.
  • Performs software requirements analysis and may lead subsystem level development teams.
  • Directs software programming and development of documentation.
  • Applies computer science, engineering, and mathematical analysis to the software development process.
  • May lead small development teams in terms of task breakdown, progress tracking, and technical issue resolution to ensure product success.
  • Provides technical support for software maintenance or use.
  • Communicates with customers and clients to establish maintenance procedures for software systems.
  • Domestic and International travel to Military Installations in support of the Program activities may be required


MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, or related field required
  • Minimum of Eight (8) years in software systems engineering, scripting and testing of constructive geospatial tools, software and associated products.
  • Geospatial product development experience collaborating across multiple engineering disciplines with expertise in the testing and configuration of Government owned simulation tools such as the Army Site Terrain Tool (ASTT) and SE Core Gateway.
  • Expertise in geospatial software design, build, scripting, integration, verification, validation, with an emphasis on validation by government agencies.
  • Prefer experience with the Program or similar constructive M&S software
  • Experience working in an Agile development environment
  • Have significant JavaScript, Java, and/or C++ experience
  • Experience working with service-based architectures
  • Experience with distributed systems
  • Junit or protractor for dev testing
  • Preferred experience with development methodologies such as Agile, Waterfall, or TSP
  • Experience using Relational Database Management Systems such as Postgres, MySQL, SQL Server, and/or Oracle
  • Experience using an IDE such as Microsoft Visual Studio or Eclipse
  • Experience using a source code repository such as BitBucket, Mercurial, GIT, or Subversion
  • Experience using a defect management system such as Bugzilla, JIRA, or Fogbugz
  • Experience using Microsoft Office Products such as PowerPoint, Word, Excel, and Outlook
  • Holds a current minimum DOD Secret security clearance or ability to obtain one
  • Due to the nature of work performed within this facility, U.S. citizenship is required


DESIRED SKILLS:

  • Understanding of FOMs (Federation Object Models)
  • Experience writing Python Scripts
  • Experience using OpenGL or WebGL
  • Military Simulation experience
