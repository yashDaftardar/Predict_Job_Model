Full Job Description

Develops Software
Develop data-driven health and medical related software and systems. Formulate and define system scope and objectives through research and fact-finding to develop or modify complex information systems. Prepare detailed specifications from which programs will be written. Assist with recommendations towards the development of new code/data/reports or reuse/enhancement of existing. Assess design options, design technical requirements for applications, identify and analyze problems, test and debug code.

Manages Systems
Apply security methodologies in management of systems. Design, develop, test, and document systems with minimal guidance from the supervisor or project manager. Use source control management and configuration management tools to document and standardize systems management. Troubleshoot applications, maintain installed systems, produce and maintain documentation, and conduct application quality assurance. Analyze and revise existing system logic difficulties and documentation. Responsible for software integration, external interface development and technical documentation.

Data Management
Assist with recommendations towards the development of new code/data/reports or reuse/enhancement of existing data. Perform data maintenance on customer data sets. This will include data maintenance and performance optimization. The candidate should expect to work with systems such as MS SQL Server, MySQL, and/or Oracle. The candidate may be required to work with semantic query language, semantic data stores, and the databases that support them.

Server Management and Issue Solving
Utilize knowledge of Linux systems to manage servers and resolve and issues that may arise.

Leadership
Must be able to function in a leadership position and provide support to the junior developers. Must be able to collaborate and interact with fellow team members. The candidate must be able to present their work before team members in biweekly meetings. They should be able to explain design concepts and software techniques. They will help train new team members. Team leads will be expected to lead small workgroups of fellow engineers.



Expected Salary:

Minimum Requirements:

Associate’s degree and two years of relevant experience; or a high school diploma or equivalent and four years of relevant experience. Appropriate college coursework or vocational/technical training may substitute at an equivalent rate for the required experience.



Preferred Qualifications:
  • Ability to work within Linux or Mac operating systems
  • Proven track record of architecting and deploying software solutions
  • Ability to plan, test, and execute software and system upgrades
  • Excellent communication skills, with both technical and non-technical audiences
  • Ability to work as part of a technical team, collaborating and sharing work
  • Willingness to do the work necessary to meet deadlines
  • Three years of professional experience in software development, familiarity with Python, PHP, Java, or
  • JavaScript frameworks and experience with at least one source control system.
  • Experience with newer technologies such as SPARQL, Semantic Data stores, and NoSQL.
  • Experience with Tomcat and Debian-based systems.
  • Experience working within a development team.
