Full Job Description

Location: Avon Park, FL Category: Professional Posted On: Wed Jan 13 2021 Job Description:

Full-time, year-round position responsible for administrative information database management programming and maintenance of College applications.

Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Codes and maintains new and existing interactive and batch administrative application programs to generate necessary information and required reports for College administration, state reporting, and federal reporting in a structured manner and in such a way that it conforms to the standards set forth in the "IT Environment Guidelines" Manual.
  • Coordinates and/or performs installation and maintenance of Banner and Business Intelligence Suite information system software (e.g. upgrades, patches, drivers, etc.) for the purpose of providing access to current version of software to college constituents.
  • Identifies issues related to the Banner and Business Intelligence Suite information system software applications for the purpose of responding to user problems and ongoing availability of Banner database.
  • Provides backup support for Banner end-user security management.
  • Provides problem determination, research, and assistance with Banner operational procedures, scripts, and programs.
  • Assists in the maintenance of the Banner Self-Service site, web pages, and database links.
  • Assists in maintaining and optimizing the Oracle database and State reporting.
  • Assists in provision and maintenance of internal and external reporting, and connectivity and interaction of external business systems with Banner.
  • Performs other programming and modification duties as necessary.
  • Contributes to unit planning, assessment, and college institutional effectiveness efforts.
  • Acts in accordance with College policies and procedures.
  • Performs other duties as assigned.

Job Requirements:


Educational: Associate degree in Computer Science/Data Processing required. Extensive computer-related experience may substitute for degree requirement.

Experiential: Four or more years of experience with one or more of the following required: Banner, Oracle, SQL Plus, PL/SQL, and shell scripting. Proficiency in C, C+, Microsoft Access, and Linux strongly preferred.
