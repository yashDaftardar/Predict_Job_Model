Reporting directly to the Site Lead, the Digital Engineer will assist with the site wide implementation of software and automated digital solutions. The associate will work cross functionally to ensure that identified solutions meet stakeholder requirements, both during implementation and as the processes evolve over time. The associate will develop end user training and provide on-going support to internal customers, as well as be the primary point-of-contact with external vendors and integrators. The associate will work within a FDA/ISO regulated facility requiring the development and execution of appropriate validation/qualification protocols for specific initiatives.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Primary responsibility is support of industrial CT Scanning program for customized dental applications.
  • The associate will become the Subject Matter Expert (SME) for end to end CT scanning process.
  • Scanning process includes physical processes of material handling, scanning, and disposition,
  • Scanning process includes automated digital processes, scanned volume file conversion to desired surface files, surface file delivery to endpoint for pick up by API.
  • Scanning process includes interaction with Salesforce CRM for tracking of jobs, including unique ID of each case and maintaining case status through process.
  • Institute process controls to ensure robustness of processes and safe operation.
  • Primary point of contact for technical troubleshooting of the scanning process for both DentsplySirona and Byte stakeholders.
  • Develop and deliver training for end users regarding physical process flow, automated CT- scanning of cases, and final disposition of completed cases.
  • Help with the development and execute validation/qualification protocols to support business/operational initiatives including customer backorder minimization, cost reduction, non-conformance reduction, complaint reduction, yield/process reliability improvement, and FDA/ISO compliance requirements.
  • As assigned, investigate and implement corrective and preventive actions (CAPAs) to meet business and compliance requirements.
  • Support value streams by recommending and implementing automated solutions to drive efficiency at the site.
  • Associate will be innovative, knowledgeable and intensely committed to ensure that every product and service we supply conforms to FDA and Quality System Regulations, as well as ISO 13485 Standards.

TYPICAL BACKGROUND

  • BS/BA degree from four-year College or university in an engineering, computer, or other technical field. Experience in a manufacturing environment is preferred. Previous experience with industrial CT preferred. 1 to 3 years of experience with responsibility for implementing and managing an automated workflow.
  • Ability to understand and document all steps and decisions involved in manufacturing processes (such as by process mapping).
  • Experience in working with industrial CT hardware and associated processing software, i.e. Volume Graphics, Geomagic.
  • Ability to create and maintain automated queries.
