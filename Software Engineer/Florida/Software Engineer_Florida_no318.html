Full Job Description

Summary


We are looking for a hands-on Java software engineer to join our growing R&D Product Engineering Team. This is an exciting opportunity to work on a talented team helping evolve and maintain our Secure Access products. If you are a knowledgeable, creative, passionate individual who is interested in designing and building unique security solutions that no one else is building, then we want you!


What You Will Be Doing


  • Design, develop, implement, and test OPSWAT's MetaAccess products
  • Provide inputs and documentation of new or existing features
  • Partner with your scrum team members on delivering sprint commitments
  • Work with local and remote engineering teams to deliver on quarterly goals
  • Collaborate with Product Management on product requirements
  • Participate in receiving and giving peer code reviews
  • Provide guidance to junior team members


What We Need from You


  • 4+ years hands on use of Java
  • Linux or Windows development experience
  • Excellent object-oriented design & programming skills in multi-threaded applications
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Self-motivated with a proven ability to work well in a fast-paced team environment
  • Technical leader who can lead technical solutions for a small team of engineers
  • Knowledge of automation frameworks and scripting languages


It Would Be Nice If You Had


  • Experience with network programming (TCP/IP)
  • Exposure to network configuration and infrastructure is a big plus
  • Experience with Spring, Hibernate, MySQL or MongoDB
  • Experience either with ActiveMQ, AD/LDAP, SAML, and other enterprise technology
  • Experience with REST API utilization and SDK integration
  • Experience with Git, Bitbucket, Jira, Confluence, TeamCity
  • Experience with Agile / Scrum working environment
  • Enterprise application development experience
  • Cybersecurity domain experience


About OPSWAT

OPSWAT is a global leader in critical infrastructure cybersecurity that helps protect the world's mission-critical organizations from malware and zero-day attacks. To minimize the risk of compromise, OPSWAT Critical Infrastructure Protection (CIP) solutions enable both public and private organizations to implement processes that ensure the secure transfer of files and devices to and from critical networks. In total, 98% of U.S. nuclear power facilities and more than 1,000 organizations worldwide spanning Financial Services, Defense, Manufacturing, Energy, Aerospace, Transportation Systems trust OPSWAT to secure their files and devices; ensure compliance with industry and government-driven policies and regulations, and to protect their reputations, finances, employees, and relationships from cyber-driven disruption. For more information, visit www.opswat.com.
