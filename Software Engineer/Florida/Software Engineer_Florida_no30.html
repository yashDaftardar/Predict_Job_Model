Job Description

You Will:

  • Provide validation and verification for avionics software on safety critical software in collaboration with the internal team and external large system integrators
  • Develop and manage complex software high and low level requirement
  • Test and document safety critical software
  • Develop and update software verification test environments
  • Review safety critical software requirements, tests, and results against specific criteria
  • Prepare and review documentation for software full development life cycle following established software development processes

You'll Need:

  • Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science or Software Engineering or a related field
  • 4+ years of experience in avionics or electronic controls related development
  • Experience in full lifecycle development including software requirements and design, system-level integration, validation, and verification
  • Experience creating engineering documentation
  • Experience in integration, verification, and testing of electronic equipment utilizing embedded hardware/software
  • Experience with DO-178B/C
  • US Person Required

Preferred Skills:

  • Experience with flight critical fly-by-wire Flight Controls, Actuators, Air Data Systems, Pilot Inceptors, Inertial Sensors and/or Autopilot systems
  • 4+ years of experience with the development of requirements and operational software concepts
  • Familiar with data bus protocols (1553B, ARINC 429, RS422/485, AFDX or CDN)
  • Working knowledge of C, C++, Java, Python
  • Experience with FACE standards
  • Experience with creating simulation and on target software test environments
