Technology and Engineering - USA Oldsmar, Florida


OVERVIEW

Nielsen is the global leader for entertainment data & technology providing insights for broadcast and on-demand video, radio, and sports. Everything we do revolves around audience and content. We are launching a 10-week Software Engineer Internship Program. Our tech teams are hyper-focused on our ability to be a cutting edge tech organization. With a keen eye on state of the art technology, we look for “what’s next” when it comes to software, frameworks, tools, and cloud-computing services.


Joining our Software Engineer Internship Program means you will:

  • Begin with an induction covering our tech strategies, products and systems, as well as an overview of our technology community
  • Dive in head-first to gain hands-on experience creating innovative solutions that make a difference for our customers, clients and employees
  • Work with other Interns, experienced Software Engineers, and the global Nielsen technology community to grow your skills, share ideas, and innovate
  • Our Agile Technology Teams in the US are primarily in 4 major hubs. For your internship, you can be located near any of these metropolitan areas: New York Metro, NY; Chicago, IL; Emeryville, CA; Oldsmar, FL


WHAT ARE WE LOOKING FOR?
If you are ready to put your passion for technology to work, you will find your place here. Successful candidates will have these competencies:

  • Open-minded and excited to learn and grow technology skills
  • Ridiculous attention to detail and pride in the work you do
  • Strong communication and relationship management skills
  • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, collaborative environment
  • Love all things data


KEY SKILLS INCLUDE:

  • Rising Juniors or Seniors that are pursuing a degree in Computer Science, Engineering or equivalent
  • Relevant technical project work around developing systems/applications with OO or functional methodologies
  • A well-rounded academic background with a minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0
  • An interest in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning concepts
  • An interest in protecting privacy in the digital world [core to Nielsen Measurements' value] as well as an interest in hard computational problems
