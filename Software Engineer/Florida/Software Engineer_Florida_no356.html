We are currently looking for dynamic, self-driven Web Tagging Specialist to join our growing team. Candidates will be responsible for developing and implementing digital analytics tagging solutions across various digital channels for PlanHub. The Specialist will be responsible for analysis, development, testing, implementing, and supporting Agile Delivery and Analytics teams.

What you'll be doing:
  • Implement digital tagging for data capture to track digital marketing efforts to determine brand and campaign effectiveness
  • Design, implement, and document customized analytics digital tagging/tracking specifications for digital content
  • Develop and publish Tag Management metrics, ensure tag audits are conducted and results published on regular basis, track and manage all tag requests (internal and external) end-to-end
  • Manage various Tag Management platforms/solutions together with team members
  • Coordinate data ingestion feeds and web tag updates while ensuring data quality and accuracy
  • Handle data preparation and data analysis portions of various projects
  • Ensure accuracy of results produced from digital tags as a result of data analysis

What you'll need to be successful:
  • Bachelor of Arts or Bachelor of Science in Computer Science or Business; related work experience may be substituted for advanced degree.
  • 3+ years of relevant work experience including development in agile delivery teams
  • Experience with Google, Tealium , Adobe Tag Management Software or similar Tag Management suite.
  • Experience with JavaScript to implement custom digital tagging
  • Experience querying data by writing SQL for analysis
  • Experience with Digital Media and exposure to web optimization strategies
  • Experience with Display Advertising and or Agency Experience
  • Working knowledge of common reporting tools and MS Office Suite of products
  • Must be a detail-oriented, creative thinker with excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Requires independent completion of work with sound judgment and discretion.
  • Ability to work on multiple projects and deliverables at the same time and work effectively in a team environment
  • Problems and issues may be numerous and are vaguely defined, and may need a high degree of reasoning and analysis of multiple variable factors to address problems and issues
  • Has the ability to complete projects of varying size and complexity
  • Experience with business support software applications such asMS Office (Word, PowerPoint, Excel, Project, Visio, Visual SourceSafe, etc.) required
