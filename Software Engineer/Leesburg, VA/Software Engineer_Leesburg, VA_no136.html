CenCore is seeking a qualified Software Engineer at the Subject Matter Expert level, in support of a U.S Varsity Level Customer.

Responsibilities:

  • Provides development engineering and programming support to mission supporting applications.
  • Designs and develops mission applications in a Web environment.
  • Develops, creates, and modifies general computer applications software or specialized utility programs.
  • Analyzes user needs and develops software solutions.
  • Designs software or customizes software for client use with the aim of optimizing operational efficiency.
  • May analyze and design databases within an application area, working individually or coordinating database development as part of a team.
  • May manage websites including design, development, deployment and maintenance activities as well as performing testing and quality assurance of web sites and web applications.
  • Work in a collaborative, team environment.

Requirements:

  • Demonstrated ability to interpret and convey technical requirements
  • Demonstrated ability to translate customer needs into efficient, functional code.
  • Demonstrated ability to develop and maintain highly fault-tolerant applications and systems.
  • Working knowledge of software redundancy Working knowledge of software development best practices and ability to apply appropriately.
  • Complex Problem Solving – identifying complex problems and reviewing related information to develop and evaluate options and implement new ones.
  • Customer and personal service – knowledge of principles and processes for providing customer and personal services.
  • Technologies used: Java, JavaScript , jQuery , ExtJS , Spring , Joomla , Hibernate , Tomcat , MySQL , PL/SQL , Ruby on Rails , Bootstrap , Angular JS , AWS , REST (endpoints, queries, services)
  • Analyze user needs and software requirements to determine feasibility of design within time and cost restraints
  • Organize and maintain documentation of application development and subsequent revisions, inserting comments in the coded instruction so others can understand the program
  • Conduct testing of program and software applications to be sure they will produce the desired results and that the instructions are correct
  • Consult with customers about software system design and maintenance
  • Consult with managerial, engineering, and technical personnel to clarify program intent, identify problems, and suggest changes
  • Design and develop database management systems, image processing, collaborative tools, data manipulation techniques, data visualization techniques, and directory services
  • Design and develop tools for the MS Windows and relevant operating system platforms
  • Design and develop tools that integrate with commercial applications
  • Investigate whether networks, workstations, the central processing unit of the system, or peripheral equipment are responding to a program’s instructions.
  • Write or contribute to instructions or manuals to guide end users

CenCore, LLC is an equal opportunity employer and offers a professional work environment designed to enhance your personal career growth.
