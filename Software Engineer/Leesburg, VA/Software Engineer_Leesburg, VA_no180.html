Full Job Description

Overview:

We are seeking a motivated Software Engineer with a strong understanding in software design and development to work in a team that will implement next generation features for iDirect satellite communication products. This engineer will work with an outstanding team of System architects, PO, Scrum master and POD Lead to design and implement software feaures and bugfixes. And will be responsible for SW development on processes/components/subsystems in Evolution Data Path network elements.

Our teams use Scrum for Agile software development, and as such we work in diverse teams where thoughts and ideas are shared and collaboration is actively encouraged. This gives team members the opportunity to work across products, getting to understand exactly how products operates, and how it fits within the larger iDirect communication solution.
Responsibilities:
  • Software Design, Development and Continuous Engineering
  • Delivery of Jira stories, meet the acceptance criteria
  • Participate in agile ceremonies and code reviews
  • Other duties as assigned
Qualifications:
EDUCATION:
Bachelor’s or Master’s Degree in Computer Science or a related engineering discipline is preferred.

PROFESSIONAL QUALITIES:
  • Strong problem solving and communications skills.
  • Must be a self-motivated individual with drive for tasks completion and proven success in a structured development environment.
  • Good software design skills using proven and best practice design methodologies & related principles; Strong command of Object Oriented Design and Object Oriented Programming techniques.
  • Understanding in designing and testing multi-threaded and multi-process applications on Linux, and strong knowledge of C++.
  • Good understanding of networking and network protocols (TCP/IP) and Idea of Radio Frequency fundamentals.
  • Strong documentation and writing skills.

POSITION SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS:

  • 1+ years of experience designing and developing C, C++, OOD/OOP software applications.
  • 1+ years of experience programming in Linux environment
  • Strong knowledge of Linux and Linux Kernel is highly desired
  • Knowledge of TCP/IP, and sockets programming
  • Knowledge of networking protocols and RF fundamentals.
