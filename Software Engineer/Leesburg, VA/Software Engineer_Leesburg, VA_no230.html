Full Job Description

Overview:
Unison Software, located in Dulles VA., is seeking an experienced Systems Engineer to deliver operational excellence for multiple hosting environments and proactively assist with new projects across the organization.
Responsibilities:
The ideal candidate will have demonstrated experience working with on-premise and cloud system deployments (SaaS) and should be knowledgeable in a wide array of technical and operations knowledge; including system administration, operating systems, infrastructure and application security, virtualization technology and networking (TCP/IP, DNS, Directory Services included). The candidate must also be able to work well with customers, management, hosting support teams, and application development teams. Knowledge of DoD Information Security directives and processes is a plus.
  • Support internal end users and external customers with both general and application-specific issues
  • Perform technical evaluations, analysis, troubleshooting and mitigation for all supported servers in response to support requests
  • Evaluate, perform and automate server maintenance activities
  • Plan and recommend hardware replacements as needed
  • Build servers by providing technical configuration, setup, installation services, hardware and coordination
  • Assist with establishing and maintaining appropriate server baseline
  • Coordinate planned server outages with management and user population
  • Troubleshoot, resolving and reporting on all unplanned server outages
  • Ensure integrity of asset management records
  • Share knowledge with and mentor coworkers
  • Respond 24x7 to Production emergencies
  • Serve as On-Call Engineer on a rotating basis
Qualifications:
  • A. or B.S. or equivalent work experience
  • 7+ years of increasingly advanced systems administration, preferably in a SaaS or Hosting environment
  • Expert knowledge of Windows Server 2K8/2K12/2K16
  • Ability to troubleshoot problems in a Linux system
  • Knowledge of automation through DevOps scripting and tools
  • Knowledge of Data Center environmental, security and operational best practices
  • Verbal and written communication skills to present information to a diverse population (technical and non-technical).
  • Ability to work well both independently and as a member of a team.
  • Ability to identify complex problems and review related information to develop and evaluate options and implement solutions.
  • Willingness to learn new technologies
  • Ability to provide innovative solutions to simplify, automate and otherwise improve routine issues and tasks
  • Availability to serve as On-Call Engineer on a rotating basis
Clearance:
Applicants may need to be the subject of a security investigation and may need to meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information, to include U.S. Citizenship.
