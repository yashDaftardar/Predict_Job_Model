Full Job Description

Overview:
Acuity is focused on building cutting edge, functional applications for many government agencies. We are a team of “doers” with a bias towards action and have a proven track record of implementing innovative technology solutions to solve complex challenges. Our team believes in the importance of usability, user experience (UX), and design within our applications. We seek to break the stereotype of clunky government applications. We use modern design and project management tools such rapid prototyping and agile development methodologies to build and customize solutions that are easy to use and effective at meeting their business objectives. We think of the applications we have developed as products and work to ship those products in weeks/months instead of years.

Acuity’s Technology Solutions Group (TSG) is hosting a part-time, paid, 10-week-long summer internship for rising IT professionals. The team of interns will work in an Agile Scrum environment to develop Microsoft 365 applications in Azure to enhance corporate operations and improve business workflows and interfaces in existing and new applications for internal company departments.

About the Internship
  • Who: Rising Juniors and Seniors in college who show initiative and drive
  • Duration : Tuesday, June 1st through Friday, August 6th
  • Compensation : Paid hourly
  • Weekly Hours : 8 hours on Tuesdays and Thursdays + 4 floating hours = 20 hours/week
  • Pandemic Considerations : Interns will work remotely with Acuity-owned laptops. Reliable home internet is required. We may attempt to work in-person at the Reston, VA HQ location for collaborative working sessions and training opportunities depending on comfort levels, vaccination status, and flexibility to work in-person.
  • What’s at Stake : Top interns may be offered contingent, full-time positions or other compensation opportunities at the conclusion of the internship.
Responsibilities:
  • Design, develop and test Microsoft 365 web applications and OOTB connectors using Microsoft PowerApps, SharePoint, Power Automate, PowerBI, etc. in Microsoft Azure which may require some custom development of web parts, features and solutions to support the improvement of internal business workflows and processes and interact with enterprise databases, web services, APIs, and applications. Develop internal business applications with quality user experiences and user interfaces (UX/UI).
  • Integrate existing business applications into the 365 framework and architecture.
  • Develop and consume new or existing web services to integrate backend systems and third-party applications.
  • Create system documentation and standard operating procedures for operating and maintaining deployed applications.
  • Integrate Microsoft Active Directory (AD) with 365 solutions with proper user permissions.
  • Design and administer content management databases with metadata and content types, information management policies, and information rights management.
  • Work with business users and technical staff to understand problems with software and develop specifications to resolve them.
  • Develop job aides and user manuals to help with the adoption of developed and deployed applications.
  • Work within an Agile team environment using Scrum.
  • As needed, coordinate "feature" and "solution" deployment testing with Microsoft 365 Admin and SharePoint Admin.
  • Coordinate with data owners to ensure appropriate controls are placed on data distribution.
  • Communicate (written and verbal) proposed designs and progress on the work to team members and the Technology Solutions Group (TSG) Operations Manager.
Qualifications:
  • Currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science or related information technology field
  • Derive enjoyment in planning, designing, developing and/or maintaining enterprise wikis, blogs, websites, applications, systems, and/or databases.
  • Craves to learn new technology and efficient IT development practices.
  • Must have experience and knowledge using at least 2 of the following: Java, Javascript, CSS, React, JSON, SQL Server, .NET, C#
  • Adept at leveraging new approaches to solutions for system design and functionality.
  • Exposure to Microsoft 365 web apps and Power Platform (Power Automate, PowerApps, PowerBI)
  • Good communicator able to clearly understand and articulate complex problems
  • Solid written, verbal, and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to work well independently as well as collaborating within a team
Nice to Haves
  • Experience developing within Azure and Microsoft 365
  • Familiarity with Atlassian collaboration tools (JIRA, Confluence, etc.) and other tools such as Teams, Slack, Zoom, etc.

Clearance Requirements

  • US Citizenship is required to maintain agency required clearance
