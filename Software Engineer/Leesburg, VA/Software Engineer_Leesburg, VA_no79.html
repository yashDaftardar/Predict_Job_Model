Overview:
As you start your career, you are looking for interesting, challenging work, but also a place where you can develop and succeed. You’ve found that place with Unison. We offer a collaborative and supportive environment with plenty of room for you to grow in your role and in your career. We highlight diversity in our company with celebrations across the calendar and regular opportunities to show that Unison is the “Greatest Place to Work.” Even while working virtually, we continue to build community with online activities and regular connection across teams.
Unison is the leading provider of cloud-hosted and on-prem solutions for federal government procurement, grants management, and contracting software. Our SaaS-based solutions deliver insight from predictive analytics rolled into our library of enterprise software, apps, and bots. We are passionate about working together to help the business of government run better with technology.
This role will support the software engineering, testing and configuration management efforts by Unison’s Product Operations department. This role will work across all our core projects and collaborate with Engineering, Quality Assurance and Configuration Management to increase efficiency, product quality, improved installation and frequency of releases.
Responsibilities:
  • Work on a cross-functional team to extend with responsibilities for managing IT Security software and services supporting Federal systems of increasing sensitivity levels.
  • Follow IT Security Program Standard Practices for managing sensors and sources to enable analysis of data and client reports
  • Follow the best business practices for IT Security Engineers and work responsibly, ethnically and within rules of behavior set forth by Federal clients. Maintain current knowledge of relevant technology, programming skills, hardware, and applications.
  • Work with a wide variety of technologies and tools to help implement continuous improvement of our software engineering processes, including automation of the software build, testing and the deployment process following SDLC process.
  • Design build, and maintain efficient, reusable, and reliable code and ensure the best possible performance, quality, and responsiveness of applications
Qualifications:
Required Skills:
  • Bachelor’s degree required in related field
  • Ability to use a wide variety of technologies and tools to help implement continuous improvement of our software engineering processes, including automation of the software build, testing and the deployment process.
  • Experience with Windows or Linux operating systems
  • Experience with scripting languages such as Bash, PowerShell, JavaScript
  • Excellent troubleshooting, collaboration, and customer service skills
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills
  • Ability to analyze and communicate reporting deliverables
  • Strong attention to detail and follow-through skills
  • Highly self-motivated and directed

Desired Skills:
  • Familiarity with Microsoft ASP.NET web development with C# or VB.NET and HTML/CSS
  • Familiarity with PL/SQL
  • Familiarity with software build tools (i.e., Team City, Jenkins, and octopus)
  • Knowledge of configuration management tools (i.e., Subversion and Git).
  • Familiarity with architecture styles/APIs (REST, Web Services).
  • Understanding of Agile methodologies

Clearance: For some positions, applicants may need to be the subject of a security investigation and may need to meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information. U.S. Citizenship is required.

Physical Requirements : Qualified candidates will need to be able to commute to and work in multiple locations and will need to communicate frequently with co-workers and clients.
