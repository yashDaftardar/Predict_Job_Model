At Contegix, we empower digital collaboration for a digital workforce. We are experts in SAFe, Agile, and ITSM and outpace other service providers with our iterative approach. Contegix is the only Atlassian Platinum Partner that offers professional services, managed application administration, private and public cloud deployments of Server and Data Center, and award-winning application support services.


As a member of the Platform Automation team, you will be responsible for assisting the company in gaining work effectiveness and productivity by enhancing its inside specialties through automations, implementations, configurations, integrations and new development.

You will be responsible for analyzing and resolving complex integration challenges based on independent, hands-on identification and diagnosis of hardware and software issues. You will be expected to employ sound judgment and precise forethought in your analysis, application, prioritizing, documenting, and communicating of your integration activities, solving “general case” problems for use in automations and not “single case” problems. You must also have interpersonal skills that allow you to communicate with both internal and external customers and solve technical-related problems quickly and efficiently.

You will be responsible for the technical design, planning, implementation, performance tuning, recovery procedures and maintenance of mission critical systems. You will also investigate and analyze feasibility of system requirements and develop system specifications. No management or leadership requirements in this role.

The Platform Automation team primarily covers daytime US hours for new work, but is responsible for systems 24x7 and is expected to participate in on-call duties. In order to maintain coverage, schedule changes may be necessary periodically.

The ideal candidate will have a strong SysAdmin background in Linux (and Windows to a lessor extent) have demonstrable development or configuration management experience.

Responsibilities:

  • Manage a number of internal linux based systems, including IPAM, Monitoring platform, Networking applications, DNS, productivity tools, etc.
  • Manage and build 100’s of servers using configuration management (Salt, ansible)
  • Develop and maintain automations for internal processes
  • Assign permissions, responsible for “access control”
  • Perform other duties as assigned or required.

Qualifications:

  • Minimum 5 years Linux System Administration
  • Demonstrable experience using at least one configuration management system (Salt, Ansible, Puppet, Chef, etc.)
  • 1 year development experience in Python development

Key Position Details:

  • Flexible work during regular business hours
  • Participation in on-call rotation required
