Full Job Description

We are looking for a motivated C# Software Developer who is looking to be given a lot of responsibility quickly. As key member of a small dedicated client IT team you will responsible for designing, developing and implementing applications using primarily C#. Work is primarily remotely with as needed in person team meetings and the potential of some travel to training, test or installation sites within the continental US.

Duties and Responsibilities (to include but not be limited to the following):

  • Designs, modifies, develops, writes, implements, maintains and updates software programming applications
  • Supports and/or installs software applications/operating systems
  • Participates in the testing process through test review and analysis, test witnessing and certification of software
  • Designs, plans, and coordinates work with teams
  • Provides technical support to project team members
  • Familiar with a variety of the field's concepts, practices, and procedures
  • Must be able to work independently and as part of team to achieve project goals. A wide degree of creativity and latitude is expected.
  • Must be able to communicate orally and in writing
  • Reports to a project tech leader

Requirements:

  • Degree from an accredited college or university in a technical field (engineering, mathematics, statistics, computer science, or related field of study) plus at least 1 year of professional service using C#
  • The ability to design, implement, read, understand and modify existing or new C# applications.
  • Thread based, multithreading , sorter programming experience is a plus.
  • The ability to incorporate documented third-party controls and interfaces
  • Experience using Visual Studio or similar IDE environment to read, modify, create, debug and version control in C#
  • The ability to read and understand SQL queries and create SQL queries to interface with databases.
  • Knowledge of the Windows operating system to configure, install, maintain and diagnose applications
  • Willing to travel to field or vendor sites for testing or attending technical reviews

Additional Requirements

  • Must be able to obtain and keep a Public Trust government security clearance
  • Must be a U.S. Citizen or Green Card holder and have been living in the U.S. for at least the last five years
  • Must not have traveled outside of the United States for more than 180 days in the last five years
