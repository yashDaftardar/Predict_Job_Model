Benefits

Knack Works is an employee-owned company that is proud to offer a comprehensive benefits package designed to meet the needs of our employees and their families. In addition to an outstanding salary, we offer each employee equity in the company a generous retirement plan, company-paid health care benefits and a flexible paid time off policy.

Knack Works funds a Profit Sharing Plan (PSP) for every employee at an additional 25% of your base salary. These funds are deposited in a tax-deferred account, with no vesting period, that allows you to decide on the investment strategy best for you.

Knack Works also funds a Cafeteria Plan (IRS Section 125) for every employee at an additional 25% of your base salary. In addition to fully covering health care premiums, these funds are available to reimburse expenses such as out-of-pocket medical costs, paid time off, child/dependent care, professional training and an array of other qualified pre-tax expenses.

Outside of the core benefits structure, Knack has multiple parties and gatherings over the course of the year, including a holiday party in December, a variety of happy hours at local pubs and wineries, and a family picnic in the park complete with a bouncy house.
