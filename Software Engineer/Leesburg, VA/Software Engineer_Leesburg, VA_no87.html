Job Summary

The team develops, deploys, and maintains Entitlement Management Commercial off-the-shelf (COTS), Free Open Source Software (FOSS) and custom component software for both the Sponsor and Sponsor's partners. This effort is a critical piece of the Sponsor and partners’ strategies for consistent, reliable, auditable, and secure access management approval and processing workflows. The Sponsor’s organization leverages cloud hosting to meet availability, scalability and security demands. The team requires support from a Software Engineer to design, develop, integrate, test, and deploy COTS, FOSS, and custom components for the services. The resources shall work as part of the existing Agile/Scrum contractor development team.

**Please note: This job requires an existing Top Secret Clearance and Polygraph**

Responsibilities and Duties

The Contractor shall work in collaboration with mission partners to deliver Entitlement Management APIs to customers.

The Contractor shall interface with contractor developers, architects, and infrastructure teams during upgrades or troubleshooting.

The Contractor shall support the development of entitlement management requirements.

The Contractor shall support the implementation of an automated unit and integration testing capability for access control policies and other service components using JUnit.

The Contractor shall work with test team, if available, or developers to ensure regression and performance testing is performed after each deployment.

The Contractor shall deploy software solutions in a cloud environment.

The Contractor shall assist in the creation of technical documentation and other routine programmatic and security accreditation artifacts.

The Contractor shall perform duties encompassing the full lifecycle of software management, to include developing against new requirements, enhancement of existing requirements, and operating and maintaining deployed systems.

The Contractor shall develop rest services that will be used by sponsor and mission partners to manage and interact with the Sponsor organization.

The Contractor shall assist in the installation, deployment, administration, and maintenance of COTS, FOSS, or custom component software.

The Contractor shall make configuration changes to COTS, FOSS, or custom component software as needed.

The Contractor shall focus initial efforts on the development of critical software components that leverage APIs.

The Contractor shall participate in the deployment and administration of COTS, FOSS, and custom component software.
