Secure our Nation, Ignite your Future

ManTech provides mission-focused technology solutions and services for U.S. defense, intelligence and federal civilian agencies. In business for more than 52 years, we excel in full-spectrum cyber, data collection & analytics, enterprise IT, and systems and software engineering solutions that support national and homeland security. More than 9,300 talented professionals around the globe make up the ManTech family and nearly half are veterans.

The Advanced Forensics Team offers the opportunity for rapid upward mobility in the areas of computer/mobile device forensics; damaged media recovery, digital forensic analysis, data recovery; reverse engineering; Android/iOS Development, intrusion analysis and methodologies; and vulnerability assessments. We take a lot of pride in being able to solve difficult technical challenges and create new capabilities and new tools that may be used in-house and/or shared throughout the Intel Community. Currently, ManTech is seeking an Associate Mobile Forensics Software Engineer to join our team in Chantilly, Virginia.

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Analyzing mobile software applications; retrieving and processing significant forensic artifacts.
  • Contributing to current in-house software projects, adding new features, and fixing bugs.
  • Developing new software solutions based on team tasking.
  • Aiding in reverse engineering efforts for assigned team task.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree or equivalent experience in software/python, or, ideally, Android/iOS Development

Preferred Qualifications:

Exposure to or experience in the following (or willing/interest to learn):

  • Software/Mobile/Python experience
  • Interest In Mobile
  • Forensics/RE Experience
  • Python
  • Android/iOS Development
  • Web Development

Security Clearance Requirements:

  • TS/SCI with Polygraph (FSP)

Physical Requirements:

  • Be able to remain in a stationary position 50% of time.
  • Occasionally move about inside the office to access file cabinets and office machinery.
