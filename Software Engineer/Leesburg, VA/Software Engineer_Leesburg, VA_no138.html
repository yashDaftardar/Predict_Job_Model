Job Description


Visa is seeking a Sr. Software Engineer to drive software development and integrations for our enterprise operations & infrastructure systems. This role is responsible for the design, development, configuration, deployment, and integration of our critical infrastructure software systems. The candidate will work as part of the Operations & Infrastructure (O&I) team to support and improve the effectiveness and efficiency of our technology & operations organization.

The position will require close collaboration with Hardware Engineering, Network Engineering, Data Center Engineering and Operations to provide critical planning, analytical, and monitoring capabilities across our global infrastructure footprint.

Success in the role requires an innovative mind, a proven track record of delivering robust software solutions, rigorous analytical skills, and a passion for creating user-friendly, performant systems.

Visa’s Infrastructure is the foundation upon which our company operates. Building and operating secure, reliable and efficient infrastructure is essential to supporting the operation and growth of our business.

Essential Functions:

  • Design, develop, test, document, and deploy high quality software solutions
  • Document and develop code according to specifications and standards
  • Coordinate with other developers and stakeholders regarding status, technical issues and creative solutions
  • Prepare test data, plan and conduct unit, system, and integration testing
  • Collaborate with business partners on user acceptance testing
  • Troubleshoot and optimize data quality and performance issues
  • Participate in project team meetings and communicate effectively with peers, business analysts, technical project managers, and across other technology team boundaries, reporting project status as required
  • Work closely with various IT and business stakeholders to drive and/or facilitate development of requirements, specifications, and use cases for assigned projects

Qualifications


Basic Qualifications:


  • 2 years of work experience with a Bachelor’s Degree or an Advanced Degree (e.g. Masters, MBA, JD, MD, or PhD)
  • At least 2 years’ experience developing and enhancing existing and new products utilizing Microsoft .NET technology stack, e.g C#, SQL, VB.NET

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Comfortable using modern integrated Development environments, e.g IntelliJ, Visual Studios
  • Building and/or consuming REST APIs, including creating and parsing data in various formats and understanding best practices.
  • Experience with SQL Databases and Data visualization using Tableau or PowerBI
  • Experience with code management systems (GIT or TFS)
  • Hands-on experience providing system administration support for Windows environments
  • Knowledge of web application development with React/Angular/NodeJS
  • Solid process orientation and exhibited planning, organizational and problem solving skills
  • Strong backend infrastructure software development experience
  • Designing and implementing automation solutions
  • Working knowledge of ITSM/ITIL methodology & production support processes such as incident/change/problem management, and escalation procedures
  • Working knowledge of IT infrastructure systems (compute/network/storage/virtualization/etc)
