Full Job Description

The Aerospace Corporation is the trusted partner to the nation’s space programs, solving the hardest problems and providing unmatched technical expertise. As the operator of a federally funded research and development center (FFRDC), we are broadly engaged across all aspects of space— delivering innovative solutions that span satellite, launch, ground, and cyber systems for defense, civil and commercial customers. When you join our team, you’ll be part of a special collection of problem solvers, thought leaders, and innovators. Join us and take your place in space.

At Aerospace, we are committed to providing an inclusive and diverse workplace for all employees to share in our common passion and aspiration – to carry out a mission much bigger than ourselves.

Job Summary

The Performance Modeling and Analysis Department (PMAD) consists of a team dedicated to generating cutting edge innovation and groundbreaking solutions to the missions of our government and commercial customers. Subject matter experts in PMAD perform detailed, complex modeling, analysis, and optimization of orbits, satellite access, and system performance to advise critical customer decision support for future space architectures. You will serve as a top performing, motivated Associate Software Developer collaborating on diverse projects conducting modeling, simulation, and optimization for scientific, high-performance computing applications relevant to space system design and performance. In this capacity, you will drive object-oriented programming in C, C++, and Python to enable users to effectively leverage The Aerospace Corporation’s high-performance computing resources.

The Aerospace Corporation is an open environment structured to foster multi-disciplinary relationships and apply the academic and industry expertise of our engineers, subject matter experts and key stakeholders to solve complex problems across a diverse portfolio of space programs.

What You’ll Be Doing

  • Developing and enhancing high-performance computing applications for space systems modeling and architecture performance
  • Designing and implementing new software tools and capabilities using modern software design principles and technologies
  • Collaborating directly with users to resolve issues and add features to augment simulation and analysis capabilities
  • Engaging in software development processes such as continuous integration, version control, and process automation
  • Working closely with other software developers to advance software testing, usability, and performance

What You Need to be Successful

Minimum Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree in engineering, computer science, mathematics, or related discipline from an accredited program (Pursuing or conferred within the last 12 months)
  • 0-1 year of Industry Experience
  • Significant aptitude and demonstrated skills in object-oriented design, C++, and C
  • Familiarity with algorithms, methods, and techniques associated with high-performance computing
  • Proficient in one or more of the following programming languages: Python, Java, Fortran
  • This position requires the ability to obtain a security clearance which is issued by the U.S. government. U.S. citizenship is required to obtain a security clearance

How You Can Stand Out

It would be impressive if you have one or more of these:

  • Experience with high-performance computing and methods and techniques used for parallel computing and programming
  • Graduate level coursework and/or research in one of the following areas: optimization, decision support, simulation software development
  • Experience developing analysis tools using C, C++, or Python
  • Ability to support multiple programs and/or activities simultaneously and to be comfortable acting as a technical point-of-contact
