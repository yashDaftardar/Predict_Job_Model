Description

The Azure Cloud Engineer is expected to provide subject matter expertise to the Agency on a range of cloud service implementation, migration, delivery, and performance management activities. This individual will directly implement specific compute, storage, network, security, and other architectures and designs required for the NRC’s continuing adoption of cloud services. They will be responsible for understanding Azure systems and deploying Platform as a Service (PaaS) services.

Job Duties will include:

  • Investigates new technology and technology insertion
  • Submission of Change Requests and supports Change Board presentations, as necessary
  • Communication with O&M teams
  • Develops cloud SOPs
  • Peer review solution documents for maintainability and ease of implementation
  • Responds/corrects security findings
  • Responsible for maintaining and implementing automation tools; e.g. scripts
  • Troubleshoots service operations
  • Coordinates and manages cloud configurations; e.g. Morpheus, PaaS

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's Degree and 9 years of experience (4 years experience in lieu of degree)
  • Able to achieve a Public Trust level of clearance
  • Experience with Azure preferred
  • Understand how to evaluate and onboard new IT systems into a cloud environment.
  • Experience working directly with customers.
  • Ability to work independently in a remote atmosphere with proven track record doing so.
  • Effectivley communicate via written, oral, and virtual media.
