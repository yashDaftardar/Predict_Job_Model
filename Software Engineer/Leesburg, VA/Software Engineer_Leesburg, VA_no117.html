NVR is seeking an experienced Full Stack Business Intelligence Developer to join our Data & Analytics team. As a Business Intelligence Developer, you will collaborate with technology and business teams to improve analytics and foster data-driven decision-making across the organization.

Primary Responsibilities

  • Build, optimize, monitor, and maintain data ETL/ELT pipeline.
  • Create/Construct reporting tools (utilizing Excel, Power BI/Tableau, and SSRS) to drive/generate business insights for sales and operational leaders.
  • Work with the business to determine and document data and reporting requirements.
  • Create mock-ups and develop reports, dashboards, and analytics that help leaders achieve company objectives.
  • Collaborate with team members to design, build, and deliver innovative solutions.
  • Establish data policies, standards, and procedures that improve data quality, availability, accessibility, security, and usability.
  • Work closely across/with all business units and engineering teams to develop strategy for long term data platform architecture.

Qualifications

  • 5+ years of experience in data management (data integration, modeling, optimization, and data quality).
  • 5+ years of experience with visualization tools (e.g. Tableau, Power BI), reporting tools (e.g. Excel, SSRS), MSSQL, and SSIS.
  • Passionate about solving customer problems and developing customer focused solutions.
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills with an emphasis on customer service.
  • Possess a strong understanding of analytics, data cleaning, model building, and statistics.
  • Exceptional critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
  • Demonstrated ability to resolve and clarify requests/requirements.
  • Excellent analytical skills, detail, and process oriented.
  • Able to multitask, prioritize work, and work well within a team.
