Working with Kyrus
Kyrus is committed to solving our customer’s hardest problems with eagerness, effectiveness, and efficiency. We reject the status quo and constantly look for new ways to push the boundaries in our field. As a Junior Software Engineer, you’ll work with a team of technical experts to create innovative and impactful solutions with true value.

Kyrus recognizes that a company is nothing without well-cared for employees. We cultivate an encouraging and innovative workplace environment and give employees flexibility to work on their terms. Kyrus offers industry-leading benefits, including
  • Employer-funded health, dental, vision, and life insurance
  • Unlimited paid time off
  • Flexible hours and opportunities to work from home
  • Reimbursements for training events, gym memberships, cell phone and internet bills, and lunches with coworkers

Why You Matter
Kyrus embraces great software engineers in all stages of their career. At Kyrus, you’ll have autonomy over your projects but you’ll be surrounded by an encouraging team eager to help when you get stuck. Your creativity and innovation will help customers achieve their missions in novel and exciting ways.

As a Software Engineer, we need you to
  • Play a key role in development projects with a small team of talented engineers
  • Develop low-level code in C, server-side code in Python
    • Android programming with Java or Kotlin - 5 years experience.
    • C/C++ programming (or Java NDK) - 2 years experience.
    • Experience integrating third party Android APIs
  • Research and implement features for native code on Android
  • Occasional reverse engineering

Who You Are
You love finding creative solutions to technical puzzles and get inspired by new challenges. You are an ambitious software development professional with
  • A bachelor’s degree in a technical field or equivalent work experience
  • Two years of experience developing code in C and Python.
