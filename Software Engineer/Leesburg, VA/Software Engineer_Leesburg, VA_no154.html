Overview:

The customer's component is focused on developing and servicing a flagship web-based application that brings together all of the daily intelligence production by the customer, as well as, analysis, clandestine reporting, and open source content in one place, providing a flexible, on-demand dissemination application to policymakers, the US military, and the Intelligence Community. The engineers will be supporting a mission critical production application that provides information for policy makers throughout the customer’s organization and all affiliated organizations.

Work Requirement:

  • Perform all work on a fully scalable, completely automated AWS platform.
  • Utilize Ruby on Rails, Linux, MySQL, Elasticsearch, Vue, and Twitter Bootstrap.
  • Provide development front end UI/UX engineering to optimize operational efficiency.
  • Develop and maintain an awareness of on-going IT projects and requirements.
  • Provide analysis for applications.
  • Provide design for applications.
  • Develop applications.
  • Deploy applications.
  • Provide lifecycle support for applications.
  • Produce high quality code through automated testing, pairing, and refactoring.

Required Qualifications:

  • Demonstrated experience working independently in an Agile environment.
  • Demonstrated problem-solving experience, covering both technical and professional matters.
  • Demonstrated experience collaborating with customers to refine requirements and provide user solutions.
  • Demonstrated experience with front-end design, specifically wireframes and mock-ups in collaboration with customers.
  • Demonstrated experience ensuring consistent UI/UX and branding throughout an enterprise site.
  • Demonstrated modern CSS development experience.
  • Demonstrated experience with version control, including Git.
  • Demonstrated experience with JavaScript.
  • Full Scope Poly
