ORBCOMM is a leading global provider of industrial Internet of Things (IoT) and Machine-to-Machine (M2M) communication solutions that remotely track, monitor and control fixed and mobile assets.

We are looking for a Platform Support Engineer to join our software product development team in Sterling, Virginia. The individual will be working in a small team dedicated to maintenance and enhancement of the newly launched ORBCOMM Platform and work closely with development and QA (Quality Assurance) teams across the organization.

Roles & Responsibilities:
  • Maintain and improve The ORBCOMM Platform, a microservices based modular Asset and Fleet Management application that has been launched in 2020.
  • Perform hands-on fixes including development of code changes and configuring systems and applications.
  • Prioritize, schedule, and administer all instances where enhancements and defect resolution are required.
  • Evaluate documented resolutions and analyze trends for ways to prevent future problem recurrence.
  • Deployment & Support Conduct research into and develop effective solutions for software application problems, incidents, or events with the goal of rapid and right first-time remediation efforts. Â·
  • Develop, implement, and provide proactive discovery activities so that user impacting events are limited, new applications deployments are successful, and code enhancements are executed with a high degree of success.
  • Communicate application problems and issues to key stakeholders, including management, development teams, end users, and unit leaders.
  • Test fixes and perform post-resolution follow-ups to ensure problems have been resolved.
  • Proactively look for opportunities to improve both production and development code (working with developers) by using code reviews and sharing problematic functionality.
  • Apply diagnostic utilities to aid in troubleshooting.
  • Manage and/or provide guidance to junior members of the team.
  • Work closely with developers, QA team, customer support and product management on delivering and supporting our service.
Requirements:
  • College diploma or university degree in the field of computer science, information systems
  • 5 to 10 years equivalent work experience in the development and maintenance of software applications
  • Experience with building and maintaining databases for queries and problem tracking.
  • Proven experience with troubleshooting principles, methodologies, and issue resolution techniques.
  • Broad knowledge of programming languages and Web Application technologies
  • HTML, CSS, C#. NET, C++, ASP, ASP. NET
  • Angular, Kendo or ReactJS frameworks
  • Able to develop and interpret technical documentation for training and end user procedures.
  • The ability to absorb new ideas and concepts quickly. Good analytical and problem-solving abilities.
  • The ability to effectively prioritize and execute tasks in a high-pressure environment.
  • The ability to conduct research into software development and delivery concepts, as well as technical application issues.
  • Strong customer service orientation.
  • Excellent written, oral, interpersonal, and presentational skills.
  • Experience working in a team-oriented, collaborative environment.
Additional Strengths:
Experience/strong interest in some of the following:
  • Security
  • Microservice architectures
  • Amazon Web Services
  • IoT
  • Database Query Optimisation
  • UX experience
About ORBCOMM Inc.

ORBCOMM (Nasdaq: ORBC) is a global leader and innovator in the industrial Internet of Things, providing solutions that connect businesses to their assets to deliver increased visibility and operational efficiency. The company offers a broad set of asset monitoring and control solutions, including seamless satellite and cellular connectivity, unique hardware and powerful applications, all backed by end-to-end customer support, from installation to deployment to customer care. ORBCOMM has a diverse customer base including premier OEMs, solutions customers and channel partners spanning transportation, supply chain, warehousing and inventory, heavy equipment, maritime, natural resources, and government.
