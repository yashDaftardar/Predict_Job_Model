Full Job Description

The Ultra Engineering function manages a portfolio of specialist capabilities, generating highly differentiated solutions and products by applying electronic, mechanical, and software technologies in demanding and critical environments to meet customer needs.

Naval Systems and Sensors is an engineering and manufacturing company that is actively involved in a diverse range of sensor processing development projects. We design, integrate and test a wide range of systems providing innovative capabilities for our customers. We specialize in high-speed data processing and digital signal processing for high-end military sensor systems including imaging, Electronic Warfare, radar and sonar processors.

We are searching for a highly motivated and detail oriented Software Engineer to support the development of an existing Radar system.

Working closely with program leads and system engineering, the Software Engineer will support the development and test of the Radar System through design, development, and finally the integration and test events. This role will include significant testing of systems and development of test tools in addition to traditional software development activities. The Software Engineer will have the opportunity to guide the development and selection of key technologies for the Navy and will have the opportunity to work with complex software algorithms and cutting-edge digital signal processing hardware. He/she will have the opportunity to contribute to key strategic approaches for future proposals, IR&D, and additional development activities.

Required Skills:

  • BS Degree in Software Engineering type of discipline

  • 4+ years of experience with a major object-oriented programming language such as C++ (preferred) and/or Java

  • 4+ years of proper coding practices and the full development life cycle

  • 1+ years of experience testing software systems and development of test tools

  • 1+ years of experience with Linux-based operating systems and code development

  • 4+ years of experience experience with basic editors such as VI, emacs, Kate, Gedit, etc.

  • The ability to obtain a US Government security clearance. Security clearances are granted to U.S. citizen

Desired Skills:

  • Working knowledge of XML XSD

  • Experience with UML or other design modeling techniques

  • Experience with middleware/inter-process communication (AMQP, CORBA, DDS, etc.) using a publish-subscribe pattern

  • Working knowledge of the use of third party tools to do code coverage, memory leak, and other code integrity checks

  • Knowledge or understanding of Qt programming

  • Experience working with a team to jointly develop a system during all phases of the development lifecycle

  • Strong attention to detail and work ethic

  • Willingness to travel (10%).

Ultra specialises in providing application-engineered bespoke solutions. We focus on our customers' mission critical and intelligent systems in defence, security, critical detection & control markets. Our capabilities and technologies can be found on many of the world's long-term military programmes. We employ over 4,500 employees across the globe and are listed on the London Stock Exchange. Ultra operates mainly as a Tier 3 (sub-system) and occasionally a Tier 2 systems provider, in the Maritime, C3 (Communications, Command and Control) and Cyber, military and commercial aerospace, nuclear and industrial sensors markets. We use both research and development , to provide innovative, mission specific bespoke technological solutions to our customer's most complex problems. Learn more by visiting our website; https://www.ultra.group/

We offer medical, dental, vision, life, and disability insurance, health savings accounts, paid time off, and a 401(k) plan with a Company match.
