Full Job Description

It takes powerful technology to connect our brands and partners with an audience of 1 billion. Nearly half of Verizon Media employees are building the code and platforms that help us achieve that. Whether you’re looking to write mobile app code, engineer the servers behind our massive ad tech stacks, or develop algorithms to help us process 4 trillion data points a day, what you do here will have a huge impact on our business—and the world. Want in? As Verizon’s media unit, our brands like Yahoo, TechCrunch and more help people stay informed and entertained, communicate and transact, while creating new ways for advertisers and partners to connect. With technologies like XR, AI, machine-learning, and 5G, we’re transforming media for tomorrow, too. We're creators and coders, dreamers and doers creating what's next in content, advertising and technology.

Verizon Media is a leading provider of media and technology brands, reaching over a billion people around the world. Verizon Media’s Platforms Engineering organization creates modern, secure, and efficient software platforms to power all of Verizon Media’s brands at scale. Our Identity and User Data platforms for consumers and businesses lead the industry in trust and security. Our Operating Systems, languages, build systems, and Developer Platform Services drive technical excellence and developer productivity. Our Monitoring, Payments, Cloud Platforms, Mobile SDKs, and many other systems accelerate growth and innovation.

Core Platforms and Services at Verizon Media provides modern, secure applications & software to optimize the development process and automate software deployment at Verizon Media . We build tools that allow engineers within Verizon Media to rapidly develop software and deploy it to hundreds of thousands of systems. Our team is focused on delivering highly effective products in an Agile environment while keeping things fun.

Responsibilities:

We are looking to hire a top-notch developer to work on developing platform, infrastructure & continuous delivery tools. The systems you will be building will be the backbone of Verizon Media’s infrastructure for many years to come. We expect skills and experience that match. Your responsibilities will include: Design, build, implement, modify, test, debug, and deploy software systems, large-scale infrastructure platforms, and network services that are the foundation of multiple Verizon Media properties and applications. Responsible for performance, reliability, availability, scalability and security. Services are delivered to applications through standard and proprietary protocols and behavior may be customized via complete APIs and/or Web services. As part of the Developer Platforms and Services (DPS) team, help develop internal applications to automate hardware provisioning and configuration, and software management and deployment. Develop tools to be used by engineers globally within Verizon Media to manage hundreds of thousands of systems. Help work on products that boast high volumes of information density and complex interactions. Deliver highly effective products in an Agile environment. Work on infrastructure tools. Help build systems that will be the backbone of Verizon Media's infrastructure for many years to come. Work with other developers, product owners, scrum masters, designers, and architects to deliver high-value features to internal customers. Work on large-scale, highly available systems, and leverage knowledge of network architecture and security needs. Participate in scalable, object-oriented design. Deliver high-quality, maintainable code, with a full test suite and with the appropriate documentation.

Required Qualifications:

  • 3+ years of experience in web infrastructure, distributed systems, and/or large-scale databases
  • Experience with container and cloud technologies such as Kubernetes, Docker, AWS
  • Experience with software engineering best practices (test driven development, continuous integration, code reviews, etc.)
  • Strong UNIX skills
  • Experience in object-oriented programming and design
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Self-motivated and able to work independently
  • Ability to collaborate actively as part of a multidisciplinary team
  • Debugging and problem solving skills
  • BSc in Computer Science or equivalent experience

Desired Qualifications:

  • Strong background in golang, PERL, Python and experience implementing Web Services (REST API)
  • Experience with build & deploy technologies such as Chef, version control
  • Knowledge of cross-browser, cross-platform implementation techniques and an advocate of web standards
  • Experience in Agile software practices, including CI and XP
  • Ability to lead and/or mentor more junior team members
  • Contributions to open source software
  • MSc in Computer Science
