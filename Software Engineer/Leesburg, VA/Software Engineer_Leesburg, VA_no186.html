Company Description

Bushido Associates, Inc. We have past performance supporting federal and commercial clients. We have led the turn-around of under-performing security operations centers and supported national cyber-crime centers. BAI consultants are subject matter experts specializing in all aspects of information assurance, cybersecurity, and intrusion investigations.
Job Description
In the role of a fully cleared Front-End Software Engineer, you will design and develop Front-Ends for scalable applications driven by Big Data technologies. You will work as part of a team of engineers and analyst to produce high quality production ready applications to meet the needs of various user groups. You will engage with various groups to identify user requirements and convey possible solutions and limitations based on those requirements. You will create a variety of interactive graphical dashboards that provide meaningful insights to users based on a variety of data types. You will produce mock-ups of dashboards on an iterative basis to continuously align to user requirements. You will also work closely with back-end engineers to ensure data formats and processing are supported by front-end software and that back-end processing aligns to frontend requirements.
Responsibilities (Expectations) :

  • Develop a variety of interactive/dynamic graphical dashboards suited for key user groups
  • Produce dynamic web pages based on current industry best practices
  • Create a variety of page types to include search pages, web-forms and filtered result pages to meet the needs of distinct user types
  • Work closely with cyber threat intelligence analysts, intrusion analysts, and incident responders to develop unique ways to present data that helps them effectively and efficiently meet their mission objectives
  • Brief small to large audiences with a mixture of senior level and working level analysts on a variety of technical topics as they relate to your area of work
  • Produce professional level written material to convey engineering concepts on an ad-hoc basis (e.g., white papers, presentations, etc.)
  • Communicate clearly with both technical and non-technical individuals to shape requirements and identify the best solution
  • Constantly learn new hands-on technologies and frameworks in support of evolving requirements as they relate to threat analytics and customer requirements

Qualifications (Must have) :

  • Active TS/SCI Clearance
  • 2 + years hands-on experience with JavaScript development
  • Skilled with common front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, JS, TypeScript, and Node
  • Deep experience in one or more relevant front-end frameworks such as React
  • Solid coding practices including peer code reviews, unit testing, and a preference for agile development
  • Skilled at building user-facing software and supporting systems and libraries for the web and/or desktop
  • Hands-on Linux experience (regardless of distro)
  • Hands-on with Distributed version control internals (Git)
  • Schema-based data storage (SQL) as it relays to front-end template rendering
  • Strong writing skills to convey engineering concepts/workflows in clear and concise language
  • Knowledge of D3 (Experience strongly preferred)
  • Experience working in a heterogeneous operating environment (i.e., one comprised of Linux, Unix, Mac, and Windows operating systems, VMs)
  • Broad knowledge of the DoD, Intelligence Community and each agency's roles and authorities

Additional Qualifications (Nice to have) :

  • Experience building reusable UX components or libraries
  • Experience with CI/CD framework (e.g. Jenkins, Spinnaker)
  • DoD Experience
  • Coding best practices and syntax across multiple languages
  • Ability to perform hands-on development of scripts (e.g., Bash, Python, C#, etc.) to better facilitate analysis of a large, complex set of unstructured data
