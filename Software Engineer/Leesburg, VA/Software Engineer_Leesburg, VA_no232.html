INCATech is looking for a highly motivated Full Stack Developer (front-end or user-interface focused) to support an innovative and transformational software development program. This position will work closely with a mid-size team to develop to design specifications in a modernized fast-paced DevSecOps environment to support the Joint Personnel Recovery Agency (JPRA) under the Joint Chiefs of Staff. The position is fully remote work/telework.
Location Full Remote
The Full-Stack Developer must have:
  • US Citizenship
  • Must have strong demonstrated knowledge and experience with Vuetify/Material Design
  • Experience with CSS (SASS, LESS), JavaScript/HTML5/CSS3
  • Experience with Microsoft Visual Studio Code
  • Experience with Vue
  • Experience with REST API web services development
  • Experience with Node.js development using frameworks and open-source technologies
  • Experience developing user-interface to design specifications
  • Experience with Agile/Scrum development methodologies
  • Strong ability to prioritize work.
  • Strong analytical and creative problem-solving skills
Education: Bachelors in information technology, computer science, engineering, mathematics, GIS, or a related field.

Benefits Summary:
  • Competitive salary and benefits package commensurate with qualifications and experience.
  • Comprehensive Healthcare benefit – Medical (multiple options to choose from), insurance for the employee and family effective the first day of employment. For the HSA option, the company provides an additional $1,200/yr. to the employee’s HSA account.
  • Up to 4% 401K dollar to dollar matching with immediate vesting.
  • Company provided life insurance with voluntary participation options to increase the limit and/or to include family members.
  • The company provided short-term and long-term disability insurance.
  • Company provided employee dental and family vision insurance.
  • Spot and Incentive bonuses for outstanding contributions of individuals who are making a difference to our company.

INCATech is an award-winning small business that has over 12 years of experience supporting the US Government. We provide both Professional Services as well as develop Software Solutions for our customers. Our highly skilled and experienced staff of colleagues provide Acquisition Support, Information Technology (IT) support, Enterprise Information Management, Enterprise Content Management System (ECMS) Modernization, User Experience Design, Geospatial implementation, and Program/Project Management SDLC services to our customers. Our culture is based on empowering our staff and customers with knowledge and tools to achieve mission success and career advancement.
