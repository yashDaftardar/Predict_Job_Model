Position: Senior DevOPs Engineer

Location: Herndon, VA

FTE

cloud-based platforms create exclusive communities within the Aerospace and Defense, Life Sciences, and other highly-regulated industries where members securely collaborate, share information, and operate compliantly.

Within these communities we build trust. By analyzing community data we provide insights and intelligence, enabling organizations to make better, timelier decisions, to mitigate risk, and operate more efficiently.

Job Overview: The Senior DevOps Engineer role is responsible for the technical operation of systems comprising Client major product lines, within a world-class, fast-paced, highly secure environment supporting the aerospace and defense industry. Charged with supporting multiple SaaS products the DevOps role is responsible for evolving our DevOps infrastructure, deploying product updates, implementing best practices, and ultimately will execute and automate operational processes fast, accurately, and securely.

Essential Job Functions Working closely with both the development and operations group at Exostar and having a strong scripting or programming background Promote application code and be able to configure Java based applications in development, test and production environments Evolving our continuous development and delivery process Managing and maintaining the micro-service architecture Monitoring service uptime to SLA Troubleshoot issues in Dev/Test/Production environments and work closely with the development team to bring issues to closure Code promotions of builds to Test/UAT/Production environments Maintain Virtual Machines (VMs) running on VMWare ESX as well as containers in Docker/Kubernetes internally, in AWS, and Azure Participating in engineering reviews of code promotion requests, architecture reviews Develop automation to integrate with internal back-end systems Build tools to reduce occurrences of errors

“must haves” for this role if you have: Bachelor’s Degree and 10 plus years of relevant technical work experience (sys admin, devops, coding)

Must be a United States citizen due to ITAR regulations

Application Services experience is essential for this job. Experience with Tomcat, Apache, IIS, Wildfly, PingFederate, or any other application server based experience is essential Working knowledge of database systems: MS SQL Server, Oracle, Couch, Mongo, or MySQL Experience with any: Kubernetes, Docker, SaltStack, Ansible, Consul, Prometheus, Selenium, Python, PHP scripting. Linux and Windows administration and scripting skills (ie Powershell, bash) Extensive experience with virtualization, micro-services, and cloud technologies in both AWS and Azure Complete of understanding of Networking operations such as DNS and IP routing and configuration Must be able to obtain Client Trusted Role, and maintain Trusted Role status while employed in this role
