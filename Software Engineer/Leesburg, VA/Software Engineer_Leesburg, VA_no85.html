The Raytheon Intelligence & Space (RIS) Space and C2 mission area is seeking a Systems Integration and Test Engineer for programs to modernize and maintain satellite ground segments.

As a Software Systems Integration and Test Engineer your duties will include but not be limited to the following:
  • Support the planning and conduct of Test activities; define test plans and sequences relative to complex software and hardware subsystems and resolves identified conflicting issues.
  • Participate in a complex testing program; establish methods and techniques, plan tests, develop test requirements, write test procedures and reports, and conduct tests utilizing comprehensive knowledge of these complex subsystems.
  • Participate in integration and test readiness reviews, design reviews, and audits of integration and test programs and facilities.
  • Utilize broad knowledge in test engineering and apply understanding of principles of related engineering and software technology to ensure test validity and integrity.

Required Skills:
  • Current/Active TS/SCI with CI Poly clearance
  • Bachelor degree and at least 6+ years demonstrated engineering experience with proven track record.
  • Integration engineering experience on a software development contract
  • Experience preparing and executing Integration procedures
  • Experience with test script or automation development
  • Enthusiastic & energetic performer able to work in a dynamic, fast-paced, & high visibility environment
  • Self-motivated and capable of performing tasks with minimal oversight
  • Experience with systems engineering principles and activities
Desired Skills:
  • Experience working in a Satellite Ground System development or operations environment
  • Experience with ground station satellite domains (command and telemetry, or mission planning)
  • Scripting (Python, Perl, CECIL)
  • Experience in an Agile or DevOps development environment.
  • Proficiency with Windows and Linux operating system
  • Experience with Java or C++
Education Requirements:
