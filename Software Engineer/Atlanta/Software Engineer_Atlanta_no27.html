Position Overview

Do you enjoy solving complex tech problems in large-scale CAD design software products along with a group of highly experienced and talented software engineers? If so, we have the perfect opportunity for you.

Your mission: improve Autodesk Inventor Platform to be more stable, secure, and faster

As part of Inventor Platform development team, you will work in a scrum team including highly talented Inventor developers, QAs, product designers and product managers, to provide first class customer-oriented product and services for both internal and external customers. Ensure the high quality of the very fundamental modules of Autodesk Inventor to satisfy customer needs.

Responsibilities

  • Develop high quality, secure, stable code for use in products and solutions for customers, making it easier for other developers to maintain, enhance, reuse, test, and localize

  • Work closely with the team to deliver results that meet project definition and schedule with a high level of quality and customer satisfaction

  • Apply continuous attention to technical excellence and drive self-growth

Minimum Qualifications

  • BS or above degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Mathematics, Mechanical Engineering or equivalent

  • 2+ years of C++ programming skills on commercial products

  • Familiar with Object-oriented analysis and design

  • Proficient communication (both verbal and written) skills

Preferred Qualifications

  • Successfully completed one or more commercial software development cycles

  • Proven experience in the development of CAD/CAM/CAE applications

  • Experience with MFC, WPF and Qt component
