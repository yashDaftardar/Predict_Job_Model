Are you passionate about building awesome web applications, developing powerful integrated monitoring and analysis solutions and the opportunity to grow a brand-new team from the ground up? We are looking for driven front-end engineers to join a new team with a mission to develop browser-based diagnostics and administrative portal UI(s) and administrative tools for the highest performance storage system in Azure. Ultra Disk is a low-latency, high speed-distributed networking cloud block-storage platform for the most demanding cloud storage workloads. The diagnostics portal handles diagnostics/monitoring and remote administration, integrating data from other Azure subsystems to present a clear picture of the heath of UltraDisk as well as powerful, efficient tooling to help engineers troubleshoot issues and manage the Ultra Disk fleet. Microsoft Azure is a dynamic and thriving engineering environment with world-class opportunities for learning and career development as well as the chance to work with exciting technologies at a global scale.

Responsibilities

The Ultra Disk team is searching for engineers who are passionate about working in a collaborative, high energy team environment. We strive to live in a diverse world where everyone on the team has an equal voice, is empowered to innovate, and can make a big impact.

Qualifications

Basic Qualifications:
  • 2+ years of software design, data structures, testing, and debugging
  • 2+ years of web development experience (JavaScript, Typescript, Angular, RxJS, or CSS)
  • 1+ years leadership experience in web-based UI/UX development and design
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills

Preferred Qualifications:
  • BS/BA in Computer Science or related field
  • Expereince with ML/AI is a plus
  • Experience in developing in languages such as C#, Java or C/C++
  • Experience with scripting languages such as PowerShell or Python)
  • Expert in building responsive, accessible single-page applications (SPA) with modern JavaScript frameworks (React, Vue, Angular)
  • Familiarity with design and prototyping tools: Figma, Sketch, Storybook, etc.
  • Experience in data storage systems, file systems, and/or networking
  • Experience developing distributed systems
