Description - External

At Xandr, our mission is to build a better advertising platform that gives our clients an alternative to the tech giants that have a monopoly in the attention economy. In building our platform we have to stay ahead of our competition by building out a multitude of features including cutting-edge data integrations, effective machine learning algorithms and innovative user experience - all while maintaining a highly-distributed platform that processes more than 150TB of data a day. Engineering is at the core of what we do at Xandr. Our Software Engineers play a pivotal role in developing, improving, and scaling our platform while sticking to high quality standards and 24/7/365 uptime requirements. Our software is complex, massively scaled, and built with a wide variety of languages and tools, lending itself to the most exciting and challenging technical problems.

About the team
We are a close-knit and collaborative feature development team made up of smart, passionate software engineers of varying degrees of experience with a variety of backgrounds, each of whom brings their unique perspective and history to the group. We partner with the product management and user experience teams to design, architect, and implement beautiful, easy-to-use, functional user interfaces that enable our users to drive their business to success. We spend our days developing best-in-class products using React, Redux and many other useful technologies in the React ecosystem, such as Reselect and Redux-saga. We also own a number of “middle layer” Node.js applications, and we contribute to the schemas that drive our implementation of GraphQL. Our projects run the gamut from re-architecting existing systems, to improving application performance and runtime, to building greenfield applications.

About the job
For the role you will

  • Be a key developer on new frontend features that address our customer's needs.
  • Learn about the complex architecture of the Xandr system to identify improvements throughout the stack.
  • Collaborate regularly with designers, a variety of engineers and product managers.

Qualifications - External

The following are expected qualifications for the role

  • 2-4 years of experience in software engineering working with production systems
  • Strong expertise in frontend technologies (JavaScript, React, state management like Redux)
  • Some knowledge of Computer Science fundamentals with regards to data structures, algorithms, time complexity, etc.
  • A commitment to code quality


Nice to have
The following additional areas of expertise that will be beneficial for the role

  • Understanding of functional programming
  • Experience with middle layer technologies (Node.js, GraphQL, Restful APIs)
  • Experience with large distributed systems


You'll love working here if you

  • Enjoy working on high-quality, intuitive, impactful products
  • Take personal pride in the quality of your work
  • Are passionate about learning and teaching
  • Constantly strive to improve yourself
  • Thrive on helping to empower and raise up your colleagues
  • Focus not just on resolving immediate, surface-level issues but also on understanding the root causes in order to build long-term solutions
  • Care about solving big, systemic problems
