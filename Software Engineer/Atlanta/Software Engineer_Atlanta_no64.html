Position Summary:

Sr Engineer on Web Platform Team designs and develops features for our in-market apps as well as upcoming new products. Mentor and provide guidance to the team and Jr team members. Promote and model engineering best practices across the team and the engineering org.

Duties and Responsibilities:

Design and code features and bug fixes for in-market applications and new upcoming projects

Supervisory Responsibilities:

None

Minimum Qualifications:

  • 4+ years industry experience and 2 years developing consumer web applications
  • Professional experience delivering compelling web applications with JavaScript, HTML and CSS
  • Solid knowledge of web standards, best practices, and browser compatibility issues.
  • Exposure to design patterns of web applications like API design and efficient implementations.
  • Fluency in developing both object-oriented and functional code, on server and client
  • Experience writing unit, integration and acceptance tests.
  • Good to have: React & Typescript experience.

Requirements and General Skills:

  • Good public speaking and presentation skills.
  • Interpersonal skills and ability to interact and work with staff at all levels.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Ability to work independently and in a team environment.
  • Ability to pay attention to details and be organized.
  • Ability to project professionalism over the phone and in person.
  • Ability to handle multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment.
  • Commitment to “internal client” and customer service principles.
  • Willingness to take initiative and to follow through on projects.
  • Creative writing ability.
  • Excellent time management skills, with the ability to prioritize and multi-task, and work under shifting deadlines in a fast-paced environment.
  • Must have legal right to work in the U.S.

Technical Skills:

  • JavaScript, HTML and CSS web standards, best practices, and browser compatibility issues
