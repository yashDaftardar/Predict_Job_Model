About this role

Elevate your career by joining the world's largest asset manager! Thrive in an environment that fosters positive relationships and recognizes outstanding performance! We know you want to feel valued every single day and be recognized for your contribution. At BlackRock, we strive to empower our employees and effectively engage your involvement in our success.

BlackRock is one of the world’s preeminent asset management firms and a premier provider of global investment management, risk management and advisory services to institutional, intermediary and individual investors around the world. BlackRock offers a range of solutions — from rigorous fundamental and quantitative active management approaches aimed at maximizing outperformance to highly efficient indexing strategies designed to gain broad exposure to the world’s capital markets. Our clients can access our investment solutions through a variety of product structures, including individual and institutional separate accounts, mutual funds and other pooled investment vehicles, and the industry-leading iShares® ETFs.

Business Unit Overview:

BlackRock Portfolio Management Group (PMG) has created a new Global Data Research team focused on alternative, primary and big data, called Data Strategy & Solutions.

Data Strategy & Solutions:

  • Leading investors to better decisions, higher conviction and inquisitive ideas by empowering them with access to new data sources and tools to makes sense of it.
  • Partnered with dozens of investment groups in BlackRock – we get first access to lots of interesting data problems every month, covering just as many strategies and asset classes
  • On a mission! To expand and make more impact using innovation, people and processes

This role offers tremendous scope for an entrepreneurial person with a keen interest in data and finance to make a significant contribution to alpha generation across our world class investment teams. You will be working with a team that shares your passion for data and its potential to positively transform the investment landscape.

Responsibilities Include:

You will be doing:

  • Invent, plan and implement solutions to common and unique data problems
  • Provide guidance in the investigation and solution of complex problems affecting our platforms or tooling, using the creative freedom we provide to think about problems at multiple levels – system, algo, human
  • Continuously challenge yourself, your peers and management to achieve success faster than the previous sprint and have fun doing it
  • Creating platforms and tools that make our team or our clients smarter, better and/or more efficient

Who you are:

  • Technical – you love technology, you love to discuss it, build it, use it, hack it and modernize it
  • Experienced – you have built cool things that people used, your fail:won ratio is just about even, technology has failed you at least once
  • Team Builder – you’re in the groove solo but often like to help others; you’ve seen the value in teamwork
  • Self-motivated – you have examples of following your passion to succeed, you don’t mind driving if you know the destination
  • Elegance is your name – you build software with consistency and utility top-of-mind, the end-user’s voice is always in your head

Skills required include:
