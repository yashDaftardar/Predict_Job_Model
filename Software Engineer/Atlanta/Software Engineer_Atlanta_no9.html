Job Title

IT Software Support Engineer

Job Description

As an IT Software Support Engineer , you will be the liaison between the Engineering Support Office (ESO) and end users of the Engineering Suite of Tools ( ESoT ) in-house software. This software package is used by Layout and Mechanical Engineers to design a system from layout to specifications in AutoCAD.

The goal of the IT Software Support Engineer is to assess the needs of end users, determine the changes required to ESoT to meet that needs , and gather all information needed for Content Builders to implement the changes. Additionally, the IT Software Support Engineer is required to test and verify all changes made to the ESoT libraries . He or she will ensure that changes and new developments are implemented correctly, accurately, and function as desired by end users .

The IT Software Support Engineer is also an important sparring partner for the software architects of the engineering tools. He or she knows the wishes and demands of the engineer departments thoroughly and will provide valuable input for improving the tool.

A lot of knowledge regarding the tool and the way of working is to be gained in the first year. You will be mainly be focusing on gathering feedback from end users, coming up with a solution to resolve their issues, as well as providing support/training of the ESoT software to end users.

Additional responsibilities include:

  • Providing 1 st line technical support for ESoT end users

  • Submitting Jira tickets

  • Investigate user issues to determine cause and identify possible solutions

  • Provide end users with basic/advanced ESoT training

  • Become familiar with all US Airports equipment
