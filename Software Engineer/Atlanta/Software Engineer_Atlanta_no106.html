About Pulse8, Inc.

Pulse8 is an innovative Healthcare Analytics and Technology Company that delivers visibility and transparency for Risk Adjustment and Quality Management programs. We enable health plans and at-risk providers to achieve the greatest financial impact in the Health Insurance Exchange (HIX), Medicare Advantage, and Medicaid markets. By combining advanced analytic methodologies with extensive health plan experience, Pulse8 has developed a suite of uniquely pragmatic solutions that are revolutionizing risk adjustment. Pulse8's flexible business intelligence tools offer real-time visibility into member and provider activities so our clients can apply the most cost-effective and appropriate interventions for closing gaps in documentation, coding, and quality.

Position Description

Pulse8 is seeking a talented Software Engineer to use best coding practices to identify, document, build and support development of the Quality Analytics suite across the full stack. Position will also be required to perform support functions and other ancillary duties as assigned. Superior technical, critical thinking and interpersonal skills are essential for success in this position.


This is a remote position.


Responsibilities:

  • Be a key player in an Agile environment, participating in daily huddles, planning, and strategy sessions
  • Be a part of our scalability strategy
  • Develop innovative new Healthcare Technology products and services
  • Develop and refine our best-in-class Healthcare Analytics algorithms
  • Work with teammates to prioritize, design, build, test, deploy, troubleshoot, document, and support technology solutions using industry and Pulse8-identified best practices
  • Perform a wide range of other ancillary duties in support of production runs and other company initiatives as necessary


Qualifications:

Academic and Professional Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience.

Experience:

Minimum Qualifications

  • Experience with relational DBMS, SQL Server / TSQL preferred
  • Experience with ETL Development, Azure Data Factory / Databricks Preferred
  • Experience using Visual Studio
  • Strong analytical and critical thinking skills
  • Strong interpersonal skills

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with cloud-based development, Azure preferred
  • Knowledge of healthcare with relative experience in: Claims data, Risk adjustment, Medicare, Quality (5-Star, QRS, HEDIS) and Medicaid
  • Agile/Scrum/Kanban
  • Experience with testing, specifically writing test cases, unit testing, and automated testing
  • EMR/Integration experience (HL7, CCOW, APIs)
  • Strong documentation skills

Travel requirements:

  • No to minimal travel

Working arrangements:

  • Monday through Friday or as defined by assignment requirements
