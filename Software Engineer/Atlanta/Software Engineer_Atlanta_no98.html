LightStream's Software Engineers develop next-generation banking and finance technologies. We're looking for Engineers with experience working in financial institutions who are ready to raise the bar, bring new ideas, and change the way that banking technology is built and delivered. As a Software Engineer, you will work on a specific project critical to LightStreamâ€™s needs with opportunities to switch teams and projects as you and our fast-paced business grow and evolve. We need our Engineers to be versatile, display leadership qualities and be enthusiastic to take on new problems across the full-stack as we continue to push technology forward.

With your technical expertise you will manage project priorities, deadlines, and deliverables. You will design, develop, test, deploy, maintain, and enhance software solutions.
As a Software Engineer for iOS and Android Mobile Apps, you will develop mobile applications, design and implement full stack app foundation features as well as core app features from ground-up that enable devices to provide delightful user experiences.

Responsibilities
  • Work on Android and iOS mobile applications, cloud services, and tools.
  • Design, develop, test, deploy, maintain, and improve mobile applications.
  • Manage individual project priorities, deadlines, and deliverables.
  • Build products that can scale for users across the country.
