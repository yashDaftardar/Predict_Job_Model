Are you passionate about Database Technology and Cloud Computing and fascinated by challenges such as optimizing the management of millions of databases across a wide price/performance spectrum?

We are the SQL Database team, part of the Microsoft Digital Transformation Platform Group (DTPG). SQL Database is a leader in managed relational database business, and we’re growing rapidly. We are operating hundreds of thousands of servers worldwide with millions of databases in over 50 Azure regions. Our products push the limits of technology to exceed requirements of the most demanding data applications in the world, and our teams love solving these planet scale high impact challenges.

With this unprecedented growth comes added complexity in our hardware ecosystem, at scales we never imagined before. This is your opportunity to take on a challenge and join a newly created Hardware Strategy and Onboarding function from the ground up. This team is on a mission to define and deliver a world class hardware strategy that position infrastructure as a key differentiator for customer value and business margins.

Responsibilities

We are looking for a Software Engineer II with a passion for technology and for helping customers succeed on our platform by building the next set of industry-leading features and capabilities that will delight our customers. You have an opportunity to work on these technologies (Azure SQL Database, SQL Server on Azure Virtual Machines) with a focus on driving product performance and reducing total cost of ownership. You will have the opportunity to work on scale-up performance of SQL on a single system (1000+ cores, 24TB+ RAM, 100 Gbit NIC...), scale-out performance (SQL big-data clusters with 100+ nodes) and optimization of SQL for Cloud. The optimizations will also aim to fully leverage capabilities in upcoming technologies (new processors, storage, networking, memory, PCI interface...) in order to deliver better performance and reduce cost.

Responsibilities include contributions to one or more of the following:
  • Strengthening the engineering process to make sure that cloud deployments (software & hardware) are done in the most efficient way and meet the targeted perf/scale/SLA goals.
  • A key area of focus is ensuring that appropriate performance scenario tests are added, and perf gates are in place. Automate existing perf tests used for SKU validation including Azure Data primitives. Add newer tests that capture scenarios not covered by the existing ones.
  • Looking into emerging and upcoming Azure SKUs and technologies and exploring new ideas to drive product improvements. (hardware/software/config/COGS). This will also include modeling, competitive analysis & benchmarks.
  • Contributing to current/upcoming product improvements that have key deliverables around performance (perf/scale/SLA); this will be done through a combination of one or more of the following (writing/enhancing performance tests, design review, investigation/analysis, prototype and production code...).
  • Identifying improvements in supportability that enable live monitoring with a focus on identifying/addressing performance related issues.
  • Collaborating with and supporting OEMs in showcasing product performance for technical marketing deliverables (benchmarks, white papers, demos, etc.).
  • Provide a monthly (or bi-weekly) presentation to key-stakeholders with respect totheprogress/new-findings/statusof assigneddeliverables.

Qualifications

Basic Qualifications:
  • 4+ years of software development experience.
  • 3+ years of programming experience in C/C++, C#, Java, or Python.

Preferred Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or Engineering or Mathematics or Physics or IT technical discipline is desirable.
  • Intellectual curiosity and willingness to question, undaunted by hard technical problems.
  • Experience designing and developing production quality software using C/C++; proven experience in improving product performance.
  • Extensive and specialized knowledge in one or more of the following areas: Database/OS internals, Hardware knowledge, (CPU/System/Networking/Storage), and Cloud related technologies.
  • Experience related to Performance Engineering skills covering. design/development/usage of performance workloads/test-suites/Tools to measure, analyze and root cause performance and scalability issues in the product.
  • Experience in both Windows and Linux system administration fundamentals.
  • Advanced problem-solving, interpersonal, and communication skills.
  • MS in CS/CSE/EE or related discipline.
