THE ROLE:

Code42 is looking for a Staff Software Engineer, focusing on back end server side development, to work on the heart of Code42's market-leading Insider Risk Management platform that helps world-class organizations protect intellectual property.


As an engineer at Code42 your day to day will include:

  • Building new AWS native services that can perform at scale and able to process upwards of 200K events per second. For example, you might build a data enrichment service that correlates risk indicators to a customer’s unique risk profile by extracting signals from endpoints and cloud data sources (Google Drive, O365, Salesforce, etc).
  • Debugging production issues across services to ensure reliability of high-volume data infrastructure.
  • Engaging in thoughtful technical design discussions to find implement solutions that minimize dependencies across teams and support mission-critical business initiatives
  • Collaborating with engineers from across the company and providing thought leadership around what technologies to adopt for scaling infrastructure.
  • Being relentless in the pursuit of better around engineering standards, tooling, and processes.
  • Supporting specialists from Code42's data analytics and science teams to identify new opportunities for improving product fidelity and signal.
  • Leading team design discussions and code reviews, asking questions, contributing and accepting feedback.
  • Documenting code and design, and reviewing code and design docs from others.
  • Helping to estimate and prioritize product backlog.
  • Helping to mentor and guide more junior staff.


WHAT YOU BRING:

  • Typically has a bachelor's degree and 8+ or more years professional experience, or can convincingly demonstrate this level of skill.
  • Advanced skills in a JVM based language.
  • Advanced object-oriented development experience.
  • Experienced with testing frameworks used by the team.
  • Is familiar with continuous integration and deployment concepts.
  • Practical experience with database technologies (e.g, relational databases, Elasticsearch, NoSQL)
  • Is comfortable taking ownership of deliverables.
  • Has a passion for building systems that work well.
  • Clearly able to communicate technical ideas to peers in written and verbal form.
  • Able to articulate business impact of technical decisions to non-technical staff.

NICE TO HAVE:

  • Experience with Kotlin
  • Experience with AWS services such as Lambda, Kinesis, Fargate, etc.
  • Have worked with agile/lean development processes
The salary range for this role is approximately $100,000- $180,000.
