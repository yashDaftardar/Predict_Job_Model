Job Description


Intrepid by VitalSource LLC is hiring for a Software Engineer, Intermediate to work 100% remotely . As a Software Engineer on our team, you will work closely with other engineers to build features for the Intrepid product platforms. The core team originates from Seattle, WA but we have a number of remote associates over the whole US.

Who we are:

Intrepid is an award-winning collaborative learning SaaS platform that empowers organizations to solve high-stakes business challenges through engaging and applied learning at scale.

What we do:

We create innovative features with huge impact for our clients. We use cloud tech, APIs, modern architecture, continuous deployment, and integrations to reach our key initiatives. Come be a part of our Engineering org! If you are a Software Engineer that is passionate about development, we can’t wait to meet you!

Required Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s in computer science or equivalent practical experience
  • 4 years of application developer/software engineer experience
  • 4 years of experience with Java and/or React

Preferred Skills:

  • Strong front-end development experience
  • Experience working with React
  • Natural tendency to be curious, positive, and creative
  • Team player who collaborates well with others

Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop creative and innovative solutions for the next generation of educational learning
  • Provide full application development life cycle support, including specifications, prototypes, development, quality assurance and deployment with assistance from Senior and Lead Application Development
  • Communicate effectively with internal and external users and stakeholders
  • Support development with appropriate documentation
  • This list is not exhaustive

  • Encouraged continued education with our tuition reimbursement program
