Job Responsibilities

We are seeking a Software Engineer with experience working in Python or C/C++. An ideal candidate will have the knowledge and desire to support work the design, implementation, integration, and test of application software. As a Software Engineer you will perform full-cycle software development work as a member of a team on various projects. The work of this team focuses on developing and supporting a Satellite ground station monitoring and control system.

Requirements

  • 10 years hands-on development experience with Python, C/C++, and/or Javascript
  • Bachelor’s degree or higher in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering or related discipline
  • Ability to develop real-time applications for streaming digital data
  • Ability to develop user interfaces for monitor and control
  • Knowledge of LINUX OS platform
  • US Citizenship Required

Preferences

  • Familiarity with networking and cloud computing concepts
  • Experience with distributed application development
  • Knowledge of virtualization and experience with virtualization tools highly desired
  • Continuous Integration & development (CI/CD) experience
  • Working knowledge using Ansible playbook based SW/system deployment
  • Experience with Monitoring & Control of sensors and devices
