Full Job Description

Ready to join a team of eCommerce professionals in a family-friendly environment with exceptional benefits? DoMyOwn.com may be your next home.

DoMyOwn.com, a division of Central Garden & Pet, is one of the largest online retailers of professional-grade pest control, lawn and gardening, and animal care products for the do-it-yourself market in the United States. Our digital product team is growing and we are looking for a software engineer to help build new customer-facing experiences. This position can be located anywhere in the US with a preference for candidates in the eastern time zone.

OVERVIEW:

We are looking to add a software engineer to our e-commerce development team. Our applications are built using PHP and we are searching for a candidate with demonstrated experience building customer-facing applications in a PHP framework such as Laravel. The ideal candidate has previous experience working in a team or squad environment for a small to mid-size company.

YOUR STRENGTHS:

As a software engineer, you can generally operate on your own to solve technical problems. You have demonstrated experience accomplishing defined acceptance criteria while building customer-facing technology. You have experience working with other software engineers on a team and work well together as a unit in a fast pace environment.
MEASURES OF SUCCESS:

  • Collaborate with a team of full-stack developers to design, develop, unit test, and support new features.
  • Work with management, departments, stakeholders, and customers on new software development, improvements, internal tools, and implementation issues.
  • Design, engineer and integrate APIs.
  • Proactively learn the code base and software frameworks used by the team
  • Manage and deploy code using GIT
  • Review project requirements, code to spec, and document code.

IDEAL CANDIDATE ATTRIBUTES:

  • Demonstrated experience building applications using a PHP framework such as Laravel.
  • Experience with Javascript frameworks such as Vue JS.
  • Experience with unit-testing and working with QA/Test engineers.
  • Familiarity with PSR coding standards (PHP-FIG).
  • Very strong problem-solving and critical thinking skills.
  • Must be highly self-motivated and a self-starter.
  • Experience building and maintaining code related to an E-Commerce platform is a plus.
  • Experience with MySQL queries and PHP queues strongly preferred
  • Strong ability and desire to learn and understand new technologies, frameworks and coding methodologies
