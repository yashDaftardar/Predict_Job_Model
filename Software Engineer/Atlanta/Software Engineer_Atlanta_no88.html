Microsoft’s mission is to empower every person and organization on the planet to achieve more, and we believe this mission has never been more important. It guides work across the company to recognize the enormous opportunity and responsibility to ensure that the technology we create benefits everyone on the planet, as well as benefitting the planet itself.

To fulfill this mission, we are looking for an individual that is passionate about our planet and Sustainability Product Innovation. Microsoft is committed to a more sustainable future backed by a $1B planned spend and carbon emission goals that are unparalleled. This work will include partnering to develop new features and tools, improve existing services, and ensure sustainability is part of engineering work across the company. This new role will be an integral part of a new team dedicated to internal and external stakeholder engagements on sustainability.

As part of the Microsoft Sustainability Calculator team, we are at the heart of Microsoft’s cloud strategy. This new team is eager to provide an insightful and delightful sustainability experience. Our vision is to provide solutions that empower organizations to make sustainable decisions through visibility into carbon emissions, circularity, water and waste that facilitates the reduction in greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions.

The team consists of highly motivated, innovative, customer-focused program managers and engineers. We’re optimizing our engineering for the cloud-first with a faster cadence of releases to deliver innovation at an exciting pace. Our team works in an agile start-up like environment where we expect each team member to think out of the box to contribute and collaborate towards the mission of the team in a feature-crew setting. As part of this team, you will get exciting opportunities to work on cutting edge technologies across a variety of platforms to build the next generation application experiences for Microsoft Sustainability Calculator and revolutionize the way businesses operate.

Responsibilities

  • Design and implement microservices to enable real time, highly scalable and Sustainability solution.
  • Collaborate with teammates in various roles to plan and execute on key deliverables.
  • Work in a culture of continuous improvement, adaptation, reflection and growth.
  • Moving to DevOps, you will also be required to participate into daily support, directly work with customers and partners to resolve their instant and urgent issues.

Qualifications

  • 4+ years of professional experience designing, building, and running Cloud Services at large scale
  • 3+ years professional software development with at least two of the following C#/C++/Java, SQL, HTML5/JavaScript/React or ASP.NET

Preferred Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s or higher degree in Computer Science, Engineering or Data Science or related
  • Experience with Continuous Integration and Continuous Deployment paradigms
  • Familiarity with SQL databases and/or NoSQL document stores.
  • Background and passion for applying AI/ML in solving business problems at scale
  • Background and passion in Blockchain
  • Experience using and contributing to Open Source or Inner Source
