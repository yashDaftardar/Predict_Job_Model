The Team

We are the Gray Systems Lab (GSL), the applied research group for Microsoft Azure Data. We tackle research problems in the areas of databases, big data, cloud, systems-for-ML, and ML-for-systems. Our mission is to advance the Microsoft Azure Data organization, and we do so in three ways: (i) innovating within Microsoft products and services (ii) publishing in top-tier conferences and journals, and (iii) actively contributing to open-source projects. The team spans multiple geographical locations (Redmond WA, Sunnyvale CA, Madison WI, Atlanta GA) with a mix of researchers, engineers, and data scientists. With team members originating from 10 countries and 4 continents, each of us brings something authentically unique to the team. In this diverse and inclusive environment, creativity, open dialogue, and a healthy work-life balance are powering GSL innovation and helping each of us grow.

Your Role

As a Research Engineer , you will have the opportunity to work on cutting edge technology to build next-generation cloud data platforms. You will collaborate closely with researchers and product engineers to push the boundaries of our technology stack. This involves quickly picking up complex systems, identifying opportunities, proposing innovative designs, and implementing them in our systems. This is an opportunity to be part of a team that impacts Azure Data’s future offerings, while leveraging and contributing to open-source and internal software. As a research engineer, you have and will further develop a unique mix of experience and skills to help design and build GSL technology that goes far beyond a prototype and is production ready. To build production-quality solutions, you will engage deeply with product teams, but long-term product ownership and ops are not part of GSL charter. There is also the opportunity for you to be part of academic publications and public presentations of the research projects you are involved in.

The following requirements are indicative and applicants with a different and diverse background are welcome to apply. We will consider both junior and senior candidates at appropriate levels.

In alignment with Microsoft’s direction, in most cases we expect the candidate to relocate to one of the lab locations, but we are open to considering remote work for the right candidate.
