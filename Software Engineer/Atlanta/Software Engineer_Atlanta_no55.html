Please review the following job description:

Deliver technically complex solutions. Consult and partner with the Digital Wealth business product owners to understand the end goal and offer solutions and recommendations during the design.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop front end applications using Angular 8/9 for presentation layer and the WebApi using Java 11 with minimum 5 years of experience.

  • Implement component tests.

  • Follow coding best practices and standards to deliver high quality maintainable code.

  • Demonstrate secure coding practices related to internet facing applications.

  • Participate in agile and iterative development approaches with your team. Build positive relationships with software engineers, product owners, and designers.

  • Understand Amazon Web Services (AWS).

  • Set up data and document use cases.

  • Sophisticated analytical skills and the ability to solve complex technical and business problems. Ability to influence others at senior levels to adopt a new perspective.
