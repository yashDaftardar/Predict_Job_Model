Full Job Description

What We Do

Florence (florencehc.com) helps advance cures by making clinical trial document management simple, secure and productive. Our industry-leading platform enables remote connectivity and electronic document workflow management for clinical research organizations around the world. Our software is used by over 8,000 study sites across 30 countries.

What You’ll Bring to the Team.

Florence is looking for an experienced Software Engineer to join our Customer Success team as we continue to grow our customer base and expand our relationships, products and value for each. We strive to be considered a highly strategic partner and this role ensures we achieve that goal post-implementation.

Here are some ways you will make an impact:

  • Hands-on customer-facing support to reproduce, manage and confirm product/data defects
  • Gather technical information as needed to prescribe resolution steps for expected vs actual outcomes
  • Develop tools and techniques to debug complex API integration issues
  • Create technical documentation to serve as a knowledge base for handling support issues and recurring data engineering tasks
  • Ensure customer inquiries are responded to within established timeframes and support service levels are achieved
  • Collaborate and communicate with internal engineering teams to measure, optimize and enhance our existing platform integrations, products, and processes
  • Collaborate with Customer Manager to drive continuous improvements

What You Bring

  • An intermediate or higher level of coding experience in Nodejs/Angular is required for this role.
  • Experience with AWS, Docker is a plus. Understanding of JavaScript, jNode.js, HTML,
  • CSS is more than welcome!
  • Proven ability to solve complex problems while also being a master of simplicity
  • Ability to diagnose issues in source code and strong working knowledge of MongoDB in order to assist in issue resolution and prioritization
  • Strong collaboration capabilities – this role is customer facing and you will routinely work with multiple internal and external teams to solve technical issues
  • Growth mindset – you grow with new products, new technologies, new customers, new relationships, and are comfortable with frequent context switches
  • Proven ability to discover root problems by asking great questions and develop creative solutions to create value for our customers
  • Bias to take action using all resources available to help our customers create a world-class delivery experience

Technologies We Use

  • Our application stack runs entirely on Docker – frontend and backend
  • Our infrastructure is on AWS
  • Javascript is our language of choice for backend services
  • MongoDB is our database of choice
  • We have a modern Javascript frontend built on Node, Angular
  • Collaboration tools include JIRA & Confluence, Zendesk

What’s in it for you?

  • Do well. We offer exceptional salary + equity compensation, fun remote activities, the PC setup of your choice and office space(remote currently due to COVID) in the heart of Atlanta, as well as medical and dental insurance.
  • Do good. We insist that health technology is the highest calling for software development. We pride ourselves on working on something bigger than ourselves; helping advance cures and therapies
  • Enjoy. Our office is in Midtown, we are currently remote due to COVID
