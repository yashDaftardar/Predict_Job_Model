Full Job Description

We are looking for a talented Full Stack Software Engineer to join our team!

Business Summary

The VMware Engineering Services team ensures our internal and external customers enjoy a high-quality experience across the entire product portfolio. Our goal is to provide compelling, innovative, scalable, and seamless engineering services, which will protect VMware’s brand reputation by continually improving customer satisfaction.

We bring together key R&D functions such as build, performance, security, developer tools, and more. We work hand-in-hand with development teams to focus on product innovation and industry-leading solutions.

Our priorities include investing in R&D talent, sharing best practices, and driving scale and agility in VMware products.

Job Role and Responsibility

The VMware Engineering Services team builds web-based services, portals, and components which provide consistent experiences across VMware products. Our applications enable VMware product teams to be able to provide the best-in-class products to our VMware customers. You will apply your imagination and skillset to create experiences that delight our customers.

You will join a world-class team respected for its innovation and execution. We are based in Palo Alto on the main campus with great facilities and a comfortable working environment. If you love shipping software that extends the technology frontier, then this is for you!

  • Collaborate with a group of software engineers (local and offshore) to deliver on software projects using proven Agile development methodologies.

  • Work with internal customers to gather requirements to deliver creative solutions that meet needs with respect to functionality, performance, scalability, reliability, and realistic implementation schedules.

  • Work with internal teams drive high levels of customer satisfaction for engineering teams.

Required Skills:

  • Hands-on experience working with large scale web applications

  • Expertise with JavaScript/TypeScript and at least one modern framework (Angular/React) as well as HTML/CSS3

  • Expertise with Java and at least one modern framework (Play/Spring Boot)

  • Proven track record in large scale, enterprise class software development

  • Ability to learn and apply new technologies, design practices, and usability patterns

  • Ability to contribute to end-to-end system architecture, working with back-end engineers, the UX team, product managers, and other technical teams

  • Interpersonal skills and proven ability to deliver on schedule

  • Hands on experience with Container technologies (Docker and Kubernetes)

  • Possess a good sense of performance tradeoffs and optimizations

  • Able to guide collaborate with other engineering and product teams

  • Demonstrate critical thinking and analytical skills

  • Bachelor’s degree in CS, Masters preferred

  • 1-2 years of related experience in the software industry

Preferred Skills:

  • UI/UX focused design experience (visual design, user behavioral design, user interactions design, usability, etc.)

  • Experience with Kubernetes, containers and microservices

  • Practical understanding of SDLC, design patterns and frameworks

  • Relational Databases like Postgres and MySQL

  • REST API usage and design

This role is flexible and can be done from home, in an office, or a combination of the two anywhere in Arizona, California, Colorado, Nevada, New Mexico, Oregon, Texas, Utah, or Washington.

For positions located in Colorado: This role has a minimum base salary which starts at $82,000. Bonus, commission, and/or equity may be eligible for this position. Additional benefits for this position can be found at https://benefits.vmware.com/. *Note: Disclosure of Colorado pay and benefits required per sb19-968.
