What You Will Do

This position will be filled at either the Computing Systems Professional 2/Computing Systems Professional 3 level, depending on the skills of the selected candidate. Additional job responsibilities (outlined below) will be assigned if the candidate is hired at the higher level.

The Sensitive & Special Operations (SSO) Division provides a secure and compliant TS/SCI level environment for the execution of National Security programs through the use of classified networks and systems. The Special Security IT & Cyber Operations group (SSO-3) currently manages several classified networks and has an ongoing commitment to provide desktop, server, and network support and services to the Los Alamos National Laboratory (LANL) Field Intelligence Element (FIE) and other Government agency Intelligence Community users.
We are seeking an experienced cloud engineer to join our IT Enterprise team as a Computing Systems Professional 2/3 Cloud Engineer. The IT Enterprise Team plans, coordinates, and implements cloud computing solutions in support of national security research and development. You will be involved in a dynamic and challenging environment, focused on meeting the needs of scientific and technical research for the DOE Field Intelligence Element (FIE).
  • Working as a self-initiated team member to develop and implement LANL classified cloud systems
  • Representing the best interests of LANL/FIE and the LANL customer base
  • Assisting in the development of new computing environments
  • Assisting in the storage and processing of large data sets
  • Partnering with LANL/FIE IT stakeholders, IT management, and federal oversight partners to support compliant IT initiatives
