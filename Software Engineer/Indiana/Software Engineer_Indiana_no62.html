Vocera delivers the leading platform for clinical communication and workflow. Our mission is to simplify and improve the lives of healthcare professionals, patients, and families while enabling hospitals to enhance quality of care and operational efficiency and humanize the healthcare experience. Hospitals and health systems around the world have selected our solutions to enable care teams to communicate and collaborate using smartphones or our hands-free, wearable Vocera Smartbadge and Vocera Badge. They can create a richer, more human connection for patients and their loved ones before, during, and after care using Vocera Ease applications. Our platform is interoperable with most clinical and operational systems used in hospitals. In addition to healthcare, Vocera solutions are found in aged care facilities, veterinary hospitals, luxury hotels, retail stores, schools, power facilities, libraries, and more. Vocera is headquartered in San Jose, California, with offices in Indiana, Orlando, Canada, India, United Arab Emirates, Australia, and the United Kingdom. Learn more at www.vocera.com and follow @VoceraComm and @VoceraEase on Twitter.

Position Description : Vocera is seeking a talented and innovative Senior Engineer to join our core platform team. In this position, you will help build distributed, cloud ready services that go into our flagship Clinical Collaboration and Communication platform. Your work will directly be used by care givers and hospital staff in coordinating patient care.

Responsibilities

  • Design and develop highly scalable, distributed, multi-tenant, low latency microservices and applications for mission critical deployment
  • Contribute in all phases of lifecycle development including analysis, design, coding, testing and implementation, writing well designed, testable and efficient code
  • Research customer issues, determine appropriate resolutions, and report findings back to the team
  • Support continuous improvement by investigating alternative technologies and presenting them for their architectural tradeoffs and review
  • Mentor software engineers, specifically for design and deployment of distributed services

Competencies

  • Excellent understanding of distributed, multi-tenant architectures deployed at cloud scale with high availability
  • Seasoned Agile development practitioner
  • Java programming language, Object oriented analysis, and design patterns
  • Desire to make a difference, Self-Starter dedicated to Personal and Team excellence
  • Resourceful and able to solve complex problems through practical use of technology
  • Excellent analytical, verbal, and written communication skills with the ability to work within a dynamic multi-functional diverse team
  • Positive attitude and work ethic. Works well with cross functional teams

Experience

  • Bachelor’s or master’s degree in Computer Science or related field or equivalent
  • 8+ years of software development experience including assignments with increasing responsibility
  • Expert in Java stack on Linux
  • Experience with Glassfish/OSGI a plus
  • Experience with Multi-active / Multi-master databases. PostgresQL a plus
  • Hands on knowledge of deploying Kubernestes. Linux administration knowledge a plus
  • Built and successfully deployed multiple Enterprise scale microservices based SaaS offerings (At least one successful product redesign involving breaking monolith into distributed microservices)
  • Ability to analyze, and resolve technical issues in production. Experienced in dealing with customer escalations
  • No travel required
