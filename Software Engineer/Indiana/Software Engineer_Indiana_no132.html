We are looking for a talented Embedded Software Engineer to join our team specializing in Electronics for our Components Business Segment. This role is passed in Columbus, IN and will be remote three days a week.

In this role, you will make an impact in the following ways:

  • Embedded Software Design and Construction - Constructing embedded software using industry best practices and robust software engineering principles; verifies software updates to ensure quality products.
  • Product Verification and Validation Management - Coordinating the development of design verification and system validation plans from a variety of inputs to identify failure modes while managing risk and relative priority
  • Evaluating analytical and experimental test results following engineering principles to verify that products meet internal technical requirements and to validate that design solutions meet customer needs; produces recommendations with technical documentation to support product decisions.
  • System Requirements Engineering - Using appropriate methods and tools to translate stakeholder needs into verifiable requirements to which designs are developed
  • Product Platform Planning and Architecting - Creating and developing a holistic product platform including key architectural feature and function variants needed to align with market level product plans of future target markets
  • Generating alternative architectures traceable to the requirements and performs trade-off analysis in collaboration with life-cycle stakeholders to ensure a balanced and optimum platform

Qualifications

To be successful in this role you will need the following:

  • College, university, or equivalent to Bachelors’ Degree and 3 to 5 years minimum of relevant experience. Experience developing SPI interface and working with SPI devices that drive/diagnose I/O and flash memory, also skills with some type of scripting language like Perl, or Python
  • Coding experience in languages such as C/C++, and experience with Generic Timer Module or enhanced Time Processor Unit micro coding
  • Experience with AUTOSAR Architecture and Tools supporting Architecture, and strong debugging experience using tools like Lauterbach and trace 32
  • Experience implementing Low level driver and diagnostic routines for the following I/Os such as PWM, Analogs, Hbridge, etc. Also, being skilled with Microprocessors such as Infineon, NXP or Arm Cores, and developing Flash driver to program memory
