Software Engineer

High Alpha
Indiana
Remote

High Alpha is hiring engineering team members for an exciting new B2B SaaS startup. This position will be with one of our latest companies, currently in stealth mode, which will leverage social media platforms, big data, and know-how to help small businesses compete effectively in the modern age of corporations.

As a Software Engineer, you will have the unique opportunity to be a creator, builder, and innovator as you help design and build our one of a kind software as a service platform and its tailored user experience. This business is obsessed with learning from customers and passionate about identifying and solving their problems. You will interact with our customers and learn from them, and then you will translate your findings into world-class products.

As one of our very first employees and will report to the director of engineering. In this role you will progress your career to its next level and show your creativity, entrepreneurial spirit, and ability to execute, while you will do the best work of your career in the service of independent businesses across the country!

What You'll Do:

  • Be customer obsessed, build applications focused on their needs - Establish and adhere to software engineering best practices and user-centric design principles.
  • Create applications that integrate seamlessly with multiple social media platforms and are performant across multiple user environments (Android, iOS, Web).
  • Work with cloud based platforms and tooling such as serverless functions and NoSQL datastores.
  • Be quality minded to ensure consistency, reliability, security, and performance.
  • Participate in regular code reviews and architecture discussions.
  • Advance your skills and career while having fun, learning, and working hard.
  • Thrive in an innovative and rapidly growing environment.

Qualifications

REQUIRED

  • Experience:
    • 2-3+ years of software development experience in both frontend and backend JavaScript (React/Node.js)
    • 2-3+ years of experience in CSS/SASS
  • Self-direction:
    • Work efficiently and effectively in a remote-first environment.
  • Mindset:
    • Must have a growth mindset, a love of learning, and a positive / can-do attitude.
    • Must have an obsessive attention to detail and relentless pursuit of excellence.
    • Must be able to move quickly, multi-task, and adapt to our rapidly changing and growing market with varying customer needs.

NICE TO HAVE

  • Expertise with REST API design.
  • Experience integrating with 3rd party APIs, specifically those of social media platforms.
  • Familiarity with common public cloud infrastructure (AWS, Azure, GCP).
  • A love of culinary arts!
