Job Title:

Software Engineer, Sr

Job Description

Concentrix is seeking a qualified DBA to support a variety of SQL Instances and database for multiple systems and functions related to the insurance industry. They must be able to work effectively with technical, QA and Business teams and possess exceptional communication skills. They must also be able to provide consulting expert assistance to development members of the team. They must be able to think creatively and provide database solutions. The candidate will be service oriented: responding immediately to high priority problems and outages. They will be responsible for installation, configuration of backup and restore, tuning, implement and upgrading SQL Server instances and databases at all levels of test and production. The DBA will aware of division standards and processes: they will often enforce policy, assist data security to coordinate DB access and provide guidance and information to auditors.
