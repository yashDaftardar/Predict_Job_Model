This role will be a part of UnitedHealthOne® - a brand representing UnitedHealthcare branded individual insurance products supported by over 75 years of personal insurance experience. The Lead Software Engineer will help the UHOne team pursue the UnitedHealthcare mission to help people live healthier lives.

You’ll enjoy the flexibility to telecommute* from anywhere within the U.S. as you take on some tough challenges.

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Work collaboratively with others to define a strategy and roadmap that encompasses multiple organizations, documenting the strategy and requirements for unified portals and how we would bridge from today to an experience integrating the Broker, Member (E.g. Healthx & Facets), Next Gen User experience and miscellaneous other portals
  • Identify and prioritize the customer self-service transactions and other informational capabilities that would reduce the volume of calls coming into the service center while improving the customer experience. Examples include billing and payment, address changes, adding a dependent and family status changes
  • Support the Technical Product Owners to define and deliver other capabilities such as product recommendations, cross sell and upsell, re-enrollment and other miscellaneous self-service transactions
  • Collaborate with Capability Management and the Engineering teams to deliver the desired capabilities

You’ll be rewarded and recognized for your performance in an environment that will challenge you and give you clear direction on what it takes to succeed in your role as well as provide development for other roles you may be interested in.

Required Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in computer science, business or other relevant degree; or equivalent professional experience
  • 5+ years of experience in and/or working closely with Software engineering, defining and delivering end user capabilities
  • Experience collaborating with a broad array of individuals and teams. Examples include technical & non-technical executive teams, Engineering leadership, and architecture and delivery teams
  • Experience with Scrum / Agile development methodologies
  • Experience defining or designing a modern customer experience, that may be one of several ways a customer may interact with our business
  • If you need to enter a work site for any reason, you will be required to screen for symptoms using the ProtectWell mobile app, Interactive Voice Response (i.e., entering your symptoms via phone system) or a similar UnitedHealth Group-approved symptom screener. Employees must comply with any state and local masking orders. In addition, when in a UnitedHealth Group building, employees are expected to wear a mask in areas where physical distancing cannot be attained

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Knowledge of current accessibility requirements and standards
  • Knowledge of some of the newer technologies that will likely be introduced into the UHO ecosystem Examples include applications such as Adobe Target, Adobe Experience Manager and Salesforce
  • Knowledge of some of the E&I applications that may be introduced into the UHO ecosystem such as ACET
  • Understanding of Identity and Access Management concepts
