Full Job Description

VIAVI (NASDAQ: VIAV) has a 90+ year history of technical innovations that have evolved to keep pace and address our customer’s most pressing business issues. We make equipment, software, and systems that help to plan, deploy, certify, monitor, and optimize all kinds of networks - like those for mobile phones, service providers, large businesses and data centers. VIAVI is also a leader in high performance thin film optical coatings and engineered diffusers, providing light management solutions to anti-counterfeiting, consumer electronics, automotive, defense and instrumentation markets.

We are the people behind the products that help keep the world connected – at home, school, work, at play, and everywhere in between. VIAVI employees are fierce about supporting customer success and we welcome people who bring their best every day to the company - to question, to collaborate and to push for solutions that will delight our customers.

VIAVI Solutions is looking for a Senior Embedded Software Development engineer interested in mobile and embedded software development for our Indianapolis, Indiana site. The position includes responsibilities spanning the entire software development lifecycle - architecture, design, implementation, and test work. Our Indianapolis location focuses on enabling video and broadband services for cable networks.

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Contribute to all phases of SDLC
  • Participate in current and new development initiatives
  • Priority management
  • Adherence to standards and defined processes
  • Management of scope
Qualifications


QUALIFICATIONS AND EXPERIENCE:

  • BSEE/BSCS/BSCE
  • High level language programming experience - C,C++/Java (4 - 10+ years)
  • Experience in architecting and designing solutions using OO methodologies (4-6+ years)
  • Embedded OS experience – Linux, iOS
  • Experienced in Waterfall and Agile methodologies (5+ years)

JOB QUALIFICATIONS (desired):

  • Advanced engineering degree (MSEE/MSCS/MSCE/PhD)
  • Qt/Angular development experience
  • Python development experience
  • Mobile application development on iOS/Android.
  • Database experience – SQL, SQLite, Oracle
  • RF/DOCSIS knowledge
  • Experienced with networking – 802.11, Bluetooth, TCP/IP
  • Experienced in markup languages – XML, QML, JSON
  • Knowledge of electrical engineering concepts

ATTRIBUTES:

  • The candidate must be a self-starter with strong analytical, problem solving skills
  • The candidate must possess strong written and oral communication skills
  • The candidate must be proactive, possess the ability to work independently, within teams and in an office environment
  • May require up to 10% US travel
