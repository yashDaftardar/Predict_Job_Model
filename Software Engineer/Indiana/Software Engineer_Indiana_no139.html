As an Engineering Team Lead for the backend development team, you will utilize both leadership skills and technical expertise to enable software engineers to deliver great work. An Engineering Team Lead will be hands-on, spending a portion of time on individual contributions, while spending the remaining time helping to grow and support other team members.
Day-to-day:
  • Design, develop and implement scalable, RESTful, multi-tenant services using Node.js
  • Configuring logging, alerting, and other observability systems to monitor and troubleshoot production systems
  • Perform code reviews, suggesting improvements and ensuring adherence to best practices
  • Lead a team of software engineers by example, while supporting your team member’s professional growth through mentorship
  • Help recruit, onboard, and retain hard-working engineers
  • Set and ensure a high bar for quality; ensure the team’s output is well-structured, unit tested, and scalable
  • Keep your team up-to-date on meaningful engineering and business decisions
  • Build and evolve processes that are right for your team
  • Maintain the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of Sharpen information assets.
  • Other duties as assigned
Requirements:
  • BS degree in Computer Science, or equivalent3+ years of experience doing backend development and at least 2+ years in-depth experience with Node.js
  • 1+ years of experience in leadership within a development team
  • Advanced JavaScript experience: Express.js, Socket.io, Knex.js, Apollo Server
  • Experience with establishing standards, setting and communicating timelines, and guiding technical decisions
  • Experience with best practices around microservices and distributed multi-tenant systems
  • Experience with relational database design (preferably PostgreSQL or MySQL)Experience with event streaming platforms: Apache Kafka
  • Experience with unit testing: Mocha, Chai, Sinon, test-driven development
  • Nice to have: Experience with Kafka
  • Nice to have: Experience with AWS: S3, ElasticSearch, Aurora, DynamoDB
  • Nice to have: Experience with build pipelines: Docker, Kubernetes, Bash
