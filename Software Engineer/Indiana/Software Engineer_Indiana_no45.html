Full Job Description

We are: fast-growing, intellectually curious, entrepreneurial, flexible, hard-working yet fun. About US

Chicago-based Azul Partners is a leading procurement technology, supply chain and commodities-focused intelligence, advisory and media company. Azul has 1.5M+ annual visitors across the portfolio and a rich book of business.

Deep analysis, market intelligence and benchmarking lie at the core of Azul’s success in addition to its editorial roots. Procurement practitioners, manufacturers, consultants, technology vendors and investors alike rely on Azul for in-depth procurement technology benchmarking and accurate metals buying strategies.

About the Opportunity

As an IT/Application Development intern at Azul Partners, you’ll have an opportunity to enhance your business acumen during a min. 6-week project-based work experience working with the development and data analyst team.

You’ll support several product/application development projects, enhancing the internal team with your front-end development skills. You’ll gain exposure to working within a fast-paced, international entrepreneurial environment, requiring strong interpersonal, communication, development, analytical and project management skills.

Azul Partners is a relatively small company with 25+ FTEs globally. The Azul team values adaptability, speed, initiative, collaboration and diversity.

Internship Requirements

The ideal candidate is an undergraduate student with an outstanding record of academic achievement and an interest in front-end development.

You should have good knowledge of CSS, be able to work on multiple assignments, work well on a team, employ excellent organizational and time management skills, and display excellent communication skills.

Qualifications

  • Technical degree in Graphic Design, CS, Engineering, etc. (recently obtained or near completion)
  • Good knowledge of CSS, Microsoft Office/Google drive products
  • Ability to work in a dynamic, collaborative environment
  • Self-starter, multi-tasker with entrepreneurial drive
  • Ability to meet tight deadlines and work independently
  • Impeccable organizational, writing and communication skills

This is a paid internship.

Job location: Virtual (collaborating with team members in North America and Europe)
