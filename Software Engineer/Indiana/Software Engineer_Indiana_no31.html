Full Job Description

Our specialty is creating easy-to-use HR technology to be used and valued by all employees. We have been doing this for over 20 years. This relentless pursuit has led us to work with some of the most innovative and recognized brands, while at the same time creating Vibe HCM - a highly acclaimed HRIS, Talent and Employee Collaboration suite that drives transformative change.

Our Team is comprised of energetic, bright, self-motivated folks that work hard and play hard. We provide a range of formal compensation and informal rewards, socialization and volunteerism opportunities. We’d love to have you as part of our Team!


Summary. The Software Engineer will be assigned to one of the groups within R&D: Product Development or Product Support.The Software Engineer will conduct limited analysis and assist on designing and developing applications or components that automate specific business processes based on reviewed and approved specifications.


Location. This role will be based in either in Saskatoon, SK, CAN or Elkhart, IN, USA


Essential Duties and Responsibilities

  • Assist in translating functional requirements and developing designs of applications, components, and modules in partnership with senior team members
  • Proactively participate in a team environment utilizing scrum methodologies
  • Assist in the design and development of user interfaces, business-layer, and back-end components in coordination with senior team members and team guidelines
  • Collaborate in an active and engaged way with team members on analyzing, developing, testing, and delivering high quality code components
  • Assist in developing web and mobile applications and components; Perform thorough debugging and unit testing
  • Participate in team and individual code reviews to ensure and improve quality of deliverables
  • Follow all coding, process, and conduct standards of the organization
  • Participate in other assigned projects

What we are looking for

  • Bachelor’s degree in IT, Information Systems, Computer Science or related fields from an accredited college or university
  • One or more years of related software development experience strongly desired, SaaS environment is a plus
  • One or more years of web-based software development utilizing the Microsoft development toolset a plus
  • Experience with: C#, ASP.NET MVC 4+ or other MVC web frameworks
  • Experience with: HTML5, JavaScript, CSS
  • Experience in SQL and a strong understanding of relational database systems
  • Experience with a JavaScript MVVM framework, such as Knockout.js, Backbone.js, or Angular a plus
  • Understanding of OOAD and OOP a plus
  • Experience with ASP.NET Web API or other web service technologies a plus

Competencies for Success

  • Communication: Effective and concise written communication target to specific audiences; Strong verbal communication and listening skills to assure understanding
  • Analytical Thinking: Approaches problems logically; Provides meaningful analyses through verbal and written presentations
  • Organizational Skills and Concern for Order: Keeps accurate records; Provides meaningful data for review
  • Initiative: Engaged and proactive; Takes appropriate action with minimal oversight
  • Agility and Time Management: Able to respond to changing priorities, demands, procedures and technology; Excellent organizational and time management skills to meet client needs

Work Environment and Physical Demands
The work environment and physical demands are representative of those an employee encounters while performing the essential functions of this job. The noise level in the work environment is usually quiet to moderate in an office setting.

The employee is frequently required to be in a stationary position and regularly use the computer keyboard and mouse and will view the computer monitor frequently. The employee is occasionally required to stand, walk and reach with hands. The employee is occasionally required to climb or balance, stoop, kneel or crouch. The employee may occasionally lift and/or move up to 20 pounds.


Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals to perform the essential job functions.
