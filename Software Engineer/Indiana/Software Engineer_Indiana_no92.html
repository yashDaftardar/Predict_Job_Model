Full Job Description

Secure our Nation, Ignite your Future

Become an integral part of a diverse team while working at an Industry Leading Organization, where our employees come first. At ManTech International Corporation, you’ll help protect our national security while working on innovative projects that offer opportunities for advancement.

Currently, ManTech is seeking a motivated, career and customer-oriented Entry Level Computer Engineer to join our team in Crane, IN.

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

· Research, design, develop, and test computer hardware and software programs. Hardware refers to computer chips, circuit boards, computer systems, and related equipment such as keyboards, modems, and printers.

· Develop the software systems that control computers.

· Ability to exclusively work with computers and computer-related equipment. In addition to design and development duties, computer hardware engineers supervise the manufacturing and installation of computers and computer-related equipment.

· Develop new computer software systems and to incorporate new technologies in a rapidly growing range of applications.

· Apply the principles and techniques of computer science, engineering, and mathematical analysis to the design, development, testing, and evaluation of the software and systems that enable computers to perform their many applications.

· Analyze users’ needs and design, construct, test, and maintain computer applications software or systems.

· Design and development of many types of software, including software for operating systems and network distribution, and compilers, which convert programs for execution on a computer.

· Ability to solve technical problems that arise.

· Must possess strong programming skills but are more concerned with developing algorithms and analyzing and solving programming problems than with actually writing code. The programming languages most often used are C, C++, and Java.

· Provides rudimentary software development support to a project in accordance with documented processes.

· Conducts and documents software requirements analysis and bidirectional traceability.

· Designs, implements, integrates, and tests software and firmware subsystems to meet software and system requirements.

· Engages in active risk management.

Position Requirement:

Required Bachelor's level degree in Computer, Electrical or Electronics Engineering or Computer Science or Mathematics with field of concentration in computer science. With Bachelor’s level degree 0-2 years of professional experience in computer design, software development or computer networks.

Security Requirements:

Candidate must have ability to gain a Secret Clearance.

Physical Requirements:

Constantly operates a computer and other office productivity machinery, such as a calculator, copy machine and computer printer.

Must be able to remain in a stationary position 50%
