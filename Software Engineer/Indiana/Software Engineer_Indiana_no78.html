Position Summary:
If you are an analytical problem solver with a passion for Node.JS development and innovation, this may be the perfect job for you! The Software Engineer II will work within an Agile team that develops and maintains modern, enterprise-level microservices. The candidate will be responsible for estimating assigned work, designing scalable solutions to complex problems, and developing and supporting an expanding cloud-based microservice platform.

Job Responsibilities:
  • Work within a team to design, develop, and implement scalable, RESTful, multi-tenant microservices.
  • Develop with security, user experience, performance, accessibility, and business objectives in mind.
  • Implement unit, integration, and end-to-end tests to ensure product quality.
  • Collaborate with developers across teams to integrate the client-facing application with the backend services.
  • Participate in agile software development, prototyping, and code reviews with a small team of developers.
  • Collaborate with team on key improvements to and design of applications, processes, and infrastructure.
  • Other duties and responsibilities as assigned
  • Maintain the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of Sharpen information assets
Education and Experience:
  • BA/BS degree in Computer Science, or equivalent practical experience
  • At least one year of hands-on experience with the Node.JS framework and database design (preferably PostgreSQL or MySQL)
  • Knowledge of Express.JS a plus.
  • Experience designing, implementing, and using RESTful web services is a plus.
  • Knowledge of microservice architecture a plus
  • Experience with version control principles, preferably using Git.
