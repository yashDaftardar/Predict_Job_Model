Description:

Responsible for the software design, development, testing and maintenance of software development projects for Kiwanis International

ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:

Software Development Team

  • Work on the software development team to meet sprint goals
  • Mentor and coach level I software developers
  • Participate in daily stand-up meetings and other meetings
  • Collaborate effectively with development team, scrum master and product owners
  • Adhere to scrum methodology Identify, address, and resolve issues in collaboration with the development team
  • Commitment to the vision, mission and values of the organization and the International Office

Position Responsibilities

  • Work on the software development team to develop quality software for Kiwanis International
  • Guide the level I software developers in the creation of quality software products through a variety of learning environments including assigning professional development tasks, pair programming and attending team training sessions
  • Mentor level I software developers on proper techniques for cleaning and refactoring code
  • Maintain overall consistency guidelines between all products, including adherence to Kiwanis family brand style guides and adherence to software development team best practices
  • Provide assistance to the level 3 software developers and the Kiwanis family as it relates to utilization of Kiwanis family technology products to build an active and engaged user base
  • Maintain integrity of software codebase so that the Kiwanis family of software products architecture remains secure, extensible and scalable by code reviews and approving pull requests from level 1 developers
  • Propose and develop innovative software applications that delight and empower Kiwanis members and staff
  • Maintain and increase knowledge of software development best practices
  • Identify, clean and refactor existing code
  • Demonstration of and continual progress in professional software development activities including attending seminars, attaining certifications, attaining higher-level degrees, etc.
  • Participate in daily stand-up meetings and report project and task status updates
  • Research, propose and implement ways to improve existing or new software applications

OTHER FUNCTIONS:

  • All other duties as assigned
  • Participate in development meetings as required
  • Assigned professional development tasks
. Requirements:

Educational Requirements:

  • Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science or closely-related degree
  • Salesforce Admin I certified
  • Salesforce Platform App Builder certified
  • Continued professional development and/or academic education in software development

Experiential Requirements:

  • Four years academic and at least 1 year of professional software development experience
  • A minimum of two years problem-solving software application bugs and performance issues
  • Minimum of 1 year professional developer experience with Salesforce CRM
  • Familiarity with cloud platform and services architecture, major programming and scripting languages, and experience in implementing and supporting multi-lingual, multi-site, multi-user- based applications (experience with membership-based users a plus)
  • At least 1 year of strong and demonstrated skills in writing secure software applications
  • Minimum 1 year of experience with Extreme Programming (XP) techniques
  • Minimum 1 year of experience with Test-driven development (TDD)
  • Minimum 1 year of experience working in an Agile/Scrum methodology/framework
  • Minimum 1 year of experience using Jira, Confluence and BitBucket
  • Minimum 1 year of experience using at least 3 AWS products and services
  • Ability to stay hyper-focused on one task and take direction and coaching from mentors in a collaborative working environment
  • Minimum 1 year of experience using object-oriented software design and implementation
  • Strong problem-solving skills and methodology
  • Strong desire to innovate, learn, adapt, grow in the role and ensure the development of mentees
  • Servant leader qualities with excellent organizational abilities
  • Ability to effectively organically lead the team when needed

Required Skills:

  • Excellent communication skills
  • Excellent problem-solving skills
  • Comfortable mentoring junior software developers
  • Passion to learn and implement new and emerging technologies
  • Ability to design, test and debug object-oriented software
  • Comfortable with refactoring and test-driven development
  • Working knowledge of design patterns and their use cases
  • Knowledge of Extreme Programming (XP) methods
  • Familiar with NoSQL data stores
  • Some familiarity with Sitefinity CMS development
  • Familiar with WordPress CMS development
  • Competent in writing, testing and debugging programming and scripting languages
  • Fluent in developing software using Apex/Salesforce, C#/.NET and PHP/WordPress development stacks
  • Fluent in source control operations including merge conflict resolution, pulling, pushing, stashing, branching, merging, committing, etc.
  • Must be fluent in SQL
