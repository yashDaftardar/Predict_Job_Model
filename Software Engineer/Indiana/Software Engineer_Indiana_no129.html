You Aspire To:

Join a team where you get to work closely and directly with clients and the Security Operations Center (SOC), Incident Response, and engineering teams to ensure that the best detection mechanisms are in place for ever changing threat landscapes.

Seek to improve on current technical skills to enhance the overall detection capabilities, continuously improve detection and alerting to enhance the existing detection in place, while driving efforts to close gaps.

Thrive in environments that promote and empower you to create, enhance and tune alerts and detections so that the Security Operations Center (SOC) can proactively hunt client networks.

About the Role:

As a Security Detection Engineer at Pondurance you will serve as a support system for our clients and the Security Operations Center (SOC). Your goal will be to continually improve the detection program and alerting that the SOC receives, as well as tune logs and data being sent by clients. You will leverage our tech stack and provide ongoing support both internally and externally.

Responsibilities:


  • Drive security detection improvements
  • Tune existing alerts
  • Work with clients to ensure the best data is being sent to the SOC
  • Lead continuous improvement efforts to evaluate detection capabilities
  • Develop methods to detect potential threats
  • Work with the SOC and Incident Response teams to enhance detection capabilities based on indicators detected in an IR engagement

Education and Experience

The Security Detection Engineer position requires a Bachelor’s degree or 1 to 3 years of experience in a related field.

  • Experience tuning alerts
  • Strong understanding of pertinent logs that can aid in detecting threats

  • A strong understanding of types of cyber-attack and MITRE Att&ck framework

  • Experience with SIEM and IDS/IPS systems
  • A strong understanding of emerging threats and ability to demonstrate knowledge of security research

  • Possess the desire to grow both technically and professionally in the information
