Position Title:

Application Development Technician


Position Summary:

This candidate will work closely and partner with ADE’s. This position will be doing mostly lab type work and testing for projects that are assigned. These projects will be helping ADE’s, Sales and Marketing to provide technical data to end-users for silicone elastomer applications including Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR) and High Consistency Custom Compounds. He/she will support customers in data reporting along with sampling lab scale materials. Along with any data that is needed for existing silicone elastomer materials or quality claim validations.


Job Description:

  • Partnership with ADE’s to support with Lab work.
  • Test and assess existing materials to verify quality claims, and work with appropriate teams to help with solving.
  • Lab scale mixing, milling and extruding of silicone compounds for proof of concept.
  • Good Communication skills with local(site)teams and manager.
  • Data research and reporting out to ADE’s and sometimes customers.
  • Help with translating work for customers requirements into material specifications to support team for new product development.
  • Provide lab support to ADE’s to help with closing and completing projects for growth.
  • Bring to closure assigned projects and communicate clearly the findings using appropriate tools. (Excel, Power Point, Word docs, Mini tab, SAP)
  • Compliance to EHS policies and standards.


Additional Details:

  • General marketplace knowledge for elastomers
  • Desire to learn technical capabilities and understanding of silicone business.
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills.
  • Strong interpersonal competencies and good team spirit.
  • Technical/bachelor’s degree preferred or relevant business experience.
  • Experience with High Consistency Elastomers and Liquid Silicone materials
  • Experience with Elastomers products and applications.
  • SAP program knowledge
