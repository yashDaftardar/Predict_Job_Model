This opportunity resides within STANLEY Security, a global division of Stanley Black & Decker. At STANLEY Security we make the world progress by focusing on electronic security, access technologies, products and healthcare solutions.

About Our Internship Program
We are currently seeking motivated Software Engineering students for the Summer 2021 Internship Program located at our Security world headquarters in Fishers, IN. Selected students will participate in a meaningful and challenging 10-week summer internship that will allow each intern to have exposure to different facets of the Security business and receive critical business skills and career and personal development training. Interns will work on a wide variety of tasks and projects within a cross functional team of professionals and get exposure to leaders within the business.

In these challenging roles…
In these various positions you will have exposure to several areas of software engineering at Stanley Black & Decker and will work on a wide variety of tasks and projects with a cross functional team of professionals.

With great rewards…

  • A utility role interacting with key members of a fortune 500 company
  • A flexible schedule featuring a max of 40 hours per week
  • Opportunity for career advancement upon graduation
  • Competitive Compensation
