Full Job Description

VIAVI (NASDAQ: VIAV) has a 90+ year history of technical innovations that have evolved to keep pace and address our customer’s most pressing business issues. We make equipment, software, and systems that help to plan, deploy, certify, monitor, and optimize all kinds of networks - like those for mobile phones, service providers, large businesses and data centers. VIAVI is also a leader in high performance thin film optical coatings and engineered diffusers, providing light management solutions to anti-counterfeiting, consumer electronics, automotive, defense and instrumentation markets.

We are the people behind the products that help keep the world connected – at home, school, work, at play, and everywhere in between. VIAVI employees are fierce about supporting customer success and we welcome people who bring their best every day to the company - to question, to collaborate and to push for solutions that will delight our customers.

We are looking for a Mobile Software Development Engineer interested in mobile and embedded software development for our Indianapolis, Indiana site. The position includes responsibilities spanning the entire software development lifecycle - architecture, design, implementation, and test work. Our Indianapolis location focuses on enabling video and broadband services for cable networks.

Job Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain a cross-platform hybrid application that interfaces with custom equipment over Bluetooth, BLE, and IP networks.
  • Define and design new functionality and improve overall application performance and usability
  • Create unit tests to exercise general functionality, usability, and edge cases
  • Work with open-source libraries
  • Collaborate and work across multiple teams
  • Coordinate testing efforts across multiple teams, diagnose and fix discovered bugs
Qualifications


Required skills

  • BS in Computer Science or other relevant technical field and 3-5 years experience
  • 5-8 years experience required if no degree
  • Software development experience in Android app development
  • Software development experience in Apple iOS app development
  • Software development experience with Javascript and Angular
  • Experience using third-party Javascript and Angular libraries, especially rxjs
  • Experience developing Javascript and Angular applications that target mobile devices
  • General knowledge of mobile architectures, platform trends, and development tools
  • Experience with the full software development lifecycle
  • Experience with Javascript and Angular unit testing
  • Experience defining and developing REST APIs
  • Solid time management skills and the ability to work in a distributed/remote environment
  • Desire to work in a highly collaborative environment

Recommended/Preferred skills

  • Experience developing Android applications that use Bluetooth connectivity
  • Experience developing Apple iOS apps that use Bluetooth connectivity
  • Experience with Cordova and third-party Cordova plugins
  • Experience with Ionic and/or Capacitor
