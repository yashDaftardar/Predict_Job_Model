Job Title: Principal Test Software Engineer
Job ID: 179985
Relocation Eligible: Yes
Job Location: Fort Wayne, IN

We have a career growth opportunity for a Principal Test Software Engineer to join our diverse Test Automation team in supporting a wide range of Electronic Warfare and Communications and Airspace System products and customers.

Test Automation & Factory Engineering (TAFE) is responsible for the design and development of test equipment hardware and software to support a range of products and customers. The Test Automation Department (TAD) within TAFE is a growing and highly motivated team, providing quality on-time creative and economical test automation solutions.

Job Summary:
Primary responsibilities of the Principal Test Software Engineer will be to develop and maintain test software for testing environments (Unit/Acceptance, Environmental Stress Screening, Device Verification Testing, etc.) which exercises and analyzes deliverable product for our customers.

Selected candidate will be expected to follow established department procedures and instructions while working closely with program/functional supervision to ensure the overall test software requirements and design objectives are met. Candidate must be able to support cradle to grave efforts including proposal generation, schedule and cost development, requirements, design, integration, validation, verification, and maintenance for all test software activities. Candidate will lead small team of test software developers through develop/maintenance activities and communicate those activities to program and functional management.

Security Clearance : The ability to obtain and maintain a US security clearance. U.S. citizenship is required as only U.S. citizens are eligible for a security clearance

Required Skills:
  • Minimum of 8 years test software development with emphasis in automated test systems
  • Experience with LabWindows/CVI, TestStand , C++, C#, C, LabVIEW or other programming languages for test software development and maintenance
  • Experience with interfacing test software with laboratory and production tests instrumentation (oscilloscopes, digital multi-meter, direct current power supplies etc.)
  • Experience with the development and integration of test software drivers for the operation of computer hardware interfaces
  • Experience in common computer hardware interface and data protocols such as TCP/IP, UDP, Serial Communication (Microsoft Windows environment preferred)
  • Experience with different types of manufacturing and testing events (Environment Stress Screening (ESS) (Thermal and Vibe), Unit and Acceptance Testing (UT/AT), or Device Verification Test (DVT))
  • Experience planning, conducting, coordinating and managing technical test software development activities (requirements, design, integration, validation, and maintenance)
  • Experience prioritizing and handling multiple tasks for a production environment
  • Experience translating test software requirements into cost effective and creative solutions for a production environment
  • Experience with software Configuration Management environments (Subversion, Git, etc.)
  • Experience leading small team of test software developers through develop/maintenance activities
  • Experience communicating to program/functional management test software activities
  • Experience with test software proposals, schedule, and cost activities
Desired Skills:
  • Knowledge of Agile utilization/environment
  • Knowledge in cost account management (CAM)
  • Strong written and interpersonal communication skills
  • Ability to close on actions, drive change and empower team members to think outside the box
  • Current and Active DoD Secret clearance or higher clearance
