About Syngenta
Syngenta Digital is changing the agriculture industry and we want you to be part of that. Digital innovations, data, and new technologies will transform the way that crops are managed in the future and enable farmers and agronomists to enhance efficiency and sustainable food production. You will help to develop solutions that turns data into meaningful information and ultimately helps to grow more food with fewer resources.
We value a culture that promotes work/life balance, celebrates diversity, and offers numerous family-oriented events throughout the year as well as a full Benefit Package that starts the same day you do.
About the Role
As a Backend Developer you will help contribute to the design, development, and delivery of the backend systems and services used across our entire technology portfolio as we work to help transform digital agriculture. You will work as part of our Digital Product team at our office inside the Loop while also getting the opportunity to connect and collaborate with the rest of our global teams.

Qualifications

  • Experience with data pipelines and working with large data sets
  • Good understanding of Python or similar language
Bonus points but not required
  • Experience working with AWS technologies
  • Experience with GIS data
