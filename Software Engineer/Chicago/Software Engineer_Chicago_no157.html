Founded by The Allstate Corporation in 2016, Arity is a data and analytics company focused on improving transportation. We collect and analyze enormous amounts of data, using predictive analytics to build solutions with a single goal in mind: to make transportation smarter, safer and more useful for everyone.

At the heart of that mission are the people that work here—the dreamers, doers and difference-makers that call this place home. As part of that team, your work will showcase both your intelligence and your creativity as you tackle real problems and put your talents towards transforming transportation.

That’s because at Arity, we believe work and life shouldn’t be at odds with one another. After all, we know that your unique qualities give you a unique perspective. We don’t just want you to see yourself here. We want you to be yourself here.


The Team

Our engineers are fueled by a passion to impact the future of mobility. They push the boundaries of telematics and transportation tech by creating and supporting cutting-edge products. The Role

At Arity, we are utilizing cutting edge technologies (AWS, Cassandra, NiFi, Flink, Kafka, etc.) to create and deliver amazing capabilities and products. We are searching for a cloud engineer who can help design, implement, and deploy our next generation analytics platform and to help us utilize our platform to deliver innovative products and solutions.
Responsibilities

  • Engineer highly available and scalable systems on AWS utilizing Arity’s next generation analytics platform.
  • Passionate about technology, software, and the Internet of Things.
  • Drive solutions and best practices by mentoring fellow architects and engineers.
  • Lead the hands-on technical delivery of new services and capabilities within our analytics platform.
  • Hands-on experience with cloud solutions at massive scale and resiliency

Qualifications

  • BA/BS degree or equivalent experience; Computer Science or Math background preferred
  • 3-5 years of software engineering and architect experience. 8-12 years preferred
  • AWS Certification is i preferred; AWS Solutions Architect, AWS SysOps Administrator, AWS Developer.
  • Deep experience with Infrastructure as Code tools like Terraform and Cloud Formation and configuration management tools like Ansible, Chef, Puppet, or Bosh.
  • Experience with infrastructure programming languages like Phython or Go.
  • Solid understanding of security and compliance frameworks within a public cloud framework.
  • Experience with infrastructure concepts such as network, compute, and storage.
  • Familiar with continuous deployment methodology and common DevOps tools like Git and Jenkins.
  • Deep understanding of cloud architectures and operations, security, automation and orchestration
  • Experience deploying applications on AWS utilizing SQL and NoSQL data layer technologies.
  • Experience migrating applications from traditional data center infrastructures to hybrid or fully-cloud based environments.
  • Experience with Data Lake and Data Streaming tools like Cassandra, Kafka, Flink, NiFi, and Hadoop.
  • Familiar with high-availability (HA) and failover implementations for network infrastructure and server systems
  • A proven track record of managing and delivering large-scale projects.
  • Strong written and oral communication skills
  • Able to work independently or within a team
  • Organized, responsive and highly thorough problem solver
