Full Job Description

Join GN Hearing Corp. in Glenview, IL and help create products that make a real difference by making life sound better to hearing-impaired people around the world.

GN Hearing Care Corp is currently seeking a college Intern or part-time Developer to drive the content management and web page configuration of our internal SharePoint web site. This is a great opportunity for an IT student to gain hands-on experience in an R&D department and can be 100% remote.

The role will require a combination of technical, design and communication skills as you work with colleagues in various roles and departments to create and configure web pages.

This position will work closely with the Head of Software Development, the Business Intelligence Intern will create reporting dashboards to track and monitor data from our work management system. This position will require a high degree of attention to detail along with the ability to understand complex data models. Troubleshooting and problem solving, as well as working closely with managers and software developers to solve these problems, will also be needed. This position requires good communication and organization skills, along with a working knowledge of day-to-day operations and priorities within their department and the company. Experience with Microsoft Excel preferred; skills in SQL, Tableau, Microsoft Power BI, and/or R/Python are helpful but not required. Assume other duties as assigned.

PERFORMANCE EXPECTATIONS (Key Success Factors)

  • Obtain, document, and analyze SAFe ART reporting needs and requirements through collaboration with department leaders
  • Translate reporting needs into production reports and dashboards
  • Work across functional lines to strive for and ensure consistent data quality
  • Conduct continuous review of reports and identify opportunities for improvements with the aim of minimizing redundant reporting and development of single source of truth where possible
  • Develop and maintain dashboards to visualize data utilizing Microsoft Power BI
  • Develop ad-hoc reports/dashboards as necessary
  • Provide increased focus on analytics and emphasis on providing valuable insights to drive improvement opportunities
  • Perform analysis and develop recommendations for continuous improvement opportunities
  • Ability to handle various data sources, using data modeling work to develop reporting capabilities

DESIRED QUALIFICATIONS

  • Experience in design, developing and maintaining BI reports & dashboards.
  • Background in business/data modeling
  • Advance Microsoft Excel skills, VBA Programming skills would be beneficial
  • Hands-on experience in Power-BI, Tableau or Spot fire
  • The role requires intellectual curiosity and a willingness to dig into and explore data.
  • The role possesses critical thinking skills and the ability to understand and manipulate data to provide insightful observations, identify trends and communicate these effectively.
  • Understanding of SQL data bases
  • Experience with TFS preferable

This is a paid internship that can last throughout summer and possibly fall.
