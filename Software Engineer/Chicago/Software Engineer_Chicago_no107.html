The Role

This Test Engineer role is part of the Arity Testing Services organization and will support and drive achievement of progressive, fully autonomous testing solutions utilizing machine learning where appropriate across our Arity Product suite. Utilizing agile methodologies and extensive cloud technologies Arity is hard at work creating the future of continuous testing and we now need a strong, energetic and inquisitive engineer to play a key role in our Arity team. This role is ideal for someone who has a real passion for technology and are advocates of software engineering best practices and continuous improvement and enjoys keeping up to date in industry trends and best practice.

Responsibilities

  • Executes upon test strategies for various products within areas of responsibility which deliver upon our goals
  • Embraces our technical strategy for continuous delivery
  • Contribute to technology strategy and engineering roadmaps
  • Initiates and implements continuous improvements in all areas of responsibility including the assessment and implementations of Chaos suite principles for each product
  • Anticipates issues and develops preventative measures
  • Managing workloads in an Agile development environment
  • Supports the strategic technical direction for highly integrated, autonomous test solutions

Qualifications

  • 5+ years of experience in software testing with emphasis on mobile applications and services
  • Experience working with development team to monitor, test, and deploy production releases
  • Experience providing performance metrics and identifying areas for product improvement
  • Proficiency in finding, submitting, and tracking defects to resolution
  • 2+ years of Test Automation Development experience with open source and market-leading vended solutions creating functional, regression, or performance tests
  • Experience with Web Services (REST, SOAP, JSON), ETL jobs and APIs; and
  • Working knowledge of scripting languages e.g. (Perl, JavaScript, Python, JACL, Node.js)
  • Nice to have:
  • Experience with common tools such as Jenkins, Artifactory, GitHub, InelliJ, JUnit, JIRA, Maven
  • Experience with delivering using Agile methodology practices
  • Experience of cloud technologies like AWS, Azure, Pivotal Cloud Foundry
  • Knowledge of security (risks and threats, authentication, authorization, certificates, encryption)
  • Familiarity with Agile backlog management tools such as Atlassian’s JIRA / Confluence, Pivotal Tracker, TFS
  • Experience in mobile development – IOS or Android
  • Experience with Cassandra, Nifi, Hadoop, Kafka, Greenplum, Flink and other Big Data platforms and applications
