Full Job Description

We are seeking a Junior DevOps Engineer to join an in-house DevOps team to help our growing engineering team move faster and more confidently. You will report directly to the Head of DevOps and help grow a culture of effective DevOps. XSELL currently has a SaaS based application, with services built in Ruby on Rails as well as Python, utilizing Postgres and Redis on the backend. Our applications are deployed into cloud environments on AWS and Azure.

Ways You’ll Contribute

  • Help keep engineers and data scientists productive by automating access to the tools and resources needed to do their job.
  • Utilize our logging, monitoring, alerting, and event-tracing platform to catch live site issues before they even happen.
  • Work with technical leads and to meet the requirements of product deployments and client needs.
  • Support in-house teams to resolve issues involving infrastructure for the live site.
  • Participate in on-call rotation and be proactive about addressing the root cause of issues.

What You’ll Bring

It isn’t a problem if you haven’t encountered all of these in your career, but the problems you’ll tackle will benefit from the following skills:

  • You’re a self-starter – given a problem, you’ll dig to find possible solutions, and work with your team when needed
  • Experience with one or more non-shell scripting languages (Python, Ruby, Go)
  • Linux/Unix CLI experience
  • Interest in automating infrastructure in one or more cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Interest in learning about configuration management tools (Terraform, Ansible, Packer)
  • Familiarity with containers and Docker
  • Familiarity with management and maintenance of database/persistence technologies (Postgres, Redis, Elasticsearch)
  • Knowledge of Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery
