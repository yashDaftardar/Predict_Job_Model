Job Description

Come join Allstate’s Enterprise Document Generation team. Our goal is to create all outbound document communication on a single platform as a service to the many Allstate teams that need to send out communications. Additionally, we want to be a one stop shop for our partners so they will not need to invest in learning about this technology. We want to offer what we build as a black box service to all business partners within the Allstate community. As a full-stack JAVA Software Engineer you will be helping us develop re-usable components that will be used in the generation of documents through our OpenText vendor. You will be responsible for keeping our many document generation applications up and running effectively. Join our team and you’ll find a challenging and rewarding environment.

Responsibilities

  • Utilize agile stories and designs to develop accurate, defect-free code
  • Create and update technical documentations using high level technical specification documents
  • Create Technical Agile stories to facilitate Refactor or reduce technical debt
  • Create unit test plans and conduct unit testing accordingly
  • Participates in planning sessions, backlog refinement, estimation and works directly with the PO to understand business needs
  • Participates in daily product stand up meetings
  • Participates in sprint/release retrospectives
  • Collaborates and strategizes with integration partners and other product teams to understand requirements and customer needs
  • Works with Production Support to ensure appropriate operational readiness before go-live and assists as needed for complex production issues
  • Supports root cause analysis with other members of the teams to understand, resolve and help prevent the recurrence of a problems
  • Works closely with test engineers to support testing of the solution
  • Ensures that code reviews are complete to meet quality standards; reviews and confirms that security standards are being maintained and evolving to meet the growing security risks
  • Establishes and maintains continuous integration, continuous delivery, and continuous deployment pipelines and practices working with the Build Engineer
  • Provides support for product in test environments
  • Works with architecture team to help define the architectural patterns and designs being used; leads the effort to identify different technology options to meet the customer needs
  • Leads and participates in knowledge sharing, and contributes to the overall growth of the team and larger development community
  • Coordinate and work with offshore resources for application development and testing
  • Understanding integration of applications with internal and external systems
  • Other responsibilities include enhancement, performance tuning and support of existing applications

Job Qualifications

  • 5+ years technology development and implementation experience. (Preferred)
  • 5 years of full-stack Java application development experience.
  • Knowledge of Agile methodologies (especially Agile Scurm), and test-driven development
  • Undergraduate degree in Computer Science preferred
  • Excellent Java and/or JavaScript coding background including familiarity with the following tools:
    • Github
    • Appfabric
    • Jenkins Pipeline
    • Spring Framework
    • ReactJS or node.js
    • Oracle
    • Java
    • Preferred – Integration with OT Document Generation (i.e. Exstream, Liquid Office, Communication Server, Content Author, etc.)
