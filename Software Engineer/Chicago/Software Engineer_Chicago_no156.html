Job Description:

SUMMARY We are looking for an exceptionally talented development lead to guide a team of developers in the creation of a platform services strategy that will support the next generation of client facing tools. This individual works independently and with other members of the online software development team, with primary responsibility to design, develop and enhance components of the Wheels.com Internet and Extranet sites, extensively focused on delivering industry leading customer-facing capabilities. Candidates must have in-depth knowledge of software development life cycle and experience in agile development methodology. You must also have excellent people skills and be resourceful in order to manage the software development process.

In this role, your primary responsibility will be to lead a small and agile development team that’s taking on multi-faceted problems and finding creative ways to solve them using the vast assortment of available technologies. This is a “hands on” position where you are expected to make significant contributions at the architectural (strategic) and prototype (tactical) level. You will play key roles in the design, build, testing and launch of industry-changing applications considered critical to our overall company growth strategies, and will be chartered with rapid launches of innovative client facing products.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

You will be responsible for leading an "A team" of independently motivated developers as they dig in, ideate and develop solutions that bring our experience visions into technical reality. You will ensure that each independent investigation accrues value to the overall strategy. You will assist and guide the team as they work in partnership with existing UX group, product management, and other teams across Wheels. You must desire to create leading experiences, not fast-follow products. “Not invented here” must not be in your vocabulary. A background of working in small team/large impact environments is critical.

This role is a demanding, highly dynamic and technically challenging position. A candidate must be experienced in all stages of the software development life cycle Requirements gathering, Design, Development, Construction and Implementation. Candidate must be skilled in n-tier web application development. The candidate must also have excellent people skills and be resourceful in order to manage the software development process. Responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • Enhancement and maintenance of existing and new web applications
  • Technical Design, Coding, and Unit Test of web applications in accordance with business requirements and technical standards
  • Design and Development of reusable components for web application
  • Participation in functional specifications, prototyping, and estimation for web development projects
  • Participate in the development of detailed design specifications for use by the technical team
  • Participation in evaluation and prototype of new technologies, coding practices or third-party technology for use in web application
  • Work with business teams to clarify and prioritize requirements and business needs

EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE

  • 8+ years experience in IT
  • 2+ years of software development experience in leading a software development team, including all phases of the development lifecycle
  • Proven track record of technical innovation
  • Strong development skills across the breadth of Microsoft technologies, excellent coding skills in .NET, SQL/T-SQL
  • In depth experience of creating enterprise level SPA applications using modern JavaScript frameworks (e.g. Angular)
  • Ability to work with complex technical projects that require collaborating with other teams, building strong relationships, and navigating effectively across division boundaries
  • Possess creative problem solving skills
  • Excellent team work skills with an ability to get the work done with minimal supervision
  • Experience with object-oriented design and programming techniques
  • Strong understanding of large scale, multi-tier web application architecture
  • Experience with Agile/SCRUM environment is a plus
  • Bachelors or Masters degree (preferred) in Computer Science or applicable field
