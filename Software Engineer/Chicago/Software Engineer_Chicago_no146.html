Full Job Description

The HON Company , the largest operating company of HNI Corporation and North America’s leader in providing workplace furniture solutions, is in search of a Senior Software Engineer to join our team! This a remote opportunity for candidates willing to travel to Muscatine, IA as needed.

Some of the essential aspects of the position include:

  • Works with product owners and agile development team to define development tasks and acceptance criteria.
  • Codes for automation of business functionalities including enhancements and modification to existing applications/processes.
  • Participates in strategic projects and/or multiple projects simultaneously.
  • Evaluates progress and results and recommends improvement changes in procedures.
  • Troubleshoots and maintains deployed applications.
  • Translates functional requirements into technical specifications.
  • Manages source control and participates in code reviews.
  • Designs, architects, develops and tests new application features based on product owner requirements.
  • Participates in activities as appropriate to ensure the success of the organization.

What it takes:

  • Associate’s Degree in Computer Information Systems or Computer Science or at least 2 years of equivalent experience.
  • Bachelor’s degree preferred.
  • At least 4 years of experience supporting higher-level or leading edge capabilities around technology strategy development and execution.
  • Demonstrated success leading and training others and effectively communicating to all levels of the organization.
  • At least 3 years of experience in web development technologies including HTML5/CSS3, JavaScript/jQuery/React, and PHP/Drupal.
  • Business and technical experience of current web based applications.
