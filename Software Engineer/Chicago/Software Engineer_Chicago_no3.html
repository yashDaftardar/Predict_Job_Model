Responsibilities

We are looking for engineers who are adaptable and creative problem-solvers, and more importantly, are excited to be part of a team that values communication and supports each other. We create solutions together in a rich, collaborative environment, value customer perspective and can work across disciplines (engineering, program management, design, and user research) to build great solutions that improve lives for everyone.
  • Understand customer's overall technical requirements, priorities and success measures to design system architecture and implementation paths
  • Maintain and advance deep technical skills and knowledge, keeping up to date with market trends and competitive insights
  • Work with program managers to understand customer needs and provide insights and best practices
  • Design, code, debug, and problem-solve with internal and external members of the team on a wide array of proposed web, desktop and mobile experiences
  • Create a track record of driving decisions collaboratively, resolving conflicts and blockers with creative problem-solving and ensuring follow through with exceptional verbal and written communication skills.

Qualifications

Required:

  • 7+ years of relative experience in at least one general purpose programming language Java, C#, JavaScript, TypeScript.
  • Familiarity with Microsoft Cloud technologies.
  • Breadth of technical experience and knowledge as well as depth in at least one of the following areas: Database architecture, ETL, Data Mining, Business Intelligence, Big Data, Advance Analytics, Data Structure, Data Mining, Data Governance, FHIR, HL7, Desktop or Web Frontend UX


Preferred:

  • Proven track record of building deep technical relationships with senior IT architects to engineers
  • Demonstrated ability to adapt to new technologies and learn quickly to enable migrating or transforming legacy customer solution to cloud-based platform as a service.
  • Ability to communicate concisely and effectively with other executives, employees, partners, and communities
  • Experience with either retail or healthcare companies.
  • Prior work experience in a Consulting/Delivery/Architecture position within a software and/or services company.
