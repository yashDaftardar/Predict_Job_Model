Overview

As a DevOps Engineer you will be joining a team of professionals working to engineer solutions for entire breath our firm, including Portfolio Managers, Analysts, Quants, Marketing, Compliance and Operations.


The DevOps team is responsible for developing and maintaining the processes and systems that support the CI/CD capabilities for multiple application development teams. Contributing in DevOps requires a commitment to best practice, advancing automation, and maintaining strong adherence to security policy

Responsibilities

  • Ensure technical expectations of deliverables are met on projects.
  • Contribute to the maturation of CI/CD through development and testing of pipelines and automated provisioning of infrastructure.
  • Support Scrum teams across the entire CI/CD spectrum.
  • Manage CI environments, including maintenance and administration.
  • Manage application platforms and infrastructure in hybrid cloud/on-prem environment.
  • Maintain and improve monitoring and alerting systems and processes.
  • Facilitate technical roadmaps through upgrade and maintenance work.
  • Collaborate with infrastructure, security, architecture, and development teams.
