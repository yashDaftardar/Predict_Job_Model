What's the role?:
Job Description

We are a team of highly motivated engineers supporting a wide range of R&D activities to build a complete and accurate dataset of places of interest for the entire world. We are passionate about our software’s quality and encourage a culture that supports curiosity, open discussions, ownership and accountability. We have an agile working environment that uses DevOps practices to build highly reliable solutions and leads the design/development of software solutions.

Role and Responsibilities

You will be developing E2E Integration test Automation and tools for Places Pipeline connecting to Internal and External applications, setting Quality as high priority before shipping the product to customer. Some of the daily responsibilities include the following:

Analyze software specifications and architecture to determine system integration points
Tackle interesting and challenging problems of large scale data management and enrichment
Support Integration pipeline with Development on Test Automation, needed tools & Integration Testing
Design and execute tests to identify integration and end-to-end issues across components and verticals.
Collaborate with stakeholders, vertical team members and E2E team members in day-to-day activities.
Working in the AWS cloud computing environment

Who are you?:
Skills

To be successful in this role, you'll have much of the following:
  • Bachelors (or) Master’s Degree in Computer Science or related field
  • Experience in developing Java applications or services
  • Experience writing and executing SQL (or) PL/SQL queries
  • Experience of working with Unix or Linux Operating Systems
  • Experience in testing integrated systems is a plus
  • Experience with automated test development is a plus
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Good analytical and problem solving skills
  • Ability to work independently, be creative, and resolve technical issues
