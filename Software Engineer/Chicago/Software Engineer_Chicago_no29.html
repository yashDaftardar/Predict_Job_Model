What you will get to do:

Perform research, development, and support related to data analytics-based technologies such as predictive maintenance to build products or features that could be marketed to customers of PanaCIM suite of products.

Under direct supervision, apply theories and principles of computer science, engineering, and technologies to create, test, and evaluate software applications and systems. Based upon analysis of requirements and technical direction, apply a systematic approach to create the design characteristics of components. Implement, test, and debug software to conform to requirements and design

Data Analytics Research/Development Activities:

  • Analyze the data from a set of customers to understand the problem and goals.
  • Develop hypotheses related to data and define experiments.
  • Test hypothesis using different machine learning algorithms and chose one that fits the problem and goals.
  • Provide input to the team regarding the right ML algorithm and define the approach for creating a model.
  • Lead model development and testing efforts.
  • Work with application development team to integrate model with data pipeline and the user interface.

Software Support Activities:

  • Provide support in rolling out pilot deployments.
  • Support in training customers to use our products.
  • Provide necessary development support to resolve bugs and issues.
  • Work with Quality Assurance, Technical Support, and other developers to resolve bugs and issues.
  • Resolve customer complaints with software and respond to suggestions for improvements and enhancements.

Documentation, Collaboration, Coordination and Training:

  • Support the creation of documentation, which may include user manuals, help documentation, tool tips, and training material.
  • Work with technical staff to learn and understand problems with software.
  • Make suggestions for problem solutions or software enhancements.


What You’ll Bring:

Education & Experience:

  • Bachelor’s (minimum) or Master’s in engineering, Computer Science, or equivalent.
  • Experience with agile (Scrum) development process
  • Minimum of 0-2 years of experience developing software in an intuitional, professional, or commercial environment in the following technologies:
  • Python
  • JavaScript
  • Jupyter notebooks
  • Machine Learning
  • ML and statistics libraries such as scikit-learn, Pandas
  • Deep Learning (TensorFlow/Keras or PyTorch)
  • Cloud development (preferably MS Azure)
  • Docker containers (optional)
  • Linux command line.

Problem Solving:

  • Provide high quality work in a timely manner with limited direction from senior technical staff.
  • Ability to translate requirements into a solid design and implementation.
  • Understanding of software engineering design principles and algorithms with the ability to translate design and analysis into code.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills.
  • Strong diagnostic skills to track, trouble-shoot, and solve issues.

Communications:

  • Ability to take direction and work well with others.
  • Ability to lead small teams of interns.
  • Ability to make presentation about products to pilot customers as well as to internal stakeholders.

Other Requirements:

  • Occasional travel.
