GENERAL SUMMARY

This position is for cutting edge Computer Vision based software development for Autonomous driving. The position requires strong analytical skills, mathematical knowledge of image processing and computer vision algorithms and performance optimization on various SIMD and DSP architectures.

Minimum Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Information Systems, Computer Science, or related field.
  • 5+ years Software Engineering or related work experience.
  • 2+ years experience with Programming Language such as C, C++, Java, Python, etc.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Master's or Ph.D degree with experience in the field of Computer Vision and Image Processing is preferred.
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills required.
  • Solid experience with C/C++ is a must.
  • Experience with Code Optimization (ARM/Neon and DSP SIMD programming) is desirable.
  • Experience/knowledge of OpenCV, OpenVX, Point Cloud library, Embedded Real Time systems/RTOS, OpenCL is required.
  • knowledge and experience with Camera, Graphics and Imaging is useful.
  • Experience with Matlab, Python, Numpy, PCL tools is useful.

Physical Requirements

  • Frequently transports between offices, buildings, and campuses up to ½ mile.
  • Frequently transports and installs equipment up to 5 lbs.
  • Performs required tasks at various heights (e.g., standing or sitting).
  • Monitors and utilizes computers and test equipment for more than 6 hours a day.
  • Continuous communication which includes the comprehension of information with colleagues, customers, and vendors both in person and remotely.
