Purpose of the Role:

The mission for this role is to improve overall best engineering practices, while hiring the best talent to build self-organized teams improving sprint team velocity and reduce time to market focused on business value driven outcomes.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Manages all engineering activities within assigned teams including design, development, production and testing.
  • Implements and maintains overall engineering objectives and initiatives and aligns on digital road map
  • Detects and solves performance/operational issues to improve team's velocity.
  • Establishes and improves a robust agile software engineering process and delivery timelines to keep strategic initiatives on schedule and on budget
  • Create engineering plans, processes, and procedures for engineering teams to use and abide by
  • Hire, train, and supervise engineering staff members

Competencies for Success:

  • Attracts Top Talent
  • Cultivates Innovation
  • Drives Engagement
  • Tech Savvy
  • Optimizes Work
