Job Description


Design and Patterning AI group under Intel's Technology Development organization is looking for a passionate, results-oriented, and innovative software engineer to develop mission-critical software solutions for our design and process teams.

As an AI software engineer you will develop computational tools and AI/ML applications that improve patterning solutions, decrease process debug time, improve design efficiency, and bridge the gap between design and manufacturing.

Your work will span solution exploration, technical design, implementation, deployment in a production environment, and maintenance of the deployed solutions. You will be required to find innovative solutions to problems, collaborate with internal teams to ensure data integrity, implement custom algorithms to perform feature engineering, develop efficient software solutions that meet the compute constraints, and ensure code quality to enable deployment in a production environment. You will work closely with a talented group of engineers who are experts in their fields and take on challenging real-world problems to advance Intel's march along IDM 2.0 strategy.

The candidate should also exhibit the following behavioral traits:

  • Effectively communicate, and able to interface with team members and customers.

  • Collaborate and align with team members, internal/external stakeholders and partnership skills

  • Demonstrate data analytics skills to communicate complex data in a simple, process issues and identification of a solution

This is an entry level position and will be compensated accordingly.


Qualifications


You must possess the below minimum qualifications to be initially considered for this position. Preferred qualifications are in addition to the minimum requirements and are considered a plus factor in identifying top candidates. Experience listed below would be obtained through a combination of your school work/classes/research and/or relevant previous job and/or internship experiences.

Minimum Qualifications:


  • The candidate must possess or be pursuing a Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Physics, Applied Mathematics, or other relevant related degrees with a software focus.


  • Candidate must have 6 + months of work or educational experience in the following: Programming (C++/Python), data analysis, CAD tool development


Preferred Qualifications:


  • Minimum 6 months of experience in:


o implementing efficient data structures for software tool development

o implementing efficient (runtime and memory) algorithms to solve CAD/AI problems

o data analysis methods including statistical methods

o numerical algorithm design

o computational geometry algorithms

o database design for big data

o Optical Proximity Correction (OPC) and lithography simulation.
