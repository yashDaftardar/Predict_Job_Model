Controls Software Engineer (Engineer IV)

Create custom Forklift solutions for key customers

Are you looking to make a difference on a dynamic team with the opportunity to grow professionally? Hyster-Yale Group is looking for a talented embedded software engineer with a strong desire to learn and grow with our controls team in the Portland, Oregon area. This global development center develops materials handling solutions for the present and future. Current projects include: current/future forklift products, fuel cells and advanced battery technology.


HYG designs, engineers, manufactures, sells, and services a comprehensive line of forklift trucks and aftermarket parts marketed globally under the Hyster and Yale brand names. As one of the global leaders in the materials handling industry, we have been building relationships while developing great products for over 90 years. We have great teams, stimulating projects, and big challenges within our engineering development center in the Portland area. Join our team as we take forklift design to the next level!

Responsibilities:

  • Develop embedded software for vehicle control and alternative energy systems .
  • Develop control system models using Matlab/Simulink, within an embedded C environment.
  • Work with platform leaders to ensure truck level requirements are clear, concise, and correct.
  • Develop system and component level requirements from truck level requirements.
  • Work with global software engineering team to develop efficient, cost effective electrical, electronic, and software architectures .
  • Develop, design, release, and support activities to ensure that the engineering outputs meet the quality, reliability, performance, and cost expectations of the customer.
  • Provide support to Manufacturing, Sourcing, Marketing, and Aftermarket groups in the area of vehicle control systems.
  • Maintain current product embedded C software.
  • Collaborate with electronic systems suppliers.
  • Provide technical leadership to junior engineers.
  • Lead occasional short-term projects.

Minimum Qualifications:

  • BS in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or similar.
  • 5+ years software development experience.
  • Proficiency in C programming language.
  • Experience with embedded system development.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Basic knowledge/skill in Matlab/Simulink.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Combination of Computer Science and Electrical Engineering, or equivalent.
  • Proficiency in Matlab/Simulink
  • Solid knowledge of data communications e.g. CANOpen, J1939

Essential Qualities:

  • Excellent teammate.
  • Innovative thinking, troubleshooting and problem solving.
  • Ability to work and think from boot code to high level abstractions.
  • Ability to follow established procedures.
  • Ability to develop and improve procedures.
  • Attentive to details.
  • Ability to take decisive actions to develop product.
  • Ability to apply basic principles and methods to resolve product issues.
  • Willingness to travel in North America and/or overseas on occasion.
  • Willingness to lead small project teams on occasion.
  • Specialized knowledge/skill in engineering analysis.

Ability and Willingness to Learn:

  • Other programming languages, as needed
  • Leadership
