Full Job Description

DeepSurface Security is changing how companies address risk in their cybersecurity systems by empowering our customers to be radically better at protecting their organizations. We’re a fast-growing, software development company headquartered in Portland, OR.

We have an immediate opening for a DevOps Engineer. They will be responsible for managing our flagship product’s build pipeline, QA infrastructure, and a small, but growing number of hosted services. The role will involve implementing and maintaining automated workflows in both Linux and Windows environments.

The ideal candidate will have 3-5 years of experience writing code to automate systems and build tools, and a solid background building and supporting cloud and on premises VM environments. Additionally, this candidate would thrive in a collaborative environment with team players, be comfortable taking initiative to produce results, collaborate effectively with a talented team, and have the ability to suggest and carry out practical actions to deal with issues. All roles are 100% remote currently, but we plan to go back into our Portland offices part time after COVID.

Responsibilities:

  • Write reusable and extendable code that defines QA and development infrastructure
  • Maintain and grow cloud hosted environments and resources
  • Build and maintain CI/CD workflows and tools
  • Work collaboratively with other engineering team members to create reliable and feature rich products
  • Define and document current and future configuration processes and operating procedures

Desired skills:

  • 3-5 years experience scripting the building, launching, maintaining, and clean-up of VM based environments
  • Experience implementing and using tools such as Puppet, Chef, Ansible or similar technologies to configure, maintain, and deploy systems
  • Experience with a variety of VM platforms, including on premises and cloud based
  • Experience building and maintaining VM images
  • Linux host configuration and administration
  • Strong scripting skills and experience writing production facing code
