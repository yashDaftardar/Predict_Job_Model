ABOUT THE ROLE

We are currently recruiting for a Firmware Engineer to join Nautilus’ Digital team and provide new product development engineering and sustaining engineering for consumer exercise equipment. The primary responsibilities will be to work with various product teams, designing and writing embedded firmware. Other areas of involvement will include scoping of development tasks and processor requirements, researching of new technologies, developing proof of concept systems, contributing to product definition, participating in board level design reviews, architecture of new platforms, identifying performance bottlenecks, and making the appropriate h/w and s/w tradeoffs during the product development cycle. This is an exciting opportunity to be part of a growing team that is providing product differentiation through electronics.


WHO YOU ARE

To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or Electrical Engineering
  • At least 3 years experience developing firmware for multiple different microprocessors and with different development tool chains including small footprint and embedded systems.
  • At least 3 years experience with C/C++ programming languages
  • At least 1 years experience identifying hardware requirements based upon system firmware requirements.
  • Able to read schematics and work through hardware level system issues.
  • Experience writing software for Bluetooth, RS-232 UART, SPI, I2C and other communication protocols
  • Experience developing on an RTOS based system
  • Able to support all phases of the product development cycle including architecture, design, development, debugging, documentation and validation.
  • Innovative and energetic personality, willing to explore new ideas, is a strong team player willing to work within a diverse product development group and able to contribute individually with little supervision.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.

Additional Desirable Experience :

  • Experience developing tools and documentation in support of manufacturing and development processes
  • Experience developing control firmware for various embedded systems including motor control and sensor input
  • Experience writing display drivers and UI implementation for Dot Matrix LCDs
  • Experience working in iOS / Android development environments, including kotlin, Java, Swift and/or ObjectiveC
  • Experience with signal processing concepts and the implementation of systems utilizing them
  • Experience with Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, BLE and other wireless communication protocols
  • Experience programming power management firmware
  • Knowledge of general Hardware board level digital and analog design
