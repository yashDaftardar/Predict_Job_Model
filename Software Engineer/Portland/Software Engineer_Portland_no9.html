Full Job Description

Siemens EDA is the longest standing Electronic Design Automation company in the world and over the last 30 years has amassed the finest technology portfolio in the business. Our software tools span the full breadth of semiconductor and electrical systems solutions including coordinated circuit design and verification, PCB design & manufacturing solutions, cable harness design tools, and embedded software.

Position Overview

Siemens EDA has an opportunity for an experienced Software Engineer to be part of the Build and Infrastructure Group for their flagship Calibre product. The group works on multiple areas of the SDLC including Source code management, building CI/CD pipelines, setting up and maintaining infrastructure both on-prem and on the cloud, licensing, clearing OSS, build and release management.

The ideal candidate would be one who has worked in a DevOps team supporting an enterprise environment across multiple geographies

Required Knowledge/Skills, Education, and Experience
  • BS/MS in Computer Science, Electrical or Computer Engineering, or other related fields.
  • Strong proficiency in programming with scripting languages like Bash, Python, Perl (C++ a plus).
  • Experience working in Git/GitLab, AWS other cloud technologies
  • Experience in Infrastructure/GRID management, Release Management, OSS Clearing will be a plus
  • Strong analysis, design, and problem-solving skills along with object-oriented programming practices.
  • Attention to details and the ability to accurately estimate software tasks and deliver on schedule.
  • Good verbal, written, and interpersonal communication skills
