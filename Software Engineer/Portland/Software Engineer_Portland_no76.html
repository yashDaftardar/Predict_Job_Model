Software Engineer
SIGMA DESIGN is a fun, fast-paced engineering consulting company currently experiencing explosive growth. We are looking for a Software Engineer to bring new capabilities to our growing team as well as assist with growing and mentoring the team. Curiosity, competency and confidence are necessary. We offer competitive compensation (DOE), Medical, Dental, and Short Term Disability insurance, a retirement plan, and profit sharing. We also offer Voluntary Benefits: Vision, Long Term Disability, and Term Life insurance.
Essential Job Functions - Responsibilities:

  • Design and implement software systems to interact with various hardware to drive/control electronic and electro-mechanical devices.
  • Create desktop (or web) applications to control systems for prototyping, test and automation solutions.
  • Work alongside other engineers on the team to elevate technology and consistently apply and enforce best practices.
  • Coordinate with project managers and various disciplines to ensure project meets system requirements and schedule.
  • Provide assistance generating engineering time estimates for client proposals.
  • Work with clients to capture system requirements and design system to meet requirements.
  • Analyze, diagnose, and debug existing code.
  • Work with SQA to ensure solution are fully tested and meet requirements prior to delivery.
  • Manage time to produce results within allocated timeframe.
  • Demonstrate behavior consistent with SIGMA DESIGN Core Values.

Education and Experience: Required.

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Computer Science or similar.
  • Minimum of 5 years work experience as a degreed engineer.

Knowledge, skills, and abilities: Required.

  • Proven ability to write software to control electro-mechanical systems including vision.
  • Capable of delivering on multiple competing priorities with little supervision.
  • Demonstrated programming capability in C#, C++ and Python .
  • Demonstrated experience working in Windows OS and with the Window Development Toolchain.
  • Experience with a variety systems that include vision, audio and variety of sensors.
  • Capable of building API’s and services using REST, SOAP, etc.
  • Demonstrated experience developing desktop applications required (web and mobile highly desirable).
  • Wired and Wireless communication protocols such as USB, Ethernet, BLE, Wi-Fi.
  • Knowledge of software testing methodology, including writing and execution of test plans, debugging, and testing scripts and tools.
  • Strong written and verbal English language communication skills.
  • Pass a post-offer background verification.
  • Travel requirements of 30%.

Work Environment
Standard office environment with normal amounts of office related conditions. Occasionally may be required to work in environmental conditions that emulate typical user environments in order to facilitate design testing and validation. Occasionally may be required to travel as required to other facilities, clients or suppliers.
