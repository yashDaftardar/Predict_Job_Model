About the Team:

As part of Workday Extend, the DevOps/SRE team shows up every day to build and develop for those who build and develop for our customers. We are a fun and collaborative team always looking for the best way to advance and automate our continuous build and deployment frameworks and public cloud infrastructure.

Our goal is to innovate constantly and use the newest and best DevOps-related technologies and services all while ensuring high quality, security, and stability in our production environments.

Solving new problems, developing better tools, and working across Workday to share and foster the best way to serve our customers is our passion and our focus. We work hard, and we're serious about what we do, but we like to have a good time while we’re at it.

About the Role:

You will be a key and valued member of the team from the very start. Your efforts will continue to keep our high bar of expectations and delivery. We are seeking the type of person that can demonstrate from their career history that they actively drive and implement the right type of change for the situation. You will:

  • Implement improved deployment frameworks for services.

  • Support our dev, test, and production environments using an Infrastructure as Code approach.

  • Develop both backend and frontend code for the internal tools we built and maintain

  • Collaborate with development teams and peers within the DevOps team on technical design, work estimation, and implementation of new features and deployments

  • Support automation of solutions for site availability, monitoring, and reporting processes.

  • Participate in and facilitate production on-call duties and events

  • Bring your whole self and sense of humor to a high performing team that welcomes newcomers

About You:

  • First and foremost, you are self-motivated and want to build your DevOps skillset

  • 1+ years’ experience related to AWS, Docker, Jenkins, and Kubernetes.

  • 2+ years’ experience building tools within a software development life cycle.

  • You own working knowledge in Unix, scripting, and networking.

  • BS/MS in Computer Science (or related field) or equivalent industry experience required.

  • You are an energetic team player looking for a great job with an awesome team.

Have you not mastered everything listed? Don’t worry, we do not believe in the ‘perfect’ candidate and are happy to teach. Learning every day is part of the fun of what we do! We invite you to apply and see what we are all about.
