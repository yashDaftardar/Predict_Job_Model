Job Overview
The Digital Vehicle Solutions Group at Daimler Trucks North America delivers value to our customers through connected vehicle products and services under the Detroit Connect product portfolio. We are the Software Engineering Team that develops and owns data streaming and analytics applications that utilizes data from connected vehicles all the way through to our customer portals.
We bundle our competences and technologies globally. We use agile development methods and tools to learn quickly. We are customer-oriented and design our products to the highest standards. We develop reliable, safe and efficient solutions.
We are looking for a motivated Junior Software Engineer to develop Big Data IoT software and shape the future connected vehicle landscape. Our ideal teammate is passionate, loves to learn new technologies and takes pride and ownership of good quality code. If that applies to you - come join us!

Responsibilities

  • Development and integration of applications in an IoT Cloud environment
  • Take ownership of the design, implementation, testing and deployment of the solution you develop
  • Monitor and resolve technical issues and application bugs
  • Design and implement automated tests at multiple levels
  • Maintain systems uptime – incident and problem resolution
  • Work under supervision of a Connected Vehicle Software Architect and other team members
