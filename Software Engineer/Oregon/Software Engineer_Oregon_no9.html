Full Job Description

Let’s do great things, together.

Founded in Oregon in 1955, Moda is proud to be a company of real people committed to quality. Today, like then, we’re focused on building a better future for healthcare. That starts by offering outstanding coverage to our members, compassionate support to our community and comprehensive benefits to our employees. It keeps going by connecting with neighbors to create healthy spaces and places, together.

Moda Health is seeking an SQL Software Engineer that under limited supervision or self-directed will be responsible for the major systems supporting the various departments. Design, code, test, maintain and implement programs and systems. Work with BSA’s and teammates to understand business requirements/processes to provide technical and system support. Creates technical design docs, test plans based on functional specifications.


Primary Functions :

1. Maintain existing stored procedures and systems.

2. Design and develop new stored procedures and systems as part of the implementation of new business requirements.

3. Work with clients to identify and understand requirements to satisfy business processes\\requirements.

4. Develop detailed technical design specifications, which include file layouts, program functions and job flow.

5. Implementing and testing database design and functionality, and tuning for performance

6. Develop and maintain documentation of all objects associated with a project.

7. Ensure that new systems integrate into current batch cycles.

8. Participate in 24x7 on-call rotation.

9. Assist with training and technical support of others on staff.

10. Maintain a detailed working knowledge of the business unit(s) being supported.

11. Perform other duties as assigned.


Required Skills:

1. Bachelor’s degree in a relevant field (Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Services, or similar) with 3+ years’ experience; or 7+ years of relevant SQL programming experience.
2. Experience in relational database design, development, and support (MS SQL Server T-SQL preferred)
3. Experience in complex database objects in T-SQL and/or PLSQL
4. Experience in systems analysis
5. Knowledge of basic database security practices.
6. Experience in health insurance, Cognizant/Trizetto Facets a plus.
7. Experience with Microsoft SSIS and/or ETL tools a plus.
8. Experience with Red Gate SQL Tools and Microsoft VSTS/Azure Dev Ops a plus.
9. Experience with Agile methodologies a plus
10. Strong interpersonal communication skills needed.
11. Strong analytical thinking and adept at problem-solving.
12. Ability to work well under pressure, work with frequent interruptions, and shifting priorities.
13. Understanding of SDLC methodologies.
14. Maintain confidentiality and project a professional business image.
15. Ability to come into work on time and on a daily basis.
