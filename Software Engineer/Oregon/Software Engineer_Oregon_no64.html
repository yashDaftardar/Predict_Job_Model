Job Description


Join us in our mission to enable breakthrough innovations for cloud computing in Intel’s Data Platform Engineering and Architecture group (DPEA). As a BIOS/UEFI development engineer you will be responsible for working with Intel’s customers and some of the world’s largest Cloud Service Providers by understanding their BIOS requirement challenges and future innovations. Then by working with both internal and external partners you will architect and help implement solutions while along the way gaining invaluable experience into the inner workings of servers and how they are scaled for Big Data and cloud customers.

Your responsibilities will include, but are not limited to:

  • Identify BIOS/UEFI firmware related issues and debug during the planning development and validation stages of the projects.

  • Partner closely with OEM, ODM and IBV customers and internal stakeholders to enable BIOS for Intel’s latest technology roadmap initiatives.

Personal Attributes Needed:

  • Demonstrate effective communication and partnership skills to work directly with customers

  • Adaptive personality that can work within a team environment with facing fast changing and sometimes complex or ambiguous stakeholder requirements.

  • Can easily pick up and learn new technologies and trends.


Qualifications


Education Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree or higher in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Mathematics, Physics, or closely related field.

Minimum Qualifications

4+ years of experience in the following areas:

  • Programming in C

  • Basic Input Output System BIOS and/or Unified Extensible Firmware Interface UEFI

  • X86, PC, and/or Server architecture and operating systems.

Preferred Qualifications

Experience within the following areas is highly desirable:

  • Latest IA chipset features such as security, RAS, and PCIe

  • Industry standard software engineering processes, principles, and project life cycles

  • Working externally with customers

  • Debugging PC or Server platforms

  • Integrating reference code and or platform support

  • Assembly language and Python scripting

  • Git SVN and Perforce

  • BMC server management features

Inside this Business Group


The Data Platforms Engineering and Architecture (DPEA) Group invents, designs & builds the world's most critical computing platforms which fuel Intel's most important business and solve the world's most fundamental problems. DPEA enables that data center which is the underpinning for every data-driven service, from artificial intelligence to 5G to high-performance computing, and DCG delivers the products and technologies—spanning software, processors, storage, I/O, and networking solutions—that fuel cloud, communications, enterprise, and government data centers around the world.
