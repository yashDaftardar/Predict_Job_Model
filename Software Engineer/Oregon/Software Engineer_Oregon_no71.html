Position Purpose

Upon joining our “Purpose-Driven Games” team, you will write code to implement UI and add features to our proprietary real-time simulation software to satisfy our U.S. Government customers’ requirements. As a Senior Engineer, you will work on-site with a team in Eugene, OR to architect and implement pieces of our proprietary real-time simulation software, work creatively with designers to figure out how best to meet our client’s needs, lead and mentor other engineers or groups of engineers.

Requirements

  • Interested in long-term involvement with our multi-year defense contract projects
  • Able to be a leader within a team of talented artists, designers and other engineers
  • An interest in mentoring and leveling up other less experienced engineers

Purpose-Driven Games Specific Requirements

  • US Citizen (due to project requirements)

Preferred by Not Required

  • Previous experience with real-time simulations or games
  • Unity game engine experience
  • Experienced programming using C# and .NET

Education and Experience Required

12 or more years’ experience as a software developer
