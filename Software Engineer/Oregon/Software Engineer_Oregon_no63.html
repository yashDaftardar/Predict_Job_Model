Why Vacasa:

We started with just one home and an idea: to bring homeowners and renters together with smart technology and caring local teams. Today, we're the largest full-service vacation rental company in the US thanks to the people who give us their best every day. You'll fit right in here if you're curious, collaborative, innovative, and thrive in a rapid-growth environment.

Why Software Engineering at Vacasa:

We build the industry-leading technology that enables Vacasa to manage over 25,000 homes and service more than 2 million guest stays per year-a scale that is otherwise unprecedented in the vacation rental management industry and continues to grow.

We have big ambitions and aim to move fast. We live in the cloud (AWS), empower our teams to design and operate their own applications & services, and release software into production systems many times per day.

What we are looking for:

We are looking for an experienced Software developer to align with the Payments team to build modern services and APIs using server-less Cloud architectures. An ideal candidate will be able to function independently in a fast paced development environment and be proficient in at least one programming language.

What you'll do with your team:

As a Software Engineer, you'll be a hands-on contributor. Here's some of what you will do with your team:

  • Help build applications and services to support the business
  • Write code with automated tests and test the code of your peers
  • Review your team's work via code reviews and discussion
  • Share responsibility with your team for provisioning, deployment, and operation of applications and services in production environments

Skills you'll need:

  • At least 3 years experience working on software projects with a cross-functional team
  • Experience with building service-oriented applications
  • Experience working with SQL or NoSQL databases
  • Proficiency in at least one programming language ( Python preferred)
  • Experience working with cloud computing platforms, such as AWS
  • Experience with serverless systems
  • Experience writing Infrastructure as Code
  • (If remote) Ability to work from home and resides in one of the followings states: AK, AL, AZ, CA, DE, FL, GA, HI, ID, IL, LA, MA, MD, ME, MI, MT, NC, NH, NM, OR, PA, SC, TN, TX, UT, VA, VT, WA, WI, or WY
