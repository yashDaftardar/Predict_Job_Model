Full Job Description

SUMMARY

Cornell Pump Company, a manufacturer of industrial pumps for more than 70 years, is preparing our first technological offering in the Industrial Internet of Things (IIOT) arena. We are looking for an entry- to mid-level software developer with enthusiasm, broad exposure to different types of development tasks, and 3 years of experience to join our team for this years-long effort.


ACTIVITIES AND DUTIES

The successful candidate will work closely with the system designers in implementing the program components of a cloud-based system. This is a ground-floor opportunity to participate in all areas of a large scale project that is just in the proof-of-concept stage.


REQUIRED EXPERIENCE

  • 2 years minimum paid professional experience do you have, not part of a school-sponsored program. This should not include internships.
  • 2 years minimum programming experience in SQL, MySQL, or Postgres preferred
  • 2 years minimum programming experience in Python
  • 2 years minimum programming with JavaScript


USEFUL ADDITIONAL FAMILIARITY

  • Cloud development, Amazon Web Service (AWS) preferred
  • Web development with client-side JavaScript frameworks (React, Angular, Vue, etc.)
  • Object-oriented programming in any language such as Java or C#
  • Experience writing or consuming RESTful Interface services
  • Windows PowerShell script development


EDUCATION

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or Computer/Electrical Engineering or related fields

ABOUT US

We are Cornell Pump Company, a wholly owned subsidiary of Roper Technologies, Inc., located in Clackamas, Oregon with more than 70 years in the business. You don’t stay in business that long without a great team. Our employees are experienced and dedicated to delivering great service. We manufacture centrifugal pumps for a wide variety of industries, including Agricultural, Industrial, Municipal, Rental and Mining.
