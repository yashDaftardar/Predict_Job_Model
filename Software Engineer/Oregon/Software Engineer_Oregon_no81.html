We are EDT (Engineering Design Team, Inc.), and we specialize in high-speed interface products and systems. We are working on the leading edge at THE EDGE where you can be actively involved in system development that will challenge you and grow your skillset as we apply our technologies in the areas of edge computing, 5G, AI and beyond. The Software Engineer III (SWE3) at EDT will design, code, test, and maintain software associated with EDT products at the edge.


Curious about learning more? The best way is to apply, but if you need some additional information visit: www.edt.com

As a Software Engineer III at EDT you will:

  • Work in a true full stack development environment writing everything from embedded and system level software to customer facing web UI code.
  • Work with a variety of programming languages including C/C++, Python, Go, and Javascript.
  • Take complete ownership of software projects starting with architecture and design through implementation, iteration, testing, packaging, product delivery, and maintenance.
  • Work closely with customers to support products in the field as well as define requirements for new product development.
  • Be creatively involved in the research and development of new products, and use fast prototyping to determine their viability.
  • Mentor junior engineers.
  • Be a part of a culture that values learning and sharing knowledge.

Requirements

Minimum Qualifications:

  • 5+ years of professional software development experience.
  • Proficient in C/C++ and experience with one or more of the following languages: Python, Javascript, and Go.
  • Experience working in a Linux environment.
  • Must be able to lead a team and take ownership of software projects. This includes the ability to identify risks, advocate for resources, formulate requirements, design software systems, and handle testing.
  • Proficient in Git and familiarity with project management tools such as JIRA, Fisheye/Crucible, and Jenkins.
  • Has a passion for writing software, learning and sharing knowledge, and takes pride in quality of work and professionalism.
  • Interest and ability to learn other coding languages or technologies as needed.
  • Excellent written, verbal, and problem-solving skills.
  • Fluent in English.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • BS/MS in Computer Science or related field.
  • Experience writing device drivers in Linux or Windows.
  • Experience developing low level or system level software such as embedded systems, micro-controllers, or integrating with FPGA designs.
  • Experience with GUI and/or Web development. Familiarity with front-end frameworks such as Vue.js.
