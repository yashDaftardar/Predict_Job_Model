Full Job Description

CloudControlMedia (a subsidiary of QuinStreet) is a unique performance-based digital marketing team that tracks key milestones in consumer interactions, enabling clients to optimize campaigns, meet marketing objectives, and maximize ROI.

Our mission is to build honest and successful client-focused relationships through mutual accountability, keen data analysis and analytics, and the most experienced digital marketing team in the business.

Covid-19 Hiring Update: As a company, we’ve transitioned to a work-from-home model and will continue to interview and hire during this time. This role is expected to begin as a remote position. We understand each person’s circumstances may be unique and will work with you to explore possible interim options.

Job Category:

Do you want to work with a collaborative, growing, digital educational-marketing company and use your experience in online advertising? CloudControlMedia, LLC (a QuinStreet subsidiary) is hiring for a full time, on-site or Remote (EST) position to help grow our owned and operated lead management delivery, reporting software and web properties.

Responsibilities:

  • Front end / UI updates : client side updates to landing pages, dashboards, and reporting interfaces (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • Backend / server-side app development and updates : updates to dashboards, routing logic and rules, exports (JAVA, .NET, Python)
  • Testing / QA : Landing pages, forms, UI, reporting
  • Server / database maintenance : checking error logs, verifying backups, documenting best practices
  • Data management / analysis : verifying data is flowing correctly to the client systems, re-posting leads

Requirements:

  • Constant communication with colleagues
  • Strong organizational skills and ability to juggle multiple tasks within the constraints of timelines and budgets
  • Ability to work and thrive in a fast-paced environment, learn rapidly and master diverse web technologies and techniques
  • Excellent written and verbal communication
  • 4-6 years of professional experience in development, web design, cloud-based software or relevant fields
  • Strong in .NET Framework 4.x (VB, C#), JAVA, and Python
  • Strong in MS SQL Server (2014 - 2019), and mySQL. Able write well designed, testable, efficient SQL queries
  • Basic knowledge of common content management systems, such as WordPress & InstaPage
  • Experience building and using web APIs (SOAP, XML, REST, JSON)
  • Strong understanding of UI and UX, cross-browser compatibility, general web functions and standards
  • Expertise and hands on experience with front-end web technologies and programming languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, BootStrap and API's
  • Cooperate with designers to match visual design intent, and able to create working website out of design files
  • Create and maintain system documentation, help system, and shared technical documents
  • Gather and refine specifications and requirements based on technical needs of our company and clients
  • Integrate data from various back-end services and databases including vendor and client databases in testing and production environments
