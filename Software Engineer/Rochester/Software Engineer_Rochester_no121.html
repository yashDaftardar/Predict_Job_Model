Junior Developer


We are looking for a full-time Junior Developer to join our R&D team! We maintain a growing number of web applications using technologies such as: Ruby on Rails, Electron, React, Test Driven Development, and more.


A little about us: CatPrint is made up of a fun-loving group of professionals who enjoy what we do. We are dedicated in our commitment to serving each customer to the best of our ability, supporting each other, and preserving the environment. CatPrint offers high-quality printing to people locally, nationally, and globally. Using teamwork, innovation, and evolving technology we partner with each customer to help them achieve their unique vision. Consequently, it is extremely important that we provide only the very best service and quality for our customers. Visit our website (www.catprint.com) to learn more about our team and the work we do.


Responsibilities


  • Analyze and estimate software change requests
  • Design and implement robust, maintainable code
  • Use test-driven development to ensure the change meets requirements and does not introduce regressions
  • Document code changes both inside and outside of the code
  • Submit regular status updates to keep supervisor/other departments up to date on progress
  • Participate in team meetings
  • Handle day-to-day software/infrastructure issues


Qualifications


  • Dependable and dedicated with a professional attitude
  • Able to confidently and efficiently work both independently and within a small team
  • Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal
  • 2+ years of Ruby experience
  • 2+ years of Ruby on Rails experience

Experience with:


  • Test-Driven Development
  • Source Control
  • UNIX

Awareness of:


  • React.js
  • MySQL


Experience


  • 4 Year Degree (Computer Science, Information Technology, Software Engineering) or comparable work experience
  • 2+ years professional experience preferred


Benefits


  • 100% Employer paid health insurance
  • Life insurance
  • Matching 401K
  • Paid holidays, vacation and personal days
  • Complimentary coffee
  • Pet friendly


If this sounds like something you would like to be a part of, and you meet the above-mentioned qualifications, please use this link (https://catprint.bamboohr.com/jobs/view.php?id=7), fill in your information, and supply the following:


  • A resume in PDF format no more than 2 pages long
  • A one-page letter, also in PDF format, explaining why you want to become part of our team


Submissions in any other format will under no circumstances be considered or replied to. Please do not contact CatPrint directly.
