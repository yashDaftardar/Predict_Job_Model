Full Job Description

Your Contributions

As a Systems Engineer focusing on Site Reliability on our SaaS Operations team; you focus on the availability, reliability, and operational efficiency of our software production environments in Amazon Web Services (AWS). Bringing your passion and experiences for site reliability you will pioneer efficiencies and reduce manual tasks through automation. Delivering self-healing software across our entire Human Capital Management (HCM) product suites. You will monitor our systems and own the documentation of our response plan that reduces our time resolving customer challenges. Working closely with the DevOps, SecOps, and our Development teams you improve code for smooth deployment. Impacting our customer support experience and success of our product releases.
Preferred locations: Burlington, Vermont (dog friendly), or Tampa, Florida.

Our technology stack: AWS (EC2, EBS, VPC, IAM, Dynamo, SSM, Lambda, and more), Linux, Windows, IIS, MS SQL, Terraform, Python, Docker, BASH, Powershell, Elastic SIEM, New Relic, Run Deck, Prometheus, and PRTG

Your Responsibilities
  • Solve support escalation issues by creating mature operations and reliability with fewer critical incidents in production and fewer support escalations. Responsible for routing challenges to the right people and teams. Improve system reliability through optimization– leading to better real-time collaborative responses.
  • Build software that impacts operations and support teams. Solve weaknesses in software delivery and incident management. Implement services making operations and support more effective. Adding automation and context to alerts for better real-time collaborative responses.
  • Document historical knowledge over time. Continuous upkeep of runbooks, tools, and documentation to prepare for future incidents and ensure that teams get the information when they need it.

  • Conducting post-incident reviews     to keep our teams honest and ensure all teams are conducting post-incident reviews, documenting their findings, and acting on their learnings.
Your Contributions
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, related technical field, or a technology business management program.
  • 4+ years of experience as a Systems Engineer, DevOps, SRE, or Software Engineer.
  • Eligible to work in the US without sponsorship, as we are currently unable to sponsor or transfer Visas for this position.
  • Adept in the development of automated tools, systems, and services in multiple technology domains.
  • Advanced knowledge of one or more infrastructure components, such as: networking, cloud services, orchestration tools, containerization, virtualization, compute, and storage systems.
  • Experience with cloud environments (AWS) and Infrastructure as Code using Terraform.
  • Experience with Kubernetes is a plus.
  • Proficient in Bash, Python, and Powershell.
  • Experienced at leveraging application monitoring systems.
  • Your good sense of humor- we work hard but have a good time doing it!

Our Profile
Asure sees Human Capital Management (HCM) through the lens of entrepreneurs and executives with an owner’s mentality. We help businesses develop their “Human Capital” to get to the next level, stay compliant, and allocate their time, money and technology toward growth.

Our HCM platform enables more than 60,000 clients to build great teams and better manage their people while staying compliant in an ever-changing HR legislative landscape. Asure’s HCM offering includes Payroll & Tax, HR, and Time & Attendance software and HR Services ranging from online compliance tools to a fully outsourced HR department.
