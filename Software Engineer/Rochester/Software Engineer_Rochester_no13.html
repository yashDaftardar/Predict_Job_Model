Full Job Description

MKS Instruments is a global provider of instruments, subsystems and process control solutions that measure, monitor, deliver, analyze, power and control critical parameters of advanced manufacturing processes to improve process performance and productivity for our customers. Additional information can be found at www.mksinst.com .

MKS Instruments Power Solutions products provides the plasma and control solutions that is a key supplier to the worldsâ€™ largest electronics producers.

Plasma control presents many technical challenges that needs innovative thinking and inquisitive engineering minds. If this sounds like you then we want to meet you.

Your primary role as an Embedded Software Engineer is to design, analyze and develop embedded software for controlling RF power delivery systems used in semiconductor manufacturing processes.

We are looking for an outstanding Embedded Software Engineer with C++ skill who can:

  • Design, implement, troubleshoot and support embedded firmware involving electronics/software for RF power delivery systems and/or subsystems (i.e. high-power switching circuits).
  • Produce design documentation.
  • Interface with customers to determine and develop specifications.
  • Participate in firmware design reviews and recommend improvements.
  • Support new technology development initiatives and other critical projects.
  • Interface with product engineering and other cross-functional teams as required.
  • Analyze and troubleshoot application issues.

What will you bring to the team, along with your talented software development skills:

  • BS in Electrical or Computer Engineering and 2+ years of experience desired (recent graduates may be considered).
  • Relevant knowledge of embedded microprocessors required
  • Experience with high-speed communication interfaces desired.
  • Highly skilled with C++ Object Oriented Design principles
  • Hands-on laboratory experience and familiarity with test equipment a plus.
  • Proficiency with software configuration management tools desired.
  • Knowledge of Atlassian tools (Jira, Confluence) desired.
  • Experience with Python is desired.

Other job details:

  • Domestic & international travel (occasional)
  • Occasional work hour flexibility to accommodate international conferencing and deadlines
