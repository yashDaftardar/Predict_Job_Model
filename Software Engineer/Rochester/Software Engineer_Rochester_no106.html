Software Tester/QA Engineer

at (remote 100%,)

Perm. full-time role.

$85k plus benefits

As a member of a Scrum team, the Quality Engineer is primarily responsible for that ensuring quality products are delivered to the organization. The candidate will work with a cross-functional team to understand business requirements and break them into user stories, develop test plans, test cases and execute test scenarios to ensure we are delivering high quality code. The ideal candidate is a highly motivated, self-starter who is passionate about quality assurance and can work effectively in an agile environment to ensure commitments are met. The engineer works with the Scrum team to promote quality, innovative, and scalable solutions.

Position Qualifications
Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science/related field or relevant work experience

Minimum of 5 years experience working as quality engineer required

1-3 years’ experience working in an agile environment required

Healthcare Industry experience preferred

Understanding of Agile Development Methodology (SCRUM)

Understanding of the software development lifecycle (SDLC)

Experience in DevOps preferred

The role includes but is not limited to the following:

· Leverage DevOps concepts that promote good quality and automation practices

· Create detailed, comprehensive, well-structured test cases that are automatable

· Create system or business process flow diagrams

· Utilize structured query language (SQL) for data mining and data seeding purposes

· Conduct root cause analysis and participate in debugging activities

· Provide an hours’ estimate for quality assurance tasks being performed for user stories

· Accountable for regular status updates to all assigned sprint tasks during daily stand-up meetings
