Full Job Description

Are you looking for an exciting new career or to advance your career? Due to continued growth, Envative is looking for talented Software Engineers to work with our growing team of experienced engineers. You will be responsible for developing the web client and web tier of our products, including the WebView components for mobile apps. You will also have ample opportunity to aid in the design of our back-end services. You’ll play a key role in identifying and investigating new technologies, prototyping and testing, and rapid iteration releases. Your experience here will include application development for a wide-range of services and products involved with enhancing customer experience. This position requires experience using C#, Visual Studio, Microsoft SQL Server, JavaScript and Windows Server.

RESPONSIBILITIES & ESSENTIAL DUTIES

Designs, develops and modifies enterprise-wide systems and/or applications software . Use sound software engineering principles to ensure that developed code is modifiable; efficient; reliable; understandable; and fault tolerant. Provide software process management and control throughout the coding portion of the software development process.

  • Designs, develops, codes, tests and debugs highly complex new software products or makes significant enhancements to existing software.
  • Develops and applies advanced methods, theories, and research techniques in the investigation and solution of complex and advanced software applications and challenges.
  • Resolves highly complex hardware/software compatibility and interface design considerations.
  • Conducts investigations and tests of considerable complexity.
  • Factors emerging technologies and product supportability into design and implementation.
  • Ability to troubleshoot to define issues, and resolve them along with your team.
  • Must be a team player who takes ownership of projects and drives them through to completion thoroughly and efficiently.
  • Must enjoy the camaraderie of a fun environment with great people.

NOTE: Please note that Visa Sponsorship is not available for this position

REQUIREMENTS

Experience as a software developer supporting software architecture development; requirement analysis; process execution and evaluation; and integration (with both new and legacy systems).

  • BS college degree required in Computer Science, Engineering or related technical discipline.
  • C# / Microsoft.NET
  • ASP.NET MVC
  • JavaScript (experience with jQuery, AngularJS or NodeJS highly preferred)
  • Microsoft SQL Server or similar RDBMS
  • Entity Framework, nHibernate or a similar ORM

COMPENSATION

DOE (includes Medical, Dental, 401K with match, and PTO)

ABOUT ENVATIVE

Since 1997, Envative, Inc., located near the cultural district in Rochester, NY has been developing customized mobile and web applications across a multitude of vertical markets. Our capabilities extend into more advanced engineering services, such as database management and system integration. Clients use our expert consulting, design, and programming skills to build innovative systems on a myriad of platforms. We pride ourselves on creating a relationship with our clients and help them implement technology to help them reach their business goals.

WHY ENVATIVE?

Envative offers a low stress, professional, small-company experience where your input and expertise is valued, your skills are challenged and your potential to directly impact company success is huge. When you combine all of this with a competitive compensation and benefits package that includes paid vacation and holiday time, personal days, 401(k) plan with employer match, medical and dental coverage, it’s easy to see “why Envative?”
