Software Engineer, Senior (IMSWorkx Division)
REDCOM is seeking an expert software/networking developer to enhance our IMSWorkX Software Development team. The ideal candidate will enjoy hands-on design and implementation of applications across the REDCOM IMSWorkX platform, as well as a willingness to coach and mentor others. This newly created position will report to the Director of IMSWorkX.
Essential Functions:
  • Participate in engineering projects, as a senior engineer, or project lead, including responsibility to plan the project, execute the plan, and report and manage deviations from plan to engineering management
  • Investigate and evaluate technologies and methodologies and apply them within the product development team
  • Assist in providing guidance to product definition, design, and development activities across the full range of REDCOM IMSWorkX Products
Knowledge, Skills & Abilities
  • Strong proficiency in one or more of the following: Java, Python, C++, PostgreSQL, IP Networking Protocols
  • Expert-level knowledge and current superuser of Linux is essential to success in position
  • Highly motivated with strong communication, time management, and leadership skills
  • Strong technical acumen and practical experience designing and developing software solutions
  • Produces quality products on time and within scope
  • Proficiency with some, or all, of the following: Ansible, Jenkins, SVN, Boost
  • Experience designing and developing RESTful APIs
  • Ability to manage multiple concurrent projects utilizing internal and external resources
  • Bachelor of Science in a technical discipline preferred; equivalent knowledge and demonstrated proficiency of industry-recognized software, engineering, telecom-related products and services will also be considered
Additional Desired Skills
  • Demonstrated experience working with development teams of varying size through the entire software development lifecycle
  • Experience with Telecommunications protocols; including SIP, SS7, Sigtran
Special Working Conditions
  • No special working conditions – work in office environment / Current at-home work environment is likely based on the COVID-19 Pandemic
  • Travel – light travel may be required based on customer needs
  • This position requires some on-call availability and extended work hours to meet project or customer requirements
Physical Requirements
  • Must be able to work at a computer/computer workstation.

About REDCOM Laboratories, Inc.
REDCOM Laboratories, Inc. is a private woman-owned small business based in Victor, New York. Since 1978, REDCOM has been developing and manufacturing the world’s most reliable communications systems for all branches of the U.S. military, our NATO allies, government agencies, emergency responders, service providers, and enterprises.
REDCOM was awarded a Top Workplaces award in 2020 and 2021 by The Democrat and Chronicle. This dedication is based solely on employee feedback gathered through an anonymous survey that uniquely measured 15 drivers of engaged cultures. These drivers are critical to the success of any organization: including alignment, execution, and connection, just to name a few. As a first-time participant in the program, REDCOM surpassed hundreds of other companies for the top distinction.
At REDCOM, our mission is to provide the most secure communications solutions in the world. Our trusted and proven products are designed and backed by our relentless drive for quality, innovation, and world-class support.
REDCOM allocates substantial resources to research and development. In fact, the name REDCOM is an acronym that stands for REsearch and Development in COMmunications.
Our principal office is located in our 140,000 sq. ft. headquarters in the town of Victor, a suburb of Rochester, New York. This state-of-the-art campus is home to a large engineering and development team, along with our customer service, sales, manufacturing, and leadership teams.
REDCOM designs, engineers, and manufactures all of our products right here in the United States. We work only with vetted and trusted suppliers to ensure that we source the best and most reliable components for every product. REDCOM has proactively addressed every step of the supply chain to reduce risk, making our products TAA compliant.
What really makes REDCOM stand out from most other companies is our family-oriented culture. We encourage our employees to innovate, and empower them to work autonomously. This enables REDCOM to not just respond to customer requirements but to generate truly innovative solutions. We understand and anticipate user needs and develop solutions to meet them. We love solving problems that other companies can’t — or won’t — tackle.
