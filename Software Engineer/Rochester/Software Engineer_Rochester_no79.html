Overview:

As a Site Reliability Engineer with Mitel Global Cloud Operations, you will be a part of driving improvement in the way we monitor, manage, and maintain our portfolio of cloud service offerings. This will include the deployment and support of tools and frameworks consumed by our cloud services as well as the evangelization of DevOps/SRE philosophies, practices, and tools with our operations and development teams.

The ideal candidate will have experience operating in a distributed, highly available, multi-tenant environment with a strong emphasis on security, scalability, and resiliency. A strong background in cloud deployments is required, as is an inherent ability to troubleshoot and resolve issues in complex environments.

We are looking for a highly motivated team player with a positive attitude and a desire to learn and grow within the organization. You will play an integral part in helping Mitel grow as the global market leader in Unified Communications.

Responsibilities:

  • Manage and maintain our public cloud presence in our data center deployments, and in GCP

  • Use of SRE methodologies to drive operational excellence

  • Work independently and be a strong individual contributor

  • Work with development teams to design and implement technical solutions to meet developmental and operational requirements

  • Help measure and monitor application availability and performance

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent

  • 5 to 10 years of industry experience

  • Strong working experience in a Linux environment, and scripting skills

  • Experience working in any public cloud environment (AWS, GCP, Azure, etc) preferred

  • Experience in Configuration Management Systems (Chef / Puppet / Ansible) or equivalent

  • Experience with infrastructure as code solutions (Terraform, CloudFormation) preferred

  • Experience with monitoring solutions (Cloudwatch, Stackdriver, New-Relic) or equivalent

  • Experience with containers and container orchestration platforms (Kubernetes, Mesos/Marathon, GKE, EKS) a plus

  • Ability to use a wide variety of open source technologies and tools

  • Excellent communication skills both oral and written

  • Comfort with frequent, incremental code testing and deployment

  • Comfort with collaboration, open communication and reaching across functional borders

  • Strong understanding of message queuing systems and protocols

  • Experience with standard web platforms (PostgreSQL, NGINX, HAProxy)

  • Solid knowledge of core IP services (DNS, DHCP, LDAP, NTP)

  • An understanding of fundamental technologies like TCP/IP, HTTP, and DNS
