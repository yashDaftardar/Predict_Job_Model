Detailed Job Description
RIT is looking for an accomplished Associate Programmer / Analyst to join our Data Warehouse and Integration development team. The Associate Programmer / Analyst will work with key business leaders and technical staff, to develop data models and structures to support the rapidly growing data analytics needs of the university.

Department/College Description
Rochester Institute of Technology is a diverse and collaborative community of engaged, socially conscious, and intellectually curious minds. Information and Technology Services (ITS) is committed to serving the university's information technology needs. We provide development, maintenance, and support for centralized administrative applications, technology infrastructure, and client computing, as well as support and advice for a wide variety of other information technology needs. The ITS Application Development team is responsible for the implementation and support of third party and custom applications that are used by prospective students, current students, parents, faculty, staff, and other members of the RIT community.

Required Minimum Qualifications
  • Assist in the implementation of solutions that meet our customers business needs.
  • Meet with customers to understand business requirements.
  • Assist with requirements analysis and collaborate with peers to determine the best solution or approach.
  • Create new and/or modify existing programs to implement selected solutions.
  • Test programs created or revised to ensure they meet customer requirements.
  • Troubleshoot technical and functional problems, bugs and issues related to RITs implementation of third party, open source, and custom solutions as well as related customizations and interfaces.
  • Develop and follow department standards on the software development lifecycle, source code control, knowledge management, and change management.
  • Responsible for understanding and following information security best practices, university policies, and information security standards impacting this role including use of any tools, technologies, services, and processes required to protect the universitys information assets.
  • Other duties as assigned.

Additional Details

Rochester Institute of Technology, the 10th largest private university in the U.S., is among the worlds leading technological universities. Its 18,000 undergraduate and graduate students from all 50 states and more than 100 countries are enrolled in over 200 academic programs, including 7 interdisciplinary Ph.D. programs. A pioneer in experiential education, RIT was the first to offer undergraduate degree programs in biotechnology, information technology, software engineering, and microelectronic engineering. As home to the National Technical Institute for the Deaf, the university offers unparalleled academic opportunities and services for the deaf and hard-of-hearing. Founded in 1829, RIT has 115,000 alumni throughout the United States and around the world. RIT has been named by the Chronicle of Higher Education as one of the Great Colleges to Work For.
