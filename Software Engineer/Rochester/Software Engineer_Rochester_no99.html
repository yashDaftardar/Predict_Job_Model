Full Job Description

Summary
This position ensures the development, monitoring, implementation and integration of the care management programs that impact the utilization of medical services. The Manager communicates with departmental and plan administrative staff to facilitate daily department functions. The incumbent provides leadership for all aspects of work related to safety net programs and analysis. The Manager serves as a subject matter expert for the development of staff training.

Essential Responsibilities/Accountabilities
  • Assures compliance with established referral, pre-certification and authorization policies, procedures and processes by related Medical Services staff.
  • Assures compliance with established onsite and concurrent review, case management, referral, pre-certification and authorization policies, procedures and processes.
  • Assures compliance with emergency management policies, procedures and processes.
  • Facilitates on-going communication between case management staff, utilization management staff and contracted providers.
  • Assists with the implementation of policies and procedures regarding medical case management.
  • Maintains compliance with federal and state regulations and contractual agreements.
  • Coordinates and communicates medical service functions with other departmental functions.
  • Acts as medical resource for appeals function.
  • Assists Medical Services staff with computer systems issues related to case management.
  • Conducts telephonic review of cases with doctors, hospitals, and other providers.
  • Compiles and reviews multiple reports for statistical and financial tracking purposes to identify case management trends and assist in financial forecasting.
  • Supervises the day to day operations of the case management function/team.
  • Analyzes gaps in the current programs and identify opportunities for and barriers to program enhancement
  • Identifies programs with marginal impact and establish criteria for eliminating programs with limited impact.
  • Conducts comparative analysis of benchmark and competitor results and compiles reports to management.
  • Conducts research on best practices for benchmark. Provides reports and recommendations to the Care Management team for action.
  • Organizes and facilitates educational and re-engineering sessions.
  • Collaborates with other clinical departments in regards to cross functional business processes, accreditation requirements, and regulatory issues.
  • Develops and maintains policies and procedures, as needed.
  • Consistently demonstrates high standards of integrity by supporting the Lifetime Healthcare Companies’ mission and values and adhering to the Corporate Code of Conduct.
  • Maintains high regard for member privacy in accordance with the corporate privacy policies and procedures.
  • Maintains knowledge of all relevant legislative and regulatory mandates and ensures that all activities are in compliance with these requirements.
  • Conducts periodic staff meetings to include timely distribution and education related to departmental and Ethics/Compliance information.
  • Regular and reliable attendance is expected and required.
  • Performs other functions as assigned by management.

Minimum Qualifications
  • Registered Nurse with valid and current NYS nursing license or Bachelor’s degree in a health related field with a minimum of five year of experience working with utilization management/case management programs, data or equivalent. In lieu of Bachelor’s degree, an Associate’s degree in nursing with a minimum of twenty-five years of nursing experience in an acute care setting particularly in a medical/surgical, pediatrics or obstetrics environment.
  • Minimum of two years management experience.
  • Demonstrates effective verbal communication skills and superior writing capabilities. Reads, analyzes, and understands complex statistical documents.
  • Ensures accuracy of data. Demonstrates expert level ability of using statistical mathematics, research skills and calculations, and the use of software in the Utilization Management/Case Management process.
  • Exhibits excellent organizational, planning, and project management skills. Makes decisions using solid judgment skills to impact identified problems.
  • Working knowledge of a specialized or technical field such as clinical nursing, case and/or utilization management.
  • Maintains current and thorough understanding of federal and state regulations including NCQA, HEDIS, CMS, URAC and NYSDOH requirements for Commercial, Medicaid, and Medicare products.
  • Maintains knowledge of medical policies, medical protocols, InterQual and Milliman & Robertson guidelines, and key benefit features of Health Plan products. Ensures member confidentiality of data used for quality improvement activities.
  • Strong computer skills with proficiency in Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint
  • Must have the ability to travel.
