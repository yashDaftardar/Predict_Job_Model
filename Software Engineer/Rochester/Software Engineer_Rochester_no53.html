Position Summary

The Software Support Engineer will be part of the Services team responsible for providing technical software leadership to the worldwide regions by becoming a subject matter expert of our Document Management Software Products and Services. The individual will be instrumental in providing enabling tools, techniques, and information to the Application Specialists in the field.

Together with the Application Specialists, the Software Support Engineer will work through corrective actions utilizing department tools and process to manage the customer base. The Software Support Engineer will also be instrumental in bringing software knowledge and field experiences to the commercialization teams as the teams work to develop and release new products to the market.

Key Responsibilities

Assist with commercialization of new offerings following established processes and procedures.Provide technical insight to the Product Manager in the development of the product support strategy. Input includes technology transitions, technical skill set requirements, and product evolution.
Develop process flows and delivery plans for each new support offering.
Provide input to market analysis from a technical perspective to include product alignment, barriers to entry, and skill requirements.
Working with the Technical Knowledge Management group, develop service and support manuals, training materials, support procedures and diagnostic procedures in preparation for launch.
Provide product serviceability evaluations for support requirements pre- and post-customer availability.
Monitor worldwide performance and reporting. Roll-up summaries of performance on a monthly basis. Illustrate on-going trends for products.
Make recommendations on product support discontinuance and assist in the execution of associated plans.
Develop and implement support initiatives to control and manage support costs.
