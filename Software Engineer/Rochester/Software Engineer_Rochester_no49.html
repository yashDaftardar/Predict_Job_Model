Full Job Description

We are looking for a Lead Software Engineer with a background in full-stack cloud-based development. You will be responsible for leading a team dedicated to building EagleView’s next generation image viewing applications. We are looking for a candidate who is passionate about software development, dedicated to building scalable high-quality applications and services, as well as leading and developing members of the team.

Primary Responsibilities

  • Active participant in the Agile process
  • Works with the team to deliver story details and estimates
  • Tracks development progress and communicates with leadership
  • Active participant in the design and development process
  • Works with the team to ensure code quality, security, and scalability goals are met
  • Works with the team, operations, and support to ensure high availability
  • Coaches and develops members of the team
  • Other duties as assigned
  • Track record of leading development projects
  • Continuous learner
  • Experience with 3D rendering (WebGL, Three.js, Unity)
  • Experience with client-side web technologies (React, Node.js, Angular, Vue)
  • Experience with web backend technologies (Golang, Node.js)
  • Experience with container-based deployments (Docker, Kubernetes, ECS)
  • Experience with cloud development (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Experience with modern development paradigms (Agile, Scrum, CI/CD, Service-oriented architecture)
  • Experience with geographic information systems (GIS) and/or mapping technologies (MapBox, Cesium, ArcMap) preferred
  • Computer Science or Software Engineering B.S. or equivalent preferred

EagleView® offers competitive pay and robust benefit plans along with the opportunity to grow your career in a fast-paced, fun and casual environment.

EagleView and its subsidiaries are committed to leveraging the talent of a diverse workforce to create great opportunities for our business and our people. EOE/AA. Minority/Female/Disability/Veteran.
