Job Title – Sr. Spec, Software Engrg
Job Location – Rochester, NY
Job ID – CS20212603-58721

Job Description:

The successful candidate will be responsible for the design and development of embedded software for next generation and legacy tactical radio waveforms. This position requires a solid knowledge of software development principles, as well as a working knowledge of digital signal processing and/or communications theory principles. Strong communication and interpersonal skills, coupled with the ability work within a large engineering team are required.

  • Responsible for leading a team through research, design, and development of embedded software/firmware systems, in conjunction with hardware product development.
  • Design and implement software for real-time embedded systems. Perform design simulations and capture design specifications.
  • Apply principles and techniques of software development, computer engineering, electrical engineering, and mathematical analysis to daily tasking.
  • Analyze requirements to determine feasibility of design within time and cost constraints.
  • Consult with electrical engineers and other engineering staff to evaluate interfaces between hardware and software/firmware with consideration for operational performance requirements of the overall system.
  • Mentor and coach junior engineers.
  • Develop project plans, provide technical oversight, design, implement, test, and maintain embedded software for real-time control of advanced tactical radio equipment.
  • Develop and direct software systems testing procedures, programming and documentation. Perform wireless systems integration and testing.
  • Provide technical support to internal and external customers.
  • Execution of software development process in CMMI Level 3 environment.
  • Develop code according to coding guidelines and division-defined software development processes.
  • Provide unit testing of software/firmware.
  • Perform software/firmware component integration.
  • Participate in and lead code reviews.
  • Identify, track and repair software/firmware defects.
  • Support continuous improvement, process innovation and lessons learned activities.

Qualifications:

Bachelor's Degree (BSEE, BSCS or BSCE) adn minimum 4 years of prior relevant experience. Graduate Degree and a minimum of 4 years prior related experience.

Preferred Additional Skills:

  • MS Degree Desirable.
  • Minimum of 7 years relevant industry experience with embedded firmware and embedded softare development.
  • Strong firmware development background.
  • Proficiency with C/C++ programming language and Object-Oriented Design (OOD) required.
  • Experience with software emulators/debuggers required.
  • Proficiency with multi-threaded design and/or real-time operating systems is required.
  • Experience with flash file systems, XML, networking protocols and/or Ethernet switching.
  • Experience with wireless communications systems is highly preferred.
  • Knowledge of IP Networking and Wireless Networking desirable.
  • Experience with DOORS.
  • Ability to obtain a security clearance.
  • Demonstrated leadership and broad knowledge of project management
  • Knowledge or familiarity with JTRS Radio and Waveform Development concepts, SATCOM waveforms, IP Networking and Wireless Networking, GPP Development concepts; experience in C/ C++ development; CORBA and embedded distributed systems.
