【所属】

IT統括部 デジタルサプライチェーン部 ADI APAC Platformチーム

Are you looking for a role where you can influence the digital transformation and web presence for a beloved athletic brand? At ASICS Digital we are looking for a Full-Stack Software Engineer to help build solutions that drive the global business and digital engagement for ASICS's worldwide brand.

In this role you’ll be part of a small team working on project-specific deliverables for our e-commerce storefront. You will work on internally and externally-facing systems and make core contributions to our e-commerce codebase (Salesforce Commerce Cloud). You will also help support several third-party integrations that contribute to our end goal of delivering a fantastic user experience. Along the way, as you develop rapport amongst your teammates, you will implement optimizations to how we build, maintain, and improve our software.

ASICS Digital is a division of ASICS. We are a global company headquartered in Boston, Massachusetts, US. Our goal is to build digital technologies to better connect the ASICS brand to its consumers. We are responsible for the continued development of mobile-fitness solutions such as ASICS Runkeeper and ASICS Studio, global eCommerce platforms, and supporting digital services that encourage people to move, get fit and stay balanced.

【職務内容】

Responsibilities:

  • Implement performant, reliable code on our Salesforce Commerce Cloud platform
  • Solve problems by working with Product Managers, Quality Assurance Engineers, UX/UI Designers, and fellow Software Engineers
  • Establish a solid understanding of our third-party integrations and how to best utilize their value
  • Contribute to the development of the engineering organization by sharing ideas during team meetings and discussions.
  • Strong communication skills (English preferred, Japanese a plus)

【応募要件】

Requirements

Must:

  • 3+ years of full-stack web development experience (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and backend such as Java, Nodejs, PHP, Scala)
  • 1+ years CMS and/or E-Commerce experience (such as Demandware/SFCC, Wordpress, Adobe Experience Manager, or similar), particularly creating templates and working with different types of assets.
  • Experience with API's and databases (JSON, OCAPI, SQL, headless commerce/CMS, Postman)

Nice to have:

  • Server/Cloud/DevOps experience (AWS, Google Cloud, Apache, IIS, SSL)
  • Additional Salesforce product experience (Sales Cloud, Marketing Cloud)
  • Familiarity with OAuth
  • Familiarity with frontend libraries, build tools, and modern patterns/frameworks ( jQuery, React, Webpack/Grunt/Gulp, Progressive Web Applications)
  • QA Automation experience
  • A/B Testing experience
  • Experience working in an Agile environment.
