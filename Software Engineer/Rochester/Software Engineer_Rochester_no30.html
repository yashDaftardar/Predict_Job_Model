POSITION SUMMARY

Meta Advanced Signals is looking for an Embedded Software Engineer to join our busy team specializing in state of the art military communications and signals intelligence products.

All of our teams operate in a small and agile environment which allows our engineering personnel to work independently on a wide variety of systems and products. Our typical product development cycle is less than one year which provides our engineering staff a constant flow of new and interesting projects.

Essential Functions Statement

    • Design and implement embedded systems software from the requirements phase to the delivered product
    • Analyze, design, and implement firmware subsystems given a set of high level requirements
    • Analyze and improve system performance as needed
    • Interface with hardware design and development engineers

Competency Statements

    • BS degree in Computer Science/Engineering or Electrical Engineering
    • 3+ years experience in embedded software development
    • Proficient at programming in C or C++
    • Embedded Linux experience preferred
    • Python programming experience preferred
    • Experience in hands-on development on embedded targets, including multi-processor systems
    • Familiarity with software configuration management tools
Work Location
Our preference is for this position to be based in Victor, NY. However, remote working applications will also be considered

Additional Eligibility Qualifications
Must be able to accomplish any and all duties with little or no assistance.
Must have good command of the English language; oral and written

Work Authorization/Security Clearance
Employee must be a US citizen.
Ideally hold some level of Security Clearance or able to apply for one
