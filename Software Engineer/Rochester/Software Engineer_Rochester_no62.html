Senior Software Engineer

At Skillsoft, we are all about making work matter. We believe every team member has the potential to be AMAZING. We are bold, sharp, driven and most of all, real. Join us in our quest to democratize learning and help individuals unleash their edge.

Overview

Skillsoft is looking for a full-time, full-stack senior software engineer to join an agile and talented team in delivering a rich e-learning experience to users around the world. In this role, you will be involved in a variety of activities ranging from developing secure and performant micro-services using Java to building beautiful user interfaces using ReactJS to leveraging Amazon Web Services to using numerous other technologies such as Kubernetes, Docker, Postgres, Rabbit, Apache Kafka and many more. The ideal candidate is enthusiastic about building quality software and is completely comfortable in an agile continuous-delivery model. This is a very hands-on role on a team with great culture and technical know-how.

Opportunity Highlights:

  • Join our team as we build out our flagship product using modern programming languages, tools, and methodologies.
  • Develop tools and solutions to build and deliver learning content to users around the world.
  • Become a key contributor to an agile development team solving problems in a hands-on manner.
  • Opportunity to work on our Award-Winning Global E-Learning platform

Skills & Qualifications:

  • 5+ years of work experience working with Web applications
  • Strong knowledge in working with REST API, Web services and SSO standards (OAuth, SAML)
  • Experience with Java , Node.js, (JavaScript, React.js. a Plus)
  • Experience with code versioning tools, preferably Git (Github, Gitlab, etc)
  • Experience with Databases and SQL (Postgres a plus)
  • Experience with Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins and AWS services a plus
  • Experience in coding applications on Unix/Linux based systems.
  • Experience in Test frameworks (REST Assured, Selenium, PhantomJS, Mocha, etc.)
  • Experience in working with Agile/Scrum environment.
  • B.S. Computer Science or similar degree

SUCCESS QUALITIES:

Personally Accountable for Team Success. We unleash our edge together.

Confident Achievers. We are bold.

Intellectually Curious. We are sharp.

Adaptable, Agile & Resilient. We are driven.

Customer First. We are real.


MORE ABOUT SKILLSOFT:

Skillsoft delivers online learning, training, and talent solutions to help organizations unleash their edge . Leveraging immersive, engaging content, Skillsoft enables organizations to unlock the potential in their best assets – their people – and build teams with the skills they need for success. Empowering 36 million learners and counting, Skillsoft democratizes learning through an intelligent learning experience and a customized, learner-centric approach to skills development with resources for Leadership Development, Business Skills, Technology & Development, Digital Transformation, and Compliance.

Skillsoft and SumTotal are partners to thousands of leading global organizations, including many Fortune 500 companies. The company features three award-winning systems that support learning, performance and success: Skillsoft learning content, the Percipio intelligent learning experience platform, and the SumTotal suite for Talent Development, which offers measurable impact across the entire employee lifecycle.
