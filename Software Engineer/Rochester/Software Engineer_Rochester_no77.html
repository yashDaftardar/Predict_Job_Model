Your Contributions

As a growth focused Human Capital Management (HCM) solutions company, we are building out new product focused application development teams in the areas of Payroll & Tax, Time & Attendance, and Human Resources. These teams will help inform the roadmap in their area of ownership and own the technical output. We expect our engineers to have empathy for our user base and to be able to collaborate with their peers across the organization including both products and operations.

As a full stack Delphi engineer on the team you will apply your craft to help grow the business and improve the end user experience. You are up for the challenges of integrating greenfield development with existing infrastructure. Being a senior member of a team means you have an integral part in raising the collective skills of your teammates and you are able to function at a high level both independently and as part of that team.

A humble, passionate, collaborative, entrepreneurial individual who thrives in a dynamic and fast-growing tech company? Do you want to work with a team from some of the world's top tech and HCM companies as we innovate the HCM industry? Come join us!

Our technology stack: JavaScript (React, Angular, Node.js, Jest, Cypress), .NET Core/Framework, Kubernetes, Docker, Terraform, and Cloud-based infrastructure using Amazon Web Services (AWS), MS SQL Server
Legacy: VB .Net, jQuery, ASP.NET, Delphi, Firebird DB

Our Profile

Asure sees Human Capital Management (HCM) through the lens of entrepreneurs and executives with an owner’s mentality. We help businesses develop their “Human Capital” to get to the next level, stay compliant, and allocate their time, money and technology toward growth.

Our HCM platform enables more than 60,000 clients to build great teams and better manage their people while staying compliant in an ever-changing HR legislative landscape. Asure’s HCM offering includes Payroll & Tax, HR, and Time & Attendance software and HR Services ranging from online compliance tools to a fully outsourced HR department.

Our Mission
To provide Human Capital Management (HCM) software and services that help companies grow, while nurturing a culture of growth around us.
  • Helping customers grow by getting the most from their human capital.
  • Helping our employees grow personally and professionally.
  • Growing relationships in our communities that inspire goodness.
  • Do all these things in a way that grows shareholder value.

Our Vision
Be the most trusted Human Capital Management resource to entrepreneurs everywhere.
