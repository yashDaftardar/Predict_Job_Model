Software Engineer


Company Overview:

Brand Networks simplifies social, delivering award-winning social advertising software and services to enterprise customers throughout the world. We are proud to be one of the few companies in the industry formally recognized as an early Snapchat Partner, Instagram Marketing Partner, Pinterest Marketing Developer Partner, LinkedIn Sponsored Updates and Custom Apps partner, and Twitter Ads API Developer.


Description:

The Software Engineer will create best-in-class products that simplify social with a cross-functional team using modern software languages and methods and will optimize them to ensure a rich experience. This industry reinvents itself almost daily so you’ll need to keep up with the changing scenery – including everything from changing channel APIs to the latest JavaScript frameworks.


Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering or equivalent technical skills.
  • 2-4 years of hands-on experience with web application development in a professional environment. Bright and hard-working applicants with less experience will be considered.
  • Must be strong with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (proficiency with React and Vue is a plus). Familiar with Python, standard database technologies and source control.
  • Experience with client-side performance optimization.
  • Understand SDLC and what it means to write testable, maintainable code.
  • Able to manage multiple projects at once and adhere to tight deadlines.
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills.
  • Be a team player, looking to collaborate with cross function teams.


Areas of Focus:

  • Successfully and efficiently execute your functional role.
  • Improving your ability to manage your time, workload, and deliverables so less oversight is required over time.
  • Understanding our company’s domain of Social Marketing and how your specific sphere of knowledge applies.


Location

  • As a key member of the technology organization, this role is located in our Rochester, NY center of excellence. While we offer flexibility in working arrangements, candidates must be either living in the area or willing to relocate to the area to be eligible for the role.
