About the Role:


The Senior Software Engineer will be responsible for creating scalable, robust features and processes that leverage cloud technologies at their core. As an integral member of a cross-functional agile team, the Senior Software Engineer will participate in all phases of the software development lifecycle from requirements definition with Product Management to acceptance testing with a Quality Engineer to maintaining solutions in production environments. By providing leadership and guidance in development best practices, the Senior Software Engineer will help ensure the team produces high-quality and well-designed solutions. The Senior Software Engineer will learn and implement new technologies on the job, and will teach and mentor others.


Job Duties:

  • Collaborate with team members to produce proven scalable, performant, and maintainable solutions
  • Participate in design and code reviews
  • Investigate and resolve issues in development, test, and production environments
  • Implement new technologies and techniques to meet business requirements
  • Additional duties and projects as required by the business


Expectations:

  • Work on the full stack, with a specialty in web, server, or data-driven development
  • Mentor and guide others
  • Be an engaged member of a cross-functional, agile team
  • Learn and grow by taking on challenges and collaborating on solutions
  • Drive innovation, service our employees, and treat everyone fairly & respectfully
  • Enable & embrace change
  • Work independently in a fast-paced, dynamic startup environment
  • Excel in a collaborative environment & be a bridge between cross-functional teams
  • Committed & passionate about your work & achieving team objectives
  • Self-motivated; entrepreneurial spirit with a positive, proactive, leadership attitude

  • Familiarity with several of our core development tools & technologies, including React.js, Javascript, AJAX, SQL Server, AngularJS, CSS, Redshift, Jest, Yarn, Cake, Amazon Web Services, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud Platform
