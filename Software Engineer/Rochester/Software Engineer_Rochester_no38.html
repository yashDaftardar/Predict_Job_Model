Together we are powering the future of Higher Education

Here at Ellucian, we are motivated by a mission ~ the power of education to transform lives and change the world. We are the global market leader in EdTech for Higher Education, serving more than 2,700 customers and reaching over 20 million students in 50+ countries. We are dedicated to helping Higher Ed thrive by transforming their institutions to agile, digitally connected campuses that enable student success.



About the opportunity

Higher education institutions are dependent on intelligent insights to help them deliver the best possible student outcomes. With the uncertainty of COVID-19, it is even more important that higher ed institutions are able to quickly turn new data into actionable business insights for academic and financial success. Ellucian is seeking a lead Clojure developer within the Ellucian Analytics team. The Analytics team designs and builds dynamic analysis, reports and solutions to support high performing and scalable analytic applications. We gather and analyze business requirements and transform them into innovative BI solutions that effectively facilitate and enhance the development of the companyâ€™s suite of reporting and analytics products. You will be responsible for changes and enhancements within the open source visualization tool such as modifying the reporting options, changing layouts to meet UI standards and enhancing permissions capabilities. You will work closely with architects and other data engineers to drive our vision forward.


Where you will make an impact

Enhance open source visualization software to assist our team with changing product requirements.

Translating designs and wireframes into high-quality code to support higher education data insights.

Mentor for other developers in relation to the technology and direction; to include code reviews, pair programming, and other activities associated with team development.

Partnering with internal clients and customers to gain understanding of business functions and informational needs then working with team members to implement supporting visualizations.

Approaching challenges as opportunities to help yourself, the team, the processes and the product grow.

Daily communication: both in writing and via video. Includes a brief standup meeting to check on progress, call out interesting problems and solutions and ask questions.


What you will bring

Strong backend development experience in Clojure

Strong SQL knowledge. Experience in modern databases (Postgres, Oracle, Redshift)

Experience with tools and processes such as Terraform, git, Jenkins

Experience with Python a plus

Experience in frontend development with JavaScript. Node, React a plus

Knowledge with data visualization (dc.js, d3.js)

Knowledge of Metabase preferred

Familiarity with BI visualization software such as Tableau, Power BI

Independent worker and team contributor

Proactive and self-motivated. Ready to jump in on Day 1
