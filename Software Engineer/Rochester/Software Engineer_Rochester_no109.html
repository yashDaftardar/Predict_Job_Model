Job Description:


General Code is the nation’s leading provider of Municipal Code publishing and maintenance services (codification). Our passion for excellence, our focus on client service, and an ongoing investment in our people and technology have helped us to revolutionize the codification industry. General Code offers challenging work in a diverse, flexible, supportive environment where an appropriate work-life balance is emphasized. The successful applicant for this position will demonstrate strong technical capability, ability to complete tasks efficiently, and a keen desire to have a meaningful impact in a values-based, team-oriented business environment.

GENERAL DESCRIPTION OF THE POSITION:

At General Code, Software Engineer is a full-stack development position. You will be developing and maintaining a number of internal software applications. Projects will include back-end software development, web-based front-end development, and various automation tools used both for testing and deployment purposes. This is a small team environment where individual contribution, collaboration, and communication are critical for success.

ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES OR TASKS PERFORMED:

  • Develop and implement software and tools to increase efficiency in production operations
  • Provide enhancement and maintenance support for existing applications
  • Consult with Software Development Manager as needed to clarify project requirements, suggest improvements, and develop technical specifications
  • Create and maintain documentation for development, troubleshooting, and training purposes.
  • Provide estimates of efforts required to complete development task
  • Rigorously follow company source control, QA testing, and project management processes
  • Diagnose software system problems, both technical and business, and help provide appropriate solutions
  • Understand the corporate goals, strategies, philosophy, and profitability objectives, and work within and toward those objectives
  • Obtain and maintain a thorough knowledge of the business’s products, production systems, and production tools

Required Experience:
  • Software development experience using Java, C# SQL, React/other modern JS framework, CSS
  • Demonstrated experience with agile, rapid prototyping, or any other iterative development process is a plus
  • Organized thinking, with excellent problem-solving ability and a willingness to think "out of the box"
  • Demonstrated ability to work cross-functionally and manage multiple tasks simultaneously
  • Ability work flexibly in a variety of development environments and technologies, both current and legacy
  • Self-motivated and able to work independently
  • Effective written communication skills

WORK ENVIRONMENT AND PHYSICAL DEMANDS:

  • This is an office environment with a moderate noise level. While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to sit for long periods of time, typing and reading on a computer. The physical demands and work environment characteristics described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
