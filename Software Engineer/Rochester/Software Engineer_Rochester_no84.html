Belong to something bigger.

At ESL, it’s more than a job. It’s your unique journey.

Your Journey Begins Here

ESL has an immediate opportunity to join our inclusive, collaborative, and people-oriented organization as an INTEGRATION DEVELOPER (Programmer Analyst). We are creating a business architecture that will enable our company to scale and automate our banking operations and enable features to improve our employee and member experiences. We are seeking software developers with the ability to participate in the design, architecture, build, test and maintain the services and integrations of APIs supplied by third party vendors. You will be designing and building a middleware layers to automate on-demand and scheduled interactions triggered by customers and systems across our network.

Things You'll Work On:

  • Requirements Gathering, Design, communicate, and development of solutions to complex problems
  • Building and running integrations, using Microsoft Azure technologies including the API Management Service, containers, SQL server databases, .NET CORE (C#) development
  • Apply practical data governance, security standards and techniques to ensure efficient coding logic and trusted data integrity
  • Leverage the Agile/Scrum and DevOps methodologies

What You Bring with You

  • Bachelor's degree in Information Systems/Computer Science or other related discipline or 4 additional years of relevant experience
  • 1+ years (including co-op/internship) experience developing SOAP, RESTful web services using .NET Framework and/or .NET Core using C#
  • Experience working with agile project development
  • Solid experience with source/version control platforms.
    • Use of Azure DevOps tools, Git, etc CI/CD pipelines a plus
  • Experience in using API Gateways and data pipelines
  • Experience in cloud development environments

  • Demonstrates alignment with ESL’s Core Values, mission, vision, and Purpose to help our community thrive and prosper
