Full Job Description

The Cloud Engineer is a rare and exciting opportunity for a motivated and passionate individual to join the team at DCG Foundry, a newly formed subsidiary of the blockchain industry’s most prolific and active investor, Digital Currency Group. The role will be focused on monitoring and maintaining Foundry’s cloud based mining pool.

Candidate Must Haves:

  • Bachelor's degree in a technical field such as computer science, computer engineering or related field, or equivalent experience.
  • Amazon Web Services Certification Required – Associate Level or higher – Solutions Architect or SysOps

Administrator

  • Knowledge of load-balancing / distributed load systems /server clustering
  • Passionate about technology and interested in various crypto assets, blockchain, and tech entrepreneurship
  • Strong understanding of web services, databases, and data analysis
  • Have a working knowledge of databases and monitoring systems
  • Working knowledge of Proof of Stake and/or interest to learn development

Technical Skills

  • Ubuntu Linux system administration
  • Cloud servers such as Amazon AWS and Digital Ocean, Alibaba Cloud
  • Experience in operating staking and running validating node efforts
  • Responsible for the maintenance, configuration, and reliable operation of servers and virtualization
  • Automate deployment, validation, and quality control processes across our entire company
  • Build services and infrastructure that help to automate the deployment of blockchain network infrastructure
  • Knowledge of data protection, recovery, failover procedures
  • Experience in containerizing modern applications such as Docker and Kubernetes
  • Experience with obtaining, maintaining, deploying physical server hardware
  • Ability to implement and administer network infrastructure to servers
  • Ability to monitor and maintain nodes and staking services

To date, DCG has invested in over 150 companies across 30 countries.
