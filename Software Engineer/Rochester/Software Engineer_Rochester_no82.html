Full Job Description

This position assesses the development of Web based business application. We are looking for self-motivated, team-oriented and highly organized individuals with excellent communication (written & verbal) skills. Normal hours of work will be from 9:00 am to 6:00 pm, but this position is required to be flexible to stay late night as needed. Please understand that the position will require flexibility on your part. Work location will be at our office, but required to travel to our client’s site in NY/NJ or other area occasionally.

Primary Responsibilities


1. Responsible for system requirement hearing and Business Requirement Document creation.

2. Design and develop user interface using VB.NET and C#, ASP.NET 4.5, HTML, JavaScript.

3. Design and develop data access and processing.

4. Plan and execute Integration test and implementation.

5. Troubleshooting of web application and database.


Knowledge and Skill Requirements


1.Environment: Visual Studio 2012 or older versions. 2. Development languages: VB.NET and/or C#.

3. Database Platforms: Microsoft SQL Server 2012 or older version.

4. Must be able to write and debug Stored Procedures, create test case and conduct.

5. Design document creation and management.

6. Must be bilingual – English /Japanese (verbal, reading & writing – native level Japanese preferable)


Required Education and Experience


1. Bachelor’s Degree in Business, and at least 3 years of experience in above language

Supplementary Qualifications


1. More than 3 to 4 years of actual development experience. 2. More than 1 year of Team Leader experience.

3. Excellent professional level documentation skills.

4. Experience of SSIS package

5. Operation of SAP system

6. Experience of logistic system

7. Experience of SQL Server Reporting Services 2012

8. Must be authorized to work in U.S. (Under certain condition, we will support working visa.)
