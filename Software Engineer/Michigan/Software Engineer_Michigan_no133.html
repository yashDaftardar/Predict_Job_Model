Software Engineer ( Technical Lead)
Description
The Software Engineer, Technical Lead role is a critical contributor to the planning and execution of Battery Management System software. This includes coordinating the efforts of team members and third-party contractors or consultants in order to deliver software according to plan. The Software Engineer will also define the projectâ€™s technical objectives and oversee quality control throughout its life cycle.
Responsibilities
  • Lead software technical development
  • Act as the primary software technical interface to the team members
  • Support customer and internal meetings as needed
  • Support release content meetings and coordinate with software project lead to develop both long and short term delivery plans
  • Create release planning in Jira and assign tickets to team members
  • Conduct technical status meeting and ensure all software artifacts are developed as per the plan and address any open points.
  • Track and report weekly status to software project leads (includes weekly progress and release challenges)
  • Review team member work products to ensure compliance with process
  • Estimate the resources and technical skills required to achieve plans
  • Coordinate day to day development challenges with software project lead
  • Develop and maintain realistic project milestones and deliverables using appropriate tools
  • Lead software anomaly triage activities and ensure that anomalies are address in a timely manner.
  • Develop best practices and tools for project execution and management
Minimum Role Requirements
  • BSCS/EE or equivalent and 5+ years of software development experience
  • Strong familiarity with project change and configuration management software, such as Jira, SVN, IBM Rational Tools, Polyspace, Crucible
  • Embedded systems and C programming experience
  • Solid working knowledge of CAN & LIN networking
  • Experience with software maturity models, such as CMMi or Automotive SPICE
  • Experience working both independently and in a team-oriented, collaborative environment
  • Reacts to project adjustments and alterations promptly and efficiently
  • Flexible during times of change
  • Strong written and oral communication skills
  • Strong interpersonal skills
  • Ability to effectively prioritize and execute tasks in a sometimes demanding environment
  • Two plus years of team leading experience
Preferred Role Requirements
  • Tier 1 supplier experience
  • Experience with UDS and OBD Diagnostics
  • Model Based Development
  • Experience with software maturity models, such as CMMi or Automotive SPICE
  • Tier 1 supplier experience
  • Software troubleshooting experience
  • Experience with CAN, LIN, CCP/XCP tools for flashing, data logging, and debugging
  • Knowledge of ISO26262
Work Environment
  • Fast-paced development environment
  • Extended hours may occasionally be required to meet project deadlines
