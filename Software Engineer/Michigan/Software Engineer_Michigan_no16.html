Full Job Description

Nestle Nutrition is a company founded on the principles of nutrition, health and wellness. At Nestle Nutrition we understand the importance of giving our consumers food and beverages that taste great and help them enrich their lives. That's what "Nestle. Good Food, Good Life" is all about.

At Nestle Infant Nutrition, supporting the healthy growth, development, and eating habits of the first 1000 days of life, remains at the heart of everything we do. OUR MISSION is to help nurture a healthier generation, one baby at a time.

Nestle Nutrition - Technical Operations Group is currently recruiting for a:

Product Development Intern

Each intern will receive project assignments related to ongoing research and/or product development for infant and/or toddler food in the Baby Meals and Drinks category. Interns will be exposed to all phases of the product development process, including benchtop/lab scale research and development, pilot scale testing, factory trials/scale up, experimental design, sensory testing, and finished product analysis. Interns will work under the supervision of an experienced mentor and will be expected to provide regular updates formally and informally throughout the internship, including a final presentation. There will be multiple opportunities to interact professionally and socially with interns in other functions. The intern will be expected to function in a cross-functional team environment in all aspects of the internship.

Three month (min) duration. Preferred start date May/June 2021 but start date can be flexible

Nestle is an 'Equal Opportunity Employer' and is looking for diversity in candidates for employment.

Graduate and undergraduate students in Food Science or Engineering: must have at least some food science or engineering curriculum completed (i.e. food chemistry, food analysis, food microbiology, food processing, material and energy balances, introduction to process design and control, etc). Previous experience in a product development course, chemistry laboratories, product development team or as a product development intern is a benefit, but not required.

Desired GPA: At least 3.0.

International Student Eligibility (Proficiency in English language preferred)

We accept international students as long as they are eligible to work in the US and can provide that documentation.
