Local Unit & Position Description:
Join the Analytics and Technical Solutions team that is developing services and products to improve the delivery of energy efficiency, demand response, storage, and renewable technology.

DNV - Energy System’s Program Development and Implementation group is seeking an Analytics Engineer to join our team. Think strategically and apply skills in data engineering, data visualization, deployment, continuous development and integration to implement full stack data solutions. This position will be based at our office in Detroit, MI or Okemos, MI

Tasks May Include
  • Develop data visualizations using business intelligence tools
  • Utilize statistical or machine learning techniques to assess customer performance and potential
  • Affinity towards problem solving and ability to perform difficult analytical tasks with limited supervision
  • Develop and maintain products that deliver insight through data visualizations to enable users to grasp concepts, identify patterns, and take action to exceed client targets
  • Embrace continuous integration and continuous delivery to add features quickly while maintaining solution stability
  • A strong communicator — you don't just focus on producing statistical outputs and charts, you can also explain what they mean and their implications
Position Qualifications:
Requirements
  • Bachelor’s degree required
    • Concentration in an analytical discipline such as Engineering, Mathematics, Statistics, Finance, or Computer Science is strongly preferred
  • At least 2 years of experience with Python and/or SQL required
  • Experience using tools such as Power BI (DAX) and/or Tableau (TQL) required
  • Familiarity with various ETL tools used to execute end to end data pipelines required
  • Willingness and ability to travel up to 15% of the time
  • Experience with Pandas, Numpy, Scikit-learn, and Jupyter notebooks preferred
  • Experience with Git, GitHub, JIRA preferred
    Experience with Django and/or Flask preferred
  • Experience with JavaScript (D3.js) and R preferred
  • Experience working in a fast-paced environment, managing multiple deliverables, and remaining flexible
  • Most importantly, this individual should display a positive, team-oriented attitude to match our friendly work environment
  • Experience in the energy industry preferred
  • Strong written and verbal English language communication skills
  • We conduct pre-employment drug and background screening
