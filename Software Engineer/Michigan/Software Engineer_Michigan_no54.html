Full Job Description

You can make a salary. Or you can make a difference. Or you can work as a Senior Engineer at Herman Miller and make both.

About this Opportunity

As a Senior Engineer, you'll apply engineering theories and concepts to assignments under minimal supervision and in support of business objectives and provide engineering leadership and expertise. You will design fixtures, machinery, and fully automated cells, conduct cycle time studies, and present the designs to end users and leadership. You'll also perform analyses to develop design options or recommendations for structures, systems, and components. In addition, you'll apply originality, ingenuity, and knowledge of other disciplines in solving complex problems. Emphasis in your work will be placed on your team participation, ability to plan, and ability to achieve results on assigned projects.



What You'll Do
You'll have opportunities to speak up, solve problems, lead others, and be an owner every day as you...

  • Actively participate in the development of engineering and quality processes and recommend process improvements.
  • Apply learned engineering skills to your work assignments.
  • Apply problem-solving skills and execute solutions to difficult problems independently.
  • Apply supply chain cost, value stream capabilities, and economic principles.
  • Coach engineering tasks and skills to others.
  • Define and complete engineering tasks with minimal oversight of leadership.
  • Identify opportunities for improvements to products and processes.
  • Independently develop and apply engineering approaches to projects and assignments.
  • Perform additional responsibilities as requested to achieve business objectives.
  • Present work output in a clear and concise manner.
  • Provide proposal and planning of projects to the budget owner using the appropriate request process.
