The Position

Gentex is seeking an Associate Software Engineer to join our team based in Zeeland, Michigan near the beautiful shores of Lake Michigan.

We are seeking candidates who are in the early stages of their career and are eager to learn and grow as part of a team. As a member of this multi-disciplinary team you will be working closely with engineers from many other teams, such as Software Development, Test and Measurement, Prototype, Product, Algorithms, etc., to support them as they develop Gentex products.

As an Associate Software Engineer at Gentex you will be responsible for the design and development of software applications that support other teams. Additionally, you will be responsible for:

  • Prioritizing Customer Service
  • Automated software testing
  • Reviewing work products (design, code, tests, documentation, etc.)
  • Documenting work products
  • Collaborating in group settings, including attending various meetings
  • Communicating and escalating key issues affecting your assigned projects
  • Meeting deadlines
  • Deploying applications to other teams

As an Associate Software Engineer you will understand and perform defined tasks. You will develop the skills to identify and triage issues to help reach resolution. You will learn to identify the steps necessary to accomplish assigned tasks. You will learn and understand the process steps in order to effectively execute the process as part of a project team member.

Effective communication is a key aspect of an engineering role at Gentex. You will communicate designs, code, tests, documentation, etc. to various stakeholders. You will communicate clearly and objectively, with the right information for the right stakeholders.
