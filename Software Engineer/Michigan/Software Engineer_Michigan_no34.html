Full Job Description

CapSquare Systems, Inc. has an opening for the position of Software Engineer (Ref# SE93AY) to work in Troy, MI and various unanticipated locations throughout the U.S. Job Duties: Design, develop, implement, test, and support business and computer applications software or specialized utility programs, including CRM applications, and major enhancements of existing systems, using Salesforce technologies. Gather and analyze user and business requirements, document specifications, and develop software solutions to meet client needs and data. Responsible for requirements analysis, architecture design, custom development, modifications, testing, maintenance, and operational support of software applications. Involved in Saleforce.com setup, configuration, customization, administration, data migration, mapping, and integration. Perform organization setup using the Salesforce configuration. Setup users with roles and profiles to access the Salesforce application. Create actions that are tailored to the specific business activities and use cases. Develop Visualforce pages, custom components, custom objects, reports, dashboards, and tabs. Migrate the application to cloud environment, evaluating data migration. Map the data from the legacy system into cloud system. Build REST API for legacy application for interacting with Salesforce cloud application. Modify existing software to correct errors, allow it to adapt to new hardware, or to improve performance. Write test classes to support unit testing and achieve minimum code coverage to meet Salesforce threshold limit for code migration. Develop and direct software testing and validation procedures, programming, and documentation. Confer with system analysts, programmers, and others to design system and obtain information on project limitations and capabilities, performance requirements, and interfaces. Store, retrieve and manipulate data for analysis of system capabilities and requirements. Providing technical and post-production support. Use of Salesforce, Service Cloud, Force.com IDE, Lightning Platform, Apex, Apex Classes, Visualforce, Salesforce Object Query Language (SOQL), Salesforce Object Search Language (SOSL), Apex Data Loader, Java, J2EE, JavaScript, Eclipse, ANT, Jenkins, IBM Rational Team Concert (RTC), SQL Server, UNIX, and Windows. Travel and/or relocation required. Requirements: This position requires a Master’s degree or foreign academic equivalent in Computer Science, Electrical / Electronics Engineering, CIS, MIS, or a related field. Plus 2 years of experience in the job offered or in a related occupation. Will accept a Bachelor’s degree plus 5 years of progressive experience in lieu of the required education and experience. Position requires 1 year of experience with Salesforce, Force.com IDE, Apex, Visualforce, SOQL, SOSL, and Eclipse. Will accept any suitable combination of education, training, or experience as per the requirements cited herein. 40hrs/week.
