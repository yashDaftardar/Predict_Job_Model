Who we are:
Dynatrace is software intelligence for the enterprise cloud! What you will be a part of:
The Detroit office is the epicenter for Dynatrace Customer Services. The R&D team in Detroit focuses on “Digital Business”. Digital Business is a combination of platforms, tools and services to deliver an unrivaled, world-class customer lifecycle experience. The software services developed in Digital Business range from the Dynatrace product administration portal, to customer intelligence tools, to various communication and support functions. With these digital services we get deep insights into the customer journey enabling us to offer proactive support and services, identify new business opportunities, drive operational excellence and ultimately ensure customer success. The R&D team focuses on a BizDevOps (Business, Development, Operations) approach to software development that encourages developers, operations staff and business teams to work together so the organization can develop software more quickly, be more responsive to user demand and ultimately maximize revenue.

The Role:
As Dynatrace grows its platform, services and SaaS offerings, the Digital Business team needs to accelerate development and ensure our customers have a world class experience with Dynatrace. Join the rapidly growing dev team in our Detroit Lab, advance the “Digital Business” Platform, and innovate on a day-to-day. As a Full Stack Engineer, you and the team will own the end-to-end development and continuous improvement on the platform while using full stack web technologies like React, NodeJS/NestJS, and AWS.

Qualifications/Requirements:

    • 2+ years of experience designing and building complex JavaScript web based software systems
    • Demonstrable Full Stack development experience with React and Node.JS in Typescript.
    • Knowledge of multiple modern computer languages and frameworks with an understanding of using the right technology for the right purpose
    • Experience with global development teams working on the same platform
    • Experience with writing testable code and driven by best practices
    • Agile development process knowledge/experience
    • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field

What will make you stand out:

    • "Technologist" mindset, using the right technologies for the right reasons

    • Strong communication and presentation skills
    • Self-motivated with a strong work ethic
    • Convinced that continuous delivery needs continuous feedback
