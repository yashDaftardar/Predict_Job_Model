Job Description & Qualifications

The Connectivity Software Engineer will be part of the Research and Advanced Engineering team at Ford Motor Company and work on developing next generation vehicle connectivity software platforms, staying in sync with industry standards, and crafting scalable and portable in vehicle and cloud software solutions. The position requires developing connectivity software projects along with designing, implementing, and providing the requirements for the production. Work in a creative environment to explore, develop, and advance software and hardware for smart vehicles in an intelligent world.

Responsibilities

  • Design and implement Connectivity Software applications for In Vehicle ECU’s and provide the interfacing API’s to cloud and other ECU’s
  • Develop complex technical specifications at vehicle sub-system level and correlating with ecosystem level architecture specifications by developing necessary Proof of Concepts
  • Develop methods for data collection using AI/ML techniques.
  • Demonstrate project features to feature owners through proof of concepts using target hardware, production hardware platform, and with simulation tools to feature owners
  • Develop research proposals in the area of vehicle connectivity with universities and external partners
  • Collaborates with a team of multi-functional domain authorities for developing vehicle connectivity architecture
  • Perform analyses of emerging automotive connectivity technologies
  • Socialize findings and demonstrate proof of concepts to managers and production teams
Basic Qualifications
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering or related degree
  • 2+ years of experience in computer and automotive networking using TCP/IP, Ethernet and/or CAN
  • 2+ years of experience with programming C/C++ and/or Java
  • 1+ year of experience in Machine Learning
  • 1+ year of experience in handling Computer Vision
Preferred Qualifications
  • Master’s Degree or PhD in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering or related degree
  • 2+ years of experience working with Machine Learning, AI and Computer Vision
  • Experience with distributed feature development
  • Experience with vehicle to cloud communication protocols
  • Hands-on experience with developing Embedded systems software
  • Experience in Linux and QNX operating systems
  • Hands-on experience in C, C++, and Java as well as inter-process communication protocols
