Full Job Description

The Senior Middleware Engineer operates as the subject matter expert of middleware technology to manage all activities in full lifecycle of middleware service. This role collaborates with stakeholders from application and infrastructure team based in multiple regions to provide consistent and qualified service delivery.

Provide professional consultancy and support in response to the queries from developers and application team.

Provide technical supports on ESB, EDI, MQ and other middleware product for system build, configuration, performance tuning, trouble-shooting, upgrade and vulnerability patching.

Collaborate with architects, developers, project managers to deliver suitable middleware solution which can cope with the business need.

Study, design and implement new middleware technology according to the standard and governance.

Ability to support middleware service for NA region.

Ability to play the role of a technical lead about middleware technology on projects.

Ability to perform assigned tasks independently and complete deliverables on time.

On call for emergency support as needed.
