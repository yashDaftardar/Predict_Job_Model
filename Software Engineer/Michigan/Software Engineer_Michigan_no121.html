THE COMPANY
Continental Automotive is one of the largest automotive manufacturers in the world and a leader in automated driving. Through our pioneering technologies and services, we’re changing the future of mobility to make it more safe, smart, and sustainable. Our Auburn Hills, MI location is seeking a Security and Privacy SW Engineer to join our team.

Are you ready to shape the future with us?

THE POSITION
The Cybersecurity Software Engineer in Continental VNI HMI, Detroit area will be responsible for the design and development of cybersecurity embedded software for products like Instrument Cluster, Head-Up Display, and HPC cockpit products. Will integrate 3rd party cybersecurity SW modules, create cybersecurity related new module or review existing modules and software systems. In addition, he or she will follow Continental Security and Privacy (S&P) processes to work with global S&P team to develop S&P plan, perform TARA at system level, implement countermeasure including SW solutions, and make sure a proper testing is being conducted.
  • Work with global Cybersecurity teams to safe launch, provide guidance and work hand-in-hand with security, software, testing and manufacturing engineers on threat analysis and risk assessment (TARA), architecture, design, implementation and verification for security tasks including key management
  • Support global cybersecurity team to develop technical solutions to help mitigate security vulnerabilities from HW and SW attacks, document security requirements and support process improvement initiatives
  • Support the customer interface discussions, technical design reviews and business quotations on cybersecurity aspects
  • Develop and maintain cybersecurity requirements that comply with internal policy, customer cybersecurity requirements and external regulations to meet functional and security objectives
  • Develop and implement cybersecurity related SW solutions for secure boot, secure diagnostic, authenticated communication, secure programming, and OTA.

Qualifications


BASIC QUALIFICATIONS
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field
  • Minimum of 2 years of experience of software security implementation in embedded real-time operating systems, experience in QNX, Android, and embedded Linux is a big plus.
  • 5+ years of embedded software experience in Automotive industry and familiar with software language such as C, and C++.
  • Good knowledge and understanding of FCA and/or Ford Cybersecurity requirements
  • Knowledge of symmetric and asymmetric cryptographic algorithms
  • Experience in FOTA design and implementation
  • Familiarity with device security concepts such as Secure boot (using HSM, SHE etc.), Secure programming, Secure communications (CAN-FD, Ethernet etc.) and applied cryptography
  • Working knowledge of Keys, random number generation, and handling of crypto material
  • 2+ years of experience of software security implementation in embedded real-time operating systems, experience in QNX, Android, and embedded Linux is a plus.
  • Ability to work effectively in a team-oriented environment.
  • Strong technical communication skills (written and verbal)
