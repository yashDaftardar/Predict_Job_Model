Job Description & Qualifications

Ford Motor Company was built on the belief that freedom of movement drives human progress. It’s a belief that has always fueled our passion to create great cars and trucks. And today, it drives our commitment to become the world’s most trusted mobility company, designing vehicles for a smart world that help people move more safely, confidently and freely. To do this, we are investing in technology, and businesses focusing on micro-transit, commercial solutions for fleet customers, enhancing the vehicle ownership experience and developing the Transportation Mobility Cloud, which will host mobility-related solutions.

This request is to fill a Software Engineer position within the Enterprise Connectivity organization. The Software Engineer role will work on a balanced team and collaborate with a Product Owner, Product Manager, and Product Designer to deliver products to support our customers. The team will deliver a portfolio of products providing robust customer experiences across the globe. The Software Engineer will deliver value through Agile software development practices in a fast-paced collaborative environment.

What you’ll be able to do:
  • Software engineer with responsibilities to define, design, develop, deploy and support services and applications in a micro service / cloud-based architecture.
  • Ensure functional and non-functional quality and on-time delivery using agile and lean principles.
  • Embrace extreme programming practices including paired development, test driven development, and continuous delivery to release early and often.
  • Collaborate and work closely together with a balanced team of developers.

The minimum requirements we seek:
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field
  • 3+ years’ Java/Kotlin development experience building micro service and cloud-based solutions using Spring Platform (Spring MVC, Spring Boot, Spring Cloud) (including internship/co-op/course work)
  • 3+ years’ JavaScript experience for front end user experience or similar (Vue.js, React, or Angular) (including internship/co-op/course work)
  • 2+ years’ web service delivery experience (using REST) for backend services (including internship/co-op/course work)
  • 1+ years’ experience with cloud environments (Google Cloud Platform, Google Kubernetes Engine, Azure, Pivotal Cloud Foundry, AWS or similar) (including internship/co-op/course work)

Our preferred requirements:
  • Experience with Test-Driven Development and Agile Methodologies.
  • Experience with software development tools (backlog management, source code management, automated and unit test management, CI/CD, etc)
  • Familiarity with eXtreme programming:
    • Pair and/or Mob programming
  • Proven ability to work both individually and collaboratively
  • Strong communication skills and ability to think above and beyond baseline requirements.

What you’ll receive in return:
As part of the Ford family, you’ll enjoy excellent compensation and a comprehensive benefits package that includes generous PTO, retirement, savings and stock investment plans, incentive compensation and much more. You’ll also experience exciting opportunities for professional and personal growth and recognition.

If you have what it takes to help us redefine the future of mobility, we’d love to have you join us.

Visa sponsorship may be available for this position.
