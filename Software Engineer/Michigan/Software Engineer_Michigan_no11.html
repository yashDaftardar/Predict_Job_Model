Tyler Technologies is looking for a talented Software Engineer to join our Public Safety product development team. The engineer will be responsible for the design, development, and maintenance of mission-critical software applications used by 911 dispatchers and police officers. Our ideal candidate will have a depth of knowledge and skills in software design and development using Microsoft .NET platform technologies. The engineer will have an opportunity to work in highly collaborative teams that follow Agile practices and bring new ideas and creative thinking to our team.

Responsibilities

  • Be involved through the entire lifecycle of analysis, design, coding, testing, and implementation, and support
  • Follow the development practices of the public safety product teams, including test-driven development
  • Ensure the ongoing success of projects by designing high-quality technical solutions
  • Collaborate with other software developers, business analysts, and software architects to solve complex technical problems
  • Continually grow personal skillset and knowledge base with independent research

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related technical field, or equivalent work experience
  • 1+ years of experience in designing and programming applications
  • 1+ years of object-oriented design experience
  • 1+ years of C# development experience with a good knowledge of its ecosystem
  • Familiarity with the .NET framework, multi-threaded, and asynchronous programming models
  • Strong communication skills especially around technical team interaction
  • Ability to adapt and learn new technologies quickly and proactively

Location

Remote | Troy, Michigan

0-5%
