Job Description :

Key Objective

The Integration Lead role is responsible for the design, development and operations of the systems-to-system interfaces, dataflows, APIs, and integration middleware for the Ignite platform. It combines aspect of integration and technical architect skills to model to systems involved, lead the development of the APIs and select the integration approaches and patterns to use, with technical management skills through the delivery lifecycle to plan and manage the transitions and dependencies and manage any 3rd parties involved, and senior/lead developer skills to hands-on lead key aspects of the build and quality assure the rest.


Main Duties & Responsibilities

** Frontending the systems integration discussions with customer and different vendors to align the integrations with the Ignite delivery plan.

** Continuous updating the knowledge on the Ignite APIs to stay updated with the latest enhahcements in the platform.

** Define the integration technology needed, and the transitionary steps needed to ensure continuity of the systems, data and business.

** Defining the integration and dataflow architectures, and collaborate with development teams to design the APIs that can be a point of stability to develop against in the short term and will endure as the platform technologies around the changes

** Creating and managing a best-in-class, complete and managed external-facing API layer to be used for business-partner integration, to support external thick-clients and by third-party applications

and services

** Leading the selection of specialist integration technology required, and being the prime technical contact person for any vendors or support partners

** Collaborating with the support-teams to/around production incidents involving integration to directly or indirectly ensure their timely resolution and recovery

and preventative measures are taken to avoid recurrence.

** Developing a strong team, skills and culture around integration that crosses the development, support, infrastructure and data teams




Role Requirements and Personal Qualities

** Experience in and opinions on good and bad approaches to systems integration, software development, message-based middleware and SOA, APIs, API management, data modelling, legacy integration techniques inc DB staging tables and stored procedures

** Expertise in many key technical aspects of integration such as how REST differs from SOAP, and pub-sub differs from request-reply, when to use message queuing vs not, and whether an ESB is a good thing

** Experience with developing integration in many technologies and languages, including some of Java, Open Source web languages such as Javascript, PHP and Python, as well as scripting in Linux and other OSs using FTP/SFTP/etc,

** Experience with Agile Methodologies such as Scrum and XP, but also with more traditional structured approaches

** Expertise with distributed development and major version control systems such as SVN and Git, automated testing and modern development approaches.

** Good communicator at multiple levels and across multiple disciplines

** Team Player, able to provide input into a multifunctional team

** Self-motivated, capable of working with limited supervision, pro-actively reporting on status and tasks

** Adaptable. The role requires a willingness to take on tasks outside of a narrowly defined role, and ideally a desire to be multi-skilled

** Capable of working to both tactical and strategic timescales

Duration : 6 months
