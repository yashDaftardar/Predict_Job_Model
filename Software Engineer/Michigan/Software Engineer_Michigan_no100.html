Full Job Description

Job Summary:

As a Web Developer you will design, create, test and modify Connected Services class-leading web-based automotive business intelligence tool. Creativity and self-motivation are essential for this role.

Your Role:

  • Work as a member of a team designing and developing the world’s most advanced web-based automotive business intelligence tool.

  • Collaborate with systems analysts, engineers, programmers, and others to design systems and to obtain information on performance requirements and interfaces.

  • Work with users to develop and implement acceptance test plans to ensure that product meets customer requirements and expectations.

  • Develop and direct software system testing and validation procedures, programming, and documentation.

  • Stay current in training of the latest software and computer technology.

Your Background:

  • Bachelor’s Degree – Computer Science/Engineering, preferred.

  • 3- 5 years of solid web development experience working with latest technologies.

  • Strong working experience with object oriented programming.

  • Front end development experience with React, Typescript, HTML, Javascript, and CSS.

  • Good problem solving skills and being able to come up with designs for complex engineering problems.

  • Strong written and oral communication skills.

  • Enjoy working in a high-energy, unstructured environment as part of a project team.

  • Have the ability to work under pressure with strict timelines.

  • Passionate for technology, software, and data.

Additional Background Preferences:

  • Familiar with UI design, user interaction experience, creating UI templates and components.

  • Experience working with source code management tools in a shared code environment.

  • Knowledge of debugging tools and performance optimization.
