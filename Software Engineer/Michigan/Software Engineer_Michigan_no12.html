With a focus on Mobility, Operational Excellence, Value to our Customers and the Electrification of vehicles, you can expect to be part of something exciting. From the sleek design of our vehicles to the unique opportunities we offer around the globe, Nissan exemplifies ingenuity in everything we do. Our people are what drive the business forward.

We’re currently looking for an Infotainment Test Engineer to join our team in Farmington Hills, MI .

Job functions include but are not limited to:

  • Perform vehicle validation of the Infotainment System and be technical lead for the Navigation and Rear Camera components

  • Oversee and support with software development by: managing the program schedule, coordinating and performing system testing in support of safe launch, and align with Japan/Supplier to bring issues to closure. (system tests include audio, NAVI, camera, BTHF, Carplay, Android Auto, Meter/Cluster communication, TCU, etc.)

  • Manage a vehicle program’s deliverables, such as progress meeting materials, test completion status, etc., as well as internally manage the test scheduling for team members within the Audio test team Negotiate with manufacturing personnel and conduct validation of infotainment systems at manufacturing facilities

  • Lead and support customer usage testing of multiple different systems

  • Coordinate technician time in support of vehicle validation deadlines

  • Report vehicle program status to upper management and write weekly report

  • Utilize various test equipment to analyze and root cause issues (CANalyzer, voltmeter, power supply, oscilloscope, etc.)

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in a related Engineering discipline

  • 1-3 years of directly related professional-level Engineering experience

  • Experience in automotive electronics preferably with Infotainment features such as, Navigation, Rear Camera, Vehicle Settings, Bluetooth, Android Auto, Apple Carplay, Configuration, etc.

  • Familiar with CANalyzer, communication tools and electrical diagnostics tools

  • Good understanding of the user experience utilizing infotainment functions in the vehicle

  • Hands on experience conducting functional validation and regression testing is preferred

  • Well versed with upcoming / trending technologies within Infotainment and connected field

  • Firm understanding of automotive technology and engineering practices, with a hands –on background in the automotive industry

  • Thorough computer knowledge such as word processor, spreadsheet, other PC applications, or mainframe applications to resolve complex problems in day to day situations

  • Between 25% and 50% day and overnight travel
