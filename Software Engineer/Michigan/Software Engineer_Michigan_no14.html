Integration Engineer (Software Coordinator)

Location: Northville, MI, US, 48168

Req ID 17657

We are seeking a new colleague to join our Controls & Calibration department in Northville, MI!



Responsibilities

  • Acts as the central technical point of contact for implementing functional software changes and is responsible for the planning of such changes according to internal and external requirements within R&D projects assigned. The SWC represents the assigned software project(s) within the Project Core Team and to the customer. In addition, the SWC is responsible for customer software product development initiatives and to ensure the seamless and timely integration of ZF software products into customer engineering projects by applying ZF processes.
  • Managing tasks for the Development Project Team for a software (SW) project and providing direction/instruction to project team members.
  • Responsible for the development scope of a SW project within the Project Core Team.
  • Representing the development scope of a SW project vis-à-vis to the ZF expert departments and the ZF System Team, as well as to the customer.
  • Main point of contact for the customer, which includes conducting regular customer development status meetings and technical reviews, as well as synchronizing the customer/supplier product evolution process (PEP).
  • Overseeing software content, creating schedules and adhere to deadline of milestones of the assigned development projects while concurring with global Program Management directions.
  • Reviewing requirements to ensure compliance with engineering principals, ZF company standards, customer
  • specifications, and the regulatory framework.
  • Being the liaison for software between the R&D basic development team (worldwide), the core technology team (worldwide) and the direct customer team (North America).
  • Collecting and presenting effort estimations of planned software changes from internal teams.
  • Determining feasibility of requested software changes regarding local and global government regulations, ZF internal processes and availability at coding and integration departments.
  • Participating in customer milestone planning meetings and hosting internal time planning meetings.
  • Acting as the main ZF contact for release timing planning conflicts with customers. The SWC has full responsibility to deliver software releases on milestone dates provided by the customer.
  • Supporting root cause analysis and error correction of occurring software problems.
  • Documenting the Production Recommendation Process (PRP) of every software release to the customer.
  • Collaborating and developing good working relationships with customers and ZF departments worldwide.
  • Participating in continuous internal improvement trainings and providing support to team efforts.
  • Maintaining professional and technical knowledge by attending educational workshops, formal training, reviewing professional publications, establishing, and expanding professional networks, and participating in professional societies.

Qualifications

  • B.S. in Engineering (Electrical, Mechanical, Software, etc.) and 5+ years experience in the Automotive and/or Electronics industries, including project experience
  • Legally authorized to work in the U.S. without sponsorship now or in the future.
  • Ability to interface with cross-functional teams and all levels of management
  • Self-starter with strong problem solving and analytical skills
  • Excellent project planning and organizational abilities
  • Able to work independently and manage multiple responsibilities
  • Superior interpersonal and communication skills to mediate among different projects on critical decisions
  • Strong drive for teamwork with a customer-oriented mindset
