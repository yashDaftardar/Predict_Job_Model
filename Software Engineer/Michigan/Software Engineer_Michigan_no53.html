Overview:

Perficient currently has a career opportunity for a QA Automation Tester in in our Livonia, MI, office.

At Perficient you’ll deliver mission-critical technology and business solutions to Fortune 500 companies and some of the most recognized brands on the planet. And you’ll do it with cutting-edge technologies, thanks to our close partnerships with the world’s biggest vendors. Our network of offices across North America, as well as locations in India and China, will give you the opportunity to spread your wings, too.

We’re proud to be publicly recognized as a “Top Workplace” year after year. This is due, in no small part, to our entrepreneurial attitude and collaborative spirit that sets us apart and keeps our colleagues impassioned, driven, and fulfilled.

Responsibilities:
  • Design QA automation strategy and lead testing efforts
  • Helps establish project test strategy, methodologies, test plans and assist with test execution
  • Create manual/automated test scripts by leveraging requirements documentations, wireframes, and comps
  • Participate in standing team meetings
  • Contribute to weekly status reports, interact with clients and vendors as needed
  • Build trusted client relationships through proactive account management and effective project delivery
  • Establishes knowledge of client business challenges and priorities to translate into opportunities
  • Maintains regular client communication and satisfaction, ensuring opportunities and challenges are addressed in a timely manner.
  • Helps deliver presentations to clients and management teams
  • Contributes to project discoveries, kickoff, prepare proposals and statements of work following company standards.
  • Works with customers and internal creative teams to establish approved wireframes and mock-ups for solutions
  • Helps to establish critical path, milestones, delivery dates, and review client deliverables to ensure they meet client acceptance criteria.
  • Provides leadership throughout the lifecycles of a project, guiding the understanding the project team has through design and development to ensure project requirements are met
  • Drive quality control around CI/CD pipeline process and implementations
Qualifications:
  • 7+ years of software testing experience
  • 5+ years of experience developing test automation frameworks
  • Proven track record of successfully implementing and deploying automation frameworks across web, API and mobile platforms
  • STRONG hands-on automation expertise using Selenium, Protractor, Cypress or any JavaScript based automation frameworks
  • Experience with Java script, HTML/CSS to support writing automated tests
  • Experience with Web Services automation tools using SoapUI, Postman and Rest Assured
  • In depth knowledge of Java, C# or Python
  • Strong hands-on experience using Azure devops and building QA pipelines
  • Ability to read code and participate in code reviews
  • Knowledge of the technical stack in an n-tier application and the ability to identify different types of testing that can be applied in each tier
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to analyze business requirements and technical design documents
  • Design, develop and execute test cases
  • Ability to understand and respond strategically to client needs
  • Knowledge of Agile and iterative development methodologies
  • Highly detail oriented, well organized, and able to coordinate multiple projects simultaneously
  • Be research savvy and be a subject matter expert of current industry-wide quality & test processes, tools and technologies
