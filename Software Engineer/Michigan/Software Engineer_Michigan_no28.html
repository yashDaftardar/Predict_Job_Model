Known for our innovative components, Mitsubishi Electric Automotive America manufactures autonomous-ready infotainment and ADAS solutions, premium audio systems, high-definition displays, and powertrain electronics for standard, electric, and hybrid vehicles. Since 1979 we have served the major automotive, heavy-duty truck, and coach manufacturers across the US, and continue to grow each year! Be a part of a select team that is making a real impact on the automotive mobility of tomorrow!


This is a long term contracting Infotainment Engineer position providing device mirroring support, utilizing experience in the area of Apple CarPlay and Android Auto certification. They will assist the Product Development team and other feature teams in performing activities related to device mirroring (DVM).


  • Create documentation for Device Mirroring with respect to Apple and Google Projection requirements.
  • Work through self-certification activities for Device Mirroring.
  • Monitor and evaluate the performance of software releases & Create test reports.
  • Work with customers and support certification.
  • Collaborate with global teams working on Projection software solutions.
  • Schedule test runs and test the software in real time whenever required.
  • Collect data from the tests and flag risks.
  • Planning and scheduling certification activities.


Qualifications

  • Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science or similar technical discipline, or equivalent in other education and training.
  • 2+ years of work experience in vehicle infotainment services.
  • Experience with CarPlay and Android Auto certification a plus.
  • Working Knowledge of JIRA & Confluence.
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills.
