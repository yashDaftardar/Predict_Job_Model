Software Engineer

Location: Livonia, MI, US, 48150

Req ID 17543 | Livonia Tech I, United States


ZF Braking is seeking a highly motivated Software Engineer to work as part of a global team developing and releasing software products for real-time, safety-critical, automotive products.

Responsibilities may include development and analysis of software requirements, high-level design, detailed design, software development, implementation, integration, and/or validation of the software. The candidate will apply the appropriate software standards, processes, procedures and tools throughout the software development life cycle. The candidate will be expected to take a “systems view” and exhibit troubleshooting skills in the lab and on the vehicle. As such the candidate must be capable of interfacing with both internal and external stakeholders.


Requirements:

  • Bachelor degree in Electrical, Computer Engineering, and/or Computer Science.
  • Knowledge of C/C++
  • Embedded Systems software (development, programming, debugging)
  • Ability to work well within a team and with stakeholders
  • Proficient in English
