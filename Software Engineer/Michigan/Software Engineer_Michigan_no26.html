Title: Associate Software Engineer

Location: Ann Arbor, Michigan

Position Summary

This is an entry-level position to develop and maintain software solutions utilizing analysis, design and coding skills. There is a fair amount of cross-team collaboration within Infor on projects requiring good team and communications skills, both written and verbal.

Please note pursuant to government requirements tied to FedRAMP, this specific position requires US Citizenship status.

A Day in The Life Typically Includes :

  • Analysis and design of software solutions for business issues
  • Using Java to code programs that fulfill customer requirements and needs
  • Communication and collaboration with various teams within Infor
  • Requirements gathering to aid in the design of software solutions
  • Developing written specifications detailing software solutions to requirements
  • Providing technical assistance to implementation and support teams

What You Will Need :

Basic Qualifications:

  • BS in Computer Science or equivalent
  • Java Development Experience
  • Javascript, HTML5, XSLT, JSP, SQL
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Windows environments and Microsoft Office Applications
  • Excellent written and verbal communications
  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Ability to work alone or in teams

What Will Put You Ahead?

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with supply chain solutions is a plus
  • Automotive industry knowledge is a plus
  • EDI knowledge is a plus
