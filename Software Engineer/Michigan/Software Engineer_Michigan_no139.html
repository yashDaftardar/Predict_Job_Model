Software Engineer III, Infotainment - DENSO TEN

The Software Engineer is involved in efforts for system integration and system development while interfacing with OEM program team, partner suppliers, internal program team, and corporate product development team. Involved with ensuring that all the features of the infotainment head unit perform at the optimal system efficiency to reduce the processing time and increase reliability.

Job Duties & Responsibilities:
1.) Track efforts on system integration involving server applications, smartphone applications, and embedded communication modules on the vehicle side. Work with team to ensure that all the features of the infotainment head unit perform at the optimal system efficiency to reduce the processing time and increase reliability.
2.) Gain in-depth understanding of questions, concerns, and issues in order to get quick resolution from customers, partner suppliers, and corporate product development team.
3.) Track this information and report issues to related entities in appropriate format.
4.) Serve as a point of contact for our customers, partner suppliers, and the corporate product development team regarding the design, specifications, testing and debugging of system integration.
5.) Ability to analyze existing external and internal object-oriented, embedded software to add features, make modifications, and/or port to different platforms.
6.) Recommend proposals on functions, interface specifications, and application specifications as needed for continuous improvement concerning development efficiency, and quality/performance of the final product.
7.) Prepare proof of concept/working prototypes as part of architectural planning and development when necessary.
8.) Review and recommend software related specifications and requirements. Identifies product requirements/specification or performance issues.
9.) Prepare necessary documentation, material, reports, etc. for meetings with customers, partner suppliers, and corporate product development team.
10.) Monitor and track work progress of partner suppliers periodically and conduct design reviews to identify trouble and reflection at early stages for prevention of delivery delay and quality issues.
11.) Recommend and maintain system validation plan in cooperation with partner suppliers and corporate product development team.
12.) Perform system tests in accordance with validation plan and suggest opportunities for improvement at our head office.
13.) The job is not only limited to testing, it will also require regular paperwork, reporting, analysis, vehicle prep, and basic lab and equipment set up.
14.) Evaluation may include long drive for testing purposes, which indicated that the position will spend extensive amount of time in vehicle for testing.
15.) Customer plant support during product launch; engineering support for Manufacturing as needed.
16.) Maintain awareness of technology and market trends relating to IT security, server applications, and smartphone applications in North America, and prepare reports as necessary.

Minimum Qualifications:
  • Bachelor's degree in Software Engineer, Computer Science, or Computer Engineering
  • 5+ years' experience
  • Strong background with coding and debugging
  • Expertise in designing software validation approaches
  • Familiar with both internal and external infotainment software architectures
  • Strong with C++
  • Familiar with Hypervisors, and how they relate to the infotainment domain space
  • Familiar with Open Source Software (OSS) the rules for usage, and how best to include in automotive systems
  • Completed or participated in at least one ground up automotive development product
  • Ability to prepare and perform technical presentations to OEM's

Preferred Qualifications:
  • Background with Java and/or Python
  • Exposure to Android development and operating systems
