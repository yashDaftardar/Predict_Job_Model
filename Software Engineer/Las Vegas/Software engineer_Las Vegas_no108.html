Responsibilities: A Day in the Life
As a Web Developer II you will be one of the pillars connecting the floor and ceiling, holding the structure in place. You will be developing and supporting both internal and public-facing web sites. You will be responsible for the coding, implementation, and maintenance of websites and web applications, both front-end (user interface) and back-end (content management system and server). You will work directly with the client providing guidance and assistance in day-to-day matters related to the products you build/support for them. Your involvement will vary depending on where you are needed most
Qualifications: Role Prerequisites - What knowledge, skills and experience you already have.

  • Solid experience (3-5 years) in web application development including its operational components (HTTP/S, Web Servers, DNS)
  • Solid experience (3-5 years) in front end web design (HTML5, CSS2/CSS3, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, JQuery, JSON, Bootstrap. ASP.Net)
  • Well-rounded JavaScript programming experience, including experience with at least one JS frameworks such s React, Angular, or Cue
  • Solid experience with web service integrations and working with APIs (REST, SOAP)
  • Experience with build tools (such as Grunt, Gulp, Bower, NPM)
  • Particular skills emphasis on CSS/ASS, Cross browser (desktop/devices).
  • Experience with WCAG guidelines for accessible web sites, including ADA compliance with PDF files.
  • Solid experience working with and developing websites for and with content management systems.
  • Experience working with WordPress, Drupal, Ruby on Rails and other CMS.
  • Expert with the Adobe Creative Suite: Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign.
  • SEO and Google Analytics experience.

Core Capabilities - What you will bring to this role and how can deliver the expectations of this position.

  • Ability to conduct training and one on one help related to website design and support.
  • Develop clear and concise written communication and documentation (writing samples may be requested).
  • Working knowledge of relevant social media.
  • Knowledge of developing mobile applications for iOS and/or Android a plus.
  • Team member and champion in the development and implementation of a new corporate website.
  • Take responsibility for adding content consistent with the design and style of the corporate website.
  • Proactive nature with resourcefulness to identify challenges and propose solutions.
  • Manage several projects simultaneously, while meeting deadlines.
  • Attention to detail and strong organizational and communication skills.
  • Stay current with emerging trends and technological advances in the web design industry.
  • Prior higher education experience is a plus.
