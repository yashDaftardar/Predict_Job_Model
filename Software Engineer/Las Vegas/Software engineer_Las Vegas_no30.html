Your Opportunity: Curtis 1000 is looking for a Software Development Supervisor in our Bloomington, IL location. The Software Development Supervisor is responsible for all aspects of software development for internal and external applications. This position will oversee the onshore and offshore personnel involved in software development and is responsible for the successful technical planning and implementation of web solutions used by clients. The Software Development Supervisor also participate in project planning during the business development stage. Additionally, this position is responsible for setting and adopting standards, processes, tools and platforms and providing oversight of these standards and processes in carrying out world class web development, implementation, and automation.

Your Responsibilities:
  • Working with the product team, manages each project’s documented scope and timeline.
  • Coordinate and facilitate sprints, retrospective meetings, daily stand-ups and sprint demos.
  • Coach team members in Scrum and development life cycle.
  • Facilitate internal communication and effective collaboration with the team, stakeholders, and client.
  • Be the point of contact for communications to internal stakeholders.
  • Work with product owners to handle backlogs and new requests.
  • Resolve conflicts and remove obstacles that occur.
  • Help teams adapt and implement changing priorities effectively.
  • Ensure deliverables are up to quality standards at the end of each sprint.
  • Guide development teams to higher Scrum maturity.
  • Identifies needs, coordinates schedules, and monitors results of the team’s professional development.
  • Implements and enforces processes, policies, and procedures.
  • Help build a productive environment where team members ‘own’ the product and enjoy working on it.
  • Supervises team on-shore and off-shore: Reports directly to the Software Development Manager.

You Must Have:
  • Bachelor's degree in a Technology related area.
  • 4+ years of experience in the field or in a related area.
  • 5+ years IT management and/or technical project management experience.
  • 5+ year’s software / applications development management experience. Experience should include hands-on experience writing code and technical lead experience.
  • Extensive knowledge of software development methodology.
  • Excellent knowledge of SDLC and Scrum Techniques.
  • Microsoft Office product suite (Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint, Visio).

We Would Also Prefer:
  • Knowledge of or experience in direct marketing industry, ad agency protocol, graphic arts and/or printing industries will be helpful.
