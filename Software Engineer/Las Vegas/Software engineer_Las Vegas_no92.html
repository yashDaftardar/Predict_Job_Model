Location:

Las Vegas, Nevada

Become one of the stars behind The SHOW and become part of the world’s most powerful entertainment brands. Our Company has one exciting mission: To entertain the human race.

The Executive Director, Engineering will be responsible for the hiring, team structure and leading software development and execution for complex new Customer Insights and Growth (CIG) software systems, such as our Customer Data Platform, Recommendations, Realtime Communications Orchestration, or Loyalty products. This is an Engineering Management and Leadership role that requires both seasoned management skills as well as superb technical skills. While this position does not require hands on coding, the Executive Director will be well versed in modern software patterns and distributed systems architecture. This person will report to the Vice President of CIG and will have responsibility for the design, build, and operations of his/her software.

PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Collaborate with cross‐functional teams to develop and drive our identity strategy and roadmap across the products.

  • Provide technical leadership and expertise for designing and implementing powerful, secure and highly‐available identity services.

  • Recruit, train and mentor a team of and data and software engineers.

  • Investigate new and developing technologies as they emerge in industry and academia and determine how to leverage these new technologies into our software solutions

  • Influence MGM service strategy and help to drive data platform initiatives

  • Create and maintain a work environment that promotes client service, teamwork, performance feedback, individual recognition, mutual respect, and employee satisfaction ensuring quality hiring, training, and succession planning processes that encompass the company s diversity commitment

  • Perform other job‐related duties as requested.

REQUIRED:

  • Bachelor’s degree in a related field, or equivalent experience

  • Four (4) years of relevant experience

  • Expertise in a broad range of cloud technologies, including IaaS, PaaS, Serverless, and the application of these technologies to solving business problems.

  • Hands‐on experience in the design, development and delivery of complex, cloud‐based services.

PREFERRED:

  • 10+ years of progressive experience in Software Engineering and online services.

  • Extensive experience in NoSQL database technologies.

  • Experience with RESTful services.

  • Strong understanding of encryption and security concepts.

  • Expertise in an object‐oriented programming language and design patterns.

  • Distributed systems.

  • Experience in a team practicing Combined Engineering/DevOps approach.

CERTIFICATIONS, LICENSES, REGISTRATIONS:

  • Gaming registration in applicable jurisdictions may be required

  • Proof of eligibility to work in the United States
