Full Job Description

Location:

Las Vegas, Nevada

PRIMARY PURPOSE:

Troubleshoots software and hardware failures and identifies network problems when they relate to personal computers. Strong understanding of Windows operating systems, experience with all Microsoft Office products as well as troubleshooting experience and understanding of e-mail. Provides technical on-site maintenance and support for new and existing systems. This position will also provide second level support, including resolving and completing ServiceNow tickets, process refinement and create or update documentation. This job may include any aspect of field support, and is not limited to system hardware and software, PC’s, and IP networking. Other duties as assigned.

PRINCIPAL DUTIES, and RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Work on hardware/software issues on company supported devices
  • Break fix for desktop, printer, point of sale, tablet, wireless (etc.) devices
  • Work on projects as necessary
  • Create/update documentation as necessary
  • Track work time, update ticket status and resolutions in ServiceNow ticketing system.
  • Participate in stakeholder discussions identify opportunities to improve design, delivery or execution of services.
  • Perform all other job-related duties as assigned.

KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, and ABILITIES:

  • Customer-focused mindset
  • IT desktop services disciplines absolutely required.
  • Demonstrated leadership to raise process awareness and issues to executive leadership, engage leaders and stakeholders in understanding their roles in delivering solutions.
  • Excellent communication, documentation and reporting skills utilizing MS. tools and ability to learn the environment quickly.
  • Results oriented, willing to take responsibility and accept accountability for action. Standardized Job Description.
  • Genuine desire to provide superior customer service. Strong verbal and written communications skills.
  • Strong interpersonal and collaboration skills.

MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS:

  • High School Diploma or equivalent
  • Three (3) years of experience in Information Technology in a similar role.

PREFERRED:

  • Two-year college degree in Information Systems/Technology, Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or a related field
  • A+ Certified
  • MCP preferred
  • 2+ years working with ITIL
  • Previous experience in a similar resort setting
