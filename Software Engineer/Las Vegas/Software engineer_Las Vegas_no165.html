Job Summary:

Backend Engineers are responsible for delivery of high quality software and capable of taking projects through all phases of the software development lifecycle.


Duties/Responsibilities:

  • Delivering high-quality software to consistent with Doctor On Demand and Industry best practices
  • Train and support other engineers to help develop skills for Engineering at large
  • Capable of debugging and optimizing highly complex software

  • Taking projects and breaking them down into manageable tasks that can be accomplished by multiple members of the engineering team

Required Skills/Abilities:

  • Extensive knowledge of Python and Django or a similar web framework

  • Experience building APIs or SDKs
  • Knowledge and experience with a scalable database technology (Postgres, MySQL, etc.)
  • Good understanding of distributed systems, modeling, indexing and associated trade-offs
  • Commitment to software development best practices (code review, automated testing and documentation)

  • Knowledge of healthcare-specific technologies (HL7 standards, 270/271 Inquiry / Response and other EDI transactions)

Education and Experience:

  • 3-5 years experience in professional software development
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, or equivalent
  • Experience building backend software that has scaled well
  • Experience with asynchronous task queues such as Celery
  • Experience using Redis in a production environment

  • Experience with front-end web technologies like HTML5, CSS, JS, etc


Physical Requirements:

  • Ability to remain seated in a stationary position for prolonged periods.
  • No heavy lifting is expected, though occasional exertion of about 20 lbs. of force (e.g., picking up and computer / laptops) may be required.
  • Good manual dexterity required to use common office equipment (e.g., computers, laptop, and mobile devices).

  • Some business travel may be required.
