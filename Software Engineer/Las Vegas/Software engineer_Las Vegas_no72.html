 Establish, implement and document best practices with regards to system maintenance, configuration, deployment, monitoring, and general DevOps responsibilities.

 Work closely with developers during the deployment and testing phases to provide insight into  operational, security, and performance considerations.

 Keep systems and data secure at all times, complying with the latest security standards.

 Audit, troubleshoot and resolve system availability and integrity issues.

 Constantly evaluate existing systems against accepted and evolving industry standards.

 Advocate and implement industry best practices for configuration management, build and deployment  automation, and operational monitoring and analysis.

 Develop and help maintain operational administration, system and data backup, disaster recovery, and  security/performance monitoring policies and tools.

 Act as first responder and system owner during triage and analysis of abnormalities in system operation.  Be prepared to take on a dynamic variety of IT roles and tasks as needed.
