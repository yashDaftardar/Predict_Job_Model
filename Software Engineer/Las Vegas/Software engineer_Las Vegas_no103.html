Overview:
Rakuten Super Logistics presents an exciting opportunity to join the leader in e-commerce order fulfillment solutions. Based in Las Vegas and having a national network of operating facilities, Rakuten Super Logistics is a subsidiary of Rakuten, one of the world's leading internet service companies.

We are looking for a technical lead and hands on DevOps Engineering Manager. The DevOps Engineering Manager will work closely with clients and IT team members to understand the departmental stakeholder requirements that drive the analysis and design of quality infrastructure technical solutions in our Azure DevOps environment.

The DevOps Engineering Manager will be working with their team and our IT and software development organizations to:
Gather requirements
Prototyping
Architecting
Implementing/updating solutions
Building and executing test plans
Performing quality reviews
Managing operations
Triaging and fixing operational issues

Qualifications:
Team development skills.
Positive interpersonal, conflict resolution skills.
Ability to work with engineers from various global locations and time zones.
Ability to takes ownership of small and medium sized tasks and deliver while mentoring and helping team members.
Strong oral and written English language communication skills.
Excel in a rapid iteration environment with short turnaround times.
Deal positively with high levels of uncertainty, ambiguity and shifting priorities in our fast-paced environment.
Accept a wide variety of tasks and assist wherever needed.
Constructively present, discuss and debate alternatives.
Strong interpersonal skills and effective team building.
Take shared ownership of our SDLC, change/release management, and our applications' 24x7 availability.
Positive collaboratively problem-solving abilities.
Ability to see and understand technical data, systems' small LCD status panels, notice various color differences, in-person hardware alert conditions, etc.
Strong problem-solving abilities and capable of articulating specific technical topics or assignments.
Expert in breaking down problems and estimate time for development tasks.
Evangelizes how our technology solves customer problems from a technology and business perspective.
Business / professional appearance, attire, and a polite demeanor / eager to help.
Provide full visibility into schedules, projects, and daily tasks per management's framework.
Increase job related knowledge out-side of business hours / on personal time.

Project and program management with executive level presentations as well as tracking KPIs.
Exceptional executive persuasion skills including making the business case and data driven decision documentation to get buy-in for your recommendations.

Technical Experience:
5-8 years of Cloud infrastructure engineering experience and Strong understanding and knowledge of Azure Services
Experience in building scalable and highly available distributed systems.
Applied experience with modern application technologies and design patterns including Public Cloud infrastructure (Azure) distributed computing, horizontal scaling, and database technologies.
Significant experience with Windows Operating systems and one or more scripting languages (e.g. python, PowerShell)
Candidate should have a strong infrastructure architecture background with experience in virtualization technologies.
Candidate should possess broad knowledge of systems infrastructure that power today’s modern and highly available web and mobile applications, with deep domain expertise in one or more areas: compute, networking, storage, high availability, cloud security, application performance,
Strong understanding of DevOps tools such as: Puppet, Terraform, Ansible, DevOps frameworks – should have implemented one or more of these frameworks,
Experience with bug tracking tools such as JIRA, Version One, and Pivotal Tracker, Rally, or any other similar tool.

College degree or commensurate professional experience.
