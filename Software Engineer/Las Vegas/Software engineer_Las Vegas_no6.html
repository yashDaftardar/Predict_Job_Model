Combine two of the fastest-growing fields on the planet with culture of performance, collaboration and opportunity and this is what you get. Leading edge technology in an industry that’s improving the lives of millions. Here, innovation isn’t about another gadget, it’s about making health care data available wherever and whenever people need it, safely and reliable. There’s no room for error. Join us and start doing your life’s best work. (sm)

You’ll enjoy the flexibility to telecommute* from anywhere within the U.S. as you take on some tough challenges.

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Design, implement and maintain client and server-side web and service applications within all phases of the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC)
  • Develop, test, implement and maintain web applications working with established devops lifecycle processes based on Agile methodologies
  • Communicate effectively with other engineers and QA
  • Proactively monitor and identify production and non-production application issues. Analyze, fine-tune and optimize client/server-side web and service developed applications
  • Provide technical support and consultation for developed applications and infrastructure configuration

You'll be rewarded and recognized for your performance in an environment that will challenge you and give you clear direction on what it takes to succeed in your role as well as provide development for other roles you may be interested in.

Required Qualifications:

  • High School Diploma or GED
  • 3+ years of experience with HTML & CSS
  • 3+ years of experience working with Native JavaScript or JQuery
  • 3+ years of experience using Javascript Design Patterns (MVVM, MVC) specifically Angular 5+
  • 3+ years of experience working with ASP.NET & C# MVC
  • 3+ years of experience in Service Oriented Architecture (REST/SOAP, Web API, WCF)
  • 1+ year of experience working with T-SQL or PL-SQL

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Health care industry experience
  • Experience with public facing, high volume transaction Websites and portals
  • Experience in responsive-design UX Frameworks (Bootstrap UI, etc.)
  • Demonstrated experience in SOLID Principles
  • Demonstrated experience in Architectural Design Patterns
  • Experience with SSIS packages and stored procedures
