PRIMARY PURPOSE:

It is the primary purpose of the Sr. Deployment Engineer to serve as a subject matter expert (SME) for the planning/execution of PMOled and IT Operations hardware and/or software deployments. Will understand the business context of client computing solution requirements, make recommendations for project/deployment process improvement, document project metrics, complete operations readiness hand-offs, and mentor IT Operations specialists on technical best practices . This position is located in Las Vegas, NV with minimal to slight business travel expected. This role will be an individual contributor.

PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Act as project lead accordingly on assigned projects for necessary tasks to deliver upon committed project goals within assigned scope of responsibility.
  • Performs site survey walkthroughs with PMO for new deployments to identify necessary components such as network, power, physical space, and/or accessibility.
  • Document project metrics for presentation to Sr Management and coordinate tasks for deployments.
  • Complete operations readiness hand-off during project close phase.
  • Execute installations/deployments, updates, and/or publications using Microsoft Intune, SCCM and/or VMWare Horizon environments.
  • Provides technical guidance/leadership, business process expertise, knowledge management documentation, coaching and mentoring to IT Operations/Field Services team.
  • Build and maintain business relationships with IT stakeholders and partners to generate new and innovative solutions to complex IT problems while proposing improvements to processes.
  • Perform other job-related duties as requested.

KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, AND ABILITIES:

  • Demonstrate solid operational skills, and abilities to effectively manage, and ensure productivity on assigned projects at multiple locations
  • Ability to mentor others on a technical level
  • IT Operations, systems support and infrastructure.
  • Working knowledge in Microsoft SCCM.
  • Ability to move multiple projects forward simultaneously, prioritizing various and changing objectives and initiatives.
  • Basic fluency in command line scripting or higher.

MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS:

  • High School Diploma or equivalent.
  • Four (4)+ years IT Endpoint Support.

PREFERRED:

  • MCSE, A+, Network+ certification.
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent.
  • Previous experience in a similar hospitality setting.
  • Previous IT helpdesk and IT project management.
  • Excellent customer service and interpersonal skills.
  • Basic fluency in command line scripting or higher.
  • Two (2)+ years of IT Endpoint deployment.
