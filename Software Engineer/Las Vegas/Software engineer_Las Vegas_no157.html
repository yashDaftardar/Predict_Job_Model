Full Job Description

Streamline Media Group is a global company with teams in North America, Europe, and Asia. We are looking at self-motivated and energetic team players to join our growing development team. While Streamline is primarily known for working on games, we actually also produce our own in-house tools such as Streamframe and provide development for non-game partners.

We are hiring for a mid-to-senior level user experience designer for our platform team. This is a fast-paced role on a nimble, and innovative team that focuses on results over perfection.


Requirement:


  • Senior-level experience with developing digital products across a variety of mediums, including web and mobile.
  • Proficient with wire-framing and prototyping tools such as Sketch, InVision, Figma and Axure.
  • Proficient with Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign).
  • Strong critical thinking, analytical and problem-solving skills with the ability to understand, interpret and leverage data to optimize UX solutions.
  • Ability to prioritize design issues and demonstrate independent decision-making.
  • Experience designing client-facing applications in a lean and agile environment.
  • Team player, with a strong work ethic and high energy levels.

Pluses

  • Proficiency in HTML/CSS.
  • Experience with JavaScript (Angular/React).
  • Experience with analytics platforms such as Tableau and Google Analytics.
