Full Job Description

Location:

Las Vegas, Nevada

Become one of the stars behind The SHOW and become part of the world’s most powerful entertainment brands. Our company has one exciting mission: to entertain the human race.

The Executive Director of Software Engineering will provide strong leadership and management for one of our engineering teams. The incumbent will help define and execute a comprehensive strategy for leveraging modern technologies to develop highly available services, code, QA, and release processes. The Executive Director will champion a unified architecture and work with service, front-end and security engineering teams to drive the development and delivery of sophisticated and secure software products. All duties to be performed in accordance with departmental and MGM Resorts International policies, practices, and procedures.

PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Collaborate with cross‐functional teams to develop and drive the technology strategy and roadmap for your specific area
  • Provide technical leadership and expertise for designing and implementing powerful, secure and highly available products and services
  • Recruit, train, and mentor a team of engineer
  • Investigate new and developing technologies as they emerge in industry and academia and determine how to leverage these new technologies into our software solutions.
  • Create and maintain a work environment that promotes teamwork, performance feedback, individual recognition, mutual respect, and employee satisfaction ensuring quality hiring and training.
  • Perform other job‐related duties as requested.

REQUIRED:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering or equivalent training.
  • Four (4) years of software engineering management experience
  • Expertise in a broad range of cloud and other technologies, including IaaS, PaaS, Serverless and the application of those technologies to solve business problems
  • Hands‐on experience in the design, development, and delivery of complex systems and services

PREFERRED:

  • 10+ years of experience in software engineering
  • Extensive experience in database technologies
  • Experience with RESTful web services
  • Strong understanding of encryption and security concepts
  • Expertise in an object‐oriented programming language and design patterns
  • Distributed systems
  • Experience in a team practicing Combined Engineering/DevOps approach

CERTIFICATIONS, LICENSES, REGISTRATIONS:

  • Gaming registration in applicable jurisdictions may be required
  • Proof of eligibility to work in the United States
