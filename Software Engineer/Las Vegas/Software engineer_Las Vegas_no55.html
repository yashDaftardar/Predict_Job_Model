Life at GES U.S.

Software Development Engineer to provide design, development, deployment and maintenance of software projects; and, as necessary, hands-on development support for prototyping and bug resolution. This position primarily focuses Analytics on global enterprise data and industry leading customer-facing web and mobile applications including Business Process Modelling and Analytics. Our IT environment offers the latest technologies, an opportunity to build a career and competitive compensation and benefits.
Responsibilities:

Principle responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Analyze, design and develop comprehensive application solutions servicing key business functions to ensure application execution and implementation plans are aligned with business/IT objectives and priorities
  • Collaborate with technical, functional, and product management representatives to define requirements, design, develop and support applications
  • Provide tier-3 support for all deployed projects
  • Periodically re-evaluate development technologies and application platforms, and manage update/upgrade paths for deployed projects
  • Delivery of technical and functional design documents
  • Work with business analysts and quality assurance analysts to ensure effective and high-quality releases
  • Testing and business acceptance support
  • Ensure that all developed applications include mechanisms to actively monitor the application’s health and operation
  • Comply with all established software development and security standards
Qualifications:

EDUCATION / EXPERIENCE

  • Minimum of three years software development experience building web applications using Web/mobile on Azure or on premise.
  • Experience in software development efforts during the entire lifecycle of design, coding, deployment and maintenance
  • Bachelor’s Degree, or greater, in Computer Science or equivalent studies

SKILLS / COMPETENCIES

  • Extensive development experience with SharePoint 2010 and SharePoint online
  • Extensive development experience with Agile Point or equivalent
  • Experience with integrations ETL processes and modern Datawarehouse techniques, good understanding of data lake, delta lake and data ingestion pipelines
  • Experience with data modelling and good understanding of data structures
  • Experience designing and building mobile web user interfaces using HTML, Javascript (jQuery experience preferred), Bootstrap, Angular, other JavaScript libraries, CSS, Apex
  • Experience with C#, Microsoft SQL (T-SQL), Oracle databases
  • Experience with SaaS applications such as Salesforce (Apex code) preferable
  • Programmer analyst skills for delivering technical design with code including but not limited to pseudocode, system workflows and navigation, market-friendly end-user screen presentation layouts, data flow diagrams and data input integration requirements
  • Experience designing and developing mobile applications is a plus
  • Excellent written and verbal skills required to effectively document and communicate requirements and other software documentation
  • Well-developed interpersonal skills required to communicate with business stakeholders to gathering requirements and effect follow-through as software is developed, deployed and maintained
  • Demonstrated ability to communicate cross-functionality with other IT groups (server engineers, network engineers, etc.)
  • Passion and drive to create software solutions to meet real-world business needs
