About Doctor On Demand

Doctor On Demand’s mission is to improve the world’s health through compassionate care and innovation. We believe that health is personal, and means so much more than treating illness. We're proud of the care we've provided over the years and the relationships we’ve developed with our patients, as evidenced by the 5-star reviews we continually receive. People use our service to gain access to some of the best physicians and licensed therapists in the country, all whenever and wherever is most convenient. It’s as simple as opening the Doctor On Demand app on a smartphone or computer.

Through live video visits, our hand-picked, US-trained doctors take patient history, perform an exam, and recommend a treatment plan. Prescriptions, if needed, go directly to the pharmacy of choice. While insurance isn’t required, tens of millions of Americans enjoy covered medical and mental health visits through employer and health plan partnerships. To learn more about the hundreds of medical issues we treat, visit us at DoctorOnDemand.

Job Summary:

The Frontend team is responsible for crafting high quality web applications supporting both our medical providers and patients. As a web developer, you are a self-sufficient engineer comfortable collaborating on a small team and working with the larger engineering group to implement mission-critical healthcare applications.

Duties/Responsibilities:

  • Develop and maintain consumer facing web sites primarily using VueJS and AngularJS 1.5 integrating with internal Django RPC. REST and cloud services. We are currently in process of migrating to VueJS

  • Refactor older code to bring up to current standards
  • Work closely and collaborate with Product teams to iron out requirements
  • Work with closely and collaborate with QA to ensure proper test plans are produced
  • Create/review technical specifications as needed

  • Other duties as assigned

Required Skills/Abilities:

  • VueJS, AngularJS 1.5, ReactJS, ES6

  • Strong HTML/CSS background
  • Git experience using command line (ex BASH, ZSH)
  • Familiarity with build tools such as WebPack
  • Must be a team player with desire to collaborate
  • Strong communication skills
  • Able to take ownership and accountability for all areas of his/her role
  • Desire to work fast without hurrying
  • Organized and skilled at time management, multitasking

  • Be available (possibly off hours) to triage/fix production problems, if they arise

Bonus Skills/Abilities:

  • Tutoring/coaching
  • Healthcare/HIPAA compliant experience
  • Docker Desktop and docker-compose scripting
  • Accessibility and/or Localization coding
  • Google Analytics/Google Tag Manager
  • Storybook/Figma exposure

  • GraphQL


Education and Experience:

  • 3-5 years of experience in Frontend Web Technology

  • Bachelor in Computer Science


Physical Requirements:

  • Ability to remain seating in a stationary position, as well as the ability to move about
  • No heavy lifting is expected, though occasional exertion of about 20 lbs. of force (e.g., picking up and computer / laptops) may be required

  • Good manual dexterity required to use common office equipment (e.g., computers, laptop, and mobile devices)
