Job Description:


The most security-conscious organizations trust Telos Corporation to protect their vital IT assets. The reputation of our company rests on the quality of our solution and the integrity of our people. Explore what you can bring to our solutions in information assurance, secure networks, secure enterprise messaging, and identity management.


Be a part of the Telos culture and see what sets us apart! Telos offers an excellent compensation package with benefits that include generous paid time off, medical, dental, vision, tuition reimbursement, and 401k. Our employees enjoy more than just a great work environment!


This position will be based in Las Vegas, NV.

Responsibilities:

  • Perform as an AWS Cloud Engineer for a government contract.
  • Build and Deploy AWS Infrastructure and Applications. Manage Software defined networks, routing and security in the Cloud Environment.
  • Serve as a Subject Matter Expert on Agile Development Teams, coordinating work with other teams and stakeholders as a Cloud Engineer.



Job Requirements:


Qualifications:

  • A bachelor’s degree is required. A Master’s degree preferred.
  • A minimum of 5-7 years’ experience supporting Cloud Infrastructure, such as AWS, Azure, VMware etc.
  • Experience designing network infrastructure to deliver zero down time in large enterprise networks .
  • Ability to maintain a government clearance at the highest level, such as Public Trust 5 or Top Secret.
  • Expert level familiarity with government security standards such as NIST, FISMA, DHS, CIS, ISO.
  • Industry Certifications valued for position: CISSP, CCNP, CHFI, CEH, CSM, CNDA.
  • Expertise in Cloud based technology products such as EC2, S3, Cloudwatch, Cloudtrail, RDS, VPC, ELB, EIP, IAM, Directory Services, Amazon Connect, Lambda, SES, Office 365, TenableIO, Route53, Beanstalk, VMWare, Splunk, ServiceNow.
  • The successful candidate must meet eligibility requirements to access sensitive information, which requires US citizenship.
