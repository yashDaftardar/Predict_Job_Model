Software Development Manager - Microsoft Dynamics (Remote)
Power the Casino Floor by joining the Enterprise Solutions Team at Everi. You will be supporting all internal customers of Everi by building out the new ERP solution. There is a lot of room for growth in this organization, so it's an exciting time to join.



“We are interested in every qualified candidate who is eligible to work in the United States. However, we are not able to sponsor visas.”

Key Responsibilities

  • This person is responsible for working with the team on solution designs, managing the development release efforts and addressing any skill gaps in their teams and capacity for a smooth-running team.
  • Build out the new ERP and help in the transition from Microsoft Dynamics NAV to Microsoft Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations.
  • Complete story point exercises with the team to provide estimates to Product Owner/Managers for planning
  • Actively review the level of effort of tasks, and assigns appropriate resource allocation within the release timeframe
  • Organize and oversees development shifts and tasks
  • Review change requests for impact to current work effort and provides an impact summary to the Project Manager & Product Owner
  • Oversee cyclical refreshes
  • Help remove roadblocks and keep everything on track
  • Establish code standards to be followed
  • Complete code reviews and ensures they meet established standards
  • Review deployment package (chainset, OBE, release branch) and provides to Product Owner for approval and updates as necessary
  • Review and approve deployment and rollback plans
  • Conduct staff meetings as needed to follow-up on critical issues or technical meeting outcomes and action items, in addition to root cause analysis
  • Ensure work management systems are updated timely and accurately by staff
  • Ensure department methodologies, frameworks, standards, procedures, processes, and deliverable templates are leveraged and followed
  • Ensure approved team standards, procedures, processes, and templates are reviewed and validated or updated annually
  • Recommend improvements to established methodologies, framework, standards, processes, procedures, or deliverables to the Product Owner
  • Review and validates staff augmentation invoices for accuracy
  • Address gaps in skills and capacity for a smooth running team and coaching and mentoring

Skills, Knowledge, & Expertise

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering, Software Development, or other technical / science field preferred.
  • 8+ years experience in object-oriented technologies, preferably .NET, C# or JavaScript
  • Prior management/lead experience preferred, but if you've been an individual contributor for years and are ready to manage that works too.
  • Knowledge of Microsoft Dynamics 365 ERP Platform is a must. Experience managing a team going through a version migration preferred
  • Knowledge of Agile Scrum methodology.
  • Ideally looking for some leadership knowledge as there is a lot of room for growth in this group.
  • Experience with Microsoft SQL server database.
  • Be comfortable with a largely remote working environment.

Job Benefits

  • Opportunity for growth and advancement in a growing team with upwards mobility!
  • Flexible PTO and 10 paid holidays throughout the year
