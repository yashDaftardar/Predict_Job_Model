Web Developer for Growing Company

Nevada Corporate Headquarters, Inc. (NCH) launches the best careers in the nation! We focus on inspiring our team, collaborating for excellent results, and developing marketing solutions to set us apart from the competition. We promote an environment that is fun, energetic, and supportive!

Right now, we're growing fast! We're looking for a Web Developer to join our innovative marketing team. We're seeking dynamic candidates who are interested in a fast-paced environment that promotes creativity, uniqueness, and teamwork.

The Web Developer position will support the marketing technology needs of Nevada Corporate Headquarters, Inc. (and its sister companies), working with other web developers to design and create customer solutions, as well as internal solutions for business processes. This position reports to the Director of Marketing.

Duties include but are not limited to the following:

  • Design overall architecture of the web applications.
  • Maintain quality and ensure responsiveness of applications.
  • Collaborate with the marketing team to design and launch new features.
  • Maintain code integrity and organization.
  • Experience working with graphic designers and converting designs to visual elements. (Ability to personally design and develop UI/UX is preferred.)
  • Understanding and implementation of security and data protection.
  • Development experience for both mobile and desktop.
  • Writing clean, high-quality, high-performance, maintainable code.
  • Develop and support various applications, including database integration, interfaces, and new functionality enhancements.
  • Support test and deployment of new products and features.
  • Must have experience consuming REST web services and working with JSON.

Requirements:

  • 3+ years of PHP (experience with Laravel framework is preferred).
  • 3+ years of experience building web applications from start to finish.
  • 3+ years of experience in developing solutions to business operations.
  • Ready and willing to tackle any problem.
  • Team player with positive attitude.
  • Ability to multi-task and prioritize.


Salary and Benefits:

  • $70,000 - 80,000/year
  • Paid holidays and PTO
  • Health Benefits after 60 days
  • Education Assistance
