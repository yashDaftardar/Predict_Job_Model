Job Posting Title

Senior Software Engineer

Summary

We are looking for a Senior Software Engineer, C++ expert, and a solid software contributor who loves making and playing fun and interesting games to join a team that lives to make the greatest games in the industry. If you are a creative thinker with an analytical mind; if you love your craft and are dedicated to making your work and yourself better every day, then you are certainly an individual that we want to consider.

You will be a Software Engineer, developing in C++. You will interact with stakeholders, present ideas, and provide design guidance for new features and games. You will need to design and implement software as part of a team and collaborate with many to create solutions that fit the game vision while maintaining the project schedule.

What you'll do

  • Understand, debug and adapt software that was written over several years by countless other contributors

  • Provide coaching, training and mentor of other software engineers

  • Work closely with managers in planning of team projects by providing complexity and time estimates.

  • Design and understand the game code base and remain aware of all features that are currently underway as well as planned in the future

  • Interact with stakeholders from across the business, attending meetings and presenting solutions

  • Maintain knowledge of core technologies, provide fellow developers technical guidance, identify new technological opportunities

  • All other duties as assigned

What we're looking for

  • Expert C++, experience with the latest C++ standards, and a minimum of 6 years of Object-Oriented Design and Development experience

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field

  • Proven ability to keep projects on schedule

  • Ability to debug C++ software in Linux with a minimum of four years of GDB experience

  • Ability to design and assist others in the design of the many new features that are being added
