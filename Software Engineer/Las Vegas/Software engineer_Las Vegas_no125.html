Full Job Description

Caesars Entertainment is seeking an subject-matter expert, partnering with the Lead Manager, the focus of this position is to design, modify, code, and configure Caesars Digital Screens & Digital Asset Manager products within the Las Vegas Region.

Your Focus

  • Provide innovative business solutions that fit system constraints, meet user needs, and match the company's strategic outlook.
  • Perform application analysis and design, development, integration and enhancement activities for existing and new applications.
  • Implement and maintain support documentation for applications.
  • Partner with business users to define system requirements for new functionality.
  • Train business super-users on new program functionality prior to deployment activities.
  • Provide innovative solutions to problems, as well as new development and guidance to other developers.
  • Define, design, develop and deploy complex systems analysis projects.
  • Maintain all tools associated with product development and enhancement.
  • Document activities from design to completion to assist future analysts in understanding work solutions.
  • Review support incidents for technical problems, and assist in solutions and root cause analysis.
  • May assume project management responsibility over simple projects.
  • Utilize programming languages including Java & Javascript
  • Act as administrator for tools such as Adobe Screens & Google Chrome Device Manager

Required

  • Bachelors degree in Information Systems, computer science, or similar related disciplines; or combination of equivalent education and experience will be considered.
  • A minimum of three (3) years of programming experience and in analysis, proven programming and problem solving skills acquired through college courses, technical training and/or previous work experience.
  • Must have the fundamental knowledge of systems design theory and project management.
  • Requires functional skills in two major areas or technical functions or has developed an exceptional expertise in one area or technical function. Knowledge of Java is required.
  • Experience with Adobe Screens is a plus, but not required as we are willing to train the right candidate.
