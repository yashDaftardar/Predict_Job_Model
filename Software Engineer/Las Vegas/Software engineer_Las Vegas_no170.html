VadaTech is a world leader in the design and manufacture of embedded computing solutions. Our products include configurable application-ready systems and their building blocks of boards, chassis and enabling software. Due to growth, we are seeking additional Sr. FPGA Design Engineers to design new digital hardware products and create the circuitry that is necessary for the products to function. This role will be responsible for the test and verification of circuitry such as mixed signal.

Essential Functions:

  • Work with board designer to determine optimal pin out of FPGA/CPLD parts during schematic design
  • Prototype FPGA/CPLD interface logic to ensure pin-put/design feasibility
  • Develop RTL reference designs and /or final implementations using cutting edge Xilinx, Altera, and Lattice parts.
  • Document designs using design diagrams, user manuals, and register specifications
  • Debug/troubleshoot designs in the lab on new board designs using oscilloscopes, logic analyzers, in-circuity debugging, etc.
  • Develop(or be able to work closely with a software engineer who will develop) device drivers and diagnostic application to drive the FPGA/CPLD design
  • Support customers as needed via e-mail, phone, or in person if any.


Requirements

  • BSEE/BSCS
  • Minimum of 7 years of experience in digital design
  • In depth understanding of the VHDL language
  • Prior experience with high performance FPGA interface design and optimization techniques
  • Understanding of how to interface FPGA/CPLD designs to external CPU/Software is required
  • Programmable logic design experience with VHDL, Xilinx, and Altera FPGA's
  • Experience with high speed Serdes interacing of FPGA's using off the shelf IP is a plus(PCIE, XAUI,1000Base-X/SGMII, etc,)
  • Hands on experience testing and debugging systems implemented with single board computers, complex microcontroller, or digital signal processing systems
  • C/C++ programming experience, an understanding of the software lifecycle, and strong structured coding practices
