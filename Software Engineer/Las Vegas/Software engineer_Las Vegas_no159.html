Tinuiti is looking for a passionate, senior level data guru to join our Growth Media team. As the Technical Specialist, Growth Media , you will primarily be responsible for supporting account managers and clients across our Growth Media division with technical requests, ranging from product feeds to tracking pixel support, and working directly with clients to connect our platforms together.

Technical Specialist, Growth Media will support several technology team projects ranging from maintenance of current tools, to the inception and creation of new tools and functionality for our internal teams. You will report to the Manager of Growth Media, Technology.
