Responsibilities: A Day in the Life
As a Web Developer you will be responsible for maintaining and developing Web Applications utilizing a variety of web technologies including, but not limited to PHP, SSL, JavaScript, CSS, HTML and ADA, DRUPAL. As a Developer, you will play a key role in dispatching support requests for those customers that subscribe to application support services. This includes troubleshooting and resolution of web development support requests and managing tickets via an online ticket management utility
