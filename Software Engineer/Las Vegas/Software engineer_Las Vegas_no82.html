Job Title: Technical Program Manager

Role Overview

Arctic Leaf Inc. is a US and Canadian based UI/UX design and development agency that specializes in E-Commerce and web-based application services. Our bottom-of-the-funnel focus separates us from other agencies in the market, and thus are struggling to keep up with demand! We are looking for an addition to the team who is flexible and enjoys working with others in a team-oriented environment.

With consistent growth year over year, and the fact that we are looking to maintain this growth, the ideal candidate will possess the desire to assist in this process and collaborate on different trends to identify key strengths and opportunities.

Our E-Commerce teams work with store owners on a daily basis, both smaller stores looking to grow and larger stores with millions in revenue flowing through their properties. We help re-invent the shopping experience through custom integrations with existing E-Commerce platforms (such as Shopify, BigCommerce and Miva), backend ERP and CRM systems, and modern cloud-based architectures.

We are looking for a Technical Program Manager to drive our more challenging custom integrations from concept through final delivery and deployment. Our Account Managers are great at keeping projects organized, and our technical teams deliver great work, but being able to understand and communicate both the technical aspects of a project and the business aspects is a critical skill which we need to inject into our process.

The ideal candidate for this job will be someone with a software development background who has diverse experience, but has moved away from day-to-day development and into a more abstract problem solving, project management or leadership role.

This position is a full-time position. They would work closely with other members of the account management team as well as the production team.

NOTE: 2-5 year commitment preferred.

Responsibilities

  • Translating complicated technical questions into prescriptive requirements for development team.
  • Maintaining a good understanding of clients and their business strategies.
  • Supporting project Account Managers with scoping items outside of their technical knowledge.
  • Negotiate scope and timelines with technical dependencies that have to make changes to support the launch of a product.
  • Assisting on client calls with Account Managers when projects become complicated.
  • Monitoring tasks and ensuring that task descriptions are filled out correctly.
  • Following up with clients and team members to ensure that projects progress in a timely fashion.
  • Technical program managers are in charge of handling all aspects of one to many technical projects for their organization
  • Work the full life-cycle of projects from idea generation through deployment and optimize the full release value stream.
  • Responsible for initiating programs, driving delivery to completion, and providing support in case issues arise.
  • Oversee non-functional aspects of software delivery such as application telemetry, performance, reliability, resilience, security, and compliance.
