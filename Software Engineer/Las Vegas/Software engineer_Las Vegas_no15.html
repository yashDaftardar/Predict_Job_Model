Full Job Description

Have you ever wanted to be at the heart of a design team at the forefront of building revolutionary video casino games? Are you good at solving complex problems and developing long-term solutions? Do you have a passion for outstanding game design? Do your talents also lie with web app development and backend integration? If so, Incredible Technologies may have the position for you. We are looking for a junior game programmer to join our development team. This talented individual will work with artists, sound designers, and mathematicians to help develop exciting new video slot games under the direction of the team lead. In addition to technical skills, applicants must have clear communication skills, a solid work ethic, and be able to thrive in a team environment.


JOB REQUIREMENTS

DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • As a Junior Full-Stack Developer you will be responsible for creating and maintaining game software that runs on a ROC3 remote gaming server (RGS).
  • You will work alongside Game Designers, Mathematicians, Artists, QA Testers and your fellow developers to build our own internal games
  • Provide clear and honest status to department heads as projects progress
  • Assist in the development of new projects and features
  • Understand and support existing code bases
  • Occasional travel may be required

REQUIRED TECHNICAL SKILLS & ABILITIES

  • Highly motivated, self-starter, creative thinker
  • Exercise great communication skills, as well as be organized and helpful
  • Capable of working in a team environment
  • Object-oriented programming and design pattern knowledge
  • Experience with JavaScript, Python, JSON, MongoDB, HTML 5, NodeJS, and jQuery
  • Fundamental understanding of mathematical probabilities and statistics
  • Pluses:
    • Industry experience with remote gaming systems
    • Phaser 3 experience
    • Unix shell (BASH, etc.) experience
    • Art tools (Photoshop, Alienbrain)
    • Perforce and Git version control software
    • Agile process experience
    • Passion for video games

EDUCATION & EXPERIENCE:

  • Bachelors degree in Computer Science or equivalent experience required
  • Familiar with all phases of the software development life cycle
