Description :

We are looking for an experienced Web Developer. The ideal candidate for this position is a hands-on professional with strong knowledge of HTML, JavaScript (ES6), Three.js, and CSS. The HTML Developer is responsible for working on our current and new game applications for desktop environments, mobile as well as existing support for backend Admin clients. At Z4 Poker you will enjoy the opportunity for long-term employment, great salary, benefits, and being part of a team creating new revolutionary products.

Responsibilities

  • Actively participate in the game development process and production of new games and web applications
  • Engaging with team members to build next-generation gaming products
  • Working directly with product owners and technical leads to ensure the continuous development of products
  • Design and implement game logic and interface with the data services

Requirements

  • Expert knowledge of HTML, JavaScript (ES6), js, and CSS
  • Experience working on a large-scale production web application
  • Experience with a component-based framework like React, Svelte or Vue
  • Experience with modern build tools like WebPack
  • Experience with NPM or Yarn
  • Expert at importing/exporting CommonJS or ES6 modules
  • Experience with WebGL or OpenGL in visual displays or games programming
  • Self-starter who makes forward progress when dealing with ambiguity
  • Can break big problems down into their components and solve them
  • A can-do, positive attitude, and not afraid to ask for help
  • Takes product ownership, identifies opportunities across the product and team to make efficiencies and improves the product
  • Passion for learning and always improving yourself and the team around you

Preferred

  • Experience with GIT in a team setting
  • Experience with Three.js
  • Experience with Node.js
  • Experience with Electron or node-webkit
  • Experience with HTML template engines like Pug/Jade or Handlebars
  • Experience with a CSS preprocessor like LESS, SASS or Stylus
  • Experience with Docker

Required education:

  • Bachelor's in Computer Science

Required experience:

  • JavaScript (ES6), HTML, and CSS: 5 years
  • Cross-platform game development: 3 years

PHYSICAL, MENTAL AND ENVIRONMENTAL DEMANDS:

Assigned duties are accomplished primarily in an office setting where walking, sitting, bending, reaching, talking, hearing and good vision is required. May be required to lift up to 25 lbs. Must have the ability to climb stairs.
