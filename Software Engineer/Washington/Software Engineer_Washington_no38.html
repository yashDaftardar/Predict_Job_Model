This portfolio impressively powers the world with more than one billion monthly active devices.
Geneva Actions is a platform Service built to provide the safe, secure, and compliant serverless execution gateway to interact with production environment and resources across Azure. It's designed for access by on-call engineers, customer support (CSS) teams, or other audiences who want to access management operations in production environment to resolve customer and system issues. It is already used by 300+ services and teams across Microsoft and an integral part of Azure wide multi semester effort called CAT to make all production touches across all services in Azure to be measured, safe, secure, repeatable, and automated.
We are looking for a hands-on engineer who will join us in our journey of building Geneva Actions as a cloud scale, multi geo, always available, dial-tone grade distributed platform and help us solve challenging technical problems along the way. In this position, you will be part of a team that seeks challenges, loves learning and values collaboration and the ideas of working better together. You will collaborate with team members and our partners to design, build and deliver full stack web and client applications using latest technologies in a dynamic and agile environment.

Responsibilities

  • Design and develop new features and help us evolve our architecture and engineering techniques.
  • Coach and influence peers and partners through collaboration, feedback, and code reviews
  • Help design and drive our next generation tech stack.
  • Partnering with other teams across Microsoft to drive adoption.
  • Participate in scrum and effectively communicate status, issues, and risks.

Qualifications

Required qualifications

  • 5+ years of experience in software designing and development in C# or equivalent language
  • BS in computer science or related discipline
  • Strong coding, design and architectural skills
  • Strong oral and written communication

Preferred qualifications

  • Fluent in core web technologies such as REST, HTML/CSS, JavaScript etc.
  • Experience with building multi-tier distributed large scale services.


Professional Traits

  • Positive, can-do attitude to drive issues to resolution.
  • curiosity and a willingness to learn, share and improve.
  • Team player, willing to work and help others be successful.
  • Ability to work in a fast paced, agile environment.
