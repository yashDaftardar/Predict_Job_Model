Software Development Engineer II

Would you like to be part of a team that powers Expedia Cruises through the latest and best on AWS and contributes to the next generation experience and platform for our customers and partners? Then come join our team!

The Cruise team is seeking a highly motivated software engineer who will design and build fault-tolerant and scalable solutions. We are looking for someone to work with the team to implement and iterate on features quickly and efficiently.

Our team is looking for an Engineer who is dedicated and creative with a passion for technology and fluent in both front end and back end coding.

What you'll do:

  • Design and implement products and solutions that are highly scalable with high quality, clean, maintainable, and well-documented code across the full technology stack

  • Work collaboratively with all members of the technical staff and other partners to build and ship outstanding software in a fast-paced environment

  • Find opportunities for process and technology improvements, and work towards adoption and implementation

  • Assist with supporting Production systems (includes investigating issues and working towards resolution)

  • Exercise creativity and provide alternative solutions to a given problem removing roadblocks and driving issues to closure

  • Create/update documentation for the purpose of sharing knowledge between team members

  • Actively participate in group technology reviews to critique work of self and others

Who you are:

  • Reliable and collaborates well with others with excellent written & verbal communication skills

  • Experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and/or other JavaScript libraries & frameworks such as JQuery, React.js

  • Experience with Java and Spring MVC along with good grasp of OO design with strong design patterns knowledge

  • Experience with cloud-computing platforms such as Amazon Web Services

  • Good knowledge of full web stack and modern web application architecture

  • 1+ years relevant work experience along with a bachelor's Degree or higher in Computer Science/Engineering, or related work experience.
