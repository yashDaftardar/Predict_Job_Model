The Boeing Defense, Space and Security (BDS) P-8 Poseidon program is the premier Anti-Submarine Warfare (ASW) platform for the US Navy and several international partners . Recent contract awards have enabled team expansion and growth. This franchise program is anticipated to continue to be a stable program for the foreseeable future.

The P-8 Mission Systems organization is seeking an Associate Software Test Engineer to be part of our Mission Systems Integration team. The organization is seeking engineers adept at software/system testing, including documenting system test plans, procedures, and results to support our P-8 software and system qualification process. The system supports both domestic and international customers and we operate in an Agile environment. The job site is Kent, WA.

Position Responsibilities:

  • Assess and Update Software Requirements for Testability

  • Creation of test plans, procedures, and reports

  • Perform System Integration & Test

  • Use case development for system requirements/test

  • Participation in peer reviews

  • Support design and development of Mission Systems software

  • Support software project management

  • Select, tailor and deploy processes and tools; establish metrics

  • Provide customer demonstrations

This position requires an active U.S. Secret Security Clearance (US Citizenship Required). (A U.S. Security Clearance that has been active in the past 24 months is considered active)

Basic Qualifications (Required Skills & Experience):

  • Active security clearance – Secret or higher

  • System/software testing experience

  • Familiarity with C++ or Java programming language

  • Familiarity with Linux and Windows operating environments

Preferred Qualifications (Desired Skills & Experience):

  • Usage of software development and problem reporting tools such as JIRA and ClearQuest

  • DoD software processes

  • Mission Systems Architecture

  • Familiarity with Agile software development processes or use case development

Typical Education & Experience:

Education/experience typically acquired through advanced technical education from an accredited course of study in engineering, computer science, mathematics, physics or chemistry (e.g. Bachelor) and typically 2 or more years' related work experience or an equivalent combination of technical education and experience (e.g. Master). In the USA, ABET accreditation is the preferred, although not required, accreditation standard.

Relocation:

Relocation assistance is not a negotiable benefit for this position. Candidates must live in the immediate area or relocate at their own expense.

This is a union represented position
