This Position:
We're looking for a self-motivated, hands-on engineer, who will be responsible for DocuSign's Signing application. The engineer will create and maintain a variety of features related to the Signing application. The ideal candidate will be passionate about working on a user experience that will be utilized by millions of users.

You will do this while working with a team of passionate people, who care about their customers and want to deliver quality solutions that improve customer productivity for complex workflows. The desired candidate will be a well-rounded developer, who is not afraid to jump into any level of the stack and has proven experience in web development, a flexible attitude and aptitude to learn and share. This person enjoys working in a fast-paced agile environment, cares about their decisions, and is focused on quality driven development.

This position reports to the Engineering Manager.

Responsibilities:
  • Work as part of a passionate and intelligent development team to design and create industry-leading experiences
  • Build high-quality, ship-ready code with logging and monitoring
  • Work with product and project team to understand and translate product requirements into software requirements that are well defined, measurable and attainable
  • Own and deliver accurate effort estimations for each release cycle
  • Assist team with automated test coverage
  • Work in a fun yet productive environment at a company voted one of the best places to work!
Basic Qualifications:
  • BS degree in Computer Science or related field
  • 2+ total years industry experience in Software Engineering
  • 2+ years of experience developing web applications, with focus on performance and scalability
  • Experience with Microsoft stack .Net, C# or equivalent
  • Experience with web technologies, like TypeScript, JavaScript, CSS, HTML
  • Experience with React or similar client-side framework
Preferred Qualifications:
  • Experience with telemetry software
  • Knowledge and experience in high scale, highly available (99.99+) systems
  • Experience with Git, continuous integration and deployment tools
  • Experience working in an agile development environment
  • Curiosity and ability to learn new technologies and tool sets
  • Experience creating reusable components in React
