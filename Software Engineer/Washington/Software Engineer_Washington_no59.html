Requisition Summary

This position is responsible for the maintenance and development of applications on iSeriers including involvement with major 3rd party vendor support. The maintenance and development activity will predominantly use COBOL to support ASI application on the iSeries.

A successful candidate will be highly self-directed and have experience and knowledge of software design, build, and testing methodologies. Candidates will need to demonstrate the ability to research, analyze, troubleshoot, and solve software problems. Willingness to support existing system implementations is a must.

The applicant will work on small and strategic projects among other support and development assignments.

A successful candidate will have aggressive learning goals and may support business partners using 24x7 applications that require on-call responsibilities.


Job Functions / Responsibilities

  • Requirement analysis, design and effort estimations.
  • Identification of functional and non-functional requirement and based on the identification creation of technical documents like impact analysis document, understanding document.
  • Prepare detailed technical specifications.
  • Development of New Requests and enhancements in existing functionalities.
  • Development and Modifications of programs using COBOL, RPGIV, RPGLE and CLLE.
  • Code reviews.
  • Prepare unit test plan. Do unit testing and integration testing.
  • Impact analysis, coding and unit testing for problems reported for Incidents Reported.
  • Documenting all changes made to facilitate tracking and future enhancements.
  • Analyze Batch Jobs and abnormal termination of Jobs.
  • Status reporting, Communication with customer, Co-Ordination with onsite and offshore team.
