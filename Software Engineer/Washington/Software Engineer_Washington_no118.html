Project Kuiper is an initiative to launch a constellation of Low Earth Orbit satellites that will provide low-latency, high-speed broadband connectivity to unserved and underserved communities around the world.

This is a broad scoped position regarding testing, integration, and characterization of mechanical systems supporting the project Kuiper mission. You will lead the charge to develop a scalable and reliable way of manufacturing, assembling, and integrating precision mechanisms and systems to the vehicle. You will also run trade studies and tests related to system design and material selection to ensure the mission success of current and future satellite designs. This role will require a thorough understanding of material testing for space environments, as well as a deep understanding of manufacturing processes. Everyone on the team needs to be entrepreneurial, wear many hats, and work in a highly collaborative environment with many other teams. We will need to tackle difficult problems that span a wide variety of domains.

A day in the life
This role will be responsible for the heartbeat of new product development, and will be essential in the on-time delivery of hardware capable of achieving mission success. Team members will work with a broad scope and autonomy to fill any and all gaps between product concept and delivery. You will work within the mechanical design group to interface with many external groups and accelerate hardware development and test. You will own test campaigns to validate material selection and processes both for design verification and production scenarios. Tests will focus on minimizing design risks in function, reliability, supply, and manufacturing. You will become the subject matter expert for the systems you support, and will shepherd new and complex assemblies from the stages of early design to production stability.

About the hiring group
This team is organized within the solar, payload, and mechanisms team to accelerate the design iteration process and ensure on-time delivery of the most stable and successful product possible. The tasks and daily focus change rapidly, and you will often take on new and exciting responsibilities as required to ensure project success. Expect to be deployed to complex problems that are driving the design, identify the scope of the issue, and quickly establish functional solutions able to be tested and integrated into the design.
