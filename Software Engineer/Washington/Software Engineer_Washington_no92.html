Microsoft Dynamics 365

Microsoft’s Dynamics 365 is one of the fastest-growing SaaS businesses in the industry, trusted by some of the world’s most iconic brands and institutions. Dynamics 365 has a family of modern, interoperable, and individually adaptable, multi-tenant SaaS business applications built atop the Microsoft Cloud.

We are building a modern SaaS solution that uses emerging technologies like computer vision, IoT, and machine learning to transform operations and customer experiences in retail stores. Intuitive experiences built on AI-driven insights enable retailers to enhance the shopper experiences and empower their floor associates to operate more effectively.

Our team is working hard to bring our internal AI tools to the market quickly and at scale. We are looking for a confident and solid .NET/C# developer that understands advanced asynchronous programming with a strong understanding of content management and networking.

Responsibilities

Responsibilities:

  • Continued design and development of our tools and services.
  • Working with other developers and teams to deliver releases in tight timeframes.
  • Adherence to coding standards, project metrics and making sure these are delivered on time under tight schedules.
  • Attending or leading daily sprint meetings
  • Working closely with PM and other upstream source system teams to address production issues.
  • Be accountable for the service uptime and SLA.
  • Ensure all compliance and security considerations are adhered to.
  • Manage partnerships with internal teams.

Qualifications

Required Qualifications:
  • 6+ years of professional experience coding and shipping services with C# & .NET
Preferred Qualifications:
  • Strong skills with the .NET Framework/Core (C#, ASP.NET, XML).
  • Strong understanding of networking and asynchronous programming.
  • Experience with Document DB systems (Example: MongoDB/CosmosDB).
  • Experience with Dev Ops and Continuous Integration.
  • Experience with Azure.
  • Demonstrated partnership, communication, and cross-group collaboration skills.
  • Bachelors degree in Computer Science, or a related field, or equivalent alternative education, skills, and/or practical experience.
