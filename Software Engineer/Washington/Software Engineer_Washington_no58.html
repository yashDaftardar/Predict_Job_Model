Full Job Description

An awesome online shopping experience takes an awesome team. Join the Production Engineering squad that builds reliable and scalable solutions for infrastructure management and monitoring of the Zulily website.

Our values at Zulily are mutual respect, empathy, and support for each other. We win and fail as a team. We strive to improve our quality of lives as engineers. We don’t say “minimally viable product”; we say “minimally loveable product”. In our debates, data wins over opinion. We don't say "it's not my job"; we say "I wish this were our problem; then we can fix it".

If this reads like the culture you want to live by, give us a shout – we've been looking for you.

Qualifications:

  • 1+ years programming experience.
  • 1+ years Linux experience (any distribution)
  • Proficient in an object-oriented programming language (e.g. Java, PHP)
  • Experience with networking and security (e.g. with AWS or data centers)
  • Ability to work in a fast paced, evolving, growing and dynamic environment.
  • Predilection for systems thinking and translating business requirements into code.
  • Strong analytical skills with a data driven approach and ability to measure impact of project and product deliverables.
  • Excellent software design and architecture skills with an eye toward avoiding and reducing technical debt.
  • Willingness to explore new ideas and have a passion to make them happen.
  • Ability to influence and communicate effectively with others.
  • Container knowledge (Kubernetes/Docker) a plus.
  • Prior experience with payment systems and eCommerce a plus.
  • Bachelor’s Degree in computer science or related field required. A combination of education plus equivalent work experience may be considered.
