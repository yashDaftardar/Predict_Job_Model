Schweitzer Engineering Laboratories (SEL) seeks a professional, innovative , and detail-oriented individual for our Software Engineer position with an emphasis in application and desktop development. If you are looking for an opportunity to apply new computer technology to help solve our world’s energy challenges, then this may be the position for you!

Qualifications

  • B.S. in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or equivalent
  • Knowledge and experience in object - oriented languages (C#, Java, C++, Delphi)
