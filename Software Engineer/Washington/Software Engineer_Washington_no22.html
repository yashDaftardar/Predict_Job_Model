Software Developer Engineer I

We’re looking for an engineer to work on web applications used by hundreds of thousands of our hotel partners around the world and localized to 26 languages. Our team has adopted a dev-ops model and our engineers are involved in every aspect of the application lifecycle. We release software to production multiple times a week and measure how our applications perform, so we can keep improving our features and our underlying platform.

This is a unique opportunity to join the largest online travel agency in the world, and help shape the future of the top lodging initiatives within the Expedia Worldwide Engineering organization.

What you’ll do:

  • You will develop and test standard software applications and related programs and procedures to ensure they meet design requirements.

  • You will apply software design principles, data structures, algorithms and computer science fundamentals to write code that is clean, maintainable, optimized, modular with good naming conventions.

  • You will apply knowledge of system and technology integration and the basic features and facilities involved in the integration process.

  • You will execute tasks and/or provide data to support implementation of holistic solutions that forge linkages between structure, people, process and technology.

  • You will collaborate with team members to co-develop and solve problems.

  • You will take advantage of opportunities to build new technical expertise in a specific engineering area.

  • You will apply formal training methods to current workload.

  • You will report clearly on current work status.


Who you are:

  • Bachelor’s degree in computer science, mathematics or related technical field and/or equivalent work experience with Strong communication skills

  • 2+ years overall development experience in scalable web applications (work experience or school projects)

  • Experience using code versioning tools (e.g. Git) and Agile development methodology

  • Proficient in Java or other object-oriented languages (e.g. Python, C#/C++, Ruby, etc.)

  • Familiarity with React.js, Angular.js or similar Javascript frameworks

  • Understanding of software development theory and design.

  • Demonstrates an understanding of testing, monitoring and alerting

  • Ability to debug code.
