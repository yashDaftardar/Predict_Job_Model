What We’re Up to at Tango Card

Tango Card helps thousands of companies reach their goals by making rewards and incentives easy to send and awesome to receive. Our customers genuinely love working with us, and it shows: We’ve made the Inc. 5000 list of the fastest-growing private companies in America for the last five years. We’re backed by a stellar group of investors, including the private equity firm FTV (which led a $35M growth equity round in 2018), Fyrfly Venture Partners, Floodgate Capital, Eric Schmidt’s Innovation Endeavors, SWaN & Legend partners, and WTI.

We work with 2,500+ enterprise customers today, including some of the world’s most recognizable businesses: Twitter, Google, Extole, Microsoft, Salesforce, and F5. We also partner with the top gift card brands, including Amazon, Starbucks, Target, Walmart, The Home Depot as well as 600+ brands across 40+ countries. Our customers send millions of gift cards each month, and we’ve facilitated $2B gift card transactions since our creation. Last but not least, we proudly partner with many of the most respected non-profits in the country, including the National Park Foundation, Special Olympics, and Habitat for Humanity.

Tango Card is proud to have been rated one of the best companies to work for by Built In. We’re on an incredible path to be the #1 rewards provider, and to get there we need amazing people (like you) in customer support, sales, engineering, and finance to accomplish this vision.

The Role:
The Software Development Engineer at Tango Card plays an essential role in our mission to make rewards easy to send and awesome to receive! You will work with our growing Engineering team on projects that impact thousands of businesses and reward recipients every day, all while using the newest technologies to solve real-world problems. Far from your typical Engineering role, you will have the chance to work with all aspects of our business.

Reports to: One of our Engineering Managers

As a Software Development Engineer at Tango Card, you will:

    • Develop creative software products and applications
    • Value collaboration and the importance of supporting your team by working on both front-end and back-end engineering projects
    • Ensure our enterprise-level products and applications are stable, secure, and scalable
    • Complete rewarding work while working in a small, versatile environment
    • Work both solitarily and as part of a tight-knit unit

Our ideal Software Development Engineer at Tango Card will have most of the following skills and experience:

    • Experience working with Vue or another modern frontend framework
    • Experienced working within Agile project management methodologies and practices
    • Understanding of cloud-based software development environments
    • BS in Computer Science OR equivalent experience
