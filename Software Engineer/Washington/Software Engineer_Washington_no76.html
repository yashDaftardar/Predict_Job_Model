Job Description


We are a team of Engineers in the Surface Co-Engineering team within the Customer Enabling team(CCE). Our primary charter is to work on reproducing, root-causing and driving to resolution ship-blocking bugs reported by our Customer. We are looking for an energetic and experienced Software Debug Engineer with BIOS focus to join our team located in Redmond, WA. The Software Engineer will be working directly with Customers and development teams for debugging Software/Firmware on the customer products, as well as make interactive updates to the Customer UEFI in support of debug efforts. Responsibilities will include but are not limited to the following: Building Customer Based UEFIs for debug, Work cross organizationally to debug and optimize our customers product Software/Firmware, Manage day to day activities with customer, engineering and cross-functional teams to debug issues. Lead and drive internal cross functional teams to meet program deliverables and quality criteria according to program milestones. Manage and track technical issues, risks and priorities.


Qualifications


You must possess the minimum qualifications to be considered for this position. Preferred qualifications are in addition to the minimum requirements and are considered a plus factor in identifying top candidates.
Minimum Requirement:
Bachelors Degree or higher in CE,CS, EE or similar technical STEM degree.
7+ years of industry experience
7+ Years of experience w/ BIOS development and Software /System Debug experience
7+ years of experience debugging System ad Windows OS level issue
7+ years of experience with system level product development
5 +years of experience with UEFI BIOS or EDK2, Reviewing source code, reviewing patches, submitting bug reports, writing convincing arguments to get change requests or bugs accepted.
5+ years of experience with one or more of the following debug tools or similar: ITP, Lauterbach, design/verification tools, lab equipment

Preferred Requirements:
Masters Degree or higher in CE,CS, EE or similar technical STEM

Inside this Business Group


The Client Computing Group (CCG) is responsible for driving business strategy and product development for Intel's PC products and platforms, spanning form factors such as notebooks, desktops, 2 in 1s, all in ones. Working with our partners across the industry, we intend to continue to advance PC experiences to deliver the real-world performance people demand. As the largest business unit at Intel, CCG is investing more heavily in the PC, ramping its capabilities even more aggressively, and designing the PC experience even more deliberately, including delivering a predictable cadence of leadership products. As a result, we are able to fuel innovation across Intel, providing an important source of IP and scale, as well as help the company deliver on its purpose of enriching the lives of every person on earth.
