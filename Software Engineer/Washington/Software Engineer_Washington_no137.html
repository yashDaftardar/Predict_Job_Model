Full Job Description

WHO WE ARE

Seagull Scientific’s BarTender® software transforms information into labels, barcodes, RFID tags and smartcards that drive business. Our software enables organizations around the world to improve safety, security, efficiency and compliance.

Headquartered in Bellevue, Washington, with branch offices in Madrid, Taipei, and Tokyo, Seagull Scientific's software is used in more than 150 countries and across virtually every industry. For more information about Seagull Scientific, please visit https://www.seagullscientific.com/

This position is located in Bellevue, WA.

WHO YOU ARE

You are a strong team player who appreciates excellence, who is passionate about your work and fair in your interactions with people. You enjoy learning new systems, processes, and technology. You enjoy to code, design, and engineer solutions from the big picture all the down to the nitty gritty. If you are looking for a job where your programming skills will make a significant impact on the real world, then we want to talk to you!

WHAT YOU WILL DO

You will develop new product features and processes. This will include a range of low, mid and high-level technical challenges, where you will have the chance to learn a lot of new things.

  • Design and code new features for both our internal and external products
  • Respond to break/fix requests by troubleshooting and resolving issues
  • Respond to bug reports by identifying, reproducing, patching, and releasing fixes
  • Develop integrations with third parties via APIs, custom code, or other solutions

You will learn more about the following topics while you work:

  • C#, .NET Framework, .NET Core, ASP.NET Core, SQL (MS), JSON, XML
  • Windows Services, DNS, IIS
  • React and Redux
  • Integrations, APIs, Web Technologies
  • Virtual PCs & Servers

WHAT WE REQUIRE

  • Basic C# or C++ skills
  • Knowledge of Windows operating systems
  • Problem-solving ability and active listening skills
  • Ability to successfully collaborate with different teams, as well as being self-motivated to work individually
  • Ability to quickly learn and work with new technologies
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

  • Computer Science, Information Systems or similar degree
  • Database familiarity (SQL, NoSQL, Mongo, etc)
  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript, NodeJS, React, and Redux
  • Knowledge of Web/Http API technologies (JSON, Get, Post, etc)

TERMS OF EMPLOYMENT

  • This is a full-time position located in Bellevue, WA. Currently conducting interviews and working remotely until we return to the office later in the year.
  • Ability to provide documentation that proves authorization to work in the U.S.

BENEFITS

  • Work in an international, dynamic and passionate environment with a great company culture.
  • Full time from the office (from home until we return to the office, the option to be hired on a remote basis is only if you are not living in WA, applicants within WA will be considered for a Bellevue office based role)
  • Competitive salary.
  • Private Medical Insurance.
  • Flexible compensation benefits program (Transportation card, Lunch tickets etc.).
  • Professional training, and development opportunities available.

OUR CULTURE

We are fair, we are passionate, we appreciate excellence and we act as a team. We offer a dynamic, culturally diverse and fun environment in which people with energy, creativity and passion work together to deliver amazing product experiences for our customers. You will be part of a team where your insights and ideas are valued.

DIVERSITY AND INCLUSION

Seagull Scientific has a company culture that respects a diverse and unified team from a range of backgrounds whose different perspectives enrich our business. Everyone at Seagull is treated fairly, with respect, and is encouraged to voice their opinion.
