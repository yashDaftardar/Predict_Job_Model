Apple’s Pages team is looking for an engineer to join us to develop our world-class app that delights millions of users every day, ranging from school children to published authors.

  • Software development experience with Swift or Objective-C
  • Solid understanding of object-oriented design and computer science fundamentals
  • Comfortable working in a large code base
  • Willingness to ask for help and consult with others
  • Passion for growing and learning

Description
We are looking for an engineer to help maintain and evolve the user interface and user interaction of our macOS Pages app. Our team develops new word processing, page layout, and publishing features and regularly works with other iWork teams to ensure seamless integration with Numbers and Keynote, as well as with all three iWork apps on iOS, iPadOS, and the web. We also often work with Apple’s OS group as first adopters, proving grounds, and showcase for new macOS technologies. The ideal candidate is detail oriented, self-motivated, and has a passion for creating great software. Your contributions will have a major impact on well-established but fast-moving software used and loved by millions of people to get things done at school, at home, in their communities, and on their jobs. You will work closely with other engineers, human interface designers, quality assurance teams, and different groups across Apple as we continually advance the state of the art in consumer productivity. Working on Pages, you’ll be part of a team of people who love our products and our customers, enjoy learning new things every day, and draw on their variety of backgrounds and experiences to create apps that work for everyone. Engineers on our team regularly have the opportunity to contribute to every facet of our products, from high-level UI to low-level data management to server code running on iCloud supporting shared document collaboration, as well as going beyond code to influence everything from user interface to project planning. iWork engineers have exposure to nearly every technology available on Apple’s platforms, get to ship code targeting many of our products, and get to learn from the many experienced people in the iWork organization. You’ll be trusted to work with your teammates to come up with the best solutions to technical problems and drive their adoption, and will make use of code review, automated testing, and other tools to make sure your code meets our software and customer quality goals.
Education & Experience
Bachelors Degree, or equivalent work experience.
