Within C+E (Commerce and Ecosystems) division we are looking for talented early to mid-career Software Engineers to help us deliver highly scalable distributed cloud services and world-class product experiences as well as build the platform that empowers Microsoft's partners to thrive in our ecosystem.

You will have opportunities to work on multiple layers of the technology stack, ranging from customer-focused user experience work, working with distributed systems, big data, machine learning, and everything in between. You will collaborate with team members and other engineering groups in Microsoft to build full stack web applications using latest web technologies in a dynamic and agile environment. You will be part of a fun-loving, diverse team that seeks challenges, loves learning and values teamwork. You will have opportunities for mentorship, career growth, and work on high-business impact areas.

As a part of C+E organization we’re responsible for driving 3 major initiatives within Microsoft:

Make it Easier for Customers and Partners
Enable an easier experience for customers and partners to grow, as well as to buy and do business with us by:
  • Delivering a dependable, safe and secure commerce infrastructure
  • Completing the migration to Modern
  • Furthering our 1st and 3rd party alignment
  • Deliver compelling new business models

Drive Discovery and Awareness
Design services and experiences that drive discovery and awareness within Microsoft ecosystem of products and services, as well as reach online and real-world technical communities around the globe - your customers will range from Fortune 500 companies to startups, and the biggest Xbox game publishers to Indie developers.

Drive Online Engagement
Help customers gain technical proficiency with Microsoft products by delivering relevant and powerful content including product documentation, tools with a focus on skilling, solutions, learning paths, access to events, and compelling technical and business materials on popular third-party sites, forums, and channels while also creating engagement with our partners through our online assets.

Responsibilities

In this role you will work on projects with some of the following attributes:

  • Full stack web and application development using technologies and languages such as C#, Azure, .NET
  • Build dynamic and reliable user experiences using React, Angular, JavaScript/Typescript, Node.js, and CSS
  • Big data using modern technologies such as Apache Spark, No SQL Cosmos DB, and Blobs
  • Use machine learning to build robust systems for anomaly detection and analysis
  • Use Kubernetes and other container based tools to develop highly scalable cloud based micro-services

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science or equivalent experience
  • 2+ years of Industry software development experience programming languages such as C#, Python, and Java
  • Experience/Exposure with full stack software development and/or data processing
  • Strong problem solving, design, coding, and debugging skills

  • Microsoft Cloud Background Check: This position will be required to pass the Microsoft Cloud background check upon hire/transfer and every two years thereafter.
