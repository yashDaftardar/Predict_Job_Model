Johnson & Johnson is currently recruiting for a Staff Software Engineer ideally located in Seattle, WA or San Francisco, CA.


Johnson & Johnson, through its operating companies, is the world's most prominent manufacturer and supplier of healthcare products, as well as a provider of related services, for the consumer, pharmaceutical, and medical devices. We strive to provide scientifically sound, high-quality products and services to help heal, cure disease, and improve the quality of life.


Johnson & Johnson Digital Solutions is committed to offering a personalized interventional performance management platform developed by a team of surgeons, engineers, and biostatisticians from the most reputable institutions worldwide.


The Opportunity

Johnson & Johnson is groundbreaking the way in developing AI platforms that are crafted to support smarter and safer surgery. Our systems and processes are on the verge of extensive scale and require sophisticated, automatic, predictive modeling to measure intra-operative data. The insights gained from this data can help inform surgical learning in efforts to improve surgeon technical skill and most meaningfully, patient outcomes. We are seeking a highly self-motivated and driven professional in software engineering. This individual will help advance our C-SATS platform as well as other critical platforms with our Digital Solutions ecosystem. Opportunities for this role lie in the realms of machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing, and deep learning. The scale of our solution requires expertise in model deployment, cloud infrastructure, data versioning, cluster management, orchestration, model optimization, and accelerated computing.


Why This Matters

You will be responsible for a critical part of the success of Johnson & Johnson, and your work will have a direct impact on patient care. Johnson & Johnson Digital Solutions measure technical skills in healthcare practitioners, skills that directly impact clinical outcomes; poor performers generate 3x more complications and 5x more deaths than top performers. Our system generates 10x the amount of information about an individualsâ€™ skills in 1/10th the time of traditional peer review. Major medical centers have completed millions of assessments with Johnson & Johnson, and they depend on our system to ensure the quality of their practitioners and the safety of their patients!


Working on our capability of better approaching problems, prioritize our data-streams according to development needs, scale-up research, and development, and deploy solutions is part of the critical path to our goals!


Responsibilities
  • Craft and develop infrastructure on the cloud that allows engineering teams to build highly scalable services
  • Review cloud infrastructure and cloud deployment strategies for, reliable and secure implementation.
  • Creation of Python scripts for cloud-based infrastructure deployment and automation
  • Drive CI/CD capabilities to achieve continuous deployment
  • Develop infrastructure architectural documentation
  • Work closely with the Security and Compliance teams to identify and resolve cloud security and compliance issues.
  • Works closely with a team of software engineers, machine learning engineers, product managers, and R&D researchers.
  • Foster an open and transparent culture of creativity and collaboration with intelligent risk-taking and encouraging questioning


Qualifications

  • BS or Master's degree from an accredited four-year college in computer science or a related field or equivalent proven experience
  • Minimum 2 years experience in building and managing data engineering services in the cloud
  • Strong programming background, with extensive experience in Python. Experience with C, C++, Java, or more general-purpose programming languages is a plus.
  • Experience with cloud computing: S3, IAM, Lambda, EC2, AWS Batch, Cloud Formation, AWS CDK
  • Experience with data engineering tools: Sagemaker, MLFlow, Airflow.
  • Familiar with ML CI/CD pipelines, testing and validating code and components, testing and validating data, data schemas, and models.
  • Experience working alongside data scientists and researchers
  • Excellent written and spoken English
  • Strong sense of responsibility for the happiness of customers and coworkers
