The Microsoft Devices Design Verification an System Engineering team is on a journey to build and qualify a quality device with Surface Duo. We believe in building devices that fully express the Microsoft vision. A fundamental part of our strategy is having high quality devices that enable the experiences people want.

Building quality into our devices involves close partnership with hardware engineers, software engineers, electrical engineers, designers, and manufacturing along with the various industry partners that closely collaborate to create industry defining firsts. Our obsession for high quality experiences along with our passion to enable everyone to do and be their best is reflected in the devices we make. Come join us as we build an organization that scales with the challenges of creating quality experiences and improving the existing ones.

The Surface DVSE team is seeking a talented engineer with a strong background in shipping consumer devices. #DSQO

Responsibilities

  • Work closely with engineers across multiple product lines to understand and communicate the quality of the products we work on.
  • Build tools and processes to measure quality, gather data, analyze data, and report on issues in the product.
  • Verification of end-user experiences is core to the position.
  • Creation and execution of test plans required for software platform subsystem validation
  • Work with Surface software development teams, HW engineers, and external partners to resolve software related bugs
  • Provide clear and timely reporting into product teams as to the status of your testing
  • Run several scenarios designed to exercise Surface products with complex use cases
  • Be responsible for all aspects of test engineering and validation of their features, with opportunities to stretch more broadly. This will include working closely with software and hardware developers and program managers to create solid core designs and enable testability of the products.
  • Develop new test architectures, methodologies, tools and comprehensive test plans. This includes documenting test architectures, test plans and test cases.
  • Conduct technical reviews throughout the development process. This would include reviews of the hardware design, software/firmware design, test architectures, test plans, test cases, and results.
  • Create appropriate test infrastructure for all aspects of assigned features including core scenarios, performance, reliability, and stress.
  • Work on new devices at various stages of hardware development including proto-type hardware.
  • Drive defect reproduction and analysis, study of data produced from static and dynamic code analysis tools, and participate in specification reviews, design reviews, and code reviews.
  • Drive test analysis through test suite code coverage and data analysis tools.
  • Identify product risk areas and mitigate them through targeted testing.

Qualifications

Qualifications
  • Bachelor’s degree in Electrical, Computer Engineering or equivalent experience
  • 5+ years’ experience in software validation/test
  • Strong data writing skills for effective communication of test results
  • Proven track record of testing/qualifying high volume consumer devices
Preferred Qualifications
  • Experience with debugging or developing Android applications, tools, or features.
  • Familiarity with the standard Android debugging processes
  • Ability to quickly ramp-up on complex and unfamiliar product areas
  • Experience in working with geographically diverse teams
  • Enthusiastic, motivated and self-driven
  • High tolerance to ambiguity and ability make progress in the face of it
  • Strong communication skills and ability to work across multiple disciplines and functions
