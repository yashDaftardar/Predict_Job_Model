We make robots, but we hire people. Would you like to be part of this journey?

The cloud platform team owns the mission critical functionalities of the UiPath Automation Cloud such as tenancy management, identity, request routing etc. and exposes them as SaaS offerings/SDKs which are consumed by every other UiPath Cloud Service to meet their business requirements.
In simpler terms, if we go down the UiPath Automation Cloud goes down.
Our mission is to build a platform for scale and is also easy to consume for our customers. Quality is baked into our DNA and we believe in being agile and gritty.

Here is What You Would be Doing at UiPath

  • As a Software Engineer I, you will directly design and engineer significant areas of the cloud platform, and ensure our technical deliverables meet the expected service level
  • You will work closely with other engineers and product managers to envision and deliver product capabilities.
  • You will ensure our software meets all requirements of quality, security, scalability and high performance
  • You will participate in and influence our best practices across the engineering organization


Here is What You Will Bring to UiPath

  • 1+ years' experience of architecting and engineering world-class, large scale commercial applications and services
  • Comprehension of object-oriented principles, backed by solid computer science fundamentals
  • Knowledge of one object-oriented language (such as C#/C++/Java)
  • Ability to collaborate with globally distributed teams
  • Knowledge of relational and/or non-relational databases (SQL, MongoDB etc.)
  • Knowledge of cloud providers like Azure, AWS, Google Cloud, etc. and cloud services such as AKS, GKE, etc. is a plus
  • Full-stack development experience is a plus
