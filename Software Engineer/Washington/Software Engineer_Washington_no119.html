About the team

The Zillow Group Ecomms Discovery Team builds the infrastructure to deliver listing notification updates across email and push for the Zillow brand. We are passionate about working with billions of notifications per day to deliver relevant content quickly to users inboxes and devices.

About the role

On the Engagement Communications Discovery team, a software development engineer will be responsible for crafting and delivering critical products and services to distribute home updates and recommendations to users through email and push notifications. Do you have performance-driven backend skills surrounding data pipelining and love to solve sophisticated problems?

You Will Get To:

  • Design and implement reliable, performant, and scalable backend services relating to data pipelines

  • Build and maintain business-critical products relating to push notifications and email that drives over a third of Zillow’s traffic

  • Collaborate with partner teams - such as the Ecomms Platform team as well as the AI Personalization team - to innovate, deliver, and maintain our products

  • Be an owner of core areas and own the quality of your work

  • Advocate for the highest quality of work through unit testing, automation testing, and monitoring

  • Have fun!

This role has been categorized as a Remote position. “Remote” employees do not have a permanent corporate office workplace and, instead, work from a physical location of their choice which must be identified to the Company. Employees may live in any of the 50 US States, with limited exceptions. In certain cases, an employee in a remote-designated job may need to live in a specific region or time zone to support customers or clients as part of their role.

Who you are

  • 3+ years of software development experience with high proficiency in Java, C++, C#, or another object-oriented language.

  • Knowledgeable with SQL and NoSQL databases - such as T-SQL, MySQL, PostGres, MongoDB, etc - Redis/Memcache, or Scylla/Cassandra is a plus

  • Bachelors or higher degree in Computer Science or related field; or 5+ years of relevant experience

  • Proven experience in designing, developing, debugging, and delivering backend services

  • Efficient communicator who is open to ideas and suggestions

Transferable Skills

Here at Zillow - we value the experience and perspective of candidates with non-traditional backgrounds. We encourage you to apply if you have transferable skills or related experiences.

In addition to a competitive base salary this position is also eligible for equity awards based on factors such as experience, performance and location.
