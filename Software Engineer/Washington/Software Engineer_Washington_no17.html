Overview:

Two Barrels is hiring a Software Engineer for $85,000/year. You will be a traditional company employee. This is a full time 40 hour/week position with company benefits.

Two Barrels is always hiring great Software Engineers. We primarily code in Ruby and Javascript, and use Ruby on Rails and Vue as our frameworks. In this position you will work with our Mid Software Engineers and Senior Software Engineers to work towards becoming a valuable part of our Ruby engineering team.

This position is for Engineers who have a few years’ of relevant web-based engineering experience and want to work towards developing new skills. Eventually our goal is for you to become an expert in Ruby on Rails to help make a big impact on our company.

How to Apply:

Please apply through the link below. It’s a coding job, so showing us some code would be helpful. Preferably a flavor of Ruby or Javascript, but anything for now will do. We will do an initial phone interview, a video call with a small group of our team, and then if everyone is still liking things, typically we’d have you come in and meet everyone. However, with COVID-19, we’re doing group video calls right now while we’re all working remotely.
