Costco is looking for a dedicated and experienced C#, ASP.Net developer to design, develop, maintain, extend applications and implement projects in accordance with the Costco development and project management methodologies and practices. This is an excellent opportunity to work on various applications focused on automating and modernizing the E-Commerce pharmacy processes for a fortune 500 company.

ROLE

  • Designs, builds, and maintains efficient, reusable and reliant C# code for relational database applications. Including (but not limited to) the Pharmacy eCommerce System.
  • Designs and implements solutions into existing environments and designing business workflows to ensure proper application mapping/flow.
  • Creates conceptual and detailed technical design documents.
  • Provides accurate and timely estimates (work breakdown schedules).
  • Ensures the best possible performance, quality and responsiveness of applications.
  • Documents and executes unit and integration tests.
  • Defines and implements standards and best practices for software development.
  • Participates in code review sessions as a coordinator, owner, reader, recorder or observer.
  • Collaborates with Business Analysts, Solution Architects, Quality Assurance Analysts, Software Developers, and Release Analysts in a DevOps model to extend the capabilities of the applications.
  • Provides 3rd-level application support for the Pharmacy E-Commerce applications.

REQUIRED

  • Minimum 4 years’ experience with C#, ASP.Net, with a strong knowledge of its related technologies.
  • Familiarity with the .NET framework 4.0 or later.
  • Must have working knowledge of Web Services, SOAP, and XML technologies.
  • Experience programming in major database and middleware technologies.
  • Proficient working knowledge of code versioning tools such as Git, SVN, etc.
  • Strong knowledge of object oriented concepts.
  • Familiarity with continuous integration.
  • Must have strong communication skills, both oral and written.
  • Demonstrated ability to collaborate with other technical experts, develop solution and functional design documents to support development of final solutions, and provide project estimates and lead technical teams to consistently meet commitments.
  • Integrity, accountability, a positive attitude, and be willing to do what it takes to make the team successful.
  • Ability to manage priorities and timelines.
  • Ability to provide project estimates and work-breakdown schedules.
  • Extremely responsive, able to work under pressure in crisis with a strong sense of urgency.
  • Responsible, conscientious, organized, self-motivated and able to work with limited supervision.
  • Detail-oriented and possess strong problem-solving skills and ability to analyze for potential future issues.
  • Able to support off-hours work as required, including weekends, holidays, and rotational on call.

Recommended

  • Experience in ASP.net (C#) (Web forms and User Controls), AJAX concepts, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, Telerik (3rd party controls), OOPS Concepts and Design Patterns.
  • Working knowledge of backend and Pharmacy Management components, web services, related interfaces and configuration.
  • Previous E-Commerce experience in production support team lead or related role.
