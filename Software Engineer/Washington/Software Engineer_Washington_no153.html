Microsoft Teamsis a unified communication and collaboration platform that combines persistent chat, video meetings, file storage and apps. Teams is a ground up product, completely built on Azure cloud platform and cutting-edge Web technologies, serving millions of users world-wide, at scale.

We, in Teams Platform, are looking for experienced engineers/technical leads to join us and be part of an exciting journey in redefining workplace productivity solutions, that scale to our worldwide user base, across industry verticals and multitude of platforms and devices. In this position, you will be empowered to make product-wide technical decisions and directly impact the outcomes for this flagship product, working in a dynamic and collaborative environment.

Our culture is inclusive, casual, and high energy; our team members come from diverse backgrounds and grounded in our customer needs. This is a fantastic opportunity to build services and experiences that millions of people worldwide will use at home, at school, at work, and across their daily lives.

Responsibilities

  • Defining and implementing system level architecture for large-scale components
  • Working with multiple teams across the company to align dependencies and build compelling user scenarios
  • Implementing new features with high quality designs, strong execution, and robust deployment plans
  • Providing technical leadership for strategic initiatives and guiding the broader team
  • Demonstrating passion for customers and partners

Qualifications

  • 8+ years relevant software industry experience
  • Excellent solution design, problem solving, debugging and communication skills
  • Experience in building and shipping large-scale distributed services and APIs, preferably on Azure or AWS platforms
  • Experience with modern web technologies like Node.js, Angular, React, Typescript
  • Customer obsession and experience in running live site for large-scale services
