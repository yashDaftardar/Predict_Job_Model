Software Development Engineer

Job Description

If you are a Colorado resident and this role is a field-based or remote role, you may be eligible to receive additional information about the compensation and benefits for this role, which we will provide upon request. You may contact 888-367-7223 , option 5, for assistance.

In this role, you have the opportunity to

Be involved with the manufacture of technology that is helping to save lives, improve patient outcomes and reduce the cost of medicine across the globe. The technology is a cornerstone in the Philips Healthtech portfolio and has proven to be elusive to our competitors. As an international organization, Philips is well placed to offer a broad range of technical and leadership opportunities in various organizations throughout the world.

You are responsible for

  • Design, implement and test ultrasound components
  • Feature design & implementation
  • Application, infrastructure & signal path development
  • Successful delivery of software components
  • Multi-phase software process deliverables including software test & requirements management
  • Analyzing & solving complex problems

You are a part of

The Ultrasound Software Engineering team, where you will get to work with the best engineers in medical ultrasound and develop ultrasound products that are helping to save lives every day. We have product development activities taking place across multiple locations in the United States and around the world. This position is located in Bothell, WA.

To succeed in this role, you should have the following skills and experience

  • BS or MS in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering or equivalent
  • 2+ years work experience
  • Object-oriented programming languages, design and coding experience (C++)
  • Familiarity with embedded system development a plus
  • Knowledge of software development lifecycle
  • Medical device experience a plus
  • Regulated environment experience a plus

US work authorization is a precondition of employment. The company will not consider candidates who require sponsorship for a work-authorized visa.

Company relocation benefits will not be provided for this position. Candidates need to live within the territory or within commuting distance to Bothell, WA.

In return, we offer you
The Opportunity to sharpen your talents with new challenges in our dynamic organization. As a market-driven company, we are used to listening to our customers & apply the same thinking to our employees. We offer a competitive salary, outstanding benefits and flexibility in a career with a positive and supportive atmosphere in which to develop your talents further.
