The Modern Life and Learning Customer Insights and Platform team is looking for a passionate and motivated software engineer for cloud platform development leveraging Microsoft Power Platform, Dynamics 365, and Azure. The Modern Life and Learning team seeks to empower every learner, family, and educator on the planet to connect, learn, and achieve more. The Customer Insights and Platform team delivers products, platforms, and services that enable the Modern Life and Learning team, Microsoft partners, and Microsoft customers to unlock the potential of data and analytics to drive positive outcomes in modern life and lifelong learning.

As a software engineer, you will be expected to work collaboratively across engineering teams to solve complex technical challenges that achieve business goals and provide excellent quality of service. You will also be expected to demonstrate a growth mindset and adopt relevant new technologies, tools, methods, and processes. As a platform developer, you will build features to be reusable across multiple solutions both internal and external to Microsoft, as well as leveraging the work of others in your solutions.

In this role, you will be developing solutions that will allow the Modern Life and Learning team to amplify its impact by driving efficiency in customer and partner relationship management. Building on Dynamics 365 and Power Platform, integrating telemetry and analytics, these solutions will enable the Modern Life and Learning team to understand, reason about, predict, and support the adoption of their services by customers, and to accelerate growth through a global network of partners.

Responsibilities

  • Participate in design, ideation, and technical discussions
  • Practice Agile software development as a member of a SCRUM team
  • Develop solutions per Microsoft standards, aligned with portfolio roadmaps
  • Own and deliver complete features, including design, architecture, implementation, test automation, deployment, support, and maintenance

Qualifications

Required:
  • 2+ years of experience in full stack software development
  • Experience in Dynamics 365 or similar CRM environment
  • BS/MS in Computer Science or related field

Preferred
  • Experience in Dynamics 365 and Power Platform
  • Experience with C#, ASP.NET, REST, SQL, HTML5, and JavaScript/jQuery
  • Demonstrated collaboration skills with the ability to work in a dynamic / agile environment
  • A passion for improving engineering practices and producing high quality software with a DevOps mindset
  • Self-motivation and proactive attitude
  • Customer-focus with excellent communication and presentation skills
  • A minimum of a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or Engineering, or a related field, or equivalent alternative education, skills, and/or practical experience is required
  • Experience in Azure technologies and Data Engineering
  • Experience in UI technologies like REACT/Angular JS
