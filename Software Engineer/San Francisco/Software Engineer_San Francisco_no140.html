Full Job Description

Rakuten Rewards is looking for a software engineer with strong back end experience to join our rapidly growing engineering team. We’re a team based out of San Mateo responsible for building software used by millions of users. As part of the Rakuten Rewards team, you’ll experience the best of both worlds, a large profitable multinational company with the rapid growth and agility of a startup.

The position will be working with our Rakuten Ad Settings squad. Working in a Tribe/Squad structure, our team is responsible for developing the next generation of services that will enhance Rakuten Rewards’ ability for user personalization and relevancy to fulfill the needs of our merchant partners and members alike. We strive to build the best technology that fits our business needs.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
Responsible for hands-on development of backend microservices used in a fast paced and highly scalable environment.

Responsible for hands-on development of solutions for our internal Admin tool.

Understands how to break work down into concise deliverables with a focus on iterative product delivery

Operate independently to help develop and iterate on the next generation of services

Own solutions end to end from conception to release by managing complexity and collaboratively working with various stakeholders to achieve desired outcomes

Debug and resolve issues across multiple technology stacks and services

Contribute to weekly product releases and code reviews with a small and dynamic team

Collaborate across multiple disciplines and teams

MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS (Knowledge, Skills, Abilities)
Excellent understanding of OOP design principles (loose coupling, encapsulation, etc.)

Thorough knowledge of programming paradigms (multithreading, caching, performance tuning).

Good experience with CI/CD pipelines (Jenkins, Github)

Good experience with cloud infrastructure and deployment frameworks (AWS, Kubernetes, Docker)

Strong SQL and data-analysis skills

Strong orientation towards data and data driven decisions

Front-End experience (React, Vue, JS ES5, HTML/CSS) a plus

Experience with data pipelines and data warehouses (Redshift, Snowflake) a plus

QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
B.S. or M.S in Computer Science or equivalent experience

2+ years of application development using Java, Python or Golang

2+ years of working on and/or building microservices from the ground up

Experience with AWS, Kubernetes, Docker, Ci/CD tooling

Experience with React, Node.js a plus

RAKUTEN SHUGI PRINCIPLES
