About the Team

Product teams at Uber create the vision for the future of urban mobility: deeply understanding our customers to solve their transportation needs with innovative technology. Data underpins our products, enabling intelligent decision making and improved user experiences. Leveraging the latest ML, Big Data, data visualization, and NLP technologies, the Product Platform team works at the intersection of engineering and data science to enhance our services and deliver actionable insights.

About the Role

As a Backend Software Engineer at Customer Obsession team at Uber, you will architect highly-performant, reliable, scalable applications used by millions of external customers every day. During the Covid19 situation, we have accelerated to build a complete virtual support experience for our customers building Chat, Video products for end consumers and building Help Center experiences with automation workflows. You will have an outstanding opportunity to build a premier and first of its kind support experience for our users. You will work closely with our internal stakeholders across all lines of business - Rides, Eats, Freight, etc. - to build and match customers with the best care channels (phone, chat, video) for their specific needs. At Uber, we have a high bar for quality & efficiency - you will collaborate closely with Product Managers and Business Operations teams to build for scale, yet solutions focused on providing delightful customer experiences. The team is focused on high business impact, where every product we ship is measured and tracked with cost savings and/or efficiency improvement.

What You'll Do

  • Your technical expertise will have a direct impact on hundreds of millions of daily customer interactions
  • Own end to end delivery of features across Uber's entire technical stack
  • Adapt to meet the needs of our massive growth and rapidly evolving systems.
  • Write maintainable and extensible code with high quality tests and documentation.

Basic Qualifications

  • BS/MS in Computer Science or related field required
  • 2+ years backend development experience.
  • Experience implementing complex projects with multiple dependencies
  • Experience with Node.js, Java, Go, or other backend technologies

Preferred Qualifications

  • Extensive Go experience
