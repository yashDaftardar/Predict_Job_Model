About the role

We are looking for imaginative individuals well versed in the customer experience, Buy-Cart Web experience specifically. We’re looking for an individual who has utilized cutting-edge, front-end technologies to lead and deliver complex and spectacular solutions on a large scale. Join us and flex your imagination with designing and developing industry-leading features that are reliable, scalable, and create a great customer experience!

What you'll do

  • Take ownership, work collaboratively with technical and product/design team members to develop and maintain UX product features following Agile development principles.
  • Create robust solutions and multi-task across initiatives while building out a dynamic and productive development, continuously improving practices and methodology
  • Engage actively with team members to continually enhance engineering design, practices, and methodology.
  • Generate strategic thinking and creating sustainable solutions that span across projects and domains while maintaining, simplicity, reliability, manageability, scalability, extensibility, performance, and re-use
  • Install and reinforce best-in-class DevOps practices with end-to-end “design, build and run” responsibilities
  • Guide and advocate with technical expertise in the areas of architecture, design, implementation, and testing
  • Develop code using standards-based solutions, drive adoption via mentoring and guiding others

Who you are

  • 5+ years of experience in software engineering with relevant front-end technologies like Advanced JavaScript and MVC frameworks
  • Experience with running workloads on Public Clouds such as AWS, Azure, or GCP, with experience with container-based technologies like Docker and Cloud Foundry a plus.
  • In-depth knowledge and technical expertise with ReactJS, NodeJS, and NPM
  • Extensive experience and proficiency with cross-browser, cross-platform development including Mobile Web
  • Expertise in designing and delivering with performance, security, and accessibility in mind. Expertise in designing and integrating with service-based architectures.
  • Experience with modern DevOps development tools including Git, Jenkins, JIRA, PagerDuty and logging/monitoring tools such as New Relic and Splunk
  • Knowledge of i18N (internationalization) and A11y (accessibility) best practices a plus
