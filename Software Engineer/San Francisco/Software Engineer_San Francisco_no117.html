Worldcoin is a new global digital currency that will launch by giving a share to every single person on earth.

We believe that the transition to a native financial layer on the internet is an important step for humanity. It is free, frictionless and not controlled by anyone. This transition has already started on a small scale for insiders, but cryptocurrencies have failed to reach mass adoption over the last 10 years.

Worldcoin aims to rectify this by creating a new cryptocurrency and distributing it to everyone on earth through a novel approach: a dedicated hardware device ensuring both humanness and uniqueness of everybody signing up, while maintaining their privacy and the overall transparency of a permissionless blockchain.

Software Engineer (IoT)

As an IoT software engineer, you will work on building a production-ready IoT device. You will work with Linux, Python, and Rust to implement an efficient solution that takes into account the restricted resources of our IoT device. You will also be responsible for building the technologies and tools that surround the device, including the technologies used for CI/CD, the scripts used by the hardware team for development, and the tools for enabling the large scale manufacturing. This role requires flexibility, as well as some familiarity with embedded devices and technologies.

Responsibilities

  • Architecture and implementation of the firmware
  • Implementation of the software tooling used by other teams
  • Close collaboration with the Product team to create a world class UX
  • Design and implementation of CI/CD
  • Close collaboration with the infrastructure to fit your design with the backend requirements

Requirements

  • Prior experience with embedded devices
  • Deep knowledge of Rust
  • Deep knowledge of Linux
  • Experience architecting and implementing a commercially-used IoT device
  • Familiarity with CI/CD practices and the ability to own an end-to-end development process
  • Good understanding of security and how it relates to firmware development
  • Good knowledge of Linux key security principles
  • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment
  • Fast learner
  • Proactivity and independence
