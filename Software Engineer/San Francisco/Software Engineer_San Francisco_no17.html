Job Description

Zendesk is a service-first CRM company that builds powerful, customizable software designed to improve customer relations. At Zendesk, we encourage growth and innovation, and we believe in giving back to the communities we call home.

The Zendesk Custom Data team is looking for a proven backend engineer with experience in large scale systems to help craft and build our next-gen custom data platform.

What you will enjoy doing:

  • Put the customer first and have quality in mind.

  • Collaborate with different teams to build a new platform to unlock our data extensibility for our customers.

  • Work closely with product management when new features are being considered by being an advocate for not impacting the performance or quality of the existing product.

  • Participate in technical discussions and communicate your ideas or vision to others. You thrive in dealing with sophisticated problems, open to debates and brainstormings.

  • Collaborate with product managers, engineering managers, and support advocates to estimate and prioritize units of work.

  • Be responsible for the full release cycle from the development to deployment

What you bring to the role:

  • 2+ years of proven background in building, scaling and operating large backend infrastructures.

  • Proven experience in building object models, schema management, object record management, and/or rule engine.

  • Proficient with either Ruby, Java, or both.

  • An ability to review and understand an unfamiliar language would be preferred.

Nice to have:

  • Experience with Ruby is a plus

  • Experience with building RESTful API’s is a plus.

  • Experience with agile development processes, working in a fast-paced environment with continuous integration.

  • Experience with Continuous Deployment systems like Travis, Jenkins and Spinnaker

At Zendesk, we believe that every great customer relationship stems from a conversation. So we built a customer service software company that designs solutions to foster better customer relationships. From large enterprises to startups, powerful, innovative customer experiences should be within reach for every company, no matter the size, industry or ambition. Zendesk serves more than 160,000 customers across a multitude of industries in over 30 languages. Zendesk is headquartered in San Francisco, and operates offices worldwide.
