Job Overview
The Modern Life Experiences (MLX) team builds innovative new customer experiences for families that work on Windows, Xbox, mobile, and web. We focus on providing peace of mind through digital and physical safety and helping families learn, grow, and have more quality time together. Millions of families rely on our easy-to-use software.

We're looking for an Android engineer who is passionate about human-centered design, finding simple solutions to complex problems, excels at solving real issues for real people, and enjoys working with the latest technologies. If you feel you’ve got the skills, experience, and the desire to create a meaningful impact in the lives of millions, we’d love to hear from you!

Responsibilities

Job Responsibilities
  • Develop and release features for the Android Family Safety app.
  • Work with our product and design teams to implement an intuitive and engaging user experience.
  • Ensure high quality, stable and performant code.
  • Participate in code and design reviews.

Qualifications

Basic Qualifications
  • A total of 3+ years of experience building and shipping software at scale
  • In that time, 2+ years of Android experience working in Kotlin or Java and releasing apps to the Play Store.
  • Bachelor's in computer science or a related field.
  • Knowledge of object-oriented software development, algorithms and data structures.

Preferred Qualifications
  • Strong UI design
  • Experience with Jetpack and Kotlin coroutines
  • Familiarity with web services, API development or database development.
