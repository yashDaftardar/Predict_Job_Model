Full Job Description

Commure enables innovators to build modern health software that improves the quality of care and its efficiency of delivery. We believe that better software for doctors, nurses and patients – and ultimately for the entire healthcare system – comes from connecting the top minds in technology, healthcare and design.

We are seeking talented Software Engineers to join our growing Engineering team. At Commure, engineers work side by side with designers, architects, researchers, product managers, healthcare partners, as well as physicians within our company to design, implement, and maintain solutions that meet the concrete needs of providers.

We are a welcoming and diverse team with a wide range of backgrounds and experiences. We pride ourselves on building robust, high-quality software using a modern tech stack. If you share our commitment to improving healthcare innovation and value close partnerships between developers and doctors, come work with us!

As a Software Engineer at Commure, you will:

    • Build and ship enterprise-grade software
    • Solve impactful engineering and product problems
    • Provide feedback and collaborate on a variety of projects, including both design and code reviews
    • Work on cutting-edge cloud technologies while leveraging leading open-source standards in healthcare
    • Have end-to-end ownership of projects throughout their lifecycle, supporting both engineers at Commure and third-party developers
    • Join our collaborative Engineering, Product, and Design team and provide a valued voice as we scale our team and culture

We are looking for Software Engineers who:

    • Are inspired by our mission to improve health software and reduce burnout among providers
    • Enjoy wrangling real-world complexity into deceivingly simple solutions
    • Share a commitment to high-quality engineering practices using modern tools and languages
    • Exhibit grit, high integrity, a team mindset, and an affinity for continuous learning
    • Thrive in a collaborative, diverse environment
    • Have a solid foundation in at least one programming language
    • 2+ Years of Product Engineering experience within a cloud environment

Benefits:

    • Medical, Dental, and Vision Coverage
    • FSA & HSA Accounts
    • Commuter Benefits
    • Flexible Time Off
    • 401k
Commure is committed to creating and fostering a diverse team. We are open to all backgrounds and levels of experience, and believe that great people can always find a place. We are committed to providing reasonable accommodations to all applicants throughout the application process.
