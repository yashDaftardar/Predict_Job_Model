We are GapJumpers and we’re changing the way companies hire the best people, by removing the implicit bias that exists in the current hiring process.

By making skills the focus of the hiring process, more diverse talent can easily prove that they are good enough for the job.

We're a funded international startup and we're looking to create a great dev team. Are you interested in gaining exposure while working directly with international clients and an extremely talented home-team from around the world.

We are looking for an engineering intern (paid) who’ll join our team and grow into a full time Software Engineer role.

Our technology stack consists of:
Django, Python, Postgres, nginx.

You're going to play a key role in:
Working closely with the users, founders, designers and business developers, etc.

Developing the platform/ even choosing our technologies as we grow.

Setting our product direction with innovative and creative technology implementations.

Building a product that shall change the way top companies hire forever.

Work with a team that is extremely passionate about their product and interested in taking on a hard social problem. We're a team with international experiences and aim to meet the best industry and social standards for quality of output.

Your past experiences should include:
Code deployment (python)

The desire to take on tough challenges and find creative solutions.

Can grok a new technology pretty fast.

Good understanding of databases (SQL/noSQL/etc.)

Good understanding of a full web stack.

At least basic front-end skills.

Plus:
Above average frontend skills (CSS/JS knowledge, maybe some JS frameworks like Backbone or knockout or Angular)

Good understanding of RESTful services

Experience with APIs (facebook/twitter/other services) and OAuth 2.0

Experience with version control (git/subversion)

Basic understanding of text/content parsing technologies.

This is a paid position and is open to applicants from any part of the world.
