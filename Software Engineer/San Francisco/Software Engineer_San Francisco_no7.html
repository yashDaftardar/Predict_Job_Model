About Us:

Launched in 2011, Twitch is a global community that comes together each day to create multiplayer entertainment: unique, live, unpredictable experiences created by the interactions of millions. We bring the joy of co-op to everything, from casual gaming to world-class esports to anime marathons, music, and art streams. Twitch also hosts TwitchCon, where we bring everyone together to celebrate and grow their personal interests and passions. We're always live at Twitch. Stay up to date on all things Twitch on Linkedin ( https://www.linkedin.com/company/twitch-tv/), Twitter (https://twitter.com/Twitch ) and on our Blog (https://blog.twitch.tv).

About the Role

Creators are the backbone of Twitch, and their sustainability relies on their ability to earn a living doing what they love. As a remote Engineer in the Consumer Organization, your main focus is to build products that allow creators to earn a living.

You Will:

  • Work with leads and other engineers to design platform and services
  • Be customer-focused to contribute to project deliverables
  • Work with other teams to clarify product requirements
  • Participate in design and code reviews

You Have:

  • 1+ years with software engineering experience (intern or part-time)
  • Minimum of Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or prior relevant engineering experiences
  • Experience with data structures, algorithms, data modeling and schema design
  • Proficient experience in one of the following languages: Go, Ruby, Java, and C++

Bonus Points

  • Familiar with AWS ecosystem
  • Twitch user, broadcaster, or partner or experience in the gaming industry
  • Bachelor's degree or above in Computer Science or relevant field
