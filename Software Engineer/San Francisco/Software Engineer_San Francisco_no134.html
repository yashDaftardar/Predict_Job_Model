We’re looking for a strong full-stack engineer to join our Ads Engineering team. You’ll be joining an early stage team that is growing quickly and building the best merchandising and advertising experience on customer-facing produce surfaces. The ideal candidate should have experience building full-stack and user-facing products, be driven to become a technical expert in their domain, and have a passion for product quality and building systems to ship high quality releases.

What you’ll do:

  • Design and lead the implementation of highly-reliable and easy-to-understand product features for advertisers and merchants
  • Run A/B experiments of product features and perform data analysis on results
  • Influence and lead technical decisions across wider engineering teams in different domains
  • Collaborate with Design, Product and Engineering teams to shape product design and specifications

What we’re looking for:

  • 2+ years of full-stack development experience (Javascript/HTML/CSS , React/Redux, ExpressJS, Next.js, etc.)
  • Experiences in building consumer facing products from scratch to end
  • Holistic knowledge and passion for full stack development
  • A data-driven analytical thinker who leverages experiments and data to make product decisions
  • Strong communication skills and great product intuitions
