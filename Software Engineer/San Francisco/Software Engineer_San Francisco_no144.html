Full Job Description

At Plato, we’re on a mission to help engineering managers become great leaders. Our platform intelligently matches “mentees” to highly experienced engineering leaders of various backgrounds to help resolve their most pressing management challenges. After raising over $3M from SaaStr's Jason Lemkin, Slack, Zoom CEO Eric Yuan, and more earlier this year, we've experienced exceptional and rapid growth.

In joining us, you’ll enjoy a scrappy startup environment, collaborative and thoughtful team members, and full flexibility as part of a 100% remote company. Plato provides everything you personally need to get your job done. We are deeply passionate about our mission and lucky to see the impact of our work, everyday!

We’re looking for someone who’s passionate about building products that can last.

If you are looking for a new challenge and want to have a substantial impact in a fast growing company, come and join us!

Who you are

    • A mission-driven person: At Plato, we're all passionate about our mission to elevate the leadership game in the world
    • A product-minded engineer: You will not be building features without fully understanding how they add user value
    • A strong technologist: You are a back-end expert or with strong front-end capabilities and the team can rely on you for any technical issue
    • A team player: You're a natural team player, and you can communicate with other non-technical team members with ease
    • A pragmatist: Your solutions are not ivory towers, but driven by context and coherence
    • A thinker: You are logical, can evaluate a problem quickly, abstract it, and propose creative solutions

      • We strongly encourage people from historically underrepresented communities in technology to apply, including but not limited to women, people of color, people with disabilities and those who identify as LGBTQ.

Requirements

    • Experience in development: 2-5 years of professional experience in software engineering
    • Proficiency with the following programming languages: Java, Javascript / Typescript, Python
    • Natural leadership skills : Communication, Pedagogy, Grit, Patience
    • Contributing to open source project is a plus

Responsibilities

    • You will define the technical architecture for our product
    • You will establish the development norms and methods for the team
    • Ensure quality by reviewing the team’ s code
    • Learn from the best engineering and product leaders in the world (https://www.platohq.com/mentors)
Very few companies have the potential to do something truly meaningful to actually help others. We believe that making managers better, more successful, and happier results in a happier overall workplace. And improving that even 10% leads to a ripple effect.

We spend nearly all of our waking lives at work. Let's make that better.

Please note: Our team is fully remote - the only requirement for your location is that you are able to commit to a 3-4 hour overlap with PST (the majority of our team is based out of San Francisco).
