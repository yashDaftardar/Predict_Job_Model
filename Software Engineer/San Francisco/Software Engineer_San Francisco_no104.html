Description

SS&C Advent, a leading provider of award-winning software and services for the global investment management industry, is seeking a Software Engineer at our office in San Francisco. Advent’s software solutions provide investment professionals with tools for research and investing. Our clients are analysts, portfolio managers, and traders at some of the industry's leading hedge funds, traditional asset management funds, endowments, foundations, funds of funds, and private equity firms.

We are looking for someone who:

  • Passionate about building world class solutions
  • Produces high quality code through TDD
  • Experiments with new technology
  • Enjoys solving both technical and business problems
  • Puts team success over personal achievement
  • Comfortable working in an agile environment

Responsibilities

  • Design, implement, and test business and technical components for a microservices architecture
  • Leverage cloud computing platform to build highly scalable, low latency solutions
  • Define and implement REST APIs
  • Work in interdisciplinary teams to design, define and implement product features
  • Improve the quality and stability of the codebase through continuous refactoring

Minimum Qualifications

  • BS or MS in Computer Science or related field
  • Strong object oriented and design and coding skills (Java, C#)
  • Solid foundation in data structures and algorithms and problem solving skills
  • Experience with the Java technology stack
  • Experience developing REST based Web Services
  • 2-5 years of professional programming experience

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience developing financial applications
  • Experience using in memory computing frameworks like GridGain or Hazelcast
  • Experience with Kubernetes and Docker or other container management systems
  • Experience building large scale, distributed systems
  • Experience with relational and NoSQL persistence models
  • Experience using iPaaS tools such as DELL Boomi
