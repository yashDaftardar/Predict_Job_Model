Backend Software Engineer

Panther Labs
San Francisco, CA

The Job

As a Backend Software Engineer at Panther Labs, you will be part of a team designing and delivering Panther's core services including real-time analysis engines, databases, APIs, monitoring, testing, and CLI tools.


The Company

Panther Labs is a security startup founded in San Francisco, CA with the mission to help companies detect and respond to security breaches at a cloud-scale. The company was founded by security practitioners who wanted to solve modern detection challenges using code and automation, developer workflows, and big data for security investigations. Panther's platform allows security engineers to focus on what they know best, reducing risk, detecting attacks, and protecting their organizations while avoiding the common pitfalls of overhead and operations.

Panther is a fast-growing company with employees spread across the world. Our customers include companies like GitLab, Canva, to LaunchDarkly, and we were recently named #4 on the EnterpriseTech30's startup list!


The Responsibilities

  • Owning end-to-end development of multiple microservices
  • Integrating with 3rd party APIs
  • Cross-functional collaboration to deliver the very best user experience
  • Designing, developing and maintaining large scale data processing systems

The Requirements

  • 2+ years development experience with Golang
  • Experience with multiple AWS serverless technologies: DynamoDB, S3, SQS, Lambda
  • Familiar with secure development practices
  • Cyber security experience, such as application, infrastructure, host/network instrumentation, or incident response is a plus
