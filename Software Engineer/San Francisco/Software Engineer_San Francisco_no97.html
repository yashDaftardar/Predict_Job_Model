Company Description Square builds common business tools in unconventional ways so more people can start, run, and grow their businesses. When Square started, it was difficult and expensive (or just plain impossible) for some businesses to take credit cards. Square made credit card payments possible for all by turning a mobile phone into a credit card reader. Since then Square has been building an entire business toolkit of both hardware and software products including Square Capital, Square Terminal, Square Payroll, and more. We’re working to find new and better ways to help businesses succeed on their own terms—and we’re looking for people like you to help shape tomorrow at Square.
Job Description


The Conversations team builds and operates Square Messages, a messaging platform for merchants to manage conversations with their customers across multiple channels (SMS, email, etc.). Messages is also home to Square Assistant - a virtual assistant to help merchants serve their customers 24/7. The product is scaling up and out: to new channels with Apple Business Chat, Instagram, and Facebook integrations, and to new use cases, such as ecommerce, invoicing, 3rd party APIs, and eventually a virtual business phone. The team itself was created in mid-2019 with the acquisition of Eloquent Labs.

We're looking for engineers with an interest in backend/server engineering to help build out Square Messages, and to support scaling out our NLP backend infrastructure for Square Assistant.

You will...

  • Play a key role in implementing a new messaging product within Square.
  • Build highly available and fault tolerant systems for real-time messaging.
  • Partner with AI, iOS, and web engineers to ship features.
  • Collaborative with product managers and designers to refine and establish product scope and design.
  • Ship cutting-edge AI to businesses that traditionally haven’t had access to it.

Qualifications
  • 4+ years of relevant industry experience with a bachelor's degree in computer science or equivalent.
  • Experience with system design and implementation
  • Experience with AWS and Java is recommended
  • Experience with microservice meshes is a plus
  • Experience in Python and an interest in machine learning is a plus
  • Interest to help shape the mission and roadmap of a new team
  • Eagerness to share your ideas, and openness to those of others
