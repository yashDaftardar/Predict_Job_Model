Job Summary

We are seeking a Software Operations Engineer to work on our Engineering Operations team to maintain and support our Micro Services Architecture. This is a hands-on opportunity to work on various technologies and interface with all aspects of the business. The Engineering Operations team primarily work on short projects so there is a lot of variety and very little chance to get bored of any one tech stack. In addition, there's a lot of exposure to a wide variety of styles and sizes of projects, as well as numerous opportunities for growth and development. This is great opportunity for someone who is interested in progressing their career into software development.

GLG's Core Engineering team builds the technology eco-system that enables effective and efficient operations for the company globally. Our team of engineers are laser focused on deploying technology solutions that instrument the business, modernize the systems stack, increase cybersecurity maturity, and enable enterprise productivity.


Specific responsibilities include (but are not limited to):

  • Be in charge of the continuous operations and reliability of over 500 applications
  • Monitor ticket queue for incoming service disruption reports
  • Interface with Helpdesk and ticket originator to determine affected applications
  • Depending on the issue either alter the application to fix the issue, writing code to do so or write scripts (SQL/bash) to fix issues
  • When applicable create monitoring to alert of future similar issues
  • When applicable program feature sets if approved by management
  • Determine true effect of any service interruptions, i.e. whether any data corruptions may have occurred and fix them
  • Regularly interface with our legacy deployment architecture and our new AWS based deployment architecture.
  • Communicate resolutions to stakeholders and be able to explain technical solution to non technical people.

An ideal candidate will have the following:

  • At least 1 year in a related tech role (i.e. tech support/helpdesk, QA, if experience less than 2 years in development) and at least 1 years of experience in software engineering or substantial project experience
  • Experience with Javascript or Node.js. is required
  • Familiarity with React.js or Angular.js is preferred
  • Ability to write basic SQL queries is preferred
  • Must be a team player and good collaborator
  • Good people and communication skills are required, as well as applicable customer support or tech support experience
  • Must have a desire to learn new and different technologies
