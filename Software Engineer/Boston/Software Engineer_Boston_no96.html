Embedded Software Engineer

Do you love collaborating with teams to solve complex problems?

Do you want to accelerate your career?

Be part of a successful team

The team at Panametrics, a Baker Hughes business, designs and manufactures a range of sensing instrumentation and sensors that are utilized in a vast array of market segments.

Partner with the best

Panametrics is looking for a Embedded Software Engineer to work collaboratively with Global cross functional teams to develop state of the art, next-generation industrial sensing products, solutions and platforms.

As a Embedded Sodtware Engineer, you will be responsible for:

  • Executing of product design, validation, and certification of industrial grade embedded systems
  • Performing cross platform development of C/C++ code while maintaining code quality through best practices and code quality automation
  • Developing system architectures for multi-channel data acquisition and signal processing products
  • Supporting and sustaining activities across a broad product portfolio
  • Evaluating system level requirements by brainstorming with Product Specialists, Product Managers, and other peers throughout the organization
  • Supporting the creation and implementation of process-driven design decisions (CPO; NPI and NTI; Operations, Sales, and Service support)

Fuel your passion

To be successful in this role you will:

  • Have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, or equivalent from an accredited college or university.
  • 1-2 years' experience with developing embedded C/C++ applications for embedded operating systems such as Micrium Î¼C/OS, Nucleus, Integrity RTOS, FreeRTOS etc. on ARM targets
  • Understanding of low-level component communications, including SPI, I2C, UARTs
  • Familiarity with data acquisition and control, A/D converters, PWM
  • Ability to code for hardware at the device driver level
