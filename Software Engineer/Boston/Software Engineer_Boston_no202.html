About The Opportunity:
We’re all about connecting hungry diners with our network of over 300,000 restaurants nationwide. Innovative technology, user-friendly platforms and streamlined delivery capabilities set us apart and make us an industry leader in the world of online food ordering. When you join our team, you become part of a community that works together to innovate, solve problems, grow, work hard and have a ton of fun in the process!

Why Work For Us
Grubhub is a place where authentically fun culture meets innovation and teamwork. We believe in empowering people and opening doors for new opportunities. If you’re looking for a place that values strong relationships, embraces diverse ideas–all while having fun together–Grubhub is the place for you!

Our Ruby on Rails Developers build and maintain the core set of systems that power the restaurant ecosystem, an online ordering interface and network of dozens of mobile payments apps (maybe even the ones you use to order lunch) and point-of-sale integrations.

This engine powers a set of features used by dozens of major brands, enabling them to own their communication channel to incentivize first time and return visits for a varied customer base. Whether it's engaging diners for a new store opening or keeping your most loyal diners hooked, our rewards engine is the backbone of these interactions. Brands have the ability to segment their customer base on a number of dimensions from ordering frequency to brand engagement and in turn use these segments to better target users, communicate intent and distribute rewards. Our reward constructs are always evolving to stay ahead of the industry with features like a progress rewards store where diners use their hard earned points to buy tasty rewards.
The Impact You Will Make:
  • You will help build the tools that empower businesses to excite and reward their customers in new ways.
  • Work on the infrastructure that powers rewards for grubhub.com and numerous other nationally-recognized restaurant brands that serve millions of diners.
  • Explore the limits of our current system to ensure that we’re able to meet the scale and reliability needs for a always-on, highly visible part of the Grubhub product.
  • Deliver, deploy, maintain, and monitor the performance of the underlying application and the platform.
  • Evaluate new tools, technologies, and vendors.
  • Write code that meets our standards of quality, performance, and security. Review the code of peers and apply the same criteria.
What You Bring to the Table:

5+ years professional software development experience with a modern web framework like Django or Ruby on Rails. Rails experience is strongly preferred

  • Experience architecting, designing, and developing testable, fault tolerant, and performant software.
  • Strong knowledge of computer science fundamentals such as data structures and algorithms.
  • Knowledge or experience with developing distributed systems.
  • Previous experience with relational database design fundamentals (PostgreSQL).
  • Experience working with RESTful APIs.
  • Distributed version control expertise with Git.
  • Experience with or desire for mentoring/coaching of more junior engineers.
  • Ability to operate independently, stay motivated and organized, and act as a team player.
