Job summary:
The Red Hat Ecosystem Engineering team is looking for a Principal Software Engineer to join our Telco Solutions and Enablement team. In this role, you will serve as a key member of our architecture team, providing technical leadership and contributions to strategic 5G and next-generation telco service provider offerings and solutions. You will lead complex projects and deliver architectures including Red Hat’s platforms and technologies from Red Hat’s partners. You will be a member of a geographically distributed team which engages early and works closely with partners in Red Hat’s partner ecosystem, customers, and peer groups in the Ecosystem Engineering team, our product and sales teams, and open source communities and standards bodies. The project activities in which you will participate will include devising and reviewing technical architectures, building proofs of concepts using Red Hat’s platforms, and designing and delivering repeatable solutions. As a Principal Software Engineer, you’ll need to be able to thrive on designing and delivering positive outcomes to technically challenging problems while embracing and amplifying Red Hat’s core values of freedom, courage, commitment and accountability. You’ll also need to be comfortable working in an environment where each project may be different from the last and where your skills, expertise, determination, and ability to improvise will be key. Successful applicants must reside in a country where Red Hat is registered to do business.
Primary job responsibilities:
  • Work with partners, service provider customers, Red Hat’s product teams, and internal and external communities to identify challenges and opportunities in the telco and service provider space
  • Collaborate within and across Red Hat’s teams to identify feature gaps; specify, design, and realize telco service provider features and solutions
  • Build proofs of concepts using Red Hat’s platforms and partner workloads
  • Lobby internally for technology development and support needed to deliver strategic next-generation offerings
  • Advocate your work and Red Hat’s position in open source communities and appropriate standards bodies
  • Provide technical thought leadership within the Ecosystem Engineering team
  • Work with managers to define and refine work items
  • Mentor and lead within the group, fostering continuous improvement in a rapidly changing environment
  • Contribute to collective success through regular knowledge sharing within and outside the company
  • Present at relevant conferences, representing Red Hat and the projects with which you are associated
  • Serve as a voice of Red Hat for partnered development activities
Required skills:
  • Industry experience working in a telco or service provider
  • Industry experience serving in a leadership role delivering into production environments
  • Deep understanding enabling you to solve challenging technical, business, and operational problems
  • Proficiency in specifying and communicating requirements, architectures, designs, and intent
  • Knowledge of containers, virtualization, cloud computing, or cloud-native architecture, development, and automation
  • Flexibility to work with a geographically distributed remote working team
  • Record leading and contributing to open source communities, industry forums, or working groups
  • Proficiency in Linux operation and administration
  • Excellent interpersonal and verbal skills; excellent written communication skills
  • Ability to present at industry and summits and conferences
  • Record of involvement in standards bodies

Relevant knowledge of and experience with the following domains and standards:

  • Network functions virtualization (NFV)
  • Software-defined networking (SDN)
  • Routing and switching protocols and network architecture
  • Operational support systems/business support systems (OSS/BSS)
  • Cloud-native applications and architectures
  • 4/5G Core, RAN, and virtualized RAN (vRAN)

The following are considered a plus:

  • Experience with Red Hat OpenShift, Red Hat CoreOS, other Red Hat’s offerings, or industry cloud or virtualization technologies
  • Knowledge of Red Hat Ansible Automation Platform
  • Record of development of Operators for Red Hat OpenShift or Kubernetes
  • Distributed system design and development experience
  • Networking software development experience
  • Experience with Linux system programming
  • Experience in software testing or continuous integration (CI) and continuous delivery (CD) pipelines
