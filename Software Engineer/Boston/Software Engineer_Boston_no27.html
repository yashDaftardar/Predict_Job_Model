Full Job Description

The Solutions team is looking for an experienced software engineer to work on our contractual obligations with customers. We need a service-minded engineer that can meet and build creative solutions to customer problems and requirements. If you love to learn different technologies, have a passion for non-traditional interfaces and would like to work in a role where you will grow tremendously, the Solutions team at Pison is the place for you.

***Due to our work with the United States government, US citizenship is mandatory.***

The Role:

  • Own components or entire applications on customer contracts.
  • Work on projects across numerous languages and frameworks, initially focusing on Android and C# based work, and enabling novel methods of human computer interaction.
  • Learn something new every day - constantly augmenting existing experience with emerging tech required for cutting edge USG customer projects.
  • Work cross functionally with the USG team - getting requirements and iterating on functional products.
  • Integration of disparate systems into a single, cohesive product
  • Autonomous software development execution

About You:

  • Experienced Software Developer, having worked with Android, Java/Kotlin, or C#
  • Service minded - you have the customer in mind whilst building solutions, and are flexible and excited to adapt to feedback.
  • Git experience
  • Knowledge and prior use of data interchange formats (e.g., XML, JSON)
  • Entrepreneurial spirit; you enjoy diving into new requests and working out the problems.
  • Experience working on a team, including source and revision control
  • LOVE to learn

Bonus:

  • Experience working with:
  • Robotics
  • UAV/UGV Drones
  • Machine Learning
  • Knowledge of networking infrastructure and transmission protocols
  • System architecture experience

We understand that candidates can feel discouraged if they do not tick every box. We encourage you to apply. Pison is building a diverse team with people from all backgrounds who can help us build an inclusive organization.
