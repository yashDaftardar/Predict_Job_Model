AWS External Security Services (ESS) is redefining how enterprises are protecting their cloud-based applications and services. We are innovating in cloud security with services like Amazon GuardDuty, Amazon Macie, Amazon Detective, and AWS Security Hub.
This role is within a newly-established team that will be launching a customer-facing service in the security analytics space. It's a great opportunity to get in on the ground floor and help build something significant from scratch. You will be the primary Console/UI developer for this new product, setting internal code architecture, working with Product and UX, and implementing the customer experience.

Our team puts a high value on work-live balance. It isn’t about how many hours you spend at home or at work; it’s about the flow you establish that brings energy to both parts of your life. We believe striking the right balance between your personal and professional life is critical to life-long happiness and fulfillment. We offer flexibility in working hours and encourage you to find your own balance between your work and personal lives.
