About Us:

As a not-for-profit organization, Mass General Brigham is committed to supporting patient care, research, teaching, and service to the community by leading innovation across our system. Founded by Brigham and Women’s Hospital and Massachusetts General Hospital, Partners HealthCare supports a complete continuum of care including community and specialty hospitals, a managed care organization, a physician network, community health centers, home care and other health-related entities. Several of our hospitals are teaching affiliates of Harvard Medical School, and our system is a national leader in biomedical research.

We’re focused on a people-first culture for our system’s patients and our professional family. That’s why we provide our employees with more ways to achieve their potential. Partners HealthCare is committed to aligning our employees’ personal aspirations with projects that match their capabilities and creating a culture that empowers our managers to become trusted mentors. We support each member of our team to own their personal development—and we recognize success at every step.

Our employees use the Mass General Brigham values to govern decisions, actions and behaviors. These values guide how we get our work done: Patients, Affordability, Accountability & Service Commitment, Decisiveness, Innovation & Thoughtful Risk; and how we treat each other: Diversity & Inclusion, Integrity & Respect, Learning, Continuous Improvement & Personal Growth, Teamwork & Collaboration.


General Summary/Overview Statement

We are seeking a senior level candidate with no less than 10 years experience in information technology and architecture to work with various multidisciplinary project teams in support of the Partners eCare program. Qualified candidates must have extremely strong technical and communication skills and be able to thrive in a fast paced, ever-changing environment. A successful candidate will be a highly motivated individual with a very strong technical and software development background. Expertise in systems integration, SOA and various programming languages (including but not limited to Cache and .NET) are required. Excellent communication and technical writing skills are a must.


Principal Duties and Responsibilities

The Software Engineer, under the guidance of the Manager, will gather requirements and implement software, including APIs and Graphical User Interfaces. This role includes but is not limited to the following responsibilities:

  • Review integration requirements and propose technical architectures to meet project goals.
  • Works closely with the PHS Enterprise & Partners eCare teams to design, develop and implement technical integration solutions as they relate to interfaces, conversions, services and applications.
  • Must take direction from leadership, but strong ability to work independently without close daily supervision.
  • Ensure all necessary technical documentation is properly written, managed and communicated within project timelines.
  • Develop, manage and support necessary application and services that help the Enterprise integrate 3rd party applications with the Partners Epic implementation.
  • Provides technical consultation to others on the team

  • Expertise in the following areas are required for this position:
  • FHIR, SMART on FHIR
  • InterSystems Caché / Ensemble / Healthshare
  • .NET Application Framework
  • Web development, including JavaScript, JQuery or similar frameworks
  • REST and SOAP WebServices Development
  • Service Oriented Architecture
  • Object Oriented Programming
  • Database Technologies
  • Experience in any of the flowing areas is beneficial for this position:
  • Mobile app development, e.g. React, Angular, or similar frameworks
  • Epic Internals
  • Development
  • WebServices
  • Chronicles (Import, Export, etc.)
  • Clarity
  • Bridges
  • Report writing
  • Ensemble Development
  • HL7
  • Any Epic certification or familiarity with any Epic modules
  • Generally holds a Bachelors Degree in MIS, CS or other related field.
  • 10+ Years of IT experience.

Skills/Abilities/Competencies Required

  • Strong written and verbal communication.
  • Strong presentation skills.
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills.
  • Ability to interact and cooperate with developers and analysts
  • Ability to effectively work with people from all organizational levels and build consensus through negotiation and diplomacy.
