Full Job Description

Join us as we work to create a thriving ecosystem that delivers accessible, high-quality, and sustainable healthcare for all.

We are looking for a Software Engineer to join our Platform Development team within our Engineering division. Your job will be to contribute to accurate, unambiguous technical design specifications to the appropriate detail and deliver customer value in the form of high-quality software components and services. But enough about us; let’s talk about you.

You are a software engineer with experience building full stack web applications, preferably in public and private cloud environments. You are an excellent communicator that is capable of quickly picking up the complicated healthcare space.

The Platform team within athena is primarily responsible for developing, enhancing and maintaining all the core foundational components (messaging, database, file storage, faxing etc) that are required by all other development teams within athena to successfully build their products. As a key member of the platform team, you will work on a messaging application that is used athena wide and supports upwards of 49 Billion messages in a year.

Job Responsibilities

  • Design and develop code on an agile team of Engineers, a Scrum Master, a Product Owner, and Experience Designer

  • Collaborate with team members to promote productivity and effectiveness; share business and technical insights with the broader engineering and product organization

  • Develop knowledge and domain expertise. Take ownership of what you and the team builds and coordinate your efforts across the teams to ensure proper completion


Typical Qualifications

  • About 2+ years of software engineering experience

  • Proficient in at least one modern programming language and relational databases

  • We work in Agile environment, so any Agile experience or CI/CD concepts is helpful

  • Knowledge of Unix/Linux, SQL, RESTful API, and various other technologies is helpful
