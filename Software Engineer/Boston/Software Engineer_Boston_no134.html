Medical Device Electrical Engineer - 1

Must be a US Citizen or Green Card

Cambridge, MA 02139 - Must be within commuting distance of Cambridge, MA

  • OVERVIEW:

Healthcare Optics Research Lab is a young organization that is gaining momentum. We are partnering with world class hospitals in the Boston area to turn cutting-edge medical research into usable, meaningful products. This Medical Device Electrical Engineer (Engineer) position is part of a medical device development team responsible for the design, implementation and verification of an innovative medical imaging device. It is a hands-on role inventing practical solutions to develop imaging hardware while meeting various functional and non-functional requirements of novel medical devices.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

    • Support/assist the electrical analysis, design, implementation, and verification of novel medical imaging devices that consists of electrical hardware design and data acquisition
    • Ensure electrical design and documentation complies with medical safety and EMC/EMI standards
    • Create documentation adhering to quality procedures, regulations, standards and industry best practices under Design Control
    • Execute unit and integration test plans
    • Lead board bring-up activities and work with other engineers to insure product functionality
    • Support technical risk retirement
    • Work effectively in cross-functional teams consisting of Research, Engineering, QA/RA, and Marketing
    • Supports third party safety testing
    • Analyzes test data and draws conclusions from findings to determine efficiency/reliability of prototypes
    • Contribute to patent analysis and invention creations
      • Deliverables
      • Define and author board level specifications
      • Generate circuit models and simulations
      • Complete component de-rating analyses
      • Complete electrical design and failure mode effect analyses
      • Create electrical schematics and bill of materials
      • Designs printed circuit boards (PCB) for unit testing
      • Review and/or creates board layout designs
      • Develop PCBA level test cases and procedures to ensure that the PCBA meets electrical specifications

QUALIFICATIONS:

    • Degree in Electrical Engineering (BSEE)
    • 3+ years of hands-on design experience in analog and/or digital design for circuit design and board layout
    • Knowledge of IEC 60601-1, 3.1 edition Safety and Essential performance and IEC60601-1-2 4th edition Electromagnetic disturbances
    • Must have experience integrating, testing, and verifying complex electrical systems
    • Medical Device experience required
    • Must have working experience with analog simulation, schematic capture, and PCBA testing
    • Have the flexibility and curiosity to develop new skillsets, as the need requires
    • Knowledge working with various optical sensors and detectors
    • Knowledge of power supplies and motor drives
    • Experience in intellectual property development

The company will not pursue or support visa sponsorship for this position.

Must be within commuting distance of Cambridge, MA
