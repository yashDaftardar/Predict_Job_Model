About The Opportunity:
We’re all about connecting hungry diners with our network of over 300,000 restaurants nationwide. Innovative technology, user-friendly platforms and streamlined delivery capabilities set us apart and make us an industry leader in the world of online food ordering. When you join our team, you become part of a community that works together to innovate, solve problems, grow, work hard and have a ton of fun in the process!

Why Work For Us
Grubhub is a place where authentically fun culture meets innovation and teamwork. We believe in empowering people and opening doors for new opportunities. If you’re looking for a place that values strong relationships, embraces diverse ideas–all while having fun together–Grubhub is the place for you!

Our Ruby on Rails Developers sit on our Platform engineering team to build and maintain the core set of systems that power the resturant ecosystem, an online ordering interface and network of dozens of mobile payments apps (maybe even the ones you use to order lunch) and point-of-sale integrations. We are a team of smart, highly motivated engineers that place a high value on writing well crafted, thoroughly tested, and maintainable code. We’re growing extremely fast and are taking on the exciting challenges of keeping up with new product development while scaling our services to support higher volume and more clients. As a software engineer for Restaurant Integrations, you will be responsible for building, maintaining, monitoring and scaling the systems responsible for streamlining restaurant onboarding, menu management and direct-to-POS order processing.
The Impact You Will Make:
  • You empower businesses to excite and reward their customers in new ways
  • What interesting things can we do with SKU-level data when a user places an order?
  • Who might want to build on top of our platform and how can we make sure our API supports their needs?
  • When will our system reach its breaking point? What must be done now to push back that date?
  • You’ll be understanding the domain needs and building a highly scalable & reliable platform for the Resturant Intergrations Team
  • You’ll be accountable for delivery, deployment, maintenance, monitoring the performance of the application and the platform
  • You’ll be evaluating the tools, technologies, vendors for the platform along with other senior team members
  • Writing the code with defined standards, reviewing the code of peers, using security standards in the code and ability to measure the performance of the features
  • Creating the backlog of Tech Debts/ Features, suggesting the areas of improvement
  • Actively working with other team members in the domain and being a team player
What You Bring to the Table:
  • 2-5 years professional software development experience with a modern web framework like Django or Rails
  • Rails experience strongly preferred
  • Knowledge of, or experience with developing distributed systems
  • Previous experience with relational database design fundamentals (PostgreSQL)
  • Experience developing & consuming Restful API
  • Familiarity in working with containerized environments
  • Distributed version control expertise with Git
