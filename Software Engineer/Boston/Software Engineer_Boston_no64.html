Join our world-class Edge Technology organization

Our team is part of the User Developer Communication & Control Interfaces group within our Edge Technology organization. We help thousands of the largest enterprises around the world manage their websites and secure their transactions over the internet. Our globally distributed network can solve problems at a scale that few others can.

Help shape the future of the Internet

Our team develops globally managed services that support our internal customers by distributing configuration changes quickly and safely throughout our network. This 'behind-the-scenes' team is in charge of developing, testing and supporting this highly available system. We also serve numerous internal clients and interface with a variety of downstream systems.

As a Software Engineer, you will be responsible for:

  • Creating new features from scratch, enhancing existing features and optimizing existing functionality
  • Designing and maintaining interfaces to the system and support tools
  • Working with our QA teams to develop regression tests that cover new changes to our software
  • Partnering with 3rd level engineering support to troubleshoot complex problems on our network for our customers
  • Improving the testability, deployment, monitorability, and maintainability of our software
  • Analyzing and optimizing system performance

Do what you love

To be successful in this role you will:

  • Have 2 years of relevant experience and a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Management Information Systems, another related field or its equivalent
  • Display knowledge of Object-Oriented programming and test-driven development in Python
  • Have experience building scalable systems in distributed environments
  • Have experience with Linux and source control systems like Git
  • Have experience with databases, SQL, and/or distributed key-value storage solutions
  • Possess good communication skills, be self-driven, positive and an effective team player
