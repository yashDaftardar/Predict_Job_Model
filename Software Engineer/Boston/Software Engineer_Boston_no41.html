Job Description:

The Role

Develop solutions / capabilities through a combination of both analytical and software development skillsets. The activities include capturing the ideas, conceptualizing them through a programming language and crafting a packaged solution that can be used by the investment professionals. This role will:

  • Intelligently apply analytics and financial concepts to support investment needs and develop new solutions.
  • Own the implementation of a project through the entire software development lifecycle utilizing a full-stack implementation.
  • Add scale, rigor, and repeatability through software development standard methodologies.
  • Assist teams by developing high-quality maintainable software solutions in an agile environment that meet functional and non-functional business requirements.

The position can be located in Boston, Merrimack, Raleigh, Denver and New Jersey.

The Expertise and Skills You Bring

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a Computer Science or Related degrees
  • Minimum of 1 year’ experience working with solution development partnering with investment professionals focused in either Equity or Fixed Income asset classes.
  • Domain knowledge in either equities or fixed income asset classes
  • Proven track record in hands-on development of end to end technology solutions
  • Full-stack software development knowledge.
  • Technical and programming skills including Microsoft Excel with VBA, Programming Language (like R, .Net, Python, Java), SQL and BI Tools (Tableau or Spotfire).
  • Ability to effectively connect with multiple partners, including investment professionals, technology partners and senior management

The Team

Embedded Tactical Development team partners with the investment teams in Strategic Advisers on various projects including portfolio construction, risk management, and alpha research. We build high quality, robust, and efficient high-responsive solutions that are used to improve Strategic Advisers efficiency and decision-making processes.
