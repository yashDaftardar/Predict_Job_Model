Purpose
Working at Centiro is different. Inspiring, challenging and rewarding. We are on a clear and rapid path of global expansion and are looking for talents with a passion and drive to make a difference. This is an exciting time to be joining the team and be a part of something special.
What you will do
As a Software Developer your everyday work will entail new and further development of our product environment – an environment which comprises web development, Windows applications, backend services, web services and more.
You will work in a team together with Application Specialists and other developers managing our customers around the globe. Our teams consist of individuals with varied skills who solves the complete assignment, from analysis, design and development to test and commissioning. We work in Microsoft environment and in the team you develop a solution together, which is implemented in code by you as a developer.
Our work is based on a clear architecture which sets the boundaries and safeguards requirements for quality and accessibility.
Who you are
You are a team player who understands the importance of cooperation and helping each other. A collaborative mindset and aptitude are more important than prestige and personal priorities. With all tasks, everyone is equal in helping each other succeed.
You are expected to try, test and learn. You are encouraged to seize opportunities and continuously develop with Centiro’s robust confidence in your ability. You are spurred by continuous improvements and seeing things from a new perspective.
