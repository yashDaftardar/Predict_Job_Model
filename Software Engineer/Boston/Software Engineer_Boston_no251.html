Who We Are:

Vistaprint is the industry leader in personalized physical and digital products. We empower over 17 million global customers to make an impression – and we’re fueled by forward-thinking and innovative technology. We encourage our engineers to think like owners – to continue to act small as we grow. Every team builds their own roadmaps, and chooses the programming languages and technologies that suit them best. This helps us have a big impact at the enterprise level, while still feeling small and nimble.


Just last year, Vistaprint generated $1.508B in revenue through customized print products, signage, apparel, websites and more.


As a member of the Design Discovery team, you’ll be a full stack developer working on the Vistaprint website to allow customers to find their perfect design among the millions of designs, the backend systems that drive that experience, and the configuration tooling that allows Vistaprint to manage and curate those designs. We support 25 localized Vistaprint websites serving various global markets and have thousands of employees in offices worldwide.


What You Will Do:

  • Implement new features in a backend process that imports millions of design assets into a document store for efficient retrieval
  • Add features to Vistaprint’s frontend website that enables customers to browse, search, and select designs
  • Grow your skills every day by working on exciting problems with talented engineers
  • Make a significant impact on a critical aspect of the business
  • Collaborate with a talented engineering team to build a new Vistaprint platform and participate in code reviews

  • Experience in at least one programming language (C#, Java, JavaScript, etc.)
  • Understanding of data structures and algorithms
  • Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science or related field
  • Applicable experience from projects/internships/co-ops as a software developer in a fast-paced, technical environment

Nice to Have:

  • Ability to communicate with diverse audiences (including technical and non-technical stakeholders)
  • Excellent collaboration and the desire to work with a cross-cultural team
  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Experience with Agile development
  • Knowledge of databases (design, querying, management)
  • Experience with front-end frameworks using JavaScript/TypeScript, HTML, CSS and some MVC frameworks preferably React

Remote First-Culture:

In 2020, Vistaprint adopted a Remote-First operating model and culture. We heard from our team members that having the freedom, autonomy and trust in each other to work from home and, the ability to operate when they are most productive, empowers everyone to be their best and most brilliant self. Vistaprint also provides collaboration spaces for team members to work physically together when it's safe to do so or believe in office working will deliver the best results. Currently we are enabled to hire remote team members in over 20 US States as well as several countries in Europe: Spain, Germany, UK, Czech Republic, the Netherlands and Switzerland.


About Us:

As an e-commerce powerhouse, Vistaprint creates customer value (and delight) through accessible, cutting-edge technology. We are the marketing partner to millions of small businesses around the world. For more than 20 years, we have helped small businesses look and feel credible through high-quality marketing products and solutions that include signage, logo apparel, promotional products, face masks, flyers, postcards, business cards, websites and digital marketing. With Vistaprint, small businesses are able to create and customize their marketing with easy-to-use digital tools and design-templates, or by receiving expert graphic design support. In 2020, Vistaprint acquired 99designs to expand its design offering via a worldwide community of more than 150,000 talented designers to make it easy for designers and clients to work together to create designs they love. Vistaprint is focused on making great marketing and design accessible to every small business owner, allowing them to create a cohesive brand image for use in-store, online and on-the-go.
