Teradyne is a global technology company delivering progressive solutions to complex testing challenges. If you are excited at the prospect of joining a global team, that is both exciting and dynamic, then Teradyne may be the place for you!


Teradyne is looking for software engineers to join a hardworking, engaging and collaborative team. As an individual contributor, you will be involved in all aspects of software engineering, working with different functions to define, implement and test new features and functionality. The main focus for this group would be within the core services of our IG-XL software platform, whether it is maintaining and extending existing functionality, redesigning existing components to meet current or future needs, or developing entirely new components to integrate into our software platform. To be successful in this role, you will need to develop great skills in C# and C++, design and debug, as well as software testing. This will also require you to be an excellent communicator both verbally and through documentation or writing.


The changes that the team will develop in IG-XL will be used for our UltraFlex and UltraFlexPlus platforms that our customers used to test silicon devices. This will service all different industries including automotive, RF, discrete and applications processors; just to name a few.


Your responsibilities will include:

  • Understanding customer requirements and finding solutions that fit within our existing software architecture
  • Create robust designs with a focus on scalability, high performance and maintainability
  • Discuss and review deliverables with your fellow team members and provide feedback
  • Work with software verification engineers to devise an effective test strategy
  • Tackle difficult problems and come up with innovative solutions
  • Participate in brainstorming sessions and contribute ideas to improve our product, our process and our team
  • Continuously learn new things and become a better software engineer


Required Qualifications:

  • Solid grasp of Object-Oriented Design and software data structures
  • Understanding of core computer architecture concepts like multithreading, managing memory leaks, and analyzing performance of the software.
  • Proficiency in OOD and some OO language (C++, C# preferred)
  • You can clearly express your ideas and concepts both written and verbally


Desired Qualifications:

  • Knowledge of UML
  • Experience with scripting languages (Python, Javascript, Perl as examples)
  • Existing experience at Teradyne would be preferred but not required


Basic Qualifications, Experience, Skills & Education Required:

  • BSCS, BSCE, BSEE or equivalent
  • 0-2 years experience with general software development experience with commercial applications desired
