Software QA Engineer

DSP Concepts
Boston, MA

DSP Concepts is a leading provider of audio signal processing technology for a broad range of product areas. Our technologies enable key new product features in products like voice enabled speakers and high fidelity car audio systems. Through our Audio Weaver suite of software products and our extensive library of audio signal processing algorithms, we enable our customers to quickly develop, integrate and deploy advanced audio features into new products.

DSP Concepts fosters a creative, challenging, and supportive work environment with a focus on professional growth. Teamwork, technical acumen, customer interaction and strong communication are part of the culture here.

We are seeking a Mid-level Software QA Engineer to help us write the next chapter of our success story.

Contribution

The engineer will be a member of the QA & Validation team and report directly to the QA Manager, interacting with software development and project management teams to test and validate the DSP Concepts’ software solutions on various customers’ hardware platforms based on the industry’s test criteria and support DSP Concepts’ development on the AudioWeaver platform.

The primary responsibilities for this position are:

  • Executing QA/Testing processes to ensure product and service excellence
  • Creating test cases for new features to prevent future software regressions
  • Participating in design reviews, assisting product team in defining requirements, and contributing to lowering design risk from potential problems
  • Building automated solutions to validate software performance on a regular cycle
  • Work in a multi-disciplinary environment to build scalable, efficient software solutions to varied audio customers
  • Documenting test plans, procedures, and results.

Beyond these responsibilities you will have the latitude to grow professionally and apply yourself in many domains. As the team and business grows, so will the responsibilities of this candidate.

This position is based in our Dedham, MA office. DSP Concepts offers a full suite of benefits including 401K, dental and medical benefits.

Qualifications

The ideal candidate would have three to five years of experience with the following qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in CS, EE, Audio Engineering, or other related engineering field with 3+ years experience
  • Experience writing, refining, and executing both manual and automated test cases against software products
  • Proficiency in MATLAB, C/C++, and Python
  • Experience using embedded DSP firmware
  • Experience testing GUI software products, both manually and with automation
  • Strong background or interest in Audio Software or Acoustics
  • Exceptional verbal and written communication skills
  • Ability to work well in a team as well as individual
