Description

Staples is focused on our customer and our community. As a Software Engineer Intern for Staples, you will be part of a fast-paced, cross-functional team responsible for delivering world class software solutions that will be utilized across the retail setting. You will be challenged with identifying innovative ideas and proof of concept to deliver against the existing and future needs of our growing business.

The Staples Summer Internship Program is full time position for 8 - 10 weeks. As a member of our Software Engineering team, all interns will have regularly assigned work, will participate in team meetings and events, have an opportunity to network with other professionals in the field, and serve as a fully functioning member of our dynamic team. There will be ample opportunity to work collaboratively with other software engineering professionals on assignments that address current business issues and challenges them to build on their knowledge base and skill set.

Responsibilities:

Please note that when applying to this Intern position, you may be considered for a number of Technology roles within the Software Engineering team, such as a front-end or back-end developer, a quality engineer, a mobile developer, a release engineer, or an integration developer.

Your responsibilities as an Intern will vary based on the specific role and organization, and may involve;

  • Gathering business requirements and collaborating with business partners.
  • Researching, designing, testing, deploying and maintaining software solutions.
  • Interacting with engineers and other cross-functional teams such as Product Management, Release Engineering, Quality Engineering, Data Science, Data Security or Operations, to develop innovative solutions that meet Retail needs with respect to functionality, performance, scalability, reliability, realistic implementation schedules, and adherence to development goals and principles.
  • Identifying and fixing stability/scalability issues in a very time critical environment.
  • Participating in implementations, helping to resolve issues, and developing industry and product technical expertise.
