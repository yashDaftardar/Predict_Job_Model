What you will do

The Software Engineer is a web development role that reports to the Engineering Manager in Westford. This role is part of a team of software web developers that are responsible for development and maintenance of access control and video web applications. Those responsibilities will include but not be limited to web application development, interaction with representatives of other cross functional teams to understand requirements and strategies for the project, writing user stories, resolving QA issues, and attending SCRUM meetings.

How you will do it

  • Member of agile team (SAFe Agile)
  • Attend daily stand-ups
  • Break complex features into user stories and tasks and provide estimates
  • Develop new features based on user stories

Qualifications


What we look for

Required:

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, Software Engineering or Electronic Engineering (or equivalent relevant degree); or alternative qualification with equivalent industrial experience
  • 2 years of experience with Agular, JavaScript, or Typescript development
  • 2 years of experience with Node.js
  • Familiarity with Material Design, Bootstrap framework, or similar responsive framework
  • Excellent diagnostic and troubleshooting skills, problem solving, and an ability to learn quickly
