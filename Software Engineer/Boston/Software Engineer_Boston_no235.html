About the Job

We’re thrilled to announce that we’re hiring to grow our team and our products! 3Play Media offers a truly unique opportunity to work in a small team of developers in a company that offers both a startup-minded size and culture while being robustly backed by a steady stream of customers and projects. Every day, our system processes hundreds of hours of video content, helping us to improve and expand the way people consume online media to make more of the web’s video content accessible. We’re also very excited to have a few brand-new products in our pipeline!

We are looking for an experienced engineer to help instill DevOps best practices in our organization. The successful candidate will work closely with our development team and hosting provider to automate, scale and secure our infrastructure, while also implementing continuous integration and deployment tooling and techniques. This would be the second dedicated DevOps role in the organization, and as such represents a significant opportunity to positively impact our business.

Duties and Responsibilities

  • DevOps Engineer reports to the Director of Engineering and will partner with the development team to build out the tools and infrastructure to get their ideas into product faster and safer
  • Current tech stack is: Ruby on Rails, MySQL, Mongo, AWS, Chef, CircleCI, GitHub, and Trello
  • Propose and implement new tools and infrastructure to the 3Play technology stack, which will include building out some of the following:
    • CI/CD pipeline
    • Automated test infrastructure
    • Improvements to our AWS infrastructure
    • Monitoring and alerting
    • Upgrades to all pieces of the tech stack, including Rails
    • Automated linting and open source policy enforcement
    • Containerization
  • Alongside the development team, you’ll play a key role in triage efforts.
  • Work closely with the developers and train them on new tools and processes (making their lives easier in the process)
  • Set the standard for “3Play DevOps Engineer” and influence the direction of the DevOps team as it grows in the coming years
  • Potentially take on the role of “security officer”, but definitely consider security in all that you do.

Position Requirements

  • At least 2 years of experience in a DevOps role
  • Adequate experience to see the same things go wrong in multiple different ways across a wide variety of domains
  • Demonstrated experience making solid technology decisions
  • Strong understanding of configuration management (Chef, Ansible, Puppet)
  • Experience with CI/CD pipelines (Circle, Travis, Jenkins)
  • Fluency in shell scripting and at least one other high-level scripting language (Perl, Python, Ruby, Node.js, etc)
  • Knowledgeable about security
  • Deep experience managing AWS services (EC2, S3, IAM, etc)
  • Unix system administration experience

About the Company

3Play Media is a rapidly growing, profitable technology company with a startup culture making a huge impact on the way people consume online media. We provide premium captioning, transcription, audio description, and translation solutions to over 5,000 higher ed, enterprise, media, and government customers. Our goal is to simplify the workflow through flexible APIs, a secure online account system, and integrations with a multitude of players, platforms, and lecture capture systems. We got our start in 2008 out of MIT Sloan and we are based in Boston, MA.

Work Environment

We have an awesome work environment! Our team is hard working, laid back, and fun. There are no politics or wasteful meetings and projects get done quickly and efficiently. The team is dedicated to building a successful company. We have a spacious office with lots of natural light located in the North End. The work we do is fun, interesting, and gratifying. Your work will have a direct impact on our company’s success.

Location

3Play Media is located in Boston, MA – North Washington St on the Green & Orange line and near North Station. We are in the heart of Boston’s thriving North End District. We are located near some of the best restaurants and surrounded by Boston’s buzzing tech scene.
