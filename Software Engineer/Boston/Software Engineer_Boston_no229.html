Referrals

Earn Bonuses for Consultant and Client Referrals

Masterminds is a great place to work, and we want you to have every opportunity to share our success. Earn a share when you refer a friend or associate who becomes a Masterminds Consultant or provides a business lead that results in staffing assignments.

Client Referral Program*

Masterminds pays bonuses for information on new projects and clients. If your information results in the placement of a consultant within 90 days of your call, you will earn up to $1.00 per-hour-worked as long as they are on the project (up to 1 year). At an average of 2080 working hours in a year, the bonus can really climb!

Consultant Referral Program*

We're always looking for new, qualified computer professionals to join our team. You can also earn referral bonus by helping us with our search.

We will continue to pay a referral reward of $1000 to employees who refer eligible candidates for Programmer Analyst/Software Engineer exempt open positions and are subsequently hired into the position within 6 months of the referral.

Once Masterminds verifies that your referral's resume is not already in our database, you become eligible for the referral reward. If Masterminds hires your referral for any project (more than 60 days in duration), you receive a bonus after the newly hired consultant has worked 320 hours. Bonuses vary based on skills in demand and market conditions.

  • You do not need to be a Masterminds employee to participate. Referral program bonus payments vary depending skills in demand and type of referral.
