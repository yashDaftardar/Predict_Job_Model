Full Job Description

As an Associate Site Reliability Engineer at Veson Nautical, you will be responsible for building, monitoring, and supporting the infrastructure that underpins our rapidly growing SaaS platform. We are looking for a candidate that that is ready to learn complex systems at scale.

The Veson Nautical cloud is hosted on AWS, and we use many industry standard technologies including Terraform, Kubernetes, Splunk, Octopus Deploy, and Slack. We run a global deployment across three AWS regions, with a geographically dispersed user base.

We are excited about incorporating containers and additional PaaS offerings into the Veson cloud. The successful candidate will help us improve the reliability, security, and cost effectiveness of our infrastructure.

You'll work with software engineers and our support team to improve our processes and tools. Finally, you’ll automate, automate, automate to maximize the efficiency of our team!

Veson Nautical’s clients are the world’s leading shipping organizations. Our SaaS platform is transforming vessel communication and operations for this truly global industry; you will take on the challenge of delivering performance and reliability to our users from London to Singapore. Your contributions will implement our architectural vision and improve our ability to rapidly expand the reach and impact of our solutions.

Experience and Skills

  • Basic knowledge and interest in Amazon Web Services, Azure, or Google Cloud Platform
  • Programming skills in any of PowerShell, Bash, Ruby, Python, Go, C#, Node.js or Java is required
  • 1-2 years of experience in site reliability engineering or systems administration is required (Windows, Linux or both)
  • Experience with infrastructure as code (Terraform/CloudFormation), desired state configuration (Chef / Puppet / Ansible / SaltStack) or similar tools is highly desirable
  • Previous experience working on a large-scale SaaS platform is a plus

Work Location

This role is targeted for our headquarters in Boston, MA
