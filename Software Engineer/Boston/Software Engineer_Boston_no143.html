Full Job Description

Working at MIT offers opportunities, an environment, a culture – and benefits – that just aren’t found together anywhere else. If you’re curious, motivated, want to be part of a unique community, and help shape the future – then take a look at this opportunity.


RESEARCH SOFTWARE ENGINEER, CENTER FOR CONSTRUCTIVE COMMINUNICATION (CCC) , Media Lab , to undertake software development, data-management, and data-analysis projects in support of CCC's mission. Will collaborate with research staff to set direction and scope projects, independently design and develop methods and build core software systems, serve as lead for multi-member projects, and mentor researchers and junior engineers.

The MIT Center for Constructive Communication (CCC), based at the MIT Media Lab, seeks to design human-machine systems that improve communication across divides and increase opportunity for underheard communities.

A full description is available at https://www.media.mit.edu/about/job-opportunities/.

Job Requirements
REQUIRED : at least five years’ experience delivering production software systems in an industry setting, including experience with Python, JavaScript, and SQL; demonstrated ability to work with a project plan and to execute with minimal oversight; proficiency with and fundamental knowledge of software engineering, including programming languages, basic data structures, databases, and operating systems; excellent communication and time management skills; and the ability to work in a fast-paced, uncertain environment, pivoting as research and social needs develop. PREFERRED : bachelor’s or master’s degree; React+Redux experience; experience with cloud computing platforms; experience working in a small start-up environment; and familiarity with common machine-learning methods and/or data visualization techniques.
