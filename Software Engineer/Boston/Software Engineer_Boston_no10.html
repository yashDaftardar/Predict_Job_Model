Intern – Software Developer – Cambridge, MA – Summer 2021

Are you interested in an Internship opportunity with Philips? We welcome individuals who are currently pursuing (or have recently completed) an undergraduate (BS) degree to participate in 3-6-month paid opportunities at our sites across the US. Through these roles, you will gain meaningful, hands-on experience working for a Health-Tech company.

Full-time hours, starting in June/July 2021, can work remotely but required to come to office for regular meetings.


In this role, you have the opportunity to

Work in an exciting advanced development project to explore, design, build and validate innovative HW/SW prototype for patient monitoring in the connected world.

The intern will have rich opportunities to learn the HW/SW system architecture of medical devices, as well as understanding the network/data infrastructure to enable patient monitoring in hospital and out-of-hospital. The primary assignment will be on embedded SW development for ARM Cortex-M based devices with various connectivity options. The intern will also have opportunity to work on a mobile app and cloud-based backend app development.

You are responsible for

  • Learning the HW/SW system architecture of medical devices

  • Developing embedded application SW for ARM Cortex-M based devices

  • Developing connectivity SW for the prototype built by the project team

  • Developing mobile app to test the prototype (optional)

  • Developing cloud app to test the prototype (optional)

To succeed in this role, you should have the following skills and experience

  • BS or MS student in Computer Science and/or related fields

  • Excellent C/C++ programming skill

  • Experience with ARM Cortex-M development is a plus

  • Experience with Bluetooth LE development is a plus

  • Experience with mobile app development (Anrdoid/iOS) is a plus

In return, we offer you

The ability to collaborate with, learn and grow from colleagues in a highly complex, global organization where you can use your strengths to help drive strategic business initiatives for Philips. Additionally, we provide you a dynamic working environment in an innovative business, paired with a competitive salary, excellent benefits, and a supportive atmosphere where you can sharpen your talents with new challenges and career opportunities.

  • US work authorization is a precondition of employment. The company will not consider candidates who require sponsorship for a work-authorized visa.

  • Company relocation benefits will not be provided for this position. Candidates need to live within the territory or within commuting distance to Cambridge, MA.
