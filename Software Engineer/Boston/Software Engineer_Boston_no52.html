We are looking for a Software QA Engineer to join CooperSurgical and help build Embryo Options, the first business of its kind to help guide patients through the process of storing cryopreserved embryos, eggs, and sperm. Embryo Options partners with IVF centers to educate and assist fertility patients with making disposition decisions for their cryopreserved tissue. The IVF center and Embryo Options partnership enables patients to conveniently pay storage fees online, as well as gain access to comprehensive educational content and the world's largest embryo donation platform.

As more and more patients are freezing embryos, eggs, and sperm, our goal is to provide tools to fertility clinics that help their patients to navigate the complicated financial, legal, and ethical issues associated with cryopreservation.

As a Software QA Engineer on the Embryo Options team, you'll oversee the planning, design, development, and execution of manual and automated test suites for our applications. You’ll work closely with members of our product and engineering teams to help ensure the delivery of high-quality, valuable features to patients and clinics.

Essential Functions:

  • Work closely with product and engineering teams to develop and execute test plans for new and existing application features
  • Apply a deep understanding of business requirements and the needs of our clinics and patients when testing our applications
  • Clearly document and communicate any issues discovered during testing
  • Write and maintain clear and concise test suites
  • Assist our Center Support team with triaging and resolving issues reported by our users
  • Take ownership with our engineering teams for the rapid delivery of high-quality software
  • Help our team develop QA best practices and expand our use of automation

Qualifications:

  • 2+ years of QA in an agile development environment (healthcare experience is a plus)
  • Bachelor’s degree in a technical field preferred
  • Experience creating and maintaining manual or automated software test suites
  • High attention to detail and strong technical problem solving skills
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Desire to understand and advocate for the needs of our clinics and patients
  • Familiarity with foundational web technologies
  • Ability to grow your knowledge of automated testing and CI pipelines
