About the team:
Reporting to our Software Engineering Manager, you will join one of our teams that focuses on simplifying how our customers manage their use of the Quick Base platform. You will work with other developers, Product Management and UX Designers to create solutions to business problems that delight our customers, and adhere to our engineering standards. The team works in diverse areas and technologies, we are constantly learning and honing our tools.
What We're Looking For in Your Profile :
  • You have some experience in one or more of the popular object oriented languages, such as C# (preferred), Java, C++
  • You are proficient in SQL and CI/CD technologies
  • Familiarity with cloud technologies such as AWS or GCP is a plus
  • You are a lifelong learner and embrace learning new technologies and tools
  • You are customer obsessed and derive job satisfaction from delighting people with products you build
