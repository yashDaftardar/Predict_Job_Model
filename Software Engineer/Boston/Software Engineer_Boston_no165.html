The Role

Rheos Medicines is developing a platform for drug discovery and development in the novel area of immunometabolism. We are looking for a highly motivated, collaborative, and innovative member for the Informatics team, who will work at the leading intersection of informatics and bioinformatics. This individual will be the interface between informatics and computational biology team, as well as working closely with biologists, immunologists, and chemists to develop the data management and visualization platform to enable the data science and machine learning capabilities at Rheos.

Responsibilities

  • Build and maintain scientific tools and infrastructures for drug discovery research through researching, developing, and integrating new technologies and computational approaches to enhance and accelerate scientific research
  • Data preparation, analysis and visualization utilizing programming and scripting using R, Python and SQL
  • Develop and deploy informatics pipelines for processing and managing scientific data
  • Partnering with other departments and project teams to develop informatics solutions to satisfy business needs
  • Lead requirement gathering and documenting requirements
  • Build, maintain and optimize in-house databases/warehouse for data analysis and mining
  • Managing the electronic laboratory notebook system and other 3rd party informatics software including developing integrations and templates as well as monitoring compliance across the organization
  • Strive to continuously improve Rheos informatics platform by identifying unmet needs and implementing creative solutions with buy-in from users
  • Ensure appropriate training and assistance is provided to the users of Informatics software and tools
  • Develop and promote best practices for scientific data capture, integration, and analysis
