Software Integration Engineer (remote)

iComponent Inc.
Lowell, MA
Remote

Summary of primary duties and responsibilities

  • Participate in defining customer requirements and project scoping.
  • Assist with the project budget and schedule creation.
  • Assist with generation of the technical design and integration architecture.
  • Design, develop, test, deploy, and maintain custom CPQ web solutions using the KBMax software tools.
  • May design 2D and 3D graphics
  • Ensure that all designs are well-documented and maintainable.
  • Report status including risks, test results, and performance against budget and project schedule.
  • Identify technical and schedule issues and potential process improvements.
  • Participate in customer status meetings and demonstrate progress and implementation via video conference or telephone.
  • Provide occasional technical support to end-users via telephone, e-mail, and video conferencing.
