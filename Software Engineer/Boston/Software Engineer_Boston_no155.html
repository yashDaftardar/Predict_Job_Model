Job Title

Software Project Engineer

Job Description

If you are a Colorado resident and this role is a field-based or remote role, you may be eligible to receive additional information about the compensation and benefits for this role, which we will provide upon request. You may contact 888-367-7223 , option 5, for assistance.

In this role, you have the opportunity to

Lead the software effort for new development projects that will help to save lives, improve patient outcomes, and reduce the cost of medicine across the global. You will play a key role in bringing innovative ultrasound software products and solutions to market.

You are responsible for

  • Working with software development engineers, managers, scrum masters, and the program team to lead the planning, definition, specification, design, implementation, integration, and test of software solutions for new development projects.
  • Ensuring project execution is in line with project guidelines and directives. Determines project risks, defines corrective actions, and drives projects to closure. Determines and allocates project resources in accordance with guidelines.
  • Working with project stakeholders to communicate and review plans, status, project risks, metrics, and resource requests.
  • Working with other project leaders, scrum masters, and product owners to manage cross-discipline and cross-project dependencies.
  • Working with team members in Product/Clinical Marketing, Customer Services, and Supply Chain to ensure that implementations will meet customer and business needs.
  • Leading software development efforts distributed across Philips sites in North America and India.
  • Keeping abreast of developments in project management tools and processes, both internal and external. Ensures compliance to corporate and regulatory agency policies/rules/procedures.

You are part of

The Ultrasound Software Engineering team, where you will get to work with the best engineers in medical ultrasound and lead the development of ultrasound solutions that are helping to save lives every day. We have product development activities taking place across multiple locations in the United States and around the world. This position is located in Cambridge, MA.

What We Are Looking For

  • BS or MS in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or equivalent.
  • At least 3 years of software development experience.
  • At least 2 years of experience leading software development efforts.
  • Experience with working in an Agile development environment.
  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills with the ability to interface in a cross-disciplinary manner, including with engineers, project leaders, and functional managers.
  • Self-motivated individual with good organizational and time management skills.
  • Strong potential and aspiration to develop and grow in the organization.
  • Experience with the Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe) a plus.
  • Familiarity with medical devices and/or working in a regulated environment a plus.
  • Experience with working in a cross-site environment a plus.
