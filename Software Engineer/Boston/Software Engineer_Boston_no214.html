Full Job Description

Join a high-performing, tight-knit team at a fast-growing company that is using the Internet of Things (IoT) to transform how organizations sense, monitor, and make decisions. Founded out of MIT in 2005, SmartSense is trusted by more than 2,000 organizations, including Walmart, SpaceX, Apple, CVS Health, Coca-Cola, and the US State Department to help them make sensor-driven decisions. We have a solution that our customers rely on every day to make mission critical decisions; we are looking for team-oriented change agents to help shape the future of IoT.
Members of the Data Services team are passionate about engineering data pipelines, data ingestion, storage, notification, reports, API's, and analytics. We are inspired by software engineering and in building tools, infrastructure, frameworks and services that enable customer insights through new innovative data products. As good stewards of our data we contribute to all aspects related to the handling of data from our field sensors. You will help us secure access to API's, authenticate and authorize use of embedded reports in our platform, distributing reports directly to customers, assist in report integration with our platform, or help to optimize internal processes native to our unique supply chain.
In this Software Engineer role, you will be involved in the distribution of sensor data, instrumenting effective monitoring and reporting of the environments being sensed and democratizing the data from the sensors and networks we have deployed. The challenges are diverse as are the potential solutions, and you will have freedom to explore approaches as a contributing team member delivering effective analytic products. You will have the opportunity to perform across a range of Software Engineering responsibilities and be constantly challenged to extend your skills and the solutions the Data Services team is able to provide.
Core technologies:
.NET, Python, SQL, JavaScript, Java, Git, ODBC, HTTP, AWS
Core qualifications:
BA/BS degree in a technical/quantitative field, 2+ years of experience working on web services, data structures, performance, and multi-threaded programming techniques. Candidates must have an enthusiasm and passion for software engineering and bring an eagerness to learn. Strong candidates will have proven coding skills, familiarity with data access, knowledge of enterprise integration patterns and or micro-services, able to work independently and as part of a larger team, and a proven ability to solve problems and learn quickly. Candidates must have experience in at least one Software Engineering area (RESTFul Web Services, Relational and Non-Relational databases, Big Data infrastructure, AWS Cloud Infrastructure (Azure acceptable substitute for AWS experience)
Preferred Qualifications:
Candidates with experience building software solutions using AWS. Web Services built on .Net. Experience with .Net Core. Candidates with experience in handling time series telemetry data. Candidates with experience handling data streams and message patterns. Candidates with experience working with Kubernetes or other container-orchestration system. Candidate with experience in distributed systems including utilization and development of RESTful web service API's and micro services patterns.

Immigration or work visa sponsorship will not be provided.

WITHIN ONE MONTH, YOU'LL:

    • Learn about the various sensors and environments critical to our customers’ success
    • Learn the scripts and tools that are currently in use to transform raw data into internal and customer reports
    • Take ownership over the scheduled generation of reports to existing customers
    • Build relationship with the awesome team members across other functional groups

WITHIN THREE MONTHS, YOU'LL:

    • Analyze requests for new and extended applications of reporting functions
    • Take initiative in improving the efficiency of our data services and automation
    • Develop systems and processes to implement and automate quality on data services deliverables
    • Enhance the pipeline after working closely with the product team and stakeholders to understand how our products are used
    • Contribute to on-boarding processes and make recommendations to make on-boarding process better
    • Actively participate in code and design reviews with other members of the team.

WITHIN SIX MONTHS, YOU'LL:

    • Take ownership over defining, implementing, and delivering reports based on our sensor data
    • Become a contributor to the definition of the teams future data services
    • Be able to own the entire planning, scoping, design, and implementation of new features
    • Identify opportunities to improve our infrastructure, operational performance, and data pipeline deliverable
    • Perform end-to-end delivery of data services, data warehouses, and other projects, aided by your team lead and your team members as part of our Software Development Lifecycle.
    • You will be responsible for the design of individual components of the services and will collaborate on a daily basis with senior technical staff in- and outside of the team.

WITHIN TWELVE MONTHS, YOU'LL:

    • Contribute substantially to Data Services team efficiency
    • Demonstrate mastery of existing capabilities through stakeholder interactions
    • Evaluate new technologies and build proof-of-concept systems to enhance Data Services abilities and products
    • Consistently deliver products that exceed expectations
    • Contribute to the definition of new roles, mentoring, and training new hires into the Data Services team
    • You will participate in the evaluation new technologies and implement POCs for future services.

Our team is motivated to improve the world by providing real-time information that ensures medication and food safety. We endeavor to be both the best in the world at what we do, as well as, to be the best for the world because of how we do it; if you’re the right candidate, we will reach that goal together – and have a great time doing it!
