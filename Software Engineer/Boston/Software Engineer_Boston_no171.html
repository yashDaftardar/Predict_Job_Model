Welcome to Allscripts! Our Mission is to be the most trusted provider of innovative solutions that empower all stakeholders across the healthcare continuum to deliver world-class outcomes. Our Vision is a Connected Community of Health that spans continents and borders. With the largest community of clients in healthcare, Allscripts is able to deliver an integrated platform of clinical, financial, connectivity and information solutions to facilitate enhanced collaboration and exchange of critical patient information.

We are looking for talented Software Engineer to help develop, deliver and scale our market-leading software solutions. Our software engineers are critical for our growth as our B2B offerings continue to rapidly expand in scope and scale. Our Engineering culture is built around iterative development, constant deployment and continuous improvement. In this role, you'll contribute designs and code through every level of the stack: from the UI through API to the database. Whether you have great programming skills or you want to take your programming skills to the next level please read on. Ideal candidates are quick learners, self-starters, and take ownership of their work.

Job Responsibilities:

In this role you will

  • Contribute to design and development of new product features, enhancements, and bug fixing
  • Work as part of a team to plan, develop, and implement high quality software solutions within the required timeframes using Agile Scrum iteration methodology.
  • Develop at all levels of the stack, from database to UI and everything in-between
  • Perform comprehensive unit and integration testing of all software produced and contribute to overall quality processes
  • Work to integrate new technologies and systems into existing infrastructure
  • Collaborate with Product Management and Business Owners/Analysts to design and develop new features in our products
  • Provide documentation for all software components and applications.
  • Practice and follow various Engineering Processes we have implemented
  • Be an owner of our process and team culture, continually iterating on how we build product, not just what we build
  • Participate in 24/7 rotating on call rotation.

Qualifications:

  • 2 - 5 years' experience in software product development with direct experience building large scale, high-performance distributed .Net systems
  • Experience designing and developing high-volume WCF services
  • Experience developing software applications in C#
  • Development experience with WCF, WinForms/WPF/Web Forms, IIS
  • Development experience/awareness with REST API, Angular
  • Experience working in development teams utilizing Agile methodologies
  • Ability to provide code reviews
  • Experience with database development and design using a major DBMS tools â€“ SQL Server preferred
  • Solid understanding of OOP, coding and multi-threaded implementation techniques in .Net.
  • Entrepreneurial spirit, can do attitude, and strong drive to innovate
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or any related field
  • Exposure to TFS (Team Foundation Server), Salesforce.com would have added advantage
