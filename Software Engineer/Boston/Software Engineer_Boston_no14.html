Full Job Description

If you’re eager to expand your experience, advance more quickly in your career, and are hungry for your share of challenging work, this is the job for you! You’ll be the first member of Beeyonder’s technology team (reporting directly to the CTO) and will enjoy a dynamic set of responsibilities across Web Development, Product, and Innovation.

Beeyonder is a marketplace platform for virtual travel experiences, catering to those who have a disability or condition that prevents or inhibits travel in-person, as well as others who want to give their loved ones international experiences without the financial burden.

We’re growing quickly and have exciting concepts to build, so we’re looking for someone who can work methodically to deliver features and fixes while collaborating on Product and Innovation efforts. We expect that you have at least a few years of experience both writing code in various languages and collaborating with technical and non-technical colleagues, and while we’re flexible with regard to working fully remotely, it is preferable that you live within reasonable travel distance to the Boston area so you don’t miss out on company offsites and outings; company culture is very important to us and we want you to be a part of it!

Our tech stack will be evolving significantly over the next few months (you’ll have input on the direction that we go in), but this is where we stand today:

  • Nuxt.js (https://nuxtjs.org/)
  • CosmicJS (https://www.cosmicjs.com/)
  • Firebase (https://firebase.google.com/)
  • Heroku (https://www.heroku.com/)
  • Various REST API integrations

We use Atlassian products to manage our Agile sprints, repositories, and CI/CD.

Requirements:

  • 2+ years’ Front-end experience (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • 1+ years’ experience with SPAs (Vue, React, Angular, etc.)
  • Experience with a back-end language (Node/C/Java/PHP) a plus
  • Experience with cloud or on-premise infrastructure a plus

Interviews: Will take place on a rolling basis, and an offer may be made at any time. A list of three references will be required if invited for an interview. Background checks will be completed prior to employment.

Start date: June 2021
