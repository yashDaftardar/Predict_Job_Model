Vizgen is a rapidly growing company developing the next generation of spatially resolved genomic profiling tools that enable researchers to gain new insight into the biological systems that underlie human health and disease. The company's groundbreaking MERFISH spatial transcriptomics technology images RNA molecules with high accuracy and unrivaled detection efficiency at subcellular resolution. MERFISH provides transformative insight into a wide range of tissue-scale basic research and translational medicine in oncology, immunology, neuroscience, infectious disease, developmental biology, and regenerative medicine. For more information, go to www.vizgen.com

Summary:
We are seeking a Senior Software Engineer with interest in systems biology to build tools for spatially resolved genomic profiling. As a Senior Software Engineer at Vizgen, you will work developing and optimizing software to enable the detection of biomolecules within their native tissue contexts, including software to control Vizgen's automated imaging instrumentation. This is an exciting opportunity for a motivated engineer to apply their software engineering expertise to help address key biological questions within a rapidly growing company.

Key Responsibilities :

  • Rapidly build, prototype and test code with an appropriately high level of engineering and best coding practices
  • Build and optimize robust software for controlling Vizgen instrumentation
  • Establish tests and standards to ensure each software release for the instrument control software is sufficiently robust for production
  • Enable Vizgen instrument control software to elegantly recover from minor hardware errors and report informative diagnostics for severe hardware errors
  • Design and expose well tested APIs to other components in the software stack for efficient integration
  • Coordinate with hardware and wet lab teams to ensure software is efficiently addressing the scientific needs


Requirements and Qualifications:

  • BS/MS in computer science, electrical engineering, mathematics, computational biology or related computational field
  • 6+ years of experience building robust and responsive software
  • Experience working and deploying tools in local compute environments
  • Experience writing software for scientific instrumentation, including motor
  • Experience writing firmware is a plus
  • Fluent in python and other commonly used programming languages
  • Excellent communication skills with strong work ethic and motivation


We offer a competitive salary, meaningful stock ownership, 401K, health benefits, a great work environment and the opportunity to enter a high-growth startup company. We are recruiting candidates who have demonstrated leadership and excellence throughout their careers and are excited by the prospect of tackling challenging technical problems.
