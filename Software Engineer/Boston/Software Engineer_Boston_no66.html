MassChallenge Inc. is a global startup accelerator with existing programs in Boston, Rhode Island, Houston, Austin, Lausanne, Jerusalem, and Mexico City. We are the most startup-friendly accelerator on the planet. No equity and not-for-profit, we provide startups with the access and resources they need to succeed - from free office space to mentoring, workshops and global network access. If you want a challenging and rewarding career in the center of the startup ecosystem, this opportunity is for you!

As the Software Engineer you will report to the Senior Director of Software Engineering and will focus on the MassChallenge Accelerate Platform, a Django-based web application that facilitates the coordination of the thousands of organizations and people associated with MassChallenge. The software engineering team is a globally based team that will work closely to support all locations.

Our Commitment to Inclusion, Diversity, Equity, and Accessibility (I.D.E.A.) : We are looking for individuals from underrepresented races, sexual orientations, genders, identities, abilities, and experiences to join our team, enhance our culture, and increase our diversity of thought. We recognize that skills are gained in many ways, whether it be through professional, academic, or lived experience. We also know that it’s not always easy to apply for a job if you’re not sure you check every box in the job description. There is no such thing as a ‘perfect’ candidate, so no matter your background and how you identify, we encourage you to apply for this job if it would make you excited to come into work every day.

A note on location: this position will be a remote position managed out of our Boston, MA office. You can be located anywhere in the U.S. while working east coast hours.

What You’ll Do
Development

  • Collaborate with product management, data and analytics, and company staff in the development of the MassChallenge Accelerate Platform.
  • Contribute to new platform architecture with both planning activity and a significant amount of hands on development.
  • Work with various web-based software development tools and infrastructure services (GitHub, JIRA, Amazon Web Services, Docker).
  • Understand web protocols, standards, and technologies (HTML, CSS, JS).
  • Use and promote standard python coding conventions (PEP8, pylint).

Agile Team Participation

  • Participate in code reviews, create automated and functional testing, and participate in other aspects of our quality assurance process.
  • Participate and be able to lead a scrum-based, agile development team.
  • Assist in pair programming, collaborative development, and coaching other developers.
  • Maintain up-to-date knowledge of technology standards, industry trends, emerging technologies, and software development best practices.
  • Take on the responsibility to lead new projects that might arise.

Who You Are and What You Bring

  • You have a bachelor's degree in the field of computer science or equivalent software development experience (3+ year's experience in a web-based technology role).
  • You have scrum certification (not required but a plus).
  • You have 3 or more years of commercial software development experience including 1-2 years of python experience.
  • You have experience with full-stack web application development in Django.
  • You have experience with using Javascript frameworks like REACT.
  • You have experience with data storage systems. SQL at minimum.
  • You have knowledge/familiarity with agile software methodology.
  • You have an understanding of automated testing.
  • You’re able to excel in a team-oriented collaborative environment.
  • You have a keen attention to detail.
  • You are exceptionally self-motivated and able to work independently on projects.
  • You have the ability to set and manage priorities judiciously.

Who We Are
MassChallenge is the global network for innovators who are working to solve massive challenges. We connect startups, experts, corporations, and communities to grow and transform businesses and economies. At MassChallenge we:

  • Help entrepreneurs build the next generation of transformational businesses by connecting them with the resources, partnerships, and communities that help them launch, grow, and scale their startups.
  • Help our partners build, grow, and evolve their corporate innovation practices.
  • Leverage the power of entrepreneurship to strengthen global innovation ecosystems.

Why You Should Join Us
We believe working at MassChallenge is fulfilling because of the mission, challenging because of the pace and work, and rewarding because you get to build to the next step in your career here. When you join MassChallenge you join an incredible team and network of innovators and entrepreneurs, you take ownership over significant and strategically important projects, you focus on impact and output, and you help strengthen the global innovation ecosystem.
