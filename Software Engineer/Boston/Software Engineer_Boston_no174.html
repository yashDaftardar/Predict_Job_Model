The Software Engineer/Programmer is responsible for creating and maintaining online databases and programs to conduct HIMC's servics and/or creating and maintaining the user interface for the HIMC website.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Work with program leads and business analysts to understand and refine the requirements for selling and marketing services online
  • Transform business rules into use cases and logical scenarios for controlling event-driven interactions between site visitors and content
  • Configure promotions, user profiles, and scenarios
  • Manage content repositories (catalogs, price lists, web content, images,etc.)
  • Identify new and innovative ways to serve customer needs through websites
  • Perform corrective maintenance to the website, including fixing broken links, adding missing documents, correcting missing data tags, and removing/updating outdated content
  • Prepare specific analyses and status reports on request
  • Maintain up-to-date and, accurate descriptions of major processes and data structures
  • Exercise judgment within generally defined practices and policies in selecting methods and techniques for obtaining solutions
  • Translate technical and non-technical requirements into prototypes, screen flows, and page layouts
  • Coordinate site architecture plans

QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Understanding of logical data models is a plus
  • Able to define use cases and scenarios
  • A solid understanding of web application technology, including: using templates, HTML, JavaScript, scripting language (e.g. SQL), ASP, PHP, Windows, and UNIX applications along with current technologies
  • Previous experience developing and/or supporting solutions on UNIX, WinNT, Web (HTML/Browser)
  • Expertise with relational databases or other database technologies, SQL, and various platforms along with current technologies
  • Solid understanding of IP network services and protocols
  • Familiar with programming using Macromedia Dreamweaver MX
  • Strong written, verbal, and interpersonal skills, specifically the ability to work well with a broad range of personality types and styles
  • Capable of identifying complex problems and determining appropriate solutions
  • Able to multitask and work on several projects concurrently
  • Able to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment
  • Understand and follow project management and development guidelines
  • Outstanding organizational skills
  • Attention to detail
  • Willingness to learn new technologies
