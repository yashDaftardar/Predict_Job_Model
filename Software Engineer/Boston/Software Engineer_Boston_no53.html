Job Description

Position Overview

As a member of the Intelligent Automation team you will assist and lead in the research, development, build, and support of scale remote procedure call, automation, orchestration, and heuristics-based DevOps/AIOps solutions across a wide swath of Workday’s technical areas. Our IA team has built several critical tools that support Workday’s SaaS properties, its engineers, and its management. Our products and tools typically go from a need based conceptual discussion with our stakeholders to production deployment. We follow agile methodologies. We look to amplify or reuse our existing frameworks where possible but are always developing and evaluating new technology to meet our customer’s needs:

Our key focus:

  • To maximize Workday’s automation investments by creating platforms and services that enable engineers the ability to compose, execute, schedule, and track highly repetitive automation and orchestration tasks through a consistent, intuitive, and scalable self-service framework.

  • Provide cost optimization by improving operational efficiency and business agility through automation

  • Provide risk mitigation by converting error prone human driven manual tasks into data, event, or response initiated repeatable automation as code at scale

Prior Experience or Education Equivilent

10 or more years of experience in industry performing a variety of technical and leadership roles but primarily in a development lead engineering capacity. A MS in Computer Science will be considered to be an acceptable equivalent if applicant has less than years of experience in industry.

Core Education Requirements

Bachelor’s in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or equivalent. Degree should be composed of coursework structured to build a core foundation in the theory of computation. Degree must include course work in computer programming and structure, calculus and discrete math, physical science and engineering curriculum (biology, physics, chemistry). Programming course work should be inclusive of common algorithms, data structures, compilers, OS Principles, database design, and statistics.
