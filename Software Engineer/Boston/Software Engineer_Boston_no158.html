Job Purpose:

Our engineers are responsible for the technical aspects of designing, implementing and maintaining oneZero Financial software products and features, including both commercial products and internal tools.


The Lead Software Engineer is a senior software engineer, expected to be able to understand and work on any area of the product, but who is primarily responsible for managing multiple software engineers and coordinating with other teams to ensure that the development process runs efficiently.


Lead engineers should expect to spend, on average, around 75% of their time managing the engineering team and development process, and 25% on actual coding .

This position reports to the Executive Vice President of Technology.


Primary Duties:

  • Learning all areas of the codebase, domain, and operational infrastructure in order to be able to lead other engineers with authority, triage issues effectively, and guide other engineers successfully.
  • Monitoring and managing the workloads and performance of engineers, in close coordination with remote engineering team leads as well as the Operations, QA, Business, and Technical Project Management teams, to ensure software is released on schedule.
  • Daily triage of incoming issues to ensure that critical issues are rapidly addressed, other issues are scheduled and assigned appropriately.
  • Mentoring engineers to ensure their work product meets expectations and improves over time, including bi-annual performance reviews.
  • Ensuring that our software development practices are adhered to, and that our product meets the very high standards of efficiency, reliability, clarity, and usability expected by ourselves and our customers.
  • Writing and maintaining documentation for our tools, processes, practices, and software systems.


Secondary Duties:

  • You will write software primarily in C++ with a focus on performance and reliability; you may use modern language features so long as they don’t hurt performance or readability. Occasionally you will need to develop and debug in C# and Typescript/JavaScript.
  • As needed, you will assist the operations team to efficiently diagnose and resolve issues that occur in production (e.g. forensic log and crash dump analysis, hotfix build generation).
  • Other duties as assigned.


Requirements:

  • Significant, recent, and demonstrably strong C++ development experience.
  • Proven track record as a successful leader of engineering teams.
  • Strong inter-personal and communication skills, backed up with matching technical skills.
  • Able to reliably estimate and complete assignments with a minimum of oversight, but able to take direction as necessary.
  • A positive, team-oriented attitude. Our team has exceptional productivity and high employee morale because we respect one another and work well together.
