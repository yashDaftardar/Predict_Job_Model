Full Job Description

At SS&C Intralinks we pride ourselves on giving each intern a real-world experience and opportunity to hone their skills, build their portfolio, network with professionals and build relationships, in addition to preparing them for a full-time position after graduation. Motivated interns play an important role in making this a reality, but we’re here to help!

About the Team:

You will be a part of a highly motivated, dynamic and professional team where you have a voice, and you have a chance to make a contribution and impact the future products. You’ll be part of the Infrastructure as Code team that works on developing and securing the infrastructure for all Intralinks microservice application. Team follows agile methodologies which helps in planning the tasks ahead and utilizing the resources well. You’ll be part of a team that has access to the bigger picture, not only their code section. You’ll be involved in all the processes needed to brainstorm, create and release new features as per the real time client requirements.

As an Intern on Our team You Will:

  • Works closely with Product Owner, architects, other team members to understand story definition and its acceptance criteria in order to translate requirements to design and implementation
  • Develop new features as well as work on stabilizing the products
  • Contribute in deploying application to various environments and perform regression testing
  • Extend the automation testing for the various Infrastructure as Code components and services

Required Qualifications:

  • Ability to solve a given problem and to work independently
  • Good command of one of the following programming languages: golang, Python, node.js
  • Good Understanding of Kubernetes and related technologies

Relevant Experience:

  • Experience developing SDKs for integration with RESTful services
  • Experience integrating and developing RESTful APIs for specific functions and services

Education

  • MS in Computer Science or relevant fields

  • We’re constantly developing and introducing new frameworks and technologies to ease up the development of new applications and bring them to life within months and deliver value. We as a team understand the value of guidance for new individuals who are looking for challenging work and enhancing their skillset to bring best in themselves.

At Intralinks you’ll have the chance to learn with and get support from our best architects, security experts, software engineers to grow into the expert you want to be. You’ll be working at our core business platform, so you’ll have great visibility and the opportunity to make a difference. *
