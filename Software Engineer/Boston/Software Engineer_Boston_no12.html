Full Job Description

Job summary:
The Red Hat Engineering team is looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join our Windows Containers team. In this role, you will work as a part of a team responsible for evolving and supporting our Kubernetes-native Windows operating system management for Red Hat OpenShift. As part of a geographically distributed team, you will collaborate with multiple Red Hat’s engineering teams and open source communities to help our customers succeed. It will be the enthusiasm that you’ll bring to contributing to projects and collaborating with a team to address the challenges that emerge as we evolve within Red Hat OpenShift’s industry-leading solution that will make you an essential asset to this team. Successful applicants must reside in a state where Red Hat is registered to do business.
Primary job responsibilities:
  • Collaborate with multiple skill sets and disciplines to design, support, and debug enhancements to Windows Nodes and Windows Containers
  • Work with engineers from multiple teams to continue our investment in supportable, scalable, performant, and secure architecture
  • Own your team’s component, measure and track its health, help colleagues and customers understand its functionality, and resolve issues that do not align with intended functionality
  • Mentor other engineers and provide and receive thoughtful feedback
Required skills:
  • Experience with and enjoyment of cloud infrastructure management
  • Ability to contribute to technical architecture discussions and help guide technical decisions
  • Clear communication skills; ability to represent contributions and ideas with clarity while remaining open to the contributions and ideas of others
  • Eagerness to share your extensive knowledge of Linux and Windows systems
  • Regarded as an expert in expressing business needs in languages like Golang, Python, Java, or C or C++
  • Ability to work as part of a team and take pride in team accomplishments
