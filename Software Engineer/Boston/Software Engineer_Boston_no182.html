Full Job Description

PatientPing is an innovative care collaboration platform that reduces the cost of healthcare and improves patient outcomes by seamlessly connecting providers to coordinate patient care. The platform enables providers and payers to collaborate on shared patients in real-time to reach their shared goals of improving quality of care for patients and efficiency of our healthcare system. Our investors include tech and healthcare investors (A16Z, Google Ventures, Transformation Capital, and F-Prime) and we are in our most exciting growth stage yet.
