Full Job Description

The UI Programmer will be responsible for developing, implementing (integrate, configure, and/or write code as needed), documenting, and testing software applications that support our Internal organization as well as our customer's. This role will work closely with our project management team in agile methodology. This role will also work with customers' team in a less formal method from time to time and will have to self-organize/self-project manage.

Communication internally as well as externally will be key for success in this fast paced role. This role will work with internal and external product teams as well as business owners as needed to understand the business goals, problems and challenges the software solutions are designed to address. This role will be required to think creatively about the most effective technologies, tools, and methodologies that can be implemented.
