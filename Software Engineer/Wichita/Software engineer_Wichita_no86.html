Full Stack Developer

Area: Corporate Office- North Kansas City, MO

(This role could be outside of the KC area with weekly visits to the Corporate Office)

Start Date: TBD

Cascade Health Services is a healthcare staffing agency that has been staffing top-notch facilities throughout Missouri & Kansas for the past 30 years. We were established in 1988, today Cascade is the largest and most established healthcare staffing agency, giving you security of a proven leader. Our reputation is extremely important to us and we take pride in what we do. Our standards are high, business is stable and strong.

Being an industry leader in the healthcare staffing industry we see it fit to design and build an innovative platform that will not only replace our existing systems, but scale to meet the demands of the industry nationally which will fill a large gap in the healthcare service industry.

Cascade is looking for a Full Stack Developer to join our team that wants to push themselves, and wants the opportunity to be part of a startup from its origins to growth , and ultimately through to established success.

What the role entails:

  • Full stack development across Java, & React
  • Creation and enhancement of modules & components
  • Use front and back end technologies to help design new structures
  • Create and maintain micro/web services for our new platform
  • Focus on developing scalable and adaptable software
  • Participate in code reviews & paired programming
  • Identify and take ownership of problems
  • Be part of the technical discussion & further architecture of the platform
  • Evaluate new technologies, techniques and tools to advance our development standards and ensure we maintain our technical advantage.
  • Work independently and as part of a team in an agile environment.
  • Adhere to and improve our foundational design standards.
  • Contribute to overall system architecture and design discussions
  • Other tasks as required, that you are qualified to perform

What we require from you:

  • Must hold US Green Card or US Citizenship as we are not in a position to sponsor any visas.
  • At least 2+ years' of experience in Java developing high-volume, full-stack, distributed/scalable, web applications and APIs.
  • 2+ years' experience of client side React (or similar) development using UI frameworks in an enterprise setting
  • At least 1 year of experience Spring/Spring Boot (preferably with Gradle)
  • Solid understanding of Java concurrency and multi-threaded application development.
  • Experience designing, troubleshooting and optimizing relational SQL databases.
  • Experience in developing RESTful APIs, & microservices
  • Strong analytical, research and problem solving skills.
  • Not afraid to ask questions, to challenge/be challenged, and able to work to tight deadlines.
  • Aspirations to lead development teams in the future
  • Growth mindset eager to work in a fast paced environment
  • Comfortable in a startup environment eager to roll up your sleeves to take on unfamiliar challenges to help the team succeed
  • Perseveres through failures and appreciates the valuable lessons of those failures.
  • Adapts to change; we will need to change processes or bend the rules to adapt to lessons quickly, so you need to be ready for this challenge

Preferred skills that will set you apart:

  • Experience with cloud deployment in google, azure or AWS.
  • Containerization (Docker, Kubernetes, Swarm, etc.)
  • Application performance testing and tuning techniques
  • Agile Collaboration Tools e.g. JIRA, Confluence, Slack, etc.
  • Git
  • Continuous integration tools
