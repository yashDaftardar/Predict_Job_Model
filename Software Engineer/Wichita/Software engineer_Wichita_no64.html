Full Job Description

What we want you to do…

You’ll be a critical member of a small development team. This isn’t an average position where you might be restricted to one small piece of the company’s technology. First of all, you will contribute to our website portals written in .NET. On top of that you will participate in brainstorming sessions and have the chance to contribute innovative and original ideas to our technology, algorithms, and products. You will work with our talented product and design team to understand end-user requirements and use cases, then translate that into a pragmatic and effective technical solution.


Who you are…

You want to work on challenging problems with smart people. You’re someone who could get positions at most software companies, but you’re looking for the perfect role that has great leadership, a roster of top talent, game-changing product vision, and a work hard/play hard company culture. You want to bring donuts for the staff each morning. Okay, fine, that last one’s not required, but you can’t blame us for taking a shot.


Some of the Preferred Skills…

C#, ASP .NET MVC, .NET Core, Javascript, CSS, and SQL


Some of the Perks…

  • Profit Sharing Bonuses
  • Health, Dental, and Vision Benefits
  • Company Match 401K
  • Company Paid Life Insurance
  • Company Paid Short and Long-Term Disability
  • Unlimited Supply of Caffeine
  • 100% Support from us in helping you master your craft


Minimum Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree in computer science or equivalent knowledge, skills and experience.
  • Experience in Web Development: 2+ years (Required)
  • Two or more years of experience with SQL Server development such as writing queries with TSQL and creating database objects such as stored procedures and functions.
  • Knowledge of SQL Server administration is highly desired.
  • Strong general technical knowledge with a minimum of 2+ years professional programming work experience.
  • Proficient development skills in Web programming languages including JavaScript and CSS.
  • Understanding of coding methods and best practices.
  • Ability to work with minimal supervision, strong problem-solving skills, results driven, and common sense.
  • Ability to follow projects through to completion, adhering to tight timelines, excellent verbal and written communication skills, ability to learn new technologies quickly, step outside of your own comfort zone, and handle unfamiliar challenges enthusiastically.
  • Enjoys work, loves to help people, and can work well independently or in groups.


Who we are…

PK Technology was the first to develop and create the concept of a Digital Data Management System (DDMS) designed to revolutionize data management through mobile technology. Basically, we provide real world solutions for real world issues.
