Who Is Affinity ?
Affinity is a privately held national Solutions Consulting firm based out of Milwaukee. We serve as strategic advisors and thought leaders to our Startup, Mid-Level, & Fortune 500 clients. Our clients and employees value our approach as a company “big enough to do complex, high impact work, but small enough to truly care”. We focus on hiring motivated, intelligent professionals who have a passion for their work and a desire to grow along with us! Visit our website at www.affinityit.com.

What You Will Do In This Role :
As a full-time Affinity employee, you will build front-end mobile applications hosted on AWS that have a variety of API integrations. Generally, the work entails designing application flows that contain one or more possible paths depending on the user data received through APIs as well as the user actions. Attention to detail is key as all your work is client-facing from the POC stage to full production.
Minimum Skills & Experience:
  • 1-2 years experience
  • Strong attention to detail including documentation
  • Exceptional communication & collaboration skills, with the desire to learn & grow with the team along with your career at Affinity
  • Experience building at least one iOS and Android application from scratch

Must Have:
