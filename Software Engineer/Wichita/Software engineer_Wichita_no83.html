Full Job Description

GET TO KNOW US

Hampel Oil Distributors, Inc. is a family-owned and operated wholesale marketer and distributor of finished petroleum products that was started in 1976 by Al Hampel. Now, with eight locations throughout Kansas, four in Oklahoma, three in Texas, as well as locations in Arkansas and Pennsylvania, Hampel Oil continues to grow with the times. Above all, we value Safety, Customer Service, Continuous Learning, Respect, and Value Creation.

JOIN HAMPEL OIL AS A PROGRAMMER

Are you a forward-thinker with a strong ability to bring solutions to the table? Do you have a strong technical aptitude and customer service mindset? Does finding solutions to technical problems with sometimes unclear solutions really excite you?

What You Will Do:

  • Participate as a member of a developer team focused on bring solutions to multiple business units within Hampel Oil
  • Gather and interpret business requirements
  • Own the Software Development Lifecycle for assigned projects
  • Organize and/or attend meetings with key stakeholders
  • Support existing applications
  • Collaborate with infrastructure and security team throughout the development lifecycle
  • Serve as the lead for the mobile application development project
  • Support corporate sponsored data automation project
  • Identify shortcomings recommend, and implement solutions

What Skills and Experience We are Looking For:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Management Information Systems, Computer Science, or related field
  • At least 4 years’ experience in a programming role
  • Experience with data driven applications
  • Microsoft SQL database experience

What Will Make You Stand Out:

  • Experience developing in SAP
  • Experience developing in Microsoft Dynamics
  • iOS development experience
  • Unix Shell Scripting experience
  • Database management and development experience
  • Experience with the following development environments: Microsoft Visual Studio (C# and Java), SAP (ABAP and BSP), Xcode (objective C and Swift)

The Team

You will be a member of Hampel Oil’s IT team which consists of 80 years of collective experience. As a member of the team you will share in the responsibility of supporting, designing, building, and implementing applications across the enterprise.
