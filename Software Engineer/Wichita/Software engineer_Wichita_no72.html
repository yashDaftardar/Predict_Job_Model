Full Job Description

Berry Companies, Inc. - Wichita KS is the administrative support group, which provides IT, accounting, data processing, payroll, insurance, marketing, and financial services to each of our 8 operating divisions. We are engaged in the sale, rental, and support of construction and agricultural equipment and located in Wichita, KS.

Berry Companies, Inc. is looking for a Front End Web Designer who will develop and maintain websites, landing pages and other web properties to support our locations from our corporate headquarters in Wichita KS. This position is utilized for web and interactive media knowledge and will be expected to provide expertise as it relates to those areas. Working alongside each of Berry Companies divisions, the Web Designer is an integral part of the growing, fast paced and energized Marketing team.

Responsibilities
  • Implementing website updates/changes and creating website layout/user interfaces by using standard HTML/CSS practices and WordPress page builders.
  • Customizing page layouts and templates into fresh, customized websites.
  • Collaborating closely with the family of Berry Companies divisions and the Marketing team to complete new and existing website requests.
  • Troubleshooting content issues.
  • Minor application feature development.
  • Assisting stakeholders in developing feature specifications.
  • Troubleshooting layout and content issues and determine resolutions.
  • Suggesting improvements, SEO needs, best practices, etc.
  • Collaborating with internal teammates and clients on marketing and creative objectives.
Qualifications
  • 3+ years’ experience in demand generation/inbound marketing and Digital Media Marketing.
  • Associates degree or Equivalent Experience in technical or related field.
  • A solid understanding of web application development processes.
  • Experience in website security and server maintenance a plus.
  • Demonstrated ability to execute successful projects with minimal guidance in a fast-paced entrepreneurial environment. •
  • Construction equipment or automotive industry experience a plus.
  • Experience with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Experience with popular Web CMS platforms such as WordPress or Joomla.
  • Experience with Responsive design best practices for mobile usability.
  • Strong understanding of branding and the creative process.
  • Strong understanding of page layout and design best practices.
  • Ability to manage project tasks, timelines, and communication.
  • Self-educator that stays on top of the industry trends.
  • Basic knowledge of lead generation strategies, API integrations and Google Tag Manager.
  • Google My Business and location listing manager (I.e., Yext) experience preferred.
  • cPanel or WHM control panel experience a plus.
The successful candidate
  • Has excellent oral and written communication skills.
  • Enjoys dealing with customers and employees/co-workers.
  • Has excellent computer knowledge and skills - business operating systems.
  • Prefers working with others than alone, and being connected with others on the job.
  • Is capable of working with team members in virtual settings across multiple geographies.
