JOB SUMMARY:

Seeking a result driven, detail-oriented front-end developer to join our growing team. In this role, you will take the designs created by application developers and use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to deliver a modern, visually appealing design consistent with corporate brand and marketing strategy. You will work closely with various members of the business to play a key role in the successful launch of web and mobile applications.

JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Participate in code reviews with the team
  • Review pages / sites developed by other team members to make appropriate visual and usability changes
  • Team with 3rd party vendors and ad agencies to create and implement new layouts and templates
  • Review designs created by web developers; ask necessary clarifying questions to provide visually appealing and intuitive user screens
  • Write code to implement visual elements into various web applications
  • Create prototypes, mockups, and staging sites for review and feedback
  • Optimize websites and applications to ensure fast load speeds and instinctual user navigation
  • Maintain websites and implement any necessary additions or enhancements
  • Collaborate with members of the team, including designers and back-end developers, to consistently improve functionality and user-friendliness
  • Maintain updated knowledge of the development industry and leading advancements in technology
