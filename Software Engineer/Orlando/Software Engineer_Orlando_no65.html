Will have an opportunity to work with a diverse team ensuring the successful completion of software
development activities aimed at standing up a new Lockheed Martin holistic horizontal full life-cycle environment integrating both COTS and in-house built systems

Will design, develop, integrate, document, test, and debug
software that contains graphical, logical, and mathematical solutions taking into account project constraints of scope, cost, risk and schedule in support of meeting critical mission requirements by use of software
technological means. Requires standardized programming development and user experience design using
existing industry-proven coding standards for internal and military applications in concert with corporate security
and backup and recovery compliance requirements for both hardware and software infrastructures. Technical
fortitude and dedicated focus shall be required for successful design implementations and software lifecycle
support for a model-based digital twin environment and holistic system that will both data interface and
physically integrate with multiple systems and tools in its support of operational requirements.

In this role, you’ll be standing up and integrating existing developed digital twin systems into a brand-new holistic user experience environment and web portal for all Program/project life-cycle processes. You’ll have a major stake in building from the ground-up the entire underlying software and implementation infrastructure, integrate existing and newly purchased COTS systems into enterprise data pipelines, and develop a universal web portal, user experience, and data orchestration environment for all MFC employees

This role is considered a software lead
and self-starter position. It requires knowledge of developing microservice and web-based system of systems from scratch and having knowledge of standing up and integrating out-the-box digital twin or enterprise data systems. You will be directing a small team of developers in achieving fast-paced milestones with Kanban agile processes.

This position will require the qualified applicant to articulate technical requirements to non-technical
customers and ensure their understanding of capabilities being developed and released to both internal and
external users. The position will require operational processes, procedures and technical documentation to be
developed that will ensure consistent, repeatable, and scalable support aimed to provide software development
and integrations required by both internal and external system stakeholders. This position interacts with
personnel on complex technical matters often requiring coordination, collaboration and decisive actions to assure
project success. Position develops technical solutions to complex problems, which require the regular use of
ingenuity and creativity while keeping management informed with status reports, metrics and key milestones

As a leading technology innovation company,
Lockheed Martin’s vast team works with partners around the world to bring proven performance to our
customers’ toughest challenges. Lockheed Martin has employees based in many states throughout the U.S., and
Internationally, with business locations in many nations and territories. Join us at Lockheed Martin, where we’re
engineering a better tomorrow.
Basic Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree from an accredited university in Computer Science or related technical discipline
  • Ability to obtain Secret clearance
  • Energetic self-starter able to learn new skills and implement rapidly
  • Ability to see the big picture and help guide and inspire others
  • 7+ years of implementing / integrating enterprise systems and developing crosstalk interface services
  • 4+ years’ experience developing scalable frontend web, web services, SaaS offerings, and REST APIs
  • Experience with systems such as Anark MBEweb, Ansys Minerva, OpenMBEE, or equivalent model-based or digital twin data environments/tools
  • Experience with Amazon AWS Cloud technology services
  • Experience with software version control systems (e.g. Git)
Desired Skills:
  • Master’s degree in Computer Science or related technical discipline
  • Experience with engineering, digital twin, and data simulation processes
  • Experience with deploying to or interfacing with Enterprise service buses (e.g. Spring Integration, Apache Camel, JMS, TIBCO)
  • Hands-on experience with API configuration
  • Implementation experience with a focus on change management and technology controls
  • Solid understanding of object-oriented design and programming concepts
  • Experience with modern JavaScript frameworks (e.g., React, Vue, Angular)
  • Experience with Relational DBs and/or NoSQL (e.g. MongoDB, MSSQL, MYSQL)
  • Experience implementing system monitoring tools such as Prometheus/Grafana
  • Demonstrated experience developing test plans
  • Experience with IT Cyber Security best practices for data system implementations
  • Experience with storing and transferring large data files over services
  • Experience with containerization technologies such as Docker
  • Experience with container orchestration technologies such as Kubernetes
  • Experience with agile software development and the Atlassian Tool Suite (JIRA, Confluence)
