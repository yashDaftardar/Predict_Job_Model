Full Job Description

Aurora North is a Burlington, Vermont based software company that develops software focused on enhancing legal operations, increasing efficiency, and reducing risk. Aurora North, an industry leader known for providing advanced technology solutions to law firms all over the world, is currently hiring an experienced Implementation Engineer to contribute to our growth and success.

Our company specializes in the installation and implementation of industry standard legal systems and enterprise solutions. We provide the highest quality software services to the legal industry, including software products for intellectual property law firms throughout North America and Europe. Come work with an outstanding team of passionate, focused individuals while continuing to build your technical expertise and business acumen.

The ideal candidate is hard working, detail oriented, has a strong ability to multitask, is a fast learner, is goal driven, proficient with technology, demonstrates excellent customer service skills and has at least 3+ years of experience with solid knowledge of: software development, SQL Server, data and system integration best practices, project life cycles, and collaborative development. We are looking for an energetic, fun addition to the team who is willing to take ownership of their role and grow within our team.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

  • Installing and implementing industry standard legal systems and enterprise solutions.
  • Creating integrations between standard and third-party systems using SQL, APIs, and other tools.
  • Upgrading production systems to the latest versions.
  • Migrating on premises solutions to the Cloud using REST APIs via 3rd party tools.
  • Owning the end-to-end implementation of Aurora North and partner software products and services. This includes planning, development, installation, configuration, deployment, and support.
  • Owning client communication process and setting client expectations on a project basis.
  • Assisting with product rollouts by ensuring client product owners and trainers are trained to be fully conversant with the software.
  • Prioritizing and managing own workload to meet client expectations.
  • Maintaining professional and technical knowledge by attending educational workshops; reviewing professional publications; establishing personal networks; bench-marking state-of-the-art practices; participating in professional societies.
  • Contributing to team effort by accomplishing related results as needed.

Qualifications

  • 3+ years of experience in a customer-facing role
  • 3+ years of experience writing Microsoft SQL Server queries with a solid understanding of relational database structure and normalization
  • Strong experience in the following technical areas: XML, JQUERY/JSON, REST/SOAP APIs

Experience with the following is helpful, but not required

  • Experience with Legal systems such as Aderant Expert, Elite Enterprise, Elite 3E, iManage, OpenText, NetDocuments
  • Intapp Time, Intapp Walls, Intapp Open, Intapp One Place, Intapp Pricing
  • Integration tools: Dell Boomi, Intapp Integrate/Integration Builder
  • ETL Processes and Best Practices
  • Sisense BI & Analytics
  • Microsoft Exchange Web Services/Microsoft Graph
