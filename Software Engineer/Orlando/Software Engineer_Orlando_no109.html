Full Job Description

KBR is seeking a Computer Programmer to support our team in our Orlando, Florida location.

Responsibilities include collaborating with product management and engineering to define and implement innovative solutions for the product direction, visuals and experience.

Illustrate design ideas using storyboards, process flows and sitemaps. Build page navigation buttons, develop User Interface (UI) mockups and prototypes that clearly illustrate site presentation and function, create original graphic designs (e.g. images, sketches and tables), identify and troubleshoot User Experience (UX) problems (e.g. responsiveness), and conduct layout adjustments based on user feedback.

Basic Qualifications:

Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Design, or a related field and at least 5 years of design, development or prototyping experience.

Experience must include the following: Electron application framework, Redux, PostgreSQL, Node.js, HTTP, C++

Must be able to obtain and maintain a secret level security clearance. Must be eligible to obtain CAC Access. US Citizenship is required.

Must have demonstrated experience in designing usable interfaces for popular web-based products, and Body of design work demonstrating a strong design process with interaction and visual design skills.

Proven record of high-quality UI/UX design and creating engaging digital experiences.

Demonstrated responsiveness and adaptive design principles and UI/UX best practices.

Must have working knowledge of development technologies such as SQL, JavaScript (ES5), HTML, CSS, React framework, etc.

Must have prior experience working closely with Project Managers and Application Developers to implement versatile front-end solutions to tricky web development problems.

Preferred Experience:

Creating visuals that elegantly organize and present complex data. Work to plan, wireframe, prototype, and design and develop software to create visually engaging, intuitive and easy-to-use products.

Developing high level and/or detailed storyboards, mockups, and prototypes to effectively communicate interaction and design ideas.

Executing visual design stages from concept to final hand-off to back-end software engineering team, and ability to take initiative within the remote environment. Comfortable working in a fast-paced iterative/agile design process based on frequent feedback loops.

Desired Skills:

The ideal candidate should have an eye for clean and artful design, possess superior UI/UX skills and be able to translate high-level requirements into interaction flows and artifacts, and transform them into beautiful, intuitive, and functional user interfaces.
