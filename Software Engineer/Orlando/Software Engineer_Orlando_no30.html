Full Job Description

WANTED: Intern Software Engineer with interest in software developmental, integration, and test. The work will support the efforts of the American Systems office in Orlando, with a variety of programs and team executions.


As the Intern Software Engineer you will:

  • Support customer goals for development of specialized applications needed to produce the required products.
  • Work with a team of driven, supportive, and highly skilled professionals.
  • Enjoy flexibility managing your work hours, college courses, and personal needs.
  • Perform software development, integration, and test to assist in the transformation of a proof-of-concept implementation into a minimum viable product.
Job Requirements

THE IDEAL CANDIDATE

  • Completed at least two years of under-graduate studies in pursuit of a degree in Computer Science or Computer Engineering from an accredited college or university with a 3.0+ GPA.
  • Experience with Python, C/C++, and C# development.
  • Experience with embedded systems development.
  • Experience with the development of user interface applications.
  • Experience configuring and using virtual machines.
  • Experience with the adaptation or use of open source for new applications.
  • Experience with network protocols, socket programing, and cryptography as utilized for TLS 1.3.
  • Is a good communicator and self-starter, capable of working independently and solving problems without the need for constant oversite.
  • Can provide recommendations from faculty and/or employers regarding applicable skills, experience, and work ethic.
  • Must be a U.S. Citizen and meets requirements for a Secret clearance
