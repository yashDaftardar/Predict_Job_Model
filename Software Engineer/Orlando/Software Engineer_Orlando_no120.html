Position Summary


In your day-to-day responsibilities, you will be analyzing user stories’ functional requirements, acceptance criteria, and design artifacts. You will create test notes that identify risks, edge cases, non-functional requirements, and test data. You will conduct QA kickoff, Developer in Test, and Blitz test sessions. You will assist developers with developing and maintaining automated unit and integration tests. You will balance the test automation pyramid of unit, component, and integration test coverage and perform exploratory testing. You will optimize/reduce dependency on end-to-end/automated UI regression tests. You will translate bug reports into reproducible test cases, track QA-related KPIs/OKRs, and Implement and maintain CI/CD pipelines.

Position Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in computer science, statistics, engineering, math, or related field required; a graduate degree is a plus.

  • Must have 7+ years of experience programming in C- style languages on professional software development teams, with 3+ years in a test engineer role for UI-rich Windows applications.

  • Demonstrable proficiency with automated testing, version control, databases, and XML required.

  • Must be detail oriented, have excellent communication skills, have a positive attitude, and be motivated to learn.

  • Knowledge of SmartBear TestComplete, TDD, BDD, and CI/CD desirable.

  • Experience working in an Agile/Scrum environment and with Jira desirable.

  • Helpful to have knowledge of project planning/scheduling processes/tools (e.g. the critical path method, Microsoft Project), or project management, or the construction industry.
