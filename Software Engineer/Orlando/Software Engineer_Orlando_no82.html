Job Summary

The role of Senior Software Engineer requires extensive experience in NodeJS, Java, and well-honed problem-solving abilities. The ideal candidate has a broad understanding of NodeJS, Java best practices and has significant experience working with development teams to deploy and support applications. To succeed in this role, one must be comfortable working on an Agile team, as well as be a collaborative, great communicator who thinks performance, scalability, and standards first to mindfully assist the engineering team members within an Agile team environment.


Responsibilities and Duties

  • Deploy company products utilizing fast, agile, and reliable software development practices
  • Identify design and infrastructure problems and create solutions in collaboration with other engineers in the engineering department
  • Ensure a consistent, high-quality product that works well across both web and mobile environments
  • Effectively communicate status, obstacles and challenges in the development process.
  • Execute on a strong understanding of SaaS based Enterprise tool development
  • Research, identify, and select the most appropriate frameworks to use with the product
  • Write and maintain code for the backend company products
  • Be accountable for code quality and writing tests (unit and integration)
  • Perform code reviews and participate in suggesting and supporting coding best practices


Qualifications and Skills

  • 5 years of position related experience
  • Demonstrated experience with NodeJs/Java
  • Demonstrated experience with building APIs and services


About OneRail

OneRail is an innovative technology startup located in Orlando, Florida. We are a delivery orchestration platform that provides multi-modal delivery automation, optimization, and real-time visibility for enterprise clients, all in one place. OneRail connects shippers to an extensive courier ecosystem to enable same day, parcel, LTL, next day, scheduled, and economy delivery selected through automation and rate shopping across all modes.
