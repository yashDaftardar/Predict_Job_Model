What DOMA’s Internship Program Offers

DOMA provides an internship program to provide students with real world experience of their field of study. Our goal is to develop learning objectives into the experience and provide valuable, real-time feedback on performance and application. Under the oversight and direction of experienced professionals, interns are able to practically apply the knowledge,skills and principles they’ve studied and be a contributing member of a work team. DOMA is committed to providing interns a fulfilling and challenging experience to invest in their professional development.

DOMA wants to ensure that all internships are productive
and beneficial to the interns/students. DOMA will support interns through:

  • Offering challenging projects and tasks.
  • Providing projects that complement academic programs and/or career interests.
  • Providing adequate, reliable, and regular supervision and mentoring.
  • Ensuring interns are keeping pace and accomplishing goals.
  • Orientation.
  • Enabling intern to create a professional network.
  • Industry Relevance.
  • Real life experience.

Intern Requirements and Qualifications

  • High school degree or equivalent; must be enrolled in an accredited university/college program to receive credit. Or have recently graduated from an accredited university/ college.
  • Proficient computer skills, including Microsoft Office Suite (Word, PowerPoint, and Excel)
  • Must be 18 years of age
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Self-directed and able to work without supervision
  • Energetic and eager to tackle new projects and ideas

Departments at DOMA Technologies include

  • Information Technology
  • Software Development
  • Human Resources
  • Accounting & Finance
  • Account Management
  • Sales & Marketing
  • Facilities
