Trader Interactive is looking for an experienced Software Development Manager to lead the development of groundbreaking new product initiatives utilizing cutting edge web and cloud technologies.

You will manage and drive a team of dispersed and off-shore engineers to deliver new online products for Trader Interactive’s online marketplaces. You will provide the technical vision and lead the development of new digital retailing tools.

You must have a proven track record of hands-on software engineering management, delivering internet-scale web products that reach millions of consumers. You must have demonstrated experience as a “full-stack” engineer with special emphasis on API integrations and SaaS. Additionally, you must have expertise with AWS, Azure or similar Cloud platforms. Experience building web-enabled widgets that plug-n-play onto any website is a plus.

Our products are enabled through a suite of technologies, scaled to support millions of pageviews every day:

  • Nginx

  • PHP

  • NodeJS

  • Vue

  • MySQL

  • MongoDB

Having exposure to these and the design patterns used in high traffic web environments will serve you well in managing a team of engineers charged with bringing market-changing solutions to market.


Primary Responsibilities

  • Own software engineering and delivery of large scale web-based products

  • Provide technical vision for new and emerging products

  • Lead architecture and technical design

  • Manage a team of in-house and outsourced engineers

  • Plan and supervise the integration of several external API-based resources into our own hub

  • Work closely with Product Managers and business stakeholders to create products loved by users

Basic Qualifications

  • 7+ years experience in Software Development with at least 2 years in management

  • Solid tech skills around the basics: software design patterns, implementation, unit testing, debugging

  • Experience building highly available complex web applications with AWS, Azure or similar Public Cloud platforms

  • Acute sense of ownership and the resolve to dig deeper into product and technical issues

  • Experience working in an API service oriented architecture supporting mobile applications and websites

  • Detail-oriented, organized, and capable of prioritizing workload to complete multiple tasks while meeting aggressive deadlines.

  • Strong knowledge of software architecture best practices, and relational database design is required.

About Trader Interactive

Here at Trader Interactive, we pride ourselves on the size and quality of audience that we deliver to our customers, a fun and collaborative work environment, and a unified company vision we call ONE TEAM ONE DREAM. We are a team of driven professionals with passion for our work and an infectious team spirit. Our expertise in digital has made us the leading online marketplace and marketing solutions provider in the categories we serve. Our talented team consists of over 350 employees who run 11 unique businesses from our home office in Norfolk, VA.

We run the world’s biggest marketplaces for lifestyle vehicles (RV, motorcycles, ATV, Snowmobiles) and commercial equipment. Some of our sites include RVTrader, CycleTrader, CommercialTruckTrader, and EquipmentTrader. Collectively, we reach millions of consumers, power thousands of dealer websites, and offer smart tools to assist dealers in more effectively running their businesses and serving their customers.

Trader proudly supports a diverse workforce, and we encourage candidates from underrepresented groups to apply. Trader is an equal opportunity employer where hiring is based entirely on business needs, job requirements, and individual merit.
