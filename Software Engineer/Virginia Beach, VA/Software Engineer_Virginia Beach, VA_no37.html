Place of Performance: Norfolk, VA

Duties:

The Contractors’ Full-Stack Developers, embedded within Innovation Lab JISR team, will under project manager supervision develop incrementally JISR Assets Planning Application (JISRAPA) by execution of the following tasks.

  • Design and Development of web application offering FMNREP services.
  • Design and Development of JISRAPA including:
    • Front-end web application
    • Back-end services utilizing micro-services architecture, and exposing WebAPIs for an easy integration with existing NATO ISR systems.
    • DevSecOps pipeline for JISRAPA .
    • Functional tests for both front and back-end.

Minimum Requirements:

  • Demonstrable experience with REST APIs design and development.
  • Demonstrable experience with Microservices Architecture.
  • 5 or more years demonstrable experience in full-stack JavaScript/Python/Java development.
  • 2 or more years’ experience in front-end development (e.g. React, Angular,etc…).
  • 2 or more years’ demonstrable experience in back-end development (e.g. Node.js, Django/Flask, SpringBoot, etc…).
  • 2 or more years’ demonstrable experience in database design and programming (mySQL, PostgreSQL, etc…).
  • Fluent in English (written and Oral).

Education
