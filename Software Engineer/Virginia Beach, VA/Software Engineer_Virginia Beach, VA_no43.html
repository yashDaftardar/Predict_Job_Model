LOOKING FOR: A mission-driven, Mid-level engineer with an active secret clearance to join our AMERICAN SYSTEMS team as an Associate Software Engineer in Dahlgren, VA. Candidate will support the United States Navy.

As an Associate Software Engineer you will:

  • Support to the United States Navy and Naval Surface Warfare Center Dahlgren Division Dam Neck Activity (NSWCDD DNA) .
  • Join a well-established team that gives you the opportunity to grow.
  • Receive robust benefits package that includes Employee Stock Ownership Plan!
  • Be a part of an empowered accountability culture where each employee-owner has a meaningful stake in the future success and growth of the company.

A week in the life of an Associate Software Engineer:

  • Conduct and coordinate individual projects required to support this effort, which is primarily focused on development of 3D gaming environments to support fleet training requirements
  • Design, develop and maintain complex, combat system simulations
  • Establish and maintain software development schedules and monitoring the technical progress of computer program module development toward these schedules
  • Utilize computer program development tracking tools that capture all new development requirements, tracks computer program defects, accurately reflects current implementation status of each item, and with job estimation activities.
