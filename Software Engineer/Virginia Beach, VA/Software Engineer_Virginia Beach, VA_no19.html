Job Description



The objective of the Office of Information Systems is to design, implement, manage, and support reliable and secure voice, video, and data services to meet the instructional and administrative needs of the College. The scope of this objective includes providing technical leadership, project management, maintenance, training, staff supervision, and other support for telecommunications, audiovisual, and network equipment and software, including applications that are hosted by the Virginia Community College System’s Technology Utility.


This position will support users of Administrative Systems by implementing projects that meet user needs and Tidewater Community College goals and objectives. This includes: requirements definition, identification of alternatives, technology recommendation, analysis, design, programming, documentation, user demonstrations and training, troubleshooting problems and implementation. Application development is performed using Oracle APEX. Serves as backup administrator for the college’s Student ID card system.
