Full Job Description

Resite, a growing Virginia Beach-based digital marketing agency, serving the Multifamily Industry, is looking for a Junior PHP Developer.

This position is an entry level position into a Software Engineer role, which is focused on back-end development. The Software Engineer will be working with our senior development team to design, implement, test/debug, deploy and maintain internal tools for our team and web applications for our clients.

Resite provides marketing websites along with add-ons to support the ongoing needs within the multifamily industry. Our proprietary CMS and frameworks dynamically serve marketing website content, as well as software applications for our client's customers (implemented and accessed via the same CMS) to provide value-added-services. Most notably around online rental applications and resident communication portals which includes the ability for residents to make online rent payments. An understanding of web development is essential. Our stack requires knowledge in Linux, PHP, MySQL, HTML, CSS, and Javascript libraries used by the front end team.

Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Willingness to learn and be mentored by senior developers for professional growth and contribute to the forward movement of the business.
  • Critical thinking, decision making, and planning skills to design the software.
  • Analyzing upsides, downsides, and risk of your design will be crucial.
  • Excellent communication skills to educate internal teams and clients on how to accurately use tools and products will be necessary.
  • Focus will be on the SDLC of the value added web applications. CMS, and the internal tools we use to manage our products.
  • Support of various 3rd party services: includes integrations with various APIs and creating tools specific for their integration
  • Provide Tier 2 Support changes for customer marketing websites
