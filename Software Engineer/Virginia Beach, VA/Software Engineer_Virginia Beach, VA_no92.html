Full Job Description

Dominion VUE , a division of Dominion Enterprises, is hiring an Experienced UI/UX Developer to join our Boca Raton, FL based team. This position will begin as a remote role, but will require applicants be able to work in our Boca Raton, FL office once it reopens.

Responsibilities

As an Experienced UI/UX Front-End Web Developer, your responsibilities will include working with the User Interface Design and Engineering Team, developing rich user interfaces and UI components for our enterprise-level Software as a Service (SaaS) applications, and providing valuable input on establishing policies and documenting guidelines for development methodologies in the UI. A primary mission of this position is to be a driving force behind creating consistency in our DominionVUE UI; not just from the standpoint of “look and feel” but function as well. In addition, you will be able to clearly communicate your ideas both verbally and on “paper” and both openly and honestly provide and receive regular input from your peers. While teamwork is of the utmost importance, you can also work independently with minimal supervision, and take the initiative to constantly keep yourself engaged. You never settle for second best. You possess a strong focus on quality and attention to detail and display the ability to quickly understand and solve unique and even undocumented programming problems. Naturally curious, you have a penchant for and drive to quickly learn and master skills in new technologies.

As part of the DMS team, you have a unique opportunity to join an exciting, dynamic and closely-interactive group of people, whose focus is to power the tools that run car dealerships and create robust browser-based user interface functionality, using the latest developments in front-end technology. This position will be responsible for working with interaction architects and other front end engineers to implement the web experience.

Skills

  • Keen eye for design and development
  • Hands-on experience creating high fidelity designs, wireframes, and clickable prototypes a MUST
  • Strong knowledge of HTML5 and CSS3 a MUST
  • At least 3 years of experience developing modular and maintainable HTML and CSS
  • At least 3 years of experience working with CSS pre-processor such as SASS, LESS, etc.
  • At least 3 years of experience working with web standards, CSS-driven layouts and a master of the DOM
  • Team player who is comfortable checking others' work and being peer-reviewed, as well as sharing knowledge with team members as needed
  • Evaluate, recommend, and customize tools & techniques used by the team
  • Excellent time management, problem-solving, teamwork, organization and communication skills
  • Understanding of design for scalability, performance, and reliability
  • Troubleshoot UX related problems, such as responsiveness
  • Strong skills translating functional requirement documents into UI design using HTML and CSS
  • Ability to follow technical specifications and production processes
  • Experience with source/version control like Git using Github or AzureDevOps
  • Basic understanding of JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, JSON
  • Prior experience with Vue.js a plus

Qualifications and Experience

  • Prior experience designing graphic elements using wireframe and design software; with an existing design project portfolio
  • 3+ years of experience developing enterprise level sites
  • URL samples and/or a Web project portfolio

Education

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering or in a Design discipline or equivalent experience.
