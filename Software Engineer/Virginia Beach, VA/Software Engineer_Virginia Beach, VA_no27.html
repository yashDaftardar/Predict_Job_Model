Full Job Description

The Junior Programmer position supports the securing, implementation, maintenance, and upgrade of Hampton University’s Student Information System (SIS)/Enterprise Resource Program (ERP), Banner. The successful candidate will provide initial Tier I/II technical support and functional assistance to campus faculty and staff. The Junior Programmer is required to assist in writing, testing, maintaining, and supporting PL/SQL scripts in a heterogeneous Windows, Linux, and macOS environment. As a member of a technical team, the successful candidate will provide backup support for other Programmer team members as necessary.

  • Duties and Responsibilities
  • Serve as a junior technical member providing technical assistance
  • Maintain technical documentation
  • Provide and support ad-hoc and audit reports
  • Assist with performing SIS/ERP system backup and disaster recovery
  • Assist with applying SIS/ERP security updates, patches, and upgrades
  • Support and troubleshoot Procedural Structured Query Language (PL/SQL) scripts
  • Automate data extraction, insertion, and integration with the SIS/ERP environment
  • Provide functional support to various Banner ERP modules
  • Analyze daily and real-time reports for several Banner ERP modules
  • Assist campus faculty and staff on various Banner ERP modules
  • Providing Tier I/II support for Windows, macOS, and Linux clients
  • Perform other technical duties as directed

Requirements

  • Associates’ degree in related field and/or equivalent training or relevant experience
  • Equivalent technical training and/or work experience may suffice in lieu of academic requirements
  • Current CompTIA, Microsoft, or other industry related certification desired
  • Must be able to pass background and drug screening tests

Qualifications

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Able to demonstrate strong critical thinking and analytical skills
  • Some familiarity with computer programming fundamentals
  • Understanding of Oracle Procedural Structured Query Language (PL/SQL) scripts
  • Experience using and querying Oracle 11g and 12c databases
  • Ability to plan, document, and communicate
  • Preferred experience working in a Linux/UNIX environment
  • Preferred working knowledge of enterprise relational databases (MSSQL, Oracle, etc.)
  • Working knowledge of Microsoft Office 2013/2016/2019 Products
