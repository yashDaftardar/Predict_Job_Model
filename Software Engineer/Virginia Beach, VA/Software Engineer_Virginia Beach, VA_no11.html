Peraton is seeking a Software QA Engineer with automated testing focus to provide web applications development and maintenance of DoD business management applications for Commander, Navy Installation Command (CNIC) programs. In this position, you will become a member of a small development team providing quality software solutions to DoD customers. You must be eligible to obtain a DOD Security clearance, which requires a US citizen.

We develop software to support management of our nation’s DoD installations. Some of the key focus areas in the software pertain to enterprise business modeling, performance data calls, and inspection systems Fleet and Family Support Programs (FFSP), Morale, Welfare, and Recreation (MWR), and Child and Youth Programs (CYP). We also provide cloud-based application hosting, cybersecurity, and management for CNIC applications.

As a Software QA Engineer, you will do the following:
Apply and grow your expertise and your experience in full life-cycle software development and operations while testing business management applications
Work in small to medium size agile environment to define, document, analyze, perform, and interpret tests for on-line applications that are deployed in a cloud environment to users across the world.
Adhere to sound software verification processes where high-quality delivery is expected
Create software test and automated test cases, perform application testing, validating bugs, and isolating issues
Analyze, summarize, and report test results
Assist in the review and analysis of verification methods and quality assurance processes
Work with government stakeholders who rely on you to verify and validate solutions for business requirements
Participate in planning, daily progress, periodic review, and lessons-learned meetings
Use tools to track your tasks, manage test cases and configurations, and document your work
Have access to on-line training to further your knowledge in technology

Required Qualifications:
A Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering, or other related technical field with 0 - 2 years of professional experience
1 - 2 years of academic or professional experience with testing and quality assurance of software applications
1 - 2 years of academic or professional experience with test automation tools such as Katalon, Selenium, Junit, or Sencha Test
1 - 2 years of academic or professional experience with SQL, JavaScript, HTML, CSS
1 - 2 years of academic or professional experience developing or maintaining software applications
Experience or training with web development frameworks, database technologies, web services, web application servers
Ability to obtain a Security Clearance, which requires a US citizen

Desired Qualifications:
Experience or training with Ext JS framework, Rest, XML, JSON

Experience or training with server page technologies such as PHP, ASP, JSP, ColdFusion

Web Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.0) Level A and AA

Experience or training with performance and load testing, and analysis of the results

An understanding of software development methods, such as Agile or Iterative development

Experience or training with Atlassian tools such as Jira, and Confluence

Experience or training with configuration management, automated build and continuous integration

Ability to perform as a positive, self-motivated, independent, detail oriented, and responsible team-player

Strong written and oral communication skills
