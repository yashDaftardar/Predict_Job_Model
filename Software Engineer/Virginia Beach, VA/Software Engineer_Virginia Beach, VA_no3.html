Come join HII! Where hard stuff is done right

Excellent opportunity to join an elite team in the development and testing of autonomous control software. What you will do

Internships are available to help students gain real-world experience in the work force. The intern will actively contribute to meaningful projects and work closely with a mentor and with senior leadership. Support the Engineering team with engineering functions in one or more of the following specialties: electronics, electrical, mechanical, civil, naval, and/or chemical. Assist with troubleshooting issues and provide technical support.

What we are looking for

  • High School Diploma or equivalent + Must be working towards a Bachelor's degree
  • Must be able to obtain a confidential clearance #LI-MH1

  • Understanding of object-oriented programming
  • Familiar with various design, concurrency and architectural patterns
  • Knack for writing clean, readable code
  • Understanding fundamental design principles behind a scalable application
  • Proficient understanding of code versioning tools such as SVN / GIT
  • Linux command line proficiency
  • Ability to quickly learn new concepts and software
  • Ability to use electro-mechanical equipment, measurement devices, and test equipment
  • Experience debugging within an Integrated Development Environment
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills
  • Excellent teamwork and interpersonal skills
  • Experience with Python and Java

Job performance will normally require only minor lifting and carrying of boxes of records or equipment.
