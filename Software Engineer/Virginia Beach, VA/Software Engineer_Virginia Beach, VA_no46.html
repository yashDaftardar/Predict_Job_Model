The Challenge:

Are you looking for an opportunity to not just develop software, but to build a system that will have a positive impact? As a full-stack developer, you can solve a problem with a complete end-to-end solution in a fast-paced, agile environment. We’re looking for a developer with the skills needed to build software from vision to production-ready system.

This role is more than just coding. We need a full-stack developer who will use their passion to learn new tools and techniques and identify and implement system improvements. Using Java, JavaScript, C++, and C#, you’ll work with the development team to build applications and embedded solutions that contribute to the satisfaction of business and mission needs. You’ll analyze the needs and the environment to make sure the solution you’re developing considers the current architecture and operating environment, as well as future functionality and enhancements. Join us as we build systems to change the defense industry for the better.

Empower change with us.

You Have:

  • 3+ years of experience with Java, JavaScript, C#, C++, .Net, or React
  • Experience with database design and development, including SQL, PostgreSQL, or MongoDB
  • Experience in working as an integral part of a team with a solid delivery background
  • Experience with software development, including Agile development
  • Knowledge of standards-based Web development, including HTML, CSS, or JavaScript
  • Ability to obtain a security clearance
  • BA or BS degree

Nice If You Have:

  • Experience with DoD software development
  • Experience with Containerization, including Docker or Kubernetes
  • Experience with ELK Stack, including Elasticsearch, Logstash, and Kibana
  • Experience with Telerik
  • Knowledge of web design principles, including navigation, usability, and accessibility
  • Possession of excellent oral and written communication skills

Clearance:

Applicants selected will be subject to a security investigation and may need to meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information.
