TQI Solutions is currently seeking a Web/SharePoint Developer ready to deliver outstanding support to our Navy client located in Norfolk, VA.

JOB DESCRIPTION:

Design, develop, test, modify and maintain web and SharePoint sites using one or more of, but not limited to, the following: SharePoint Designer, SharePoint Dashboard Designer, Visual Studio, InfoPath, HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, .NET, C#, XHTML, XML, XSL, Access or Visio. Develop complex and customized SharePoint tools, projects, and deliverables. Develop tailored solutions to dashboard capabilities using SharePoint. Design and develop user interface features, site animation, and special-effects elements. Design the SharePoint site to support the organization's strategies and goals relative to internal and external communications. Develop scorecards, reports, and dashboards utilizing SharePoint “out of the box” resources. Develop applications based on current, new and future net-based applications. Perform lifecycle support for application, web, and database development. Follow System Development Life Cycle (SLDC) methodology and software engineering standards. Capture user requirements and develop solutions to meet requirements for delivery. Develop Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) and provide updates quarterly or as tasked.

REQUIRED

  • Candidates must possess and maintain an active Secret level security clearance.
  • Four (4) years of related Web/SharePoint development experience. Up to two (2) years of job-related experience may be substituted for each year of IT/Cyber related college completed or for each current certification held.
  • Minimum of three (3) years job-related experience as a Site Owner or one (1) year as a Site Collection Administrator (SCA) of SharePoint 2013 (or later).
  • Minimum of two (2) years job-related experience utilizing SharePoint content management, including comprehension and permissions model, and training user’s in computer based skills.
  • CompTIA Security+CE, GIAC Security Essentials Certification (GSEC), Systems Security Certified Practitioner (SSCP) certification, or Certified Secure Software Lifecycle Professional (CSSLP) (or higher level certification) or Bachelor’s Degree (or higher level degree) from an accredited University in the field of Information Technology, Information Systems, Information Management, Computer Science, Software Development, Software Engineering, or Cybersecurity required.
  • Experience and expert knowledge of client side development and customization (e.g. site creation, solution development, creating lists, libraries, and workflows), using tools (e.g. InfoPath, SharePoint Designer, etc.) and programming languages (e.g. HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, .NET, C#, XML, XSL, etc.) for development.
  • Experience with Internet Information Services (IIS) and Microsoft SQL Server 2014 (or later).
  • Knowledge of SDLC Management, system analysis, structured programming, version control, quality assurance, methodologies, software testing, documentation, and end user support.
  • Must possess excellent communication and customer service skills

DESIRED:

  • Microsoft Certified Solutions Developer (MCSD) certification desired.
  • ITIL Foundation certification desired.
  • Familiarity with U.S. Navy processes and procedures and experience supporting Navy Marine Corps Internet (NMCI) and/or associated Navy network requirements is preferred
