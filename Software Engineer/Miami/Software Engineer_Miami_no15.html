About this Job


Spirit Airlines has an opportunity for a Senior DevOps Engineer position in a fast-paced environment that will be responsible for key enterprise applications. This position will manage several responsibilities concurrently and demands a high degree of initiative. Your strong technical leadership skills, and ability to communicate effectively at all levels will help to ensure your success in this role.
Responsibilities:
  • Support production application when required to resolve Tier 3 issues.
  • Follow the documented engineering handbook and enterprise standards to ensure a high-quality automated environment.
  • Participate in the product teams to move towards best class frameworks and DevOps disciplines to move into additional cloud services.
  • Partner with other technology teams to ensure correct technology decisions, deliver designs, technology writeups, and technology POC.
  • Improve speed to market and application quality by utilizing testing best practices, release automation, component releases, and release strategies.
  • Partner with the teams to help mature test-driven development practices and leverage continuous integration and delivery standards and best practices.
  • Mentor and coach the other teams on technology, DevOps methodologies, and delivery strategies.
Qualifications:
  • BS in Computer Science or equivalent.
  • 1+ years’ experience in writing software systems.
  • 4+ years of experience deploying and maintaining high-traffic environments serving a high level of requests per second.
  • Strong experience in Microsoft Azure.
  • Strong PowerShell scripting experience.
  • Experience with Team Foundation Studio (TFS.)
  • Experience working with AppDynamics & Splunk.
  • Experience with deployment testing and automation tools.
  • Concurrent Versioning Software Experience (Git, GitHub or Bitbucket).
  • Exposure in building scalable, fault-tolerant infrastructures.
  • Eagerness to learn and apply new technologies and skills.
  • Experience with 2 or more programming languages such as C#, JSon, XSL, Python, Java or JavaScript etc.
  • Experience with of configuration management tools.
  • Capable of troubleshooting application and system issues at any level of the stack
  • Strong communication skills - able to work effectively with technical and non-technical teammates.
  • Command strong leadership and critical thinking skills
  • Unafraid to tackle difficult challenges
  • Aptitude to build innovative solutions to real world problems.
  • Excellent communicator who summarizes technical details in a precise and succinct manner understandable to business users.
