Join our growing security team

Our team is part of the Cloud Security organization, which is responsible for technologies that power Akamai’s Security Products and protect some of the world's major brands. Working in partnership with software development teams we test, validate, bulletproof and act as end to end quality assurance team for every security feature implemented on Akamai’s webserver before they can be released on to our network.

Be part of improving our solutions

As a Software Engineer in Test, you will be responsible for testing the performance, reliability, scalability, security and functionality of our DDOS mitigation, monitoring and reporting applications, and contributing to our test automation portfolio. You will partner with highly technical individuals utilizing best software test practices in a collaborative and agile environment.

As a Software Development Engineer in Test, you will be responsible for:

  • Analyzing application, specification and design documents to determine test scenarios
  • Writing test plans, testing cases and testing reports as needed while also managing defects through to resolution
  • Designing and building test environments to cover functional and performance test scenarios
  • Developing and executing automated tests to ensure quality of the application under test
  • Collaborating with other team members during the development process
  • Working with product, project and development teams across multiple locations

Do what you love

To be successful in this role you will:

  • Have 2 years of relevant experience and a Bachelor’s degree
  • Demonstrate experience programming automation tests in Python
  • Have experience with software test and/or development engineering experience overall
  • Have exposure to network protocols such as TCP/UDP/HTTP/GRE/BGP
  • Demonstrate hands-on experience with UNIX/Linux Operating System
  • Have experience with shell scripting
