Beckman Coulter Life Sciences’ mission is to empower those seeking answers to life’s most important scientific and healthcare questions. With a legacy spanning 80+ years, we have long been a trusted partner to our customers, who are working to transform science and healthcare with the next groundbreaking discovery. As part of our team of more than 2,900 associates across 130 countries, you’ll help drive our vision of accelerating answers—and our commitment to excellence.

Beckman Coulter Life Sciences is proud to work alongside a community of nine fellow Danaher Life Sciences companies. Together, we’re pioneering the future of science and medicine, developing products that enable researchers in the fight to save lives.

Do you want to make a difference to patients' lives? Outstanding opportunity for an experienced DevOps Lead to enhance the development and deployment of our cloud-based data analytics application .

Fully remote position anywhere in the US.
Role Responsibilities
Partner with software development team during architecture and design phases including rapid prototyping to ensure the cloud-based data pipeline and web application are being engineered to be optimized for performance, scalability and reliability.
Guide globally distributed DevOps team to plan and execute on deployment of artefacts across Dev, QA, Staging and Production environments using Agile methodologies.
Design and support high-availability AWS services
Define and deploy monitoring, metrics and logging systems on AWS
Debug and triage issues, including off-hours as necessary
Design, manage and maintain tools to automate operational processes
Know the latest on emerging AWS trends, propose improvement opportunities

Experience:
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or other technical / IT field, or similar related industry experience
12+ year of overall experience with last 5+ years as AWS DevOps engineer
AWS Certification (AWS Solutions Architect, AWS DevOps Engineer) required
Experiences in building infrastructure using Terraform or AWS Cloud Formation
Expert in Docker and Puppet/Ansible
Experience with Linux OS
Experience with web application servers (Tomcat, IIS)
Experience with database management systems (Oracle, MS SQL) with AWS RDS deployment preferred
Excellent scripting and automation skills in Python, Ruby, PowerShell or Bash
Strong hands-on experience in deploying, monitoring, upgrading and troubleshooting following AWS cloud services : Elastic Cloud Compute (“EC2”), Elastic Block Store (“EBS”), Fargate, Batch, Elastic Container Registry (“ECR”), Simple Storage Service (“S3”), Simple Queue Service (“SQS”), Simple Notification Service (“SNS”), Lambda Framework, API Gateways, DynamoDB, AuroraDB, ElastiCache, EC2 Simple Systems Manager (“SSM”)
Experienced in designing and deploying AWS security policies in a multi-tenanted environment

Preferred Experience:
Experience with Jira, Maven, Jenkins, Slack/Confluence, Prometheus, Grafana
ITIL principles
Systems analysis and architecture experience
5+ years of prior hands on Java based web development (with exposure to JRuby preferred)
