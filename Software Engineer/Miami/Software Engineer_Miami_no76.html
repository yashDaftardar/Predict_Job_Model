Job Description Summary

The Digital Workplace Software Engineer Specialist will be responsible for working closely with the Digital Workplace Technologies team to drive development of enabling tools that allow employees to self-serve across their technologies, including the provisioning and management of their core technology platforms. They will be part of a broader software engineering community, working on various platforms and ecosystems to improve the overall experience and deliver additional capability across existing tools to our end user community. The projects they will be working on will be globally impactful, touching every employee across GE.

Job Description

Roles and Responsibilities

In this role, you will:

  • Develop high performing, enterprise scale, innovative end-to-end web applications

  • Design, implement and maintain React components

  • Document components and APIs developed

  • Collaborate with a multifaceted distributed team to design, build, and implement core platforms and solutions

  • Apply principles of key methodologies such as Lean/Agile/XP, software and product security, scalability, documentation practices, code refactoring, and testing techniques/automation

  • Participate in all phases of the software development lifecycle


Education Qualification

  • Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science or “STEM” Majors (Science, Technology, Engineering and Math) and 3+ months of software engineering internship experience.

Desired Characteristics

Technical Expertise:

  • Knowledge of ReactJS including its benefits and pitfalls

  • Experience implementing and maintaining React components

  • Experience working with JavaScript and CSS

  • Experience working with and integrating RESTful APIs into web applications

  • Experience working in a team environment

  • Knowledge of fundamental software development concepts

  • Functional understanding of TDD principles

  • Solid problem solving skills


Business Acumen:

  • Have a solid understanding of the underlying infrastructure needed to run a large distributed web application

  • Has the ability to break down problems and estimate time for development tasks

  • Has the ability to make basic technology choices based on experience

  • Demonstrates awareness about competitors and industry

Leadership:

  • Has the ability to take ownership of small tasks and deliver without supervision while using their discretion to seek help when necessary

  • Voices opinions and presents clear rationale. Uses data or factual evidence to influence

  • Recognizes collaborative behavior and participates in collaborative activities

  • Learns organization vision statement and decision making framework. Able to understand how team and personal goals/objectives contribute to the organization vision


Personal Attributes:

  • Demonstrate the ability to help team members

  • Seeks to understand problems thoroughly before implementing solutions. Asks questions to clarify requirements when ambiguities are present

  • Identifies opportunities for innovation and offers new ideas. Takes the initiative to experiment with new software frameworks

  • Adapts to new environments and changing requirements. Pivots quickly as needed. When coached, responds to need & seeks info from other sources
