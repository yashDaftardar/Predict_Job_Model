Job Description:


The most security-conscious organizations trust Telos Corporation to protect their vital IT assets. The reputation of our company rests on the quality of our solution and the integrity of our people. Explore what you can bring to our solutions in information assurance, secure networks, secure enterprise messaging, and identity management.


Be a part of the Telos culture and see what sets us apart! Telos offers an excellent compensation package with benefits that include generous paid time off, medical, dental, vision, tuition reimbursement, and 401k. Our employees enjoy more than just a great work environment!


This position will be based in Homestead, FL, and supports the execution of intelligence-related analytic functions and services. Candidates applying for this position must be motivated, creative, and able to work collaboratively across a wide spectrum of mission and technical personnel and technology and be able to work different shifts as required. Candidates applying to this program should have experience in military and intelligence operations, or other similar mission-critical environments. Candidates should be trustworthy, dependable, and capable of functioning effectively in a high-tempo environment where the candidate is part of a larger, integrated team of multi-functional professionals focused on mission accomplishment.


Responsibilities:

  • Serve as a member of the analytic development team , helping to implement an overall intelligence program designed to support United States and Host Nation forces within the United States Southern Command’s Area of Responsibility.
  • Demonstrate continuous efforts to improve operational performance, streamline work processes, and work cooperatively and jointly to provide quality, seamless customer service.
  • Front end/WEB development utilizing such tools as JavaScript & React/JS.
  • Complete assignments with independent thought and action within the scope of the contract.
  • Work cooperatively with other Telos Corporation employees, government agencies and other industry partners as required.



Job Requirements:
  • Bachelor’s Degree (in Computer Science, preferred), or the equivalent combination of education, professional training or work experience (6 years).
  • Must possess 8-10 years of experience in programming and writing algorithms to automate analytics.
  • Work for the US Government (as employee or contractor) for 8 years in this field is highly desirable.
  • Minimum active Secret Clearance is required – Must be TS/SCI Eligible.
  • Experience conducting intelligence in the areas of tactical and strategic SIGINT, Commercial Data, Collection, Cyber, and management is desired.
  • Must be able to demonstrate a thorough understanding and ability in at least three of the following languages (Python, Javascript, Java, C/CPP, PHP, Swift, Ruby, SQL).
  • Previous WEB development experience in a small to medium size team is highly desired.
  • 6 years of experience in developing software using the previously mentioned programming languages is required, with at least 4 years of this experience having been obtained in the last 7 years.
  • At least 4 years of either C/CPP Windows/Linux programming experience.
  • Experience developing solutions integrating and extending COTS products and technical writing expertise.
  • Firm grasp of modern computer programming and development with a minimum of 6 years experience in programming and development of application software for complex system and software testing.
  • Experience “wrapping” legacy systems or components as Web Services within a Service Oriented Architecture (SOA) framework.
  • Proficient rating (2+ reading and 2+ listening) in one of the following foreign languages, Russian, Chinese, Arabic, Spanish, Portuguese, and/or Farsi is highly desired.
  • Strong written and verbal communications skills and the ability to interact with people at all levels are required.
  • A calm, professional attitude regarding attention to detail and customer service and excellent organizational skills are required.
  • The successful candidate must meet eligibility requirements to access sensitive information, which requires US citizenship.
