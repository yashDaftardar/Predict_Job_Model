POSITION SUMMARY:

Job Title: DevOps Engineer

FLSA Status: Exempt

Type: Full-time

Department: Information Technology

Reports To: Vice President of Information Technology

We are looking for an experienced DevOps engineer that will help our team establish DevOps practice and a high potential that will grow into a management role. You will report directly to the VP of Information Technology to identify and establish DevOps practices in the company.

You will help us build scalable cloud infrastructure to support our various technology initiatives. You’ll implement monitoring for automated system health checks. Lastly, you’ll build our CI/CD pipeline, and train and guide the team in DevOps practices. You will be responsible for managing and maintaining SaaS integrations.

2. ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Build and maintain highly available production systems.

  • Design and configure continuous integration systems

  • Build out test and production environments via infrastructure as code automation

  • Create and enhance application and infrastructure monitoring

  • Develop cost optimization strategies

  • Assist in the architecture, design, implementation, and lead AWS/GCP public cloud build (connectivity, network, security, containerization, monitoring)

  • Automate infrastructure provisioning to stand up servers, install software and applications

  • Manage storage, compute efficiency, and optimization activities, including evaluating the configuration of compute size, storage solutions, and other services (network services, automation, and load balancing)

  • Assist with Integrations with SaaS systems.

  • Ensure that proper operational plans and documentation are delivered with each initiative rolled into production.

  • Collaborate with the Information Security Manager to ensure the company Network and Infrastructure systems conform to company Security policies and procedures.

  • Acts as a trusted advisor, and builds and maintains relationships with other IT leaders and business executives to develop a clear understanding of business needs. Ensures cost-effective delivery of IT services to meet those needs, and is able to respond with the agility required to address changing business priorities.

Required Education and Experience

  • Bachelor's or master's degree in computer science, information systems or a related field.

  • Background in DevOps, Software Development, Site Reliability, or Systems Engineering

  • Public Cloud Infrastructure Expertise (GCP, AWS or Azure)

  • Experience with Linux infrastructures, database SQL, CI/CD tools, scripting such as JavaScript, PHP, Python, Perl, Ruby

  • Full-stack knowledge of IT infrastructure, from applications & databases to hypervisors and networking.

  • Linux System Administration

The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.

  • Regularly required to talk or hear.

  • Regularly required to use a keyboard and computer.

  • Frequently required to stand, walk, sit, use hands, handle documents, and reach with hands and arms.

Position Type/Expected Hours of Work


This is a full-time position and hours of work and days vary Monday through Friday 9:00 am to 5:00 pm. Occasional evening and weekend work may be required, as needed.

Travel


Occasional travel will be required for this position.
