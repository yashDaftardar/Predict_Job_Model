Job Description

You will join a stimulating, fast-paced environment to contribute in the development of the next generation of wearable spatial computing devices. The role involves optimizing the performance and manufacturability of electro-acoustic transducers by integrating mechanical, acoustical, and psychoacoustic principles.

Responsibilities:

  • Acoustic software and manufacturing test bring-up, build validation, debugging and support.
  • Interface with manufacturing and QA organizations to ensure successful prototype and builds.
  • Liaison with acoustic technology suppliers or contractors (some international travel may be required).
  • Create clear and thorough documentation for internal and cross-functional reference.
  • Track milestones to ensure performance targets are met at each design gate.
  • Collaborate effectively and proactively within a team responsible for the design and deployment of a complex audio subsystem under strict schedule and architectural constraints.

Qualifications:

Required:

  • Extensive experience (minimum 3 years) in acoustic design/engineering and transducer characterization.
  • Solid experience with acoustic testing and measurement equipment such as Soundcheck, Audio Precision, Head Acoustics ACQUA, Bruel & Kjaer HATS.
  • Solid experience with software scripting tools/languages (Batch, Python, Matlab, etc.).
  • Solid working knowledge of audio, acoustics, and psychoacoustics.
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills.

Preferred:

  • Familiarity with audio signal processing, audio effects/enhancement, virtual/augmented reality.
  • Experience with hardware and software, wired and wireless, digital audio interfaces and related functions in mobile and/or wearable consumer devices
  • Experience with Android/Linux; Github/Gerrit, Perforce.
  • Experience with interactive audio software frameworks such as Unity, Max/MSP, AudioMulch, Audio Weaver or similar.
  • Experience with digital audio workstations and sound design/editing software tools.
  • Experience with team and project coordination, including Agile methodology, task scheduling, requirement and milestone tracking
  • Experience with authoring, editing and presenting technical documents
  • Experience with technical publication and patent application/prosecution.

Education:

  • Bachelor's degree in a relevant field (Mechanical, Physics, Acoustics) or equivalent experience required. MSc/MA or PhD preferred.

Additional Information:

  • All your information will be kept confidential according to Equal Employment Opportunities guidelines.
