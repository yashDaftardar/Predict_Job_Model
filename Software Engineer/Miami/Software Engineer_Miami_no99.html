Full Job Description

Manages Office of Business Services and FlU One Card IT systems including on campus and cloud based infrastructure, CBORD CS Gold interface, Oracle databases, web systems and applications, software integrations and other associated IT systems deemed necessary for the success of the Office of Business Services. Designs, implements, configures and maintains all system software programs, integrations, database and application development. Develops, monitors and generates system reporting for the campus card program. Oversees all IT hardware inventory and implementation for the Office of Business Services. Ensures the safety and security of the IT systems within the Office of Business Services.

  • Leads the IT department for the Office of Business Services including web and application development, system administration, hardware installation and maintenance, reporting, and developing and coordinating long term strategies.
  • Acts as primary resource regarding FIU One Card computer systems operations. Maintains the stability of One Card IT systems including web and physical servers, the campus card database system, and other relevant systems.
  • Sources, develops, generates and implements new systems designed to increase revenue, increase relevance in technology, increase ease of use and customer service in OBS operations and increases visibility of OBS within web and application based programs.
  • Develops timelines and plans for implementation of all new IT based systems for internal and external vendor applications.
  • Designs, configures, tests and implements new methods of payment services including and not limited to meal plans, stored value credit, and mobile payments.
  • Oversee the import of data to and from relevant university systems to one Card databases with specific emphasis on FIU's PeopleSoft systems.
  • Maintains software reporting and writes programs to interface with different systems. Produces regular activity reports for relevant FIU departments including FIU One Card.
  • Coordinates and ensures the electronic transfer of relevant information from partner university departments. Oversees the import of student and faculty and staff meal plans from University Housing and FIU Human Resources into the One Card database. Oversees the electronic transfer of Financial Aid Book Advance funds in to the One Card database and ensures the timely activation and transfer of these funds.
  • Oversees the management of all physical and virtual servers. Insures all servers are updated appropriately with the latest relevant software and hardware requirements.
  • Oversees data recover and back up plans for all servers and databases including maintaining and developing the One Card and OBS disaster recovery IT plans.
  • Acts as primary IT liaison for the Office of Business Services to all university departments including but not limited to Housing, Athletics, HR, University IT.
  • Oversees, hires and develops a team within OBS IT including employees evaluations, coaching, training and development.
  • Assists the OBS team with contract review of vendor IT systems and requests, presentation and training of new and relevant IT systems, updates and changes.
  • Performs other related duties as assigned or as directed. The omission of specific duties does not preclude the supervisor from assigning duties that are logically related to the position.
